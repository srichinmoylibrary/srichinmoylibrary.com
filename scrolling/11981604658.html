12430

Who wants to hear
The endless debate
Of the aggression-vital
And the suspicion-mind?

12431

Who loves God most?
He who never expects
Anything from God —
No, not even his dreams!

12432

To me you are
A failure incarnate
If you do not even attempt
To please God
In God's own Way.

12433

Don't be disappointed.
God will grant you
Another God-Hour
To love and serve Him.

12434

Today's happiness-drop
Is undoubtedly the promise
Of tomorrow's happiness-sea.
Just keep on praying and meditating.

12435

God has infinitely
More important things to do
Than be tired of humanity's
Sleeping heart.

12436

Doubt, insecurity
And impurity
Are the three prime
Spiritual death symptoms.

12437

I am extremely worried
Because my heart has not
Heard from God lately.

12438

My Lord is always tired
Of hearing my mind's sad news,
But He is never tired
Of hearing my heart's good news.

12439

A gratitude-heart
Immediately knows
How to multiply
Life's satisfactions.

12440

Smilingly to forgive
And blessingfully to forget
Is a typical day
In the life of our Lord Supreme.

12441

My aspiration-heart
Takes me
To the Giver of Love,
And my dedication-life
Brings me
To the Taker of pain.

12442

Nobody can always escape
The teachings of
Ignorance-tyrant.

12443

The mind sees the opening
Of frustration-gates.
The heart sees the death
Of an insecurity-seeker.

12444

Sincerity and purity
Can definitely reach
The highest oneness-height.
