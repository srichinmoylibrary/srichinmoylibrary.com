16101

First things first!
Don't delay anymore.
Go and invite God
To participate in
Your surrender-oneness-game.

16102

God Himself
Has given me the capacity
To declare war against
My doubting mind,
Dethrone it and replace it
With my soulful heart.

16103

God needs everything
From human beings.
Therefore, let us start
With our small heart-contribution.

16104

When ignorance tries
To snatch us away
From God's Oneness-Love
And we surrender to ignorance,
God sheds helpless tears.

16105

God does not need
Your explanation,
No, never!
He needs only
Your soulful dedication
And prayerful aspiration.

16106

If you want illumination
In your life,
Then immediately discard
All your barren mental speculations.

16107

If you do not live
In the Heart of God
For the Smile of God,
Then your life-boat
Will definitely sink.

16108

If you want to reach
The pinnacle-heights
Of ecstasy,
Then turn your life
Into a self-giving surrender-breath.

16109

To please God, my Lord Supreme,
In His own Way
Right from the morning
Until I go to sleep at night
Is my fondest aspiration-dream.

16110

The mind's emptiness
Is needed
Before God's Fulness can enter
Into the mind.

16111

If your life is God-oriented,
Then definitely God will reveal
His Oneness-Life
In and through you.

16112

Do you want to claim
God's Affection and Intimacy?
Then immediately kindle
Your aspiration-heart's
gratitude-flames.

16113

Your mind does not need
Fear, doubt, anxiety,
Inferiority and insecurity,
For it already has these things.
Your mind
Needs only love,
Affection, concern and compassion
From its sister-heart.

16114

O my mind,
Are you not embarrassed
That every time
God comes to your door,
You do not open the door
Through sheer unwillingness and obstinacy?
