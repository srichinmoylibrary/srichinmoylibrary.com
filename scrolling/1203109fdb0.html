17360

I am so happy
That I have finally started
To piece together
My shattered aspiration-life.

17361

He who is supremely great
Cannot be tormented
By man's ingratitude-thorns.

17362

Sacrifice
Is my inner name.
Determination
Is my outer name.
Lo, I have started tearing down
The towering walls of my doubts.

17363

My heart's
Gratitude-breath
Is my life's
Sacred flower-fragrance-garden.

17364

My Compassion-Lord Supreme
Every day tells me
That He will give me
Another chance.

17365

Who is your Master?
Not he who asks you
To touch his feet,
But he who asks you
To be his Eternity's friend.

17366

Only an unconditional surrender-heart
Has the capacity
To learn fast, very fast,
The God-manifestation-game.

17367

O my mocking mind,
O my suspecting mind,
There shall definitely come a time
When you will be utterly disgusted
With yourself.

17368

My life's outer success
May please my Lord Supreme,
But my heart's inner progress
Definitely fulfils my Lord Supreme.

17369

I am sure that
Tomorrow's heart-illumining dawn
Will teach
My God-searching mind.

17370

The happiness of my life
Cannot fly
Without my heart's
Aspiration-wings.

17371

God needs
My pure heart.
I want
God's sure Power.

17372

My meditation is
My aspiration-sky.
My meditation is
My dedication-ocean.

17373

Every day my heart
Takes a tremendous risk
In trusting my mind.

17374

Since God alone
Cannot satisfy me,
He takes help from
His Compassion-Light.
