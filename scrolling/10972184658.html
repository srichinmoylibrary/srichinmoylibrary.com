27 If You Sincerely Aspire ...

If you sincerely aspire,
You are bound to be
On an extremely high level
Of consciousness.

28 The Ruling Mind ...

The ruling mind
Eventually becomes
Its own prisoner.

29 My Aspiration-Garden ...

My aspiration-garden
And my dedication-plants
So lovingly and self-givingly
Live together.

30 May My Aspiration-Boat ...

May my aspiration-boat
Sail fast, very fast,
On the river of my life.

19 December 2005 ...

19 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

31 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I promise to You
That I shall once more please You
In Your own Way.

32 If Only I Had Listened ...

If only I had listened
To my Lord Supreme,
By today undoubtedly
I could have become
An excellent seeker.

33 My Prayerful And Soulful Singing ...

My prayerful and soulful singing
I place only
At my Lord’s Feet.

34 How Can The Mind ...

How can the mind
Have an iota of peace
When it is overwhelmed
By desire-thoughts?

35 God’s Most Precious Discovery ...

God’s most precious discovery:
Man’s tiny gratitude-heart.

36 O My Mind ...

O my mind,
Your excuse list is so rapidly
And painfully increasing.

37 Every Day ...

Every day
The seeker must enjoy —
And not just accept —
God’s living Presence.

38 If You Really Want To Do ...

If you really want to do
Something good for God,
Then do not plan ahead.
God’s Will will blossom
In God’s Way
Inside your heart.

39 God’s Only Desire ...

God’s only desire
Is man’s selflessness.

40 God Wants To Be ...

God wants to be
Your sole possession
Only if you have no question,
And also if you have
Only one longing:
God-realisation.
