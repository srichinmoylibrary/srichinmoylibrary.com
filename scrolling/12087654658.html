22930

At sunrise
I meditate
On God's Cosmic Sound.
At sunset
I meditate
On God's Universal Silence.

22931

Even my mind is astonished
At its own
Stupidity-cyclones.

22932

If I have anything
To be proud of in my life,
Then it is the purity
Of my heart-temple.

22933

God's Forgiveness
Does not depend on
My mind's clever stories,
But on
My heart's pure songs.

22934

Of all the planes that fly
In the inner world,
Surrender-plane flies the highest.

22935

In vain
God's Compassion-Light
Has toured
My unwillingness-mind-world.

22936

God never drags me.
God never pushes me.
It is my soul that drags my mind
Towards God.
It is my soul that pushes my heart
Towards God.

22937

My mind's determination
Means
My life's transformation.
My life's transformation
Means
My Lord's complete Satisfaction.

22938

A spiritual Master
Is at once
The safest and the longest
Ladder
To climb towards Heaven.

22939

I am filled with fear
When I have to touch
God's Eyes.
I am inundated with delight
When I am able to feel
God's Heart.

22940

Love of God
Is my real name.
Desire-fulfilment-cry
Is my false name.

22941

God performs
His unconditional Duty:
He implores and implores.
I perform
My determined duty:
I ignore and ignore.

22942

O my Lord Supreme,
If You really love me,
Then take away my mind's
False freedom,
And place me at Your Feet
To enjoy real freedom.

22943

My heart's purity
Has finally silenced
My mind's desire-storm.

22944

My Lord,
You want me to be happy,
But do You not see
That jealousy's eye
Is still looking
Through me?
