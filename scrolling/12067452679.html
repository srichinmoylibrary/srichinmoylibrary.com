20915

Each desire
Is to be praised
Not when it comes,
But when it goes away.

20916

If you do not want to expose
Some things in your life,
No harm.
But expose your disobedience
To your own inner life,
So that your outer life
Will be ashamed of what it does
And will be ready
To be a perfect instrument
Of your inner life.

20917

For whom is enough too little?
Only for those
Who want to be blessed
By God's infinite Light
And Delight.

20918

If you want to prove
That you love God,
Then do not ask God
To satisfy your wants.
Ask God to satisfy your needs,
If so is His Will.

20919

You cannot claim God
As a true Friend
Who will come to your rescue
Whenever you need Him,
If you continue to live
In your same old way.

20920

God does not want you
To remain unchanged by time.
He wants you to be changed
Every day for the better.
He wants you to become
A choice and perfect instrument of His
By transforming your earth-life.

20921

You want to know where
My aspiration-flames were born?
My aspiration-flames were born
In my Lord's ceaseless
Compassion-Smiles.

20922

I am safe
Only at one place
And that place is
God's Fulfilment-Heart-Home.

20923

Nothing is as close
To the Heart of God
As my own soulfulness-heart.

20924

If my foot slips and I fall,
God is bound to come to save me
Only if I have the crying heart
Of a child.

20925

O my obedience-happiness-day,
I have long lost you.
Do come back.
Your reappearance and constant presence
In my life of aspiration
I sleeplessly need.

20926

Love, devotion, surrender —
These are not experiments
That my mind can make.
These are the real heart-experiences
That my life can treasure.

20927

Nothing divine,
Nothing illumining,
Nothing fulfilling
Can ever go away
From my gratitude-heart
And surrender-life.

20928

O my mind,
If you want to compromise
With the business-mind-world,
Then do it.
But rest assured,
My heart will make no compromise.
My heart will always be
In the Heart of my Lord Beloved Supreme,
Only for Him, only for Him.

20929

My heart needs only one thing:
My Lord's Compassion-Smiles.
My heart will remain always
In my Lord Supreme,
With my Lord Supreme
And for my Lord Supreme.
My heart is always, eternally, breathlessly
On its way
To the Golden Shore.
