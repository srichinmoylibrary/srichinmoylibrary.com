10430

If your aspiration-heart
Is not functioning well,
Then invite its best friend,
Dedication-life,
To come and help it.

10431

If you are in your heart,
You will be in your aspiration-boat.
If you are in your soul,
You will be at your Golden Shore.

10432

A purity-heart is at once
A seeker's life-protection
And his mind-illumination.

10433

Anxiety stabs
The human mind.
Purity gladdens
The divine heart.

10434

To invite a few thoughts
Is not enough.
To invite new thoughts
Is not enough.
To become the owner of divine thoughts
Is more than enough.

10435

He is happy in the morning
When he offers
His heart's aspiring cry
To the rising sun.

He is happy at night
When he offers
His life's dedication-smile
To the beaming moon.

10436

God is getting ready
To enter into your doubting mind,
And you are getting ready
To enter into God's smiling Heart.

10437

Unwillingness is draining
Your life-energy.
Therefore, how can God sing
His Satisfaction-Song
Inside your heart?

10438

He begs God
To grant him the capacity
To work
And grow into a sea of light
Under God's Justice-Supervision.

10439

If you come out
Of your insecurity-cave,
God will not only show you
His supreme Palace
But also allow you
To live there permanently.

10440

With your faltering aspiration-steps,
How far can you go?
With your weak dedication-hands,
How much can you offer?

10441

The fragrance
Of a purity-flower-heart
Is cherished
By God's earth-energising Breath.

10442

The desire-world
Is made of falsehood.
But alas,
It has the tremendous capacity
To fascinate the entire human life.

10443

Explore, every day explore
Your unexplored gratitude-life
To expand your divinity's
Light-existence.

10444

God's Compassion-Eye
And my heart's aspiration-flame
Immensely liked each other
Right from the very beginning.
