23830

What we need is a freedom-breath
For a newness-eye
And a fulness-life
To be God's choicest instruments.

23831

Your prayers will, without fail,
Be sanctioned
If you can develop moonbeam-eyes
Of purity
In your self-offering-life.

23832

My heart-bird soars
Into Infinity's sky
When my mind is inundated
With enthusiasm
And my vital is surcharged
With dynamism.

23833

Yesterday
My mind deliberately enjoyed
Turbulent confusion.
Today
The same mind enjoys
Unreservedly opulent illumination.

23834

O my mind,
You desperately need something
Radically different
From what you have now.
You need to enter into the life
Of self-offering and God-becoming.

23835

God's Compassion-Will
Has prepared the way.
Now we need our own aspiration-intensity
To accompany us
If we want to reach the ultimate Destination
Sooner than the soonest.

23836

The Smile of God's Eye
And the Embrace of God's Heart
Are meant only for those
Who sleeplessly and breathlessly cry
For God's Satisfaction in God's own Way.

23837

I want my body, my vital,
My mind and my heart
To enjoy every day
A gratitude-festival.

23838

You may know how to climb,
You may know how to cling,
But as long as you do not know
How to offer yourself unconditionally,
Your God remains a distant goal.

23839

The effacement of the mind
And the enlightenment of the heart
Can definitely carry us
To the Shores of the furthest Beyond.

23840

When we take pride
In our doubting mind,
In sinking tragedies
Our lives we find.

23841

My climbing aspiration
Is the fulfilment
Of my Lord's all-illumining Dream.

23842

Without an Olympian courage
We cannot dive deep within
To swim in the sea of ecstasy.

23843

Only one thing
Can satisfy my soul,
And that thing is
My mind's unconditional surrender
To my heart's ever-blossoming beauty
And ever-enduring fragrance.

23844

Success is our mind's
Non-stop journey.
Progress is our heart's
Ever-illumining destination.
