12815

With a tireless self-giving,
We can see the arrival
Of our Beloved Supreme
At our heart's aspiration-door.

12816

Truth confidently marches
Along Eternity's Road.
Falsehood timidly runs
Along darkness-thickening lane.

12817

Your own unhorizoned greatness
Will help you appreciate
The freedom of the birds.
Your own infinite goodness
Will help you enjoy
The flight of the birds.

12818

The more you observe
What the mind has done for you,
The more you will appreciate
What the heart has not done for you.

12819

I may not know
Where my mind of simplicity is,
But I do know
Where my heart of sincerity eternally is.
It is inside the Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Supreme.

12820

The mind thinks
That the heart is stupid.
The heart feels
That the mind can be made better.

12821

A mind without doubt
Is indeed
A colossal surprise.

12822

A soulful heart
Sleeplessly radiates
Its beauty-smiles.

12823

Lethargy loves
To live permanently
In the body-room.

12824

Selflessness
Is a seeker's greatest capacity
For keeping the peace-torch illumined.

12825

My heart's peace-temple
Is always open
To all.

12826

Swim, swim, swim
Across the mind's
Wide ignorance-channel!

12827

Gratitude
Is by far the best virtue
For nourishing aspiration-plants.

12828

Temptation is never invited
To purity's
Illumination-convention.

12829

My heart sleeplessly cries
To be in the company
Of self-giving God-seekers.
