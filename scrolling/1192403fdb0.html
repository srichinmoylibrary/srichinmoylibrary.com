6760

To awaken
The seeker's sleeping silence
The spiritual Master
Unconditionally knocks
At the seeker's heart-door.

6761

You fool!
Have you not realised
That your gratitude-plant
Has stopped growing completely
In God's Heart?

6762

Humility
Opens the heart-door.
Purity
Receives the Absolute Supreme.

6763

Who can be more miserable
Than a seeker who is a dropout
From God's Illumination-School?

6764

He struggled with his conscience
For a long time.
Alas, finally he surrendered it
To temptation-night.

6765

I simply do not know
How a human being can live on earth
Without having hope-plants
In his heart-garden.

6766

If your heart's cry
Is persuasive,
Then God's pervasive
Compassion-Smile
Shall be all yours.

6767

You can live without God,
But poor God does not
And cannot
Live without you.

6768

I get a pain in my heart
When my mind
Speaks ill of God's Compassion
And ignores God's Forgiveness.

6769

He lives
To do only one thing:
To forgive
The unaspiring being in himself
Every day.

6770

My first impression of God:
He is infinitely more indulgent
Than I thought.

6771

This choice moment of mine
I shall offer only to him
Who loves God infinitely more
Than I do.

6772

My thought-world
Prepares me.
My deed-world
Builds me.
My God-world
Immortalises me.

6773

There is not a single day
When his doubting mind
Does not enjoy
The fear-fabrication-game.

6774

Now that you have discarded
Your mind-rubbish,
Your life-prosperity will flourish
In God's Heart-Garden.
