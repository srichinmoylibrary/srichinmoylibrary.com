13 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart is swimming
In the sea of tears
Because my sister Lily
Is suffering so much.
She is helpless in India
And I am helpless here in America.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 May 1999, 7:54 a.m. Before lifting 280 lbs. seventy-eight times with one arm. (38 right, 40 left)

14 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I am sending my soul's joy,
My heart's love and my life's concern
Into the world unreservedly.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 May 1999, 7:16 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 210 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

14 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I must illumine
My outer life-tree
With my inner heart's purity.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 May 1999, 7:28 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty-six times with one arm. (28 right, 28 left)

14 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am extremely, extremely
Grateful to You
For allowing my life to be near You.
I am extremely, extremely
Grateful to You
For giving me the capacity
To live only for You,
Only for You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 May 1999, 7:45 a.m.

16 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please, please bless me
With an ever-blossoming
Gratitude-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 May 1999, 5:28 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 230 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

16 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please bless me
With a sleeplessly unconditional
World-service-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 May 1999, 5:45 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm sixty-one times. (29 right, 32 left)

16 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart love
Each and every human being
In the world
The way You love my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 May 1999, 6:10 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 180 lbs. with each arm separately.

17 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Compassion-Eye
Is the only sweetness
In my aspiration-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 May 1999, 7:28 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 230 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

[The morning after the passing of Sri Chinmoy's beloved sister Lily]

17 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Encouragement-Heart
Is the only fulness
In my dedication-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 May 1999, 7:47 a.m.

18 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have four special desires:
May I have a God-searching mind,
May I have a God-aspiring heart,
May I have a God-serving life,
May I be a God-manifesting seeker.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 May 1999, 7:06 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 230 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

18 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart sing
A sweetness-oneness-song with You.
May my life dance
A oneness-fulness-dance with You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 May 1999, 7:25 a.m.

18 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must come back to Your Feet,
To Your Lotus-Feet,
To Your Nectar-Feet.
I must come back immediately,
I must come back breathlessly,
I must come back unconditionally.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 May 1999, 7:39 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. sixty-four times with one arm. (32 right, 32 left)

19 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The human in me
Is the flickering of an eye.
The divine in me
Is Eternity's aspiration,
Infinity's vision
And
Immortality's manifestation.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 May 1999, 5:51 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 230 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

19 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May each human life be
A divinely manifested
Dream of God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 May 1999, 6:10 a.m.

19 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life reject
Once and for all
The slavery of endless desires.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 May 1999, 6:32 a.m. Before lifting 210 lbs. four times with one arm. (2 right, 2 left)
