9 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O seeker,
Your God-Compassion-doubt
Is
Your spirituality's immediate death.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 September 2000, 6:14 a.m. Before doing 640-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

9 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The Smiles of Your Compassion-Eye
Have liberated me from my mind's
Bondage-ignorance-night-abyss.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 September 2000, 6:23 a.m. Before doing 840-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

9 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must conquer my mind's frustrations.
I must conquer my heart's depressions.
I must conquer my life's failures.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 September 2000, 6:39 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift.

10 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I do not want You to be on my side.
I want my very existence on earth
To be always on Your side.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 September 2000, 6:43 a.m. Before doing 650-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

10 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May each morning be for me
A golden God-yearning, God-loving,
God-serving and God-manifesting
Opportunity.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 September 2000, 6:49 a.m. Before doing 850-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

10 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
How far my mind is away from God,
God alone knows.
But how far my heart is away from God,
Not only God knows but I also know.
The distance is most astonishingly
Negligible.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 September 2000, 7:01 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

12 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When the human in me ascends,
You smile and smile and smile.
When the divine in me descends,
You cry and cry and cry.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 September 2000, 11:16 p.m. Before doing 660-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

12 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Will it ever happen
That I shall think of You
Before You think of me?
Will it ever happen
That I shall love You
Before You love me?
"My child, I shall help you.
When you succeed,
I shall be infinitely happier
Than you can ever imagine."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 September 2000, 11:29 p.m. Before doing 860-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

13 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My sleepless and breathless
God-gratitude-tears
Are infinitely sweeter and purer
Than anything else.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 September 2000, 8:45 a.m. Before doing 660-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

13 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind, there is only one way
To have happiness,
And that way is to surrender
To God's Will
All the time.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 September 2000, 8:54 a.m. Before doing 860-lb. dumbbell bench press.

14 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God will never allow a true seeker
To escape His Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 September 2000, 8:39 a.m. Before doing 670-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

14 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind,
Not thick attachment-fog,
But blue detachment-sky!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 September 2000, 8:50 a.m. Before doing 870-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

14 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The divine beauty of life
Depends on the purity of the mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 September 2000, 9:03 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

15 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my mind be silenced
By my soul's ecstasy-breath.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 September 2000, 1:21 a.m. Before doing 680-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

15 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life be liberated
By my soul's ecstasy-embrace.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 September 2000, 1:32 a.m. Before doing 880-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.
