21860

O my doubtful experience-life,
I do not want
To waste my time anymore
With you,
For my soul's God-realisation
Is beckoning me.

21861

When I see myself
Through my own eyes,
I see and feel
That my life is not an inch better
Than the life of a beast.

When I see myself
Through God's Eye,
I see and feel
That I am on the fair road
To Godhood.

21862

When I belong to my heart's cry,
I see God right in front of me.
When I belong to God's Smile,
I feel my inseparable oneness
With my Lord Beloved Supreme.

21863

God tells me
Not to be disappointed
When I see I must lower myself
In order to rise high, higher, highest.

21864

The farthest reach
Of my inner faith
Is not my God-realisation
Or God-revelation,
But my God-manifestation.

21865

O ignorance-night,
Are you not ashamed
Of harassing me and my life
Constantly?

21866

Do not squander time,
For time is the breath
Of your prayer-mind
And aspiration-heart.

21867

God is always ready
With His Praise for you.
When will you be ready
With your divine receptivity?

21868

Love, devotion, surrender:
These three words
Are absolutely foreign
To a doubting mind
And strangling vital.

21869

When we are constant
In our humility,
God's Divinity
On its own
Comes down and befriends us.

21870

My very desire
To make progress
Is the first step
Of my progress.

21871

Since somebody has to have
The last word,
I have decided my soul
Should perform that task
In my life.

21872

Let us restore
Our ancient aspiration-land
Inside our own hearts.

21873

I have been to God many times
Empty-handed,
But this time I have decided
That I shall give Him
What I truly consider my own:
My little "i."

21874

God is under no obligation
To accept me as His consultant
For everything that He does.
