6515

Each man
Consciously or unconsciously
Tries to shelter
His shattered dreams.

6516

I may not know many things,
But I do know one thing.
I do know how to dance
To my Lord's Compassion-Cadence.

6517

Do not try to hide
Your ignorance-idleness,
For it is almost
An impossible task.

6518

This world has brought you
To your knees,
Yet at every moment
You speak ill of God
Instead of begging for
His Compassion-Light.

6519

You are a self-styled seeker.
Therefore
You do not care
For God's Manifestation-Play.

6520

Since the inner life
Is not for you,
What more can I expect from you
Than an ingratitude-life?

6521

Your life knows
The philosophy of failure
Because your mind has surrendered
To ignorance-ordeals.

6522

God does not need
Your suggestions.
He is quite satisfied
With what He has
An who He is.

6523

Do not take the world's criticism
Personally
If you want to make progress
Swiftly and unquestionably.

6524

You have deliberately cast aside
Countless opportunities
To realise God,
Yet you blame God breathlessly.

6525

Why is God so silent?
Because you have not answered
His first question:
Who are you?

6526

Food for thought:
You have walked along
Your mind's road
And you have come to a dead end.
Now you wonder why
You do not see
Your inner streak of progress-light.

6527

Do you not realise
That it is high time
For you to offer yourself
Unconditionally to God
So that God can claim you
As His own, very own?

6528

A God-lover knows
That life is a supreme gift
And not
An unbearable imposition.

6529

If you walk
Along the paths of long ago,
How can you forget
Your life's dry failure-tears?
