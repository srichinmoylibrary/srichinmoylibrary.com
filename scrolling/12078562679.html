22015

You are disaster-prone
In the inner world
Because you have implicit faith
In your doubting mind.

22016

If you nurse hope
In your aspiration-heart,
Then God will come and join you
With His Promise-Eye.

22017

No matter what I learn
Through my eyes,
It is all wrong and absurd.
No matter what I learn
Through my heart,
It is not only beautiful but perfect.

22018

My mind's disobedience
Will never be able to arrive
At my Lord's Fulfilment-Satisfaction-Door.

22019

I love to tell God
About my spiritual poverty.
God loves to tell me
Blessingfully and sleeplessly
About my spiritual potentiality.

22020

Unless God is known first,
This world of ours
Will confuse our mind
And strangle our heart.

22021

One doubt-cloud
Is more than enough
To totally eclipse
The inner sun.

22022

Neither the unknown
Nor the unknowable
Will ever frighten us.
It is our imagination-fear
That tortures us.

22023

No, never!
The power of forgiveness
Can never fail any human being
In the inner world.

22024

Impurity does not need
Any outer punishment.
Its very existence
Is its own inner punishment.

22025

O unhappiness,
Is it true that you can never
Even for one moment
Forget this world?

22026

There is only one blessing
That can save me and my life.
That blessing is
My Lord's unconditional Concern for me.

22027

Only a sleepless God-lover
Is entitled to embark
On the God-Summit-expedition.

22028

Alas, nobody knows
When his mind's
God-disobedience-disaster
Befalls.

22029

Alas, I thought
That my absurd stupidity
Would help God forget
All His troubles,
But unfortunately it does not.
