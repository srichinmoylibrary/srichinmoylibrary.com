42 My Meditations ...

My meditations
Awaken
My sleeping life.

43 My God-Surrender Expedites ...

My God-surrender expedites
My God-journey
To my God-Destination.

12 December 2004 ...

12 December 2004

Xiamen, China

44 May Each Thought Of Mine ...

May each thought of mine
Embody
Rainbow-beauty.

45 Never Wait ...

Never wait
For the next aspiration-train —
Never!

46 O My Mind ...

O my mind,
You must not try to correct
Others’ way of life.

47 For Centuries And Centuries ...

For centuries and centuries,
I depended on human beings.
From now on I shall depend
Only on my Lord Beloved Supreme.

48 The Higher Visions ...

The higher visions
Are fast approaching
The God-lovers and God-servers.

49 Every Day God Reveals Himself ...

Every day God reveals Himself
In a most striking manner
To the sincere God-seekers.

50 Every Day ...

Every day
Is a new heart-cry
Of poor Mother Earth.

51 Any Evil Thought ...

Any evil thought
Can extinguish
Our heart’s climbing flames.

52 I Prayerfully Clasp ...

I prayerfully clasp
My Lord’s Compassion-Feet.

53 My Lord ...

My Lord
Most powerfully grasps
My earthbound life.

54 My Lord Invites Me ...

My Lord invites me
To ride with Him
In His Heart-Car
Only when He sees that
I am no longer interested
In my ignorance-desire-life.

13 December 2004 ...

13 December 2004

Xiamen, China
