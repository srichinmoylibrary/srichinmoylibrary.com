7490

From the quick inspiration
To the strong aspiration
Is a very short distance.

7491

Two things you daily need:
Mental disarmament
And
Psychic armament.

7492

Sincerity tells you
What to do.
Purity shows you
How to do.
Divinity does not tell or show;
It just does everything
For you.

7493

My Lord,
This time I can give You
Only my frustration.
When You come next time,
I shall give You
My aspiration.

7494

Today
I shall call my God
My ignorance-overlooker.
Tomorrow
I shall call my God
My illumination-giver.

7495

The world suspects you
Because
You have not loved the world
According to your heart's capacity.

7496

Truth may be bitter at times
While you are practising it.
But it is always sweet
While you are offering it to others.

7497

Not only a purity-heart
But also a serenity-mind
Has the rare capacity to take
Peace-breathing exercises.

7498

While you were away
With your mind,
God came and left a message
With your heart
To call Him as soon as you come back.

7499

Only your aspiration-voice
Is entitled
To the greatest choice.

7500

Each heart knows
How to dream of fulness-beauty
Within God's God-Reality.
