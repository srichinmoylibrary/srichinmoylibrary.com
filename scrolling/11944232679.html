8715

I can see that your inner fire
Is growing and glowing.
I urge you not to allow it
To be extinguished!

8716

He often wonders:
Does God really exist
In his stony ignorance-life?

8717

The secret of this world:
It wants to avoid wisdom-light.
The secret of the inner world:
It wants to claim God
As its very own.

8718

No human being —
No, not even one —
Is doomed to live
Inside the sea of ignorance-night.

8719

I definitely see
A divine change in you
Now that your mind has become
A perfect stranger to doubts.

8720

It is quite strange
That your vital enjoys
Remaining unhappy
In a tiny life-corner
Of the Infinite.

8721

Go to the inner school
Of silence-light
And start mastering
The mind's outer thoughts.

8722

If you are waiting
For the Dawn of God,
Then wait not only soulfully
But also unconditionally
To please Him in His own Way.

8723

Yours is a slow and confused
Self-search.
Therefore, how can you expect
Anything illumining and fulfilling
From your life?

8724

Nobody has compelled you
Or even begged you
To be fettered
By puny and silly thoughts.

8725

Your weaknesses
Will always chase you.
Do not run away from them,
But put up a brave fight
And defeat them once and for all!

8726

Suffering is not
A mere teacher,
But an unforgettable teacher!

8727

If you learn
The art of thanksgiving,
It will be so easy
For you to learn
The art of life-receiving.

8728

God wants you
To converse with Him
Only on one condition:
If you are ready to be
His Eternity's friend.

8729

When he secretly breathes
God-Breath,
He and God become
Earth's perfection-cry
And Heaven's satisfaction-smile.
