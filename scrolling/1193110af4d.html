7475

You have failed
Because your heart has not cried.
You have failed
Because your mind has not tried.
You have failed
Because your life does not need God.

7476

Desires are truly fulfilled
Only when
They are perfectly transcended.

7477

Every day
You must practise humility
And fold your proud hands
In Heaven-climbing prayers.

7478

O my mind,
Do not be afraid
Of the outer bondage.

O my heart,
Do not be afraid
Of the inner freedom.

7479

My mind,
You must not blame anybody else
For your untold suffering
From your self-chosen
Responsibility-loads.

7480

Long before I proclaim
God's Victory over my mind,
He claims my life.

7481

How can you see
God's all-pervading smiling Face
Before you have given Him
Your loving heart?

7482

How can you ever fail
When your heart wants to try
Again and again?

7483

Are you hungry
For only favourable news from God,
Or are you hungry
For any news from God?

7484

Your miserable past
Wants you to surrender to it.
For God's sake, do not!

7485

It is the mind
And not the heart
That is disturbed
When any soul comes
Uninvited.

7486

Who has manufactured
Your life-problems?
You alone!
Who is now cherishing them?
You alone!

7487

Your mind is telling
The same old baseless story:
God does not love you,
Yet you love Him.

7488

Suspicion is the mind's
Invention.
Forgiveness has to be the heart's
Discovery.

7489

The smile of your self-giving
Inspires God
To dance for you.
