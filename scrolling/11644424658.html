30.

A prayer-heart
Is a divine opportunity.
A meditation-life
Is God's Silence-Continuity.

31.

What humility is
If you truly know,
Then your life and deeds
Are bound to glow.

32.

God smilingly loves
Even sad failures.
His powerful Smile
With them endures.

33.

I know, I know,
I am alive today
Because my heart
Enjoys devotion-play.

34.

O my mind's wild
Desire-fire,
Today at last
You must expire.

35.

Your Lord Supreme
Created your soul
To be a partner of His Dream.
Your heart must please Him
In His own Way
To satisfy His soulful Scheme.

36.

My Lord Supreme
Is His Compassion-Tower.
My life shall be
His choice Heart-Garden flower.

37.

Be a beautiful, soulful
Surrender-infant.
God will grant your prayers
His Answers instant.

38.

God has forgiven
Your shameless meanness.
Can you not give Him
Your whole life's oneness?

39.

God's Heart of Oneness-Love
You have refused.
Therefore, you and your life
Are all confused.

40.

The human life is short.
The human mind is insecure.
The divine life is eternal.
The divine mind is pure and sure.

41.

Think not of your days of success
Nor of your failure-nights.
Just meditate and meditate
On your progress-delights.

42.

Each and every sweet dream-smile
Increases fast our true God-love
And makes us better God-servers
Within, without, below, above.

43.

Do not give up, do not give up!
Fight until the last.
You and your soul will triumph
Even in the dust!

44.

The mind attacks others
And then tries to defend itself.
The heart surrenders to God
And becomes the Universal Self.
