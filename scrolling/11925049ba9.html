6845

Behold,
God's Fulness-Heart
Wants to accompany
Your goodness-life!

6846

My Lord,
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
You have made me
Inseparably one with You.
Now, if even for a fleeting second,
I wish to enjoy my previous will —
Which is none other than
My undivine will —
Please do not allow it.
Never again do I wish to have
Any sense of separativity.

6847

You fool!
Not by studying books,
But by discovering
The spontaneous reality in yourself,
Will you experience the Highest.

6848

I do not like
The wild dance of your vital.
I only like
The sweet song of your heart.

6849

If you do not dare
To call your Inner Pilot
Your own, very own,
How can your Inner Pilot
Sing His Eternity's Silence-Song
For you?

6850

Discipline
Is joy misunderstood.
Aspiration
Is satisfaction misunderstood.

6851

If you have the eagerness
To dominate your desire-life,
Then God will be true to you
At every moment.

6852

Divine love
Never has to cry.
Divine surrender
Never has to sigh.

6853

If you do not have sleepless faith,
Your life will be
An agonising marathon
Of unending miles.

6854

If you are cherishing
Your doubting mind,
You will never be able to run
In the progress-race
Sponsored by your soul.

6855

Something is missing.
What is it?
Peace, amid a storm of doubts.

6856

You have given so much joy
To your doubting mind.
Can you not give
Your remaining years
To God's Satisfaction-Delight?

6857

If you are always thinking
Of being attacked
By this and that,
How will you ever dare
To develop a special relationship
With God?

6858

His life is lost
Between hope's blue sky
And despair's dark cave.

6859

You are so stupid!
How can you be jealous
Of your heart's progress-life?
