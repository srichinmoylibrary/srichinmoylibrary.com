40 My Heart’s Sobbing Whispers ...

My heart’s sobbing whispers
Reach my Lord’s Heart
With lightning speed.

41 I Wish To Find My Life ...

I wish to find my life
In between my aspiration-intensity
And my Lord’s
Compassion-Immensity.

42 God Comes ...

God comes
Closer and closer
To my heart-tears.

43 God Becomes ...

God becomes
Dearer and dearer
To my life-smiles.

44 Alas ...

Alas,
Why do I live
In my unlit mind-cave,
And not
In my fully illumined heart-home?

21 January 2002 ...

21 January 2002

Ho Chi Minh City, Viet Nam

45 In The Morning ...

In the morning
God examines
My mind’s purity
And my heart’s beauty.

46 In The Evening ...

In the evening
God examines
My life’s tolerance
And patience.

47 In The Morning ...

In the morning
I soulfully gather
Hope-petals.

48 In The Evening ...

In the evening
I prayerfully sow
Surrender-seeds.

49 My Lord’s Soulful Eye ...

My Lord’s soulful Eye
Shelters my heart-beauty
And my soul-fragrance.

50 At Night ...

At night
My Lord wants me to count
The unconditional love-moments
That I had during the day.

51 Insecurity And Pride ...

Insecurity and pride
Have the same source:
Ego.

27 January 2002 ...

27 January 2002

Brunei Darussalam

52 My Mother India Tells Me ...

My Mother India tells me:
“Silence, my son, silence!
Silence in your inner life,
Silence in your outer life.”
