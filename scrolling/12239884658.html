Aum Vardhanaya Namaha

July 15th, 1992

Aum vardhanaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the One who embodies
Prosperity Infinite.

Aum Purujate Namaha

July 15th, 1992

Aum purujate namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the Absolute Supreme who is
The Conqueror of all our adversaries.
