60.

Nothing can be achieved
Without God's Grace.
This I have learned
From my life's inner race.

61.

If your life can become
One long stillness-prayer,
God will teach you to be
An ignorance-slayer.

62.

Every long step I take
Brings my Lord nearer.
Every God-thought I think
Makes my mind clearer.

63.

Start each day
With determination-fire.
Your high sun-goal
The world will admire.

64.

If you purely love
Below, Above,
God will make you
His fondness-dove.

65.

Even an infant-step
Of progress-light
Can bring you eventually
To God's ultimate Height.

66.

Earth's final song
Is surrender-light.
Heaven's first song
Is Compassion-Delight.

67.

Oneness is the name
Of the seeker-soul.
God's Love-Fulfilment
Is its supreme goal.

68.

If you can find
No time to pray,
How can God save you
From ignorance-clay?

69.

Do not roam
In the futile fields of yesterday.
God has come
To teach you His Perfection-Play.

70.

Within and without
You pretend not to see
Your own creation:
The ignorance-tree.

71.

When your heart learns
Aspiration-skill,
Your life will enjoy
Perfection-thrill.

72.

God's Compassion is searching for me.
What am I doing?
God's Forgiveness is looking for me.
Why am I hiding?

73.

My soul is my God's dream-seed,
My life His reality-tree.
Therefore, within and without
I am a blue bird ever free!

74.

O seeker, be not
A promise-breaker.
You are your world's
Destiny-maker.
