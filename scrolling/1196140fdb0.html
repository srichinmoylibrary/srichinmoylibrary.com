10460

If you want to keep peace with yourself,
Then care for your heart first.
At the beginning of your spiritual journey,
Care for your heart first!
At the middle of your spiritual journey,
Care for your heart first!
At the end of your spiritual journey,
Care for your heart first!

10461

The fearful mind
Needs salvation.
The soulful heart
Needs liberation.

10462

If you are
An inner challenge-lover,
Then God's Satisfaction-Tower
Is yours.

10463

Man thinks
Perfection is unattainable.
God knows
Perfection is not only reachable
But also available
In God's Vision-Creation.

10464

You can claim
Your realisation-birthright
Only when your mind
Is ready to enjoy
The divinity of silence-ecstasy.

10465

If you can keep
Your heart's sincerity-candles
Always lit,
Then there can be no ignorance-night
To keep you away
From God's Satisfaction-Light.

10466

The aspiration-knight
Of a seeker
Will always destroy
The ingratitude-night
Of the seeker's heart.

10467

There are so many places
To visit.
Why do you have to visit
All the time
Your mind's desire-chamber?

10468

Stay in your newborn
Hope-heart.
All your silver dreams
Will turn into
Golden realities.

10469

Because he has spread
His heart's concern-net
All-where,
God's Satisfaction-Sun
Embraces his life.

10470

Before you do anything
Either in the inner world
Or in the outer world,
Devotedly wait
For the Supreme's
Seal of Approval.

10471

Do not allow doubt-thoughts
To stay
On your mind's highest floor
Or even
In your mind's basement.

10472

Pray to your soul
To whisper
Again and again
Your heart's aspiration-songs.

10473

A critical mind
Is bound to live
In a delight-forsaken clime.

10474

If you can be true
To your aspiration-heart,
There is no desire-attack
That you cannot conquer.
