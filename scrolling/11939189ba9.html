8245

Desire's dance
Can be terminated
When aspiration-cries
Start rising.

8246

God seeks undemanding friends,
But it takes a very long time
For Him to get one.

8247

Slowly across time
He will sail
His soul's dream-boat.

8248

Two shores:
The shore of aspiration
That soulfully cries,
The shore of transcendence
That powerfully smiles.

8249

The descent
Of the infinite Consciousness-Bliss
Is for the total transformation
Of the present hopeless world.

8250

I touch God's Blessing-Feet
In my song-life's
Purity-breath.

8251

When he came out of
His heart's aspiration-room,
He was captured and taken away
By night's giant arms.

8252

The silence-souls have thronged
To applaud the victory
Of the unconditional God-servers
On earth.

8253

Earth's helplessness
Will one day meet
With the power
Of Heaven's fullness.

8254

We claim
Freedom-sun
While treasuring
Bondage-night.

8255

He who loves God
Unconditionally
Receives God's transcendental Crown
From God Himself.

8256

Terror's triumph
Does not last,
Cannot last.
This is a fact
Permanently undeniable.

8257

O earth's
Dissatisfaction-grumble-life,
Can you not rest a little?
You deserve it so richly!

8258

I am thanking all those
Who have strengthened my aspiration
By pointing out
All my weaknesses.

8259

O deceased night of tears,
Sleep
And never wake up!
