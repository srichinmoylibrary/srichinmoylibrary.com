1 October 1999﻿(I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You want me to grow
Into Your highest Excellence.
   But how can I?
"My child,
Certainly you can."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8:37 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

1 October 1999﻿(II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Love divine
Is Your Compassion-Strength
   In my arms.
Peace divine
Is Your Satisfaction-Light
   Inside my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 1999, 8:46 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

1 October 1999﻿(III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only the unconditional God-obedience
Knows the surest and shortest way
   To God's Heaven-Palace.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 1999, 8:54 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

1 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You want me to keep
   My heart-eye open
Sleeplessly, soulfully
   And self-givingly
To Your all-guiding Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 1999, 9:09 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-two times. (32 right, 30 left)

1 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My past is nothing but dust.
I shall satisfy You
   Here and now,
Totally, unreservedly
And unconditionally
   In Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 1999,﻿9:30 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (95 reps)

2 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   May my life-boat
Today and every day
   Ply between
Your Compassion-Eye-Beauty-Shore
   And
Your Forgiveness-Heart-Fragrance-Shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1999, 9:30 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 900 lbs.

2 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   My sleepless heart
Has taken the aspiration-road
   To arrive at God's
Transcendental Heights.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1999, 9:46 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

2 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My God-gratitude-heart
   Has given me
A God-Beatitude-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1999, 10:01 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

2 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I offer You my aspiration,
You make my road to Your Palace short.
When I offer You my gratitude,
   You make it shorter.
When I offer You my surrender,
   You make it shortest.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1999, 10:12 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

2 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Compassion-Eye,
Your Forgiveness-Heart
        And
Your Protection-Feet
Come to me together
   Every day
Running at full speed.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1999, 10:17 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 600 lbs.) 700th Prayer.

2 October 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
If I do not become a God-dreamer,
God-lover and God-server first,
How can I become a God-pleaser?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1999, 10:30 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-five times. (33 right, 32 left)

2 October 1999 (VII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
This morning
I had a very, very long
   Walk with You.
You simply said to me
   Only one thing:
"Shut up! Shut up! Shut up!"
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1999, 11:50 p.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

5 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   This morning
You and I had a very long
   Private talk.
During our marathon conversation
We shed ceaseless tears
Of mutual affection, love, sweetness
   And oneness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 1999, 9:24 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

5 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Cry, cry, cry, my heart,
   Cry, cry!
Only then will you be able
   To fly
In Infinity's Freedom-Sky.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 1999, 9:30 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-eight times. (33 right, 35 left)
