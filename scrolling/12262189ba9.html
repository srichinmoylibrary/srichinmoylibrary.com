You Have Given Me a Full Eternity

December 31st, 1969

You have given me a full Eternity.
I have given You my heart’s gratitude-tree.
You have given me Infinity’s Light.
I am offering You my heart’s service-might.

My Tears Ascend

December 31st, 1969

My tears ascend.
My smiles descend.
While ascending, I become
The joy of Truth.
While descending, I become
The love of ruth.

My Soulful Heart

December 31st, 1969

My soulful heart,
My only all,
I shall awaken you
With God-Manifestation-Call.

How Can I Bark?

December 31st, 1969

How can I bark?
How can I shout?
Divine I am
Within, without.

Splendid, My Silence-Sun

December 31st, 1969

Splendid, my silence-sun.
Save you, my life
Shall always remain
Pitifully undone.

You Are Calling Me

December 31st, 1969

You are calling me.
I smile and smile.
I make no reply.
Why, you know;
Because I see in me
Your own Heartbeat flow.

O Soul of Endless Beauty

December 31st, 1969

O soul of endless beauty,
Do teach me the song:
Unity versus multiplicity.
You are the only one
Who knows the song.
Therefore, all souls
To you must throng.

You Pray Well

December 31st, 1969

You pray well.
Do you know why?
Because you cry well,
Because you love well,
Because you surrender well,
In the heart of silence-night,
In the soul of silence-light.

Two Friends Have I

December 31st, 1969

Two friends have I:
My hope-sky and my faith-sea.
They always let me fly
And swim unreservedly
And unconditionally,
Since the very day
Our friendship-banner we unfurled,
Under the sun-vast golden ray.

O Sweet Inspiration

December 31st, 1969

O sweet inspiration,
Stop not,
Stop not.
Carry me farther down.
O my soulful aspiration,
Stop not,
Stop not
Until you have helped me
Reach my source,
My soul’s destination-crown.

My Secret I Reveal

December 31st, 1969

My secret I reveal,
God is my pole-star-promise.
Therefore, I always dare and fulfil.

I Spoke to God

December 31st, 1969

I spoke to God with a soulful smile.
I spoke to man with a doleful cry.
I spoke to the world with a truthful eye.
I spoke to myself with a fruitful heart-song.

How I Cried

December 31st, 1969

How I cried
When I heard
I shall not see
Beauty-soul,
Purity-goal
And Divinity-role.
How I cried!

Inspiration Takes Me to God

December 31st, 1969

Inspiration takes me to
God the Length.
Aspiration takes me to
God the Height.
Realisation takes me to
God the Omnipotent.
Manifestation takes me to God —
Where He dances
With earth’s penury
And with Heaven’s Eldorado.

No Haste, No Waste

December 31st, 1969

No haste, no waste;
Taste nectar-delight.
Then on you proceed
To fight against teeming odds
Of ignorance-night.
