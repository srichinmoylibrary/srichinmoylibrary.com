He Mount Fuji he Mount Fuji he Mount Fuji

Hat chani diye dakiche karuna baridhi Buddha

Keman Kare Kandi Balo

July 11th, 2006

Keman kare kandi balo keman kare kandi
Keman kare charan kamal bandhi balo bandhi

Translation:

Oh, tell me how to cry, how to cry.
Oh, tell me how to bind Your Lotus-Feet.
