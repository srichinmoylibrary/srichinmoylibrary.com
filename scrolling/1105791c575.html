90.

My spirituality-tree
Climbs up to reach
The vault of Heaven.

91.

My spirituality-flower
Grows to rise and reach
The blossoming morning sun.

92.

In my outer life,
My spirituality
Is
A God-blessed sword.

93.

In my inner life,
My spirituality
Is
A God-blessed flute.

94.

My spirituality
Accepts one God
And many gods as well.
My spirituality
Equally believes in
A very special future God:
Man.

95.

My spirituality
Does not want me to become
A lion-king.

96.

My spirituality
Wants me to become
A God-lamb.

97.

My spirituality
Is not
My self-attainment.

98.

My spirituality
Is
My ever-fulfilling
God-commitment.

99.

My spirituality's code of life:
In the immediacy of today,
HERE AND NOW!

100.

My spirituality
Means
My unprecedented
God-manifestation-opportunity.

101.

My spirituality
Is
My world-perfection-
God-dreamer-sower.

102.

My spirituality
Is
My world-satisfaction-
God-warrior-revealer.

103.

My spirituality
Is not
My life insurance policy.

104.

My spirituality
Is
My God-assurance-ecstasy.
