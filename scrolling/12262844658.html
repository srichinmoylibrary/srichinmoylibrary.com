I Think, I Think Only to Sink

December 31st, 1969

I think, I think
Only to sink.
I pray, I pray
Only to decay.
I meditate, I meditate
Only to mediate
Ignorance-knife,
Perfection-life.

Each Man

December 31st, 1969

Each man is a new dream of God,
Each man is a new life of truth,
Each man is a new soul of love,
Each man is a new flood of ruth.

Deep Compassion

December 31st, 1969

Deep Compassion of Your Face
Saves me, my life, my all.
I shall obey Your Call.
With me start a new race.

Why Do I Cry?

December 31st, 1969

Why do I cry?
Why do I sigh?
My Lord I love,
I am His dove.

How Many Blessings?

December 31st, 1969

How many blessings
I really enjoy?
Two, only two:
I daily destroy
Ignorance-dream,
Constantly I vote
For the Supreme.

The Song of Freedom Divine

December 31st, 1969

The song of freedom divine
Smashes the pride of death.
The song of human freedom, lo,
Loses precious breath.

Slowly and Steadily My Prayer Ascends

December 31st, 1969

Slowly and steadily
My prayer ascends,
Like a blue bird.
Softly and divinely
My service descends,
Like a heavy rain.

Are You Dying for a Dream?

December 31st, 1969

Are you dying for a dream?
Then quickly come to me.
Beyond the welkin-rim
I shall set your vision free.

Your Childlike Faith

December 31st, 1969

Your childlike faith
I love, I enjoy, I treasure.
Your saintlike heart
I worship beyond measure.

I Fear No More

December 31st, 1969

I fear no more;
I am now completely free.
I doubt no more;
My mind, a sheltering tree.

A Sudden Thought of God

December 31st, 1969

A sudden thought of God
Has freed his human life
From the world of clamour-din.
He tastes God-freedom within.

To See My Pilot Supreme

December 31st, 1969

To see my Pilot Supreme
In vain I tried,
In vain I cried,
In vain I sighed,
In vain I died.
My dream remains the dream.

Star-Like You Soar

December 31st, 1969

Star-like you soar,
Lion-like you roar.
Child-like you stay,
God-like you play.

Lost in Stormy Vision

December 31st, 1969

Lost in stormy vision,
Caught in murky mission,
Dead in ignorance-dream;
Yet he loves his Supreme.

One Lightning Hope

December 31st, 1969

One lightning hope
And he became
Realisation-soul.
One surrendered thought
And he became
Perfection-goal.
