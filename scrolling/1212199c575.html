26390

As long as we have a mind
That loves to fuss and kick,
We will never have satisfaction
In our life.

26391

The beauty and fragrance
Of gratitude
Illumine us in every aspect
Of our lives.

26392

My Lord,
I have pleased myself
For centuries.
Will You not give me the capacity
To please You even for a day?

26393

What is human pride?
Human pride is something
That cannot see itself
In wrong action.

26394

Decrease the number
Of your complaints.
Increase the power
Of your gratitude.

26395

God will teach you
His most favourite tune.
Just lend Him your ear.

26396

The sweet sound
Of a devotional song
Is bound to expedite the journey
Of a spiritual seeker.

26397

God is already up
And is knocking at your heart-door.
Have you any idea
When you are going to be ready?

26398

God always loves those souls most
Who are genuine lovers of light
And who are genuine self-givers.

26399

If we pray and meditate regularly,
Then, without fail, our hearts shall radiate
God-Bliss, God-Light
And everything that God has and God is.

26400

O Absolute Lord of my heartbeat,
May I always be in cadence
With Your Heart-Dance.
