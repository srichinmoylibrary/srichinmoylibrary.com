19615

If you run the desire-race,
No matter which place you get,
Hopeless helplessness
Will ruthlessly chase you.

19616

As a dethroned king
Is still a king,
Even so, a failure-doomed seeker
Is still a chosen child of God.

19617

O my heart, be brave!
Face your destiny
With silence-fortitude.
Your victory-day
Will fast approach you.

19618

When I sing peace-songs,
God's Breath smiles at me
And makes me a God-partner
In life.

19619

I still hope and feel
That my non-stop fault-finding mind
Will one day surrender
To my God-longing heart.

19620

When my life is a friend
Of eagerness,
My God-home does not remain
Far away.

19621

I do not want
To have my Lord for the asking.
I lovingly and devotedly want
To reverse the game.

19622

May my mind-forest
And my heart-garden
Equally cry
For the Compassion-Eye of God
To visit them.

19623

O self-centred thoughts,
Do not torture
My poor God-searching mind.

19624

There is no difference whatsoever
Between
Our idle hopes
And our vain expectations.

19625

God's Trust in me
I must keep
Not only secret
But also sacred.

19626

It is true
That God's indulgent Compassion
At times frowns at
His own iron Justice.

19627

Our divine qualities
Can easily decay
Due to our mind's
Supremacy-pride.

19628

Very often
When we swim
In material wealth,
We sink
In spiritual tears.

19629

An aspiration-heart is not afraid
Of running uphill.
On the contrary,
It enjoys the run,
For it sees that the Goal itself
Is beckoning it.
