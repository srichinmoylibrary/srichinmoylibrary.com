Confidence

July 23rd, 1995

Confidence
Is not in doing
Or becoming,
But in being.

Man’s Human Glory

July 23rd, 1995

Man’s human glory begins
When his animal story ends.

The Fastest Speed

July 23rd, 1995

Silence not only has,
But is, the fastest speed.

Somebody Has to Listen

July 23rd, 1995

Somebody has to listen.
Why not let me try
To be that fortunate one?

Comparison

July 23rd, 1995

Comparison
      Is
Progress-prison.

The Mind Must Desire

July 23rd, 1995

The mind must desire only
What the heart requires.

Silence Is Beyond

July 23rd, 1995

Silence is beyond
Both the knower and the known.

My Life Swims Every Day

July 23rd, 1995

My life swims every day
In the sweet sea of hope.

My Unconditional Surrender

July 23rd, 1995

My unconditional surrender
Is the topmost branch
Of my life-beauty’s tree.

Each Desire

July 23rd, 1995

Each desire is
A bottomless nothingness-abyss.

Goodbye, Ego, Goodbye

July 23rd, 1995

Goodbye, ego, goodbye!
Hello, surrender, hello!

Nothing Can Be More Powerful

July 23rd, 1995

Nothing can be more powerful
      Than eagerness.

Nothing Can Be More Fruitful

July 23rd, 1995

Nothing can be more fruitful
Than surrender.

Nothing Can Be More Beautiful

July 23rd, 1995

Nothing can be more beautiful
Than forgiveness.

Pleasure Perishes

July 23rd, 1995

Pleasure perishes.
Joy lasts.
Delight forever lasts.
