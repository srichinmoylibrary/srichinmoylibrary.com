16915

My heart's necessity-blossoms for God,
My heart's capacity-blossoms for God,
My heart's willingness-blossoms for God
Are always unlimited.

16916

Every morning my heart and I
Drink together only one thing:
Hope-nectar.

16917

Hope-flames
Must climb up
High, higher, highest.
They are not meant to sink.

16918

For year and years
My heart has been studying
At patience-school
To know the purpose
Of human life.

16919

My Master's
Life-transforming touch
I await
Prayerfully and eagerly.

16920

You looked enough
At your outer world.
Now look at your inner world.
Your own soul-beauty
Will not only inspire you
But also immortalise you.

16921

Each night
Before I go to sleep
I pray to my Lord Supreme
To plant
Beautiful and positive dream-seeds
Inside my gratitude-heart-garden.

16922

If we have no cheerfulness,
How can God bless our mind
Even for a moment
With an angelic Vision-Light?

16923

You are a fool.
That is why you have cancelled
Your inner commitment
To your Lord Supreme.

16924

Where do I live?
I live in between
My Master divine
And
His life-transforming touch.

16925

Aspiration-intensity,
Aspiration-immensity,
Aspiration-luminosity
My heart needs sleeplessly.

16926

Only a surrender-heart
Or a silence-mind
Can hear God's Angel-Whispers.

16927

Nature's message to mankind:
Do not kill me!
God will bless you
With His Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Heart.

16928

In the spiritual life
The loss of a simple life
And the loss of a pure heart
Can only be felt
And never adequately described.

16929

It is very strange
That each human being
At times is afraid
Of his own tiny mind.
