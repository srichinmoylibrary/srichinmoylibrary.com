14 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

14 God Wants My Inner Experiences ...

God wants my inner experiences
To guide my outer life.

15 My Life ...

My life
Without fail
Tremendously improves
When I inspire others.

16 My Heart ...

My heart
Makes the fastest progress
When it establishes its oneness-delight
With the entire world.

17 Move Not Backward! ...

Move not backward!
Stark ignorance-night
Will once more tempt you
And devour you.

18 Move Forward! ...

Move forward!
God’s Compassion-Eye
And God’s Affection-Heart
Are beckoning you.

19 God Loves Me ...

God loves me
Not because I also love Him.
God loves me
Because I am ready,
I am willing
And I am eager
To love Him.

20 The Human ...

The human
In each human being
Always thinks differently.

21 The Divine In Us ...

The divine in us
Always thinks
Exactly the way
God wants us to think.

22 The Child-Heart-Loser ...

The child-heart-loser
With tremendous difficulties
Makes his spiritual progress
Inch by inch.

23 He Who Has Not Lost ...

He who has not lost
His child-heart
In his spiritual life
Is surprised by his own
Unimaginable progress.

24 When I Doubtfully Think ...

When I doubtfully think
That God loves me,
God immediately
Sheds tears of sorrow.

25 When I Unmistakably Feel ...

When I unmistakably feel
That God loves me,
God’s Eye immediately starts swimming
In His Infinity’s Ocean of Delight.

26 I Tell The World ...

I tell the world
That God dearly loves me.
The world asks me,
“Where is the proof?”

27 When The World Tells Me ...

When the world tells me
That God loves the world,
I tell the world,
“I need no proof,
For God has repeatedly told me
That loving
Is the only thing He knows.”
