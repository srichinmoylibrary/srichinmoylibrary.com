545.

I shall feel completely safe
And satisfied
Only if peace brings me
To my Heaven-Home
At my earth-journey's close.

546.

God asks me to speak
To His creation
With His Peace-Heart
And not with
My blaring trumpet-mind.

547.

The outer fortune —
Name and fame —
Comes only to go.
The inner fortune —
Peace —
Comes and occupies
Its permanent seat
In the aspiration-hearts
Of God-lovers.

548.

Be sure
That what you want is
Peace
And not comfort.

549.

Peace
Does not believe in
Any opposition or division.
Peace
Believes only in
Oneness-connection.

550.

Make no demand
On the future.
Peace
Will come and garland you.

551.

What is peace?
Peace
Is Eternity's Infinity.

552.

Discover where peace really is.
Peace
Is in God-dependence
Solely.

553.

Let tomorrow
Worry about tomorrow.
You just breathe in today's
Peace.

554.

I cannot separate
My peace-wish
From my peace-goal.

555.

Do not allow worry
To devour you.
Just face the truth.
Peace will immediately
Befriend you.

556.

I do not want to discover
My peace
In Eternity and Infinity.
I want to discover
My peace
Inside my sweet little heart-nest.

557.

Adjust yourself
To the stark realities
Of your life.
Peace is bound to take your side.

558.

Expectation
Eagerly arrives.
Peace
Secretly departs.

559.

Alas, my expectation
And my peace of mind
Are never on good terms.
