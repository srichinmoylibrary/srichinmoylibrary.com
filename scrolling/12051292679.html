19315

You can make the fastest progress
If you can say,
"I came into the world
To love God, only God.
I came into the world
To serve God, only God.
I came into the world
To manifest God,
Only God."

19316

The day I offer my life's surrender
And my heart's gratitude
To my Lord Supreme unconditionally
Is my real birthday,
The beginning of my new, divine life.

19317

God observes the seeker's birthday
Only when He sees the seeker
Crying most sincerely
For his own perfect perfection in life.

19318

Success is visible
In the outer life.
Progress is visible
In the inner life.
But unlike success,
Progress brings with it
Abiding satisfaction.

19319

If you intensify your eagerness,
You receive more from God.
The increase of your inner wealth
Is the evolution
Of your inner, even outer, life.

19320

Every day
My aspiration-heart is singing songs
Of love, devotion, surrender
And gratitude
To my Lord Beloved Supreme,
Him to please
In His own Way.

19321

Music is the universal language.
May we all live,
Through soulful music,
In the world-heart's oneness-home.

19322

It is God-songs alone
That melt my heart
And touch the very depths
Of my inner being.

19323

Spontaneous God-love
Does not need
Any explanation.

19324

Each time I devotedly dive within,
I feel I am going deep,
Deeper, deepest,
Where I find Infinity's Bliss.

19325

Some seekers shed crocodile tears
In the twinkling of an eye.
Alas, they never feel the beauty
Of their real heart-tears.

19326

What we need is soulful oneness,
Not outer mastery.
We must offer our oneness-height
To the God-lovers and truth-seekers
Who are looking for peace
In their lives.

19327

There is not a single moment
When our heartbeat
Is not singing a song.
But we must make sure
It is a God-fulfilling song.

19328

Fulfilment you want?
Before the interference
Of the mind begins,
Fulfil yourself by identifying
With the spirit's voice within.

19329

For God-lovers, music-lovers
And divinity-lovers,
Each soulful song
Is a most delicious inner banquet.
