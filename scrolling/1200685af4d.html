14975

Stark disappointment
Gave a hasty chase
To his fame-fed life.

14976

I have put my hope-friend
In my heart's aspiration-boat
To arrive at perfection-fulfilment-shore.

14977

My heart's abiding aspiration-cry
Is now being protected and perfected
And eventually will be liberated
By the watchful Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Supreme.

14978

Every morning I do
My heart's oneness-stretching exercises
So that I do not suffer
From my mind's insecurity-cramps.

14979

The sweetness-fruits
Of my surrender-life
My Lord Supreme
Dearly treasures.

14980

To gain confidence-might,
Use your imagination-mind
And aspiration-heart.
You are bound to be successful!

14981

To forgive humanity unconditionally
Is, indeed, to meet
An unparalleled challenge in life.

14982

O stupid mind,
When will you have the wisdom
To search for eternal Truth
And not for temporary, fleeting truth?

14983

Based on what I have observed
In my spiritual life,
I believe that nothing good
Can ever remain unachieved
By a sincere God-lover.

14984

Alas, I do not know how long
I shall have to hang on to
A tiny, feeble faith-thread
In my aspiration-life!

14985

I have never felt
Anything like this before!
One single purity-thought
Has kept my entire being
Extremely happy
For many long hours.

14986

My mind says to my heart:
"O heart, tell me
What you have learned
From God's Satisfaction-Smile."

My heart says to my mind:
"O mind, do not search
For earth-explanation,
But long for Heaven-illumination!"

14987

Alas, how many times
Does God have to tell me
That it is my aspiration-seed
That will one day grow into
A fully-blossomed manifestation-tree?

14988

Every morning
My soul teaches my aspiring heart
A new, self-giving happiness-song.

14989

The Eastern view of life:
Time will wait for you
As long as you want.

The Western view of life:
Alas, what was I doing
When the time disappeared?
