42 Only Sincere Heart-Seekers ...

Only sincere heart-seekers
Can see
God’s golden Throne.

43 A Limitation-Mind ...

A limitation-mind
Is
A frustration-life.

44 My Inner Life ...

My inner life
Is
An indomitable power.

45 I Never Cared ...

I never cared
And I will never care
For countless sounds.

46 I Care Only ...

I care only
For boundless silence.

47 Only Silence-Joy ...

Only silence-joy
Can show us the way
To God-realisation.

48 When I Pray To God ...

When I pray to God
To come down,
He tells me,
“Come up, come up,
My child!
This place
Is infinitely safer.”

49 Every Day ...

Every day
God wants me
To spring a new surprise
On Him.

50 The Ingratitude ...

The ingratitude
Of the mind
Is ruthlessly shocking.

6 January 2005 ...

6 January 2005

Qingdao, China

51 Sleeplessly I Keep ...

Sleeplessly I keep
My heart-room open
For my Lord Supreme.

52 I Search ...

I search
For God-closeness
Everywhere.

53 I Search ...

I search
For God-oneness
Deep within.

54 Heaven Showers Blessings ...

Heaven showers Blessings
Upon me
Every morning.

55 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I treasure my Lord’s Feet
More than I treasure
My Lord’s Eye.
