39 A Self-Giving Life ...

A self-giving life
Always basks in the sunshine
Of God’s Paradise-Presence.

40 When I Go Far Beyond The Domain ...

When I go far beyond the domain
Of my earth-bound mind,
God immediately stops the clock.

41 At Times God May Close ...

At times God may close
His Ear to me,
But He never closes
His Eye to me.

42 I Shall Never Allow ...

I shall never allow
The beauty of my aspiration-heart
To fade.

43 No Anxiety, No Worry ...

No anxiety, no worry
Can ever perturb
A God-surrender-life.

44 My Sleepless God-Devotion-Heart ...

My sleepless God-devotion-heart
Does not allow doubt-fog
To cloud my horizons.

45 This Morning My Lord Supreme ...

This morning my Lord Supreme
Came to me and said,
“My child, I am now making you
A permanent member
Of My Universal Heart-Society.”

12 December 2002 ...

12 December 2002

Taupo, New Zealand

46 My Outer Race-Victory-Smile ...

My outer race-victory-smile
Makes God
Enormously happy.

47 My Inner Race-Victory-Smile ...

My inner race-victory-smile
Makes God
Boundlessly proud of me.

48 O My God-Pleasing Readiness ...

O my God-pleasing readiness,
Willingness and eagerness-life,
Wake up, wake up!
Sleep no more!

49 The Misuse Of God’s Compassion ...

The misuse of God’s Compassion
Extinguishes our heart’s
Heaven-climbing flames.

50 God Does Not Mind ...

God does not mind
When I tell Him
Whatever is on my mind.
He does not mind
Even when I ruthlessly
Speak ill of Him.
But He does mind
When I think ill of myself.

51 Each Self-Giving Act ...

Each self-giving act
Expedites our inner journey
To God’s Heart-Home.

52 In The Spiritual Life ...

In the spiritual life,
There is only one
Unpardonable blunder:
Ingratitude.
