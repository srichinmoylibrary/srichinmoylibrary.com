Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Istanbul and Belek, Turkey in November and December 2006.

21 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

1 I Pray And Pray ...

I pray and pray
And pray
To see God’s Feet.

2 I Meditate And Meditate ...

I meditate and meditate
And meditate
To feel God’s Heart.

3 I Contemplate, Contemplate ...

I contemplate, contemplate
And contemplate
To become one with God’s Will.

4 When My Prayer ...

When my prayer
Is sincere,
God blesses my head.

5 When My Meditation ...

When my meditation
Is deep,
God embraces my heart.

6 What Do I Need? ...

What do I need?
A heart-throbbing prayer.

7 What Do I Need? ...

What do I need?
A time-stopping meditation.

22 November 2006 ...

22 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

8 I Was Born ...

I was born
With a heart
Of God-beauty.

9 I Was Born ...

I was born
With a life
Of God-duty.

10 The Desire-Life ...

The desire-life
Is always busy —
It has no free time.

11 God Wants Our Heart’s Hopes ...

God wants our heart’s hopes
Always to be hopeful,
And not hopeless.

12 I Ask God ...

I ask God
How He spends His time.
He says to me,
“My child, I spend My time
Without thinking of Me.”
