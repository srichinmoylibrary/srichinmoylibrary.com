10775

The landlord of the heart-room
Is extremely strict.
He only allows the faith-tenant
To live there.

10776

If you can play
On your life's transcendence-violin,
Only then will God grant you
His Heart's Nectar-Nest.

10777

The seeker's soulfulness-heart-shops
Are always open
To both God the Dreamer
And God the Lover.

10778

If you do not want insecurity-insects
To bite you,
Then every day
Learn a new cheerfulness-song
From God the Compassion-Illumination-Singer.

10779

How can you expect God
To wait for you
With His Illumination,
If you allow your life to be
Your mind's desire-weight?

10780

Every day if you sing
Your heart's oneness-song,
Then God will grant you
His own Heart's Satisfaction-Song.

10781

All the keys are available
Either here or elsewhere,
But your life's gratitude-heart-key
Is not to be found
Anywhere.

10782

If there is no enthusiasm
In the seeker's heart,
Then he will never be able to enjoy
The God-Beauty of God's
Birthless and deathless Time.

10783

Each realisation-bird has the capacity
To build a God-worshipping nest
Where blossoms God-Beauty's Smile.

10784

My soul runs
On Heaven-Beauty's road.
My heart runs
On earth-beauty's road.
My mind runs
On life-curiosity's road.

10785

Every time you forget to think of God,
God becomes
An unnecessary need in your life
And your heart's oneness-cord with God
Is weakened.

10786

If you go to your mind's
Doubt-meetings,
You are bound to lose
Your life's God-pleasing
Brightness-dawn.

10787

I have seen many things in my life,
And many more things I shall see.
But one thing I have not seen
And, alas, shall never see,
And that is my mind's
Climbing aspiration-steps.

10788

If you are your heart's gratitude,
Then God will give you His own Hand
To sound your and your God's
Victory-Gong.

10789

When you ask your mind
To show you the enlightenment-apartment,
It takes you immediately
Into its darkness-basement
And tells you
This is what you actually need.
