18660

When we meditate
Inside the very depths
Of our heart,
We see a cosmos of satisfaction
And not a world of despair.

18661

At long last
My mind's doubt-life is over!
The selfishness of my mind
Has been replaced
By the selflessness of my heart.

18662

I can move my life
In God's direction
Only when I am not trying
To please God out of fear.

18663

Each desire-satisfaction
Becomes my heart's
Aspiration-starvation.

18664

God's good news is:
He has not given up on me.
My good news is:
I am determined
To please God in His own Way.

18665

Do you know
What God's secret Name is?
His Justice-Light-Surrender
To His Compassion-Height.

18666

The unaspiring mind says to God:
Give me freedom!
The aspiring heart says to God:
God, I wish to be imprisoned
In Your Love-Embrace!

18667

There can be various service-roads
To approach God
But there is only one aspiration-road
To realise God.

18668

The increase
Of my heart's faith
Is my life's capacity
Expanded.

18669

I no longer want to remain the owner
Of my unrealised possibilities.
From now on I want to be the owner
Of my manifested capacities.

18670

Hesitation and doubt
Are oneness-friends
Who indefinitely delay
The seeker's progress-march.

18671

If you pay no attention
To your nature's perfection,
Then God's complete Satisfaction in you
Will always remain a far cry.

18672

God writes to you every day
Using your heart's
Surrender-address
And not your mind's
Hesitation-address.

18673

God does not want our hearts
To be sent to Him.
He wants our hearts
To be hand-delivered to Him.

18674

There is never a cease-fire
Between the arrogance of the vital
And the frustration of the mind.
