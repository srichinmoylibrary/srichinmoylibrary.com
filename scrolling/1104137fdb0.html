6 April 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must cross over
All my temptation-hurdles
In order to reach my life's
Liberation-Destination.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 April 2000, 8:15 a.m. Before doing fifty-three one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (23 right, 30 left)

6 April 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I have replaced
My fault-finding mind
With my world-oneness-loving heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 April 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
You value my heart-smiles.
Again, out of Your infinite Bounty,
You value my life-tears
Infinitely more.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 April 2000, 8:52 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

10 April 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I do not allow my mind to have
Even an inch
Of negativity-space.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 April 2000, 9:08 a.m. Before lilting 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously from a seated position.

10 April 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In my life there is only one
Supreme Blessing
And that Supreme Blessing
Is to kiss the dust of Your Feet
At every moment.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 April 2000, 9:16 a.m. Before doing sixty one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (31 right, 29 left)

12 April 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My final Destination
Is my Lord's full manifestation
Here on earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 April 2000, 7:56 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

12 April 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God has not sanctioned
Even one single holiday
For a God-realised person
In the inner world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 April 2000, 8:14 a.m. Before doing fifty-seven one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (25 right, 32 left)

12 April 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I equally love and value
My heart's aspiration-flames
And
My life's dedication-blossoms.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 April 2000, 8:20 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

14 April 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The human life is nothing but
The morning joy
And the evening sorrow.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 April 2000, 8:15 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

14 April 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Each soul has a unique way
To manifest God here on earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 April 2000, 8:34 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

20 April 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My God-aspiration shows me the way.
My God-dedication takes me
To my Destination.
My God-manifestation is my Lord's
Complete Satisfaction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 April 2000, 7:54 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

20 April 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind's God-imagination
Is a real friend
Of my heart's aspiration.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 April 2000, 7:36 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

20 April 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart, dream, dream.
Dreaming is realising.
Realising is becoming
The Highest Absolute.
Therefore, my heart, stop not dreaming.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 April 2000, 8:11 a.m. Before doing fifty-three one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (24 right, 29 left)

22 April 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A God-willingness-seeker
Is always the winner
In the inner race.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 April 2000, 8:05 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

22 April 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
For me to choose is to lose
In my God-oneness-game.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 April 2000, 8:26 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.
