26160

Your heart does not need
Your compassion.
What your heart needs from you
Is your constant willingness
To do the right thing
And become the right person.

26161

If you have adamantine will-power,
Then it will irresistibly lead you upward
To God's Kingdom-Light.

26162

Nobody wants to hear
The monologue of the mind.
It is for the mind to enjoy
Its own stupid absurdities.

26163

At your heart-shrine,
Imagine yourself
As the most beautiful angel
While you are praying.

26164

There is always time for us
To take spiritual exercises
To please our Lord Supreme.

26165

Each aspiring heart
Has a sweet and unfading yearning
To be in God's company.

26166

The soul's sunlight
Knows no shadow
Of the mind
And no darkness
Of the vital.

26167

Alas, it is we who confuse ourselves
With our mind's constant
World-enquiries.

26168

Your heart-eye must look straight
And travel faster than the fastest
To the Feet
Of your Lord Supreme.

26169

Only a flower-heart
Can be drawn easily and effectively
Towards the soul.

26170

Alas, the human mind
Cannot see
That its ingratitude is ever-increasing!

26171

This morning
God the Compassion Himself
Has come down to replenish
My faded heart-hope.

26172

If you have the sharp edge
Of intensity
In your heart, mind and vital,
Then who can prevent you
From making the fastest progress?

26173

May the light of the new millennium
Successfully spread
All over God the creation.

26174

Every day my soul begs
My heart, mind, vital and body
To hoist
Their aspiration-sails.
