9875

The time has not yet come
For you to feel
God's Compassion-Sea.
But now is the time
For you to be fully prepared,
Before the God-Hour dawns.

9876

Your life-achievements
Amount to nothing
Because God's Satisfaction-Eye
Is not smiling at you.

9877

Earth is my soul's
Adventure-land.
Heaven is my heart's
Rapture-land.

9878

His existence-reality
Is always available
In his mind's
Secluded silence-woods.

9879

Pray and meditate daily.
Otherwise
Your mind's misfortune-clouds
Will astonishingly eclipse
Your heart's illumining sun.

9880

Day and night
He does only one thing:
He plays the melody of world-separation
On his self-interest-guitar.

9881

My prayer-life sponsors
My clarity-mind.
My meditation-life sponsors
My purity-heart.

9882

To increase
My inner progress-delight,
I have renewed my attention
To the self-discipline-life.

9883

Do you know what he makes
In his mind-factory?
He makes indifference-frowns.

9884

My Lord,
Do give me
The capacity to aspire
Only for Your Satisfaction,
And thus let me not remain
An immature aspiration-seeker.

9885

If your mind is prepared
And your heart is ready,
Your Master will help you claim him
As your own, very own.

9886

For the benefit of all,
Never give up playing
God's earth-transformation-game!

9887

Unless you master your shortcomings,
Your life-history will be full
Of meaningless incidents.

9888

Before your mind
Changes your thought,
You can send your thought
To change the world.

9889

You have been wasting your time
In self-pity.
Can you not now spend your time
In adoring God's all-loving
Universality?
