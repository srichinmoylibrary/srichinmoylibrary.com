3615 The Trouble-Shooter Knows

The trouble-maker thinks
That he can destroy the world.
The trouble-shooter feels and knows
That God's Grace is always with him
And God's Satisfaction is always for him.

3616 Remember What You Said

Remember what you said to God:
"My Lord, I shall always
Fulfil You unconditionally."

Remember what you said to man:
"My friend, I shall always
Help you unreservedly."

3617 You Can Easily Feel The Truth

You cannot understand the truth
If you do not listen to the truth-knower.
But you can easily feel the truth
Even if you do not understand the truth.

3618 Divinity Does Not Know

Humanity knows;
Divinity does not know.
Humanity knows how to hide
Its stark ugliness.
Divinity does not know how to hide
Its perfect beauty.

3619 The Divine Heart Proceeds

The human mind succeeds in experiencing
Only what it sees.
The divine heart proceeds through experiencing
Only what it is.

3620 My Mind's Confusion

My life's imperfection
Is nothing but my mind's confusion.
My mind's confusion
Is nothing but my heart's
Lack of communication
With the Compassion-Flood
Of my Beloved Supreme.

3621 Delight Is An Unlimited Capacity

Delight is an unlimited capacity
Which our heart has
And our soul eternally is.

3622 To Say Something Deeper

To say something deeper,
Be someone higher.
To be someone higher,
Have something purer.
To have something purer,
Sleeplessly and unconditionally
Remain a self-giver.

3623 The Highest Progress

The highest success
Is to put an end
To teeming self-conflicts.
The highest progress
Is to transform the mind's brooding night
Into the heart's blossoming day.

3624 Try To Belong To Your Heart

Try to belong to your heart.
Perfection-flood will be all yours.
Try to belong to your soul.
Satisfaction-ocean will be all yours.

3625 Do You Want To Win?

Do you want to win
In the battlefield of life?
Then, for God's sake, do not run away!
Stay at least where you are now
Until the beauty of a new inspiration
Shows you the most powerful sun
Of eternal Day.

3626 Resist The Power Of Darkness

Insist on bringing to the fore
What you inwardly have.
Resist the power of darkness within and without
That tells you
Yours is the long, longer, longest way
To reach the fulness of Perfection-Shore.

3627 One More Way

If all the other ways have failed,
There is one more way to solve
All our teeming problems,
And that is by outlasting them
Once and for all.

3628 One Way To Win

There is only one way to win
In the battlefield of life,
And that is to surrender
To the express Will of the Lord Supreme.

3629 You Will No Longer Tolerate

Tell your vital
That you will no longer tolerate
Its outrageous demands.

Tell your mind
That you will no longer tolerate
Its poison-doubt.
