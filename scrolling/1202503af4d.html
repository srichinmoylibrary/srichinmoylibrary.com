16776

Unconditionally meditate
On God's infinite Compassion.
God will ask you to wait
For His choice Hour.
His choice Hour will, without fail
Liberate you from
Your age-old weaknesses.

16777

An iota of gratitude
Can keep a seeker
On the right path
In his aspiration-life.

16778

Out of His infinite Bounty
My Beloved Lord Supreme has transformed
My mind's anthill-frustration
Into my heart's star-high aspiration.

16779

Not before me,
O my mind,
But before my Lord Supreme,
You must be able to say
That you have all along
Been quite sincere with my heart.

16780

May a sleepless
Gratitude-heart
Be my life's
Chief attribute.

16781

My aspiring heart's astute observation
Is that my doubting mind
Has ruthlessly deceived me
Limitless times.

16782

Where were you
When God told your mind
That His creation
Is full of suffering?
Where were you
When God told you
That His Heart alone
Is the real sufferer?

16783

God is not surprised
By your undeserved deceit.
But He is surprised
By your unwillingness
To change your mind's loyalty
To the command of ignorance.

16784

Just imagine
How much more effective
Your dedication could have been
In the world
Without your insecurity, jealousy
And pride!

16785

Truth
Is the heart's
Absolutely strongest conviction.

16786

God the Eye is Reality.
God the Heart is Reality
Plus necessity,
My only necessity.

16787

Truth-beauty
And
Happiness-fragrance
Are inseparable.

16788

It is simply impossible for me
To live on earth
Without having God the Witness
And God the Advisor
On my side.

16789

God's Affection-Garland
He has received from God
Because he makes
His enemies happy.
