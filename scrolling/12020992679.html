16315

O my Lord,
You have so many friends!
Do You not think it
A sheer waste of time
To want to make friends
With my worthless and useless mind?

16316

How can the Supreme
Fail to fulfil
The Peace-Dream of His own Heart
In His own creation
On earth?

16317

Long before your life
Is possessed by possessions,
Can you not sing
Renunciation-song
And dance renunciation-dance?

16318

Sleeplessly God's Tears
Are falling on earth.
Why?
Because He sees clearly
That world-harmony
Is a far cry.

16319

He tries to steal
Compassion from Heaven.
He fails!
He tries to steal
Gratitude from earth.
He fails!

16320

When everything else fails,
Cry!
With your gratitude-heart-tears,
You are bound to succeed.

16321

Never worry about things
That you are unable to change.
Change your own way
Of looking at truth.
Look from a higher
And more encompassing
Life-tree branch.

16322

Make your compassion-heart
And dedication-life
Your divinely inspired trademarks.

16323

To make the fastest progress,
Enthusiasm must come first
In your life!

16324

If you do not open your mind
To new, uncharted horizons,
There will be a definite decline
In your aspiration-life.

16325

Your God-loving dreams
And your God-serving hopes
Will be anchored by God Himself
In His own Reality-World.

16326

Summon your soul-courage!
It will wrestle
Anxiety, doubt and impurity
Into submission.

16327

Do not wait until you have
A perfect consciousness!
It is always better
To serve God
And to do something good
For humanity
Immediately.

16328

Sympathise with others
In their mind-burden-pain
And you will also be able to share
In their heart-kite-joy.

16329

You will lose
Your friendship with God
If you think
That your version of truth
Is the only accurate
And perfect one.
