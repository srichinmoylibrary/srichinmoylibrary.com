23075

Although I am losing badly
On the battlefield of life,
I am absolutely sure
That I shall ultimately
Sound my victory-drum.

23076

If you want to remain always
In your heart-garden,
God will be there with you,
Full of Gratitude and Pride.

23077

Gratitude
Is my aspiration-heart's
Only unfailing God-searchlight.

23078

Do not forget
That inside your mind
Is your life's imperfection-lock,
And inside your aspiration-heart
Is the key.

23079

A spiritual Master
Has a life of beauty
And a heart of fragrance
To transform
Your earth-bound life
Into a Heaven-free life.

23080

"Humanity's
God-Gratitude-Heart-Tears and Smiles"
Is the only book that God has written
So far.

23081

The human in me
Has only one request
For God:
"My Lord, do tell me how good I am."

23082

Mine is the self-giving heart
That thrills
At the sacred and secret whispers
Of my Lord Supreme.

23083

Only a God-surrender-heart
Enjoys the safest haven
On earth.

23084

My restless mind
Needs a slower speed,
And my selfless heart
Needs a faster speed
To arrive at God's Heart-Home.

23085

True,
I cannot consciously converse with God,
But my heart's silence-tears
At every moment speak with God.

23086

Although the human in me
Is a poor listener,
My Lord Supreme never stops
Speaking and advising.

23087

My Lord Supreme
Blessingfully offered me many special things.
But to my sorrow, the human in me
Politely declined everything,
And the animal in me
Vehemently rejected everything.

23088

I am so fortunate
That God the Compassion,
Not God the Justice,
Checks my aspiration-pulse
Every morning and every evening.

23089

Because my Lord Supreme
Is pleased with me,
My mind is rich in hope
And my heart is rich in promise.
