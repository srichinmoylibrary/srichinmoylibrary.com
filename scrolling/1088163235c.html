105.

My Lord Supreme,
Scientists are so bad that they have to find spots on my beautiful face.
I shall never forgive them.
I beg of You,
You also must not forgive them.

Shashanka Menard

106.

My Lord Supreme,
I never see You visiting museums.
Is it because the artwork of the artists
is worthless, or because they make
the Artist in You insecure?

Gochar Mursinna

107.

My Lord Supreme,
You are always keen in finding my mistakes.
Can I not be keen in finding
all Your good qualities
so that I can be, before long,
as perfect as You are?

Bichakshan Hodding

108.

My Lord Supreme,
Somebody gave me the key to open up the door of Heaven and I am successful always in opening up the Door of Heaven. But then I made another key for myself to open up the door of earth. Just because I made it, alas, that key does not work. Either give me a gift key or loan me one to open up the earth-door. Earth needs my key, for earth has to be opened up immediately to receive Your Light. Otherwise, it will always remain in ignorance-night.

Peter Benedict

109.

My Lord Supreme,
Is it because You have forgotten my telephone number, or are You deliberately ignoring me so that I can pray and meditate more lovingly and devotedly? I am pleading with You to be satisfied with me, since I have done everything in my capacity to love and please You in Your own Way.

Durjaya Pliske

110.

My Lord Supreme,
My only prayer is:
grant me the middle path.
My fat father is not my goal:
neither is my thin mother!

Vajra Henderson

111.

My Lord Supreme,
Your Way is too long, too arduous, at times even too confusing and puzzling. Since my capacity is nothing other than incapacity, can You not make my road shorter than the shortest and brighter than the brightest so that I can arrive at my Goal sooner than the soonest?

Pantha Shapiro

112.

My Lord Supreme,
Our parents, especially our mothers, make a fuss when we are ill. Do You do the same when Your children in Heaven fall sick?

Sakshat Flowers

113.

My Lord Supreme,
I wanted to become an instrument. Alas, is it my fate to make You my instrument? Be ruthlessly strict with me if You really love me. Your Love infinite is, indeed, my only Saviour.

Sujantra McKeever

114.

My Lord Supreme,
I want to be proud of myself all the time.
Is it because I am not ready
or You are too busy to make me great?

Gaurob Zimmerman

115.

My Lord Supreme,
I know how to fly,
but I do not know how to cry.
Is it because flying upward is infinitely easier than diving inward? I am not at all in a rush, but I do expect an answer from You, either in the near future or in the distant future.

Nibir Cole

116.

My Lord Supreme,
Do You not mind when we cut jokes with You in season and out of season? If so, let us know. We shall stop cutting jokes with You immediately. But one thing we must tell You, that Your Joke-Meals we always greedily devour.

Ken Kresge

117.

My Lord Supreme,
Your supreme Philosophy is: do not give up, never! Why then have You given up on us?
Be sincere, once and for all. Is it because You are powerless, or we are useless?

Vinaya Lebenson

Editor's preface to the first edition

The questions in this book were asked by children between the ages of six and ninety-nine.
