25060

My Lord, do show me the way
To please You
So that I can devotedly
And self-givingly be worthy
Of Your Compassion-flooded
Love and Need.

25061

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me the capacity
To open my heart
As often as I open my hands.

25062

God's Eye
Can and shall cradle
Always
My self-giving oneness-heart.

25063

May my heart
Every day become
A sacred and portable
God-shrine.

25064

My heart, cry and cry,
For this is the only way
To drink deeply
From God's Nectar-Fountain-Heart.

25065

Life becomes an all-fulfilling song
When God invites us
To powerfully strike His Heart-Gong.

25066

A most powerful meditation begins
Only after God has touched
Our aspiration-heart.

25067

My heart is afraid of God's Face
Only when
My mind ruthlessly disregards
My Lord's Grace.

25068

Let us immediately respond
To God's Call,
For His Call is only to divinise our mind
And immortalise our heart.

25069

If we really love God
And if we really need God,
Then we must never hide
Behind the mask
Of our human weaknesses.

25070

A new life is crying in me.
A new life is crying for me.
A new life is pushing me forward
Towards God's Lotus-Feet.

25071

If anything stops
My heart's aspiration-flames,
Then how can I expect
God's celestial Smile?

25072

My Lord, do give me the capacity
To drive my heart-car
On the fastest lane
Of my surrender-life-highway.

25073

Newness is not enough.
Inside newness we must feel holiness.
Holiness is not enough.
Inside holiness we must see and feel
God's Compassion-flooded Fulness.

25074

Not even for a fleeting second
We must remain inside our mind-desert
If we want to grow faith-seeds.
Faith-seeds grow
Only inside our heart-garden.
