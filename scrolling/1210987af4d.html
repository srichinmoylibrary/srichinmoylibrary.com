25175

Do not make a truce with your soul.
Ask your soul to take the lead
If happiness is what you want.

25176

First receive
And then assimilate
All your God-experiences
For your perfect perfection-life.

25177

Alas, the indulgence of my vital
Has covered the beauty and fragrance
Of my heart.

25178

Stop, stop the swiftness
Of your criticism-arrow
If you want to extinguish
All your earthly desire-fires!

25179

Finally God has commanded
My doubting mind
To surrender to my aspiring heart.

25180

Sooner or later,
Our desire-mind
Will give way
To our aspiration-heart.

25181

There should be at least
Some soulful seekers on earth
Who will daily wipe away
God's Heart-Tears.

25182

Do not allow yourself to be haunted
By constant regret
That you are not doing well
In your spiritual life.
Be determined!
Go forward!
Go forward!

25183

O seeker, do not take any request
Of your Master lightly
If you want to make
The fastest progress.

25184

If we do not destroy
Our insecurity-ant,
Then it will eventually grow
Into a ferocious tiger.

25185

I pray
Through the beauty of tears.
I meditate
Through the fragrance of happiness.

25186

A self-giving soul
Is, indeed,
The radiance of Eternity
And Infinity.

25187

Alas, most of our inheritances
Are nothing but sad memories
Of the past.

25188

If we have the armour
Of a clear conscience,
Then fast, very fast we can run
In our spiritual life.

25189

Only a doubting mind
Enjoys the wild gossip
Of the street corner.
