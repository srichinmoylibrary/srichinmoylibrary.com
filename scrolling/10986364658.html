27 I Am Absolutely Certain ...

I am absolutely certain
That my Lord will never ask me
To do anything
Beyond my capacity.

28 I Fill My Days And Nights ...

I fill my days and nights
With my Lord’s
Compassion-Eye-Smiles.

29 My Heart’s Dream ...

My heart’s dream
Is
God-realisation.

30 My Life’s Dream ...

My life’s dream
Is
God-revelation.

31 My Soul’s Dream ...

My soul’s dream
Is
God-manifestation.

3 February 2007 ...

3 February 2007

Cha-Am, Thailand

32 When I Dive Deep Within ...

When I dive deep within,
I breathe the fresh air
Of a new dawn.

33 God Himself Plies My Life-Boat ...

God Himself plies my life-boat
Between two shores:
The known and the Unknown.

34 When I Veil My Mind ...

When I veil my mind,
I unveil Heaven.

35 My Only Choice ...

My only choice:
God’s earth-lifting
And Heaven-climbing
Voice.

36 God Includes ...

God includes
Each and every human being
In His Cosmic Play.

37 God Asks Us ...

God asks us
Direct questions.
He expects from us
Direct answers.

38 A Spiritual Master ...

A spiritual Master
Gladly gives himself away,
Although he badly suffers.

39 God Forgives Us ...

God forgives us,
Not because we deserve
His Forgiveness,
But because He surrenders
To His own Compassion-Heart.

5 February 2007 ...

5 February 2007

Cha-Am, Thailand
