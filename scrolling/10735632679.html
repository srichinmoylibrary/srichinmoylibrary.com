Zina: Did God think of making schools?

Sri Chinmoy: God wanted to make only one school — and that school is our heart.

Mayra: How did God get His Name?

Sri Chinmoy: God got His Name from the smiles and tears of His children.

Keith: How does God help us?

Sri Chinmoy: God helps us by giving us a sincere mind and a pure heart.

Artem: How helpful is God?

Sri Chinmoy: The more sincere we are, the more helpful God is to us.

Omar: How does God make all the sick people better?

Sri Chinmoy: God makes all the sick people better by using the Beauty of His Eyes and the Fragrance of His Heart.

Nadja: Is God in Heaven?

Sri Chinmoy: When I am good, God is on earth. When I am bad, God is in Heaven.

Howard: How did God make men?

Sri Chinmoy: Once God was extremely hungry. Then, lo and behold, somebody came out from God's Body to feed Him. God was highly pleased and gave him the name 'man'. Then God said, "There shall come a day when I shall come out of your body to feed you, and you will give Me the name 'God'."

Shaunce: Does God have a mother?

Sri Chinmoy: Yes, He has a mother. His Mother has two names: Compassion and Forgiveness.

Vikram: How old is God?

Sri Chinmoy: If you are asking this question while you are in your heart, then God is exactly as old as you are. But if you are asking this question while you are in your mind, then God is infinitely older than your great, great, great grandparents.

Veanna: Where does God live?

Sri Chinmoy: God has three places to live. When He is hungry, He lives inside your heart. When He is thirsty, He lives inside your eyes. When He is not hungry or thirsty, He moves around inside your tiny body.

Colin: When did God make Adam and Eve?

Sri Chinmoy: Unfortunately, I was not born then.

Pradel: Why did He die for our sins?

Sri Chinmoy: He died to make us not just "sort of" good, but really good.

Jessica: Does God hate people who lie?

Sri Chinmoy: God does not hate people who lie, but He cries and cries in front of them very pitifully when they tell lies.

Josabad: Why did God make people?

Sri Chinmoy: God likes play. Therefore, He created people to play with Him.

Blair: Does God have a house in Heaven?

Sri Chinmoy: God does not need a house of His own because He visits every day a number of houses owned by His dear children.
