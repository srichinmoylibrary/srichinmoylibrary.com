1 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

13 My Lord Supreme Tells Me ...

My Lord Supreme tells me
To practise every day
Forward sprinting,
And never, never
Backward sprinting.

14 My Aspiration-Heart ...

My aspiration-heart
Is determined today
Not to allow the mind
To walk along
The godless shore.

15 My Lord, Do Capture Me! ...

My Lord, do capture me!
Can You not see
Each and every breath of mine
Is for You, only for You?

16 God And I Barter Every Day ...

God and I barter every day
My gratitude-tears
And His Pride-Smiles.

17 My Lord Loves To Hear From Me ...

My Lord loves to hear from me
My heart-songs
For hours and hours.

18 My Lord Has No Time ...

My Lord has no time
To hear even one story
Of my life.

19 Humanity’s Total Transformation ...

Humanity’s total transformation
Is only a local train,
And can never be
An express train.

20 I Devour The Dust ...

I devour the dust
Of my Lord’s Feet.

21 My Lord Devours The Sands ...

My Lord devours the sands
Of my life’s
Never-ending mistakes.

22 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
Be always on the alert!
At every moment
Try to please God in His own Way.
His Compassion-Eye
May not visit you again.

23 My Oneness With The Will ...

My oneness with the Will
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme,
Alas,
Is still at the beginning stage.

24 God Gives Me His Heart-Breath ...

God gives me His Heart-Breath
Far beyond my necessity.
Needless to say,
I deserve it not in the least.

2 December 2003 ...

2 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

25 My Aspiration-Heart ...

My aspiration-heart
Is not brittle.
Therefore,
It cannot be broken.
