14101

Your heart's soaring aspiration-bird
Will before long
Fly to God, without fail.

14102

He who has
Life-transforming determination
Is already on his way
To victory supreme.

14103

O my soulful heart,
True, you are suffering
But I am sure before long
You will be washing the Compassion-Feet
Of our Lord Beloved Supreme.

14104

Now that my mind's unwillingness-reluctance
Has reached its ultimate limit,
My heart and I together
Will cry for my Lord's Victory-Smile.

14105

If you are sleeplessly loving
Either God or yourself,
How can you have time
To see the imperfections
Of the world?

14106

The desire-demon cannot devour
Your aspiration-hunger
And cannot slow down
Your deer-speed progress.

14107

O my heart,
Be not a fool!
Not only you, but no heart
Can be forsaken
By the Compassion-Lord Supreme.

14108

Every morning without fail
My body, vital and mind
Sing our heart's aspiration-song.

14109

God does not care for
Your mind's mystery.
He cares only for
Your life's mastery.

14110

If you can live
In God's Patience-Heart,
Then there is no distance
That you cannot travel in the inner world.

14111

Be vigilant, O seeker!
Before you realise what is happening,
Your mind may enter into
The abysmal abyss of ignorance-night.

14112

Alas, alas,
Can you not see
That your mind has powerfully
Slammed the door
On perpetual truth?

14113

O my mind,
I do not expect you
To be perfect,
But I do expect you
To be totally receptive
To new and inspiring ideas.

14114

Every day God blesses me
With love-blossoms
In my aspiration-heart.
