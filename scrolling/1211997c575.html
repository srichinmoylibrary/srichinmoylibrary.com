26190

The unaspiring mind will never be able
To smile properly,
Not to speak of divinely.
It only pretends to smile.

26191

God says to the spiritual Master
That since He does not know
What retirement is,
He will not be able to grant retirement
To the spiritual Master.

26192

If God wants my life to be examined
Even by my worst foe,
I shall be more than eagerly ready,
For that is the way I will be able
To make the fastest progress.

26193

When my heart
Does not keep in touch with God,
I see that my monkey-mind
Pinches me infinitely harder.

26194

God, my donkey-back is really strong.
Please allow me to carry
Some of Your burden.
I assure You, I will not collapse.

26195

When aspiration declines,
Frustration becomes
An all-devouring hunger.

26196

My sound-life wants to be sovereign,
But my Lord Supreme
Wants only my God-aspiration-heart
To be my life's sovereign.

26197

God has sent you to earth
Not to collect your complacency-drop
But to harvest
Your God-willingness-thoughts.

26198

O seeker, choose God
As soon as possible.
Then God will definitely
Choose you
One day, without fail.

26199

God will richly reward you
If your heart's progress-life
Moves forward
One heartbeat at a time.

26200

God's Heart-Line
Is longer than the longest.
He needs no extension cord
To reach me.
