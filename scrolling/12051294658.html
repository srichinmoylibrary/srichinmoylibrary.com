19330

Each soulful song
Gives the quintessence
Of one hundred pages
Full of inspiration, aspiration
And delight.

Each soulful song
Is a shortcut
To our destination:
The Golden Shore.

19331

With our soulful prayers
Let us enter into the consciousness
Of the truth-seekers and God-lovers,
Together to create a new life and a new world
For a new generation.

19332

O seeker,
The Supreme is more than willing
To give you something,
But you also have to be willing
To receive it.

19333

The endless fountain of creativity
Comes from only one Source,
And that Source
Is the unconditional Compassion
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

19334

Our Lord Supreme bestows upon us
His unconditional Compassion-Light,
And according to our receptivity
He creates in and through us.

19335

The outer world
Has to be consciously awakened.
Only then will it be willing
To devotedly accept
The beauty and divinity
Of the inner world.

19336

My heart-cries are invited
By God's blue-gold soaring birds
To accompany them to the skies
Of the ever-transcending Beyond.

19337

My Lord Supreme,
Every day I fail You,
Yet repeatedly
You give me another chance.

19338

Fearful are my mind's tears.
Deceitful are my vital's tears.
Beautiful are my heart's tears.
Powerful are my soul's tears.
Fruitful are my life's tears.

19339

Once you enter
Into the spiritual life,
You have to realise
That your human pride has no place,
But your divine pride has.

19340

May my prayer-mind-flower
Be multiplied every morning.
May my meditation-heart-fragrance
Be multiplied every evening.

19341

Each aspiring heart
Eventually becomes fluent
In God's Oneness-Heart-Language.

19342

Although God's Plans
Are inconceivable
And God's Dreams
Are unimaginable,
God Himself
Is always lovable.

19343

Devotion empty of gratitude
Does not and cannot please
The Inner Pilot Supreme.

19344

When the mind lives under
A cloud of suspicion,
The life is forced to accept
The arrival of destruction.
