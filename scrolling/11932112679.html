7515

A patient in God's
Heart-Hospital:
This is what my mind
Must always be.

7516

You cannot fulfil
God's Dream
Without God's Help.

7517

Ignorance does not hesitate
To speak ill of God.
Wisdom does not hesitate
To beat God's Victory-Drum.

7518

What kind of prayer is it
If you have to worry
Even after you have prayed?

7519

In Heaven and on earth
His life of unconditional surrender
Enjoys dual citizenship.

7520

My self-offering-deeds
Are always sponsored
By God Himself.

7521

An aspiration-heart
Is a sure cure
For a depression-vital.

7522

The extinction
Of your doubting mind
Is the beginning
Of your life's perfection-smile.

7523

God's sacred Love for you
Can never remain
A secret.

7524

Do not lose
Your spirituality's
Perfection-hunger,
Never!

7525

To shop in the market
Of ignorance
Your soul did not bring you
Into this world.

7526

Alas,
The thirsty world will drink
Perhaps only
When it is too late!

7527

Your desire-river
Will soon lose
Its existence-life
In your aspiration-ocean.

7528

Lost and found:
Earth has lost
Its aspiration,
Heaven has found
Its compassion.

7529

May my life be
A continuation
Of my unconditional surrender
To God.
