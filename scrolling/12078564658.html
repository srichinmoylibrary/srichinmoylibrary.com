22030

May I be the hope-bird
Between my life's
Rising sun and setting sun.

22031

O my Lord Supreme,
Do tell me why
It is so difficult even to imagine
Something pure in someone.

22032

Alas, man's modern reality
Has not come
From man's ancient dream.
It has come
From somewhere else.

22033

I was born
For God's Victory supreme,
My satisfaction unparalleled.

22034

Alas, I do not know
Why I am destined
So helplessly to travel
Unlit streets.

22035

I do not expect anything
Illumining and fulfilling
From the mind that is schooled
In the art of self-deception.

22036

How can we make progress
In our spiritual life
If we do not have
Doubt-devouring faith
In our aspiration?

22037

Perfection is
When willingness gives
And readiness accepts.

22038

Alas, each human being
Is eager to proclaim
That he is a truth-finder
Without first being a truth-seeker!

22039

Continue to fight
With your frustration-foe.
One day you are bound to triumph!

22040

Your aspiration-preparation
Will inevitably lead you
To your perfection-realisation-goal.

22041

The joy of inevitability
You may not know.
The strength of possibility
You may not know.
But the depth of sincerity
You must know.

22042

Loneliness does not catch us
Tenderly.
It catches us almost always
Ruthlessly.

22043

I do not know
Why the heart has to carry
The heavy weight
Of the mind's ingratitude-breath.

22044

When a human being
Sincerely shows God
The ugliness of his past,
God immediately shows him
The unmistakable beauty
Of his future.
