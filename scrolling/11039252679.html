3 November 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Each prayer is an indispensable step
Towards humanity's progress-delight.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 November 1999, 1:28 a.m. Before doing one-hundred one-arm pushes with 400 lbs. (50 right, 50 left)

4 November 1999 (VI)

﻿

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I serve my Master-Lord, I serve.
Never my heart and I swerve.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/4 November 1999, 8:57 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (104 reps)/

4 November 1999 (VII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

A world-computer-mind.

6 November 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My freedom without peace
Is impossible.
My peace without my surrender
To God's Will
Is impossible.
My surrender to God's Will
Without God's infinite Compassion
Is impossible.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 November 1999, 6:34 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

6 November 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
For the transformation of human nature
And for the perfection of human life,
This world needs peaceful hearts
And not powerful heads.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 November 1999, 6:46 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

And

6 November 1999, 6:48 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

6 November 1999 (IV)

6 November 1999, 6:51 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
With my heart's love, devotion
And surrender-flower
I shall today prayerfully
And soulfully encircle
The Lotus-Feet
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 November 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God's Eye plays with our smiles,
God's Heart plays with our tears
And
God plays with our satisfaction-hearts,
Satisfaction-minds, satisfaction-vitals
And satisfaction-bodies
Only when they live
In their divine consciousness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 November 1999, 6:58 a.m. Before doing one-hundred one-arm pushes with 400 lbs. (50 right, 50 left)
