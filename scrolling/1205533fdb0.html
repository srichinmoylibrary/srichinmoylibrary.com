19760

The human life
Is nothing other than
Struggling and smuggling.
The divine life
Is nothing other than
Self-giving and God-becoming.

19761

I have peace of mind
Only when my Lord Supreme
Places His Compassion-Feet
Upon my cheerful receptivity-heart.

19762

Love
Turns its radiance-eye,
Peace
Turns its satisfaction-eye
To the aspiration-heart-world.

19763

To serve the aspiring world,
May my eyes twinkle
With my soul's Heavenly concern
And sympathy.

19764

My Lord, do give me
A climbing aspiration-heart
Which will disentangle
My mind's fear-doubt-knot
On the way
To my soul's illumination-heights.

19765

The heart's satisfaction-room
Does not allow any resident
Of the mind's doubt-room
To knock at its door.

19766

A spiritual Master
Comes into the world
Anxious to intensify,
Not to verify,
Humanity's God-eagerness-hunger.

19767

God has made a promise
To my aspiration-heart
That there shall come a time
When His fully-blossomed Peace
Will be within my easy grasp.

19768

If you do not spend
A great deal of your time
Loving, serving and pleasing God,
Then how and why
Should God bestow upon you
His all-fulfilling Delight?

19769

When I do not sincerely aspire,
I see nothing but dark fingerprints
Of insecurity, jealousy, fear and doubt
On my life.

19770

Each human being
Is expert at playing
The old ingratitude-game
Inside his thick mind-forest.

19771

A man of constant self-giving
Is indeed a Heaven-sent boon
To humanity.

19772

Each self-giving breath
Is a direct representative
Of God's Dream-Fulfilment-Reality.

19773

You will never be seized
By a severe doubt-attack
If every day
You lovingly take care of
The faith-plant inside your heart.

19774

There shall come a time
When all feet will proudly tread
The oneness-peace-road.
