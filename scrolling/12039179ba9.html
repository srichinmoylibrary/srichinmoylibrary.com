18145

Do not worry about
Your belated gratitude to God,
For whenever you offer
Your gratitude to God,
God will be more than pleased
With you.

18146

Nobody can clip
Your hope-bird's wings
If you do not want them to.

18147

Alas, my mind does not want to know
That I can think
Beyond what it thinks.
Alas, my heart does not want to know
That I can feel
Beyond what it feels.

18148

You expect so much
From God.
How is it that you do not
Allow God
To expect a little more
From you?

18149

The sincere heart knows
How to appreciate.
The clever mind knows
How to appropriate.

18150

Alas, alas, I did not know
That my desire-dust
Would blind the Eyes
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

18151

If it is true
That I have disappointed
My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Then it is equally true
That I am starting my life
All over again today.

18152

My bondage-heart
And my freedom-mind
Have equally made me
Unhappy.

18153

As my life
Is growing shorter every day,
Even so my faith in God
Is growing brighter every day.

18154

This morning
My Lord Beloved Supreme told me
That He would like to give me
Two new names:
Readiness-humility
And
Willingness-purity.

18155

Only when I live inside my heart
Do I see
That my life's confidence-road
Has no obstruction.

18156

May I every day grow into
The unparalleled beauty
Of my heart's streaming tears.

18157

My Lord Supreme,
You have taught me many things.
Please teach me only one more thing:
Gratitude-multiplication.

18158

The human in us
Has to become divine
If it wants to greet the soul
For God-manifestation.

18159

I am lost between
My heart's confidence-light
And my mind's arrogance-night.
