15345

Every morning
My soul-priest
Collects my heart-blossoms
Lovingly and proudly,
And places them on the altar
Of my Lord Supreme.

15346

Like my heart,
If my body, vital and mind
Also cry for progress-delight,
Then the world will be flooded
With God-Beauty and God-Prosperity.

15347

Two meals I take daily:
My unconditional life-surrender
And
My unconditional heart-gratitude.

15348

God, do You not need
A short vacation?
"I do not need a vacation.
What I badly need
Is your life's
Immediate perfection!"

15349

God will help you
Command your mind-thoughts
If you offer Him
Your heart's surrender-gift.

15350

Only one enthusiasm-step
Shortens my destination-distance,
Quickens my speed
And enlightens my life.

15351

Unless you are eagerly ready
To approach God on His own terms,
You will remain a complete failure
Both in your inner life
And in your outer life.

15352

Ask yourself only one question,
The question of questions:
Do you belong to God's Heart
Or do you belong to your own mind?

15353

Each temptation-thought
Is finally forced
To join life's extinction-parade.

15354

When you keep your eyes turned inward,
You are blessed with
A sweetness-satisfaction-surge.

15355

Your wisdom-soul-teacher
Does not mind
Teaching you the same lesson
Again and again
Provided you want to learn it.
What is that lesson of life?
God-manifestation on earth!

15356

Who has ever seen
Satisfaction-famine
Inside his unconditional
Surrender-life?

15357

I tell you, O my mind,
Your encyclopedia-memory
Has nothing to do with
God-remembrance-ecstasy.
You are permitted only to remember
Earth-bound facts,
Not Heaven-free realities.

15358

God has a Heart of sympathy
Even if He doesn't have
Anything else.
The spirituality shortage
In today's world
Is causing severe pangs
In His Heart.

15359

O my heart, offer to God
Your oneness-flame.
He will immediately grant you
His Fulness-Sun.
