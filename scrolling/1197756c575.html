12090

God is always ready
With His Compassion-Train.
When will you regain
Your aspiration-reign?

12091

The life of an aspiration-lover
Never suffers from desire-cancer.
Every day his life enjoys
God's Satisfaction-Delight.

12092

Your mind's illumination-perfection
Is not a far cry
Because your life is steadily heading
In the right dedication-direction.

12093

One dark desire is bold enough
To eclipse your heart's aspiration-sun
For a longer than the longest time.

12094

Just because my mind
Is refusing to accept truth,
Do you think my heart will stop
Choosing truth - the only right thing?

12095

Satisfaction is no luxury.
It is God's absolute necessity
In man's life
And man's absolute necessity
For God's Heart.

12096

Because you are your heart's
Genuine aspiration-cry,
God does not want you
To allow your mind
Near the revolving temptation-door
Of confusion.

12097

If you do not protect your purity-heart
With your security-mind,
Your life's satisfaction-boat
Will never start sailing.

12098

Your life is doomed
To an insignificant end
Because even in your dreams
You do not care to satisfy
God the Compassion.

12099

With your heart's silence-prayers
When you arrive at God's Door,
He immediately opens it
And happily and proudly
Asks His Eye and Heart
To dance for you.

12100

Lord, do tell me just once more
That soon I shall be able
To please You
In Your own Way.
