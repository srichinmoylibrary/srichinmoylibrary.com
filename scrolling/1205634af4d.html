19875

Do you love God,
Or do you love yourself?
If you love God,
Then both my heart and my mind
Will be for you.
But if you love yourself,
Not even my mind,
Not to speak of my heart,
Will be for you.

19876

Believe me,
Sincerity saves our mind,
Purity saves our heart,
Simplicity saves our life.

19877

May my life-pendulum swing
Between
God's Compassion-Eye
And God's Forgiveness-Hand.

19878

You do yourself a great disservice
When you do not allow God
To speak to the world
On your behalf.

19879

Human love
Blooms with a thorn.
Divine love
Blossoms with
An ever-brightening dawn.

19880

The mind's complacency
Has to be replaced
By the heart's
Action-perfection-adequacy.

19881

When I ask God
If He loves me unconditionally,
He blesses me
With an unquestionable "Yes!"

19882

O my heart,
Why do you so often allow
Your helplessness and hopelessness
To torture my life?

19883

Now that you are free
Of your ignorance-mind,
Can you not love all the time
With your wisdom-soul?

19884

The mind,
Even while looking,
Sees nothing.

The heart,
Even without looking,
Sees everything.

19885

O my Lord,
When I doubt You,
Do turn my life into
A voiceless victim of fear.

19886

Each day
May a thousand God-dreams
Energise my mind
And thrill my heart.

19887

At last
My mind is set free
By God's
Compassion-Miracle-Eye.

19888

No mental illusion
Can ever be shattered
Without God's
Compassion-flooded Smile.

19889

The mind is fond of scattering
Thousands of meaningless
And useless fantasies.
