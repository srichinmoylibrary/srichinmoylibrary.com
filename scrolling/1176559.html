3601 Obedience Is The Fruitful Goal

Obedience is the blessingful seeker.
Obedience is the hopeful journey.
Obedience is the soulful road.
Obedience is the fruitful goal.

3602 My Obedience

My obedience
Has God the eternal Purity.
My obedience
Is God the immortal Duty.
My obedience
Shall become God the infinite Beauty.

3603 The Divine Capacity And Inclination

The human capacity and tendency
Is to confuse and complicate the truth.
The divine capacity and inclination
Is not only to unravel the truth
But also to keep it always straight, direct,
One-pointed and God-revealing.

3604 God-Manifesting Light

In the outer world,
Light replaces darkness
And darkness replaces light.

In the inner world,
Light is replaced by more light,
More light is replaced by infinite Light
And infinite Light is replaced by
The ever-transcending
And God-manifesting Light.

3605 I Have No Time

I have no time to govern others' lives.
Therefore, I secretly cry.
I have no time to govern my own life.
Therefore, I openly and untimely die.

3606 If You Do Not Glow

I know
If you do not glow,
You will not allow me to grow.

I know
If you do not grow,
You will not allow me to sow.

I know
If you do not or cannot sow,
You will consider me your eternal foe.

3607 Your Body Annoys You

Your body annoys you because
It is blindness incarnate.
Your vital annoys you because
It is aggressiveness incarnate.
Your mind annoys you because
It is meanness incarnate.
Your heart annoys you because
It is weakness incarnate.
And you annoy them because
You have no real concern for them,
You have no time to transform them,
You have no eagerness for their perfection.

3608 Immediate Neighbours

Self-examination and life-deception
Are always perfect strangers.
God-realisation and truth-revelation
Are eternally immediate neighbours.

3609 The Inner Conquest

The outer conquest makes you feel
Dubiously and, at most, partially happy.
The inner conquest makes you see
The beauty of happiness,
Makes you feel
The purity of happiness
And makes you one with
The divinity of happiness.

3610 We Have Undivine Thoughts

We have undivine thoughts
Because we give them
Undue and undivided attention.
We lead undivine lives
Because we are afraid
Of the fire-pure transformation
That the divine life
Is graciously and anxiously
Waiting to grant us.

3611 Why Cry?

Why cry,
Since you know how to smile?
Why smile only,
Since you know also how to sing?
And while singing,
Try to remember that dancing
Definitely and considerably adds
To the soulfulness and fruitfulness of singing.

3612 Your Task, God's-Task

Your task is to manifest God
Here on earth sleeplessly.
To His infinite Sorrow,
You are not doing it.
God's Task is to inspire you
And energise you constantly.
To your wide surprise,
He is doing it constantly,
Cheerfully and self-givingly.

3613 If You Are An Unaspiring Man

If you are an unaspiring man,
Then you are bound to spread two things:
Anxiety and fear.
If you are an aspiring seeker,
Then you are bound to spread two things:
Your heart of love
And
Your life of concern.

3614 If You Know How To Think Properly

If you know how to think properly,
Then you do not have to attempt
To perfect others constantly.
If you know how to think properly,
Then your thought-perfection
And their life-perfection
Will always go together.
