8330

You have not done your duty well
On earth!
How can you ask for a position
In Heaven?

8331

To be a member
Of God's Heart-Club,
You must not be found travelling
On desire-train.

8332

Life's complete dedication
Means a giant step
Towards perfection.

8333

God's inner Thunder
Has awakened your mind.
Don't go to sleep anymore!

8334

God is not at all proud
Of your mind's
Proud renunciation.

8335

Power is meant
For greatness.
Peace is meant
For goodness.

8336

Your mind's wild laughter
Must surrender
To your heart's sweet smile.

8337

Inspiration
Comes from the known.
Aspiration
Comes from the unknown.
Realisation
Comes from the Unknowable.

8338

Be careful
Of your vital emotion
And your mental frustration.
Both of them are expert thieves!

8339

God's Compassion
Will always try to protect
Your soulful self-confidence.

8340

Increase your love of God
Daily.
Then only
No disaster will befall you.

8341

Tell me a new story!
Your desire-life story
Is an extremely old story.
Do I get any joy from it?

8342

I cannot separate you
From your vital's endless chain
Of bitter complaints.

8343

Your mind is blocking
Your heart's heavenward journey
With the help of the depression
Of your vital.

8344

The sweetness of your heart
Is the most precious Wealth
Of God.
