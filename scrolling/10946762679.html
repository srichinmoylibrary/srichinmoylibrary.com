13 At Every Moment ...

At every moment
We must be careful!
Ruthless and baseless
Worries and anxieties
Invade our lives
When we enter into
Our mind-jungles.

14 January 2002 ...

14 January 2002

Ho Chi Minh City, Viet Nam

14 Nothing Remains The Same For Long...

“Nothing remains the same for long.”
Not true!
My unconditional
God-surrender-life
Always remains the same.

15 My Lord ...

My Lord,
How will I recognise You
If or when You come to see me?
“My child,
You will be able to recognise Me
Easily
From your ecstasy-flooded
Heart-throbs.”

16 When We Are Aspiring ...

When we are aspiring
For divine qualities,
There is no such thing
As too much.

17 When We Greedily Crave ...

When we greedily crave
For earthly possessions,
Each possession can decrease
Our God-hunger.

18 If You Think And Feel ...

If you think and feel
That you are working extremely,
Extremely hard,
Just think of the Old Man
Upstairs in Heaven.
Your embarrassment
Shall beggar description.

19 Today’s God-Adoration ...

Today’s God-adoration
Invariably tomorrow
Becomes God-destination.

20 All my prayers

All my prayers
And all my meditations
Have only one secret desire
Inside them:
God-fulfilment in God’s own Way.

16 January 2002 ...

16 January 2002

Ho Chi Minh City, Viet Nam

21 To My Greatest Joy ...

To my greatest joy,
Every day my God-oneness-heart
And my man-oneness-life
Are expanding.

22 To Fulfil Our Heart’s ...

To fulfil our heart’s
Every divine need,
God Himself
Comes down.

23 In The Doubting And Suspecting ...

In the doubting and suspecting
Mind-jail,
It is extremely difficult
For the seeker-heart
To survive.

24 The Mind-Newspaper ...

The mind-newspaper
Carries only accidents,
Catastrophes, crimes,
Obscure and impure news.

25 The Heart-Newspaper ...

The heart-newspaper
Carries only the news
About the Supreme Lord,
About the cosmic gods
And goddesses
And about the archangels
And angels.
