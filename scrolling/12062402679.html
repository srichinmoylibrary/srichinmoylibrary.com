20415

When there is a tug-of-war
Between my desire-mind
And my aspiration-heart,
God always takes the side
Of my heart.

20416

My Lord,
As You need no audience
When You speak to me,
How is it
That I always long for an audience
When I am with You
And around You?

20417

My fame-fed ego
Has forced me
To be the possessor
Of constant unhappiness.

20418

If you heart and mind
Are schooled in patience,
Then there is nothing
That you cannot accomplish
Either in the world of aspiration
Or in the life of dedication.

20419

There is and there can be
No specific "right moment"
For God-adoration
And God-satisfaction
In God's own Way.

20420

The mind's jealousy-jungle
Does not have a long life.
Sooner or later,
It has to wither away.

20421

As ignorance does not want
To let me go,
Even so, I do not want
To let God go from me.

20422

My aspiring heart shuns
Everything that is detrimental
To my spiritual development.

20423

Plunge deep,
Spread wide
And rise high!
Lo, your heart becomes one
With God's Smile-Blossom.

20424

If you have unwavering aspiration,
Then God will appear before you
With His Rainbow-Happiness.

20425

Each time a doubt
Attacks my mind,
My soul-bird suffers
In my heart-cage.

20426

Disobedience-destruction,
Disobedience-danger,
Disobedience-disaster
Is lowering the consciousness
Of the loving, aspiring, self-giving
And peace-dreaming humanity.

20427

O my compassion-heart-eye,
My mind needs you
For its purification and perfection.

20428

God has blessed us
With not one,
But a thousand exits
If we want to escape
From our mind's doubt-prison.

20429

The mind-cancer
Is doubt.
The heart-cancer
Is insecurity.
The life-cancer
Is fear.
