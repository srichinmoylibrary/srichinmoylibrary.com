15775

There will be a new world
Which will be created
By genuine truth-seekers and God-lovers.
In this new world,
Only God and His choice children
Will play, sing and dance.

15776

The mind oscillates
Between truth and falsehood.
Alas,
How can I free myself
From this mind-oscillation?
What I need is a mind
That permanently lives
In the heart of truth.

15777

To me, indecision is not something
Insignificant.
It is something significant,
Something destructive,
For it does not allow me
To go forward
To see the beauty of truth
And the light of joy.

15778

Alas,
When shall I be able
To liberate myself
From my mind's ever-thickening
Jungle-night?

15779

God has a very special Concern
For the newly-arrived souls.
He does not want them to mix freely
With the old souls,
Who have surrendered to
ignorance-night.
He wants these newly-arrived souls
To fight right from the beginning
Against ignorance-night
And establish His supreme Victory
Here on earth.

15780

Each human being by nature
Wants to know the truth.
But very few
Want to grow into the truth
And become truth-representative-messengers
On earth.

15781

God wants to see
This universe of His
Flooded not with equality-rivalry
But with equanimity-satisfaction.

15782

Man has turtle-speed.
Man has insect-ability.
Yet man is fond of being
Extolled to the skies
By the whole world.

15783

From our heart's aspiration-airport
We shall have to come
To our life's dedication-airport.
From our life's dedication-airport
We shall have to go to God's
Manifestation-Satisfaction-Airport.

15784

When I see the eagerness
Of a starving spiritual seeker,
I feel that the full manifestation
Of God on earth
Will soon take place.

15785

Do not allow your fear, doubt,
Anxiety and worry
To march before you
Proudly and shamelessly.
Capture them and transform them
Into choice instruments of God.

15786

Each human being
Should long for the time
When he will be flooded
With peace-prosperity.

15787

What has gone wrong
With your aspiration-cry?
I see there is no sincerity,
No purity, no intensity in it.
Alas,
Your aspiration-cry has lost
All its beauty, purity and divinity.

15788

My heart of unconditional surrender
Is God's supreme spokesman
On earth.

15789

Someday
No one will be born,
No one will live,
No one will die
In ignorance-prison-captivity.
