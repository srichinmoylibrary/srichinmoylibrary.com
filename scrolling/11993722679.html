13615

When the world
Deliberately and ruthlessly
Attacks your aspiration-heart,
God's Compassion-Eye
Will, without fail, defend you.

13616

A self-giving heart
Will never be mistaken
For a life-deceiving mind.

13617

A sleepless protection-prayer
Always expedites
Our Heavenward journey.

13618

You are so obstinate
In your insecurity-life.
How can God ever
Be happy with you?

13619

When God comes first
In everything that we say and do,
Then our minds cannot be paralysed
By self-doubt.

13620

God's Compassion
Has a fond grip on me.
Therefore, I will not and cannot fail
In my God-manifestation on earth.

13621

A seeker without humility
Can, at best, become
A flower without fragrance.

13622

How can God invite you to study
At His Perfection-Satisfaction-School,
If you treasure your Master's Degree
In failure and sadness?

13623

God tells me that He will be homeless
If I do not invite Him
To come and stay with me
In my heart-room.

13624

I know, I know
How fast I could have run
In my inner life
If only insecurity and doubt
Had left me alone!

13625

When self-doubt is in full command,
Neither the inner world
Nor the outer world
Needs the seeker.

13626

Every day you must
Prepare yourself to be
A better citizen of the world.

13627

No, not an outer enemy
But your inner enemy, fear,
Has blinded you completely.
Therefore, you see no light
In your heart-garden.

13628

As our life-book
Does not start with frustration,
Even so,
It does not end with frustration.
As our life-book
Starts with aspiration,
Even so,
It will continue with aspiration.

13629

Alas, I am completely lost
Between my heart's foggy morning
And my life's frosty evening.
