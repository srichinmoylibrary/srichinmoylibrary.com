18430

Beautiful
Is
My heart-flower.

Fruitful
Is
My life-garden.

18431

Each time we miss
A Heaven-sent opportunity,
We automatically forget
The special road that leads
To God's highest Heights.

18432

I pray
For God to grant me
The opportunity
To cover the length and breadth
Of the world
With His immense Fragrance-Name.

18433

O my mind,
For such a long time
You have been walking
Along the path of spirituality.
Can you not start running now?

18434

I am not going to be satisfied
By rebuilding my aspiration-bridge
To the Golden Shore.
I want to build a totally new bridge
With new aspiration-flames
And new dedication-flowers.

18435

O my mind,
Why do you forget
That your sole duty
Is to explore
And not to explode?

18436

What I need
Is a mind-expanding thought.
What I need
Is a self-giving heart,
And nothing less,
Nothing more.

18437

Alas, my heart
Can no longer stand
My Lord's
Compassion-Arrival-suspense.

18438

The human mind carries
An unauthorised version
Of God's Transcendental Vision.

18439

What I desperately need is
God-submissiveness
And not
God-possessiveness.

18440

No road is easy.
Therefore, we must walk and run
Along the road
That has already been accepted
By our heart.

18441

My heart's cheerfulness-sunshine
Has at last illumined
My mind's despair-night.

18442

God-news may travel slowly,
But it travels effectively,
Convincingly
  And supremely.

18443

I have finally far transcended
The exorbitant strength
Of my commanding mind
And demanding vital.

18444

I do not know
Precisely what it is,
But there is something
Inside the Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Supreme
That cheerfully and proudly
Claims me.
