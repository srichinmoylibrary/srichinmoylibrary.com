24375

My joy knows no bounds
When I see every day
My heart-flower blooming
At the Feet
Of my Beloved Lord Supreme.

24376

We love God
Only when we need Him.
God loves us
Even when He does not need us
At all.

24377

The mind
Is apt to use
Counterfeit love-coins.

24378

The streaming tears
Of the aspiration-heart
Run faster than the fastest
Towards the Goal.

24379

The smile
Of a gratitude-heart
Has no equal.

24380

The effort needed for God-realisation
Is a constant effort,
Not a sudden stupendous effort.

24381

How can the sunlit path
Have darkness-difficulties?
No, the sunlit path always remains sunlit.
But the seeker must be ready,
Willing and eager
To walk only on the sunlit path.

24382

He who does not use
His argumentative mind
Is definitely a choice instrument
Of God.

24383

There are many sad stories
In human life.
The saddest one
Is man's continuous self-doubt.

24384

Who does not need another chance?
Even God needs chance after chance
To transform humanity.

24385

God will really reward you
If you are always prepared
To keep God as the only Goal
In your life.

24386

The beauty
Of a totally surrendered soul
Is far beyond
The beauty of a sunrise.

24387

The river of time is welcoming
All God-lovers to join it
While it is sleeplessly running
Towards the infinite ocean
Of Light and Delight,
The infinite sea of Peace and Bliss.

24388

Ask your heart!
It will tell you
What God wants you to do.

24389

You cannot cultivate
Your aspiration-field
Unless you have
Ceaseless heart-tears.
