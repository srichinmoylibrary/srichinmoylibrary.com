21730

My spirituality will sink
If my mind does not
Start thinking of God
And my heart does not
Start loving God.

21731

Only a self-giving heart
Can have uninterrupted zeal
For pleasing the Absolute Lord Supreme.

21732

My mind-knowledge
Is always
Wisdom-poverty-stricken.

21733

For me
The most difficult question to answer
Is this:
Will I ever be able to love God
In God's own Way?

21734

A God-disobedience-mind
And a heart-agony-experience
Are inseparable.

21735

I do not want to succeed
In anything
Without my Lord's blessingful Consent
And powerful Cooperation.

21736

A fruitful meditation
Is nothing other than
Diving into the sea of silence.

21737

The best way
To lose doubt-weight
Is to sing, play and dance
Inside your own
Fragrance-heart-garden.

21738

If you want to be happy,
Then breathe in
Compassion and endurance,
And breathe out
Frustration and anger.

21739

Do not ask the same old question
Again and again:
What can God do for you?
Ask a totally new question:
What can you do for God?

21740

O my seeker-friend,
If you want to ascend
The stairway of ecstasy,
Then discard immediately
Your mind's long-cherished
Frustration-chains.

21741

My loving surrender
To God's Will
Is, indeed, my life's
Future dawn-harbinger.

21742

Alas,
Slow, slower and slowest
Will always be
Humanity's upward climb.

21743

Alas,
Man's mind is surrounded
By useless thoughts,
And man's heart is surrounded
By Godless feelings.

21744

My Lord,
I have come to realise
That my lifestyle will change
Only if You mete out
Your Thunder-Justice.
