16360

Once you see the height and depth
Of your inner life,
You will be able to run
Faster than the fastest
Along Eternity's Road.

16361

Even if you work mechanically,
Without enthusiasm,
The Supreme will be more pleased with you
Than with someone who works
With countless negative forces.

16362

The mind does not know
Where the heart is.
The heart does not know
What the mind wants.

16363

In our inner life of prayer
We want to love the Supreme.
In our outer life of action
We want to serve the Supreme
And be true members
Of the universal family.

16364

Alas,
Many older seekers feel
That they have one foot in the grave,
Or that they can enjoy
Retirement-relaxation.
Absolutely untrue!
God wishes all His children,
Young and old,
To have the same sleepless determination,
Enthusiasm and willingness
To serve Him.

16365

Continue, continue
With your oneness-sympathy,
Oneness-compassion
And oneness-love
For God's manifestation!

16366

You may not have
Many undivine forces
Inside you,
But if you do not have enthusiasm
And eagerness
How can you make satisfactory progress?

16367

You and I and everybody
Have to be faithful
Only to one Being,
And that Being
Is the Lord Beloved Supreme
Inside our hearts.

16368

If you look
From the mountaintop of aspiration
At your many problems,
They will seem very small.

16369

You are very happy and proud
That you have climbed up
The aspiration-tree.
But still you must reach
The high, higher, highest branches!

16370

Why are you unhappy
While you are with your Master?
Because although your body is with him,
Your mind is somewhere else!

16371

You may not be able
To work for God
In a perfect consciousness.
But at least work for Him
With your imperfect consciousness!

16372

Many fail at times
In the life-examination.
But even among the failures,
Why must you be the worst?
You can be the very best!

16373

When your heart's
Aspiration-flower
Smiles,
That is the time
God comes and claims you
As a choice instrument
Of His.

16374

You may be angry with God
For something He has asked you to do.
But why must you add
Other negative factors:
Haughtiness, disobedience and impurity?
