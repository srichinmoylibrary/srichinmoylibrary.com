10715

This morning I gave God
What I had:
My openness-heart.

This evening God gave me
What He eternally is:
His Fulness-Victory.

10716

When did God tell you to live
In your darkness-hermit-mind-cave
And not in His illumination-flooded
Heart-Palace?

10717

My secret desire is to see
God's Fondness-Eye.
My sacred desire is to give God
The fragrance of my oneness-heart.

10718

You want to know if I have
Anything special to show you.
Yes, I have!
I have my aspiration-love-flames
That play upon the Heart-Ocean
Of my Lord Supreme.

10719

This morning
God told my aspiration-heart
That it has nothing to do
With my stupid mind's
Failure-peaks.

10720

My life's Lord Supreme,
Today You are Your Heart's
New Love-Forgiveness
And I am my heart's
New-found faith.

10721

My God-loving heart
Is crying tomorrow's tears
To smash the confines
Of earth-bound time
And fly away
Into Infinity's Heart-Delight.

10722

Imitation can either limit
Your life's ultimate capacity
Or awaken your heart
To the highest Reality.

10723

My long-forgotten aspiration-sun
Is once more rising
Because today I bravely dared
To lose my mind's little "I".

10724

Every morning my soul teaches me
A new surrender-song,
And every morning I teach myself
A new gratitude-dance.

10725

Be not your mind's
Devouring negative tree.
Be your heart's
Climbing positive plant.

10726

If your heart's oneness
Is hard at work,
Then you are chosen to see God
Walking with His Sweetness-Smile
Inside your flower-heart-garden.

10727

The human mind cannot rest
Because it is constantly restless.
The divine heart does not rest
Because it has always something to do
For its Beloved Supreme.

10728

When God passes by me,
It takes Him only
Three fleeting seconds.
Should I not then think
Of His Compassion-Heart
And His Vision-Eye
Instead of thinking
Of my own insecurity-heart
And my impurity-mind?

10729

Who has written
My Heaven-climbing prayers?
God the universal Compassion-Heart.
Who is going to read them?
God the transcendental Satisfaction-Eye.
