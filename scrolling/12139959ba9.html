645.

A God-inundated heart
Each human being needs
To make himself really,
Divinely and supremely happy.

646.

O my Lord,
I am praying to You
To grant me the adamantine will-power
To completely loosen
My mind's possession-grip.

647.

God can easily remain forever untouched
By man's ignorance-eye,
But His Compassion-Heart allows
Man's ignorance-eye to touch Him
Whenever it wants to.

648.

The wiser man becomes,
The easier it is for God
To give to man,
And the more man can receive
From God.

649.

Who can satisfy
A selfish person?
No, not even God Himself.

650.

I do not need
A thought-provoking mind.
I need
A God-invoking heart.

651.

If we really want to make God happy,
Then we have to make a daring attempt
To scale God's Perfection-Peak.

652.

My Lord,
I pray to You
To make me my Master's
Constant joy-fountain-smile.

653.

There is a grand staircase
Between Heaven and earth.
You are allowed to use that staircase
Only when your life becomes
An unconditional surrender
To God's Will.

654.

My Lord Supreme
Definitely loves
Even my bullock cart-speed-progress.

655.

Indeed a major surprise:
Even my mind is now
Entirely dependent on God.

656.

My jungle-mind
Has surprisingly entered
Into my heart-garden-home
To sincerely become
A member of its family.

657.

The more
Man speaks unkindly
About God's creation,
The less
God listens.

658.

A sleeplessly God-loving heart
Is fully responsible
For my mind's radical and magical
Miracle-transformation.

659.

O my heart,
At every moment
Keep yourself away from the vital's
All-poisoning God-resentment.
