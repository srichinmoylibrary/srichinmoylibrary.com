18630

I do not want to know
How real my Lord is.
I just want to know
How real my love of my Lord is.

18631

Two songs every day I sing
To my Lord Supreme:
My mind's obedience-song
And
My heart's surrender-song.

18632

It seems that
The mind's expectations
And
The life's futilities
Are inseparable.

18633

Meditation is fruitful
Only when
The mind becomes a reservoir
Of stillness-patience-bliss.

18634

To say that spirituality
Is mental poverty
Is absurdity.
Spirituality is mental receptivity.
Spirituality is the joy of awareness
And the love of self-giving.

18635

Do not postpone
Your self-improvement-task.
Your soul wants you
To make good time
And go beyond
The limits of time.

18636

A true self-giver
Knows not what
A God-fulfilment-
Opportunity-shortage is.

18637

God does not want me to have
Any aspiration-substitute
Since He does not want to have
Any Satisfaction-substitute.

18638

I have employed
My aspiration-heart-guard
To catch
My doubt-mind-thief.

18639

I can afford to experience
The rise and fall of my mind-kingdom,
But I can never, never afford to experience
The rise and fall of my heart-kingdom.

18640

How can the human mind
Solve the doubt-problems
Since the mind itself
Cherishes doubts?

18641

Attention-craving people
Cannot dive deep within
To see the reality
Of their own infinite capacities.

18642

The divine security
Is not meant for
The unaspiring
And uncommitted heart.

18643

A heart of gratitude
Finds divine Blessings
And divine Love
All along its way.

18644

The very breath of faith
Is not only
Divinely powerful
But also
Supremely fruitful.
