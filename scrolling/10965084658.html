29 The Love Of The Heart ...

The love of the heart
Is accepted
By the four corners
Of the globe.

30 Not Imperfection ...

Not imperfection
But perfection
Hides deep within us.

31 God Does Not Want ...

God does not want
To capture us
With His temptation-snare.

32 God Wants ...

God wants
To capture us
With His Eye’s Compassion-Care.

33 O My Mind ...

O my mind,
Why do you try
To find fault with others?
You will gain nothing,
Absolutely nothing.

34 My Heart ...

My heart
Has gained everything
Precisely because of
Its cheerful oneness with all.

35 I Cannot Imagine ...

I cannot imagine
How God still has hopes
For me!

36 My Prayers ...

My prayers
Cry and cry
To see God’s Face.

37 My Meditations ...

My meditations
Dive and dive
To feel God’s Heart
And become one with God.

38 Mind-Confusion ...

Mind-confusion
Destroys
Our purity-hearts.

39 Complexity And The Mind ...

Complexity and the mind
Never want to remain alone.

40 We Search For Everything ...

We search for everything
But not for our spirit —
The only Real in us.

41 Hope Tells Us ...

Hope tells us
To look upwards.

42 Faith Tells Us ...

Faith tells us
To look all around.

43 Promise Tells Us ...

Promise tells us
To look up first
And then look down
For our God-manifestation.
