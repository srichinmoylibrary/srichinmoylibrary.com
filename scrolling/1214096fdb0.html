760.

My soul proudly smiles
At my heart's
Deeper than the deepest
Meditations.

761.

My soul's dynamism
Has finally found a way
To throw out my body's lethargy.

762.

The sweetness of a devotion-heart
Can easily counteract
The bitterness of a cynical mind.

763.

God is always anxious and eager
To answer man's
Surrender-gratitude-inspired questions.

764.

Like the outer sun,
The inner sun is always radiating
Its divinity's beauty in all directions,
Although at times we may not see it.

765.

The Sweetness-Flute of God's Voice
Enchants not only the aspiring heart,
But even the doubting mind.

766.

At long last
My aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
Are all ready
For our Lord's Compassion-Arrival.

767.

What is the mind's negativity?
The mind's negativity
Is a poisonous mushroom
That is found in the mind-jungle.

768.

The edifice of goodness
Is built upon
A firm oneness-foundation.

769.

The easiest way to God-realisation
Is the way
Of sleepless and breathless surrender
To God's Will.

770.

An ordinary life:
What is it?
It is a dry desert of desire.
A spiritual life:
What is it?
It is the blossoming of a heart-garden.

771.

Only my heart's silence-sea
Can stop my mind
From racing around.

772.

Just because the mind
Is an impossible fool,
It wants to trample the truth.

773.

The human mind wants to know
All the myths about God.
The divine heart wants to realise
An iota of God's Love.

774.

To have a heart of aspiration
And a life of dedication
Unquestionably means
Doing God's Will.
