13775

My heart's purity-flames
Will one day dance with
The twinkling stars of Heaven.
This is the hope-promise
I treasure.

13776

I love sorrow-sweetness
Because it at once
Purifies my heart
And illumines my mind.

13777

O my heart's hope-river,
May you always remain
Close, visible and available!

13778

Depression-darkness
And hope-enthusiasm
Speak in two
Totally different languages.

13779

A powerful promise-heart
Can freely fly
In Heaven's vastness
And fulness-sky.

13780

You have two soulful eyes
Of hope.
You have two powerful arms
Of promise.
Therefore, nothing and nobody
Can obstruct your way
To Infinity's Shore.

13781

My hope may die,
But not my promise.
My promise may die,
But not my Lord's
Dream-Fulfilment-Reality
In and through me.

13782

My Lord,
You do not have to prove to me
That You love me.
But do give me the capacity
To prove to You
That I love You.

13783

My Lord,
Every day I long to swim
In the river of
Your measureless Compassion-Smiles.

13784

Is there any division-mind
That does not need God?
Is there any oneness-heart
That God does not need?

13785

Doubt-freed,
My mind has at last decided
To sing and play
In freedom's fragrance-garden.

13786

Every peace-blossoming heart
Is a chosen instrument
Of the Absolute Lord Supreme.

13787

O whispering voice of delight,
Do accept
My heart's streaming tears.

13788

An unconditionally surrendered
God-seeker
Is bound to arrive quickly
At God's Promise-Fulfilment-Shore.

13789

Live in the heart's purity;
You will see paradise everywhere.
Live in the mind's impurity;
You will see prison everywhere.
