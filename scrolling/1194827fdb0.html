9160

Life itself is always an opportunity.
If you are in the animal life,
You have the opportunity
  To become human.
If you are in the human life,
You have the opportunity
  To become divine,
     Plus perfect.

9161

What is your old self?
Your old self is
  An unsatisfactory self
That you have created
By not emptying your life-vessel
  Of its brooding imperfection.

9162

The new year comes
To give you another opportunity,
  Another hope.
But the new year will come and go.
So begin your self-transformation-play
  Immediately!

9163

The doubt-cancer in your mind
Is spreading every day.
  Be careful!
Faith-radiation is of immediate need.

9164

Is there anybody
Who does not indulge
  In opinions?
Is there anybody
Who does not wish to adhere
  To his convictions?

9165

He who can soulfully smile at God
Can definitely sail fast
On life's progress-river.

9166

When your dedication becomes irregular,
  Your faith in yourself
Cannot do anything else except wane.

9167

An impure mind
Quite often desires to use
  Its volcano-irritation
To destroy the world.

9168

Keep aside three special minutes a day.
The first minute you will use
  To realise God,
The second minute, to manifest God,
The third minute, to become
  God's sleepless Satisfaction-Delight.

9169

When the suspicious mind
And the dynamic vital
  Engage in a tug-of-war,
The heart invariably
Takes the side of the vital.

9170

The simpler the life, the better.
The purer the mind, the better.
The deeper the heart, the better.
The higher the self-giving, the better.
The quicker the God-realisation, the better.
The stronger the God-oneness, the better.

9171

Everyone loves to blame somebody else.
But who is that somebody else
  If not a portion of
The unrealised universal Self?

9172

His new inner boss, faith,
Dispensed with his old colleague, doubt,
The moment faith assumed
  Legitimate office.

9173

How long can you cling
To the shore of hope
Without using your heart's
  Determination-strength
To win in the battlefield of life?

9174

  If you see
Through the heart's eye,
  You will see
That you have already started
Your life's perfection-race.
