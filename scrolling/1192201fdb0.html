6560

Do not think of your future,
For God has already prepared it
Inside your heart's
Aspiration-cry.

6561

A mind of doubt
And a heart of despair
Do not like each other.
Yet they are immediate neighbours.

6562

My Lord,
Since I am wasting Your time,
Shall I give up?
"My child,
If you give up,
I shall be jobless."

6563

God's Music is sweet.
God's Silence is sweeter.
God's Oneness-Soul
Is the sweetest.

6564

His was a life of self-giving.
Therefore
When he left his body,
A chorus of God-Smiles
Greeted him in Heaven.

6565

The Hand of God
Opens
Only when the heart of man
Is ready to cry
Sleeplessly.

6566

Fear cherished,
Oneness perished.
Who can deny
This experience universal?

6567

If you do not dare
To meet God's Eyes,
How will you dare
To feel His Heart?

6568

Yesterday proved
That my mind was something.
Today proves
That my mind is absolutely nothing.
Tomorrow will prove
That my heart has and is everything.

6569

If you do not include God
In your outer life,
Then God does not want to become
A member of your inner family.

6570

For him
There is no yesterday.
For him
There is no tomorrow.
For him
There is only now,
An eternal Now.

6571

If you want your future
Only to glow with promises,
And not with actions,
Your life will dance
On nothingness.

6572

If you allow your life
To be enfeebled by fear,
You will never be able to reach
Your heart's perfection-room.

6573

In the twinkling of an eye
My Beloved Supreme
Abandons the mind
Of indifference-desert.

6574

Why do you repeatedly ask God
To grow His Perfection-Plants
Inside your barren heart-garden?
