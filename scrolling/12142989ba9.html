945.

God's Compassion-Eye
And God's Perfection-Life
Are not different.
They are one and the same.

946.

In the spiritual life
The obedience of a oneness-heart
Is of supreme importance.

947.

My heart's mounting cries
Have gladdened God's Heart
Far beyond my mind's
Imagination-flight.

948.

God's Justice-Hand
Can abandon my disobedience-mind,
But not God's Compassion-Eye —
Never!

949.

My heart's sleepless
God-satisfaction-cry
Has liberated me from my mind's
Earthbound fear.

950.

The beauty and fragrance
Of an aspiration-heart
Far surpasses all.

951.

O my doubting mind,
No matter how many times you criticise
My ever-blossoming heart,
You cannot decrease the beauty and fragrance
Of my heart-flower.

952.

O insecurity,
O jealousy,
O impurity,
I am determined
To conquer you all
Before I make
My final God-satisfaction-journey.

953.

O my complete God-surrender-heart,
Your perfect perfection-life
Is within your easy reach.

954.

God and God's Compassion-Eye
Have entered into my mind-poison
To transform my mind-poison
Into God's own Heart-Nectar.

955.

The path of the surrender-heart
Is always the right path
For each and every aspiring soul.

956.

God and Heaven have chosen this day
To bless my life
With the beauty and fragrance
Of an unconditionally surrendered breath.

957.

God was, God is
And God will forever remain
The loving Companion
Of my heart's soaring hopes.

958.

My peace-loving heart-book
God has read many times
From cover to cover.

959.

My God-manifestation-task
Has been assigned
By the Absolute Lord Himself.
