17801

There was a time
When I was
My mind-volcano.
But now my Lord Supreme
Has turned me into
A oneness-heart-fountain.

17802

Sweet
Is willingness.
Sweeter
Is obedience.
Sweetest
Is surrender.

17803

Each pure thought creates
A new heart-song
And a new life-story.

17804

The mind, and even the heart,
Cannot
But the soul can and does
Answer all our God-questions.

17805

If you carefully and soulfully count
Your lost God-Blessings,
You can regain your aspiration-height.

17806

A gratitude-heart
Always takes very long strides
Towards God's Compassion-Sea.

17807

The mind either is afraid of or dislikes
God-homework,
But the heart loves
God-homework
And multiplies its inner light.

17808

Be contemplative first
And then talk.
You will see that God
Will definitely be happy
To become your most attentive listener.

17809

Do you want to find God?
Then just expand and widen your mind.
Lo and behold,
God is inside your utterly vast mind.

17810

Nothing can blind
Your illumining soul.
Nothing can bind
Your aspiring heart.

17811

What you need
Is an aspiration-army
And a realisation-commander
To defeat your mind's
Ignorance-troops.

17812

Every day my heart sings
A new peace-song.
Every day my soul dances
A new peace-dance.

17813

My spirituality-death
Will not take place
Only because of God's
Constant Forgiveness-Breath.

17814

Inside my heart's life-boat
I never keep
Even one doubt-passenger.
