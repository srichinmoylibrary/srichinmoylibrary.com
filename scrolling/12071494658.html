21330

A oneness-heart
Does not greedily devour
But lovingly houses
A division-mind.

21331

God neither hires us nor fires us
For His Light-manifestation on earth.
It is we who choose Him, love Him
And manifest Him
To swim in the sea of happiness.

21332

God's most extraordinary Gift to humanity
Is His Compassion-Eye.
Humanity's most extraordinary gift to God
Is its gratitude-heart.

21333

The compassion-sun of the soul
Purifies the mind,
Intensifies the heart
And beautifies the life.

21334

If yours is a heart
Of indomitable courage,
Then God will entrust you
With His world-manifestation-Light.

21335

The absence of disobedience-darkness
Is good.
The end of disobedience-night
Is by far the best.

21336

My Lord,
Is there any place
Where You enjoy my presence
And where You dislike my presence?

"My child,
I have a real liking
For your heart-garden-fragrance,
And I have a strong dislike
Of your mind-thorn-jungle."

21337

When the mind and the heart
Are in dispute,
God outwardly does not interfere,
But inwardly He always takes the side
Of the heart.

21338

Poor God is crying and dying
Just because
My heart is not loving
And my mind is not surrendering.

21339

When we have a childlike trust,
Nothing can keep us away
From God's Happiness-Pride.

21340

The mind and its confusion-fog
Cannot obscure our heart-vision
If our life becomes a selfless service-song
To God.

21341

O my mind,
Is it such a difficult thing
For you to treasure some prayerful thoughts?
O my heart,
Is it such a difficult thing
For you to become dynamic
And not remain perpetually restless?
O my life,
Is it an impossible thing
For you to remain sleeplessly and breathlessly
At the Feet of the Lord Beloved Supreme?

21342

When I am inside my gratitude-heart,
I find it quite easy
To accompany God everywhere.

21343

Wave after wave of bliss
Will envelop us
If we can make our love of God
Unconditional.

21344

God asks me if I have any questions.
I tell God that I have only one question:
Will I ever be worthy
Of His constant Forgiveness?
