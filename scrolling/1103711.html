1 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My pinnacle-height-experience
Tells me
That every human being is Your Heart.
My abysmal abyss-experience
Tells me
That I am Your only Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 September 1999, 6:54 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 540 lbs.)

1 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I have put up
Welcome signs for You
Not only in my heart-garden,
But also in my mind-jungle.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 September 1999, 7:04 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm thirty-five times. (18 right, 17 left)

1 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my heart surrenders to Your Will,
You smile at my heart
And You also ask my heart
To smile at You.
When my mind surrenders to Your Will,
You ask my mind
To come and sing with You.
When my entire life surrenders
To Your Will,
You ask my life
To come and dance with You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 September 1999, 7:13 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred and ten times. (55 right, 55 left)

1 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I know, I know,
You never turn a deaf ear
To my heart's sincere prayer-cries.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 September 1999, 7:34 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 200 lbs. with each arm separately.

1 September 1999 (V)

Before doing bench press
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do come down from wherever You are
To inspect my mind,
To correct my heart
And to perfect my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 September 1999

1 September 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall no more keep You,
My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Waiting.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 September 1999

1 September 1999 (VII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Sleeplessly I must think
Only of my Lord's Happiness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 September 1999

2 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart, invoke patience-light.
Behold, Infinity's Peace-Delight
Is fast approaching you.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 September 1999, 6:37 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 50 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (41 reps)

2 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In the morning and in the evening
Your Forgiveness and Your Compassion
Ceaselessly talk
To my doubting mind,
And Your Joy and Your Pride
Ceaselessly talk
To my aspiring heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 September 1999, 6:56 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 540 lbs.)

2 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Day and night,
Around the clock,
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Pays blissful Attention
To my unconditional
Surrender-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 September 1999, 7:07 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm forty-two times. (20 right, 22 left)

2 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
At every step
On my aspiration-dedication-road,
I clearly see
That my inner fragrance
Is multiplying and multiplying
And multiplying,
And my outer beauty is growing
And growing and growing.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 September 1999, 7:17 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm ninety-five times. (56 right, 39 left)

2 September 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, You shed bitter Tears
When my mind enjoys
Brooding and spreading doubts.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 September 1999, 7:36 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

2 September 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
Every morning
You write something encouraging,
Inspiring, illumining and fulfilling
On the tablet of my aspiration-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 September 1999, 7:52 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

3 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My real spirituality
Is my heart-offering
And my life-blossoming,
Not my God-possessing desire.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 September 1999, 8:03 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 540 lbs.)
