19375

Divine success will be found
Not in the persistence-vital,
Not in the insistence-mind,
But in the self-giving aspiration-heart.

19376

When you do not expect anything
From this world,
The whole world will be ready
To be at your express command.

19377

I am not proud of what I have.
I am not proud of what I am.
I am only proud of being
A regular student
In the inner aspiration-school.

19378

Purity, sincerity, humility:
These divine gifts I am receiving
From my aspiration-heart
On a day-to-day basis.

19379

Every day
For God's powerful Presence
Inside my mind,
I pray.
Every day
For God's peaceful Presence
Inside my heart,
I meditate.

19380

Our earthly attachment
And our Heavenly disobedience
Are together found.

19381

I do not want to be
A world-competition-fighter.
I want to be
A self-transcendence-seeker.

19382

Technology-supremacy
Cannot and must not
Dominate
The heart's oneness-ecstasy.

19383

When faith is absent
From the mind,
Hope disappears
From the heart.

19384

I am trying to live
On only two things:
My aspiration-breath
And my dedication-heart.

19385

Success comes to me
When I say, "I can."
Progress comes to me
When I say, "God alone can,
God alone does,
In and through me."

19386

Everything in this world
Is similar and familiar.
But nothing can I dare
To claim as my own.

19387

Alas, an earth-bound life
Is empty of aspiration,
Even of imagination.

19388

Divine love cannot be exported
Or imported,
But it can blossom everywhere.

19389

My unwillingness-mind,
I am not looking for
Your extinction.
I am only waiting for
Your transformation.
