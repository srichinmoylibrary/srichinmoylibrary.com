15190

Even long before I see God
At a distance,
I can touch His Feet
And start living in a world
Of unknowable ecstasy.

15191

How can fear and doubt trap you
If you have already made acquaintance
With your heart's faith
And your soul's joy?

15192

You are your daring disobedience-mind.
Alas, even now
God is His caring Compassion-Heart.

15193

When I think of my heart,
I see how sweetly persuasive it is.
When I think of my mind,
I see how cleverly evasive it is.

15194

Who would have thought
That his giant doubt-mind
Would debilitate his long-cherished
Spirituality-life!

15195

When I watch the dance
Of purity-light inside my heart,
My life grows into
The beauty of an angel.

15196

My Lord,
May I join my soul in climbing
The transcendental height
Of Your illumining
And fulfilling Stairs?

15197

When I am in the mind-hospital,
I am forced to encounter
The useless debris
Of my collapsed heart-building.

15198

Do you think that you are wise?
Then prove it to me
By starting your spiritual life
Right now, immediately,
Without waiting for tomorrow's arrival!

15199

Who is responsible?
It is you who allowed the doubt-storm
To approach your mind
And batter your life.

15200

My Lord,
May my life always live
Inside my gratitude-heart-nest,
Sheltered and protected
By Your Compassion-flooded Eye.
