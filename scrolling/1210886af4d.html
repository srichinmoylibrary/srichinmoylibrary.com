25075

I have covered a very long distance
From my mind-thorns
To my heart-blossoms.

25076

Desire-life is the emptiness
Of nothingness —
Nothing more and nothing less.

25077

My heart-yearning
Is flying towards Heaven.
How and why?
Because my God-Hour has struck!

25078

My aspiration-heart
Is always homesick
To be with God
And to place itself
At the Feet of God.

25079

My Lord, where are You?
"My child, I am at three different places
At the same time.
I am inside your silence-mind.
I am inside your silence-heart.
I am inside your silence-life."

25080

My Lord tells me
That as long as I have
A doubting mind,
He will not be able to free me
From my mind's darkest bondage-night.

25081

I feed my Lord's big Eye
With the tears
Of my small heart.

25082

My Lord feeds my small heart
With the Smiles
Of His big Eye.

25083

I do not know
How I can ever show my face
To my Master
If I deliberately absent myself
From his grace.

25084

It is, indeed, utter stupidity
When we think that God
Has turned a deaf ear
To the helplessness of our
Prayer-cries.

25085

Every day God shows us the way
To be an exact prototype of Himself,
But we pay no heed to Him.

25086

Alas, for centuries
I have forced my Lord Supreme
To remain fast asleep
Inside my darkness-mind-room.

25087

My Lord Supreme,
Excellence I do not long for.
Existence in You only
I long for.

25088

My Lord, You have performed
A supreme miracle for me:
To house Yourself inside my puny heart,
You have transformed Your Vastness
Into the smallest atoms.

25089

My Lord, give me the capacity
To claim You devotedly
The way You claim me
Proudly.
