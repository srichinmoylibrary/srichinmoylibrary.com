Prabhur Adesh Sanjhe Bhore Manina Hiya

December 14th, 1995

Prabhur adesh sanjhe bhore manina
Hiya kusum phutbe kabe janina

Hiyar Jale Pujte Habe Chokher Jale Nahe

December 14th, 1995

Hiyar jale pujte habe chokher jale nahe
Ei kothati nitya jeno mane amar rahe

Translation:

I shall have to worship
My Lord Beloved Supreme,
With the tears of my heart,
And not with the tears of my eyes.

Tomar Karuna Labhiyachi Bale

December 14th, 1995

Tomar karuna labhiyachi bale
Dakite tomare pari
Paramanande ghuriya berai
Agyanatare chari

Translation:

Because I have received Your Compassion,
I am able to invoke You.
With my heart’s deepest delight I roam,
Leaving aside ignorance.
