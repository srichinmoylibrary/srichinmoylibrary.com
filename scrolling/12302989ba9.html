Nivedita Phute Uto Arbar Ullashita

November 5th, 1986

Nivedita Nivedita Nivedita
Phute uto arbar ullashita
Nivedita Nivedita Nivedita
Our Lord Beloved Supreme loves You;
You give delight-songs to every heart;
You give pure oneness to every life.
Nivedita, Nivedita, Nivedita —
Be once more.
We pray to the Lord Supreme
For your fully blossomed life,
O Eternity’s happiness-life,
Enthusiasm-nectar-delight,
Nivedita, Nivedita.
