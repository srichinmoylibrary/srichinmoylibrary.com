9960

Why do you allow doubt
To be your guest?
Why do you allow your mind
To offer doubt
Its beautiful nest?

9961

My heart's consciousness-river
Every day is flowing
Towards my soul's illumination-ocean.

9962

If you want to free yourself
From ignorance-obstacles,
Then breathlessly love the fragrance
Of your purity-heart.

9963

Who is fond of the earthly food,
Doubt?
The human in me.
Who is fond of the Heavenly food,
Delight?
The divine in me.
The human in me
Is an earth-bound doubter.
The divine in me
Is a Heaven-free God-lover.

9964

Today
Your mind is dreaming
Of purity-songs.
Tomorrow
Your mind will not only learn
Purity-songs from your soul
But also teach them
To your vital and body.

9965

You can easily conquer
The limits of your narrow faith
If you can conquer
Your mind's doubt-territory.

9966

I wish to see you every day
In your heart's sincerity-garden
And not in your mind's
Insincerity-graveyard.

9967

I love my divinity's heart-temple.
It is there that I never fear
The thunder of the doubt-war.

9968

What you are
Is a peaceful heart,
But what you have
Is a bewildered mind.

9969

There is a constant tug-of-war
Going on between
His aspiring heart's soulfulness
And his doubting mind's bitterness.

9970

Everything in him is dangerous,
Especially his disobedience-life
And his suspicion-mind.

9971

If it is beyond your capacity
To invite God directly,
Then you must try at least
To invite aspiration-meditation.

9972

This morning
When his soul came to please him
At God's choice Hour,
He lost his heart
In breathless tears.

9973

Your immortal childlike heart
God will be proud of utilising
In the field
Of His Perfection-Manifestation.

9974

Unless you can recognise
The mind's tricks,
You will not be able to offer
To the world at large
Your heart-revealing God-experiences.
