1215.

I am so proud of my soul,
For it enjoys
The immeasurably longest
And the intimately closest
Companionship
With my Lord's
Transcendental Will-Power.

1216.

O my doubting mind,
Do not try to bind the flight
Of my Heaven-soaring hope.

1217.

God loves me
In my wakefulness.
I love God
In His ever-new Fulness.

1218.

God proudly salutes
His great guest:
The ever-climbing aspiration-heart.

1219.

When our Mother-Earth
Was newly blossomed,
God danced His Infinity's
Ecstasy-Dance.

1220.

My mind loves God
The cosmic Magician.
My heart needs God
The cosmic Healer.

1221.

At every moment
My gratitude-heart is prayerfully enjoying
The universal Melody
Of God's Compassion-flooded
Life-Breath.

1222.

No human mind will ever be able
To minimise or annihilate
The perfection-beauty of the soul.

1223.

God renders Himself accessible
Only to those whose hearts pine
For His Compassion-Eye.

1224.

My simplicity-life
Is always radiant
With God's own
Unimaginable Lustre.

1225.

God has unconditionally given
My aspiration-heart
The job of unfolding
All His Heaven-cherished Secrets.

1226.

God deeply appreciates
My struggling life's sincere eagerness
For His complete manifestation on earth.

1227.

Even an iota of unwillingness
Brings disgrace
To the sincere seeker.

1228.

When my Lord's Victory-Song
I sing
In my heart's silence-depth,
God immediately shows me
That I come from
His Infinity's Peace,
And I am heading towards
His Immortality's Bliss.

1229.

Each aspirant has to cross beyond
The sound-life-horizon
To enter into the silence-kingdom.
