20801

I do not want to be
The thought
Of a great thinker.
I long to be
The breath
Of a good server.

20802

Earthly duty
Is its fleeting moment.
Heavenly duty
Is its everlasting life.

20803

An unaspiring man
Is his own boundless
Darkness-ignorance-pride.

20804

May I be satisfied
All the time
In my God-belief-freedom.

20805

Fear, doubt and jealousy:
These are the things
That endlessly waste
Our human life.

20806

When I do not
Pray and meditate self-givingly,
I roam
In sorrow's despair-desert.

20807

The only mistake
That seriously displeases God
Is my unwillingness to be
A choice instrument of His.

20808

My Lord,
I need from You
Only two kinds of smiles:
My enthusiasm-multiplying smile
And my frustration-destroying smile.

20809

God is always ready to bless you
With His Divinity's Simplicity-Boon,
But you prefer to have
The web of complexity.

20810

If you are relentlessly unselfish,
Then no fear
Can torture your mind.

20811

My daily prayer to God
To grant me a pure heart
Protects me
From my own selfishness-mind.

20812

My Lord Supreme feels sad
And not mad
When I want to change the world
Untimely,
Plus in my own way.

20813

The heart-runners
Every day run
The self-transcendence-joy-marathon.

20814

Insecurity
Spreads its jealousy-wings
When I water
My mind's negativity-plants.
