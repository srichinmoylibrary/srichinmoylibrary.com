1 November 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The body's earthly time
Is passing away.
The soul's Heavenly time
Is blossoming and blossoming
And blossoming
At the Feet of the Lord Beloved Supreme.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1999, 8:47 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 80 lbs. (78 reps)

1 November 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What I desperately need
Is an aspiration-heart
And not a commotion-mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1999, 9:07 a.m. Before doing one-hundred one-arm pushes with 400 lbs. (50 right, 50 left)

1 November 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My joy knows no bounds
That my inner life's progress-river
Is running fast, very fast.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1999, 9:19 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with each arm simultaneously (Total: 400 lbs.)

1 November 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My patience-heart,
You are an unparalleled server
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1999, 9:24 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

1 November 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I am triumphantly enjoying
The ashes of my ego's unmistakable
And permanent death.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1999, 9:31 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

1 November 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O my gratitude-heart,
Do not delay.
Do establish your soulful friendship
With sleepless Eternity.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1999, 9:44 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

1 November 1999 (VII)

Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart,
Cry and cry and cry!
Were born to cry for God.
My mind,
Smile and smile and smile!
You and I
Were born, to smile at God.
My life,
Surrender and surrender and surrender!
You and I
Were born to surrender to God's Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1999, 10:08 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

2 November 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must challenge fear
Fearlessly
And conquer it
Unmistakably.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 November 1999 9:22 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

2 November 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my Lord Beloved Supreme
Smilingly, lovingly, blessingfully
And compassionately
Occupy every space
In my Heaven-climbing aspiration-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 November 1999, 9:34 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

2 November 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, it seems that my mind's
Self-indulgence
Will never be aware of
My God-effulgence-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 November 1999, 9:38 a.m. Before doing one-hundred one-arm pushes with 400 lbs. (50 right, 50 left)

3 November 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My spiritual life must entirely depend
Upon my unconditional God-obedience.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 November 1999, 1:00 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

3 November 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Pride can parade
Only through our division-minds
And never, never, never
Through our oneness-hearts.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 November 1999, 1:13 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

3 November 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Yours is the Smile
That immediately answers
Every sorrow of my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 November 1999, 1:19 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

3 November 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To make the fastest progress
In my spiritual life,
I must enliven my aspiration-heart
And dedication-life
With tremendous eagerness, enthusiasm
And energy.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 November 1999, 1:20 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)
