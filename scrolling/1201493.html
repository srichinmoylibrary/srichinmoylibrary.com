15701

My life
Is not a passing pleasure.
It is a lasting treasure
Of God.

15702

Do not stand against
God's Compassion-Rule.
God's Justice-Light
Will not tolerate it
Indefinitely.

15703

Again and again
God's Compassion-Rain descends.
But alas, we do not value
Its all-loving, all-caring
Descent.

15704

You are your mind's disobedience.
You are your heart's disobedience.
You are your life's disobedience.
Therefore,
You and your sad defeat
Must live and breathe together
In failure-jungle.

15705

A powerful Smile
From my Lord Supreme
Is my heart's happiness-heritage.

15706

So many times
God has saved you
With His Compassion-Life-Preserver.
But you have never offered
Your gratitude-heart
To your Lord Supreme.

15707

God will allow me
To enter into
His Promised Peace-Land
Only after I have become a member
Of His Oneness-Heart-Garden.

15708

I surprised God
With my heart's genuine God-hunger.
God surprised me
With His Compassion's
Unconditional Nectar-Rain.

15709

If it is true
That God loves my reality-life,
Then He loves
My heart's dream-boat-journey
Infinitely more.

15710

Do you want to touch
God's Heart?
Then become either
The beauty and fragrance
Of a flower
Or the cry and smile
Of a child.

15711

My Lord is telling me
That the more I widen
My heart's happiness-smile,
The sooner He will be able
To manifest
His transcendental Divinity
In and through me.

15712

What can poor God do?
He will never be satisfied
With your mind's insincerity-cry.

15713

Do not travel temptation-road
Either with your mind
Or with your vital.
Pray to your soul
To transform your temptation-road
Into aspiration-road
For your safety
And for your perfection.
Your soul will do it for you.

15714

Every day
God the Creator
Sends His Smiles
To God the creation,
And God the creation
Sends its cries
To God the Creator.
