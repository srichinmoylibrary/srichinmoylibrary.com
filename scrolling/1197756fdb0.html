12060

I am convinced that in the near future
My heart, my mind, my vital and my body,
Like my soul,
Will win the man-oneness-race
And the God-fulness-trophy.

12061

My most precious heart,
I simply cannot understand how and why
You have let yourself be confined
For so long
By your blind mind-limits.

12062

My mind is thirsty for attention,
My heart is thirsty for affection,
But I am trying to be thirsty
For perfection.

12063

Just try to love God unconditionally.
You will soon see how beautifully
Your life's aspiration-transcendence-tree
Is flowering.

12064

If you are a genuine God-lover,
Then God will definitely make you feel
The weight of His disapproval
When you speak ill of His creation,
The human race.

12065

Even one soulful prayer
Early every morning
Can cure your spiritual malnutrition.

12066

Alas, because my aspiration-heart
Is not strong,
Its friendship with my doubting mind
Has turned it into a battered faith-flower.

12067

In the morning
I pray for the seeds of hope.
In the evening
I meditate on the fruits of fulfilment.

12068

Your Himalayan realisation
Can easily become
A temptation-chaser
For all God-lovers around you.

12069

Everybody will see
Teeming destruction-mines
If they walk along the doubt-road.
No exception!

12070

Delay not!
Soulfully invoke God the Compassion
To burst your mind's
Over-inflated ego-balloon.

12071

Kick hard
Your body's lethargy
If you want to grow into
Your soul's ecstasy.

12072

God is His own God-Heart's
Compassion-Service.
Man is his own man-mind's
Ingratitude-practice.

12073

Alas, my mind enjoys
Doubt's dubious future;
My body enjoys
Its excess lethargy-weight.
How will I enjoy
My divinity's goal,
Or do I even have a goal?

12074

My surrender-life
Is the straightest line
To God's blue-gold Fulfilment-Palace.
