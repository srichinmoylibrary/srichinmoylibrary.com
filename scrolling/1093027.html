Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers each time before he started to exercise on his stationary bicycle.

1.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
I am my doubtful mind.
God is God's tearful Heart.

1 January 2000

8:00 p.m.

Curitiba, Brazil

2.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
I wish to be the golden dust
Of Your Feet,
But You want me to be
The Himalayan pride
Of Your Heart.

2 January 2000

5:44 a.m.

Curitiba, Brazil

3.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The deeper our heart-roots,
The stronger our love of God.

3 January 2000

7:05 a.m.

Curitiba, Brazil

4.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
Each happy moment
In my inner life
Builds a new castle-glory
In my outer life.

5 January 2000

10:21 a.m.

Curitiba, Brazil

5.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
Every day at sunrise
I shall aim at
A new horizon.

7 January 2000

7:59 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

6.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The desire-days of my mind
Are invariably followed by
The worry-nights of my heart.

8 January 2000

8:13 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

7.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The strength of the mind
Fails to disarm
The hope-power of the heart.

9 January 2000

8:07 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

8.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
This morning You came down
To awaken me
To Your Infinity's Silence.

10 January 2000

6:26 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

9.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
I have tamed my outer life
To remain still.
I have trained my inner heart
To enjoy the thrill.

13 January 2000

6:35 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

10.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
My forgiveness-smile
Is the golden vastness
Of my heart-sky.

13 January 2000

6:35 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

11.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The tears of my heart
And
The smiles of my soul
Every day play hide-and-seek
To entertain You.

14 January 2000

7:43 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

12.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
Whenever angels see
An unconditionally
God-surrendered seeker,
They immediately start trumpeting
God's supreme Victory.

15 January 2000

9:56 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

13.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
Man-made friendship
Is a foolish hardship.
God-made friendship
Is a rainbow boat trip.

17 January 2000

Asuncin, Paraguay
