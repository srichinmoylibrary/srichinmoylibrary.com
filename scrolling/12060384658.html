20230

My Lord,
When I pray to You soulfully
And meditate on You self-givingly,
I will undoubtedly be aware
Of Your Forgiveness-Light.

20231

Challenge
Your destiny's frown.
God will proudly bless you
With His most precious Crown.

20232

What can God's Grace
Do for us?
It can do in a minute
What would take a lifetime
Otherwise.

20233

The eagerness of the heart,
The oneness of the heart,
The fulness of the heart
Are three in one
And one in three.

20234

I am happy
When God speaks to me
About something new.
I am infinitely happier
When I place myself
At the Feet of my Lord Supreme
With a new and pure heart.

20235

The mind-landlord
Does not realise its stupidity.
It has chosen anxiety and worry
To be its tenants.

20236

For its survival,
An unaspiring mind
Thinks it has either
To detest, to contest
Or to protest
Everything.

20237

Short is the span
Of man's fame.
Long is the span
Of man's God-surrender-song.
Not only longest but also immortal
Is the span
Of man's God-surrender-life.

20238

What I want
From my mind and vital
Is a oneness-confirmation,
Not
A supremacy-confrontation.

20239

Either
We can be limitless
In our stupidity-night
Or
We can be priceless
In our wisdom-light.
God wants us to make the choice.

20240

When illumination
We are within,
Perfection
We become without.

20241

If we fortify
Our aspiration-breath,
God will magnify
Our glorification-life.

20242

When slowly, steadily and unerringly
The oneness-heart appears,
Unwillingly, reluctantly and miserably
The division-mind disappears.

20243

Each good thought
Increases the beauty and splendour
Of our life's God-affinity.

20244

O my mind,
You are so treacherous.
Yet I do not know how
I can disown you.

O my heart,
You are so precious.
Yet I do not know how
I can own you, reveal you
And manifest you.
