8145

The world will frown
At your pride.
You will be found
In oblivion's hush.

8146

Your heart
Is like a blossoming tree.
Therefore
I come to you
For your blessing-smile.

8147

To close your heart's
Morning eye
Is to reach folly's
Goalless heights.

8148

The unchallenged secret
Will surrender
To the heart' s sleepless cry.

8149

What is your secret?
Why is earth
So proud of you?
Why is Heaven
So fond of you?

8150

Keep your pride
For yourself,
Since nobody
Has any use for it.

8151

Here cries
The unborn hope.
There cries
The unfulfilled promise.

8152

Life's play
Is not monstrous.
Just try to play
More carefully
And more soulfully.

8153

In him is the beauty
Of God's
Ever-changing Reality-Forms.

8154

He walks along the way
Of self-preparation
And God-manifestation.

8155

How can you get anything
From God
When you have given nothing
To His choice child,
Creation?

8156

I do not expect thanks,
I do not even expect any comment,
But I do expect
A world of silence
When I sing God-songs.

8157

All have come
One by one
To tell me
That God wants to see me
As another God.

8158

I was invited
To sing the song
Of the Unknowable,
And I am enjoying doing it.

8159

Because your life
Is secure,
God wants you to advance
Fast, very fast.
