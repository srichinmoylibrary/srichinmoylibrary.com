1 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Happy New Year!
Happy New Year!
Happy New Year!
From today on,
Every day You expect from
Your supremely chosen children
A divinely dignified spiritual life.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

My Mother Earth is beauty.
My Father Heaven is light.
And I am their Eternity’s
Reality-existence.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

You are Your Compassion-Eye
And I am my aspiration-heart.
Together we live and fulfil each other.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Every day
Do give me the capacity
To sow a new aspiration-seed
In my heart-garden.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Every morning
My aspiration-heart runs and runs
To touch the peace-sea-shore.
Every evening
The peace-sea-shore comes and blesses
My aspiration-heart.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

My morning prayers and meditations
Give me the joy of the higher worlds.
My evening prayers and meditations
Give me the joy of the inner worlds.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

May the singing and dancing
Of divine melodies
Awaken my heart
Every morning.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

My heart-bird sings and sings
Your Victory-Songs
Even when my body, my vital,
My mind and I
Are fast asleep.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

All are caught by Time.
Nobody—nobody!—can hide
From Time.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

I am sailing and sailing and sailing
My life-boat
Towards the shore
Of the Unknowable.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Now that I live
In aspiration-sunshine-days,
I must not think of
My desire-ignorance-nights.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

My God-worship
Is my heart’s intimacy
And not my life’s secrecy.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

May my heart be
An ever-steady and ever-climbing
Aspiration-flame.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 January 2006

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

My sleeplessly climbing aspiration-heart
Is the richest, fondest treasure
Of my soul.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
