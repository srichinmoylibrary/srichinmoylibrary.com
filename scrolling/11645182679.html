15.

My Lord Supreme, Your Flute
Is my heart's only solace-sun.
Because of its melody pure
The Heavenward race I shall run.

16.

All your climbing dreams are owned
By God's Compassion-Eye.
He grants your aspiration-heart
Ecstasy-moon, ecstasy-sky.

17.

O break your body-chains today.
Then it will not be long
'Til God begins to teach you
His Heavenly Ecstasy-Song.

18.

To reveal God-Divinity's Reality
I saw the light of day
And also to be His chosen Voice
In His Sun-Vision-Play.

19.

So divinely sweet and pure
Is your aspiration-cry
That it is dancing freely
In Heaven's Ecstasy-Sky.

20.

As long as you surrender to
Your vital's giant eye,
Your life on earth is doomed to be
Your soul's stupendous sigh.

21.

He who does not listen to
His gentle conscience-sound
In the world's dark quagmire
Is invariably found.

22.

Your dedication-life
Will disappear
If your mind does not stay
Pure and clear.

23.

My seeker-friend, please stop at once
Your homeward-flying soul.
It has not yet fulfilled on earth
Its high God-commanded role.

24.

If you can embrace your faith-life,
Your teeming doubts will die.
If you can conquer your lower self,
Your higher self will fly.

25.

If you live in your past,
You will cry in dark shadows.
If you live in your present,
You will dance in God-meadows.

26.

O lifeless man,
What can you give?
In nothingness-chasm
Or cave you live!

27.

Action and enthusiasm
Together live
In God's vast world
Of take and give.

28.

Remember, when impurity
Invades your life,
In no time it uses
Destruction-knife.

29.

If your aspiration leads you
To the gate of sleepless love,
It means the sure descent
Of God's Blessings from Above.
