20060

Inspiration
Is the God-journey's start.
Aspiration
Is the God-speed.
Realisation
Is the God-destination.

20061

O my suspicion-mind,
I thought you had left me
For good.
Alas, you have come back again
To torture me, my entire being.
Unless and until, my mind,
You are ruthlessly under arrest,
Peace will remain a far cry.

20062

Do not complain;
Do not complain.
Just train your mind
To look at the world
With a compassion-eye.

20063

Are you not the worst fool?
If not, why do you allow your mind
To lord it over you
Day and night?

20064

The outer world thinks
My little heart is useless.
But the inner world knows
My little heart is both
Priceless and measureless.

20065

In the inner world
My name is tolerance.
In the outer world
My name is perseverance.

20066

May my inner
God-aspiration-necessity
And my outer
God-manifestation-capacity
Remain always inseparable.

20067

Where is
My mind's freedom?
It is inside
My heart's peace.
It is inside
My life's surrender.

20068

The vital is always eager
To fight.
The mind is always eager
To doubt.
The heart is always eager
To cry.
The life is always eager
To surrender.

20069

Truth is not
A new invention.
Truth is not
A new discovery.
Truth is
Eternal self-evidence.

20070

O my oneness-heart's rose-beauty,
Where are you?
O my division-mind's thorn-torture,
Why are you here?

20071

From ancient times,
Even until now,
The transformation of human nature
Has seemed to be
An impossible task.

20072

The smiles of victory
And the tears of defeat
Exist only
In the aspiration-famine
Of the human mind.

20073

I know
My spiritual riches will grow
Only when I go
Straight to my heart's
Aspiration-garden
Every morning.

20074

We may not see
But we do feel
That there is a Hand
Which is beckoning us
To a supreme Destination.
