19360

Never give up hope
If you want to become
The beauty of your heart
And the fragrance of your soul.

19361

A complicated mind
Is afraid of joy.
Therefore,
It does not allow joy
To come in.

19362

My aspiration-heart-blossoms
Are indeed the certificates of merit
From my Lord Supreme.

19363

The heart receives
Blessing-Light from God.
The life achieves
Perfection only in God.

19364

I do not want to become
The king
Of the desiring minds.
I want to become
The brother
Of the aspiring hearts.

19365

What are you doing, my mind?
When are you going to invite God
To bless you, love you
And illumine you?

19366

What makes you think,
O my mind,
That I need you desperately?
Have I not told you time and again
That you have always been wrong
And you have always misled
And misguided me?

19367

This is the human life-story:
Confusion within,
Confusion without.
Confusion reigns supreme
All-where in human life.

19368

The great dream
Is love.
The greater dream
Is surrender.
The greatest dream
Is peace.

19369

Study your own life
Until the end of your life.
See how often and how much
You have loved God
In His own Way.

19370

The more we walk towards happiness
For our personal gain,
The farther away we go
From true happiness-shore.

19371

Believe in your heart's
Purity-strength.
You will be able to conquer
The farthest length
Of your impurity-mind.

19372

If you keep your gratitude-heart
Locked within,
How can you ever see
God's Rainbow-Smiles?

19373

Who deceives me most?
My deception-mind.
Who occupies my thoughts?
My impure mind.

19374

Age need not bind you.
Age need not blind you.
Age should only help you
To see the real in you
As soon as possible.
