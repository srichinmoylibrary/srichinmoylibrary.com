21360

In the world of aspiration,
O seeker,
Always try to be inspired
By Heavenly deeds
And not by earthly thoughts.

21361

My eyes are glowing and glowing
Because my heart's gratitude-tears
Are ceaselessly flowing.

21362

Only a oneness-heart
Has the capacity
To challenge and defeat
The ignorant division-mind.

21363

A mind of harmony
Has to be founded
Upon a oneness-heart of bliss.

21364

God will bless you
With a vast reservoir
Of inner wealth
If you are ready to give Him
Your outer possessions:
Fear, doubt, jealousy, anxiety,
Meanness and selfishness.

21365

To easily know what God's Will is,
We have to feed the Divine in us
And illumine the human in us.

21366

Try to have a new heart
If you want to succeed.
Your old heart is infected
By darkest impurity.

21367

My Lord makes me happy
With the breath of a moment
And not with the lives
Of hours.

21368

God's Compassion-Heart
And my aspiration-heart
Have forever and forever
Sealed their partnership.

21369

My mind needs the God
Who reprimands.
My heart needs the God
Who commands.

21370

When I get up early in the morning,
My God most affectionately says to me:
"My child,
I shall do everything for you."

When I get up late in the morning,
My God says to me:
"My child,
You be the doer and the enjoyer as well."

21371

I give God
What my life has:
A prayer.
God gives me
What His Heart is:
A Smile.

21372

I stand upon the summit of my years
To sing happily and proudly
My soul's unsung songs.

21373

Sound is the pride
Of my fleeting time.
Silence is the pride
Of God's own Eternity
And Infinity.

21374

How can you have satisfaction
If you do not care for
Total perfection in your life?
