18775

Aspiration is a deer
And not a lion.
What you need is the deer-speed
And not the lion-roar
To reach the Golden Shore.

18776

My self-giving heart-fragrance
God treasures
More than anything else.

18777

When my mind and I talk
About sincerity,
And when my heart and I talk
About purity,
We never want to end
Our illumining and fulfilling
Conversations.

18778

Light, more light, abundant light,
Infinite light
We need every day
To illumine our ignorance-sufferings.

18779

I see no difference
When the mind lives
Inside the suspicion-jungle
And when it lives
Inside the abysmal abyss.

18780

A silence-flooded mind I need
To receive the Compassion-Rain
Of my Lord Supreme.

18781

When it comes to deception,
My own mind deceives me
Infinitely more
Than anybody else does.

18782

The mind does not do anything
Without asking for a salary.
When the heart does something,
The heart simply expects a salary.
Even when the soul does everything,
The soul never, never thinks of a salary.

18783

Self-doubt
Is bound to increase
When we treasure
Our animal-anger.

18784

O mind-confused body,
Delay not in pleasing God,
For your earth-life is fleeting.

18785

I pray to God daily,
So that my life's surrender
And my heart's gratitude
Never go on strike.

18786

Alas,
It seems that the theoretical God
Is found everywhere,
But the practical God
Is so rarely to be found.

18787

Day in and day out
You must threaten insecurity.
Then it will be impossible
For insecurity
To blight your heart.

18788

When I pray,
I invoke God-Light.
When I meditate,
I distribute God-Delight.

18789

In the morning I see God
Inside His Beauty's Dawn-Eye.
In the evening I see God
Inside His Compassion's
Satisfaction-Heart.
