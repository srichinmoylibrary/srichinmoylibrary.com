14260

My soul was shocked to notice
That my heart's sorrowful sigh
Could be so devastating.

14261

Can you not see
That God is in a special mood
To speak to you?
Immediately start listening to Him!

14262

My life's transcendence
Is indeed God's transcendence
For His complete manifestation
On earth.

14263

Every day
Our body, vital, mind and heart
Are being transformed automatically.
But if we want our heart, mind,
vital and body
To be transformed devotedly,
Soulfully and unconditionally
In God's own Way,
We must place our earth-bound cry
And Heaven-free smile
At every moment
At the Feet of our Beloved Lord Supreme.

14264

Every day
God starts a cry-change
In my aspiration-life.
But alas, I do not have
The heart-eye to see it.

14265

O earth,
You may not believe it,
O Heaven,
You may not believe it,
But my Lord told me this morning
That I am making satisfactory progress
Just by intensifying my heart-cry.

14266

Every morning,
Compassionately plus pitifully,
My Lord Supreme begs me
To empty my sordid mind-room.

14267

Do you know who is preparing
God's Peace-Sun-Arrival
Here on earth?
Definitely not my desire-life,
But my heart's sleeplessly mounting
Aspiration-cry.

14268

In silence supreme
Every morning
I see the golden sunrise
Of my inner perfection-beauty.

14269

Beautiful
Is my aspiration-life.
Powerful
Is my dedication-life.
Fruitful
Is my surrender-life.

14270

The quiet voice
Of my patience-heart
Every day is proudly fed
By my Lord Supreme.

14271

My Lord Supreme
Is extremely fond of the music
Of my sweetness-flooded
Surrender-soul.

14272

My Beloved Lord Supreme
Is celebrating today
The sunset of my desire-life.

14273

My Lord Supreme,
No matter where I go,
I see Your two most powerful
Forgiveness-Shelter-Wings.

14274

My Beloved Supreme,
I know, I know,
That my life will remain
Meaningless, fruitless and useless
Until I have sung
Your Eternity's, Infinity's
And Immortality's
Victory-Songs on earth.
