16215

The earth-atmosphere
Never welcomes
The breath and life
Of newly God-loving souls.

16216

Do not waste
Your precious time.
Every day
Try to make it a point
To make real sense
Out of your life.

16217

There is only one right way
For me to go to God,
And that way
Is my heart's unconditional
Surrender-way.

16218

Suppose that God
Does not reveal Himself
To your mind.
What will you do?
Will you wait
Or will you give up?

16219

Pinnacle-purity-light
You can bring into your heart
Only by taking
Regular surrender-exercises.

16220

Today I can clearly see
That my life's
Self-discovery-voyage
Is well under way.

16221

If I do not improve
My aspiration-dedication-relationship
With God,
God is not going to consider me
A choice instrument of His.

16222

Suppose God forgets
To bless me.
What shall I do?
Only one thing:
I shall love Him more,
Infinitely more,
For He knows what is best for me.

16223

There is not
A single route to God
That is not paved with
His Compassion-Vision-Light.

16224

For each individual
There is a right way
To go to God, see God
And sit at God's Feet.

16225

To appreciate others highly
For their good deeds
Is, alas, foreign
To human nature.

16226

As long
As the unlit human mind
Enjoys stupidity,
Fear will not be forced
To disappear
From earth's atmosphere.

16227

Each peace-prayer
Is a God-worshipping
Purity-garland.

16228

The confusion-mind-territory
Ruthlessly defies
The capacity of a compass.

16229

If we do not soulfully pray
And self-givingly meditate,
We will not be able
To make divine sense
Out of life.
