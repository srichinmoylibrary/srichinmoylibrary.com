21960

May each thought of mine
Be constantly found
Inside my God-gratitude-heart.

21961

When God Himself
Is your private Aspiration-Tutor,
You will long to drive
Your life-car
Along aspiration-avenue,
And not before.

21962

Although it has had only
A fleeting glimpse of God,
The seeker in me
Is flying
In the sky of freedom
And diving
In the sea of universality.

21963

If you have an unsettled mind,
How can you ever have
A peace-settled heart?
Impossible!

21964

It is simply impossible
For us to live on earth
Without swallowing the world-poison.
But we can save ourselves from destruction
With our heart's mounting prayer.

21965

Alas,
I was totally unarmed
And unready
When the darkness-forces
Invaded my mind.

21966

As the mind thinks
It is always swamped
By responsibilities,
Even so the heart feels
It is always filled
With God-manifestation-opportunities.

21967

If we do not feed God
With our heart-prayers,
All our God-satisfaction-strength
Will before long wither.

21968

I do not want to be
A many-splendoured genius.
I want to be only
A God-fulfilment-promise.

21969

Every day I magnify
My ego-eye.
Alas,
When shall I start
To simplify my life
And purify my heart?

21970

No world-criticism
Can take our life-breath away
If we live in the heart-garden
Of our Inner Pilot.

21971

Now that wisdom
Has dawned upon me,
I am shunning
My age-old companionship
With ignorance-night.

21972

O my heart's hope-tears,
Ascend!
O my soul's promise-smiles,
Descend!

21973

My Lord,
How can I erase my past?
"My child,
Lose yourself in tomorrow's
Beauty, purity and divinity.
You will be a stranger,
A perfect stranger,
To your past misdeeds."

21974

Politics is man's
Man-made power-play.
Spirituality is God's
God-made Oneness-Play.
