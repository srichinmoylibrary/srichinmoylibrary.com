19645

The vital, the mind and even the heart
Quite often fail
When they are put to temptation-test,
But never the soul.
The soul is far beyond
The temptation-snare.

19646

The desiring vital
And the suspecting mind
Simply do not want to be appeased
By the illumining advice
Of the soul.

19647

O my mind,
Whenever I try to make some improvement
In your life,
I invariably meet with your resistance
And your resentment!

19648

Alas, why do I allow
The outer world
To compel me to think
That God does not love me,
And why do I allow
The inner world
To compel me to feel
That God does not need me?
Why? Why?

19649

When I meditate,
God's Silence-Eye
Watches me.
When I pray,
God's Sound-Mind
Questions me.

19650

My heart's silence
Is my life's eloquence.
This is the message
That sleeplessly I hear
From my Lord Supreme.

19651

My mind knows
How to make everything difficult.
My heart knows
How to make everything simple.
My soul knows
How to make everything perfect.
And I want to know
How everything I make
Can be self-giving.

19652

Only for one purpose
I live on earth:
I want to feel my life's
Gratitude-breath
Inside my Lord's
Heart-Smile.

19653

To You, my Lord, I offer
My purity-heart's silver tears
So that You can make of me
Your perfection-manifestation-instrument
Here on earth.

19654

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me the capacity
To value my heart's tears
And my life's years
So that I can have
The fastest speed
While running towards You.

19655

My heart cries and cries
When my mind makes
Only slow and uncertain attempts
At self-improvement.

19656

From God's point of view
There is nothing as important
In my life
As God-awareness and God-service
On earth.

19657

If your heart is not consumed
With aspiration-fire,
Then you will definitely have
A strong inclination to self-doubt.

19658

Only when I love God intensely
Am I able to feel
That God loves me sleeplessly.

19659

My heart
Is faithfully walking
Along the path of devotion.
My life
Is prayerfully walking
Along the path of surrender.
Lo, my Lord Supreme
Is beckoning me.
