26575

May the new millennium
Usher in God's new Dream,
God's new Promise
And
God's new Satisfaction
To liberate humanity from the throes
Of ignorance-bondage.

26576

Away with my joyless days!
I shall vehemently fight and win
The war within.

26577

Life is either to soar
In the inner sky
Or to sink
Into the abysmal abyss.

26578

To my greatest joy,
My pessimism-mind
Is fading fast, very fast.

26579

There is only one need,
And that need is great, greater, greatest:
An unconditional love for God.

26580

I do not recognise God's Greatness,
But as long as God recognises
My smallness
And wants to turn my smallness
Into greatness,
Then I am all gratitude to God.

26581

One voice of hope
In my aspiration-heart
Is sufficient for me
To fight against all misfortunes.

26582

Do not think that you deserve
To hear all the time from God
How well you are doing.
As long as God knows
How well you are doing,
You should be satisfied.

26583

Beautiful are the tear-drops
Of my hope-heart.
Powerful are the smile-drops
Of my promise-eye.

26584

Make your heart full,
Then start giving,
And not before.

26585

What is the best way
To welcome the future?
Just create it!

26586

I never knew
The ever-ascending wings
Of my aspiration-heart
Would be greeted
By God's Transcendental Vision.

26587

When you postpone
The Hour of God,
God, like a human being,
Loses heart.

26588

My Master,
No matter how many times
I fail you,
Please keep me again and again
In your obedience-hope-orbit.

26589

Each time I listen
To the dictates of my mind,
Alas, I am drawn into
A whirlpool of confusion.
