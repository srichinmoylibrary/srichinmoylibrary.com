15 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every sincere seeker
Has been blessed by You
With the capacity to please You
In Your own Way
Always.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 August 1999, 7:46 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

15 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my mind be a ceaseless search
For Your divine Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 August 1999 8:16 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

15 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart be a sleepless hunger
For Your supreme Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 August 1999, 8:21 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and nineteen times with one arm. (54 right, 65 left)

16 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I need absolute faith
In everything
That is created by You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 August 1999, 7:12 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and twenty-one times with one arm. (56 right, 65 left)

16 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have received
Three inner educations from You:
An aspiration-heart,
A dedication-life
And
A surrender-breath.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 August 1999, 7:29 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

16 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind believes in You.
I am now happy with my mind.
My heart believes in You.
I am now happy with my heart.
My life believes in You.
I am now happy with my life.
My mind's new awakening,
My heart's new awakening,
My life's new awakening
I now place at Your Lotus-Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 August 1999, 7:44 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. nineteen times with one arm. (7 right, 12 left)

16 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
Never to turn my spirituality
Into scientific research.
Spirituality is self-discovery.
Self-discovery is God-realisation.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 August 1999, 7:58 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

17 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In my God-obedience
I see the smiling Eye
And the dancing Heart
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 August 1999, 8:23 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 90 lbs. (18 reps)

17 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In my God-obedience I see and feel
The infinite affection
And infinite pride
Of both Heaven and earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 August 1999, 8:45 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

17 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Because of my God-obedience
You have made me
Your Satisfaction-Heart-Pride-Banner-bearer.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 August 1999, 9:10 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

17 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Because of my God-obedience
You have blessed me with
A happiness-rainbow-heart-sky.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 August 1999, 9:20 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. nineteen times with one arm. (7 right, 12 left)

18 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must keep my aspiration-heart-flames
Constantly climbing
And ever-transcending.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 August 1999, 8:12 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

18 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life be
My heart-temple-beauty
And my soul-shrine-fragrance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 August 1999, 8:20 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. sixteen times with one arm. (9 right, 7 left)

18 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must never be caught
By the desire-temptation-snare.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 August 1999, 8:26 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and eleven times with one arm. (55 right, 56 left)

18 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May I develop a ceaseless hunger
For Your Forgiveness-Smile.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 August 1999, 8:42 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.
