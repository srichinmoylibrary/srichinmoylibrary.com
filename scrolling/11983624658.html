12630

If you want to realise God
And if you want to manifest God
In God's own Way,
Then your life has to walk always
On a one-way sunlit aspiration-road.

12631

I have warned my vital
Very seriously,
And it has promised me
That it will never visit
Despair-domain.

12632

Alas, you are totally lost
Between your selfishness-mind
And your selflessness-heart.

12633

The luminous and penetrating eyes
Of faith
Can easily save a seeker
From doubt-destruction's jaws.

12634

He who does not claim
God's Satisfaction
As his ultimate goal
Will undoubtedly be a spiritual failure.

12635

Look carefully once more!
I am sure the peace-guest
Is waiting for you
In the depths of your heart.

12636

You first have to open your accounts
At the aspiration and dedication-banks
If you ever hope to open your account
At the realisation-bank.

12637

The older the joy
Of the aspiration-heart,
The younger the seeker-eyes look.

12638

Alas, my cheerful surrender
To God's Will
Had an unbelievably short life!

12639

If you are your mind's resentment
Then God will never be able to increase
Your heart's oneness-receptivity.

12640

Can you not see
That your mind's expectation-dreams
Are torturing your aspiration-life ruthlessly?

12641

True aspiration
Is bound to fly
On the fastest inspiration-wings.

12642

Be always ready
With your heart's troops
To fight against
The armies of doubt.

12643

My heart feels
That it is not necessary
To keep its eye open
While walking along the path of faith.

12644

Do not allow your mind
Even for a fleeting second
To be blinded by dark doubt-night.
