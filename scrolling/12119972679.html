26115

Humanity's peace-prayers
And peace-meditations
Are treasured
By the Absolute Lord Himself.

26116

The team of God-servers
Must know that it is God's Will
That they are serving
In each and every human being.

26117

O seeker, do not be afraid
Of your insecurity-barriers.
Be brave!
To your wide surprise,
You will be able to cross
All the insecurity-barriers.

26118

The heart of God-realisation
Is progressive.
It always sings the song
Of self-transcendence.

26119

If you are determined
To resist the temptation
Of your ignorance-pursuing life,
Then you are bound to feel and see
That God is all for you.

26120

Self-adulation-disease
Is so common.
Yet it is very difficult
To free oneself from that disease.

26121

Each human being meets with
Countless desire-disappointments.
Yet nobody wants to give up
The desire-life.

26122

To become an overnight success,
You need to prepare
Inwardly and secretly
For a long time.

26123

God sends us joy
To be our first teacher.
When the joy-teacher fails,
He sends the pain-teacher
To teach us.

26124

If you are not eagerly willing
To jump into the Light,
Then you will always remain
In your mind's darkest night.

26125

Learn to live
With as few things as possible.
This is the only way you can have
Peace of mind.

26126

First learn to look at yourself
Lovingly and compassionately.
Then look at yourself
As strictly as possible
To run the fastest.

26127

Work with what you have.
God will bring you and give you
What He is.

26128

O my heart,
Do not cry.
O my heart,
Do not feel that you are lost.
O my heart,
Do not think that nobody cares for you.
Just move on and move on!
You will see that everybody and everything
Is right in its own way.

26129

We look for God's Crown
And God's Throne above.
We look for God's Crown
And God's Throne below.
But they are neither above nor below.
They are inside our heart-breath.
