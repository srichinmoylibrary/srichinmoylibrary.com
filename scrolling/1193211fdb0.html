7560

Let me totally abolish
The distance
Between my mind's unwillingness
And my Lord's Compassion.

7561

Soul, my soul,
Where are you?
Mind, my mind,
Can you not find another place?

7562

His heart's aspiration-life
Is made of
Infinity's golden Dreams.

7563

No God-lover
Avoids or evades
Reality's existence-life.

7564

Who can correctly estimate
The worth
Of a gratitude-heart?

7565

His mind
Is a desert-dweller
And his heart
Is a paradise-lover.

7566

Being a child
Of God's own Eternity,
How can you be an alien
In God's Kingdom?

7567

O owner of a lonely heart,
You and I are sailing
In the same boat,
Unappreciated
And even unnoticed.

7568

He works in his mind's
Confusion-corporation.
A satisfaction-heart
Is not meant for him.

7569

The purity-petals
Of your life-flower
I admire infinitely more
Than you can ever imagine.

7570

The anguish of self-doubt
Is more dangerous
Than slow poison.

7571

To pray
Is to see God.
To meditate
Is to become God.

7572

Daily practise
Your patience-lesson
So that you can be
Perfectly perfect.

7573

Because I calmly live,
I fruitfully grow.
Because I fruitfully grow,
I eternally am.

7574

To change you
Tomorrow,
God has to own you
Today.
