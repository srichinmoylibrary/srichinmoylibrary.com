24 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my soulful actions speak
And not my boastful words.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 May 2000, 6:02 p.m. Before doing seated double-dumbbell lift with 360 lbs. New record.

24 May 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your timetable never fails.
You are always prompt
In answering my prayers.
It is I who fail again and again
In obeying You promptly.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 May 2000, 6:09 p.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.
