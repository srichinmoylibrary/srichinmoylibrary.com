Hernan Padilla

November 15th, 1980

Doctor Padilla, a soul of perfection-choice.
Mayor Padilla, a man of oneness-voice.
Puerto Rican heart you have gloriously won.
O island-beauty, O island-duty, victor-son!

Hostages Are Now Amazingly Free

December 31st, 1969

Hostages are now amazingly free;
American heart is in a nectar-sea.
No war, no war!
Harmony and peace must reign
To speed up humanity’s progress-train.

Fifty-Two Hostages

December 31st, 1969

Fifty-two hostages are now
Enjoying freedom-light.
America, America, America!
Withnin, without, ecstasy-height.

Four Hundred and Forty-Four Days

December 31st, 1969

Four hundred and forty-four days
Of anguish fathomless.
In a tiny hole-world
Of torments ruthless.
Welcome home, brave soldiers,
To your freedom-clime.
Victory, victory, victory!
America’s compassion-time.

Free, Soulfully Free

December 31st, 1969

Free, free, free, free!
Soulfully free, honourably free!
Compassion-father, President Carter,
Is universally a vctory-tree.
No more sleepless struggle-nights. No more!
All-where, all-lit ignorance-shore.
Heart-power is the power of wisdom-height.
Bondage nowhere, delight, delight.

A Swing of Flowers

March 31st, 1981

A swing of flowers,
God’s sweetness-showers.
Man’s oneness-role,
No division-dole.

Krishna’s Consciousness

March 31st, 1981

Krishna’s consciousness,
Radha’s selflessness.
Krishna-Radha delight:
Blue perfection-height.

Misha Toyber

June 21st, 1981

Misha Toyber, acrobat, gymnast, champion-light!
You teach body’s fitness and perfection-height.
In you, your students have found their confidence-sea;
The soul and the body are in oneness-ecstasy.

Brother Narasimha Rao!

December 31st, 1969

Brother Narasimha Rao!
To your blue heights we bow.
Mother India’s soulful son,
Oneness-life you have won.
Brother Narasimha Rao!
Literature, culture in boundless measure;
Politics, poetry, a fruitful tree.
We sound the giant gong
Of your sky-vast victory-song.

Dana Zatopek

December 31st, 1969

Sister Dana, Sister Dana, Sister!
Helsinki Olympics gold medal winner.
Farther than the farthest, javelin you threw;
World’s surprise-attention you drew.
Your Emil swam in ecstasy-sea;
Perfect oneness-joy-satisfaction free.

Acapulco

December 31st, 1969

Acapulco, Acapulco, Aca!
Within, without a life soulful, blanca.
“The pearl of the Pacific”, your motto sublime.
O rare owner of ancient time and modern time!

Beauty’s Blossom-Heart-Forest!

December 31st, 1969

Beauty’s blossom-heart-forest!
Far away temptation-rest.
Everywhere love-garden.
Joy, bliss, a sleepless hand.

Good Morning

December 31st, 1969

Good morning, good morning, good morning.
Good afternoon, good afternoon, good afternoon,
Good evening, good evening, good evening.
Good night, good night, good night.
Bonjour, bonjour.
Bon apres-midi, bon apres-midi.
Bon soir, bon soir, bon soir.
Bonne nuit, bonne nuit, bonne nuit.
Buenos dias, buenos dias, buenos dias.
Buenas tardes, buenas tardes, buenas tardes.
Buenas noches, buenas noches, buenas noches.
Guten morgen, guten morgen, guten morgen.
Guten Abend, guten Abend, guten Abend,
Gute Nacht, gute Nacht, gute Nacht.
