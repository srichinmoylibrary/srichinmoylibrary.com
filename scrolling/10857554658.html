23.

Peace I feel in abundant measure
In the inmost recesses of my heart
When I do not allow my mind-elephant
And my vital-tiger
To attack my heart-lamb.

24.

Peace I feel in infinite measure
Only when I give the key
To my Lord Supreme
To open my heart-door
At His sweet Will.

Note

University of Vienna
Vienna, Austria
1 April 1989

25.

I get peace of mind
The moment I ask my mind
To stop running
And I ask my heart
To start singing.

26.

I receive peace
In abundant measure
When I take my mind
To fly with me
In my simplicity, sincerity,
Humility and purity-plane
In the Sky of God's Compassion,
Protection and Satisfaction.

27.

What I need is a life of peace.
And to have a life of peace,
What I need is a heart of bliss.
And to have a heart of bliss,
My entire being has to become
A mounting flame of aspiration.

Note

Kurhalle Oberlaa
Vienna, Austria
1 April 1989

28.

I can have peace
In abundant measure
Only from a non-stop surrender
To the Will
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

29.

The moment I pray to God
For peace,
He immediately promises me
A better world.

30.

Whenever I soulfully meditate
On world peace,
My Lord Supreme gives me
His blessingful surprise Visit.

31.

Peace is in my secret
And sacred pathway
To my supreme gratitude-destination.

Note

Friedrich Ebert Halle
Ludwigshafen, Germany
3 April 1989

32.

Peace I feel
In abundant measure
When my heart becomes
The rainbow-beauty
Of my soul.

33.

Peace I feel
In infinite measure
When my life becomes
The fragrance
Of my gratitude-heart.

34.

Every day,
Out of His infinite Bounty,
My Lord Supreme comes to visit
My heart-garden
With His Compassion-flooded Peace.
