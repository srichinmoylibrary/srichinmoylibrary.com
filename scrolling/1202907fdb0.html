17160

Alas,
I am lost between
The endless questions
Of my mind
And the streaming tears
Of my heart.

17161

Two songs are most melodious:
Life's surrender-song
And heart's gratitude-song.

17162

Just a tiny aspiration-flame
Has the capacity to illumine
The mind's dark abyss.

17163

My Lord,
I wish to go far beyond the domains
Of my life's tragedy
And my heart's rhapsody.

17164

If you do not cling to your mind's
Hallucination-cave,
God will invite you to come and visit
His Heart's Illumination-Palace.

17165

My surrender-sweetness
Perfectly paves the way
To God's infinite Delight.

17166

When adversity challenges you,
Be brave.
Inspire and energise yourself
To go beyond the reach
Of adversity.

17167

God never encouraged me
To learn the world-denial-song.
On the contrary,
He inspired, encouraged
And blessed me
To sing the world-acceptance-song
And dance the world-transformation-dance.

17168

My heart's progress-train
Is bound to slow down
If I do not have my soul
Inside the train as the conductor.

17169

Now that I have
Brought to the fore
My indomitable faith-capacity,
My old doubt-popularity cannot last.

17170

When I live inside my heart,
I see that my life has become
A source of inspiration
To all human beings.
When I live inside my mind,
I see that my life has become
Nothing but confrontation-desert
And confusion-jungle.

17171

Since the pride-problem
Is universal,
Can we not try to place this problem
At the Feet of
The Transcendental Lord Supreme?

17172

To avoid danger
Is indeed a difficult task.
But to make friends
With safety continuously
Is indeed an impossible task.

17173

I lose my heart's
Flower-fragrance
When I allow myself
To be owned by anger.

17174

Since a new hope-dawn
Is blossoming inside your heart,
Say good-bye to your mind's
Deathless confusion-night.
