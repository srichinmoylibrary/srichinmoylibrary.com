54 Doubt Dies ...

Doubt dies
When our love of God
Speedily flies
In our heart-sky.

55 God Is Ready To Bless Me ...

God is ready to bless me
With experience after experience,
But alas,
Am I ready?
Will I ever be ready?

56 The Aspiration-Life ...

The aspiration-life
Is not a life of secrecy
But a life of ecstasy.

57 God’s Blessings ...

God’s Blessings
I take
As unimaginable surprises.

58 When Aspiration Is Our Only Choice ...

When aspiration is our only choice,
Then God gives us a voice —
His own Voice.

59 It Is Not A Difficult Task ...

It is not a difficult task
To catch ignorance-thief
Red-handed.

60 Silence Is, Indeed ...

Silence is, indeed,
The best kind
Of peace-preparation.

22 December 2005 ...

22 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

61 I Promise, I Promise, I Promise ...

I promise, I promise, I promise:
From now on
I shall listen
To my Lord Supreme
Wholeheartedly.

62 A Soulful Heart-Singer ...

A soulful heart-singer
Immediately brings joy
To God.

63 When God Calls You ...

When God calls you,
You must remember
To bring with you
Your heart’s light
And your mind’s darkness
Together.

64 Your Mind, Vital And Body ...

Your mind, vital and body
Will be dumbfounded
When God’s Light descends.

65 Very Few Visible Miracles ...

Very few visible miracles
Of the Master
Do we see.
The inner world of the Master
Is, indeed, full of
Mushroom-density-miracles.

66 God Created Endless Ways ...

God created endless ways
So that we can please Him
At least in one way.

67 To My Greatest Joy ...

To my greatest joy,
My aspiration-heart-boat
Is under full sail.
