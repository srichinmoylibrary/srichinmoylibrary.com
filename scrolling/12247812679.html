Nutan Barashe Dibo

December 27th, 1991

Nutan barashe dibo shudhu dibo
Chahibona kichu pratidane

Translation:

I shall give and give,
And I shall not ask for anything in return
In this New Year.

I Shall Give and Give

December 27th, 1991

I shall give and give
And I shall not ask for anything in return
In this New Year.

Nutan Barashe Choitya Purushe

December 27th, 1991

Nutan barashe choitya purushe
Priti bhara chite dakibo

Translation:

With a loving heart,
I shall invoke my psychic being.

With a Loving Heart

December 27th, 1991

With a loving heart,
I shall invoke my psychic being.

Nutan Barashe Priyatame Mor

December 27th, 1991

Nutan barashe priyatame mor
Pujibo tushibo ami

Translation:

I shall worship,
I shall please my Lord Supreme
In this New Year.

Nutan Barashe Usha Devi Sathe

December 27th, 1991

Nutan barashe usha devi sathe
Ajanar deshe jabo

Translation:

With the Goddess Dawn
In her chariot,
I shall go to the country unknown
In this New Year.

With the Goddess Dawn in Her Chariot

December 27th, 1991

With the Goddess Dawn
In her chariot,
I shall go to the country unknown
In this New Year.

Nutan Barashe Ami Habo

December 27th, 1991

Nutan barashe ami habo
Mor hiyar surabhi

Translation:

I shall become my heart’s fragrance
In this New Year.

I Shall Become My Heart’s Fragrance

December 27th, 1991

I shall become my heart’s fragrance
In this New Year.

Nutan Barashe Golap Kamal

December 27th, 1991

Nutan barashe golap kamal
Habe mor shudhu sathi

Translation:

In the New Year
Only rose and lotus
Will be my friends.

Nutan Barashe Khuliya Rakhibo

December 27th, 1991

Nutan barashe khuliya rakhibo
Sathek ashar duar

Translation:

Hundreds of hope-doors
I shall keep open
In this New Year.

Nutan Barashe Ramdhanu Sathe

December 27th, 1991

Nutan barashe ramdhanu sathe
Gahibo nachibo

Translation:

With a rainbow,
I shall sing and dance
In the New Year.

With a Rainbow

December 27th, 1991

With a rainbow,
I shall sing and dance
In the New Year.

Nutan Barashe Nashibo Amar

December 27th, 1991

Nutan barashe nashibo amar
Ahamika arati

Translation:

O my pride-foe,
I shall destroy you
In this New Year.

O My Pride-Foe

December 27th, 1991

O my pride-foe,
I shall destroy you
In this New Year.
