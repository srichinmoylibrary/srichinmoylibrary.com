15615

If you only conceal your problems,
And do not offer them
To your Lord Supreme,
Then they will become
Infinitely stronger.

15616

Your heart likes to sing —
Not to get God's Attention,
But to proclaim God's Compassion
And Satisfaction on earth.

15617

O tears of my mind,
I do not trust you,
I do not need you,
I want nothing to do with you!
You are false,
You are deceitful!
You want to feed me
With false satisfaction-promises.

15618

My mind is waiting
For the disappearance
Of my heart-faith.

My heart is waiting
For the appearance
Of my mind's transformation-face.

15619

You must listen
To God's Heart-Advice
Before you begin
To spend time with your mind.

15620

The disappearance
Of your mind-problems
Was not due to
What you said
Or what you did,
But because God wants you,
For His own inner Reason,
To be a choice instrument of His.

15621

Nobody has forced you
To swim in the sea
Of multitudinous problems.
It is you who wanted,
In secrecy supreme,
To enjoy this present life of yours.

15622

His heart-progress
Has been thwarted
By his exorbitant
Mind-success.

15623

There is not a single mortal
Who has not received
A Compassion-Call from God.
But there are so many human beings
Who shamelessly do not respond
To God's Call.

15624

Alas, poor God every day receives
False hunger-cries
From humanity.

15625

There is nothing wrong
If you cry like a child
To draw God's Attention.
Only if you do not cry
Is everything wrong with you
And everything wrong for you!

15626

When I look at my heart,
I feel so sad
That it has taken upon itself
The torture of my doubt-mind.

15627

God,
I have only one prayer.
That prayer is to love You only
And also, more and more,
At every moment.

15628

When I cherish
Doubt, suspicion and negativity
In my mind,
God suffers from a storm
In His Oneness-Heart-Life.

15629

My Beloved Compassion-Lord Supreme
Forgives me
Even when I offer Him
Unhealthy and unsolicited advice.
