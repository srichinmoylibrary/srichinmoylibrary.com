1.

Power devours.
Love feeds.

2.

Power says to me,
"Come here!"
Love says to me,
"Let us be together,
Always together."

3.

Power says to me,
"Go away!"
Love says to me,
"We are our Eternity's oneness."

4.

Power does not know
What it is doing
And
Why it is doing.
Love knows
What it is doing
And
Why it is doing.

5.

Power is superiority's head.
Love is equality's heart.

6.

Power and truth
Are often strangers
To each other.
Love and truth
Are two mutual
Admirers and supporters.

7.

The hands of
Power
Are often destructive.
The hands of
Love
Are always creative.

8.

Power's blindness
Power is rarely aware of.
Love's oneness
Love is always conscious of.

9.

Power, name and fame
Together march.
Destination: nowhere.
Love, oneness and fulness
Together fly.
Destination: Heaven's smile-blossoms.

10.

Power is
Heaviness-haughtiness-mind.
Love is
Sweetness-fondness-heart.

11.

Power is
Duty-emptiness.
Love is
Duty-willingness.

12.

Power is narrow.
Power is shallow.
Power is shallow.
Love is auspicious.
Love is gracious.
Love is precious.

13.

Power does not think;
Therefore, it sinks.
Love sings God's Songs;
Therefore, it is a God-bliss-singer.

14.

Power is
Self-aggrandisement.
Love is
God-enlightenment.
