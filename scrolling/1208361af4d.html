22575

It is not possible to be both
A truth-defiance-life
And a God-reliance-life.

22576

May my aspiration-heart
Every day
Chase away my frustration-mind.

22577

My heart-garden
Is not only a beautiful place,
But also a cheerful place
For my Lord to work.

22578

God does not plan my future
In every detail
Unless I love Him only
And need Him only.

22579

O my mind,
Do tell me
Why you complicate my life.
O my soul,
Do tell me
How you liberate my life.

22580

My life's perfection-progress-hunger
Always receives
God's proud Attention.

22581

Not only the entire aspiration-life,
But each and every moment's
Aspiration-breath
Has a very special significance.

22582

O my mind,
Your independence-existence-hunger
Has turned my life
Into a complete failure.

22583

Quickly the mind distorts
The truth.
Slowly the heart feels and fulfils
The truth.

22584

God's Compassion-Eye
Is constantly blessing me
With the privilege of discovering Him
In and through my life.

22585

O my mind,
Be not too rigid.
O my heart,
Be not too timid.

22586

Not only does the mind not know,
But also it does not want to know
What divine love truly is.

22587

Alas, how can a human life be happy
When it has two bad bosses:
A dictating mind
And a dominating vital?

22588

God does not question
My disobedience-mind.
He just avoids and ignores it.

22589

O my mind,
My heart is the only
God-messenger of hope.
How do you dare
To crucify my heart?
