7215

Alas,
He does not feel
Any inner urge
To declare his independence
From eternity's ignorance-home.

7216

You can easily pay back
Your God-debts
Through your life's
Service-smile.

7217

Even an emperor
Cannot claim
To equal the power
Of a tranquility-flooded mind.

7218

My heart is employed
By God's Compassion-Eye.
My soul is employed
By God's Satisfaction-Heart.

7219

Your heart's aspiration-river
Has to do only one thing:
It must flow and flow,
And never be allowed to ebb.

7220

God wants you to come to Him
Whenever you want to,
For your frequent coming
Is the only way you can establish
Your heart's oneness with Him.

7221

Desire-thoughts are strangling you.
Every day can you not try
To free yourself
With the help of your soul's
Adamantine will?

7222

God is nearer
Than you imagine.
Therefore, your mind of doubt
And your life of fear
Will before long be illumined
By God's Compassion-Eye.

7223

Every day throw your doubt-javelin
As far as you can,
For that is the only way
You can derive happiness
From what you say
And what you are.

7224

You say that you do not see God.
I believe it.
But can you ever say
That you do not hear God's Voice
Inside your heart every day?

7225

God has forgiven
Your ingratitude-crime.
Now He wants you
To do Him a big favour
By forgetting all about
Your ingratitude-crime.

7226

My Lord Supreme,
Do grant me a boon.
Do give me a life
That will long for only one thing:
God's Satisfaction-Fulness
Inside my aspiration-heart.

7227

The easiest way to manifest God
And please God
Is to grow the devotion-plant
To satisfy the human in you
And the divine in God.

7228

God invites me every day
To join Him
In His Satisfaction-Dance.
Alas, why do I allow hesitation
To lord it over my mind?

7229

My Lord,
Do give me the capacity
To love You
Infinitely more than ignorance-night
Loves my entire being.
