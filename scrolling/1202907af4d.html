17175

A constantly cheerful mind
Can make my life's despair-night
Obsolete.

17176

The mind's old conflicts
Can be conquered
Only by the heart's
New solutions.

17177

The simplicity of the mind,
The purity of the heart
And the divinity of the vital
Are abiding realities.

17178

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me a little rest
From my self-made,
Torturing miseries.

17179

What is the difference
Between self-aggrandising thought
And breath-suffocating thought?
None!

17180

God's Compassion-Eye
Embraces even
Our endless unkindness
And useless sentiments.

17181

Expectation is not
Self-deception.
But expectation can be
Fatal life-frustration.

17182

It is so difficult for our mind
To sing silence-song.
It is so difficult for our heart
To sing gratitude-song.

17183

God's Compassion-Heart-Temple
Is for all and sundry
To sing, pray and dance in.

17184

The difference between
Your path and my path
Is this:
Your path thinks
That one has to do everything good
To please God;
My path thinks
That nobody can please God
Unless God wants to please Himself
In and through us.

17185

Each time a human being
Doubts his own divinity,
He loses
His oneness-world-citizenship.

17186

I see God
Inside the smile
Of His oneness-fulness-creation.

17187

What I need
Is Infinity's Peace
In Divinity's Space.

17188

If you want your mind
To fly freely in God's own Sky,
Then make friends with your heart
Immediately.

17189

Nobody has asked you
To believe in the Unknowable.
Just try to believe
That a higher Source
Is guiding your earthly journey.
