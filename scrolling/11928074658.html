7130

If you only please
Your desire-life,
Who is going to please
Your God-ordained aspiration-life?

7131

God is within,
God is without,
God is beyond,
But never beyond discovery.

7132

In the morning
My Lord is
His loving Heart.

In the afternoon
My Lord is
His tearing Heart.

In the evening
My Lord is
His fulfilling Heart.

At night
My Lord and I are
Our dancing hearts.

7133

Yesterday God was his Lover.
Today God is his Admirer too.
Do you know why?
Just because he has been
Pleasing God sleeplessly.

7134

Be careful at every moment!
Do not allow despair
To take over your aspiration-life.

7135

Tomorrow will take care
Of your tomorrow's fears.
Be a perfect friend
To your today's joy.

7136

Stillness of the heart
Alone
Can cure the illness
Of the mind.

7137

You are not
Your tension-tortured life.
You are
Your God-pleasing,
Aspiration-discovered heart.

7138

He is idling away his life,
Yet he thinks
That he will be able to succeed
Without even trying.

7139

If you do not fulfil
Your life's perfection-promise,
You will not be able to see
God's transcendental Satisfaction-Smile.

7140

Your whole life
Is totally lost
In your mind's
Unnecessary falsehood.

7141

God will give you
A sweet Smile
If you can offer Him
Two soulful eyes
And one prayerful heart.

7142

This world is full of
Peace-announcers.
This world does not have
Peace-finders.

7143

Every day
You learn the lesson
Taught by your soul
Inside your heart-garden.

7144

I am just learning
To think of God.
Therefore
Please do not expect
Anything spectacular
Either from my heart
Or from my life.
