8 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

27 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That He secretly and proudly treasures
My prayer-life
And my meditation-heart.

28 The Sleepless And Selfless ...

The sleepless and selfless
Heart-giver
Expects no return.

29 The Earth-Time Ticks And Ticks ...

The earth-time ticks and ticks
To awaken me.
The Heaven-time beckons
And beckons me
To raise me high, higher, highest.

30 Who Is My Mother Earth ...

Who is my Mother Earth,
If not a ceaseless flood
Of God-Tears?

31 Who Is My Father Heaven ...

Who is my Father Heaven,
If not an ocean
Of God-Smiles?

32 God Quickly Comes ...

God quickly comes
To the seeker
Who does not claim anything.

33 God Proudly Comes Running ...

God proudly comes running
To the seeker
Who lovingly, devotedly and soulfully
Claims God.

34 I Wish Every Step Of My Life ...

I wish every step of my life
To be guided
By the Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

35 God Is Exceedingly ...

God is exceedingly
And equally pleased
With my sleepless heart-tears
And my breathless soul-smiles.

36 What Was I Doing? ...

What was I doing?
I was repeating and chanting
God’s Forgiveness-Name.

37 What Am I Doing? ...

What am I doing?
I am repeating and chanting
God’s Compassion-Name.

38 What Will I Be Doing? ...

What will I be doing?
I will be repeating and chanting
God’s Inspiration, Encouragement
And Enthusiasm-Names.

39 Centuries Ago ...

Centuries ago
I started devouring
The golden dust
Of my Lord’s Feet,
And I have not stopped
Even for a fleeting moment.

9 December 2003
