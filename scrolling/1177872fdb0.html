4960 You Wear The Crown Of Miseries

You wear the crown of miseries,
Not because you have sadly failed
In your life,
But because you have not surrendered soulfully
To the Beauty of God's Omniscience-Light.

4961 We Do Not Need Anything Else

Deeply I love God's Beauty.
Smilingly God loves my sincerity.
Therefore, we do not need
Anything else for ourselves.

4962 What Can Stand?

Before impurity, what can stand?
No, not even my powerful mind!

Before insincerity, what can stand?
No, not even my forgiving heart!

Before ingratitude, what can stand?
No, not even my oneness-soul!

4963 The Only Reality-Passport

Your purity has the capacity
To unlock everybody's heart-door.
Something more:
Your purity is the only reality-passport
Which enables you to enter into
Each and every human heart.

4964 No God-Realised Soul

No God-realised soul of the Himalayan height
Can be vanquished
Either by earth's powerful arms
Or by Heaven's bountiful charms.

4965 An Unfortunate God-Seeker

An unfortunate God-seeker
Is he who unconsciously forgets
His own courage in the inner world.

4966 Two Divine Realities

Two divine realities
Have never-ending beginnings:
God's Eternity-Hunger in me
And
God's Infinity-Delight for me.

4967 Perhaps You Made A Promise

God is keeping His Promise.
He is holding you up
Carefully and firmly
And keeping you from swaying.
Perhaps you, too,
Made a solemn promise to God.
Perhaps!

4968 If You Have The Determination-Fire

If you have the determination-fire,
You can succeed in your outer life
Easily.

If you have the faith-glow,
You can proceed in your inner life
Amazingly.

4969 God Will Like You More

If you think of God silently,
God will like you more.

If you pray to God breathlessly,
God will love you more.

If you meditate on God unconditionally,
God will need you more.

If you contemplate on God transcendentally,
God will claim you more.

4970 May My Life Have The Capacity

May my doubting mind
Have the capacity to unlearn.

May my longing heart
Have the capacity to learn.

May my serving life
Have the capacity to return darkness-night
To its possessor:
Ignorance.

4971 Certain And Precious Thoughts

Uncertain and anxious thoughts
Belong to the future.
Therefore, I have nothing to do
With the future.

Certain and precious thoughts
Belong to the present, the eternal Now.
Therefore, I shall always remain
In the eternal Now.

4972 My Perfection Is The Heir

My perfection is the heir
Of my salvation.
My satisfaction is the heir
Of my illumination.
My gratitude is the heir
Of my realisation.

4973 My Life Is Not A Transient Dream

My life is not a transient dream,
But my death is.
My God-realisation-light
Is not a transient reality,
But my struggle through ignorance-night was.

4974 Your Amazing Victory-Song

Keep your sky-vast silence
Within.
Your amazing victory-song
Will be sung by the world
Without.
