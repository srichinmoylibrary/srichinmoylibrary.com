23115

The soulful music
Of the heart
Is the voice
Of divinity's perfection.

23116

Only a life of surrender
Can sit
On the throne of tranquillity.

23117

We treasure
The power of independence.
God treasures
The power of interdependence.

23118

My soul does not use
A loud voice.
It always speaks
In whispers.

23119

The fragrance
Of a gratitude-heart-blossom
Is unparalleled
In running the fastest
To God's Kingdom.

23120

The oneness
Of aspiring hearts
Embodies and reveals
The most haunting illumination-melody.

23121

True God-seekers
Are Heaven-pilgrim-souls
That throng before
God's Lotus-Feet.

23122

The difference between
Detachment and indifference
Is this:
Indifference blinds us;
Detachment illumines us.

23123

My life's happiest moments
Are those
When I am blessed
With brief glimpses
Of my Lord's Smile.

23124

If you do not forgive and forget
Your past unhealthy experiences,
God will not be able to bless you
With His Enthusiasm-Dynamism.

23125

When courage is founded
Upon oneness-love,
We can challenge the pride
Of ignorance-night.

23126

May my entire day be blessed
With the unparalleled beauty
Of the morning's rising sun!

23127

How do we regain
Our long-lost divinity?
Only through constant
God-obedience-willingness
And eagerness.

23128

Each time I have
A God-surrendering thought,
I immediately multiply
My heart's goodness.

23129

O seeker, be patient!
Sooner or later
You will definitely reach
Your sunlit aspiration-summit.
