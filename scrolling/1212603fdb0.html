26760

The stark stupidity
Of human beings
Even baffles
Divinity's Wisdom-Light.

26761

Our heart does not know
How our mind can be
So enamoured
Of stark ignorance-night.

26762

The tears of my heart
Smile through my Lord's Eye
Every day.

26763

Ignorance is so cruel
That it tells me
It has temptation
To intimidate me.

26764

Wisdom-heart tells me
That it has its purity-sword
To challenge ignorance.

26765

The happiness of the heart
Is the freedom of life,
Even when we live
In ignorance-prison.

26766

The human mind does not comprehend
Anything divine
That is shaping and moulding our lives
For our perfection.

26767

God is looking for you.
Can you not even for a second
Stay inside your heart-garden?

26768

There is no stop sign
On my
God-pleasing road.

26769

God may become tired at times
Of our stupidity,
But God-Forgiveness
Never becomes tired.

26770

The mind by nature
Is poverty-stricken
In the realm of spirituality.

26771

May my heart, mind, vital
And body
Always have plenty of hope
To live on.

26772

My Lord is telling me
Again and again
To give Him the opportunity
To worry about all my problems.

26773

Alas, I have given up everything
Save and except one thing:
My conscious God-dividing mind.

26774

I have decided to tell the world
That I have nothing else to do
But please God
At every moment.
