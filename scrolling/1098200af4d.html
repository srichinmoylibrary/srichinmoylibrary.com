66 Each Heart-Song ...

Each heart-song
Is a very long step forward
Towards God.

67 Sleep Like A Child ...

Sleep like a child,
Dream like a child,
Act like a child.
Lo, God is granting you
A free access
To His Heart-Home.

20 December 2006 ...

20 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

68 Poor God ...

Poor God
Is all the time calling us,
But we deliberately
Do not respond.

69 My Lord Supreme Returns ...

My Lord Supreme returns
My love-devotion-surrender-calls
Immediately.

70 God-Disobedience-Train ...

God-disobedience-train —
Once it starts,
It never stops.

71 The Ancient God Blessed Me ...

The ancient God blessed me
With the message
Of unity in multiplicity.

72 The Modern God Is Blessing Me ...

The modern God is blessing me
With the message
Of diversity and complexity.

73 In Our Spiritual Life ...

In our spiritual life,
Eagerness-speed
Knows no equal.

74 No Human Heart ...

No human heart
Can ever dare to resist
God’s Heart-Attraction.

75 God Never Sanctions ...

God never sanctions
Any seeker’s
Retirement-enjoyment.

76 Nothing In My Life ...

Nothing in my life
Is as sacred
As my heart’s
Sleepless and breathless
God-devotion.

77 Never ...

Never,
Never become tired
Of your inward dive!
