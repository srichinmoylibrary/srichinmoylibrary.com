45.

Because you are not one with
Your Pilot's Oneness-Plan,
Within, without you still remain
Eternity's unlit man.

46.

To doubt is to drink
From poison-cup.
No more doubt-sleep.
Wake up, wake up!

47.

Run, run, daily run
On aspiration-road!
Your new life will discard
Your old life's ignorance-load.

48.

Since your surrender-list
Is very long,
Your Lord will teach you
His Smile-Song.

49.

I live, I live,
In my ignorance-tree.
God lives, God lives,
In His Forgiveness-Sea.

50.

My hopes shall not fail,
My dreams shall not fade,
Because the right choice
My heart and I have made.

51.

God will grant you
His brightest Satisfaction-Look
If every day you study
His Earth-Transformation-Book.

52.

You and you alone
Can determine your earthly life
By challenging every day
The world's destruction-knife.

53.

God will descend
Every day from Above
If you will accept
His Gift: Oneness-Love.

54.

To receive the best
From the coming day,
Become God's partner
In self-giving play.

55.

Remember, you are not
Your mind's ego-mountain.
What you are is your heart's
Constant oneness-fountain.

56.

Have patience, have joy
And have determination.
You will be visited
By God the Liberation.

57.

If you want to see
God's Cosmic Ray,
Then sing a song of gratitude
Every new day.

58.

Our deepest longings
Will be answered someday.
Let us soulfully join
Our Lord's Cosmic Play.

59.

My seeker-friend,
Keep the Goal always in sight.
Do not be afraid
Of ignorance-darkness-fight.
