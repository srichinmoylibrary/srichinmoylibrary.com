16460

My sleepless gratitude-heart
Is a first-class passenger
On God's Satisfaction-Boat.

16461

Each human mind
Is full of
Unreality-questions.

Each divine heart
Is full of
Reality-answers.

16462

I have commanded my mind
To change,
And it has devotedly
Obeyed me.

16463

My Lord, do tell me,
Where is Your hiding place?

"My child,
My hiding place is inside
Your unlit mind's thought-cave."

16464

How can anybody
Make any progress
If he lives in the land
Of mere chance?

16465

To live in thought-squalor
Is not an uncommon experience
For the human mind.

16466

O my doubting mind,
Do not misunderstand
My all-loving heart!

16467

If you yourself
Do not give serious thought
To your own spiritual life,
Why should anybody else
Do it for you?

16468

O my heart's aspiration-cry,
You are my only
Trustworthy friend.

16469

My heart
Lovingly and compassionately
Teaches my mind
How to read
The God-manifestation-map.

16470

Temptation-maker
Is caught.
Temptation-shooter
Is sought.

16471

A purity-heart
Is indeed
One of the supreme trophies
Of life.

16472

No matter how useless
A human life is,
It cannot exhaust
God's infinite Compassion.

16473

Of all my soulful gifts,
My heart's gratitude-garland
God likes most.

16474

There is no life
Without a sigh.
There is no heart
Without a cry.
