1.

The Lord will draw
Your great life-plan
If you become
His greatest fan.

2.

An unwilling mind
Can nothing find.
An unwilling life
Is shockingly blind.

3.

I recognise
Impurity
By its face of
Insecurity.

4.

Earthly prosperity
Comes and goes.
Heavenly satisfaction
Comes and grows.

5.

Because you have erased your past
Without leaving a trace,
You are seeing within, without
God's Satisfaction-Face.

6.

You have the mind
That creates pains.
You are the soul
That breaks chains.

7.

A casual commitment
To spirituality
Is nothing other
Than useless futility.

8.

My ego-mind sings
My vital's attachment-song.
My oneness-heart strikes
My soul's enlightenment-gong.

9.

My prayer-life
Makes my heart divinely pure.
My surrender-life
Makes my world supremely sure.

10.

The fearful mind
Lives in a cave.
The doubting mind
Lives in a grave.

11.

His greatness wants to conquer
The truth to enjoy.
His goodness wants the truth
Him to employ.

12.

Where else can you find
Aspiration-flame
If not inside your
Inspiration-game?

13.

I call it
My heart's simplicity.
God calls it
His Life's Majesty.

14.

O leave the past!
Why do you care?
Live in the present
To smile and dare.
