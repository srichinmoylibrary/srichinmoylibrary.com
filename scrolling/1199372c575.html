13690

How can you look through
Your mind's happiness-window
Unless you have already entered
Your heart's oneness-room?

13691

Each self-giving moment
Is undoubtedly
A nectar-delight-feast.

13692

I dare to imagine
Everything except
God's Displeasure-Eye.

13693

You say you have nothing to do.
Tell me, have you ever thought
Of uprooting your mind's doubt-tree?

13694

Insecurity is man's ancient
Imperfection-night,
Which challenges man's blossoming
Perfection-light.

13695

This is not the time
For you to disappoint God.
Can you not see Him
Running towards you
With your Himalayan future joy?

13696

There is no pleasure
That can ever equal
A human being's self-giving-joy.

13697

Do not run away from
Unsolved problems
If you ever care to realise
Your soul's unhorizoned and fantastic dreams.

13698

Do not seek truth
In the mind's confusion-market,
Even after you have inundated your heart
With light.

13699

Be not a victim
Of your heart's self-denial
And your mind's self-betrayal.

13700

My Lord,
May I eternally live
As a soulful gratitude-life-plant.
