8215

You call it
Your selfless service.
I call it
God's exhaustless Pride
In you.

8216

Your ceaseless stupidities
Have exhausted
Even God's Hope for you.

8217

His human life
Wants to enjoy
The grandeur of vastness-power.

8218

From morn to eve
He collects in silence
Silent griefs.

8219

No aspiration
Clearly means
A life full of separation.

8220

His aspiration rides
In the first-class compartment
Of Eternity.

8221

The illumination
Of your life
Depends on the active faith
Of your heart.

8222

Do not allow
Your mind and your vital
To be entangled
In sad contradictions.

8223

Do not forget
That once upon a time
You had a very long contact
With the higher worlds.

8224

To have a task
From Above
Means to have a pleasing
Experience-reward.

8225

If you are longing
For a new life,
Tell death
You have cancelled
Your appointment.

8226

Because you are a fool,
You have abandoned the struggle.
No victory shall ever touch
Your life's shore!

8227

By virtue
Of his self-giving life
He is leading the train
Of human progress.

8228

A heart of love
Has to be used
Always for a sacred purpose.

8229

Definitely
God does not want
To hear from you
That you are too old!
