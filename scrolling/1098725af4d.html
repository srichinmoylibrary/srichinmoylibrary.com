66 My Prayer ...

My prayer
Is my life’s
Morning rose.

67 My Meditation ...

My meditation
Is my heart’s
Morning lotus.

68 A God-Seeker ...

A God-seeker
Has to be on the alert
Twenty-four hours a day.

69 Each Thought Comes From ...

Each thought comes from
The unknown,
And each will comes from
The Unknowable.

70 Alas, Every Day ...

Alas, every day
There is a new barrier
To cross.

71 Patience ...

Patience
Eventually becomes
Victorious.

72 My Desire-Life ...

My desire-life
Was planted
By my mind.

73 My Aspiration-Life ...

My aspiration-life
Was planted
By God’s Heart.

19 February 2007 ...

19 February 2007

Chiang Mai, Thailand

74 O My Sleep, I Love You ...

O my sleep, I love you —
You take away
All my worries and anxieties!

75 Alas, When Will Truth ...

Alas, when will Truth
Reign supreme on earth,
When?

76 My Eyes Can Fool Me ...

My eyes can fool me
And betray me,
But not my heart,
Never!

77 Another Name ...

Another name
For meditation
Is God-communication.

78 Another Name ...

Another name
For prayer
Is God-pleasure.

79 In Vain, My Mind ...

In vain, my mind
Wants to interview God.
