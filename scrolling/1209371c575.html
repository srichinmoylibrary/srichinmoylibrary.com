23590

Not my mind's new and false
Truth-invention,
But my heart's old and true
Truth-discovery
Is the unparalleled road
To my God-destination.

23591

Like a child I must cry
To express my gratitude
Through my soulful smile.

23592

O my God-searching mind,
Go forward, go forward.
Do not allow your ancient fears
To haunt you.

23593

May my heart's hope
And my life's promise
Remain always inseparable
To please my Lord in His own Way.

23594

God the Fulfilment
And God the Satisfaction
Are within your heart's
Aspiration-reach.

23595

O my mind,
Your desire to eclipse
My heart's goodness
God will never be able
To satisfy.

23596

My mind the prison
And my heart the palace
Every day, without fail, invite me.
Alas, indecision is my name.

23597

I want to live
In a progress-worshipping era
And not in a success-demanding era.

23598

Each time I offer my gratitude-heart
To my Lord Supreme,
I am blessed with a deeper and richer
State of existence.

23599

When God wants to go on vacation,
He invites only my self-giving heart
To accompany Him.

23600

To embrace the whole world
In its totality,
I must develop the quality
Of infinite adaptability.
