5 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my mind every day
   Sing only peace-songs.
May my heart every day
   Sing only gratitude-songs.
May my life every day
   Sing only surrender-songs.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 1999, 9:42 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

5 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To win each and every inner race,
   My Supreme,
I need only Your Grace.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 1999, 10:06 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

6 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   This morning
Unconditionally You have shown me
The measureless depth
   Of Your Compassion-Eye.
Do accept the tears
   Of my gratitude-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1999, 8:01 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

6 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am most devotedly seeing
   Your Protection-Arms
Around my aspiration-love-devotion-surrender-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1999, 8:07 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-five times. (31 right, 34 left)

6 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I pray to the Feet
   Of the unknown God.
I meditate on the Heart
   Of the knowable God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1999, 8:21 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

6 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have prayerfully and soulfully
   Established
My heart, my mind, my vital,
   My body and my life
Upon my God-realisation-foundation.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1999, 8:29 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

6 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The mind's freedom-march —
   No, no, no!
The heart's devotion-walk —
   Yes, yes, yes!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1999, 8:35 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

6 October 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   I have thrown
My body, my vital, my mind
   And my heart
Into Your Compassion-Forgiveness-Arms.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1999, 8:54 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 80 lbs. (72 reps)

9 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O my inspiration-mind,
O my aspiration-heart,
O my dedication-life,
O my gratitude-breath,
Where are you hiding?
   Where?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 October 1999, 8:41 a.m. Before using seating double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

9 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My doubting mind is known
   As a volcano
In the inner world of my spirituality.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 October 1999, 8:47 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty times. (30 right, 30 left)

9 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My suspicious mind is known
   As an earthquake
In the inner world of my spirituality.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 October 1999, 9:01 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 400 lbs.)

9 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My jealous mind is known
   As a famine
In the inner world of my spirituality.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 October 1999, 9:07 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

9 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   O my doubting mind,
   O my suspicious mind,
   O my jealous mind,
You immediately need
The blooms and the blossoms
   Of a oneness-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 October 1999, 9:10 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

11 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Life is afraid of seeing
   The face of death.
Death is afraid of seeing
   The immortality of the soul.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 October 1999, 12:55 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 96 lbs. (18 reps)

11 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   You are commanding me
Only to listen to my aspiring heart
And never to my doubting mind.
   I shall prayerfully,
Soulfully and unconditionally
   Listen to Your Command.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 October 1999, 1:22 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.
