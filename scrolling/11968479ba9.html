11145

Time will eventually pardon you
And all your untold crimes,
But the question is
Whether you will have the daring capacity
To forgive yourself.

11146

Vaster than vastness,
Sweeter than sweetness,
Is the seeker's humility-fulness.

11147

O my mind,
How can I ever forgive you?
Do you think I do not know
All about
Your cleverly secret meeting
With the doubt-dragon?

11148

Your mind's small eyes of curiosity
And your mind's big ears of curiosity
Will not be able to receive
Your soul's luminosity -
Never!

11149

You must daily pray and meditate.
A simple thing like that
Will bring God
The infinite Compassion and Satisfaction
To you.

11150

God excuses us
For each and every possible fault
Of ours.
Again, it is He who expects
That we shall not repeat
The same old story
Over and over again.

11151

A carefree,
Vagabond satisfaction-life
Is founded upon
Man's hallucination-mind.

11152

If you are a clever man,
Then you will try
To surprise the world
With your mind's brilliance.

If you are a wise seeker,
Then you will try
To inspire the world
With your heart's cry.

11153

Why are you so anxious
To be left alone?
If you want satisfaction
From your life,
Then there is only one way
To achieve it,
And that is by establishing
Your sleepless oneness
With the world.
There is no other way!

11154

A God-hungry soul
Will never be threatened
By a doubtful mind.

11155

Your life's progress-journey
Will stop nowhere
If your heart is the possessor
Of a universal oneness-belief.

11156

If your heart is a God-dreamer,
Then eventually
You are meant to be
God's creation-lover.

11157

He who has faith
In God's infinite Compassion-Light
Is definitely basking
In God-Eternity's Sunshine.

11158

The doubting mind enjoys
Tornado-perplexity.
The searching and aspiring mind enjoys
The emptiness-fulness
Of silence-light.

11159

Each unhealthy thought
Is an immediate destroyer
Of your heart's confidence-plants.
