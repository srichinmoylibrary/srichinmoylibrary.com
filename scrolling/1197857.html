12101

My Lord Supreme,
May my life,
Even in its unconscious moments,
Not repeat
Its past imperfection-performances.

12102

Because you will remain faithful
To your final breath,
You are not going to miss
Either the Golden Boat
Or the Golden Shore
Of your Inner Pilot.

12103

Yesterday my life was
The full growth of supreme greatness.
Alas, today I have become
Unforeseen frustration-night
And the hurtful sighs
Of ceaseless sorrows.

12104

What is my inner silence doing?
It is searching for my outer life's
Cheerful acceptance.

12105

How can you ever succeed
If yours is a poorly lit
Mind-street of self-confidence?

12106

What I give God
Is a tiny drop
Of my mind's humility.
God in return gives me
His Heart's Infinity-stretching
Appreciation.

12107

Why do you allow your heart's faith
To be blown away
By your mind's unhealthy, uncomely
And conflicting ideas?
Why?

12108

If I can soulfully and unconditionally
Wait for my Lord's choice God-Hour,
Then I have nothing to lose -
Only everything to gain.

12109

Anger, O anger-beast,
You are, indeed, the greatest,
Quickest and surest waste
Of man's life-energy.

12110

Alas, I do not know
How long I shall remain
With my failure-fed fate.
Alas, I do not know
When I shall be illumined
By my problem-solving
Compassion-soul.

12111

His life is in between
His mind's amazon ambition
And his doubt's
Indiscriminate destruction.

12112

A doubting mind
Is nothing short of
A fruitless reality
Rising from ashes of uselessness.

12113

God demands your heart's
Time-tested faith
Before He grants your life
Liberation-perfection.

12114

Be a surrender-superstar.
Your life will be
Your victory's delight
In profuse measure.
