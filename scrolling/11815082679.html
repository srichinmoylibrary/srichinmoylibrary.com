8515 Gratitude Is All-Powerful

If humility is mighty,
Then purity is almighty.
If surrender is powerful
Then gratitude is all-powerful.

8516 The Scorn Of Multitude-Tongues

No matter how divine and how perfect
A human being is,
He is subject to the scorn
Of multitude-tongues.

8517 If God's Mind Is Subtle

If God's Mind is subtle,
His Heart is simple.
If God's Compassion-Eye is powerful,
His Satisfaction-Life is blessingful.

8518 Each Human Desire

Each human desire
Is an arrow
Which is quite often apt
To miss the target.

8519 Each Human Being Is Blessed

As a flower is blessed
With fragrance and beauty,
Even so each human being is blessed
With inner purity and divinity.

8520 His Unparalleled Inheritance

Because he has divine thoughts
As his unparalleled inheritance,
His life is a constant expression
Of his heart's intense gratitude.

8521 His Firm Belief

His firm belief:
Unless his entire being cries
For fulness-splendour in life,
Tears will drain
His aspiration-heart dry.

8522 When Imagination Fails You

When imagination fails you,
Do not feel sad.
Just smile and remain serene.
From above you will get another chance
To catch the imagination-bird.

8523 Refuse To Sing With Your Mind

Just refuse to sing with your mind.
Behold!
Your heart is all ready
To teach you its own soulful songs.

8524 God Will Treasure You

If you can cherish
What you have already received from God,
God will, without fail,
Grant you His Eternity's Treasure-Smile.

8525 What Hurts You More

Be sure to know
What hurts you more:
To teach the world
Or to learn from the world.

8526 Inside Your Heart

No matter what your physical appearance,
There is always a most beautiful person
Inside your heart
Who can make your face truly beautiful.

8527 There Is No Perfect Desire

As there is no such thing
As a perfect desire,
Even so there is no such thing
As imperfect aspiration-surrender.

8528 The Causes Of Sudden Death

A doubtful mind,
A fearful heart
And a hateful life
Are the unmistakable and powerful causes
Of sudden death in the spiritual life.

8529 We Need Many Things From God

We need many, many things from God
To become His divine instruments.
But what we need most of all
Is to keep our heart's door
Sleeplessly open to Him.
