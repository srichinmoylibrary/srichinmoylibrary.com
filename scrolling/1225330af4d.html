Since You Are a Candidate for Power

December 31st, 1969

Since you are a candidate
For power, you will succeed.
Since I am a candidate
For light, I shall not only succeed,
But also proceed.

God Opens His Morning Eye

December 31st, 1969

God opens His morning Eye
     And it is all Duty.
God closes His evening Eye
     And it is all Beauty.

O Endless Night

December 31st, 1969

O endless night,
I have a new friend,
A new leader-guest,
A new leader bright:
Satisfaction-delight.

One Aspiration-Drop

December 31st, 1969

One aspiration-drop
Fills the vast immensity.
One dedication-flame
Kindles the unlit mortality.

Some Men Were Destined to Fly

December 31st, 1969

Some men were destined to fly.
I am not one of those
     Who flew.
Some Gods were meant for knowledge-sun.
I am not one of those
     Who knew.

I Loved Yesterday’s Unconditional Grace

December 31st, 1969

I loved
Yesterday’s unconditional grace.
I love
Today’s winning race.
I shall love
Tomorrow’s perfection-face.

Yesterday I Loved

December 31st, 1969

Yesterday I loved
Imagination-flight.
Today I love
Aspiration-white.
Tomorrow I shall love
Realisation-light.

I Longed to Give My Heart and Soul

December 31st, 1969

I longed to give my heart and soul
To the animal world.
My ex-friends were confused.
I longed to give my heart and soul
To the human world.
My human fellows
Were not enthused.
I longed to give my heart and soul
To the worlds divine.
My divine friends
Were quite amused.

I Shall Obey You

December 31st, 1969

I shall obey You.
I shall be at Your Feet.
Constantly loving You
And serving You.
In You perfection I shall meet.

A New World of Peace

December 31st, 1969

A new world of peace,
A new world of light,
A new world of height,
Can alone give me
Satisfaction-delight.

I Know the Secret Desires

December 31st, 1969

I know the secret desires of your heart:
You want to bind, you want to blind
This world of beauty’s glow.
Good luck! Indeed, you are so smart.

Power Influences My Life

December 31st, 1969

Power influences my life.
Light inspires my soul.
God loves my seeker’s role.
Man augments my strife.

Faith Is a Secret Way

December 31st, 1969

Faith is a secret way
To go and see God’s Face.
Surrender is an open way
To win God and God’s Grace.

Heavenly Time and Earthly Space

December 31st, 1969

Heavenly time
     And
Earthly space
We must measure,
We must treasure.
Meaningful is time,
Beautiful clime.

Ancient Night

December 31st, 1969

Ancient-night,
I salute your soulful cry.
Ancient-light,
I salute your fruitful sky.
