18901

You have an invisible Friend
Who is always eager to help you.
You may not see Him
With your human eyes,
But you can feel
His compassionate Presence
Inside your aspiring heart.

18902

My Lord Supreme,
Do meditate on my heart
With all Your Compassion-Love,
And do accept
My gratitude-flower-heart.

18903

Our inalienable birthright
Is to claim God.
Our only goal is to become
His choicest and most perfect instrument
To love Him and serve Him
In His own Way.

18904

God celebrates
His creation's birthday
On earth
With His Vision's
Blessingful Joy and Pride.

18905

Inspiration-lion will come
And energise you
If you can bring your inner joy
To the fore
On the strength of your prayer-life.

18906

My heart of silence
Is not only God's Answer
To my life's teeming questions
But also His divine Pride in me.

18907

Abandon the dying realities
Of the past!
Welcome the smiling dreams
Of the future!

18908

I really do not want
To remain lost anymore
Between
My mind's hesitation
And my heart's fear.

18909

An inspired action
Is a confidence-step forward
To arrive at the perfection-goal.

18910

The shortage of faith
Immediately hastens the birth
Of destruction-crisis.

18911

What I always need
Is a God-hungry heart
And not
A man-nourished mind.

18912

Each self-giving thought
Descends
From a higher world
Of illumination-delight.

18913

Alas,
Many human beings
Have fragile and fleeting
Gratitude-hearts.

18914

O my confusion-mind,
Do you think my heart's light
Will allow you to remain
Forever unchallenged?
