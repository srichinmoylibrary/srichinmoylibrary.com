22175

The law-maker-mind
Is also
The law-offender-mind.

22176

Is there anything
More difficult
Than to please
One's own mind?

22177

My heart does not need
To read any book
To become wise
And remain forever wise.

22178

Nature's perfection
Unavoidably requires
Endless time.

22179

Nothing really belongs to us.
No, not even
Our aspiration-heart.

22180

The spiritual life
Can easily live
Far beyond the understanding
Of the mind.

22181

My aspiration-heart
Definitely has the right
To destroy my ego's vanity.

22182

My self-giving heart
Is the only thing
That God enjoys
When He is sad.

22183

My divine thoughts
Are the blooms and blossoms
Of my aspiration-breath.

22184

No matter how great and good
You are, alas,
You are bound to be discredited
By the jealousy of the world.

22185

The mind is a train
That is always late
In thinking of God
And praying to God.

22186

Do not think
Of your undivine qualities
All the time.
Indeed, that is the worst
Torment of all.

22187

Spirituality
Is nothing other than
A road that winds uphill
All the way.

22188

My heart's happiness
Is marred
By my constantly reasoning mind.

22189

If we do not try to satisfy God
In God's own Way,
Then we are not above
The level of animals.
