20960

God has every right
To reveal and manifest
What He eternally embodies.

20961

Human life is not a collection
Of mere anecdotes.
It is the revelation and manifestation
Of one's inner capacities.

20962

Among all the human
Weakness-experiences,
Self-doubt is undoubtedly
The worst possible
Weakness-experience.

20963

Truth is self-evident.
God tells us that there can be
No more important necessity
Than asserting the truth.

20964

Self-giving for a noble cause
Is in itself
The most illustrious reward.

20965

Greatness tells me
How far
I can eventually go forward.
Goodness tells me
How deep
I can sleeplessly grow and glow
In God's own Way.

20966

O my ever-ready speech-mind,
I am renouncing you
Once and for all.
O my ever-soulful silence-heart,
I am welcoming you
To speak and listen
On my behalf.

20967

There is no such thing
As compromise
With impossibility's
Commands and demands.

20968

If you can love
The smiles of your soul
And the cries of your heart,
Then God will definitely carry you
Lovingly and proudly
To your ultimate destination.

20969

Life is to be judged
Not by conversation.
Life is to be judged
By soulful action.

20970

You can say
What you want to say.
God-realisation in this life
Is an indisputable necessity
For me.

20971

No matter where you apply
Your oneness,
You feel safe
And you feel at home.

20972

Each time I soulfully pray
And self-givingly meditate,
I see my Lord Supreme blossoming
Beautifully and radiantly
Inside my heart.

20973

O my doubtful division-mind,
It is high time for me
To accuse you and prosecute you.

20974

God-realisation
Does not believe in
The mind-explanation.
It believes only in
The heart-satisfaction.
