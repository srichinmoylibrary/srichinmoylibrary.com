9175

By defying doubt,
His mind is flying
In the sky of God's
Satisfaction-Delight.

9176

Behold, his old friends,
  Jealousy and insecurity,
Have left him quite unexpectedly.
Therefore, his delight
Is beyond the flight of his imagination.

9177

When you meditate,
Try to disconnect the thought-current.
  You will see and feel
A torrent of God-Delight
  Descending from Above
Into your aspiration-heart.

9178

I open my heart-door
Only in order to lock
  My Lord's Faith
Inside my heart.

9179

I have given God what I have:
  A self-indulgence-life.
God has given me what He is:
  His Effulgence-Heart.

9180

After a long time,
Today he is having
  A happy reunion
With his self-giving life.

9181

Your mind is enmeshed
In imperfection.
  Therefore
How can it see the perfection
In God's Perfection-Vision?

9182

I truly felt miserable
When I could not save my mind
With my heart's light
From drowning in selfishness-sea.

9183

Familiarity can take away all charm.
  But we must be wise,
For inside familiarity is also housed
Tremendously illumining
  And fulfilling opportunity.

9184

God the Beloved Supreme
Is waiting for you.
Never be afraid
Of entering the heart's
Cheerfulness-territory.

9185

Be careful!
You will be taunted
  By temptation
Even when you are on the verge
  Of God-realisation.

9186

Who says that being a mortal
Means you are insignificant?
Your heart's gratitude-flowers
Are the feasts of Infinity.

9187

Ambition comes not
  From the hungry heart
But from the disproportionate greed
  Of the vital.

9188

  Yesterday
He was at one
     With unwillingness.
  Today
Willingness has conquered
     His aspiration-heart.

9189

Only the unconditional souls
  Can sing with God
When He sings,
  And carry the melody perfectly.
