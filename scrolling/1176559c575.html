3690 Do Not Ignore Your Doubting Mind

Do not ignore your doubting mind.
It fears no defeat.
Something more,
It will reach and destroy
Your satisfaction-heart
Unless you make brave attempts
To nip it in the bud.

3691 Not Like You

I am glad that I am not like you:
The owner of self-preoccupation.
I am glad that I am not like you:
The lover of perfection-procrastination.

3692 A Child's Sweet Hopefulness

A child's sweet hopefulness
And God's immediate fruitfulness
Are always found together
In a beautiful perfection-satisfaction-garden.

3693 A Golden Shower From Above

If you have a young mind
And if you have a vigorous vital,
Then every day you will be blessed
With a golden shower from above.

3694 Our Inner Stillness

Our inner stillness
Helps us enjoy God's Life-Breath.
Our inner stillness
Helps us manifest God's secret Eye
And sacred Heart.

3695 What Can Poor God Do?

What can poor God do
When man makes unreasonable claims?
What can poor man do
When God justifiably demands his gratitude?

3696 The Question Of Questions

Here is the question of questions:
What is of more immediate need,
Earth's receptivity-life
Or
Heaven's Compassion-Flood?

3697 As I Prefer Knowledge-Light

As I prefer knowledge-light
To ignorance-night,
Even so I prefer my heart's insecurity
To my mind's ignorant security.

3698 An Isolated And Deserted Life

An isolated and deserted life
May cause the heart to lose some of its fragrance,
But it cannot produce
An ultimate failure-reality
In God's cosmic Plan.

3699 The Rising Of My Soul-Rainbow

Painful yet beautiful
Is the sinking of my life-boat.
Powerful and blessingful
Is the rising of my soul-rainbow.

3700 Your Heart's Gratitude-Plant

Give what you truly
Never want to give:
Your heart's secret and sacred joy.

Keep what you truly
Never want to keep:
Your heart's tiny, innocent gratitude-plant.
