8445

My mind has worries,
Teeming worries.
My heart has prayers,
Climbing prayers.
My soul has promises,
God-fulfilling promises.

8446

When surrender-plants start growing
In your aspiring heart-field,
God will bless you with a new name:
Perfection.

8447

Do not challenge ignorance-strength
With your mind's very limited capacity.
You can challenge ignorance-strength
Only with your soul's unlimited,
All-conquering will power.

8448

Not your gratitude-hands
But your gratitude-heart
Can and shall
Reach God's Feet
Of Satisfaction-Delight.

8449

He who prays to God
Soulfully
And he who meditates on God
Devotedly —
For him and only for him
His Beloved Supreme
Will always be on duty.

8450

How long will you be playing
Foolishly and unnecessarily
With your ugly desire-toys?

8451

All undivine thoughts
Must fade away
Under the perfection-light
Of the heart's morning sun.

8452

Choose your way
Among teeming ways.
No matter which way you go,
The destination will be the same.

8453

Your unwillingness
Has disfigured you,
Including your inner beauty's
Heart-flower.

8454

Your God-invoked heart-will
Will stand far above
Your mind's volcano-will.

8455

The Immortal
Dances with the hopes,
Frustrations
And sighs
Of mankind.

8456

An unthinking will
Is responsible
For the untold sufferings
Of this world.

8457

The Compassion-Depth
Of God's Heart-Sea
Always remains unfathomable.

8458

His life's lethargy-lease
Has expired.
Therefore, his heart and soul
Are extremely happy
And his vital and mind
Are extremely unhappy.

8459

Alas, in life
Nothing is as transient
As satisfaction-spring.
