Speak, Silence, Speak

December 31st, 1969

Speak, silence, speak.
Teach me how to seek.
Stop, sound, stop!
You are soulless,
You are fruitless,
You are weaker,
You are poorer
Than a tiny drop.

O Piercing Ray

December 31st, 1969

O piercing ray!
Do pierce this body of clay.
O illuming light!
Do illumine this life of night.

Breathless with Adoration

December 31st, 1969

Breathless with adoration,
Sleepless with aspiration,
Thoughtless in meditation
     You are.
Therefore, not far,
From liberation-strength
And realisation-length.

Blissful Solitude

December 31st, 1969

Blissful solitude,
Peaceful plenitude,
Truthful beatitude:
To you three
My soulful gratitude.

Ecstasy of My Psychic Fire

December 31st, 1969

Ecstasy of my psychic fire
No soul can endure.
It is so sweet and dire,
So soulful and pure.

Always to Be

December 31st, 1969

Always to be inside
The heart of life.
Sleeplessly I shall pray.
Some day a cosmic ray
Shall embrace my selfless way.

I Take the Endless Way

December 31st, 1969

I take the endless way,
I play the endless game,
I cry the endless cry,
To see God-Compassion-Flame.

There Is a Star Not Far

December 31st, 1969

There is a star
Not far, not far,
Inside my silence-heart.
Its beauty’s reign
Redeems my pain.
An express train
Of love, its domain.

Cease Not Fighting

December 31st, 1969

Cease not fighting,
The inner fight!
Ignorance-night
Must kiss the dust.
And God-hunger forever
On earth must last.

Life, What Is It?

December 31st, 1969

Life, what is it?
A soulful prayer.
Love, what is it?
A fruitful power.
Death, what is it?
A journey to the silence-clime
In the Heart of vision-time.

My Eyes Behold

December 31st, 1969

My eyes behold God-Beauty’s Eye.
My ears hear man-beauty’s frustration-sigh.

Give Me a Little Time

December 31st, 1969

Give me a little time.
I shall tell you, my friend,
Where our Lord Supreme is.
He is at the very end
Of your desire-train
     And
Before your bondage-chain.

My Heart Shall Die

December 31st, 1969

My heart shall die
In Your Silence long.
My life shall fly
With Your Compassion-Song.
