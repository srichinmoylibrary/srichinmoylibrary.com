9190

God is performing His Task.
He is keeping your heart-door open.
  Are you performing your task?
Are you keeping your mind-door closed?

9191

If you do not believe
  In your inner purity,
How can you be divinely satisfied
  With your outer beauty?

9192

Two eternal rivals:
A doubting mind,
The possession of this world,
And a climbing heart,
An inheritance from the higher worlds.

9193

He who has civilised his savage vital
  Has an added advantage
In reaching the final destination.

9194

The man who depends on his mind
  All the time
Has a life filled with futility.

9195

God definitely wants you
To follow in His Footsteps
And enter into the blossoming satisfaction
  Of His Heart-Garden.

9196

He prepared himself anew each day
For his Supreme Lord's arrival
With his heart's soulful prayer
And his life's peaceful meditation.

9197

True, Truth and God are one,
  But I love God more.
True, God and God-Ecstasy are one,
  But I need God more.

9198

Unless God's Compassion nourishes you
  And strengthens you,
You cannot stand on your own two feet
   In the inner world.

9199

Although he came to God,
Because of his Himalayan pride,
He did not accept anything from God.
He went away empty-handed!

9200

  I wish to surrender,
But how to surrender?
  I wish to be perfect,
But how to be perfect?
I can surrender my existence-life
  To God's Will
And I can become perfect
  In God's Heart
Only when I can sincerely claim
  My Lord Supreme
     As my own, very own.
