22315

I can have no fulfilling relationship
With the Supreme
Unless I am sleeplessly
All for Him.

22316

I do not call it a heart
If it is not
An all-loving and all-serving
Fulfilment-promise to God.

22317

My heart's purest
And sweetest secret is:
I need God, only God,
Only God's Compassion-Eye
And only God's Forgiveness-Feet.

22318

When I live peacefully
In my heart-cottage,
I see no stranger —
No, not even one.

22319

Divinity's love blossoms
Only in an expected place:
A gratitude-heart.

22320

When I suspect the world,
I actually hurt the trust
Of this blameless world.

22321

God's Heart is waiting.
God's Heart is calling.
Are you blind?
Are you deaf?

22322

You can fly
At faster than the fastest speed
If you can learn the art
Of silencing your mind.

22323

Today I am planting
A God-loving thought.
Tomorrow I shall have
A God-fulfilling harvest.

22324

May my God-searching mind,
My God-loving heart
And my God-serving life
Radiate to ever-transcending horizons.

22325

I have caught God's Feet
With my tears.
God has caught my heart
With His Cheers.

22326

My Lord,
I wish to please You.
Just tell me how,
Oh, only how!

22327

Only a divine thought
Can come down
From a supernal height
Without being obstructed and marred
By mundane absurdities.

22328

God does everything unnoticed.
Why?
Because His actual appearance
Might frighten us to death.

22329

The breath of unconditional surrender
Is proudly treasured
By both God the Love
And God the Power.
