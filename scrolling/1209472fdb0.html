23660

My heart's aspiration
And my life's dedication
Are perfect for each other.

23661

If you can graduate
From your God-obedience-school,
Then there will be nothing unattainable
For you.

23662

An unconditional service-life
Is at once gratifying
And edifying.

23663

O my mind,
Can you not see that my heart
Is always ready to purify you
With its sunshine-thoughts?

23664

To my extreme delight
My heart's Heaven-climbing tears
Have reached God's Satisfaction-Heart.

23665

Both our God-aspiration-record
And our God-dedication-record
Should remain unblemished forever.

23666

Alas, we mortals
Walk along so many roads;
But somehow we fail to walk along
The gratitude-path.

23667

An unconditional surrender
Is indeed
The achievement-pinnacle.

23668

Spirituality is not a part-time
But a full-time relationship
With divinity.

23669

I know
I have made considerable progress
In my spiritual life,
All due to my heart's
Indispensable assistance.

23670

Behold,
Because of God's
Unconditional Compassion-Descent,
My life's imperfection-day
Has drawn to an end.

23671

Although humanity has kept
Very few of its promises so far,
God still has perfect faith
In humanity.

23672

Make your mind
At every moment
Sweeter,
And make your heart
At every moment
Purer.

23673

Although I am totally lost within them,
My Lord Supreme tells me
That He has practically no interest
In my mind's archives.

23674

When I live in my uninspired mind,
I do not even want to know
Who God is and where God is.
