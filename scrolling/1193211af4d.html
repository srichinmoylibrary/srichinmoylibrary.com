7575

Listen to God.
Otherwise, ignorance-tiger
Will devour you.

7576

Look what his disobedience-life
Has done to him!
His decline-glory
Has already begun.

7577

He is celebrating
His heart's safe return
From ignorance-kingdom.

7578

What I need is
An indivisible heart
And not
A giant mind.

7579

Frustration-sigh
Is the dead end
Of the desire-life.

7580

If you do not have
A purity-heart,
Your soul is bound
To suffocate.

7581

He who has told you
That you cannot untie
Your attachment-strings
Is a rank fool.

7582

This moment
He hesitates to speak
About God.

Next moment
He fabricates stories
About God.

7583

You must value
Your devotion-heart
And your surrender-life
If you want to see
Your conscious progress.

7584

Earth treasures
His peace-seeking mind.
Heaven treasures
His peace-supplying heart.

7585

The mind-terrorists
Must be overthrown
Before the heart-specialists
And life-perfectionists
Begin their respective jobs.

7586

His mind is full
Of nothing but worry-waste.
Therefore, avoid him consciously,
Even unconsciously!

7587

He does not realise
That every day
His mind covers him
With nothingness-dust!

7588

To you
Everything is a burden,
Even the beauty of inspiration
And the divinity of aspiration.

7589

Perfection
Is undoubtedly possible
In this lifetime.
