17760

We are such fools
That we are seeking
Permanence-joy
In a fleeting world.

17761

How can I make spiritual progress
Without
The aspiration from within
And the inspiration from without?

17762

Alas, the desire-mind
Will never have the capacity
To transform its own nature.

17763

My Lord, do give me the capacity
To pray to You every day
For my aspiration-heart's
Lightning-oneness-speed.

17764

My Lord, do not fulfil
My mind's freedom-desires,
But do fulfil
My heart's surrender-desires.

17765

A special Grace is needed
From Above
For the mind to escape from
Anxiety-worry-prison.

17766

Aspiration
Is sweetness.
Dedication
Is fulness.
Surrender
Is sweetness-fulness-fragrance.

17767

What I need is not
A God-questioning mind
But a God-crying heart.

17768

Each time we pray and meditate
God helps us expand
Our heart's oneness-realm.

17769

How can we ever expect to find
Protection-security
Inside our division-barrier-mind?

17770

What is a human mind
If not a conscious and constant
Comfort-search-attempt?

17771

When I soulfully pray,
I fly with the flames
Of my wisdom-radiance-sun.

17772

Every morning
I cry for God's Smile
To clear away
My yesterday's problem-clouds.

17773

I am afraid of many things,
But I am mostly afraid
Of my mind's
Binding and blinding thoughts.

17774

Each good thought of mine
I see
As a star of my heart
In my soul's Infinity-sky.
