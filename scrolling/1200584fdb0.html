14860

When the mind gives purity-sermons,
I laugh only to cry
And I cry only to laugh.

14861

Do not worry if you have lost
Your passport to paradise.
God will bless you with a new one.
Just offer Him one sincere gratitude-tear
From your heart.

14862

Just imagine, just imagine
That you are not responsible
For your confusion-mind!
You are responsible
Only for your illumination-heart.

14863

Each step
Along my God-surrender-road
Is my life's tremendous progress.

14864

I shall enjoy God's Command
Infinitely more
Than His standing Ovation.

14865

God the Teacher does not suspend
The disobedient student.
He just spends His precious Time
Only on
The willingness-obedience-student.

14866

If you do not soulfully persist
In your aspiration-dedication-life,
At any moment you can enter into
Your mind's abysmal abyss.

14867

God's Compassion-Song
And my heart-thrill
Live together permanently
And satisfactorily.

14868

God does not care if you win
Your Heavenward race.
But He does mind if you do not
Participate in the race.

14869

The meeting place of my heart's tears
And my soul's smiles
Invites me to celebrate
Their oneness-victory.

14870

My soul does not care for its supremacy.
It cares only for its intimacy
With my purity-heart
And my dedication-life.

14871

My self-offering
Is the after-effect
Of my Lord's unconditional Smile.

14872

God wants me to lead
A hallowed life.
He also wants me to have
A secret and illumining place
Where He and I can play
Hide-and-seek.

14873

Beyond the sound-life
Is the breath of silence,
And beyond the breath of silence
Is God's Victory-Trumpet.

14874

God wants us to aspire.
God wants us to serve.
Only one thing He does not want:
To remain even for a second
Unclaimed by us.
