12201

A true truth-seeker's life
Every day ascends
With a rising rainbow
Of supreme delight.

12202

May my mind's thought-world
Peacefully rest
In my aspiring heart's
Silence-nest.

12203

You are your heart's
Breathless willingness.
Therefore, God will grant your life
Abundant fruitfulness.

12204

My mind is wrapped
In the ever-growing hope
That I will go beyond the domain
Of insecurity-pain.

12205

May my heart never stop dreaming
Every night
My life-transformation-dream.

12206

Because of my heart's
Humility-light,
I never come near my mind's
Ego-tree.

12207

There can be no hurtful conflicts
Between an aspiration-heart
And a dedication-life.

12208

If you do not pray
For your heart's oneness-courage,
Then yours will be a life
Of ceaselessly accumulated insecurity.

12209

O priceless moments
Of unconditional surrender-delight,
Do you really exist?
Or is your existence-light
The product
Of man's hallucination-mind?

12210

From my mind's obedience
My heart receives cheerfulness.
From my heart's cheerfulness
My Lord receives supreme Satisfaction.

12211

Surprisingly vast
Is my mind's sound-sea.
Astonishingly pure
Is my heart's silence-sun.

12212

Although lethargy carries iron weights,
It wants to roam
In the expectation-fulfilment-garden.

12213

You can neither fly high
Nor dive deep
With your aspiration-hobby.
You can never set your heart-sails
For your life's sunrise
With your aspiration-hobby.

12214

I surprisingly
Found God
Right where I
Deliberately left Him.
