26101

Every day I have made it a point
To walk lovingly and faithfully
Along the path of gratitude.

26102

God lovingly and proudly
Tells my surrender-heart
To walk freely with Him.

26103

The first duty of a God-seeker
Every day
Is to use his smiles
To look at God the creation.

26104

When I am in my mind,
I become a contradiction-mind.
When I am in my vital,
I become a destruction-vital.

26105

No, not our body, vital, mind or heart —
Not even our soul —
But God Himself is the last authority
On spirituality.

26106

If you do not take your mind away
From the travail of daily life,
How can you ever receive
God's choicest Blessings?

26107

The heart knows
The direct road to God.
The mind knows
Only a roundabout route to God.

26108

Alas, I do not know
How my mind has become
A never-ending regret-sigh!

26109

The strength of self-effacement
Can never be measured
Adequately.

26110

Each thought is meant
To become a prayer
For God to smile at us.

26111

The mind's negativity
Quite often runs
From danger to destruction.

26112

God Himself wants to take
An immortal picture
Of my surrender-life
And gratitude-heart.

26113

God does give
Our heart the capacity
Every day
To lift us up to new heights.

26114

God and God's Silence
Are two inseparable and immortal
Oneness-Friends.
