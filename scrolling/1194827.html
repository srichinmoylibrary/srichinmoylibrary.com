9101

I am so happy
Now that I am beginning
  To give my all
To my Beloved Supreme.

9102

The darkness within you
Will die
Only when the devotedness within you
Invokes illumination from Above.

9103

Each soul is special
Because each soul is made
Of God's Vision-Reality.

9104

If you are a God-lover,
That means God has already baptised you
  In His Satisfaction-Sea.

9105

A selfless man
Sees in everything
His perfection's God-song.

9106

Impurity caught his mind
  Off guard,
But the purity of his heart
Has come to his mind's rescue.

9107

My Lord Supreme,
Where are Your chosen instruments?
"Alas, My chosen instruments
Are enjoying ignorance-sleep
Instead of fulfilling
Their promised tasks."

9108

If you do not preserve
Your heart's purity-flower,
Nobody, not even your dear ones,
  Can save you
In your life of aspiration.

9109

Slowly and steadily
You have to silence your mind
So that the peace-dove
Can nest in it.

9110

My heart's gratitude-tears
Are the only jewels
That my Lord Supreme
Most powerfully appreciates.

9111

Since you are
  Your closed mind,
You will have
  Open enemies.

9112

He is happy
Because every day he tries
  And actually does
A little more than he thinks he can.

9113

  Surrender
Is the cornerstone
  Of man's
God-manifesting palace.

9114

Aspiration is a vow you took.
Now you should take a dedication-vow.
Then only will God be able
  To plow your heart-field
     Most satisfactorily.
