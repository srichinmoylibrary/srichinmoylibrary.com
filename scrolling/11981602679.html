12415

If you are not spontaneously
Kind to yourself,
Then all your God-satisfaction-attempts
Will badly fail.

12416

A self-giving purity-heart
Is the satisfaction-delight
Of the Lord Supreme.

12417

Two monumental tasks:
To have a sleepless
Aspiration-heart-cry
And an unconditional
Service-soul-smile.

12418

My gratitude-heart
Is the perfect yardstick
To measure
My aspiration-height.

12419

If you do not sleeplessly
And breathlessly cry,
How will you see your heart's
Blue aspiration-bird
In full flight?

12420

Alas, how can I be perfect,
How can I be happy,
Since there is so much
Aspiration-neglect in my life?

12421

May each moment
Of my life
Be a heart-awakening song!

12422

Alas, in vain
I have been trying hard
To recover from
Frustration-intoxication.

12423

An ungrateful heart
And an impure mind
Are always in collusion
To dethrone God.

12424

A doubting mind
Thrives on misquoting
God the Compassion.

12425

Sleepless dynamism
Is God's greatest Boon
To the human body.

12426

A self-giving heart
Knows how to make friends
With world-forgiveness.

12427

Every day a secret
And sacred conversation
Takes place between Heaven's smile
And earth's cry.

12428

To arrive
At the wisdom-light-shore
Is almost an impossible task
For the weak human mind.

12429

If you want to see
Your heart's aspiration-beauty,
Then you must first set off
Your inspiration-fireworks.
