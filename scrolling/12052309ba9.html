19445

It is so easy
Either to disgrace my life
With desire-night
Or to grace my life
With aspiration-sun.

19446

God tells me
That His faith in me
Is infinitely stronger
Than my self-doubt.

19447

Walk no more
On yesterday's dream-road,
But walk
On today's reality-road.

19448

Every day I pray to God
To bless my heart
With His Eternity's Hope.

19449

A godless mind
Is where
A heartless life
Abides.

19450

I am so fortunate
That God and God's Patience
Have followed me
So far and so long.

19451

God tolerates all my weaknesses
Except one:
My unwillingness.

19452

Years and years ago
I had the inner strength
To make my Lord Supreme happy.
Alas, I am now following the footpath
That my confusion-mind has chosen.

19453

God eagerly encourages me
To ask Him
Many life-illumining questions
Every day.

19454

If you are a man of a ready heart,
Then all the dreams of your soul
Will be fulfilled
Here on earth.

19455

Alas, I do not know why
My doubting mind,
My strangling vital
And my sleeping body
Are not convicted
By God the Justice-Light.

19456

A thought,
No matter how small it is,
Can easily be
My heart's poise-invader.

19457

God is
God's Fondness-Promise
Only when I am
My oneness-manifestation.

19458

Before you make a home
For God,
God wants you to share
Your compassion
With suffering humanity.

19459

As Heaven
Has most warmly recommended
Your soul to earth,
Even so earth should recommend
Your heart to Heaven.
