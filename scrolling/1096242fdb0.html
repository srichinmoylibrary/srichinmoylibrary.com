27 November 2004

Sanya, China

57 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I have only one desire:
I wish to place my head at Your Feet,
And my head will remain at Your Feet
Forever and forever.

58 Ultimately ...

Ultimately,
Every human being has to surrender
To God’s Will,
Willingly or unwillingly.

59 My Lord Says To Me ...

My Lord says to me,
“My child,
You always make Me laugh.
Can you not make Me smile
Once in a while?”

60 My Lord Says To Me ...

My Lord says to me,
“My child,
Now that you have made
Your unconditional surrender
Cheerful, complete and perfect,
Your life will be the uppermost
On My list of priorities.”

61 If I Do Not Have ...

If I do not have
Inner peace,
I shall never be able
To make my Lord Supreme
Proud of me.

62 Every Second ...

Every second
Is a golden opportunity
To make my Lord
The sole Possessor
Of my inner life
And my outer life.

63 How Can I Be A Loser ...

How can I be a loser
On the battlefield of my life
If I have already claimed
My Lord Supreme
As my own, very own?

64 As My Lord’s Compassion-Eye ...

As my Lord’s Compassion-Eye
Thrills me,
Even so, my gratitude-heart
Thrills my Lord.

28 November 2004 ...

28 November 2004

Sanya, China

65 During The Day ...

During the day
I keep myself
Before my Lord’s Eye.

66 During The Night ...

During the night
I keep myself
At my Lord’s Feet.

67 In My Outer Life ...

In my outer life
God is
His Compassion-Eye.

68 In My Inner Life ...

In my inner life
God is
His Sweetness-Fondness-Heart.

69 In My Higher Life ...

In my higher life
God is
His bright, extremely bright
Satisfaction-Smile.
