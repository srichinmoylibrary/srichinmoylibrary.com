6 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God's Compassion-Eye
Has introduced me
To God's Pinnacle-Height.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 March 2000, 12:49 a.m. Before doing sixty-two one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (29 right, 33 left)

6 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My God-obedience is the shortest
And the safest road
To my God-Destination.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 March 2000, 12:56 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

7 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must give a heart-smile to have
A true life-friend.
A true life-friend is, indeed,
A rare achievement.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 March 2000, 8:44 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

7 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My old mind says
That there is nothing new
And there will not be anything new.
My new heart says
That everything is not only new,
But inspiring, encouraging, energising,
Illumining and fulfilling.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 March 2000, 8:59 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously six times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

7 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every morning do bless me
With indomitable waves
Of enthusiasm-energy.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 March 2000, 9:15 a.m. Before doing ninety-one one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (44 right, 47 left)

9 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
An unconditional self-giver
Is the winner
Of God's Peace Award.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 March 2000, 8:50 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

9 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My sleepless and breathless
God-gratitude-heart
Is the shortest and safest stairway
To arrive at
God's Transcendental Height.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 March 2000, 9:02 a.m. Before lilting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously six times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

9 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall never allow
My aspiration-heart, my dedication-life
And my God-manifestation-accounts
To ever be empty.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 March 2000, 9:20 a.m. Before doing sixty-seven one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (31 right, 36 left)

11 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind says to me,
"Stop! Stop! Danger ahead."
My heart says to me,
"Go on! Go on! Look,
Your Destination is beckoning you."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 March 2000, 4:37 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously seven times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

11 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I know how to maximise and amplify
My prayers.
God knows how to glorify
My prayers
And intensify His Grace.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 March 2000, 4:58 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

11 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My prayers lengthen My God-service-life.
My meditations strengthen
My God-oneness-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 March 2000, 5:04 a.m. Before doing fifty-nine one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (29 right, 30 left)

12 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind knows only
How to assail the world constantly.
My heart knows not only
How to sail my life-boat
But God's Life-Boat, too.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 March 2000, 9:00 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

12 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I love God the man to be happy.
I serve man the God to be perfect.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 March 2000, 9:14 a.m. Before doing fifty-seven one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (28 right, 29 left)

12 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I look at my Lord's Eye,
He gives me a most beautiful flower
From His Heart-Garden.
When I smile at His Heart,
He gives me His All.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 March 2000, 9:31 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

16 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please help me fly far beyond
The skies of fears, worries
And anxieties.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 March 2000, 8:39 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.
