14145

Insecurity and impurity
Come into our minds and hearts
Disguised as lifelong friends.

14146

If we can see
Through the eye of surrender
And feel
Through the heart of gratitude,
Then we can be perfect
Ignorance-fighters.

14147

The eye of the heart
Can see the God
Of sleepless earth-perfection-dreams.

14148

All problems can be surmounted
If we can cherish
The beauty and fragrance
Of a world-oneness-heart.

14149

God asks us to believe in
At least one thing:
That every night He comes to see us
If we are sleeping and dreaming divinely.

14150

Enjoy the infancy
Of your heart first.
Then only will you be able
To enjoy the intimacy
Of God's Oneness-Heart-Home.

14151

If you cannot dare
To follow,
God will not hire you
To lead.

14152

There is no such thing
As a little effort.
In God's Eye, everything that we do
Is meaningful and purposeful.
Therefore,
Each and every effort-incident in life
Is supremely significant.

14153

Today my mind,
The trouble-maker,
Has unconditionally bowed
To my heart,
The trouble-shooter.

14154

My soul has
Only one piece of advice
For my mind:
Become humility's fragrance-breath.

14155

The world is definitely
Progressing
Since world-destruction-dreams
Are regressing.

14156

Your surrender-life
And gratitude-heart
Will always help you
To climb up the aspiration-mountain.

14157

Self-praise is almost as bad
As surrender
To ignorance-temptation-night.

14158

I do not need
A God-expert-mind.
What I need
Is a God-surrender-heart.

14159

Because of your surrender-life
And your receptivity-heart,
God is eagerly waiting for you
To claim Him as your very own.
