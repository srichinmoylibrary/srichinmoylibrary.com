Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Solo and Yogyakarta, Indonesia in December 2003.

13 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

1 Today I Am Determined ...

Today I am determined
To do everything
In God’s own Way.

2 To Have Purity ...

To have purity,
The seeker has to feel
That he is a child
Of the Eternal Now.

3 If You Are Searching For God ...

If you are searching for God
With a genuine thirst and hunger,
Then God will without fail
Appear before you.

4 My Life Is In Between ...

My life is in between
God’s Compassion-Arms
And His Forgiveness-Heart.

5 God’s Will Does Not Operate ...

God’s Will does not operate
In a doubting mind.
God’s Will operates only
In a self-giving life.

6 It Is Far Beyond The Capacity ...

It is far beyond the capacity
Of the mind
To think that God
Is all Forgiveness.

7 The Heart Feels ...

The heart feels
That God is sleepless
And unconditional Forgiveness.

8 Shut Up, My Mind! ...

Shut up, my mind!
My heart and I are ready
To fly
On our aspiration-wings.

9 Alas ...

Alas,
When God gives talks
On surrender,
The number of the seekers present
Is disappointing.

10 A Gratitude-Heart ...

A gratitude-heart
Is a very rare reality.

11 God-Closeness Depends Upon ...

God-closeness depends upon
The brightness and sincerity
Of our smile.

12 My Gratitude-Heart ...

My gratitude-heart
And my surrender-life
Are both needed
To fulfill God’s Dream on earth.

13 My Soul Allows My Heart ...

My soul allows my heart
To talk to God
Only on one condition:
My heart has to speak to God
Only about God.
