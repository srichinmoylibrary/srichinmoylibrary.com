8701

The two rainbow-wings
Of his life's dedication
Are for the world to use.

8702

In the silence of your heart
Your life's perfection
Cannot remain a far cry.

8703

O my mind,
When are you going to be ashamed
Of your deathless, savage greed?

8704

His purity-mind
Is singing and dancing
On the altar of his heart's stars.

8705

The creation is the work,
The masterpiece,
Of only one Master-Artist Supreme.

8706

He is always seen
At one place:
In his Eternity's
Dream-filled boat.

8707

Turn the bold desires
Of your vital
Into the timid desires
Of your mind,
And then destroy them!

8708

Aspire, aspire!
Otherwise,
The whole world's emptiness-night
Will capture your entire being.

8709

Man's audacious mind
And man's treacherous mind
Are not far away
From man's God-intoxicated,
Self-giving heart.

8710

You must renounce
Servitude to
The ignorance-tiger in you.

8711

Go forward
With your volcanic will power!
Otherwise,
Yours will be a life
Of huge destruction.

8712

What is your heart's choice:
An excitement-man
Or an enlightenment-soul?

8713

Along with his soul,
His body, vital, mind and heart
Are journeying
Through unknowable Infinity.

8714

An illumined life
Is a God-cherished treasure
On earth.
