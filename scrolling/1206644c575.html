20890

Since your belief in God is lukewarm,
How can you ever become
A perfect stranger
To your own life of misery?

20891

Alas,
Every day
God has to face
The naked sword
Of my doubting mind.

20892

O my mind and vital,
There is only one real path,
And that path is
The path of the heart.
Walk along that path
If full satisfaction is something
That you want and need.

20893

Pray and meditate self-givingly.
Your life-possibility-blossoms
Will turn into
Inevitability's beauty-fragrance.

20894

You do not find what you seek.
Why?
Precisely because
You do not try to silence
Your life's monkey-mind-activities.

20895

Finally I have dethroned
My deception-mind-king
And enthroned
My illumination-heart-emperor.

20896

What is deception?
It is something that goes
Round and round
In the unaspiring human mind.

20897

Remain always inside
Your heart-blossoming hopes
And not inside
Your mind-multiplying jungle.

20898

Vast
Is love-field.
Fertile
Is devotion-field.
Invincible
Is surrender-field.

20899

Each human heart
Is the most desirable fountain
To quench hope-thirst.

20900

If you can put your earthly life
Back on the Heavenly aspiration-track,
Your ignorance-intoxication
Will sooner than at once
Come to an end.
