14 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, quite often our mind-inventions
Are founded upon
Our evil intentions.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 January 2000, 8:11 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

14 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every morning You command me
To walk with You for an hour
With my heart’s beauty
And my soul’s fragrance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 January 2000, 8:26 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before doing fifty-seven one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (28 right, 29 left)

14 January 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart be as beautiful,
Charming, illumining
And perfect
As a smile born in Heaven.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 January 2000, 8:39 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously twice. (Total: 800 lbs.)

14 January 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I cried for You,
But nothing happened.
I smiled at You,
But nothing happened.
When I cried with You,
When I smiled with You,
Everything happened —
Inspiring, aspiring, illumining
And fulfilling.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 January 2000, 8:53 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before using bench press machine up to 110 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

15 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are calling me.
Alas, my ears do not want to listen
To You.
They do not want me to hear
Your Blessing-Message.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 January 2000, 8:53 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

15 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
How fortunate I am!
Every morning You cradle my heart
With a Smile of Your Heart’s Beauty.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 January 2000, 9:05 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before doing fifty-three one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (26 right, 27 left)

18 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Spirituality blind:
A computerised mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 January 2000, 6:31 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

18 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A computerised mind
Alas, nowhere can find
Supreme Lord’s Beauty-Eye –-
A total failure-sigh.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 January 2000, 7:02 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before doing fifty-two one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (25 right, 27 left)

18 January 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A computerised mind
Remains farthest behind.
It values not God-quest
And God hides His Heart-Nest.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 January 2000, 7:18 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 800 lbs.)

18 January 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A computerised mind
Can never be as kind
As a God-surrender-soul.
God-Joy his only goal.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 January 2000, 9:19 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before using bench press machine up to 90 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

20 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God-slaves are more reliable,
More dependable, more sociable,
More serviceable and more capable
Than anybody else.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 January 2000, 6:14 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

20 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The slave says:
“Master, throughout Eternity,
I shall be with you, in you, for you.”
The Master says:
“My slave, you are my heart’s
Most treasured wealth here on earth,
There in Heaven.”
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 January 2000, 7:21 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before using bench press machine up to 90 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

20 January 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today’s faithful slave
Is
Tomorrow’s blissful Master.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 January 2000, 6:48 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before doing 53 one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (26 right, 27 left)

20 January 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A self-giving slave
Eventually becomes
A God-flowering Master.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 January 2000, 6:57 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times (Total: 800 lbs.)

21 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I deeply appreciate, admire and adore
And I simply love
Your firm grip on my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 January 2000, 7:08 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before using seated double-arm machine.
