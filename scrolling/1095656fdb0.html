54 What Is God Doing? ...

What is God doing?
God is merrily dancing
Inside my singing.

55 God’s Will ...

God’s Will
Is not my master.
God’s Will
Is my Eternity’s partner.

56 Our Prayers ...

Our prayers
Inch by inch
Take us to God’s Feet.

57 Our Meditations ...

Our meditations
Mile by mile
Take us to God’s Heart.

58 As We Want To Be Loved ...

As we want to be loved
By God,
God also wants to be loved
By us —
Even more so.

59 Hard To Believe ...

Hard to believe,
But it is true:
God loves not only
My aspiration-heart,
But also
My recreation-life.

60 Mother Earth ...

Mother Earth
Dearly loves
Her running children.

61 Father Heaven ...

Father Heaven
Proudly loves
His meditating children.

62 Mother Earth Expects ...

Mother Earth expects
Smile-lustre
From her children.

63 Father Heaven Expects ...

Father Heaven expects
Peace-hunger
From His children.

64 Be Neither A Clever Seeker ...

Be neither a clever seeker
Nor a stupid seeker.
Publicly you smile;
Privately you sigh.

65 All Sincere Seekers ...

All sincere seekers
Agree on one thing:
We need God the Love
More than God the Power.

12 December 2003 ...

12 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

66 My Singing Heart ...

My singing heart
Is God’s proud Eye.

67 There Was A Time ...

There was a time
When I was a lover
Of God’s Feet,
But now I am a devourer
Of God’s ambrosial Feet-dust.
