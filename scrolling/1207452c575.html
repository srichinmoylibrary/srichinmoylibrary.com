21690

O God-lover,
If you are genuine,
There can be no justifiable way
For you to find fault with God.

21691

If we cherish the dry intellect
Of our mind,
We shall never be able
To give joy or to receive joy
From the world.

21692

If you have the courage
To die for God's Cause gallantly,
Then how is it
That you do not have the courage
To live for God on earth
Unconditionally?

21693

It is the weakness of the mind
That neglects God,
Not the strength of the mind.
The strength of the mind
Always invites God
To fulfil Him in His own Way.

21694

In the outer world, the mind says
That it wants perfection.
But in the inner world, the mind craves
Only satisfaction.

21695

Each wrong deed
Will definitely be accompanied
By fear.

21696

Man is worse than an animal
When he greedily eats
At jealousy-table.

21697

To please God in His own Way
Is to enjoy oneself
Most lovingly and most effectively.

21698

A sleeplessly self-giving heart
Has already travelled
Halfway to Heaven.

21699

If I do not surrender
My mind's greatness
To my heart's goodness,
How can I ever have
God's Fulness in my life?

21700

A day will come
When this world of ours
Will be liberated from misunderstandings,
Limitations and weaknesses.
It will be a happy, illumining
And fulfilling oneness-family.
