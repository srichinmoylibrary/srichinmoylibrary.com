27 Ceaselessly I Ring ...

Ceaselessly I ring
Most devotedly
My God-gratitude-heart-bell.

28 Each Life Has ...

Each life has
Countless ascending steps
Before it arrives
In Heaven.

29 Be Brave! ...

Be brave!
Be not stunned
By the present stark reality.

30 If I Really Wish ...

If I really wish
To be a God-devotion-singer,
Then God will give me
The sweetest range of voice.

2 January 2006 ...

2 January 2006

Kuantan, Malaysia

31 God Enters Into ...

God enters into
The mind-jungle
With His Light, Peace and Bliss.
The mind hides.
After a while the mind comes
And touches God’s Feet.
God immediately transforms
The mind-jungle
Into the Garden of Eden.

32 Desire Sees The Moon ...

Desire sees the moon
But wants to remain
In the mud and clay
Of the earth.

33 The Doubting Mind ...

The doubting mind
Has no entry form
To enter into
God’s Heart-Palace.

34 Spiritual Progress ...

Spiritual progress
Ends
The moment the desire-life
Succeeds.

35 Age Has Nothing To Do ...

Age has nothing to do
With a sage life.

36 God Is Starving And Starving ...

God is starving and starving
For an iota
Of my inner progress.

37 Prayer ...

Prayer
Protects us
From ignorance-night.

38 Meditation ...

Meditation
Is
Ignorance-night-illumination.

39 Do Not Waste Time ...

Do not waste time
Even for a moment.
God is eagerly waiting
For you.

40 No Backward Step ...

No backward step
With doubt,
But forward,
Ever forward steps
With faith.
