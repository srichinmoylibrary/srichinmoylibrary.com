26275

Alas, I came to my Lord Supreme
Too late!
Alas, I came to my Lord Supreme
With too little!

26276

The ambition-driven mind
Will never succeed in the outer life
And will never proceed in the inner life.

26277

Be careful, be cautious,
O my mind.
You can fall asleep at any moment
At the wheel of your life's progress.

26278

Every day, every hour
My Lord is desperately trying
To liberate me
From my mind's self-created hell.

26279

You will pass the examination
Of your life
When your very breath
Is a God-Satisfaction-quest.

26280

Talented God-deceivers
Are everywhere.
Self-giving, talented God-lovers
Are hardly available.

26281

There is no such thing
As an abbreviated spiritual course
In God's entire creation.

26282

Finally I have reduced my singing
Of my life's and my heart's
All-insecurity-songs.

26283

My heart was born
To win God's Compassion-Eye
And God's Forgiveness-Heart.

26284

I need a simple heart
That gives constant joy
To others.

26285

O seeker, take care of yourself.
Be not distressed by others' disobedience.
Your own progress-life
Is your only responsibility.

26286

If God-belief
Is nothing but your mind-philosophy,
Then God-discovery
Will never be within your reach.

26287

My Lord has blessed me
With a self-giving purity-heart
That will never deny Him.

26288

Every day I see
A new Compassion-Eye of God
Blessing His creation
With infinite Ecstasy.

26289

I keep no account
Of my
Frustration-lamentation-vital.
