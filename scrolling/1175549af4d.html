2675 The Tears Of My Heart

The tears of my heart
Have decreased my journey's length
And increased my God-hunger
For God in God Himself.

2676 Rich And Poor

He is at once rich and poor.
His heart is soulfully rich
Before God's Vision-Eye.
His mind is powerfully poor
Before God's Reality-Day.

2677 Truth Never Descends

Truth never descends.
Truth never compromises.
Truth is satisfied only when
It sees everything and everyone
At its own Himalayan height.

2678 Man's Honesty-Boat

Man's honesty-boat,
Earth's appreciation-river,
God's Satisfaction-Shore:
These three live for one another.

2679 A Flowing Dream

Cheerfulness
Is a flowing dream.
Willingness
Is a glowing reality.
Fruitfulness
Is a fulfilling peace.

2680 O My Aspiration-Child

O my aspiration-child,
I know, I know
You will always succeed
In winning the Perfection-Eye
And Satisfaction-Heart
Of our Beloved Supreme.

2681 The World Loves To Examine

The world loves to examine me.
I love to examine
My tomorrow's goal.
God loves to examine
His own Perfection-Seed inside me.

2682 Ego-Life

The morning and the evening
Are two totally different realities.
But the morning of ego-life
And the evening of ego-life
Always and always
Remain the same,
Unchangeable in oneness-bond.

2683 Drown Your Glory

Drown your own glory
If you want a life of peace.
Frown at your own victory
If you want a heart of delight.

2684 Revive Not Old Desires!

Revive not your old desires!
If you revive your old desires,
In a moment
Your inner life and outer life
Will become a perfect failure
In annihilation's waste.

2685 Three Everlasting Penalties

Three everlasting penalties
In my life:
God will not knock
At my heart's door.
I do not know
Who I truly am.
My mind has sold me
To ignorance-night.

2686 God-Hearers

God-hearers are
At every place.
God-doers are
At a few places.
God-lovers are
Nowhere to be found.

2687 You Have Accomplished Something

Once you know that enthusiasm
Is the immediate neighbour of lethargy,
Then you have really accomplished something
Great and good
In your life of alertness
And your heart of willingness.

2688 O My Beautiful Hope-Soul

Hope, O my beautiful hope-soul,
You are always celestial.
Reality, O my hard reality-body,
You are always terrestrial.

2689 Gratitude Is Something In Our Heart

Gratitude is something in our heart
That helps us
Transcend our experience of earth
And
Intensify our realisation of God.
