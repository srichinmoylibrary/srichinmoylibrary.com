3 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart goes to Your Heart
With prayer-cries.
Your Heart comes to my heart
With Blessing-Smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 September 1999, 8:13 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm thirty-five times. (15 right, 20 left)

3 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my heart is on its knees
Before You,
You tell my heart,
"My child,
I am all for you,
I am all for you."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 September 1999, 8:21 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred and fifteen times. (55 right, 60 left)

3 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am indeed
Your blessingful gift
To Mother Earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 September 1999, 8:51 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

4 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
No desire-dream can forever last.
There comes a time
When it has to burst,
Like a bubble.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 September 1999, 7:50 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 90 lbs.

4 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
No expectation, no expectation,
No expectation!
Alas, the expectation-boat
Quite often capsizes.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 September 1999, 8:06 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 540 lbs.)

4 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The pure prayer-heart of a seeker
Is bound to succeed.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 September 1999, 8:16 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm forty-four times. (29 right, 15 left)

4 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
At long last I have come to realise
That ignorance-night
Is terribly and helplessly afraid
Of my prayer-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 September 1999, 8:25 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred and seven times. (55 right, 52 left)

4 September 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My true spirituality
Is the love of Your Breath
In every heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 September 1999, 8:37 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

4 September 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
It is indeed an impossible task
To recognise You
In all Your infinite disguises.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 September 1999

5 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please, please bless me
With a perfection-searching mind,
Perfection-aspiring mind,
Perfection-loving mind
And perfection-living mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 September 1999, 7:48 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 540 lbs.)

5 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my mind at every moment
Experience peace-light
And peace-delight,
And not anxiety-worry-night.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 September 1999, 7:57 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm thirty-three times. (16 right, 17 left)

5 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
That there is only one way
To achieve inner freedom,
And that is through
Constant God-obedience.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 September 1999, 9:03 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (65 and 75 reps)

6 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do bless me
With a cheerfully, enthusiastically
And eagerly correctable
Heart, mind, vital and body.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 September 1999, 8:02 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 540 lbs.)

6 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do liberate my aspiring heart
And my dedicated life
From this thought-encumbered mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 September 1999, 8:12 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm twenty-four times. (13 right, 11 left)

6 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I know, I know,
Peace-achievement
Is not a one-day task.
It is a most serious
Lifelong endeavour.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 September 1999, 8:19 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred and twenty times. (61 right, 59 left)
