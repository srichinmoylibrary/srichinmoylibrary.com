21345

When I decided to love God
Sleeplessly and breathlessly,
God immediately heard the news
And was completely overjoyed.

21346

My Lord,
My world-complacency days
Are now all dead.
My world-loving days
Have just begun.

21347

Each time I tell God
That I love Him only,
He gives me two special wings
To fly in His Infinity's Heart-Sky.

21348

I am just a tiny drop
In the ocean of God's
Hope-manifestation.

21349

For the past few years
My aspiration-heart
Has been crying and crying
Only to unlearn the things
My desire-mind has taught me.

21350

If you are undaunted
By earthly defeats,
Your Heavenly victories
Will before long dawn.

21351

O seeker,
Be careful at every moment!
If not, at any moment
Desire-lightning
Can easily strike you down.

21352

Your aspiration-heart
And God's Compassion-Eye
Will definitely help your heart-boat
Sail the fastest
Toward the Golden Shore.

21353

Alas, I am totally lost!
Fear
Is haunting my heart
And doubt
Is daunting my mind.

21354

There was a time
When I prayed to God
For my mind's grandiosity,
But now
I am meditating on God
For my heart's luminosity.

21355

O seeker,
Your half-hearted meditations
Will not only delay
Your God-realisation
But will also weaken
Your heart's inner cry.

21356

God's Compassion
Has raised us up.
Now allow His Satisfaction
To swiftly come down.

21357

A desire unfulfilled
Is not a tragedy
But a real triumph
Of the heart over the mind.

21358

As the aspiration-road
Is long, longer, longest,
Even so the temptation-flow
Is strong, stronger, strongest.

21359

I came into the world for two things:
To notice the limitations
Of desire-strength
And to see the illumination
Of aspiration-peace.
