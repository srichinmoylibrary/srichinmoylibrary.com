12860

For God,
You can definitely
Do something infinitely better
Than give Him yesterday's
Second-hand, fading smile.

12861

Even though from God
I turn away,
He invites me again and again
With Him to play.

12862

Because my aspiration-heart
Cannot go astray,
Because my dedication-life
Cannot go away,
Every day my Lord announces
Our mutual victory-day.

12863

My Lord Supreme painfully sighed
When I told Him
That I tried very hard
To please Him.

12864

My Lord,
Do make my mind divinely brave
And my heart supremely brave,
So that I do not feel sad
When the world eagerly watches
The disintegration of my success-life.

12865

I unconsciously want God
To be my slave.
God consciously, compassionately
And self-givingly wants me
To be His friend.

12866

What your mind needs
Is intensive light-surgery.
What your heart needs
Is progressive delight-discovery.

12867

My Lord becomes
His Compassion-Heart
To cushion
My imperfection-fall.

12868

My Lord Supreme, in those days
I really loved You.
But alas, what is wrong with me now?
My Lord Supreme, in those days
I sleeplessly needed You.
But alas, what is wrong with me now?

12869

Give, give,
Soulfully give yourself away!
Your name will be
Prosperity's sky.

12870

I love my Lord's
Compassion-Perfection-Eye.
God loves my life's
Aspiration-purity-heart.

12871

Be kind, be all sympathy,
For each and every human being
Is forced to fight against himself.

12872

I love the God-Giant
Inside my heart
Infinitely more than
I love the God-Dwarf
Inside my mind.

12873

Hello hope,
Hello promise,
Hello!
Good-bye doubt,
Good-bye hesitation,
Good-bye!

12874

You do not have to mourn
Your misspent life.
Be pure in heart.
God will give you another chance.
