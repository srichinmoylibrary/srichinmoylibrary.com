25330

Perfection dawns
Through regular practice
In a punctual manner.

25331

The moment I say and feel
That I am an eternal beginner,
I become the possessor
Of boundless joy.

25332

Alas, why do I not pray to God
Every day
To soulfully grow into something
Infinitely more divine?
Alas, why?

25333

God's beloved is he
Who loves to cry and cry
For God's constant Satisfaction
In his life.

25334

Do not think that whatever is inside you
Is all yours.
No, no, no!
It all belongs to God,
And to God-Compassion
And God-Forgiveness.

25335

When your inner consciousness awakens,
You will at once become the beauty
Of your mounting aspiration-flames.

25336

My aspiration-heart
Is definitely my life's
God-surrender-smile.

25337

Willingness is of paramount importance
To have a free access to God's
Satisfaction-Smile-Kingdom.

25338

What can purify
My impurity-mind
Is my heart's
Ever-increasing tears.

25339

"We obey because we love."
This is so beautiful.
But "we love because we obey"
Is infinitely more beautiful
And infinitely more perfect.

25340

Do not depend on others.
Depend only on God's Compassion-Eye.
It will immediately tell you
'Yes' or 'no'.

25341

May my life every day glow and grow
With the Compassion-flooded Silence-Smile
Of my Lord Supreme.

25342

Whenever I cultivate
Unconditional surrender
To God's Will,
I see God and God's Pride
Dancing together.

25343

My Lord never takes any break
From the world's anxieties and worries.
Why then do I beg God
Even for a short break?

25344

The day I miss my morning meditation,
My heart suffers from darkest fears
For the entire day.
