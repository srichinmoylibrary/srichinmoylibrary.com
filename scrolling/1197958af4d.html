12275

No other place
But my heart-garden,
No other time
But God's choice Hour -
Here and now.

12276

How can the tides of desire
Disappear
If you do not invoke God
To bathe you
In His Compassion-Wisdom-Light?

12277

I have one constant friend
And one constant enemy:
My morning hope
And
My evening frustration.

12278

You may not know where
Your heart's purity is,
You may not know what
Your soul's divinity is,
But I see your heart's purity
And your soul's divinity
Right on your soulful and powerful face.

12279

Each life has to be a surrender-tree
So that God's
Love-Manifestation-Fruits
Can gladden the human life.

12280

Alas, when my mind does not
Accept the invitation from God,
It is my poor heart that suffers miserably,
And not my stupid mind.

12281

You have to keep
Your surrendered oneness-heart
Always pure
If you want to explore
The beauty and the divinity
Of the truth-world.

12282

God will make you
His Eternity's partner
Only when your human life
And your divine breath
Live for God alone.

12283

As soon as you see the doubt-thief
Entering your mind-room,
Sound your heart-alarm!
You will be able to catch the thief
With no difficulty.

12284

Long before my heart-flames
Flicker and die,
I must do something supremely significant,
Supremely fruitful,
For the affection-heart of Mother-Earth.

12285

God does not want to know
What you have done
With His Forgiveness-Delight.
God only wants to know
What you have done
With His Compassion-Height.

12286

The first and worst mistake
Of your life
Is that you have become
A self-ordained teacher
Of world-transformation.

12287

How can you have satisfaction-delight
If you allow yourself
Even unconsciously
To be held up by hesitation-hooligan?

12288

Do not remain standing all the time
On possibility-platform.
Come down and shake hands
With the aspiration-fulfilment-seekers.

12289

If you do not believe
In God's unconditional Compassion-Light,
Then in the inner world
You are undoubtedly an owner
Of useless nothingness.
