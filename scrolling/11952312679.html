9515

If you listen
To what silence says to you,
Then you will not be struck
By desire-hand.

9516

What do you want?
A heart of aspiration
Or a life of admiration?
Make up your mind,
And make the right choice!

9517

A true seeker knows the value
Of his inner progress.
His inner progress outshines
His life's outer prosperity.

9518

Breathe the air
Of aspiration.
Live under the sky
Of dedication.
Perfection-Emperor
Will fast approach you.

9519

Cherish not
The long-dead treasures
Of dark attachment,
For you are a chosen runner
In God's earth-illumining Race.

9520

Your heart's sweet smile
Can easily conquer
The unconquerable stark frown
Of the outer world.

9521

It is not enough
To chain your heart's
Ingratitude-beast.
You must slay it
Once and for all!

9522

You are your world-loving
Mountain-thoughts.
Therefore, God is going to be
Your lifelong Satisfaction-Partner.

9523

He who ignores ignorance mercilessly
Can easily adore
His heart's ever-blossoming
And ever-radiating
Wisdom-sun.

9524

O prisoner of possession-life,
Can you not see
That nobody wants to stay
In your heart-cave?

9525

Do not surrender
If the ferocious ignorance-tiger
Attacks you.
Be brave
And conquer the devouring tiger!

9526

You do not have to go to the length
Of speaking ill of others
Outwardly.
With your negative feelings,
Cleverly and secretly,
You are already creating
Untold problems for them
Inwardly.

9527

Faith and meditation go together.
From faith,
A seeker's meditation
Becomes more powerful.
From regular meditation,
Automatically a seeker's faith
Increases.

9528

Each supreme Request
God makes
Only for the expansion
Of your aspiration-heart.

9529

You do not need to know
Who is right and who is wrong.
In the Court of the Supreme
The supreme Judge
Is Oneness-Light and Oneness-Delight
For both parties.
