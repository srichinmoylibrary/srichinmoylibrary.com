14 To Say That I Will Try ...

To say that I will try
Is nothing short of a joke.

15 To Say That I Must ...

To say that I must
Is, indeed, the brave utterance
Of a real seeker.

16 It Seems That ...

It seems that
To have an enduring bliss
On earth
Is almost an impossible task.

17 Peace ...

Peace
Is founded upon
Forgiveness.

18 When My Heart ...

When my heart
Starts blooming,
My Absolute Lord Supreme
Starts dancing.

19 When We Harmonise Anything ...

When we harmonise anything,
We divinise it
At the same time.

20 What Have I Been Doing? ...

What have I been doing?
I have been taking shelter
At my Lord’s Nectar-Feet.

21 Pleasure-Comfort ...

Pleasure-comfort
Is fleeting.

22 Sacrifice-Joy ...

Sacrifice-joy
Is everlasting.

23 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our sleep-body.

24 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our aggression-vital.

25 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our suspicion-mind.

26 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our insecurity-heart.

27 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our pride-life.

28 The Mind Is Extremely Afraid ...

The mind is extremely afraid
Of raising itself
Upward.
