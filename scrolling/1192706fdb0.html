7060

An unaspiring man
Is a slave of his passions.
An aspiring seeker
Is a child of God's Compassion-Eye.

7061

How can you expect divinity-flower
To blossom here on earth,
Where unprecedented materialism
Is reigning supreme?

7062

No, it is not
An impossible task
To come bravely out
Of anxiety-prison!

7063

To destroy the frown
Of ignorance-night,
I need only
One oneness-smile.

7064

The hour of willingness
Does not know
What the power of obligation is.

7065

To me
God's Justice-Might
Is not indispensable.
To me
God's Compassion-Light
Is not indispensable.
To me
Only God's Forgiveness-Delight
At every moment
Is indispensable.

7066

Even though
You have made the right choice
Only at the eleventh hour,
God's Satisfaction-Delight
Shall be all for you.

7067

Has God given you the authority
To dare to criticise
His Compassion-flooded creation?

7068

One soulful service
Can grant your aspiration-life
The genuine peace
Of the Beyond.

7069

I have heard of satisfaction-delight
Many times from many people,
But I simply cannot understand
What it actually means.

7070

No, it is not true!
It is not the intention of earth
To make you perfect by blessing you
With ceaseless sorrows.

7071

His heart has the thing
That you need:
Compassion-sky.

His mind has the thing
That you deserve:
A life of discipline.

7072

Because your mind today
Is exceptionally pure,
God has decided to grant your mind
A supremely beautiful sunrise.

7073

He has many qualities that amaze me,
But the one that amazes me most
Is his perfect oneness
With his untiring adversaries.

7074

Be prepared!
Your soul is going to teach you
How to ignore earth-bound time.

Be prepared!
Your Lord is going to teach you
How to embrace the Silence-Peace
Of Eternity.
