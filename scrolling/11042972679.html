16 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only when I love You only
Do You show me how
We belong to each other eternally.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 July 2000, 7:50 a.m. Before practising for 740-lb. dumbbell bench press.

16 July 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The attachment-chain
Is extremely difficult to break,
But the aspiration-fire
Can burn it completely
And turn it to ashes.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 July 2000, 8:07 a.m. Before doing 470-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

17 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please give me a little more time.
"My child, what have you been doing?
It is already so late!"
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 July 2000, 7:39 a.m. Before practising for 750-lb. dumbbell bench press.

17 July 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart, you are my true friend.
Why?
Because you are the only one
Who is eagerly longing to please God
In His own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 July 2000, 8:00 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift four times.

17 July 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I really love all the games
That You play with me,
Except one game: indifference.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 July 2000, 8:13 a.m. Before practising for 480-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

18 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Finally, my mind's ignorance-night
Has completely surrendered
To my heart's wisdom-light.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 July 2000, 9:13 a.m. Before practising for 760-lb. dumbbell bench press.

18 July 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To a purity-heart,
Everything is the Beauty
Of God's Eye
And the Fragrance
Of God's Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 July 2000, 9:30 a.m. Before practising for 480-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

19 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A sleeplessly self-giving life
To God's Will
Takes the least possible time
To discover God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 July 2000, 8:21 a.m. Before practising for 770-lb. dumbbell bench press.

19 July 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God cannot convince the human mind
Of anything.
Yet God loves the human mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 July 2000, 8:42 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift four times.

19 July 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The mind does not believe
In the existence-reality
Of the peace-horizon.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 July 2000, 8:56 a.m. Before doing 480-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

19 July 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A new record means a new joy.
I am prayerfully offering
My heart's new joy to the world
To increase its inspiration
And aspiration
For the betterment of the world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 July 2000. Aspiration-Ground. After doing 2,100-lb. standing calf raise. New record.

21 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today is my 800-pound bench press
Journey's start.
I am my gratitude-heart
And
I am my surrender-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 July 2000, 9:13 a.m. Before practising for 800-lb. dumbbell bench press.

21 July 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A desire-life can never be liberated
From the stumbling blocks
Of misfortune.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 July 2000, 9:35 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift five times.

21 July 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I practise, I practise, I practise
Only to make myself perfect
And to make You proud of me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 July 2000, 5:35 p.m. Before practising for 800-lb. dumbbell bench press.

21 July 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A life without heart-tears for God
Is no life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 July 2000, 5:50 p.m. Before practising for 500-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.
