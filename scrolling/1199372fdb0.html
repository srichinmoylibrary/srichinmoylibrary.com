13660

Is there any human being
Who will not ultimately respond
To the Message-Light of the Beyond?

13661

God's Compassion-Feet
And God's Forgiveness-Heart
Are not meant to accept
Any permanent defeat.

13662

Just try to love
Your today's heart-purity.
God will take care of
Your divine tomorrows.

13663

An unaspiring man's life
Is nothing but
The attempt to conquer unrest.

13664

When I hear my soul-bird sing,
I see the transformation
Of my mind's clarity
Into my heart's purity.

13665

When you make an outer mistake,
You may suffer a little,
But when you make an inner mistake,
You are compelled to pay
A severe penalty.

13666

Simplicity, sincerity and purity:
These three are God's
Supremely chosen children on earth.

13667

Don't be a fool!
Don't try to invent a new path.
Be wise!
Take the path
Trodden by the illumined souls
Of the hoary past.

13668

In each human life
There comes a time
When self-assertion surrenders
To self-illumination.

13669

He has wronged you!
Alas, this is what he can do.
Can you not forgive him?
I am sure this is something
You can easily do.

13670

Great he became
To please his mind.
Good he will become
To satisfy God's Heart.

13671

The heart of every human being
Can leave behind a legacy
Of world-illumining compassion.

13672

A gratitude-heart
Will, without fail,
One day reach
Infinity's Fulfilment-Shore.

13673

Self-doubt and self-pity
Are two common things.
Self-perfection is always
A rare thing.

13674

There can be no ultimate failure.
At God's choice Hour
Every individual
Will reach the Destination.
