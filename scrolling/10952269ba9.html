40 The Golden Age Has Decided ...

The Golden Age has decided
Not to sleep any more.
It is about to wake up
To illumine the world
Once again.

41 Falsehood ...

Falsehood
Is sensation.
Truth
Is compassion.

42 Falsehood ...

Falsehood
Is fearless.
Truth
Is deathless.

43 Falsehood ...

Falsehood
Ultimately fails.
Truth
Sleeplessly sails.

44 Falsehood ...

Falsehood
Attracts.
Truth
Rejects.

45 My Lord Supreme Himself ...

My Lord Supreme Himself
Proudly hoists
My obedience-flag.

13 January 2003 ...

13 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

46 No Streaming Heart-Tears, ...

No streaming heart-tears,
No express God-Arrival.

47 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Visits
My sleeping body.

48 God’s Pride-Head ...

God’s Pride-Head
Visits
My serving life.

49 God’s Delight-Heart ...

God’s Delight-Heart
Visits
My surrendering breath.

50 My God-Thoughts Dream And Dream ...

My God-thoughts dream and dream
Inside the silence
Of my sweet and deep sleep.

51 My God-Actions ...

My God-actions
Are invited by God Himself
To fly and fly
In His Infinity’s Sky.

52 God Is Not Displeased ...

God is not displeased
With my feeble weakness-body.

53 God Is Extremely Displeased ...

God is extremely displeased
With my strong, bold and wild
Disobedience-vital.
