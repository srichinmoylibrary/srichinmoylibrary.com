45.

Mithya

False

Everything is false: so thinks the doubting mind.

Everything can be transformed: so feels the crying heart.

Everything can be perfected, liberated and made into a perfect instrument of the Supreme: so knows the all-knowing soul.

46.

Moksha

Liberation

Liberation from attachment-clouds I admire.

Liberation from enchantment-sky I adore.

Liberation from attainment-snare I love.

47.

Mriduta

Gentleness

Gentleness is the most satisfactory beginning of life's perfection-bliss.

48.

Mrityunjaya

Conqueror of death

The heart of the conqueror of death is all compassion for humanity's sleepless progress toward its own ever-transcending reality-existence.

49.

Mukhya

Chief

The chief cause of man's failure-life lies not in his incapacity but in his lack of eagerness to see the satisfaction-face of glorious success.

50.

Mukta

The liberated one

The liberated one compassionately dances inside the heart of man's nothingness and also inside the Vision-Eye of God's Fulness.

51.

Mula

Origin

The origin of man's foolishness is earth's ignorance.

The origin of earth's ignorance is an inconceivable plan of God.

52.

Murkha

Fool

He is a fool who thinks that God is only in Heaven.

He is a greater fool who thinks that God does not care for him.

He is the greatest fool who thinks that he will never be able to realise God.

53.

Nada

The mystic sound

The mystic sound at once embodies both the unimaginable Omnipotence of God and the indescribable impotence of man.

54.

Nadi

Nerve

Each desire-cry weakens our subtle nerves.

Each aspiration-cry strengthens our subtle nerves.

Each gratitude-cry and each gratitude-smile liberates our subtle nerves.

55.

Nama-Rupa

Name and form

I have no name and I have no form. What do I have? I have Eternity's nameless Vision. I have Infinity's formless Realisation.

56.

Namrata

Humility

Humility's vision-eye always has a free access to God's omnipresent Reality.

57.

Neti neti

Not this, not that

Not this, not that. What, then, do I need? I need God's Forgiveness-Heart on earth and God's Satisfaction-Eye in Heaven.

58.

Nidra

Sleep

A sleepless aspiration-heart is God's endless Satisfaction-Pride.

59.

Nimesha

In a twinkling

In a twinkling God can open up my heart's door. The paramount question is this: am I going to welcome Him alone or someone else alone, or am I going to welcome Him along with others? If I welcome Him alone as my only Guest, then only in a twinkling shall I get the satisfaction-sun which will forever last in me and for me.
