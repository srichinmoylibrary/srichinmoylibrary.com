16430

My mind,
I am not utterly displeased
With you,
For you are at least
A curable atheist.

16431

You are so fortunate that
God Himself has commissioned you
To reveal and manifest Him
In His earth-transformation-Promise.

16432

God wants
Your undivided attention.
Are you ready?
If not, you and your mind's
Bumpy road
Can go in your own way.

16433

A sleeplessly self-giving soul
Is not an excitement-elevator
But an enlightenment-elevator.

16434

I am so happy and blessed
That my very first attempt
To go beyond the mind
And enjoy the bliss of silence
Was completely successful.

16435

My Lord,
Your Justice-Light may not,
But Your Compassion-Height can easily
Tell me how
To separate my mind
From the whirlpool of worries.

16436

O how beautiful
Is desire's natural death!
O how fruitful
Is aspiration's spontaneous birth!

16437

Daily
Everybody has to capture
And tame
His monkey-mind and vital-horse.

16438

Compassion is the temple
And forgiveness is the shrine
Of all truly God-loving souls.

16439

My Lord,
Will You love me more
If I escape from my mind-jail?
"My child, infinitely more!"

16440

From now on,
Silence-peace
Will be my only
Conversation-partner.

16441

I am sure that
Right behind my life's teeming clouds
God's Compassion-Forgiveness-Sun
Shines.

16442

My Lord,
Your blessingful Nectar-Invitation
Even my unsearching mind
Loves.

16443

Your insincerity-mind
And your ingratitude-heart
Enjoy collusion.

16444

O my mind, delay not!
Sink, sink
Your doubt-arrogance-submarine!
