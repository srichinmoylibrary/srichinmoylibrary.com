12301

Every day
Become your heart's purity-cry
To fly into the Heaven-ascending glow
Of your God-Satisfaction-Delight.

12302

My poor God is utterly embarrassed
To show me
His daily disappointment-list.
Alas, I too am included on it.

12303

How can a desire-bound life
Ever be released
From the deathless spell
Of insecurity?

12304

An undisciplined life
Loves only one thing:
Excursions into a madness-wilderness.

12305

When my heart's inner cry
Is soulfully increasing,
It means my life's perfection-smile
Is definitely progressing.

12306

To my seeker-heart's great joy,
All the activities in my mind's
Doubt-department
Are amazingly slowing down.

12307

My heart, cry!
Cry only for God's Love
So that your earthly years
Will not be filled
With insecurity-tears.

12308

There can be no perfection
In your life
If you are not unshakeable
In your aspiration-devotion
To God.

12309

Because you do not have
A purity-oneness-heart,
You and your mind live
In yesterday's scepticism
And today's suspicion.

12310

Three things of mine
I daily treasure:
My obedience-mind,
My gratitude-heart
And my surrender-life.

12311

Each and every doubt-challenge
Of my mind
Will eventually surrender
To my heart's faith,
I know, I know.

12312

In your soul I see
Your mind's sincerity-cry.
In your soul I see
Your heart's enthusiasm-smile.
In your soul I see
God the Universal Life-Transformer.

12313

Victory is assured
Only when my life's
Surrender-action is completed.

12314

Because you are no longer
A God-lover,
You will soon fall victim
To the torrential tide
Of frustration.
