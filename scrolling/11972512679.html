11515

Every day try to climb up
Your faith-Himalayas,
So that every moment
God's Vision-Eye and Satisfaction-Heart
Can claim you as their very own.

11516

Either I may or I may not
Respond to God's clarion Call,
But I do feel and know
That the rest of the world
Sooner or later
Will respond to God's clarion Call.

11517

If you have a determination-mind
And an aspiration-heart,
Your faith will help you climb
God's sunlit Mountain-Height.

11518

Each human being who comes into the world
Embodies earth's hope-seed,
Which eventually grows into
Heaven's promise-tree.

11519

God's Compassion-Eye
Is counting your aspiration-mileage
And granting you
His Heart's Satisfaction-Prize.

11520

Insecurity and impurity
Do not warn human beings.
They just attack us
Whenever they want to,
At their sweet will.

11521

Not God's Justice-Light
But His Compassion-Height
Evaluates
Humanity's aspiration-cry-performance.

11522

A true God-seeker
Will definitely be spellbound
By the inner beauty
Of a selfless God-lover.

11523

Once I lost my mind's doubt-night,
I never found it again.
Once I found my heart's aspiration-day,
I never lost it again.

11524

If you really know
God the Transcendental,
Then you are bound to serve
God the Universal.

11525

If you play before God
Your ignorance-confession-game,
Then God will not only play before you
His Wisdom-Perfection-Game
But will also teach you
How to play it.

11526

I use my heart-eye
To see the Feet of God.
God uses His Soul-Eye
To see His own Perfection
Inside my mind.

11527

My express aspiration-plane
Is flying through the sky
To where ignorance-night
Will never be allowed
To take birth.

11528

There is only one joy
That is lasting and everlasting,
And that joy
Is my oneness-silence-heart
With my Pilot Supreme.

11529

God's Compassion-Rain
Will shatter
My mind's ignorance-chain
Only when I welcome
My heart's sovereignty-reign.
