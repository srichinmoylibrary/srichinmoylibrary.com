13930

My Supreme Lord,
You do not have to explain,
For I shall no more complain.

13931

When I go even one step beyond
My ego-mind,
God tells me
That my life is perfect.

13932

My Lord Supreme
Asks me only to transmit His
Forgiveness-Message-Light.

13933

My life's only serious mistake
Is that every day
My stupidity prevents me
From receiving Light
From God's Compassion-Eye.

13934

God wants me to
Take Him soulfully
And take my own life seriously
At every moment.

13935

My silence in action
Means my life is travelling
On the perfection-road.

13936

What I have
Is a daring mind,
And what I am
Is a weeping heart.

13937

My aspiration-heart,
Cry, cry!
I want to see you crying.
My dedication-life,
Smile, smile!
I want to see you smiling.

13938

God's Compassion-Smile
Is my life's only
Secret and sacred collaborator.

13939

Readiness runs,
Oneness flies,
Fulness dives.

13940

What today I call
My heart's devotion-plant,
Tomorrow I shall call that very thing
My life's perfection-tree.

13941

It is you who have chosen
Your misery-life.
What can poor God do
To save you or help you?

13942

My oneness-heart
Needs only God-lovers.
My supremacy-mind
Wants for itself
Not only admirers
But also adorers.

13943

If there is no purity
In your heart,
That means your spirituality-train
Is off the track.

13944

Devotion-offering
Is my soulful task.
Compassion-showering
Is my Lord's bountiful Task.
