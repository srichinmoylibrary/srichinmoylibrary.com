6890

What can your prayers do?
They can help you enter
The world's closed heart,
And they can help you bring down
God's Smile from Above.

6891

Behold!
The body, the vital and the mind
Are campaigning
To conquer earth's insecurity.

6892

How can you be happy
When your heart has become
A helplessly fast-dying hope?

6893

Only one thing
I would like to see
In my lifetime:
God's Voice echoing
In every heart.

6894

Do not think
Of a success-future,
For your lack of faith
In yourself
Has made you friendless,
Helpless and homeless.

6895

What does your faith
Do for others?
Your faith
Brings their best to the fore
All the time.

6896

You have the capacity
To control your vital rebellion.
Just use your aspiration-heart's
Sleepless oneness-power.

6897

For God-realisation
You need not acquire anything special.
You have only to become aware
Of what is already
Deep inside you.

6898

How to know
When you have realised God?
The day you can look in the mirror
And use your inner will power
To see not only your face
But countless other faces
In and around your face,
And know that these faces
Are all yours —
On that day you will know
That you have realised God.

6899

On the strength of our present
Concentration on the physical,
In the process of evolution,
Humanity will achieve
More confidence-light,
More success-might
And more progress-delight
In the body-consciousness.

6900

My Lord Supreme,
When I realise You,
Will I be different?
"Yes, My child!
Previously you thought
You were meant to live
An ordinary human life.
But when you realise Me,
You will feel that you have
Something very special
To do for Me on earth."
