2690 What I Call Enthusiasm

What I call my own enthusiasm,
God calls His own Self-expression.
What I call my own sincerity,
God calls His own Self-perfection.

2691 My Self-Transcendence-Life

My prayer-life
Is my sound-life.
My meditation-life
Is my silence-life.
My surrender-life
Is my self-transcendence-life.

2692 I Choose Freedom

I choose freedom.
Therefore, I monitor my words.

I choose freedom.
Therefore, I restrict my mind.

I choose freedom.
Therefore, I encourage my heart.

I choose freedom.
Therefore, I enjoy my surrender-oneness
With my Beloved Supreme.

2693 God And I Shall Call It

Yesterday I called it
My inspiration.
Today I am calling it
My Lord's instruction.
Tomorrow God and I shall call it
Our Satisfaction-Manifestation.

2694 I Claim

I claim and I claim.
In the inner world I claim
God's Acceptance-Sea.
In the outer world I claim
Man's renunciation-waves.

2695 Your Prayers Are Important

Your prayers are important
Only if you think
That God is important in your life.

God is important
Only if you think
That you are important to your soul.

2696 God Cares For You

If you are apt to think
That you have problems,
Can you not be apt to think
Something more:
God cares for you
Beyond your imagination's flight.

2697 One Thing More

My Lord, You are telling me
That I am free.
Needless to say,
I am happy to hear that.
My Lord, if You really want
To make me happier than the happiest,
Then do tell me one thing more
Once and for all:
"My child, you are forgiven."

2698 Have Faith

Have faith in yourself.
You will have a new heart of assurance.

Have faith in God.
You will have a new life of accomplishment.

2699 My Lord Strengthened

My Lord strengthened
My aspiration.
My Lord guides
My realisation.
My Lord shall direct
My satisfaction.

2700 My Search Ends

In the morning
My search for realisation ends
Inside my Lord's Compassion-Heart.

At noon
My search for perfection ends
Inside my Lord's Vision-Eye.

In the evening
My search for satisfaction ends
At my Lord's Beauty-Feet.
