12175

O jealousy-dwarf,
You do not belong to me.
O oneness-giant,
I am of you
And I am all for you.

12176

O my heart's aspiration,
Do you not think
That you are oversleeping?
Do you not think
That it is high time
For you to wake up?

12177

Who or what can help you conquer
Your insecurity-addiction
If not your own
Mightier than the mightiest
Willingness?

12178

O happiness-rainbow,
My heart's sky is sleeplessly
Crying for you.

12179

Live a humility-oneness-existence
If you want to swim in the Sea
Of God's Fulness-Delight.

12180

An unwillingness-life
Was born to be frustrated
And is destined to continually fail.

12181

Say farewell to frustration-mind
If you want to be
A permanent member
Of your heart's aspiration-group.

12182

Even your midget-sized willingness
God can utilise,
And grant you satisfaction-delight
Far beyond your imagination.

12183

If you want to qualify
For a spot on God's Team,
Then make your heart
An aspiration-flame
And make your life
A dedication-plant.

12184

Man's favourite refrain:
"God, give me!"
God's favourite refrain:
"Man, take Me!"

12185

Envy based on stupidity
Is the real destruction
Of a true happiness-life.

12186

When I fail
To aspire regularly,
My life becomes wilted petals
Of expectation-flowers.

12187

Every day, every hour, every minute
The human mind-night
Has to be brightened
By the divine heart's
Delight-stars.

12188

There are many dark spots
In your life's dedication-smiles.
Therefore,
You are not able to please
Either man's eyes or God's Heart.

12189

O my poor vital-fool,
I simply do not know
Who will ever be able to end
Your deathless aggression-sufferings.
