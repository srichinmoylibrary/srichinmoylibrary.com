1030.

My mind does not care for directions
To Heaven.
My heart needs directions,
And my soul gives directions
Immediately, lovingly and proudly
To my heart.

1031.

What I need
Is a heart that has the capacity
For an instant aspiration-ascent
To God's Compassion-flooded Feet.

1032.

Every moment can hold
The blessings of Eternity
If we can live in the heart
Of God's Luminosity-Infinity.

1033.

If we can conquer
The proud forces of ignorance-night,
Then God will address us
As His Olympic heroes.

1034.

You can be a choice instrument of God
If your heart's sleepless and breathless choice
Is God's Compassion-Eye.

1035.

My heart's silence-language
Is the only language
That God perfectly understands.

1036.

O my heart,
Sit for perfection-examination.
I am sure you will pass
Without any difficulty.

1037.

My heart's inner happiness
Can easily dispel the heaviness
Of my life's outer burdens.

1038.

No matter how hard you try,
You can never tame the restless mind
Without the help of
Your silence-peace-heart.

1039.

A God-curiosity-seeker
Is undoubtedly
An ignorance-misguided life.

1040.

Today's
Secretly cherished desires
Tomorrow
Will openly disappoint you.

1041.

Mother-Earth secretly begs us
To live together peacefully.
Father-Heaven openly commands us
To live together peacefully, lovingly
And soulfully.

1042.

I know
Each aspiration-breath
Is a God-manifestation-promise
On earth.

1043.

A God-searching mind
Is meant to bloom.
A God-aspiring heart
Is meant to blossom.

1044.

My heart's aspiration-cries
Are always guarded and cherished
By my illumination-soul.
