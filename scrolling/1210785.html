24901

Every morning
God asks me to give Him
My aspiration-report.
Every evening
He asks me to give Him
My dedication-report.

24902

My Lord Supreme,
If You really love me,
Then kidnap my heart
Here and now!

24903

Every God-aspiration-attempt
Will eventually grow into
A God-satisfaction-fulfilment.

24904

The modern world
Desperately needs
The beauty, purity and divinity
Of hermitage.

24905

God is both
The Ideal and the Real.
When I am in my mind,
He is the Ideal Divine.
When I am in my heart,
He is the Real Supreme.

24906

May my mind become
An aspiration-student,
A dedication-teacher
And a surrender-professor.

24907

You may quarrel
With your desiring mind,
But never quarrel
With your aspiring heart,
For your aspiring heart
Is treasured by God Himself.

24908

Worldly fame
Is nothing but a fleeting flickering
Of the restlessness-eyes.

24909

I am sure my Lord is there
Inside my heart
To wait compassionately
Until I come.

24910

God showers His choicest Blessings
And Pride
Upon those who have
Happiness-wings-hearts.

24911

My soul wants me
To run the fastest.
Therefore, it is extremely sorry
To see that I have kept
My inner life
Always under construction.

24912

Our faith in God
Has to be indomitable.
Then only do we not have to stop
At the doubt-fear-hesitation-roadblocks.

24913

There was a time when I was
An aspiration-army-soldier.
Now my Lord Supreme
Wants me to volunteer
For His Divinity-manifestation-army.

24914

The beauty of peace
And the fragrance of silence
Are not only interdependent
But also inseparable.
