15.

Live not, live not
In the lap of death.
Fear not, fear not
God's Perfection-Breath.

16.

To illumine
My ancient mind,
My soul-bird
I shall quickly find.

17.

I am learning the language
Of Eternity's silence-time.
I am forgetting the experience
Of truth-abandoned clime.

18.

Never expect
From this world of ours, never!
You will find it is
Incredibly clever!

19.

An aspiration-prize
From God you mill receive
If your doubting mind at last
Can dare to believe.

20.

Be not your mind's
Giant ego-mountain.
Become your heart's
World-love-fountain.

21.

Where are you,
My aspiration-cannon?
Do destroy
My desire-demon!

22.

Do not lose
Your ingratitude-life.
It is your
Self-destruction-knife.

23.

Purity is beauty's
Natural face.
Perfection is divinity's
Natural grace.

24.

What you need is
Aspiration-skill
Before God grants you
Ecstasy's thrill.

25.

I know, my Lord's
Compassion-Presence
Is my earth-life's
Divinity-essence.

26.

The outer man
With his orphan-life cries.
The inner man
Speedily Godward flies.

27.

I try, I try,
I always try
To love and please
My inner cry.

28.

The perfume
Of your aspiration
Awakens
My determination.

29.

God is sought;
Therefore, He is found.
God is loved;
Therefore, He is bound.
