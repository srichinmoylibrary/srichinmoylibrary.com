17915

A God-loving heart
Takes the doubting mind
As an unwelcome intruder.

17916

Quite often
Our expectations go downhill
Faster
Than our own imagination.

17917

I may not always know
What God's Will is,
But I do know
What a good thought is.
Why do I not invite
The presence of a good thought
To lift me and my surroundings up?

17918

The beauty of the morning
Inspires us.
The beauty of the evening
Purifies us.

17919

May each morning come to me
With an inspiration-message-light.
May each evening come to me
With a gratitude-message-light.

17920

Be brave
To see the face of newness
And feel the heart of newness
If you want to start
Your life's Godward journey.

17921

Each man has qualities
Good and bad.
God wants me to look
Only at man's good qualities.
To correct his bad qualities
Is God's Task.
I must not interfere
In God's cherished activities.

17922

My Lord Supreme
Dearly treasures
My life's progress-report.

17923

Nothing can be
More valuable and fruitful
Than to cherish
Each gratitude-breath
Of our heart.

17924

He is so sad
That his life-car
Is helplessly stalled
By doubt-giant.

17925

Life is nothing
Other than
An opportunity-fountain-smile.

17926

Desire-life
Always brings in
Ruthless frustration-night.

17927

Can you not see
That God is crying and weeping
For you to join Him in His Boat
To reach the Shore
Of the ever-transcending Beyond?

17928

If your mind is brave,
Then challenge thought!
If your heart is pure,
Then transcend thought!

17929

Love God unconditionally!
You will not be chained
By earth-bound time.
