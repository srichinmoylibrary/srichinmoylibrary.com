19 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I must revive
All my God-satisfaction-memories
Of the remote past.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 August 1999, 8:26 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 45 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (26 reps)

19 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do bless me with
A completely shattered
Pride-existence-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 August 1999, 8:57 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

19 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must either destroy
Or give a determined chase to
All the ruthless and shameless doubts
That attack my poor mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 August 1999, 9:05 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. thirty-one times with one arm. (13 right, 18 left)

20 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The full awakening of my heart
Is the beauty
Of my aspiration-tears
And the fragrance
Of my dedication-smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 August 1999, 8:19 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. forty-eight times with one arm. (20 right, 28 left)

20 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The sweet awakening
Of my heart-lotus
Is the glorious blossoming
Of my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 August 1999, 8:30 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

20 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am my sleepless heart's
Aspiration-awakening tear.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 August 1999, 8:40 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and eighteen times with one arm. (57 right, 61 left)

20 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am prayerfully and soulfully offering
The breath of my heart-awakening
To You
For Your full manifestation
Here on earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 August 1999, 9:03 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

20 August 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You have been waiting for centuries
For an iota of my heart's awakening.
I promise I shall not delay You
Any longer.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 August 1999, 9:40 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

21 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart's eagerness
Is an absolute necessity
To satisfy my Lord Beloved Supreme
In His own Way
At every moment.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 August 1999. Before lifting 270 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

21 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My eagerness-heart does not house
Even an iota
Of hesitation-negativity-mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 August 1999, 8:40 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. twenty-seven times with one arm. (14 right, 13 left)

21 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My sleepless and God-pleasing
Eagerness
Is my closest association with God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 August 1999. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

23 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I know, I know,
No pure heart-devotion,
No true God-satisfaction,
No, never!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 August 1999, 8:01 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. thirty-one times with one arm. (16 right, 15 left)

23 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my ego-vital is
The winner,
My confusion-mind becomes
The ruler.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 August 1999, 8:11 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

23 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My self-doubt immediately opens
The floodgates
Of my anxieties and worries.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 August 1999, 8:36 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 180 lbs. with each arm separately.

24 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life-boat every day
Ply time and again
Between
My mind's God-obedience-shore
And my heart's God-gratitude-shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 August 1999, 5:20 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 90 lbs. (15 reps)
