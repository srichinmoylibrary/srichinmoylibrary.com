41 From Now On ...

From now on,
My heart’s inner eye and inner ear
I shall immediately welcome
And eternally treasure.

27 November 2003 ...

27 November 2003

Singapore

42 In The Small Hours Of The Morning ...

In the small hours of the morning
God comes to me
Not to preach, not to teach,
But to love me, my entire being.

43 God Comes To Me In The Evening ...

God comes to me in the evening
Not to bless, not to smile,
But to examine my love, devotion
And surrender.

44 God Comes To Me At Night ...

God comes to me at night
Only for one thing:
He comes to dream in and through me
His Infinity’s Dream.

45 When I Run Forward ...

When I run forward,
I easily receive God’s Smile.
When I climb upward,
I more easily receive God’s Smile.
When I dive inward,
I most easily receive God’s Smile.

46 My Meditation ...

My meditation
Is my God-invocation
In the heart of my silence-smile.

47 My Meditation ...

My meditation
Is my animal life-purification.
My meditation
Is my human life-transformation.
My meditation
Is my divine life-manifestation.

48 No Mind-Emptiness ...

No mind-emptiness,
No heart-oneness,
No God-fulness.

49 My Streaming Heart-Tears ...

My streaming heart-tears
Strengthen my prayer-life.

50 My Beaming Soul-Smiles ...

My beaming soul-smiles
Lengthen my silence-meditation.

51 My Madness-Mind ...

My madness-mind
Challenges God.

52 My Gladness-Heart ...

My gladness-heart
Embraces God.

53 God Is All The Time Crying ...

God is all the time crying
For the illumination
Of the doubting and suspecting mind.

54 The Doubting And Suspecting Mind ...

The doubting and suspecting mind
Is spying all the time on God
To discover some imperfection in God.
