7015

Your heart must take
A solid step in Heaven.
Then only
Your life can take
A solid step on earth.

7016

A heart of inner purity
Is bound to have
A life of outer beauty.

7017

Nobody can force you
To stumble on ignorance-stone.
Again, nobody can teach you
To dance with angels
Unless you develop
The willingness and eagerness
To do so.

7018

God grants very special Love
And very special Blessings
To him who is on his way to becoming
An unconditionally surrendered seeker.

7019

Man sees everything
Except one thing:
He does not see his life
Flooded with divine opportunity.

7020

God is giving you
His last warning:
Either love Him for His sake
Or live your earth-bound ego-life.

7021

Not only your heart
But even your mind
Has the capacity to uproot
Cheerfully and selflessly
The jealousy-tree.

7022

You must realise
That God the Compassion
Is always ready
To stop your heart's
Spiritual disaster:
Your lack of faith
In your own aspiration-life.

7023

It is not an uncommon thing
For the doubting mind
To prefer man-made destruction
To God-made Salvation.

7024

Lovingly and blessingfully
Eternity whispered to me:
"My child,
Your heart's peaceful breath
Has nothing to do with
Your mind's powerful desire-life."

7025

What separates you
From God?
Your mind's lack
Of necessity
And your heart's lack
Of urgency.

7026

It was you who wanted to be
An ignorance-puppet.
It is you who now have to pay
The penalty!

7027

If you have nothing else to do,
Then bravely uproot
Your mind's jealousy-tree.

7028

Station yourself beyond your mind.
Then you will realise
That the world needs you
Infinitely more
Than you can ever imagine.

7029

You must learn how to swim
Every day
Against your mind's
Age-old thought-current
To satisfy the divine in you.
