Aum Damanaya Namaha

August 9th, 1992

Aum damanaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the One who compassionately disciplines
All human beings.

Aum Suchaye Namaha

August 9th, 1992

Aum Suchaye namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the One who is all purity
And has no blemish.

Aum Vishishtaya Namaha

August 9th, 1992

Aum vishishtaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the One who excels all in every way.

Aum Taranaya Namaha

August 9th, 1992

Aum taranaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the One who takes me across the ocean of birth.

Aum Suraya Namaha

August 9th, 1992

Aum suraya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the Supreme Possessor of unparalleled valour.

Aum Abhabe Namaha

August 9th, 1992

Aum abhabe namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the One who is not to be found
In the creation and who is
Beyond the creation.

Aum Sughoshaya Namaha

August 9th, 1992

Aum sughoshaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the One who is the supreme Proclamation-Voice.
