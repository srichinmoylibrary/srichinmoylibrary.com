13 God Wants Me ...

God wants me
To fly with Him
With my ever-blossoming
Heart-songs.

14 My Soul, My Heart And I ...

My soul, my heart and I
Will never be found
In the thick of desire-forest.

15 Constant God-Choice ...

Constant God-choice
Is the only antidote
To self-doubt.

16 The Inner Joy ...

The inner joy
Gives more.
The inner joy
Receives more.
The inner joy
Becomes more.

17 In Silence-Secrecy ...

In silence-secrecy
My heart talks
To my Lord Supreme.

18 Our Sleepless God-Surrender ...

Our sleepless God-surrender
Is the architect
Of God’s new world.

13 December 2006 ...

13 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

19 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That my ever-blossoming
Gratitude-heart
Is His most favourite song.

20 My Heart’s Bliss ...

My heart’s bliss
Is the announcer
Of God’s express Arrival.

21 Earth Says To Heaven ...

Earth says to Heaven,
“Why are you
So beautiful?”

22 Heaven Says To Earth ...

Heaven says to earth,
“Why are you
So useful?”

23 Aspiration ...

Aspiration
Is
My heart-song.

24 Dedication ...

Dedication
Is
My life-story.

25 When I Desire ...

When I desire,
I become a body-cage.

26 When I Aspire ...

When I aspire,
I become a soul-bird
Flying in Infinity’s Sky.
