8575

My Lord,
May my aspiration-heart
And my surrender-life
Be forever united
And remain forever inseparable.

8576

At every moment
You are being awakened
By the Touch of God.
Alas,
Always you fall back
Into your ignorance-sleep.

8577

Each soulful thought
Is a flame
That illumines the human
In the divine.

8578

Nothing can remain far beyond
Our eager reach
If we surrender our way
To God's Way.

8579

His is the heart
That has been commissioned by God
To bloom every day
At the Feet of God.

8580

The day I proclaimed
My life's dependence on God,
He immediately granted me
His own transcendental Independence.

8581

If God could create
Immortal Delight,
I thought I, too, could create
Something.
Alas, I immediately created
Ignorance-night.

8582

When I look within,
I see my enlightenment-tree.
When I look without,
I see my amusement-zoo.

8583

Imperfection, imperfection,
Once and for all
I am telling you,
I was born to arrive
At the acme of perfection.

8584

Every day
When you meditate,
Try to feel that your life
Is in your heart's fragrance-garden.

8585

You fool!
God the Compassion-Heart
Does value
Your gratitude-heart.

8586

Begin again!
Human life is not to be surrendered
To the power of ignorance-frown.
Human life is for God
To cherish.

8587

My heart implores
God's Perfection-Eye.
My life implores
God's Forgiveness-Heart.

8588

Your faith is frail.
Therefore, you are bound to fail
Both in your aspiration-heart
And in your dedication-life.

8589

Your gratitude-heart
Is a distinguished guest
At God's Satisfaction-Banquet.
