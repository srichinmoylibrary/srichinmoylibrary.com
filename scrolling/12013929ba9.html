15645

My purity-heart
Not only admires
But literally adores
All-giving God-lovers.

15646

To look at God's Feet
Is not enough.
You have to place
Your life's humility-breath
At His Feet.

15647

It is too much
For my faithful heart
To answer each and every
Absurd question
Of my doubt-cherishing mind.

15648

If you have a mind
Of great intentions,
If you have a heart
Of good aspiration,
And if you have a life
Of sleepless dedication,
Then your nature's perfection
Cannot remain a far cry.

15649

A false teacher
And a false student
Have a very deplorable thing
in common:
An idle promise.

15650

In the inner world,
Whoever dreams of peace
Is undoubtedly a God-lover.
In the outer world,
Whoever works for peace
Is undoubtedly a God-server.

15651

Only your gratitude-heart
Has the capacity to value
God's Satisfaction-Smile.

15652

Show the world what you have:
A dynamic promise.
Show God what you are:
A helpless breath.

15653

This world of ours
Is extremely familiar
With two hard realities:
A shallow mind
And a narrow heart.

15654

To bask in your heart's
Happiness-sun,
Never be inside
The cave of frustration-darkness.

15655

Every day
I pray to see
My heart's sunrise.

Every day
I meditate to be
A citizen of my soul's
Vision-flooded clime.

15656

I do not pray to God
For sweet dreams.
I pray to God
To become the smile
Of my self-giving realities.

15657

If you do not have
A soulful love
For God the creation,
You will not be able
To sing your heart-melody
For any human being.

15658

When I heard my soul's
Final departure-song,
I cried for my loss.
And, again, I smiled
Because of my soul's gain.

15659

A mind of indecision
And a heart of hesitation
Create tremendous, sleepless sorrows
Inside the Compassion-Heart
Of our Lord Supreme.
