23530

If we use each day properly,
We can fulfil God and fulfil ourselves.
If we misuse and destroy our life,
Then earth will not care for our existence,
And Heaven will not care for our arrival.

23531

With utmost Affection, Compassion,
Fondness and Willingness,
God is prepared to carry you
On the longest road:
The birthless and deathless perfection-road.

23532

The Supreme owns only those
Who are with Him constantly,
Who are in Him constantly
And who are for Him constantly.

23533

You are bound
To climb upward immediately
High, higher, highest
If your heart's cry is sincere, simple,
Pure and sleeplessly self-giving.

23534

The more soulfully and prayerfully
You can sing divinity-songs,
The sweeter you will feel
Your own heartbeat
And your own life-breath.

23535

God's choice Hour
And man's choice hour
Are never the same,
For God's choice Hour
Will always be ahead,
Far ahead.

23536

God's Vision-Eye
And God's Compassion-Eye
Are constantly feeding us,
Nourishing us and inspiring us
To wake up.

23537

We fear that God will come
Without informing us,
But God need not inform us.
It is we who have to keep
Our willingness and eagerness
Always ready for His Arrival.

23538

My heart of love has become
The Creator's beaming Smiles
And the creation's streaming tears.

23539

You tell me that
God can be seen,
God can be felt
And God can be realised
And manifested.
I want to follow
In your success-footsteps.

23540

O my mind,
Never, never betray
My heart's inner voice,
Which is my Eternity's polestar-friend.

23541

God-creation is our heart's
Only true gospel.
Self-destruction is our mind's
Only true gospel.

23542

Simplicity has the master key
To open sublimity's room
And reality's safe.

23543

When I pray to God
With severe austerity,
God asks me instead
To play with His creation self-givingly.
He says that if I can play self-givingly
With His creation,
I shall please Him infinitely more.

23544

Careless, we play
In our mind's obscurity.
Sleepless and selfless, we sing
In our heart's profundity.
