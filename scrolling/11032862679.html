15 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life become
An ever-blossoming God-gratitude-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 February 1999, 3:30 a.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 130 lbs. with both arms simultaneously from a seated position.

15 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The rose-beginning of the day
Is not my mind's emptiness-fantasy,
But my heart's fulness-ecstasy.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 February 1999, 3:42 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. sixty-three times with one arm. (29 right, 34 left)

15 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You want each and every human being
To be a God-manifestation-hero-warrior.
How I wish we could fulfil
Your blessingful Command
Lovingly, cheerfully, unreservedly
And unconditionally!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 February 1999, 3:54 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. thirteen times with one arm. (6 right, 5 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

15 February 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind, my mind, my mind,
It matters not how much you know.
For me to have joy and peace,
It only matters to what extent
You are ready, willing and eager
To unlearn.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 February 1999, 4:01 a.m. Before lifting 130 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 260 lbs.)

16 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do tell me when I will be able
To need You desperately
The way a drowning man
Needs to catch a breath!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 February 1999, 5:42 a.m. Before using double-arm machine.

16 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I need peace, I need peace,
I need peace.
Today I must put an end
To all my frustrations, irritations
And exasperations.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 February 1999, 6:04 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. twelve times with one arm. (5 right, 5 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

16 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My soul has zero tolerance
For God-disobedience-seekers.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 February 1999, 6:10 a.m. Before lifting 130 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 260 lbs.)

20 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I must win
My love-devotion-surrender-trophy
From You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 February 1999, 7:39 a.m. Before using double-arm machine.

20 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must run faster than the fastest
To my beckoning Goal
To fulfil my Lord's express Desire.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 February 1999, 7:57 a.m. Before lifting 210 lbs. twelve times with one arm. (5 right, 5 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

20 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please tell me what You want from me.
I shall immediately give it to You.
"My child, I need only one thing from you:
Your surrender-blossom-smile."
My Supreme, my surrender-blossom-smile
Is all Yours.
I place it at Your Feet.
Do make me Your own, very own.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 February 1999, 8:04 a.m. Before lifting 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 280 lbs.)

21 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
Do bless me with a God-adoration-mind,
A God-devotion-heart
And a God-worship-breath.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 February 1999, 7:49 a.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 130 lbs. with both arms simultaneously from a seated position. (four repetitions)

21 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am avidly devouring
The golden dust of Your Feet.
My Supreme, please, please
Promise to me
That You will blessingfully feed
My life's devotion-tree
Whenever it is needed.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 February 1999, 8:05 a.m. Before lifting 210 lbs. sixty-five times with one arm. (30 right, 33 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

21 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day, in the small hours
Of the morning,
I devotedly send You an express telegram
With a prayerful gratitude-message-cry.
My Supreme, do You ever read
My express telegrams?
"My child, not only do I read them,
But I love them and I treasure them."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 February 1999, 8:16 a.m. Before lifting 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 280 lbs.)

22 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please tell me how I can be
A choice instrument of Yours.
"My child, just try to be
A constant, sleepless server
Of Mine.
You can be not only a choice
But the choicest instrument of Mine
If you can become
My sleepless eagerness-server."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 February 1999, 7:08 a.m. Before using double-arm machine.

22 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
There was a time when I used to dream
Of having a world of my own.
But now, my Supreme, I wish to live
Most devotedly in Your own World,
And nowhere else.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 February 1999, 7:32 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. forty-seven times with one arm. (21 right, 24 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)
