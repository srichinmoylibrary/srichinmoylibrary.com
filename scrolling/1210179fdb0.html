24360

To repeat what God has said to me
Is the only way
I can transform my desire-life
Into aspiration-life.

24361

I have forgotten to ask God one thing:
Will He wait for me indefinitely
Even though right now I do not have
A conscious need for Him?

24362

The strongest of God's Hopes
Is for my instant and constant
God-surrender-life.

24363

Whether we hasten or delay
Is up to us.
But at God's choice Hour,
We must wake up
And begin to please Him
In His own Way.

24364

Every morning my Lord Supreme
Visits my heart-garden
And helps my aspiration-plants
To thrive and multiply.

24365

God never likes our sad loneliness.
He likes our heart's and mind's
Cheerfulness
More than anything else.

24366

No beauty is as divine
As God-devotion.
No fragrance is as perfect
As God-surrender.

24367

If we all do not pray and meditate
Soulfully and self-givingly,
Then who is going to save God's universe
From joy-annihilation?

24368

We must establish
A stupendous self-giving love
Between God's Will
And our eagerness-heart.

24369

God's Heart is full of hope
Because of our heart's
Genuine love of God.

24370

O my mind,
Do not wait anymore
In the sleep of Eternity.
Wake up!
Pray and meditate
So that God can claim you
As His very own.

24371

There shall come a time
When the negativity-mind
Of the whole world
Will disappear
Into an abysmal abyss.

24372

My Lord,
I wish to do each and every thing
With Your blessingful
And direct Permission.

24373

God is all surprise
When He sees
A gratitude-mind is welcoming Him.

24374

My Lord Supreme,
Do grant me the aspiration-dynamism
To conquer my unwillingness-mind
And crown my willingness-heart.
