21901

If you aspire
To reach the Highest,
Your own highest height
Someday
Will definitely respond.

21902

If our meditations are deep,
Then our meditations
Will give us the capacity
To extend
Our happiness-boundaries.

21903

A true seeker feels
That God's Forgiveness
Is a fortune
That he will never be able
To exhaust.

21904

Our abiding happiness
Depends upon
Our self-offering
To the entire world.

21905

Our adamantine determination
Will definitely triumph
Over all our past misdeeds.

21906

Disaster may appear
To crush my hope,
But my God-manifestation-promise
Will never be affected.

21907

If you see through
Another man's eyes,
If you feel through
Another man's heart,
Then you realise
That there are other people
On earth
Who are infinitely more miserable
Than you are.

21908

I am happy because
God's Eye
Tells me what to do.

I am happy that
God's Feet
Do everything for me.

21909

O ignorance-world,
Give me back,
Give me back
My ancient aspiration-heart!

21910

Each time I ask God
For material satisfaction,
God answers my prayers
With a very heavy sigh.

21911

My gratitude-filled heart
Sadly fails to touch
My aspiration-empty mind.

21912

Like the world of imagination,
The world of frustration
Will always remain limitless.

21913

Man's disobedience-attachment-mind
Is definitely stronger
Than his obedience-devotion-heart.

21914

Each time I meditate,
Prayerfully and soulfully,
My meditation enlarges
My experience-world
Far beyond my imagination.
