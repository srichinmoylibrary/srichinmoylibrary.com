24160

Those who have
Invaluable inner insights
Will have the power
To help transform man into God.

24161

The transformation
Of a single bad thought
Can be the manifestation
Of something divine, absolute
And supreme.

24162

Our heart's cries
Are our special messengers
From our soul to God.

24163

When I am determined
To have God-choice
As my only choice,
My goal does not remain
A far cry anymore.

24164

My Lord,
Do tell me the choice Hour
That You have selected
To liberate me
From unending bubbles of thought.

24165

My Lord,
Will we ever realise
The gravity of our spiritual disaster
When we criticise You
And do not see eye to eye with You?

24166

The choicest Blessing-Smile of God
Descended upon him
When he remained unmoved
By world-appreciation
And unperturbed by world-deprecation.

24167

No, not even hell
Can smash a gratitude-heart
That wants to please God always
In His own Way.

24168

Our aspiration-thirst
God will gladly quench
At His choice Hour.
But our ego's thirst
Definitely He will not quench
Permanently.

24169

Peace will be
By far the best promoter
Of God-manifestation on earth.

24170

Be not caught
In your self-criticism-net.
Be not caught
In your world-criticism-net.

24171

To have a good idea
In the mind,
And to perform a better action
From the heart,
Will definitely expedite
Our journey towards our goal.

24172

The best way to liberate ourselves
From the confusion-mind
Is to live inside
The cries of our heart.

24173

Every morning
I get tremendous satisfaction
When I look at the delight-rainbow-smiles
Of the dawn.

24174

We must offer
Our gratitude-heart to God
For our every progress-breath.
