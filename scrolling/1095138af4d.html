68 Spirituality ...

Spirituality
Is a breathless
Self-transcendence-adventure.

69 My World-Improvement-Service-Joy ...

My world-improvement-service-joy
Is more precious to God
Than anything else.

70 My Meditation-Time ...

My meditation-time
Is my Lord’s
Only resting-hour.

71 My Lord And I Deeply Enjoy ...

My Lord and I deeply enjoy
Our mutual appreciation:
My Lord tells me
That my heart-tears
Are inimitable.
I tell my Lord
His Eye-Smiles
Are inimitable.

5 JANUARY 2003 ...

5 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

72 I Must And I Must Discover ...

I must and I must discover,
As soon as possible,
The key to my Lord’s Heart-Home.

73 Nothing Human In Me ...

Nothing human in me
Exists any more.
I have become today
A supremely choice instrument
Of my Lord Supreme.

74 God’s Heart ...

God’s Heart
Is in each moment.
God’s Eye
Is in each movement.

75 No More My Aspiration-Heart ...

No more my aspiration-heart
Shall suffer
From God-separation-pangs.

76 May Every Morning ...

May every morning
My aspiration-heart-garden
Produce a most beautiful
God-gratitude-rose.

77 The Disobedience Of The Mind ...

The disobedience of the mind
Is a very, very old story.
The obedience of the heart
Is a most soulful song
That has neither beginning
Nor end.

78 My Beloved Supreme ...

My Beloved Supreme,
Out of His infinite Bounty,
Tells me that my entire life
Is a most powerful song-bridge
Between humanity’s tears
And Divinity’s Smiles.

79 Today My Heart ...

Today my heart
Is a meditation-plant.
Tomorrow my life
Shall become a Heaven-climbing tree.
