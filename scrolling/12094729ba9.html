23645

For every obstacle-mountain
There is
A God-Compassion-Fountain.

23646

Do not depend on tomorrow.
Do not depend even on today.
Just depend on now,
This very moment.

23647

Each seeker
Must develop a mind
That will be averse
To ignorance-food.

23648

I live in between
My heart's God-realisation-hope
And
My mind's God-manifestation-promise.

23649

What is faith?
Faith is a meteoric aspiration-rise.
And what is doubt?
Doubt is a meteoric aspiration-fall.

23650

We sadden God
When we rest
On our yesterday's laurels.

23651

How do you dare to think
That God is pleased with you,
When you allow yourself
To be buffeted
By desire-storms?

23652

My Lord Supreme,
May my life be non-stop
Service-delight.

23653

If you do not pray
Every morning and evening,
Then you must realise
That you are descending
Into spiritual poverty.

23654

Earth sleeplessly depends on
Heaven's Compassion.
Heaven ceaselessly depends on
Earth's dedication.

23655

My Lord,
I have done so many things
To displease You.
Do You still love me?
God's one-word reply:
"Yes."

23656

The mind's superiority-brilliance
Does not last.
The heart's unity-radiance
Forever lasts.

23657

Anything that is human
Is an invalid passport
To Heaven.

23658

Because our spiritual eyes
Are still shortsighted,
We do not see God
When He compassionately looks at us
From a distance.

23659

If the mind is filled with sincerity
And the heart is filled with purity,
Then there cannot be a collision
Between the mind and the heart.
