30.

God is eager to teach you
His Vision-Reality-Dance.
You just show Him openly
Your weakness-destruction-lance.

31.

Kindle every day and feed
Your aspiration-fire,
Or your useless life will be
Devoured by desire.

32.

Idleness never lives alone;
With it abides dark failure-night.
Action alone can save yon with
Its bright all-conquering light.

33.

The outer moon shines
With nature's light.
The inner moon shines
With God's Delight.

34.

In God-Dawn awakens
My heart's aspiration-cry.
With God-Sun I enter
My life's liberation-sky.

35.

Each uncomely thought springs up
From the wasteland-mind
And is cherished there by man
The feeble failure blind.

36.

The mind-desert cannot escape
The flood of the heart-river.
The river inundates with songs
Of Oneness-Fulness-Giver.

37.

To discover who I truly am
Throughout the world I need not fly.
To satisfy this breathless quest
I open up my vision-eye.

38.

The conscious journey of the heart
Begins at God's choice Hour.
Its progress is inspired by
God's Satisfaction-Power.

39.

If your body's useless idleness
Does not disappear,
Then how can your aspiring heart's
Eagerness appear?

40.

The soul travels through
Eternity's time
And makes light-friends
In every clime.

41.

Mind is a struggle
For supremacy-voice.
Heart is a cry
For God-oneness-choice.

42.

If you are not determined
To break your doubt-chain,
Yours will be a life of
Fruitless, pointless pain.

43.

If you can renounce
Your desert-mind-ownership,
God can then grant you
His Vision-World's partnership.

44.

The teeming errors of the past
In you no longer linger.
Self-perfection is beckoning you
To be a God-song-singer.
