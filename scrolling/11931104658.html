7430

Man wants your mind.
Be careful, you and your mind.
God needs your heart.
Be ready, you and your heart.

7431

Alas, I do not know why
I am spending so much time
With my mind-enemy.

7432

Expectation:
That is human.
Frustration:
That is not even human.

7433

He is unimaginably fluent
In God's transcendental Language:
Satisfaction.

7434

Indeed,
It is a fruitful thing
To collect your golden dreams.

7435

Without God's Grace
If you want to run
Faster and faster,
You will meet with one disaster
After another.

7436

God wants to teach my heart
How to speak.
My mind wants to teach God
How to remain silent.

7437

You do not know
What to do?
Then practise, at least,
How to wait.

7438

God commands
My stupidity-mind.
God wants my purity-heart
To command Him.

7439

Are you so stupid
To think that you are
Your yesterday's
Puny failure-life?

7440

You have decided
Not to take a chance.
God does not approve
Of your hasty decision.

7441

My mind wants me
To be careful.
My heart wants me
To be soulful.
My Lord wants me
To be fruitful.

7442

A surrender-heart knows
That there is nothing
Difficult for it to achieve.

7443

You wanted to still your mind.
God has done it for you.
God wants to be fulfilled.
Can you not do it for Him?

7444

Your business
Is to take the first step.
God's Business
Is to grant you a fast step.
