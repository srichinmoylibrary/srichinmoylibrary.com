18160

The outer success-road
Is short
And full of apprehension.
The inner progress-road
Is long
And full of illumination.

18161

With each good thought
That we offer to mankind,
We perform a new miracle.

18162

Your self-giving heart
Is sweet,
Extremely sweet,
Breathlessly sweet to God.

18163

Cheerfulness, readiness and willingness
If we have,
Then we need nothing more
To please the Lord Supreme
In His own Way.

18164

O my Lord Supreme,
When will I realise
That I am not only in You
And with You,
But also always for You,
Only You?

18165

In the spiritual life,
Anything divine
Cannot be overdone.

18166

If gratitude cannot satisfy
Your God-joy-thirst,
Nothing and nobody can do it.

18167

My Lord,
Do blessingfully govern my life
As You do with the heart
Of the Heaven-life.

18168

Doubt we measure
Before we conquer.
Faith we measure
Before we offer to God.

18169

God tells me
That my earth-responsibility-shoulders
Can always carry more,
Infinitely more.

18170

Love divine
Definitely has the capacity
To conquer and illumine
Imperfection-life.

18171

The divine love
Can never withdraw
From any human being
Who aspires.

18172

By faith you can not only
Seek your way
But definitely arrive
At your destination.

18173

The dust of self-doubt
Can easily blind
The heart of faith.

18174

I never economise
On my concern-life
For aspiring humanity.
