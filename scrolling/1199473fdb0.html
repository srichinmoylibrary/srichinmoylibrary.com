13760

Your constant unwillingness-negativity-mind
Is nothing short of your unworthiness
In the Kingdom of Divine Life.

13761

Be constantly self-giving
If you want to be happy.
Then you will not be forced
To experience expectation-hunger.

13762

Confront your self-doubts!
Be brave!
They are bound to be vanquished.

13763

Your purity-heart is blossoming.
Therefore, God is blessing you
With His Satisfaction-Smile.

13764

O purity-beauty,
O purity-divinity,
O purity-immortality,
My sleepless heart longs for you.
Do tell me where you are!

13765

Is there anybody
Who is not forced to learn
Some insecurity-songs
During his entire lifetime?

13766

Do not allow your mind's
Desire-hunger
To collide with your heart's
Constantly blossoming peace.

13767

There should always be a distance
Longer than the longest
Between you and doubt.

13768

Be not lost
Between your mind's unwillingness
And your heart's enthusiasm.
Choose your heart.
Remain in your heart,
With your heart
And for your heart.

13769

It is not an easy task
To muster the courage
To surrender consciously and soulfully
To God's Will.

13770

Now that you have passed
The willingness-examination,
Your life's perfection
Cannot remain a far cry.

13771

I call it my aspiration-life.
God calls it His Eternity's
Perfection-Satisfaction-Journey.

13772

Self-indulgence is
An insatiable thirst
That can never be quenched.

13773

He who deliberately lives
In ignorance-night
Wants to destroy the beauty
Of freedom-light.

13774

The darkness of ignorance
Cannot devour
The beauty, light and delight
Of the rising sun.
