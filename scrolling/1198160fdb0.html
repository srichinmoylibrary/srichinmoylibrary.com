12460

If you want to accelerate
Your self-transcendence-pace,
Then do not be afraid
Of your life's adversity-surges.

12461

If you want to run
Faster than the fastest
In the inner world,
Then what you need
Is a non-stop self-giving smile.

12462

Cast off immediately
The painful memories of yore,
And try to be inseparably one
With your heart's
Life-awakening and
God-fulfilling dreams.

12463

What is detachment?
It is our aspiring heart's
Wisdom-perfection-delight.

12464

Unless and until you make your heart
Your instructor,
You will not be able to become
God's satisfaction-builder
On earth.

12465

Ancient God-discovery-eagerness
Will inevitably illumine
The confusing and confused mind
Of modern human beings.

12466

O my God-Doctor,
I entirely depend on
Your Compassion-Medicine
To protect my heart's aspiration-life.

12467

Both man and God
Are apt to tell the stories
Of their victory-flooded lives.

12468

We can easily tame
Our wild doubt-tiger
If we long to be
A mild oneness-lamb.

12469

Nobody has compelled you
To follow the confusion-path.
You have made
This unconscious temptation-choice
Yourself.

12470

Doubt has infected
Your mind.
Fear has invaded
Your vital.
But you must challenge
Both doubt and fear
So theirs is a short (life.) (fn:1)

(fn:1) The word (life) has been added by the editor. In the first edition of the book the verse was incomplete.

12471

My heart's obedience
Is my life's
Spontaneous love for God
In action.

12472

The sadness of my lost faith
I can neither describe
Nor even imagine.

12473

Obedience is the cheerfulness
Of the outer life.
Gratitude is the fulness
Of the inner life.

12474

Alas, who could even imagine
That my heart's willingness-obedience
Would be mercilessly attacked
By my mind's unwillingness-disobedience?
