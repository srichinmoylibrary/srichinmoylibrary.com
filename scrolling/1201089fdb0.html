15360

My heart's open invitation
Is for all.
There are no intruders -
No, not even unwanted
Or undesirable visitor-guests.

15361

Gradually
Perfection will come to the fore
If you continue offering
Your heart's life-breath
To the world.

15362

Every day
Good and evil thoughts
Are increasing in the world.
Therefore, the mind-hotel
Never enjoys vacancy.

15363

What you need
Is a silence-mind.
What you need
Is a sacrifice-heart.
What you need
Is a willingness-life.

15364

If you are even a little negative
Towards God's unconditional
Compassion-Light,
The skies of Heaven will never allow you
To enjoy peace of mind.

15365

If you do not disobey
Your ignorance-leader,
The inner progress-summit
Will always be denied to you.

15366

As soon as God opens His Eyes,
My life runs towards Him
For perfection.
As soon as God closes His Eyes,
My mind runs towards Him
For forgiveness.

15367

You will be equal to the task
Of manifesting God on earth
If your life is a constant
Willingness-eagerness.

15368

Because the smile of my soul
Is strong,
God asks my soul's smile
To come and stand beside Him.

Because the tears of my heart
Are weak,
God tells my heart's tears
To come and hide inside His Heart.

15369

No matter how much complexity
Your mind cherishes
Either consciously or unconsciously,
God will bless your mind one day
With a childlike simplicity,
For that is the only way
Your mind will be able to enjoy
Delight infinite.

15370

O hungry impatience-mind,
How I wish that you could enter
Into Eternity's patience-sky
To recognise your divinity's
Transcendental form.

15371

You cannot live
In the outer world
Divinely and supremely
If you are trying to live
Beyond your heart's surrender-income.

15372

God-Compassion-flooded human beings
Have a very special way
Of receiving
God's inner Glory supreme.

15373

God's Sweetness-Whisper
Is my climbing heart's
Aspiration-breath.

15374

Every morning
My heart's fondness-faith feeds me
So that I am not devoured
By the wild doubt-tiger.
