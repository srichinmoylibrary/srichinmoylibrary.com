9015

Now that he lives
Inside his soul,
He enjoys the expanding shore
Of Infinity.

9016

There is no such thing
As drinking too much
Of God's Nectar-Delight.

9017

Give to God what you have:
Your mind's slow train to hell.
God will give you what He has:
His Heart's fast Boat to Heaven.

9018

Death can never bring you
Any relief.
It is your willingness
To abide by God's Will
That can give you relief
From earthly pangs.

9019

Allow not ambition-appetite
To grow
If you want to enjoy
Satisfaction-feast.

9020

Unless you aspire
Devotedly and soulfully,
There is every possibility
That your life will be buried
In the cave of doubt.

9021

What you need
Is an extra dimension of courage
To bravely pass through
The corridors of your mind's darkness.

9022

Every day
Pray to the Lord Supreme
To bring to the fore
Your inner sun.
It will silence
Your mind's confusion-candle.

9023

I pray every day
For two things:
An illumination-thirsty mind
And a liberation-hungry heart.

9024

He has an exceptional mind.
His mind is completely immune
To doubt-disease.

9025

Keep your heart's purity
And your mind's simplicity
At the head of your life-parade.

9026

A blossoming faith
Should always play the role
Of your life's
Inner traffic controller.

9027

Slowly and steadily
But unmistakably
Your happiness can blot out
Your life's age-old depression.

9028

If your mind is enjoying
The looming shadow of distrust,
The world is not going to be the loser
In any way.

9029

What my mind needs
Is a fresh breeze
Of sweet hope
To succeed once more
Gloriously
With my life.
