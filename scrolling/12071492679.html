21315

O my God-loving heart,
It is your faithfulness
In everything that you do
That is keeping me divinely alive
Here on earth.

21316

What is the real American dream?
The real American dream is
A universal oneness-ecstasy -
Nothing more and nothing less.

21317

Alas, I really do not know
When and how
I will be able to come back safely
To my aspiration-heart-home.

21318

When my mind tells me
That it can and will
Obey God's Dictates,
I always suspect
That it has an ulterior motive.

21319

A God-loving soul
Must pray and meditate
Every morning and evening
Without fail.
Otherwise, he has to carry
An extremely heavy burden
Of self-blame.

21320

My unconditional surrender
And my cheerful gratitude
Help my Lord to smile, sing and dance
Right in front of the world-assembly.

21321

O seeker,
Your aspiration-heart
Is bound to realise
That your life
Is not fruitlessly pursuing
God-Satisfaction-Award.

21322

O seeker,
Your eyes can deceive you.
Your body, vital, mind,
Even heart can deceive you.
But no, never,
Your constantly God-blossoming soul.

21323

My hope-heart blossoms
Only when I worship God
Smilingly and self-givingly.

21324

O my mind,
When are you planning
To come out of your
Thoughtless and useless
Frustration-jungle?

21325

The ego-bound human being
Will always remain blind
To tomorrow's dawn.

21326

God's Heart-Intimacy I feel
Only when
I prayerfully accept
His 'never say no' policy.

21327

God can bless the seeker
With His Himalayan Smile
Only when he is all ready
To give up his desire-hunger-life.

21328

Each purity-thought in our mind
Helps us achieve the racehorse-speed
To arrive at
Our ecstasy-flooded destination.

21329

To reach the mind's oneness-
And the heart's fulness-summit,
I must wake up and delay not
In coming out of
My ignorance-pleasure-abyss.
