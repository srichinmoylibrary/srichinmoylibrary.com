130.

We can be shattered in body,
But we can never be shattered
In spirit
While loving God.

131.

The doubt-monster
Does not want to share
Its sovereignty
With anybody else.

132.

In order to keep
Failure at a distance,
Live an obedience-life
All the time.

133.

Breathe the fragrance
Of your aspiration-gratitude-heart,
And you will always be
Spiritually triumphant.

134.

The mind chooses
Its own path.
God chooses the path
For the heart.

135.

With whom does God
Lovingly commune?
No, never with my mind,
But ever with my heart.

136.

Every day
A seeker must marshal
Sufficient enthusiasm
To achieve victory for God
Here on earth.

137.

If you want to become
A choice instrument of God,
A sleepless God-surrender
Is at once invaluable
And indispensable.

138.

You can at times be independent
Of the outer sun,
But you cannot be independent,
Even for a fleeting second,
Of your all-illumining
Inner sun.

139.

Let us go forward
Into the heart of the future
Bravely and self-givingly.
Lo, the Golden Destination-Shore
Is beckoning us.

140.

We can never be happy
If we close our eyes
To life's difficulties.
We can only be happy
By challenging and conquering
The pride of difficulties.

141.

Who said that we do not know
What precious blessings we possess?
We do know that our God-hunger
Is the most precious blessing
In our life.

142.

No matter how strong
Our weaknesses are,
We must conquer them,
Or we shall never be able to arrive
At our God-Destination.

143.

When we live in the cruelty-mind,
We definitely live
In a state of deplorable barbarism.

144.

Not because God is indifferent to us,
But because He is dreaming
A very special dream
In and through us,
We frequently meet with
Calamities and misfortunes.
