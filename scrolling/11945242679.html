8815

There should not be
A single day
When you do not ignore
Your mind's fruitless
Ignorance-threats.

8816

From time immemorial
His aspiration-life
Has been living inside
God's Compassion-Ocean.

8817

No, I do not want to become
A professional peace-lover
Of man.
I want to be
An unconditional peace-lover
Of God.

8818

If you want to enlighten
Your aspiration-heart
And dedication-life,
Then awaken
Your drowsy mind.

8819

For the best success,
Consult your illumining mind.
For the best progress,
Consult your liberated heart.
For the best satisfaction,
Consult your compassionate
Inner Pilot Supreme.

8820

If God can be peaceful
In spite of having
Countless problems every day,
How is it that
I cannot remain peaceful
Even for a fleeting second?

8821

If you have no time
For truth-seeking,
How can you see
A man-loving God?

8822

Alas, my human heart
Is not magnanimous enough
To establish its oneness
With another's victory-delight.

8823

Unless you become totally
A man for God,
How can you unmistakably be
A man of God?

8824

You want to fulfil
Your desires?
Then you have to do it
All at your own risk.

8825

Why are you such a fool?
Can you not see
That you have most strikingly
Outgrown
Your own desire-life?

8826

Now that every day
I eat gratitude-fruits,
My life will not remain
The same unfulfilled hunger.

8827

You are asking God
For what you already have:
Your heart's unconditional
Oneness-surrender
To God's Will.

8828

You fool!
You ask your mind
To speak to your heart
About the supreme necessity
Of God.

8829

When we make
An unconditional surrender
To God's Will,
We live in God's Sweetness,
Fondness and Oneness-Clime.
