14245

If we think that God's Justice-Light
Wants us as badly
As God's Compassion-Height needs us,
Then we are obviously enjoying
Self-delusion.

14246

Freedom-fighters and
Tolerance-builders
Must always sail
In the same boat.

14247

If you see darkness-night
Here, there and everywhere,
It is because even by mistake
You do not give God
The right of way.

14248

If you continue doing
Your silence-meditation daily,
God will definitely strengthen
Your inner life-muscles.

14249

Each good thought
Can eventually be blessed
With two flying wings:
Surrender-flower
And gratitude-fragrance.

14250

Beautiful
Is the earth-illumining song
Of my heart.
Fruitful
Is the life-transforming dance
Of my soul.

14251

Intensify
Your God-satisfying hunger-cry
To be a conscious partner
Of God's Vision transcendental.

14252

Ambition-success
Wants to be visible and tangible.
Aspiration-progress
Wishes to be unnoticed and unacclaimed.

14253

My Lord Supreme tells me
That you can do anything
You want to do with your life,
Except one thing:
Do not cover your heart's gratitude-sun.

14254

Is there any human being on earth
Who has never experienced
Incredible self-indulgence-capacity?

14255

Smooth sailing
On obedience-ocean
My heart is learning
From my soul.

14256

Today I am most powerfully
And most convincingly
Putting an end to my mind's
Excessive devotion to reason.

14257

Alas, alas!
Today is another day for me
Without a glimpse
Of my Lord's Compassion-Smile.

14258

I shall never allow
My searching mind
To be stung
By the outer world's scorpion-doubt.

14259

If you do not have a searching mind,
If you do not have a climbing heart,
Then your life will be forced to descend
Into the confusion-spiral.
