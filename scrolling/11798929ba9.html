6945 The Beauty Of Human Perfection

The beauty of human perfection
Is the unparalleled necessity
Of human aspiration.

6946 Defy Or Deny

Either defy or deny
Your sombre past
If you wish God to certify
Your heart's present smile.

6947 One Thing To Learn

Every day there is only
One thing to learn:
How to be honestly happy.

6948 A Special Love

God's Heart has a special love
For him
Because he has unconditionally placed
His heart at God's Feet.

6949 God Wants To Celebrate

Two things God wants
To do for you cheerfully:
He wants to celebrate
Your mind's self-mastery,
And He wants to celebrate
Your heart's God-discovery.

6950 My Heart's Only Unfailing Desire

My heart's only unfailing desire
Is to remain perpetually a God-lover,
A perfect stranger
To the world of fame.

6951 My Mind Never Dares

My mind never dares
To wander
Inside my heart's
Will power-garden.

6952 Always Hopeful

I may not be helpful to you,
But I shall always be hopeful
For your success-life
And progress-heart.

6953 I Shall Not Give Up

Although my mind is useless,
Although my heart is worthless,
Although my life is soulless,
I shall not give up,
Never!

6954 My Two Questions

Let me see who can and will
Answer my two questions:
Why do I still love man
When he secretly thinks so poorly of me?
Why do I not think of God
When He openly speaks so highly of me?

6955 Defy And Enjoy

Your mind's bondage-night
You must defy
If you want to enjoy
Your heart's freedom-sky.

6956 My Heart Shall Defend

You may offend my mind's pride,
But my heart shall always
Defend your right to do so.

6957 I Carry Only One Thing

I carry only one thing:
My Lord's sleepless Smile.
My Lord carries only one thing:
My shadowless satisfaction.

6958 My Heart's Sacred Flame

My heart's sacred flame
Sings only one song:
Earth's self-transcendence-song.

6959 In Collusion

When my teeming doubts
And my tormenting fears
Are in collusion,
Frustration becomes my outer name
And destruction becomes my inner name.
