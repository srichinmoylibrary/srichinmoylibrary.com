23775

Only the multitude
Of God-hungry souls
Can and will save this world
From the destruction-blows
Of ignorance-night.

23776

My service to God
Paves the way.
My love of God
Makes the day.

23777

My Lord commands me
Not to be ashamed
Of myself,
But to face my weaknesses
And transform them.

23778

Trust not what you hear,
Trust not what you see,
But trust
What you feel.

23779

Today is the day
My mind has chosen to be
The child of sincerity,
My heart has chosen to be
The child of purity
And my life has chosen to be
The child of humility.

23780

O my mind,
Do not run away.
O my vital,
Do not hide.
You both can change
And will change.

23781

From kindness,
Goodness blooms.
From goodness,
Fulness blossoms.
In fulness, we transcend ourselves
And become the Satisfaction-Smile
Of God.

23782

Be pure,
My jungle-mind.
Be self-giving,
My garden-heart.
Be perfect,
My God-surrender-life.

23783

O my mind, be not stupid.
Do not run away
From your illumination-heart-home.

23784

Today I am caught in a flurry of fears
Because I neglected
My morning prayer-mind
And meditation-heart.

23785

My Lord is available
In all delicious flavours
Only when I most prayerfully
And most soulfully
Do Him little favours.

23786

We came into the world
To be dynamic for God-manifestation
And not to enjoy slumber-complacency.

23787

The mind is ready to pray to God
For God-satisfaction in God's own Way
Only when life has lost
All its desire-charms.

23788

Is there anything forbidden
In our spiritual life?
Yes!
Our self-doubt-mind
And God-doubt-heart.

23789

My Lord,
Do bless me with a single boon:
Do allow me to decorate Your Feet
With my heart-tears.
