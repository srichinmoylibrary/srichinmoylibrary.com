19175

Man is endlessly repeating
His blunders,
At times, it seems, deliberately.
Yet God has not given up on him.

19176

Perfection-peaks are not inaccessible.
What we all need
Is a readiness-mind
And a willingness-heart.

19177

The general decay of our faith
In God
Is not only displeasing
But also disheartening God.

19178

I get satisfaction-joy
Only when I receive from God
His blessingful Appreciation
For what I do for Him
And what I am to Him.

19179

My Lord Supreme,
Immediately do open
My willingness-heart
And shut
My unwillingness-mind.

19180

In the presence of my Lord Supreme,
No matter what happens
I get an inexpressible inner thrill.

19181

I must and must abandon
Once and for all
My helplessness-thinking
And helplessness-feeling.

19182

When we are in front of ignorance-sea,
God wants us to be
Brave swimmers.
When we are in front of a high mountain,
He wants us to be
Brave climbers.
No obstacle in our way must prevent us
From reaching our destined goal.

19183

Those who have taken shelter
Consciously and soulfully
In the Benediction-Heart
of the Lord Supreme
Will not fail.
No, not a single one will fail!

19184

The knowledge of the mind
Comes and goes.
The wisdom of the heart
Not only remains
Permanently
But also increases
Immeasurably.

19185

Do not deny and do not defy
The old ways of tradition,
For tradition often embodies
Both the beauty and the duty
Of God Himself.

19186

How can you make
Any satisfactory progress
If you do not or cannot believe
That your Master always understands you
To the core?

19187

If you cannot banish desire
From your God-searching mind
And God-pleasing heart,
How can you have
Divinity's satisfaction-joy?

19188

My Lord Supreme,
When I soulfully meditate,
Please bless me with only one boon:
Grant me the ability to immerse myself
In the divine sublimity
Of my God-manifesting soul.

19189

I must shatter
My old doubts and fears
That God-realisation
And God-manifestation
Are not meant for me.
