22760

If we do not regularly
Pray and meditate,
Then our God-realisation-hope
Will fight against ignorance-night
Only to die.

22761

When the heart is silent,
God is seen to be
Profoundly occupied inside it.

22762

When the mind is ready and eager
For the fulfilment of God-duty,
The Goal itself
Most affectionately beckons the mind.

22763

An unconditionally surrendered soul
Is selected by God Himself
As the example of perfect Perfection.

22764

Why should you cry?
Why should you die?
God has not yet pronounced your life
A sad and total failure.

22765

Each aspiring heart
Has its own eagerness
For God's Arrival-Hour.

22766

Poor God!
Even His Patience
Has now begun to despair
Because of my mind's constant refusal
To accept light.

22767

When I deliberately do not care
For my spiritual life,
Not only the divine in me,
But also God Himself,
Is puzzled and disturbed.

22768

There are a few things
That God particularly dislikes.
One of them
Is my insincere self-abnegation.

22769

Alas, my heart's gratitude-flower
Has not yet made its appearance
In my life.

22770

God never denies me opportunities.
It is I who never avail myself
Of my God-given opportunities.

22771

Among life's spiritual disasters,
Negativity
Is by far the quickest.

22772

Today
My heart is all sadness
Because, contrary to its own conviction,
My Lord Supreme is telling my heart
To raise its aspiration-standard.

22773

My mind's sincerity-smile
Holds even God
Fascinated and spellbound.

22774

The God-experience is guaranteed
If the mind is absolutely sincere
And the heart is entirely pure.
