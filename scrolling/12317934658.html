La Foi Du Coeur Propose

December 31st, 1969

La foi du coeur propose.
Le doute du mental oppose.

Translation

The confidence of the heart proposes.
The doubt of the mind opposes.

Le Doute Humain Voyage Vite, Mais La

December 31st, 1969

Le doute humain voyage vite,
Mais la grace divine le rattrape toujours.

Translation

Human doubt travels very fast,
Yet the divine Grace overtakes it.

L'Aspiration Apparait Rapidement

December 31st, 1969

L'aspiration apparait rapidement tandis que
Le desir disparait lentement.

Translation

Aspiration’s existence quickly appears
When desire's existence slowly expires.

La Sincerite Protege Notre Vie. La Purete

December 31st, 1969

La sincerite protege notre vie.
La purete la perfectionne.

Translation

Sincerity is our life's protector.
Purity is our life's perfector.

Aimer Dieu Est Une Opportunite

December 31st, 1969

Aimer Dieu est une opportunite.
Etre aime par Lui est une necessite.

Translation

Opportunity means to love God
And necessity means to be loved by God.

Celui Qui Aime Ne Vieillit Jamais

December 31st, 1969

Celui qui aime ne vieillit jamais.
Dieu en est la preuve eclatante.

Translation

He who loves never grows old.
God is a shining example.

L'Effort Humain Est Necessaire

December 31st, 1969

L'effort humain est necessaire.
La grace divine est indispensable.

Translation

Self-effort is necessary.
God’s Grace is indispensable.

L'Ame Est Preparation

December 31st, 1969

L'ame est preparation.
La vie est determination.
Le temps est perfection.
