8 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I have looked far beyond the clouds,
And I wish to tell You
What I have discovered.
I have discovered
Tomorrow's brightest sunshine.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

8 July 1999, 8:48 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 210 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

9 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Do give my heart the capacity
And opportunity
To completely surrender to Your Will.
Do cast aside
My world-supremacy-desiring mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

9 July 1999, 8:25 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 190 lbs. with each arm separately.

9 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart grow into
An ever-blossoming devotion
To worship
Your Golden Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

9 July 1999, 8:39 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

9 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I am extremely, extremely, extremely
Grateful to You
For giving me the opportunity
To decorate every day
A new heart-home for You.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

9 July 1999, 8:49 a.m. Before lifting 350 lbs. sixty times with one arm. (29 right, 31 left)

10 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall pray to You,
I shall meditate on You,
I shall love You
And I shall serve You
Even long after I have made
My surrender complete.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

10 July 1999, 8:16 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

10 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord Beloved Supreme,
My sleepless devotion to You
Is my mind's newness,
My heart's sweetness
And my life's fulness.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

10 July 1999, 8:24 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

10 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
To realise You,
What I need is inner wealth:
Aspiration, and nothing else.
The outer wealth
Is infinitely worse than useless.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

10 July 1999, 8:35 a.m. Before lifting 350 lbs. fifty-four times with one arm. (27 right, 27 left)

10 July 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I am receiving
Your divine Message of beauty
And Your supreme Message of duty
From everything
That I am looking at.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

10 July 1999, 9:21 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

11 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
What we need is faith in our mind.
What we need is love in our heart.
Lo, God-realisation
Does not remain any more
An impossible task.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

11 July 1999, 8:34 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 85 lbs. with one arm.

11 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
My Supreme, You are
My Lord Beloved Absolute Supreme.
This is my realisation,
And this realisation throughout Eternity
Shall remain with me.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

11 July 1999, 8:56 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 900 lbs.

11 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
There was a time when I was
A part-time God-seeker,
God-lover and God-server.
To my greatest joy, I have now become
A full-time God-seeker,
God-lover and God-server.
My Supreme, may my gratitude to You
Remain sleepless and breathless
Throughout Eternity.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

11 July 1999, 9:32 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. sixty-six times with one arm. (33 right, 33 left)

12 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Indeed, the smiles of my life
Are beautiful and inspiring,
But the tears of my heart
Are exquisite and perfect.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

12 July 1999, 8:36 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 190 lbs. with each arm separately.

12 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart, my faith and I
Shall be satisfied
Only after
You have illumined
Our unlit mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

12 July 1999, 8:48 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

12 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Your absolute Commands
Are my life's
Only true fulfilment.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

12 July 1999, 8:59 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. fifty-seven times with one arm. (27 right, 30 left)

13 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
The very sight of Your Lotus-Feet
Thrills my soul, my heart,
My mind, my vital, my body
And my entire being.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

13 July 1999, 10:00 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.
