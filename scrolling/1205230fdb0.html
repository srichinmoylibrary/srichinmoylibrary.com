19460

When it is a question of happiness,
You will find it inside
Your world-service-wisdom.

19461

God invites
A tiny drop of enthusiasm
From me
To become part and parcel
Of His Heart's Ecstasy-Sky.

19462

A reasoning mind
Is forced to play the role
Of a failed character
In God's Cosmic Play.

19463

God made forgiveness enough
For each and every human being,
But alas,
Nobody cares to use it.

19464

My Lord Supreme,
Your Heart of Forgiveness-Concern
Has removed my old human cravings.
My life shall no more fly on wings
Of desire-temptation-frustration.

19465

O my seeker-friend,
Be patient.
Your cloudy inner days
Cannot and will not last
For long.

19466

You can save yourself
From desire-dust
Only by releasing your heart-joy
From your mind-prison.

19467

I don't have to go far,
I don't have to go high,
But I have to go deep within
To satisfy my God-hunger.

19468

For my sake
My mind and I
Constantly want to be great.
For God's sake
My heart and I
Perpetually long to be good.

19469

If we can make
Unconditional surrender to God's Will,
Then all our burdens
Will turn into gains.

19470

God's Forgiveness
And my heart's secret and sacred joy
Together go
To accomplish something divinely great
And supremely good.

19471

Drink, drink
God's Compassion-Light
From His Blessing-Cup!

19472

No peace-traveller
In the aspiration-world
Will ever return home
Empty-handed.

19473

As I love
God's Compassion-Eye,
Even so I love
My gratitude-heart,
For together
They make me complete and perfect.

19474

Each soul
Flies from place to place
With God-manifestation-messages
Every day.
