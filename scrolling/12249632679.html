Pran Gabhire Jete Habe

July 12th, 2006

Translation:

I shall have to dive deep within,
   Inside my heart.
There only I shall be able to meet with
   My inner sun.

Manbona Ar Manbona

July 12th, 2006

Manbona ar manbona go khudra amir shasan
Aji hate karbo shudhu brihat amir sadhan

Translation:

I shall no longer tolerate the torture of the little ‘i’.
The little ‘i’ is ‘I and mine’.
From today on I shall listen to the big ‘I’.
The big ‘I’ is the universal ‘I’ —
“I am of God, I am for God.”
