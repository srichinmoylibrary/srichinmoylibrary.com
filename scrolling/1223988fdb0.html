Aum Pranaya Namaha

July 29th, 1992

Aum Pranaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the Supreme who owns
The Universal Life-Breath.

Aum Krishnayam Namaha

July 29th, 1992

Aum Krishnayam namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the Existence-Consciousness-Bliss.

Aum Rishikeshaya Namaha

July 29th, 1992

Aum rishikeshaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the Supreme Ruler of the senses.

Aum Dhatre Namaha

July 29th, 1992

Aum dhatre namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the all-nourisher Supreme.

Aum Shivaya Namaha

July 29th, 1992

Aum Shivaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the Lord Shiva,
The silence-flooded trance.

Aum Nandanaya Namaha

August 4th, 1992

Aum nandanaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the One who gives Delight to all.

Aum Anandaya Namaha

August 4th, 1992

Aum Anandaya namaha

Translation

I prayerfully bow
To the One who is Infinite Bliss Supreme.

Aum Aparajitaya Namaha

August 4th, 1992

Aum aparajitaya namaha
