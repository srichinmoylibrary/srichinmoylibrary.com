19345

Each time I become a desire-victim,
I go farther and farther away
From my Lord's Compassion-Eye
And Perfection-Feet.

19346

Each time you disappoint your soul,
You get a sound scolding
From your soul,
Although you may not be aware of it.
When the scoldings do not produce any effect,
There comes a time when the soul dances
Its destruction-dance
In your life.

19347

Everybody can claim to be
A God-seeker.
Everybody cannot claim to be
A God-server.

19348

God loves those
Who have constant faith-energy
And faith-dynamism
In God and for God.

19349

The obedience-passengers
In the Golden Boat
Are the true pride
Of the Golden Boatman.

19350

God sings a Satisfaction-Song
At the end
Of each self-giving deed of ours.

19351

A spiritual Master is the collector
Of countless experiences
From countless lives.

19352

My Lord Supreme tells me
In strict confidence
That my life's perfection will dawn
Only when I see cheerfully
His blessingful and purposeful Presence
In all human beings with no exception.
Needless to say,
This includes my enemies.

19353

Aspiration-opportunity,
Dedication-opportunity,
Manifestation-opportunity:
These three are indeed
God's unconditional Blessing-Boons.

19354

The very purpose of time
Is to lead mankind forward,
Not backward,
To its supreme destination.

19355

Love, devotion, surrender:
These three divine boons
Not only can save humanity,
But also can raise humanity
To the sky of Infinity's Delight.

19356

Immortal progress
Belongs to
Eternity's self-offering.

19357

Today is the day
For me to claim God
As my own, very own.
Today is the day
For me to prove to God
And the whole world
That I live on earth
Only for God-manifestation
And God-Satisfaction.

19358

When wisdom dawns,
We invite our new
Aspiration-brothers and sisters
To replace our old desire-friends.

19359

God is not the one who recognises
My earthly calendar-age.
God is the one who tells me
I live inside the heart
Of Eternity's here and now.
