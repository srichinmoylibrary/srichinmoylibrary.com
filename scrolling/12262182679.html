My Ancient Sadness

December 31st, 1969

My ancient sadness still
Haunts me, my soul.
I have not heard from God
My cosmic role.

Buddha under the Tree

December 31st, 1969

Buddha under the tree
Drank deep the fount of Light.
You can do your task.
You fell the Titan tree
Of eyeless ignorance-night.

A Too Familiar Song

December 31st, 1969

A too familiar song:
“I do not know.”
A too unfamiliar song:
“My life shall grow;
My heart shall glow.”

A Silver Cry

December 31st, 1969

A silver cry
Has lifted my psychic sky
High, very high.
A silver cry
Tells me how, when and why
I shall in Heaven’s vaults fly.

Two Are the Tutors I Need

December 31st, 1969

Two are the tutors I need:
Faith-beauty’s cry,
Love-duty’s sky,
To feed my perfection-need.

My Heart — Swimming in Silver Light

December 31st, 1969

My heart —
Swimming in silver light.
My soul —
Swimming in ecstasy’s height.

Your Lively Lustre

December 31st, 1969

Your lively lustre
Challenges death in life.
Your lovely life
Destroys the breath of strife.

Flow Forth My Tears

December 31st, 1969

Flow forth my tears!
My promise I fail to keep.
I shall go back secretly
To ignorance-sleep.

O Silence-Speed

December 31st, 1969

O silence-speed,
I love your beauty’s flow.
O silence-seed,
I love your duty’s glow.

Lord, Give Me Your Speed

December 31st, 1969

Lord, give me Your Speed,
I need, I badly need.
Lord, give me Your Poise,
My heart’s only choice.

Come Out and Bloom

December 31st, 1969

Come out and bloom:
No doom, no doom.
Come out and glow:
No blow, no blow.

Because I Love Your Soul

December 31st, 1969

Because I love your soul,
You will win again.
Because I love your life,
Yours will be one life
Of constant gain.

Come, Come to Me

December 31st, 1969

Come, come to me.
I shall give you
My ecstasy-sea.
Come, come to me.

My Face Tells Me

December 31st, 1969

My face tells me
How far I am, how far.
Your Grace shows me
How close, how kind You are.

Each Day I Die with Sleep

December 31st, 1969

Each day I die with sleep, each day.
Each day I forget my God-Vision-Ray.
