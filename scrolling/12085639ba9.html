22745

Let failure say
Whatever it wants.
I shall never surrender
To the pangs of failure,
But to the happiness of triumph:
God-triumph in me,
Through me and for me.

22746

God never forces me.
He does not even force
My wild ego
To surrender to Him.

22747

My Lord,
There was a time
When I prayed to You
To bless me with what You have.
But now I am praying to You
To bless me with what You are:
Love.

22748

Alas, how can I succeed
When I try to liberate myself
With fragile hopes
And when I try to bind God
With brittle promises?

22749

Alas, why does my aspiration
So often disappear
Like a quicksilver dream?

22750

I know each individual human being
Will one day be blessed
With the nectar-bliss
Of God's Dream.
But, alas, when?

22751

Nothing mortal can ever
Be enduring.
Nothing immortal can ever
Be hiding.

22752

In our spiritual life,
When we lose a material object,
It is no loss.
Rather, it can be a great advantage.

22753

Never swerve
From your God-satisfaction-promise,
Either today or any day.

22754

What can poor God do
When a genuine seeker
Deliberately enjoys unspiritual things?
He just watches
In sad amazement.

22755

The beauty
Of hope's outer life
And the fragrance
Of hope's inner life
Are keeping humanity sane.

22756

God can never and will never
Postpone
Any seeker's God-realisation.

22757

No matter how unaspiring
The human mind is,
It is not yet considered hopeless
By God.

22758

The mind's sincerity
And the heart's purity
Have to be kept alive all the time.
If not,
The aspiration-flames can perish
In a single moment.

22759

When God teaches my heart its duty,
My heart always willingly, eagerly
And self-givingly
Learns it.
