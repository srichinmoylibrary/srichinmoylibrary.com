24415

He who has become
A victim of failure
In the inner life
Has already separated his very existence
From the boundless Love of God.

24416

Just because
It has never happened before
That I have loved God
For God's sake,
That does not mean
I shall never be able
To love Him in His own Way.

24417

When I go down on my knees
And ask God for help,
He says to me
Most compassionately and affectionately,
"My child,
What have I been doing all along?"

24418

During my deep meditation,
I clearly see
That I am participating with the sky
In singing its freedom-songs.

24419

My gratitude-heart
Always has a free access
To God's
Peace and Bliss-Treasures.

24420

God tells me I need only
My old faith-friend.
I really do not need
Any other friend,
Specially not my new friend,
Self-doubt.

24421

Each God-lover
Has entered into the world
To propagate as fast as possible,
On a large scale,
God's Views with regard to His creation.

24422

My soul believes
In only one pilgrimage,
And that pilgrimage
Is to God's Compassion-flooded Feet.

24423

What is surrender?
Surrender is something
In which gratitude
Can take birth.

24424

My Lord,
Do give me the capacity
With my prayerful smiles
To wipe the Tears
From Your Compassion-Eye.

24425

Spirituality
Will never be out of date
If Mother Earth always has
Even a few thousand God-lovers.

24426

It is our outer lives
And never our inner lives
That contradict one another.
In our inner lives
We are all one,
All trying to illumine the world
And fulfil God's Message-Light
Here on earth.

24427

Our obedience-mind
Gets the greatest opportunity
To become one, inseparably one
With the gratitude-breath
Of our heart.

24428

O earth-bound worldly knowledge,
Can you not see
Once and for all
That I am suffocated
By your ignorance-darkness-night?

24429

What God has been trying to tell me
Over the years
Is that I can become
A choice and perfect instrument
Of His.
