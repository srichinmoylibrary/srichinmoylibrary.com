13030

My aspiration-elevator is ascending fast,
Not because I have done something great
But because I have made
A solemn promise to God
That I am ready to make
My unconditional surrender to Him.

13031

My heart's every teardrop
Gives birth to
A rainbow-radiance-smile.

13032

God always remains
His Compassion-Eye.
Man remains quite often
An ungrateful heart.

13033

If you really want
Your own perfection
And the perfection
Of the whole world,
Then how can you even dream
Of living on
An island of isolation?

13034

What I need
And what I shall always need
Is an undying surrender-smile
To God's Will.

13035

What does the mind do?
It cleverly separates
The satisfaction-breath
From my aspiration-heart.

13036

Every day you must carefully cross
The willingness-bridge
Between your God-hunger-heart
And your God-fulfilment-life.

13037

Victory is destined, but when?
Not until you have become
The breath of doubt-defying faith.

13038

Everything in life needs
Exercise and practice,
Practice and exercise,
Save and except the human ego.
What it needs and deserves
Is starvation-death!

13039

Two divine realities in me
Do not need appointments to see God:
My gratitude-heart
And my surrender-life.

13040

May every aspiration-morning
Of my life
Be my soul's
God-fulfilment-fragrance-joy.

13041

Now that you have demolished
Your self-importance-tower,
You will be speedily approaching
Your God-realisation-border.

13042

Alas! The disobedience of my vital
Appoints the arrogance-giant
To disappoint God.

13043

I need to treasure only one thing
Forever and forever,
And that is my Lord's Forgiveness-Gift.

13044

A neglected aspiration-tree,
A neglected dedication-flower
And a neglected manifestation-fruit
Will all decay.
Therefore, O seeker,
Be wise, be wise, be wise!
