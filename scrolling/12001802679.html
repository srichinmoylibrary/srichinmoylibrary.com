14415

The life of spirituality
Not only invites possibility
But also transforms possibility
Into manifested reality.

14416

Your sweet Heaven-memories
Will enter into your mind
If you can awaken and preserve
The beauty of your purity-heart.

14417

Shatter, shatter
Your impossibility-fear
And your impossibility-challenges
Both in your inner life
And in your outer life!

14418

God is so sad
That your aspiration-candles
Have been extinguished
By your world-desire-life.

14419

If you can nurture
Your devotion-bud,
Your realisation-blossoms
Will be seen before long.

14420

Even God's Compassion-Eye
Does not care to read
Your ceaseless
Daily ignorance-multiplying reports.

14421

My Lord,
Do give me the capacity
To turn my heart's wish-power
Into my life's will-power.

14422

Because you have lost
Your enthusiasm-heart,
Your life's progress-tide
Is ebbing.

14423

A seeker has to start
His spiritual journey
With a ceaselessly dreaming
Oneness-heart.

14424

A self-giver
Does not have to pay anything
When he shops at God's
Heart-Mart.

14425

Use your hungry God-hunger-heart.
You will easily be able to rescue
All your long-lost
God-manifestation-dreams.

14426

I am breathing today
New, soulful and powerful hopes
Into my old, decayed and fading dreams.

14427

Once you find yourself
In the mind's worry-boat,
It is extremely difficult
To jump out of it.

14428

God does not care for you.
He cares for your heart,
Although it is as dry as dust.

14429

Just one pure celestial thought
Of your mind
Can satisfy God's great Hunger.
