17830

The mind's negativity-capacity
Is no match
For the heart's God-receptivity.

17831

Love God the Silence
Within.
Manifest God the Sound
Without.

17832

I am so happy
That every morning
My soul, my heart, my mind,
My vital and my body
Enjoy a unity-train ride.

17833

A soundless prayer
Is an endless
God-Compassion-achievement.

A breathless meditation
Is an ever-transcending
God-Satisfaction-unfoldment.

17834

There should be
No empty room
In a seeker's
Aspiration-heart-home.

17835

Death without God
Is as untrue
As life without God.

17836

You can break
All your life's attachment-chains
Only when your heart becomes
An unconditional God-obedience-smile.

17837

Today I am locking
For good
My mind's ingratitude-door.

17838

An unaspiring mind
Deliberately enjoys
A ceaseless doubt-downpour.

17839

O my mind,
Do not descend
Into the chasm
Of ingratitude-frown.

17840

Your heart-dreams
Will soon without fail
Awaken you
From your age-long mind-sleep.

17841

A new day
Is a new way
To participate in
God's new Cosmic Play.

17842

God's silent Heart-Wish:
Each human being must have
A transformed mind
Inside a perfect heart.

17843

From earth
Frustration goes up.
From Heaven
Indifference comes down.

17844

He is
His God-loving heart
To touch
The God-Love-Breath.
