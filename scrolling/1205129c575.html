19390

May my hope grow.
May my promise glow.
May my compassion flow.

19391

O my aspiration-heart,
Show no compassion to my mind
When it deliberately wants
To be surrounded
By the thickest ignorance-fog.

19392

When I am in my ignorance-mind,
I ask the world to change for me.
When I am in my wisdom-heart,
I ask the world to change only for God.

19393

I am entranced
Not by God's Justice-Light,
But by His Forgiveness-Delight.

19394

The human mind is made of
Godless and aimless centuries.
The human heart is made of
God-aspiration and
God-dedication-millennia.

19395

God-obedience expedites
Our life-boat-journey
Far, far beyond our imagination.

19396

Your mind-prayer cannot grant you
Silence-perfection
And silence-satisfaction.
Your heart-meditation
Can and does.

19397

The expectation-mind
Is sooner or later responsible
For a frustration-life.

19398

Self-doubt
Tortures hope-plants
And uproots confidence-trees.

19399

O ignorance-night,
I am cancelling once and for all
All our forthcoming appointments.

19400

At every moment,
Each time we breathe in
Or breathe out,
May our life-breath sing
The supreme Victory-Song
Of our Lord Beloved Supreme.
