18560

Use your heart
To love God more and more,
Infinitely more.
Your mind's God-fear-barrier
Will disappear.

18561

My Lord,
Out of His infinite Bounty,
Has made me a free member
Of His twenty-four-hour
Tolerance-Enterprise.

18562

Spirituality is neither
Obligation nor fascination.
Spirituality is
The seeker-heart's God-perfection-dream.

18563

My heart is
A born-again God-lover.
My life is
A born-again God-server.

18564

Not only the cosmic gods
But also the God Supreme
Will give you a standing ovation
If your life becomes
A ceaseless God-satisfaction-song.

18565

God's Heart
Wants me to wake up.
God's Eye
Wants me to stand up.
And God
Wants me to speak up.

18566

O God-seekers of the world,
Perhaps you do not know
That God depends on you
Infinitely more than you can imagine.

18567

O my unwillingness-mind,
I am sure that
Soon your fate will change
And you will long for
God's Compassion-descending Rain.

18568

It is only
When I leave my mind
Outside my heart-temple
That I am allowed to come in
To pray, meditate and serve.

18569

Obedience-oars:
This is what your mind needs.
Surrender-sails:
This is what your heart needs -
Nothing less and nothing more.

18570

Don't allow your mind
To be a self-doubt-ocean
If you want to live
Inside the heart of ecstasy-sea.

18571

Each man thinks
That he is
An indispensable human being.
I am so proud of God
That He does not
See eye to eye with us.

18572

O my mind,
Who are you to question God
And who are you to ignore God?
You and your own ignorance
Will strangle each other soon!

18573

Cure
Your inner despair-malady!
Lo, you have become the possessor
Of an outer hope-melody.

18574

May my heart's hope-rainbow
Shine bright
Above my mind-clouds.
