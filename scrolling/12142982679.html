915.

No thrill is as illumining
And as fulfilling
As one's own self-transcendence-thrill.

916.

When a seeker's life
Becomes empty of hope,
His heart's God-realisation-cry ends.

917.

I do not want to be
An independent mind.
I want to be
A God-dependent child.

918.

Two friends
Who consciously and proudly love each other:
Desire and bondage.

919.

A perfection-life
Is indeed living
A philosophy of the Beyond.

920.

If you do not have a searching mind
And a crying heart,
Then your life will be shrouded
In veils of illusion and delusion.

921.

God's Heart
And God's Heart-School
Are open
To all and sundry.

922.

My heart's God-longing tree
Must grow high and wide
At every moment.

923.

A satisfaction-heart
Belongs equally
To both Heaven and earth.

924.

My enthusiasm-mind
Must become once more young
To sing and dance with God's
All-illumining and all-fulfilling Heart.

925.

How can a God-willingness-heart
Be empty
Of God-manifestation-strength?
No, it is impossible.

926.

Today I will make a huge effort
To unite my inner-world smiles
And my outer-world tears.

927.

My self-effacement
Is another name
For my God-enlightenment.

928.

My mind's
Misunderstanding-nightmare
Has shaken my world
To its foundations.

929.

I assure my aspiration-heart
That there can never be any shortage
Of God-Peace-supplies.
