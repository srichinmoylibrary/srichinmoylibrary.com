Dirgha Sharani Hai Harit Bale Aguan

July 14th, 2006

Dirgha sharani hai harit bale aguan aguan
Thamo thamo thamo taba lal bale sabdhan sabdhan

Translation:

The road is long, very long.
My Lord’s green tells me,
“Come forward, come forward.”
“Stop, stop, stop!” says my Lord’s red.
“Be careful, be careful.”
