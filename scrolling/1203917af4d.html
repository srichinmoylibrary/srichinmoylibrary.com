18175

When self-offering
Is sleepless,
Nothing fruitful
Can remain unattained.

18176

Each aspiration-blossom
Of man
Will be immortalised
By God
With His Pride divine.

18177

What we need
At every moment
Is an iota of humility-peace
To make us happy.

18178

It takes great courage
To become sincere.
It takes greater courage
And greatest courage
To become holy.

18179

God loves two things:
Our eagerness-preparation
And our soulfulness-dedication.

18180

In the morning, inner runners
Running along the Road of Eternity
Offer their prayerful and soulful salutation
To the rising Illumination-Sun.

18181

My Lord Supreme,
Do help me reclaim
My heart's long-forgotten
Aspiration-ground.

18182

Alas,
Will it ever be over,
The doubting mind's
Suspicion-night?

18183

O my mind,
Believe me,
God is not going to jump
Into your empty excuse-pool
To save you.

18184

If you walk
On the aspiration-road,
Dedication-travellers
Will accompany you.

18185

If you are a true seeker,
You must bravely remain
Above the smoke
Of past experiences.

18186

My unconditional surrender
Is nothing other than
My heart's confidence-freedom
In God.

18187

I am a God-believer.
At God's choice Hour,
The answers to all my life-problems
Will descend from Above.

18188

When I pray,
God gives me
The Joy of His Eye.
When I meditate,
God gives me
The Love of His Heart.

18189

If we do not pray,
Then desire will lead us
To an unattainable shoreless shore.
