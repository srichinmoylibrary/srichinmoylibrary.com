27 September 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My today's self-giving dreams
Shall become
My tomorrow's God-satisfying realities.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999, 9:31 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 700 lbs.

27 September 1999 (VII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I tell God what I can do:
I can pray and meditate for hours
Every day.
God tells me what I must do:
I must sing His Victory-Songs
Sleeplessly.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999

27 September 1999 (VIII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord tells me
That in the inner world
There is nothing to discover,
Only to uncover.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 800 lbs.

27 September 1999 (IX)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God tells me that my mind-voice
Hurts Him badly
Because it is so sharp.
He also tells me that my heart-voice
Gives Him tremendous joy
Because it is so sweet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999. Before using seated calf raise machine.

28 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May Your blessingful Presence
And Your cheerful Guidance
Play hide-and-seek
Inside my aspiration-heart-temple.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 September 1999, 8:06 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

28 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall never, never try
To understand You.
This is my sole promise to You.
My Supreme, do increase my faith
At every moment
In Your Compassion-Eye,
Forgiveness-Heart
And Protection-Feet.
This is my sole prayer to You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 September 1999, 8:16 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

28 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Patience, patience,
I need patience
For the complete fulfilment
Of Your Will
In and through me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 September 1999, 8:21 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 500 lbs.)

28 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must realise
That You will come to me
At Your choice Hour —
Neither too soon nor too late!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 September 1999, 8:29 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 600 lbs.)

28 September 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O my unconditional surrender
To God's Will,
You are my heart's only joy,
You are my life's only satisfaction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 September 1999, 8:41 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-eight times. (33 right, 35 left)

28 September 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am offering You my prayerful gratitude
For touching my tearful heart.
I am offering You my soulful gratitude
For allowing me to sit
At Your blessingful Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 September 1999, 9:04 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

30 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My self-doubt is a ruthless insult
To the fulfilment
Of Your Vision-Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 September 1999, 8:29 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

30 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Self-doubt is indeed
An almost incurable disease.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 September 1999, 8:34 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 500 lbs.)

30 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My self-doubt takes me away
Farther than the farthest
From Your Compassion-Eye.
Alas, alas!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 September 1999, 8:41 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

30 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O self-doubt,
Get out of my breath,
Get out of my heart,
Get out of my mind,
Get out of my vital,
Get out of my body,
Get out of my earth-existence-life.
Get out, get out immediately,
O self-doubt!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 September 1999, 8:49 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-six times. (33 right, 33 left)

30 September 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I enjoy my self-doubts,
You pitifully cry,
You helplessly shed tears.
Alas, alas,
This is what I do to You,
My Lord Beloved Supreme!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 September 1999, 9:10 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 70 lbs. (94 reps)
