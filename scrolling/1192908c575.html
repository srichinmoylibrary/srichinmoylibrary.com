7290

Each silence-moment
Is a supremely glorious
Aspiration-ascent.

7291

Can you not ask your mind
To do you a big favour
And exile all impurities?

7292

Look what your stark stupidity
Has done!
It has overthrown
Your Lord's Compassion-Eye.

7293

Your prayerful life
And your soulful heart
Do have the capacity
To tame the temptation-tiger
For you.

7294

The life-boat
Of a gratitude-heart
Is always tied
To God's supreme Satisfaction-Ship.

7295

Ask your purity-heart
To feed your faith.
Ask your clarity-mind
To starve your fear.

7296

Every day
My mind goes to the hospital
To visit with the sinners.
Every day
My heart goes to school
To study with the God-lovers.

7297

Can you not see
That your wild desire-life
Has led you
To a dark frustration-destruction?

7298

Alas, every day
My mind is engulfed
In a shoreless sea
Of complaints!

7299

You are right.
God loves you dearly.
You are perfect.
God needs you daily.

7300

God's Heart-Door
Is always wide open
For you to come and go.
But if you are wise,
You will come only to stay.
