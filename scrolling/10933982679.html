13 The Hour Of God ...

The Hour of God
Accepts
No excuses.

14 Who Is My Lord Supreme?...

Who is my Lord Supreme?
My Lord Supreme is He
Whose Concern for me
Echoes and re-echoes
From sky to sky.

15 Nobody Could Stand Against Me ...

Nobody could stand against me
When God's God-blossoming Moment
Began inside my heart.

16 My Lord, I Know, I...

My Lord, I know, I know,
Your Tolerance
Of my disobedience-life
Has cost You dearly.

17 My God-Gratitude ...

My God-gratitude
Is my divinity's
Only source.

12 December 1999 ...

12 DECEMBER 1999

SĂO MORITZ, BRAZIL

18 My Heart ...

My heart
Is a God-aspiration-choice.
My life
Is a God-manifestation-voice.

19 May My Master's Eye ...

May my Master's eye
Proudly occupy
Every space in my heart.

20 I Was My Mind-Dryness, ...

I was my mind-dryness,
But now I am
My Lord's Compassion-Rain.

21 I Was Born To Smile ...

I was born to smile
At God the Creator
And God the creation,
But alas,
My life's sadness-clouds
Are hanging in my heart-sky.

22 My Lord, ...

My Lord,
All I have
Is ignorance-frown.
All I need
Is Your Compassion-Smile.

23 Today My Soul ...

Today my soul
Is smiling and dancing
Inside my heart-tears.

24 Today God Wants My Heart ...

Today God wants my heart
To be only one thing:
A roaring strength of cheerfulness.

25 Today God Wants My Mind ...

Today God wants my mind
To be only one thing:
An ever-climbing consciousness-altitude.

26 I Must Not Allow My Days And...

I must not allow my days and nights
To sleep any more
Inside my idle eyes.
