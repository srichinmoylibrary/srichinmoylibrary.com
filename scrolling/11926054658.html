6930

If you wait and wait
To break your fate,
Your fate will destroy
Your soulful and powerful
Inner capacities.

6931

Who told you
That your fate can never be changed?
Fate is destined to be changed,
For God does need you
As His supreme partner.

6932

Know for certain
That the hour has come for you
To discard your desire-life
And embrace your aspiration-life.

6933

The unaspiring human mind
And impossibility
Are two eternal friends
And are fond of living together.

6934

You want
The vision of victory
Without the willingness
Of aspiration.
Indeed, an impossible task!

6935

His heart is too pure
To be jealous.
His mind is too strong
To be doubtful.
His life is too beautiful
To be imperfect.

6936

He wants to possess
And be possessed by the world.
Unconditional self-giving
Is not his life-style.

6937

His aspiring heart
Lives on hope.
His unaspiring mind
Lives on the rumours of hope.

6938

God for God's sake
Is a most difficult experience
For a desire-man to have.

6939

If you think that your future
Will be infinitely heavier
Than your present,
Then meditate on God soulfully.
He will cheerfully place
Your future-weight
On His Shoulder.

6940

All along
You have heard of God,
You have talked about God.
Can you not try, from now on,
To become sleeplessly one
With God?

6941

Although he has allowed his mind
To be drowned in destructive thoughts,
He has kept his purity-heart
Only for his Beloved Supreme
To enjoy.

6942

The perfection of life
Can never be
An earthly chance,
But only
A heavenly choice.

6943

Do you not see
That the Satisfaction-Smile
Of God's Compassion-Eye
In you
Has completely stopped?

6944

Do not worry
If you have lost God's Message
In your mind's ignorance-mire.
Just ask your heart
To beg God
To grant you another Message.
But be sure, this time,
Not to lose it!
