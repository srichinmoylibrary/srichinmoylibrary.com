10801

Every day collect love-flowers
From your heart-garden
And place them on your soul's
Peace-shrine
As a prayer to your Beloved Supreme.

10802

How can you expect to get
God's Compassion-Sun
With your feeble heart's
Tight budget of gratitude?

10803

He who has a great appetite
For flattery
Will never be able to learn
The art of self-mastery.

10804

Two insoluble problems:
How can the doubting mind
Ever be illumined?
How can the insecure heart
Ever become indomitable?

10805

If you are not acquainted
With God's Forgiveness-Light,
You will always be found
Inside your mind's prison cell.

10806

Poor human mind!
What else does it know
Except
Thought-mud-pleasure?

10807

Each soul is God's
Direct representative on earth.
Therefore
It is authorised to exercise
Its God-ordained dignity.

10808

Little drops of gratitude-rain
Can liberate the human mind
From all its bondage-chains.

10809

The human mind is afraid of
The tormenting conscience.
The divine heart is fond of
The illumining conscience.

10810

Each man is fallible,
Yet
Each man will eventually become
God's perfect representative
On earth.

10811

When God sees you at His Heart's Door,
Be sure you are not accompanied by
Your hesitation-eye
And your confusion-mind.

10812

If your meditation
Is truly high and deep,
Then you are bound to have
A silent dialogue with peace.

10813

Nobody wants you to keep track of
Your inactive file of lethargy.
Both Heaven and earth want you
To be a dynamic action-hero.

10814

If you are a true God-lover,
Then it is quite possible
For you to foresee
That in the battle between
Aspiration-heart and suspicion-mind,
Aspiration-heart will eventually win.
