19690

If I do not make God happy
In His own Way,
Then no matter what I achieve,
It will not give me joy,
For the indifferent Heaven
And the ruthless earth
Will torture my heart.

19691

Ignorance and I are such good friends.
Although I know perfectly well
That my relationship with ignorance
Will eventually end,
I still have tremendous fondness
For the temptation-flooded ignorance-life.

19692

If we do the right thing
At the right moment
For the right person, God,
Then we do not have to wait for Eternity
For His Eternity's Satisfaction-Smile.

19693

If you are a genuine seeker,
You will not feel that
You are crippled
By fruitless hopes.
On the contrary,
Your aspiration-life
Will be tripled
By your heart's satisfaction-smile.

19694

You say that you want to enjoy
The discovery of your inner faith.
How can you then
Give so much importance
To your self-doubt-mind?

19695

To go beyond our present limits
Is always a divine achievement
And supreme fulfilment
In the Heart-Garden
Of our Eternity's Lord Supreme.

19696

The timeless hope is:
I shall be a better world-citizen.
The timeless promise is:
I shall manifest and fulfil God
In His own Way.

19697

You and your mind
Every day must try
To love God infinitely more
Than your mind wants you to.

19698

O my mind,
You are wandering everywhere.
You have made yourself
A perfect stranger to your reality-life.
When will you return to your true self
To enjoy your inner sea of peace?

19699

O my heart,
Just cry one more time
For God's Compassion-Sun to descend
And transform your breath.
Just try!
This time you are bound to succeed.

19700

God does not mind
If you do not learn
His Compassion-Enlightenment-Song,
Which is immeasurably long.
But He does mind
When you do not learn
His shorter-than-the-shortest
Gratitude-Song.
