15560

O my Lord Supreme,
May my mind become
Another obedience-angel,
Like my heart-angel.

15561

My fond memories
Of my Lord Supreme in Heaven
Are still vivid.
And it is these memories
That are sources of inspiration,
Aspiration and dedication-progress.

15562

If there is no devotion
In your meditation,
Then your meditation
Will not be able to reach
The perfection-height.

15563

Be a good inner runner.
You will be able to run away
From temptation-attacks
Easily, effectively and bravely.

15564

When God saw
My aspiration-sunset,
He cried and cried
Like a helpless child.

15565

It is not an uncommon reality.
God-representatives and Heaven-messengers
Are quite often badly misunderstood
By humanity's unlit mind.

15566

When my soul
Does something great for me
And I want to reward my soul,
My soul smilingly says,
"I don't need it!"

When my heart
Does something great for me
And I want to reward my heart,
My heart says,
"I don't expect it!"

When my mind
Does something great for me
And I want to reward my mind,
My mind takes the reward
And immediately demands something more.

15567

Alas!
My dark attachment-life
Has entangled my purity-longing
And world-oneness-longing heart.

15568

True, my past life
Did not surrender to God
And my present life
Is reluctant to surrender to God.
But my future life
Will not only surrender to God
But also promote God-Peace, God-Light
And God-Delight on earth.

15569

Your heart's gratitude-songs
Are so faint
That, sadly,
God fails to hear them.

15570

When my mind enjoys
Doubt-fiction,
My Lord Supreme gets irritated
And quickly disappears.

15571

If there is no enthusiasm
In your mind's God-search,
If there is no intensity
In your heart's surrender-life to God,
Then God-realisation will definitely remain
A far cry.

15572

When God sees my life of love
And my heart of gratitude,
He feels that His Manifestation-Dream
Is worth continuing.

15573

If your mind forgets to value God's Love,
Then you are bound to lose it, someday,
Perhaps forever.

15574

God's Compassion
Comes to inspect
My aspiration-start.

God's Compassion and God's Pride
Come to inspect
My realisation-goal.
