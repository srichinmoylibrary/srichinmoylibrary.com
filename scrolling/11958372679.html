10115

Alas, there are so many miles
Between what I deliberately
Do not want to know
And what I am supposed to know
In the inner world.

10116

If you do not offer
Your total commitment to God,
How do you expect God to grant
His full Enlightenment to you?

10117

God sings
His Silence-Songs of Delight
Only for the seekers
Who fulfil their inner and outer promises.

10118

The past is a country
Only for foreigners and strangers.
Be wise!
Do not visit that country.

10119

Where is the difference
Between your desire-thoughts
And your failure-life?
No difference!

10120

Today you are taking infant steps
Of progress.
Do not give up!
Continue!
Tomorrow you will be able to take
Giant strides
In your progress-life.

10121

One book I always want to read,
And that book is God-Eternity's
God-Satisfaction-Book.

10122

Unless you don
Your soul's purity-robe,
Your earth-life
Will not look beautiful.

10123

His Inner Pilot is pleased
With his insight.
The rest of the world is pleased
With his foresight.

10124

Sit down and listen
To your inner whispers.
Your outer life will no longer remain
A meaningless failure.

10125

Remember, remember
Your aspiration-nights
And dedication-days
And your self-giving life
To God's Compassion-Eye.

10126

Where does love grow?
Love grows on the top
Of your heart's aspiration-hill.

10127

This is the moment of choice!
Either accept the world of noise
Or accept God-Beauty's Silence-Voice.

10128

Keep your aspiration-heart-door open
So God can come in at any moment
To grant you His Satisfaction-Award.

10129

Because you are a stark fool,
You think that God's creation
Is humanity minus divinity.
