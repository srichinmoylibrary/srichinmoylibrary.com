15845

O my mind,
You are such a fool!
Can you not see
That you are chasing
The shadow of insecurity?

15846

Give what you have:
Insecurity.
Become what you are:
Wisdom.
Then you are ready
To reach for the summit
Of your soul's bliss transcendental.

15847

O my purity-heart,
I am so fond of you,
I am so proud of you!
I shall always remain
Inside you,
Not above or below you.

15848

When my mind predicts
My uncertainty-flooded future,
My loving heart
Cries and cries
And my illumining soul
Laughs and laughs.

15849

Alas, alas!
I do not know
Why and how
The mind of humanity
Has become so fatality-prone.

15850

If you are a believer,
Then immediately I love you.
If you are a doer,
Not only do I love you
But also I need you,
And broadcast and extol you
To the skies.

15851

Because your mind
Is totally empty
Of divine grace,
Your imprisoned life
Is full of disgrace.

15852

Nothing was certain
In my life
Until I met my Lord's
Forgiveness-flooded Feet
And Compassion-flooded Eyes.

15853

O my mind,
I am absolutely certain
That it is not beyond your capacity
To appreciate and admire
My all-loving heart.

15854

When your mind
Is short on sermons
And your heart
Is long on dedication-service,
Then your life is ready
For God's Perfection-Manifestation.

15855

I am so happy
That my heart has now
Two extremely divine friends:
A God-loving tear
And a God-fulfilling smile.

15856

There is only one thing
That I never want to lose:
My heart's perfection-cry
In my Lord's Satisfaction-Smile.

15857

My Lord,
Nowadays You do not fulfil my desires.
Therefore, I am leaving You.
"My child,
Do you not think
That I have the capacity
To haunt you and chase you
And bring you back?"

15858

A sleepless and breathless
God-oneness-heart
Is larger
Than ten thousand climes.

15859

If you can learn
The gratitude-heart-language,
Then God will give you
His own Infinity's Wisdom-Bliss.
