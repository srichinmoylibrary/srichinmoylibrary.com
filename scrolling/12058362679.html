20015

A pure thought
Brightens the mind,
Sweetens the heart
And enlightens the life.

20016

Be careful
Of your lethargy-body.
It can, at any moment,
Devour
Your heart's inspiration-joy.

20017

Every day
I try to celebrate
The oneness-dream of my heart
Even inside
The division-enjoyment-mind.

20018

To a seeker,
Nothing can be more precious
Than his sacred
Morning meditation-hour.

20019

We can sweeten and gladden
Our thoughts
Only with
Our heart-fragrance-prayers.

20020

It is you
Who have to push aside
Your mind's mountain-doubts
With the help of
Your soul-abundance-power.

20021

A receptivity-heart
Keeps all its inner doors open
To welcome
Its Lord Beloved Supreme.

20022

You must never allow your mind
To drag you inside
The dark, darker, darkest
Thought-tunnel.

20023

God knows
My gratitude-songs
By heart
And quite often sings them
To increase my aspiration-intensity.

20024

O my heart,
When are you going to be
The possessor of Infinity's
Self-giving and God-fulfilling
Capacities?

20025

Alas, what a rogue
The human mind is!
With its shameless doubts
It tries to harass and embarrass
The divinity of the heart.

20026

My realisation-soul
And my aspiration-heart
Have the same message:
This world of ours
Will definitely have peace one day,
For the omnipresent God
Will reveal and manifest
His universal Peace.

20027

Needless to say,
God has countless attributes.
But by far
The most significant attribute
Is peace.

20028

Peace is founded upon love.
Love is fulness-perfection
In the outer world.
Love is oneness-fragrance
In the inner world.

20029

God the Vision and God the Reality
Are inseparable.
Alas, when will man the dream
And man the reality
Become consciously, inseparably one?
