13575

Confidence comes not from self-denial.
Confidence comes not from self-assertion.
Confidence comes from self-sacrifice
And self-perfection.

13576

How many human beings can take
God-Elevator to God-Height?
None, unless God's Grace
Does the miracle of miracles.

13577

There are so many fragile things
Here on earth.
Hope is fragile,
Faith is fragile,
Gratitude is fragile,
To name a few.

13578

My heart's morning dream
For God's Happiness
Is unconditionally entering
Into my life.

13579

What can poor God do for you
If you choose every day
To knock at despair-door?

13580

There can be no time
Better than now
To please God
And be His choice instrument.

13581

You can surprise God
Not with your mind's dissatisfaction
But with your heart's satisfaction.

13582

What is human life, after all,
If not a constantly hopeless
And helpless effort
To climb the Everest-heights?

13583

If you are your mind's
Careless God-search,
How can you be your heart's
Breathless God-Satisfaction?

13584

When your heart is touched
By bitterness,
You are bound to miss
God's Nearness.

13585

A high meditation is
A soulful, peaceful and natural
God-Beauty's Heaven-Smile
In a seeker's aspiration-heart.

13586

A sleepless devotion to God
Can give us a deathless heart-smile
To transform our unlit human mind.

13587

What can meditation do?
Meditation can do many things.
In your case, your meditation
Is determined to stop your vital
From resurrecting its long-dead frustrations.

13588

God-obedience
Is nothing other than happiness.
Happiness
Is nothing other than our inner progress.
Our inner progress
God not only appreciates but also treasures.

13589

If you will regularly use
Your surrender-prayers,
Then God will turn your life
Into His Satisfaction-Tower.
