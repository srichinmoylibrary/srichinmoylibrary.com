7330

There is only one place
That is safe and perfect
And that place is
The Heart-Garden of God.

7331

The Golden Boat
Will not reach the Golden Shore
Empty.
Never!
The Pilot Supreme will carry
Blessingfully and proudly
Only those who will forever remain
Faithful, devoted and surrendered
To His Will transcendental.

7332

To disobey God
Is to feed the animal in us,
Starve the human in us
And delay the divine in us.

7333

If you have physical pain, disdain!
If you have vital pain, disdain!
If you have mental pain, disdain!
But if you have psychic pain,
Then try to gain
As much as possible from it.

7334

O my heart,
While you were out with the mind,
God came
And left a message for you:
"Be careful, heart!
You will be in very serious danger."

7335

Give God the original:
The soulful cry of your heart.
Do not give God the copy:
The wild frown of your vital.

7336

The duty of a spiritual Master
Is to teach the disciple
How to love God unconditionally,
Not how to receive love
From God cleverly.

7337

You are your life's
Attachment-bondage.
Therefore, victory's sweetness
And God's Completeness
Can never be yours.

7338

Your sincere willingness
Is itself a tremendous increase
In your inner and outer capacities.

7339

Purity at any price
In your inner life,
Sincerity at any price
In your outer life.
Then perfection will claim you
As its very own.

7340

God's Compassion-Eye
Owns my life
And just rents it to me
At a very cheap rate.

7341

Do you want to know
What you should wear
When you go to visit God?
You should wear
A gratitude-garment that covers
The entire length and breadth
Of your body.

7342

If you have a heart
Rich in divinity,
If you have a mind
Rich in sincerity,
And if you have a life
Rich in purity,
Then God will visit you
Long before you actually invoke
His Presence.

7343

Sorrows seized my heart
When I discovered
That I had been living
In a foreign land:
My suspicion-mind.

7344

If you are determined to be loyal
To your Inner Pilot,
Then He is more than ready
To help you discover
Your royal life.
