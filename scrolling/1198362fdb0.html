12660

God's Compassion-Height
Always precedes
God's Justice-Light.

12661

God-realisation means God-discovery,
And God-discovery is nothing other than
The reclaiming of one's own
Ancient God-roots.

12662

If you are an obedient soldier today,
Then tomorrow God will make you
His Cosmic Game's perfection-captain.

12663

Do not cherish your mind's
Sound-drum,
But cherish your heart's
Gratitude-silence-songs.

12664

Once you pass your heart's
Faith-examination,
God takes you to watch
His Splendour-Exhibition.

12665

I see no difference
Between a negative mind
And a poisonous life.

12666

Have faith in God's Compassion-Power.
This Power can liberate you
From your centuries-old insecurity-heart.

12667

God's Confidence-Light
Can live in me
Only when I exercise
My determination-might.

12668

If you can maintain your mind's
Monumental indifference
To earthly demands and commands,
You are bound to succeed in your inner life
Faster, infinitely faster,
Than you can ever imagine.

12669

God is always ready to remove
Bitter frustration from my mind,
But my mind cleverly hides
From God's Compassion-Protection-Eye.

12670

If your heart suffers
From purity-failure,
Then it is also bound to fail
In achieving God-oneness-satisfaction.

12671

Run at full speed
To come out of
Your self-chosen ignorance-night.
Your Lord Supreme
Is eagerly awaiting your arrival.

12672

May our human mind enjoy
The beauty of inspiration-light
And not the battle of opinion-night.

12673

If you are your searching mind,
And if you are your aspiring heart,
Then your realising life
Cannot remain a far cry.

12674

God lovingly invites
Man's happiness-heart
To come and blow
His Satisfaction-Whistle.
