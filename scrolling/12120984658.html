26230

One of the most amazing
Of God's Mysteries
Is my doubting mind's God-acceptance
In its totality.

26231

Alas, how many people are here on earth
Who are trying to manifest God's Light
Sleeplessly and breathlessly?

26232

Man's silence-God-prayers
Reach God's Ear
Sooner than the soonest.

26233

Do not allow
Your self-offering-heart-flower
Ever to be blighted.

26234

The mind-orator says:
"God has."
The heart-audience says:
"God is."

26235

I have been trying to protect
My aspiration-heart-plant.
I really must succeed!

26236

If you are a true seeker,
Then every day you must launder
Your filthy, dirty doubt-mind.

26237

Every day
I discard adamantly
The negativities of my mind.

26238

To have a soulful thought
Is infinitely more significant
Than to visit a God-worship-temple.

26239

My love of God
Will definitely play the role
Of my mind's darkness-extinguisher.

26240

Realisation is something
That spontaneously reveals itself
To us.

26241

Punctuality-necessity
In our spiritual life
Will strengthen
Our aspiration-heart.

26242

If we feel we need not surrender
Our will to God's Will,
We are bound to suffer
The nightmare of despair.

26243

O Heaven-train-passenger,
Are you carrying with you
Your heart's gratitude-ticket?
If not, you will be forced
To leave the train.

26244

May the aspiring heart of humanity
Taste Nectar-Delight
From God's Compassion-Eye.
