10401

What have I to call my own
If not the Compassion-Eye
Of my Eternity's Beloved Supreme?

10402

Remain inside your heart.
Gratitude-life
Will soon be your speciality.

10403

Because your mind-boat has no sail
You are unhappy.
Because your heart-sea has no peace
You are miserable.

10404

Indeed,
The heart's way is the better way.
But the soul's way
Is not only better
But also easier.

10405

God gives trophies
To the believers.
The devil gives crowns
To the quitters.

10406

Be not afraid
Of your mind's darkness-life.
A single aspiration-flame
Of your heart
Can illumine your darkness-mind.

10407

My Lord Supreme,
Yours alone
Is the future life
Of my earthly existence-cry.

10408

The heart of faith
Predicts.
The mind of doubt
Contradicts.
The Light of God
Illumines the mind
And fulfils the heart.

10409

Alas,
Man the stupid thinker
Ignores
God the wise Lover.

10410

Meditate on perfection.
The mind of peace
Will awaken
In your heart's perfection-dream.

10411

If you do not climb
Your own aspiration-Everest,
How can you shine
Even for a fleeting second
In God's supreme Satisfaction-Firmament?

10412

If you are asleep
In your mind's weakness-room,
Nobody will come -
No, not even God -
To wake you.

10413

Your service-life will tell you
What God is doing for you.
Your prayer-life will tell you
Where God is waiting for you.

10414

Since your mind is getting older,
Can you not ask your heart
To become bolder
And stop your mind's ruthless
Aspiration-exploitation?
