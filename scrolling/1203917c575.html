18190

Prayers
Are the beautiful wings
And meditations
Are the soulful birds
Of my heart.

18191

The journey's end of my heart
I may know someday.
The destination of my soul
Will remain forever unknowable
To me.

18192

The message of heart-expansion
Can never be false.
The heart has received this message
From the ever-transcending Beyond.

18193

God's Compassion-Sunshine
Will always bless
Our aspiration-heart-garden.

18194

There shall come a time
When the doubting mind
Will fall
At the illumining feet
Of faith.

18195

Do you want to know
What lethargy has done to you?
Lethargy has made your mind
Unthinkably impure,
And your heart
Unbearably insecure.

18196

God does not need
Your mind's explanation.
He needs only
Your heart's aspiration
To make Him happy.

18197

My Lord,
To better
My heart-life,
Do shatter
My mind-pride.

18198

Anger
Loudly demands justice.
But it is poise
That happily receives justice
From the Lord Supreme.

18199

God tells me
That if I do not study
At humility-school,
Then I will not be able
To go to Divinity-College
And Immortality-University.

18200

My Lord Supreme,
Today You have not given me something
For my own good.
But I know You will give it to me
When I am ready,
For You are all Wisdom-Compassion.
