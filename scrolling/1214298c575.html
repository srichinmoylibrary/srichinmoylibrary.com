990.

When I live in my God-doubting mind,
I fail to possess
Even a glimmer of sweetness-hope.

991.

May my heart
Sleeplessly and breathlessly be bound
To my Lord Supreme's Compassion-Feet.

992.

My heart-room's light
Shines constantly
For the transformation of my mind.

993.

Not anything else
But God's Compassion-Magnet-Eye
Has drawn me close, closer, closest
To Him.

994.

O my mind,
Make your speech egoless.
Then everyone
Will appreciate, admire and adore you.

995.

Each soul
Is born to illumine.
Each heart
Is born to love.
Each mind
Is born to search.
Each life
Is born to serve.

996.

My mind wants to become
A miracle-monger.
My heart wants to become
A God-dreamer,
A God-lover
And a God-achiever.

997.

God has bound my heart
Not with His omnipotent Power
But with His omnipresent Love.

998.

O my mind,
Can you not see that you have become
The shocking betrayal
Of your God-manifestation-promise
On earth?

999.

My heart's willingness
Shall always be equal
To any world-assignment.

1000.

God deeply loves my gratitude-heart,
But He loves
My cheerfully unconditional surrender-life
Infinitely more.
