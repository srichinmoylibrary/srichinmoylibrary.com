10701

I may not be or cannot be a member
Of God's Vision-Manifestation-team
On earth,
But I dearly love the team's
God-oneness-heart
And profoundly admire
Its God-fulness-hands.
But I do know and I do feel
That God will eventually make me
A member of His Vision-Manifestation-team.

10702

God Himself has told me
That there are no speed limits
On my self-transcendence-road.

10703

God is proud of me
Because of my life's firmness.
God is fond of me
Because of my heart's tenderness.

10704

Because I have overfed my body,
God is crying,
Standing outside my body.

Because I have starved my soul,
God is crying,
Lying inside my soul.

10705

If your unconditional surrender
Is complete, perfect and enduring,
Then you are fast nearing
Your God-realisation-zone.

10706

A barren nothingness is your life
Because you have strongly supported
Your mind's unwillingness to welcome
God's Compassion-Heart.

10707

Your mind's jealousy-train
Will never be able to reach
Your heart's purity-station.

10708

If you enjoy
Your mind's jealousy-storms,
Then God will not grant you
His Divinity-Manifestation-Dream.

10709

My soul is showing me
The sure surrender-way,
The only way to live
In God's Heart's
Compassion-Inspiration-Nest.

10710

Since you are a victim
Of inactivity,
The temptation-snake has a free access
To your life.

10711

O my Lord's Silence-Eye,
For You, only for You,
My heart's sleepless
Gratitude-flood!

10712

A desiring mind
Raises expectation.
A self-giving heart
Traces illumination.

10713

O my arrogance-vital,
Do you not know that before long
You will collapse
Into a dark nothingness-abyss?

10714

Pay proper attention to your vital's attacks.
You will see that nobody
Will ever be able to force you
Out of your heart's aspiration-abode.
