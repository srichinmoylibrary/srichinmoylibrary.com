19301

Because I am devoted
To the inner life only,
Whatever my Inner Pilot
Asks me to do,
I do it soulfully
Plus cheerfully.

19302

I do not compete
With the rest of the world.
I compete only with myself,
For my progress is my true victory.

19303

If and when
Human beings get peace of mind
And inner security,
Only then will there be
Real peace in the world.

19304

I do not create the message;
God is supplying me
With His Message.
My job is only to deliver it
To the aspiring and crying humanity.

19305

The Music of our Lord Supreme
Is for those
Who are aspiring to become better,
Who love God's creation
And want to serve it,
Who are sleeplessly dreaming of a world
Of peace and oneness.

19306

God is fully responsible
For my life.
I am responsible only
For my soulful willingness
To surrender my body, vital,
Mind, heart and soul
To God, my Inner Pilot.

19307

From this day on
I shall not underestimate
My heart-capacities
And not overestimate
My mind-capacities.

19308

I must be aware of the fact
That any God-idea
Can completely change my life.

19309

The perfection-dream
Of my life today
Was founded on
My yesterday's self-giving.

19310

A comfort-seeking life
Will be
A victory-denied being.

19311

Command yourself first!
Only then will God
Demand everything from the world
For you.

19312

The remembrance
Of the past failure-life
Leads us to slow death.
The remembrance
Of the past surrender-heart
Leads us to God's Embrace.

19313

God-dreamers are good.
God-lovers are better.
God-servers are the best.

19314

God the Compassion
Wants me to stand
Side by side with Him.
