30.

Age does not matter,
Unless you are all sympathy
For your poor, overworked
Body, vital and mind.

31.

Age does not matter,
Unless the beauty of your life
And the fragrance of your heart
No longer inspire you.

32.

Age does not matter,
Unless you feel your mind’s desire-thirst
And your heart’s aspiration-hunger
Are deceased.

33.

Age does not matter,
Unless there is nothing on earth
To sweeten
Your breathing days.

34.

Age does not matter,
Unless you think
The wheels of your life-chariot
Are punctured.

35.

Age does not matter,
Unless you feel you have been forced
To extinguish
Your earth-attachment.

36.

Age does not matter,
Unless you are all eagerness
To bid farewell
To Mother Earth.

37.

Age does not matter,
Unless you are tortured
By the eagerness of meeting with
And being with Father Heaven.

38.

Age does not matter,
Unless you are fed up with
The impermanence-experiences
Of the world.

39.

Age does not matter,
Unless you are not sure
Of God’s Concern for you.

40.

Age does not matter,
Unless you feel your hopeless life
Will never be blessed
With a new chance.

41.

Age does not matter,
Unless your life is full
Of unhappy, torturing arrows.

42.

Age does not matter,
Unless your life has become
A most powerful sigh.

43.

Age does not matter,
Unless you feel earth is cruel to you
And Heaven is indifferent to you.

44.

Age does not matter,
Unless you feel that you have become
Your mind-desert everywhere.
