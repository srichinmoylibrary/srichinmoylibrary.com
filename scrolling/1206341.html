20501

A million soulful dreams
Thrill my heart
When I do not allow my mind
To be encaged
By ruthless unhappiness-dragon.

20502

Each aspiration-opportunity
Must work side by side
With a dedication-smile.

20503

The stupidity of man compels him
To dash against
His own mind's desire-rocks.

20504

When the heart-compass
Points only to God,
We can free ourselves
From the nightmares of the past.

20505

If you hold conversations
With frustration-despair-darkness,
Then your life will never grow into
A perfection-flower.

20506

Each time you allow doubt
To enter your mind,
Your heart's aspiration-flame
Is bound to waver.

20507

When I do not pray
And do not meditate,
I unmistakably see my life
As an endless and fruitless
Desert-journey.

20508

You already have wasted
Many incarnations.
Why not this time
Lovingly, soulfully and fruitfully
Walk along the broad avenue
Of God's smiling spirituality?

20509

There can be no security
In power.
There is all security
Only in
The heart's oneness-love.

20510

If you become
A childlike heart,
Then nothing can cloud
Your heart's enthusiasm-sky.

20511

God has definitely
Singled out certain souls
To take most significant parts
In His Cosmic Drama.

20512

The aggression of the mind
And the depression of the vital
Must agree to a cease-fire
Before they can make any progress
In the spiritual life.

20513

To cherish doubts
Is to clip
One's own aspiration-heart-wings.

20514

The mind's disobedience
Will, without fail, create
A purposeless and fruitless life.
