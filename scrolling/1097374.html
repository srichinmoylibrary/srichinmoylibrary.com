Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Kuantan and Kijal, Malaysia in December 2005 and January 2006.

30 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

1 My Heart’s Blooming Smile ...

My heart’s blooming smile
I always offer
To my Lord Supreme
First.

2 God-Dreamers ...

God-dreamers
Eventually become
God-manifestation-fulfillers.

3 The More We Travel ...

The more we travel
Within,
The faster we get good results
Without.

4 My God-Gratitude ...

My God-gratitude
Is
My true life-transformer.

5 How Do I Find Happiness? ...

How do I find happiness?
I find happiness
By loving the world more,
Ever more.

6 The Source ...

The source
Of my inspiration
Is
Mother Earth.

7 The Source ...

The source
Of my aspiration
Is
My inner sun.

8 The Source ...

The source
Of my success
Is
My mind-determination.

9 The Source ...

The source
Of my progress
Is
My surrender-life to God.

10 Every Hour ...

Every hour
God sows
A new seed of transformation
In my heart-garden.

31 December 2005 ...

31 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

11 The Heart Is Always Ready ...

The heart is always ready
To answer the questions
Of the mind,
But the mind is not satisfied.

12 A Life Of Meditation ...

A life of meditation
Gives tremendous joy
To God.
