9501

Are you a seeker?
Then for you
There is only one reality-smile,
And that reality-smile
Is your heart's aspiration-cry.

9502

If you do not start shortening
Your wishful-thinking list,
You will not be able to free yourself
From the unforeseen,
Inescapable consequences.

9503

He who has become a God-lover
Cannot be a stranger
To self-giving.

9504

A seeker's faultless faith
In God's Heart-Nest
Is God's Vision-fulfilling Happiness.

9505

Do you know the gift
Of your silence-mind?
It is the vastness
Of illumination-sky.

9506

Disobedience is firmly standing
On your life's doorstep.
How then can God
Kindle your heart's aspiration-flame?

9507

Once you have accepted
The spiritual life,
Your mind's hesitation
Is nothing short of ruthless disobedience
In disguise.

9508

God will not grant you leave of absence
From your spiritual life.
But you can absent yourself -
Of course, without receiving
God's Satisfaction-Salary.

9509

If your mind is weak
In accepting your heart's delight
As a true treasure,
Then yours will be the life
Of utter confusion.

9510

O my mind,
When are you going to announce
The death of your unrecognised enemy,
Doubt?

9511

If you push the desire-button,
You will see the temptation-deer
Coming out
Only to charm and destroy your life.

9512

If you can consciously raise
The value of your faith-life,
You will before long see
The downfall of your doubt-mind.

9513

Are you a fool?
Why do you want to repair
Your broken ego?
Break it once and for all!

9514

Possession-trees eventually become
Fruitless, flowerless and even leafless.
Detachment-plants grow and glow
In rapid succession.
