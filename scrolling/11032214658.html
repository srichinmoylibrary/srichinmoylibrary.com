5 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I do not want to have a mind
That either ridicules me
Or extols me to the skies.
The opinion of the mind
Is of no consequence to me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I only need a heart
To love You and be loved by You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 5 January 1999, 8:17 a.m. Before lifting 220 lbs. six times. (3 right, 3 left)

5 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My name is self-confidence
Only because my self-confidence
Is founded upon
My God-obedience and God-reliance.
When my God-obedience
And my God-reliance
Want me to have self-confidence,
I accept it.
Otherwise, I only want my life to be
God-obedience and God-reliance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 5 January 1999, 8:29 a.m. Before using double-arm machine — up to 130 lbs. with each arm simultaneously from a standing position.

6 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My heart's gratitude-attitude
Is
My life's plenitude-altitude.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 6 January 1999, 8:58 a.m. Before lifting 220 lbs. thirty-two times with one arm. (16 right, 16 left)

6 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I purposely forgot my lower self
To discover quickly my higher Self:
God, my Lord Beloved Supreme.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 6 January 1999, 9:17 a.m. Before using double-arm machine. New record: 120 lbs. with each arm simultaneously from a seated position; and up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously from a standing position.

7 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Why do You not love me
Anymore?
"My child, your question deserves
No answer."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 7 January 1999, 10:53 a.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 130 lbs. — with each arm simultaneously from a standing position.

7 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Even if You do not love me
Anymore,
Even if You do not speak to me
Anymore,
I shall not leave You.
"My child, My child, My child,
You will not be able to leave Me.
You will not be able to run away
From Me.
You will not be able to escape
From My Infinity's Heart-Home,
My child, My child, My child!"
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 7 January 1999, 11:05 a.m. Before lifting 220 lbs. twenty times with one arm. (10 right, 10 left)

8 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My heart is all gratitude to You
For telling me
That my tremendous concern
For humanity
Certifies my genuine love
For You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 8 January 1999, 8:17 a.m. Before lifting 220 lbs. twenty-four times with one arm. (12 right, 12 left)

8 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You are telling me that spirituality
Is not a matter of
Rushing towards something.
Spirituality means self-giving
And God-becoming.
Deep within the fulness of time,
Each and every human being
Will realise God, reveal God
And manifest God.
This blessingful Message I am receiving
From You today.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 8 January 1999, 8:32 a.m. Before using double-arm machine. New record: 130 lbs. with each arm simultaneously from a seated position.

9 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I am able to see You every day.
This is, indeed, not my highest privilege.
I am able to speak to You every day.
This is, indeed, not my highest privilege.
I am able to sit at Your Feet
Whenever I want to.
This is, indeed, not my highest privilege.
My Supreme, I am able to kiss
The dust of Your Feet
Every day to my heart's content.
This is, indeed, my highest,
Absolutely highest privilege.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 9 January 1999, 5:46 a.m. Before lifting 220 lbs. twenty-two times with one arm. (10 right, 10 left, plus once each arm to maximum extension)

9 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You are asking me to be swift of foot
So that I can love You
Unreservedly and unconditionally.
You are asking me to be pure of heart
So that I can serve You
Sleeplessly and breathlessly.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 9 January 1999, 5:59 a.m. Before using double-arm machine — up to 130 lbs. with each arm simultaneously from a standing position.

10 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Today my heart's God-revelation-hope
And
My life's God-manifestation-promise
Are so happily swimming together
In my soul's God-oneness-river.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 10 January 1999, 7:18 a.m. Before lifting 230 lbs. twenty-five times with one arm. (10 right, 13 left, plus once each arm to maximum extension)

10 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Life is not
A tormenting fear-cloud.
Life is
A challenging, cheering, inspiring,
Illumining and fulfilling
Courage-sunburst.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 10 January 1999, 7:34 a.m. Before using double-arm machine — up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously from a standing position.

11 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My life is made of excuses.
Your Heart is made of Forgiveness.
I give You what I have:
My nothingness.
You give me what You have:
Infinitude.
Plus, You give me what You are:
Your Eternity's Cry,
Your Infinity's Smile,
Your Immortality's Self-Transcendence-Dream-Reality.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 11 January 1999, 5:43 a.m. Before lifting 230 lbs. twice. (once with each arm)

11 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You are so kind to me,
So compassionate to me.
You have given my gratitude-heart
And my surrender-life
Two front-row seats to watch,
Prayerfully and soulfully,
Your world-fulfilment-peace-performance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 11 January 1999, 5:56 a.m. Before using double-arm machine — up to 130 lbs. with each arm simultaneously from a standing position.

12 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I am piloting my heart-boat
To my surrender-harbour,
Where You are so indulgently,
Compassionately and proudly
Waiting for my prayerful
And soulful arrival.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 12 January 1999, 5:36 a.m. Before lifting 230 lbs. twenty-four times with one arm. (10 right, 12 left, plus once each arm to maximum extension)
