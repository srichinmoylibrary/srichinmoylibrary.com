19245

There is only one way
To self-perfection,
And that way is
Cheerful and unconditional
Self-dedication.

19246

Can you not make time
To catch God's Peace-Dove
Which is so beautifully and self-givingly
Winging its way towards you?

19247

The heart-host
Knows no stranger.
The mind-host
Seldom has real friends.

19248

Your mind's anxiety-clouds
You can remove
When your life becomes
A perfect stranger to idleness.

19249

"Nobody
Is too insignificant.
Nobody
Is too insufficient."
So says my Lord Supreme.

19250

Alas, the human mind
Shamelessly enjoys itself
At the cost of another's misery.

19251

Although I begged and begged God
To enter into my restless mind,
God simply refused.

19252

If you do not pray to God
And meditate on God,
Why should God not withdraw
His Shelter-Concern?

19253

Alas, I am unwilling to lose
Even a moment
In making friends
With frustration and despair!

19254

God the Confidence
And God the Pride
Enter into my heart
When gratitude is available.

19255

I have been looking all my life
For one thing:
God's complete reliance on man.

19256

A true man of renunciation
Is a perfect stranger
Both to earthly ambitions
And to Heavenly praises.

19257

The most valuable lesson
To learn in life
Is that no human being
Is indispensable.

19258

Let us accept,
Transform and illumine
Human life
To make it worthy of
Divinity's satisfaction-breath.

19259

What my mind actually wants
From God
Is not only unknowable
But also inconceivable.
