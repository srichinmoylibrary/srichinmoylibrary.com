30 September 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I do not know what I am doing,
But I do know that You know
What You are doing
In and through me
For Your own Manifestation-Light
Here on earth
And for Your own Satisfaction-Delight
There in Heaven.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 September 1998, 4:53 a.m. Before lifting 127 lbs with each arm once.

1 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
When I cry for You,
The Beauty of Your Eye
Embraces my heart.
When I smile at You,
The Fragrance of Your Heart
Pilots my life-boat
To Your own Golden Shore
Of the ever-transcending Beyond.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 1998, 2:33 a.m. Before lifting 83 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

1 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My prayer to God tells me
How far away my mind is
From God.
My meditation on God tells me
How close my heart is
To God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 1998, 8:40 a.m. Before lifting 140 lbs with each arm twice.

2 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May my self-giving
Remain ever-increasing
To see Your creation smiling
And Your Heartbeats dancing.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1998, 3:02 a.m. Before lifting 150 lbs with each arm once.

2 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Your Compassion-Eye
Spoke a Nectar-Word
To my aspiration-heart
And changed my life entirely.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1998, 7:08 p.m. Before lifting 160 lbs with each arm twice.

3 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May I prayerfully breathe in Love
From Your Heart
And soulfully breathe out peace
To all my brothers and sisters
Of the world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 October 1998, 4:27 a.m. Before lifting 93 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

5 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O God-faith-heart-telescope,
I love you, I love you, I love you.
O God-doubt-mind-microscope,
I detest you, I detest you, I detest you.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 1998, 1:31 a.m. Before lifting 180 lbs with each arm three times.

5 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O greatness-mind,
You are a part-time God-worshipper.
O goodness-heart,
You are a full-time God-worshipper,
God-lover, God-server
And God-fulfiller
Here on earth and there in Heaven.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 1998, 1:42 a.m. Before lifting 100 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

5 October 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind tells God that it wants to be
A great leader of mankind.
God says to my mind,
"My son, wait, wait.
When the time arrives,
I shall inform you."

My heart tells God that it wishes to be
A humble server of mankind.
God says to my heart,
"My child, My child, come!
Come and sit with Me
On My Golden Throne;
Embrace Me, embrace Me,
I am all yours.
I have so many things, My child,
For you to do for Me.
You may begin here and now."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 1998 1:50 a.m. Before lifting 131 lbs fifty times. (25 right, 25 left)

6 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May my heart love You
Exactly the way my soul loves You.
May my life need You
Exactly the way my heart needs You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1998, 7:00 a.m. Before lifting 190 lbs with each arm once.

6 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May my past life
Not remain in me as a thorn.
May my present life
Be the beauty of my heart.
May my future life
Be the fragrance of my soul.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1998, 7:10 a.m. Before lifting 110 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

6 October 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Do stop my mind's
Wild interruptions
When You and I enjoy
Our heart-to-heart
Power-conversations.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1998, 7:20 a.m. Before lifting 137 lbs fifty times. (25 right, 25 left)

6 October 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My dear Supreme, My Lord Supreme,
My Beloved Supreme,
My Absolute Lord Beloved Supreme,
To You I bow,
To You I bow,
To You I bow,
To You I bow.
My prayer
Is my God-search.
My meditation
Is my God-discovery.
My prayer
Is my God-love-infancy-cry.
My meditation
Is my God-oneness-intimacy-smile.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1998, 8:27 p.m. Before lifting 200 lbs nine times. (5 right, 4 left)

10 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Do transform my heart, my mind,
My vital and my body
In the Compassion-Flames
Of Your Love-Sun.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 October 1998, 3:57 a.m. Before lifting 210 lbs with left arm eight times.
