24 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Not an early death
But an immediate death of my ego
I need.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 June 2000, 8:52 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

24 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
No hesitation-mind,
But a determination-mind.
Not my capacity,
But God's unconditional Bounty.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 June 2000, 9:08 a.m. Before doing 440 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

24 June 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
You have taught me many, many,
Many things.
Please teach me one more thing:
How I can be prayerfully, soulfully,
Sleeplessly and breathlessly
Grateful to You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 June 2000, 9:32 a.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

26 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall never allow
My unconditional God-obedience-book
To be out of print.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 June 2000, 9:09 a.m. Before doing 450 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

26 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Humanity's aspiration-hearts
Are moving the world inward,
Driving the world forward
And
Raising the world upward.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 June 2000, 9:18 a.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

26 June 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
How fortunate I am to have You
As my heart's inner Instructor
And my life's outer Instructor.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 June 2000, 7:08 p.m. Before doing 470 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

26 June 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall not allow
Your expectation-fulfilment of my life
To remain a far cry.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 June 2000, 7:30 p.m. Before doing 480 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

26 June 2000 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord Supreme,
As my life has taken shelter
At Your Protection-Feet,
Even so, I am placing my Goal
At Your Satisfaction-Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 June 2000, 7:47 p.m. Before doing 500 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

27 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart be made of
God-gratitude-smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 June 2000, 5:45 p.m. Before doing 510 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

27 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I please You in Your own Way,
I clearly see the celestials
Dancing Your Victory-Dance
In my heart-sky.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 June 2000, 6:02 p.m. Before doing 520 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

27 June 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my God-love, God-devotion
And God-surrender
Be as sincere, as serious
And as pure as possible.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 June 2000, 7:22 p.m. Before doing 530 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

27 June 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I please You in Your own Way,
You ask me to carry Your Heart-Smiles
Wherever I go,
And You will carry my heart-tears
Wherever You go.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 June 2000, 7:34 p.m. Before doing 540 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

28 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your ceaseless Compassion-Rain
Has totally destroyed
My mind's limitation-bondage-chain.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 June 2000, 12:25 a.m. Before doing 550 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

28 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day, every hour,
Every minute, every second
You try to keep me happy,
Yet I do not have a gratitude-heart.
Alas! Alas!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 June 2000, 8:58 a.m. Before doing 560 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

28 June 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What I need is patience, patience,
Patience.
You have Your own Hour to please me
In Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 June 2000, 9:03 a.m. Before doing standing double-arm lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.
