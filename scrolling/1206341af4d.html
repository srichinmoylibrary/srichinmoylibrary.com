20575

There is only one way
To cure the mind's doubt-cancer,
And that way is the heart's
Conscious and constant expansion.

20576

The realised souls do not parade,
Because they do not need to.
The unrealised seekers are quite often
Victims of self-exhibition-parade.

20577

O my obedience-heart-garland-fragrance,
Because of you
I am a member
Of God's inner circle.

20578

If you multiply your opportunities,
Then God will supply you
With endless achievements.

20579

If you do not cry,
God will not blame you.
If you do not smile,
God will not blame you.
If you do not sing God-songs,
God will not blame you.
But God will blame you
If you try to end your earth-life
Untimely.

20580

If you want to be
A reformed character,
Then every day
Keep your heart-lotus
Fresh, beautiful,
Prayerful and soulful.

20581

God is not
An empire-mind-builder.
But He is
A cottage-heart-lover.

20582

What language
Does God speak?
God speaks only
Self-giving language.

20583

O my heart's hope-shrine,
You are my life's
Oneness-sunshine.

20584

Do not allow unhappiness
To capture your heart.
Do you not know that unhappiness
Is the heart-cancer?

20585

My idle life
And my impure mind
Talk and talk incessantly.

20586

To my widest surprise,
The farthest reach
Of the mind
Is unimaginably short!

20587

O my mind, from now on
You can be a self-ruler.
But I shall not allow you
Any longer to rule my heart.

20588

I want to master
Mind-discipline
And not
Mind-freedom.

20589

How can you expect
Your soul to sing for you
Its celestial songs,
When you have betrayed
Your God-hunger-life?
