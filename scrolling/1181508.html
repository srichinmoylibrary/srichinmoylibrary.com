8501 No Need Of Miracles!

No need of miracles!
Ask man and God for nothing.
Let the beauty of silence
Reign supreme in your life.

8502 Do Not Try To Bind Love

Do not try to bind love,
For love alone
Can change the face
Of this ill-fated earth.

8503 He Is Not The Loser

He is not the loser
And he can never be the loser,
For his heart's surrender-flower
And his life's gratitude-fruit
God has proudly accepted.

8504 Two Questions

Your first question is:
Is God-realisation
Everybody's birthright?

Your second and last question is:
How can the human cry
Be transformed into a divine smile?

8505 A Sacred Thirst

If your life has a sacred thirst,
Then God is bound to grant you
His own secret Well to quench it.

8506 Love Your Heart Ever More

Love your heart ever more.
And do something further:
Reawaken your imprisoned heart-flames!

8507 Stay Where You Are

My desire,
Stay where you are.
I am running towards you.
What for?
To reduce your life's
Loud clatterings.

8508 Sleepless Perfection

Sleepless perfection,
Will you ever be mine?
Sleepless satisfaction,
Can I be yours, only yours?

8509 I Once Promised Myself

I once promised myself
That I would not try to escape
Any appropriate punishment
If I misbehaved in my inner life.
Yet my promise remains unfulfilled.

8510 Beautiful Is His Heart's Loyalty

Beautiful is his heart's loyalty.
Soulful is his mind's enthusiasm.
Fruitful is his life's obedience.

8511 The Mind's Barrier

Unless I cross the mind's barrier,
How can I see beauty's face,
Purity's heart
And divinity's soul?

8512 What Can My Little Hands Do?

What can my little hands do?
They can paint the Face
Of God the Beauty.
What can my heart's soulful will do?
It can liberate man
The bondage-slave.

8513 A Purity-Heart Has The Capacity

A purity-heart
Has the capacity
And is the capacity
To live inside
The heart of Infinity's Love-Light.

8514 My Mind's Ignorance-Dreams

If I can keep my life safe
From my mind's ignorance-dreams,
Then I do not see why and how
I cannot become a perfect instrument
Of my Beloved Supreme.
