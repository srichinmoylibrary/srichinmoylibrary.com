490.

Alas, I do not know
How my mind has left my heart
And disappeared into the arms
Of ignorance-night.

491.

I have drunk deep
The sorrows
Of an unsearching mind
And an unaspiring heart.

492.

May my God-realisation-hope
Spread from me
To each and every human heart.

493.

No one can prevent you
From leaving your mind's prison cell
If you want to leave.

494.

With any forward step you take
You are bringing yourself
Nearer to God.

495.

How I wish I could have
The electrifying beauty
Of a purity-heart!

496.

My heart-tears
Have always resolved
My mind-problems.

497.

I am praying and praying
To my Lord Supreme
To bless me with
A roaring
Perfection-manifestation-life.

498.

The power of concentration
Can easily silence
All the clamour of the mind.

499.

I enjoy it immensely
When my gratitude-heart
And God's Compassion-Eye
Have their endless conversation.

500.

God and God's Compassion-Eye
Will never leave anything divine on earth
Undone.
