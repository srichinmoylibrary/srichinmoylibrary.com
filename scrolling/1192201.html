6501

O heart, my heart,
I have only one prayer:
Do become the banner of hope
In my life.

6502

Do not waste your precious time
In thinking of your mind's
Doubt-tree.
Just think of your heart's
Faith-plant.

6503

If you daily take your faith-ferry
Across the doubt-river,
Then God will grant you
His Heart's Satisfaction-Song.

6504

You want to know where you can find me.
Either you will find me and my head
At God's Feet,
Or you will find me and my feet
On the head of ignorance-night.

6505

This ignorance-game will end
Only when I feel that God
Is my Eternity's only Friend.

6506

God knows how to do everything
Except one thing:
He does not know
How to underestimate
My capacities.

6507

Every day God welcomes you.
He comes to see you
With new hope and new determination,
But you prefer to live
In your endless frustration-maze.

6508

One wasted moment
Has the capacity to force us
To learn the song of surrender
To ignorance-prince.

6509

God is always on your side,
But can you not give Him
Your freedom-choice:
Surrender-obedience?

6510

To him who says
That without God he knows nothing,
God will always be there
To grant His eternal Friendship.

6511

God,
Who are You?
"My son,
I am your Eternity's
Unrecognised Friend."

6512

If you enter the desire-race,
You will not be allowed to participate
In God's Oneness-Game.

6513

If you have the will to do,
God will definitely give you
The courage
To dare and succeed.

6514

Death's inseparable friends:
Man's depression-vital
And man's frustration-mind.
