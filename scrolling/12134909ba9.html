145.

My heart begs God for news.
God's Heart begs me
Not just for news,
But for good news.

146.

When next God and I meet together,
I shall tell God
That I am all ready to become
A God-Dream-server-heart.

147.

My soul is God's assistant.
It spends all its time
Opening and locking God-Gate
For humanity's upward and inward progress.

148.

There is not a single day
When God does not visit
My ignorance-mind-prison
With His Compassion-Heart-Eye.

149.

When I do not pray and meditate
Beautifully and soulfully,
I see my entire life
Wrapped in a haze of uncertainty.

150.

My Lord Beloved Supreme
Is sure that I will not object
To His early Arrival on earth.

151.

Alas, why do I meddle
In God's Affairs,
Since I do not have the capacity
To create a perfect solution?

152.

Not only the light of your future,
But also the light of your present
Will shine all-where
If you have a God-surrendered heart.

153.

I am so proud of my heart
That it is always eagerly willing
To offer what it has and what it is
To my Beloved Supreme.

154.

The mistakes of yesterday
Make us feel we are useless,
Although we are not.
The perfection of tomorrow
Is beckoning us at this very moment.

155.

Do not lose faith,
Either in God or in yourself,
If you want to succeed in life
With absolute certainty.

156.

The lower you look,
The farther backward
You go.

157.

The spiritual inheritance
That we have received from the past
Is never and can never be adequate
For our God-realisation.

158.

A true God-lover's life
Is a constant
God-Victory-Song.

159.

Just by ceaselessly repeating,
"I am of God and I am for God,"
I am marching fast, very fast
Towards my golden future.
