9475

Wasting time
Is the connecting link
Between temptation and frustration.

9476

Do not expect God
To make the stupid attempt
To please the human in you.
If He pleases you
In your own way,
That will be your bitter failure,
Your total spiritual defeat.

9477

You are with God,
But you are not for God.
Your mind and vital
Are somewhere else.
What can poor God do?

9478

When he is in his mind,
His soul sends him
Extra-small garments to wear,
To cover his face immediately.

When he is in his heart,
His soul sends him
Extra-large garments to wear,
To adorn his eye perfectly.

9479

God's Mailbox receives
Every day
Earth's frustration-complaints
And Heaven's
Indifference-satisfaction-notes.

9480

"Be quiet, be quiet!
I am descending."
This is what God says to earth.

"Be quiet, be quiet!
I shall return soon."
This is what God says to Heaven.

9481

If your mind enjoys every day
A new cyclone of destruction,
How will you ever approach
In this lifetime
Your heart's satisfaction-tunnel?

9482

What I see in you
To my wide surprise:
The sudden blooming
Of life-transformation-movement-bud.

9483

A cheerful mind
Has always been a perfect guide
To a healthy body.

9484

O great student of the brain,
No matter what you proudly gain,
You will be nowhere near
Satisfaction-light.

9485

No matter how successful you are,
Your seeker-heart will tell you
That to live on earth forever
Fulfilling and fulfilling your desires
Would be the worst possible curse.

9486

My sweet Lord Supreme,
In some ways
I have made tremendous progress
In my inner life.
But alas, I am going backwards
In other ways.
Will I ever please You totally
With my life's
Perfection-progress report?

9487

One day he meditates
For seven hours.
Then he thinks
That for seven days
He does not need to meditate.
Who else is a seeker-fool,
If not he?

9488

Alas,
Your inner moon
Of purity, soulfulness and divinity
Has been eclipsed
By the clouds of doubt.

9489

Be a pioneer
In God's Music-World.
Your dedication-life
Can offer God-Music
To many receptive hearts.
