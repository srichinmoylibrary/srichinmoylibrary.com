18415

My morning prayer
Wishes to become
The beauty
And the fragrance
Of a morning rose.

18416

My Lord Supreme,
Do help me grow into
My hope-immortalising dreams.

18417

Our God-thirst
Must climb
To God's Satisfaction-Height.

18418

The sweetness of our soul
Illumines the breath
Of our life.

18419

I offer my gratitude-letters
To my Lord Supreme
Through my surrender-post.

18420

Each aspiration-flame
Brightens the heart
And enlightens the life.

18421

The doubtful mind
Is secretly afraid
Of a peaceful heart.

18422

What I need is
A sincerity-loving heart
And a simplicity-loving life.

18423

At last God is ready
To take birth
In the mind of man.

18424

The hope-train
And the promise-train
Must go side by side
To reach the destination.

18425

What is a human mind made of?
A human mind is made of
Centuries of dissatisfaction
And frustration.

18426

Doubt has nothing to do with
Our outer success,
And especially not with
Our inner progress.

18427

Every day man's life
Becomes shorter,
But his hope
Grows deeper.

18428

O my deplorable mind,
I beg of you
To start your life
All over again.

18429

O my ignorance-life,
I have been with you
For a very, very long time.
It is now high time
For me to acquire a new friend:
Wisdom-light.
