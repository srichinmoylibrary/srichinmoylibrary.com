8660

I serve God
Not because God loves me.
I serve God
Not because God needs me.
I serve God
Because my existence
Has no existence
Without God's Existence in it.

8661

A life that has no life-energy
Daily meets serious tragedies.
A life that has life-energy
Is the complete song
Of God's Satisfaction-Ecstasy.

8662

Delight
Is God-Eternity's
Infinity-Fulfilment.

8663

If you are your soul-power,
God Himself will build
Your victory-tower.

8664

God loves to play
His Beauty's Game
In earth-clay,
For earth-clay.

8665

An innocence-heart
Is blessed
With God's Satisfaction-Smile
On earth.

8666

The rising of the sun
Tells me where to go.
The setting of the sun
Tells me where I eternally am.

8667

The secret heart
Of Heaven
Is the sacred life
Of earth.

8668

His eyes of smile
Reveal the heart-love
Of all the ages.

8669

Advance, advance
To the inmost core
Of your soul's radiance!

8670

God grants a true God-lover
The rare right
To eternal peace.

8671

Allow not your vital-horse
To frighten
Your heart-bird.

8672

God has given you
The chance to choose
The outer power
Or the inner peace.

8673

What I want to know from you
Is this:
Are you still interested
In God-realisation?

8674

He cannot separate
His mind
From its frustration-desert.
