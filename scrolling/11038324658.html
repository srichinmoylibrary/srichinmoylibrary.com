11 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only the unconditional seekers
   Are invited by You
To enjoy the ambrosial feast
   Of Your Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 October 1999, 1:39 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 400 lbs.)

11 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only the tears of my heart
Can carry me and place me
   At Your Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 October 1999, 1:40 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

11 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
From now on I shall never swerve
   Even an inch
From Your Compassion-flooded Will.
This is my prayerful, soulful
And solemn promise to You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 October 1999, 1:42 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

12 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You teach me only when
   I am ready.
Your Compassion teaches me
   Even when I am not ready.
   Your Forgiveness teaches me
All the time —
   Alas, often in vain!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 October 1999, 8:41 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 50 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (61 reps)

12 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am tired, extremely tired.
"My child, I am also extremely tired.
   But we must carry on.
The world needs your life's
   Service-plants.
The world needs My Heart's
   Compassion-Skies."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 October 1999, 9:04 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

12 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My doubting mind,
I am challenging you here and now,
   At this very moment,
   At this very place.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 October 1999, 9:21 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

12 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, my mind, my vital and I
Are vehemently vetoing
Your World-Vision-Plans.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 October 1999, 9:24 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-four times. (31 right, 33 left)

13 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Although my body-weightloss-failure-bites
Are very sharp and very painful,
   I am sure they are curable.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 October 1999, 6:52 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

13 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I measure my aspiration-heart
With my life's dedication-scale.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 October 1999, 7:07 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

13 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my faith in You every day
Increase in infinite measure
So that my body, vital, mind and heart
Every day succeed and proceed
And sing the song
   Of self-transcendence.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 October 1999, 7:14 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

13 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My sleepless and breathless
   God-obedience
Shall carry me to God's
   Transcendental Excellence
At His choice Hour.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 October 1999, 7:17 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 600 lbs.)

13 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not know what to do —
Whether to cry or to laugh —
   When I say
That mine is the only way
   To realise God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 October 1999, 7:29 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-seven times. (34 right, 33 left)

13 October 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only my self-doubt makes me feel
Lonely, weak, useless and miserable.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 October 1999, 7:50 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

14 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I see Your Compassion-Eye,
   Your Forgiveness-Heart
        And
   Your Protection-Feet
Inside my heart's silence-fragrance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 October 1999, 9:46 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

14 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Even until yesterday
I was a self-enamoured trumpeter.
But from now on I shall only be
Your Compassion-inspired
   Victory-trumpeter.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 October 1999, 9:53 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-six times. (31 right, 35 left)
