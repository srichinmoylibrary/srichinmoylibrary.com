13530

Your happiness-heart-flower
Will not fade
If you choose your soul-illumination
To guide you every day.

13531

Nothing succeeds as beautifully
And as powerfully
As our mountain-aspiration-cries
And our fountain-dedication-smiles.

13532

If you sleeplessly pray for the death
Of your mind's ignorance-night,
Then God is bound to give you
His Heart's everlasting Breath.

13533

A self-giving
And God-becoming heart
Is humanity's peace-flooded home.

13534

God's Compassion-Rain
And man's aspiration-plants
Can together change the face and fate
Of fast-descending humanity.

13535

If your heart is flooded
With gratitude,
Then you can never fail to find
Your life's perfection-summit-sun.

13536

The birth of ignorance-night
Nobody knows,
But the death of ignorance-night
Everybody is bound to know.

13537

My Lord, my Lord,
I humbly and soulfully pray to You
Not to stop
My life's self-transcendence-pilgrimage.

13538

Every day
God enjoys reading
My heart's aspiration-blossoming
Messages.

13539

No, not even the compassionate God,
Not to speak of human beings,
Will ever say a word
In defence of your heart's insecurity!

13540

The sweetness
Of a purity-heart
Is immediately embraced
By the fondness
Of God's Satisfaction-Eye.

13541

God does not see any urgency
In transforming your life.
Do you know why?
Just because you enjoy complacency
More than anything else in your life.

13542

No matter how undivine you are,
God wants you to look
Straight into His Eyes
So that His Compassion can enter into you
And transform your guilt-loaded life.

13543

To my wide surprise,
My heart is still in the lead
Although I have not fed my heart
For a long time.

13544

Do you know why
You are acting like a demon?
Because you daily enjoy listening
To your unlit mind's
Wild sermons.
