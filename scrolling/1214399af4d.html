1075.

He places his heart
In his prayerful eyes
And on his soulful face.

1076.

My earth-life will satisfy God
Only when
I can bring down God's Glory
Inside my heart-cries.

1077.

If we can become
Our heart's mounting cry,
Then we shall not remain
In age-long ignorance-slumber.

1078.

If we do not love God the Creator
And serve God the creation,
Then we shall always remain
Infant and orphan cries
In the bosom of ignorance-night.

1079.

To revive our mind's lost inspiration
And our heart's lost aspiration,
We must approach God's Compassion-Eye
With the blossoming heart-smile
Of a child.

1080.

God is always ready and eager
To obliterate our teeming disappointments,
But are we ready in any way
To allow Him to do
What He wants to do
In and through us?

1081.

No heart can ever be
A stranger to peace.
It is the mind that was and still is
A perfect stranger to peace.

1082.

How can I please God
In God's own Way?
Indeed, this is the question
That is uppermost
In my God-searching mind
And God-crying heart.

1083.

I do not have to know
How I can gain entrance
To God's Heart-Abode.
God's Compassion-flooded Eye
Will do the needful
When I become my heart's
Ceaseless and helpless cries.

1084.

O my mind,
Be ever watchful.
O my mind,
Be ever prayerful.
O my mind,
Be ever soulful.
Lo, God's Pride is all yours.

1085.

Who is God?
He is God
Who has unsolicited Compassion
For the future redemption of mankind.

1086.

I must become a conscious receptacle
Of God's Compassion.
If not, how can God ever fulfil Himself
In and through my life?

1087.

The mind, which is stupidity incarnate,
Is always anxious and eager
To dismantle the heart's peace-bridge,
Which leads to God's
Nectar-flooded Kingdom.

1088.

When I become
My morning heart-cry,
God becomes
The fastest Sprinter
To approach me.

1089.

I feed and treasure
My desire-mind.
God loves and fulfils
My aspiration-heart.
