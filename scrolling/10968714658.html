27 Intensity ...

Intensity
Shows me the Way.

28 Immensity ...

Immensity
Leads me to the Goal.

29 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Lifts us high, very high,
With a single Glance,
But, alas, we prefer to come down.

30 My Complete Surrender ...

My complete surrender
To God’s Will
Is God’s proudest
And happiest news.

31 Individuality-Lovers ...

Individuality-lovers
Are
Universality-strangers.

32 The Affection ...

The affection
Of the heart
Is the sunshine
Of life.

33 In The Morning ...

In the morning,
My heart is
A God-worship-flower-beauty.

34 In The Evening ...

In the evening,
My life is
A God-surrender-flower-fragrance.

35 I Enjoy ...

I enjoy
My heart’s God-beauty-tears
And
My life’s God-duty-smiles
Equally and unreservedly.

36 With My Sobbing Eyes ...

With my sobbing eyes
I touch
My Lord’s Feet.

37 With My Throbbing Heart ...

With my throbbing heart
I feel
My Lord’s Heart.

38 Clasp ...

Clasp,
Clasp God’s Feet
If you want to grasp
God’s Beauty.

5 February 2005 ...

5 February 2005

Nanjing, China

39 The Source Of Real Greatness ...

The source of real greatness
Is humility’s beauty.

40 The Source Of Real Goodness ...

The source of real goodness
Is the fragrance
Of selflessness.
