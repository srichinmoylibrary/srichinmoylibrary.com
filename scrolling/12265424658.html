Kato Usha Elo Kato Usha Gelo

December 31st, 1969

Kata usha elo kata usha gelo
Nahi ghuche ghuma ghor
Gyan ankhi hai nahi khule mor
Nahi khule hiya dor

Translation:

O, how many dawns
Have appeared and disappeared?
Yet, my sleeping world
Has not come to an end.
