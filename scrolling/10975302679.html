13 I Came Into The World ...

I came into the world
To feed Godís chosen children
With my heartís love.

14 Whatever Comes Into My Life ...

Whatever comes into my life,
I shall never make friends
With ignorance-night.

15 The Past ...

The past
Must not discourage
Our forward march.

16 The Past ...

The past
Must not discourage
Our inward dive.

17 My Prayer ...

My prayer
Is the loving breath
Of my heart.

18 Undivine Thoughts ...

Undivine thoughts
Imprison
Our mind.

19 Divine Thoughts ...

Divine thoughts
Liberate
Our mind.

20 The Traveller In Me ...

The traveller in me
Carries hope
Wherever it goes.

21 Concentration-Training ...

Concentration-training
Is absolutely needed.

22 The Aspiring Heart ...

The aspiring heart
Has no enemy.
It sees and feels
Friends everywhere.

12 January 2006 ...

12 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

23 Everything ...

Everything
Sings in the heart
Of a genuine seeker.

24 Not Only Godís Dreams ...

Not only Godís Dreams
But also His Realities
At times change.

25 I Have Only ...

I have only
One thing to do:
To stay peacefully
Inside my heart.

26 God-Intoxicated Souls ...

God-intoxicated souls
Are the ones
Who will manifest God
Here on earth.
