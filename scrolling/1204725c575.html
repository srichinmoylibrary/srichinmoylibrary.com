18990

If we have implicit faith
In our Lord Supreme,
Then we are bound to see
That this world will have
A new, all-illumining dawn,
Not when we want,
But at His choice Hour.

18991

There is no human being on earth
Who does not have the capacity
To offer the message of peace
To the world at large.
But what is needed now
Is the soulful willingness.

18992

Peace does not mean only silence.
Peace is also something
Soothing, thrilling, enchanting,
Illumining and fulfilling.

18993

What is the source
Of our Heavenly achievements:
Peace, bliss and perfection?
Spirituality is the source.
What is spirituality?
A sincere inner hunger for God.

18994

The only Blessing-Boon
I need from You,
My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Is this:
If it is Your Will,
I want to succeed,
And if it is not Your Will,
Then I do not want to succeed.
This is my life of surrender-light
Which I offer to You
At every moment.

18995

There is no such thing as a miracle.
When I throw myself
Into God's Compassion-Sea,
My life becomes
A natural God-serving experience-delight.

18996

Everything I am doing
Is aimed at one goal:
A prayerful and soulful awareness
Of a universal oneness-family
Founded upon peace.

18997

If we feel secure
In the depths of our heart,
We shall not challenge anybody,
For inner confidence
Is nothing short of complete satisfaction.

18998

What can prayer
And meditation do for us?
Prayer and meditation
Give us inner strength,
And this inner strength
Can lead to a lofty peace
And a world-oneness-family.

18999

I want to live on earth
Not by means of material food,
But by means of my heart-to-heart talks
With my Lord Beloved Supreme.

19000

Not because I am a poet,
Not because I am a philosopher,
But because I am a God-lover,
I see my Beloved Supreme
In each and every human being.
All human beings
Are my heart's brothers and sisters,
And the whole world
Is my oneness-home.
