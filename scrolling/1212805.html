26901

God reads my life's
Love-devotion-surrender-book
Every day, without fail,
Blessingfully.

26902

God reads my life's
Gratitude-heart-book
Every day, without fail,
Proudly.

26903

Each Heaven-climbing
Aspiration-heart
Is a God-treasury.

26904

My division-mind does not dare,
But my aspiration-heart
Dares to imagine
A world without suffering.

26905

Criticism
Is nothing short of
A wisdom-barren mind.

26906

Alas,
My heart is always
Painfully haunted
By the absence of God.

26907

A confusion-mind
Never knows
What the heart-prosperity is.

26908

It is the God-lovers
And not the God-speakers
Who eternally live for God.

26909

My aspiration
Is
My God-foundation-certainty.

26910

My obedience-heart
Is
My God-perfection-life.

26911

O soul of mine,
Can you not see
How often I pine
For you?

26912

A purity-thought
Is the ecstasy
Of sweetness.

26913

Not your bravery
But your stupidity
Says that it can live
Without God.

26914

My thinking
And God's twinkling
Always live together.
