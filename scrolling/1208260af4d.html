22475

Conviction tells me
What I can eventually become.
Realisation tells me
What I eternally am.

22476

The beauty of my heart
And the fragrance of my soul
Always fly together.

22477

I do not know how powerful
The mind is,
But I do know how powerful
The fear of the mind is
For me.

22478

An unwillingness-mind
Will eventually have to fight
Against immense dangers.

22479

Nothing
Can always remain the same —
Alas,
Not even my intense love
For this earth-planet.

22480

Each time I have a new hope,
A new and illumining world
Lovingly welcomes me.

22481

O my mind,
Pray and pray to God,
So that you can escape
Ignorance-mind-impurity.

22482

What my searching mind
Propounds
My crying heart
Expounds.

22483

The attachment of the vital
Cannot escape from
Eventually sobbing and moaning.

22484

As long as God's Compassion
Is in charge,
Our stupidity will not meet
With its well-deserved
God-Justice-Light.

22485

Although our aspiration is ancient,
It can remain untouched
By earth-dangers.

22486

O ingratitude-mind,
It is high time
For you to leave the world
For good.

22487

When I am in a rage,
I find myself in my mind's
Inescapable cage.

22488

As an artist delights
In painting,
A seeker must delight
In aspiring.

22489

O sincerity-heart,
Where are you?
O stupidity-mind,
Where are you not?
