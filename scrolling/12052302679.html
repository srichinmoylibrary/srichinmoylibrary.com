19415

I do not want to remember
My negativity-mind.
I do not want to remember
My futility-life.
I want to remember only
My God-intensity-heart.

19416

Because your God-surrender
And your God-gratitude
Are still a far cry,
Your earth-life will eventually
Swim in a sea of tears.

19417

There can be no unlisted
Heart-number
For God's Compassion-Eye.

19418

The aspiring heart believes in
Only one road,
And that road is
Oneness-happiness-road.

19419

The invisible faith
Is often visible
To our heart's warrior-strength.

19420

Thought-batteries
Always supply the mind
With insignificant and useless
Incidents in life.

19421

The purity of the heart
And the sincerity of the mind
The soul carries
When it enters into
God's universal Oneness-Shrine.

19422

Anything positive
Will finally outlast
Anything negative.
Positivity is a God-made ocean;
Negativity is a man-made wave.

19423

If you treat your ignorance-night
Gently, compassionately and self-givingly,
Yours will be the life of
Quick destruction-arrow.

19424

The soul of each and every religion
Needs God's
All-fulfilling Compassion-Height
And not man's
Tempting ignorance-pleasure.

19425

Despondency can be fatal!
Enthusiasm is a divine spark
Within us
To challenge the pride of ignorance
Around us.

19426

Yesterday is too old.
Do not mix with the old reality.
Enter into today's
World-illumining
And world-fulfilling dream.

19427

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me a very special boon:
I would like to become stuck to You
As to a powerful magnet.

19428

His mind is the lion
Of dauntless power.
His heart is the God-fulfilling
And earth-illumining tower.

19429

If you want to breathe
With your Lord's own Breath,
Then do not breathe inside
The mind-desert
But inside the heart-garden.
