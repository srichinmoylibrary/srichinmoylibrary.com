6775

Your heart
Is always on the alert.
It is your mind
That is fast asleep.
Therefore, the forces undivine
Attack your mind at every moment.

6776

He does not believe
In pleasure-trips.
He only believes
In his heart's progress-journey.

6777

Your heart-boat
Is meant for one thing
Very special:
It is supposed to dance
On your life's gratitude-waves.

6778

The Compassion-Wind
Of his Beloved Supreme
Sails his life-boat
Smilingly, lovingly
And blessingfully.

6779

You are telling the whole world
That you are unhappy,
And I am telling the whole world
That you are not suffering
From unhappiness
But from ingratitude.

6780

If your heart has genuine aspiration,
Then it will definitely know
How to dance
To God's Life-Transformation-Flute.

6781

Just because you are getting old,
Don't think that your vital
Will stop
Staging its unthinkable revolts
Against divinity.

6782

Man does not know
And does not want to know
That it is he who is responsible
For his self-created separation
From God.

6783

Do not talk about impurity.
It will blacken
Not only your face
But also your pure heart.

6784

If you can make your heart
Once more
Purity's gold,
Then easily you will be able to dream
Once more
God-dreams.

6785

The sole duty of a Master
Is to transfer his spiritual children
From the purity of the known
To the beauty of the Unknowable.

6786

If you are dealing
With God the Compassion,
Then even His Justice-Punishment-Light
Cannot be too severe.

6787

If you do not pray and meditate
Regularly,
Soon you will be stranded
In your unfulfilled desire-desert.

6788

If you cherish doubt
In your mind,
Peace will have no reason
To stay in your heart.

6789

You wanted to give
Your life-breath to God
For training.
Lo, God has accepted it
In a twinkling.
