17215

Love-power
In abundant measure
Is available always
Inside the silence-tower.

17216

Give yourself unconditionally.
Then you will see
That God will ask you
To claim rightfully
Your victory-crown.

17217

The human mind searches
For something strengthening.
The divine heart longs
For something soulfully lengthening.

17218

God-inspiration helps me
Run forward.
God-aspiration helps me
Fly upward.
God-surrender helps me
Dive inward.

17219

Each aspiring heart
Is a fruitful blessing
From Above
And a powerful challenge
From within.

17220

My Lord,
Do show me every morning
My sincerity's gratitude-heart-sunrise.

17221

The conscious and continuous
Suspicion-mind
Is destined for self-destruction.

17222

Laugh at yourself
Openly
If you want
To transcend yourself
Secretly.

17223

A constantly desiring mind
Is nothing other than
A whirlwind of confusion.

17224

Show God
What you have for Him:
Your hungry heart.
God will give you
What He has for you:
His Compassion-Feast.

17225

Your outer deeds
Must soulfully depend
On your inner needs.

17226

Cry to God
For a self-giving heart
To protect you
From the deluge of disappointment.

17227

Do not live with tomorrow's
Stupid procrastination-mind.
Live with today's
Wise aspiration-perfection-heart.

17228

What kind of spiritual Master is he
Who is unwilling
Consciously and deliberately
To teach all and sundry?

17229

Hope is the beauty
Of a seeker-mind.
Courage is the divinity
Of a giver-heart.
