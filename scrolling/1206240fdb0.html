20460

What I need is
Sleepless receptivity
To throw open
All my heart-doors.

20461

Impurity-thoughts
Immediately weaken the mind.
Purity-thoughts
Immediately sweeten the heart.

20462

My surrender-life
God declares
As His own stupendous success.

20463

The invitation of eagerness
God always
Delightedly accepts.

20464

There really are no shortcuts
To reach
God's Perfection-Satisfaction-Shore.

20465

O seeker,
You must never violate
The rules
Of your aspiration-heart-temple.

20466

Those who yield to threats,
Although they eventually surrender,
Will never become the friends
Of their tormentor.

20467

If you can happily surrender
Your earth-existence to God,
Then no hope of yours
Will ever be able to desert you.

20468

In your spiritual life,
If your heart-flames are not mounting,
Then there may come a time
When you will be buried
By your own doubt-avalanche.

20469

Hard is it
For any human being
To go beyond
His desire-reach
To arrive
At his destination.

20470

Now that your heart
Is free of ignorance-night,
Your heart-love-blossoms
Will have no thorns.

20471

Educate at and graduate from
The soul-university
To be a perfect human being.

20472

God surprised me
By coming into my life
Quite unexpectedly.
I am surprising God
By staying inside His Heart-Garden
Permanently.

20473

Only our surrender-joy
Has the capacity
To climb up to
God's Transcendental Height.

20474

Dream of the future's sweetness
By casting aside
The bitterness of the past.
