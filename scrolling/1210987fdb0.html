25160

Spirituality
Is a divine art
And not a human science.

25161

Where the battle is,
The goal also
Has to be there.

25162

My fear of God
Again and again saves my life
From the all-devouring doubt-tiger.

25163

It seems that
The mind's disobedience-chain
Never ends.

25164

My heartbreaking helplessness
Quicker than the quickest
Has carried me to my Lord's
Compassion-Feet.

25165

Not only do I know,
But also I feel every day
That I belong to
My Lord Supreme alone.

25166

We mourn for everything else,
But not for the loss
Of our spiritual qualities.

25167

Can you not see
That your Master is creating
Tremendous receptivity inside you
For his outer scolding?

25168

Self-offering
Is the lightning-progress
Of a God-lover.

25169

If you are a genuine God-lover,
Then God will give you the capacity
Today to walk faster than the fastest,
Tomorrow to run faster than the fastest
And the following day to fly
Faster than the fastest.

25170

Alas,
Why is my gratitude-ecstasy
Fast asleep?

25171

Follow,
Follow sleeplessly,
Follow your Master's
Beckoning hand!

25172

If we do not pray to God
For the purification of our thoughts,
Then our thoughts will remain
Dismal and depressing.

25173

If we can silence our mind completely,
Then God will give us the capacity
Completely
To run at lightning speed.

25174

Do not waste your time
In your mind-forest.
Spend all your time
In your heart-garden.
