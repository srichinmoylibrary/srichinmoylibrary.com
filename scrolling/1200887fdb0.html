15160

Once a human being
Enters into the spiritual life,
His perfect God-union is guaranteed.
It is only a matter of time.

15161

When I think of my mind,
I see it indulging in self-expression.
When I think of my heart,
I see it enjoying devotedly and soulfully
God-experience.

15162

God loves a simple meal
Made of small quantities of
Mind's love, heart's devotion
And life's surrender.
He never cares for an elaborate meal.

15163

O earth-bound time,
Do not be afraid of me.
I shall not break you asunder.
I shall only turn you into
Endless Heaven-free time
With my aspiration-heart
And dedication-life.

15164

Just a soft and gentle push
From above
Has brought me and my mind
To my heart's illumination-door.

15165

My yesterday's fame-fed life
Is starving for
Today's life-giving joy.

15166

Each time
I consciously and deliberately enjoy
The power of my doubting mind,
I see myself entering
The precincts of inner death.

15167

Do not be afraid
Of your torture-troubles.
Have faith in your own self-giving life.
All your troubles will be dwarfed
To fruitless inconsequence.

15168

It is you
Who will eventually suffer more
When you decline
God's Invitation to dine with Him.

15169

O my mind-doubts,
One by one all my outer friends
Will withdraw their support from you.
You will die a most ignoble death!

15170

When he felt sincerely guilty
For his self-praise,
God's Forgiveness-Heart
Gave him a very high raise
In his spiritual life.

15171

Depression-mind is a subject
I have studied for years.
I shall now discontinue it.
From now on I shall study
Determination-heart.

15172

O my mind,
Do not doubt your heart.
Your heart is your real friend.
Give your heart a chance
To erase your failure-fear.
It will succeed,
And real happiness
Will fast approach you.

15173

Every morning when I do not
Pray soulfully and meditate powerfully,
I feel an irreparable sense
Of inner loss.

15174

When I threw away insecurity
From my aspiration-heart,
A most powerful relief
Flashed through my life.
