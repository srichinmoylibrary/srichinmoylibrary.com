1090.

A self-giving life is always inspired,
Not only divinely but also perfectly,
By God Himself
To manifest God amazingly
On earth.

1091.

My Lord,
Why are You so unkind to me?
Can You not see
That I am trying so hard
To rebuild my long-lost friendship
With You?

1092.

The wall that separates
The heart from the mind
Is the wall of ruthless misunderstanding.

1093.

O my mind,
You think you are
A messenger of light.
If so, why are you not
Fearless and spotless?

1094.

My mind's self-indulgent thoughts
Were abruptly interrupted
By God's Thunder-Feet.

1095.

O my Lord Supreme,
Do give my heart love-lessons
So that my heart can remain
Ever young, ever cheerful
And ever self-giving.

1096.

Nobody can prevent
The inevitable collision
Between the inner aspiration-world
And the outer desire-world.

1097.

The smiles of Mother-Earth
Are always blossoming,
But only the oneness-heart can see them,
Admire them, adore them and cherish them.

1098.

The arrogance of the mind
Totally destroys
The tranquillity of the heart.
But the same arrogance-mind
Helplessly trembles
At God's distant Thunder-Voice.

1099.

When I love the world,
God asks me to come to Him.
When I serve the world,
God Himself comes to me.

1100.

With His Compassion-Eye,
God speaks to me.
With my aspiration-heart,
I speak to God.
