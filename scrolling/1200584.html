14801

One day I shall see God.
This hope is nothing other than
A Rainbow-Smile from God Himself.

14802

The human mind
Is always slow to awaken
To God's earth-manifestation-Need.

14803

If God's Satisfaction
Is not for God's sake,
Then we shall never be worthy
Of our divinity's loftiest height.

14804

God does not mind
If we limit Him.
God does not mind
If we criticise Him.
But He does mind
When we limit our own capacities
And indulge in self-criticism.

14805

To deliberately criticise
Another individual
May cause an indelible stain
On the critic.

14806

Is man in search of
A new self-discovery
Or a new self-interest?

14807

Do not underestimate
At any moment
Your heart's God-commitment-capacities.

14808

When I look at him,
I immediately see
That he is nothing other than
A sheltering shield
Of humility-light.

14809

God does not want you
To suffer from thought-starvation.
He only wants you
To have thought-illumination.

14810

Since you have infinite capacity
To surprise the world in many ways,
Can you not surprise the world
Just by saying that you will
Never, never find fault with the world?

14811

Each time I do not fulfil
An inner promise of my heart,
I feel that my mind and vital
Are two completely smashed wings
Of my soul-bird.

14812

Who can be against me
When I take shelter
At God's adamantine Feet?
Not even the world's
Strongest and darkest foe!

14813

If God-invocation is in your heart,
Then you will never be doomed
To life-isolation.

14814

A heart of humility
Has always something special
To offer,
Anywhere in the world.
