14 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Do inundate my entire life
With Your infinite Compassion
So that I can soften the sorrows
And decrease the sufferings
Of the world.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

14 July 1999, 6:55 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

14 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I am my heart's deep sorrow.
I am still unable to concentrate
Every second of my life
On Your Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

14 July 1999, 7:07 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

14 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Today my heart would like
To introduce my mind to You,
But my mind is terribly afraid of You.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

14 July 1999, 7:21 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. seventy-two times with one arm. (36 right, 36 left)

14 July 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I know, I know,
You are not in my earthly house.
You are not in my Heavenly Home.
You are only in the tears
Of my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

14 July 1999, 9:09 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs. New record.

15 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
My soul's patience-light
Is the peace-dreamer
Of my mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

15 July 1999, 8:29 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 45 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

15 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
My soul's confidence-height
Is the peace-maker
Of my body, vital and mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

15 July 1999, 8:57 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 210 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 230 lbs. with each arm separately.

15 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Dire challenges from the outer world
Enter into my inner world.
My soul's indomitable will-power says,
"Get out! Get out!
I have no time
To pay any attention to you."
My Supreme, my Supreme!

15 July 1999, 9:12 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and nine times with one arm. (56 right, 53 left)

16 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
The moment I touch Your Lotus-Feet
Lovingly, prayerfully, soulfully,
Unreservedly and unconditionally,
My tiny heart-hut becomes
A larger than the largest
And mightier than the mightiest
Palace.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

16 July 1999, 9:09 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

16 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I am so fortunate.
The moment I was born,
Gratitude accompanied me.
Right from the very first breath
Of my life,
Gratitude has been living
Inside the very depth of my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

16 July 1999, 9:17 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

16 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I do not have the eyes
To see You,
But I do have the heart
To feel Your Presence
Inside my heart.
My soul is telling me
That is what You actually
Need from me and want from me.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

16 July 1999, 9:27 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and seven times with one arm. (52 right, 55 left)

17 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I shall have purity
In my thoughts,
I shall have beauty
In my speech
And I shall have sincerity
In my actions.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

17 July 1999, 8:50 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

17 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I shall wait devotedly
And unconditionally
To hear from You
What I am supposed to do
To please You only
In Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

17 July 1999, 8:34 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

17 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
My appreciation of You is good,
My admiration of You is better,
My adoration of You is best,
But my devotion for You
Is infinitely, infinitely better
Than my appreciation, my admiration
And my adoration.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

17 July 1999, 9:08 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 70 lbs. with one arm.

17 July 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Only my unconditional surrender
To Your Will
Will forever succeed.
Everyone else will fail me,
And I shall fail them as well.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

17 July 1999, 9:39 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 900 lbs.

19 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart shall keep dreaming,
My life shall keep smiling,
My soul shall keep promising
And
I shall keep fulfilling.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

19 July 1999, 7:21 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 190 lbs. with each arm separately.
