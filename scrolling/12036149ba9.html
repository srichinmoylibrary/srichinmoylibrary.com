17845

A man of desire
Either consciously or unconsciously
Cherishes
Possessiveness-hunger.

17846

If you hesitate
To give yourself completely,
Then you will not be supplied
With new energy,
New light
And new perfection.

17847

Who says
That all paths are difficult?
Try sincerely
The path of the heart.
To your wide surprise
You will see
That the heart's road
Is extremely easy.

17848

I may not allow
God the Justice
To enter into my heart
Any time He wants to,
But I shall always allow
God the Compassion
To claim me.

17849

The shameless mind
Begs God to grant it
The authority
To perfect the human race.

17850

The human muscle-power
Will be worse than useless
If God does not come first
In everything.

17851

The audacity
Of the destruction-mind
Tries to bring to the fore
World-extinction.

17852

I am looking for a new road
That will lead me
To God's infinite Compassion-Light.

17853

I do not know
Who the beggar is
Inside my mind.
But I do know that the emperor
In my heart
Is steering my life-boat
To reach my Destination supreme.

17854

Who can ever be happy
Under the tyrannical mind's
Dictatorship?
Who can be unhappy
Under the heart's self-giving
Friendship?

17855

What is my heart doing?
My heart is devotedly building
My soul's
Love-compassion-wisdom-temple.

17856

The sorrows of the outer life
Cannot touch
The unshakeable poise
Of the inner life.

17857

How can you desire
An imperishable success?
For desire itself
Has a very short breath.

17858

God's selection process
Is indeed
Humanity's satisfaction-progress.

17859

My heart is always in favour
Of my life's
Perfect satisfaction-change.
