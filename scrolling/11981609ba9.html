12445

If you have
A surrender-gratitude-heart,
Then God Himself will adorn you
With His Fragrance-Heart-Flowers.

12446

Never breathe "Why?"
If you wish to fly
In God's Heart-Sky.

12447

Procrastination
Is a fruitless production
Of unlit minds.

12448

A spiritual king is he
Who is the possessor
Of an empty mind
And a full heart.

12449

Because of your unused heart,
Today you have become
A total failure
In your spiritual life.

12450

Morning is the time
For me to offer
My gratitude-heart.
Evening is the time
For me to offer
My peace-life.

12451

For aspiration-flames,
Every day I force my mind
To go to my heart's
Aspiration-faith-garden.

12452

At last I have thrown away
All my mind's impurity-presents
And all my heart's
Insecurity-presents.

12453

To lead a spiritual life
Means to enjoy
The beauty and purity
Of one's own heart-garden.

12454

I have many homes
To live in,
But my heart's gratitude-home
Is by far the best.

12455

My heart-troops have to conquer
My mind-territory
So that I can have a fruitful
Satisfaction-life.

12456

A humility-heart
Is bound to win
In humanity's
Perfection-search-life.

12457

The Master tells the unaspiring seeker
That the Hour of God
He will not be able to escape.

12458

Practise, practise every day
Your life's aspiration-dedication-songs
If you want to be
A God-fulfilling dreamer.

12459

No fame can buy
Any lasting happiness.
True happiness is in
Sleepless self-giving.
