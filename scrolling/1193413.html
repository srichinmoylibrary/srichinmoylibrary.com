7701

As soon as my heart-day dawns,
I shall claim my Inner Pilot
As my own, very own.

7702

God's preparation in you
You have not seen.
But your perfection before the world
You will see.

7703

You have peace of mind.
That means you love God
Unconditionally.

7704

I desire
To become great.
I aspire
To become good.
I surrender
To become perfect.

7705

Do not feel sad!
God is definitely going to do
Something extraordinary
With your failure.

7706

I do not want to know
Who God is
As long as I know
That God is for me.

7707

Ask your mind how far away
You are from God.
Ask your heart how close
You are to God.

7708

His doubting mind
Discourages him.
His unwilling mind
Fails him.

7709

Do you remember
That once upon a time
You only loved God
And never even imagined
Becoming another God?

7710

You have done exactly the opposite
Of your promise.
You have betrayed your heart.

7711

Self-interest
Cannot rest
In the heart
Of peace-delight.

7712

You do not understand.
Therefore, you criticise.
You criticise.
Therefore, you will remain
Unfulfilled.

7713

You hide your ignorance.
As if that is not enough,
Why do you have to treasure it
Unreservedly?

7714

If you have inner courage,
Then you will be able to believe
That God is for you.
