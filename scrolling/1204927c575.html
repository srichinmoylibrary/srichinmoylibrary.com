19190

The uncertainty of the changing times
Will certainly be transformed
By the beauty, purity and divinity
Of God's Eternal Time.

19191

When I am in my deep meditation,
I go far beyond all the horizons
Of my mental thoughts,
And it is then that I feel
That I am only for God.

19192

If your heart does not reach out
To the suffering world,
How can you ever dream of pleasing God
In God's own Way?

19193

When my aspiration climbs up
High, higher, highest,
I laugh and laugh
At my mind's ultimate frontiers.

19194

My heart, your future perfection
Is already assured
By God Himself.
What you need is constant expansion
Of your receptivity-delight.

19195

Is there any heart
That does not care for perfection?
Again, is there any life
That dares to be perfect?

19196

Do not find fault
With the self-indulgent ones.
Let them wait
For God's supremely chosen Hour.

19197

Today my heart
Has said good-bye for good
To insecurity,
And my mind, to doubt.

19198

My Lord Supreme, do bless me
With a special blessing:
I wish to remain permanently
An aspiration-heart
And a dedication-life.

19199

O my mind,
The doubts that snatch away
Your heart's happiness-light
Must not be your friends
No, not even fleeting friends.

19200

Surprise after surprise
Will arrive at your life-door
If you make your heart
A constant consecration to God's Will.
