6960 Between Two Shores

Your mind-boat is plying
Between criticism-shore
And cynicism-shore.

6961 I Bow

I bow to the light
That never dims.
I bow to the delight
That never fades.

6962 If You Wage War

If you wage war against fear,
You are divinely great.
If you wage war against doubt,
You are supremely good.

6963 Your Mind's Imperfection-Impurity

You do not have to seek
Your mind's imperfection-impurity.
Your mind's imperfection-impurity
Will inevitably find you.

6964 Another Opportunity-Smile

Another opportunity-smile
God has granted me today
To meditate on Him unconditionally.

6965 If Your Heartbeats Cling

If your heartbeats cling
To God's Golden Boat,
Then your life will never sink.

6966 All My Achievements

May all my achievements,
Good and bad,
Die of inattention.

6967 His Mind's Inner Poverty

He is his mind's inner poverty.
No wonder his outer life
Is a barren desert.

6968 Now I Am Going Back

I have already gone
To the very end
Of the world's doubt-voyage.

Now I am going back
To the very beginning
Of my faith-voyage.

6969 Two Unnecessary Questions

Two unnecessary questions:
Does God love me?
Do I need God?

6970 I Am Crying Inside

I am crying inside
My sincerity-heart
To humble the pride
Of my haughty intellect.

6971 Not Used To Deception

Since your heart
Is not used to deception,
Your life will before long
Be granted perfection.

6972 Your Simplicity-Mind-Tree

Your simplicity-mind-tree
Must not bend.
Your sincerity-heart-tree
Must not break.

6973 His Heart's Silence-Smile

Alas,
His heart's silence-smile
Has been chased away
By his mind's reckless thoughts.

6974 Inside His Searching Mind

The thoughts that nestle
Inside his searching mind
Are pure, man-loving
And God-serving.
