21075

Disobedience-road leads us
Nowhere.
Obedience-road shows us
The satisfaction-goal
Everywhere.

21076

O obedience,
You have made my eyes beautiful,
You have made my heart pure,
You have made my breath self-giving,
You have made my mind clear.

21077

As the world will manage nicely
Without me,
Even so I shall manage my life wisely
Without the world.

21078

My gratitude is worthless
If there is no abiding God-faith
In my gratitude.
My surrender is useless
If there is no abiding God-faith
In my surrender.

21079

You do not have to fly
To the blue-vast sky.
The blue-vast sky will enter into you
If you turn your mind into a silence-home.

21080

My Lord, I implore You
Not to make me happy
In my own way,
But to make me perfect
In Your own Way.

21081

O seeker, if you do not want
To believe in your crying heart,
At least believe in
Your illumining soul.

21082

The living breath
Of my real existence
Is my Lord's Compassion-Rain.

21083

I came into the world
To make my Lord Supreme happy.
Alas, what am I doing?
I am desperately trying
To make myself happy!

21084

People become infinitely more jealous
When you climb up
The ladder of success
Than when you climb up
The ladder of progress.

21085

My simple but urgent
Request to God
Is not to fulfil
Any desire of mine
Whatsoever!

21086

The only indispensable thing
In my life
Is my ceaseless attempt at
Perfection.

21087

Since you cannot fathom
Your Master's heart,
It is good for you to remain silent
And not to ask him the reason
He does things in an unusual way.

21088

Who can prevent you from thinking
The right thing,
If not your mind?
Who can prevent you from doing
The wrong thing,
If not your heart?

21089

You should not delay
When the gates of the aspiration-world
Beckon you!
