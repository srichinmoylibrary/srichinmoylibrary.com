16615

I am so happy
That your inner beauty-seeds
Are growing so rapidly
In your outer life.

16616

My gratitude-flower
And God's Satisfaction-Hour
Keep my life-boat
Sailing smoothly.

16617

Every morning I send my mind
For humility-training
And I send my heart
For purity-training.

16618

How affectionately my soul
Is teaching my heart
To fly on the blue-gold silence-wings
Of Eternity!

16619

My prayerful eagerness
Can devotedly accomplish
Everything that God wants me
To accomplish.

16620

A true heart-hero
Pleases God at every moment
Of his life.

16621

Your mind's fiery will-power
I admire.
Your heart's tenderness-tears
I love.

16622

At last I have found
My Lord's Footprint
On the altar
Of my sacred heart's
Secret temple.

16623

My doubt-challenging confidence-soul,
Do take me with you
Wherever you go!

16624

Every day I enjoy watching
My rainbow-radiance-soul
From my gratitude-heart-garden.

16625

Because of my morning's
Delight-heart
My Lord is exceedingly pleased
With me.

16626

This morning
I forcefully evacuated
All the dark doubt-fog
From my mind.

16627

Every day I am supposed to renew
My heart's silence-licence.
I do it cheerfully and gratefully.

16628

Every morning
My soul compassionately teaches
My heart
How to run at top enthusiasm-speed.

16629

O my vital,
You must not scatter
Your frustration-thorns everywhere.
Don't make me suffer so helplessly!
