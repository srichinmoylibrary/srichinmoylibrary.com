20730

Another name for the mind-cancer
Is suspicion.
Another name for the heart-cancer
Is ingratitude.

20731

My mind thinks that my God-service
Is an obligation.
My heart feels that my God-service
Is an illumination.

20732

According to me,
I am God's frustration.
According to God,
I am still in preparation.

20733

What my inert mind needs
Is enthusiasm-lightning-speed
To make the fastest progress
In the spiritual life.

20734

O my stupid mind,
Can you not see
That all your satisfactions
Are superficial and artificial?

20735

No matter how hard we try
To conquer
Our voracious tiger-thoughts,
We simply fail.

20736

When will my mind become
A God-obedience-qualifier
And my heart
A God-gratitude-qualifier?

20737

O seeker,
God is not asking you
To prove His existence on earth.
He is asking you only to be
His constantly self-giving instrument.

20738

O my mind, O my heart,
I am so proud of you two,
For today God Himself
Will be proudly present
At your obedience-gratitude-graduation.

20739

If you magnify your self-importance,
Then you are bound to lose
Your Lord's Satisfaction-Smiles.

20740

Nobody can expect anything
Great or good
From his moody mind
And bloody vital.

20741

Nobody can deny that at times
His aspiration-life and dedication-life
Are strewn with upheavals.

20742

Idleness
Is always nourished
By hundreds of excuses.

20743

A God-attention-monopoliser
Can never be
A true God-lover.

20744

A world-saviour
Who is not first and foremost
A God-worshipper
Is indeed an off-key note.
