13990

Surrender has been a friend of mine
For a long time.
Alas, still I do not know him very well!

13991

When Heaven helps you
Readily,
Can you not offer Heaven
Your gratitude-heart
Unreservedly?

13992

Why don't you listen to God?
He has asked your guilt-harbouring mind
So many times
To forgive and forget,
And proceed onward.

13993

Alas, I cannot control my own mind
Even for a fleeting second.
Then how do I dare to try to control
The mouth, face and eye of the world?

13994

Your God-defending thoughts
Can and will save you
From your self-defeating life.

13995

Life's tragedies have only one remedy:
God's Compassion-Eye
In God's Satisfaction-Heart.

13996

If you want to choose happiness in life,
Then choose willingness first;
Happiness will automatically follow.

13997

To lose material things
Is to lose the power of earth.
To lose spiritual things
Is to lose the beauty of Heaven.

13998

Alas, I did not know
That my mind's impatience-moments
Have the capacity to destroy
My life's God-approaching dream-boat-shore.

13999

Your confession of your mistakes
Is God's
Perfection-Preparation-Satisfaction
In and through you.

14000

My prayers know how
To touch God's Feet.
My meditations know how
To feel God's Heart.
My surrender knows how
To please God
In God's own Way.
