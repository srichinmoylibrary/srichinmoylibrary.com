23915

God always takes for granted
Man's insincerity-mind
And his ingratitude-heart.

23916

My mind's lack of readiness
And my heart's lack of willingness
Can never be acceptable to God.

23917

God loves me,
Not because of what I say and do,
But because of what I truly am.

23918

When you are determined
To face bravely
The difficulty ahead of you,
The difficulty itself surrenders to you —
To your wide surprise.

23919

The mind-river is terribly afraid
Of flowing onward.
The heart-river enjoys
Flowing onward.
Therefore, it arrives unmistakably sooner
At the destination-ocean.

23920

From possession-hunger
To renunciation-meal
Is, indeed, an extraordinary distance.

23921

My Lord,
May the beauty of my heart
Forever remain
And not disappear
The way a rainbow disappears.

23922

A hero-warrior
Achieves extraordinary things
In his life
Because he does not allow himself
To be pushed by destiny's wind.

23923

The sword of time
Does not destroy us
Or punish us.
It only helps us transform our nature.

23924

O my mind,
Try sincerely to love silence
And not be inspired to broadcast
Your love.

23925

Who can save you,
Who can help you,
O my mind,
If you do not step aside
From doubt-suspicion-world?

23926

He is extremely happy
Because silence garlands
His morning prayers
And his evening meditations
With tremendous pride.

23927

He is his indomitable will-power.
He has said his final good-bye
To ignorance-night,
To breathe in the light and delight
Of Heaven.

23928

If you do not
Soulfully pray and meditate daily,
Then you will be overwhelmed
By a tidal wave of self-doubt.

23929

No mighty soul
Can ever be enslaved
By the tyranny
Of endless desires.
