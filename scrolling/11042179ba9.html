18 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
This morning, out of His infinite Bounty,
God has struck my name
From ignorance-night-list.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 June 2000, 7:54 a.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

18 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am my God-oneness-hope.
God is His God-Fulness-Promise.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 June 2000, 8:09 a.m. Before doing 310 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

18 June 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do bless me with one million hearts
To love You.
Do bless me with two million hands
To serve You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 June 2000, 9:46 a.m. Before doing 330 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

19 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Those who are now peace-dreamers
And God-lovers
Were definitely born with
God-aspiration-flames deep within.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 June 2000, 9:25 a.m. Before doing 350 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

19 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am my heart's gratitude-song.
You are Your Heart's Sweetness-Flute.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 June 2000, 9:32 a.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

20 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today my heart's
God-manifestation-bird
Is flying high, very high,
And far, very far.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 June 2000, 9:45 a.m. Before doing 360 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

20 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My inspiration says, "I can."
My aspiration says, "I have."
My realisation says, "I am."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 June 2000, 10:56 a.m. Before doing 370 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

20 June 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Who am I?
I am an insignificant God-lover
In my inner life of aspiration.
I am an insignificant God-server
In my outer life of dedication.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 June 2000, 11:08 a.m. Before doing 380 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

20 June 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Mountains appear only to disappear
The moment God's Grace descends.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 June 2000, 11:33 a.m. Before doing 400 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

21 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Forgiveness ends
My helpless days.
Your Compassion begins
My hopeful days.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 June 2000, 8:30 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs.

21 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
At long last I have unmistakably won
My life's self-discipline-battle.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 June 2000, 8:55 a.m. Before doing standing double-arm lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

22 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In the inner world
Our deliberate blindness
Disappoints Your Mind
And
Disheartens Your Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 June 2000, 9:16 a.m. Before doing 420 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

22 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What is my confidence,
If not my Lord's
Unconditional Compassion?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 June 2000, 9:28 a.m. Before doing seated double-dumbbell lift with 420 lbs.

23 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every morning I must quicken
My aspiration-heart
And stretch out
My aspiration-hands.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 June 2000, 12:10 a.m. Before doing seated double-dumbbell lift with 430 lbs.

23 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must never allow
My God-manifestation-readiness,
My God-manifestation-willingness
And
My God-manifestation-eagerness
To slow down.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 June 2000, 12:14 a.m. Before doing 430 lbs. dumbbell bench press.
