29 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A soulful smile
Is a mind-purification-guarantee.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

29 August 1999, 8:33 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left)

29 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Is extremely fond of my heart's
Unconditional love, devotion
And surrender-whispers.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

29 August 1999, 8:42 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and nine times with one arm. (54 right, 55 left)

29 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
An iota of insecurity-mind
Can easily create
An ocean vast of unhappiness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

29 August 1999, 8:59 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

30 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my mind be always
Dynamic and enthusiastic,
And may my heart be always
Pure and self-giving.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 August 1999, 8:24 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. thirty-three times with one arm. (16 right, 17 left)

30 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am so happy that I never miss
My God-obedience-classes.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 August 1999, 8:12 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. four times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

30 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do make me Your most perfect
Satisfaction-Smile-stamp.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 August 1999, 8:02 a.m.

Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

30 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
By Your infinite Grace
I am paving my progress-road
By sprinting,
And not by walking or running.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 August 1999, 8:56 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs. (2 lifts)

31 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my mind and I every day
Read my Lord's Forgiveness-Book.
May my heart and I every day
Read my Lord's Compassion-Book.
May my life and I every day
Read my Lord's Will-Book.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 August 1999, 8:31 a.m. After doing clean and press from the ground up to 90 lbs. (21 reps)

31 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
That my gratitude
Is my heart's purest tear
And my life's sweetest smile.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 August 1999, 8:43 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

31 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must never go back.
My victory's gong
Is my tomorrow's song.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 August 1999, 8:53 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. forty-one times with one arm. (17 right, 24 left)

31 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I would like my heart always to live
In the sweetness and openness
Of my tiny Indian village-life,
While my present life lives
In the newness and fulness
Of America's New York.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 August 1999, 9:02 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and three times with one arm. (47 right, 56 left)

Editor's preface to the first edition

These morning prayers were offered by Sri Chinmoy during his weightlifting sessions at his home in New York in August 1999.
