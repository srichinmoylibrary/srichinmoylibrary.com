14290

Every morning God comes to me
And helps me weed
My mind's thought-garden.

14291

I am ready to fight
With the omnipotent God.
But a single tear
Of the compassionate God
Can easily destroy my life,
Inner and outer.

14292

You have simply no idea
How soulful, beautiful and fruitful
Your heart's ecstasy-cry is.

14293

It is good to know,
Both from Heaven and from earth,
That although God can live
Without me,
He prefers not only to live
With me
But also to live for me.

14294

How can you hear
Your aspiration-alarm-clock ringing
If you are not at all serious
In your aspiration-heart
And your dedication-life?

14295

Do you want to live longer
Or do you want to become
A God-lover?
If you want to become
A God-lover,
Then come and take service from me.
If you want to live longer,
Just tell death to postpone
Your Heaven-arrival!

14296

To me,
Desire is as young
As the mind of man
And aspiration is as old
As the heart of man.

14297

An insecurity-mind
Is, alas, condemned
To be a prisoner of itself.

14298

The future world
Will not belong
To the world-fighters
But to the God-dreamers only.

14299

The delay in your
Unconditional surrender
Is the final barrier
Between you and God.

14300

Illumine your mind's
Ignorance-night.
Lo, your life-dreams are flying
In God's Heart-Sky!
