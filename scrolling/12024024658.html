16630

Every day receptivity-river
Beckons me to swim in it
Free of charge.

16631

If you ignore
The God without,
How can you adore
The God within?
Impossible!

16632

Your faith-heart
Is absolutely faultless.
Again, your doubt-faults
Are unpardonably countless.

16633

At the end of the inner race,
A goodness-heart
Is bound to be the winner.

16634

O my mind,
Because of your unruly thoughts,
I am now prepared to jump
Into an unknown abyss.

16635

Be supremely patient with yourself.
Say a cheerful 'yes'
All the time to your life!

16636

I must turn away from
My endless material needs.
It is high time for me to look at
My life's higher goals!

16637

The wheel of good fortune
Has spun once again
My way.
My aspiration-heart is back!

16638

How can you reach
Your soul's mountaintop
Without crying intensely
And self-givingly?

16639

Anything new
Is an excitement.
Anything perfect
Is an enlightenment.

16640

Human fulfilment
Is hopelessly confined.
Divine fulfilment
Never sets limits.

16641

At the aspiration-gate
We must consciously wait
And carefully listen
For God's blessingful Whispers.

16642

My Lord,
One last Smile from You
Today,
And I shall love You
Infinitely more
Tomorrow!

16643

If you really want to win,
There can be no
Division-thought-response
Either in the inner
Or in the outer fight.

16644

My Lord,
When I do not love You
Unconditionally,
Please bless me
With a heart-stopping fear!
