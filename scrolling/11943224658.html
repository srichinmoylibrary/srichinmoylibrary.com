8630

When ancient dreams invade you,
Be happy to be
In their sweet company.

8631

His heart's deep yearning
Is the only thing
He sleeplessly and deathlessly
Enjoys.

8632

Increase your love of God
Every day.
Your great relish for worldliness
Will soon disappear.

8633

Every day
Your heart's faith-bird
Must fly far above
Your mind's teeming clouds.

8634

His heart's oneness-cry
Left an everlasting mark
Here in the soil
Of aspiring earth.

8635

You and your life
Are under no obligation
To be mercilessly caught
By the web of your mind's questions.

8636

He is beautiful
Within and without
By virtue of his silver faith
In his Inner Pilot.

8637

Each soul comes into being
To enjoy a pilgrimage
To the Unknowable.

8638

If you can catch
Even a glimpse of the Truth,
Yours will be a life
Of genuine satisfaction.

8639

O Vastness-Blue,
My mind tells me
You are unreachable,
But my heart tells me
You are not only reachable
But also available.

8640

Your mind knows more
Than it is supposed to know.
Your heart does not feel
What it is supposed to feel.

8641

Man's aspiration
Is God's own Progress
In and through man.

8642

God and God's Consciousness
Enjoy their marathon
Satisfaction-Conversation
Every day.

8643

You have forgotten
To love God,
But God has not forgotten
To forgive you.

8644

I pray to God
To give me another chance.
God tells me
That it is all done,
But I shall have to wait
Another aeon
For my life's perfection.
