6490

Beyond my imagination!
Not only did God grant me
Illumination,
But He also remembered
My desire-life
So that He could purify it
And make it His Aspiration-Life.

6491

My Lord,
This is my sincere prayer:
Please do not fulfil my desires.
Only manifest Yourself
In and through me.
I do not want anything from You
Except whatever You want to give.

6492

Because you have become
Your mind's untouchable thoughts,
God has withdrawn from you
His Heart's snow-white Dreams.

6493

Every day you are late
For your prayer-appointment,
Yet every day you expect God
To arrive on time with Enlightenment.

6494

Not God the Justice,
Not God the Compassion,
But God the Forgiveness alone
Sits upon my heart's love-throne.

6495

When you dive
From the springboard of faith,
You see within,
You become without
God-Infinity's Satisfaction-Delight.

6496

Hasten your realisation-hour
And your perfection-days
If you want to become inseparably one
With God's Manifestation-Hour.

6497

Make friends
With the perennial Peace-Source
Deep within you.
Then you will be able to shake hands
With God's Ecstasy-Heart.

6498

My life's supreme necessity
Each day blossoms
With a sea-deep hope
And a sky-vast promise.

6499

First I say,
"The Highest in me is God."
Then, when I realise the Highest,
I say,
"My own Highest is God."

6500

Do you not see?
Inside your heart
Somebody is continuously
Praying and meditating.
Can you not feel?
The divine Heartbeat
Of the Supreme Beloved
Is constantly beating within you.
