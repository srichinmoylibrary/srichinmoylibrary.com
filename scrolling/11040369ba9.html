21 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O New Millennium,
You have brought
The beauty of newness
To my mind.
You have brought
The fragrance of newness
To my heart.
You have brought
The perfection of newness
To my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 January 2000, 7:16 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 800 lbs.)

23 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must not surprise You any more
With my life’s new ignorance-night.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 January 2000, 7:00 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

23 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Tomorrow God wants me to be present
Inside my heart
When He comes to visit me.
This is, indeed, His express Command.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 January 2000, 7:11 a.m. Asunción, Paraguay. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously three times. (Total: 800 lbs.)

26 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
This morning I embarked
On a new journey
To fulfil all my impossible dreams.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 January 2000, 8:31 a.m. Brasilia, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

26 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am such a fool!
I expect Infinity’s Nectar-Delight
From my desire-mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 January 2000, 8:45 a.m. Brasilia, Brazil. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 800 lbs.)

26 January 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every morning I love to do
My inner intensity-training.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 January 2000, 9:03 a.m. Brasilia, Brazil. Before doing sixty one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (23 right, 37 left)

27 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
He who gives is loved;
He who becomes is claimed;
He who surrenders becomes
A supremely chosen member
Of God’s inner circle.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 January 2000, 5:49 a.m. Brasilia, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

27 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A God-realised soul comes into the world
To make spirituality,
Which is God’s inner life,
Crystal clear for the God-dreamers,
God-seekers, God-lovers
And God-servers.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 January 2000, 6:03 a.m. Brasilia, Brazil. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 800 lbs.)

27 January 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The day a seeker can prayerfully,
Soulfully, sleeplessly,
Breathlessly and self-givingly
Cry for God
Is, indeed, a triumph-day for him.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 January 2000, 6:22 a.m. Brasilia, Brazil. Before doing forty-three one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (23 right, 20 left)

27 January 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God says to my mind,
“Please do Me a big favour:
Shorten your complaint list.”
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 January 2000, 8:56 a.m. Brasilia, Brazil. Before using bench press machine up to 100 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

28 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A sleepless and breathless
God-surrender
Is an inexhaustible God-Love-fund.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 January 2000, 7:51 a.m. Brasilia, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

28 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Time is too short,
But my love of God is not only long,
Very long,
But also eternal.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 January 2000, 8:07 a.m. Brasilia, Brazil. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 800 lbs.)

3 February 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
There are certain things
That hurt me so deeply.
How is it that I do not want
To renounce them immediately?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 February 2000, 7:10 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

3 February 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
As unconsciously I cherish my problems,
Even so, consciously I want
To place them at Your Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 February 2000, 7:24 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously six times (Total: 800 lbs.)

3 February 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God has no time for those
Who deliberately do not listen
To Him.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 February 2000, 7:36 a.m. Before doing multiple one-arm pushes with 500 lbs.
