7445

A man of purity
Enjoys free transportation
To God's Palace
In God's own Bus,
Driven by God Himself.

7446

God came,
But I forgot
To kiss His Compassion-Feet.

7447

One thing
I should not have said:
"I do not need
Any human being."

7448

There is no difference
Between a frustration-lover
And a destruction-monger.

7449

Aspiration,
Still you are my boss,
Although
I have far surpassed you.

7450

Possessors
Of earthly ability
Are quite often unaware
Of heavenly possibilities.

7451

Poor jealousy
Is forced to hear everything,
Although it does not want to.

7452

It is quite proper that God,
Being the Creator,
Has the right to be fully in charge
Of His creation.

7453

If you know
How to disobey,
Then God also knows
How to forgive.

7454

Disdain your pain!
Your earth-chains
Will without fail
Disappear.

7455

Your faith-boat
Will definitely take you
To your and God's
Satisfaction-Shore.

7456

God already has your heart,
The original.
He does not need your mind,
The carbon copy.

7457

Not God's Justice-Hand
But His Compassion-Heart
Wants to discover
My mind's hiding place.

7458

Who has told you
That you are under an obligation
To love your self-doubt?

7459

The searching mind's
Favourite acceptance-songs
Are extremely challenging
And illumining.
