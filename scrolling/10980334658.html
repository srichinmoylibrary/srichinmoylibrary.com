26 Limitless Delight I Enjoy ...

Limitless delight I enjoy
When I remain nameless
In my God-love,
In my God-devotion
And
In my God-surrender-service.

27 Pride ...

Pride
Openly challenges.
Soulfulness
Secretly wins.

28 Every Morning When I Meditate ...

Every morning when I meditate,
The singing flowers,
The dancing branches
And the climbing trees
Join me in my meditation.

25 November 2006 ...

25 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

29 God’s Outer Presence ...

God’s outer Presence
Is
Definitely needed.

30 God’s Inner Presence ...

God’s inner Presence
Is
Absolutely indispensable.

31 The Mind Thinks Only ...

The mind thinks only
Of pleasing itself.

32 The Heart Dreams ...

The heart dreams
Of pleasing its own
Absolute Lord Beloved Supreme.

33 The Tears Of My Heart ...

The tears of my heart
Are most devotedly following
The Footsteps
Of my Lord Supreme.

34 I Wish My Heart ...

I wish my heart
To be
A ceaseless God-hunger-cry.

35 I Wish My Life ...

I wish my life
To be
A breathless God-Touch-thrill.

26 November 2006 ...

26 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

36 I Have Made ...

I have made
My heart-cottage
With my tears.

37 I Have Built ...

I have built
My soul-palace
With God’s Smiles.

38 When My Meditation Is Deep ...

When my meditation is deep,
Very deep,
My heart breathes in
God-hunger-breaths.
