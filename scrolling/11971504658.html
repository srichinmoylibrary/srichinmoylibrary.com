11430

A true seeker's heart
Will, without fail,
Drink a large quantity of devotion-drink
Every day.

11431

Act not like a beggar!
Fly the banner
Of your oneness-divinity
With your Absolute Lord Supreme.

11432

The Golden Boat is sailing.
If you have not yet established
Your oneness-love with your Inner Pilot,
How can you be invited to be a passenger
In the Golden Boat?

11433

If you give up
Living inside your heart-world,
At every moment you have to bow
Either to your tricky mind
Or to your tyrant-vital.

11434

Follow not
Your blindness-mind;
Follow only
Your kindness-heart!

11435

Leave your ignorance-'I'!
Your bumper crop of realisation
Does not depend on your mind's brilliance
But on your heart's surrender-joy.

11436

For the betterment of this world
What have you done?
Have you ever said even one thing nice
About this earth?
Have you ever thought of pouring
Your concern-light
Into the heart of each and every human being?

11437

You cannot get confidence-light
And competence-might
Unless you dare to compete
With your own Heavenward achievements.

11438

I shall not blame
Your curiosity-mind,
But you also must not criticise
My heart's purity-plant.

11439

If you become
A humility-instrument-doll,
You are bound to hear
God's clarion Call.

11440

God asked you not to compromise
With ignorance-night.
Alas, you have not only compromised
With ignorance-night,
Bu you have also established
Your oneness-heart
With the destruction-blows
Of ignorance-night.

11441

Because of your newly acquired
Purity-heart,
Your life's transformation-gate
Is opening for you today.

11442

God is so proud of you
Because you are spreading
Ripples of cheerfulness
Inside His Heart-Garden.

11443

You show God what you have:
Your heart's aspiration-fountain.
God will give you what He has:
His Heart's Satisfaction-Mountain.

11444

What you have is nothing other than
An attention-seeking vital.
What God wants you to become is
A surrendered, fulfilling willingness.
