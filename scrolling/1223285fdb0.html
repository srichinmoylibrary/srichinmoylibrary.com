God, Do Not Worry

July 16th, 1995

God, do not worry.
I do need You.
Give me another chance.

Lord, I Have Come to You as a Son

July 16th, 1995

Lord, I have come to You as a son.
“Then sit at My Feet.”

Lord, I Have Come to You as a Friend

July 16th, 1995

Lord, I have come to You
As a friend.
“Then let us shake hands.”

Lord, I Have Come to You as Another Lord

July 16th, 1995

Lord, I have come to You as another lord.
“Then embrace Me; let Me embrace you.”

Every Day the Human in Me

July 16th, 1995

Every day
The human in me
Is becoming sweeter.

Every Day the Divine in Me

July 16th, 1995

Every day the divine in me
Is shining brighter.

Use It or Lose It

July 16th, 1995

Use it or lose it.
What is it?
Love.

Build It or Break It

July 16th, 1995

Build it or break it.
What is it? Hope.

Love It or Hate It

July 16th, 1995

Love it or hate it.
What is it?
Surrender.

God Has No Hands of His Own

July 16th, 1995

God has no hands of His Own.
We are His hands.

We Have No Eye of Our Own

July 16th, 1995

We have no eye of our own.
God is our Eye.

My Hidden Failure

July 16th, 1995

My hidden failure:
My dark ingratitude.

My Hidden Success

July 16th, 1995

My hidden success:
My climbing love for God.

At God’s Heart-Door

July 16th, 1995

At God’s Heart-Door the sign reads,
“Only the willingness-seekers are welcome.”

The Buddha’s Message

July 16th, 1995

The Buddha’s message:
“Life-liberation.”
