La Vie Est L'Ecole

December 31st, 1969

La vie est l’ecole.
L’ame est l’eleve.
La compassion divine est le professeur.

Translation

Life is the school.
Soul is the student.
God’s Compassion is the Teacher.

L'Experience Humaine Inclut Et Exclut

December 31st, 1969

L'Experience humaine inclut et exclut.
L'experience divine inclut et conclut.

Translation

Human experience includes and excludes.
Divine experience includes and concludes.

La Conscience Nourrit La Confiance

December 31st, 1969

La conscience nourrit la confiance.
La confiance nourrit l’action.
L’action nourrit Dieu.

Translation

Conscience feeds confidence.
Confidence feeds action.
Action feeds God.

Le Grand Homme Desire Apparaitre

December 31st, 1969

Le grand homme desire apparaitre.
L’homme de Dieu aspire a devenir.

Translation

A great man desire to appear.
A God-man aspires to become.

La Verite Est En Tout (1)

December 31st, 1969

La verite est en tout,
Mais l’amour est tout.

Translation

Truth is in all,
But love is all.

Mon Coeur Aime Celui Qui Aime Dieu

December 31st, 1969

Mon coeur aime celui qui aime Dieu.
Mon ame aime celui que Dieu aime.
Ma realisation aime celui
Qui aime Dieu sans en rien attendre.

Translation

My heart loves the man who loves God.
My soul loves the man whom God loves.
My realisation loves the man who loves God
Without expecting anything from Him.

La Lumiere Detruit L'Obscurite

December 31st, 1969

La lumiere detruit l'obscurite.
L'aspiration detruit l'esclavage.
La realisation detruit l'ignorance.

Translation

Light destroys darkness.
Aspiration destroys bondage.
Realisation destroys ignorance.
