60.

Forgive the blind and weak
Human dance
To see the Lord's sweet
Compassion-Glance.

61.

What can save me,
If not my constant oneness?
What can fulfil me,
If not my surrender-fulness?

62.

Don't magnify, but purify
Your future torture.
Don't belittle, but treasure
Your faith-rapture.

63.

In vain God comes to me
Again and again.
Does it not pain His Heart?
Does it not pain?

64.

Tell me, my Lord Supreme,
Tell me,
Is my heart every day
Nearing Thee?

65.

Purity's breath today
In my heart springs.
God-Satisfaction
It measurelessly brings.

66.

Daily I ask
My obedience-mind
My Supreme's
Blessing-Light to find.

67.

O God-loving
Heart of mine,
Surrender-joy
Is your only shrine.

68.

Can you believe this truth,
Can you believe?
My mind is prepared today
Peace to receive!

69.

O give me
The silver sincerity to ask,
What is my
Task supreme, my life's only task?

70.

What is an advanced degree
In spirituality?
What else, if not the seeker-heart's
Diamond-purity?

71.

My life is in my Lord's
Sleepless Compassion-Heart-Nest.
In Him alone my life's
Surrender-gratitude-harvest.

72.

No more, no more
Stark despair-dart!
God has given me
A hope-heart-start.

73.

Because you have thrown off
Your heart's impurity-load,
You can walk today on
Eternity's ecstasy-road.

74.

Your mind-diary must be burned
By your pure heart-fire.
Only then can your God-life
Climb steadily higher.
