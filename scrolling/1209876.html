24001

The best way
To stay on any spiritual path
Is to claim the path as one's own, very own
Unreservedly.

24002

The human in me thinks
That if I cry,
Then only will God smile.
But the divine in me knows
That if I offer God my heart-smiles,
Then only will God smile most proudly.

24003

My aspiring heart
Is the bridge between
Humanity's tears
And Divinity's Smiles.

24004

O God-seeker,
Be not afraid to take
A giant step forward.
God Himself is dreaming
His loftiest Dreams
In and through you.

24005

God's Compassion-Eye
My aspiration-heart follows.
God's Forgiveness-Feet
My clever mind follows.

24006

My mind is always the loser
Every time,
No matter how cleverly
It betrays the soul.

24007

A sincerity-mind and a purity-heart
Will never get lost
On their way to the God-destination.

24008

In my aspiration-heart
I always strongly feel
That the peace-seekers
Are the best God-lovers.

24009

The inner chaos
Will be infinitely worse
If we mix with
The outer darkness.

24010

A doubt-dominated mind
Will never be able to hear
The sound of God's Doorbell.

24011

Each human being
Either consciously or unconsciously
Is a student of hope.

24012

If you constantly and deliberately
Disobey God,
God may forgive you,
But you will never be able
To forgive yourself
Until you allow God-awareness,
God-obedience and God-gratitude
To fully blossom in you.

24013

If there is no obedience-seed,
There can be no love-plant.
If there is no love-plant,
There can be no devotion-tree.
If there is no devotion-tree,
There can be no surrender-fruit.

24014

The beauty of my morning prayer
I treasure.
The fragrance of my evening meditation
I treasure.
