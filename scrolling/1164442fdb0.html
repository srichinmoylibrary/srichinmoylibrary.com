60.

Your true longing for
Purity's newness
Is the first step toward
Your Lord's Forgiveness.

61.

Every night God empties
Your darkness-mind.
To His Compassion-Light
Be not so blind!

62.

Confidence has to be
Strong and sure.
Surrender has to be
Sweet and pure.

63.

The mind's long road
Leads to God's Justice-Light.
The heart's short road
Leads to God's Oneness-Height.

64.

Before you and God
Meet face to face,
Your dark ego-life
You must erase.

65.

A man of compromise
Is a real fool!
Impossible for him
To attend God-School.

66.

What you wanted:
An expectation-road.
What you now have:
A frustration-load.

67.

All out of proportion
Is your dark ego-life.
Soon you will be stabbed
By self-destruction-knife.

68.

The strength of harmony
Is admirable.
The power of peace
Is unfathomable.

69.

You will someday escape from
Your taut bondage-chain
If you soulfully welcome
Enthusiasm-reign.

70.

From world-competition
If you bravely refrain,
You can ride constantly
The self-transcendence-train.

71.

Now that you have put God
In full command of your life,
You cannot be threatened
By the world's destruction-knife.

72.

Who can fulfil God's
Compassion-Messages
If he has not studied
God's Love-Book-pages?

73.

Do not follow
The desire-man.
He belongs to
The peril-clan.

74.

God the Compassion
Is always adorable.
God the Forgiveness
Is always approachable.
