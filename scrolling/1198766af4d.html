13075

True, God cannot expect you
To think of Him all the time,
But He does expect you
To think of Him
At least in your free time.

13076

We long to liberate ourselves
From ignorance-night.
God wants to imprison Himself
In our love-devotion-surrender-heart.

13077

Day in and day out
His eyes hold nothing
But an expression
Of impatience-destruction.

13078

God grants me His Harvest of Smiles
When I tell Him
That I love Him only
And I need Him only.

13079

God is going to give us
Aspiration-opportunity
Again and again,
But are we ever going to use it
Devotedly, soulfully and self-givingly?

13080

Man's failure-life begins
Only when his mind wanders
With scattered thoughts.

13081

If you are not devoted
To your soulful dreams,
Then how are you going to blossom
Into a fulfilling reality?

13082

To be a choice instrument of God,
I must also learn the art
Of constant self-giving.

13083

There will always be a war
Inside your mind
Unless and until your mind surrenders
To your heart's sea of peace.

13084

Perfection-dawn will appear
When God is satisfied
With your heart's surrender-songs.

13085

There can never be
A sunlit, straight road
For a doubtful and fearful
Aspiration-runner.

13086

O Lord,
How long will my heart remain
Inside the useless nothingness of fear?
Where is my sleepless love
For You?

13087

If you do not master
The art of listening,
How can God grant you
His boundless Wisdom-Light?

13088

He who sleeplessly aspires
Can never be frightened
By man's suspicion-lightning.

13089

In the inner aspiration-world
A doubting mind
Is a short-lived cloud.
