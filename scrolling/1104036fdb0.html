5 February 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Long before I say something,
Long before I do something,
I must eagerly take
Your cheerful Approval.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 February 2000, 8:02 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

5 February 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God gives supreme importance
To the Master-disciple
Oneness-heart-tears
And
Oneness-soul-smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 February 2000, 8:24 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 800 lbs.)

5 February 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God’s Compassion-Eye
Does not criticise any human being.
God’s Forgiveness-Heart
Does not accuse any human being.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 February 2000, 8:36 a.m. Before doing multiple one-arm pushes with 500 lbs.

7 February 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A God-gratitude-heart
Is
A fulness-life-joy.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 February 2000, 8:06 a.m.Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

7 February 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
For years I have been trying so hard
To be a member of God’s
Delight-association.
Finally, today I am successful.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 February 2000, 8:18 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously six times. (Total: 800 lbs.)

7 February 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My self-giving life
Is the most powerful weapon
In my heart-armory.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 February 2000, 8:31 a.m. Before doing one-arm pushes with 500 lbs.

8 February 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today my Absolute Lord
Commands my negativity-mind
To disappear
Once and for all.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 February 2000, 7:15 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

8 February 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your unconditional Grace
Is the only way for me to make
Any progress in any field.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 February 2000, 7:29 a.m. Before lifting 450 lbs. with each arm simultaneously (Total: 800 lbs.) New record.

8 February 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Because of Your unconditional Grace,
I hope, I promise and I fulfil.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 February 2000, 7:38 a.m. Before doing sixty one-arm pushes with 600 lbs.

10 February 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A gratitude-heart is the unparalleled
Wonder of the world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 February 2000, 8:01 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

10 February 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do You really read my troubled heart
Every day?
“Yes, My child, I do.
In fact,
I pay first and foremost attention
To your troubled heart every day.”
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 February 2000, 8:17 a.m. Before lifting 450 lbs. with each arm simultaneously six times. (Total: 900 lbs.)

10 February 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The moment I demolished
My ego-mansion,
You blessed me with
Your supreme Pride-Reward.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 February 2000, 8:33 a.m. Before doing multiple one-arm pushes with 600 lbs.

12 February 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord,
Your Compassion-Delight-Voice
Is my heart’s secret and sacred choice.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 February 2000, 8:23 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

12 February 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every morning
God, in His Heart of Delight,
Invites me to sing with Him,
To run with Him
And
To play with Him.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 February 2000, 8:41 a.m. Before lifting 450 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times (Total: 900 lbs.)

12 February 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The delight in self-giving says:
Eternity I have.
The delight in God-becoming says:
Infinity I am.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 February 2000, 8:58 a.m. Before doing sixty one-arm pushes with 700 lbs.
