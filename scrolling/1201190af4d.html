15475

First simplify your mind,
Then clarify your mind,
Then purify your mind,
Then electrify your mind,
Then beautify your mind.
That's all!

15476

In your mind
There are many things.
But in God's Mind
There is only one thought:
How He can have
Your surrender-heart of oneness.

15477

To cry unconditionally
Is to collect
God's infinite inner Wealth.
To smile unconditionally
Is to spread
God's infinite inner Wealth.

15478

Self-giving and pure happiness
Are always found
In God's vaster-than-the-vastest
Heart.

15479

If you do not worry
About God's Will,
Then how do you expect God
To be involved in
Your desire-aspiration-necessity-life?

15480

Goodwill, goodwill, goodwill:
This is what each and every thought
Of yours
Must embody!

15481

What am I doing?
I am training my body,
Vital, mind and heart
To study in the sacred school
Of my illumination-soul.

15482

The mind's well-deserved punishment
God often, out of His infinite Compassion,
Obliterates.

15483

Just cross through
The channel of confusion.
You will see a new dawn
Awaiting your arrival.

15484

Unconsciously
You have chosen
Your trouble-time.
But now consciously
You are longing for
Your freedom-time.

15485

When you get
Your victory-trophy from God,
Be careful!
Your doubting mind
Can snatch it away from you.

15486

God will not allow you
To mask your world-contempt
Forever.
You will soon be exposed!

15487

When I see the world
Through God's Compassion-Eye,
I see that
The total transformation of the world
Is not a far cry.

15488

Do not allow your aspiration-heart
To skip even one meditation
If you want to see
The rainbow-dawn of your God-realisation
Sooner than the soonest.

15489

From time to time
Along life's road,
Restlessness-monkeys do come
To torment us.
But we must pay no attention to them.
We must walk fast, very fast,
Along life's road
To reach our supreme destination:
Nature's perfection.
