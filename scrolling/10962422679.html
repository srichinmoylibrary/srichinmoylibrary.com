13 Alas, God’s Compassion-Eye ...

Alas, God’s Compassion-Eye
Is no match
For our disobedience-minds
And disobedience-lives.

14 Each Moment ...

Each moment
Is a golden opportunity
For me
And for my God-obedience.

15 The Mind Shouts ...

The mind shouts.
The heart sprouts.

16 The Mind Steals ...

The mind steals
From God.
The heart feels sorry
For poor God.

17 Desire Cries ...

Desire cries
Pitifully.

18 Aspiration Flies ...

Aspiration flies
Sleeplessly.

19 The Great Life ...

The great life
Is not a fate-product.
The great life
Is a determination-achievement.

20 When I Concentrate ...

When I concentrate,
I can feel the presence of my soul
Deep within.

21 When I Meditate ...

When I meditate,
I can speak to my soul.

22 When I Contemplate ...

When I contemplate,
My Supreme Lord
Becomes extremely proud of me.

23 I Love The Joy ...

I love the joy
Of my life.

24 God Loves The Peace ...

God loves the peace
Of my heart.

25 No Escape, No Escape! ...

No escape, no escape!
This is, indeed,
Our human life.

26 All Escape, All Escape! ...

All escape, all escape!
Freedom is, indeed,
The life divine.

27 The Outer Happiness Comes ...

The outer happiness comes
Only to go.
