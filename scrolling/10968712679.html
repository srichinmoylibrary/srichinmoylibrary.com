14 There Can Be Nothing ...

There can be nothing
More natural
Than our meditation-dive.

15 I Am Happy ...

I am happy
Because my life
Is an inspiration-fountain.

16 I Am Happy ...

I am happy
Because my heart
Is an aspiration-mountain.

3 February 2005 ...

3 February 2005

Nanjing, China

17 The Soul-Singers ...

The soul-singers
Are God’s choicest singers.

18 The Heart-Singers ...

The heart-singers
Are God’s choice singers.

19 The Mind-Singers ...

The mind-singers
Are God’s failure-singers.

20 The Vital-Singers ...

The vital-singers
Are God’s Heart-collapse-singers.

21 The Body-Singers ...

The body-singers
Are God’s stranger-singers.

22 I Am, At Once ...

I am, at once,
God’s satisfaction-singer
And
God’s frustration-singer.

23 I Am God’s Satisfaction-Singer ...

I am God’s satisfaction-singer
When I sing
Only for God.

24 I Am God’s Frustration-Singer ...

I am God’s frustration-singer
When I sing
Only for
The thunder-applause of humanity.

25 When I Look Beyond ...

When I look beyond
All I have ever known,
God gives me
His sweetest Smile.

26 When I give to the world ...

When I give to the world

Far beyond my intended willingness,
God immediately embraces me
And tells me:
“My child, you are, indeed,
My tomorrow’s
Golden Dream-Boat-passenger.”

4 February 2005 ...

4 February 2005

Nanjing, China
