3645 A Sea Of Silence-Night

You have become a sea of silence-night.
Your illumination can never be
The real illumination,
Because your mind
Is forcefully suppressed
And your heart
Is unreservedly subdued.

3646 Uncover And Discover

I do not have to invent anything
To see the Face of God.
I just have to uncover and discover everything,
Not only to see the Face of God
But also to see the Forgiveness-Heart of God
And treasure the Salvation-Feet of God.

3647 The World Does Not Trust You

When you are in command of truth,
The world does not trust you.
When you are in command of peace,
The world does not trust you.
When you are in command of satisfaction,
The world does not trust you.
What can you do?
Just try to be in command
Of your heart of gratitude to your Pilot Supreme
For what He has given to you
And what He is to you.
Lo and behold,
The world will start trusting you.
Not only that, it will unreservedly love you.

3648 Absolutely Mistaken

Just because my mind is not clear,
You think that my Lord
Will not answer my humble prayer.
I tell you, you are absolutely mistaken.

Just because my heart is not pure,
You think that my Lord
Will not fulfil my sincere prayer.
I tell you, you are totally mistaken.

Just because my body is not strong,
You think that my Lord
Will not answer my soulful prayer.
I tell you, you are utterly mistaken.

3649 God Will Definitely Blame You

If you have not done
Anything remarkable for yourself,
God will not blame you.
If you have not done
Anything soulful for God,
God will not blame you.
But if you have not done
Anything meaningful for the world,
God will definitely blame you,
For this was the promise
That you made to Him in Heaven
Before you left for this world.

3650 You Enjoy Doubts

You enjoy doubts
Because you like
Your superficial mind.

You enjoy separativity
Because you like
Your artificial vital.

3651 Although I Fail

Although I fail,
I try to love God in His own Way.
Although I fail,
I shall be happy only when
God fulfils Himself in His own Way
In and through me.

3652 Make No Haughty Demands

Make no haughty demands;
You will regret them later.
Make no lofty promises;
You will regret them later.

3653 The Stillness Of The Mind

Unless and until you can really enjoy
The outer stillness of the mind,
Yours will be a life
Of worthless uncertainty.

3654 If It Is Too Late

If it is too late
For your mind to unlearn,
Then it is undoubtedly too late
For your heart to become happy.

3655 It Can Never Be Too Late

It can never be too late
To free your mind
From the meshes of doubt.

It can never be too late
To free your heart
From the prison of fear.

It can never be too late
To free your life
From the fetters of ignorance.

3656 My Hope Tells Me

The difference between my hope
And my enthusiasm is this:
My hope tells me
That whether I am ready or not,
God is coming to visit me.
My enthusiasm tells me
That God is all expectation to see me;
Therefore, I shall have to run hurriedly
Towards His Palace.

3657 The Human Mind

In the morning
The human mind runs after
Worthless uncertainties.

In the evening
The human mind runs after
Useless realities.

3658 When I Live In The Heart

When I live in the mind,
I can never hope to own
Triumphant confidence.
When I live in the heart,
God helps me to see
The barrenness of possession
And
The fulness of renunciation.

3659 What Is Active Devotion?

What is active devotion?
It is the beauty of inspiration-flow.
What is active devotion?
It is the purity of realisation-glow.
