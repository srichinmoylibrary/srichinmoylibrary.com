10930

The fragrance of a pure heart
Always intensifies
Your life's satisfaction-Delight.

10931

Do not try to win
The argument with ignorance.
It is not worthwhile!
Daily make friends with the wings
Of oneness-enlightenment-soul-bird.

10932

If you live in the darkness-depth
Of your mind-cave,
Then for you there is no clear choice
Between right and wrong.

10933

Who is the author
Of your heart's joy
If not your life's sleepless
And deathless
Surrender-seeker-soul?

10934

When my dream-life dances
In supreme ecstasy,
I go far beyond my mind's
Hurtful doubt-domain.

10935

Do not give in!
Do not give up!
Temptation-snares are everywhere;
Do not give in!
Illumination-peak is just above
Your confusion-mind;
Do not give up!

10936

Everything
Will eventually be pleased
Except one thing,
And that is man's desire-life.

10937

I accept my own faults
To purify my mind.
I accept others' faults
To perfect my heart.

10938

O hope-tears,
Where are you?
O hope-smiles,
Where are you?
O hope-realisation-truth,
Where are you?
O hope-perfection-God,
Where are you?

10939

For a long time
My Lord Supreme has been asking me
To apply for my
Aspiration-dedication-permit
And to stop my
Aspiration-dedication-demonstration.

10940

Your question is:
How far can faith take you?
My answer is:
As far as you want,
To see God-Eternity's
Infinity-Smile.

10941

Do not linger
In lethargy-land.
Otherwise, God will stop considering you
As His friend.

10942

The key to God's Heart-Kingdom
Can be found
Inside your heart's
Rose-purity-garden.

10943

If you do not banish
Your mind and its brooding doubts,
God and His Compassion
Will before long vanish
From your life-breath.

10944

God does not want you to be a victim
Of teeming expectations.
He wants you to be a lover
Of your own rising aspiration.
