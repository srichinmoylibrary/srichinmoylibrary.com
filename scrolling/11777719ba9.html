4845 You Must Not Allow

You must not allow your faith-plant
To be uprooted by thunderbolt-doubts
Even when your aspiration
Has touched the abysmal abyss.

4846 Conceal And Reveal

Conceal what you have:
Frustration-ocean.
Reveal what you are:
Aspiration-mountain.

4847 One Step

From the quick magic
Of my heart's surrender
To the abiding magic
Of my life's transformation
Is but one step.

4848 The Weapons Of Deception

If your mind uses
The weapons of deception,
How can it ever enjoy
The vastness of the sky
And
The stillness of the sun?

4849 Two Favours

I love God.
God loves me.
After I offer Him my love,
I ask Him to grant me
A big favour.
After He blesses me with His Love,
He asks me to ask Him
For any favour I want.

4850 I Talk To God

I talk to God.
God talks to me.
He also talks for me.
Alas, when shall I start
Talking for God?

4851 A Well-Read Scholar

When a well-read scholar,
If he has an iota of sincere eagerness
To see the heart of Truth,
Meets with a soul-fed and fruitful God-seeker,
He must needs heave deep-felt sighs.

4852 A Cheerful Mind And A Soulful Heart

A cheerful mind and a soulful heart
Cannot see anything wrong
In the entire world.

4853 I Cry For My Perfection

O humanity,
I cry for my perfection.
Do you want to know why?
I do it only
For your great satisfaction.
Do you not believe it?
Then ask God
If I am telling the truth.

4854 Concealing Your Weaknesses

As you feel
You can conceal
Your weaknesses from others,
Even so you think
You can conceal
Your weaknesses from God's Omniscience.

4855 Spontaneous Divinity

Spontaneous divinity
Is to the heart
What precious purity
Is to the mind.

4856 Heart-Perfection

Faith is
What may unmistakably be called
Mind-perfection.

Gratitude is
What may unmistakably be called
Heart-perfection.

4857 Your Perfect Sincerity

Your perfect sincerity
Can be appreciated
With perfect sincerity
Only by another God-lover.

4858 Dangerous Nonsense

My fear of God tells me
That it knows most accurately
How far away God is from my heart.
Indeed, this is dangerous
If not ridiculous, nonsense.

4859 The Serpent-Mind

The serpent-mind
Climbs up only one ladder:
The ladder of self-deception.
