6475

If you do not close
The eye of the mind,
Doubt-dictator
Is bound to rule your life.

6476

My intuition-eye tells me
That there shall come a time
When the world's wild criticism-chorus
Will forever stop.

6477

Do not allow your heart-strings
To play any melody except one:
Oneness-melody.

6478

Your heart's surrender-shrine
Is the beauty unparalleled
That climbs up
To touch the core of Heaven.

6479

Smile, smile with God
Inside your heart
When you see your life's
Newness-dawn.

6480

My sweet Lord,
I know that only if You come
And knock at my heart's door,
Out of Your infinite Bounty
And unconditional Compassion,
Only then will I regain
My lost opportunity.

6481

He wanted once again to march along
The inner path he once followed.
But alas,
A baseless fear caught him,
And he felt that perhaps
Again he would fail,
So the best thing would be
Not to even try.
Look at the height
Of his stupidity!

6482

Encaged in my body-cave,
I am finite.
But when I go deep within,
I see that I am infinite.

6483

By virtue of his inner capacity,
Each and every human being
Will someday become aware
Of his own Eternity,
Infinity and Immortality.

6484

A perfect combination
Of prayer and surrender:
"Not my will, but Thine."
"Let Thy Will be done."

6485

Of what use is my achievement
If I have the fear
That tomorrow
Somebody will do better than I?

6486

You do not need
An endless prayer
To penetrate Heaven.
What you need
Is a short and soulful prayer.

6487

When your aspiration-life
Disappears
And your desire-life
Powerfully appears,
Do not forget that
That is the proper time
For personal introspection.

6488

You once lived in the heart
For the soul.
Alas, what has now compelled you
To join the mind-confusion-club?

6489

In the calmness of my self-surrender
I have realised that my Beloved Supreme
Yesterday played the role
Of the Talker
And today is playing the role
Of the Doer.
