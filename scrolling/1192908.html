7201

How can you falter,
How can you fail,
When you know perfectly well
That God was, is
And forever will be
On your side?

7202

There are only two
Supreme words
That can save his outer life
And illumine his inner life:
Gratitude and surrender.

7203

Your heart can enjoy a holiday
Every day
If it gives its dedication-life
To earth
And its aspiration-life
To Heaven.

7204

Do you want to make
Your life sublime?
If so, then make
Your goal sublime first.

7205

Why do you have to die
Without hope?
Can you not live
With your soul's indomitable courage?
Try! You certainly can.

7206

Do not worry.
God will speak to you first
When you go to visit Him.
He will make the conversation
Extremely easy
And at the same time
Extremely fruitful.

7207

Since purity is your oneness-friend,
Why do you have to cry?
Why do you have to even worry?

7208

No matter how many times
I say good-bye
To my suspicion-mind,
Alas,
It never leaves me alone.

7209

His heart lives
In purity's brightness-home.
His mind lives
In impurity's darkness-cave.

7210

In the spiritual life
Constant trust
In one's inner guidance
Is of paramount importance.

7211

A spiritual Master is he
Who is a tireless guide
To God-searching human beings.

7212

Perversity and adversity
Are always attracted
To each other.

7213

My perfection does not ascend
Alone.
God-Satisfaction
Ascends with my perfection.

7214

What can I do
With a God-defiance-mind?
What can I not do
With a God-reliance-heart?
