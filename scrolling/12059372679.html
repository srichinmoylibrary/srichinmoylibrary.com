20115

Lethargy
Has millions of excuses.
Dynamism
Does not believe in excuses.
It only shatters lethargy's pride.

20116

Every day
Try to memorise
One God-dream-song
To make yourself divinely happy
And supremely perfect.

20117

Each doubtful thought
Deliberately reduces
The brightness of my life's day.

20118

I am not
A weeping weakling.
I am
God's own Confidence-Strength
In the depths of my heart.

20119

Each unaspiring thought
Is indeed as useless
As pure nonsense.

20120

What you need is
Determination-fire.
Uneasiness, unwillingness and unreadiness
Will all surrender
To the beauty and fragrance
Of determination.

20121

Each desire of the mind
Has the capacity
And is the capacity
To wither the aspiration-heart-flower.

20122

What do we gain
By interfering?
We gain nothing.
We just become a victim
Of our own stupidity.

20123

Not only your meditation-tree
But also your prayer-plant
Must never be uprooted.

20124

O my Lord,
Do give me another chance
So that I can plead
For Your Forgiveness.
"My child,
I shall definitely give you another chance
To remove the desire-list
Of your mind
And replace it with the aspiration-list
Of your heart."

20125

If you are a good man,
Then reject violence.
If you are a better man,
Then detest violence.
If you are the best man,
Then transform violence
Into the heart of oneness-love.

20126

Do not allow
Your heart-satisfaction-pocket
To become empty
If you want to fly
Higher than the highest
And faster than the fastest
In your aspiration-life.

20127

Doubt thinks
That it is extremely strong.
Faith knows
That it is infinitely stronger.

20128

Be not afraid
Of your God-dreams.
Your God-dreams are
Your life's future
Satisfaction-perfection-realities.

20129

If you can love
The beauty of the world,
Then not only the beauty
But also the fragrance of the world
Will be your permanent possessions.
