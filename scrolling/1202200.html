16401

My Lord,
Do give me the capacity
To sing every day
A few new love, devotion and
Surrender-songs
To make You happy.

16402

O my mind's
Enthusiasm-child,
Wake up!
Sleep not anymore!
It is already very late
For you to wake up.

16403

Is there anybody
Who has never suffered
From the stupid mind's
Ruthless inferiority-invasion?

16404

No!
Not God's Justice
But you alone
Have created
Your uselessness-life.

16405

Every day
God's Compassion
Teaches the seeker
How to discover
His life's deeper dimensions.

16406

O where are you,
My life's silence-speed?
O where are you,
My heart's gratitude-speed?

16407

My mind
Is now genuinely ashamed
Of its long-standing friendship
With doubt.

16408

My mind's strong convictions
Are no match
For my heart's pure aspirations.

16409

Every day
I force my ingratitude-mind
To go and visit
Its graveyard.

16410

I may not be sweet,
But my heart's gratitude-tears
Are always sweet,
Measurelessly sweet.

16411

Where do I live?
I live in my imperfection's
Despair-ridden world.

16412

A suspicion-mind
Is always a prelude
To a disaster-life.

16413

My Beloved Supreme,
Do You remember me?
Was I not once upon a time
A most favourite child
Of Yours?

16414

O my heart's peace-dove,
Fly, fly,
In Infinity's oneness-sky.
