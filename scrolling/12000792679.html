14315

Your forward surrender-steps
Make God happy
Far beyond your imagination!

14316

God is ready to give you
His blessingful Consciousness-Crown.
Just show ignorance
Your life's disgust-frown.

14317

Your God-manifestation-awareness
And eagerness
Do not underestimate!

14318

Alas, three promises we forget
When we enter into the world:
God-realisation,
God-manifestation
And earth-transformation.

14319

If you are not a genuine seeker,
God will definitely not allow you
To enter into His Heart-Temple.

14320

O my mind,
In so many years on earth
You have learned so little,
And what's more,
Even that was too late.

14321

Your mind's desire-dust
Has polluted your heart's
Aspiration-cries.

14322

May my mind every morning
Become as beautiful
As hope-dawn-rays.

14323

Your life-boat is sinking.
But even now
Your heart is not bringing God
Into the picture.

14324

Constant silent prayer
Receives a dream-boat
With golden sails
From Above.

14325

What are you searching for?
I am searching for my heart's
Birthless and deathless sun.

14326

When you have done away with
Your frustration-life,
You are blessed with
Aspiration-acceleration.

14327

If you are serious
About your self-transformation,
Then God will make
Your self-examination-course
Extremely easy.

14328

It is in the unknown sea
Of the Beyond
That I feel my permanent home.

14329

Unseal the treasures of your soul.
Your sadness-mind and madness-vital
Will no longer function.
