25645

I awake every morning
With my life-blossoming
Gratitude-heart.

25646

If enthusiasm is missing,
If eagerness is missing,
Then we shall always find ourselves
At the very, very bottom
Of our aspiration-ladder.

25647

God tells both Heaven and earth
To stop disputing over their supremacy.
He tells them that both of them
Must meet each other on equal terms.

25648

My heart, my life and my life-breath —
All I have placed beneath the dust
Of my Lord's Will.

25649

Be careful, O seeker!
At any moment
You can be trampled underfoot
By your brooding doubts.

25650

I have abandoned myself
Not to God's Smiles,
Not to God's Tears,
But to God's creation-feeding Breath.

25651

If you are wise,
Then at every moment
You are going to question
Not only your outer life,
But also your inner life,
For your own self-discovery.

25652

Every day
My soul teaches my heart
How to have
God-pleasing smiles.

25653

My soul every day reveals to me
The sweet notes
Of a new delight-song.

25654

If we do not nourish our life-tree
With compassion, concern
And oneness-smiles,
Then our service-life will never have
Beauty and fragrance.

25655

I am afraid
Of my mind's narrowness.
I am afraid
Of my heart's vastness.

25656

I try so hard every day
To be in the sweet Presence
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme
Inside His Heart-Garden.

25657

If you were
A truly God-dedicated man,
Then by this time
God would have made you
A God-liberated soul.

25658

What are you doing, my seeker-friend?
"I am building and building
An aspiration-heart-home for myself."

25659

Aspiration-requirement
Always comes first
In our Godward journey.
