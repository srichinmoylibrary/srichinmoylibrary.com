19901

God's Compassion-Eye
Is the only medicine
That can cure our shattered
Aspiration-hearts.

19902

A genuine hope
Is indeed a fort
To protect us from doubt-attacks.

19903

To fly
In the sky of freedom-happiness,
What we need
Is a faith-foundation
For our life.

19904

Our heart's tears
Must be far beyond the reach
Of our mind's thoughts.

19905

When God descends
With His smiling Eye,
We are supposed to ascend
With our crying heart.

19906

O seeker,
Inside your gratitude-heart
There will always be ample room
For your love, devotion and surrender
To grow and glow.

19907

There is only one way
For us to conquer
Our desire-madness,
And that way is the way
Of our aspiration-gladness.

19908

God is pitifully crying.
Do you know why?
Not because
You are not surrendering
To His Will,
But because
You are not allowing your heart
To claim Him as its very own.

19909

The insecurity-mask
Is larger than the largest.
Therefore, it easily hides
Our heart's Godward aspiration-flames.

19910

The hesitation and frustration
Of the mind
Always try to devour
The aspiration-flames and dedication-drops
Of the heart.

19911

We can grow old
With our desire-mind,
But we always remain young
And ageless
With our aspiration-heart.

19912

I have not one, but two places
To divinely enjoy myself:
Inside my God-manifesting soul
And inside my God-dreaming heart.

19913

Your ignorance-sleep
Will stop
When your genuine God-need
Starts.

19914

You are a victim
To constant helplessness
Because you are unwilling to swim
In God's infinite Compassion-Sea.
