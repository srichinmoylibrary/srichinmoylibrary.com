1 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have only one prayer:
Do give me the capacity
To utter Your Name prayerfully,
Soulfully and self-givingly
With every heartbeat of mine.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 May 2000, 7:23 a.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

1 May 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In sacred secrecy supreme
You are telling me
That You want from my life
The fastest progress
Both in the inner world
And in the outer world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 May 2000, 7:40 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 300 lbs.

1 May 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In my spiritual life
My only real death
Is my God-forgetfulness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 May 2000, 7:50 a.m. Before doing fifty-two one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (23 right, 29 left)

3 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Forgiveness, forgiveness, forgiveness —
This is my morning prayer.
Love and transform —
This is my evening prayer.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 May 2000, 8:08 a.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. five times.

3 May 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Injustice-night, injustice-night,
Injustice-night-everywhere.
Justice-light, justice-light,
Justice-light-nowhere.
But, my Lord, do give my life
The capacity to grow into
Your universal Justice-Light.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 May 2000, 8:22 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 280 lbs.

4 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day God sends
A special peace-message
To my mind,
But my mind summarily rejects it.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 May 2000, 8:45 a.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. five times.

4 May 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do give me the capacity
To feed only Your Need
And nothing else,
Nothing else.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 May 2000, 8:58 a.m. Before doing fifty one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (22 right, 28 left)

4 May 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Any time, any place
Is perfect to please God
In His own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 May 2000, 9:03 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 320 lbs.

4 May 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God is never interested
In my mind-explanation.
He is always interested
In my heart-illumination.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 May 2000, 9:20 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,050 lbs.

9 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A desire-life is, indeed,
A long run of expectation,
Frustration and destruction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 May 2000, 1:45 p.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

11 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I must plant my roots
Inside Your Heart-Garden.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 May 2000, 8:44 p.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

11 May 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must not picture my future.
I must live in my promise-fulfilment
Of the Eternal Now.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 May 2000, 8:57 p.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

13 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My crying heart triumphantly smiles
When it can sit at the Feet
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 May 2000, 7:33 a.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

13 May 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart's hope-flames
Are bound to grow
Into my life's promise-sun.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 May 2000, 7:49 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.
