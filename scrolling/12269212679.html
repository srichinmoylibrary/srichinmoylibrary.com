Bhakati Dao Bhakati Dao

December 17th, 1995

Bhakati dao bhakati dao pujte tomai prabhu
Bhul kareo tomai chere na jai jeno kabhu

Translation:

My Lord, give me
Devotion to satisfy You.

Tumi amai balo mago karte tomar kaj
Tomar karma karte keno pai je ami laj

Asat Pathe Chale Jara Asat Pathe Keman

Ami Asahai Chira Asahai Ei Kotha Bhebe

December 17th, 1995

Ami asahai chira ei kotha bhebe mari

Translation:

Helpless, always helpless...
