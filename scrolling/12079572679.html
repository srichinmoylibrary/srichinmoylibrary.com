22115

When we want to change
God's Will to ours,
God does not allow it.
Why not?
Precisely because
He cares for our perfection
Very dearly.

22116

We do not know
And we will never know
How clever and strong
Our self-deceptions are.

22117

Do you not see
That God's Compassion-Eye
Is chasing your runaway vital?

22118

God-disobedience
Is the ignorance-multiplication
Of life.

22119

God congratulates me
Only when I transform
My mind's 'I'-ness-pride
Into my heart's oneness-light.

22120

I am satisfied
When God's Mouth
Speaks to me.
God is satisfied
Only when my heart
Speaks to Him.

22121

God will give you many, many chances
To change your nature.
Therefore, be not disappointed
Even if you continuously fail.

22122

You do not have to take care
Of yourself.
Just give God the chance,
Cheerfully.

22123

From my gratitude-heart
I get everything.
From my surrender-life
I become everything.

22124

Every night I dream
That my mind's nightmares
Will soon come to an end.

22125

Who is not on the right track?
He who thinks
That he has nothing,
And he who thinks
That he has everything.

22126

Alas, quite often
Success creates nothing
But untold miseries.

22127

Our sleepless and breathless prayers
Show us the royal road
To self-discovery.

22128

To see God smiling,
I must drown
My questioning mind.

22129

Unless I am one hundred per cent
For God,
How can I expect satisfaction
Either from myself
Or from the world?
