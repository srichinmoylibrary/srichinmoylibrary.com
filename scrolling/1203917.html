18101

My Lord Supreme,
I know that You have
Countless secrets.
Please share with me
Only one particular secret.
What do You go through
To please
Each and every human being?

18102

I must remain
Inside my heart-fountain
So that I do not see
My mind's high barriers.

18103

God wants to tell you
His Life-Stories
Even if you do not want
To listen to Him.

18104

Two things God never does:
He does not stop
Speaking to me
And He does not stop
Waiting for me.

18105

My self-giving heart
Is the place
Where God's new Hopes
Will take birth.

18106

You can be proud of yourself
When your mind runs
Only towards God,
And not before.

18107

The beauty
Of sincere aspiration
And the purity
Of sincere dedication
Can and will
Definitely take the seeker
To his Source immortal.

18108

God tells me
That every time I move forward
I give Him
A prayerful and beautiful gift.

18109

Do not be afraid
Of going too far
With your dedication-life.
At one point
God's smiling Eyes
Will greet you.

Do not be afraid
Of going too deep
In your aspiration-life.
At one point
God's loving Heart
Will bless you.

18110

Do not try to understand God.
You will make Him cry.
Try to feel God.
You will make Him not only smile,
But also dance.

18111

When I pray,
I cry for God's Compassion-Sky.
When I meditate,
I long for God's Satisfaction-Sun.

18112

Alas, quite often
We desire so many things
That we do not actually need
At all.

18113

Before I make
Any complaints to God,
God closes not only
His Ears and Eyes
But even His Heart.

18114

With my heart's tearful songs
I try to please God,
But God wants me to please Him
With my life's cheerful stories.
