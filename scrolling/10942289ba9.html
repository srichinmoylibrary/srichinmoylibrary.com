40 Wherever I Go, ...

Wherever I go,
God's Compassion
Walks ahead of me
And His Protection
Walks side by side with me.

41 There Is Only ...

There is only
One Source:
God-Infinity's Delight.

42 My Faith In God ...

My faith in God
Is my inner freedom.
My faith in God
Is my outer confidence.

43 My God-Obedience ...

My God-obedience
Is my God-centred life.

44 God And I Always Speak ...

God and I always speak
The language of heart-fondness
And life-oneness.

45 The Love Of Power ...

The love of power
Dominates the outer world.
The power of love
Liberates the inner world.

46 Alas, ...

Alas,
When the mind's tunnel invites me,
I immediately accept the invitation
And run towards the tunnel.
But when the heart's palace invites me,
I get frightened. I hesitate.
Finally, I decline the invitation.

47 My Prayer-Tears ...

My prayer-tears
Quickly take me to God.
My meditation-smiles
Immediately bring God to me.

48 I Must Keep My Aspiration-Heart ...

I must keep my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
Far beyond
The limitations of my fate.

49 The Sweetness Of The Heart ...

The sweetness of the heart
Unmistakably nullifies
The bitterness of the mind.

30 November 2001 ...

30 NOVEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII

50 My Heart-Tears ...

My heart-tears
Are my thunders
To demolish my mind's
Pride-walls.

51 I Am Hopeless, ...

I am hopeless,
Worthless and useless
Until I sincerely feel
That I am of God
And I am for God.

52 My Heart-Flower ...

My heart-flower
Is God-Beauty.
My life-hour
Is God-Duty.

53 I Am My ...

I am my
Three hundred and sixty-five
Tearful days,
Yet my Lord Supreme
Is far, far away
From me.
