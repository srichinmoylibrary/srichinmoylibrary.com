8430

He is expert
In sailing his life-boat
On his mind's confusion-river.
Alas, what is worse,
He enjoys it immensely!

8431

O my heart,
Reject, reject!
It is high time
For you to reject
Your old insecurity-culprit-friend.

8432

His Inner Pilot
Is always pleased with him
And proud of him
Because at every moment
He breathes in and out
Only the clear air
Of innocence-light.

8433

God will definitely satisfy you
One day
Because you are always
Fond of living
Under the canopy
Of hope-delight.

8434

Alas,
Your mind is nothing
But the wild activity
Of uncomely thoughts.
In vain
I try to help you,
All in vain!

8435

His desire-cancer
Has progressed so rapidly,
Even his soul
Is alarmed at it.

8436

Every day
God enjoys reading
Your love-flooded
Heart-letters.

8437

Tomorrow's realisation
Shall never come to you.
In vain you are waiting for it.

8438

Not earth-bound
But Heaven-climbing questions
Should absorb your mind.

8439

Each ugly thought
Is the builder
Of deathless death-pillar.

8440

If you long to help the world,
Then help with your sleepless eyes
And undivided heart.

8441

Someday
The inner soul
Will claim its outer property.

8442

If you want to live,
Then dream.
If you want to dream,
Then love.
If you want to love,
Then become.
If you want to become,
Then cry with the heart of humanity
And smile with the Eye of God.

8443

To be animal
Is to sadly fail.
To be human
Is to doubtfully hope.
To be divine
Is to soulfully manifest
God the Dream on earth.

8444

You do not need any human admirer,
For God Himself is admiring you
Every day, all the time,
For your tireless and selfless efforts.
