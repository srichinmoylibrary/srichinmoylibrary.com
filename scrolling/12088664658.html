23030

God at times
Deliberately hides from us
To create in us
Our God-discovery-hunger.

23031

God is always anxious and eager
To shorten the distance
To our journey's goal.

23032

Today
My doubtful mind and I
Were forced to wade through
A great deal of misery
For ignoring God's Compassion-Eye.

23033

Since God is
A good listener,
Can I not be
A great speaker?

23034

Today,
From my God-disobedience,
God has suffered
the most powerful blow.

23035

My mind learns earthly lessons
Quickly.
My heart learns Heavenly lessons
Self-givingly.
My soul learns both earthly
And Heavenly lessons
Intuitively.

23036

As God does not allow any thought
To enter into His Mind unbidden,
Even so,
I shall not associate myself
With any unaspiring thought.

23037

Today, alas,
I have become the dying breath
Of my shattered hopes.

23038

God cries
For a dependable man.
Man cries
For an understandable God.
God cries
For an inseparable man.
Man cries
For a lovable God.

23039

Today
God-unbelieving minds
Are bound to be devastated
By their own ignorance-volcano.

23040

Before the ultimate God-realisation,
Everybody thinks and feels
That God is unknowable.

23041

The age of God-obedience will begin
When we realise
That God's Way is the only way.

23042

Today, alas,
My negativity-mind
Is destroying
My aspiration-heart-garden.

23043

O seeker,
Do not feel that you are
Doomed to disappointment.
Your failure-breath
Before long will vanish.
Wake up!
Make a fresh attempt.
Victory's crown is awaiting you.

23044

God tells the human mind
That it is hard for Him
To keep it in His Heart-Garden.
