83 I See The Authenticity ...

I see the authenticity
Of light
Even in the darkest night.

84 Not “Go Alone” ...

Not “Go alone”
But “Go together with all”
Should be our last
And lasting experience.

85 I Love My Heart’s Service ...

I love my heart’s service
And not
My life’s servitude.

86 Infinity, Eternity And Immortality ...

Infinity, Eternity and Immortality
Live together most amicably.
They are divinely and supremely
Bound to one another.

87 Difficulties Arise ...

Difficulties arise,
But we must make them feel
That we are destined
To reach a destination
Where they will not be able
To accompany us.
