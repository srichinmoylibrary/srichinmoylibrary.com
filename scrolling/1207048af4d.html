21275

Do not wrestle with
Your restless mind.
Meditate on
Your soul's delight
To silence and illumine
The pride of your ignorance-mind.

21276

I have come to realise
That my aspiration-revival
Is my only heart-survival.

21277

If we do not pray and meditate,
Then there will be no way for us
To protect ourselves
From complacency-decay.

21278

My Lord,
Your Will is my way.
My Lord,
Your Way is my only goal.

21279

When I remain inside my heart,
God becomes quite familiar.
But when I am inside my mind,
God is not only unknown
But also unknowable.

21280

I feel sad
When I do not see my soul.
I feel extremely sad
When I do not feel my soul's presence
Inside my heart.
I am doomed to disappointment
When I do not hear anything about my soul
From my Lord Supreme.

21281

The silence of my heart
Has finally silenced and illumined
My endless mind-sermons.

21282

God immediately and incessantly applauds
When His earth-transformation-team
Succeeds in manifesting Him
Even in a small measure.

21283

How much is God-realisation these days?
The price of God-realisation
Will forever remain
Unimaginably high.

21284

Alas,
Every day I spend my time
Inside my deplorable mind
And not
Inside my adorable heart.

21285

Every day I depend on
My sincerity and determination
To renew
My God-fulfilment-contract.

21286

When the human mind
Is left to itself,
Our God-fulfilment-promise
Will forever remain a far cry.

21287

God's Compassion is at work
When He appreciates us.
Our sincerity is at work
When we adore God.

21288

When a seeker realises God,
God tells him to make a choice:
Either to enjoy His infinite Bliss
Or to throw his heart and soul
Into God-manifestation on earth.

21289

God depends on me
Only when my life becomes
A ceaselessly unconditional breath.
