23615

Because He does not
See us smiling,
God every day sheds
Sorrowful tears.

23616

My Lord Supreme is planning
To write a new book —
Alas, a very short book —
On my obedience-mind.

23617

God long ago read my mind's
Disobedience-letters.
But He forgave both my mind
And its letters.

23618

Sincere willingness
Will sleeplessly and breathlessly
Advance
In God's direction.

23619

God takes
A God-gratitude-heart
And a self-giving life
As His most valuable Treasures.

23620

I rejoice
In God's Compassion-Sea.
God rejoices
In my gratitude-drop.

23621

If we take pride
In the noise of our popular acclaim,
Then down we fall
Into the abyss of self-disgrace.

23622

Simplicity's sweetness
And simplicity's fruitfulness,
Alas,
Always remain underestimated.

23623

If you want to make
Faster than the fastest progress
In the spiritual life,
Then your outer loss and outer gain
Must be less than minor considerations.

23624

O my soul-bird,
For me do not lower
Your vision's perfection-wings.

23625

Who is weakening me?
No, not any outer enemy,
But my own disobedience-mind.

23626

I beg my mind:
"Sleep and sleep and sleep."
I command my heart:
"Wake up, wake up, wake up!"

23627

My aspiration-heart
And God's Compassion-Eye
Have promised to meet
At least once a day.

23628

Aspiration
Heightens my God-journey.
Devotion
Quickens my God-journey.
Dedication
Enlightens my God-journey.

23629

My Lord, I know Your other Name
Is Compassion.
Do You have another name for me?
"Yes, My child,
I have one more name for you:
Hope."
