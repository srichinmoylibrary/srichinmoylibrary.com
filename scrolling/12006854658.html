14930

God wants me to make the choice
Either to claim Him as my very own
Or to possess His world
And be possessed by His world.

14931

Every morning I try devotedly
To sail the life-boat
Of inner progress-delight.

14932

My life's hope
Blossoms
Only in my heart's
Surrender-garden.

14933

If you are an advanced seeker,
Then God's one-way soliloquy
Will illumine you and fulfil you.

14934

I shall remember
That my life's stupendous
Heavenward journey
Commenced with a fragile hope.

14935

How can you make any inner progress
If you allow yourself all the time
To be assailed
By blind impatience-night?

14936

Once you have established
Your inner contact
With your Inner Pilot,
Your life's transformation-victory
Will not remain a far cry.

14937

Immortality does love
The beautiful achievements
Of fleeting time.

14938

No matter how hard you try
To annoy or ignore God,
You will fail to escape
God's all-pervading Compassion-Eye.

14939

Man has only two duties:
To offer God an orphan-cry
And to offer God a prince-smile.

14940

Alas,
We unmistakably treasure
The boons
And cleverly forget
The Boon-Giver.

14941

God is impartial,
But the human mind
Fails to understand it.
It judges God's Wisdom-Light
In its own very limited way.

14942

God tells me
That I have many times given Him
My crying heart.
Now He wants me to give Him
My smiling life.

14943

If you pay attention
To God's timely inner Warnings,
Then in your outer life
You can avoid danger-accidents.

14944

It seems that most human beings
Secretly enjoy
An overdose of doubt.
