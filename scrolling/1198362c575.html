12690

Now that your mind is ready
To love God the creation,
God the Creator will proudly help you
Reach His Delight-Heights.

12691

May there be no
Misunderstanding-bomb-explosion
Between my truth-searching mind
And my God-loving heart.

12692

When I pray,
I pray to touch
The Feet of God.
When I meditate,
I meditate to become
The Heart of God.

12693

No, not even God the Compassion-Heart
Can liberate you
If you allow your mind
To enjoy thickening doubt-clouds.

12694

All the parades of the world
God has carefully watched.
Now He wants to watch proudly
Only one parade:
The parade of the peace-flooded
Oneness-world-family.

12695

My Lord Supreme,
I have a very special Christmas gift
For You.
My inspiration-mind,
My aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
I am cheerfully and soulfully placing
At Your Protection-Perfection-Feet.

12696

If I soulfully long
For an illumination-mind,
Then only will God grant me
His Protection-Umbrella
And His Satisfaction-Sun.

12697

Every day my heart's aspiration
Helps me embark upon
My
God-discovery-satisfaction-expedition.

12698

Your life has become
A daily ordeal
Because you please
Your pride-inundated mind
And not
Your humility-blossomed oneness-heart.

12699

What you desperately need
Is first
Your long-lost aspiration-rediscovery
And then
Your life's perfection-mastery.

12700

Into the world I came
To sit at God's Compassion-Feet
And serve Him sleeplessly.
This message from above
Is crystal clear to me.
