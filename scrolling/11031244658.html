23 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
The world cannot see You,
But You can see the world.
May this Blessing
Be enough for the world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 October 1998, 5:23 a.m. Before lifting 130 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

23 October 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
We do not realise
That in order to be perfect
In our outer life,
We must be perfect
In our inner heart first.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 October 1998, 5:29 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times — (50 right, 50 left) — for a new record of 2 mins 40 secs.

24 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Voices I hear, inner and outer,
That lead me nowhere.
Yours is the only Voice,
My Lord Supreme,
That is carrying me
To Your Golden Shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 October 1998. 7:10 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

24 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Please tell me when I am going to learn
That I am nothing,
I can do nothing.
Everything is done in and through me
By You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 October 1998, 7:19 a.m. Before lifting 130 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

24 October 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Although the world does not love me,
The world does not need me,
My Supreme,
I want to love the world,
I want to need the world.
If I can love the world
And if I can need the world,
Then I shall be able to blossom
In my heart-garden
With the purity of a lotus
And the beauty of a rose.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 October 1998, 7:28 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times. (50 right, 50 left)

24 October 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
No dream of mine will ever be lost,
For I know that Your choice Hour
Will fulfil all my soulful dreams.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 October 1998, 11:37 p.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

24 October 1998 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O pleasures and pains,
You are not of me.
Therefore, how do you dare
To claim me as your own,
Very own?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 October 1998, 11:45 p.m. Before lifting 130 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

27 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My prayer waits for Your Arrival.
My meditation opens my life-door
For You.
My contemplation begs You to occupy
My heart-seat.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 October 1998, 7:26 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

28 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
No more ingratitude in my thoughts.
No more ingratitude in my words.
No more ingratitude in my actions.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 October 1998, 2:11 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

30 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I am fulfilling three supreme Commands
Of Yours:
I am loving my prayer-life.
I am loving my temple-heart.
I am loving my shrine-breath.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 October 1998, 6:24 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

1 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Marathon I run, marathon I run,
Marathon —
To be the smile of Your Infinity's Dawn.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1998, 6:20 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times. (The morning of the New York City Marathon.)

2 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May Your Light dispel my mind's
Darkness number one: ignorance.
May Your Oneness dispel my life's
Weakness number one: ingratitude.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 November 1998, 6:19 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm five times.

3 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Where is my ultimate victory?
My victory is inside
My heart's God-gratitude-tears;
My victory is inside
My life's God-surrender-smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 November 1998, 5:02 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

4 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Every day I pray to You
Only for one thing:
May today be the last day
Of my desire-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 November 1998, 4:02 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

6 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Do break me into millions of pieces
Before, long before,
My ego plans to break me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 November 1998, 4:23 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.
