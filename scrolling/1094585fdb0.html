54 A Devotion-Heart ...

A devotion-heart
That has intensity
Is of paramount importance.

55 A Doubting Mind ...

A doubting mind
Can influence other minds,
But never an aspiring heart.

56 Every Morning I Receive ...

Every morning I receive
My Lord’s Blessing-Rain
With my gratitude-heart-tears.

9 January 2002 ...

9 January 2002

Ho Chi Minh City, Viet Nam

57 My Heart-Breath Descends ...

My heart-breath descends
From my Lord’s
Transcendental Delight.

58 I Must Multiply ...

I must multiply
My heart’s sweetness
And my life’s oneness.

59 When The Hour Of God Strikes ...

When the Hour of God strikes,
Do not delay!
Just run your fastest
To your final Destination.

60 Every Morning ...

Every morning
Prayerfully, soulfully and blissfully
I fly on the wings
Of my God-love.

61 Do Not Mind ...

Do not mind
If you fall.
Only allow God
To lift you up again.
He is all ready
To do you this big favour.

62 In The Marketplace Of Life ...

In the marketplace of life,
Nothing is essential
And nothing is permanent.

63 Sweet Is God’s ...

Sweet is God’s
Compassion-Eye.

64 Sweeter Are God’s ...

Sweeter are God’s
Affection-Arms.

65 Sweetest Is God’s ...

Sweetest is God’s
Fondness-Heart.

66 The Mind Gives Millions Of Reasons ...

The mind gives millions of reasons
Why it does not want to accept God
As its Creator.

67 The Heart Says ...

The heart says,
“I cannot live without God
Even for a fleeting moment.
No reason-snare
Can ever catch me.”
