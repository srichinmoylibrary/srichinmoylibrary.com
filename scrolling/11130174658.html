4.

An ant like me to save
You came into this world of dust and clay.
A beggar like me to love
And give me a role sublime in
Your Cosmic Play.

5.

Only once I loved God,
His Love and Height are won.
He now is my perfect slave;
I am His Fulfilment-son.

6.

None came to me, none came and none
shall come.
Only my Supreme Beloved came, only He.
I cried for His Smile, and He, for my
selfless love.
Sleepless in me He grows His Vision-Tree.

7.

O high I flew to see God's Home
And far I ran to see God's Eye
And deep I dived to feel God's Love.
Behold the wings of my victory-sky!

8.

Today, today, today
Alone with God I stay;
Tomorrow and forever
With His Compassion-Ray.

9.

There was a time when I stumbled
and stumbled,
But now I only climb and climb beyond
And far beyond my Goal's endless Beyond.
And yet my Captain commands:
"Go on, go on!"

10.

For just a little smile from You,
You I gave my body, heart and soul!
For just a little love from You,
You I made all my lives' only Goal!

11.

I sighed and wailed with frustration-night,
And then I sailed with Salvation-Light,
And now I sing with Perfection-Height:
Delight, delight, a flood of delight.

12.

Long dead the night of my mind,
Long awake the dawn of my heart,
Long revealed the sun of my soul.
God and man! Am I not smart?

13.

O Supreme, my Father-Son,
Now that we two are one,
And won by each other, won,
Nothing remains undone.
