11001

My Lord,
Do make my life a heart
Of ceaseless, selfless prayer.

My Lord,
Do make my life the victory
Of Your Vision-Manifestation on earth.

11002

For speedy God-realisation,
The seeker needs the sleepless throbbings
Of a gratitude-heart.

11003

Every day God visits me
And invites me to ride
His Illumination-Train
To His Perfection-Kingdom.

11004

If you want to expedite
Your God-appointment-hour,
Then wear the armour
Of world-detachment.

11005

The mind feasts on
Uncertainty-nothingness.
The heart feasts on
God's Satisfaction-Manifestation-Delight.

11006

Silence, you are
My unparalleled friend.
You are
My God-blossoming heart-garden.

11007

To your Beloved Supreme
Offer a monument
Of your heart's gratitude.
Lo, transcendence-bliss
Is beckoning you!

11008

Bid farewell to frustration-clouds.
God will be truly happy.
Declare war on your unwillingness.
God will immediately embrace you.

11009

Each gratitude-teardrop
Of my heart
Will eventually be transformed
Into my soul-beauty's
Satisfaction-smile.

11010

Am I a fool,
That I continually force myself
To carry the mountain-heavy weight
Of expectation?

11011

In man's body-family,
His mind and its well-fed doubts
Are his strongest foes.

11012

What I need every day
To please my Lord Supreme
Is a whisper-sweet prayer-cry.

11013

How can you see
God's smiling Face
If your enthusiasm-mind
Is poverty-stricken?

11014

My obedience-mind,
My surrender-heart,
You two are my dedication-life's
Quick-spreading fulfilment-joy.
