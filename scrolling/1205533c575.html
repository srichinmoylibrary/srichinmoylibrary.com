19790

The mind's peace-lamp
Can easily give light
If the aspiration-flame
Inside the heart
Is ready to inspire the mind.

19791

Yesterday my mind indulged
In pomp and ceremony.
Today my mind needs only
Simplicity's beauty and purity's divinity.

19792

God does not agree with me
When I tell Him that
The distance to my life's
Perfection-destination
Is long, very long.

19793

The cosmic gods
Do not charge us
When we register
At God's Compassion-Hotel.

19794

I do not want to see
My heart-friend, faith,
Ever in trouble!

19795

Each purity-breath
Is the fairest bud
In my aspiration-heart-garden.

19796

A mind of sincerity
And a heart of purity
Together
Always win the God-satisfaction-game.

19797

O my mind,
Consciously pray for patience-light.
Otherwise, impatience-dog
At any moment
Can attack you.

19798

I need a heart
That will always be watching
My mind-door
So that no doubt-thief
Can enter.

19799

I shall be able to drink
God's Nectar-Delight
Only when my life is charged
With God-belief.

19800

If you sleeplessly feed
Your heart's sacred aspiration-flame
With your meditation-peace,
Then your entire life will be
The Beauty of God's Eye
And the Lustre of God's Face.
