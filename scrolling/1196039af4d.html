10375

A desire-life
Is a disobedience-life.
A disobedience-life
Is the beginning of death.

10376

Good news lies ahead!
You will be able to run
Far beyond your imagination.
You will become another God
Even if you do not want to.

10377

One great mistake
Is enough to make you accept
The life of total failure
And the breath of a permanent sigh.

10378

You are stumbling
On turmoil-soil
Because every day
You go out shopping
In the desire-market.

10379

Yours is a life-structure
Built on gratitude-foundation.
Therefore
God will teach you
His ecstasy-flooded
Universal Oneness-Music.

10380

Your mind has descended
Into the deepest abyss.
As a real compensation
Can you not help your heart ascend
To the highest peak?

10381

There will be a few major changes
In tomorrow's schools.
They will stop teaching
War-subjects.

10382

If you desire the presence of a miracle
For the transformation of your life,
Then throw yourself into
Your life's dedication-days.

10383

Be meditation-minded
If you want God
To change your life
Or if you want to change your life
Yourself.

10384

You are longing for
A oneness-friend.
No one can be a better friend
Than your own heart's
Sincerity-song.

10385

If you wander
In forgetfulness-forest,
You will become a victim
To your own bondage-life.

10386

Doubt has to diminish
Eventually.
Faith has to increase
Immediately
Plus sleeplessly.

10387

Deliberately
You are holding the hand
Of darkness-night.
Therefore, there is a huge blight
On your aspiration-tree.

10388

Abandon your old excuses.
Your failure-life will become
A forgotten story.

10389

Give up giving up!
Study regularly and devotedly
The divine course that is offered
By your heart's soul-light.
