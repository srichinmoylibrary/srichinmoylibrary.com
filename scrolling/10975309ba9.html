41 God The Helper ...

God the Helper
And God the Supporter
We need
When we want to achieve
The impossible.

42 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Give me tears in my heart.
Give me cheers in my life.
Give me divine freedom in my thought
To make me a perfect instrument
Of Yours.

43 I Shall Never, Never Go ...

I shall never, never go
To the expectation-world.
Time and again
It has failed me.

44 Exorbitant Pride ...

Exorbitant pride
Is
The God-renouncer.

45 The Heart Can Protect ...

The heart can protect
The outer life,
But the outer life cannot protect
The inner life.

46 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I love Your Thunder-Kick-Anger
To make the fastest progress
In my inner life.

47 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I love Your Strictness
The way I love Your Kindness.

14 January 2006 ...

14 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

48 Everything Divine ...

Everything divine
Begins in the heart
And then spreads.

49 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Anything that I have in my lifeó
Divine or undivineó
Rest assured, it is all Yours.

50 My Lord Tells Me That ...

My Lord tells me that,
To love Him more,
I shall have to shed soulful tears
In abundant measure every day.

51 My Mind No Longer ...

My mind no longer
Dares to block
My fastest progress.

52 Every God-Created Heart ...

Every God-created heart
Consciously or unconsciously sings
Godís Victory-Songs.

53 To My Greatest Surprise And Joy ...

To my greatest surprise and joy,
My stone heart
God melted long ago.

54 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Give me not greatness,
Not goodness,
But oneness with Your Will
In everything that I say and do.
