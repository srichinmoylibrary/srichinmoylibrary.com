8401

Once upon a time
Your life was claimed
By God Himself
Because you felt
That there was nobody to claim
But God.

8402

God treasures
More than anything else
The sweetness
Of a satisfied soul.

8403

If you are a good man,
Then every day you must offer
Gratitude-gifts
To your supremely compassionate soul.

8404

Current news from Heaven:
God is going to give another chance
To humanity
For its perfect perfection.

8405

He was surprised to see
That there are so many professors
Available
At the university of self-deception.

8406

My heart's willingness
Constantly widens
God's Satisfaction-Road.

8407

He is prepared to fight
For the illumination
Of ignorance-night
Although he is attacked mercilessly
By the heavy clouds
Of ignorance itself.

8408

From aspiration-river
We enter into perfection-sea.
From perfection-sea
We enter into God's
Satisfaction-Ocean.

8409

Your love of God
Is extremely beautiful,
And your love of God
Is extremely powerful.
Do not allow it to mix
With the expectations
Of your stupid mind.

8410

You are telling me
That you are looking for
An inner shelter.
You fool!
Can you not see
That your soul is always ready
To grant you its sanctuary-shelter?

8411

When I look at my mind,
I unfortunately see
That darkness has defeated me.

When I look into my heart,
I clearly see
That darkness will before long perish.

8412

My mind tells me
That there is nothing as valuable
As success-march.
My heart tells me
That there is nothing as beautiful,
Meaningful and fruitful
As progress-walk.

8413

Not to speak of human beings,
Even God is spellbound
By your heart's aspiration-songs.

8414

His doubts are falling
From his mind-tree.
His fears are falling
From his heart-tree.
His imperfections are falling
From his life-tree.
