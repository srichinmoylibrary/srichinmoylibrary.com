190.

No negativity of my mind
Will ever be able
To drown my heart
In ignorance-sea.

191.

My gratitude is always ready
To light the lamp
Of my God-realisation-hope.

192.

As God walks along my life-shore,
I become at once
My heart's beauty
And my soul's fragrance.

193.

Do not seek to embrace
The outer world
Unless you are graced
By the inner world.

194.

God tastes my gratitude-heart
And my surrender-life
And finds them extremely good.

195.

God's Heart has been aching
For such a long time.
Alas,
How is it that my mind
Is still fast asleep?

196.

To ease a little of God's excruciating pangs,
I have decided to increase
My aspiration-hours and dedication-hours.

197.

What is self-offering?
It is something that
The more you give,
The sweeter you become.

198.

Man's hope-birds are crying.
God's Promise-Birds
Are not only consoling
But also illumining.

199.

Like God,
God's Forgiveness
Also never goes to sleep.

200.

Every evening
God garlands my gratitude-heart
With boundless Delight.
