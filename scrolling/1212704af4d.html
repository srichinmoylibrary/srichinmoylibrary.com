26875

God the Love
Is the satisfaction-giver
To my life.

26876

Asceticism cannot be
The fully perfect way
For God-realisation.

26877

My Lord,
May my gratitude-heart
Be a sea of tears.

26878

The parents of the mind
Are
Stupidity and audacity.

26879

Unlike the mind,
The heart is always eager
To be the partner of the mind.

26880

My heart is fond of
Unity.
My mind is proud of
Diversity.

26881

My Lord wants me to be with Him
Only when I am sincerely ready
To spend quality time.

26882

My mind,
Your aspiration-dedication-house
Needs transformation
Or at least a major repair.

26883

My Lord's Compassion-Eye
Quietly and proudly
Beckons my aspiration-heart.

26884

A frustration-mind
And an aggression-vital
Highly speak of each other.

26885

My heart's compassion-tears
Meet with
My Lord's proudest
Satisfaction-Smiles.

26886

My aspiration-vacation
My Lord Supreme
Never approves.

26887

Nothing can be better
Than a self-giving oneness —
Nothing!

26888

My Lord,
Do take away
All my time,
For You, only for You.

26889

God is all ready
With His open Arms,
But alas,
Where is my surrender-life?
