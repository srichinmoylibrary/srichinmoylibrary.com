O Crow, O Kak

June 12th, 1995

O crow, O kak, O crow, O kak, O crow, O kak
Tor chetanar rupantarer asiyachhe dak

June 12th, 1995

Bhaye bhaye bhaye kena sada tumi kampa tharathar

Concentration

July 23rd, 1995

Concentration has and is
The deer-speed.

Meditation Has and Is

July 23rd, 1995

Meditation has and is
The elephant-confidence.

Contemplation

July 23rd, 1995

Contemplation
Has and is
The lion-victory.

Friendship-Birds Can Fly

July 23rd, 1995

Friendship-birds can fly
Only on the wings of sincerity.

I Am a Pig

August 15th, 1999

I am a pig, I am a pig!
My stomach is big, very big.
I eat all kinds of junk food.
No good, no good, no good, no good!
