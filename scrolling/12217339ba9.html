Jukti Jethai Bhakti Sethai

January 9th, 1998

Jukti jethai bhakti sethai
Pabona je kabhu
Ei kathati chupi chupi
Bale moder prabhu

Translation:

In secrecy supreme,
My Lord tells me
That wherever there is
An argumentative mind
You cannot find devotion.
