13 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

27 When We Seek ...

When we seek
Soulfully and self-givingly,
A new frontier welcomes us,
Garlands us and embraces us.

28 Those Who Do Not Pray ...

Those who do not pray
And meditate
Will undoubtedly have a future
With no destination.

29 Self-Doubt ...

Self-doubt
Is a very powerful member
Of the mind-society.

30 Non-Believers ...

Non-believers
And disbelievers
Are very rich in obscurity.

31 Self-Indulgence ...

Self-indulgence
Is a fatal flaw.

32 My Heart-Room ...

My heart-room
Is the place from where
I begin everything.

33 I Need The Gods ...

I need the gods
Of the present
And not the gods
Of the long-buried past
To guide and lead
My present life.

34 Every Day ...

Every day
Without fail
I go to God’s
Forgiveness-Feet.

14 January 2007 ...

14 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

35 Every Time My Aspiration ...

Every time my aspiration
Dies down,
God most compassionately
Revives it.

36 Not Every Day ...

Not every day
But every minute
My Lord examines
My surrender-breath.

37 The Miracle Of Miracles ...

The miracle of miracles
Is my constant
God-gratitude-heart.

38 Every Day ...

Every day
My aspiration climbs up
A more difficult mountain-height.

39 A Cheerful Obedience ...

A cheerful obedience
Is all my life needs.
