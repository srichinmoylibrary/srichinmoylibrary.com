22130

If you can offer God
Your heart's streaming tears,
God will cheerfully carry you
All the way to His Peace-Abode.

22131

Your heart's sorrows will turn into
Your soul's joys
If you can surrender each thought of yours
To fulfil God's Will.

22132

You always converse
With your division-mind.
Can you not once in a while converse
With your oneness-heart?

22133

God sends an invitation to you
Every day,
But unfortunately you are always absent
From your heart-home.

22134

Eagerness of the mind
And willingness of the heart
Can bring about the greatest miracle:
The transformation of life.

22135

Who is responsible,
If not my mind,
For compelling me to be
The slave of imperfection?

22136

My cheerful surrender
To God's Will
Is indeed the master
Of my fate.

22137

When readiness is your name,
You are bound to see
Your divine qualities
Come to the fore.

22138

Alas, even when I am totally successful
Only because of God's Compassion,
I forget to give credit
To God's Compassion-Eye.

22139

How can you participate
In God's self-giving Game
When you are completely in love
With your name and fame?

22140

The heart shows me its depth
Unconditionally.
The mind fails to show me
Its real height
Even when I plead with the mind.

22141

The summit of God's Divinity
God wants to show
In humanity's tears.

22142

To make us divinely happy,
What we need
Is the light of silence,
And not the power of sound.

22143

You are looking for
The perfect seeker.
Why not train yourself
To be that perfect seeker?
Start with yourself.

22144

How long can my mind resist,
Since God Himself is insisting
On my life-perfection?
