12145

My Lord, do help me eat
Sweetness-gratitude-heart-fruits
Every day, without fail.

12146

O my aspiration-heart,
Do not go visit my mind
In its swelling
Selfishness-compartment!

12147

O my mind,
You are the reigning monarch
Of self-doubt.
How can you ever please me?

12148

Surrender, cheerfully surrender
To God's Will.
You will not be buffeted
By the strong winds of worry.

12149

I am simply shocked
To see the constant escalation
Of your mind's blind suspicion.

12150

His final descent into his mind's
Confusion-doubt-dungeon
Is utterly deplorable,
If not despicable.

12151

May my love of God
Remain for me
Something indefinable
So that I will remain
Eternally joyful.

12152

Nobody is an exception -
At least, not I.
We all live in the fruitless
Frustration-world of desire.

12153

What a fool I am!
I made my desires out of nothing,
And now I am allowing them
To be my everything.

12154

God, You are my heart-accepted
Reality's Divinity.
God, You are my mind-rejected
Stupidity's futility.

12155

When I close my eyes,
I become the dreamer
Of golden dreams.
When I open my eyes,
I live in the purgatory
Of eyeless fears.

12156

Do not embark
On self-indulgence-journey.
You are bound to arrive
At self-destruction-destination.

12157

I am sure there is a way
To escape from the grinding jaws
Of ignorance-tyrant.

12158

O quicksand-doubt,
I do not want you anymore;
I do not need you anymore.
You are totally forgotten
Folly of yore.

12159

Your head is full
Of profit-measurement,
But your heart is full
Of love-enlightenment.
