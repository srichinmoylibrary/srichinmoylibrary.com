25475

To restore God's confidence in you,
Cry like a motherless
And fatherless child.

25476

God does not believe in
Your plethora of excuses
As to why you fail
Time and again
To please Him.

25477

What is life,
If not absolutely short-term
Name and fame?

25478

We must follow our Lord's Footsteps
Wherever He wants to lead us
For our perfection-life.

25479

The oneness-song
Pure hearts
Never stop singing.

25480

If it is real love,
Then it must ultimately surrender
Happily and cheerfully
To God's Will.

25481

Nobody has the capacity
To choose God.
It is God Himself
Who chooses us all.

25482

At every moment
A God-orbit
Is of supreme necessity.

25483

God's Compassion-Eye
And God's Forgiveness-Heart
Always remain absolutely perfect,
No matter from where you look.

25484

Every day I pray to God
To give me the capacity
To fly in
My love, devotion and surrender-skies.

25485

When I sympathise with someone,
I create the beauty and smile
Of a new God.

25486

Alas, I do not know
When I shall be able to discover
The golden pathway
Of my aspiration-heart.

25487

Each good thought
Is a living shrine
In the Heart of the Supreme.

25488

When I look at my heart-eye,
I become the energy
Of the whole cosmos.

25489

God wants to intensify
Our mind's search for Him
And amplify
Our heart's inseparable oneness
With Him.
