23545

If you do not bury
Your dead disappointments,
Who can and who will
Bury them for you?
Bury all your disappointments
Here and now!
Then run towards the effulgence
Of your soul's light and delight!

23546

O my mind,
You house the threat
Of nuclear annihilation.
But my heart,
Which is all aspiration-illumination,
Will, without fail,
Conquer you.

23547

The wonders of the outer world:
A sincerity-mind
And a sincerity-life.
The wonders of the inner world:
A purity-heart
And a purity-life.

23548

My prayerful mind,
My soulful heart
And my self-giving life
Are the three most significant boons
That I have received
From my Lord Beloved Supreme.

23549

If you want to manifest God's Will,
Then you must brave
The world's wild criticism-barrages.

23550

Each breath can be pure
If it is placed at the Feet
Of the Absolute Lord Supreme.

23551

All sincere, self-giving
Aspiration-attempts
Are bound to succeed
Either in the near future
Or in the distant future.

23552

A man of conscious, constant
And unconditional surrender
To God's Will
Is indeed a living miracle.

23553

To please God
You must renew and fulfil
Your old promises.
Only then will you be entitled
To make higher promises.

23554

Whenever I become a victim
To insecurity,
I give my Lord Supreme
A migraine headache.

23555

Man's mind is the story
Of separation-destruction.
Man's heart is the song
Of oneness-illumination.

23556

What I need
Is an ancient heart of purity
And a modern life of activity.

23557

God considers it a great joke
When my mind challenges my heart
To please God
More than my mind can please Him.

23558

God Himself is shocked to see
That His world-saving Patience
Is running short.

23559

My Lord,
All my heart's tears
I am ready to barter
For Your one fleeting Smile.
