24945

What my soul expects from me
Is nothing unusual.
It is something absolutely normal
And natural:
My selfless love for God.

24946

Self-admiration-disease
I have.
God-adoration-medicine
I need.

24947

If we settle for less than the highest,
The human in us may be satisfied,
But never the divine in us.

24948

My life-boat plies between
God the Compassion-Eye-Shore
And God the Forgiveness-Heart-Shore.

24949

The modern world needs
The simplicity of the ancient mind
And the purity of the ancient heart.

24950

To be happy and perfect,
I must look at myself lovingly,
And I must not only look at
But also enter into
My Lord Supreme self-givingly.

24951

To be sleeplessly self-giving
Is to reach the summit
Of all virtues.

24952

The human mind
Is nothing short of
A God-unwillingness-rock.

24953

If I am in my mind,
I am forced to accept
The cross of life.
If I am in my heart,
I am invited to be part of
The sweetness of life.

24954

There is nothing as sweet,
Beautiful and fruitful
As losing ourselves
In the very Compassion-Heart of God.

24955

No more shall I enjoy
The raging torrents
Of depression and frustration.
From now on, I shall enjoy
The loftiest victory
Of my inner faith.

24956

Each human mind,
Either knowingly or unknowingly,
Is a humour-rumour-columnist.

24957

What you need
Is a converted mind.
What you need
Is an unconverted heart.

24958

Every day
God comes down to blessingfully offer
His Pride-Reward
Only to those who rightly deserve it.

24959

No matter how long it takes,
My aspiring heart
Is ready to stay in darkness
While it is waiting for God.
