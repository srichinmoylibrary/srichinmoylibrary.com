60.

God wants me to live
In the temple
Of aspiration, love
Devotion and surrender,
And not in the house
Of world-challenging
Competitions.

61.

You can hear God's Voice
Only in
Your silence-heart-temple.

62.

When I enter into
My heart's silence-temple,
Even before I call,
God replies.

63.

If I have anything
To be proud of in my life,
Then it is the purity
Of my heart-temple.

64.

I am so happy and proud to know
That my heart-temple is blessed
By God's Peace
And God's Delight.

65.

God wants me to strike
His largest Victory-Gong
Inside my
Aspiration-heart-temple.

66.

From time immemorial
Man has been building temples
To worship God.
But God made only one temple,
And that temple
Will forever and forever stand.
What is that temple?
Man's aspiration-heart.

67.

:PROPERTIES: :GENRE: poem :CITE-KEY: MHT:67

68.

Never miss
Your morning prayer
And meditation
If you want to adorn
Your aspiration-temple-heart.

69.

Your life shall blossom
Like roses
In your heart-garden,
If you kindle the flames
In your aspiration-heart-temple.

70.

If you can keep illumining
The temple within,
God will blessingfully
And proudly
Broadcast your name.

71.

Do not carry
Your mind-bundle with you
When you enter into
Your heart-temple
To meditate and illumine
Yourself.

72.

You will be allowed to enter
Into the heart-temple
Only when you have
Radically changed your mind.

73.

Keep the body-temple
In a soulful frame
Of consciousness
To lead a goodness
And fullness-life.

74.

Where is the shrine
Of fulfilment?
Inside the temple
Of self-effacement.

75.

May my aspiration-heart-bell
Ring every morning
And every evening
Like a temple bell.
