24 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must liberate my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
From my mind's uncertainty-prison.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 September 1999, 7:30 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 600 lbs.)

24 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
With everything that I have
And everything that I am,
May I become
A God-fulfilment-eagerness-dynamo.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 September 1999, 7:47 a.m. Before lifting 275 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 550 lbs.)

24 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May I be blessed every morning
With a newness-hope-heart
And a fulness-promise-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 September 1999, 7:57 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm seventy-two times. (36 right, 36 left)

24 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I embark
On my heart's pilgrimage
To Your celestial Palace,
I must always carry with me
My heart's devotion-shrine.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 September 1999, 8:12 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

26 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
If I can have God-closeness,
God-faithfulness and God-devotedness,
Then I have everything that I need.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 September 1999, 6:27 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

26 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today and every day
I shall pray to You
To see Your smiling Face
Inside each and every
Aspiring heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 September 1999, 6:33 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

26 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your earthly Tears are inside
The broken-hearted.
Your Heavenly Smiles are for
The broken-hearted.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 September 1999, 6:45 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

26 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
This morning I looked far beyond
My mind's clouds
And immediately found
My heart's sunshine.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 September 1999, 6:56 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 500 lbs.)

26 September 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
As the flowers bloom
To receive Your Smile,
Even so, my heart blooms
To love You
And my life blossoms
To serve You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 September 1999, 7:07 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm seventy-one times. (36 right, 35 left)

26 September 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
On my behalf, my Lord Supreme,
You have chosen
Aspiration-cries for my heart
And dedication-smiles for my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 September 1999, 7:40 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 700 lbs.

27 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In my outer life
You live in the beauty
Of my heart's streaming tears,
And nowhere else.
In my inner life
You live in the fragrance
Of my soul's beaming smiles,
And nowhere else.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999, 8:28 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

27 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must increase my faith in You
Immensely.
Otherwise, I shall continue
To stumble and stumble
In my inner running.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999, 8:34 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

27 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You have taken away
Many uninspiring, unaspiring things
From my life.
Now please take away one more thing:
Frustration, frustration,
Frustration.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999, 8:43 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

27 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My darkness-mind,
Get out, get out!
My heaviness-heart,
Get out, get out!
My soulfulness-life,
Let us live together
Forever.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999, 8:56 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 500 lbs.)

27 September 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please inspire my mind,
Please protect my heart,
Please lead my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999, 9:15 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm.
