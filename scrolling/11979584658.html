12230

Aspiration-perfection achieved,
Lo, the bondage of self-will
Long deceased!

12231

Your sleepless unwillingness
Is bound to suffer from the fever
Of ruthless ego-frustration.

12232

O faith-deserter,
You have next to nothing,
Yet you expect everything,
Including God's supreme Pride
In you.

12233

Alas,
Your unused heart-capacities
Are decaying
Faster than your fastest
Imagination-flight!

12234

My aspiration-heart was born
To be daily fed
By God's Compassion-Height
And not to be ruthlessly tormented
By negative thoughts.

12235

Fight off your ego-fever.
Otherwise, you will suffer
From a dislocated mind.

12236

I loved my soul's
Ancient illumination-journey.
I love my life's
Modern perfection-goal.

12237

My mind,
I have accepted you
As you are.
How stupid you are
Not to accept yourself!

12238

I can never become
A God-pleaser
Without being blessed
By God's unconditional
Compassion-Sea.

12239

Yesterday I was
A God-chooser.
Today I am
A God-prayer.
Tomorrow I shall become
A God-surrenderer.

12240

Yesterday my heart
Was a God-invoker.
Today my heart
Is a God-praiser.
Tomorrow my heart
Will be a God-supplier.

12241

Yesterday my mind
Was a God-adorer.
Today my heart
Is a God-follower.
Tomorrow my life
Will be a God-lover.

12242

Yesterday I was
A God-invoker.
Today I am
A God-affirmer.
Tomorrow I shall be
A God-satisfier.

12243

Yesterday I was
A God-meditator.
Today I am
A God-embracer.
Tomorrow I shall become
A God-publiciser.

12244

Yesterday my soul
Was a God-dreamer.
Today my heart
Is a God-beginner.
Tomorrow my life
Will be a God-producer.
