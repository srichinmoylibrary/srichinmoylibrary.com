16690

Yesterday my mind was standing
And today my vital is standing
In line
For stupidity-throne.

16691

Your mind suspects
God-existence.
Your heart rejects
Your mind-stupidity.

16692

Every morning God comes to me
With His Perfection-Rainbow-Smiles
To feed my heart's
Aspiration-hunger-flames.

16693

There is no problem
That cannot be solved
By perseverance and persistence-light.

16694

Each human being is expected to be
A soulful ambassador of goodwill
To improve the standard of mankind.

16695

At times a little nostalgia
Is helpful
To intensify the heart's
Aspiration-cries.

16696

Good fortune
Will definitely smile on you
If you stand divinely firm
Behind your own supreme convictions.

16697

Start your self-discovery-journey
Toward your life's integral
And universal freedom.

16698

Challenges disappear
When you unconditionally depend
On God's Compassion-flooded Eye.

16699

Peace is not a compromise.
Peace is
A oneness-fulness-satisfaction-embrace.

16700

God does not want to hear from you
All your ignorance-experiences.
God wants to hear from you
How you are planning to realise Him.
