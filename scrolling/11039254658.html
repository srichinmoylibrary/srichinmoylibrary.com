8 November 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
At every moment each seeker has to be
Extremely alert.
At any moment his spiritual life
Can descend into steep decline.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 November 1999, Before doing one-hundred and five one-arm pushes with 400 lbs. (53 right, 52 left)

8 November 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In my spiritual life,
I shall have to be
A dauntless runner
With determined and measured strides
To arrive at my Destination.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 November 1999, 8:37 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

14 November 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not want me to be
A God-preacher.
He wants me to be
A God-dreamer.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 November 1999, 8:36 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

14 November 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not want me to be
A God-scholar.
He wants me to be
A God-lover.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 November 1999, 8:45 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

14 November 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not want me to be
A God-author.
He wants me to be
A God-learner.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 November 1999, 8:52 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with each arm simultaneously (Total: 500 lbs.)

14 November 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not want me to be
A God-drummer. He wants me to be
A God-flutist.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 November 1999, 8:55 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

14 November 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not want me to be
A world-renouncer.
He wants me to be
A God-server.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/14 November 1999, 9:03 a.m. Before doing sixty-four one-arm pushes with 400 lbs. (32 right, 32 left)

14 November 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not want me to be
A world-mind-computer.
He wants me to be
A world-heart-commuter.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 November 1999, 9:27 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 800 lbs.

15 November 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O ignorance-night-sleep,
Not a temporary
But a permanent break
I am taking from you.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 November 1999, 7:57 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

15 November 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart's unconditional surrender
To God's Will
Is the full blossom of my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 November 1999, 8:06 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

15 November 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
That I have chatted enough
With You.
Now I must start chanting
Your Name.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 November 1999, 8:11 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

15 November 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Long before I sought,
My Beloved's Lotus-Feet
Had caught my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 November 1999, 8:14 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

15 November 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are asking my mind's readiness,
My life's willingness
And my heart's eagerness
To hurry up,
And You are also asking
My doubting and suspecting mind
To slow down and stop forever.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 November 1999, 8:21 a.m. Before doing fifty one-arm pushes with 400 lbs. (25 right, 25 left)

26 November 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The life-expectancy
Of a God-lover
Is unlimited.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 November 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My Beloved Lord Supreme,
You are spoiling me
With Your Indulgence-Abundance.
"My child,
I cannot help it."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
