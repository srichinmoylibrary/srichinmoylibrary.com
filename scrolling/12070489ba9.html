21245

O my heart's forgotten hope-river,
I do not know how
I can once more swim in you
To arrive at
My God-satisfaction-destination.

21246

Only an aspiration-heart
Can and does sing
God's Universal Heart-Songs.

21247

You do not have to die
To be indifferent to world-criticism.
You just have to climb up
High, higher, highest,
Along with your heart's
Heaven-climbing aspiration-flames.

21248

Since there is nothing useless,
Nothing meaningless
In God's creation, O seeker,
How can you allow yourself to ignore
Any God-manifested vision?

21249

Be not free with
Your God-fulfilment-promises.
Be free with
Your man-service-life.

21250

Each time
I surrender myself cheerfully
To God's Will,
I see right in front of me
A free and fair highway
Leading to the Home
Of my Beloved Supreme.

21251

Unconditional surrender
Is the greatest contribution
Made by a sleepless aspiration-heart.

21252

Attach yourself
To the blossoming beauty within,
And detach yourself
From the brooding desire-mind.

21253

Unlike the human mind,
The divine heart knows
That we can never overestimate
God's Compassion and Forgiveness.

21254

O seeker,
Smile and smile
To lessen the strength
Of your inner struggle.

21255

God's unhorizoned Grace
Enters into our hearts
Not to surprise us,
But to awaken us.

21256

The inner voice
Always warns us
Long before
It reprimands us.

21257

My Lord Supreme,
May I be blessed every morning
By the magnet
Of Your Compassion-flooded Feet.

21258

Alas,
I do not know why and how
My mind ruthlessly attacks
Everything that my heart does
Or even decides to do.

21259

Close your mind-book
Completely,
And then open your heart-book
Slowly, lovingly and devotedly.
