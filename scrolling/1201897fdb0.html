16160

Intensify your mind's
Concentration-power
If you are longing for God's
Compassion-Shower.

16161

My heart is always eager
To treasure
The divinity-fragrance
Of my soul.

16162

You have lost your enthusiasm.
Indeed, it is a sad experience.
But do not allow it to continue.
Restore your enthusiasm
As soon as possible!

16163

To go beyond
Earth-bound time,
We need our soul's
Adamantine determination-fire.

16164

If it is an aspiration-heart,
Then it will definitely
Take flight
On God's Compassion-Airline.

16165

The mind-boat
Of an unaspiring human being
Is bound to rock
With stark doubts.

16166

Only an unconditional surrender-heart
Can become a sleepless
God-satisfaction-instrument.

16167

No genuine seeker
Can ever forget
The Satisfaction-Smile of God
Inside his heart-garden.

16168

There is only one spirituality-road,
And that road
Is the road of the heart's
Intensity-cry
For God's Immensity-Smile.

16169

My heart's confidence-light
Does not want to conquer
My mind's night,
But wants to illumine it
And transform it into courage-day.

16170

His inner life
And outer life
Are thriving
On his sleepless heart-dreams.

16171

In vain
The mind tries and tries
To destroy the beauty,
Purity and divinity
Of the heart of sweetness.

16172

Do not blame God!
He is all eagerness
To help you climb to the top
Of your aspiration-summit.

16173

With the purity
Of their gratitude-hearts,
The God-lovers can and will
Save the imperfection-flooded world.

16174

I call it
My self-giving.
God calls it
My recognition of
Oneness-living.
