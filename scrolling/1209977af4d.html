24175

We must transcend
Our inferiority feelings
At every moment
To be one with
The ever-transcending Reality.

24176

The ignorance-mind plays
Win-and-lose games.
The wisdom-heart plays
Only oneness-game.

24177

Right from the very beginning
Conquer your small worries and anxieties.
Otherwise, they can grow into
Very serious problems.

24178

O my Lord Supreme,
Do grant me one single boon.
Do liberate me
From my human one-track mind
That always runs towards
Ignorance-destruction.

24179

If we act from the very depths
Of our inspiration-heart
Sleeplessly,
Then God is bound to extol our virtues
To the skies.

24180

Wherever there is a oneness-heart,
God the Singer of Joy-Songs
Is available there.

24181

I thrive on my life's
Unending
God-surrender-victories.

24182

What is ignorance?
Ignorance is something
That always tempts us
To run backwards.

24183

God Himself shows our hearts
How to walk along
His Compassion-flooded Path.

24184

Wherever God goes,
He always allows
His Peace and Delight
To accompany Him.

24185

Of all the inner world's hooligans,
Disobedience
Is the worst.

24186

God has invited my soul
To give a lecture on obedience,
And He has compelled
My heart, mind, vital and body
To attend.

24187

When your life is clothed
In obedience and gratitude,
God-realisation will not remain
A far cry.

24188

Only our inner gratitude
Has the capacity
To multiply and magnify
The beauty of our aspiration-hearts.

24189

Because my heart cries
Prayerfully and soulfully,
God always has a soft spot
For my heart.
