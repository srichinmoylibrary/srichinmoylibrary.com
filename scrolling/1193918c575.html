8290

Earth's silent suffering
Is totally helpless
But not useless.

8291

Come out of your mind.
Let your heart love you
And claim you.
You will then feel
What true happiness is.

8292

Allow not your active vital
To fly with the winds
And dance with the waves
Of emotion.

8293

Today's helpless child
Is destined
For tomorrow's stupendous
God-responsibility.

8294

Your consciousness-sleep
Has to be awakened
If you want to be
A choice instrument of God.

8295

Remember,
Every divine thought
Is precious!

Remember,
Every day the Lord Supreme
Is gracious!

8296

A longing heart
Looks upward
To see God's Compassion-Feet.

8297

I am not a giant,
But my mind's stumblings
Are gigantic.

8298

Man's secret mind-thoughts
May not have even an iota
Of sacred breath.

8299

See for yourself
Who is the stronger of the two:
Your doubting mind
Or
Your aspiring heart.

8300

You follow
Your heart's love.
Your life's happiness
Will follow you.
