23160

God will definitely take care of you
And make you a happiness-king
If you just love your heart
Infinitely more than you do now.

23161

If you pray to God sincerely,
Is there anything
He cannot and will not
Do for you?

23162

You are bound to feel God's Love
Infinitely more
If you love Him, not in theory,
But in practical reality's intensity.

23163

My Lord,
This is my very special prayer to You:
Do give me the capacity every day
To offer You a new gratitude-heart.

23164

O seeker,
Why do you give God
Your worst possible spiritual defeat
When you have the capacity to give Him
Your supremely glorious victory?

23165

Today imagine
That God loves you
And God needs you.
Tomorrow you will see
That your imagination was nothing more
Than a reality unblossomed.

23166

Do not worry about the future.
The present moment is heavy enough
For you to carry.
How do you know that tomorrow
A bright and illumining dawn
Will not invite you?

23167

God wants you constantly to cherish
Your own aspiration
Exactly the way He enjoys
His own Satisfaction.

23168

You are bound to make progress
And make yourself really happy
If you take spirituality
As your real and only life.

23169

Only when we dive deep within
And meditate self-givingly
Does the cosmic sound reverberate
In our heart's aspiration-sky.

23170

God gives you the beauty
Of aspiration
To love Him.
God gives you the fragrance
Of dedication
To manifest Him.

23171

My God-surrender-blossoms
God Himself places
On His own Satisfaction-Heart-Shrine.

23172

Every day we must bring to the fore
The roaring lion in us
And manifest the divine Light,
To transform the face and fate
Of mankind.

23173

God always watches
Our heart-hunger
And, according to our heart-hunger,
His Compassion-Eye feeds us.

23174

God begs us for two things:
To give Him the opportunity
And the certainty
Of remaining permanently
Inside our life-breath.
