17290

The mind's simplicity
And the heart's generosity
Often go together.

17291

At times I may forget
My own name
But I never forget my Lord's
Express Heart-Phone Number.

17292

May my heart radiate
Every day
A God-willingness-fountain.

17293

The heart says:
"Come in, come in, come in!"
The mind says:
"Get out, get out, get out!"

17294

Alas,
I do not know why
Every morning I miss
My Lord's Compassion-flooded
Arrival.

17295

My unconditional surrender
To God's Will
Marks the very end
Of the unknown tomorrow's will.

17296

By God's boundless Grace
I shall definitely rescue
My helpless hopes
From doubt-destruction-night.

17297

If you do not have
Courage in your heart
And willingness in your mind,
How can you hope for a new dawn
And a fulfilling promise?

17298

God never wanted to remain
Unknowable,
But we made Him unknowable
By consciously and deliberately
Making friends
With the darkest abyss.

17299

Ignorance and darkness
Must not be taken
As a permanent reality-existence.

17300

O my Lord Supreme,
Every day do bless my inspiration-eyes
With an unlimited supply
Of sweetness
And do bless my aspiration-heart
With an unlimited supply
Of soulfulness.
