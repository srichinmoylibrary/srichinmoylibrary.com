15 At The Appointed Hour...

AT THE APPOINTED HOUR

At the appointed hour
Humanity said to me:
"Give me, give me.
This is the only way
For you to become great."
At the appointed hour
God said to me:
"Accept Me, receive Me.
This is the only way
For you to become good."

16 Three Cheers...

THREE CHEERS

At long last
I have conquered
Ignorance-night.
I share the good news
With earth, Heaven and God.
Earth said: "Not true, not true!"
Heaven said: "When? How!"
God said: "Three times three cheers
For My son! Bravo!"

17 Three Dangerous Things...

THREE DANGEROUS THINGS

I warn myself
Against three dangerous things:
My indulgence-capacity,
Earth's preference-capacity,
Heaven's indifference-capacity.
My Lord Supreme
Loves and blesses me
For my great discovery.

18 Do You Love?...

DO YOU LOVE?

Do you love man?
Then give him a cheerful smile.
Do you love God?
Then give him a soulful cry.
Do you love yourself?
Then give yourself a powerful assurance.

19 Heaven Is Puzzled...

HEAVEN IS PUZZLED

World-publicity
Declares him a great man.
Earth-sagacity
Declares him a perfect rogue.
Heaven-necessity
Is totally puzzled.
It knows not whether to accept him
Immediately
Or to reject him
Unreservedly.

20 After He Had Conquered...

AFTER HE HAD CONQUERED

After he had conquered earth,
He discovered
That he had conquered nothing.
After he had conquered Heaven,
He discovered
That he had conquered something.
After he had conquered himself,
He discovered
That he had conquered not only much,
But everything,
Even the monstrous things that he thought
He would never be able to conquer.

21 Who Comes Here?...

WHO COMES HERE?

Who comes here?
God?
Please wait.
Who comes here?
Man?
Why are you so late?
Who comes here?
Satan?
I am sorry.
This is the wrong place.

22 Your Mind, Your Heart...

YOUR MIND, YOUR HEART

Yours is the mind
That sees everything
And
Knows everything;
Therefore
God tells me that you are
Divinely great
And that He will utilise you
In the distant future.
Yours is the heart
That feels everything
And
Loves everything;
Therefore
God tells me that you are
Supremely great
And that He is using you
Proudly and incessantly.

23 What Do You Want?...

WHAT DO YOU WANT?

What do you want?
God's Infinity?
It is inside the smile
Of your eyes.
What do you want?
God's Eternity?
It is inside the cry
Of your heart.
What do you want?
God's Immortality?
It is inside the silence-beauty
Of your soul.

24 What Do I Like?...

WHAT DO I LIKE?

What do I like?
The simplicity of your life.
What do I like?
The sincerity of your heart.
What do I like?
The luminosity of your soul.
Do I like anything more?
Yes, I do.
I like the stupidity
Of your mind,
Although not much.

25 My Heart, My Soul...

MY HEART, MY SOUL

I am my heart.
I do not need
Anything more
Since my heart is soulful in itself.
I am my soul.
I do not need
Anything more
Since my soul is fruitful in itself.

26 God Is...

GOD IS

God is my Way;
Therefore
I like him.
God is my Home;
Therefore
I need Him.
God is my Room;
Therefore
I love Him.

27 Give What You Have...

GIVE WHAT YOU HAVE

Do you want
Himalayan joy?
Then give to God
What you have:
Your heart's aspiration-cry.
Do you want
Pacific peace?
Then give to man
What you have:
Your life's dedication-smile.

28 When I Measure God...

WHEN I MEASURE GOD

When I measure
Your God,
I laugh and laugh
Because
Your God is not great.
When I measure
His God,
I laugh and laugh
Because
His God is not good.
When I measure
My God,
I smile and smile
Because
My God is trying,
My God is striving.

29 On Earth, ...

ON EARTH,

IN HEAVEN

On earth
I am the frustration-silence
Of hunger.
In Heaven
I am the hunger-cry
Of silence.
