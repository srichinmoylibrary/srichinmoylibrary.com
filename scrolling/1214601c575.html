1290.

My Lord's Compassion-Eye
Is always anxious and eager
To be my life's sole spokesman.

1291.

My mind literally loves
Earth's colourful confusion.
My heart sleeplessly loves
Heaven's powerful illumination.

1292.

The day I start my morning
Without a prayer,
I become a hopelessly lost child
The entire day.

1293.

It is our heart's wisdom-light
That tells us to remain always
Outside our mind-gate.

1294.

My soul tells me
That my aspiration-journey
Is a one-way trip.

1295.

When we quote
Our Lord Beloved Supreme perfectly,
His Joy knows no bounds.
When we misquote
Our Lord Supreme consciously and deliberately,
His unhappiness, too, knows no bounds.

1296.

When I am in my heart,
I like to be as playful as a child.
When I am in my mind,
I like to be as prayerful as a saint.

1297.

My Lord's Compassion-Eye
Sleeplessly feeds
Humanity's light-hunger-cries.

1298.

When I please God
In God's own Way,
The Beyond beckons me
With unbounded pride.

1299.

A soulful aspiration-heart
Is indeed
A piece of the highest Heaven.

1300.

My Lord Supreme,
Are You not displeased
With my quantity-hunger-mind?
"My child,
I am not at all displeased
With your quantity-hunger-mind.
Your quantity-hunger-mind
Is helping Me in a unique way
To swim in the sea
Of ever-increasing Delight."

1301.

My Lord Supreme,
I vividly saw Your Compassion-Eye
At my marathon journey's start.
My Lord Supreme,
I throbbingly feel Your Satisfaction-Heart
At my marathon journey's close.
