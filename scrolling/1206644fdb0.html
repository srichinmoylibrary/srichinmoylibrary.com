20860

Can you not see
That God is swimming
In the sea of tears
Because of
Your love-devotion-surrender-decline?

20861

O seeker,
Since you have
An astonishing capacity
For self-deception,
How can you ever expect
To see God's Satisfaction-Face?

20862

If you make yourself
An easy target
For ignorance-night,
How can you be a lover
Of wisdom-light?

20863

To clear the horizon
Of self-doubts,
I must challenge
Fear within
And fear all around.

20864

How I wish I could have
The heart of a child,
So that I could become
A perfect stranger to sorrow!

20865

Let us all try
To make our mind silent
And peaceful,
And give no importance
To the dramatic mind.

20866

I am simply mortified
When I consider
The long-neglected promise
That I made to my soul!

20867

Peace is the only inner beauty
That constantly sings
God's Glory.
Therefore,
It can never be defiled.

20868

My Lord,
My heart is all gratitude to You,
For You have saved me
Time and again
By adamantly ignoring
My endless pleas.

20869

Deliverance does not come from
Renunciation.
Deliverance comes from
Sleepless self-offering.

20870

My Lord tells me
That unless I seek Him
With my heart
And not with my eyes,
He will not fulfil
My aspiration-cries.

20871

What have you
To give to God?
Your inner assurance
That you cannot do anything
Without His Compassion-Light.

20872

The moment I see and feel
God's Footsteps
Pressing upon my heart,
I sing
God's world-illumining Songs.

20873

God has come to you
So many times.
Alas, each time
You fall asleep
Just before His Arrival.

20874

God is always kind
And compassionate to you.
Can you not give Him some rest
From your constant world-complaints?
