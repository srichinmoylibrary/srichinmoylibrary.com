Chandika Chandika (3)

December 31st, 1969

Chandika chandika
Mahadev pranadhika
Barabhaya jagamata
Asimata amarata

Translation:

Mother-Power of the cosmos vast,
Dearer than the dearest
Life-consort of Lord Shiva.
Your Compassion, birthless and deathless.
O Mother, Infinity You are,
Immortality You are.
