16530

My mind-room must invite
God's Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Feet.
God will definitely accept
The invitation of my mind-room.

16531

Alas!
Why and how can doubt
Arise, take birth, prosper
And become victorious
Inside the human mind-temple?

16532

O my heart, O my soul,
O my God,
May my life's
Transformation-dream
Grow into reality!

16533

The life of peace
Has the capacity,
Is the capacity,
To untangle all human anxiety,
Worry and insecurity.

16534

Nobody is destined
To be perpetually wedded
To doubt and suspicion.
Indeed, it is a matter
Of personal choice:
Doubt or faith!

16535

Sweetness-soul,
Humility-life
And perfection-smile
Are always found together.

16536

O my resentment-mind,
Do you not see
That you are taking me away
Far, farther, farthest
From my enlightenment-soul?

16537

Lethargy-body
And unwillingness-mind
Die to live together
Inside the same
Dark ignorance-room.

16538

Doubt
Makes your mind dark.
Fear
Makes your heart dark.
Insecurity
Makes your life dark.

16539

The blossoms
Of my aspiration-heart
Every day are fondled
By the Compassion-Eye
And Satisfaction-Hands
Of the Supreme Lord.

16540

Alas,
Why do I allow ignorance
To come again and again
To disturb my mind's
Peace-silence-privacy?

16541

If you want to make
Considerable progress
And very fast progress
In your spiritual life,
Then every morning
Turn your attachment-mind
Into detachment-sword.

16542

You want to know
How freely and quickly
Insecurity-mind runs?
It runs faster than
Your fastest imagination-flight!

16543

The golden light
Of my soul-divinity's silence
Creates a new life
For me to share
With the world at large.

16544

Your heart's silence-ecstasy
Every day is devoured
By the Lord Supreme Himself.
