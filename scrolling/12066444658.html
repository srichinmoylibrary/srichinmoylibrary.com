20830

O seeker,
Never be complacent.
There is always time
For more deplorable mistakes
In your life.

20831

It seems that there is
A constant battle
Between
The desire-follower-mind
And the aspiration-inspirer-heart.

20832

My life's outer prosperity
And my heart's inner poverty
Are generally found together.

20833

My Lord tells me
That He has given me the mind
To charm His Ear,
And He has given me the heart
To feed His Heart.

20834

You do not have to find out
If God loves you and needs you.
You just need to ask your heart
To cry inwardly
And ask your life
To smile outwardly.

20835

I am not living on earth
To see tomorrow's sunrise.
I am living on earth
To be one with Eternity's sunshine.

20836

World-withdrawal
Is no solution.
World-acceptance,
World-transformation
And world-perfection
Are the real solutions.

20837

Alas,
I did not know
That I was all along living
On unfounded dreams.

20838

Irrespective of their development,
The souls proudly orbit
Around their Lord Beloved Supreme.

20839

If you do not aspire,
Your life will definitely
Be riddled with
Useless feelings of unworthiness.

20840

Not to see good in others
Is not only a serious
But also a dangerous
Defect.

20841

You make a monumental blunder
When you do not respond
To God's Compassion-flooded Eye.

20842

Prayer and meditation-highways
Can be used only
By constantly self-giving seekers.

20843

God's Compassion
And God's Concern
Champion the God-hunger-cries
Of the seeker.

20844

Happiness is not
A hallucination.
Happiness is not
An illusion.
Happiness is
An unmistakable illumination.
