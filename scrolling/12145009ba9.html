1145.

Nobody
Can ever deprive Truth
Of its ever-fragrant beauty.

1146.

My mind chooses greatness,
My heart chooses goodness,
And I choose oneness.

1147.

The human life
Is nothing other than a feeble attempt.
The divine life
Is indeed an enduring success.

1148.

My Lord Supreme shed bitter Tears
When He saw
The most deplorable condition
Of my spiritual health.

1149.

Childhood
Is a beautiful dream.
Youth
Is a powerful reality.
Age
Is a soulful surrender.

1150.

Action instigated by power
God never loves.
Action inspired by love
God not only loves
But also treasures.

1151.

Alas, in vain I look for God's Presence
In my mind's frustration-market
Instead of my heart's aspiration-garden.

1152.

Alas, I do not know
How my mind can be so convincing.
Alas, I do not know
How my heart can be eternally charming.

1153.

My Lord,
Do give me the capacity
To carry the fragrance
Of my heart-beauty
Wherever I go.

1154.

God desperately needs
Your heart's sincerity,
And not your life's severity.

1155.

To please God in God's own Way,
I need a learning life
And a yearning heart.

1156.

My Lord tells me compassionately
That every day is the right day
For my mind's spiritual check-up.

1157.

Rest assured,
Whatever is manufactured
By the doubting mind
Is venomous.

1158.

God will share His Divinity with you
If you are sincerely sick
Of your mind's stupidity.

1159.

Fortunately my heart
Still loves God,
And God's Heart
Still needs me.
