19 May 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My gratitude-heart has nothing to do
With fleeting time.
My gratitude-heart has everything to do
With Eternity.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 May 1999, 6:39 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 190 lbs. with each arm separately.

21 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have climbed the mountain
In search of You,
But You are not to be found.
Where are You, my Supreme,
Where are You?
I have crossed the desert
In search of You,
But You are not to be found.
Where are You, my Supreme,
Where are You?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 May 1999, 7:06 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 230 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 260 lbs. with each arm separately.

21 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I do not believe in my ability
At all.
I believe only in
My God-dependability.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 May 1999, 7:24 a.m.

23 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my surrender to Your Will
Be without even an iota
Of expectation.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 May 1999, 6:18 a.m.

23 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May I offer to You
My failure-life
Exactly the same way I offer
My success-life —
Happily, happily, happily.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 May 1999, 6:42 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (26 right, 26 left)

23 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every breath
Of my gratitude-heart
May I offer to You consciously.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 May 1999, 6:00 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

23 May 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I know, I know,
For the God-obedience-seeker
God's Heart-Door
Always remains wide open.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 May 1999, 8:48 p.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 230 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

23 May 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
If I feel Your Presence inside my heart,
Nothing else do I need,
Either from this world
Or from any other world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 May 1999, 9:00 p.m.

23 May 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A moment without my God-oneness
Is the worst possible crisis
In my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 May 1999, 9:21 p.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty-six times with one arm. (28 right, 28 left)

23 May 1999 (VII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my love for You
Grow into
An ever-increasing hunger.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 May 1999, 9:26 p.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 190 lbs. with each arm separately.

25 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart-shrine-candle
Steadily glow,
And may I sleeplessly aspire.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 May 1999, 6:07 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 230 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

25 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please be the real and only
Owner and leader
Of my life, my heart and my breath.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 May 1999, 6:28 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm sixty times. (29 right, 31 left)

25 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my life is without
God-devotion,
My heart becomes a sea of tears.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 May 1999, 6:39 a.m.

25 May 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do give me the capacity
To sail my heart's aspiration-boat
Much faster
Towards the Golden Shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 May 1999, 6:59 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

26 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May You desire my soul, my heart,
My mind, my vital, my body,
Everything that I have
And everything that I am
Every day
Only for Your personal use.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 May 1999, 7:29 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 230 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.
