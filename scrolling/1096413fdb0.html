55 Faith Shows Surrender ...

Faith shows surrender
The way to God.

56 The Heart And Hope ...

The heart and hope
Are inseparable.
The soul and promise
Are inseparable.

57 In The Inner World ...

In the inner world,
A God-crying heart
Has the fastest speed.

58 The Soul Translates ...

The soul translates
God’s Transcendental Messages
To the heart.
The heart translates
The soul’s lofty messages
To the life.

59 God Is All Love ...

God is all Love,
But alas,
God’s Creation
Is empty of love.

60 An Unconditional Surrender ...

An unconditional surrender
Is the solid foundation
Of God-realisation.

61 God Looks Here, There ...

God looks here, there
And everywhere
To find a tear-flooded
Heart-seeker.

17 December 2004 ...

17 December 2004

Xiamen, China

62 My God-Obedience ...

My God-obedience
And my heart-happiness
Always stay together.

63 My Heart ...

My heart
Always longs to be
At my Lord’s Haven-Feet.

64 God Loves To Parade ...

God loves to parade
The seeker’s fragrance-soul
And beauty-heart.

65 Each Self-Giving Thought ...

Each self-giving thought
Is a sacred heart-shrine.

66 Self-Admiration ...

Self-admiration
Is quite common.

67 God-Glorification ...

God-glorification
Is quite uncommon.

68 Today God Has Sent My Soul ...

Today God has sent my soul
To sing and dance
On my desire-grave.
