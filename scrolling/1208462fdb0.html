22660

Alas, there is not a single human being
Who is spared the experiences
Of physical pain, mental agony
And vital frustration.

22661

My aspiration-heart
Is regaining its strength once more
Under God's tender Handling.

22662

I am amazed to see
How my soul keeps God up-to-date
On everything in my life.

22663

In spite of my repeated
Himalayan blunders,
God's Compassion has concluded
That I am not past correction.

22664

The difference between God and me
Is this:
I do not allow God to run fast
Inside my heart.
He not only allows me, but also begs me,
To run the fastest inside His Heart.

22665

I am nothing.
I can do nothing.
Alas, will You not accept
My life's failure-tears
As my only gifts to You?

22666

Alas, I do not know
When and how
My mind became
The towering rock of pride.

22667

My Lord,
If You once come into
My little heart-room,
I assure You,
You will not only like it,
But love it.

22668

Both God and I
Desperately need speed.
I need speed in my God-hunger.
God needs speed
In my nature-transformation.

22669

Human life can become perfect
Only when the life-breath of the heart
Self-givingly loves the Lord Supreme.

22670

God's Compassion-Eye
Never pierces anybody,
But illumines and enlightens
Everybody.

22671

How can your heart find peace,
When you allow your mind
To roam everywhere?

22672

My Lord,
The Compassion-Smile
Of Your Compassion-Eye
Has fed my heart
Beyond my imagination.

22673

My Lord,
I want to love You unconditionally
Every day.
If I miss even one day,
Then that very day I want to die
And be buried,
Along with my ignorance.

22674

God the Justice
Either cannot or does not want to
Plumb the depths
Of God the Compassion.
