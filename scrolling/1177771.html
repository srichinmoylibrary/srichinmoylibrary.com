4801 Yearn For The Sea Of Purity

Yearn with intensity
For the sea of purity,
If you want your life
To be as vast and as perfect
As the sky of divinity.

4802 Three Heaven-Born Messages

Three Heaven-born messages:
My heart shall feel God this morning.
My eyes shall see God this afternoon.
My Lord shall fully manifest Himself
In and through me this evening.

4803 His Eyes Cry In Silence-Light

His eyes cry in silence-night.
His heart smiles in silence-light.
His soul dances in silence-delight.

4804 The Cheapest

Sincerity is the cheapest
Of all the divine qualities.
Yet it is so useful,
So fruitful,
So joy-giving
And
So God-loving.

4805 Yet Everybody Expects

Few seekers pray soulfully.
Fewer still meditate unconditionally.
Yet everybody expects,
If not demands,
Satisfaction-delight in infinite measure.

4806 Man Admires Truth

Man admires truth
The way his doubting mind
Admires his aspiring heart.

4807 Enthusiasm Begins The Journey

Enthusiasm begins the journey.
Determination continues the journey.
Surrender completes the journey.
Gratitude not only fulfils the journey
But also pleases God
In God's own Way.

4808 Failures In The Singing-World

There are two kinds of failures
In the singing-world:
Those who sing without having
A good singing voice,
And those who do not sing
In spite of having a good singing voice.

4809 Give Freedom To Your Heart

You have given freedom to your mind.
Therefore, your mind causes worries for you
About the future.
Why don't you give freedom to your heart?
Your heart will definitely prepare you
For the future.

4810 Perfect Quietness Of Heart

If you have perfect
Quietness of heart,
Then you are bound to realise
That the Grace divine
Is never slow.

4811 Loving Cures Everything

Learning cures nothing.
Aspiring cures a few things.
Serving cures many things.
Loving cures everything.

4812 My Victory Is Yours

My Lord, do give me the capacity
To sincerely feel
That my defeat is entirely mine
And that my victory is unmistakably,
Unreservedly, plus unconditionally
Yours.

4813 Purity Is The Music

Impurity is the voice
Of a tornado.
Purity is the music
Of a running river.

4814 A Precious Truth-Diamond

God loves me unconditionally.
This most precious truth-diamond
I shall carry every day
To my Lord Supreme.
