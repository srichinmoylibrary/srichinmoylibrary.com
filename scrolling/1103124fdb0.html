16 November 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May my mind always remain
Wide awake, anxious and eager
To run towards You
To make You happy.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 November 1998, 7:06 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

16 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
When I touched the God-realisation
Finish line,
You said to me, "My child,
I am extremely happy with you;
I am extremely proud of you.
But if you want Me to be
Infinitely more pleased with you
And infinitely more proud of you,
Then you must continue running
And reach the God-manifestation
Finish line."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 November 1998, 7:11 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times. (50 right, 50 left)

17 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I know, I know, I know,
No volcano-mind-determination,
No success-life-glory.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 November 1998, 6:06 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

17 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I know, I know, I know,
No mountain-heart-aspiration,
No progress-life-happiness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 November 1998, 6:14 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

17 November 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Alas, why do we allow our body's fear
And our mind's doubt
To weaken our heart's faith in You?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 November 1998, 6:19 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

17 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
What is the meaning of disobedience?
"My child, My child,
Disobedience means an unavoidable
Disaster-life."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 November 1998, 6:24 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times. (50 right, 50 left)

18 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I am happy
When You guide my heart.
I am more happy
When You govern my mind.
I am infinitely more happy
When You enslave my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 November 1998, 6:33 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

18 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I do not learn my age
From the earthly calendar.
I learn my age
From my life-surrender to the Will
Of Your Compassion-Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 November 1998, 6:40 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

18 November 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I do not need and I do not want
An exciting mind and an exciting vital.
My Supreme, I need from You
Only an inspiring mind
And an energising vital.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 November 1998, 6:48 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

18 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
An ocean-vast fact-knowledge
Cannot properly guide my life,
But an iota of wisdom-delight
Can guide my life properly,
And it is doing so.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 November 1998, 6:54 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times. (50 right, 50 left)

21 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Please, please promise to me
That You will never allow
My aspiration-lotus-heart
To wither.
"Never, My child, never!
Your aspiration-lotus-heart
Shall blossom and blossom and blossom."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 November 1998, 4:45 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

21 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My Heaven
Is inside Your Compassion-Eye.
My Heaven's Heaven
Is at Your Forgiveness-Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 November 1998, 4:52 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

21 November 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Everything else can be separated,
But two things can never, never
Be separated:
Your Compassion-Eye
And
Your Forgiveness-Heart.
These two abide eternally together.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 November 1998, 4:57 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

21 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May my heart aspire
To Your complete Satisfaction.
May my life serve
To Your complete Satisfaction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 November 1998, 5:03 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times — (50 right, 50 left) — for a new record of 2 mins 13 secs.

22 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I know, I know, I know,
Far above
All human punishment-laws
Is Your Forgiveness-Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 November 1998, 5:21 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm twelve times. (in three sets)
