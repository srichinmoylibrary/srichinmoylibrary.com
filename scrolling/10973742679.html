13 My Mind ...

My mind,
If you stay with doubt,
Then I shall have
Nothing to do with you!

14 Hesitation ...

Hesitation
Slows down the speed
Of everything divine
That we have.

15 When We See God ...

When we see God
Within ourselves,
He is beautiful and powerful.
But when we see God
In others,
He is only powerful.

16 No Matter Where ...

No matter where
God’s Feet are,
God’s Heart
Has given me the capacity
To clasp Them.

17 Only The God-Listeners ...

Only the God-listeners
Are my true friends.

18 Spirituality ...

Spirituality
Without surrender
Will always remain
Incomplete.

19 God Equally Loves ...

God equally loves
Those who aspire soulfully
And those who serve devotedly.

20 God Does Not Love ...

God does not love
The sleepers.
He has concern
For the sleepers,
But not love.

1 January 2006 ...

1 January 2006

Kuantan, Malaysia

21 When God Introduces Himself ...

When God introduces Himself
To me,
The finite in me
Immediately becomes the Infinite.

22 When I Introduce Myself ...

When I introduce myself
To God,
The Infinite in Him
Immediately becomes the finite.

23 I Am Enamoured ...

I am enamoured
Of each moment that I get
At the Feet
Of my Lord Supreme.

24 My Gratitude-Heart ...

My gratitude-heart
Has the capacity
To speak to my Lord Supreme
At every moment.

25 O Lord ...

O Lord,
Do purify my outer voice
And immortalise my inner voice.

26 My Lord ...

My Lord,
When will my mind
Realise You?
“My child,
I am more eager than you are
For your mind to realise Me.”
