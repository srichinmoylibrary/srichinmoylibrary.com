9060

Can you not see
That temptation-thief is hiding
Behind your heart-door?

9061

The divinity of gratitude
Is not richly rewarded
Here on earth, alas!

9062

If you love only God,
He will love the whole world
For you.

9063

Ignorance-tiger
Is not going to surrender to you
On its own.
It is you who have to compel the tiger
To surrender to you.

9064

If your hope-life dwindles,
Then there can be no real God-life
Inside your heart.

9065

Cleverness can never bring
Perfection-fulness in life.
It will always be deprived of
God's Satisfaction-Smile.

9066

Every day God sponsors
My aspiration-race
And I sponsor
God's Forgiveness-Race.

9067

May I become my heart's soulful
Purity-flooded surrender-dawn!

May I become my soul's powerful
Divinity-manifested perfection-sun!

9068

On earth you call it
Your God-realisation.
In Heaven God calls it
His Joy-Day.

9069

What can your life expect
From your lethargy-infested body?
What can your heart expect
From your doubt-poisoned mind?

9070

Beautiful
Is my moonlit mind.
Fruitful
Is my sunlit heart.

9071

My Lord, do teach me
In the morning
Your Heart's Oneness-Song
And in the evening
Your Life's Perfection-Dance.

9072

How can you reach the Golden Shore,
Since you have not yet even embarked
On your aspiration-journey?

9073

Be a faithful dog
To your heart,
The way your heart has become
A faithful dog
To your soul.

9074

The Abode of God's Compassion
Is the hospital
For sinners.

The Abode of God's Delight
Is the lighthouse
For seekers.
