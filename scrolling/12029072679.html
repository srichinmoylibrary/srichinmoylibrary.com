17115

My Lord, do give me the capacity
To sincerely pray to You
For the removal
Of each and every drop
Of my ego-pride.

17116

How can you be
A perfect instrument of God
If you do not care
To attain the impossible:
The perfection of your nature?

17117

My Lord has already kept open
My heart's door.
I do not know why
My heart is so foolishly hesitating
To come in.

17118

When your God-hunger becomes
Ceaseless and insatiable,
God will stand compassionately
Right in front of you -
And not before.

17119

Your mind may not know
What will make you happy,
But your heart does know
How to make you happy.
Listen to your heart.

17120

What my heart needs
Is aspiration-speed,
What my life needs
Is dedication-speed,
And what I need
Is surrender-speed.

17121

Only a constantly surrendered seeker
Can drink peace-nectar -
And nobody else.

17122

God tells me that
He always presides over
Our heart's opening ceremonies.

17123

If you want to beat the drum
Of your success-life,
You can.
But your Lord Supreme will recognise
Only your progress-life.

17124

I am praying to God most soulfully
To ask His Forgiveness-Heart,
Or at least His Compassion-Eye,
To take the wheel of my life-boat.

17125

If you are afraid of
God's Thunder-Feet,
How can your soul-bird
Smile in and through you?

17126

You have shown God many times
Your fearful eyes.
Can you not show God at least once
Your cheerful heart?

17127

What is aspiration?
It is our heart's quest
For the perfect manifestation
Of God's Fulness on earth.

17128

May my heart
Every day be blessed
With a sea-deep cry
To see Your blue
Compassion-Eye.

17129

Compromise with ignorance
Is not sweetness
But real bitterness
In utter disguise.
