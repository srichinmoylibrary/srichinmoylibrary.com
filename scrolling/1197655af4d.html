11975

Do you want to know what I am doing?
I am developing fast, very fast,
An aspiration-heart, soulfulness-eyes
And a self-giving life.

11976

I guard my mind
So that no uncomely thoughts
Can enter into it.
My heart guides my life
So that my life can
Cheerfully, soulfully and devotedly
Play with God.

11977

An open mind is needed
To see the flying inspiration-birds.
An open heart is needed
To see the climbing aspiration-flames.

11978

Without a devotion-heart,
To make the fastest progress
In the spiritual life
Is almost an impossibility.

11979

To be tense in your mind
Can never be the way
To solve any problem.
But to be intense in your heart
Is the surest way
To solve your life's teeming problems.

11980

If your heart has the capacity to dream,
Then definitely, sooner or later,
Your life will be able to soar.

11981

Do not cherish frustration.
To cherish frustration
Is to roam along serpentine roads
That ultimately lead nowhere.

11982

You call it
Your loyal obedience.
God calls it
His Victory's flying Banner.

11983

My Lord, when I make a mistake
Do strike me hard, very hard.
But do not crush me
With Your Indifference-Eye.

11984

Not killing others ruthlessly
But loving others affectionately
Is the real courage
In the battlefield of life.

11985

A soulful gratitude-heart
Is the fountain-spring
Of a genuine seeker's aspiration-life.

11986

Even unconscious insincerity
Will steal away
Your God-given dignity-light.

11987

My Lord Supreme tells me
That a gratitude-heart
Is indeed a powerful crown
From the treasure-house
Of Divinity's Perfection-Truth.

11988

Give God even a smaller-than-the-smallest
Devotion-plant.
God will grant you in return
The sweetest fruits
From His Vision's Satisfaction-Tree.

11989

Consciously and bravely destroy
The confusion-night
Of your God-searching mind.
God will grant you the capacity
To welcome His blessingful Heart
And powerful Eye.
