2601 Nothing To Become

The human in me tells me:
I want to be.

The divine in me tells me:
I can be
Because I want to be.

The Supreme in me tells me:
I have nothing to become
For I am already
God's Eternity's Satisfaction-Fruit.

2602 Take The Tempest Out

My Lord,
Do take my mind
Out of the tempest.
"My child,
Let Me take the tempest
Out of your mind first."

2603 What Is Precious?

What is precious?
My dedicated oneness-heart.
What is not precious?
My accumulated ego-strength.

2604 I Need Your Heart

My Lord, my Lord,
I need Your Heart.
Therefore,
I love Your Eye.

"My child, my child,
I love your heart.
Therefore,
I need your life."

2605 Who Says That God Forgets?

Who says that God remembers
Everything?
Does He remember
My animal past?

Who says that God forgets
Everything?
Does He forget
My heart's gratitude-bud?

2606 I Was Unprepared

I was unprepared for two things:
My mind's illumination-dream
And
My heart's perfection-cry.

2607 I Shall Not Interrupt You

My Lord said to me:
"I shall not interrupt you, My son.
Say whatever you want to say."
I said to Him:
"I shall not interrupt You, Father.
Give me whatever You want to give."

2608 One Thing Wrong

There is just one thing wrong
In my life:
My Lord is still missing,
Missing inside my imagination-sky.

2609 I Do Not Know

My Lord, I love You.
But I do not know
How deep my love is.

My Lord, I need You.
But I do not know
How sincere my need is.

2610 A Free Access

I just want to tell you
About the illumination inside my mind.
The beautiful thing about it is,
It has a free access
To the ultimate Goal.

2611 Duty Comes First

My Lord, duty comes first
In Your Life.
Therefore, I love You
And I am dying to make You
My own.

My Lord, pleasure comes first
In my life.
Therefore, I hate myself
And I am desperately trying
To disown myself.

2612 Your Arms Are For Extension

My Lord,
Your Arms are for extension,
To extend toward me.
My arms are for perfection,
To fight for perfection
In my outer life.

2613 My Lord Loves Me

Because
My mind is not confused,
My Lord loves me powerfully.

Because
My heart asks me who I am,
My Lord loves me proudly.

2614 When We Share Our Struggles

My heart,
When you and I share our struggles,
We at once reduce
Our imperfection-height
And increase
Our perfection-length.
