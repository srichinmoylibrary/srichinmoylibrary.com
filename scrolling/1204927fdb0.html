19160

Do not be a fool!
Never shake hands
With the giant desire-demon.

19161

Each self-giving action
Is supremely brighter
Than the Heavenly stars.

19162

The love of the heart
And the compassion of the soul
Never suffer from fatigue.

19163

The hesitation of the mind
Is the unanswered
Question of life.

19164

God never thought
That I was so stupid!
I never thought
That God was so forgiving.

19165

Each impure thought
Takes us farther away
From our aspiring heart-land.

19166

At every moment
We have to decide
Whether we want
The division-desiring mind
Or the union-aspiring heart.

19167

Every desire-dream
Eventually ends
In frustration-reality.

19168

I do not need my mind's
Power-voice.
I need only my heart's
Silence-choice.

19169

When your aspiring heart
Asks you not to mix
With your desire-mind,
Do not turn a deaf ear!

19170

God asks me to be
The best friend of my silence-heart
And to be always careful
Of my sound-mind.

19171

I do not calculate anything
In advance,
For that is the task
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.
I just pray and meditate,
Him to please in His own Way.

19172

God can be anxious to command me
Only if I am eagerly waiting
For His express Command.

19173

It seems that God's Compassion
And man's frustration
Are ruling this unaspiring earth.

19174

Only an aspiring heart
Will find the vision-light
To fulfil God
In God's own Way.
