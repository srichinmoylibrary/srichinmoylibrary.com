14601

My Lord, a tiny bird
Claims the vast sky.
Similarly, the finite in me
Longs to claim
Your Infinite Absolute.

14602

O my ego-life,
I have covered a very long distance
With you, all in vain.
Now I am returning to my journey's start
For a new goal,
Definitely without you.

14603

At last my inner confidence-light
Has come to the fore
And is burning cheerfully
My long-treasured frustration-pyre.

14604

I need safety at every moment
In my life.
Therefore, I do not mind using
My bullock cart-speed
To Infinity's Shore.

14605

The outer freedom
Has a short breath.
The inner freedom
Is at once birthless and deathless.

14606

My unwillingness-mind
Is still tormenting
My heart's soulfulness-smile.

14607

O my mind,
I neither need nor deserve
Your destructive discouragement-arrows
While I am speeding towards
My Lord Supreme.

14608

My vital, do you really mean
What you say?
Do you really want to say 'no'
To the Beloved Lord Supreme?

14609

If your self-confidence is genuine,
Then God's supreme Pride in you,
You are bound to feel.

14610

Each soulful thought
Is a God-world
In its surrender-flooded light.

14611

How will you ever reach
Your destined goal, my friend,
When you are such an easy-going
Truth-seeker and God-lover?

14612

My soul's divinity-torch
Has been illumining
My life's Eternity-Road.

14613

God's Oneness-Love
We see, feel
And one day shall become.
Therefore, we are not awestruck
By God's transcendental Height.

14614

I feel miserable
That my inner being has been absent
For such a long time
From my earth-home.
