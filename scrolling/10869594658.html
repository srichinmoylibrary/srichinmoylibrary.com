I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
My name is ego-indulgence.

I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
My name is oneness-starvation.

I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
I dearly enjoy
Sleepless ignorance-appetite.

I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
I have never developed
A distaste for ignorance-food.

I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
My inner faith
Has deserted me for good.

I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
I have not yet discovered the antidote
To my expectation-poison.

I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
I am afraid of taking
Aspiration-fitness-course.

I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
I do not ignore
Constant anxiety-punches.

I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
I do not dare to think
That God the Creator is within me
To awaken me
And God the creation is for me
To please me.

I know why I am helpless

I know why I am helpless.
I am helpless
Because
I think that my life
Is a curse of the devil
And not a Love-Gift from God.

Mine is a God-dream

Mine is a God-dream

Mine is a God-dream
That can never be shattered.

Mine is a God-dream
That can never be shaken.

Mine is a God-dream

Mine is a God-dream
That never forgets
Its God-appointment Hour.

Mine is a God-dream

Mine is a God-dream
That tells me
That my heart's aspiration
Is nothing other than
My life's re-creation.

Mine is a God-dream

Mine is a God-dream
That embodies only
The mountain-silence-peace.

Mine is a God-dream

Mine is a God-dream
That knows only
My heart's rising aspiration-sun
And never the setting
Of the aspiration-sun.
