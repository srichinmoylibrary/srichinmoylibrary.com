13475

Your constant self-sacrifice
Will make you
An impossibility-achievement-victor.

13476

Man without God's Satisfaction
I have seen many times.
But God without man's complaints
I have never seen.

13477

My every soulful smile
Is a beautiful plant
In God's Heart-Garden.

13478

I do not want
A satisfaction-mind-life,
But a perfection-aspiration-heart.

13479

Man consciously or unconsciously
Forgets to offer
His heart's gratitude-gift
To God the constant Giver.

13480

You will be a failure
Only if you allow your heart's joy
To be eclipsed
By your mind's clouds.

13481

Now that I have become
An aspiration-mendicant,
The desire-prince has left me
For good.

13482

Make up your mind!
What do you want -
Ego-mastery or ego-slavery?
Choose the right thing!

13483

At God's Heart-Restaurant
Your devotion can enjoy a divine meal
Free of charge.

13484

The impurity of your mind
Does not allow your heart
To be in the company of God.

13485

Give what you have
Cheerfully.
Give what you are
Constantly.
Then you will hear God's Footsteps
Approaching you fast,
Very fast.

13486

My poor mind!
Every day you are forced
To climb up
Endless confusion-staircases.

13487

Is there any human being
Who has not misused
God's Compassion-Light?
Is there any human being
Who will not eventually be illumined
By God's unconditional Compassion-Light?

13488

A fully satisfied life
Is indeed a rare creation
Of God the Dreamer.

13489

Your aspiration
Is nothing other than
Your heart's pilgrimage
Along Divinity's God-flooded Road.
