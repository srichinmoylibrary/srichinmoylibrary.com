23575

If you do not have
A divine tenacity of purpose,
Then you can never fulfil God
In His own Way.

23576

May my heart's aspiration-cry
Belong to the heart
Of all humanity.

23577

We do not have to be
Confronted by our fate
If we pray to God
To make His Will our will.

23578

My Lord Supreme,
Do make my heart
A heart of conscious surrender
To Your Will,
So that I can enjoy
Your Nectar-Compassion-Flood.

23579

When my heart has gratitude
In boundless measure,
I need nothing more to please God.

23580

Every day you must try
To increase your love for God.
Otherwise your life may still
Be dashed upon the rocks
Of self-doubt.

23581

Those who live in the mind-forest
Make a foolish mockery
Of God's Peace-Dream-manifestation
On earth.

23582

When I allow doubt to guide me,
It invariably leads me
To a land of nowhere.

23583

My Lord Supreme,
May my aspiration-heart
Ascend and ascend
Towards the unknowable heights
Of God-intoxicated Vision.

23584

If I have a God-pleasing life,
Then God will take me far
Beyond the boundaries
Of Infinity and Eternity.

23585

May my spontaneous love of God
Transform everything and everyone
That comes to oppose me.

23586

God definitely wants me to remain
Here on earth to serve Him,
Instead of flying back
To my soul's kingdom.

23587

O my doubting mind,
Do not dare to reappear
In the guise of an advising friend!

23588

My Lord tells me
That nothing can be more illumining
And more fulfilling
Than my heart's soulfulness-tears.

23589

My aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
Feed only
On God's Compassion-Smile.
