54 God Is At Once ...

God is at once
A Compassion-Teacher
And an Illumination-Student.

55 The Beauty Of A Devotion-Heart ...

The beauty of a devotion-heart
And the fragrance of a surrender-breath
Are the supremely choice Blessings
From the Unknowable.

56 Alas, ...

Alas,
There is not a single day
That our doubting minds
Do not torture us
And our Lord Beloved Supreme.

14 January 2003 ...

14 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

57 I Love My Soul-Commands — ...

I love my soul-commands —
I really do!

58 I Love My Heart-Whispers — ...

I love my heart-whispers —
I really do!

59 I Am Not A Singer, ...

I am not a singer,
But the angels come and sing for me.
Why?
Because they have been asked
By my Lord Beloved Supreme.

60 I Am Not A Seeker, ...

I am not a seeker,
But the cosmic gods come and bless me.
Why?
Because they have been asked
By my Lord Beloved Supreme.

61 My Lord’s Feet ...

My Lord’s Feet
Beckon
My prayer-tears.

62 My Lord’s Heart ...

My Lord’s Heart
Beckons
My meditation-silence.

63 We Must Not Anticipate ...

We must not anticipate.
At God’s choice Hour,
God will definitely
Do the needful.

64 Those Who Pray ...

Those who pray
For a world-oneness-heart
Are the harbingers
Of tomorrow’s golden dawn.

65 My Hope-Heart-Bloom ...

My hope-heart-bloom
Takes me to God.

66 My Promise-Soul-Blossom ...

My promise-soul-blossom
Brings God to me.

15 January 2003 ...

15 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA
