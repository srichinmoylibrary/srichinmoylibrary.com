December 31st, 1969

Sanga habe jiban mamo
Asha marubhume
Tushi jena tomai prabhu
Shesh aratir dhume

Shakti Pujari Nirvik Kaya Kothai Aji Kothai

December 31st, 1969

Shakti pujari nirvik kaya
Kothai aji kothai aji
Shudhu ache ek ghora samudra
Nai je tari nai je majhi

Sharirer Bal Hariye Phelechi Mor Sharirer

December 31st, 1969

Sharirer bal hariye phelechi
Mor sharirer bal
Hridayer bal rayeche hridaye
Prashanti achapal

Sushobhan Hiya Ban

December 31st, 1969

Sushobhan hiya ban
Bahu dure pralobhan
Charidhar premahar
Ananda anibar

Translation:

Beauty’s blossom-heart-forest!
Far away temptation-rest.
Everywhere love-garden.
Joy, bliss, a sleepless hand.

Tanu Mana Pran Sada Hoiran Hriday

December 31st, 1969

Tanu mana pran
Sada hoiran
Hriday satata
Karme lipto
Atmar joy
Mor parichoy

Tomai Bhule Raibona Prananath

December 31st, 1969

Tomai bhule raibona prananath

Translation:

O my heart’s Beloved Lord Supreme,
I cannot live without thinking of You.

Tomare Janabo Pranati Dao More Puta

December 31st, 1969

Tomare janabo pranati
Dao more puta bhakati
Tomare janabo pranati
Dao more prema bhakati

Tumi Sundaro Hiya Dibakaro

December 31st, 1969

Tumi sundaro hiya dibakaro
Kemane tomare pujibo

Translation:

You are beautiful, O my heart’s inner sun.
Please tell me how I can worship You.
