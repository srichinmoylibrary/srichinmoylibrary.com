15590

My Lord, my Lord,
You do not have to save me
From anything else
Or from anybody else,
If only You will free me
From my mind-prison!

15591

The invasion of doubt
Into our mind:
Does it ever end?
No!
It never ends,
It never does!

15592

God-lovers are loved and adored
By our God-searching
And God-invoking mind.

15593

You can establish your bond
With the rest of the world
By feeling its heart,
And not by examining its mind.

15594

Can you not see
That you have lost yourself
In your mind's illusion and delusion-sky?

15595

It is simply an impossible task
To escape from the mind's
Suspicion-downpour.

15596

God's Presence
In my mind-cave,
In my heart-room,
In my life-street
I see, I clearly see!
Therefore, I feel that
My God-realisation-days
Are fast approaching.

15597

God offers you
What He has and what He is.
He has infinite Love for you
And He is infinite Concern
For you.
Can you not give Him
What you have and what you are?
You have stupidity
And you are your present unwillingness.

15598

The heart of faith disappears
When the mind of doubt
Starts dancing.

15599

God is expecting every day
A break in my foul mind-weather.
He expects better weather —
Alas, all in vain!

15600

O my heart, do help me.
I want you to be the creator
Of my purity-mind.

O my mind, do help me.
I want you to be the preserver
Of my sincerity-heart.
