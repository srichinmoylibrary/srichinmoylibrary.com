16375

The embodiment of faithfulness
Is what we have to be
In the Heart
Of our Lord Beloved Supreme.

16376

Divine Grace will descend
On those who want to cultivate
Aspiration and dedication
At every second.

16377

Replace your mind's fear
With your heart's faith.
You will be able to zoom
Forward, upward, inward
All-where.

16378

This is the message
You are giving to me
And I am giving to you:
We must be faithful to God!

16379

I may escape
From my responsibilities
For the moment,
But how long can I continue
To escape?

16380

The day is always
Filled with negative forces,
But by using your enthusiasm,
You can conquer them.

16381

The higher you climb up
The God-realisation-tree,
The better your God-manifestation
On earth.

16382

If your spiritual life-roots
Are not strong,
Then you will not be able
To manifest God satisfactorily
In any part of the world.

16383

O stupid mind,
Why do you try
To delay the inevitable?
Can you not see that God
Is fast approaching
Your mind-jungle-thorns?

16384

God will never delay
Quenching your God-thirst,
If your God-thirst
Is breathless and desperate.

16385

What has happened to your inner life?
Over the years
Lethargy has devoured you,
And now you don't want
To budge an inch.

16386

The seeker gives God
A special delight
When he asks God
For the gift of God-Vision.

16387

O seeker,
Use your familiarity-fragrance wisely.
If not, you are bound to lose
Your most precious wealth:
Devotion-light.

16388

To be happy,
You don't have to be
Near your Master outwardly -
Just inwardly!

16389

No matter where they go,
People who have lots of complaints
Will never be happy.
