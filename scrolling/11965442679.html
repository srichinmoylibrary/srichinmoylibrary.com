10815

My inspiration tells me
That it is in need of
Daily correction.
My aspiration tells me
That it is in need of
Daily perfection.
My soul tells me
That it is in need of
Daily revelation.

10816

The sadness of your heart
Will dissolve in your soul's joy
If you never run out of
Self-giving and God-loving.

10817

If death wants to show you
Its face,
Can you not show death
Your God-given life-transforming
Grace?

10818

My heart's hope-eye
Will always pierce through the storms
Of my failures
For God's eventual Victory.

10819

How can your heart
Be flooded with aspiration-satisfaction,
When you consciously allow your mind
To be shrouded in dismal suspicions?

10820

Each negative thought
In the mind
Is another destroyer
Of a security-heart.

10821

O my human heart,
When will you stop enjoying
Tornado-perplexities?

O my divine heart,
When will you start enjoying
The beauty of a childlike sincerity?

10822

Each soulful thought
Is not only the life-cleaner
But also the God-distributor.

10823

If intuition-light is my guide,
Then the illumination of Heaven
And the perfection of earth
I shall see at the same time.

10824

Three wonders we carry within:
A God-dream,
A perfection-life
And a transformation-earth.

10825

God loves you
When He sees your heart
Flooded with silence.

God loves you
When He sees your mind
Empty of its usual contents:
Confusion-forest.

10826

Experience is a tiny plant
That grows and grows.
Realisation is a huge tree
That gives and gives.

10827

Each perfect thought
Is sweetness in the mind,
Fulness in the heart
And completeness in the life.

10828

My heart's confidence-smiles
Shall win
God's Silence-Beauty's Eye.

10829

Every day God visits
My heart's aspiration-eye
And feeds it with
His Oneness-Satisfaction-Smile.
