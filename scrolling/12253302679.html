One Thing I Know

December 31st, 1969

One thing I know:
Heaven is unkind.
One thing I see:
This world is slow.
One thing I sow:
Sincerity.

Heaven’s Will Leads Me

December 31st, 1969

Heaven’s will leads me, my mind.
Earth’s will feeds me, my heart.
God’s Will removes destruction-dart.
My life nothing can bind.

I Love Because I Must

December 31st, 1969

I love
Because I must.
I serve
Because I last.
I pray
Because I need.
I smile
Because I feed.

How Can I Forget You?

December 31st, 1969

How can I forget you?
     You are so kind.
     Am I so blind?
How can I forget you?
     You are so true
     In all you do.
How can I forget you?
     You are God’s choice.
     You are man’s voice.

I Think of God Because He Is So Kind

December 31st, 1969

I think of God
     Because
He is so kind.
I think of man
     Because
He is so sad.
I think of me
     Because
I am so blind.
I think of you
     Because
You are so mad.

O Deaf Heaven

December 31st, 1969

O deaf Heaven,
   I have two ears.
   You can have one.
   Gladly I shall share.
O eyeless earth,
   I have two eyes.
   You can have one.
   Sincerely I care.

Two Distance-Friends Meet

December 31st, 1969

Two distance-friends meet:
The finite cry
     And
The infinite smile.
Soulfully I love the two.
Equally I greet.

He Sang His Swan Song

December 31st, 1969

He sang his swan song:
      “Man is great.
      God is good.
      Life is hunger.
      Earth is food.”

Your Service-Tree

December 31st, 1969

Your service-tree
Is blossoming now.
Your surrender-fruits
Have kept your vow.

I Love My Task

December 31st, 1969

I love my task.
Daily I unmask my inner flame,
Daily I invite God’s Compassion-Game.

I Am Not Ready to Feel

December 31st, 1969

I am not ready
To feel the heart of man.
I am not ready
To see the Face of God.
     But
I am all ready
To give my soulful ken
     To man.
     But
I am all ready
To give my crying face
     To God.

Great Is He

December 31st, 1969

Great is He who lives
In Heaven-charming beauty.
Good is He who dies for
Earth’s fulfilling liberty.

On My Way Back to Life

December 31st, 1969

On my way back to life,
Life offered me its blossoming soul.
On my way back to death,
Death offered me its resting goal.

Your Need

December 31st, 1969

Your need and love are one;
You see their oneness white.
In them you see and feel
Your satisfaction-light.

Four Important Failures

December 31st, 1969

Four important failures:
Earth has lost its sincerity.
Heaven has lost its beauty.
Man has lost its divinity.
Truth has lost its purity.
