2645 The Lamp Of Faith

You cannot force faith
Into a man's mind.
You can only tell him
That his heart's home of peace
He will never discover
Unless he constantly burns
The lamp of faith
Within himself.

2646 He Knows The Secrets Of Heaven

He knows the secrets of Heaven
Because
His mind is now
All attention,
His heart is now
All obedience
And his life is now
All willingness.

2647 An Atom-Weight Of Goodness

An atom-weight of goodness
Has helped him shake off
The heaviest weight
Of self-deception and life-imperfection.

2648 God Will Wait For You

God will wait for you
With patience-light,
Only if you are willing
To abide by His Will,
Even in the evening of your life.

2649 If You Do Not Know The Truth

If you know the truth,
You will not be questioned.
If you do not know the truth,
You will be not only tortured
By your superiors
But also ridiculed
By your inferiors.

2650 Two Supreme Realities

Do not descend from Heaven
After me.
My heart will feel miserable.

Do not descend from Heaven
Before me.
God will not appreciate it.

God has two supreme Realities:
Togetherness-life
And
Oneness-heart.

2651 The Splendour Of The Firmament

My mind will be able to climb
To its appointed heights
And feed itself
With the splendour of the firmament
Only when it plays the role
Of the preserver-smile
And not the role
Of the destroyer-laughter.

2652 The Road Of A Defeated Life

If you live
In the chaos of thought
And in the market of temptation,
Then fear will chase you
Along the road of a defeated life.

2653 False Discoveries

So little to love,
So much to be loved!
So little to give,
So much to receive!
Indeed, these false discoveries
Have marked the end
Of humanity's progress.

2654 Every Day I Water Heaven

Every day I water Heaven
With the tears of my heart
So that I can live
My beautiful, soulful
And powerful childhood
Once again.

2655 His Eternity's Sunrise

Last night my Lord Supreme
Secretly came,
Secretly stole away
My night of desire
And secretly left
His Eternity's Sunrise
Inside my heart
For me to use in my own way.

2656 If Truth Comes First

If truth comes first
In your life,
Then you do not have to
Explain anything to the world,
For your eyes become
The explanation of all things
Big and small.

2657 Unless You Reform Yourself

Unless you reform yourself
And enjoy the fruits
Of transformation-light,
You will deny your mind
And rebel against your vital.

2658 God Does Not Forget!

God does not forget!
You will be questioned.
Before you
Will be God the Frustration.
Behind you
Will be God the Destruction.

2659 A New Hunger-Dawn

My desires have destroyed me.
My doubts have destroyed me.
I am troubled constantly by fears.
I must invent a new hunger-dawn
In my life.
