15490

God has given your heart faith
So it can play the role of a policeman.
God wants your heart-faith-policeman
To arrest your uncomely thoughts
Immediately.

15491

Life is too short
To enjoy powerful misunderstanding
And nurse animal anger!

15492

Alas,
Finally I have realised
That I am extremely overburdened
With my sleeplessly fault-finding mind.

15493

My willingness-heart,
God is extremely proud of you,
God is extremely fond of you;
You are the choicest instrument
Of my Lord Supreme.

15494

This morning
An amazing-discovery dawned
In my aspiration-heart:
This world of unrest
Will before long be flooded
With Infinity's peace, light and delight.

15495

God wants
Each and every human being
To feel
That His Satisfaction-Eye is for all.

15496

O my heart,
You are allowing your insecurity
To enjoy itself like anything today.
But rest assured,
Tomorrow frustration-wolf
Will, without fail, devour you.

15497

Finally today
I have realised how shocking
Is my faithful obedience-life
To my ugly desire-master.

15498

By striking you hard,
God does not get any pleasure.
But He does expedite
Your self-discovery.

15499

Hurry up! Hurry up!
Your God-manifestation-train
Is faithfully waiting for you.

15500

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
I do not want to see anymore
The face of my aspiration-sunset.
Please, please allow me
To see once more
The heart of my aspiration-sunrise.
