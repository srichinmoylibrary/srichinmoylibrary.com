15875

Today's unhappiness
Must not be kept for removal
Until tomorrow.
Tomorrow will be another day
For me to either suffer new unhappiness
Or enjoy a flood of delight.
Today's game I must play today
Wisely and perfectly.

15876

Go slowly, go steadily,
Go unerringly
Towards your
Destination supreme.
But never go back into your mind's
Unlit uncertainty-cave.

15877

God is extremely unhappy
That man thinks and feels
That he has only fragile ties
With God.

15878

The human mind is cramped
By confusion-night
Because it does not believe in
The fragrance of the heart's
Ever-new life.

15879

O my mind,
Do not give up!
You must continue chasing
Your unruly and wild thoughts.
You must capture them
And tame them.

15880

It is so strange
That the mind allows itself
To be bitten
By the doubt-mosquito.

15881

The human mind
Can also enjoy ecstasy-sea
If it makes friends with faith
The way the human heart
Has established its friendship
With faith.

15882

Only a God-representative
Can sumptuously feed
Your God-hungry heart.

15883

Each time my soul sings
A soul-song,
My life and I enjoy
The fastest Heaven-climbing flights.

15884

Self-hatred
Is a fatal disease.
Even the soul-doctor
Finds it difficult to cure.

15885

Two supreme necessities
Of today's world:
A universal peace-blossomed heart
And the perfection-beauty
Of God's Transcendental Smile.

15886

Nobody wants to hear
Your mind's empty words.
Everyone wants to feel
Your heart's self-offering feelings.

15887

An unwillingness-mind
And a failure-life
Are destined to remain
Inseparable.

15888

No matter how big
Or how small
The ego is,
It will have to surrender
To the vastness-heart.

15889

Once you come out
From behind your mind-barrier,
You can enter into the world
Of silence-ecstasy-dreams.
