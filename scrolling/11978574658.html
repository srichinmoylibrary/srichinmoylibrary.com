12130

As one can drink water easily,
Even so the human mind
Can twist truth easily
And mercilessly.

12131

Try harder, infinitely harder!
Your heart's aspiration-cry
Will last longer.

12132

No matter how softly and secretly
Doubt walks,
Its footsteps are unmistakably heard.

12133

Allow not your heart's
God-manifestation-soldiers
To be plagued by bold 'perhaps'-troops.

12134

Every day I pray to God
To keep me surrounded
By my heart's God-surrender-will.

12135

You are bound to succeed
Since you have
An inexhaustible supply
Of hope-flowers.

12136

He is flooded with happiness
Because he was able to eavesdrop
On the golden future.

12137

Lethargy,
Do not dare come near
My body-temple!
I do not want you
To clip the wings
Of my soulful willingness-bird.

12138

With your ebbing faith-stamina,
How can you please God
The way you did once upon a time?

12139

I know not how or why
Suddenly a life of success
And a life of progress
Are beckoning me.

12140

To my extreme sorrow
You have buried yourself
In a tomb of self-pity.

12141

Every morning I tell my heart
What to save,
And promptly teach it
How to save.

12142

Every evening I tell my mind
What to throw away,
And compassionately teach it
How to throw away.

12143

To conquer your mind's
Daily frustration-fatigue
With your heart's
Cheerfulness-light
Is always worth a try.

12144

A long-lasting
Gratitude-heart-tree
Is, alas,
Beyond man's grasp.
