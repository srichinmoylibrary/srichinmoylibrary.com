7260

The sound-sermon
Is for man's tongue
To appreciate.

The silence-sermon
Is for God's Heart
To love.

7261

A sudden purity-shower
From Above
Has completely changed
Not only his mind
But also his entire being.

7262

A peace-receptivity-mind
Can easily strengthen
All insecurity-nerves.

7263

His present hope
Always enjoys interviews
With his future determination.

7264

Ignore the warnings of hesitation
If you want to succeed
In the battlefield of life.

7265

Every day he walks
Along the road of misinformation
That endlessly stretches
Into nothingness.

7266

The mind's suspicion-attacks
Can easily be destroyed
By the heart's aspiration-sword.

7267

At long last
He is able to transfer
His mind's frustration-funds
To his soul's
Illumination-savings bank.

7268

Either you have to enjoy
Your heart's oneness
With Immortality
Or live in your mind's
God-oblivion-chasm.

7269

When aspiration-plants
Start growing,
Frustration-trees
Start dying.

7270

Do not delay, do not delay!
Your soul is asking you
Every day
To kidnap your life's
Ignorance-criminal.

7271

I really do not have
Anything to say
Against God.
I wish only to love Him
The way He loves me:
Sleeplessly and unconditionally.

7272

A hero-soul is he
Who is always eager to lighten
God's universal Burden.

7273

God's Heart-Clock
Is neither slow nor fast.
It goes at precisely the right speed
To give God the opportunity
To grow inside my mind
And to glow inside my heart.

7274

Your unaspiring life
Will not allow you
To walk along the road of truth.
You will falter,
Your life will falter,
Your entire being will falter
While walking
Along the road of truth.
