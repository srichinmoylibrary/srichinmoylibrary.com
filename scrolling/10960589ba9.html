40 God’s Forgiveness-Heart ...

God’s Forgiveness-Heart
Blessingfully teaches me
How to smile.

41 I Make No Mistake ...

I make no mistake
When I tell the world
That I am all God’s.
I make no mistake
When I tell the world
That God is all mine.

42 My Lord Says To Me ...

My Lord says to me,
“My child,
You are totally mistaken.
You must say
That you are all God
And I am all you, only you.”

43 With Every Single Breath ...

With every single breath,
My heart and I
Are rising towards
Our Lord Beloved Supreme.

44 At Long Last ...

At long last
I am able to enter
Into my life’s
Beauty-fragrance-inundated horizon.

45 Transcendence And Expansion ...

Transcendence and expansion
Work together
Happily and proudly.

46 When I Offer ...

When I offer
My life’s power-of-attorney
To my Lord Supreme,
He immediately embraces me
Lovingly, powerfully and self-givingly.

47 A Sleepless Self-Giver-Seeker ...

A sleepless self-giver-seeker
To humankind
Has no time for himself.

48 During My Deep Meditation ...

During my deep meditation,
When God-ecstasy appears,
My inefficiency-life
Immediately disappears.

10 January 2004 ...

10 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

49 From This Moment On ...

From this moment on,
My Lord shall take His seat
Inside my stupidity-flooded mind.

50 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Never says ‘no’
To me,
But I do say ‘no’
To Him.

51 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Never doubts me,
But I do doubt Him.

52 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Never insists —
But I do.

53 Where Do I Live? ...

Where do I live?
I live in between
My life’s eagerness-heart
And my life’s intensity-breath
To fulfil my Lord Beloved Supreme.
