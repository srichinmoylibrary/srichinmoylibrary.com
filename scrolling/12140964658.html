730.

My heart and I are so proud
That we are able to welcome
The peace-heralds.

731.

My devotion-heart
Stems from
My life's love of God.

732.

O my mind, do not worry
If you take a false start
In your spiritual life.
God will give you chances
Again and again
To take a good start
In your spiritual life.

733.

O seeker, be careful!
Never allow your heart-shrine
To fall into utter neglect.

734.

When I live in my mind,
I am addicted to power.
When I live in my heart,
I am devoted to love,

God-love.

735.

You have not only to diminish
Your pleasure-seeking activities,
But to eliminate them completely
To serve God and please God
In God's own Way.

736.

If you constantly cherish doubt,
Then you will be ruthlessly exiled
From God's Compassion-Favour.

737.

My heart's cries
And my Lord's Smiles
Daily exchange telegrams.

738.

If you truly and sincerely love God,
Then you do not have to
Resign yourself to your fate.

739.

Every day during my meditation
God comes and blessingfully gives me
A private lecture
On how to please Him all the time
In His own Way.

740.

God consoles the broken-hearted
Only when He sees
That they are going to become sincere,
Self-giving and God-aspiring.

741.

There was a time

When I joined my mind
In its self-congratulation.
But now I join my heart
In its God-invocation.

742.

A self-disciplined life
And a God-caring heart
Are treasured by God Himself.

743.

A self-giving life
Is indeed
Man's peace-freedom-fighter
On earth.

744.

It is only with the heart-eye
That we can see the truth
Correctly and perfectly.
