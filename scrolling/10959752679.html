13 May My Dedication-Life ...

May my dedication-life
Fly, dancing,
Towards the Skies Unknowable.

14 The Tears Of My Heart ...

The tears of my heart
Are always ascending
Towards Heaven.

15 The Smiles Of My Soul ...

The smiles of my soul
Are always descending
Towards the earth.

16 God Has Chosen ...

God has chosen
His Cosmic Eye
To be the sole Conductor
Of His Cosmic Orchestra.

17 My Devotion-Tears ...

My devotion-tears
My Lord Supreme enjoys
As His most delicious feast.

18 My Cheerfulness-Temptation ...

My cheerfulness-temptation
God cannot resist.
He comes running
To grab it!

19 My God-Gratitude-Heart ...

My God-gratitude-heart
Is, indeed, a fact,
Not a legend.

20 God Created Each Human Being ...

God created each human being
In a unique way
So that He can be pleased
By each human being
In a unique way.

30 December 2003 ...

30 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

21 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Takes His Compassion-Exercises
Twenty-four hours a day.

22 Love The World ...

Love the world
And serve the world
Unconditionally.
You will be the golden bridge
Between today’s world
And the years ahead of you.

23 My Lord ...

My Lord,
It is Your flaming Breath
Inside my heart
That is keeping me alive
Here on earth.

24 No More Shallow Promises ...

No more shallow promises,
My Lord!
This time I am determined
To fulfil my promise.
I shall please You at every moment
In Your own Way.

25 The Silence Of The Mind ...

The silence of the mind
Embodies
The Power of the Infinite.

26 A Wasted Moment ...

A wasted moment
Is the very beginning
Of my failure-life.
