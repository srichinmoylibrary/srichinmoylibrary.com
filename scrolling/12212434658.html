No Heart Is Too Weak

January 13th, 2004

No heart is too weak
To receive Love from God.

No Life Is Too Insignificant

January 13th, 2004

No life is too insignificant
To love and serve God.

The Mind’s Proudest Verdict

January 13th, 2004

The mind’s proudest verdict:
God does not know what the mind does.

I Use My Vision-Eye

January 13th, 2004

I use my vision-eye
When I am in the inner world.

I Use My Mission-Heart

January 13th, 2004

I use my mission-heart
When I am in the outer world.

When I Most Sincerely Pray

January 13th, 2004

When I most sincerely pray,
My Lord says to me,
“My child, you are, indeed,
My Beauty’s child.”

My New Inner Name

January 13th, 2004

My new inner name
      Is
God-blessing-abundance.

My New Outer Name

January 13th, 2004

My new outer name
      Is
God-surrender-ecstasy.

To God, Nothing Is More Important

January 13th, 2004

To God, nothing is more important
Than my tearful heart
And my blissful eyes.

My Mind and I

January 13th, 2004

My mind and I,
When we depend on each other,
We miserably fail.

My Heart and I

January 13th, 2004

My heart and I,
When we depend on each other,
We invariably succeed.

My God-Obedience

January 13th, 2004

My God-obedience is
My God-given
Golden flute-melody-life.

My God-Disobedience

January 13th, 2004

My God-disobedience
Is the thunder-drum-sound
Cherished by the hostile forces.

My Master Reveals the Unseen God

January 13th, 2004

My Master reveals the unseen God to me
At the very right moment.

God Is Ready to Discuss With Me

January 13th, 2004

God is ready to discuss with me
Not about my past,
Not about my future,
But about today.
