22445

If my life does not live
In my heart-nest,
My Lord will have no chance
To rest.

22446

Inspiration-flames
I was.
Aspiration-fire
I now am.
Realisation-sun
I shall become.

22447

My Lord has left it
In humanity's hands
To build
The Kingdom of Heaven
On earth.

22448

It is not difficult for me
To tell the world
How undivine I am.

22449

The idleness of the mind
And the timidity of the heart
Are always found together.

22450

Alas,
My Lord is so little understood
In this world,
Yet I do nothing
To illumine the mind of the world
Or to feed the heart of the world.

22451

Peace
Is neither my new home
Nor my old home.
Peace
Is my only home.

22452

Do not try to make
The future visible.
Try to make the present
Not only as visible,
But also as useful as possible.

22453

My Lord of Compassion tells me
That my nature's perfection
Is now not too far off.

22454

Whenever I live in the mind,
I am forced to pass through
The depths of ignorance-night.

22455

The desire-life is the life
That knows
What thirst is,
But knows not
How to quench it.

22456

God starts laughing at me
Even long before I admit
That I am hopelessly useless.

22457

If ever you do something
Good for humanity,
You will be thanked
By God Himself.

22458

Only because of
God's unconditional Forgiveness-Light
Am I able to see Him,
Not for any other reason.

22459

May the eyes of my heart
See my Lord Supreme
In the very near future.
