10745

The seeker's heart-cry
Is his escalator to go up
To his own highest height
In God's Love-Fragrance-Heart.

10746

Every day cry
For illumination-light
And illumination-delight.
Otherwise,
Every day you will have to face
A host of temptation-sellers.

10747

An ingratitude-thought-ant
Is enough to disturb
Immortality's love-flooded soul.

10748

There is no quitting!
Only rekindle your enthusiasm.
Indeed,
This is an unparalleled gift
That you can offer
Both to the creation and to the Creator.

10749

Have you lost the boarding pass
For your Heaven-flight?
If so,
Enter into your heart-garden
And collect some beauty-flowers
And offer them to the ticket-checker.

10750

Confusion does not have to be
The monopoly of our human mind.
It is also the powerful destroyer
Of our heart's illumination.

10751

Each true God-lover
Has to be the standing record-holder
For selfless service.

10752

Your gratitude-life
May escape God's notice.
Your surrender-life
May escape God's notice.
But your love-oneness-life
Can never escape God's notice.

10753

The inevitable happened:
My doubt-mind became
My life's disastrous failure.

10754

Not by hiding
From God's Compassion-Light
But by confiding
In God's Forgiveness-Heart
Can and will a seeker's life
Receive God's Perfection-Light.

10755

The main reason
For my being here on earth
Is to see God smiling sleeplessly
In His Beauty's creation-flower:
Man.

10756

Centuries of corrupt power
Have finally discovered
That they have done nothing other
Than lay a frustration-foundation.

10757

Do you want to invent
A new name for God?
Then clip immediately the wings
Of your God-oneness unwillingness-bird.

10758

His sweet breath of sincerity
Has stopped his mind
From feeding the fantasies of fame.

10759

Live not in the heart
Of false hope.
Live not in the mind
Of false disappointment.
If you live in these two places,
You will be inducted into
The hall of stupidity-fame.
