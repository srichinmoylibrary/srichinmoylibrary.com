16760

O ruthless ignorance-tyrant,
I am determined
To part company with you!

16761

Each person should feel
In the depths of his heart
His sleepless need for
A God-loving and God-serving life.

16762

When you do not live in the heart,
There is every possibility
That you will be forced to live
A disaster-prone doubt-life.

16763

My Lord,
May I pray to You every day
For a surrender-dawn
And a gratitude-eve.

16764

The heart knows
What to choose.
The body, vital and mind know
How to lose.

16765

The human in us
Hopes to exist.
The divine in us
Dares to live.

16766

To change
Is to make friends
With the unknown beyond,
Which is the source
Of constant opportunity.

16767

A man of belief
Compels the impossibility-tiger
To sit at his feet.

16768

Circumstances
Cannot change man's life.
His attitude
Can and does.

16769

A sincere and powerful smile
Is undoubtedly
A problem-solving capacity.

16770

True happiness is achieved
Only when the body, vital, mind and heart
Have completely surrendered
To the express will of the soul.

16771

To increase our inner progress
What we need
Is constructive encouragement
And not destructive criticism.

16772

Confidence
Is success-strength.
Oneness
Is progress-length.

16773

The physical in us
Touches the moon-land.
Indeed, this is man's greatness.
The spiritual in us
Loves the moon-consciousness.
Indeed, this is man's goodness.

16774

Self-acceptance
Is the dawn of satisfaction.
Self-confidence
Is the noon of perfection.
