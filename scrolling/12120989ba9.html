26245

When I pray, I pray to God
For my success-smile.
When I meditate, I meditate on God
For my progress-dance.

26246

Who can put an end
To our mind's suspicions,
If not God the Light?

26247

Who can stop
Our vital frustrations,
If not God the Compassion?

26248

We try to judge God.
Alas, if God wanted to judge us,
Where would we be?

26249

My Lord, allow me to tell You
Only one thing:
I love You in spite of
My ignorance-cherishing life.

26250

If we do not feel God's Love
All-where,
Then our love of God
Remains imperfect.

26251

My anger cannot break my mind.
Only my heart's tears
Can break my mind.

26252

When willingness and eagerness
Are running together
Towards God's Compassion-Eye
And God's Satisfaction-Heart,
Nothing, nobody can stand in their way!

26253

My mind says to God:
"God, tell me how I can help You."
My heart says to God:
"My Lord, do tell me how
I can ever be of service to You."

26254

To know and feel
The mission of my soul
Here on earth,
I must keep my desire-life
Far, far away from me.

26255

Stop immediately
Criticising your divinity.
If not, it is you who will be
Eternity's beggar,
Both in your inner life
And your outer life.

26256

The seekers of truth
And the lovers of light
Always have a very special place
In God's Satisfaction-Heart.

26257

O my mind,
Pray for just one moment
For God to release you
From your self-chosen prison.

26258

What is life,
If not a God-given opportunity
For God-manifestation?

26259

Alas, God's Compassion-Eye
Is a foreigner
In our mind-territories.
