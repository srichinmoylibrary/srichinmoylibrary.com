26530

My doubting mind
And my strangling vital
Astonish and bewilder
Even God.

26531

The era of God's
Indomitable Will-Performance
Is fast approaching.

26532

Where do I actually live?
I actually live
Inside the faith-blossoms
Of my heart-temple.

26533

In my spiritual life,
Nothing can equal
One atom of my gratitude-heart.

26534

God will never allow science
To take charge
Of God-realisation.

26535

You are so shameless
That you criticise God
Right in front of
His astonished Eyes.

26536

God steers His Life-Boat
Every morning
Between my aspiration-shore
And His Satisfaction-Shore.

26537

There can be no final destination
For our love-devotion-surrender-
Pilgrimage-life.

26538

I do hope that I can make God happy
With my heart's new hope
And my life's new promise.

26539

God's Forgiveness-Heart
Means every word
Of what it says.

26540

God wants me every day
To forgive and forget,
So that I can happily and proudly
Run towards Him alone.

26541

An enthusiasm-life
And an eagerness-heart
Know no insurmountable barriers.

26542

Do you want to know
Why God is extremely fond of me?
He is extremely fond of me
Because not only do I love Him only,
But I also depend on Him solely
In my inner life of aspiration
And in my outer life of dedication.

26543

If there is no complaint
Against you
And if you also have no complaint
Against anybody,
Then you are a living saint.

26544

A spiritual Master is, indeed,
A God-manifestation-giant
Of the earth.
