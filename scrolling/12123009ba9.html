26445

My Lord,
You do not have to take my side
When my spiritual height is low.
If You use
Your Tolerance and Patience-Light,
I shall regain my spiritual height.

26446

Every moment God visits
My body, vital, mind and heart.
My heart smilingly and lovingly
Welcomes God,
But the rest of the members of my family
Remain fast asleep
When God visits my inner home.

26447

Alas, if we count how many days a month
We do not pray and meditate,
Our sincerity-heart will be
Unimaginably shocked.

26448

What is obedience?
Obedience is our sincere belief
That God cares for us
And is doing everything
For our fastest progress
And brightest smile.

26449

Today my mind is parched
For inspiration-light
And my heart is parched
For aspiration-delight.

26450

When the forces of nature
Become destructive,
We can overcome them
On the strength of our purity-prayer
And divinity-meditation.

26451

O my mind,
It is you who have stranded yourself
On the island of uncertainties.

26452

In our spiritual life,
We must not expect anything
Good, divine, illumining and fulfilling
From our iron-cast, stubborn mind.

26453

A sprinkle of God-Light
And God-Delight
Is enough to give us
Abiding satisfaction.

26454

My Lord tells me
That when He illumines my mind
With His Justice-Light,
He is more satisfied
Than when He illumines me
With His Compassion-Height.

26455

At each moment
I enjoy the Beauty and Fragrance
Of God's Compassion-Heart.

26456

My mind blindly sports
In demonic play
Which is utterly self-destructive.

26457

No more shall I allow myself
To be suffocated
In the dust and sand of the past.

26458

We definitely do not know
And we do not want to know
That we are all part of
God's Master-Plan.

26459

We enjoyed the desire-feast,
But alas, we are starving.
We will always starve
Unless we can satisfy God
With the right thing,
And that right thing is our aspiration-cry.
