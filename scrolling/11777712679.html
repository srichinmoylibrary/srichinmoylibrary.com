4815 Rise Like A Pure Candle Flame

Drag not your past with you.
With soulful thoughts
Rise like a pure candle flame
To touch Heaven's Perfection-Beauty.

4816 Your Life Will Be Cradled By God

If your heart is made
Of constantly multiplying loving deeds,
Then your life will be cradled by God
In His Eternity's Satisfaction-Smile.

4817 The Golden Chain

My heart of gratitude
Is the golden chain
That binds my life
To the infinite Life
Of the Absolute Supreme.

4818 Unless You Unfold Your Faith

Unless you unfold your faith divine,
How can you expect to be
A choice instrument
Of God the Compassion Infinite?

4819 The Mightiest Sound

If your heart owns
The bravest silence,
Then your life can create
The mightiest sound
To announce the Manifestation-Victory
Of your Beloved Supreme.

4820 What Does Failure Do?

What does failure do?
Failure improves my life,
Strengthens my mind
And heightens my heart.

4821 Each Soulful Effort

Each soulful effort
And each resolute action
In the countless incidents
Of man's human life and divine life
Is proudly treasured
By the Absolute Supreme Himself.

4822 Doomed To Suffer

An ingratitude-heart
Is doomed to suffer
From the serious sickness
Of unbearable impurity.

4823 To Transform My Unlit Life

I love and need
God's iron Hand
In God's velvet Glove
To transform my unaspiring and unlit life.

4824 To Conquer Its Sleeping Life

Why does the body want to meditate?
The body wants to meditate
Because it wants to conquer
Its sleeping life.
The body knows that meditation
Is the answer.

4825 To Conquer Its Fighting Life

Why does the vital want to meditate?
The vital wants to meditate
Because it wants to conquer
Its fighting life.
The vital knows that meditation
Is the answer.

4826 To Conquer Its Doubting Life

Why does the mind want to meditate?
The mind wants to meditate
Because it wants to conquer
Its doubting life.
The mind knows that meditation
Is the answer.

4827 To Love The Supreme More

Why does the heart want to meditate?
The heart wants to meditate
Because it wants to love
The Supreme more.
The heart knows that meditation
Is the answer.

4828 To Manifest The Supreme More

Why does the soul want to meditate?
The soul wants to meditate
Because it wants to manifest
The Supreme more.
The soul knows that meditation
Is the answer.

4829 The Supreme Meditates

Why does the Supreme want to meditate?
The Supreme wants to meditate
Because in His entire universe
He loves to enjoy only one thing,
And that is meditation.
Therefore, He sleeplessly meditates.
In His transcendental Life
And in His universal Life
The Supreme meditates.
