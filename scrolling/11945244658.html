8830

As long as God wants me
To sponsor His inner race,
I shall be more than willing
To satisfy Him
No matter how few participants
I get for the race.

8831

When I was rowing
My own boat,
I was watched
By only one spectator:
My frustration.

8832

Unless and until
Your towering mind
Admires your kneeling heart,
Your God-realisation
Will remain a far cry.

8833

A heart of plenty
Says big things -
God's God-Things -
In simple and soulful terms.

8834

If you care only
For your mind's great plans,
God will not allow you to see
His Vision's Prosperity.

8835

Activate
Your aspiration-heart.
God will then ask you
To cultivate
His Satisfaction-Heart-Field.

8836

Not God's Justice
But God's Compassion
Has predicted
That man's heart shall succeed
And not fail.

8837

You must know
That your mind is yours.
How, then, can you allow your mind
To stand in God's way?

8838

God invented
Tranquility-satisfaction.
Man has invented
Tension-destruction.

8839

Only when we please God
In God's own Way
Shall we feel comfort.
Otherwise,
Everything will be an ordeal.

8840

If you are a true God-lover,
Then you will see
That God has created everything
For your pure heart
And not for your critical eyes.

8841

Just between you and me,
God has finally decided
To starve the doubting mind
To death.

8842

God has given you
The capacity to please Him,
And you have given ignorance
The capacity to destroy you.

8843

If you lock your mind
Inside the anxiety-prison cell,
How can anybody
Liberate you?

8844

Your old mind
Is not going to study
With your new heart.
Never!
