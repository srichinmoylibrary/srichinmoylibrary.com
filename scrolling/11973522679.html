11615

My heart-temple welcomes
Only the seekers
Of earth's newness
And the lovers
Of Heaven's fulness.

11616

My Lord's Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Feet:
These are the only things I keep
Inside my heart's gratitude-temple.

11617

There is nothing
And there can be nothing
Slower than the speed
Of an unwillingness-mind.

11618

To achieve victory
Over the vital,
Invoke your purity-heart's
Oneness-delight.

11619

My mind is made of
A thousand promises.
My heart is made of
One single deed:
Sleepless love of God.

11620

The mind's selfishness
And the heart's ingratitude
Are sleeplessly inseparable.

11621

Before perfection dawns,
You have to disobey
All the commands
Of lethargy and anxiety.

11622

Now that you have withdrawn
Your expectation-application,
God is knocking at your perfection-door
With His Vision's Satisfaction-Smile.

11623

If your mind is wise,
Then you will be able
To walk with God for a mile.

If your heart is wiser,
Then you will be allowed
To fly with God for ten miles.

If your soul is wisest,
Then you will be able to run and fly with God
At every moment.

11624

You and your undisciplined life
Have forced God to delay His Arrival
At your heart's aspiration-gate.

11625

Since you have chosen self-adoration
As your only occupation,
Poor God is compelled to withdraw from you
Helplessly.

11626

Cheerfulness, unlike frustration,
Does not want to rule your life.
It only wants to awaken your dormant life,
Illumine your mind
And satisfy your heart.

11627

If your mind's enthusiasm goes stale,
Then earthly pride
Will automatically find you
And Heavenly Compassion
Will not acknowledge you.

11628

Believe it or not,
God has given you another chance,
So give up your shameless infatuation
With ignorance-night!

11629

You are always safe,
My seeker-friend,
Inside your heart's
Spontaneous surrender-smile.
