God-Remembrance

August 25th, 2003

God-remembrance
Means life is
A oneness-performance.

I Am a Constant Mistake-Maker

September 17th, 2003

I am a constant mistake-maker.
God’s Heart is a constant eraser.

God Counts My Pleading Heart-Tears

September 17th, 2003

God counts my pleading heart-tears.
I count God’s beaming Eye-Cheers.

Let Me No More Roam

September 17th, 2003

Let me no more roam.
Please, please, take me Home.

My Life Has Lost Its God-Oneness-Soul

September 17th, 2003

My life has lost its God-oneness-soul.
No goal, no goal, alas, for me no goal.

What Do I Have?

September 17th, 2003

What do I have?
An imposter-mind.
Oh, who am I?
A joy-beggar blind.

God Speaks

September 17th, 2003

God speaks.
My prayerful heart
Soulfully treasures.
God speaks.
My doubtful mind
Falsely measures.

Earth-Popularity

September 17th, 2003

Earth-popularity,
No Heaven-proximity!

When My Home Is My Heart

September 17th, 2003

When my home is my heart,
I smash the division-dart.

Disobedience-Deeds

September 17th, 2003

Disobedience-deeds
Kill our inner joys.
Obedience-actions
Are God’s proudest toys.

My Days Are for God’s Bountiful Grace

September 17th, 2003

My days are for
God’s bountiful Grace.
My nights are for
God’s beautiful Face.

My Mind-Machine

September 17th, 2003

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Please do not stop
Until my mind-machine
Is completely off.

I Am Not Afraid

September 17th, 2003

I am not afraid
Of God’s Power-Eye.
But I am afraid
Of God’s Heart-Sigh.

Eat and Drink

October 5th, 2003

Eat and drink
And drink and eat
Before you touch
The Lord’s Feet.

O My God-Reaching Heart

October 13th, 2003

O my God-reaching heart, speed up.
You look strong and smart.
