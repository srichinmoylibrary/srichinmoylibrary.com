6801

Surrender, surrender, surrender!
Let your life
Be a garland of surrender.
Your soul will grant you
Its satisfaction-tower.

6802

Ignorance-obstacles
Are bowing to you
Because
You have become a student
At the school of wisdom-light.

6803

Alas, I do not know
How long I shall have to continue
Loving my worthless mind
In my sleepless life.

6804

You have so many enemies
Because you do not live
In your soul's universal
Oneness-delight.

6805

Do not turn
The pages of the past
If you want an inspiration-mind
And an aspiration-heart
To be your intimate friends.

6806

A God-lover and truth-seeker
Has the capacity
To save even those
Who have lost all hope.

6807

A fallen life
Need not be
And cannot be
A fallen life forever.

6808

Do not spread rumours
About your God-realisation.
Neither God nor man
Will appreciate them.

6809

Since faith is known
All the world over,
Can you not have
An all-illumining faith-heart?

6810

It is you who have dug
Your disobedience-grave.
It is you, therefore,
Who have to be buried
In that grave.

6811

Do not think
That everybody has the capacity
Or will have the capacity
In the near future
To correspond regularly with God.
Impossible!

6812

Can you not see
That you have become
A most deplorable victim
Of your own destruction-deeds?

6813

O my heart,
Do not become a blind agent
Of ignorance-night
The way my body, vital and mind
Have already become.

6814

I am a seeker-heart.
I have simply nothing to do
With my mind's far-fetched fantasies.
