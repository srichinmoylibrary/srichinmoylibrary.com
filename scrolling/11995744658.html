13830

Nobody told me,
But I definitely know
That my purity-heart's gratitude-life
Is the only shortcut
To my God-realisation.

13831

What can a God-Smile do?
A God-Smile can glorify
The human in me
And manifest the divine in me.

13832

Unlike us,
God's Heart does not believe in
"Do not disturb" signs.

13833

My enthusiasm-heart
Quickly and unmistakably sows
Perfection-satisfaction-seeds.

13834

Each human being
Is a fulfilment-blossoming
Hope-dream-promise of God.

13835

I love to live
Either inside my heart's hunger-cry
Or inside my soul's feast-smile.

13836

At every moment
God wants us
To smile at Him outwardly
And cry for Him inwardly.

13837

Every day
My Lord Supreme proudly plays
With my mind-ball —
Simplicity,
And my heart-ball —
Purity.

13838

God sleeplessly treasures
The purity-smile
Of my heart
And the gratitude-cry
Of my life.

13839

Expect many changes,
Especially in your inner life,
If you want to make progress
Faster than the fastest.

13840

Even an iota
Of love-peace and oneness-power
From God
Can save not only today's fearful world
But also tomorrow's doubtful world.

13841

My Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
I came into the world
To make You happy.
At the end of my journey's close,
You will take me back into Yourself
To make me happy.

13842

My heart,
I am so proud of you —
That you can see God
Even when He is far away.
My mind,
I am so ashamed of you —
That you cannot see God
Even when He is right in front of you.

13843

My Lord's Whisper-Voice
Is my life's
Only illumination-perfection-choice.

13844

My heart lovingly
And gratefully
Claims the world.
My mind constantly
And endlessly
Blames the world.
