74.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because your inner cries
Have helped me achieve
Outer competence.

75.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because you alone
Have the capacity
To unveil God's Face for me.

76.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because humanity's
Frustration-stories
Cannot take away
Your love of God.

77.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because every morning
You lovingly introduce me to God.

78.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because of you
I have countless
Faith-friendship-flames.

79.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because you surprise me
With your ever-increasing love
For God.

80.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because you have inspired me
To feel
That God and God's Heaven
Can be won
With my life's patience-light.

81.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because you so lovingly help me
Cling to my prayer-God.

82.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because you have given me
The great capacity to arrest
The God-doubt-intruder.

83.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because of you
The breath of goodness
Has entered my life.

84.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because of you the whole world
Puts its long arms around me.

85.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because you are
More than willing
To take care of my mind,
The completely spoiled child
In God's Family.

86.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because you warn me
Not to walk
Along the resentment-path.

87.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because you have given me
A large space
To bury my pride-giant
In your humility-garden.

88.

My heart, I love you.
Why?
Because every day you inspire me
To soar on selflessness-wings.
