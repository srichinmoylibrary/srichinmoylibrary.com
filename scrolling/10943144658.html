27 To Please God ...

To please God
In His own Way,
I must become a giant personality
In spirituality.
I must become unique
In God-achievements
And a real asset to humanity.

6 December 2001 ...

6 December 2001

Kauai, Hawaii

28 The Seeker’s Inner Peace ...

The seeker’s inner peace
Is the strongest power
In his outer life.

29 The Supreme Victory Is Certain ...

The supreme victory is certain
Only when God’s Eye
Touches our hearts.
What is the supreme victory?
God-realisation for God’s Sake.

30 An Obedience-Mind ...

An obedience-mind
Receives
God’s proudest Smile.

31 A Disobedience-Mind ...

A disobedience-mind
Is a seeker’s greatest failure
In his spiritual life.

32 When I Pray ...

When I pray,
The sound-life of the day
Joins me proudly.

33 When I Meditate ...

When I meditate,
The silence-heart of the night
Joins me self-givingly.

34 God’s Sadness Is Measureless ...

God’s Sadness is measureless
When He sees
My world-aloofness-mind.

35 God Sees Inside My Sweetness-Heart ...

God sees inside my sweetness-heart
The blooms and the blossoms
Of His own countless divine Qualities.

36 The Unaspiring Life Lives ...

The unaspiring life lives
In the thickening clouds
Of the mind.

37 The Aspiring Life Lives ...

The aspiring life lives
In the brightening sun
Of the heart.

10 December 2001 ...

10 December 2001

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

38 My Happiness ...

My happiness
Does not start
Until God’s Compassion-Eye
Arrives.

39 My Lord Is Always ...

My Lord is always
An eager Listener.
Can I not be the same,
At least once in a while?
