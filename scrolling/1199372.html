13601

Each chosen seeker
Has the miracle willpower
To manifest God's all-illumining Light
On earth.

13602

If you do not have faith
In your aspiration-life,
Then all your golden dreams
Will wither away.

13603

If you do not want to make
A clean break
With your deplorable past,
Then your present and future
Will be not only helpless
But also useless.

13604

Willingness:
This is what God wants
From your self-giving soul,
And this is what God needs
From your mind-illumining heart.

13605

No seeker will ever be truly happy
Unless and until he has cheerfully stood
On the surrender-summit.

13606

How far can you go?
Not very far with your elephant-ego!
How deep can you go?
Not very deep with your elephant-ego!
How high can you go?
Not very high with your elephant-ego!

13607

You do not have an iota of confidence.
Therefore, you are forced to watch
The devastation of your dedication.

13608

If your heart
Is genuinely seeking,
Then your life
Will never be able to enjoy
Aspiration-inertia.

13609

Is there any human being
Who has never gone through
A confidence-crisis?

13610

He who has placed his entire being
At the Feet of the Lord Supreme,
Will not be forced to ride
The waves of self-doubt.

13611

In his aspiration-heart and dedication-life
The seeker needs only one thing:
Perfection-promotion in God's own Way.

13612

If your surrender never dies,
Then you become a choice member
Of God's immediate Family.

13613

A life of sleepless self-giving
Is the surest way
To God-discovery.

13614

Every morning I do two things:
With my prayer-wings
I fly.
With my meditation-boat
I sail.
