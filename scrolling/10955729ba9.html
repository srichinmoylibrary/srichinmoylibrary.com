40 My Heart’s Each New God-Song ...

My heart’s each new God-song
Is a forward step
Towards my Lord’s Heart-Home.

41 I Am My Life’s ...

I am my life’s
Inner tears.
God is His inner Heart’s
Unending smiles.

42 Nothing Is As Charming ...

Nothing is as charming,
Illumining and fulfilling
As my inner boat-ride
With my Lord Supreme.

43 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Is always desperately eager
To keep the genuine seekers happy.

44 With His Own Hands ...

With His own Hands
Our Lord Supreme feeds
His unconditionally surrendered
Children.

45 A Complete Surrender-Life ...

A complete surrender-life
Means a oneness-dance
Of God and the seeker.

46 Aspiration-Life Means ...

Aspiration-life means
Countless
God-fulfilment-opportunities.

47 All My Devotion-Heart-Letters ...

All my devotion-heart-letters
God’s Heart immediately reads
And His Hands immediately fulfil.

48 The Mind ...

The mind
Is the strength
Of only one individual.

49 The Heart ...

The heart
Is the power
Of multitudes.

4 December 2003 ...

4 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

50 I Must Walk, March, Run And Sprint ...

I must walk, march, run and sprint
Behind my Lord Beloved Supreme,
For He knows
Where He is taking me.

51 God Surrenders To Me ...

God surrenders to me.
God says to me,
“My child, I have been leading you
For centuries after centuries.
Now I feel you can lead Me.”
“My Lord,
You must always lead me.
The child has always to follow
The Father.
Please do not break Your Cosmic Law
For me.”

52 Life Needs Courage ...

Life needs courage,
Courage invincible.
Heart needs oneness,
Oneness universal.

53 Eternity Is Within ...

Eternity is within.
Infinity is without.
Immortality is at once
Within and without.
