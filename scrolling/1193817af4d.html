8175

Today you are a sleeper.
Tomorrow
You will not only be
Awakened,
But an earth-awakener.

8176

Run not after miracles!
All miracles
Are shockingly fragile.

8177

Darkness-mouths
Desire to devour
The hearts of light.

8178

The human mind
Is apt to be armed
With illusions and delusions.

8179

He who does not
Consciously aspire
Lives in the empire
Of nothingness.

8180

It is not uncommon
For the desire-mind
To be entangled
In the meshes of ignorance-night.

8181

Shake off,
Shake off the fetters
Of the seven lower worlds!

8182

God will not allow your mind
To destroy
The beauty of your heart's
Vision-eye.

8183

Let your entire being know
The beauty and divinity
Of aspiration-flames.

8184

A seeker
Can every day enjoy
Clear views
Of golden horizons.

8185

Do you not see
That your ignorance-life
Is costing you enormously?

8186

O my vital,
Why do you want
A life of fiery competition?

8187

Death is not
And can never be
Life's final examination!

8188

Self-dedication
Is the quickest way
To release oneself
From old habits.

8189

O my mind,
Every day try to swim
In the heart of God's
Compassion-Sea.
