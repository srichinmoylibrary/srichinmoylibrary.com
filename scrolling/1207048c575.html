21290

I do not want to know
What God plans to do with my life.
I just want to know
If I can remain eternally inside
His Heart-Garden.

21291

My soul's brilliance
Will eternally outshine
My life's achievement-splendours.

21292

I thought that I had done so much
For God-manifestation,
But God took one look
At my God-manifestation-progress-report
And burst into roaring laughter.

21293

As there is no difference
Between the mind of the intellect
And the voice of the intellect,
Even so there is no difference
Between the heart of the seeker
And the life of the seeker.

21294

God keeps His Heart's Oneness-Door
Always open
Not only for my dear friends
But also for my stark adversaries.

21295

When I look at my mind,
I see nothing but a life
Of lost opportunities.
When I look at my heart,
I see nothing but a life
Of blossoming opportunities.

21296

Why am I crying?
I am crying because
My Beloved Supreme
Has come to see me,
The human in me.
Why am I smiling?
I am smiling because
My Beloved Supreme
Has come to take me,
Both the divine in me
And the undivine in me.

21297

If we do not give God
The exact thing He wants from us,
He will never be satisfied
With a compromise.

21298

Our enlightenment-progress
Depends on how seriously we have taken
Our life-detachment-process.

21299

What is faith and what is doubt?
Faith is something
That God's Heart plays with.
Doubt is something
That my mind plays with.

21300

How have I made my life secure?
I have done absolutely the right thing.
What is the right thing?
At every moment I listen
To God's Command,
Seated at His Compassion-Feet.
