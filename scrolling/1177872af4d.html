4975 You Are Wise, You Are Perfect

You are wise
If you can gain knowledge-light
From the illumining minds of the world.

You are perfect
Only if you can gain wisdom-delight
From the all-fulfilling Heart of the Supreme.

4976 Disown Your Mind

If your mind does not want
To be inspired by your pure heart,
Then disown your mind.

If your heart does not want
To receive instructions from your wise soul,
Then disown your heart.

4977 What Really Counts

What really counts
Is not what I see
In God's Compassion-Eye,
But what God sees
Inside my aspiration-heart.
And what does He see?
He sees His own Immortality's Face.

4978 Love-Power Sets The Pace

In all our inner races,
Love-power sets the remarkable pace
And gratitude-power helps us win
The unforgettable race.

4979 Only I Can Damage

Only I can damage
My Eternity's beauty-face
And only I can damage
My Infinity's purity-heart.

4980 Is There Any Man?

Is there any man of ignorance
Who can never feel
My soulful heart?

Is there any man of intuition
Who cannot read
My doubtful mind?

4981 My Satisfaction

My salvation
Is from my sin.

My liberation
Is from my ignorance.

My realisation
Is in my aspiration.

My satisfaction
Is in my Lord's Satisfaction.

4982 Your Burning Determination

Your burning determination
Should always be kept
Beyond the reach
Of deathless lamentation.

4983 They Do Not Want To Remember

My mind does not want to remember
How much God has done for it
Although God blesses its breath
Time and again, unconditionally.

My heart does not want to remember
How much it has done for God
Although it serves God every day
Devotedly, soulfully and unreservedly.

4984 To Become A Satisfaction-Emperor

Try to long for
The fruitful peace within
And the soulful action without.
Indeed,
This is the only way
To become a satisfaction-emperor.

4985 I Shall Feel Sad

My Lord Supreme,
I shall not feel sad if Your Compassion-Sky
Does not care for my heart's
Iota of aspiration.
But I shall definitely and sleeplessly feel sad
If Your Forgiveness-Sun
Does not care for my life's
Himalayan blunders.

4986 God's Own Beauty-Plants

God has planted His own Beauty-Plants
So lovingly and carefully
Inside our hearts.
Does it not pain His Heart
When we carelessly destroy them?

4987 Where Can I Find A Man?

Where can I find a man
Who says that he loves God
Although God does not love him?

Where can I find a man
Who loves God the seen creation
And hates God the unseen Creator?

4988 A Self-Worshipper

Do not say anything
To a self-worshipper,
For God knows how and why
He is stabbing his own heart.

4989 There Is Always Hope

There is always hope
For any man
Who can pitifully cry in the morning
And heartily laugh in the evening.
