18945

The soul may be invisible,
But the soul's wisdom-light
Is not only visible
But also usable.

18946

My mind's simple thoughts
God loves.
My heart's simple feelings
God loves.
My life's simple faith
God loves.

18947

My Lord,
Do never allow me
To be dumbfounded
By my mind's doubt-strength
And suspicion-power.

18948

My Lord,
May I thrive on
My heart's soulful hope-plant
And my life's fruitful promise-tree.

18949

His overcalculating mind
Is utterly responsible
For his life's despair-day
And his heart's despair-night.

18950

O humanity-inspiring heart,
Where are you, where?
I sleeplessly need you!
O Divinity-serving life,
Where are you, where?
I breathlessly need you!

18951

My Lord,
May I be sleeplessly enslaved
By Your Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Heart.

18952

My Lord,
May my heart be
A newborn champion
Of hope-light
And promise-delight.

18953

May my aspiration-life
Eventually become God's own
Cosmic Vision-Fulfilment-Delight.

18954

Who wants
A life that shuttles
Between 'maybe' and 'certainly'?
Not me!

18955

Thought-bridge
I wanted.
Will-bridge
I want.
Oneness-bridge
I need.

18956

Is there any human being
Who is not expert
In the ego-collision-game?

18957

Alas, why do I remain
All the time
A dormant God-gratitude-seed?

18958

It is not an easy task,
But we must try to succeed
In liberating ourselves
From earth-frustration
While living on earth.

18959

Long
Is the life of love.
Longer
Is the life of oneness.
Longest
Is the life of fulness.
