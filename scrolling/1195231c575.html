9590

A God-lover comes into the world
Only to love
And, through love,
To raise humanity's consciousness.

9591

God is a Dreamer.
He dreams of only one thing:
The transformation of His Vision's creation.
You can also be a dreamer
If you do not allow
The imperfections of life
To forever torture you.

9592

A seeker belongs to only one school:
The inner school.
This school has only one Teacher:
The Beloved Supreme.

9593

O vast ocean of peace,
In you I see all perfection,
For your infinite expanse
Easily engulfs
All my imperfections and limitations.

9594

If you do not remember
Your aspiration-past,
How do you expect God to remember
Your satisfaction-heart?

9595

If you think
Religion is nothing other than ritual,
Why not try
To go beyond the ritual
And enter into the heart
Of the world-heart:
Yoga?

9596

Put your wisdom-light to work.
The life of aspiration-dedication
Is for daily use.

9597

Your ignorance-days are numbered!
Your mind does not want to study
The doubt-doctrines anymore.

9598

Flow with the consciousness-river
Within.
You will be able to learn
The language of God-perfection.

9599

There is only one book you should read,
And that book is inside your heart-cave.
The name of that book is
Oneness-satisfaction.

9600

Aspiration is a land
Where beauty's love
Grows on divinity's trees.
