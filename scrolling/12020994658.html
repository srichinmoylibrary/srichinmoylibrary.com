16330

O my heart,
Always try to remain far above
The fruitless ignorance-dreams
Of the mind.
Never allow yourself
To be caught by them!

16331

Unwillingness
To do the right thing,
Unwillingness
To feel the right thing
And unwillingness
To become the right thing
Have destroyed
Humanity's God-fulfilment-promise.

16332

Inspiration I need
To appreciate the beauty
Of earth.

Aspiration I need
To appreciate the duty
Of life.

Realisation I need
To appreciate the Divinity
Of God.

16333

Every day
My soul is working
Extremely hard
To liberate my aspiration-life.

16334

If you do not stand
Face-to-face
Against ignorance-night,
God may love you,
But He will never
Need you.

16335

Ego does not live alone.
It always lives with
Its dear friends:
Frustration and destruction.

16336

Alas,
When I look for
Expectation-fulfilment-fountain,
I see everywhere nothing but
Lifeless and useless rocks.

16337

It does not take much time
For a seeker to learn
That his doubtful mind
Is nothing other than
A frustration-carrier.

16338

God-believers are crucified
Only because God wants them
To be infinitely more glorified
Than they otherwise would be.

16339

Every day God wants us
To look for a remedy
To cure our mind's
Unwillingness
And our body's laziness.

16340

Do not give up!
God does not want you to be
A defeated hope-life.

16341

To free yourself
From earth-desires,
You need to sow aspiration-seeds
In your heart-garden.

16342

I shall conquer my twenty defects
Faster than you will conquer your one,
If you don't have eagerness
And enthusiasm
In your spiritual life!

16343

If you serve the Supreme
In a low consciousness
Or out of fear,
How can you expect Him
To give you a passing mark?

16344

If you are
All the time absorbed
In your mind-satisfaction,
How can you ever think
Of God-manifestation?
