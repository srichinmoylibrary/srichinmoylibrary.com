27 My Mind Wants To Be ...

My mind wants to be
The God-knower.
My heart wishes to be
The God-lover.

28 My God-Dependence-Joy ...

My God-dependence-joy
Has no equal.

29 My Mind Carries Yesterday’s ...

My mind carries yesterday’s
Failure-bondage.
My heart carries tomorrow’s
Victory-garland.

30 The Finite Power ...

The finite power
Challenges.
The infinite Power
Embraces.

31 Earth Is A Stranger ...

Earth is a stranger
To Heaven’s ceaseless Smiles.
Heaven is a stranger
To earth’s helpless tears.

32 God’s Golden Heart ...

God’s golden Heart
Descends.
Man’s broken heart
Ascends.

33 When My Mind ...

When my mind
Salutes God,
He immediately plays
His Rhapsody-Flute.

34 God’s Greatness ...

God’s Greatness
Puzzles and blinds me.
God’s Goodness
Cradles and nestles me.

2 December 2002 ...

2 December 2002

Taupo, New Zealand

35 May A Gratitude-Flower ...

May a gratitude-flower
Be found
In each heartbeat
Of mine.

36 Mine Is A Heart ...

Mine is a heart
Of eagerness.
God’s is a Breath
Of Closeness.

37 O My Stupid Mind ...

O my stupid mind,
How long are you going to enjoy
Negativity’s torrential rain?

38 God Says To My Heart ...

God says to my heart,
“My child, I have a beautiful story
To tell you.”
My heart says to God,
“Father, I have a soulful song
To sing for You.”
“My child, My story
Is all about your previous life
When you were My fondness-lamb.”
“My Father, my song
Is all about my immediate future
When You will make me
Your inseparable oneness-heart-hero.”

39 Mine Is A Greedy Mind ...

Mine is a greedy mind
For God’s constant
Outer attention.

40 Mine Is A Hungry Heart ...

Mine is a hungry heart
For God’s ceaseless
Inner Compassion.
