25515

Human action loves
The power of achievement.
Divine action loves
The beauty and fragrance
Of aspiration.

25516

Unconditional surrender
Is an indispensable course
In the life-school of a seeker.

25517

The beauty that embodies a dream
Has also the power
To fully manifest a reality.

25518

Where to begin?
With a readiness-mind,
With a willingness-life
And
With an eagerness-heart.

Where to end?
Nowhere,
Nowhere,
Nowhere!

25519

Aspiration is the only bridge
Between our today's heart-bloom
And our tomorrow's life-blossoms.

25520

How to nourish your eagerness?
To nourish your eagerness,
Daily feed your selflessness.

25521

The suspecting mind,
The doubting mind
And even the searching mind
Do not care for the beauty, purity
And divinity of solitude.

25522

The destination of the desire-mind
Is frustration-thorns.
The destination of the aspiration-heart
Is perfection-roses.

25523

My God-realisation
Is not humanity's gift
But Divinity's Boon.

25524

The heart-experience
And the mind-experiment
Are absolutely essential
In our spiritual life.

25525

The inner ascent
Is infinitely steeper
Than the outer ascent.

25526

If you do not shake your fist
At your doubting mind,
Your doubting mind
Will never change.

25527

Give up your old, crystallized
Mind-vital-comforts
If you want to make a sincere effort
At God-discovery.

25528

Science thinks that it can live
Without God.
Spirituality knows and feels
That its very breath
Is God Himself.

25529

Science, like a dying man,
Secretly believes in God
And needs God.
