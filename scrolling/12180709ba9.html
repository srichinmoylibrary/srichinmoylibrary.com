Sab Aparadh Kariyachho Khama

June 21st, 2000

Sab aparadh kariyachho khama sab aparadh mor
Taito ajike dakichhe amai amar purir bhor

Translation:

All my misdeeds, today You have forgiven.
Therefore Heaven’s dawn is beckoning me.
