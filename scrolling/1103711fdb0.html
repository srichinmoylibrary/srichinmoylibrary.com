14 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Sleeplessly
I must keep my heart-eye open
To Your Compassion-flooded Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 September 1999, 8:30 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 200 lbs. with each arm separately.

14 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I know, I know,
My insecurity gives You
A severe headache;
My jealousy gives You
A real heart attack.
"My child, My child, My child,
To My greatest Joy,
It is your gratitude-heart
That cures My headache;
It is your surrender-life
That cures My heart attack."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 September 1999, 9:00 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (84 reps)

15 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must stop crying
For Your constant Attention.
I must start praying
And meditating
For my mind's purification
And my heart's expansion.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 September 1999, 7:50 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

15 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every morning,
Out of Your infinite Compassion,
You come to plough
My life's gratitude, obedience
And surrender-fields.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 September 1999, 8:09 a.m. Before lifting 275 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 550 lbs.)

15 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Will has travelled
The entire length and breadth
Of the world.
Alas, only very few aspiring souls
Have responded.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 September 1999, 8:15 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm.

15 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Those who are living in
The mind-supremacy,
The vital-supremacy
And the body-supremacy
Are destroying the peace-blossom-dream
Of the world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 September 1999, 8:25 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

15 September 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only when I am ready —
When my mind is willing
And my heart is eager —
Do You teach me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 September 1999, 11:48 p.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

15 September 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You gave me the capacity
To surrender unconditionally to You.
Now You have given me the capacity
To reach the zenith-heights
Of spiritual excellence.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 September 1999, 11:55 p.m. Before lifting 275 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 550 lbs.)

17 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind's thirst for You,
My heart's hunger for You
And my life's tears and smiles for You
Lovingly, prayerfully, soulfully
And self-givingly
I place at Your Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 September 1999, 7:06 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 600 lbs.)

17 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May I sleeplessly and breathlessly cry
For the ever-transcending Beyond.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 September 1999, 7:19 a.m. Before lifting 275 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 550 lbs.)

17 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my constant self-giving
Be an inspiration
To the citizens of this world
And a joy
To the denizens of the higher worlds.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 September 1999, 7:53 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 70 lbs.

17 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The Tears
Of Your Compassion-Eye
And the Smiles
Of Your Forgiveness-Heart
Are the answers
To my life's questions.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 September 1999, 9:40 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

18 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My God-devotion
Is the beauty of my heart's tears.
My God-surrender
Is the divinity of my soul's smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 September 1999, 8:33 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 96 lbs. (18 reps)

18 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To place myself at Your Forgiveness-Feet
Once again
Is the crying, tearing
And burning hunger
Of my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 September 1999, 9:15 a.m. Before lifting 275 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 550 lbs.)

18 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O my stupid mind,
My stupid vital, my stupid body,
Come out!
Come out of your spiritual coma.
Our Lord Beloved Supreme
Has so many things
For you to do for Him
Here on earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 September 1999, 9:20 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred times. (50 right, 50 left)
