19130

Only the negative minds
Are invited to work
In life's unhappiness-factory.

19131

Can you not see
That your Inner Pilot
At every moment
Is steering your life-boat
Towards His own Delight-Harbour?

19132

You must never allow
Your doubting mind
To strangle
Your blossoming hope-heart.

19133

O my heart,
Be a sleeplessly soaring bird
So that no frustration
Can ever dare to cripple you.

19134

You can shorten your spirituality-road
And quicken your speed
If you are under the guidance
Of a true, able and spiritually rich Master.

19135

A sleeplessly desiring life
Must without fail
Include a slow but certain
Spiritual death.

19136

His aspiring heart
Is always homesick
For Eternity's Peace,
Infinity's Light
And Immortality's Delight.

19137

At last my fractured trust
In mankind
Has been completely cured
By God's Compassion-flooded Eye.

19138

You do not have to surprise the world.
You should only try to surprise yourself,
Especially your mind,
By becoming a chosen instrument of God.

19139

If you are always concerned
With yourself,
Then your heart-door
You will never be able to keep open
For God, the Supreme Guest, to enter.

19140

Abandon not your heart's helpless cry,
For your heart's helpless cry
Is the magnet that pulls God
Towards your life-breath.

19141

He who treasures you
Is your only true friend,
And that Friend is none other than
God Himself.

19142

I call it faith.
God calls it
His own evolving Experience
In and through my life.

19143

My unconditional surrender to God
Is the only strength I need
To make myself truly happy
And fulfilled.

19144

O my depression-mind,
Can you not see
That every day God is planting
His own Smile-Seeds inside you?
