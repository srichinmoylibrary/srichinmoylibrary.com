6615

If you are a true God-lover,
Then when do you have
The time to rest?
And if you need rest,
Where else must you rest
If not in the Heart
Of your Beloved Supreme?

6616

To feel the throb of your heart
In all hearts
During your meditation,
Every day you must destroy
Your uncomely thoughts.

6617

My Lord Supreme
Does not allow me to sing
Wherever I want to.
He only allows me to sing
In the silence of my soul-room.

6618

In the small hours of the morning
Every day
My Lord Supreme comes to inspect
My heart's burning aspiration.

6619

I want to possess God:
This is my old discovery.
I want God to possess me:
This is my new invention.

6620

When I see God,
The first thing I will do
Is place Him on the throne
Of my silence-heart.

6621

Each divine thought
Comes into existence
From the boundless ocean
Of love divine.

6622

Is there any human being
Who has not lived
In the wasteland of wrong beliefs,
Even for a few fleeting days?

6623

Each good thought
Is a piece of Heaven
That a God-lover carries
Wherever he goes.

6624

Your parrot-prayers
Will never be able to lead you
To God's Garden
Of celestial blossoms.

6625

It is good
To run a hopeful race.
But it is by far the best
To run a soulful race.

6626

How can you succeed?
Can you not see
That your mind is lost
In yesterday's failures
And that you are losing your heart
In tomorrow's doubts?

6627

With your second-rate aspiration
You can do only one thing:
You can disappoint God
Far beyond your imagination.

6628

You will always have
A better view of God
From the soulfulness-window
Of your heart-room.

6629

I always take very special care
Of my aspiration-compass,
For it always points me
Toward the Heart
Of my Beloved Supreme.
