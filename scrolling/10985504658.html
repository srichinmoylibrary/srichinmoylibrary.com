26 When I Suffer From Loneliness ...

When I suffer from loneliness,
The peace-moon and bliss-sun
Come down and comfort me.

27 Peace-Talkers ...

Peace-talkers
As usual
Are everywhere.

28 Peace-Servers ...

Peace-servers
As usual
Are few and far between.

29 Peace-Givers ...

Peace-givers
Are nowhere to be found.

30 We Are So Fortunate ...

We are so fortunate
That our hearts do not believe
In limitation-boundaries.

31 My Prayers ...

My prayers
Are my
God-satisfaction-revelations.

32 My Meditations ...

My meditations
Are my
God-fulfilment-manifestations.

33 My Devotion-Heart-Flames ...

My devotion-heart-flames
Sing only
God-ecstasy-songs.

25 January 2007 ...

25 January 2007

Cha-Am, Thailand

34 God’s Only Representative ...

God’s only representative
On earth
Is our implicit faith.

35 My Very First Goal ...

My very first goal
Is God’s Feet.

36 My Second Goal ...

My second goal
Is God’s Heart.

37 My Ultimate Goal ...

My ultimate goal
Is my God-oneness
Throughout Eternity.

38 As Problems Come Back Again ...

As problems come back again,
Even so, God’s Compassion
Comes back more powerfully,
Again and again.

39 I Challenge My Mind ...

I challenge my mind.
My mind ultimately surrenders.
