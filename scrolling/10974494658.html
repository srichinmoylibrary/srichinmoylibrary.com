28 A Delayer ...

A delayer
Will never be chosen by God
To be a player
In His Cosmic Game.

29 The Sweet Whispers ...

The sweet whispers
Of my soul
Give my heart
Tremendous joy.

30 My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Please tell me
If there is a place
In Your entire Creation
Where I can live in peace.

31 Each Doubt-Invasion ...

Each doubt-invasion
Must be challenged and conquered
By our most powerful faith
Deep within.

32 The Unaspiring Human Beings ...

The unaspiring human beings
Sooner or later will drown
In the vast sea
Of ignorance-night.

33 I Belong Solely To God ...

I belong solely to God
In the inner world of silence
And
In the outer world of sound.

34 The Seeker’s Aspiration-Life ...

The seeker’s aspiration-life
Ends
When he allows the mind
To push him
And the vital
To pull him.

7 January 2006 ...

7 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

35 Every Morning ...

Every morning
My heart becomes
A rainbow-dream-lover.

36 A Desire-Life ...

A desire-life
Is nothing short of
A weakness-mind.

37 Happiness-Stars ...

Happiness-stars
Are found only
In the heart-sky.

38 The Mind Divides ...

The mind divides.
The heart brings
Everything together.

39 My God-Obedience ...

My God-obedience
Is a resounding success
And progress
In my spiritual life.

40 Every Day ...

Every day
There will be new challenges,
And again,
There will be the brightest sun
From deep within
To help us brave the challenges.

41 The Seeker Does Everything ...

The seeker does everything
To build a spiritual shrine.
The Master gives
The finishing touches.
