6945

From time immemorial
There have been two partners:
Man's willingness
And
God's Fulness.

6946

No matter how bad you are
In God's inner School,
He will never allow you
To become a drop-out,
For He considers your failure
As His own failure.

6947

You have a mind
Rich in simplicity.
You have a heart
Rich in sincerity.
You have a life
Rich in purity.
Therefore
God tells the world
That you are rich in divinity.

6948

At the end of the desire-night
Of each human mind,
There shall definitely dawn
An aspiration-day of the heart.

6949

You are absolutely doing
The right thing
By asking your desire-life
To be extremely proud
Of your aspiration-life.

6950

His is the divine heart
That every day goes smilingly
Around and around
His soul's illumination-circle.

6951

For the mind to be confused
Is not an unusual thing.
For the heart to be illumined
Is not an unusual thing.
For the soul to be perfect
Is not an unusual thing.

6952

Something is missing
In his mind:
Faith.

Something is missing
In his heart:
Love.

Something is missing
In his life:
Satisfaction.

6953

Since you have not given
Anything worthwhile
To your Beloved Supreme,
Try at least to give Him
The remaining years
Of your life.

6954

The divine Will
Is a special Messenger of God
That comes directly from Heaven
To fight for human beings
Against ignorance-titan.

6955

You can climb fast, very fast,
The progress-tree
If you give what you have:
Inspiration-drops
And aspiration-flames.

6956

The suspicion-mind
Has to suffer
From an agonising inner marathon
In the world of aspiration.

6957

You want to live
In a world of temptation.
Again,
It is you who want to see
The face of satisfaction.
I tell you,
They never go together!

6958

If you feel that you have gone
Too high to fall,
Then you are
Completely mistaken.

6959

A sincerity-heart
Has the capacity to start
A peaceful race
In its heavenward journey.
