26130

Self-giving and self-transcendence
Are inseparable friends.
They always meet together.

26131

If the modern world had more simplicity,
Then the modern world
Would have infinitely more joy
Than it has now.

26132

The worst problem in our life
Is that the mind does not want
To doubt its own doubt.

26133

If you are afraid
Of your failure-life,
Then progress will never knock
At your heart-door.

26134

What is human life?
It is the heavy and strong bondage
Of unnecessary extravagance.

26135

The mind cannot, but the heart can
Distinguish the outer wants
From the inner needs.

26136

Do not waste any more time!
Just ask yourself in all earnest
What you actually need
From God.

26137

If there is sincerity in your prayer,
Then God's Answer
Will definitely be audible.
If there is no sincerity,
Then God
Will definitely turn a deaf ear to you.

26138

Does God give us another chance?
He does, but alas,
We misuse the chance —
Not only twice and thrice,
But time and again.

26139

Every day God comes to me
With new answers
To all my old questions.

26140

A life of constant surrender
Can constantly breathe in
God's own Breath.

26141

Alas, we are prone
To be receptive to absurdities
And not to reality.

26142

Man tells God:
"God, You do not know
How much I suffer from passion."

God tells man:
"My child, you do not know
How much I suffer from you
When you do not believe
In My Compassion."

26143

I tell you, my seeker-friend,
That there shall come a time
When you will laugh and laugh
At your past achievement-peaks.

26144

My utter stupidity
Is catching up with me.
I thought that I could live easily
Without God.
