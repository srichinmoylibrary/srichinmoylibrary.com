21 April 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your compassionate Heart
   Responds immediately
Only to those who need You
   Only, only, only.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 April 1999, 7:34 a.m. Before lifting 230 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

21 April 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
How often I do things
And tell the world
That I am doing everything
   For You,
When actually I am doing everything
For my own fulfilment!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 April 1999, 7:45 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.) Lifts number thirty-four and thirty-five.

22 April 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You have opened my heart-eye
So I can see and feel
How much You love me,
Care for me and need me —
And not so I can see
How many weaknesses I have
Or how insignificant I am.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 April 1999, 6:28 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

22 April 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   Today do bless my mind
With a new inspiration-joy,
   Do bless my heart
With a new aspiration-joy
   And do bless my life
With a new dedication-joy.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 April 1999, 6:37 a.m. Before lifting 240 lbs. fifty-five times with one arm. (26 right, 27 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

22 April 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Mine are the self-giving dreams
To manifest You in Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 April 1999, 6:51 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.) Lifts number thirty-six and thirty-seven.

25 April 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
No devotion,
   No destination.
No destination,
   No God-realisation.
No God-realisation,
   No God-Satisfaction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 April 1999, 8:24 p.m. Before using seated double-arm machine — up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

25 April 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
That a seeker does not need
Strict spiritual disciplines.
He does not need austerities.
He does not need the highest
   And deepest meditation.
What he needs to realise You
Is only pure, pure, pure devotion.
You are also telling me
That all are equal in Your Sight,
But those who have sincere
   Devotion to You
Have a very, very special place
   In Your Heart,
And their hearts
   Are Your choicest homes.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 April 1999, 8:34 p.m. Before using standing double-arm machine — up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 200 lbs. with each arm separately.

25 April 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Command is
My golden opportunity
To become all Yours
In the twinkling of an eye!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 April 1999, 8:46 p.m. Before lifting 240 lbs. fifty-five times with one arm. (27 right, 26 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

25 April 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O my stupidity-flooded mind,
Do not try to understand God.
You will never, never, never succeed.
Just try to love God.
Lo, you have already succeeded.
   You have become
A truly choice instrument of God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 April 1999, 8:59 p.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.) Lift number thirty-eight.

27 April 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart's soulful smile
Is my God-satisfying
   Sacred secret.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 7:21 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

27 April 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart's streaming tears
   Shorten
My God-Destination-distance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 7:31 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 180 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

27 April 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I need Your blessingful,
   Infinite Grace
To change my inner life
   Completely
And my outer life
   Immediately.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 7:45 a.m. Before lifting 220 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 440 lbs.) New record.

27 April 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Forgiveness-Heart
And Your Compassion-Eye
Do not allow You to remember
How long You have been waiting for me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 8:40 p.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

27 April 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I breathe in,
Do allow me to breathe in
   Gratitude-tears.
When I breathe out,
Do allow me to breathe out
   Surrender-smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 8:49 p.m. Before using standing double-arm machine — up to 180 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 210 lbs. with each arm separately.

27 April 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My prayerful and soulful determination
Has a special name given by You:
You call my determination
   Your Heart-hero.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 9:01 p.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)
