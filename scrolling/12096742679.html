23815

Our heart's gratitude-ecstasy
Shortens the road
To our God-realisation.

23816

Two of my long-lost companions
Have now come back:
My inner poise
And my outer courage.

23817

When you use your doubting mind
In your spiritual life,
You just place a dead and withered flower
On your heart-shrine.

23818

God has definitely come down
From His blue Infinity.
But where is my heart's
Receptivity's intensity?

23819

To brush aside wrong thoughts,
What we need
Is a mind flooded
With divine obedience.

23820

When our life becomes
A fully blossomed gratitude-heart,
God synchronizes His Infinity's Breath
With our finite breath.

23821

What we need is a heart
That will always remain untouched
By fear and doubt-shadows
To arrive at our destination:
The Golden Shore of our Lord's
Satisfaction-Smile.

23822

Meditate, meditate, meditate
Soulfully, sleeplessly and self-givingly,
For meditation is nothing other than
Our supreme ignorance-shattering vision.

23823

A man-made foundation
May eventually collapse.
But a God-blessed foundation
Will daily prosper
And fulfil humanity's dire need.

23824

If you need the Breath
Of God's living Presence,
Then pray and meditate.
While praying,
Cry like a child.
While meditating,
Smile like a child.

23825

Silence is always the best teacher.
It can take us safely
Deep into every heart.

23826

A single Whisper
From my Lord Supreme
Can easily keep my ego's ceiling
Low, extremely low.

23827

O my mind,
When will you be hungry
For an iota of illumination?
When will you be thirsty
For an iota of perfection?

23828

Either we must imprison our desires
And keep them in prison forever,
Or we must transform
Our earth-binding desires
Into Heaven-free aspiration.

23829

Every morning,
Smilingly and self-givingly,
My soul helps me dive
Into my heart's vision-sea.
