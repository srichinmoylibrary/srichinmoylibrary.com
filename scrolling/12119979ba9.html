26145

O my heart,
Do not walk anymore
Under the clouds
Of your darkness-mind.
Allow your inner sun to burst forth!

26146

The human life
Is a life that enjoys
Deliberate falsehood
And bold doubts.

26147

God wants us
To spread our dreams
All-where
And not to hide them
From the rest of the world.

26148

My Lord, tell me,
Will You ever allow Your Forgiveness
To give up on me?
"My child, never!"

26149

My Lord, will You ever apply
Your Indifference-Justice
And give up on my life?
"My child, never!"

26150

My Lord,
When will You allow Yourself
To give up on me?

"My child,
The day you give up on yourself
And prevent Me from interfering
In your life!"

26151

God has many more
Important things to do
Than to console
Your constantly brooding
Heaviness-mind.

26152

Every day you must honour
Your own heart-cry
To make the fastest progress.

26153

If you do not dismiss
Your mind's absolute decisions,
Then you will never succeed.

26154

Do not forget that each heart
Is made of God's own
Sweetness-Heart.

26155

How is it that you are not aware
Of the thoughts that are hiding
Inside your mind-prison?

26156

Alas, I am fully responsible
For the failure
Of my soul's promises to God.

26157

Do one thing, my seeker-friend.
Every day make it a point
To spare poor God
From your countless complaints.

26158

All the mind's confusion-thoughts
Are always
Ill-founded.

26159

Only our tearful hearts
Can hear
God's Sweetness-Whispers.
