55 The Red Light ...

The red light
Fights against ignorance-night
To establish
God’s Victory on earth.

56 The White Light ...

The white light
Illumines
Ignorance-night.

57 The Blue Light ...

The blue light
Devours
Ignorance-night.

58 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Watches me.

59 God’s Justice-Mind ...

God’s Justice-Mind
Catches me.

60 God’s Forgiveness-Heart ...

God’s Forgiveness-Heart
Liberates me.

7 February 2005 ...

7 February 2005

Nanjing, China

61 Alas, God-Awareness ...

Alas, God-awareness
Is still individual
And not universal.

62 The Sadness ...

The sadness
Of Mother Earth
Has an undying breath.

63 There Is No Dearth Of ...

There is no dearth of
False smiles —
False smiles are all-where.

64 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I am too weak
To be crushed
By Your Indifference-Feet.

65 My Heart-Sun ...

My heart-sun
Knows no eclipse.

66 Alas, I Have No Idea ...

Alas, I have no idea
When I became fond
Of my expectation-mind.

67 Our God-Surrender ...

Our God-surrender
Has the longest
Progress-strides.

68 We Suffer So Much Daily ...

We suffer so much daily
From our uncertainty-mind,
Yet we allow it to linger.
