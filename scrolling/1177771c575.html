4890 You Are Loved

You are loved
By both God and the prince of gloom.
God loves you because
You have a fearless heart.
The prince of gloom loves you because
You have a cheerless mind.

4891 How Long Will They Last?

Your desire-waves -
How long will they last,
How long?
Nobody knows.

Your aspiration-sea -
How long will it last,
How long?
Both Heaven and earth know
And even I can know it,
If I care to.

4892 Show Sympathy

If you feel that your mind
Is not past correction,
Then you can show sympathy
To your dubitation-mind
So that it will come
To your illumination-soul.

4893 Natural And Supernatural

To give what you proudly have
Is natural.

To give what you soulfully are
Is supernatural.

4894 Your Mind Insisted

Your mind insisted
On creating a volcano-confusion
To frighten the world.
And now what is happening?
The volcano is destroying
Its stupid creator.

4895 You May Not Be Wrong

You may not be wrong
When people badly criticise you.
Again, you may not be right
When people highly admire you.

4896 Your Answer Must Be Perfect

God comes to you every day
And He will continue doing so
Forever and forever.

But have you ever gone to see God,
Or will you ever go to see God?

l want you to answer this question.
You can take as long as you want to,
But your answer must be perfect.

4897 God's Compassion-Eye

God's Compassion-Eye
Chides and corrects you
Precisely because it wants to
Inspire your inner life
And
Perfect your outer life.

4898 If You Do Not Aspire

If you do not aspire,
That means either consciously or unconsciously
You enjoy your presence
Inside the living death.

If you aspire,
That means you want to enjoy
And you are enjoying your presence
Inside the deathless life.

4899 Go To God

Go to God
With a heart of sincerity.
He will tell you
What to do
To change your life.

Go to God
With a life of gratitude.
He will not only tell you
What to do
But also do it for you.

4900 If You Love God

If you do not love God
And do not want to love God,
Yet want to do something for God,
Then your unsolicited help
Is nothing but presumptuous.

If you love God
And want to love God,
And therefore want to serve God,
Then your soulful service
Is more precious to God
Than you can ever imagine.
