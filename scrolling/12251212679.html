Sabai Amare Chhariya Jaibe Sabai Amare

January 11th, 1994

Sabai amare chhariya jaibe
Sabai amare chhariya
Nahi jena thaki nimesher
Tare kadapi tader smariya

Jibaner Bhore Dekhechi Tomare Jibaner

January 11th, 1994

Jibaner bhore dekhechi tomare
Jibaner sanjhe dakibo
Mananer bhore maraner sanjhe
Toma paye more rakhibo

Esechi Phiriya Taba Pada Tale

January 12th, 1994

Esechi phiriya taba pada tale
Chiratare rakho more kripa bale

Translation:

My Lord Supreme, once more
I have come back to Your Feet.
Please, this time,
On the strength of Your Compassion,
Do keep me forever and forever.

Tomar Adesh Na Shunile

January 12th, 1994

Tomar adesh na shunile
Tomar adesh
Bhitar bahir bedan ashesh
Kandan ashesh

Translation:

If I do not obey You,
My Lord Supreme,
Then endless is my suffering,
Endless are my heart’s pangs.

Tomar Ashish Tomar Sneha

January 12th, 1994

Tomar ashish tomar sneha
Rekhe amar mathe
Chalbo ami gaibo ami
Tomar sathe sathe

Translation:

My Lord, Your Blessings
And Your Affection,
Placing on my head,
I shall walk with You,
I shall sing with You.

Hiya Pure Jete Habe

January 12th, 1994

Hiya pure jete habe hiya pure jete
Pete tomar khama kripa pete hasi pete

Translation:

I must enter into my heart-city
To receive Your Forgiveness,
Your Compassion and
Your Satisfaction-Smile.

Paran Bhariya Sabare Dakite Sabare

January 12th, 1994

Paran bhariya sabare dakite sabare basite bhalo
Manas hayite niye jao mor agyan ghor kalo

Phuler Sathe Phutbo Ami

January 12th, 1994

Phuler sathe phutbo ami tomar charan tale
Tomar hasi dekhte pabo atmahutir bale

Translation:

With flowers, I shall blossom at Your Feet.
On the strength of my self-giving,
I shall receive Your Satisfaction-Smile.

Hiya Pure Jete Habe Amar

January 12th, 1994

Hiya pure jete habe amar hiya pure
Gaite tomar bijoy giti atmatyager sure

Translation:

I must enter into my heart
To sing Your Victory-Song
With my self-giving life.

Nahi Jani Ami Chahi Kahare Nahi Jena

January 13th, 1994

Nahi jani ami chahi kahare
Nahi jena bandhi ami tahare

Amar Hriday Khulite Chahina Sabar Hriday

January 13th, 1994

Amar hriday khulite chahina
Sabar hriday khulite chahi
Muktir giti gahite chahina
Bandhan giti gahi

Tomar Katha Balte Giye Kebal Bali Amar

January 13th, 1994

Tomar katha balte giye kebal bali amar katha
Tomar ankhi dekhe shudhu amar praner chapalata

Sanpi Chutte Chahi Ami Druta Gati Dao

January 13th, 1994

Sanpi chutte chahi ami druta gati
Dao amare param prabhu shubha mati

Shanti Deshe Nahi Raje Bhranti Raje

January 13th, 1994

Shanti deshe nahi raje bhranti
Raje kebal param prabhur kanti

January 13th, 1994

Maner bane heri shudhu kantar akraman
