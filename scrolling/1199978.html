14201

When I live in my heart,
God tells me
That it is my first and foremost chance
To love and be loved by Him.
When I live in my mind,
God tells me
That it is my very last chance
To love Him.

14202

Do not waste your time
Looking for a truth-home
Inside your mind.
Your heart-home is surcharged
With peace.
Remain therein always!

14203

Each spiritual mistake
Is followed
By destruction-demand
From the world of ignorance.

14204

Everyone has missed the God-alarm,
Including my heart.
My soul is the only one
That has not missed
The God-alarm
And I am sure that
My soul will never miss it.

14205

Heaven is not my permanent home.
Earth is not my permanent home.
My permanent home is
My Lord's Compassion-Feet.

14206

As there is no
Heart-aspiration-contest,
Even so there is no
And there can be no
God-realisation-contest.

14207

God's Greatness is loved
By the possession-loving mind.
God's Goodness is treasured
By the liberation-loving heart.

14208

When a seeker is suffering
From Compassion-shortage,
His life will eventually become
A sorrowful, fruitless failure-cry.

14209

If you allow yourself to be attacked
By a swarm of uncomely thoughts,
God will not grant you
His Heart-Guest-Room.

14210

Every day God helps us
Lessen our mind's
Untold destruction-miseries.

14211

The human mind secretly loves
To read forbidden
Insecurity-impurity-insincerity-stories.

14212

Who knows where God is?
Who cares for God after all?
In the future these two questions
Will not be asked
By earth's aspiration-cry.

14213

Alas,
You do not have the time
To see your heart's sunrise,
But you have all the time
To see others' sunset.

14214

We do not know
Where we are heading,
But we do know
That God's Compassion-Forgiveness
Will follow us.
