6730

His sleepless faith
In his Beloved Supreme
Defies all fear
From both the inner and the outer world.

6731

If you have a purity-heart,
Then you will know
Where your secret and sacred
Heart-garden is.

6732

God has pierced your ego-mind
So that He can give
More love and joy
To your surrender-heart.

6733

Ask no more questions!
Even God
Does not want anything to do with
Your tireless, questioning mind!

6734

Do not allow your vital
To be caught in the impurity-jungle
If you want your entire being
To live in your heart's purity-garden.

6735

When are you going to recognise
That the fire of your desire-life
Has burned all your beauty,
Inner and outer?

6736

A truth-seeker and God-lover
Knows
That the Eternal Now
Is always the best time.

6737

My Beloved Lord Supreme,
Where are You going?
"I am disappearing
Because you do not want Me.
But I shall come back
For the asking."

6738

Today
Make your life hopeful.
Then tomorrow
Your life will automatically
Become fruitful.

6739

Do you think that God
Will make an exception for you
In everything you do and say
Just because you feel
That God is very close to you?

6740

If man's promise is not reliable,
Then try God's Compassion.
I am sure God's Compassion
Will always be reliable.

6741

Do not worry about
What grade God will give you.
Just continue studying
At God's School.

6742

Right from the very beginning
His desire-life has not been able
To come out of
Life's bottomless abyss.

6743

Do not examine
The possibilities in life.
Just accept them,
Better them
And, finally,
Satisfy the divine in you.

6744

How can the world forget you
Since you have shown the world
How to touch God's Feet
And how to clasp God's Eye?
