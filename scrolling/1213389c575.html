90.

Just because you cannot recognise
Your Inner Pilot,
Never, even for a moment,
Think that the Inner Pilot
Does not exist.

91.

In my aspiration-life
I have many, many books,
But I love my heart's
Devotion-dictionary most.

92.

Only those who are fluent
In the language of love, devotion
And surrender
Are the supremely chosen
Instruments of God.

93.

Each time I pray soulfully
And meditate self-givingly,
I see myself smiling
In my soul's infinite delight-sky.

94.

Each time you allow a desire
To increase,
You are entangling yourself
More and more
In a strong, fruitless attachment-net.

95.

My mind needs many things,
But my heart needs only one thing:
A glimpse of God.

96.

Nobody forces us.
It is we who allow ourselves
To be assailed
By voracious ignorance-hunger.

97.

Cry like a helpless child.
God Himself will give you the capacity
To leave desire-forest
And enter into aspiration-garden.

98.

There is only one reason
Why the world is not spiritually progressive:
The world wants to cherish material successes
More than anything else.

99.

May I have a new name,
My Lord?
I want my name to be:
My soul's infinite and eternal
Silence-peace.

100.

What does indifference do?
Indifference takes us
To the mind's abysmal abyss.
What does detachment do?
Detachment takes us
To our soul's infinite light and delight.
