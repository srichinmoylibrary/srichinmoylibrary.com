20375

Attack ignorance!
Defeat ignorance!
These are the only things
Your soul wants you to accomplish.

20376

You will be allowed to enter
Into the heart-temple
Only when you have
Radically changed your mind.

20377

My soul wants me to accept
And not reject
God's earth-manifestation-Victory.

20378

Since God Himself
Is knocking at your heart-door,
Do let Him come in.
He only wants to make you
Truly, divinely and fruitfully happy.

20379

Do not dismiss
Your simplicity-life,
Purity-mind and divinity-heart
From your life
If you really want to become
The choicest instrument of God.

20380

If you want to please God
In His own Way,
Then you must allow
Simplicity to visit your mind,
Purity to visit your heart,
And surrender to visit your life.

20381

God has hidden
My aspiration-failure-breath
In secrecy supreme.
Alas! How can I ever be worthy
Of His Compassion-Sea-Eye?

20382

My heart's hope
Keeps me alive.
My soul's promise
Keeps me dynamic.

20383

Your mind's negativity
And your heart's creativity
Will always remain perfect strangers
To each other.

20384

Self-praise
Eventually becomes
Helplessly and hopelessly
Joyless.

20385

Be always
Prayerfully and soulfully receptive
To God's sacred Whispers.

20386

Unlike us,
God never finds anything tedious,
Even in our desire-bound life.

20387

When our pride makes us feel
That we are the doers,
And we do not need any help
From the inner world
Or the outer world,
That pride will be forced
To crumble into dust.

20388

If God wishes me to come last
In the Heavenward race,
Then cheerfully I am saying,
"So be it."

20389

If we unconditionally surrender
Our life to God's Will,
Then our destination itself
Will run towards us.
