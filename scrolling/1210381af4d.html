24575

There is a constant battle going on
Between my self-doubt and my God-faith,
But I am happy because I know
My God-faith is bound to win.

24576

When I think of my mind,
I immediately come down
To humanity's pitiful standard.
When I think of my heart,
I immediately climb up
To Divinity's blessingful, powerful
And fruitful standard.

24577

The unaspiring mind
Enjoys flying
Beneath the clouds of darkness-night.

24578

When I enjoy
My sense of superiority,
I completely destroy
God's faith in me.

24579

Every day we must spread
Our God-dreams
The way we sow
Our earth-aspiration-seeds.

24580

My soul and my heart
Are opening new silence-horizons
To manifest God
In an unprecedented way.

24581

Nothing can be as sweet
As the fragrance
Of an awakened gratitude-heart.

24582

God does not mind
If I sleep for Eternity.
But my soul does mind,
Because my soul wants
My Lord Supreme
To be proud of me.

24583

My Lord,
Although now I am totally lost,
I do feel that one day
I shall see my way
With Your Vision-Eye.

24584

A real God-lover
Will be helped by God Himself
To come out of the whirlpool
Of frustration and irritation.

24585

Spiritually we are dying
Because we still cherish
The ashes of our past life.

24586

A spiritual Master is he
Who self-givingly tries to lead
Each and every human being
Towards God's own Perfection-Dream.

24587

My genuine surrender
To my Lord Supreme
Has enabled me to travel swiftly
Along my life's freedom-road.

24588

If you want to regain your faith
In yourself and in God,
Then come out of your mind-jungle
And run into your heart-garden.

24589

My Lord,
I pray to You to grant me
Every day
The fragrance of a newly awakened
Gratitude-heart.
