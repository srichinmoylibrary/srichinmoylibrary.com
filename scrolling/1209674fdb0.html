23860

Unconditional surrender
Will always remain
The champion of champions
In the transformation-contest.

23861

His mind's whirlpool of desire
Compelled his aspiration-boat
To sink.

23862

Unless and until
We have a genuine cry in our heart
For the illumination of our mind,
We will never be able to transport ourselves
From this world of chaos.

23863

A cheerful surrender
Is the only way
To see our imperfection
On its death-bed.

23864

Man's gratitude-heart
And God's Compassion-Eye
Prepare the way for man to realise
The Transcendental Truth.

23865

O my mind,
Apart from your curiosity,
Something radically different you need
To have even an iota of peace.

23866

Once we surrender permanently
To God's Will,
It is only a matter of time
Before we can enter into and conquer
The domain of falsehood.

23867

Every day
My heart-bird soars into blue Infinity
To sing the song
Of God's supreme Glory.

23868

If our love
Is only for God,
If our devotion
Is only for God,
If our surrender
Is only for God,
Then each breath of ours
Is a miracle-fulfilled life.

23869

Our heart's gratitude
Produces a sleepless energy
Which helps us bring about success
In each and every aspect of our life.

23870

When the pulling of the mind
And the pushing of the vital cease,
We become
The crying of the heart.

23871

The mind's insecurity
And the vital's jealousy
Are two impossible fools
Living within us.

23872

If the reasoning mind
Is extremely active,
Then divinity's wisdom
Will remain far away.

23873

The happiest time in God's Life
Is when I tell God
That my heart is unconditionally
Inside His Heart
And my life is unconditionally
At His Feet.

23874

There is only one most efficient
Trouble-shooter
For all the problems of human life,
And that trouble-shooter is
God's birthless and deathless
Compassion-Eye.
