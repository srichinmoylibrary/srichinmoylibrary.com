21760

God is proud of me
Because even in my dreams
I serve Him not only powerfully
But also peacefully.

21761

My happy heart tells me
That what I have
Is the entire universe.
My peaceful mind tells me
Who I am:
Another God.

21762

Keep your God-appointments.
Peace-accomplishments
Will be yours.

21763

Life is too short.
Therefore, I have decided
To be better and not to remain bitter.
And I have also decided
To see inside my heart
A wisdom-sun and a sea of peace.

21764

We need a truth-volcano
To destroy
Our impurity-mind-land.

21765

Because you do not believe
That the soul has come into the world
To speak on God's behalf,
You will never succeed
In your spiritual life.

21766

What are the credentials
Of a genuine seeker?
The credentials of a sincere seeker
Are a purity-heart
And a sacrifice-life.

21767

May my Lord's Compassion-Eye
Eternally be
My only heart-home.

21768

He who cannot be satisfied
With the philosophy
Of 'live and let live'
Will always find his life intolerable.

21769

My Lord,
I desperately need
Some assurance from You
That in this lifetime
I will be able to become
Your unconditionally surrendered child.

21770

You can lighten
God's burden
Only by enlightening
Your own mind.

21771

Can you not see
That your aspiration-heart-sun
Is completely eclipsed
By your suspicion-mind-night?

21772

Silence
Your disobedience-mind!
God's Happiness
Will be all yours.

21773

O suspicion-mind,
Is there anything
Or can there be anything
As ugly as you are?

21774

O oneness-heart-smile,
Is there anything
Or can there be anything
As beautiful as you are?
