9145

The only wish of my life
  Is to discover
My cheerful and unconditional surrender
  To God's Justice-Light.

9146

  On principle
Mortals do not like to learn
Their hearts' aspiration-songs.

9147

You must realise that every day
  You enjoy
Your self-created crucifixion.
Why do you then blame God
  For not granting you
His Heart's Ecstasy-Ocean?

9148

If you want to make yourself happy,
If you want to make God happy,
Then self-giving has to be
The motto of your life.

9149

I do not mind
If all my other promises
  Remain unfulfilled,
But my God-realisation-promise
In this lifetime
  I must fulfil.

9150

Give what you have
And what you are.
Then every day God will observe
Your supreme victory-anniversary.

9151

Time hangs heavy on your life
Because you do not want God
To use your time
On your behalf.

9152

God at times may shut His Ear,
But He never shuts His Eye,
And never, never, never
Does He shut His Heart!

9153

Develop your inner confidence-power.
You will be able to become
  A perfect builder
Of God's Manifestation-Temple on earth.

9154

Unless you are a rank fool,
  You will always
Take the side of your soul.

9155

If you can be soulfully happy
  And remain happy,
God considers it
Your real meditation.

9156

Once you have established
  A solid inner link
With your spiritual Master,
Yesterday's Master, today's Master
  And tomorrow's Master
Will all become one in your life.

9157

From his desire-life
He has inherited nothing
But an endless series of failures.

9158

If you have to go slowly
In your aspiration-life,
  Go slowly.
But I don't want to see
  Your aspiration-life
On the brink of extinction.

9159

  My Lord Supreme,
At the end of my life
Will I remember
That when newness knocked
  At my door
I chose to remain closeted
Inside my old, tiny self?
