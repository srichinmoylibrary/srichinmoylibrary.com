16115

Each human being may be today
An unfinished portrait
Of the Absolute Supreme.
But there shall come a time
When each human being will be
A supremely perfect portrait
Of his Beloved Supreme.

16116

There is no harm
In dreaming of,
There is no harm
In looking for
A God-sanctified day.

16117

The spark that will awaken
Man's ignorance-mind
Is already there
In his own aspiration-heart.

16118

Nobody remains forever
An unheralded messenger-light of God,
For God wants His messenger
To be known
At His choice Hour.

16119

How can we desert
Ignorance-army
Unless we take shelter
At God's omnipotent Feet?

16120

Aspiration awakens us.
Dedication energises us.
Realisation fulfils us.

16121

O my mind,
Is it not too late
For you to say a final good-bye
To your desire-friend?
But do it anyway!
You must do it.
Better late than never!

16122

A constant swing
Between desire-life and aspiration-life:
This is what human life is.
The constant increase
Of aspiration-life:
This is what the divine life will be.

16123

Each aspiration-effort
Either today or tomorrow
Will bear fruit,
For our aspiration comes directly
From God's Compassion-Eye.

16124

The world criticises you,
My seeker-friend.
I see your heart is full
Of the world's criticism-scars.
But God's Compassion-Eye
Will liberate you
From the world's wild criticism.
Just work for God,
And God alone will please you
And fulfil you.

16125

My Lord Supreme,
Am I destined to see
Only Your Tear-flooded Eyes
And not Your Smile-flooded Face?

16126

Without determination-fire
There can be no
Progress-satisfaction-smile.

16127

O my mind,
When will you be tired
Of your ego-life's
Self-styled arrogance-dance?

16128

If any morning
I forget to feed
My heart of devotion-light,
Then the entire day
Misery ruthlessly haunts me.

16129

Within
I see nothing
But my Lord's
Bliss-Compassion-Eye.
Without
I see nothing
But ignorance and its army.
