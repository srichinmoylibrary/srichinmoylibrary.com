601.

My soul is advising me
To astound my Lord Supreme
With my heart's aspiration-leaps.

602.

What I need
Is not a doubt-destroying light,
But a doubt-illumining
And faith-fulfilling light.

603.

How can a reasoning mind
Ever be happy?
How can an aspiring heart
Ever be unhappy?

604.

If you do not wake up
At God's choice Hour,
Then you will be
The inevitable loser.

605.

Although we are mortal,
God has given us
Ample capacity and opportunity
To sing immortal songs
To please Him.

606.

The life's simplicity,
The mind's sincerity
And the heart's purity
Are fellow pilgrims upon the path
That leads to God's Kingdom.

607.

God descends
With His Compassion-Feet
To rescue each aspiring human soul
From the chains of ignorance-night.

608.

My heart sings
With the rising sun.
My heart dances
With the rising sun.
My heart pleases God
With the rising sun.
My heart fulfils God
With the rising sun.

609.

This world of ours
Can truly progress
Only on a tidal wave
Of self-giving lives.

610.

Aspiration always has
A cheerful, full-time job:
To bring us to the goal
At a very fast speed.

611.

The delight of the soul
Takes birth on earth
To manifest God's Light
And to challenge death's supremacy.

612.

Alas, I sometimes forget
That my heart has wings
To fly to God.

613.

My heart always writes
God-letters
On its beautiful and soulful
Surrender-gratitude-stationery.

614.

The pride of disobedience
Is indeed the worst possible
Inner calamity.
