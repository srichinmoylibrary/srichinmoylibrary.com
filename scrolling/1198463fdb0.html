12760

If you present
Your unwillingness to God,
Then God will remain indifferent
To your mind's confusion-forest.

12761

Every day
You must muster courage
To make a firm commitment
To your obedience-life.

12762

You say that
You have nothing important to do.
But I am telling you,
Try at least to break
Your life's unworthiness-shackles.

12763

Slowly, steadily and unerringly
My inner faith has overthrown
My outer doubt-dictator.

12764

Alas,
I cannot even remember
The last time God smiled at me.

12765

Destiny's decisive hour
Has arrived.
Man's inner freedom
Has fully and fruitfully
Come to the fore.

12766

O my heart's hope-boat,
When will you cross
My life's promise-sea?

12767

Every morning and every evening
God wants me to pay
My perfection-toll
At His Satisfaction-Bridge.

12768

God has a very special fondness
For the rising
Aspiration-flame-songs
Of my heart.

12769

My mind's impossibility-thoughts
Must surrender
To my heart's confidence-will.

12770

A God-lover
Must welcome every day
A newness-flower-dawn.

12771

My prayer is the devotion-escalator
That takes me to God.
My meditation is the love-elevator
That brings God to me.

12772

Alas,
Will there ever be a day
When the human mind
Will not run in worry-circles?

12773

My Lord always, always opens
And never returns unopened
My ignorance-letters.

12774

Nothing
Can be beyond the reach
Of a oneness-peace-lover.
