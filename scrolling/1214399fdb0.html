1060.

May my outer life
Be fearless.
May my inner life
Be guileless.

1061.

When I love my Lord Supreme
In His own Way,
I see a million hearts soaring
With my heart.

1062.

You must not reject
The physical world
If you want to feed
The spiritual world.

1063.

A mighty wave of joy
Caught my heart
When I turned my life
Into an unconditional surrender-song.

1064.

When God takes His morning Walk,
I feast upon the Beauty of His Eyes
And the Purity of His Feet.

1065.

O truth-seeker,
Show God your happiness-face.
He will immediately bless you
With His Fulness-Heart.

1066.

It is a heart
Of unimaginable beauty
That lives to love
Only God's Compassion-Eye.

1067.

The human in us wants God
To stop transcending
So that it can capture
God's Forgiveness-Feet
Permanently.

1068.

A single Smile
From my Lord's Eye
Is enough for me to recapture
The beauty and fragrance
Of my aspiration-past.

1069.

No human being
Can evade the power
Of God's Justice-Hour —
None!

1070.

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Please do me a big favour.
Just devour, as soon as possible,
My heart's sweetness-devotion-smiles.

1071.

Nobody needs God more
Than a sleepless aspiration-heart
And a breathless dedication-life.

1072.

My Lord commands me
Not to listen to my body's
Unfounded and ceaseless complaints.

1073.

Alas, I do not remember
When and where
I ever pleased God
In God's own Way.

1074.

God never listens
To my desire-mind.
I must never listen
To my desire-mind either.
