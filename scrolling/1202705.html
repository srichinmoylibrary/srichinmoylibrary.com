16901

Finally I have discovered
My heart's perfect beauty:
Gratitude.

16902

God loves
Our hope-heart-smiles
More than we can ever imagine.

16903

Negativity never cherish!
It weakens the heart's
Beauty, joy and power.

16904

We must uproot
Not only the negativity-jungles
But also the separativity-trees.

16905

Today I am extremely proud
Of my mind,
For it is for the first time
Basking in my heart's silence-sun.

16906

Desire-life
You cannot deceive me anymore.
Aspiration-life,
You cannot escape me anymore.
Dedication-life
You cannot hide from me anymore.

16907

Expectation-clouds
Are always available.
Frustration-downpours
Are always available.
Surrender-sun
Is never available.

16908

If only this world of ours
Loved God a little more,
Our suffering hearts
Would grow into smiling lives.

16909

What I need
At every moment in my life
Is constant
Satisfaction-expansion-heart.

16910

My mind's eloquence
Must come from
My heart's sleepless silence
To feed the world-hunger.

16911

Willingness is
More than capacity.
Willingness is
The nearness of God.

16912

Economise
Rather than revolutionise
Your thinking mind-activities.

16913

When I pray and meditate,
I feel that each thought
Is either a God-thunder
Or a God-flute.

16914

It is I
Who have to make the choice
To make myself
Happy and perfect.
