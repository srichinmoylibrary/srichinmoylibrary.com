23930

There is a God-manifestation-dream
In each aspiration-heart.
This dream is, alas, imprisoned
And has to be liberated.

23931

We have inside us
A miniature world.
Let us not hide
But illumine
This small world
Of imperfection.

23932

Infinity multiplied by Infinity
Are the aspiration-flowers
That you are offering
To the Supreme.

23933

Today
God's Name is Happiness
Because His children have proved
Their loving oneness
With His creation.

23934

Each divine Word from God
Is a most powerful aspiration-flame
To illumine and immortalise us
In the Heart of our Beloved Supreme.

23935

The secret of secrets
Of the dearest child of God:
God is dearest
Not because He is omniscient,
Omnipotent and omnipresent,
But because He is all Love.

23936

Just remember:
If you really love the Supreme
In your Master,
You will not do anything
To disappoint or displease Him.

23937

God's loving Life-Breath is all yours,
For the manifestation of what you are:
The unmanifested God.

23938

When I meditate soulfully,
I make myself five years old.
When I do not meditate,
I become eighty years old.

23939

When you listen to
And obey
The Master's divine wish,
You are fulfilling
The Divine Itself.

23940

Disobedience
Is much worse than falsehood,
For it breaks the divine law.

23941

The Supreme is offering you
His continuous Blessing,
So you can learn how to bring forward
The eternal child inside you.

23942

The real gift
That God deeply cherishes
And treasures
Is your soulful smile.

23943

Humanity's appreciation
Of God's creation
Is its true gift
To the Creator.

23944

Love is the greatest force;
Who can dare to deny it?
But God's Command-Force
Is also the greatest Love.
