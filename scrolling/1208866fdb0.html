23060

Today
God's Compassion-Eye
Sees that I am an aspiration-dwarf.
Therefore, God's Compassion-Eye
Does not want God to be
A Self-Transcendence-Giant.

23061

I do have faith
In God's Compassion.
But alas, I need faith
In God's Patience.

23062

Only my heart's sleepless tears
Can keep my mind
Out of ignorance-prison.

23063

Today,
Alas, God has confided in my soul
That He is not satisfied
With my heart's aspiration-homework.

23064

If you are shopping
In the desire-market,
Nobody will flatter you.
On the contrary,
The cosmic gods may batter you.

23065

God the Teacher
Is always eager
To give you the highest marks
In your soul's fastest progress
And your heart's highest evolution.

23066

If you want to live
In God's Heart-Nest,
Then you have to be
Devoted and faithful
To His Heart-Nest.

23067

If all our good qualities
Are traced to one source,
That source is undoubtedly
Obedience.

23068

Claim God's Vision
As your own, very own,
Since you want to bask
In God's Vision-Glories.

23069

Your impurity-mind
Will never be able to blight
God's Compassion-Eye-Delight.

23070

Because of your soul's promise
To God's Eye,
And because of your heart's promise
To God's Heart,
God has accepted the challenge
Of transforming your life.

23071

Think only of others'
Good, divine and self-giving qualities
To stay inside God's Heart-Garden,
Instead of your own mind-jungle.

23072

Gossip-mongers, beware!
Today you are enjoying
Malicious pleasure-gossip
But tomorrow you may become
The most deplorable gossip-victim.

23073

The Absolute Lord Supreme
Is only for those
Who are sleeplessly singing God-songs
In God's own Way
And carrying only His Tune.

23074

My life was built on
My soul's promise.
My life is now being built on
My heart's hope.
My life shall be built on
God's Smile.
