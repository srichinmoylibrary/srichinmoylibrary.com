26701

The human life is, indeed,
A golden
God-serving opportunity.

26702

A soulful smile is, indeed,
A messenger descended from
God's celestial Palace.

26703

Self-giving is, indeed,
An indispensable piece
Of God-Wisdom in us.

26704

When God hears that His dear ones
Have given up the spiritual life,
He deeply sighs and mourns.

26705

When God sees that His dear ones
Have increased their aspiration,
He congratulates them
Far beyond their imagination.

26706

God the Compassion only
Connects the road between
My determination
And God-Satisfaction.

26707

O disobedience-mind,
Be careful!
You will soon be imprisoned
By my aspiration-heart —
Indefinitely!

26708

My faith in the world
Goes far beyond
The deplorable deeds
Of the world.

26709

My heart-bird
Sleeplessly enjoys
Soaring aspiration-flight.

26710

Each time I soulfully pray,
I become the beauty
Of my heart-blossom.

26711

A God-crying heart
Is
A God-manifesting smile.

26712

A desire-bondage-freed chain
Our life becomes
When our aspiration reaches
Its zenith-height.

26713

What is humility,
If not our soul's nobility
Revealed in and through
Our lives?

26714

May each human being become
A peace-loving God-dreamer,
God-lover and God-server.
