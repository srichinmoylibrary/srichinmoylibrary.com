18730

The mind
That is eager to criticise
  I had.
The heart
That is eager to sympathise
  I now have.

18731

If you smile at temptation,
You will be the deplorable victim.
If you laugh at temptation,
You will be the formidable victor.

18732

My heart's pressing necessity
Is God-realisation.
My soul's pressing necessity
Is God-manifestation.

18733

If you do not have
A self-giving heart,
Your life will never be able
To radiate your divinity's
Perfection-splendour.

18734

The difference between
The mind's sermons
And the heart's life
Is the difference between
The promise and its execution.

18735

Every day
A seeker must try
To cleanse his doubt-mind
With his heart's devotion-tears.

18736

I want my heart's
Willingness-bells
To ring sleeplessly.

18737

Where
Does each human being live?
Inside
His self-made expectation-prison.

18738

Compassion-height
You desperately need
If you want to escape
Justice-light.

18739

What does surrender look like?
It looks like the purity-smile
Of a child.

18740

Doubt
Knows no cure.
Faith
Knows no ailment.

18741

If you hesitate
When God beckons,
You are bound to remain
Forever enslaved
By your doubting mind.

18742

One aspiration,
One goal,
  But many winners.

18743

A battle is going on
Sleeplessly
Between the heart's compassion
And the mind's deception.

18744

It is not human nature
To enjoy what we get
With no effort.
