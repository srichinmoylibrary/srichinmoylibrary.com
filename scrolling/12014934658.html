15730

For so many years
I was enslaved
By my mind's insecurities,
Jealousies and impurities.
But now my Lord Supreme
Has unconditionally saved me
From my titan-captors.

15731

Every time a human being
Does something divinely great
And supremely good,
God immediately broadcasts it
On His Heart-Radio.

15732

My soul observes
My soul's birthday promise
Every day.
And what is that promise?
"God for God's sake,
God's Satisfaction for God's sake.
Everything on earth,
Everything in Heaven,
Only for God's sake."

15733

A desire-life
Will, without fail,
Need radical purification
Before it is allowed
To enter into the heart-beauty's
Aspiration-garden.

15734

If you are
Entirely dependent on God
In your inner life
And your outer life,
Then God is sleeplessly responsible
For the perfection of your outer life
And the satisfaction of your inner life.

15735

It is our psychic promises
That are eventually fulfilled.
Our mental promises,
Emotional promises
And all other promises
Quite often are not fulfilled.
Why?
Because they do not have
The necessary perseverance,
Their way of seeing the truth
Is not clear
And their goal itself
Is unclear.

15736

My Lord Supreme,
I have prayed to You
For many things in this life.
Now I would like You
To fulfil only one desire of mine.
My Lord,
Only save me from myself!

15737

When my mind-ego
Plays the role of a genuine seeker,
I see the aspiration-theatre
Is completely empty.

15738

I shall not allow
My soul-child
To be completely lost
In my mind's confusion-jungle.

15739

My desire to know,
My desire to love
And my desire to become divine and perfect
Are my only desires in life.

15740

Forgiveness
Is by far the best
Freedom-delight
In God's entire creation.

15741

In God's Eye
Your heart's aspiration-cry
Is as valuable as
Your soul's illumination-smile.

15742

O my human mind,
It is high time for you to realise
That your ancient desire-disease
Will lead you to utter destruction.

15743

My heart is always
An eager volunteer
In God's world-illumining
Activities.

15744

You do not have to face
A fruitless future.
It is you who can make
A fruitful, powerful
God-satisfaction-future
With your own self-giving life.
