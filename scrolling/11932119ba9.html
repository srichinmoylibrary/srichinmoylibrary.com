7545

Purity is always
Of paramount importance
To fortify the heart-citadel.

7546

God never hesitates
To accept
Your realisation-application.

7547

Remember,
Forced love is weaker
Than the power of an ant.

7548

Never take a false step!
You will have
A fatal fear-attack.

7549

Every morning
I cheerfully do one thing:
I make a solemn perfection-promise.

7550

If you are only for God,
Then you can come
And sail with me
Any time.

7551

You fool,
Still you are not afraid
Of your imminent
Disobedience-disaster!

7552

You no longer have
A truth-heart.
Therefore, your progress-life
Has come to a halt.

7553

Your heart
Is a God-appointed emperor.
Your mind
Is a self-styled monarch.

7554

Mind, never stop surrendering!
Heart, never stop climbing!
Life, never stop transcending!
Soul, never stop dreaming!

7555

Why do you allow your mind
To enjoy the sleep
That will last for centuries?

7556

Do not allow
Your reality-heart
To collapse
Into your hallucination-mind!

7557

Do you not think
That someday
You will be forced to pass
God's Examination?

7558

You are my own,
Very own,
Especially when you love God
Unconditionally.

7559

Your mind
Is a God-traitor.
Your heart
Is a God-soldier.
