26260

My Lord forces my mind
To believe in His Greatness.
My Lord does not tell my heart
How great He is.
My heart knows not only how great He is,
But also how good He is.

26261

No matter how the mind tries,
It only revolves again and again
Through the unaspiring thought-door.

26262

My morning surrender-meditation
Is infinitely more beautiful,
Powerful and fruitful
Than I can ever disclose.

26263

I was once a student of self-deception.
Now I have become
A student of self-correction,
Plus God-satisfaction.

26264

We can be happy
And we can be confident
Only when we place ourselves
In the Elysian lap of Mother Earth.

26265

O Lord,
You decide whether I should be
At the foot of my aspiration-mountain
Or on top of my aspiration-mountain.
My surrender is my goal.

26266

May my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
Proclaim my readiness, willingness
And eagerness
To manifest God every day.

26267

My little faith-blossom-plant,
I love you.
I appreciate your beauty.
I admire your fragrance.

26268

There is no such thing
As a minor paradise.
Paradise is always major, sublime
And perfect.

26269

O my mind, O my vital,
Nobody forces you to embrace
Frustration-blindness,
Nobody!

26270

My Lord,
One Compassion-flooded word
From You
Clears my path ahead.

26271

My Lord, the Smile-Beauty
Of Your Eye
I see not only inside my heart
But also inside every limb of mine.

26272

O my mind, do not forget
That you are also assigned
To manifest God's Dream
On earth.

26273

My heart has caught a rainbow
To decorate
My heart-shrine.

26274

How can you succeed in your inner life
If you allow your mind to be puffed up
By constant outer success?
