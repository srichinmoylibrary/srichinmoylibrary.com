12190

What is insecurity?
It is indeed a limitless negative force
That destroys the beauty and purity
Of human life.

12191

Easy to predict
The compassionate God's next move,
But not man's whimsical moves.

12192

I love the company of hope.
I shall not allow
My garden of hope
To be uprooted by doubt.

12193

Wish power tells me
That one day, someday,
I shall definitely be able
To realise God.
But will power infallibly tells me
That I am another God.

12194

Surrender now,
And you will see
Satisfaction will soon follow.
Aspire always,
And remain
An eternal gratitude-student.

12195

To feed the hungry fear
With ceaseless failure
Is a common experience.
Each seeker, no matter how great he is,
Will agree.

12196

My mind's confusion-flag
Is cut to pieces
By my heart's
Illumination-sword.

12197

I have decided to utilise
Both desire and need.
Desire, you I shall keep
Beneath my thunder-feet.
Need, you I shall keep
At my Lord's Lotus-Feet.

12198

The human mind stumbles
Through a suspicion-wilderness
Before it reaches
The heart's oneness-garden.

12199

To achieve unconditional surrender
To God's Will
Is undoubtedly man's hardest struggle.
But the struggle is finally embraced
By the Light Supreme.

12200

I was born to be perfect.
I was born to be a choice
Instrument of God.
I was born to fulfil God
In His own Way
In every way.
