9775

Your mind wants to learn
The secrets of a withering rose.
Your heart wants to become
A sacred and ever-blossoming rose.

9776

My Lord,
I know I do not care for You,
But if You do not care for me,
I know that I shall sadly fail.

9777

If your heart is robed in purity,
Then your life will become
The blossoming Light of Divinity.

9778

Why are you so rich
In stupidity?
Are you not afraid
Of living alone
Without beliefs?

9779

All I truly want from God
Is His Compassion-Ocean,
And all I want from man
Is his sincere attempt
To understand me.

9780

I shall try
To shield you from shame
Only when you do not try
To blame the world
For your downfall.

9781

Unless you aspire,
You will always be compelled
To remain a prisoner
Of bewilderment.

9782

Leave me alone, O world!
Blame me not!
I shall be liberated
Without your help,
For the blue bird of my soul
Has come to the fore
To liberate me.

9783

Your heart has come into the world
To work for God
And not to be worshipped by man.

9784

You must take each day
As the blessing-touch
And compassion-smile
Of your new birth.

9785

Newness,
Do come and take birth
In my mind.

Fulness,
Do come and take birth
In my heart.

Satisfaction,
Do come and take birth
In my Beloved Supreme.

9786

Little soulful efforts
Can make not only a good seeker
But also a perfect instrument of God.

9787

The only perfection I know
Is Your Vision-Eye.
The only satisfaction I know
Is Your Oneness-Heart.

9788

Your inspiration-mind
May love the sound-life,
But your aspiration-heart
Will always love the silence-life.

9789

If your mind studies
In the forest of intellect,
Life-transformation
Will always remain a far cry.
