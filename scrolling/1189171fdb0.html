3560

An insecure seeker
Cannot be a great sprinter
In the aspiration-world.

3561

O my poor soul,
To me you are nothing
But a shipwrecked navigator.

3562

You have lost
Your aspiration-heart.
That means
I shall not see
Your dedication-life
Anymore.

3563

Brighten your heart's
Aspiration-flames.
God will enlighten
Your God-approaching life.

3564

An illumination-heart:
This is what you are.
A confusion-mind:
This is what you have.

3565

God has given man's faith
The capacity to shorten
God's Justice-Hour.

3566

Where is the difference
Between your hesitation-heart
And your confusion-mind?
Where?

3567

What makes you think
You are a total failure?
I tell you,
In God's Eye,
You have never failed
And you never will.

3568

The soul compassionately
Waits and waits
For the mind to be ready
To receive God
And His infinite Compassion-Light.

3569

Every day
Sincerity and humility
Go to God to see
If they can do something
For Him.

3570

God's Compassion-Sun
And my aspiration-flame
Have decided on a merger.

3571

To reach
The dream-flooded heights,
I need a soulful cry
And not
A hurtful despair.

3572

Do not be exasperated
With yourself.
Go and present yourself
At God's special Heart-Room.
He is all ready to receive you.

3573

He likes to keep his life
Always
Under the special Heart-Canopy
Of God.

3574

Your determination
And God's Compassion
Enjoy seeing your life
Under perpetual construction.
