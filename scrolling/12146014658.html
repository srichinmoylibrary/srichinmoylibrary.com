1230.

O my mind,
Do you know why you are so unhappy?
You are unhappy because
You do not even want to know
How to doubt your doubts.

1231.

Each heart is a hope-flower
That blooms and blossoms
At God's choice Hour.

1232.

Nothing can make God smile
As quickly and wholeheartedly
As my enthusiasm-mind.

1233.

God's love for your aspiration
Is so deep
That God has opened for you
A galaxy
Of God-manifestation-opportunities.

1234.

O my mind,
I am telling you the absolute truth:
Our Compassion-Lord becomes
Whatever we truly need.

1235.

I have become
My heart's constant prayer
To open God's sacred Heart-Door.

1236.

O my mind, be patient.
Continue your self-research.
Your total illumination
Is inevitable.

1237.

The difference between
My mind and my heart is this:
My mind questions God's Justice;
My heart bathes in God's Compassion.

1238.

How does one become spiritual?
Not by exercising his mind's
Independence,
Not by exercising his heart's
Interdependence,
But by exercising his life's
God-dependence.

1239.

God came and gave him
The strongest Embrace
When God saw him embracing
The world's suffering-night.

1240.

My heart's secret and sacred
Friendship with God
Has made my soul
Unimaginably happy.

1241.

When my heart's aspiration-flames
Dance continuously,
God invites me to His Palace
To dine with Him.

1242.

A unconditional surrender-life
Is the most significant achievement
In spirituality.

1243.

I must fulfil God
Instantly and constantly
To claim Him as my own,
Very own.

1244.

Only when my heart cries
Desperately and helplessly
Does God appear
Blessingfully and proudly.
