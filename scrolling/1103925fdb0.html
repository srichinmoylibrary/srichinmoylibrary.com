8 December 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme! How beautiful, how peaceful And how blissful Is the silence of my heart's Meditation-room. My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 December 1999, 8:14 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

8 December 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Our mind's frustration-sighs
Are
Our life's God-denials.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 December 1999, 8:32 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before lifting 350 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 700 lbs.)

8 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must think of God's
Frustration-Heart first.
Then I should look at God's
Compassion-Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 December 1999, 8:44 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before doing one-arm pushes with 500 lbs.

9 December 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May every heart be cradled
By boundless faith.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 December 1999, 6:37 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

9 December 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I talk sleeplessly.
You talk so little.
Why? Why? Why?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 December 1999, 6:44 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before lifting 350 lbs. with each arm simultaneously twice. (Total: 700 lbs.)

9 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am telling the truth
That my heart is as beautiful
As Your Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/9 December 1999, 6:51 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before doing one-arm pushes with 500 lbs.

13 December 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The problem of problems
For my poor Lord Beloved Supreme
Is my mind's unwillingness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 December 1999, 8:13 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine.

13 December 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
That we all must develop
Inner muscles of iron.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/13 December 1999, 8:20 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before lifting 350 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 700 lbs.)

13 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall not allow You
To overstrain
Your Compassion-Eye for me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 December 1999, 8:27 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before doing seventy one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (29 right, 41 left)

13 December 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord Beloved Supreme,
I shall no more
Permit Your Heart
To bleed
For my abominable misdeeds.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 December 1999, 8:30 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil.

14 December 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Invariably
Every heart-plus needs
A mind-minus first.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 December 1999, 6:29 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine.

14 December 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I desperately need help
From You.
I really want to escape
From my mind-prison.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 December 1999, 6:45 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before lifting 350 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 700 lbs.)

14 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do You really love me?
Do You really care for me?
If so, do scold me most powerfully
Every day.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 December 1999, 6:53 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before doing fifty one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (25 right, 25 left)

15 December 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A God-disobedience-life
Is
A happiness-heart-killer.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 December 1999, 7:30 a.m. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

15 December 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
For years and years and years
I shall have to climb
To reach my transcendental heights.
Please do not allow me to miss
Even a second.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 December 1999, 7:42 a.m. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Before lifting 350 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 700 lbs.)
