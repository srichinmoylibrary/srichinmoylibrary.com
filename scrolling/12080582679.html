22215

The tireless and breathless
Aspiration of the heart
Is always far above
The mind's poor understanding.

22216

Not because my mind
At times denies the existence of God
Am I a failure,
But because I have no time
To kindle the flame of aspiration
In my sleeping heart-room.

22217

The conflict between
The mind and the heart
Can be solved only
By the express illumination-presence
Of the soul.

22218

I have come to realise
That only God
Or His own personal Peace
Can comfort
My heart's streaming tears.

22219

Everything else fails
Or will fail,
But not self-giving love.

22220

To receive joy
In infinite measure,
I always choose God's Feet,
And not God's Face.

22221

A heart that is completely dedicated
To God-service
Will never be able to appreciate
The noise of the mind-factory.

22222

Each sincere seeker
Eventually becomes
His own life-transforming
Peace-apostle.

22223

The rarest gift to God
From the twentieth century
Will be humanity's
Satisfaction-smile.

22224

When temptation knocks
At your mind-door,
Immediately say, "No!"
And continue nourishing
Your "No!"

22225

A seeker's rise and fall
Depend entirely on
The mind's sincerity
And the heart's purity.

22226

When I am in the mind,
I foolishly desire
Satisfaction.
When I am in the heart,
I wisely long for
Perfection.

22227

What is the end?
The end is nothing but
The beginning
Of a new and unfulfilled
Dream-reality.

22228

Winning
Is not everything.
Self-giving
Is more than everything.

22229

May my heart's purity-smile
Always have
A soulful access
To every seeker's heart.
