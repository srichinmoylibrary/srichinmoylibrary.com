27 My Love Of God ...

My love of God
And my gratitude to God
Are keeping me alive.

28 God Gets Tremendous Satisfaction ...

God gets tremendous satisfaction
When we pay attention
To His Messages.

29 God-Willingness ...

God-willingness
Is needed
At every moment of life.

30 The Necessity ...

The necessity
Of God-obedience
Can never be exaggerated.

29 January 2006 ...

29 January 2006

Penang, Malaysia

31 I Wish To Have ...

I wish to have
God-surrender
As my life-partner.

32 Action! Action! ...

Action! Action!
No more feeble words.

33 God Loves ...

God loves
To obey
His own iron Laws.

34 The Creator Enjoys ...

The Creator enjoys
His Cosmic Game
Seated on His golden Throne.

35 God’s Cosmic Law ...

God’s Cosmic Law
Admits exception.

36 Conquer Temptation ...

Conquer temptation,
If perfection
You sincerely need.

37 Each Heart-Cry ...

Each heart-cry
Takes us away
From the meshes of ignorance.

38 Alas ...

Alas,
We are apt to invoke
Heaven’s Power
And not
Heaven’s Light.

39 Ignorance-Night ...

Ignorance-night
Again and again
Loves to fight
Against Heaven’s Light.

40 The Beauty And The Fragrance ...

The beauty and the fragrance
Of happiness
Lead and guide us
To the Higher Worlds.
