Akashe Batase Shuni

December 15th, 1995

Akashe batase shuni ekatar bani
Tripta hayeche amar hridaya khani

Nirabata Sathe Khelite Chahigo Nirabata

December 15th, 1995

Nirabata sathe khelite chahigo nirabata sathe
Sangsaya mane rakhibo sudure ami dibarate

Shunte Habe Bishwa Mayer Bishesh Adesh

December 15th, 1995

Shunte habe bishwa mayer bishesh adesh
Chaho jadi asimatar mukti bishesh

Kandte Habe Shishur Mato Pratikhane Ta

December 15th, 1995

Kandte habe shishur mato pratikhane
Ta na hale bhugte habe achetane

Chutte Habe Dibanishi Alor Sandhane

December 15th, 1995

Chutte habe dibanishi alor sandhane
Phutte habe prati bhore hiya bagane

December 15th, 1995

Magi ami nirabata udayan

Jabo Ami Jabo Ami Hiya Pure Tushbo Ami

December 15th, 1995

Jabo ami jabo ami hiya pure

Chitta Aji Nritya Kare Bhatir Bajare

December 15th, 1995

Chitta aji nritya kare bhatir bajare
Paran bhare dekhbo sethai bishwa rajare

Translation:

Today my heart is dancing in the market of Light.
I shall unreservedly see the King of creation.

Jatoi daki tatoi tripti pai
