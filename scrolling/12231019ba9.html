My Lord, My Only Prayer to You (3)

August 26th, 2005

My Lord, my only prayer to You
Is this:
Do continue to remain
Fully in charge of my life.

Every Little Thing

August 26th, 2005

Every little thing
That my Lord does for me
Thrills me, my heart,
My very existence.

My Heart Is Made Of

August 26th, 2005

My heart is made of
My Lord’s sweet Dreams

I Belong to God’s Will

August 26th, 2005

I belong to God’s Will.
What more do I need?
What more do I care for?

A True God-Lover

August 26th, 2005

A true God-lover
Is he who cannot breathe
Without self-giving.

I Must Pray, Not Only for Myself (2)

August 26th, 2005

I must pray, not only for myself,
But also for those
Who do not care to pray.

Do Not Blame Anybody

August 26th, 2005

Do not blame anybody for the world.
The world is entirely God’s Creation.

My Heartbeat and God’s Heartbeat

August 26th, 2005

My heartbeat and God’s Heartbeat
Love to speak in whispers.

It Matters Not

August 26th, 2005

It matters not
How much we know
About God.

It Matters Only

August 26th, 2005

It matters only
How much we have given
To God.

There Is Only One Home

August 26th, 2005

There is only one home,
And that home is our love of God.

God’s Greatest Treasure

August 26th, 2005

God’s greatest treasure
Is man’s smile.

Just One Smile

August 26th, 2005

Just one smile
Immensely increases
The beauty of the universe.

Do Not Depend

August 26th, 2005

Do not depend
On earthly applause.

Depend Only

August 26th, 2005

Depend only
On God’s Compassion-Smile.
