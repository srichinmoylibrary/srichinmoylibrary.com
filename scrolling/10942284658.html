27 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God is asking me
Not to blame Him any more
For the world-crisis.
God tells me,
"My child,
I am wide awake,
I am wide awake."

28 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
In my outer life
I have become a lamb
To charm You.
In my inner life
I have become a lion
To manifest You.

29 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God tells me
That if I unreservedly love Him,
Then I must bury my past
In oblivion-cave.

26 November 2001 ...

26 NOVEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII

30 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Heaven bountifully descends
Only when earth cheerfully offers
Its bleeding tears to God.

31 Hope And Faith ...

Hope and faith
From deep within
Can challenge and defeat
The outer cruel fate.

32 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
He who rejects his Master's advice
Summarily
Loses immediately
His God-identification-life.

33 The Inner God-Surrender-Heart ...

The inner God-surrender-heart
Has to become
The outer world-prosperity-life.

34 The Mind-Admirer ...

The mind-admirer
And the mind-enjoyer
Cannot be
The heart-sky-climber.

35 My Prayer-Life ...

My prayer-life
Tells my mind
Where it must go.

36 My Meditation-Life ...

My meditation-life
Tells my heart
What it must become.

37 Aspire, Aspire! ...

Aspire, aspire!
God is extremely eager
To share with you
All His supreme Secrets.

38 My Prayer Loves ...

My prayer loves
My bended knees
Inside my mind-cave.

39 My Meditation Loves ...

My meditation loves
My deepening heart
Inside my soul-temple.

28 November 2001 ...

28 NOVEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII
