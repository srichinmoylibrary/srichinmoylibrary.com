40 God comes to my surrender-angel ...

God comes to my surrender-angel
Inside my heart
On tiptoe
With His infinite Joy
And infinite Pride.

41 Every Day Our Inner Faith ...

Every day our inner faith
Needs newness-joy
To grow into
The Fulness of God.

42 Every Morning My Heart And I ...

Every morning my heart and I
Hoist our Supreme Lord’s
Victory-Flag.

6 January 2002 ...

6 January 2002

Phnom Penh, Cambodia

43 There Is Nothing ...

There is nothing
That a hope-heart
Cannot achieve.

44 Alas ...

Alas,
I fool myself miserably
By thinking that I know
Who I truly am.

45 I Must Replace The Pleasure-Life ...

I must replace the pleasure-life
Of my outer world
With the heart-joy
Of my inner world.

46 God Proudly Smiles At My Mind ...

God proudly smiles at my mind
When He sees that it has
Even an iota of peace.

47 If I Really Want Peace ...

If I really want peace
In my life,
Then I must not busy myself
With what others do and say.

48 To Be Perfect In My Outer Life ...

To be perfect in my outer life,
I must harmoniously live
With my inner
God-aspiration-heart.

49 A Famous Life ...

A famous life
Is no match
For a God-Pride-heart
And a God-faithfulness-life.

50 To See My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

To see my Lord Beloved Supreme
Smiling,
I am ready to sacrifice everything
In my life.

51 I Must Not Postpone ...

I must not postpone
Even for a single day
My unconditional surrender
To God’s Will.

52 Not What The Mind Says ...

Not what the mind says,
But what the heart feels
I must immediately believe
And accept.

53 My Heart’s Soulful ...

My heart’s soulful
Devotion-skill
Is my life’s momentous
Enthusiasm-thrill.
