26 November 2003 ...

26 November 2003

Singapore

27 God The Eye Beckons Me ...

God the Eye beckons me.
God the Heart speaks to me.
God the Breath embraces me.

28 When God The Eye ...

When God the Eye
Beckons me,
I offer my heart’s gratitude-tears.

29 When God The Heart ...

When God the Heart
Speaks to me,
I offer my life’s gratitude-smiles.

30 When God The Breath ...

When God the Breath
Embraces me,
I become the flood
Of oneness-gratitude-delight.

31 Who Is Safe In Today’s World? ...

Who is safe in today’s world?
No, not even multibillionaires,
Needless to say
The poorer than the poorest.

32 Who Is Safe In Today’s World? ...

Who is safe in today’s world?
Not he who is wallowing
In the supremacy-pleasures
Of unbounded power.

33 Who Is Safe In Today’s World? ...

Who is safe in today’s world?
He who is endowed
With God’s Protection-Assurance
Here, there and all-where.

34 What Do I Learn From God’s Tears? ...

What do I learn from God’s Tears?
I learn from God’s Tears
That He has infinite concern for me.

35 What Do I Learn From God’s Smiles? ...

What do I learn from God’s Smiles?
I learn from God’s Smiles
That He has confidence in me
In measureless measure.

36 My Lord, Please Tell Me ...

My Lord, please tell me,
Why am I so unkind to You?
Why, why?
Why do I keep You waiting for me
For Eternity?

37 My Child, If You Come To Me ...

“My child, if you come to Me
Even beyond Eternity’s Eye and Heart,
I shall not mind,
As long as you come to Me,
Enter into My Heart
And become the Beauty of My Dream
And the Duty of My Reality.”

38 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That He is not at all worried
About my transformation,
Since He has no fixed schedule for me.

39 O Ignorance-Night ...

O ignorance-night,
I am not afraid of conversing with you,
But I am terribly afraid
Of your unthinkable vibration
That indefinitely lasts.
I am preparing myself
To conquer your vibration-power,
So that I can inundate my Mother Earth
With Infinity’s Delight.

40 No More ...

No more
My mind’s outer eye!
No more
My mind’s outer ear!
