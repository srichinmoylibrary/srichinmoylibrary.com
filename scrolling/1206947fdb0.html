21160

Each good thought
And each good action
Is indeed a descendant
Of God's Heart-Fragrance.

21161

Neither God nor even death
Wants to hear from me.
I am worthless and useless
In every way!

21162

O disobedience-fool,
Will you ever know
That you do not even care
To understand yourself?

21163

If you really want to realise God,
Then do not postpone your obedience
Until the endless tomorrow.

21164

I am so happy,
O my mind,
That your disobedience-life
Is so swiftly sinking and dying.

21165

I become a burden to myself
When I want to see everything
In my own way,
And when I want everything to be done
In my own way.

21166

God smiles at me
Most compassionately
And most proudly
When I stretch my heart-dreams
To the sky.

21167

O my mind,
I am not an inch from the truth
When I tell you that
You are nothing other than
A non-practising perfectionist.

21168

Each time I do selfless service,
God blessingfully tells me
To enjoy a feast
Of His Compassion-Smiles.

21169

The seeker who wants nothing
From this world
Is equally as wise
As the one who loves
And wants to depend
Only on God's Compassion.

21170

An unaspiring person
Is he who is sustained
By lifeless dreams
And fruitless hopes.

21171

My Lord Supreme
Every day commands me
To nurse
My soul's sleepless thirst.

21172

Try to please God
In God's own Way.
Otherwise, your life is bound
To remain saddled
With unfulfilled expectations.

21173

You are bound to dance
With your mind's tragedies
If you do not bring into your mind
Your soul's wisdom-flooded light.

21174

You want to know how
I remain spiritually so rich.
I remain spiritually rich
By keeping careful watch
Over my mind-thief.
