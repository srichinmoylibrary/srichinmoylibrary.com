25215

Do not blame God
If you fail by yourself.
God has asked you so many times
Not to toil alone and unassisted.

25216

If your heart
Is empty of intensity,
Then how will you ever
Grow receptivity?

25217

Is there any problem
That is not heavy and oppressive
In our life?
Is there any problem that is not?

25218

A seeker is he
Who basks
In the Compassion-Sunshine
Of the Lord Supreme.

25219

Spirituality does not relax,
Does not perplex.
Spirituality only injects
Divinity
Into our aspiration-heart.

25220

Countless prayer-cries
And countless meditation-smiles
We need
To realise God.

25221

Our surrender-heart
And surrender-life
Will always succeed
In our inner life and outer life as well.

25222

There are a few mind-diseases
Incurable.
Needless to say,
Disobedience tops the list!

25223

The eventual extinction
Of ignorance-night
Is not our mental hallucination.
It is a God-Promise-Announcement.

25224

I have once more
Started studying
In my childhood eagerness-school.

25225

Why waste your time
Repairing your old aspiration-train,
Once you become the possessor
Of a new aspiration-train?

25226

My Lord,
You are so kind to me.
You have allowed my heart
To speak for me.

25227

To smile soulfully and unreservedly
Is a momentous success-jump
In the inner world.

25228

God does not use His Mind
To understand our prayers.
He uses His Heart
To feel our prayers.

25229

Your heart has to be
As clear as daylight.
Your mind has to be
As plain as water.
Then God will not be able
To hide from you.
