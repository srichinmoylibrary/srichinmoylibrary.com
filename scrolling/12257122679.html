Chahina Jiban Chahina Maran

December 31st, 1969

Chahina jiban chahina maran
Chahi shudhu taba abhoy charan
Chahi shudhu mama hiya krandan
Tomar ichchha hauk puran

Translation:

I do not want life.
I do not want death.
I want to have only Your Feet
That destroy all my fear.
I want to have a crying heart.
May Your Will always be fulfilled.

Jibaner Majhe Naba Naba Saje

December 31st, 1969

Jibaner majhe naba naba saje
Utha tumi prabhu phutiya

Nite pari jena lutiya

Translation:

In an ever-new form,
My Lord, You have blossomed.
Your Compassion is Nectar infinite.
O, grant me the capacity to receive it.
