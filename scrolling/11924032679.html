6715

A perfectly God-realised man
Is undoubtedly
A human being in name only.

6716

Live inside the doubting mind.
You are bound to see difficulties
Even in golden opportunities.

Live inside the surrendering life.
You are bound to see opportunities
Even in dire difficulties.

6717

To keep your heart happy
All the time,
Ask your mind to receive
Daily faith-instructions
From your soul.

6718

God will always
Be ready to fulfil
The longing cries of your heart.
But He will never
Become a servant
To the demands of your vital!

6719

God's unconditional Compassion-Eye
Has been the biggest influence
In my life.

6720

Your mind-knowledge
Has sent you
To your confusion-market
To buy hurtful doubts!

6721

You may not be able to prevent
Your body
From getting old,
But you can certainly prevent
Your heart
From becoming impure.

6722

One soulful action
Of your aspiration-heart
Weighs infinitely more
Than your million words.

6723

The God-seeker
Who is only for God-manifestation
Here on earth
Has no time to think
Of his yesterday's failure-life.

6724

It is you
Who are totally responsible
For making your present life
A slave of your past.

6725

He may not know who God is,
But he is leading a life
That makes God
Supremely proud of him.

6726

Since self-deception
Has not pleased you,
Can you not try something else?
I mean, self-perfection.

6727

To smile
Soulfully and unconditionally
Is to carry
The satisfaction-delight of Heaven
Wherever you go.

6728

My soul has been waiting
Compassionately and sleeplessly
For my heart's full flowering.

6729

A God-seeker's purity-heart
And his oneness-soul
Can never lead an idle life.
