26 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do give me a soul of forgiveness,
A heart of compassion
And a life of tolerance
And patience.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 May 1999, 7:57 a.m.

27 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must immediately stop
My ceaseless desire-hunger,
I must.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 May 1999, 7:35 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

27 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Each time I sing my heart-songs
Prayerfully and soulfully,
I am allowed to go to Heaven
And enjoy Heaven's Bliss.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 May 1999, 7:40 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 210 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

27 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My fortitude-heart
Must triumphantly fight
Against my life's failures.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 May 1999, 8:08 a.m.

28 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To forget You is to fail
In the examination
Of my life's very existence on earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 May 1999, 6:48 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

28 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must awaken the divine lion
That is sleeping inside my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 May 1999, 6:55 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 210 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

28 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May Your supreme Will,
May Your absolute Will
Work faster than the fastest
In and through my life
For Your full manifestation
Here on earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 May 1999, 7:11 a.m.

28 May 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The deeper
My God-discovery is,
The higher
Is God's Pride in me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 May 1999, 7:32 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm fifty-three times. (27 right, 26 left)

29 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must live my life in such a way
That everyone can receive You
Inside my heart-garden.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

29 May 1999, 7:25 a.m.

31 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What a fool I am!
I try to test Your Will
Instead of tasting and devouring
Your Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 May 1999, 5:45 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

31 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me that my way
Has to be the way of aspiration
And dedication,
Not the way of resignation
And renunciation.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 May 1999, 5:50 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 210 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 250 lbs. with each arm separately.

31 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The 20th Century and the world
Gave each other the message
Of desire-enjoyment.
The 21st Century and the world
Will give each other the message
Of aspiration-enlightenment.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 May 1999, 6:09 a.m.

31 May 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my life becomes
A desire-beggar,
I would like You to immediately be
A denial-emperor.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 May 1999, 6:29 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm fifty-four times. (27 right, 27 left)

Editor's preface to the first edition

These morning prayers were offered by Sri Chinmoy during his weightlifting sessions at his home in New York in May 1999.
