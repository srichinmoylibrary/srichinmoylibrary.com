1 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind whispers to me:
"God is always busy.
I am sure He is not going to come
And visit you."
My heart whispers to me:
"God loves you so much.
Look, He is coming to visit you!"
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 March 2000, 8:04 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. each arm simultaneously.

1 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My world has two very special names:
My aspiration-heart-beauty
And
My dedication-life-fragrance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 March 2000, 8:20 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously seven times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

1 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, God-gratitude-tears
Are never to be found in my mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 March 2000, 8:37 a.m. Before doing sixty-four one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (29 right, 35 left)

2 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God wants me to carry
My love-devotion-surrender-identification
With me wherever I go.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 March 2000. Before using bench press machine up to 80 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

2 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
There are many, many charming,
Inspiring, encouraging
And illumining stations
Along the road to Infinity's Bliss.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 March 2000. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 30 lbs. with each arm simultaneously, and 40 lbs. with one arm.

2 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I say, “I do not,”
My Lord cries and cries and cries.
When I say, “I cannot,”
My Lord laughs and laughs and laughs.
When I say, “I can”
My Lord smiles and smiles and smiles.
When I say, “I have done”
My Lord immediately says,
“My child, I am making you
Another God, a future God.”
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 March 2000. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

2 March 2000 (IV) — 1000th prayer

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not want me to carry
A credit card.
He wants me to carry always with me
The mighty dollar
To remind me that I am, indeed,
A child of the Almighty.
My Lord, will people not laugh
At my cleverness?
"My child, cleverness is far better
Than foolishness.
When you use your cash, your inner pride
And the cashier's inner joy
Sing, play and dance together."
Finally, God becomes serious.
He tells me, "My child,
Money-power conquers only the inferiors.
Heart-power conquers everybody —
The superiors, the inferiors
And the equals,
Plus Me and My All."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 March 2000, Before doing 60 one-arm pushes with 700 lbs.

3 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My enthusiasm-life
Has a great and equal partner:
My eagerness-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 March 2000, 8:53 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

3 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Eventually desire-life fades
And aspiration-heart
Succeeds and flourishes.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/3 March 2000, 9:04 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously seven times (Total: 1,300 lbs.)/

3 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must pass well,
Sleeplessly and breathlessly,
All my God-surrender-examinations.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 March 2000, 9:22 a.m. Before doing 59 one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (26 right, 33 left)

5 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme! Alas, my mind has blindfolded My heart's vision-eye. My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 March 2000, 8:16 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

5 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must never allow
My sleepless God-attention
And
My breathless God-devotion
To waver.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 March 2000, 8:34 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously six times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

5 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God experienced —
God-Love, God-Joy, God-Peace
And God-Bliss
Expressed, revealed and manifested.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 March 2000, 8:49 a.m. Before doing sixty-five one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (29 right, 36 left)

6 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My God expresses
His extreme bewilderment
When I tell Him
That I am not inspired any more
To do His God-manifestation-work.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 March 2000, 12:32 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously six times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)
