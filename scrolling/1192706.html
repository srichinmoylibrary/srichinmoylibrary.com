7001

If you can climb up
Your heart's purity-ladder,
Then easily you will be able
To reach the highest floor
Of your soul's palace.

7002

Alas, how long
Can my earth-existence live
Between my heart's hope
And my mind's despair?

7003

A life of aspiration
Is not a parade
Of possibilities
But a concert
Of inevitabilities.

7004

To uproot
Your mind's teeming fears,
Every day you have to wake up
At God's Hour
And love God infinitely more
Than you love your mind.

7005

In your life
Everything is counterfeit —
Even your love for yourself.

7006

You cannot expect much from him,
For he has just started studying
In his heart's aspiration-school.

7007

God's main complaint against me
Is this:
I want complete satisfaction from Him
Before I have given Him
My aspiration-heart.

7008

If you are a true God-lover,
Then at times you are bound to break
Your old traditions
For the new transformation
Of this earth-planet.

7009

Your mind ignores your heart.
That is not news.
But if ever your heart
Ignores your mind,
That will be unforgettable news.

7010

If you cannot steal God,
Try at least to steal
God's Compassion-Heart.
God will be extremely proud of you.

7011

There is only one way
For you to repay your Master,
And that is through
Your continuous progress-life.

7012

The war-gods are always desired
By the human mind.
The peace-gods are always needed
By the divine heart.

7013

Your aspiration-life
Will definitely be blessed
With God-intoxication.
It is only a matter
Of fleeting time!

7014

Unless you every day
Double your aspiration,
There is every possibility
That you will lose all your interest
In God-realisation.
