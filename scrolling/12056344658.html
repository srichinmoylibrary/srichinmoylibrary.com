19830

God-longing you will understand
Only the day your faith in God
Reaches its summit-heights.

19831

O my mind,
When I search for you,
Why do I invariably find you
In a doubt-jungle
And not in a faith-garden?

19832

How can we expect
The wisdom of the vastness-heart
To stay inside
A tiny little mind?

19833

God swiftly comes
To rescue me
Whenever I am about to drown
In despair-sea.

19834

Aspiration itself is wisdom.
Therefore, we cannot expect it
To surrender to stupidity.

19835

O truth-seeker and God-lover,
Never think that your heart
Is made of fruitless hopes!

19836

God the creation
Tells my mind
To shut up.
God the Creator
Tells my heart
To wake up.

19837

Those who have crossed through
Desire-market
Will definitely arrive
At beauty's aspiration-garden.

19838

My doubtful mind
Is deeply indebted
To my forgiveness-heart
For its transformation.

19839

God's Pride knows no bounds
When my heart sings
A fountain-sweetness-song.

19840

The insecurity of the vital
And the fear of the mind
Always compel my heart-deer
To slow down its aspiration-speed.

19841

Only a heart of peace
Can rightfully enjoy
The birth of freedom.

19842

My heart needs
Only the aspiration-flames
That will ceaselessly mount.
It does not need
The aspiration-flames
That do not or cannot climb up
To the summit-heights.

19843

God sings for me
Many, many songs,
But He enjoys
Compassion-Songs the most.

19844

Yesterday's mind-questions
Can easily be illumined
By today's heart-answers.
