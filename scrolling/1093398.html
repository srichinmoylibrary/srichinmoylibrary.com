Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following morning prayers during his visit to Brazil and Paraguay in December 1999 and January 2000. Ten different groups recited the prayers turn by turn three times, then twice, then once.

10 DECEMBER 1999

SĂO MORITZ, BRAZIL

1 Today ...

Today
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Is examining the full record
Of my gratitude-heart.

2 May My Master Always Find Me ...

May my Master always find me
At the shrine
Of my heart-temple.

3 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Wants me to do
Something very special
For the love, joy, peace
And bliss-starved mankind.

4 A True Seeker's Heart-Tree ...

A true seeker's heart-tree
Always and always offers
The sweetest gratitude-fruits.

5 At Every Moment ...

At every moment
I must be fully awake
And not half-awake
In my aspiration-life.

6 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Is asking my heart-tears
To accompany Him
Along His own Eternity's Road.

7 My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme
Wants me to deeply enjoy
The full harvest
Of His Satisfaction-Smiles.

11 December 1999 ...

11 DECEMBER 1999

SĂO MORITZ, BRAZIL

8 Every Day ...

Every day
My God-obedience-heart
Offers its devotion-gratitude
To God's Will.

9 My Heart ...

My heart
Is an ever-increasing
God-Affection-experience-happiness.

10 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
To take my mind's impurity,
My heart's insecurity
And my life's futility
Very, very, very seriously.

11 Alas, ...

Alas,
It is always too late
When our disobedience
Takes God seriously.

12 In My Inner Life, ...

In my inner life,
God is God the Compassion-Eye
Far beyond the flights
Of my imagination.
In my outer life,
God is God the Forgiveness-Heart
Far beyond the flights
Of my imagination.
