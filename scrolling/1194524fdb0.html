8860

Who is responsible
For world-destruction
If not
Your mind's conscious enjoyment
Of confusion?

8861

You can enjoy
A limitless life of glory
If you do not allow
Your life to be bound
By your body's rules and regulations.

8862

Until the loftiest height
Is reached,
Nothing is safe,
Nothing is delightful,
Nothing is fruitful.

8863

Every day
Your soul gives you
Ample opportunity
To claim
Its illumination-beauty
As your very own.

8864

Your mind
Has to triumph
Over doubt.

Your heart
Has to triumph
Over insecurity.

Your life
Has to triumph
Over uncertainty.

8865

A frustrated life
Is an unconscious collector
Of rubbish-thoughts.

8866

I can never separate my mind
From the school
Of ancient ignorance.

8867

The inner sun
With its infinite beauty
Can shine
Only when we do not banish God
From our hope-life.

8868

There is only one safe place
To rest,
And that place is
My Eternity's aspiration-nest.

8869

The soldiers of the army of light
Do not kill any human being.
They only illumine
All human beings.

8870

God needs me.
Alas,
This is a message
Misunderstood by my mind.

8871

Why do you have to travel
Between a hopeless birth
And a helpless death?

8872

God does not want to be
Anywhere else
Except in our heart-temples.

8873

Be brave within and without!
Do not allow the tyranny
Of past memories
To destroy your heart's hope-bridge,
Which will carry you
To the other Shore,
The Shore of Light and Delight.

8874

If you are willing
To accept God's Decision,
Then how can God
Keep from you
Your sacred illumination?
