12645

God's unconditional Compassion-Mountain
Always remains infinitely higher
Than man's comprehension-mind-hill.

12646

Every day I must soulfully pray
To God
To show me the sunset
Of my mind's disobedience.

12647

Do you know why doubt
Appears ceaselessly?
It appears because it wants
To threaten, weaken
and finally dethrone
Your mind-king.

12648

Whenever and wherever
My aspiration-heart travels,
My Lord's Compassion-Eye
Lights the way.

12649

Every morning shake hands first
With your happiness-satisfaction-friend
Long before you enter into
Your daily life-activities.

12650

If you are
A sincere seeker and God-lover,
Then every day you must devotedly
Sweep away your mind's desire-dust.

12651

Unlike all our human friends,
God's Compassion-Eye
Is not a fair-weather friend.

12652

Our faith in God
Prescribes solutions
To each and every problem
That our mind creates.

12653

Aspiration-acceptance
And desire-rejection:
These two games must be played
Bravely and simultaneously.

12654

If you listen to your soul's
Meditation-message,
You can easily have a freer passage
To your life's ultimate Destination.

12655

If you cherish your mind's
Depression-night,
How can God bless you
With His Heart's Protection-Light?

12656

God always keeps His Heart-Lines open
For those who are destined to manifest
His Divinity's Light on earth.

12657

Insecurity,
You are the main barrier
I have to conquer
In order to make the fastest progress
In my conscious aspiration-life.

12658

Your hope-dreams have tremendous strength.
They will definitely be able to climb
Your perfection-realisation-heights.

12659

Progress always runs
Toward a more illumining
And satisfying destination.
