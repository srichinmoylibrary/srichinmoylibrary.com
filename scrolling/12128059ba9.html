26945

Because of his universal
Oneness-heart,
His mind-pride-tower
Has collapsed.

26946

A self-giving heart
Sees
No stop sign.

26947

A purity-heart
Lives far beyond
The snares
Of jealousy and insecurity.

26948

To decorate my heart-room,
My aspiration-heart-tears
Are of paramount importance.

26949

The reasoning mind shall doubt God,
Even if He stands right before
The reasoning mind.

26950

The aspiration-heart
Does not have to depend
On God's physical Presence
For authenticity.

26951

The gentle whispers
Of God's Compassion-Eye
Have transformed
My God-doubting mind.

26952

My mind wants to see God
Face to face
For its satisfaction.

26953

My life wants to live inside
God's Heart-Garden
For its satisfaction.

26954

My heart longs to live
At God's Lotus-Feet
For its satisfaction.

26955

Divinity may withdraw
By the time humanity
Awakens to Light.

26956

Each failure
Has a special significance
Behind it.

26957

I tell God
That He does not have to love me
As long as I can love Him.

26958

God tells me
That He abides only
By a give and take policy.

26959

Each self-giving thought
Is, indeed,
A world-peace-pioneer.
