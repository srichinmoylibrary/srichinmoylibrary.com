9830

You will be released
From the reasoning mind
Only after you have started wearing
The badge of silver faith.

9831

I have seen soulful tears
In your eyes.
That means God has definitely
Done something special for you
Inside your heart.

9832

I have nothing to do with
The mind-pendulum
That swings between
An imagination-smile
And a frustration-cry.
What I need is
An overnight transformation-mind.

9833

What is the mind,
If not a conscious fantasy
Of ridiculous and superfluous demands?

9834

He who wants to be
A God-lover and God-server
Can never be satisfied
With a limited supply of patience.

9835

Each day
Each human being
Either consciously or unconsciously
Renews his subscription
To the comfort-pleasure-life.

9836

Each undivine thought
Is a mischief-monarch
That mistreats the friends of the soul:
Love and joy.

9837

His heart is endowed
With hope-strength.
His life is filled
With frustration-blows.

9838

God's Justice-Light
Finds it extremely difficult
To live inside your life.
But His Compassion-Height
Satisfactorily and mightily
Lives inside your heart.

9839

If you need inspiration,
Look at nature
And its beauty.

If you need aspiration,
Dive deep within
To cultivate more purity.

9840

Since you have made your life
A testament of truth,
God will write an introduction
To your life-story.

9841

With your dwarf-mind
You want to understand the world.
With your giant-heart
You are ignoring the world.

9842

Can you not see
That what I am telling you
Is true?
You have built a self-importance-image.
Therefore
God's blessingful Life
Is not and cannot be
For you.

9843

Who can solve the ancient quarrel
Between your mind and your heart,
If not the soul-bird
Inside your aspiration-life?

9844

Your aspiration-life
Does not need permission
To enter into God's Heart-Nest.
It always has a free access.
