15.

You are carrying your life's
Heavy lethargy-load.
Yet you want to walk
On God's Perfection-Road.

16.

If your mind dreams always
Of human desire,
How can your life expect
Liberation-fire?

17.

If you are a lover
Of innocence-light,
How can you travel
In ignorance-night?

18.

Live by your inmost
Soul-sincerity
To become your Lord's
Sun-Reality.

19.

He enjoys his life's
Self-giving marathon.
Therefore, God gives him
His Satisfaction-Dawn.

20.

When his life becomes
All luminosity,
The seeker becomes
God's Heart-profundity.

21.

Your inner faith, the Blessing-Grace
Of the Lord Supreme
To help you quickly grow into
His Self-Transcendence-Dream.

22.

Your wasted years are running out
Of aspiration-time.
Are you content to never see
God's Satisfaction-Clime?

23.

If you read every day
Your mind's guilt-record,
How will you ever think
Of pleasing your Lord?

24.

Patience and tolerance —
Two lessons in life
Which save human souls
From destruction-knife.

25.

A prayer-life
Is a Heavenly passage.
A silence-heart
Is God's Oneness-Message.

26.

O sail your God-hunger-boat,
Every day sail,
Until God's own Hand lifts your
Ignorance-veil.

27.

Alas, you are your clever mind's
Mere aspiration-act.
Therefore, you will ever miss
God's Sweetness-Smile-Contact.

28.

Your silence-heart
Will grant you peace of mind.
Your oneness-soul
Will conquer turmoil blind.

29.

When my mind sees the world,
It sees only God's Face.
When my heart feels the world,
It feels His quick Embrace.
