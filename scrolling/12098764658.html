24030

A tranquillity-mind
Is, indeed, a very rare boon
From God.

24031

There are many ways
To expand our aspiration-heart,
But the easiest way is to imagine
A flower-purity-presence
Inside our heart.

24032

What I need
At every moment
Is a gratitude-expansion-breath.

24033

Soulfully throbbing inspiration
From the heart
Can definitely show us
The way to our destination.

24034

What God is really looking for
Is not our capacity
But our willingness.

24035

Willingness knows
That there is no time to delay.
Everything has to be done at once,
Happily and self-givingly.

24036

Unwillingness thinks
It has plenty of time.
Therefore, it does not
Have to surrender itself to speed.

24037

The mind wants to exhibit
Its towering greatness.
The heart wants to hide
Its flowering goodness.

24038

My surrender-heart
And my life-happiness
Always go together.

24039

Morning dawns.
My life's hope and my heart's promise
Meet together
For a new fulfilment.

24040

Every day
I pray to my Lord Supreme
To help my faith blossom
To its fullest proportions.

24041

Alas, is there any day
That I do not torture
God's Compassion-Eye
And His Forgiveness-Heart?

24042

My life's delight
Never fails to amply reward
My willingness-heart.

24043

May my peace-hope
And my peace-promise
Extend from horizon to horizon.

24044

God will soon make it public
That the disobedience-seekers
Will never, never arrive
At the Goal.
