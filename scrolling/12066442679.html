20815

I am so happy
That at long last I am able
To reveal my heart's
Aspiration-illumination-bridge.

20816

Unless I reduce myself
To humility-zero,
I will not be allowed
In God's ever-transcending Elevator.

20817

The path
To God's Golden Shore
Has to be discovered
And not invented.

20818

If you do not
Bring your mind's ego-trip
To an end,
You will remain always
An aspiration-dedication-dwarf.

20819

Only a heart
Of prayerful and soulful aspiration
Can compose
God-oneness-songs.

20820

My eyes
Rise and rise
To meet
The planet sun.
My heart
Dives and dives
To meet
The knowledge-sun.

20821

God's Obedience-Rules
Are not for the punishment
Of our life,
But for the enlightenment
Of our entire being.

20822

God never wants to end
Your inner conversation with Him.
It is you who allow
Your restlessness-mind
To depart from God's company.

20823

If your mind
Is a genuine truth-seeker
And your heart
Is a genuine God-lover,
Then why do you have to
Pay your visits to God
In secrecy supreme?

20824

If you do not give up
Living in the lap
Of mind-vital-luxury,
Then how can God bless you
With the Smile
Of His Satisfaction-Heart?

20825

Alas, my self-doubt
Is so powerful
That it can easily blow out
My mind's inspiration
And my heart's aspiration-candles.

20826

Compete against
And defeat
Your mind's
Ignorance-darkness-night.

20827

My Lord,
Let me ask You something:
Can I dare to cherish
The hope
That one day I will become
A supremely choice instrument
Of Yours?

20828

When I see the world
With my enthusiasm-mind,
When I feel the world
With my willingness-heart,
I feel that
There is nothing I cannot do
For the world.

20829

The more you love God,
The sooner you will be able
To declare God's Victory
Over your own nature.
