12215

There can be no choice
Between
My life's progress-light
And
My heart's satisfaction-delight.

12216

My heart's obedience-light
Flies with God.
My mind's disobedience-night
Crawls with man.

12217

Unless you fight against yourself
And win,
You cannot escape
Your doubtful and hurtful mind.

12218

Every morning God comes to me
To congratulate me
On my cheerfully surrendered
God-acceptance-life.

12219

You can be another God
The day you bravely challenge
The ceaseless pride
Of your unhappiness.

12220

Since God owns
My heart's aspiration-hunger,
I must disown
My mind's frustration-feast.

12221

Everything that is supremely fruitful
On earth
Is on temporary loan
From Heaven.

12222

Because of your humility-mind
And your purity-heart,
You will before long become
In the inner world
An ultradistance runner
With striking speed.

12223

He who enjoys self-doubt
Is indeed
An insecurity collector.

12224

Awaken
Your heart's faith-soldiers.
It is high time for them
To destroy
Your mind's doubt-demons.

12225

The seeker's
Aspiration-heart-cry-hunger
And God's
Satisfaction-Smile-Meal
Are a oneness-pair.

12226

A Ph.D. may not understand God,
But a kindergarten student
Can at once understand
And reveal God.

12227

Desire means
Emptiness-beggar.
Aspiration means
Fulness-emperor.

12228

O my mind,
Why are you such a fool?
When are you going to make God
Your very first resort?

12229

No soulful aspiration-heart-cry
Will ever fail to reach
God's Compassion-Eye.
