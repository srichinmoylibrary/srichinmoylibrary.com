17975

O sadness,
I do not know why
You came to me.
Is it because I deserve
Your ruthless presence?
Or is it because you are helpless
And it is predestined that you give me
A God-ordained experience?

17976

Where do I live?
I live in between
God's human tears
And God's divine Smiles.

17977

Each self-giving life
Has a permanent passport
To enter and live in
God's Consciousness-Light-Bliss-Realm.

17978

O my sound-mind,
How can I live
With your destruction-frown?

O my silence-heart,
How can I live
Without your fulness-love?

17979

A self-giving life
Is far beyond
The temptation-desire-snare.

17980

God needs from you
Your soul's childlike smile
And your heart's childlike cry
Before He allows you
To breathe in
His universal Breath.

17981

The Lord Supreme tells
Each and every human being
That the strength of sincerity
Knows no finite boundary.

17982

O my stupid mind,
I am urging you
To pay all attention
To your soul's invaluable,
Step-by-step
Nectar-instructions.

17983

Only the heart
Of a genuine God-lover
Can house
God's infinite Vision-Smile.

17984

Each unaspiring human being
Is hopelessly and helplessly lost
Inside his mind's darkness-cave
And his heart's ignorance-breath.

17985

The failures of the past
Want to be your boss.
The frustrations of the present
Want to be your boss.
The visions of the future
Want to be your boss.
The Compassion-Eye of God
Wants to be your boss.
It is you who have to make the choice.
I hope that you make
The right and infallible choice.

17986

Before he entered into
The life of aspiration,
What he had and what he was:
A thunder-mind.
And now,
What he has and what he is:
A flower-heart.

17987

What weighs infinitely more
Than anything in this world?
The frustrations
Of each human being.

17988

God invites only
The sleepless gratitude-souls
To participate in
His Victory-Procession through Heaven.

17989

Each morning and each evening
May I become my heart's
Rainbow-melody-flute.
