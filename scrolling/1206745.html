20901

O seeker,
If you want to remain alive on earth,
Then every day make a solemn promise
To yourself
That you are going to remain alive
Only with your heart's aspiration-cries.

20902

Dive deep within!
You are bound to discover
Your celestial virtues,
And these celestial virtues
You can offer to the world at large
At your sweet will.

20903

I do not want to swim helplessly
In the sea of good intentions.
I want to swim sleeplessly
In the ocean of selfless actions.

20904

God the Lover
Every day, every hour,
Every minute and every second
Uses His Compassion-Broom
To sweep away the dust
Of my dirty, dirtier and dirtiest
Life-mistakes.

20905

O seeker,
Every day pray to God
To bless you with a hope-home.
Only when you live
In your hope-home
Can you proceed
Far, farther, farthest.

20906

Our frustration-clouds
Will disperse
Only when we consciously feed
Our aspiration-flames.

20907

Each time I enter
Into my heart-garden,
I happily and proudly observe
A cluster of aspiration-trees.

20908

It is high time
For each and every human being
To excavate
His long-lost transcendental Self.

20909

Do not be afraid of offering
Your Lord Beloved Supreme
Your desire-shopping-list
Along with
Your aspiration-heart-cries.

20910

God never gives us
Pessimism-permission.
He only blesses us with
Optimism-illumination.

20911

If God answered
All your desire-prayers,
He would be none other than
Your worst possible enemy!

20912

Each truth-seeker and God-lover's
Parting words to Mother-Earth
Should be a prayerful
Gratitude-heart-flower.

20913

O seeker, do you not realise
That nothing is as powerful
And as cruel
In standing against
Your Heavenward journey
As disobedience that repeats itself
Every day in your life?

20914

Those who always think most highly
Of themselves
Are deliberately going away
Far, farther, farthest
From the Satisfaction-Smile
Of the Absolute Supreme.
