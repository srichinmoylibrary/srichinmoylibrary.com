A Soundless Voice

December 31st, 1969

A soundless voice
I always need.
My mind of noise,
My heart of poise
To lovingly feed.

O Frank and Baby Smile

December 31st, 1969

O frank and baby smile,
You have won my heart,
You have conquered death-dart
In life’s confusion-mart.

Your Silence I Sing

December 31st, 1969

Your Silence I sing.
It is my heart.
Your Sound I sing.
It is my mart.

You Claim Me Still

December 31st, 1969

You claim me still,
O ignorance-fool!
Don’t you know that
I have a new school?
I go to wisdom-school
To be a perfection-tool.

I Shall Not Fail

December 31st, 1969

I shall not fail,
I shall not fail.
My soul shall sail,
My heart shall sail.
I shall succeed,
I shall succeed.
My life shall take
The glorious lead.

Why, Why?

December 31st, 1969

Why, why, why, why?
Why should I take
The side of ignorance-tie?
Why, why, why, why?
Why do I fail
To please God’s Compassion-Sky?

To Be, Indeed, a Cosmic God

December 31st, 1969

To be, indeed, a cosmic God,
I shall try only to succeed.
To be, indeed, a real man,
I shall try only to proceed.

Sing Me a Song, My Lord Supreme

December 31st, 1969

Sing me a song,
My Lord Supreme.
I shall give You
My silver dream,
I shall give You
My beauty-sky,
I shall give You
My oneness-tie.

Farewell, Farewell, O Ungrateful World

December 31st, 1969

Farewell, farewell,
O ungrateful world.
So much I have done for you,
So high I have thought of you.
To please you, your all,
I totally forgot
To respond to God’s God-Call.

The Sound-Music of Humanity

December 31st, 1969

The sound-music of humanity
For the poor and feeble souls.
The silence-music of divinity
For the lovers of myriad goals.

One Fallen Flower

December 31st, 1969

One fallen flower
I see and cry;
One fallen soul
I love and die.

In Silence We Converse

December 31st, 1969

In silence we converse,
My Lord and I.
In silence we build each other
A hopeful sky.

I Shall Know More

December 31st, 1969

I shall know more;
Give me a little time.
My heart shall reach,
      Without fail,
God’s God-Satisfaction-Door.
