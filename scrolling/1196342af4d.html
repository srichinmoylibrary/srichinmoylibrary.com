10675

May my heart become
An inner long-distance
Running-hero
On God's Perfection-flooded Road
To become the Heart
Of tomorrow's God.

10676

Each great thought
Is a slow-moving success-train.
Each drop of good will
Is a fast-flying progress-plane.

10677

My Lord,
Do give me anything else
You want to,
But do not give me
A mind weighted down
With transformation-ignoring thoughts.

10678

Sooner or later
Almost all, if not all seekers
Are bitten
By the ugly doubt-dog.

10679

Inside your mind's
Prevailing worry and anxiety,
You are trying to discover
Your heart's silence-flooded
Himalayan security.

10680

Alas, in my mind's
Shopping mall of confusion
I am trying to remember
My heart's long-forgotten
Soul-illumination.

10681

Have you been to God's Door
Recently?
It seems that of late
He is showing much interest
In spreading world peace
And a oneness-world-heart.

10682

One day, quite unexpectedly,
He was breathlessly shocked
To see insufficient funds
In his heart's sincerity-bank.

10683

You want to find
God and God-Truth?
Then go beyond and beyond,
Far beyond the man-made
Confines of the mind.

10684

The human mind cleverly
Wants to know the facts.
The divine heart sleeplessly
Wants to discover the truths.

10685

Since the desire-devil
Is so dear to you,
How can God
Be near to you?

10686

My mind gave me information
About liberation.
My heart is giving me liberation itself,
The liberation that it has
Soulfully received from God Himself.

10687

The name-and-fame game
Is always tainted
By temptation's touch.
This truth is known
By both the possessors
And the observers.

10688

On oneness-road
Only heart-travellers
Are chosen by God
To walk, march and run.

10689

If you have
Two willingness-eyes
And one obedience-heart,
Then you need nothing more.
