11075

When we desire freedom
For ourselves,
We do not realise
That we enter into
A dark and dangerous desire-cave.

11076

Alas,
Where is my searching mind's
Lost purity-flower?

Alas,
Where is my aspiring heart's
Lost divinity-garden?

11077

The only answer
To my earth-existence-question is:
God and I can appreciate
Our mutual beauty's heart
And admire our mutual duty's life.

11078

To become a perfect instrument of God,
The seeker has to feel
That his aspiration-life
Is the ticking of God's Eternity-Clock.

11079

To have a good meditation means
To climb up high
In the sunlit summit-silence-light
And converse with God's
God-Manifestation-Peace on earth.

11080

A God-lover
Is he whose aspiration-heart
Has made friends with
The beauty of the dreaming night
And the duty of the singing day.

11081

Have you decided
To return to your heart-home
Once again
To dine with your
Eternity's best friend:
Soul?

11082

When the mind
Sincerely prays and meditates,
It sees it has the capacity
To journey into another part
Of its existence-reality,
A part that is always
Flooded with peace.

11083

Your future may not be visible,
But your present is visible.
Now pray to God to carry you
To your highest destination
Through the invisible ignorance-night.

11084

The God of Compassion
Will not be able to use your love
If your love does not have in it
Your heart's purity-gratitude.

11085

God is ready to spring a surprise
Every day in my aspiration-life
If I renounce my expectation-mind
Once and for all.

11086

Only a God-focused mind
Can challenge all doubt-storms
And all jealousy-trains.

11087

Confidence can flow
From your heart-river
Only when your mind receives
Compassion-Light from Above.

11088

If yours is a God-forsaken mind,
Then your heart will know the despair
Of truth-broken promises.

11089

If your life swings
On your heart's surrender-swing,
Then God will make you
Another God-King.
