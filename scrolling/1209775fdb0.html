23960

There is no neutrality in life.
Either you are on God's Side,
Or you are on the side of ignorance.

23961

It is humanity's receptivity
That can create the Kingdom of Heaven
Here on earth.

23962

To become a divine warrior,
Just develop and develop
Your sincerity-muscle.

23963

Wisdom
Is to obey the Message
Of the Inner Pilot.
There is no greater wisdom.

23964

If you want to handle your world
With wisdom,
What you need is oneness with God's Will.

23965

Aspiration, dedication,
Devotion and surrender
Are the divine jewels
That comprise your inner treasure.

23966

O God-dreamer,
God's Palace Supreme
Will always house and protect you
Inwardly and outwardly.

23967

What is perfection?
Perfection is liberation.
What is perfection?
Perfection is the manifestation of divinity.

23968

An unaspiring vital,
What is it?
A swinging pendulum
Of feeble insecurity.

23969

The day I surrendered myself completely
To God's Will,
God immediately made me
His Universal Joy-collector.

23970

Like my heart-garden-flowers,
I shall offer gratitude
To my Lord Supreme
Just for being.

23971

Alas,
It is not that I do not want
To please God.
I do want to please Him
In His own Way,
But I do not know how.

23972

No matter where I go,
My willingness-heart
And my Lord's Compassion
I see together.

23973

Our willingness, eagerness
And selflessness
Thrill and please God
Far beyond our imagination's flight.

23974

Our tears are beautiful,
More beautiful, most beautiful —
And sweeter than the sweetest —
When we place them at the Feet
Of our Lord Beloved Supreme.
