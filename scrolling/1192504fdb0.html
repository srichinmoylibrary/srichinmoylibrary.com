6860

Unless you uproot
Your deepest fears,
Your inner fate will remain
In the world of dark misfortune.

6861

My life's self-giving
Is the sweetness
Born of my soul's oneness.

6862

Just do the right thing!
Play the role of a divine seeker
Satisfactorily.
God will definitely help you
Hoist your victory-flag.

6863

Your desire-life knows
The sleep that lasts
For futile centuries.

6864

My Lord Supreme,
You forgive me
And give me another chance,
Not because
I deserve forgiveness,
But because
You want Your entire creation
To be absolutely perfect.

6865

Every day
I may not feel like
Serving others.
Still, I do serve,
Just for the purification
Of my body
And for the satisfaction
Of my soul.

6866

He accepted his life
As a challenge to himself.
He had no outer boss to please,
Only his own inner aspiration.

6867

Truth does not have to be
Always soft.
If necessity demands,
Truth can be harsh as well.

6868

If you fail to attain
Peace,
How will you regain
Your Inner Pilot?

6869

He has studied
In God's Illumination-School.
Therefore
He has vehemently discarded
The mind's division-caste system.

6870

You are your heart's gratitude-breath.
Therefore, every day
God grants you the opportunity
To swim in His Satisfaction-River.

6871

Great
Is the art of beginning.
Good
Is the art of ending.
But for you, best
Is the art of continuing
And transcending.

6872

You are not a fool.
Why then, in your inner life,
Do you not mind
When you run backwards?

6873

The human mind
Is always aiming at some conclusion,
And that conclusion
Is giant confusion!

6874

Because of his inner blindness
And unwillingness to see God's Light,
He is totally denied
The illumining flower-garden
Of his life.
