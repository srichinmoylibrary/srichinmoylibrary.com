54 We Must Brave ...

We must brave
The dire challenges of life
To become God’s
Most perfect instruments.

55 Our Powerful Meditation ...

Our powerful meditation
Is the only food
That God appreciates
Most sincerely
And most blessingfully.

56 There Is Always A Better Way ...

There is always a better way
To love God and serve God,
And we must try to discover
That particular way.

57 My Meditation ...

My meditation
Is my heart’s
God-love-sanctification.

58 Our Oneness-Heart ...

Our oneness-heart
Is God’s
Ecstasy-Dance.

59 God Wants Me To Win ...

God wants me to win
The inner world first
And then to think
Of the outer world.

60 Heaven’s Blessings And Concern ...

Heaven’s Blessings and Concern
I have brought down with me
To be of service
To the earth-planet.

61 Not The Mind’s Fantasy ...

Not the mind’s fantasy,
But the heart’s intimacy
I need.

62 The Privilege ...

The privilege
Of serving God
On a daily basis
In the inner world
And in the outer world
Is unimaginable.

63 My Devotion-Heart ...

My devotion-heart
And God’s Breath
Sing perfectly in tune.

64 If We Love God ...

If we love God
In God’s own Way,
There will be no stop
On our God-journey.

65 The Mind ...

The mind
Takes pride
In its high opinions.

66 The Heart ...

The heart
Surrenders its pride
Utterly and unconditionally
At the Feet of God.

67 If We Want To Work On Earth ...

If we want to work on earth
Soulfully and perfectly,
Then we must keep in touch
With Heaven’s
Constant Compassion and Concern.

68 Our God-Closeness ...

Our God-closeness
We must every day use
To increase
The world-oneness.
