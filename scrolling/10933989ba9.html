40 Do Not Recline, ...

Do not recline,
Do not recline!
If you recline too much,
You are bound to decline
Too soon.

41 God Does Not Hesitate ...

God does not hesitate
To welcome my heart.
Alas, I hesitate
To welcome God's Eye.

42 My Mind, Be Careful! ...

My mind, be careful!
There is every possibility
That soon you will be excommunicated
From God's inner circle.

43 Although It May Not Be A Best-Seller,...

Although it may not be a best-seller,
My heart is an open book
To both God and humanity.

44 God Needs Me ...

God needs me
As an unconditional lover
And not
As an unsolicited adviser.

45 A Devotion-Life ...

A devotion-life
Is a divine
Miracle-performer.

46 May My Mind See All Human Beings...

May my mind see all human beings
As jewels
And my heart feel all human beings
As angels.

47 God Does Not Want Me ...

God does not want me
To improve my past.
He wants me only
To build a perfect future.

48 My God-Surrender-Heart ...

My God-surrender-heart
Is
My God-Paradise-life.

15 December 1999 ...

15 DECEMBER 1999

RIO DE JANEIRO, BRAZIL

49 My Sweet Beloved Supreme, ...

My sweet Beloved Supreme,
Do give my heart the capacity
To love and adore
The golden dust of Your Feet
With every breath.

50 My Supreme, ...

My Supreme,
Only You, only You, only You
I can and I want to claim
As my own, very own.

51 My Supreme, ...

My Supreme,
Please, please bring
My dead aspiration-heart
Back to life.

52 Not God-Speakers ...

Not God-speakers
But God-lovers
Are found in God's
Remembrance-Heart-List.

53 My Lord Has Repeatedly Told Me ...

My Lord has repeatedly told me
That there is no such thing
As a negligible mistake.
Each mistake can be fatal
In the spiritual life.
