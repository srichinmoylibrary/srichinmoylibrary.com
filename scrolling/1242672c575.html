90.

My Lord,
No matter how hard I try,
I cannot surrender to You.
“My child, try, continue trying!
You can and you will.”

91.

Each broken heart
Is a self-pity-hunger.

92.

Humanity and Divinity —
Both wish for
A second chance.

93.

Transformation
   Is not
Our mental hallucination.
   It is an imagination
That will blossom into reality.

94.

Spirituality
Dares to brave
Life’s turbulent hurricanes.

95.

The aspiration-heart knows
What is real love
   And
What is counterfeit love
   For God.

96.

God’s scoldings
Frighten the mind
   And
Illumine the heart.

97.

Ego has
The loudest voice.

98.

Surrender has
The sweetest voice.

99.

At the end of God’s day,
God’s Promises fly and fly
In ecstasy-sky.

100.

At the end of my day,
My hopes sigh and sigh
In bitter disappointment.

101.

I am alive — That is
God’s outer miracle.

102.

I love God — That is
God’s inner miracle.

103.

My Lord,
Will You be
My aspiration-supplier?
“My child, first tell Me,
Will you be
My manifestation-supporter?”

104.

My Lord says to my mind-room:
“I shall come to see you
Only when you are
   Immaculately clean.”
