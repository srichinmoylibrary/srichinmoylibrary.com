22501

Gratitude
Will indeed forever remain
My heart's most valuable
God-blessed asset.

22502

God-realisation
Will definitely be a clear vision
In my days to come.

22503

In the beginning
Hope swims hesitantly
In my heart's
Ever-widening sea.

22504

Disobedience can never
Near God's Throne.
Neither can impurity.

22505

The hour of death
And the arrival
Of ignorance-night
Are unavoidable.

22506

God does not want
My heart to be heavy with secrets,
But He wants my life
To be flooded with smiles.

22507

Like the Heart of God,
I need a heart
That will enjoy
Ignorance-refusal.

22508

My newly blossomed
Gratitude-heart
Has made God eager
To return to my life.

22509

The unaspiring mind
May look sincere,
But is not really so.

22510

O my aspiration-heart,
This world will remember you
For a long time.

O my surrender-life,
This world will remember you
Forever.

22511

Nothing can help me more
Than my gratitude-heart
To reap
My aspiration-harvest.

22512

In the spiritual life
No aspirant must take leave
Of the path of hope
And the path of promise.

22513

God-Satisfaction
In God's own Way:
This message only
My aspiration-heart
Can offer to God.

22514

Nothing remains so distant
As my mind's
Satisfactory transformation.
