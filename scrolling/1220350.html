Agni Sadhak Agni Sadhika Mora

December 31st, 1969

Agni sadhak agni sadhika mora
Maha bharater jagya-bahni ghora
Amara nashibo ama rajanir kaya
Rakhibo sudure maha bandhana chhaya

Chandika Chandika (1)

December 31st, 1969

Chandika chandika
Mahadev pranadhika
Barabhaya jagamata
Asimata amarata

Translation:

Mother-Power of the cosmos vast,
Dearer than the dearest
Life-consort of Lord Shiva.
Your Compassion, birthless and deathless.
O Mother, Infinity You are,
Immortality You are.

Jatra Amar Suru Habe Nil Jamunar Tire

December 31st, 1969

Jatra amar suru habe
Nil jamunar tire
Rakal raja benu diye
Bandhbe dhire dhire

Kanya Kumari Kanya Tumi Je Anadi

December 31st, 1969

Kanya kumari kanya
Tumi je anadi dhanya
Ashesh kripar parama banya
Urdhe nimne ananya

Ami Jagibona Ami Kandibona Ami

December 31st, 1969

Ami jagibona ami kandibona
Ami bandhibona ami maribona
Ami shudhu rabo tomar charane
Prashanti lagi nidra magane

Amrita Kutir Shantir Nir

July 24th, 1981

Amrita kutir shantir nir
Dakiche amai gopane
Ma Alo Devir anadi gabhir
Nachiche amiya nayane
Andhar nishar timir prachir
Hanibo nimeshe nashibo
Bandhibo amar jiban maran
Bhibhur charane bandhibo

Hasi O Kanna Ei Jibaner

December 31st, 1969

Hasi o kanna ei jibaner
Chira lukochuri khela
Kahare dakibo kahare tyajibo
Samukhe amar bhela

Translation:

My tears and my smiles
Are eternally playing hide-and-seek.
I do not know whom to accept
And whom to reject.
My life-boat is carrying
Both my tears and my smiles.

Hiya Sarabare Sudha Santare

December 31st, 1969

Hiya sarabare
Sudha santare
Amiyo sudhar sathe
Snigdha madhur prate
Santaribo
Parinirbane prashphutibo

Translation:

Nectar-delight is floating
In my heart-boat...

Jago Rater Sathi Ghumer Sathi

December 31st, 1969

Jago jago jago jago
Rater sathi ghumer sathi swapan sathi
Premer bhati khamar bale kripar bale
Phutao hiyar shatadale

Translation:

[Partial translation]
Arise, awake,
O friend of my dream-sleep.

Nirabe Janabo Pranati

December 31st, 1969

Nirabe janabo pranati
Nirabe phutabo bhakati
Nirabe sanpibo akuti
Shuno shuno mor minati

Translation:

[Partial translation]
In silence I shall bow to You.
In silence my devotion-flower
Will be blossomed...

Bidhatar lagi manar apaman
Dibe more jani amarar sandhan
