21460

If we are not surrounded
By God's Compassion-Smiles,
Then we are bound to be bombarded
By world-worries and anxieties.

21461

We do not please God
With our mind's fastest speed.
We please God
With our life's brightest perfection.

21462

My heart is a regular
God-dream-lover.
My mind is a constant
God-dream-chaser.

21463

I need a soul
That is eager to love.
I need a heart
That is eager to aspire.
I need a life
That is eager to sacrifice.

21464

Is there any day
That I do not read
My life's progress-bulletin?
Again, is there any day
That I am not
Sorely disappointed by it?

21465

Only an unconditionally surrendered
God-lover
Can and will win
The self-perfection-race.

21466

When I am inside my aspiring heart,
I clearly notice the ascent
Of my own divinity.

When I am inside my doubting mind,
I invariably notice the descent
Of my own divinity.

21467

Try to be a peace-distributor.
If you cannot be a peace-distributor,
Then try to be a peace-lover.
If you cannot be a peace-lover,
Then try to be a peace-dreamer.
If you cannot be a peace-dreamer,
Then at least try to be a peace-thinker.

21468

Only a gratitude-heart
And a surrender-life
Are entitled to walk along
The clear road to Heaven.

21469

A heart of silence does not hide,
But reveals
The nectar-delight of peace.

21470

Lethargy attacks my body
Secretly.
Enthusiasm inspires my heart
Lovingly and dynamically.

21471

Renounce everything else
Save and except
Your heart's streaming tears
And your soul's all-illumining smiles.

21472

My morning meditation is indeed
Heaven's Boon to me.
My evening meditation is indeed
My gift to Heaven.

21473

O seeker,
Once and for all decide
Whether you are going to rule your life
With your heart's faith in God
Or with your mind's doubt of God.

21474

God will love you
If you need Him desperately.
God will need you
If you accept Him breathlessly.
