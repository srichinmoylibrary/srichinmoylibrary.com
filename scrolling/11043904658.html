16 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must aspire first,
Before I love God.
I must love God first,
Before I serve God.
I must serve God first,
Before I manifest God.
This is the only way
I can please God in His own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 September 2000, 12:08 a.m. Before doing 700-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

16 September 2000 (III

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God's Compassion-Eye
Thrills my heart.
God's Forgiveness-Feet
Thrill my heart abundantly more.
The Nectar-dust of God's Feet
Thrills my heart infinitely more.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 September 2000, 12:27 a.m. Before doing 900-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.
