13915

God is all ready to shower
His choicest Compassion-Blessings
On you.
Why are you delaying
In coming to Him
With your weary heart?

13916

A twinkle in God's divine Eye
Can illumine your darker, darkest
Earth-journey
So you can reach
Heaven's immortal Satisfaction-Smile.

13917

My Lord Supreme,
The sweetness and fulness
Of Your divine Eye
Are illumining my long-treasured
Doubting mind.

13918

God does not smash our dreams,
For God Himself has created our dreams
To be the foundation-stones
Of our Himalayan realities.

13919

In the spiritual life
How can you be cruel
If you have a prayerful heart
And a soulful life
As your self-giving friends?

13920

My sweet Lord Supreme,
Save me, save me, save me
From stabbing doubt-agonies!

13921

My heart fondly tells me
To do the right thing.
My mind forcefully compels me
To be the wrong thing.

13922

When I walk along
The sincerity-road,
I become my life's
Capacity-sky.

13923

Every day
My Lord Supreme sits
At the foot of His own
Compassion-Tree,
And I sit
At the foot of my own
Gratitude-tree.

13924

Only the willingness-boat
Carries us
To the fulness-shore.

13925

Mine is the daily morning duty
To invoke
A devotion-prayer-life.

13926

Why are you so sure
That your mind-sound
Will never lose
To your heart-silence?

13927

My greatest gift
To humanity
Is my purity-light-fragrance.

13928

If you are a genuine seeker,
Then you know that
Your prayer-aspirations
Will be fulfilled in God's own Way.

13929

You can lose everything
If you have to.
But do not allow yourself to lose
One thing:
Your hope-heart-boat.
