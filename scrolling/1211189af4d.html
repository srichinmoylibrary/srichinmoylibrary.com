25375

You must expand every day
Your mind's vision-horizon
If constant God-satisfaction you need.

25376

Our heart-enthusiasm
Can easily carry our life
Far beyond
Our mind's depression-prison.

25377

My heart-sky
Is finally empty
Of all mind-clouds.

25378

O my eagerness-heart,
Your devotion-flames
Have beauty unparalleled!

25379

I do not vote for anybody else.
I always vote
For God-Satisfaction-candidate.

25380

The unwillingness-headache
Of the mind
Can only be cured by the heart's
Deepening meditation.

25381

I start my day
With my prayer-songs —
The songs that God Himself
Has taught me.

25382

To my extreme joy,
My heart is now growing
In dynamic selflessness-strength.

25383

Peace-singers automatically
Will get a soulful voice
From God the Supreme Musician.

25384

The silence of meditation-mountain
Gladly and proudly beckons
The God-seeker-heart.

25385

A God-dependence-life
Will be blessed
With God's God-Abundance-Possession.

25386

We shall never arrive
At a point
Where we shall remain
Forever satisfied.

25387

I have made myself
A nest
Only for prayerful
And soulful thoughts.

25388

In which way is Heaven
Better than earth
If Heaven does not hear
The tearful whispers of earth?

25389

Every day
For a truth-seeker and God-lover
Begins with
A God-manifestation-promise.
