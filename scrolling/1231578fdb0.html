Mayer Lila Keha Nahi Bujhte Pare

August 27th, 2001

Mayer lila keha nahi bujhte pare
Dharar buke ase tabu bare bare

Translation:

Nobody understands the Mother’s Cosmic Game.
Yet, everybody comes again and again
And enters into the heart of this world.
