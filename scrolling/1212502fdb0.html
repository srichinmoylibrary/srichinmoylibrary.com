26660

It is God who creates opportunities for us
In boundless measure.
And what else?
He also appreciates us for using them.

26661

When I try to fool someone
In the spiritual life,
I do not realise that I am dealing with
The Real in him — God.

26662

When I give my Lord Supreme
A small quantity of my love and faith,
He blessingfully and proudly tells me:
"My child, I have come.
Receive from Me My All."

26663

I am happy when I see
First my Lord's Eye and then His Feet.
But I am infinitely happier when I see
His Feet first and then His Eye.
God's Eye is my Protection.
God's Feet are my Salvation.

26664

My Lord Supreme,
Do tell me where I am supposed to live
Every day.
"My child,
I want you to live every day
Inside your heart-garden of self-giving."

26665

Each time I disobey God,
Either consciously or unconsciously,
I am forced to take a giant step
Away from God.

26666

Every day I face
My heart-faith-challenges,
And every day I succeed.

26667

Every day increase your love for God.
Then you will see
That the darkness of your mind
Will, without fail,
Be completely dissolved.

26668

What is my heart?
My heart is a dream of God
That every day is increasing
Its beauty, its lustre and its divinity.

26669

My Lord,
I need my heart-freedom first
For the establishment
Of my mind-illumination.

26670

What I need is a mind of patience.
What I need is a heart of poise.
What I need is a life of surrender,
Constant God-surrender.

26671

Secretly the angels guide us,
And proudly they tell God
That we are quite receptive
To God's Will.

26672

You have to compel your mind
To take a long vacation
So that you and your heart
Can work together
For the manifestation of God's Will
On earth.

26673

In the mind-jungle we notice
That gratitude is out of season
All the time.

26674

All your secrets are safe
When you keep them
In your aspiration-heart
And dedication-life.
