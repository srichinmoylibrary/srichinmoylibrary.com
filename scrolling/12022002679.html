16415

My heart's hope-fragrance
Is the supreme inspirer
Of my life's promise-dreams.

16416

Today my soul
Has given me the capacity
To burn up my mind's
Unwillingness-arrogance.

16417

God's Satisfaction-Heart
Is the protection supreme
For all heart-travellers.

16418

Devotion's intensity
Multiplies
The heart's aspiration-flames.

16419

Obedience-heart-flowers
Have no
Disobedience-mind-thorns.

16420

May I find
My days and nights
Between my soul's dynamism
And my heart's eagerness.

16421

O my life's empty dreams,
Can you not see
That you are carrying me
To nothingness-land?

16422

Indeed,
There is an unbridgeable chasm
Between my mind's hallucination
And my heart's illumination.

16423

O my poor life,
Abandon, abandon immediately
All your failure-thoughts
And see
The first dawn of reality.

16424

Impossibility-frontiers,
Every day
I shall vehemently
Push you back
Farther and farther.

16425

Self-doubt
Will take you
To the danger-path,
And there you will meet
With stark disaster.

16426

Liberation you will get
At God's choice Hour.
Rest assured,
It will not come too late!

16427

A purity-heart can enjoy
A small foretaste
Of celestial peace-delight.

16428

Is there any day
When I am not embarrassed
By the harassment
Of my mind?

16429

I do not totally abhor darkness,
For there is definitely
Some light
Even in darkness.
