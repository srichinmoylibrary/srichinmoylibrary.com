11175

I am asking God
To forgive me.
God is asking me
To receive Him first.

11176

God wants you to surrender
To Him
Both your mind's ignorance-imperfection
And your heart's wisdom-aspiration.

11177

The bond of oneness,
The aspiring heart is.
The division of mutual aloofness,
The doubting mind is.

11178

Your mind may not be
Tired of seeing
The deception on your face,
But your heart is not only
Tired of it
But also mortified by it.

11179

The human mind desires
The pride of liberty.
The divine heart treasures
The beauty, charm and fruitfulness
Of liberty.

11180

There was a time
When my mind was attached
To temptation-greed.
But now my mind is devoted
To perfection-hunger.

11181

Once you have sown faith-seed
Inside your heart-garden,
You are bound to be supremely happy,
For you will have the capacity
To go far beyond
The confines of reason.

11182

The seeker can easily be blessed
By the illumination and protection-wings
Of a God-sent messenger
If the seeker can sleeplessly live
Inside the genuine core
Of his aspiration-faith.

11183

Do not be afraid of a sharp turn
At every corner of your life!
Do not forget that life
Is a constant challenge,
And only a hero-warrior
Can and will win
On its battlefield.

11184

Dream not
Of hopes for tomorrow.
Live only in today's
Promise-fulfilment-determination.

11185

Hope is sweet.
Hope is illumining.
Hope is fulfilling.
Hope can be everlasting.
Therefore, do not give up hope
Even in the sunset of your life.

11186

Poor mind!
It suffers from
Unproductive worries and anxieties.
Yet it will not welcome or invoke
Infinity's tranquility,
For unconsciously it cherishes
The powerful blows of anxiety and worry.

11187

The darkness of the thought-world
Every human being has seen,
But the light of will power
Very few people can invoke.

11188

Why do you need a diploma
For your virtues,
Since God is eagerly waiting
To place you on His own Throne
And bless you with His own Crown?

11189

The eternal grievances of mankind
Can be illumined
Only when this world is blessed
With a oneness-heart of purity.
