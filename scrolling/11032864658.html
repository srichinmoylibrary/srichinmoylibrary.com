22 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please bless me and my heart
With a confidence-umbrella
So that we can easily walk
Through our mind's doubt-storms.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 February 1999, 7:43 a.m. Before lifting 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 280 lbs.)

22 February 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You allow me to breathe with You
Simultaneously
Only when my life
Is sleepless self-giving.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 February 1999, 7:46 p.m. Before using double-arm machine. New record: 140 lbs. — with each arm separately from a seated position.

23 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My God-oneness-life
Entirely depends on
My heart's God-devotion-tears.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 February 1999, 8:31 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. one-hundred and three times with one arm. (50 right, 51 left in three sets, plus once each arm to maximum extension)

23 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The eye that sees beauty
In every human being
And in every creation of God's
Is, indeed, a very special pride of God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 February 1999, 8:42 a.m. Before lifting 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 280 lbs.)

23 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O my aspiring heart, smile, smile, smile!
If you smile,
You will be able to transform
All the obstacles that the doubting mind
Is creating for you.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 February 1999, 8:55 p.m. Before using double-arm machine.

24 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall make all my golden dreams
Come true
By sleeplessly serving God the creation.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 February 1999, 9:29 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

24 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May I cry for You every day,
Every hour, every minute
And every second
With my heart's pure sincerity-tears.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 February 1999, 9:41 a.m. Before lifting 150 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 300 lbs.) Equal to personal best set on 13 June 1988 at age 56.

26 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I feed my intense God-hunger
With my streaming
Devotion-heart-tears.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 February 1999, 8:01 a.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 130 lbs. with both arms simultaneously from a seated position.

26 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am extremely grateful to You
For telling me
That my climbing heart-cries
Gladden Your Heart immensely.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 February 1999, 8:20 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

26 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To place myself at Your Lotus-Feet
My tearful heart cries and cries
And my soulful eyes smile and smile.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 February 1999, 8:33 a.m. Before lifting 150 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 300 lbs.)

15 March 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I know, I know,
If I do not feed my God-hunger
With my devotion-heart,
Then my spiritual life will,
Without fail,
End in vain.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 March 1999, 5:02 a.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously from a seated position. (after returning from an 11-day trip to France, Slovakia, the Czech Republic, Hungary and South Africa)

15 March 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord happily and immediately hires
The God-readiness, God-willingness
And God-eagerness-server.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 March 1999, 5:21 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

15 March 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May all human beings live
Smiling, singing and dancing
Their Heavenly oneness-life
Here on earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 March 1999, 5:37 a.m. Before lifting 155 lbs. with both arms simultaneously (Total: 310 lbs.) New record.

16 March 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have come to realise
That in my spiritual life
My enthusiasm without my soulfulness
Is incomplete.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 March 1999, 6:03 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with one arm. (multiple repetitions plus once each arm to maximum extension)

16 March 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord's Newness-Dream
Is my life's progress-reality.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 March 1999, 6:19 a.m. Before lifting 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 320 lbs.) New record.
