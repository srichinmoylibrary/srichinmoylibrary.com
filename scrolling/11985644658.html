12830

One God in the heart
Is infinitely more important
Than an infinite number of gods
In the mind.

12831

I have done it,
I have finally done it!
I have silenced the nightmare
Of the ruthless past.

12832

Because you are
A true God-lover,
You can easily leap
Over the future's fence.

12833

You can escape from me,
But you cannot escape
My heart's fragrance-smile.

12834

When will you be ready?
God has been waiting and waiting for you
With His unconditional Blessing-Light!

12835

You will not be able
To do anything great
Either for yourself
Or for mankind
If you keep depression and frustration
As your bodyguards.

12836

I want to remain
For the rest of my life
In the world of self-offering.
Therefore, I have applied
For a permanent visa
From the world above.

12837

If you fail to weed
Your mind's impurity-garden daily,
How can you ever be truly happy?

12838

Be brave!
Do not allow your mind
To collapse
Under heavy depression-thought-weight.

12839

Sooner or later
Every human being
Has to graduate
From God's Heart-Academy.

12840

Temptation:
A quick, cruel and inescapable
Destruction-dance.

12841

To outsmart your ignorance-opponent,
You need a permanent friendship
With God's Compassion-Eye.

12842

What I constantly need
Is a doubt-free faith-mind.
What I sleeplessly need
Is a fear-free happiness-heart.

12843

Every day without fail
You must press charges
Against the temptation-culprit.

12844

Think whatever you want to think.
Do whatever you want to do.
Say whatever you want to say.
But do not divulge poor God's
Sacred Whisper-Secrets.
