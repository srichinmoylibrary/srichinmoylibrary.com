12245

It requires the wisdom-light
Of the heart
To enjoy the perfection-delight
Of the soul.

12246

It requires a giant exercise
Of faith
To manifest the oneness-bond
Of man with God.

12247

Because you are no longer
Restrained by self-doubt,
God's Satisfaction-Heart-Joy
Is swirling around your life.

12248

Each day is a new opportunity
For my mind's fear
To depart
And for my heart's strength
To arrive.

12249

To escape
The great grip of frustration,
Every day you and your heart
Must soulfully open new frontiers.

12250

Disobedience is happy
Only when
It astronomically multiplies
The ignorance-night
Of each and every human being.

12251

If you want to reach
Divinity's summit,
Then you must use sleeplessly
The wings of your heart's
Aspiration-bird.

12252

Devotion has the capacity
To purify the vital,
Illumine the mind
And
Simplify the life.

12253

You will be able to come out
Of the sea of insecurity
If you devotedly obey
The whisper-call
Of your Inner Pilot.

12254

How can your life's manifestation-tree
Have leaves
If your heart's inner cry
Is not genuine?

12255

Water your heart's faith-garden
Faithfully every day
If you do not want to develop
Doubt-blight
In your unaspiring mind.

12256

If you want
Your aspiration and expectation
To go together,
Then you must be prepared
To fight constantly
Against the attack of frustration-wolf.

12257

The inner life teaches us
How to be detached
From the desire-life
And how to be attached
Only to God,
The Compassion Supreme.

12258

Before you pray and meditate,
Try to see
That there is no failure-thought
In your mind's silence-room.

12259

I call it
My aspiration-heart.
God calls it
His Dream-Fulfilment-Smile.
