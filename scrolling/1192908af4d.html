7275

God the Compassion-Producer
Is the only Owner
Of my life.

7276

I have been meaning to tell God
That I am truly fed up
With my desire-life,
And I am all ready
To please Him
Even beyond His Imagination.

7277

Alas, he has not read correctly
The God-posted signs.
Therefore, he is now misled
By the world's lower nature.

7278

My Lord,
You have the time
To break my life.
Do give me not only the time
But also the capacity
To remake my life.

7279

God sang before him
When he climbed up
The outer Himalayas.
Now that he has climbed up
The inner Himalayas,
God is asking him
To dance with Him.

7280

Alas,
He is simply frightened to death.
The crowning glories of his outer life
Are too much for him.

7281

God ignores me when I go to Him
As a beggar.
God treasures me when I go to Him
As a lover.

7282

There is no difference between
My life's blossoming progress
And my heart's lasting satisfaction.

7283

God has listened to me.
He has forgiven my failures.
But alas,
I am not listening to God!
In vain He has been asking me
To forget my failures.

7284

When you realise that you are
Your soul's divinity,
You will be able to free yourself
From your mind's ego-stupidity.

7285

Since you are a sincere candidate
For your life's total transformation,
God's powerful Satisfaction
Shall be all yours.

7286

Your heart's boundless love
And your life's fathomless peace
Shall always live together
In God's stupendous Vision-Reality.

7287

Do not give up!
You are brave.
You must continue
Your yesterday's pilgrimage.

7288

If you lead the desire-life,
Then you are bound
To be haunted
By hopelessness-ghost.

7289

When the doubt-storm
Disappears,
Perfection-delight
Nears.
