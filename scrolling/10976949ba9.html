30 January 2006

Penang, Malaysia

41 Wrong Thinking ...

Wrong thinking
Is nothing short of
Poison-drinking.

42 God ...

God
Protects my heart
From wrong feeling.

43 Life ...

Life
Has to be surcharged
With gratitude
Every day.

44 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
Do not surrender to despair!
Start your spirituality
All over again.

45 We Must Put ...

We must put
A strong accent
On self-giving.

46 We Must Not Be Bound ...

We must not be bound
By our mind’s
Self-styled whims.

47 My Happy Moments ...

My happy moments
Are
God’s dancing Hours.

48 May My Sweet Hopes ...

May my sweet hopes
Be faithful.

49 May My Powerful Promises ...

May my powerful promises
Be fruitful.

50 The Price ...

The price
Of name and fame
Is astronomical.

51 The Mind Needs ...

The mind needs
Flexibility
More than anything else.

31 January 2006 ...

31 January 2006

Penang, Malaysia

52 Every Morning And Evening ...

Every morning and evening
I go to God’s Surrender-School
To study.

53 My Soul Always Passes ...

My soul always passes
God’s Examinations
With flying colours.
