17875

An unaspiring human being thinks
That God lives only in Heaven.
An aspiring human being thinks,
Feels and knows
That God is everywhere.
This definitely includes hell.

17876

I thank God
When He gives me
Something powerful.
God thanks me
When He sees in me
Something beautiful.

17877

It is not our prayers
That move God's Heart.
It is our prayer-tears
That move God's Heart
Plus feed God's Breath.

17878

God's universal Eye
Not only watches me
But also teaches me
To be a perfect citizen
Of the world.

17879

If we do not end
Our desire-party,
Then how can we be invited
By God's Illumination-Paradise?

17880

Be careful
If you do not want to walk
Side by side
With your lethargy's gigantic pride!

17881

If your life is devoid of resolve,
You will not be able to solve
Any problem that you encounter.

17882

If you are not afraid of seeing
The ashes of your mind,
Then you are bound to succeed
In your inner aspiration-life.

17883

I am happy
Only because I am a seeker
Of sacred silence-light.

17884

The division-mind
Either today or tomorrow
Has to surrender
To the heart's oneness-reality.

17885

I must illumine my mind!
How long am I willing to suffer
From my mind's tyranny?

17886

As long as man cherishes
His mind's division-capacities
And qualities,
He will have endless quarrels
With God
In the inner world.

17887

My aspiration-heart
Is my passport
To visit God's dream-filled
Reality-worlds.

17888

During our brief sojourn on earth,
We must prayerfully and soulfully
Reserve our hearts
For God alone.

17889

The songs of our hearts
Are so sweet and pure
That they can reach
Heaven-Delight-Heights.
