14915

My meditation-fountain
Quenches my heart's
Aspiration-thirst.

14916

The outer world-voice
Is so easy to hear.
The inner world-voice
Is extremely difficult to hear
Because my body, vital and mind
Are at constant war
Inside my silence-heart-life.

14917

My Lord Supreme,
My willingness-capacity to serve You
Is the only good quality that I have
In my aspiration-life.

14918

Sweetness, soulfulness and fruitfulness
Have dawned in your eyes
Because you have made
Your unconditional surrender to God.

14919

The mind's complaint against the heart
Is a birthless and deathless story.
The heart's complaint against the mind
Is a rare experience-song.

14920

My heart's gratitude-flame
Has the capacity to hasten
My life's inner cry
And my life's outer smile.

14921

Do you know what God's
First morning thought is?
He wants to see a soulful smile
From each and every human being.

14922

My Lord is asking me
To devotedly wait
For the rainbow-dawn
Of a new world.

14923

God does not believe in
Slave-surrender.
He believes only in
Cheerfulness-surrender.

14924

A division-mind
Will imprison you.
An aspiration-heart
Will liberate you.
A perfection-life
Will immortalise you.

14925

Inner faith in God
Is the very beginning
Of stupendous victory-delight.

14926

A heart of sleepless surrender
Is the treasure-house
Of God's endless, sacred Blessings.

14927

Sweetness-forgiveness has dawned
Upon your life,
Because you are sleeplessly pleasing God
In God's own Way.

14928

God does not want you
To enjoy lethargy anymore.
He wants you to be dynamic
In outer self-giving
And inner God-becoming.

14929

I have made it a point
To soulfully brighten
My heart's self-giving lamp.
