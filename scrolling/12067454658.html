20930

The mind-doubters
Can be silenced
Only by the silence
Of the soul.

20931

He who thrives on challenges
Can accomplish extraordinary things
In life.

20932

To withstand life's turbulence,
What you need
Is the confidence-poise
Of the soul.

20933

Hesitation looms large
Only when
God-fulfilment-commitments
Are fast asleep.

20934

God loves you,
God treasures you
Precisely because
You are His sleepless ignorance-fighter.

20935

If you are obedient
To your Inner Pilot,
Then without fail
You will walk, run and sprint
Along the path
Of perpetual sunshine-bliss.

20936

I like to cherish
My Lord's Immensity-Pride
And not my mind's petty pride
Or my heart's haughty pride.

20937

Temptation, you may need me,
But that does not mean
I have to need you.
What I need is illumination.
I am awaiting the express arrival
Of illumination.

20938

If you can only feel
What God thinks of you,
Then your mind's failure-thoughts
Will immediately vanish.

20939

Nobody is compelled by God
To participate
In life's misery-flooded
Desire-drama.

20940

Man the dreamer
And God the Lover
Are always searching
For each other.

20941

I wish to be found always
In between
My mind's Infinity-dreams
And my heart's Immortality-realities.

20942

A heart of God-surrender
And a life of God-gratitude
Are chosen by God Himself
To fly in His own Ecstasy-Sky.

20943

God definitely wanted you
To be His prince,
But you chose to remain
A street-beggar.

20944

Life itself
Is not a disappointment,
But what you expect from life
Often leads to
A series of disappointments.
