29 Lengthen And Strengthen ...

Lengthen and strengthen
Your heart-wings
If you want to fly
All over the world.

30 Satisfaction Lies ...

Satisfaction lies
Only between my heart’s cries
And my Lord’s Smiles.

1 January 2005 ...

1 January 2005

Qingdao, China

31 The Fragrance Of My Heart ...

The fragrance of my heart
Is
My morning God-satisfaction.

32 No More Do I Have ...

No more do I have
The mind-knowledge-thirst.
I am all my heart-hunger
For the golden dust
Of my Lord’s Feet.

33 We Can Open Up Earth’s Heart ...

We can open up earth’s heart
And Heaven’s Heart
All at once
If we can breathe in God’s Victory
At every moment.

34 Fear Hides ...

Fear hides,
Only to arrest and capture us
At its choice time.

35 May My Heart Long ...

May my heart long
For one brief glimpse
Of my Lord Supreme.

36 Never Forget ...

Never forget
Your patience-exercise.
All other exercises
You can forget,
But not your patience-exercise.

37 Justice-Light ...

Justice-Light
Is God’s
Absolutely last Choice.

38 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That He is eager
To be the slave
Of my surrender-joy.

39 To Be A Genuine ...

To be a genuine
God-seeker,
No price is too great.

40 A Path-Finder ...

A path-finder
Is, indeed,
A God-binder.

3 January 2005 ...

3 January 2005

Qingdao, China

41 God’s Blissful Eye ...

God’s blissful Eye
God unreservedly shares
With the world.
