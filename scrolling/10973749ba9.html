41 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I have only one prayer:
Please give me
Two Heaven-flying
God-obedience-wings.

42 Earth-Bound ...

Earth-bound
The mind travels.

43 Heaven-Free ...

Heaven-free
The heart flies.

3 January 2006 ...

3 January 2006

Kuantan, Malaysia

44 May My Aspiration-Heart ...

May my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
Become
Inseparable friends.

45 God Forgives ...

God forgives
Our ill-disciplined life,
But He never forgets it.

46 A Sincere Seeker ...

A sincere seeker
Never asks God
For a leave of absence.

47 If Unwillingness ...

If unwillingness
Does not accept opportunity,
Life becomes a failure.

48 When My Mind ...

When my mind
Does not obey
My Lord Supreme,
The tears of my soul
Flood my heart.

49 The Seeker’s First ...

The seeker’s first
And foremost duty:
God-acceptance
In God’s own Way
At every moment.

50 My Hopes ...

My hopes
And my dreams
Adorn my heart-garden.

51 A God-Feet-Surrender-Life ...

A God-Feet-surrender-life
Is compulsory
Until we breathe our last.

52 To Oversleep God’s Special Hour ...

To oversleep God’s special Hour
Is, indeed, a painful
But common experience
In a seeker’s life.

53 God Applauds ...

God applauds
When we cry.
God applauds
When we smile.
Is there any time
When God does not applaud,
No matter what we do?

4 January 2006 ...

4 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia
