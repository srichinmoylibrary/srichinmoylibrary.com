23015

I want to tell God
Once more
How great I am.
God wants to hear from me
Once more
That I need the breath
Of surrender-life.

23016

Alas,
You have no aspiration.
Yet
Your mind is ever rich
In expectation.

23017

My Lord's Eyes become
A Beauty-Fragrance-Garland
When I claim God
As my very own.

23018

A God-offering heart
Can neither be daunted
Nor haunted.

23019

A heart of sorrow
Comes into existence
From desire-tragedies
Of the past.

23020

At times
God lives inside
My determination-mind.
At times
God lives inside
My dedication-life.
But I would like
God to be always inside
My aspiration-heart.

23021

O seeker,
Are you such a fool
That you have to allow
The negative forces
To admire and choose
Your mind?

23022

O my soul,
To you I owe everything,
For constantly
You are inspiring
My very life-breath.

23023

A self-giving heart
Is a God-fulfilling
Earth-representative.

23024

Alas, my mind weeps
A river of tears
When my desire-life
Is not daily fulfilled.

23025

My vital longs to live
To enjoy its world-supremacy
Indefinitely on earth.
My heart wants to live
To enjoy its God-intimacy
Permanently on earth.

23026

Today,
Alas, I have given God
A severe headache
By telling Him that my spiritual life
Has sadly failed.

23027

A cheerful and eager
God-surrender-heart
Has the fastest speed
To arrive at the God-Destination.

23028

When the aspiring heart
Is misunderstood,
The seeker's breath
Will automatically be tormented.

23029

How can you be happy
When none of your deeds
Mirror any of your soul-dreams?
