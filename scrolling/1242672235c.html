105.

My Lord says to my heart-room:
“I shall come to see you
Only when you are
   Absolutely pure.”

106.

When I go to God
   Walking very slowly,
God opens and closes His Eyes
   At long intervals,
Heaving heavy sighs.

107.

When I go to God
   Whistling,
God eagerly waits for me
With His open Arms.

108.

We can turn
Each pure thought
Into an earth-fulfilling opportunity.

109.

God pushes and pulls
The unwilling.

110.

God protects and leads
The willing.

111.

Two things do not ran short:
My Lord’s Compassion
And my doubts.

112.

An ignorance-life
Sees no exit-door.

113.

May every day be
My heart-victory-day.

114.

May every day be
My mind-defeat-day.

115.

God does not take any intermission
From His Heaven-day and earth-night
   Performances.
I must do the same
To prove that I am
   His beloved child.

116.

O curiosity-consumed
God-miracle-watchers,
God-realisation will never
Befriend you.

117.

God believes me
Whenever I say anything to Him.
I disbelieve God
Whenever He says anything to me.
What an irony of fate!

118.

My mind ridicules God,
My heart loves God
And I question God.

119.

The moment I tell God
That I do not need Him,
He immediately hurls
His disappointment-arrows
   At me.
