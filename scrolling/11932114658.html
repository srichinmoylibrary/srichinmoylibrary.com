7530

If you do not fulfil God
In God's own Way,
Frustration will be stamped
All over your life.

7531

Each human being
Has two saviours:
An aspiration-heart
And a dedication-life.

7532

Despair-malady
Desperately needs
Promise-remedy.

7533

God does not want you
To waste
His Vision-fulfilling Time.

7534

Desire-life
Is a deathless journey
To a nothingness-shore.

7535

Life's barren
Desire-pursuit
Is his sole identification.

7536

I do not want to know
What is on your mind.
I only want you
To make me feel
What is inside your heart.

7537

Are you a spiritual Master?
If so, every day
You have to keep yourself ready
To be kicked
By the world's ingratitude.

7538

From today on
God wants you
To keep your heart's door unlocked
For the human to disappear
And the divine to appear.

7539

The power of silence
Has saved my life
Time and again.

7540

God's Justice
Is cheap.
God's Compassion
Is cheaper.
God's Forgiveness
Is the cheapest.

7541

If you are resigned
To failure,
What can poor God do
For you?

7542

Banish alien thoughts
If you really want
Only God-thoughts
To occupy your mind.

7543

My heart is learning
To love God the Justice
More, and ever more.

7544

This time
I am determined
Not to renew
My lethargy-license.
