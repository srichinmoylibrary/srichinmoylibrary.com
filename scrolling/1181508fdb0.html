8560 Only One Pure Heart

To serve mankind
You need only two clean hands.
To serve God
You need only one pure heart.

8561 Two Monsters Of Waste

A doubtful mind
And a fearful heart
Are indeed
Two monsters of waste.

8562 Your Earthly Role

Self-contempt will cling to you
Unless you become aware
Of your earthly role:
You are Eternity's pilgrim
On Immortality's road.

8563 God's Choice Instrument

If you allow your mind
To be chained to time,
How can you be God's choice instrument
In the heart of mankind
Or even in the Heart of God?

8564 Only One God-Trodden Path

There is only one God-trodden path,
And that path lies
Inside your heart,
Not before your eyes.

8565 If You Keep Your Heart-Eye Veiled

If you keep your heart-eye veiled,
Naturally you will be pierced
By the wild shafts
Of darkness-night.

8566 His Inner Glimpse Of God's Smile

His heart of love
And his life of service
Are always guided
By his inner glimpse
Of God's outer Smile.

8567 A Ruined Paradise

When the heart does not listen
To the dictates of the soul,
The heart is bound to become
A ruined paradise.

8568 Laugh At Yesterday's Despair-Night

How can you proceed today
If you do not laugh
At your yesterday's despair-night?

8569 A Visible Aspiration-Flame

Unless I become
A visible aspiration-flame
In the depth of my heart,
An invisible frustration
Will torture my life.

8570 God Will Cheerfully Allow You

If you are silent
And uncomplaining,
God will cheerfully allow you
To join Him
In His own eternal Hunger
For self-transcendence.

8571 Family Of Sighs

Fear, doubt and worry
Will have to leave eventually
With their family of sighs.

8572 The Universal Burden-Bearer

You do not have to run away
From yourself.
God not only will carry your burden
But He will also carry you,
For He knows that He alone
Is the universal Burden-Bearer.

8573 A Questioning Mind

A questioning mind
Does not want to perceive.
A questioning heart
Does not want to receive.
A questioning life
Does not want to achieve.

8574 O Fearful Heart

O fearful heart, believe me!
This world is not as undivine
As you think.
This mind is not as important
As you think.
Heaven is not as remote
As you think.
