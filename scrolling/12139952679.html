615.

My life is blessed because
My God-manifestation-dream
Has now become
An unmistakable reality.

616.

The power
Of each God-fulfilling breath
Will always remain
Unfathomable.

617.

Every day
God enters into my heart-garden
To collect
My gratitude-heart-flowers.

618.

You are indispensable to God
Only if you feel
That you are dependent upon God
At every moment.

619.

An unaspiring mind
Is nothing other than
A complete failure
In God's Cosmic Game.

620.

A self-giving heart
Is indeed a blessingful gift
From the highest Heaven.

621.

May the fragrance-heart of Mother-Earth
Every day purify
My heart, my mind, my vital and my body.

622.

The wisdom of a oneness-heart
Is undoubtedly
Sweeter than the sweetest honey.

623.

To please God
Cheerfully
And to love God
Unconditionally
Is to satisfy and fulfil God
Completely.

624.

Is there any mighty soul
Whose life has not suffered
From sad experiences?

625.

There is no need of rest
For a truly God-serving life.
There is no need of rest
For a truly God-fulfilling heart.

626.

God's Compassion is ever ready
To help me meet Him
Inside His Heart-Home.

627.

When I please God
In His own Way,
He blessingfully invites me
To play in His Heart-Garden.

628.

What can silence your mind?
Your heart can.
Just give your heart a real chance.

629.

To reach the Highest
You must unconditionally
Help the lowest.
