17145

My life's progress is slain
Because I am playing
With my mind's lethargy-chain.

17146

O seeker,
Open your heart-door!
God's Compassion-Eye
Is extremely eager
To visit you.

17147

The heart never forgets
The golden dreams
That are going to transform
The seeker's entire life.

17148

Now that my old friend frustration
Has gone,
I can have a totally new friend:
Hope,
Hope illumining and hope fulfilling.

17149

O my mind, be wise!
Never loosen your connection
With your heart-wisdom-light-teacher.

17150

The wall between
The body and the soul
Is not thick.
It is thin, plus transparent.

17151

I have the capacity
To make God sing and sigh.
When I say God is for me,
He sings.
When I say God is not for me,
He sighs.

17152

I am not afraid
Of God's Omnipotence-Hands.
But I am afraid
Of God's Omnipresence-Tears.

17153

The denial of life is so easy.
The full acceptance of life
For the manifestation of God
Is a task infinitely more difficult
Than you can ever imagine.

17154

May my gratitude-heart
Be based on my purity-life
To please my Lord Supreme
Always in His own Way.

17155

Forgive the tormenting past.
You will be happy.
Forget the tormenting past.
You will have every chance
To be a perfect instrument of God.

17156

Be not bound
By your mind's
Feeble beliefs.
Be bound
By your heart's
Strong convictions.

17157

God always loves
Our prayers,
Even our mind's
Feeble prayers.

17158

How can a man ascend
To his perfect perfection-height
If he does not have the courage
To stand all by himself
When the occasion demands?

17159

The mind is proud of itself
When it is filled with facts.
God is proud of the mind
When it is empty of thoughts.
