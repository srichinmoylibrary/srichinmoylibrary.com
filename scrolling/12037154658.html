17930

The mind's willingness
Is always treasured
By the heart's openness.

17931

It takes a man of courage
To enjoy
Earth-adventure-journey.

17932

In the morning
I pray
To God's Compassion-Eye.

In the evening
I meditate
On God's Forgiveness-Heart.

17933

This world of ours
Has only two realities:
Sadness
And
Madness.

17934

Alas, I do not know
If the need for peace
Will ever be on the increase
In the human mind.

17935

O my mind,
When will you stop
Allowing your kingdom
To be ruled
By ruthless ignorance-night?

17936

The golden smile
Of Heaven
And the silver gratitude
Of earth
Are extremely fond of each other.

17937

Alas, each human life
Is either consciously
Or unconsciously bound
By unlimited expectation-chains.

17938

A surrender-life
Ascends.
A gratitude-heart
Expands.

17939

A heart of faith
Sees
No mind of doubt
Anywhere!

17940

Temptation and attachment
Are two members
Of life's insecurity-team.

17941

True, a mind of sincerity
Is rare.
But rarer still
Is a heart of purity.

17942

I do not want to part with either of
My most precious possessions:
Two helpless eyes
And one hopeless heart.

17943

Where do I live?
I live in my sweet hope-home
And nowhere else.

17944

Each thought is uncertain,
Each action is uncertain,
Life itself is uncertain.
Alas, everything on earth
Is doomed to be uncertain.
