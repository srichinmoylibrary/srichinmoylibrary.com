God wants me to smile,
Bright, brighter, brightest,
To please Him.

God wants me to cry,
Soulfully and ceaselessly,
To please Him.

Of the heart,
There should be
No falsehood.

To God in Heaven.

Only with our hollow promises.
God wants us to fulfil
Our promises.

Is not enough.

Whether it is human love
Or divine love.
