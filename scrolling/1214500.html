1101.

God's Compassion
Every day descends
To water the blossoms
Of my heart-garden.

1102.

No matter what we think,
No matter what we say,
God enjoys watching
Our sincere attempts
To eradicate our ego.

1103.

O my mind,
You must know by this time
That God never appreciates
Your self-styled isolation
From world-society.

1104.

Life is not a heavy burden
Of unfulfilled desires,
But an ignorance-challenging promise
Of God-fulfilment.

1105.

When God and God's Smile
Open your aspiration-heart,
You can rest assured
Your complete God-satisfaction-day
Is fast approaching.

1106.

I enjoy my Mother-Earth's
Endless beauty
Infinitely more than I enjoy
My Father-Heaven's
Ceaseless power.

1107.

O my Lord Supreme,
Do make my heart an expert
At aspiration-multiplication.

1108.

An implicit obedience
To the dictates of the Inner Pilot
Is the first duty of a God-lover.

1109.

O my stupid mind,
It is high time for you to become
A world-peace-maker
Instead of remaining
A world-grudge-holder.

1110.

When I take my heart to God
For repairs,
He gives it His immediate Attention.

1111.

If you say that your surrender
Is a skill you have developed,
You are totally mistaken.
Your surrender is a Delight-Gift
From God Himself.

1112.

When God dictates,
My soul takes notes,
My heart watches,
My mind sleeps and snores.

1113.

No matter how hard I try
To obliterate my deplorable past,
My past remains
My present ghost-torture.

1114.

Shameless temptation
Beckoned my heart,
But my heart wisely
And immediately declined.
