Maner Baner Dukher Sukher

January 8th, 1991

Maner baner dukher sukher
Kanna amar thamena

Translation:

Inside my mind-forest
My heart-suffering-cries never stop.

Inside My Mind-Forest

January 8th, 1991

Inside my mind-forest
My heart-suffering-cries
Never stop, never stop, never stop.

At the end of the day,
