55 My Life Is All God-Service ...

My life is all God-service.
I do things not to magnify myself.
God says to magnify me
Is His Task.

56 We Have The Right To Choose ...

We have the right to choose.
We must choose
Wisely.

57 Our Inner Reality ...

Our inner reality
Is the Smile
Of God-Beautyís Dream.

15 January 2006 ...

15 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

58 I Must Know That It Is ...

I must know that it is
Only for my good
That God does not answer
My questions.

59 My Irregular Devotion ...

My irregular devotion
God does not appreciate.

60 My Mind And My Vital ...

My mind and my vital,
Please do not make
My Lord Supreme angry.
He is all Love for you.

61 Every Day I Pray To God ...

Every day I pray to God
To be fully in control
Of my life.

62 Forgetfulness ...

Forgetfulness
Is, at times,
A great blessing.

63 This World ...

This world
Is not interesting
But instigating.

64 Everything I Do ...

Everything I do
With Godís complete
Permission and Assurance.

65 Do Not Try ...

Do not try
To perfect the world,
But try to love the world
More.

66 I Call Myself ...

I call myself
A God-lover,
But God calls me
His constant partner.

67 God Does Not Mind ...

God does not mind
If I criticise Him
Without any rhyme or reason,
But He does mind
When I do not pray every day
Prayerfully and soulfully.

16 January 2006 ...

16 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia
