18545

At times, success
May not be a difficult task,
But progress
Is always a difficult task.

18546

The tears of our hearts
Eventually grow into
The beauty and fragrance
Of our God-service-life.

18547

A life without
A self-giving experience
Is indeed
A totally wasted life.

18548

What I need is
A conscious God-awareness-mind.
What I need is
A constant God-oneness-heart.

18549

Beautiful are
Our aspiration-dreams.
Fruitful are
Our dedication-realities.

18550

God's happy News is
That He will never give up.
Man's sad news is
That he is still not ready.

18551

Do you know God's secret Name?
"Yes, I do.
As a matter of fact,
He has two secret Names:
Eternity's Hunger
And
Infinity's Feast."

18552

There was a time
When I had many goals.
Now I have only one goal:
My life's perfection-progress-goal.

18553

Be careful of hesitation,
For inside hesitation
Abides destruction-doubt.

18554

O my mind,
It is high time
For you to take
Some aspiration-heart-lessons.
If not, yours will be
A total failure-life.

18555

Drastic changes are required
If you want your heart to sing
A prayerful gratitude-song
Every day.

18556

What we need is determination.
How do we get it?
By trying to have
A God-willingness-blossoming life.

18557

It will not be enough
If you become a finalist
In the purity-sprints.
You also have to be
A ceaselessly self-giving
Hero-runner.

18558

God asks my gratitude-heart
To be a collector
Of temple-heart-surrender-songs.

18559

Frustrated, I say,
"Can't there be another God?"
Elated, I say,
"Can there ever be another me?"
