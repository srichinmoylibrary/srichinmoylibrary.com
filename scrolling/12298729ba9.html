My Earth-Life

July 24th, 1995

My earth-life
Is a secret sorrow
Of my dying heart.
My Heaven-life
Is a sacred smile
Of my challenging soul.

I Look at God

July 24th, 1995

I look at God and declare:
   Truth is all.
God looks at me and declares:
   All is truth.

Sad News Travels Fast

July 24th, 1995

Sad news travels fast:
I forget to feed humanity.
Good news travels slowly:
I love to serve humanity.

Sweet Dreams

July 24th, 1995

Sweet dreams, my sweet dreams,
Are you really real?
I think you are.
Hard realities, my hard realities,
Are you not always false?
I know you are.

Believe It or Not, I Have Seen God

July 24th, 1995

Believe it or not,
I have seen God, the real God,
Inside you, inside your purity-body.
Believe it or not,
I have seen God, the real God,
Inside you, inside your beauty-eye.

My Animal Life

July 24th, 1995

My animal life
Is a rainbow of binding ropes.
My human life
Is a rainbow of encouraging hopes.
My divine life
Is a rainbow of fulfilling scope.

Silence, What Is It?

July 24th, 1995

Silence, what is it?
The root of time.
Sound, what is it?
The trunk of time.
Perfection, what is it?
The tree of time.

Faith Is Strong

July 24th, 1995

Faith is strong
In God-Assurance.
Aspiration is strong
In God-Compassion.
Love is strong
In itself.

What Kind of Master Are You?

July 24th, 1995

What kind of Master are you?
My own Master never told me
   That I was a hopeless case.
What kind of disciple are you?
My own disciples always worship me
   As God, the only God.

What Are You Doing, Master?

July 24th, 1995

What are you doing, Master?
“I am thinking of you.”
Is that so?
Thank you, Master.
What were you doing
Before I came, Master?
“I was thinking of you.”
Is that so?
Thank you, Master.
What will you be doing, Master,
After I leave you?
“I will be thinking of you.”
Is that so?
Thank you, Master.

Necessity Taught Me

July 24th, 1995

Necessity taught me
How to become a Yogi.
Integrity has taught me
How to become a poet.
Curiosity is teaching me
How to become an artist.

I Fix My Soulful Eyes

July 24th, 1995

I fix my soulful eyes
On my Master’s feet
For compassion.
I fix my soulful eyes
On my Master’s heart
For liberation.
I fix my soulful eyes
On my Master’s eyes
For perfection.

God’s Heart I Desire

July 24th, 1995

God’s Heart I desire.
God’s Feet I choose.

A Desiring Mind

July 24th, 1995

A desiring mind
         Is
A paralysing fear.

Death

July 24th, 1995

Death
      Is
Life’s unwanted sleep.
