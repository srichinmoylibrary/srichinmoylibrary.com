3675 Doubt Is Powerful And Destructive

Doubt is powerful and destructive
When you cherish it.
Doubt is painful and blighting
Even if you do not cherish it.

3676 Two Things Almost Unconquerable

Two things are almost unconquerable:
An indifference-mind
And
An interference-vital.

3677 Three Things I Desperately Need

Three things I desperately need:
An enthusiastic heart,
An unskeptical mind
And
A realistic vital.

3678 An Exceptional Privilege

It is an exceptional privilege
To have the beauty of a serene mind,
The purity of a loving heart
And
The divinity of a humble life.

3679 A New Form

Inspiration is a new form
Of aspiration.
Dedication is a new form
Of manifestation.

3680 The Seeker Who Lives A Hidden Life

The seeker who faithfully lives
A hidden life
Will ultimately see revealed
The Satisfaction-Smile
Of the Absolute Supreme.

3681 You Are Lonely Because

You want to know why you are lonely?
You are lonely because
You never want to hear
Your heart's oneness-song.

3682 Refuse To Surrender

What threatens you?
An impure thought.
Refuse to surrender to its voice.

What frightens you?
An impure thought.
Refuse to surrender to its choice.

3683 The Secret Of Satisfaction

The secret of satisfaction
Is in knowing
Not what you think of yourself,
But what God feels about you.

3684 He Who Aspires

He who does not aspire
Sees every day as a new uncertainty.
He who aspires
Knows that every day
Is not only a new certainty,
But also a beautiful smile
Of God-manifesting divinity.

3685 I Shall Not Avoid You

I shall not cleverly avoid you,
O my impurity-mind,
For I know what I should do
To transform you.

I shall not unconsciously avoid you,
O my insecurity-heart,
For I know what I should do
To strengthen you.

3686 Why Do You Listen?

Why do you listen
To your demanding vital,
Why?
Why do you listen
To your suspicious mind,
Why?
Your demanding vital and your suspicious mind
Are intimate friends.
Let them go in their own way.
You listen only to your soul's inner dictates.

3687 Two Perfect Strangers

Two perfect strangers:
The earth-bound mind
And the Heaven-free soul.

Two immediate neighbours:
A self-giving heart
And a God-becoming life.

3688 The Brightest Dawn Resides

The brightest dawn resides
Neither inside the darkest night
Nor outside the nearest night,
But inside the dearest Light.

3689 An Earthly Gift

Illumination is not an earthly gift
That we try to get, but fail.
Illumination is a heavenly boon
With which we definitely succeed
And eternally proceed.
