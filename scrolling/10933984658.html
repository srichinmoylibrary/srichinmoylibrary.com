27 I Have Come To Learn ...

I have come to learn
That there is no real God
For those who do not
Bend their knees
And extend their arms.

13 December 1999 ...

13 DECEMBER 1999

SĂO MORITZ, BRAZIL

28 God Loves And Values ...

God loves and values
My heart's God-needs
More than anything else.

29 Unlike Our Faith In God, ...

Unlike our faith in God,
God's Faith in His children
Remains unwavering.

30 My Heart, My Vital And My...

My heart, my vital and my body,
Stay away-
As far as possible-
From my mind's negativity.

31 As Today ...

As today
Is in God's Hands,
Even so, tomorrow
Will be in God's Hands.
Therefore, we must not worry.

32 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That my sincerity-mind,
My purity-heart
And
My simplicity-life
Are bound to please God
In God's own Way.

33 At Every Moment ...

At every moment
I must blend into the dust
Of my Beloved Lord's Will.

34 God's Grace ...

God's Grace
Is ever-present
And not an accident.

35 May My Life On Earth ...

May my life on earth
Be a multi-dimensional
God-service.

36 What Am I Doing?...

What am I doing?
I am planting gratitude-plants
In my heart-garden.

37 Alas, ...

Alas,
How is it that I have not seen
That my life's ignorance-time
Has expired?

38 My Lord Beloved Supreme, ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
I shall no more permit
Your Heart to bleed
For my abominable misconduct.

14 December 1999 ...

14 DECEMBER 1999

SĂO MORITZ, BRAZIL

39 I Shall Not Live ...

I shall not live
Between "can" and "shall".
I shall live only
Between "must" and "done".
