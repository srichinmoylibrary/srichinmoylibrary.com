18445

If you want to be
A true citizen of the world,
Then you must not hesitate
To exhaust your life-energy
Cheerfully and self-givingly.

18446

My Lord Supreme,
Is it really necessary
That the human heart
Has to be broken time and again
For its ultimate purification?

18447

Yesterday
My rainbow-dreams
Cradled me.
Today
My rainbow-realities
Are enlightening me.

18448

A oneness-heart-smile
Is undoubtedly a safeguard
Against our insecurity-tears.

18449

Alas, each individual thinks
That he is an unmistakable authority
On others' imperfection-lives.

18450

God's Love-List
Has made my heart happy.
God's Compassion-List
Has made my heart happier.
God's Forgiveness-List
Has made me the happiest,
By far the happiest.

18451

A God-realised soul
Is absolutely certain
As to when the ignorance-mind
Will end its hesitation, suspicion
And imperfection-role.

18452

My heart's perfection
And
My life's transformation-roles
I have taken
As my soul's top priorities.

18453

Hesitation, doubt, suspicion
And utter failure
Always live together.

18454

Our mind is definitely
Going to bow down
To the illumining oneness-pressure
Of the heart.

18455

Each time I pray
I see inside my heart
  A peace-tree growing.
Each time I pray
I see inside my heart
  A peace-flower blossoming.

18456

Alas,
I thought insecurity
Was an insect bite.
But to my wide surprise
Now I see insecurity
Is indeed
An elephant-kick.

18457

O my mind,
How can my heart
Solve your problems
If you do not dare
To surrender them
To my heart?

18458

If you cannot
Altogether delay
Your desire-fulfilment-hour,
Try at least to delay it
Indefinitely.

18459

Impurity
Has a powerful
And destructive friend:
Arrogance.
