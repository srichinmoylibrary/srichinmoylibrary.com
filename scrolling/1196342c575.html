10690

Her aspiration-heart
Has regained its faith-footing
And is bathing once again
In God's Compassion-Tears.

10691

God appreciates and even admires
Your fight with self-doubts
And your victory over them.

10692

Take the path of God-obedience.
All your resistance-barriers
Will soon disappear.

10693

When I speak ill of God,
God tells me,
"My son,
I am not going to leave
Either My Heaven or My earth
Until I have changed your mind."

10694

My mind's cries tell me
That everything on earth
Is tragic.
My heart's smiles tell me
That everything on earth
Is magic.

10695

Without God's Compassion-Smiles,
Who can have heart-tears?
Without heart-tears,
Who can ever water
God's Fondness-Aspiration-Garden?

10696

Unnoticed and unknown remain
Your heart's pure
Aspiration-cries
And your soul's sure
Perfection-smiles.

10697

God has made
His Transformation-Promise
Unconditional.
Can you not make
Your acceptance-promise
Genuine?

10698

God wears only the garland
Of your pure heart's
Sincere gratitude-tears.

10699

The only way to surprise God
Is to tell Him,
"Beloved Lord,
I love You only
And I need You only,
Not for my sake
But for Your sake only."

10700

Where is your heart's
Rising sun?
Where else, if not in your Lord's
Compassion-flooded Eye?
