15860

Finally,
His mind and vital
Have done away with
Temptation-attachment-emptiness.

15861

Alas,
Why am I so stupid?
Why do I have to be
A prisoner
Of my self-created doubt?

15862

At long last
I have unchained
My anxiety-life.
I am now enjoying
My Lord's
Rainbow-Satisfaction-Smile.

15863

What the world
Desperately needs
Is a sincerity-heart's
Purity-flower-fragrance.

15864

O my mind-child,
Come to me!
Come, leave behind you
Your self-imposed insecurity-torture.
Come, come to me!
I am all for you,
And I am all concern for you.

15865

The doubt-mind
Maliciously, without reason,
Sues the faith-heart
Only to lose the case
Badly.

15866

Last night
My mind's ego-thief
I caught red-handed.
The ego-thief was stealing
My heart-ecstasy.

15867

My Lord,
Do tell me,
What would You like me
To talk about?
"My child,
I wish to hear from you
About your sincerity-mind-cry
And about your purity-heart-smile."

15868

Neither God's Compassion-Eye
Nor I
Can convince my mind
To do the right thing
And pray to God
For sleepless willingness.
Therefore,
My Lord's Compassion-Eye and I
Have begged God's Justice-Light
To come to our assistance.

15869

I have only two questions
To ask God:
Am I pleasing Him
In His own Way?
Is there any way
I can expedite earth-evolution?

15870

God is always ready
To bless us with rewards,
But only when we deserve the rewards
Do we become
Dependable and self-giving
Instruments of His.

15871

What is a human mind?
A human mind is something
That always likes to narrate
The story of God's
Excessive Pride in it.

15872

Do not think
Of yesterday's misfortunes.
Think of today's fortune-smiles,
And think of the day after tomorrow's
God-Satisfaction-Pride
In you.

15873

God does not want us
To take steps
Into the Unknowable.
He wants us only
To be perfect instruments
Of the Knowable.

15874

Wait! Wait! Wait!
Still there is a good chance
That your Lord Supreme
Will enter into your mind
To create God-hunger there.
