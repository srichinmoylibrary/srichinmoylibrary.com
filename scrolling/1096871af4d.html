69 I Live In Between ...

I live in between
My laughable mind
And
My lovable heart.

70 Alas, We Have Nothing Better To Do ...

Alas, we have nothing better to do
Than to blame God
For our failure-sadness-life.

71 The Mind Always Lives ...

The mind always lives
In the lip-deep prayers.

72 God Tells Me That My Task ...

God tells me that my task
Is to encourage the seeker,
And that His Task
Is to perfect the seeker.

73 I Touch God’s Feet ...

I touch God’s Feet
Only to be embraced
By God’s Heart.

8 February 2005 ...

8 February 2005

Nanjing, China

74 God’s Strictness-Mind ...

God’s Strictness-Mind
Is for
The chosen few.

75 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Is for
All and sundry.

76 In God’s Heart ...

In God’s Heart,
Each seeker
Is unique.

77 Each And Every Pilgrim ...

Each and every pilgrim
Needs must visit
God’s Heart-Temple.

78 The Unaspiring ...

The unaspiring
And undivine thoughts
Never wait for an invitation.

79 Our God-Surrender-Train ...

Our God-surrender-train
Runs the fastest
Towards the Destination.

80 The Doubt-Door ...

The doubt-door
Has to be kept closed tight
All the time.

81 Spirituality Does Not House ...

Spirituality does not house
A narrowness-mind
And a shallowness-life.

82 Doubt-Storms ...

Doubt-storms
Cannot sink
Faith-ocean liners.
