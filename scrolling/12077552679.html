21915

O my mind,
I am telling you
Once and for all,
I am totally tired
Of your ignorance-festivities.

21916

Alas,
Will I ever realise
That my love of God
Can never be replaced
By anything else?

21917

The day I do not pray and meditate,
I really feel like
A boat ruthlessly tossed
On a stormy sea.

21918

May my mind's search for God
And my heart's love of God
Become two real friends.

21919

As we have to climb up the tree
To reach the fruit,
Even so,
We have to dive within
To collect our inner treasure.

21920

Like my soul,
When my body becomes
Constantly a servant
Of God's Will,
I will be able to become
A choice instrument of God,
And not before.

21921

Do not try to enslave
The thoughts of your mind.
Try to illumine
The thoughts of your mind
If you really want
Real happiness.

21922

Death laughs
When we tell the world
We can escape
The snare of death.

21923

Our morning prayers
Can definitely reveal
The ultimate excellence
Of our life.

21924

The mind thinks it knows
What happiness is.
But alas,
Happiness knows
That the mind is fooling itself.

21925

Do not shut your heart's door
Against the rising inner sun
If you want enlightenment.

21926

We can depend upon only one thing:
We can readily and happily
Depend upon God's Promise
To transform our earth-bound sufferings
Into His Heaven-free Delight.

21927

Do everything you are supposed to do
Today,
In a divine way,
For your tomorrow's heart-sun
May not rise.

21928

We must realise
That the Will
That directs our inner life
Is indeed indomitable.

21929

Let us sit smilingly
At God's Feet
Only after offering God
Our heart-blossoms,
And not before.
