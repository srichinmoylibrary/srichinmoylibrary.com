11160

If you have an eye of hope,
If you have a heart of faith
And if you have a life of dynamism,
Then God-fulfilment in and through you
Will definitely be an easy task.

11161

When Heaven descends
It does not see
Even one heart of satisfaction-light
On earth.

When earth ascends
It does not see
Even one soul of frustration-night
In Heaven.

11162

To illumine your mind's
Confusion-night,
Every day call God by His real Name:
Infinity's Compassion-Light.

11163

You may allow all your other possessions
To be taken away,
But do not allow one possession of yours
To be stolen,
And that possession is
Your Eternity's hope-boat.

11164

A God-lover keeps his heart
Always ready
For another God-lover
To come in and station himself.

11165

Do not be a fool!
God has not asked you
To visit the grave
Of your deceased dreams.
He always asks you
To treasure
Your towering dreams.

11166

Only when your aspiration is intense,
Only when your dedication is genuine,
Can you conquer the torture
Of self-doubt.

11167

Even if you do not want
To approach God with your eyes open,
No harm!
But you have to approach God
With your heart's door wide open!

11168

A seeker's God-inspired mind
Alone
Can befriend a God-illumined heart.

11169

Your success-sunshine
Can be unlimited
Only when your dedication-arms
Do not enjoy the life
Of comfort and ease.

11170

God and His Compassion-Eye
And God and His Forgiveness-Heart
Are my aspiration-life's
Faith-steering wheels.

11171

I am expecting God to forgive me.
I do not know, alas,
If God is expecting from me
My complete life-perfection.

11172

Do not give up,
Do not give up!
Your prayer-life
Is your future salvation.
Your meditation-heart
Is your future perfection.
Do not give up!

11173

Each God-inspired thought
Is God's Satisfaction-Smile
In His manifested earth-consciousness.

11174

Each time I smile at God
With my gratitude-heart,
God grants me His blessingful Hands
And powerful Eye.
