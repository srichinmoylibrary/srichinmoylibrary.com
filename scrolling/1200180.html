14401

Our heart's streaming tears
Know the secret entrance
To the world of Infinity's Delight.

14402

Are you so stupid
That you have to stir up
Your mind's confusion-dust
Every day?

14403

How can you say that you aspire,
My seeker-friend?
Can you not see
That your heart-door
Is completely sealed?

14404

Look at the rising sun.
Look at the setting sun.
Your mind's inspiration
And your heart's aspiration
Will immediately increase.

14405

My faith-flooded heart
Has accepted the challenge
Of curing my doubt-addicted mind.

14406

Plant gratitude-seeds
Inside your heart-garden.
Your life will be beautiful
And fruitful
With glowing deeds.

14407

You are spending all your time
Thinking of others' successes
And failures.
When will you have time
To do something for yourself,
Or even for God?

14408

Step out
Of stark ignorance-shadow,
O my ill-fated mind,
If you want progress-satisfaction.

14409

The illumination-compass
Of my soul
Invariably points
Towards God-manifestation.

14410

Deep inside my heart's
Ecstasy-lake,
My Lord Supreme sings
His Immortality's Songs.

14411

If your inner weather report
Is good,
Then you are bound to make
Long progress-strides.

14412

A sleepless gratitude-heart
Knows the shortest and safest
Passageway
To God's Satisfaction-Heart.

14413

Fortunate are those
Who have the capacity
To stay off
Their mind's doubt-infested path.

14414

God needs from you
A thirsty mind
And
A hungry heart,
And
Nothing more!
