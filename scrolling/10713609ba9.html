45.

Age does not matter,
Unless you have come to believe that
Nothing is new
And nobody is lovable.

46.

Age does not matter,
Unless you feel that even God
Does not pay any attention
To your secret and sacred tears.

47.

Age does not matter,
Unless you are incapable of snatching
Even a fleeting moment of joy.

48.

Age does not matter,
Unless you feel that once upon a time
You were a hero
And now you are nothing but a zero.

49.

Age does not matter,
Unless you are convinced
That human love
Has totally failed you
And that divine love
Is far beyond your reach.

50.

Age does not matter,
Unless you feel God is no longer yours
And you are no longer God’s.

51.

Age does not matter,
Age does not even know
How to count,
Unless you take the trouble
Of teaching it.

52.

Age does not matter,
Unless you fail to become
A wisdom-hunter.

53.

Age does not matter,
Unless you are willing
To be assailed
By a puzzling life.

54.

Age does not matter,
Unless you want to be lost
In the shadows
Of your declining thought-life.

55.

Age does not matter,
Unless you do not care for
God’s Hope and Promise
Operating in and through you.

56.

Age does not matter,
Unless you no longer try to become
Great and good.

57.

Age does not matter,
Unless you deliberately call your life
A weed and not a flower.

58.

Age does not matter,
Unless we give unnecessary importance
To our enemies—
Our outer enemies
And our inner enemies.
Alas, why do we forget that
Even God has detractors?

59.

Age does not matter,
Unless we decide
It is time to go
And not to prolong our stay.
