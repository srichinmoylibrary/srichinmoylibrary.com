1 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May I be a heart-cry in my inner life
For You
And may I be a life-smile in my outer life
For You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 September 2000, 10:18 a.m. Before doing 600-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

3 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A real God-seeker's life
Is
A God-manifestation-fulfilment-promise.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 September 2000, 7:30 a.m. Before doing 600-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

3 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only inside a self-giving heart
Is the home of peace.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 September 2000, 7:51 a.m. Before doing 800-lb. dumbbell bench press.

3 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only when I sincerely
Love and serve the world
Do You allow my life to blossom
In Your own Heart-Garden.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 September 2000, 7:40 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

4 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life, my heart
And my breath
Be made of my God-gratitude-tears.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 September 2000, 10:51 p.m. Before doing 610-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

5 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my mind prays to You
And when my mind thinks of You
Most sincerely,
It sees ignorance-darkness-night-abyss
Nowhere.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 September 2000, 5:55 p.m. Before doing 610-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

5 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am so fortunate that every day
Your Compassion-Eye-Flame-Waves
Feed my heart unconditionally.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 September 2000, 6:06 p.m. Before doing 800-lb. dumbbell bench press.

5 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My cheerfulness pleases You.
My soulfulness pleases You
Abundantly more.
My selflessness pleases You
Infinitely more.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 September 2000, 6:23 p.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

8 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day God enjoys watching
His Compassion-Eye
And my aspiration-heart
Playing together.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 September 2000, 9:13 a.m. Before doing 620-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

8 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My prayer-life teaches me
How to realise God.
My meditation-heart teaches me
How to manifest God
On earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 September 2000, 9:22 a.m. Before doing 800-lb. dumbbell bench press.

8 September 2000 (II)

My weightlifting is my prayer-life
To inspire my sisters and brothers
Of the entire world.

8 September 2000, 11:07 a.m. A prayerful message for television.

8 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I soulfully pray,
I see myself seated
Inside God's Dream-Boat
And God Himself is piloting the Boat.
When I sleeplessly meditate,
I see myself singing and smiling
At God's Reality-Shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 September 2000, 9:36 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

8 September 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart always live
In the rainbow-beauty
Of glowing hopes.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 September 2000, 8:32 p.m. Before doing 630-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

8 September 2000 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life always live
In the mountain-height
Of lightning-promises.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 September 2000, 8:44 p.m. Before doing 820-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.
