23975

On the spiritual journey,
Our very best guide
Is our own devotion-heart.

23976

To make oneself happy,
The easiest and most effective way
Is to find every day
A new beginning.

23977

Those who think
That there will always be
A tomorrow for them
May finish the race,
But will not be able to win the race.

23978

I hired my mind to work
In my heart-garden,
But my Lord Supreme,
My Superboss,
Dismissed my mind summarily.

23979

Because of our stupid mind's
Blind preconceptions,
We go neither forward nor backward;
We remain a stagnant pool.

23980

My soul commands me
To teach my mind privately
So that it can study
In my heart-school.

23981

The ultimate teacher
Is not an experiment,
Not even an experience,
But our own self-discovery.

23982

O my setting sun-mind,
I shall never allow you
To rise again anywhere.

23983

The dreamer in me
Is my God-readiness.
The lover in me
Is my God-willingness.
The server in me
Is my God-eagerness.

23984

My Lord, my Lord,
Do not allow
My mind-heaviness-darkness-cloud
To eclipse
My heart-delight-illumination-sun.

23985

My Lord,
How can I have perfection-lustre in my life?
My child,
Walk along the surrender-road
Smilingly and unconditionally.

23986

Sadness disappears
Like a ghost
When I see my Master smiling.

23987

There is only one thing I must do.
I must mail away the sorrows of my heart
To Heaven.

23988

We must be very careful
To guard against depression.
Sooner than the soonest
Depression can approach us
At an unguarded moment.

23989

When a divine smile grows
Inside our heart,
We breathe in God's own Breath.
