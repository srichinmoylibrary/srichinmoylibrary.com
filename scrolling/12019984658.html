16230

The stupid mind and vital
Quite often consciously visit
The pleasure-supermarket.

16231

A heart of humility
Is an excellent
Pride-prevention-medicine.

16232

There is no harm
In dreaming.
But do not forget
That dreaming is not
The best thing.
The best thing is
Reality-becoming.

16233

Alas,
The human mind and vital
Are quite often fooled
By secret temptation-trickery.

16234

If you are
A fully awakened soul,
Then you can separate
Death from fear.

16235

Without a soulful heart
You cannot have
A powerful meditation.
Indeed,
This is a lesson
We have learned
From the seekers of antiquity.

16236

God's Compassion-Eye
And His Forgiveness-Heart
Are solely responsible
For the peace-pact
Between my unwillingness-mind
And my willingness-heart.

16237

Another day
Does not mean
Another doubt-thorn.
Another day
Means a new faith-blossom
Inside my heart-garden.

16238

If you have
A sincerity-heart,
Then you can easily bring
Your aspiration and dedication
Up to date.

16239

O my mind,
I do not approve
Of your frequent visits
To the unlit vital-cave.

16240

Can you not see
That God
Is especially proud of you,
For your monumental
World-responsibility?

16241

There is a great difference
Between possession and renunciation.
If you really want
To win life's victory,
Then you must side with
Renunciation.

16242

Your life's
Satisfaction-gates
Must be guarded
By your heart's
Aspiration-soldiers.

16243

What a joy
To see the collapse
Of our mind's insecurity
And insincerity-building!

16244

Compassion-game
Is the fondest game
Of my Lord Supreme
On earth.
Satisfaction-game
Is the fondest game
Of my Lord Supreme
In Heaven.
