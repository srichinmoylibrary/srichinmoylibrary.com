9130

If you live in an atmosphere
  Of aspiration,
Perfection will be
  Your God-given name.

9131

  Alas,
Only his ego-life
  Is progressing.
Everything else
  Is drastically regressing.

9132

Do not waste
  Your God-service-hours
Thinking mundane thoughts.

9133

  Death is compulsory
Because God wants the soldiers of life
  To take rest
At the end of each battle.

9134

  Each heart-path
Is strewn with purity-flowers,
  But the human mind
Does not want to believe it.

9135

Why are you so blind
  That you cannot see
Your stupendous pride is only splashing
  In your mind's ego-lake?

9136

I am not sure whether humanity
  Is a half-formed divinity
Or an ill-formed stupidity.

9137

Although he is notorious
For his insincerity-life,
  He is vigorous
In trying to transform his life,
  Once and for all.

9138

You are bound to win always
In the inner world
If you do not allow your mind
To analyse the outer world.

9139

When you are angry with God,
Your soul, with its strictness,
And your Lord, with His Compassion,
Make arrangements for an interview.

9140

Your soulful dedication
  To God
Will determine
God's fruitful Affection
  For you.

9141

If you open your heart,
  You will feel that God
Can never be strict with you,
  Never,
For His Compassion-Height
  Is infinitely stronger
Than His Justice-Light.

9142

Others may not know,
  But I do know
One of my outstanding faults:
I feel that the world
  Badly needs me.

9143

Early in the morning
  Every day
You must do one thing
Before you do anything else:
Strike your lethargy-infested mind.

9144

When I look into God's Eye,
  My life becomes
My heart's unhorizoned delight.
