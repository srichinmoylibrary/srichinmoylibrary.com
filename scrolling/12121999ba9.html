26345

Believe in the beauty
Of your imagination.
It is true, all true!
It is not your mental hallucination.

26346

My Lord, do make my heart
The living breath
Of unconditional surrender.

26347

There is an unseen Hand.
I may not see it,
But it definitely is there
To guide my life.

26348

To play on the world-stage,
We need inner courage
And outer smiles.

26349

It seems that the golden days
Do not last forever.
They are always very brief.

26350

How can you illumine
Your mind's darkness?
Get your soul's light
To be a permanent guest
Inside your mind.

26351

O my mind,
Is it your sincere receptivity
Or your sincere curiosity
That you truly enjoy?

26352

O brooding doubts,
True, I cannot outrun you
When you chase me,
But in Heaven you will be nowhere
To chase me.

26353

An unconditional surrender
Is the culmination
Of all sincere self-giving efforts.

26354

My Lord,
I beg for Your Compassion.
I beg for Your Forgiveness.
Then only satisfaction will dawn on me.

26355

Although God works sleeplessly,
He never takes a break.
Being His choice children,
Can we not do the same?

26356

My Lord Supreme,
Please bless me
With a purity-flooded heart-life
To touch and be at Your Feet.

26357

Every day my Lord Supreme
Is extremely eager
To take me to a new destination.
Alas, how often I am not willing —
Not even ready.

26358

When I look back,
I see and become
My old destructive vital.
When I look forward,
I see and become
My deer-speed.

26359

My inner urge has brought me
To the Compassion-Feet
Of my Lord Supreme.
Nothing else could
And nothing else will.
