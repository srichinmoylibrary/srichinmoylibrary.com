19945

My Lord,
What shall I do
To make myself happy?
"My child,
Walk beside Me,
Run with Me,
Cry and smile with Me
And for Me."

19946

Each self-giving thought
Is indeed
A perfection-breath-promise
Of our Lord Supreme.

19947

The soul likes to talk to us
In our own voice,
So that we can be more understanding
And more self-giving.

19948

Each soul has its unique way
To spread God's Light
Throughout the length and breadth
Of the world.

19949

A heart of peace
Has no equal
And will always remain
Unchallenged.

19950

When I come first in my life,
And not God,
There can be no room
For my self-improvement.

19951

Heaven
Is compassionate.
Earth
Is affectionate.
May my mind
Be always dispassionate.

19952

If you ever want to meet
Silence,
Then never ride,
Even by mistake,
The temptation-train.

19953

May my life be found
Inside my heart's gratitude-flowers
And not inside
My mind's ingratitude-thorns.

19954

Alas, why do you enjoy
Doubt-downpour
And not faith-sea
Inside your aspiration-heart?

19955

Each self-giving thought
Is indeed
A God-locating compass.

19956

O body,
How long do you want to remain
Inside your self-chosen lethargy-prison?

19957

May my happiness-heart
Decorate my life
With purity-flower-fragrance
Every day.

19958

If we do not follow
Our heart-guide,
Then our mind is bound to be flooded
With disappointment.

19959

The tears of my heart
Are too weak to receive
The smiles of my soul.
