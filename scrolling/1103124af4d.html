22 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
For me, the only way of living
Is my prayerful, soulful
And self-giving life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 November 1998, 5:28 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

22 November 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
With Your infinite Grace
I am discovering
My yesterday's weaknesses
To uncover
My tomorrow's strengths.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 November 1998, 5:34 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

22 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Every day
I prayerfully and gratefully watch
My full God-manifestation-hope
Blossoming
At Your Compassion-flooded Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 November 1998, 5:41 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times — (50 right, 50 left) — for a new record of 2 mins 6.36 secs.

23 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You do not allow impossibility-plant
To grow in my life's service-garden.
Such is Your Compassion for me!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 November 1998, 6:01 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm twenty-nine times. (in four sets)

23 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Do give me the strength of self-giving.
In self-giving alone lies
My peace of mind
And
My heart of fulfilment.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 November 1998, 6:07 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

23 November 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The mind
Secretly and openly enjoys
Scattering disobedience-thoughts.
The heart
Prayerfully and self-givingly enjoys
The beauty and fragrance of obedience.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 November 1998, 6:13 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

23 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Smile, smile, smile,
So that my heart and I can feel
That You still love us.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Smile, smile, smile,
So that my life and I can know
That You still need us.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 November 1998, 6:18 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred and one times. (49 right, 52 left)

24 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Why is it
That You are more pleased with me
When I play with You
Than when I pray to You?
"My child, just because
When you play with Me
You are more sincere
Than when you pray to Me.
A very difficult question,
But a very simple answer!"
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 November 1998, 5:15 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs eighty times. (40 right, 40 left in four sets)

24 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You want me to offer You
Pure devotion at every moment.
My Supreme, I am all ready.
My heart is ready,
My mind is ready,
My vital is ready
And
My body is ready
To offer You devotion
At every moment.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 November 1998, 5:23 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

24 November 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My outer failure
Is my inner eye-opener.
My outer success
Is the beginning
Of my life's new dream.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 November 1998, 5:31 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

24 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
God-doubt is the most ruthless
Self-torture.
God-faith is Infinity's
Self-rapture.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 November 1998, 5:37 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times — (50 right, 50 left) — for a new record of 1 min 32.07 secs.

25 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Please tell me how I can free myself
From my earth-bound limited self.
"My child, just go and stay
All the time
With your Heaven-free unlimited Self.
Your earth-bound limited self
Will not dare to follow you."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 November 1998, 4:59 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs one-hundred times. (50 right, 50 left in five sets)

25 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I am extremely grateful to You
For telling me that
I need more sincerity
In my aspiration
And more faithfulness
In my devotion,
So that I can have a peaceful mind
And a blissful heart
Beyond measure.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 November 1998, 5:07 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

25 November 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You are compassionately asking me
To correct and perfect
My unbelief and disbelief-mind,
So that I can be sleeplessly
And breathlessly happy.
My Supreme,
I shall fulfil Your kind Request.
I shall fulfil Your supreme Command.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 November 1998, 5:14 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

25 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I must choose only one thing:
Your Forgiveness-Delight,
Even though I do not know
When I shall experience it.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 November 1998, 5:21 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times. (50 right, 50 left)
