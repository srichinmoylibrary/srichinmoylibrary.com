14 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart and I every day
   Please You
With our inner progress-journey.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 October 1999, 10:08 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

14 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life's surrender
   To God's Will
Be the never-ending song
   Of my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 October 1999, 10:30 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 80 lbs. (77 reps)

15 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   Today
Patience is sour,
   But tomorrow
Patience will become sweet,
   And the day after tomorrow
Patience will become
   Sweetness-perfection.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 October 1999, 9:47 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

15 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Patience is an absolute necessity
In my inner life of aspiration
        And
In my outer life of dedication.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 October 1999, 9:58 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

15 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I want to offer You
Three very special names:
   Patience-Beauty,
   Compassion-Fragrance,
   Forgiveness-Garden.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 October 1999, 10:07 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

15 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Patience-Will
   Waits for me.
Your Compassion-Eye
   Blesses me.
Your Forgiveness-Heart
   Embraces me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 October 1999, 10:08 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

15 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
It is Your infinite Patience
That enables me to achieve
   Momentous progress
In my inner life of aspiration
        And
   Tremendous success
In my outer life of dedication.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 October 1999, 10:29 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (96 reps)

16 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, what am I doing?
Am I not compelling Your Patience
   To weep for me?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 October 1999, 9:24 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

16 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
All my prayers and meditations
Are under the shelter
Of my Beloved Lord's
Compassion-Umbrella-Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 October 1999, 9:31 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm fifty-two times. (26 right, 26 left)

16 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   From now on
I shall not waste
Even a fleeting second
With my self-doubts.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 October 1999, 9:57 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

18 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May every day my aspiration-heart
   Feast upon
Your Compassion-flooded Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 October 1999, 8:54 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

18 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   The waves
Of Your Heart's Compassion-Sea
   Dance
The moment they see my heart's
Tiny aspiration-flames.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 October 1999, 9:03 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

18 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May Your Compassion-Eye
   Always keep me
In Your Protection-Arms.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 October 1999, 9:13 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

18 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today, without fail,
I must put an end
To my self-doubt-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 October 1999, 9:14 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

18 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today my aspiring heart
Is compelling me to compose
   God-Victory-songs.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 October 1999, 9:22 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred and six times. (52 right, 54 left)
