29 Today My Lord ...

Today my Lord
Is asking my heart
To be with Him
In creating a happy world.

30 When The Time Arrives ...

When the time arrives,
My Lord says
That I must be with Him
While He creates
A perfect world.

38 Every Morning ...

Every morning
My Lord expects from me
A great surprise.
At noon He expects
A greater surprise.
In the evening He expects
The greatest surprise.
The first surprise:
Sincere tears.
The second surprise:
Sincere smiles.
The third surprise:
Sincere surrender.

39 God’s Flute-Heart-Sweetness ...

God’s Flute-Heart-Sweetness
Failed to transform
My life.

40 God’s Thunder-Eye-Kicks ...

God’s Thunder-Eye-Kicks
Have transformed
My life.

41 My Prayers ...

My prayers
Sweeten
My God-crying heart.
