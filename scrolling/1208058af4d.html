22275

Attachment-diploma
I had.
Detachment-education
I now enjoy.

22276

Do not allow loneliness
To touch you.
If you do, it may one day
Devour you.

22277

The fulfilment of possibilities
Cannot be imagined,
But can be obtained
Through sleepless self-discipline.

22278

He who welcomes suspicion
Is doomed to be sneered at
By suspicion itself.

22279

We were not born
For human death,
But for divine Immortality.

22280

If we do not shun
The noise of our ego-mind,
How can we achieve
God's all-illumining Smile?

22281

No human being
Can ever become wise
By cherishing self-doubts.

22282

I am asking God
To be sincere.
God is asking me
To be receptive.

22283

Alas,
When will I learn
That pleasing God
Is indeed a delightful task?

22284

I pray to God every day
To extol my heart-life
And exile my mind-life.

22285

Self-giving to God's Will
Is, without fail,
A slow-ripening
But most delicious fruit.

22286

Our gratitude-heart
Is bound to multiply
The goodness of our life.

22287

My Lord,
The greatest of all Blessings
From You
Is my willingness-heart.

22288

A doubting mind
And an anxiety-life
Are generally inseparable.

22289

When I consciously speak ill of God,
I simply double God's Tears
And triple His Sighs.
