30.

      My heart-garden
        deeply enjoys
  the company of
        two guests:
compassion and sympathy.

31.

As beauty and fragrance
     are inseparable,
even so my heart-lotus
  and my soul-breath
are inseparable.

32.

      Alas,
will my Indian heart
      ever be as pure
  as an Indian lotus?

33.

      Alas,
     will my Indian heart
        ever be able to see
   the compassion-beauty
  of an Indian lotus
      in each human being?

34.

Each lotus
  is the
fondest child,
     greatest pride
and sweetest smile
      of an Indian goddess.

35.

The discovery
   of my
  Indian heart:
        no Indian lotus,
     no Indian goddess.

36.

My heart lotus tells me
              that I am not
     my mind's dark and
      starless night.

37.

My heart-lotus tells me
                 that I am
     my soul's
illumination-adorer.

38.

   Each petal
      of my heart-lotus
reveals a unique message
        from the Beyond.

39.

Each petal
of my heart-lotus
               receives
  a special boon from
God's Compassion-Eye.

40.

Each petal
of my heart-lotus
     is a humble prayer
  of my heart.

41.

      My mind's
ingratitude-weeds
      disappear
  the moment
         I enter into
   my heart's lotus-garden.

42.

The lotus-life
   sings
      inside
my tearful heart-nest.

43.

      The lotus-life
              dances
           inside
      my blissful
soul-kingdom

#+ENDVERSE.

44.

My lotus-heart bloom
         expedites
the arrival of God's Hour.
