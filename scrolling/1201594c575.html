15890

God will definitely amplify
Your heart's aspiration
And your life's dedication.
He is just waiting
For the right moment
And the right place,
And also for the right audience.

15891

My heart was not born
To be a weeping citizen
Of an indifferent world.
My heart was born
To be a member
Of a God-blossoming
And God-fulfilling world.

15892

How to pass
God's purity-examination?
Just develop more purity
In the mind,
More simplicity
In the life
And more self-giving
In the heart.
You will easily pass
God's purity-examination.

15893

You will come to know of God
Last, very last,
If you allow your doubting mind
To betray your world-oneness-heart.

15894

If your mind is listed
In the doubt-directory,
That marks the final end
Of your life's spiritual journey.

15895

O my mind,
I am tired
Of everything that I do
And everything that I say.
In effect,
I am tired of everything
And of every human being.
When are you going to become tired
Of your intimate friendship
With your ignorance-friend?

15896

It is not at all difficult
To find faith-medicine
In the heart-shop.
Again,
It is not at all difficult
To use it to cure
The mind's doubt-disease.

15897

If your heart has the will
To struggle for
Perfect perfection in life,
I am absolutely sure
That God will bless your heart
With tremendous success.

15898

A simple life
And a pure heart
Are two God-servers
That keep man always happy.

15899

You cannot escape ignorance-attack
On your own.
You can escape ignorance-attack
Only when God's
Unconditional Compassion-Eye
Plays the role of your Saviour.

15900

My Lord
Compassionately and blessingfully
Tells me that
He needs me
Infinitely more than ignorance needs me
Or than I need ignorance.
