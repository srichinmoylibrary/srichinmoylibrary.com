24101

The real happiness
Is an insatiable hunger
For sleepless God-Satisfaction
In God's own Way.

24102

My mind waits
For the return of God's Power.
My heart waits
For the return of God's Peace.

24103

Our Lord Supreme tells us
That He wants to be sought
And not to be bought.

24104

The surrender beyond all surrenders
Is our constant willingness
To please God in His own Way.

24105

God-Compassion
Is necessary.
God-obedience
Is more than indispensable.

24106

Expectation and despair
Always move together,
Although they do not care for each other
At all.

24107

God has been waiting
For a long, long time
To greet my eagerness-heart.

24108

There are many ways
To make God smile,
But the easiest way
Is to tell God sincerely,
"You are my All,
My Eternity's All."

24109

There are many enemies I have,
But I feel that expectation
Is my worst enemy.

24110

A heart of faith
Is not a fairytale.
A heart of faith
Is the beauty and perfection
Of the angelic world.

24111

It is simply impossible to create
An anxiety-worry-free territory
Inside one's mind.

24112

A transformation-life
Is within the reach
Of every human being.

24113

Today we call it
The darkest night.
Tomorrow that very thing
We will call the brightest dawn.

24114

Gratitude
Is the very breath
Of our constant love of God.
