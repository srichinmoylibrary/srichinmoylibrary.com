775.

When I aspire self-givingly,
I become the sunrise-smile
Of my inner life.

776.

There is a way to look beyond
The fear-curtain,
And that way is
Sleepless love of God.

777.

If you cannot see God
In someone,
That is definitely your own
Incalculable loss.

778.

Since the beginning of creation,
Each century has been blessed
With only a handful of realised souls.

779.

O my mind,
God is not going to accept
Your reluctant gratitude.

780.

God really loves
My life's perpetual
Self-enlightenment-effort.

781.

To love the unaspiring mind
Is to be satisfied
With a totally imperfect paradise.

782.

My Lord,
I constantly feel Your Love,
Yet self-indulgence
Is my sorrowful name.

783.

God will never condescend
To visit my mind-doubt-department.
God is ever ready
To enter my heart-faith-home.

784.

Truth can never be trapped
By the mind.
Truth can only be embraced
By the heart.

785.

My God-surrender-life
Has finally found the key
To my heart-home.

786.

Aspiration can speak
Constantly.
Realisation can whisper
Sleeplessly and breathlessly.

787.

From God I desire
A God-realised life.
From me God needs
A heart-perfection-life.

788.

Our minds must kneel
In order to rise.
Our hearts must dream
In order to fly.

789.

I am truly free
Only when I am
God's Heart-Home-prisoner.
