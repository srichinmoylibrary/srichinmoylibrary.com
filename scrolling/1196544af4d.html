10875

In the outer world
Your height is fixed,
But in the inner world
Your God-hunger can take you
Forever higher and higher.

10876

Even those who have reached
The ultimate heights
Have not declared, "Enough!"
For they have learned
God's supreme Secret:
The eternal self-transcendence-song.

10877

If your progress in one field
Is obstructed,
Go another way.
There are so many ways open to you
If you look within.
Only do not give up
The idea of progress.

10878

Something is missing!
You have given consciously,
But not unconditionally.
Therefore, you are unfulfilled;
Therefore, you are incomplete.

10879

You are holding all your problems inside.
Inwardly you do not ask for God's Help
And outwardly you are afraid of Him.
Blindness is your outer life;
Foolishness is your inner life.

10880

God always depends on
The seeker's sincerity-eye
And purity-heart.

10881

Year after year
Your sincerity compels you
To tell God about your weaknesses.
God tries His best to help you,
But, alas, your determination is not there
To conquer those devouring tiger-forces.

10882

The spiritual ladder has many rungs.
Now you are standing
On the sincerity-rung.
But if you do not step up
To the determination-rung
How will you ever ascend?

10883

By telling God about your problems,
What benefit do you get,
Since you do not care to harness
Your determination-volcano-power?

10884

Does God ever give up?
At the start of each year
God is filled with inspiration
To re-energise His children -
Even the hopeless cases.

10885

Like the soul,
Each human mind
Will one day believe
In the future manifestation
Of God's Perfection-Beauty on earth.

10886

Be not interested
In receiving credit from God.
Be only interested
In serving God.

10887

Just because you thought of God
In your worst possible moment,
Do you think you will receive
Extra protection from the Supreme?
God is not such a fool!

10888

You do not have
The capacity or willingness
To fulfil God's Request.
At the same time,
It is beneath your dignity
To ask others to help.
Alas, you are
Your stark stupidity-life!

10889

The vital knows only one song,
And it sings that song every day.
What is that song?
It is the song of self-indulgence.
