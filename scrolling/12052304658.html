19430

There are many keys
To open God's Gate,
But I always prefer my heart's
Unconditional surrender-key.

19431

I do not want my life to be
A play of possibilities
But a play of
God-manifestation-inevitabilities.

19432

Man speaks to God
With his brittle faith-prayers.
Yet he dares to expect
God's immediate Arrival.

19433

My heart's aspiration-life
Is quite often sweet.
My mind's desire-life
Is always bitter.

19434

It seems that
Each unaspiring human being
Has buried himself alive
In ignorance-night.

19435

Divinely beautiful
Is humanity's sincerity.
Supremely fruitful
Is humanity's humility.

19436

Start again with a new heart.
This time your mind's ego-wall
Will definitely crumble,
And your heart's oneness-smile
Will grow and glow.

19437

Every day I practise
My heart's hope-songs.
Therefore I have not given up
On man's ignorance-indulgence.

19438

Unwillingness in a seeker's life
Is by far
The greatest misfortune
And worst failure.

19439

There are innumerable
God-satisfaction-roads,
But I have chosen my heart's
Sleepless gratitude-road.

19440

The mind does not surrender
To God's compassionate Will
Even when the mind's troubles
Are at their worst.

19441

Everything that I have
Inspiring and aspiring
Is due to
God's Patience-Heart-Sea.

19442

My Lord of Compassion
Has followed me far and long.
Now it is my bounden duty
To follow Him in His own Way.

19443

God has asked me time and again
To give my ignorance
To His Compassion-Care.
But alas, I have sadly failed
To please Him in His own Way.

19444

Years and years ago
I made God happy in His own Way.
Alas, what am I doing now?
I am trying in vain
To please myself in my own way.
