25560

Your soul is always ready
To tell you everything about God,
But do you believe in your soul?
No, never!

25561

My Lord,
I am praying for that magic moment
When I shall be able
To sincerely claim You as my own,
Very own.

25562

What is the difference
Between my soul and my mind?
My soul is a supreme authority
On faith,
And my mind is a supreme authority
On doubt.

25563

When we pray to God for God's Victory,
God invites us to live
Inside His own Heart-Garden
Of Beauty and Fragrance.

25564

My Lord Beloved Supreme tells me:
"My child, My child, come quickly,
Come quickly!
I am all ready. I am all ready
To barter My Compassion
For your ignorance."

25565

My vital and my mind
Are finally ready
To see the Light of God
Through the aspiration
Of my heart.

25566

Sweet thoughts
Can definitely revive
Your mind
From its shameful death.

25567

Now it is my heart's turn
To speak about God
And manifest God here on earth.

25568

Whenever I speak from my faith-heart,
My Lord comes
Closer than the closest to me
And blesses me with all His Pride.

25569

My Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
Only one prayer:
Do awaken my heart,
My Lord, my Lord!

25570

Alas, for so many years
I have been studying
The heart-language,
Yet I have not acquired any mastery.

25571

I am finally ready to keep
Only my heart's sweet
And inspiring memories.

25572

If you speak from your faith-heart,
Then both humanity and Divinity
Will eventually trust you
And fulfil you.

25573

What do we see in God's Heart-Garden?
We see two most important
Dream-realities:
God-Beauty and God-Fragrance.

25574

The realisation of the ancients
We do not long for, alas!
The perfection of Heaven
We do not long for either, alas!
