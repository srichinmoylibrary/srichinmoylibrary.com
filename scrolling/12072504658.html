21430

Mine was not a dream
But reality itself,
When I was flying high above
My life's bondage-night.

21431

You do not have to forget
Many things.
Just forget one thing:
Forget your self-destruction-doubt-dagger.

21432

If you are not prepared
To wait indefinitely,
How can you dream
Of pleasing God
In His own Way eternally?

21433

Take a closer look at yourself.
God the Compassion loves you.
God the Perfection needs you.

21434

We can be truly victorious
Only through
Our God-surrender-breath.

21435

O my clever mind,
No matter how hard I try
To ignore your complexity,
I always sadly fail.

21436

The spiritual deaths
From God-disobedience-absurdity
Are so shocking
And, alas, so unnecessary.

21437

If you want to make abiding progress,
Then every day you have to see,
Appreciate and admire
Your own heart's inner sunrise.

21438

O disobedience,
What you need and deserve
Is death-silence
And not life-sound.

21439

O seeker,
Your spiritual death is not permanent.
You can be revived.
Just apply your sincerity-mind
And purity-heart.
Success will be yours before long.

21440

O disobedience,
How can I ever be your friend,
Since you can never dare
To have a heart?

21441

Who needs a mind
That wants to stay unchanged
Forever and forever?
No, not a truth-seeker
And not a God-lover — never!

21442

Of all the places
That God has visited,
My gratitude-heart-shrine
Has given Him infinitely more joy
Than the rest.

21443

Who has bound me,
If not my own
Mind-possession-fantasy?

21444

Not just this year
But each and every year,
God's unconditional Compassion
Is keeping me spiritually alive.
