9815

Keep your heart open
To the power of love
If you want to be
In close contact with God.

9816

Now that you have set your feet
On the right path,
The path of love, devotion and surrender,
Your days and nights will be crowned
With God's transcendental Glories.

9817

The perfection that is inside your heart
Is meant to be manifested
In your life,
Either in this lifetime
Or in future lives.

9818

The choice lies with you.
Either you take full responsibility
For your life
Or let God manifest Himself
In and through you
In His own Way.

9819

A human being
Is nothing but
A formless shape
Of uncertainty.

9820

He knows, he knows
That a rectified mistake
Is a new satisfaction-heart.

9821

Keep your determination-fire
Always burning
Inside your mind.
Then your mind will never
Be drenched with disappointment.

9822

God not only has
A listening Heart
But also He uses
His listening Heart
To welcome all our countless problems.

9823

Self-criticism is not the way
That leads to self-perfection;
Neither is self-flattery.
Through conscious self-awareness alone,
The seeker reaches his goal.

9824

Not difficult
To follow the truth-seeking life.
But difficult,
Extremely difficult,
To follow the heart-surrendering life.

9825

The mind cannot conceive of
God's Goodness.
The heart not only conceives of
But also believes in
And finally achieves
God's Goodness.

9826

A happy heart loves God
Because
God does not ask a happy heart
To study at the failure-school.

9827

You can face the world each day
If you pray and discuss with God
Your own life-problems
Early in the morning.

9828

Because of my stubborn nature
God had to go His Way,
And I had to go my own way.

9829

To uplift the earth-atmosphere,
Start from today
With the heart that gives
And the life that surrenders.
