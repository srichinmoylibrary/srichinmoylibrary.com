5 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must always remain
Inside my temple-heart
To become
A supremely choice instrument
Of Yours.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 February 1999, 4:44 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty-eight times with one arm. (27 right, 31 left)

5 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am praying to You
Not for a shorter destination-road,
But for two bright eyes
And
Two stronger legs.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 February 1999.

6 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart, my God-seeker-heart,
My God-dreamer-heart, my God-lover-heart,
True, you have a few unfortunate critics,
But certainly you have no equal,
And you do not have even one worthy rival.
My heart, my heart, my heart!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 February 1999, 4:28 a.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 110 lbs. with both arms simultaneously from a seated position.

6 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your unconditional Grace
Has made my sleeplessly self-giving heart
A God-discovery-delight.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 February 1999, 4:54 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. twelve times with one arm. (5 right, 5 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

8 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I know, I know, I know,
My physical ability, my vital ability
And my mental ability
Entirely depend on
My spiritual dependability,
And my spiritual dependability
Is all Your unconditional Compassion.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 February 1999, 6:09 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty-three times with one arm. (27 right, 26 left)

8 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall not give up, I shall not give up!
I shall continue, I shall continue!
No matter how many times I have failed,
I shall continue.
Regret is worthless, regret is useless,
Regret is totally futile.
I shall keep on!
I must reach my destination, I must!
My goal is beckoning me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 February 1999, 6:24 a.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously from a seated position.

9 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The ambition of my vital and my mind
To become another Julius Caesar
Or Napoleon or Akbar
Compels You to swim in the sea
Of sorrows.
The aspiration of my heart
To become a sleeplessly self-giving
Child of Yours
Inspires You to swim in the sea
Of ecstasy.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 February 1999, 5:28 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty-one times with one arm. (25 right, 26 left)

9 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must not choose joys.
I must not choose sorrows.
I must choose You,
Only You, only You, only You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 February 1999, 5:40 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. twelve times with one arm. (5 right, 5 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

9 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall no more compel You
To play the role of a fool
In me, through me and for me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 February 1999, 5:48 a.m. Before lifting 130 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 260 lbs.)

9 February 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart's gratitude-offering to You
Is my immediate experience
Of Heaven's Delight.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 February 1999, 5:51 a.m. Before using double-arm machine.

10 February 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What I need is resolute faith
To love more sincerely
The Heart of God the Creator
And to serve most devotedly
The Feet of God the creation.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 February 1999, 6:37 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty-one times with one arm. (25 right, 26 left)

10 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My world-opinion is the thickness
Of my mind's stupidity-night.
My world-union is the brightness
Of my heart's wisdom-light.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 February 1999, 6:48 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. twelve times with one arm. (5 right, 5 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

10 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must love You more,
I must value You more,
If I desperately need to arrive
At Your Golden Shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 February 1999, 6:54 a.m. Before lifting 130 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 260 lbs.)

10 February 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind,
Today you must go out of your way
To love the world, to serve the world
And to become
The beauty and the fragrance
Of the world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 February 1999, 6:57 a.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 130 lbs. with both arms simultaneously from a seated position.
