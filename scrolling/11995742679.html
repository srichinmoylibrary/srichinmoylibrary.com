13815

Every day
My Lord Supreme blesses me
With His Compassion-Smile,
So that I will make a monumental attempt
To go beyond
My daily frustration-horizon.

13816

A pure humility-heart
Is indeed
A sure God-intoxication-life.

13817

God Himself
Is lengthening your progress-strides
Because you are cheerfully welcoming
Each and every new God-seeker.

13818

God does not care for you:
This is your giant
Mental hallucination!

13819

Immortality is beckoning you
Because you are sleeplessly singing
The oneness-song
Inside God's Heart-Garden.

13820

Aspiration-lovers
And determination-warriors
Are bound to win,
Bound to win
The supreme victory for God
In the battlefield of life
Here on earth.

13821

Without Eternity's direct,
Secret and sacred help,
Nobody has the chance to become
A supremely choice instrument of God.

13822

My Lord Supreme tells me
That He wants to live
Only inside my heart's
Silence-fragrance-blossoms.

13823

There is always a long distance
Between my mind's loud voice
And my heart's hallowed
God-fulfilment-choice.

13824

You chose yourself
Foolishly
Only to lose God
Miserably.

13825

An unconditional God-lover
Never wants to be
Off-duty.

13826

Perfection-choice
Is the very beginning
Of a God-satisfying life.

13827

My morning promise to God
Is that I shall never measure
But only treasure
His Compassion-Light.

13828

Since the very beginning of creation
There has always been
A most deplorable shortage of
Compassion-eyes, forgiveness-hearts
And gratitude-lives.

13829

How can you become
An aspiration-specialist
If you do not have
A gratitude-heart
And a surrender-life?
