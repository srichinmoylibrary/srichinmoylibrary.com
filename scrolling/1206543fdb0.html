20760

In the spiritual life
Obedience-wisdom-delight
Far surpasses
All other wisdom.

20761

My Lord,
I am not praying to Your Heart
To love me.
I am praying to Your Eye
Not to leave me.

20762

Since my aspiration-life
Is God's creation-object,
I must have God's Life
As my manifestation-project.

20763

God's Heart carries me,
God's Eye protects me,
God's Breath feeds me
Along Eternity's Road.

20764

Where am I?
Alas, I am in between
My cheerless heart
And
My godless mind.

20765

My outer silence
Definitely helps mankind.
My inner silence
Unconditionally serves
My Lord Supreme.

20766

Pray to God
With the rising sun,
Meditate on God
With the blossoming moon,
When your life is riddled
With self-doubt.

20767

A sleeplessly self-giving heart
Is unparalleled
In God's beauty-creation.

20768

God's Satisfaction-Pride
In the soul's smile
Can easily challenge
The pride of death.

20769

Although God's manifestation
Is taking place
By very small degrees
And not by leaps and bounds,
God is extremely satisfied.

20770

God is not discouraged,
God is not disheartened,
But God is confused
About why the mind
Is so constantly unwilling
To aspire.

20771

Only a silence-mind
Can remain unperturbed
By thought-threats.

20772

Make a conscious and constant effort,
O seeker,
To end your unhappiness-indulgence.

20773

The freedom of the desire-mind
Is either absolutely shallow
Or completely empty.

20774

A God-searching mind
And a God-loving heart
Are the dependable deputies
Of the soul.
