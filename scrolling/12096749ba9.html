23845

Meditate, meditate, meditate
Only on gratitude.
There shall come a time
When you are bound to feel and become
God's own Infinitude.

23846

When my heart hoists my life's
Hope-banner,
My soul hoists my Lord's
Promise-Fulfilment-Banner.

23847

Every day
I need a blossoming faith
So that I can expedite
My God-discovery-journey.

23848

I can escape from everything
Save and except one thing:
God's Compassion-Eye.

23849

A true seeker
Can never bid farewell
To God's Hope and Promise.

23850

If we cannot silence
Our own mind,
Then peace will never be
Within our reach.

23851

God's proudest Smile
Is not for a self-sufficient man,
But for a God-supplicant heart.

23852

Those who play
The inferiority-superiority-game
Are not yet ready to enter into
The spirituality-boat.

23853

Concern is good.
Non-attachment is better.
But what we actually need
Is the fulfilment of God's Will
In God's own Way.

23854

My Lord Supreme,
May my heart and I
At every moment
Be eager to be absorbed
In gratitude-tears.

23855

An aspiring life
Knows no end
Of the spirituality-journey.

23856

Doubt is the first barrier
In our spiritual life.
Unwillingness is the last barrier
In our spiritual life.

23857

Alas,
When I look within
I see a doubt-monster.
When I look without
I see a fear-ghost.

23858

If we walk through
God's Dream-Garden,
Then we shall find all the answers
To our spiritual questions.

23859

We feel we are tired
At every moment.
But alas,
We are never tired enough
To close our mind-door.
