16130

There are many gods
To arouse curiosity
In my mind.
But there is only one God
To awaken divinity
In my heart.

16131

My Lord's Heart
And my Lord's Satisfaction
Are infinitely more important
Than my perfection-hunger-aspiration.

16132

Wisdom-light
Is free of charge in Heaven.
Alas, on earth
Its absence is deplorable.

16133

I do not mind
If my outer smiles
Come only to go.
But I shall die
If my inner cries
Do not forever stay.

16134

Can you not see that
Your possession-hunger-mind
Has ruthlessly possessed
Your renunciation-hunger-heart?

16135

Your soul's strength
And your heart's length
Are God's blessingful Smiles.

16136

God is telling me
That if I need Him
As my true Friend,
Then He will not allow me
To be with my false friends,
Fear and doubt.

16137

My inner being
Shall dance and dance
If ever my outer life
Becomes a blade of humility-grass.

16138

God does not know
How to complain.
He only knows sleeplessly
How to entertain.

16139

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me the strength
To wrestle with my titan-fate!

16140

Your life is not
Your mind-thorn.
Your life is
Your heart-blossom.

16141

O my mind-fool,
You think you can pluck
Satisfaction-fruits
From desire-tree!

16142

God does not want me to sing,
Not because I am a bad singer
But because He wants and needs
Only a good listener.

16143

Nobody will value
Your heart's sacred secrets.
Share your sacred secrets
Only with your Inner Pilot,
For He will not only value them
But also treasure them.

16144

Not for only the selected few,
But for all and sundry
My heart-garden-entrance
Is free.
