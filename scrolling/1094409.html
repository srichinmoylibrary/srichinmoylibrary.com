Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Malaysia and Cambodia in December 2001.

14 December 2001

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

1 My Lord Asks Me ...

My Lord asks me
How I can be so low
That I want to give Him
A solo performance.

2 God The Eye ...

God the Eye
Feeds my God-aspiration-hunger.
God the Heart
Feeds my God-manifestation-hunger.

3 Nothing Else Is Our Birthright ...

Nothing else is our birthright
Save and except
Our inner delight.

4 The More Possessions ...

The more possessions,
The more processions.
The less outer prosperity,
The more inner divinity.

5 God Comes Down ...

God comes down
From His transcendental Height
To measure my heart,
And not
To measure my house.

6 God’s Visible Love For Me ...

God’s visible Love for me
Is my sleepless service-life.

7 I Must Transform ...

I must transform
All my mind-stories
Into my God-pleasing
Heart-songs.

8 The Soil Of Faith ...

The soil of faith
And the seed of love
Receive from God
His transcendental Blessings
And Pride.

9 God Cries And Cries ...

God cries and cries
When the fulfilment of our desires
Gives us endless pleasures.

10 We Must Not Ask God ...

We must not ask God
Why, how, when and where.
We must tell God
That we are sleeplessly ready
With our eagerness-heart
To please Him in His own Way.

16 December 2001 ...

16 December 2001

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

11 In Our Heart-Garden ...

In our heart-garden,
Each flower reminds us
Of our meditation-hours.

12 Every Morning ...

Every morning
We shall have to make a choice:
Either to remain in our mind-sighs
Or in our heart-skies.
