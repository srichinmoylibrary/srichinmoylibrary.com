10 September 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
How I wish You could give me
More, infinitely more,
God-manifestation-responsibilities!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 September 1999, 8:22 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (87 reps)

11 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have come to realise
That when I take pride in thinking
And feeling
That You love me infinitely more
Than anybody else,
That is the time I create
An insurmountable barrier
Between You and me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 September 1999, 8:17 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 540 lbs.)

11 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Let me stop talking about
My spirituality —
My aspiration, my dedication,
My love, my devotion, my surrender
And so forth —
Once and for all.
Let me speak only about
Your infinite Compassion
And infinite Forgiveness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 September 1999 8:27 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm twenty-three times. (12 right, 11 left)

11 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my heart is bathed in tears,
I pray to the visible God
For His Compassion and Forgiveness.
When my heart is wreathed in smiles,
I meditate on the invisible God
For His own Satisfaction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 September 1999, 8:45 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

11 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O my God-disobedience-mind,
Alas, will there come a time
When you will truly be ashamed
Of yourself?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 September 1999, 9:05 a.m.

12 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life-boat every day ply
Between my heart's hope-shore
And my soul's promise-shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 September 1999, 8:15 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 600 lbs.)

12 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my mind believes,
My heart receives.
And when my heart receives,
My life achieves.
What does my life achieve?
It achieves Your Compassion,
It achieves Your Satisfaction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 September 1999, 8:28 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 540 lbs.)

12 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I am in my outer existence-life,
I am my body-temple.
When I am in my inner existence-life,
I am my heart-shrine.
When I am in my inmost existence-life,
I am my soul-deity.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 September 1999, 9:06 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

13 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My dreamboat-life
Is sailing and sailing and sailing
In my Lord's
Compassion-Heart-Dream-Sea.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 September 1999, 8:20 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 70 lbs.

13 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I was born to please You
In Your own Way,
And even when I am dying,
I shall please You in Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 September 1999, 8:43 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

13 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every morning
I renew my unconditional surrender
To Your Will
And immediately I receive
Thunderous applause from Your Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 September 1999, 8:56 a.m. Before lifting 275 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 550 lbs.)

13 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today my soul is using
Its adamantine will-power
To compel my mind to touch
The dust of Your Feet
And please You always
In Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 September 1999, 9:06 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm.

13 September 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today,
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
You want me to enjoy
The highest flight
On Your Vision-Wings
In Your Infinity's Sky.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 September 1999, 9:22 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

14 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day, every hour,
Every minute, every second
I must consecrate my earth-existence
To Your Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 September 1999, 7:56 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously five times. (Total: 600 lbs.)

14 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every second of my life
I must offer my breath
To absolute God-obedience.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 September 1999, 8:09 a.m. Before lifting 275 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 550 lbs.)
