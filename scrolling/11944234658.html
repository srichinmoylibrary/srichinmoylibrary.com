8730

Each pure thought
Comes from Heaven
With its heavenly powerful promise.

8731

If your life is confined
By common and uninspiring things,
Your heavenward passage
Will untimely be closed
By death.

8732

If you are a genuine seeker,
Then you must always get approval
From your heart
Before you do anything.

8733

You fool!
How can you think
That God's unconditional
Compassion-Eye
Will remain impassive in your life?

8734

All doubtful thoughts
Are worse than sudden invasions
From the dark unknown.

8735

The heart's satisfaction-train
Runs only
On life's perfection-track.

8736

If you want to stop anything
In your life,
Then stop your mind's suspicion-train
Immediately!

8737

Set your mind's
Captive thoughts
Free.
Make your mind's
Oneness-will
Perfect.

8738

The mind's imperfections
Fade
At the heart's perfection-gate.

8739

Although my mind is not fully awake,
I shall break asunder
My mind's prison cell.

8740

For a God-loving
Cheerful heart
There can be no mask
Of fear.

8741

My sleepless hunger
Is supremely blessed
By the Compassion-Heart of Time.

8742

A gratitude-heart
Is man's most beautiful
And powerful gift to God.

8743

Today's problems are killing you.
Why do you want to be totally annihilated
By inviting tomorrow's problems?

8744

By cursing your mind's darkness-night
You cannot conquer it.
If you want to conquer it,
Then disperse it.
