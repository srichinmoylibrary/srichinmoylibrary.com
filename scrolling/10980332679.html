13 God-Realisation ...

God-realisation
Is
A solo run.

14 God-Manifestation ...

God-manifestation
Is
A relay run.

23 November 2006 ...

23 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

15 Every Breath Of My Heart ...

Every breath of my heart
Is needed
To worship the golden dust
Of God’s Feet.

16 Every Day ...

Every day
The Godward speed
Of my heart
Baffles my mind.

17 The Aspiration-Heart ...

The aspiration-heart
Knows no sunset years,
But the doubtful mind does.

18 There Is Not A Single Day ...

There is not a single day
That God does not show
His red caution-flag
To our disobedient mind and vital.

19 Our Heart’s God-Devotion-Tears ...

Our heart’s God-devotion-tears
Are full of
God-Touch-thrills.

20 God The Creator ...

God the Creator
Blesses
My climbing aspiration.

21 God The Creation ...

God the Creation
Embraces
My spreading dedication.

24 November 2006 ...

24 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

22 The Human In Me Wants ...

The human in me wants
The world’s thunderous praise
All the time.

23 The Divine In Me Needs ...

The divine in me needs
Blessingful Grace from God
All the time.

24 To Meditate ...

To meditate
Is to have a quick dive
Into God’s Heart-Swimming Pool.

25 Standing, I Can See ...

Standing, I can see
The Golden Shore
From my wide-open
Heart’s door.
