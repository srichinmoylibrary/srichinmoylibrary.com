930.

Heart's gratitude
And life's surrender
Are two infrequently trodden paths.

931.

God's Compassion-Eye
Enjoys its authority
Over God's Justice-Hand.

932.

A surrender-heart
Completes the Heaven-climbing journey
In half the usual time.

933.

The heart is always
A promise-fulfilling God-satisfaction-student
In life's aspiration-dedication-school.

934.

An unconditional surrender-life
Is always blessed with a plethora
Of God-manifestation-opportunities.

935.

Long before God's choicest Son,
Peace,
Arrives for good in the earth-arena,
The unwillingness of the mind must resign
On the grounds of old age and incapacity.

936.

I do not blame anybody on earth
For not enjoying
The frightening anecdotes
Of my doubting mind.

937.

God commands my heart
Not to take any service
From my God-displeasing mind.

938.

God lovingly and proudly
Takes my surrender-heart
Into His Confidence-Delight.

939.

Is there any human mind
That is not a victim-slave
Of expectation-master?

940.

There was a time
When I was a great friend
Of my doubt-specialist-mind.
Now I am proud to be
The most intimate friend
Of my faith-specialist-heart.

941.

O my mind,
When are you going to stop gathering
Dangerous world-gossip-thorns?

942.

The heart of Mother-Earth
Lives always in anticipation
Of the arrival of God's
Peace-Infinity.

943.

My heart's gratitude
Must pervade each and every aspect
Of my life.

944.

Like my soul,
My heart also accepts God's Will
As eternally indisputable.
