23945

Each good thought
That you have encouraged
And nourished
Is your life's true work of art.

23946

The good will
That you offer to others
Comes to the fore
To manifest your own divinity.

23947

The moment you are about
To forget God's Dictates,
Soulfully think about
Your morning prayer
And your morning meditation.

23948

Although you have already seen
Thousands of days,
O seeker,
When tomorrow dawns,
You can create something absolutely new
In your life.

23949

You can constantly transcend yourself
Only by discovering
And maintaining your inner joy.

23950

When you have realised the Supreme
In His own Way,
You will never, never manifest Him
In a human way.

23951

Even when ignorance captures
The Supreme's dearest children,
He never accepts defeat.

23952

Just do the right thing
In the inner world and in the outer world.
Lo, you will realise the Highest
In the way that is meant for you.

23953

Physical brothers
Appear and disappear,
But spiritual brothers
Follow the path
Of eternal friends.

23954

O children of the Supreme,
You are not the children of the past,
But the children of the eternal Now.

23955

Our true satisfaction
Entirely depends
On our own sincere inner cry.

23956

If you work hard inwardly,
Automatically you will be blessed
With gratitude.

23957

Our inner vision sees
That silence
Not only has creativity inside it,
But is creativity itself.

23958

A God-lover's true discoveries:
God the Inner Pilot
Is all Beauty;
God the Inner Pilot
Is all Sweetness.

23959

Inspiring poetry is spirituality
Revealed and manifested
In a soulfully transcending
And fulfilling way.
