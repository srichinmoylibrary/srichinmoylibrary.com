7601

A new song I have learned:
My life shall become
A perfect achievement
Of my heart.

7602

Mind,
Someday you will understand
Why I am not allowing you
To doubt humanity.

7603

God does not know
At least one thing.
He does not know
How to hate Himself,
The thing all human beings
So perfectly know.

7604

Division-desire
Versus
Oneness-aspiration.
The human in me
Always chooses the wrong one:
Division-desire.

7605

Be a heart of patience
And a life of dynamism.
God will love you more.

7606

No entrance fee is needed
To enter into
God's Compassion-Heart.

7607

My heart,
I am mourning
The unexpected demise
Of your aspiration-life.

7608

The heart-interpreter
Is excellent in interpreting
God's God-thoughts
To the human mind.

7609

Aspiration
Must listen
To realisation.

7610

Desire-tree
Has all destruction-branches.
Aspiration-tree
Has four branches:
Love,
Devotion,
Surrender
And perfection.

7611

Why do you gamble away
Your shockingly limited
Life-energy?

7612

The fulfilment
Of your desire-life
Is outside the sphere
Of spirituality.

7613

The more you eat
Faith-food,
The closer you are
To God.

7614

I do not want to see
Your familiar face
Of frustration.
I want to see
Your heart of aspiration.
