Every Aspiration-Day Begins (1)

October 14th, 1986

Every aspiration-day begins
      With new possibilities.
Every aspiration-day ends
      With new achievements.

Morning Is the Time to Accept

October 14th, 1986

Morning is the time
To accept God’s Love-Light.
Noon is the time
To become God’s Love-Height.
Evening is the time
To reveal God’s Love-Delight.

No Path Can Be Too Hard for You

December 15th, 1986

No path can be too hard for you
If you have one God-Gift:
      Faith in yourself.

With My Human Songs

December 31st, 1969

With my human songs
     I wash my Lord’s
Compassion-Forgiveness-Feet.

They Came Only to Go

December 31st, 1969

They came only to go,
But I came to stay,
Forever to stay,
Inside the Golden Boat
Of my Beloved Lord Supreme.

God, If You Break My Head for Me

July 10th, 1987

God, if You break my head for me,
     I shall break my heart for You.
God, since I shall not disappoint You,
     I cannot expect disappointment from You, either.
God, You start the game and I shall end it.

Many, Many Lives Ago

March 4th, 1989

Many, many lives ago,
God taught me
How to love Him
And how to serve Him
      Unconditionally.

What Can Equal God’s Ecstasy?

April 7th, 1989

What can equal God’s Ecstasy?
Man’s bold promise to Him
To become another God.

Let My Yesterday Peacefully Sleep

February 11th, 1990

Let my yesterday peacefully sleep.
Let my today brilliantly dream.
Let my tomorrow powerfully awake.

Madal the Child, Sleeplessly Wild

August 2nd, 1990

Madal the child,
     Sleeplessly wild.
Chinmoy the teenager,
     An uncertain wager.
Sri Chinmoy the man,
     A sad failure-plan.
Guru Chinmoy, Guru Chinmoy,
     God and man’s broken toy.

Madal the Child, All Cute and Wild

August 2nd, 1990

Madal the child,
     All cute and wild.
Chinmoy the teenager,
     An earth-climbing wager.
Sri Chinmoy the man,
     God’s own Rainbow-Plan.
Guru Chinmoy, Guru Chinmoy,
     God and man’s fondness-toy.

Happy Are My Eyes (1)

December 25th, 1990

Happy are my eyes
To see You, my Lord.
Happy is my heart
To feel You, my Lord.
Happy is my life
To feel You, my Lord,
As my own, very own.

Be Not Afraid of God

December 28th, 1990

Be not afraid of God,
      Be not afraid.
Your sweet reunion
      Need not be delayed.

Enthusiasm Is God’s God-Beauty

December 28th, 1990

Enthusiasm is
God’s God-Beauty
In His seeker-children.

My Lord, Break Me

December 28th, 1990

My Lord, break me.
  My vital is ready.
My Lord, melt me.
  My heart is ready.
My Lord, mould me.
  My mind is ready.
My Lord, make me.
  I am fully ready.
