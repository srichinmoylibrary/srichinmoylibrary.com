Basanar Mane Ghuribona Ar

December 31st, 1969

Basanar mane ghuribona ar
Kamana guhai ghuribona
Akuti agun jwalabo parane
Asakti dore bandhibona

Translation:

I shall no more roam
In the forest of my mind...

O Kanaya Balbe Ki Hai Kothai Tomar

December 31st, 1969

O kanaya balbe ki hai
Kothai tomar ananda bhandar
Ami tomar kripar kangal
Amar tumi khama avatar

Prem Sagare Pulak Bhare

December 31st, 1969

Prem sagare pulak bhare
Dub dibo go dub dibo
Samarpaner bijoy nishan
Tomai ami aj dibo

Translation:

[Partial translation]
With my heart's soulful joy
I shall dive in the sea
Of Your Infinite Love.

Sanjher Tara Ami Sanjher Tara Habona

December 31st, 1969

Sanjher tara ami sanjher tara
Habona ami kabhu pathahara
Bhitare mor shatek bhanu indu
Ananda dheu nai je bedan bindu

Saral Sahaj Bale Nahi Kichu More Chale

December 31st, 1969

Saral sahaj bale
Nahi kichhu more chale
Chaudike shudhu heri
Bidhatar jaya bheri

Sonali Jyotir Ami Rupali Chhaya

July 24th, 1981

Sonali jyotir ami rupali chhaya
Param prabhur paye sanpinu kaya

Sunil Jaladhi Sunil Akash Sunil Pratibhu

December 31st, 1969

Sunil jaladhi sunil akash
Sunil pratibhu bishalata
Bhakti hiyar mukti duar
Dibe more amarata

Tulosi Pata Tulosi Nahi Rajani Tamasi

December 31st, 1969

Tulosi pata tulosi
Nahi rajani tamasi
Namra shir tulosi
Ekata snato ushasi

Vidagdha Man Dyuloker Dhan

September 13th, 1981

Vidagdha man dyuloker dhan
Ati durlabh jani
Kripa karo more asima parama
Maha devi binapani
