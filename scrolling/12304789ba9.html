My Lord, Kick Me

December 28th, 1990

My Lord, kick me.
That is the only way
I can move forward.

My Lord Supreme, Before I Die

December 28th, 1990

My Lord Supreme,
Before I die
I shall leave only one legacy:
My devoted life’s gratitude-heart.

Do Make My Life Three Dimensional

December 28th, 1990

My Lord Supreme,
Do make my life three dimensional:
“Try, cry, fly!”

My Lord Supreme, Now Is the Time

December 28th, 1990

My Lord Supreme, now is the time
For me to give You
My inner life of gratitude.

Success-Day I Like

December 28th, 1990

My Lord Supreme,
Success-day I like.
Progress-sun I love.

My Lord Supreme, Yesterday I Gave Up

December 28th, 1990

My Lord Supreme,
Yesterday I gave up
     One thing: desire-mind.
Today I am giving up
     Another thing: expectation-life.

My Lord Supreme, Your Encouragement I Love

December 28th, 1990

My Lord Supreme,
Your Encouragement I love.
Your Punishment I treasure.

My Lord Supreme, Your Universal Push

December 28th, 1990

My Lord Supreme,
Your Universal Push
  Awakens me.
Your Transcendental Pull
  Liberates me.

O My Pilot Supreme

December 28th, 1990

O my Pilot Supreme,
Your outer Name is Concern-Sky.
Your inner Name is Forgiveness-Sun.

What I Wanted

December 28th, 1990

What I wanted was a flicker of hope,
But my Lord came and stood before me
To give me His Infinity’s All.

My Hero God-Loving Soul

December 30th, 1990

My hero God-loving,
  God-serving soul,
You have won today
  The supreme Goal of Goals.

Rich God Knows How to Forgive

December 31st, 1990

Rich God knows
How to forgive,
How to forget.
Poor human beings
     Know neither.

O My Heart, Your Fondest Dream

January 3rd, 1991

O my heart, your fondest dream
Is constant God-satisfaction.
And my bravest promise is
  God-manifestation,
     Here and now.

Concentration-Power

January 7th, 1991

Concentration-power I had,
      And I still have.
Meditation-light I had,
      And I still have.
Contemplation-delight I had,
      And I still have.
But God-Manifestation-Smile I never had,
      And I still do not have.

My Self-Transcendence-Dream-Reality-Shore (1)

April 8th, 1991

My self-transcendence-dream-reality-shore.
