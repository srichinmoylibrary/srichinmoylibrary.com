16645

The divine in me
Loves everybody.
It does not know how
To blame anybody.

16646

Before, I was a punctual
God-expectation-dreamer.
But now I have become
A punctual God-surrender-lover.

16647

My soul and my heart
Every day exchange
One love-song each.

16648

My Lord, teach me only
Two most melodious lines:
"I love You only;
I need You only."

16649

God has finally allowed me
To become a citizen
Of His Satisfaction-Kingdom.

16650

My adoration-longing-tears,
Your beauty far transcends
My description-capacity!

16651

My surrender-heart
Is the kingdom
Of my life's Heavenward dreams.

16652

Alas, at times
I do sincerely feel
That I can live without God,
But God cannot live without me.

16653

I am not bragging
When I tell the world
That every day God tells me
In fondness-whispers
That I am another God.

16654

Determination minus ego
Is the beauty-smile
Of a perfection-life.

16655

Just try
A soulful cry.
Lo, God is
His powerful Smile!

16656

Silence-peace-poverty
Is overwhelming
Here, there and everywhere
On earth.

16657

My mind's sorrowful dreams
Are always followed by
My life's tearful realities.

16658

Alas, I am not looking for
Hope-trees,
Not even for hope-plants,
But only for a few
Tiny hope-seeds!

16659

Nobody can fathom
The beauty of your heart's
Sacred and secret tears.
