7615

Your soul hates you
Because you argue
With your heart.

7616

Responsibility
Has lengthened his life
And strengthened his heart.

7617

Each temptation-seed
Becomes a frustration-tree
And a destruction-fruit.

7618

He always turns
A deaf ear
To the conversation
Of his mind's desires.

7619

My mind,
Because of you
I am in constant
Failure-trouble.

7620

Your concentration-arrow only
Can silence
Your noisy mind.

7621

A heart of aspiration
Is a free pass
To the perfection
Of Heaven.

7622

No gulf
Between his heart's
Aspiration-cry
And his life's
Dedication-smile.

7623

You also can do!
You also can become!
It is absolutely unnecessary
To pretend.

7624

You used to think
That you knew nothing
About divinity.
Now you are nothing other than
Divinity itself.

7625

There was a time
When you were afraid
Of falsehood.
Now falsehood is afraid
Of your aspiration.

7626

You are supremely good.
You do not have to appear
Exceptionally great.

7627

Do not avoid
But transform
The things that need
Transformation.

7628

Before you speak
To man,
God wants you to speak
To God first.

7629

Your doubting mind
May not change,
But you can change.
You can tell your mind
That you will have nothing
To do with it.
