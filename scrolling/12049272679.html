19115

O my mind,
When are you going to realise
That you are limited
Only by your unlimited impurity?

O my heart,
When are you going to realise
That you are limited
Only by your unlimited insecurity?

O my life,
When are you going to realise
That you are limited
Only by your unlimited unreadiness?

19116

Each day is a new day.
Each new day is a new way
To see the Vision-Eye
And sit at the Compassion-Feet
Of your Lord Supreme.

19117

No, never!
Ingratitude-weeds cannot grow
In a prayerful and soulful
Devotion-heart-garden.

19118

May each day inspire me
To become a gratitude-surrender-seeker
And to remain in my Lord's
Golden Heart-Boat.

19119

Why do I meditate?
I meditate precisely because
My meditation proudly carries me
To the shore I seek.

19120

When we become
The wildfire of enthusiasm,
Nothing can remain unaccomplished!

19121

Finally my unaspiring mind
Is rescued
From endless reluctance-river.

19122

If we have a genuine
Aspiration-hunger,
God will definitely bless us
With the most delicious feast.

19123

The Infinite within Divinity
Is as sure
As the beauty inside a flower.

19124

God is happy only when
The gratitude-surrender-seekers
Beat His manifestation-drum.

19125

Only those will be successful
In the spiritual life
Who have the indomitable courage
To face their life-problem-challenges.

19126

My heart's gratitude-tears
Have finally washed away
My mind's unwillingness-barriers.

19127

God is so happily and proudly
Welcoming
My success-life and progress-heart.

19128

Doubt-producing minds
Will eventually be bound
To beat a shamelessly speedy retreat.

19129

How can we expect
To make our mind peaceful
And our heart blissful
If we do not keep God
As the centre of our attention
All the time?
