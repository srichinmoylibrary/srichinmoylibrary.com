17201

When God feeds my eyes
With His Smile,
I feel that I have become something.

When God feeds my heart
With His Smile,
I feel that I already have everything.

17202

A clear mind
And a pure heart
Can always be proper instruments
Of their inner guide:
The soul.

17203

If you are a genuine seeker,
Then your life cannot be
A chronicle of ceaseless desires
And endless doubts.

17204

Alas,
I do not know
How I can remove
My mind's countless
Unwillingness-weeds.

17205

If you sponsor
God's manifestation-race,
Then God is bound to show you
His Satisfaction-Face.

17206

Whenever there has been a race
Between the mind and the heart,
The heart has always eventually won,
Gloriously won,
Of course, at God's choice Hour.

17207

Divinity's story unfolds
With a purity-life
And a humility-heart.

17208

Before I do anything,
I always take permission
From my Heaven-climbing
Aspiration-heart.

17209

Alas,
Is there any aspiring heart
That has not been scarred
By intensity and profundity-sorrows?

17210

There is no crime
That God cannot forgive,
But we have to spend
Many sleepless nights and days
At His Forgiveness-Feet.

17211

True,
Ignorance-storm is very powerful,
But it has to pass away
Someday,
Sooner or later.

17212

Human life lives between
Noise-confusion
And choice-illumination.

17213

Each soul is the possessor
Of divine interdependence-qualities
And not of independence-qualities.

17214

O my mind,
Be quiet
And prayerfully watch
The unhorizoned dawn
Of the future.
