9115

Faith has been chosen
  By God Himself
To be the campaign manager
  For God's Victory.

9116

If you do not silence
  Your self-indulgence supreme,
How can God feed you
To immortalise your heart's inner hunger?

9117

  Never say no
To God's Compassion-Eye-Request
If you want to have a life
  Of sleepless ecstasy.

9118

Try to see only God's Satisfaction-Face.
Then only will you be able to face
This ingratitude-world every day.

9119

Satisfaction in giving,
Satisfaction in receiving,
Satisfaction in transcending,
Satisfaction, satisfaction, satisfaction
Is the only God for me.

9120

  Great achievements
Do not demand great sacrifices.
  Great achievements
Need only a Compassion-Flood
From the Absolute Lord Supreme.

9121

Cultivate purity in your heart.
You will be able to rediscover
  Before long
The Kingdom of Heaven.

9122

O unconscious mind,
By any chance
Have you ever read
God's unconditional Compassion-Book?

9123

The life of freedom
You will be able to enjoy
Only after you have climbed
The tower of wisdom.

9124

God definitely wants you
To buy stock
  To your heart's content
In His Compassion-Company.

9125

If you do not want to recognise
The seeds of doubt
  Inside your mind,
God will not grant you
The fruits of faith
  Inside your heart.

9126

His heart's simplicity-life
  Was badly misunderstood
Even by one of his immediate possessions:
  His mind.

9127

  "Does God exist?"
This is your question.
God does exist.
He exists not only for you
But also for everybody else.
See if this answer can satisfy you.

9128

Unless and until your desire-life
Is reduced to nothingness,
You cannot become a member
Of God's universal Fulness-Society.

9129

He has run his aspiration-life
  So well
That God is all appreciation
  And satisfaction.
