18715

A heart of God-surrender
Does not know
What frustration-life is.

18716

You can change the world
Only with the beauty
Of your prayers
And the fragrance
Of your meditation.

18717

An obedience-heart
Has a free access
To God's Heart-Home.

18718

Inspiration I need
To see the world properly.
Aspiration I need
To love the world perfectly.

18719

Alas,
Every day we squander
The Nectar-Smiles
Of God's Compassion-Eye.

18720

In the morning I pray to God
To illumine my mind.
In the evening I pray to God
To illumine my life.

18721

I must run forward
To the future-sun
With my back
To the past-clouds.

18722

Eventually
All our aspiration-flames
Will enter into
Our soul's luminosity-sun.

18723

What I need
Is a spark of hope.
Then promise
Will come by itself.

18724

Today the mind is enjoying
Its refusal to listen to God.
Tomorrow the same mind will be bound
To obey and fulfil God.

18725

What God wants you to have
Is a life of introspection
And not a life of deprivation.

18726

The mind craves
Life-supremacy.
The heart longs for
God-intimacy.

18727

If your heart is the possessor
Of faith-blossoms,
Then your mind's doubt-weeds
Will, without fail,
Have to surrender.

18728

There is no finish line
In the perfection-race.
Perfection is an eternal run.

18729

Alas, I need a mind
That is eager to unlearn,
And I need a heart
That is eager to love.
