11960

There shall come a day
When my heart's peace-light
Will devour my mind's empty anxiety.

11961

Alas, your demanding vital
Every day
Foolishly removes God's 'Welcome' sign.

11962

If there are any ill feelings
Between your aspiring heart
And your searching mind,
Then help them cement their friendship
Immediately.

11963

Each time I have an aspiration-cry,
God grants me a very special
And very fruitful boon.

11964

If you are eager to visit
Your soul's homeland,
Then you must never disregard the power
Of your life's precious time.

11965

Your unaspiring mind
Is responsible
For your unwanted thoughts.

Your undisciplined vital
Is responsible
For your repeated failures
In the battlefield of your life.

11966

Do not feed your ego-monster,
For it is always ready
To devour your heart's
Beautiful aspiration-cries.

11967

If you want your mind to succeed powerfully
And your heart to proceed speedily,
Then your aspiration-life must start
At the ground level of self-offering.

11968

You must destroy your mind's
Attachment-bonds
If you want to visit
Your heart-beauty's satisfaction-garden.

11969

Your heart's inner ties with God
Will definitely increase
God's Affection-Light and
Fondness-Delight for you.

11970

God may not accompany me
Wherever I go,
But God's Forgiveness-Light
Sleeplessly and invariably accompanies me
No matter where I go.

11971

Do not say no when God asks you,
Out of His infinite Compassion,
To serve Him
So that you can eventually become
A choice instrument of His.

11972

Meditation is the only thing
That can most gloriously replace
Your failure-past
With your fulfilment-future.

11973

God may break all His other Promises,
But He never breaks one special Promise,
And that special Promise is
His Heart's immortal Forgiveness-Promise.

11974

Since human life is fleeting,
Each human being must offer
Something fruitful and abiding
To the ever-compassionate Mother-Earth.
