1001.

My Lord Supreme,
May my aspiration-heart
Never be an empty promise to You.

1002.

My Lord, I keep You inside
My mind's huge bag.
Where do You keep me?
"My child, I keep you inside
My Heart's most charming Pocket."

1003.

When I offer my Lord Supreme
My life's unconditional breath,
His entire Being brims with
His transcendental Pride for me.

1004.

I have not come into the world
To derail ignorance-train,
But to transform ignorance-train
Into God-Satisfaction-Perfection-Plane.

1005.

When I was an ancient seeker,
I served God while loving.
Now that I am a modern Avatar,
I love God while serving.

1006.

A heart redolent with gratitude
Is a highly valued
God-treasure.

1007.

Not laughing at world-imperfections,
But spreading our compassion
All around
Is the right way to please our Lord Supreme.

1008.

God the Perfection-Specialist
Every day offers Himself
To save the imperfection-finalist.

1009.

My Lord,
Do give me the soulful capacity
To enjoy the fragrance
Of Your Heart-Home-Garden.

1010.

If you are nothing but
Self-giving love,
How can you have any room
For a suspicious mind?

1011.

My Lord, I want to be caught
Every day, every hour and every minute
In the sweetness of my devotion-current.

1012.

My Lord,
You have taught my heart
How to love You.
You have taught my life
How to serve You.
Can you not now teach my breath
How to fulfil You?

1013.

No matter how I cry,
No matter how I smile,
No matter how I try,
I can never receive enough
Of the Beauty
Of my Lord's Compassion-Eye.

1014.

May my aspiration-heart
Be found on the fastest flight,
On the very wings of angels,
To arrive at God's Heart-Kingdom.
