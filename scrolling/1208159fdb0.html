22360

Each time we delay
In our self-offering,
We just increase the length
Of our suffering.

22361

The rising sun of inner joy
Can easily sweep away
The dusty, ugly doubt-thoughts.

22362

Your mind's unhappy reality
Can be transformed into
Your heart's happy dream.
Just pray to God with ceaseless tears.

22363

No doubter
Can ever be cheerful.
No doubter
Can ever be powerful.
No doubter
Can ever be fruitful.

22364

Do not allow your mind
At any time
To invade your silence-heart.

22365

The darkest nights
Are behind us.
The brightest days
Are ahead of us.

22366

No miracle can be
As illumining and fulfilling
As the miracle
Of unconditional love.

22367

When I pray,
I marvel at
My own hidden divinity.

22368

Light will blossom
In the darkness of the mind's jungle
If we just pray
And grow into a patience-tree.

22369

Each time I treasure
A divine thought,
I unveil a supreme Truth.

22370

O stupid mind!
Love your heart
Instead of trying
To rule your heart.

22371

When I live inside
The beauty of my heart,
I feel that there is nobody on earth
Who is unworthy of my love.

22372

War is only
An unanswerable question
And never
An illumining answer.

22373

The unaspiring mind
Quite often invites
And even orchestrates
A self-imposed disaster-life.

22374

If you are a true God-lover,
Then you will be sleeplessly eager
To reveal God's Heart-Blossoms
On earth.
