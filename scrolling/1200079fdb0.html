14360

A perfect disciple's life
Is worth all the trouble
Previously created by him.

14361

A calculating mind
God never appreciates.
A self-giving heart
God not only loves
But also treasures.

14362

According to God,
Everybody has natural,
Illumining and fulfilling
Aspiration-capacity.

14363

The inner doctor
Always finds
That the outer patients
Are incurable
Precisely because
They are too impatient.

14364

Nothing beautiful,
Nothing meaningful,
Nothing fruitful
Can be produced
By the fear-factory
Of the human mind.

14365

My Lord,
I really do not want to wait
For Eternity
For the transformation
Of my human nature.

14366

O my mind,
When will you start running forward
Instead of walking backward?

14367

Every day my soul and I
Are desperately looking
For a burial ground
For my past blunders.

14368

There can be no dead end
On purity's
Happiness-progress-highway.

14369

O my mind, it is you
Who are deliberately enjoying
A self-imposed exile
From God's Compassion-Kingdom.

14370

God wants my soul to know
Who is who
In the aspiration-world.
God wants my heart to know
Who is who
In the dedication-world.

14371

Yesterday my aspiration-account
Was short of funds.
Today my dedication-account
Is short of funds.
Tomorrow my surrender-account
Will be short of funds.
Alas! Alas! Alas!

14372

A genuine seeker
Will definitely be found
Between his life's greatness
And his heart's goodness.

14373

He who wants to make
The fastest progress
Should have this motto:
"Now is the only time!"

14374

Alas,
Is there any day
When my mind's enthusiasm-flag
Is not at half-mast?
