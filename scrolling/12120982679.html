26215

God is a cosmic Joker,
But He never jokes about
Our heart's aspiration, life's dedication
And mind's gratitude.

26216

The Western ideal of superlatives
Will not be appreciated
By the members of the inner world.

26217

We have been finding fault
With God's activities
And we want to continue
Our argument with God.
But poor God definitely does not want
To prolong our conversation,
Not to speak of argument.

26218

God's Compassion and God's Patience
Have not slept
For millions and billions of years.

26219

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me the capacity
To garland Your Eye and adorn Your Feet
With my life's dedication-smiles.

26220

O seeker,
Do not allow waves of sorrow
To extinguish your aspiration-flames.

26221

Be regular, be punctual
And be all determination.
You are bound to succeed!

26222

O seeker, pay no attention
To the world's savage criticism.
You are more than perfect
In God's ever-blossoming Compassion-Eye.

26223

If you are enmeshed
In the net of human emotion,
How can you get divine satisfaction
From your life?

26224

Falsehood can either be
Truth in the making
Or truth in the process of breaking.

26225

What is wisdom?
Wisdom is something
That reconciles opposite truths.

26226

No matter how long or how much
Divine Love God squanders,
It always remains totally undiminished.

26227

A true God-lover
At once traverses time
With an eagerness-heart,
An eagerness-mind and eagerness-feet
To arrive at the Destination.

26228

Neither the North Pole nor the South Pole,
Nor any corner of the globe,
Can ever limit or diminish
The Beauty of God's universal Dream
And transcendental Reality.

26229

God's Will and my surrender-smile
Have been working together
Excellently.
