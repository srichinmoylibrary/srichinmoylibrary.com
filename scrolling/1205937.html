20101

O let my heart sow!
Let others' lives reap!

20102

When I mix with earth-wallowers,
I feel that it is too late
For me to aspire.
When I mix with Heaven-followers,
I feel that it is too soon
For me; I am not ready.

20103

If we do not have
A burning yearning,
We cannot succeed
Even in our ordinary human life.

20104

O humanity,
I do not want to be
Even one step above you.
I want to be
On the same footing,
Not only to make you happy
But also to make myself really happy.

20105

Where is God?
I wish I knew.
Who is God?
I wish I knew.
What is God to me?
I wish I knew.
In how many ways am I pleasing God?
I wish I knew.
Have I ever disappointed God?
I wish I never knew.

20106

O my Lord Supreme,
Do empty my mind
Of all my unpleasant
Disobedience-stories.

20107

Anger thinks that
It can rule the world.
Depression thinks that
It is ruled by the world.

20108

The divine in me is rising
With the promise-sun.
The human in me is sinking
With the hope-moon.

20109

My Lord,
Will You continue to forgive me?
"Yes, my child, I shall."
My Lord,
Will I continue to show You
My gratitude?
"My child,
You should and you must!
But even if you do not give Me
Your gratitude-heart,
My unconditional Love for you
Will forever remain the same."

20110

The mind treasures
Stupid expectations.
The heart welcomes
Wise hopes.

20111

If we do not chase away
The hostile force-clouds
The way we chase barking dogs,
How can our divinity fully blossom?

20112

Alas,
The distance between
The mind's suspicion
And the heart's aspiration
Is almost unbridgeable.

20113

If your devotion
Is a mere display,
Then your God-receptivity-reservoir
Is bound to shrink.

20114

If you are a victim of pride,
There is only one way
For you to free yourself:
Take shelter
In your humility-heart-garden.
