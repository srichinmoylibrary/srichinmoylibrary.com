21475

My heart's aspiration
Thrives
Only when my life's dedication
Multiplies.

21476

O my mind,
Aspire, aspire!
If not, you will be buried
In the dust of despair.

21477

The power of love
Is extremely beautiful and fruitful.
The love of power
Is extremely dangerous and contagious.

21478

Once you enter
Into the spiritual life,
Do not allow imaginary fears
To threaten and frighten you.

21479

The animal in me teaches me
How to ignore God.
The human in me teaches me
How to doubt God.
The divine in me teaches me
How to adore God.

21480

My soul teaches me
How to have
Mountain-high aspiration.

My heart teaches me
How to have
Fountain-beautiful dedication.

21481

Alas, when shall I realise
That I am still in bondage-chains
Because I believe
That my capacity
And not God's Compassion
Can liberate me?

21482

Mine is the soul
Made of God's golden Dreams.
Mine is the heart
Made of my broken dreams.

21483

An unfaithful God-seeker
Without fail has to walk
Along the mind's calamity-avenue
Or the vital's destruction-lane.

21484

Each time I do not accept God's Will
As my own, very own,
I am doomed to an unavoidable,
Inescapable and unbearable failure.

21485

I owe my earth-existence
And my Heaven-existence
To my inner bodyguard:
God's Compassion-Heart
And to my outer bodyguard:
God's Protection-Eye.

21486

My Lord, I long to take from You
Many lessons.
May my first lesson be in losing
My heavy ignorance-weight.

21487

Alas, will I ever be able
To receive from God
His world-tolerance-award?

21488

I am blessed with a shower
Of peace-petals from Above
Only when my meditation
Is smilingly, soulfully
And self-givingly deep.

21489

I am utterly ashamed
Of my doubt,
For it doubts even my heart's
Faith-life.
