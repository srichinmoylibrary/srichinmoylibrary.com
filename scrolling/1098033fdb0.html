52 To Walk With Life ...

To walk with life
Is beautiful.

53 To Sleep With Death ...

To sleep with death
Is peaceful.

54 My God-Surrender ...

My God-surrender
Means
A life of constant surprises.

55 Heaven Beckons Only ...

Heaven beckons only
The sleepless self-givers.

56 God Sings And Sings; ...

God sings and sings;
I sleep and sleep.
I sing and sing;
God dreams and dreams.

30 November 2006 ...

30 November 2006

Belek, Turkey

57 Each Additional Responsibility ...

Each additional responsibility
Is another golden opportunity
To please the Inner Pilot
In His own Way.

58 An Aspiration-Heart ...

An aspiration-heart
Without fail embodies
Many divine qualities.

59 Each Seeker’s Life ...

Each seeker’s life
Must fly into
The highest aspiration-sky.

60 When I Pray ...

When I pray,
I use my humility-knees.

61 When I Meditate ...

When I meditate,
I use my world-oneness-heart.

62 A Readiness-Life ...

A readiness-life
Needs
An eagerness-start.

63 Every Day ...

Every day
The Master is
His torrential Heaven-blessings,
His Heaven-love
And his Heaven-concern
For his disciples.

64 The Master Keeps ...

The Master keeps
His disciples’ sorrows
Not on his shoulders,
But deep inside
His compassion-heart.

1 December 2006 ...

1 December 2006

Belek, Turkey
