27 My Aspiration-Heart-Sunrise ...

My aspiration-heart-sunrise
Gives my Lord joy
Far beyond my imagination.

28 My Heart Lives Only ...

My heart lives only
For the Beauty and Fragrance
Of my Lord’s Closeness-Heart.

29 My Outer Life ...

My outer life
Heavily leans
On God the Duty.

30 My Inner Life ...

My inner life
Sleeplessly and breathlessly relies
On God the Beauty.

1 February 2003 ...

1 February 2003

Gold Coast, Australia

31 I Felt God’s Forgiveness-Heart ...

I felt God’s Forgiveness-Heart
Long before I saw it.

32 My Choice ...

My choice:
Either God’s Nectar-Feet
Or my pride-head.

33 Neutrality Is Not ...

Neutrality is not
Wisdom-light.
Neutrality is
Weakness-foolishness.

34 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Will the long, very long years
Of my utter helplessness,
Hopelessness and uselessness
Be ever over?

35 Hope-Heart Definitely Blossoms ...

Hope-heart definitely blossoms.
It blossoms only
At God’s choice Hour.

36 The Binding Mind ...

The binding mind
Has to be either illumined
Or blinded.

37 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Eagerly devours
The sincere seeker’s
Himalayan blunders.

38 When Oneness ...

When oneness
Embodies everyone,
Fulness it becomes.

39 There Are Many ...

There are many
Secret and sacred ways
To enter into God’s Heart-Home.
The seeker can play the role
Of the chooser.

40 Face And Brave Your Arch-Enemy ...

Face and brave your arch-enemy
With the beauty and fragrance
Of your God-smiling heart.
