15545

An obscure mind
And murky thoughts:
These are the real impediments
On our way to the Golden Shore.

15546

The love of God
Is not and cannot be
My choice.
It is undoubtedly
His Choice
Operating in and through me.

15547

Every day
My Lord descends
Into my gratitude-heart
To purify and intensify it.

15548

You can forget
All other prayers
If you want to,
But do not forget
Your God-Forgiveness-prayer.

15549

This morning
God came to see you.
To His extreme sorrow,
He saw your world-devouring mind
And not your self-giving heart.

15550

Even God cries loudly
When He sees that humanity's desire-hunger
Is increasing enormously
Every day.

15551

God may forgive
My undivine deeds.
Even my soul may forgive
My misdeeds,
But I don't think I shall be able
To forgive them,
When my aspiration-hunger-eagerness
Is intensified.

15552

Remember the real reason
Why God's Compassion-Eye has left you.
His Compassion-Eye has left you
Only because
You have allowed your mind
To desert your gratitude-heart.

15553

Hope-flowers
Do descend from Above.
But unfortunately,
We do not treasure them.
Alas, we do not even value them.

15554

What I badly need
Is my ancient heart
Of aspiration-flames
To see the divine beauty
Of my God-oneness-life.

15555

My soul sang most powerfully
When my mind was fully awakened
To receive God's Light.

15556

The truth,
As I heard it then,
Was different from now.
God was supposed to come to me, then;
Now, I am supposed to go to Him.

15557

If you want to see
The beauty of Heaven,
Then use God's Eye.
If you want to see
The patience of earth,
Then use God's Heart.

15558

What your heart needs
Is a gentle, mild and delicate Touch
From the Compassion-Eye of God
To revive its sleepless aspiration-hunger.

15559

O God, save me!
Save me from the strangling rope
Of my own insecurity,
Anxiety and impurity.
