15.

Mine is the heart
That cannot conceal peace.
Mine is the mind
That cannot reveal peace.

16.

A heart of peace
Has the supreme authority
To predict the immortal birth of peace
In the outer life.

17.

Peace is not
Property-prosperity-achievement.
Peace is
Self-enlightenment-attainment.

18.

A life of peace
Is the perfect perfection
Of the heart.

19.

God has not forgotten
To give us peace.
He is just waiting for us
To ask for it.

20.

Peace is
My heart's sacred book.
Oneness is
My life's devoted reader.

21.

Peace has no imperfection.
It never prompts us
To do anything wrong.
On the contrary, it only helps us
To do everything
Soulfully, unconditionally
And perfectly.

22.

God has always been extremely proud
Of the human beings
Who have peace,
And He always will be.

23.

Poor is he
Who does not have peace.
Poorer is he
Who does not need peace.
Poorest is he
Who does not want to hear
People talking about peace.

24.

Nothing is as high as peace.
Nothing is as deep as peace.
Nothing is as God-pleasing as peace.

25.

Peace is the only silence-power
That I need
To live happily with mankind
Without any fear.

26.

Peace is
The only divine wisdom-light
That successfully operates
In human affairs.

27.

I love you
Not because you have tremendous power,
But because
You are all peace.

28.

Because you have peace,
You are divinely great.
Because you want to share your peace
Lovingly and cheerfully with me,
You are supremely great, plus perfect.

29.

Power is the most difficult thing
To work with.
Peace is the easiest thing
To work with
And also the most rewarding.
