1275.

Why do I love God?
I love God because He is
My life's all-fulfilling
Silence-flooded Partner.

1276.

Every day
If you do not dive deep within
In your God-discovery-expedition,
How can you ever be
The darling of Heaven?

1277.

Just as day
Claims our beautiful eyes,
Even so, night
Claims our soulful heart.

1278.

The mind has two exceptional qualities
Which the wise heart does not want to share:
It loves to debate;
It loves to berate.

1279.

The heart has two exceptional qualities
Which the doubting mind cannot appreciate:
It loves to surrender;
It loves to sing a oneness-song.

1280.

Wherever I go,
I carry my God-oneness-heart-thrill,
Not proudly
But self-givingly.

1281.

My heart never needs
A recommendation from anybody
To visit God.

1282.

My mind-life
Is full of contradiction.
My heart-life
Is full of illumination.

1283.

My Lord Supreme's
Compassion-Eye
Quite often works in and through us,
Even uninvoked.

1284.

Train your mind
For transformation.
Train your heart
For contemplation.
Train your life
For perfection.

1285.

When God's Heart cries,
Nobody knows.
When God's Eye smiles,
Everybody knows.

1286.

I offer my Lord
My life's trials and tribulations.
God gives me
His Life's inner Peace
And His Heart's inner Bliss.

1287.

I tell all my aspiration-comrades
They can reach me lovingly
Only at my heart-home-address.

1288.

God definitely needs more teammates.
Therefore, my heart's aspiration-cries
And my soul's illumination-smiles
Are trying out for God's Team.

1289.

Each time my mind
Has a pure thought,
I see God enjoying
His Happiness-Heart.
