8001

In all my joys and sorrows
I see the Compassion-Eye
Of my Beloved Supreme.

8002

If you sincerely and desperately
Need God,
Then God will send
His private Satisfaction-Plane
To fly you to Heaven.

8003

O my blind mind,
Do learn the art of hiding.
Try, you will succeed!

8004

God does not mind
When you speak ill of Him.
Therefore, why do you waste
Your precious time?

8005

I have received
A last warning from God.
He is not going to wait for me
Anymore.

8006

The blind world
Wants to enjoy
The chaotic dance
Of destruction-war.

8007

Responsibility itself
Is a rich reward
From Heaven.

8008

If the pilot
Does not value the goal,
Then how can the passengers
Value the goal?

8009

God the Justice
Has a Teacher:
God the Compassion.

8010

Love your goal!
Automatically
You will make progress.

8011

God's Diary
Is full of seekers'
Heart-to-heart conversations
With God.

8012

Man's depression
Challenges God's Compassion
Every day.

8013

I cannot satisfy myself,
My Lord.
Do You think You can accomplish
This impossible task
For me?

8014

Although you are quite honest,
You enjoy
Dishonest gossip-surges.
