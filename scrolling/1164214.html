1.

Each day your snow-white heart-life
Can claim a blue soul-dream.
Each day your sweet Lord is building
In you another Supreme.

2.

To wear the bright crown
Of Immortality,
You must challenge the strength
Of fatality.

3.

O, trust and trust
Your heart of love.
It is your own soul's
Precious dove.

4.

In your fire is
Nowhere oneness.
Only your ego's
Deathless darkness!

5.

With all my might
I shall plunder
My blighting mind's
Destruction-thunder.

6.

Yesterday you were awaiting
God's Compassion-Arrival.
Today you are enjoying
God's Peace-Bliss-Festival.

7.

Pray not: you live
In the sombre lap of death.
Do pray: you grow
Into Immortality's Breath.

8.

Your purity-heart gives thanks to God,
Your purity-heart gives thanks,
Because you are a stranger to
Your mind's impurity-pranks.

9.

Your heart has to make
Only one commitment:
To spread the divinity
Of your soul's enlightenment.

10.

If you heed not your soul's
Determination-call,
You will soon suffer
A bad failure-fall.

11.

Inside your Lord's
Protection-Ring
His Victory-Song
Sing and sing.

12.

Live inside your heart's
Gratitude-tower.
You will be the hero
Of God's God-Hour.

13.

Your mind is unready;
Your body is asleep.
God's Nectar-Delight
How can you drink deep?

14.

Love more and more
Your heart's morning hopes
If you want to untie
Your life's binding ropes.
