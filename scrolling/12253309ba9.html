Do Not Give Up

December 31st, 1969

Do not give up.
Tomorrow you will pluck
Love-stars from above.
You will become within God-dove.
You will be the connection-tie
Between humanity’s sigh
And divinity’s sky.

O Sun of My Soul

December 31st, 1969

O sun of my soul,
I devotedly love your duty.
O moon of my heart,
I soulfully love your beauty.
O star of my life,
I surprisingly love your purity.
O sky of my search,
I love your generosity.

As a Seeker-Soul

December 31st, 1969

As a seeker-soul
You will always win and gain.
As a lover-soul
You will be the hyphen
Between freedom and chain.

O Saviour Dear

December 31st, 1969

O Saviour dear,
You are so near.
O Lover kind,
Bind my heart, bind.
O Father pure,
I am all yours, sure.

O Winning Speed

December 31st, 1969

O winning speed!
You are our treasure-doll.
O losing speed,
You sadden us all.

My Tiny Boat I Row

December 31st, 1969

My tiny boat I row.
My little body I drag.
All-where my ignorance-blow.
Shameless! Yet I brag.

I Prepare My Faith-Hunger

December 31st, 1969

I prepare my faith-hunger.
I declare my hunger-meal.
I open my self-giving door.
Destruction’s dark eyes I seal.

My Peace I Treasure

December 31st, 1969

My peace I treasure
Beyond all measure.
My surrender I cherish.
My ‘I’-ness I tarnish.

Believe It or Not, I Love

December 31st, 1969

Believe it or not,
I love my duty’s flow.
Believe it or not,
It spreads far and wide
Beauty’s glow.

In Your Case, What You Need

December 31st, 1969

In your case,
What you need
Is a life of self-control.
In his case,
What he needs
Is a life-denial role.
In my case,
What I need
Is a Perfection-Goal.

Everything That a Man Needs

December 31st, 1969

Everything that a man needs
He surely cares
     To have.
Everything that a cosmic God has
He surely dares
     To have.

If You Love God

December 31st, 1969

If you love God,
You will remain
Your unburied name.
If you love yourself,
You will be part and parcel
Of your deceased fame.

How to Know the Singer?

December 31st, 1969

How to know the singer
From the song?
      Easy!
The singer is God’s unparalleled
      Vision-Duty.
The song is God’s unprecedented
      Perfection-Beauty.

They Love Your Compassion-Heart

December 31st, 1969

They love your compassion-heart.
You love their blossoming soul.
They love your God-manifesting role.
You love their God-realising Goal.

Nothing I Do

December 31st, 1969

Nothing I do
Can satisfy you.
What shall I do?
I shall love you more
     And
Keep your body-soul
Closer to my heart-door.
