14490

If you do anything soulfully
And do not succeed,
Do not feel sad.
Rest assured,
God will take the blame.

14491

To lighten God's heavy burden,
Think of God ceaselessly.
To enlighten your mind,
Pray to God breathlessly.

14492

God smilingly and proudly
Breathes in
Our heart's flower-fragrance.

14493

God tells me that He loves
My soulfulness-mind.
God tells me that He needs
My fulness-heart.

14494

Before my God-realisation,
I loved God
For His Greatness and Goodness.
After my God-realisation,
I have been loving God
For His Sweetness and Fondness.

14495

Each surrender-prayer
Of my heart
Will be garlanded and crowned
By my inner divinity's Lord Supreme.

14496

Expand, expand
Your willingness-heart
Sleeplessly and breathlessly
Into the vastness-universe.

14497

With my prayerful heart
I start my Eternity's journey.
With my soulful life
I continue.

14498

An unwillingness-vital
And a hesitation-mind
Can destroy the beauty and purity
Of an aspiration-heart.

14499

When I sing before God
My heart's love-song,
His blessingful Comment is:
"Excellent, My child!"
When I sing before God
My heart's devotion-song,
His powerful Comment is:
"Perfect, My child!"

14500

When I multiply
My devotion-heart-flames,
My Lord Supreme tells me:
"My child, I have arrived!"
