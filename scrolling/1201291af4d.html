15575

Only the willingness-mind
And the soulfulness-heart
Are considered to be God's great warriors
To fight against ignorance-titan
On earth.

15576

Your doubtful mind
And fearful heart
Are taking you away
From God's Compassion-Heart-Nest.

15577

I need my heart's streaming tears
To see the full Beauty
And full Divinity
Of my Lord Supreme.

15578

It is said
That everything is possible.
Today I have got
Firsthand proof of it.
My doubting mind
Has completely surrendered
To my God-loving and God-adoring heart.

15579

With a positive mind
And a positive heart
We are all expected
To play the game
Of world-harmony.

15580

When I place my head
At God's Compassion-Forgiveness-Feet,
He immediately gives me back
My heart's rainbow-smile.

15581

Humanity
And humanity's stupendous
Stupidity-mind
Have made it impossible for God to see
In this world
His Vision's hope-plants.

15582

My soul every day
Breathes in the secret breath
Of my heart's sacred
God-manifestation-promise.

15583

God-realisation-secrets
Are not secrets at all.
God-realisation is an open book,
Open study, open course and open school.
And God the Teacher
Is also sleeplessly open
To the students' requirements.

15584

Every day
My mind is on the lookout
For satisfaction.
But my mind does not realise
That this satisfaction comes
Only when it is empty
Of world-criticism.

15585

My selfless devotion
And God's Perfection-Manifestation:
These two things
Are Eternity's invaluable treasures.

15586

With my Heavenly friends
I walk, march and run
Along Eternity's Road
To manifest my Lord's
Transcendental Vision-Smiles
Here on earth.

15587

The only thing you need
Is a heart that expands,
So God can move in
Happily and proudly.
Everything else is of no consequence.

15588

May my daily aspiration-life
Become friends with
My soul's simple delight-waves.

15589

If you want to become a passenger
In God's Vision-Speedboat,
Then you must become a peace-dreamer,
Peace-receiver and peace-server.
