15.

Each heart
Is destined to swim
In a sea of bliss.

16.

Nobody is perfect,
But perfection is guaranteed.
By whom?
By God Himself.

17.

When God’s Grace descends,
Even a mind black as clouds
Can be transformed
Into a cloudless sky.

18.

Ignorance-night disappears
When the heart-tears
Start climbing.

19.

Every day
The seeker must try
To drape his body
With his Lord’s Name.

20.

I need
God the Companion
   And not
God the Magician.

21.

Every day
My soul flies
In the sky of blossoming
And becoming.

22.

My Lord,
Blame me, shame me,
But please do not forget
To tame me.

23.

A simplified life
Is a God-satisfied heart.

24.

The outer map
   Directs us
To God’s Eye.

25.

The inner map
   Directs us
To God’s Feet.

26.

God pretends
To understand my mind,
But actually
He does not.

27.

My surrender becomes
The fragrance
Of God’s Heart.

28.

Every day
My Lord Supreme beckons me
To be under
His fear-dispelling Feet.

29.

My eyes
Are earth-bound.
