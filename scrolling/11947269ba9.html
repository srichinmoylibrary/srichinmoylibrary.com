9045

Try to see yourself
From now on
The way God has always
Been seeing you:
As His universal Vision-fulfiller.

9046

Sincerity does not mean
Deliberate strictness
With one's body, vital,
Mind and heart.
Sincerity means
Man's spontaneity
In his self-expression.

9047

God loves
Your heart's aspiration
Infinitely more than He loves
Your mind's contribution.

9048

You want to be
A roaring wisdom-lion.
Alas, the devouring ignorance-tiger
Is right in front of you.

9049

I may not know
Why I have descended,
But I must know
That God has given me
Enough strength
To ascend once again.

9050

He came into the world
To be one with the world-sorrow.
He came into the world
To fulfil God's Promise
In and through him.

9051

Do not waste
Your heart's moonlit hours
And your life's sunlit days.

9052

I guard you
When you are fast asleep.
You give me your all
When you are wide awake.

9053

In fear of tomorrow's sufferings,
You must not give up
Your heart's longing
For satisfaction-delight.

9054

Through repeated attempts
He has developed the unusual capacity
To transform the temptation-life
Into illumination-life.

9055

If you do not know
Where the aspiration-door is,
No harm!
Try to discover at least
The imagination-door.

9056

All your vital problems
Will vanish
And you will be able to weather
All your inner storms
When you develop sleepless aspiration
And when dedication
Becomes your second nature.

9057

Nobody can deceive the soul
Because
God's Omniscience is
The soul's permanent friend.

9058

Now that you have become
Your life's surrender-sunrise,
God is granting you
His Heart's Immortality-Sky.

9059

What my mind desperately needs
Is a true haven
From doubt-war.
