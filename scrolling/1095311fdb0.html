54 God’s Long Stories ...

God’s long Stories
Awaken me.
God’s beautiful Poems
Charm me.
God’s soulful Songs
Thrill me.
God’s Tears and Smiles
Perfect me and fulfil me.

22 January 2003 ...

22 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

55 When I Go To God ...

When I go to God
As a beggar,
He immediately closes
His Eyes and Ears.

56 When I Go To God ...

When I go to God
As His own child,
He immediately stands up,
Blesses me and embraces me.

57 Wisdom-Light ...

Wisdom-light
Is giving and giving.
Ignorance-night
Is self-styled and self-multiplying.

58 God Does Not Appreciate ...

God does not appreciate
My shyness.
He appreciates
My openness.

59 Earth Needs ...

Earth needs
God’s Descent.
Heaven needs
God’s Consent.

60 My Immediate ...

My immediate
And eternal need
Is God-fulfilment
In God’s own Way.

61 Every Second ...

Every second,
Many, many, many God-seekers
Are pleasing God in His own Way.
How I wish I could be
One of those fortunate ones!

62 God-Worshippers ...

God-worshippers
And
God-lovers
On any path
Are my only
Divine brothers and sisters.

63 Like My Soul ...

Like my soul,
How I wish I could spend
All my time
Only with my Lord Beloved Supreme!

64 To Realise God, Reveal God ...

To realise God, reveal God
And manifest God here on earth
Is the birthright
Of a selfless God-lover.

24 January 2003 ...

24 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

65 The Day My Lord ...

The day my Lord
Cannot come to see me,
He sends His Heart to me.

66 The Day My Lord’s Heart ...

The day my Lord’s Heart
Cannot come to see me,
He sends His Eye to me.
