15401

My Lord Supreme,
Your Forgiveness-Eye
Lights my way
To Your Perfection-Satisfaction-School.

15402

God has a very special plan for you.
He wants your heart's sunshine-smile
To play with His Compassion-Eye
As often as possible.

15403

Division-mind-thoughts
Are the real enemies
Of the peace-blooming world.

15404

Your thought-world
Is the result
Of an action-fulfilled world
On another plane of consciousness.

15405

God has reserved
A special place for you
In Heaven
Because you are a solid member
Of His inner circle on earth.

15406

Do not squander
Your heart's silence-joy.
Your heart's silence-joy
Is the only thing
That God likes to keep
As He continues on His
Eternity's Journey.

15407

The Sunshine-Smile
Of my Lord's Heart
Feeds my heart's prayer-cry
Before I start my daily spiritual journey.

15408

These are the aspiration-flames
Of my life:
Oh, where is God?
Oh, will I ever be able
To see Him?

15409

Today I have danced
My final hesitation-heart-dance.
Today I have danced
My final suspicion-mind-dance.
Today I have danced
My final unwillingness-life-dance.

15410

When my Lord claims my heart
As His own,
Out of sheer jealousy
My ego-dominated mind
Disappears.

15411

To make God's Happiness
A real and unmistakable reality,
The God-seekers and God-lovers
Came into the world.

15412

Insecurity and anxiety-dance
Will not grow inside
The peaceful oneness-heart-garden.

15413

Not God-realisation,
But God-satisfaction,
I sincerely consider every day
As my top priority in life.

15414

The more I can appreciate
The beauty, purity and divinity
Of my earth's faith-life,
The sooner I shall be able
To enjoy the downfall
Of my doubtful mind.
