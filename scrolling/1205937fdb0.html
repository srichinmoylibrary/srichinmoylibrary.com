20160

Yesterday
Temptation was treacherous.
Today
Temptation is ferocious.
Tomorrow
Temptation will be voracious.

20161

The mind is
Its own constant,
Unlit ego-impatience-supremacy.

20162

O desire-despair-life,
You may think
That you are invincible,
But definitely you are not.

20163

Aspiration,
My desire-frustration-antidote,
I love your beauty,
I need your fragrance.
Illumination,
My confusion-temptation-antidote,
I love your beauty,
I need your fragrance.

20164

Peace
Beckons the wandering mind.
Peace
Silences the roaring vital.
Peace
Garlands the aspiring heart.

20165

O my mind-train,
Why do you delay so long
At each and every progress-station?

20166

The confidence of the heart
Is the remover
Of all the doubt-obstacles of the mind.

20167

I make God happy
By offering Him
My devotion-heart.
I make God infinitely happier
By offering Him
My surrender-life.

20168

Every day
I shall keep my heart-channel tuned
To my Lord's
Compassion-Affection-Broadcast.

20169

The summit-bliss
Of aspiration-mountain
We can achieve
Only with our meditation-ladder.

20170

When we walk along
Our heart's devotion-road,
Aspiration and satisfaction
Will devotedly follow us.

20171

May my mind always remain
Beyond the reach of thought,
So that I can see, feel and grow into
God's Satisfaction-Smile.

20172

Time is very limited.
Life is very short.
Alas, when will I have
The burning desire
To realise God, manifest God
And fulfil God in His own Way?

20173

Every dream
Has a beauty of its own.
Every dream
Has a fragrance of its own.
Every dream
Embodies tomorrow's God.

20174

Inside each grain of hope,
Every day
God is dreaming
Of a new creation.
