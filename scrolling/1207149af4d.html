21375

Life's trials and tribulations
Bring to the fore
The splendour and lustre
Of our own divinity.

21376

God's outer Promise
Is the inner Smile
Of His unconditional Compassion.

21377

What is doubt?
The thing that punctures our mind,
Tortures our heart
And starves our soul.

21378

Only when we love God unconditionally
Can we enjoy the boundless peace
Of a freedom-sky.

21379

A sleeplessly self-giving disciple
Is indeed
The Master's greatest pride.

21380

May my mind swim every day
In the river of simplicity,
And may my heart swim every day
In the sea of sincerity.

21381

May I each and every day
Sing a never-ending
Surrender-song.

21382

When we do not meditate on God
For the fulfilment of God's Will,
We just allow expectation-thunder
To completely deafen us.

21383

If we do not soulfully pray,
Then our God-obedience-song
Is bound to be out of tune.

21384

My God-reliance
Is the speed and breath
Of my Golden Shore-arriving
Life-boat.

21385

A spotless life of virtue
Is indeed good,
But a sleeplessly self-giving life to God
Is better.

21386

I am not dismayed
By the world's ingratitude,
But I am definitely dismayed
By the world's God-denial.

21387

A blessingful Smile from God
Is indeed a cure
For our heart's
Streaming tears.

21388

God does not expect us
To understand Him.
He just wants us
To derive happiness
From His Will.

21389

My Lord,
You have made me truly happy,
Truly proud, truly fulfilled
By telling me
You have a limitless claim
On my love.
