26401

Each moment we can perform
A thrill of magic
If we abide by the soul's dictates.

26402

If we have a oneness-breath
With God's Will,
There is nothing that we cannot do
For the betterment of humanity.

26403

Blessed are those
Who know how to smile at God's Eye
And how to weep at God's Feet.

26404

My Lord, use me
If it is Your Will.
Do not use me
If it is Your Will.
Do freely exercise
Your Will.

26405

What is love?
It is something that has
Infinite possibilities
To enlarge and expand.

26406

If you cannot conquer your ego
Altogether,
At least keep it at the lowest profile,
So that you can make
A small amount of progress.

26407

Do not think of retiring,
Even when you think your duties
Are all done.
Retirement and enlightenment
Can never be found together.

26408

What do you see
When you look at yourself?
If you see yourself
As an exact prototype of God,
Then you are perfect.

26409

The mind actually does not know
How to smile self-givingly.
It only pretends to smile.

26410

How I wish I could change
The habits of my mind
Once and for all
To please my Lord in His own Way.

26411

Until you stand in front of God Himself,
How will you know correctly
How great and good you actually are?

26412

If you are a self-enamoured
God-seeker,
You will only sink and sink.
You will never rise.

26413

Obedience
Is the foundation
Of my heart-home.

26414

If you are a stranger to persistence,
Then excellence will never knock
At your door.
