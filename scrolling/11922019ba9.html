6545

His mind demands
An immediate answer.
His heart cheerfully waits
With God's soundless Silence.

6546

You have confessed
Your ignorance-crime.
Now start devoting
Your entire being
To God's Satisfaction-Heart.

6547

I race
Not to see God's Face,
But to sit
At God's Protection-Feet.

6548

Be wise!
Constantly keep
Your inner fears and doubts
Under your perfect control.

6549

Share openly
What you have secretly found,
And you will have a special place
In God's Heart-Kingdom.

6550

Do you know
That your oneness-heart
Is one in a million?

Do you know
That your division-mind
Is also one in a million?

6551

Just as your perfection-heart
Has no limits,
Even so God's Satisfaction-Eye
Has no limits.

6552

Even to think
That the time has come
For you to become spiritual
Is indeed
A step in the right direction
To arrive at God's Palace.

6553

Heaven does not want to know
His mind's achievements,
But Heaven wants to measure
His heart's surrender-light.

6554

My little heart,
I do not know how to console you
When you suffer,
But I do want to tell you
That I always love you.

6555

Just because
Your body, vital and mind
Have failed you,
Do not think
That your heart also
Will do the same.
Your heart is made of
God's Satisfaction-Smiles.

6556

You live
In the abysmal chasm
Of ingratitude,
Yet you expect
A life of unmeasured
Fortitude.

6557

Earth ascends to Heaven
When earth does not allow
Its inner happiness to be destroyed
By the dry, reasoning mind.

6558

He changed his name
Into God's Compassion-Eye.
He changed his heart
Into God's Forgiveness-Soul.

6559

Every day
You steal the fruit
Of the pleasure-tree,
Yet you want to be
A satisfaction-fruit
For God to enjoy!
