17390

Each desire-story
Eventually surrenders
To aspiration-song.

17391

How can you ever
Feed and please God
In His own Way,
If you continue enjoying
Your doubting mind,
Insecure heart
And impure breath?

17392

God's Will
Will begin to prevail in your life
If you can
Quite frequently and freely
Show your enthusiasm-smile.

17393

A heart of oneness-peace
Will always find a home
In everybody's heart.

17394

Your soul has done
Countless good deeds for you.
Can you not do
A good deed for your soul,
Just by offering it your gratitude-heart?

17395

Only a seeker
With unconditional surrender
Will be able to build
A new world-promise.

17396

How can I make God happy?
Only by serving God
In God's own Way.

17397

Hon do I serve God?
I serve God
With my gratitude-tears.

17398

If you can enjoy
God-oriented thoughts,
Then your mind's bondage-chains
Will disappear.

17399

Inspiration and aspiration
Are not man-made
But God-blossomed.

17400

My Lord,
May my aspiration-heart
Be given the chance
To hold Your Compassion-Eye
Just for a moment.
