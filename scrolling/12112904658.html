25430

If you use your mind,
You will see how far you can go
From God's Compassion-Eye.
If you use your heart,
You will see how close you can come
To God's Compassion-Eye.

25431

My joy knows no bounds
When my heart sits at the foot
Of God-devotion-tree.

25432

It is always the right time
And the right place
To pray to God
And meditate on God.

25433

The moment you feel
That you are indispensable,
You have dug your grave.
Your spiritual life has come to an end!

25434

In one glance
How much your Master can give you
Will always remain
Far beyond your imagination.

25435

Be careful of your dark vital!
Your dark vital can easily blackmail
Your searching mind
And even your aspiring heart.

25436

Every day I pray to God
To make me a permanent resident
Of His Heart-Country.

25437

Every morning I prayerfully embrace
God's Feet
To illumine my mind
And to perfect my life.

25438

It is so simple
To get up early in the morning
And pray and meditate.
Yet it is so difficult,
Unbelievably difficult!

25439

Unconditional surrender
Is only a few steps away
From self-discovery.

25440

Be careful of your mind!
If your mind once gets hold
Of your sweet dreams,
It will tarnish all their beauty
And fragrance.

25441

If you are too full of yourself,
You will never receive
A Love-Compassion-Blessing-Letter
From God.

25442

You have decided not to complain
Against anybody.
God has decided to entertain
Only you.

25443

I am praying to God
To bring me a pass
To come watch
His Infinity's new creation-dance.

25444

Since mankind is nothing other than
A yawning vigilance,
The hostile forces have a free access
To mankind's heart-temple.
