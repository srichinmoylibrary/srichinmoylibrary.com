25030

Is God suffering because of me?
My sincerity-heart will answer
In the affirmative.
My insincerity-mind will answer
In the negative.

25031

My Lord Supreme,
If You do not break my head,
Then who will garland my heart?

25032

When I wonder how God still loves me,
God tells me
That His Hope for me
Will remain immortal.

25033

You can read a million books;
You may at most get God-inspiration,
But not God-realisation.
For God-realisation, you have to study
Only at your heart's core.

25034

My Lord, I feel not only pure
But also perfect
When I place Your Feet
Upon my heart.

25035

My heart-tears and my soul-smiles
Always live together
Inside God's Satisfaction-Heart.

25036

O my stupidity-mind,
Why do you always hide
From God's all-illumining
And all-fulfilling Smile?

25037

I have sought shelter
Here, there, everywhere.
At long last,
I have found my only shelter
Inside the smiles of God's Name.

25038

Our heart is meant
For oneness-love.
Our life is meant
For fulness-blossom.

25039

Self-giving sees always
The secret Guidance
Of our Lord Supreme.

25040

God is not going to embrace
Your aspiration-heart
If you do not escape the embrace
Of your pleasure-life.

25041

I am extremely proud
Of my Lord Supreme.
Why?
Because He can be
Utterly indifferent to me
To make my inner life
And outer life perfect.

25042

I know there shall come a time
When my desire-life will come to an end.
And now
My desire-life's sincere plea to God:
"My Lord, do not give up on me!"

25043

My Lord, I pray to You
To bless me with a special Boon:
I would like to travel with You
Tirelessly and sleeplessly.

25044

My Lord, when I visit You
I become Your blessingful Smile.
My Lord, when You visit me
You become my prayerful cries.
