Japan, a Soulful Flower-Garden

December 5th, 1978

Japan, Japan, Japan!
A soulful flower-garden.
Clearly you see,
Quickly you do.
Silence-ecstasy
Your property true.
Japan, Japan, Japan!
High Heaven’s hallowed Plan.

Translation

Nihon, Nihon, Nihon!
Tamashii no hanazono!
Chiebukaku shunbin na,
Chinmoku no hooetsu
Shinjitsu no tamamono.
Nihon, Nihon, Nihon!
Tenkai no seinaru nagare!

Simplicity Japan Has

December 31st, 1969

Simplicity Japan has.
Beauty Japan gives.
Sincerity Japan knows.
Purity Japan reveals.
Fear Japan braves.
Doubt Japan tramples.
Humility Japan is.
