13360

Smile, smile,
Soulfully smile and powerfully smile!
You are bound to get back
Your long-lost freedom-days.

13361

Unless you desperately need God,
Your old frustration-life
You will never be able to reverse.

13362

God wants me to be always happy.
Therefore, He always shelters my heart
With His own Rainbow-Dreams.

13363

God definitely wants you to have
A confidence-start
So that your life's insecurity-shadow
Cannot chase you.

13364

Your God-loving heart's
Streaming tears
Will grant you a soulfulness-journey
To a fulness-goal.

13365

The God-seekers' dedication-lives
Are God's
Joy-blossomed Dreams.

13366

God will not allow you to sit
For your life's perfection-examination
Until you have crossed far beyond
Your mind's thought-boundaries.

13367

I keep my aspiration-heart
Teachable.
Therefore, my Lord keeps
His Satisfaction-Heart
Reachable.

13368

A desiring mind
Is very rich.
Every day it dons
A new frustration-garment.

13369

God never forgets to perform
His daily earth-Concern-Duty
To make human beings happy
In His own divine Way.

13370

My Lord, I need a new heart
Which will be filled
With nothing but gratitude-tears.

13371

How can you be happy
When you have allowed yourself
To be mesmerized
By your suspicion-mind?

13372

If you want to make progress
To your satisfaction,
Then never allow yourself
To be at the mercy
Of your demanding vital.

13373

Where is the difference between
An unfulfilled desire-life-blunder
And an earth-shaking thunderbolt?

13374

O my aspiration-heart,
Your most amazing ability is
Your constant sincerity-availability.
