14460

A soulful meditation
Can easily lengthen
The mind's compassion,
Tolerance and sympathy-limits.

14461

My life is a little boat;
It is a feeble cry.
But my soul is a representative
Of the omnipotent,
Omnipresent and omniscient God.

14462

Humanity's orphan-cry
Will definitely
One day be transformed
Into Divinity's Infinity-Smile.

14463

My early morning peace
I receive from
My aspiration-heart-flames
And my dedication-life-plants.

14464

May every human being
Be blessed with
A celestial angel-music-dawn.

14465

God Himself garlands and crowns
His hero-server
When he arrives
At God's Golden Shore.

14466

It is not enough to decrease —
You have to totally diminish
Your contact with
Your mind-doubt-agent.

14467

Gratitude is a divine gift
That we get from the inner world.
Alas, it is undoubtedly
An under-used gift.

14468

When we summarise
Humanity's deplorable situation,
We realise that humanity
Does not really know
What it actually wants.

14469

The difference between
A doer and a watcher
Is that the doer is on
His safe and certain highway
To God's Infinity,
Whereas the watcher can be
Either tomorrow's starter
Or tomorrow's sleeper.

14470

A first-class God-lover
And God-server
Says to God at every moment:
"My Lord, do tell me
What else I can do for You
To please You in Your own Way."

14471

Ambition-body-mind
I am.
Aspiration-heart-soul
I long to be.

14472

God feels extremely sad
That you feed your self-doubt-mind,
Even while your heart
Is conversing with God.

14473

Humanity's dissatisfaction-disease
Is not only widespread
But also impossibly incurable.

14474

An ingratitude-heart
Is bound to enter
Eventually
Into an unknown territory
Of self-peril.
