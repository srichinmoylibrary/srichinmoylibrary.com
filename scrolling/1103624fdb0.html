24 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall not allow my mind to remain
Even one minute more
In the land of God-oblivion.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 August 1999, 5:46 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

24 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today, to my deepest joy
And greatest relief,
My mind said to God,
"My Lord,
Your Will is not only acceptable
And serviceable,
But also adorable and lovable."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 August 1999, 6:10 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

25 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Instead of surrendering
To our ever-increasing
Shortcomings,
We must challenge them
And conquer them
Once and for all!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 August 1999, 9:11 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

25 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Instead of powerfully
Chastising ourselves
For doing the wrong things,
We must divinely
Energise ourselves
To do the right things.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 August 1999, 9:21 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

25 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day I must discover
New ways
To please You in Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 August 1999, 9:28 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. twenty-seven times with one arm. (14 right, 13 left)

25 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do make me a child
Of today's divine newness
And tomorrow's supreme fulness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 August 1999, 9:33 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and seven times with one arm. (53 right, 54 left)

26 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The newness-fulness-pioneer-discoverers
Are Your supremely chosen children.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 August 1999, 9:06 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 50 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (41 reps) New record.

26 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only those who self-givingly
And unconditionally
Try to please You
Succeed at Your choice Hour.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 August 1999, 9:50 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

26 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must not try to improve
The mind of the past.
I must only try to build
My heart-temple of the present.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 August 1999, 10:00 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. thirty-one times with one arm. (15 right, 16 left)

26 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
No hesitation-mind,
No suspicion-mind,
No God-doubt-mind
Will ever be able to arrive
At God's Palace.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 August 1999, 10:14 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 200 lbs. with each arm separately.

28 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What I prayerfully and soulfully
Do,
I powerfully and fruitfully
Become.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 August 1999, 7:58 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

28 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
At every moment I wish to be
Cheerfully and totally obedient
To Your Marching Orders.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 August 1999, 8:06 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. thirty-four times with one arm. (16 right, 18 left)

28 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To the God-worshipper, God says,
"My child,
I have everything for you."
To the God-lover, God says,
"My child,
You are everything to Me."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 August 1999, 8:14 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and four times with one arm. (52 right, 52 left)

28 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, my mind does not believe
In God-surrender.
Therefore, it is saddled
With untold worries and anxieties.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 August 1999, 8:31 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

29 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your earliest and mightiest
Promise to humanity
Is this:
The finite mortal
Shall grow into
Infinity's Immortality.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

29 August 1999, 8:24 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)
