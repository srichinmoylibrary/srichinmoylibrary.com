19101

If your life is
A constant surrender-song
To God,
Then in God's Eye
You are infinitely greater
Than any crowned king.

19102

God's Hand
Will definitely reach down
To help you
If He gets the invitation
From your humility-heart-hut.

19103

God is begging all the seekers
To keep their spiritual priorities
Straight and pure.

19104

When I live in the sweet paradise
Of God-surrender,
I feel at every moment
That I am manifesting God's God-Light
Here on earth.

19105

It seems to me
That I can survive all attacks
Except one:
Expectation-attack.

19106

When our mind falls into
The pride-trap,
Frustration is bound
To follow it there.

19107

Each time I dive deep within
I see clearly
A shortage of God-manifestation
In my life.

19108

Behold,
My gratitude-heart has enabled me
To soar with God
In His Oneness-Joy-Plane.

19109

I know for sure
That my Lord Beloved Supreme
Is not far away from me.
He is just beyond
My mind's ego-screen.

19110

Two instruments I practise
Every morning:
Mind-inspiration-piano
And heart-aspiration-flute.

19111

What is the difference
Between faith and doubt?
Faith is sleeplessly loyal
To the Inner Pilot.
Doubt is invariably disloyal
To the Inner Pilot.

19112

If we allow our mind
To be doubtful and suspicious,
And our heart to be insecure and impure,
Then one day,
To our wide surprise,
We will see that we have become
Incredibly hospitable to hostile forces.

19113

I shall follow
My compassionate Lord Supreme
No matter where.
I shall follow
My inspiration-soul-bird
No matter where.

19114

O my mind,
No more will you be able to fool me
With your false silence-peace.
