13001

Since I have started singing
God's Victory-Songs unconditionally,
God is telling me
That my ignorance-days are numbered.

13002

Ask your pleasure-life
How far away you are
From God's company.
It will tell you unmistakably!

13003

Although your mind fails
To count on God,
God's Compassion
Does count on you.

13004

A heart of purity is always
The protection-armour
Of a God-lover.

13005

If you are looking for gratitude
In the mind,
Unfortunately you are looking
In the wrong place.

13006

Self-giving
Is the brightest jewel
In the darkest
Ignorance-night-sky.

13007

O my soul,
Have no vision-rival!
O my heart,
Have no aspiration-rival!
O my life,
Have no realisation-rival!

13008

O mind,
How long will you remain
Chained to doubt?

O heart,
How long will you remain
Chained to insecurity?

O life,
How long will you remain
Chained to ignorance?

O world,
How long will you remain
Chained to indifference?

13009

I do not need
A sound-announcer.
I need only
A silence-navigator.

13010

The moment I sat
At God's Compassion-Feet,
I was out of failure's reach.

13011

My heart's soulful tears
Have enabled me to make friends
With Infinity's Smile.

13012

O my mind,
Today you are a prince of pessimism.
But I assure you,
Tomorrow you will be a perfect swimmer
In the hope-river.

13013

Do not kill
Your heart of hope
With your stupidity's failure-sword.

13014

A heart of purity
And a mind of sincerity
Are indeed the gateways
To gratitude-satisfaction.
