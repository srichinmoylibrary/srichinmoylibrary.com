20515

Neither time nor space
Can ever reduce or dull
The lustre of God-realisation.

20516

Your task
Is just to begin.
God, in His infinite Compassion,
Wants forever to continue.

20517

Two things are sadly absent
From this world:
A compassion-eye
And
A forgiveness-heart.

20518

God tells me that
My aspiration
Is my task
And my perfection
Is His Task.

20519

O my Lord Supreme,
I have come to realise that
Unless You become dictatorial
From now on in my life,
I shall make no abiding progress.

20520

You will drown
In the sea of tears
If you daily,
Consciously and deliberately
Disobey the illumination-whispers
Of your soul.

20521

O empire-conqueror,
Who needs you?
Who wants you?
Not me!
Not in the least.

O heart-conqueror,
Where are you?
Do come and live with me
In my sweet little heart-nest.

20522

The unwillingness-body,
The unwillingness-vital
And the unwillingness-mind
Will always be armed
With innumerable excuses.

20523

Wherever you go,
Just remember to do one thing:
Carry your heart's
Morning joy with you.

20524

Alas, even God,
Who has infinite Compassion,
Finds it difficult to endure
The ceaseless chatter
Of my monkey-mind.

20525

Your life shall reach
Sublime heights of progress
If you do not remain
Inside your mind,
Or if your mind does not live
Inside the vital and the body.

20526

Who can be more stupid
Than you are?
How can you refuse to listen
To your soul's
Illumination-injunctions?

20527

The worldwide shortage
Of God-surrender
Is the root cause
Of the world-failure.

20528

When God-disobedience
Is simply impossible for you,
God will be more than happy
To have you
Under His direct tutelage.

20529

Do not visit God
With empty hands.
Do not visit God
With an empty heart.
But do visit God
With an empty mind.
