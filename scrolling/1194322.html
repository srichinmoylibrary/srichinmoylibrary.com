8601

Are You there, God?
Would You mind coming over to me
With Your Compassion-Eye
And Your Forgiveness-Heart?

8602

Wisdom does want to live
In the house of ignorance,
But it is ignorance
That does not want wisdom.

8603

You do not have to give God
Either your best or your worst.
Just give Him
What you eternally are:
A Heaven-climbing cry.

8604

You can easily be
At the eternal gate
Of the immortal Self.
Just do not cling
To earth-shore.

8605

The gospel of love
Is meant to show
Neither what God has
Nor what God is,
But to tell the world
That God is all for you.

8606

Only a constantly self-giving
God-lover
Will be known as a God-giver.

8607

What the world has:
A giant mind of war.
What the world needs:
A tiny drop of oneness-peace.

8608

Because you have a soulless heart,
You are unable to obey
Your compassionate Inner Pilot.

8609

Why delay any longer
Since you know perfectly well
That you will be obliged
To manifest God on earth
In His own Way?

8610

There is no difference
Between a desire-bound life
And a futile funeral pyre.

8611

I love God
For what He has for me:
Infinity.
God loves me
For what I am to Him:
A constant necessity.

8612

Tell the world what you know
About God's Compassion-Eye.
Your heart will be flooded
With delight.

8613

Alas,
God has not given me
The capacity to endure
Heaven's incurable indifference.

8614

If you are not ready to run
With your Inner Pilot,
Then the lower worlds
Will force you to walk
With death.
