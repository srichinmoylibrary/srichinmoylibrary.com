7415

Now that you have forgotten
How to display,
God is going to teach you
How to play
His Oneness-Game.

7416

Do not postpone
Your God-appointments.
Your God-oneness-soul
Will never forgive you.

7417

Alas,
He is totally lost
Between his heart's need
And his mind's greed.

7418

You have come to God
With your curiosity-mind.
God is giving you
His Divinity's Luminosity-Heart.

7419

As possibility
Cannot be exhausted,
Even so inevitability
Cannot be annihilated.

7420

No crying and climbing prayer-letters
From your heart
Will come back from Heaven
Unanswered.

7421

Peace you want
In your heart?
Then pray for silence
In your mind.

7422

Although your mind
Has given up the sunlit path,
The heart of the sunlit path
Has not given up on your mind.

7423

Not how you sing
But for Whom you sing
Is of supreme importance.

7424

God helped my heart
To soar.
I helped my mind
To sink.

7425

Forget not
Daily to count
The God-blessed, teeming miracles
In your dedication-life.

7426

An invincible determination-hero:
This is what
His aspiration-heart is.

7427

A new sun
Is for a new heart.
A new day
Is for a new life.

7428

Believe it or not,
Every day
My Beloved Supreme gives me
His Vision-Kite to fly.

7429

A self-seeking mind
Cannot fathom
A God-discovery-heart.
