29 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In the worlds of God-realisation
And God-manifestation,
Time never runs out.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

29 September 2000, 6:05 p.m. Before doing 760-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

29 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
It is an extremely difficult task
For the mind to learn even one
God-surrender-song.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

29 September 2000, 6:12 p.m. Before doing 960-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

30 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Compassion is boundless.
Your Forgiveness is boundless.
Alas, yet I fail to love You
Wholeheartedly.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 September 2000, 9:43 p.m. Before doing 770-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

30 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Compassion-Eye
Awakens my heart.
Your Forgiveness-Heart
Fulfils my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 September 2000, 9:56 p.m. Before doing 970-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

1 October 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Since everything has an end,
I am sure my desire-life
Also will come to an end.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 2000, 9:03 p.m. Before doing 780-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

1 October 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind says: punishment.
My heart says: enlightenment.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 2000, 9:13 p.m. Before doing 980-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

2 October 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind says to me:
God is only for Sunday worship.
My heart says to me:
God is not only for everyday worship,
But also for every hour, every minute
And every second worship.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 2000, 1:56 p.m. Before doing 800-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record and ultimate goal.

2 October 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God wants my heart
To be His own Beauty's angel.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 2000, 2:09 p.m. Before doing 1,000-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record and ultimate goal.

3 October 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my Lord Beloved Absolute Supreme
Always find me willing, eager
And self-giving.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 October 2000, 8:31 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

3 October 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Compassion and Concern
Have travelled far and long
To meet with an unconditionally
Surrendered seeker.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 October 2000, 10:40 a.m. Before doing 800-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

4 October 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My desire-mind leads me
To greatness-might.
My aspiration-heart leads me
To goodness-delight.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 October 2000, 7:21 p.m. Before doing 800-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

4 October 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Two things I really love:
My heart's aspiration-height
And
My life's dedication-delight.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 October 2000, 7:37 p.m. Before doing 1,000-lb. dumbbell bench press.

5 October 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me that
My prayers to You
Shall save my life
And my meditations on You
Shall fulfil my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 2000, 7:33 p.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

5 October 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The human in me enjoys
Resting and sleeping.
The divine in me enjoys
Serving and manifesting.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 2000, 7:47 p.m. Before doing 1,000-lb. dumbbell bench press.

5 October 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, my ego-mind cannot appreciate
My Lord's Compassion-flooded Presence.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 2000, 8:01 p.m. Before doing 800-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.
