23601

When the mind's simplicity
And the heart's purity
Grow together,
They create unimaginable beauty.

23602

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me a heart of hope
And a life of promise
That will never die.

23603

My Lord is delighted beyond measure
When He sees that my gratitude-heart
Is never out of breath.

23604

God wants to heartily congratulate
My willingness-mind
On its eagerness-performance.

23605

If you give from the heart,
You win.
But if you take with the mind,
You lose.

23606

Success
Always intoxicates
My mind.

Progress
Always satisfies
My heart.

23607

There remain only two choices
In my life:
Either I look forward
Towards my destination,
Or I look backward
Towards the animal kingdom.

23608

There is no greater sorrow
For God
Than my constant unwillingness
To obey Him.

23609

I must reach
My God-Destination-Shore
Regardless of
My mind's inclement weather,
Relying upon
My heart's eternal Spring.

23610

God wants human beings
To be interdependent at least,
Since we do not depend on Him
Completely at every moment.

23611

Compassion
Is God's beginningless past
And God's endless future.

23612

My doubting mind's sincerity
Amuses God.
My loving heart's purity
Inspires God.

23613

The only reason
Why God is fond of you
Is because you are sleeplessly devoted
To Him.

23614

For want of inspiration-freedom,
His aspiration-life
Is descending and descending daily.
