70 I Do Not Want My Faith ...

I do not want my faith
To be fruitful.
I want my faith
To be Godful.

9 January 2005 ...

9 January 2005

Qingdao, China

71 We Cannot Really ...

We cannot really
Surprise God,
But He pretends
To be surprised
Even at our minor success.

72 I Long Only To Have ...

I long only to have
God’s Compassion-Eye
And God’s Forgiveness-Heart
In my heart-home.

73 The Mind Shocks God ...

The mind shocks God
By not accepting immediately
God’s Light.

74 I Have Closed The Long Distance ...

I have closed the long distance
Between me and my Lord
With my sleepless and breathless
Surrender.

75 I Must Always Keep ...

I must always keep
My aspiration-heart-standard
As high as possible.

76 There Is No Other Way ...

There is no other way
To develop my closeness to God
Than with my prayerful
And tearful eyes.

77 When I Touch ...

When I touch
God’s Forgiveness-Feet,
God immediately opens up
His Compassion-Eye.

78 The Modern Life ...

The modern life
Is blindly racing
Towards a goalless goal.

79 When I Pray To God ...

When I pray to God For His Victory, He immediately blesses me With two inspiration-wings And one aspiration-heart.

80 When The Heart, The Mind And The Vital ...

When the heart, the mind and the vital
Become real friends,
The soul dances ecstasy’s dance.

14 January 2005 ...

14 January 2005

Xian, China

81 Self-Effacement ...

Self-effacement
Is the first step
In our self-exploration.

82 Alas ...

Alas,
My heart and my mind
Have extreme
Opinion-differences.
