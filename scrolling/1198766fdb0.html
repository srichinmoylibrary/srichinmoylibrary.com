13060

My Lord,
If You have a minute,
Then do save me
From indifference-isolation.

13061

My heart's purity
Is just across the street
From my soul's divinity.

13062

A soulful gratitude-heart
Perfectly fulfils
The Vision-Eye of God.

13063

Aspiration, aspiration, aspiration
You need
If you do not want your life to be
Paralysed by frustration.

13064

A purity-heart
Can easily enjoy
Immunity from self-doubt.

13065

There is only one reason
Why God is silent.
He is silent because
You have turned a deaf ear to Him.

13066

God comes to us
With a Compassion-Suitcase
And not a suggestion-box.

13067

My soul is my Heaven's
Laughing roar.
My heart is my earth's
Crying core.
My life is my dream's
Vanishing shore.

13068

My Lord,
Do make my mind grateful
For what You have given me,
And my heart grateful
For what You have not given me.

13069

Dear Divinity, do tell me,
Is there any time
When you do not love my heart?
Is there any time
When you do not cry for my life?

13070

A real seeker has to be
A perpetual stranger
In desire-land.

13071

God wants me to tell you
That soon He is going to increase
Your love and surrender-power.
And also He asks me to tell you
To go and see Him
For further details.

13072

Once in a lifetime
You get an appointment to see God.
Therefore, be fully prepared
And be very punctual!

13073

Farewell, farewell,
My young and old ambition-friends!
Now I have Eternity
As my aspiration-friend.

13074

I know, I know, my Lord,
That what You have given me
Is beautiful.
I know, I know, I know, my Lord,
That what You have not given me
Is fruitful.
