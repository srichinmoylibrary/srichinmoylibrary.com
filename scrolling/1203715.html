17901

Every day
I must steer my life-boat
With my eagerness-heart
And my enthusiasm-mind.

17902

If you want to succeed
In anything in life,
Then you must use
Your soul's dauntless lion-power.

17903

Not only individual and collective
But also universal
Is the tendency to deliberately ignore
The dictates of the Inner Pilot.

17904

With your heart's streaming tears
And not with your unshed tears
You can erase your dark past.

17905

Today's
Self-indulgence-blessing
Inevitably becomes
Tomorrow's
Life-destruction-curse.

17906

Nobody cares to know
What I am doing.
Alas, why do I care
To bare my secret thoughts?

17907

A man of sincerity
Is dismayed
By his own mind's
Bold shout-power.

17908

Each and every flame
Of the aspiring heart
Is fed
By the blessingful and fruitful
Opportunity-sun.

17909

Unless all the aspiration
And dedication-troops
Of the world
Are mobilised,
The peace-manifestation-dream
Of earth
Will remain a far cry.

17910

O my mind,
My life is already
Too complex.
For God's sake
Do not complicate it more
With your contagious doubts!

17911

The human mind
Likes to prophesy unhappiness
And not happiness
Day in and day out.

17912

The happiness
Of an ego-bloated mind
Is as enduring
As a mound of sand.

17913

If the mind's sincerity
And the heart's purity
Walk together,
Then every obstacle
On the way
Will definitely crumble.

17914

A self-giving thought
Is indeed
Time well-spent.
