901 The Real In Me

Purity awakens the real in me:
Oneness.
Humility strengthens the real in me:
Perfection.
Gratitude enlightens the real in me:
Satisfaction.

902 The Heart-Land

Now that I have
Become an explorer,
I shall explore
A new land:
The heart-land.

903 Your Right Of Self-Encouragement

My Lord Supreme,
I need self-newness.
How can I have it?
"My child,
You can have self-newness
Only by exercising your right
Of self-encouragement."

904 The Defeat Of My Life

There is a defeat
That is more glorious
Than victory,
And that defeat
Is the defeat
Of my entire life
By Truth.

905 Since You Do Not Play

Since you do not play
The foul game
Of blind negatives,
Yours will be the Consciousness-delight,
Here, there and
All-where.

906 Something Exists Above

Something exists above
Our usual desire-life,
And that something
Is our aspiration-life.

Something exists far above
Our rare aspiration-life,
And that something
Is our realisation-goal.

907 What I Shall Have

Today what I have
Is a tiny aspiration-seed.
Tomorrow what I shall have
Is a powerfully developed realisation-blossom.

908 My Aspiration-Heart Tells Me

My confusion-mind tells me
That life is nothing
But a dreadful nightmare.
My aspiration-heart tells me
That life can be
A lovely dream.
Why not let me give a chance
To my self-giving heart?

909 My Soul Has Already Become

My mind does not know
What it must know.
My heart knows
What it must know:
The beauty of gratitude-flames.
My soul not only knows
What it must know,
But it has already become
Satisfaction-sun.

910 A God-Smile

As a vigorous shake
Can awaken a sleeping man,
Even so, a God-Smile
Can inspire an unaspiring seeker.

911 The Happy Result

Unreservedly strengthen the part of you
That wants God's Love
Above everything else,
And soulfully see
The happy result.

912 My Supreme Realisation

Darkness is light:
This is my human realisation.

Light is for darkness:
This is my divine realisation.

Darkness is for light:
This is my supreme realisation.

913 Helpless And Senseless

Lord, is it because
I am helpless
That I am senseless?
Or is it because
I am senseless
That I am helpless?
"No, no, you are helpless because
You do not cry,
Cry for Light.
You are senseless because
You do not need Light."

914 God Sees Me Differently

God sees me
In a totally different way
Than the way I see myself.
He sees me
As another God.
