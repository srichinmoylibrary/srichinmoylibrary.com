9760

When he asked God to fulfil his desires,
He took God's Silence
As His Approval.
But God was only waiting
For his foolish desires to go away.

9761

An irony of fate:
His cheerful obedience
Has turned into
Powerful disobedience,
Because God has not pleased him
In his own way.

9762

God, too, has expectation.
He expects your dynamic inspiration
And aspiration
To run the fastest
On Eternity's Heart-Road.

9763

My Lord, forgive me.
If You forgive me, who knows?
Perhaps my ignorance-life
Will never return.

9764

Those who are destined
To do something divinely good
Or become something supremely good
Do not need to know God's Philosophy.
They need only to envision His Light.

9765

The Supreme's ardent Request
To all His children:
"Every day pray and meditate,
At least to maintain your spiritual height
If you cannot transcend it."

9766

Why do you listen to your mind
When it tells you
That you are wasting your time
Looking for illumination, perfection
And God-Satisfaction?

9767

Allow not your mind
To act freely,
For it may either lead or follow
Evil thoughts.

9768

Yours is a divided mind
And an undecided heart.
How can you then
Succeed and proceed?

9769

Every day his mind is visited
By wild memories
And his heart is visited
By deathless reality-experiences.

9770

If you want to develop
A special bond of oneness-delight
With God,
Then use your unused inner cry.

9771

Not the sound of freedom
But the silence of oneness
Is calling his heart.

9772

Sing not soulful songs
With your yesterday's mind.
Sing fruitful songs
With your today's heart.

9773

No storm can uproot
The faith-plant
Of his aspiration-heart,
Because of his implicit surrender
To the Will of his Lord Supreme.

9774

When at last I found him
Deep inside his mind-forest,
I found him
Most deplorably helpless.
