21445

My Lord, do bless me
With an extra supply of aspiration
So that I can remarkably increase
My progress-speed in my inner life.

21446

Only a true seeker admits
That he himself is the author
Of his anxieties and miseries.

21447

Beautiful,
Unimaginably beautiful,
Is my tear-flooded
Gratitude-heart.

21448

O seeker,
Can you not see
That your own doubting mind
Is robbing
Your heart-progress-account?

21449

The devotion-compass
Helps us steer
Our aspiration-life-boat
To the Golden Shore.

21450

O my heart,
You must never be tempted
To tour my mind's
Doubt-suspicion-cities.

21451

To feel that at long last
God will fulfil you
In your own way
Is the unhorizoned height
Of your stupidity.

21452

Unconditionally
God has stepped on your doorstep.
Delay not, O my heart!
Open yourself unreservedly
And unconditionally.

21453

What is a negative mind?
A negative mind is a mind
That has deliberately swerved
From the path
Of God's Love, God's Compassion
And God's Concern.

21454

God does not want me
To send my mind into forest-exile.
He wants my mind to live
Inside my heart-kingdom.

21455

O seeker,
If your purity-heart-plant
Is damaged,
Then your progress-life
Will not be enough
To make your soul happy
And fulfilled.

21456

Love
Keeps the devotion-door open.
Devotion
Keeps the surrender-door open.
Surrender
Keeps the gratitude-door open.
Gratitude
Keeps the transformation-door open.
Transformation
Keeps the perfection-door open.

21457

Disobedience
Grows up quickly.
Disobedience
Spreads out overwhelmingly.
Disobedience
Does not want to die.
Disobedience
Wants to live everlastingly.

21458

Unwillingness,
You are not only deaf but also blind.
Mental sickness is your inner name.
Vital ruthlessness is your outer name.

21459

My soul is beautiful.
My soul is powerful.
My soul is perfect.
Alas, why and how
Can I be so blind to the obvious?
