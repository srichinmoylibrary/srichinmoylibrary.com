20175

The mind
And its shallow thoughts
Seem so odd
To our aspiration-heart.

20176

O seeker,
Instead of making
Whining complaints to God
Against humanity,
Can you not offer
Your soulful appreciation
Of humanity?

20177

Each man can be a perfect copy
Of God's Dream-Fulfilment-Reality
On earth.

20178

My life,
I want to surprise you
With my heart's
God-hunger-cries.

20179

My ego forgets
Even God's unconditional Compassion-Eye
And His unconditional Forgiveness-Heart.

20180

True,
Once upon a time
You were a fool,
But you do not have to remain so
Forever.
Just become the song
Of constant self-offering.

20181

O critic,
You always remain alien
To the beauty, divinity
And immortality
Of oneness-heart-delight.

20182

God's Compassion-Eye
Brings God to me
No matter where I go,
Even when I hide.

20183

God counts Himself
Extremely fortunate
When He gets
One unconditional seeker.

20184

Each desire of mine
Is a slave
To my illusion-delusion-mind.

20185

I prayerfully asked God
To name a few
Perfect instruments of His.
God did listen to my prayer,
But alas,
Somehow He forgot
To mention my name!

20186

Desire is so powerful
That it has the capacity
To compel us to run after
Even its shadow.

20187

Each choice instrument of God
Will one day prove to be
A living God-monument.

20188

If you want to climb up
To your heart's aspiration-mountaintop,
Then you must liberate yourself
From your disobedience-exile.

20189

I love the beauty of a God-dreamer.
His beauty is real;
His beauty is at once earth-illumining
And Heaven-fulfilling.
