60.

Alas, no new news:
Your heart-cry is still sleeping.
Alas, no new news:
Your progress is still creeping.

61.

Blame not your palm;
Just blame your mind.
Kill not your mind;
New light just find.

62.

O wisdom-heart,
Sing, sleeplessly sing,
And thus destroy
Doubt-failure-ring.

63.

Because you have no
Concern for integrity,
Half-bright must remain
Your sun-celebrity.

64.

To make your life ever
God's Satisfaction-Choice,
Your mind must be always
Deaf to doubt-voice.

65.

In the morn and in the eve
Learn surrender-songs.
Your Lord Supreme will pardon
All your earthly wrongs.

66.

When the seeker is transformed
Into reality's joy,
He unmistakably becomes
God's choicest, fondest toy.

67.

Do not permit your felon-mind
To blindly and stupidly think.
The doubting mind's success-smile
Compels the love-heart to shrink.

68.

O seeker, every day thank God
For your dynamic health,
For therein lies your progress-life's
Most useful, precious wealth.

69.

The soul of light
Definitely withdraws
If the mind wants not
To transform its flaws.

70.

Look! Quite unexpectedly
What has dawned in me:
My aspiration-heart-life's
Long-sought divinity.

71.

Your mind wants to play
With a huge doubt-ball.
Your heart longs to play
With God's Golden All.

72.

Once you climb up
The realisation-tree,
Your heart-life swims
In liberation-sea.
