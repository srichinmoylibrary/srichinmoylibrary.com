55 My Aspiration-Heartbeat ...

My aspiration-heartbeat
Quickens
At the very mention of God.

56 In My Spiritual Life ...

In my spiritual life,
Many, many, many more
Supremely divine things
Are yet to come.

57 Poor God! ...

Poor God!
He sincerely tries
Not to see
The darkness of the world,
But unfortunately
He sees it.

58 There Are Some Things ...

There are some things
That must be done
Before others —
Such as my aspiration
And my dedication.

59 Ignorance-Night ...

Ignorance-night
Is dauntless,
Ruthless,
Godless.
Therefore it is useless.

60 Affection, Love ...

Affection, Love,
Sweetness, Fondness
God has for me.
Every day I live and grow
Under His Protection-Tree.

61 God With His Smile ...

God with His Smile
Has descended upon my head
To shatter my ego-pride
Permanently.

62 There Is Only One Thing ...

There is only one thing
That I do not like about God:
He likes to hide.

63 There Are Many Nice Things ...

There are many nice things
That I can say about God,
But one thing I would like to say:
His Love for us
Has neither beginning
Nor end.

64 Quite Often ...

Quite often
The mind is empty
Of concern.

65 I Must Not Give Up Hope ...

I must not give up hope.
I must carry it with me
Far into the distant future.

66 Pay No Attention ...

Pay no attention
When ignorance-night
Begs to visit you.

67 Whatever God Tells My Heart ...

Whatever God tells my heart
Is absolutely true —
Forever true!

68 To Meditate Soulfully ...

To meditate soulfully
And powerfully
Is to start our homeward journey
To the Absolute Supreme.

69 My Lord Has In Mind ...

My Lord has in mind
A full service-schedule for me
Every day.
