17260

What is gratitude?
A heart's ceaseless
Smile-song for God.

17261

Imagination-seed
Reveals the beauty
Of the reality-tree
Slowly but unmistakably.

17262

In the outer world
What we call war-machine,
In the inner world
We call peace-famine.

17263

God Himself campaigns
For the victory
Of my self-giving heart.

17264

Today is a miracle-day for me,
For today I am able to claim God
As my own, very own.

17265

Quite often
A victory in the outer life
Ends the victory
Of the inner life.

17266

Nobody dares to believe
That God loves him
Unconditionally.

17267

A heart of purity
And
A mind of peace
Are next-door neighbours.

17268

I always try to remain
In between
My Lord's Compassion-Eye
And Protection-Feet.

17269

My gratitude-heart
Is my Lord's
Supremely fruitful gift to me.

17270

May my life be chained
To my surrender-heart
And nothing else.

17271

You are God's chosen child.
Do not hide.
God does not want you to hide.

17272

The mind is fond
Of God the Powerful.
The heart is fond
Of God the Merciful.
The soul is fond
Of God the Peaceful.

17273

I may not know
Why God has created me.
But I do know
Why I have created God.
I have created God
To satisfy my power-hunger.

17274

Insecurity tells me
That the road is endless.
Security tells me
That the road is cloudless.
