1

     Is
Sweetness unimaginable.

32.

1

My God-obedience
     Is
My supremely divine pride.

33.

1

I live either inside
My earth-born heart-cries
     Or inside
My Heaven-free soul-smiles.

1

God wants me
Not only to equal Heaven,
But also to surpass Heaven
In sleepless self-offering.

1

The soul perpetually smiles
Through the bondage-cage
     Of the body.

I wish to have
An enthusiasm-outburst.

We try to satisfy
Our Lord Supreme
With our extremely feeble efforts.

  1. EMP 114. Qingdao, China, 9 January 2005↩

In my aspiration-heart-home
There is no room
For uncertainty.

When I used to live
On the outskirts of hope,
But now I am living
In the very breath of hope.

By our inner light.
