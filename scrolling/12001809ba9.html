14445

Your mind's limitation-hallucinations
Are speedily and foolishly
Moving away from
Your heart's illumination-satisfaction.

14446

A soulful smile
Is the new remedy
For humanity's old ailment:
Doubt.

14447

Alas, what am I going to do
With my poor mind,
Which is afraid of light?
Alas, what am I going to do
With my poor heart,
Which is afraid of darkness?

14448

My mind's teeming defects
Are unending.
Yet I shall never give up
My life's perfection-dream.

14449

Because of your
Unconditional surrender-heart,
Your life's delight-journey
Is dawning fast.

14450

With our aspiration-passport
We do not need any visa
To enter into
Humanity's inner countries.

14451

Slowly and steadily,
Your heart-life will climb up
Divinity's satisfaction-peak.

14452

Today my Lord Supreme
Has passed a law
Against humanity's
Doubt-enjoyment.

14453

Use your heart's cry!
You will feel God at any time.
Use your life's surrender!
You will see God in any place.

14454

God's Justice-Light
Is only an inch away
From God's Compassion-Height.
Therefore, you can easily expect
Almost anything you want
From God's Justice-Light.

14455

Step forward,
No matter how slowly,
How carefully.
But do not step backward,
Even unconsciously!

14456

True,
The heart-purification-process
Is slow.
But do not give up
If you want to be the owner
Of your Lord's Victory-Smile.

14457

If your dedication is not nourished
By faith every day,
Doubt may at any moment
Derail your dedication-train.

14458

Your heart's gratitude-fragrance
Has the capacity
To respond immediately
To God's clarion Call.

14459

God-loving hearts
Are both completely calm
And unmistakably certain.
