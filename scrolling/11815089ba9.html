8545 Doubt Immediately Surrendered

His heart called out,
"Doubt, the bandit,
Must be seized and imprisoned!"
Doubt immediately surrendered,
And longed to be transformed
Into the heart's choice instrument.

8546 My Dedication To The World

My dedication to the world
Should be a beautiful gift
And not a demanding expectation.

8547 His Mind Has Unparalleled Poise

His mind has unparalleled poise.
Do you know why?
Because his heart is in constant touch
With Eternity's Silence-Smile.

8548 Your Fallen Self-Expectation-Tree

Do you know what has crushed
Your life's pride?
It is your huge,
Fallen self-expectation-tree.

8549 What The Weeping Multitudes Need

What the weeping multitudes need
Is a divine strength
Beyond human hope.

8550 The Supreme Guest Will Come

Although you are not aspiring
To His Satisfaction,
Just because you are still aspiring,
The unexpected Supreme Guest
Will come to you
With His inexplicable Splendour.

8551 Sorrow-Rain Will Fall

Sorrow-rain will eventually
Fall heavily on you
If you always remain
Self-enamoured.

8552 Aspiration Is The Beginning

Aspiration is the beginning
Of self-mastery.
Self-mastery is the halfway point
To God-discovery.

8553 My Lord's Earth-Born Child

My heart's sleepless cry
Is my Lord's earth-born child.
My soul's matchless smile
Is my Lord's Heaven-born child.

8554 Hope Is My Heart's God

Determination is my mind's God.
Hope is my heart's God.
Promise is my soul's God.
Surrender is my life's God.

8555 Why Do I Choose To Hide?

Why do I choose to hide?
Not because I am a thief,
But because the God-Hour
Has not yet struck
For me to reveal myself.

8556 My Search

My search for satisfaction within
Is yet unfinished.
My search for perfection without
Is yet to begin.

8557 Hint Of Surrender-Light

At your slightest hint
Of surrender-light
God will come to you
With His Infinity's Satisfaction-Delight.

8558 Expand Your Heart

Expand your heart,
Transcend your mind!
Lo, your ego-life
Is all gone.

8559 The Treacherous Queen

Open the door
Of your gratitude-heart.
Anxiety, the treacherous queen,
Will no longer be able
To rule your life.
