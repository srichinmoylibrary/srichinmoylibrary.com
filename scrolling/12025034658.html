16730

Never claim
Your mind's world-information
As wisdom-truth from God!

16731

Alas, my mind-road
Is always full of imperfection,
And my heart-road
Is always under construction.

16732

God has no faith
In your mind-promises.
He has faith only
In your heart-hopes.

16733

You have all along
Given ignorance-night
A chance to make you happy.
Can you not give God's
Vision-promise
A chance to make you happy?

16734

No matter how many times a day
God telephones my mind,
He never receives an answer.

16735

God and God's sunlit Road
Are waiting for you.
How long do you want them to wait,
O ignorant humanity?

16736

Life is, indeed, a two-way street.
Man's aspiration goes out
To welcome
God's Satisfaction-Smile.

16737

O my mind,
When will you be awakened
From your ignorance-hallucination-dreams?

16738

My Lord, may I sing every day
The most melodious
Oneness-heart-song
To please and fulfil You
In Your own Way.

16739

A heart of magnanimity
Every day receives
The world-transforming message-light
From God Himself.

16740

Eagerness and enthusiasm
Are the two fastest
Self-transcendence-climbers
In God's Satisfaction-Sky.

16741

A tiny prayer
Can invoke Heaven's
Brighter-than-the-brightest
Satisfaction-Smile.

16742

Each individual, alas,
Gives a very slow response
To God's blessingful Requests.

16743

He whose heart
Is a purity-smile
Makes faster-than-the-fastest progress
In the inner life.

16744

Self-giving
In aspiration-life
Is an obvious sign
Of satisfying God.
