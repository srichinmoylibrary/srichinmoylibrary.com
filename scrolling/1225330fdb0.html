Keep Your Mind-Poise

December 31st, 1969

Keep your mind-poise.
You will ascend.
Feed your heart-voice.
You will transcend.

Half Experience: Man Is Man-Night

December 31st, 1969

Half experience:
Man is man-night.
Full experience:
Man is God-height.
God’s experience:
Man is God-Eternity’s
Another God.

For a Knowledge-Teacher

December 31st, 1969

For a knowledge teacher he did not care.
He had wisdom-tutor rare.
Knowledge is preparation, uncertain and slow.
Wisdom is realisation and perfection-glow.

Man Is Dear to Man

December 31st, 1969

Man is dear to man
Because of his failure-cry.
God is dear to God
Because of His failure-success-
Oneness-experience-sky.

I Build My Hope-Tower

December 31st, 1969

I build my hope-tower
Inside the smile
Of Heaven high.
I bury my frustration-shower
Inside the tears
Of earth’s loud cry.

Why Do I Fail?

December 31st, 1969

Why do I fail?
No, not because
I am important.
No, not because
I am unimportant.
No, not because I am a fool
Who has had no gnostic school.
Oh, but because
It was not God-ordained sail.
Therefore, I fail.

One Man-Like God

December 31st, 1969

One man-like God,
One God-like man
Can only slake
My longing ken.

In Body and Soul

December 31st, 1969

In body and soul
     I know.
In life and death
     I grow.
In man and God
     I glow.
In thought and deed
     I flow.

Faith Cures My Sorrows (1)

December 31st, 1969

Faith cures my sorrows.
Love feeds my morrows.
Peace fulfils my life.
Bliss cancels death-knife.

Death, Be Not Ruthless

December 31st, 1969

Death, be not ruthless.
Life, be not helpless.
Man, be not soulless.
Journey, be not Godless.

My Silence Speaks, My Sound Is Mute

December 31st, 1969

My silence speaks.
My sound is mute.
I sing God-song.
God plays my flute.

My Feet Have Wings

December 31st, 1969

My feet have wings,
     They fly.
My hands have eyes,
     They cry.
My thought has God,
     It shines.
With God-delight
     It dines.

Your Rising Glory

December 31st, 1969

Your rising glory:
     A beauty-game.
Your setting glory:
     A purity-flame.

My Heart’s Only Friend

December 31st, 1969

My heart’s only friend:
     The humble grass.
My mind’s only friend:
     The crystal glass.

Some Are Born to Ecstasy’s Light

December 31st, 1969

Some are born to ecstasy’s light,
     But I am not.
Some are born to freedom-height,
     But I am not.
Some are born to perfection-right,
     But I am not.
Some are born to satisfaction-flight,
     But I am not.
