8675

Are you so deaf
That you cannot hear
God's compassionate
Inner Thunder?

8676

To touch the world-heart,
Give your life
To God's all-seeing Will.

8677

Just love the higher life more.
The lower life
Will automatically shun you.

8678

A self-giving heart
Is a giant step
To life-perfection.

8679

God the Oneness-Producer
Commutes every day
Between
My dry mind
And
My fertile heart.

8680

In God's cosmic Waiting Room
An unaspiring man
Endlessly and fruitlessly stays.

8681

His mind's purity-thoughts
Have built a fortress
For his aspiration-heart.

8682

Climb to a higher life!
You are not
Your helpless heart.
You are not
Your useless mind.

8683

Since you have outgrown
Your need for God,
God is going to rely on others
From now on.

8684

Like other things,
Discard your list
Of grievances.
Discard your self-styled
Importance.

8685

If you are unfaithful
In thought,
How can your life be fruitful
In God?

8686

Every day
He enjoys the divine majesty
Of meditation,
And every day
He enjoys the funeral
Of his desire-life.

8687

Every day your aspiration-heart
Can be a new morning
In God's self-unfolding new world.

8688

Every day
His mind is ruthlessly imprisoned
By the strong desire
For constant change.

8689

In pursuit of perfection,
He turned his entire life
Into a willing and self-giving service.
