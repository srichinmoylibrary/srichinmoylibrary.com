9890

Have faith
In what your heart has
For you.

Have faith
In what you have
For your life.

9891

Why do you allow doubt
To spoil the perfection
Of your mind?

Why do you allow fear
To spoil the perfection
Of your heart?

Why do you allow anxiety
To spoil the perfection
Of your life?

9892

What has caused your failures?
Not your doubt,
Not your insecurity,
But your unwillingness.

9893

As God does not have to prove
That He is God,
Even so, you do not have to prove
That you are a good seeker.
Just become one,
And God will declare you
To the world at large.

9894

When you ask the world for something,
Ask in God's Name.
That way God can also participate
In your self-fulfilment-game.

9895

When are you going to accept
God's Gifts -
Sincerity's beauty
And purity's divinity?

9896

I do not deny
That you have receptivity-power,
But I do know that your heart
Does not have enough gratitude-power.

9897

Unless you put God first
In everything you do and say,
Your aspiring life
Will wither away to nothingness.

9898

Unlike a desire-man,
A God-seeker knows
That all God has
Is also for him.

9899

The absence of aspiration
Has taken all the joy
Away from his life.

9900

O my heart's gratitude-tears,
Inside you I want to have
My permanent home.
