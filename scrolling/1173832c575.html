990 My Enthusiasm

My enthusiasm needs
No imagination-flight,
For it is always
A rich harvest.

991 The Thick Forest Of Solitude

With his tears of repentance,
His mind has entered
Into the thick forest of solitude.
Before long he and his life
Will swim in the sea
Of satisfaction-ecstasy.

992 Visitor

Stay in the lap of pleasure, you fool!
God may not come to see you,
But somebody definitely
Will come to visit you
Without a whisper.
Do you know who?
Your own destruction-tiger.

993 The Inevitability

For months I have not prayed
To God.
I have totally forgotten
God's Love-Compassion-Forgiveness.
Within and without
I see the omnipotence of oblivion.
And now I am the inevitability
Of stark disappointment.

994 What I Have Become

What I see
Is a disturbing sunset.
What I feel
Is a trembling hope.
What I have become
Is a heart-piercing cry.

995 A Train Of Sadness

My Lord, do protect me
From my friends
Who are urging me
To follow the crowds.
They are unconsciously forcing me
Into a train of sadness.

996 A Fountain Of God-Thought

A fountain of God-thought
Tells me that mine is not
A weak and mortal birth.

997 I Am Helpless As Ever

When will You return,
My Beloved Supreme?
My heart-cave is all empty.
I am helpless as ever
In my throes.

998 The Great Authority

My wild thoughts were chasing me
Like beasts in the dense forest.
But now at last I have renounced
The great authority
Of my own life.

999 The Days Of My Slavery

Yesterday I was a slave
Of my own power.
Today I am a slave
Of my own doubt.
The days of my slavery
Will tomorrow be over.
I shall become the master-friend
Of my life.

1000 My Heart's Challenging Life

The achievement
Of my heart's challenging life
Is an aspiring flame,
An illumining lamp
And
A fulfilling sun
Every day.
