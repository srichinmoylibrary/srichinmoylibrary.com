20990

God is near
When I live inside
My sweetness-heart.
God is nearer
When I live inside
My eagerness-heart.
God is nearest
When I live inside
My selflessness-heart.

20991

Every day
My soul-bird inspires me
To go and visit
My Lord's Forgiveness-Heart-Nest.

20992

My heart's gratitude-flames
Were born
The day I felt
God does everything for me
Unconditionally.

20993

Since my heart loves God
Sleeplessly and breathlessly,
My heart summarises everything
And then offers my Lord Supreme
A concise report
On my aspiration-life.

20994

What is great
Can indeed be
Meaningful and powerful.
What is good
Is indeed
Most perfect and most satisfactory.

20995

What a seeker needs
Is a ceaseless blossoming
Of his God-satisfaction-heart.

20996

God calls it
The height of absurdity
When I tell Him
That He does not need me
For anything.

20997

My soul
Has taken full responsibility
For my heart,
Which is studying
At the universality-university.

20998

You will be spiritually dead
If you ever allow
Your mind's darkness-night
To doubt
Your heart's conviction-authenticity.

20999

O seeker,
Defy your body's inertia
To enjoy
Your soul's God-blessed ambrosia.

21000

O seeker,
Do not be afraid
Of your life's tragic stories.
In the course of time
These tragic stories
Will purify your mind
Completely
And strengthen your heart
Limitlessly.
