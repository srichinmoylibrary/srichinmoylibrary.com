12115

If you are not aspiring soulfully,
Then each new moment
Is found to be
A regular dose of disappointment.

12116

The fleeting victory
Of a doubting mind
Is too inconsequential
To mention.

12117

The final expectation-breath
Of my heart
Brought me a sea of peace.

12118

God-chosen seeker-souls
Only know how to walk
On the one-way truth-road.

12119

To give humanity's heart
Ceaseless nectar-delight,
God always keeps open
His Heart's Oneness-Store.

12120

Fast, very fast
You will be able to run
If you see everything devotedly
From the angle of self-awareness.

12121

Although my mind
Is full of problems,
It does not accept
My heart's God-solutions.

12122

O torture of uncertainty,
O torture of uselessness,
When will you accept
My rejection-resolution?

12123

If you bid adieu
To your aspiration-heart,
How can you expect
A perfection-flooded
Satisfaction?

12124

Depend not too much
On chance-breath,
Or you will be standing
In a long line of failure.

12125

God once
Unmistakably felt,
God forever
Tangibly and permanently found.

12126

The human mind
Means doubt in the background.
The human heart
Means fear in the foreground.

12127

God has blessed my heart
Unconditionally
With a faith-calendar
That has no doubt-days.

12128

When it comes to God's
God-Activities,
Most of us really know nothing.
We are just useless talkers.

12129

I wanted to fly
On aspiration-plane.
Alas, I am now found
On ambition-flight
Bound for destruction-land.
