21415

A single ego-bound action
Can destroy our heart's satisfaction
Of decades.

21416

Cultivate patience every day.
You will see that
Your disappointment-walls
Will one day, without fail, crumble.

21417

If we sleeplessly pray and meditate,
The ruthless barriers
Of our mind
Will eventually be dissolved.

21418

One touch of inner silence
Can make us
A prince in the inner world.

21419

You want to know
Where I study.
I study at the university
Of God's Forgiveness-Light.

21420

Each time we surrender
To God's Will,
We feel that our life
Becomes an unending flow of bliss.

21421

When I knock
At God's Forgiveness-Door,
I clearly see
That God's Forgiveness-Heart
Has already forgiven
My confusion-mind.

21422

Happiness dances in my heart
When I feel that
My heart is of God
And my life is for God.

21423

Alas, when will I feel
That my life was born
Only to be
A supremely divine instrument
Of God,
To please Him always
In His own Way?

21424

Each aspiring thought
Must, without fail,
Mirror the illumining soul.

21425

Sweet is my ancient hope.
Powerful is my modern promise.
My ancient hope and my modern promise
Are inspiring me,
Illumining me
And fulfilling me.

21426

Do not allow
Your conscience to be guilty
If you really want to make progress
In your spiritual life.
Do the right thing
And be the right person
Always.

21427

I know for sure
That the mind's supremacy
Over the heart
Is nothing but a temporary conquest.

21428

The victory of the undisciplined vital
Is either absolutely foolish
Or dangerously fatal.

21429

O my Lord Supreme,
I pray to You,
Do not allow my heart
To be a heart
Of sleepless and breathless sorrow.
