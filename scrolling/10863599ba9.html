Do not wait
For God to approach you.
He may deliberately
Forget you.

There is no
Quick and sudden journey
To perfection.

I want my heart-compass
To turn towards
God's Polestar-Eye.

Our suffering does not pay
The admission fee to Heaven
But our God-surrender
Happily does.

A surrender life
Is a perfect
God-answer.

A great many times
My life has been saved
By a wee iota
Of God-obedience.

You fool!
Grace descends
For your immediate acceptance
And not for your total rejection.

O my God-searching mind,
Fly and fly
Far beyond the domain
Of world-complaint-criticism-torture.

My Lord,
Please give me anything else —
But not freedom.
Freedom I misuse;
Destruction I invite.

Every day God loves to have
A letter from me.
Even my volcano-anger-letter
Gives Him true joy.

God and I disagree
Only on one point:
He emphatically tells me
That I am another God.

My Lord Supreme,
I just love
Your high Commands.
They are at once
Extremely challenging
And extremely inspiring.

My Lord Supreme,
I quite dislike
Your mild Requests.
They are so weak
And completely empty
Of inspiration.

My mind,
I have already chosen
My heart
And not you
To be my destiny-maker.
You can remain silent.

The longer I sit
On my knees,
The sooner God comes down
To kiss my eyes.
