Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Xian and Huangshan, China in January 2005.

16 January 2005

Xian, China

1 I See My Lord Dancing ...

I see my Lord dancing
Inside
His own Infinity’s Smile.

2 Light And Power Together ...

Light and Power together
Are most significant
Representatives of God
Here on earth.

3 Only The Heart-Eye ...

Only the heart-eye
Does not have
The fear of death.

4 Life Asks Death ...

Life asks death
Many questions,
But death never answers.

5 When I Stopped Looking ...

When I stopped looking
At the face of expectation,
A mighty transformation
Took place in my life.

6 Knowledge-Facts ...

Knowledge-facts
Have to bow their heads
In humility
To wisdom-light.

7 Wisdom-Light ...

Wisdom-light
Has to bow its head
In humility
To a fleeting Smile of God.

8 We Can Never Imagine How Strong ...

We can never imagine how strong
Are the walls
Of our mind’s inconscience.

9 My Lord’s Invisible Eye ...

My Lord’s invisible Eye
Is behind
His visible Smiles.

10 My Inner Pilot ...

My Inner Pilot
Makes all the decisions
Of my outer life.

11 The Mind-Doubt-Arrows ...

The mind-doubt-arrows
Cannot penetrate
The silence of the heart.

12 Only The Aspiration-Fire ...

Only the aspiration-fire
Can burn down
The mind’s ignorance-walls.

17 January 2005 ...

17 January 2005

Xian, China
