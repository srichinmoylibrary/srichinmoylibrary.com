10315

Be not complacent.
If you are complacent,
Your aspiration-life
Will come to an end
Before very long.

10316

You want to know when
Your mind will be able to smile.
I tell you,
Your searching mind will be able to smile
Only after your aspiring heart
Has soulfully cried.

10317

You want to know how
You can be truly happy.
You can be truly happy
If you can reawaken
Your heart's sleeping God-dreams.

10318

Your heart's morning God-hunger
And its evening God-thirst
Are the perfect Satisfaction-Smiles
Of your Beloved Supreme.

10319

In your life of aspiration
Every day and at every moment
Be supremely cautious.
Otherwise, your mind's desire-storm
Will uproot your heart's aspiration-tree.

10320

If you need God
For God's Sake alone,
Then only will
His infinite Beauty's Smiles
Dance in each and every thought of yours.

10321

Allow not the destruction-tiger
To roam around you.
Keep only the hope-deer
To play with you
All the time.

10322

Do not waste time
Explaining to others
Why you are weak.
Just explain to them
Why you badly need God.

10323

Your cold reception to doubt
Is not enough.
Your permanent rejection of doubt
Is your only salvation.

10324

God is ready to supervise
My mind-factory.
But do I ever request Him to do so?
Will I ever allow Him to do so?

10325

There is no aspiration-heart
That cannot smash
The strength of the desire-breath.

10326

The life of surrender
Is a skilful swimmer
In God's Vision-manifesting
Consciousness-Ocean.

10327

When you assert yourself,
God feels
That is precisely the time
For Him to take leave of you.

10328

If your heart
Cannot melt God's Heart,
How can God's Heart
Mould your life?

10329

Gratitude-heart-plants
Are very rarely found
Either in the desire-life
Or in the aspiration-life.
