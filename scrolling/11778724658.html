4930 Use Your Sacred Faith

Do not use your gross doubts
To see God.
You will fail.

Do not use your subtle hesitation
To approach God.
You will fail.

Use your sacred faith
To embrace God.

4931 The Diamond Secret

Concern has the golden key
To unlock the heart of the world.
Compassion is the diamond secret
To transform the heart of the world.

4932 Prove You Are God's Instrument

Nobody can change.
Nobody will change.
But you can and you shall.

Nobody can smile.
Nobody will smile.
But you can and you shall.

Prove to the world within,
Prove to the world without
That you are God's choicest instrument
Unparalleled.

4933 God Will Be Your Announcer

You become your faultless
And dauntless self-discoverer.
God Himself will be
Your immediate and proud Announcer.
Also, like Himself, He will shape you
Into another Self-Enjoyer
Of infinite Delight.

4934 God's Sun-Vast Satisfaction

When you offer to God
Your climbing flame-loyalty,
God will grant you
His descending sun-vast Satisfaction.

4935 The World Knows You

The world knows you
For your surprisingly strange
Self-love.
But when will the world care to know
God's unconditionally bountiful
Compassion-Sea for you?
When?

4936 A Good God-Seeker

If you want to be a good God-seeker,
Then God will wash away
Your mental doubts.

If you want to be a good God-lover,
Then God will wipe away
Your psychic tears.

4937 You Are Completely Wrong

If you think God is displeased with you,
You are right.
But if you think you have
The rare capacity to displease God
Forever and forever,
You are wrong,
Completely wrong.

4938 An Amazingly Powerful Capacity

I am all admiration for you.
You are the possessor of
An amazingly powerful capacity.
Yesterday you killed God's Heart
With your self-interest.
Today you have most successfully
And most gloriously revived God's Heart
With your self-conquest.

4939 Poise Is The Hyphen

Poise is the hyphen
Between what you have heard
From God
And what you have told humanity.

4940 Heaven Is Pleased With Him

Heaven is pleased with him
Because he is ashamed of his past.
Hell is pleased with him
Because he is afraid of his future.

4941 One Thing In Favour

One thing in favour of Heaven:
It does not waste time in thinking
Of an ungrateful man.
One thing in favour of earth:
It does not waste time in thinking
Of an indifferent soul.

4942 Let Them Live Separately

A mind with teeming doubts
And a heart with blossoming faith
Hate to live together.
Why then try to torture them?
Let them live separately.

4943 Difficult For A Contemplative Man

As it is difficult for a talkative man
To keep his mouth shut,
Even so it is difficult
For a contemplative man
To keep his heart closed.

4944 Looking For The Easiest Way

Nothing is more exhausting
Than looking for the easiest way
To realise God.
