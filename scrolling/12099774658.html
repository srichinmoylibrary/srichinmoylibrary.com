24130

Each good thought
Immediately opens
The gate of inner strength.

24131

The shortest shortcut
To Heaven's highest Height
Is our sleepless and breathless self-giving
To the Will of our Lord Supreme.

24132

My Lord tells me
That for the fastest progress
In my spiritual life
I have no need for the past or the future.

24133

When God clothes Himself
In a physical body,
He is not only misunderstood
But also ridiculed
By humanity.

24134

My Lord Supreme,
I pray to You to bless my gratitude-heart-bird
With the strongest wings
To fly at every moment
In the sky of Your Compassion-Light.

24135

Do not try to bribe Fate;
You will fail!
Do not try to chase Fate;
You will fail!
Try to conquer Fate;
You will succeed,
For success is your ultimate birthright.

24136

God within me
Is not the critic
But the audience,
Filled with curiosity and concern.

24137

Nobility in the mind
And generosity in the heart
Are two most essential friends
That make our spiritual progress
Faster than the fastest.

24138

What God finds difficult to pardon
Is not our misdeeds
But our unwillingness to confess.

24139

My Lord,
Do not allow my tiny aspiration-sun
Either to fall asleep
Or to be eclipsed!

24140

O my inspiration-mind,
O my aspiration-heart,
I want you both to work together.
Never quarrel, never!

24141

God the Justice we can never bribe —
No, not even God the Compassion.
Why?
Because He has not yet developed
That weakness.

24142

God does not like to be
On the side of the past.
He does not like to be
On the side of the future either.
He likes only to be
On the side of the present.

24143

My Lord Supreme,
Keep me awake all the time
So that I can fulfil You
Whenever Your Command comes.

24144

My Lord,
I do not want to remain
Inside any man-made pocket.
I want to be always
Inside Your Heart-Pocket.
