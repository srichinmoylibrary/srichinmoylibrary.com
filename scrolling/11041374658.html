16 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
This morning God gave me
A surprise call.
He said to me, "My son,
You must keep your promise.
You must fight for Me
Against ignorance —
Sleeplessly, breathlessly, eternally."

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am at Your Feet throughout Eternity.
I shall be at Your Feet to please You
And to fulfil You in Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/16 March 2000, 9:03 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)/

16 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
If I do not improve myself,
Then hour can You wait for me
Indefinitely?
What right do I have
To beg You to wait for me?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 March 2000, 9:06 a.m. Before doing sixty-two one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (30 right, 32 left)

18 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
From this moment on, I shall see everything
In Your own Way,
I shall feel everything
In Your own Way
And
I shall do everything
In Your own Way
Without having a thought
For my own self.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 March 2000. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

20 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The Hour of God is waiting for me
Today.
The Hour of God will watch me
Tomorrow.
The Hour of God will examine me
The day after.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 March 2000, 9:12 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

20 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Finally, today I have discarded
My mind's complaint-list.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 March 2000, 9:26 a.m. Before doing sixty-eight one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (35 right, 33 left)

20 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My mind still enjoys
Avid discussions.
To my great joy, my heart enjoys
Only sweet God-conversations.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 March 2000, 9:34 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

21 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Each Smile I receive from You
Is, indeed,
Avery special Blessing-Gift
From Your Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 March 2000, 7:46 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously three times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

21 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart's aspiration-flames
And
My life's dedication-flowers
Please Your Eye, please Your Heart
And please Your Feet
Simultaneously.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 March 2000, 8:05 a.m. Before doing fifty-seven one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (25 right, 32 left)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Each obstacle I face
Along the road of my spirituality
Is an opportunity for me to express
My love for You infinitely more.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 March 2000, 8:11 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

23 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What a fool I am!
I am delaying and delaying and delaying
Looking at my Lord's Compassion-Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 March 2000, 8:40 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously three times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

23 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Can there be a greater fool than I am?
I am delaying and delaying and delaying
Placing myself at the Feet
Of my Lord Supreme
For His Forgiveness-Blessings.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 March 2000, 9:09 a.m. Before doing fifty-four one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (28 right, 26 left)

23 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What a fool I am!
I have received Forgiveness-Delight
From my Lord Beloved Supreme,
Yet I am not praying every day
In the small hours of the morning
For His Supreme Victory on earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 March 2000, 9:14. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

25 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please, please bind my heart,
Please, please bind my mind,
Please, please bind my vital,
Please, please bind my body,
So that they can be only Yours
In their inner existence
And
In their outer existence.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 March 2000, 6:18 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

25 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My undivine mind grabs
And throws away
My heart's 'Do Not Disturb' sign.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 March 2000, 6:40 a.m. Before doing 51 one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (23 right, 28 left)

25 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I know, I know.
Your powerful Warning
Is another name
For Your Protection-Blessing.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 March 2000, 6:47 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine Up to 160 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.
