25245

Do not ponder your misfortunes.
Go forward!
Life is not a risky business.
Life is a God-satisfaction-promise.

25246

God does not ask us to make
A life-risking commitment.
He asks us to make
A God-life-manifestation-commitment.

25247

If you are fearful
Of losing
God's Cosmic Game,
Then God-realisation
Will always be a far cry.
Therefore,
Be brave, be brave, be brave!

25248

There is no such thing
As ordinary prayer.
All prayers are special
In their own right.

25249

My mind's tardiness
Will never have time
To meet with the readiness
Of God's Heart.

25250

Come, come, my Lord Supreme,
To wake me
With Your Thunder-Feet
From the age-old ignorance-sleep!

25251

There are many things
That the mind needs.
The most important thing
The mind needs
Is the beauty of purity-blossoms.

25252

The God-Hour
Means the smile
Of infinite Power.

25253

If you do not feed your hope
Every day
With your aspiration-heart,
Then how can you succeed
In your life?

25254

What is hesitation,
If not
An opportunity-squanderer-mind?

25255

What is indifference,
If not the indulgence
Of self-confinement?

25256

When our mind is captured
By feeble facts,
We do not allow our mind
To sing and dance
With the beauty and power
Of realities.

25257

O where is our peace?
It is all imprisoned
By our doubting mind!

25258

I do not need
The God of distant worlds.
I do not need
The God of the past.
I need the God
Who lives here in the breath of now.

25259

Life is fruitless,
Life is useless
When God-service is missing.
