Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Singapore and Solo, Indonesia in November 2003.

24 November 2003 ...

24 November 2003

Singapore

1 My Heart ...

My heart
Shall no more remain
A short-lived God-hunger.

2 God’s Face ...

God’s Face
May at times be invisible,
But His Heart
Is always visible.

3 God’s Closeness ...

God’s Closeness
Is my life’s fulness-smile.

4 Alas, God’s Heart ...

Alas, God’s Heart
Is a sleepless Tear for me.

5 Doubt Is Dangerous ...

Doubt is dangerous,
And self-doubt
Is infinitely more dangerous.

6 By Nature, Man Is Not ...

By nature, man is not
A God-Conversation-attention-ear.

7 My Heart’s Solemn Promise ...

My heart’s solemn promise
To God
Is my aspiration-return.

8 My God-Unwillingness ...

My God-unwillingness
Does not become me.

9 Today I Am My Heart’s ...

Today I am my heart’s
Aspiration-candle.
Tomorrow I shall become my soul’s
Illumination-sun.

10 My Mind Never Opens ...

My mind never opens.
God’s Eye never closes.

11 I Need Only One Thing ...

I need only one thing:
I need a tireless
God-surrender-heart.

25 November 2003 ...

25 November 2003

Singapore
