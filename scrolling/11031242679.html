10 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O humanity's mind,
Love God, love God, love God.
If you love God,
You will never run the risk
Of entering into the darkness-abyss.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 October 1998, 4:07 a.m. Before unsuccessfully attempting to lift 120 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

10 October 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
You have freed my soul
From all weaknesses.
Can You not do the same
For my heart, mind, vital and body?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 October 1998 4:17 a.m. Before lifting 120 lbs fifty times with left arm only.

15 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Many, many difficulties I have
In every aspect of my life.
That does not mean
That I shall doubt You
Or I shall doubt myself.
No, I shall just take shelter
Under Your Protection-Canopy,
And with the help
Of Your Compassion-flooded Eye,
I shall conquer all my difficulties
Within and without.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 October 1998 3:27 a.m. Before lifting 220 lbs with each arm five times.

15 October 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You are far beyond my clasp,
Yet You have chosen
To be within my easy clasp.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I do not dare to claim You,
Yet You have chosen me to claim You
As my own, very own.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 October 1998, 3:40 a.m. Before unsuccessfully attempting to lift 120 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

15 October 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
There is only one real failure in my life,
And that failure is
When I do not come to You
Lovingly, cheerfully and self-givingly.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 October 1998, 3:53 a.m. Before lifting 120 lbs fifty times. (25 right, 25 left)

16 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May the doubting mind of the world
Accept the all-illumining Light
Of the world's Inner Pilot,
And that Inner Pilot is You
And You
And nobody else.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 October 1998, 8:18 a.m. Before lifting 230 lbs with each arm four times.

18 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Every morning You stand before me
With Your infinite Compassion-Light.
Alas, alas, I do not know how
To receive Your Light.
Please, my Lord,
Since You take the trouble
Of coming to me every morning
With Your infinite Light,
Will You not teach me how to receive
Even an iota of Your Light
To transform my nature
And fulfil You inside my life?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 October 1998, 10:40 p.m. Before lifting 240 lbs with each arm four times.

18 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My Beloved Supreme,
Only when Your unconditional Grace
Descends,
My heart, my life
And everything that I have and am
Can ascend.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 October 1998, 10:50 a.m. Before unsuccessfully attempting to lift 120 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

18 October 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My happiness is the creation
Of Your Feet.
My happiness is the inspiration
Of Your Eye.
My happiness is the manifestation
Of Your Life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

[After completing the lifts:]

My Lord, I have made a new record. Your unconditional Grace has enabled me to make a new record in my bodyweight lift.

18 October 1998, 10:55 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times (50 right, 50 left in 2 sets) — for a new record of 2 mins 45.07 secs.

20 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
The closer my heart and I come to You,
The harder my mind and vital strike You.
Why? Why? Why?
Why do You not stop my mind and vital
From this wild insolence-behaviour?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 October 1998, 4:01 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs with each arm four times.

20 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You are telling me that it is Your task
To hide my mind's ignorance-night,
And it is my task to bring to the fore
My heart's wisdom-light.
My Supreme, I am all gratitude to You,
All gratitude to You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 October 1998, 4:17 a.m. Before unsuccessfully attempting to lift 120 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

20 October 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My soul has placed its victory-crown
At Your Lotus-Feet
Lovingly, prayerfully and self-givingly.
May my heart, mind, vital and body
Do exactly the same.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

[After completing the lift:]

My Supreme, at long last I have done it, with Your boundless Grace. It is not my capacity, it is Your Compassion that has enabled me to lift up 120 pounds. It is Your Compassion, Your Compassion, Your Compassion and not my capacity.

20 October 1998 11:25 p.m. Before successfully lifting 120 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

21 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Do give me what You have:
Your Infinity's Compassion-Power,
And do receive from me what I am:
My mortality's surrender-strength.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 October 1998, 8:02 p.m. Before lifting 250 lbs with each arm four times.

21 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Please do not allow me to pray to You
For the removal of my sufferings.
Please allow me to pray to You
Only to conquer all my sufferings.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 October 1998, 8:11 p.m. Before lifting 125 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

23 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May this be my last prayerful promise:
I shall never, never, never
Lose faith in You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 October 1998, 5:15 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs with each arm four times.
