13330

Just because you have escaped
The expectation-net,
God is inviting you to come and live
In His Heart's Satisfaction-Palace.

13331

Your heart's mounting aspiration-flames
Mark the glorious beginning
Of your fruitful surrender
To God's Vision-Light.

13332

Beautiful
Is the prayer-river
Of man's mind.
Fruitful
Is the aspiration-ocean
Of man's heart.

13333

May my aspiration-heart
Eventually grow into
My divinity's angel-song.

13334

When I pray most devotedly
And meditate unconditionally,
I hear instantly
A chorus of cheers in Heaven.

13335

God has no time to convince you
That your doubtful mind will be
The cause of your utter failure,
But He has endless time to help you
Keep your heart's aspiration-flames burning.

13336

Do not say
That you do not like human beings.
Do not say
That all human beings are rogues.
Do not say
That human beings are not meant for
Spiritual life.
God does not want to hear it!

13337

Every day I pray to God
For His Compassion-Message-Light
To echo and re-echo
In my aspiration-heart-room.

13338

My heart's aspiration-cry
And my Lord's Satisfaction-Smile
Are always extremely fond of each other.

13339

In the morning
I place my aspiration-heart
At my Lord's Compassion-Feet.
In the evening
I place my aspiration-heart
Inside my Lord's Protection-Heart.

13340

My Lord tells me
That His Eyes are glowing
Precisely because
My heart is growing.

13341

There is no such thing
As an unforgiving
God-Heart.

13342

What actually challenges
The pride of ignorance-night?
Faith, our implicit faith in God!

13343

His heart's manifestation-flute
Has too many holes in it -
Abundantly more than usual!
Therefore, it fails to produce
Any soulful melody.

13344

God wants your mind to aspire
Daily and faithfully.
If your mind fails to do that,
Then it will be found inside
Its own doubt-volcano.
