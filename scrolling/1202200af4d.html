16475

His eyes never forget
To show his heart's smile
To humanity.

16476

What is making me
So happy?
I am clearly seeing
My fading doubt's failure.

16477

A supremely
Peace-flooded oneness-heart
Knows no limits.

16478

If you are unrealistic,
Then the frustration-train
Will arrive at your mind-station
Much sooner.

16479

Each human being
Has to run
A compulsory ultramarathon
To arrive at perfection-truth.

16480

Alas,
How is it that
We human beings
Are not tired
Of a non-stop
Circus-life-performance?

16481

God's deadline:
Either today or any day!
Man's deadline:
Now or never!

16482

O my mind,
How and why do you enjoy
The same old failure-stories?

16483

Two things
I need at every moment:
A sleepless aspiration-life
And a breathless meditation-heart.

16484

O my mind,
When will you learn
That each good thought
Is a delight-spreading
Rainbow?

16485

Today I have liberated
My mind
From the world's
Comparison-prison.

16486

Good news
For all God-lovers:
God's Compassion-Fountain-Eye
Is fast approaching us.

16487

Weakness,
Why do you appear and reappear
So frequently
In my aspiration-life?

16488

Ignorance,
You have offended me
In so many ways!
But I shall offend you
In a special way.
I shall completely
Transform you.

16489

At last
Everybody in my family —
Soul, heart, mind,
Vital and body —
Has become a hero-climber
Of aspiration-mountain.
