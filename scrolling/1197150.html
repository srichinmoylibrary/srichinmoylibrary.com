11401

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Your Compassion-Eye I love,
Your Thunder-Feet I need.
Your Compassion-Eye teaches me
How to aspire, aspire for the Highest.
Your Thunder-Feet teach me
How to realise, realise the Absolute.

11402

I give God what I have:
A volcano of earth-complaints.
God gives me what He has:
His Heart's infinite Compassion-Smiles.

11403

O my mind,
My heart is all ready
To help you clear
Your forest of thoughts.
Why do you not accept
My heart's unconditional help?

11404

If your life studies
In the school of Eternity's patience,
Then your heart will become
Infinity's immortal pride.

11405

The logic of the seeker's faith:
His life exists precisely because
His God exists.

11406

He is totally lost
Because his eye denies the truth
And his mind defies the truth.

11407

You must not allow
Your inspiration to decay
Because a life of aspiration
Is still a far cry.

11408

Do not accept
The nearness of death,
For God Himself
Wants you to enjoy
The fulness of life.

11409

A life of unconditional surrender
Is God's matchless
Beauty-toy.

11410

Only a purity-heart
Is permitted to pray
In the soul's silence-temple.

11411

If you live in your mind's
Sleeping complacency,
How can God grant you His Heart's
Perfection-Satisfaction-Home?

11412

Every day you have to study
At God's Compassion-School
If you want to reach your life's
Unhorizoned realisation-goal.

11413

I am absolutely certain
That your austerity-mind
Can never live
Near God's Spontaneity-Heart.

11414

You fool!
Who told you that God
Will give you innumerable chances
To change your life's direction-flow?
He may give you one or two
Or even three chances,
But never more!
