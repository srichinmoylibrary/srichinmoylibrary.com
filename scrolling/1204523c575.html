18790

Each pure thought
Is indeed
Blessing-light
From Heaven.

18791

God's Greatness
Puzzles the curiosity-mind.
God's Goodness
Feeds the purity-heart.

18792

At the root
Of all our suffering
Is the lack
Of our inner God-obedience.

18793

To me there is nothing
In God's entire creation
As valuable, as meaningful
And as fruitful
  As my God-obedience.

18794

My God-obedience
In the inner world
And my God-obedience
In the outer world
Are equally important.

18795

Just because we know
That we are not perfect,
We follow a spiritual path
To try to become perfect.
Otherwise, there is no necessity
Of our following the spiritual life.

18796

A spiritual Master is indeed
A wise spiritual farmer.
He does not expect
Satisfaction-blossoms
From all his spiritual children.

18797

Some seekers start
With sincerity-purity,
While others gain it later on.
Without embodying sincerity-purity,
No seeker can reach
The ultimate Goal.

18798

There is a special kind of joy
In accomplishing something
Beautiful and good for God
That transcends the body's tiredness
And the mind's exhaustion.

18799

Do not confuse
The body's genuine need for rest
With the body's
Lethargy-pleasure.

18800

As long as you stay
In a room
Or even in a corner
Of God's Compassion-Home,
There is no such thing
As failure.
