7901

I have many things
To tell God,
But He has only one thing
To tell me:
He loves me unconditionally.

7902

Smile at the world,
Smile,
If you want to reach Heaven
Peacefully.

7903

Morning prays to God
For the universal beauty.
Evening prays to God
For the transcendental peace.

7904

Do not sleep.
Be careful!
Man is coming
To knock at your mind's door.

Do not sleep.
Be ready!
God is coming
To knock at your heart's door.

7905

When you sing,
God attentively listens.
But when God dances,
Where do you go?
Why are you not available?

7906

You are lost
Between
Your deceptive mind
And your receptive heart.

7907

Sleep,
If you want to be
At God's Feet.

Wake up,
If you want to be
In God's Heart.

7908

O my mind's unreality,
You are the stark frustration
Of my heart.

7909

One solemn oath:
I shall go
Far beyond
The domain of expectation!

7910

No crowding thoughts
Inside your mind.
That means
A crowning life
Inside your heart.

7911

Purity
Is of paramount importance
To climb
Earth's heavenward ladder.

7912

May the breath
Of spirituality
Become my life's
Only strength.

7913

Stay wherever you are
Soulfully.
God's Silence-Voice
Will illumine you.

7914

To me,
My hope-life
Is not only sweet
But also perfect.
