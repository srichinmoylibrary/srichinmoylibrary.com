275.

The incorrigible mind
Must surrender
To the invincible heart
Of peace.

276.

As I was born
To love God,
Even so,
Peace was born
To show me the way.

277.

He who has peace
At every single moment
Of his life
Is at once a God-lover
And God-possessor.

278.

O my peace-nest-heart,
I shall cherish you
To my last breath.

279.

Nothing is as good
In my heart
And nothing is as perfect
In my life
As peace.

280.

Peace is at once
My mind's sure
Success-power
And my heart's pure
Progress-delight.

281.

Peace
Is oneness-love universal
In action.

282.

Peace is at once
The fragrance of humanity's heart
And the beauty of humanity's life.

283.

My mind says,
"God, do stand by me.
I need Your Power."
My heart says,
"Lord, do stay inside me.
I need Your Peace."

284.

My peace
Embodies Heavenly news
And illumines earthly views.

285.

Peace
Does not speak.
Peace
Loves God in man
And serves man in God.

286.

My gratitude-breath
Is my peace-invocation
To my Lord Supreme.

287.

My peace
Is my soul's unhorizoned strength
And my heart's limitless breath.

288.

In the inner world,
Peace
Is God's private plane.

289.

In the outer world,
Peace
Is God's fastest flight.
