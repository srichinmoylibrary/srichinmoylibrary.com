23890

When we dive deep within,
There comes a time
When we unmistakably realise
That the ultimate choice
Is not ours.

23891

Happiness, happiness, happiness —
Only happiness within
And happiness without
Can free the bondage-chained life
Of humanity.

23892

If you have a self-giving heart
To the Will of your Lord Beloved Supreme,
Then there can be no darkness-doubt
Inside your aspiration-life.

23893

Our souls have only one dream,
And that dream is to reach the end
Of all earth-sufferings.

23894

O human mind,
Why do you constantly interfere
In the activities of our divine heart?

23895

A doubting mind can feed only
An unaspiring heart.
A light-searching mind can assist
A self-giving heart.

23896

If we aspire early in the morning,
We become the breath
Of ecstasy's dawn.

23897

My Lord Supreme,
I do not want to be independent.
I want to be always dependent,
Dependent on Your Will
And on Your Heart's Choice for me.

23898

Only a weak soul
Allows his heart's aspiration-cry
To be postponed by the mind's
Constant doubt-nightmare-attacks.

23899

When I am my heart's gratitude-tears,
Not only do I see God face to face,
But also I become
God's perfection-messenger
Here on earth.

23900

O my Master,
You do not have to physically lead me.
Just give me your smiling face
In my dream.
That dream will lead me
To my divine destination.
