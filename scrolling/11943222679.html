8615

What I want
More than anything else
Is a heart
That will eventually become
God's beautiful angel-messenger.

8616

I feel extremely sorry
For you,
For your self-discovery
Is extremely insufficient.

8617

Each small good thought
Is nothing other than
A miniature divinity.

8618

What he has
Is a world of peace
Inside the heart
Of a God-Smile.

8619

A chosen instrument of God
Is always a good starter
And winner
In the Godward race.

8620

Your sleepless aspiration
Is your most authentic passport
To enter into Immortality's Land.

8621

In a limited human way
A seeker-heart can enjoy
The unlimited beauty
Of God the creation.

8622

Unless the body
Becomes a willing servitor
Of the soul,
One cannot manifest
One's Inner Pilot
On earth.

8623

The doubting mind
Knows what night is.
The aspiring heart
Sees the moonlit night.

8624

He has made
His unconditional surrender
To his Lord Supreme.
This is the secret sweetness
That he has started breathing.

8625

You are of God,
You are for God.
If you can remember this
Sleeplessly,
Yours will be
A tremendous progress-jump.

8626

O true God-lovers,
Come and join together
And build a road
For God's Peace-Descent.

8627

Alas,
His aspiration-heart lives
Between his audacious mind
And his ferocious vital!

8628

You have given up
Your aspiration-life.
That means
You no longer want to see
The Infinite
In the heart of the finite.

8629

If you linger,
You will lose God.
I tell you,
Definitely God does not want
To lose you!
