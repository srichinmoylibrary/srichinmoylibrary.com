The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I feel inspired from deep within
To write God every morning
A gratitude-heart-letter.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He makes me an excellent player
In His Cosmic Game.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He commands me
Not to ever ride
My mind's ego-horse.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I see nowhere
Temptation-snares.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child, happily and proudly
I shall become
The slave of your slave."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, I am burying
All your past
Evil deeds
And hurrying your future
Divine deeds."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child,
I have conquered your head;
You have conquered My Crown."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, you have given Me
What you presently have.
I am giving you
What I eternally am."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child,
Now that you have pleased Me,
Ask Me for a boon.
I shall immediately fulfil you."

My Lord,
Please give me the capacity
To love You infinitely more
Than You love me.
"My child, ask for another boon.
How can I allow you to steal
My own most precious desire?"

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child,
You have played your first role
As My lover most successfully.
Now I want you to succeed
In exactly the same way
As My Eternity's partner."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, I am placing
The animal in you
Before My Eye,
The human in you
Inside My Heart,
The divine in you
At My Feet."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, as it is true
That I am your shrine,
Even so it is equally true
That you are My temple."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child,
From today on I shall be
Your dearer than the dearest
And better than the best Messenger."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, you and I
Have cried for each other
For a very, very long time.
Now let us start smiling
At each other
Throughout Eternity."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He whispers
Enlightenment
And I whisper
Astonishment.
