24145

There is nothing
That is not within the reach
Of an aspiring human being.

24146

Problems there are,
But they do not have to exist
Any longer
If we make our surrender
Absolutely complete.

24147

The darkest nights of the mind
Must eventually surrender
To the illumination
Of the brightest dawns.

24148

My Lord Supreme,
I am offering my gratitude-breath
To Your Eternity's Patience-Life.

24149

Upon mankind's obedience-rock
Only
Can a peace-world ever be built.

24150

Promise
Is only a first step,
And not the goal.

24151

Today's God-realisation
Will definitely add to
Tomorrow's God-manifestation.

24152

Two inconstant realities:
My self-doubt
And my self-assertion.

24153

I am precious
In God's Eyes
Only when my heart becomes
Sleeplessly gracious.

24154

As century after century dawns,
Humanity's heart
Will eventually awaken
To Divinity's Light.

24155

The day I made
My unconditional surrender complete,
I was embraced by God
With all His Enthusiasm-Pride.

24156

We get joy
In keeping some secrets,
But God does not subscribe
To our philosophy.
He keeps no secrets
From His close associates:
Self-giving God-lovers.

24157

Alas,
I never knew how beautiful
My God-surrender-tears would be!

24158

As it requires
Full-time devotion
For mankind to please God,
Even so it requires
Full-time attention
For God to please mankind.

24159

The mind, vital and body of those
Who do not want to be
Acquainted with God's Will
Will eventually sink into the abysmal abyss.
