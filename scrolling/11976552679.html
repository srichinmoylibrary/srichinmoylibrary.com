11915

The moment you show God
Your aspiration-heart and meditation-eye,
He gives you His Compassion-Eye
And Satisfaction-Soul.

11916

My mind's inspiration brightens
My heart's aspiration.
My heart's aspiration enlightens
My life's dedication.

11917

A soulful heart-prayer
Is the powerful beginning
Of a God-manifestation-life.

11918

If you do not plunge into
God's Compassion-Heart-Sea,
How can you see the beauty
Of your life's transformation-smile?

11919

Every day try to memorise
God's unconditional and
Compassion-flooded Messages
If you want to claim the highest Satisfaction
As your own.

11920

If you do not increase
Your heart's receptivity,
Your seeker-life will be forced
To remain in captivity
Indefinitely.

11921

God does not scold you,
Not because you are perfect,
Not because you are aiming at perfection,
But because the God-Hour has not struck
In your sleeping life.

11922

With my heart's flying faith-bird
I am longing to reach
God's transcendental Compassion-Feet.

11923

When your dedication-life
Drives only along purity's road,
Your destination will beckon you
Sooner than the soonest.

11924

If you want salvation
From your selfishness-insecurities,
Then every day learn
A beautiful, self-giving heart-song.

11925

My inner world contains
Nothing but fulfilment-smiles
No matter what happens
In my outer world-possibilities.

11926

Every day you find a new excuse
Why you cannot pray to God
Early in the morning.
God is asking me to tell you
That soon His Compassion-Net
Will lovingly catch you
And transform you and your deception-mind.

11927

The role of forgiveness in a seeker's life
Is as essential as
The silence of the sky
And the power of the sun.

11928

Self-seekers go down and down.
And what do they see?
They see the God-lovers going up and up.

11929

What I really like about God
Is not how much He loves me
And how much He needs me,
But that He still continues to have faith
In the ignorance-loving world.
