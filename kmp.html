Part II â€” Questions and answers

KMP 12-38. During his numerous public meditations and university lectures over several years, Sri Chinmoy has been asked hundreds of questions about Kundalini Yoga. This is a selection of some of the more interesting of these, with Sri Chinmoy's answers. (This section was added in the 1974 edition of the book.)

  • Question: Where are the chakras located? Are they in the body?
  • Question: Are there specific mantras we can use to open up the different centres?
  • Question: When a centre opens up, will it give any kind of signal?
  • Question: What happens if we open the centres before the system has been purified?
  • Question: When one enters into the spiritual life, does the kundalini automatically arise, or does this happen only in certain people?
  • Question: Is the awakening of the kundalini a sign that God-realisation is imminent? At what stage of the soul's evolution does the kundalini rise?
  • Question: So the awakening of the kundalini does not mean that one is realising God?
  • Question: If it is not necessary for the kundalini to awaken, why does it awaken to some Masters?
  • Question: Does sexual indulgence prevent one from acquiring occult power through Kundalini Yoga?
  • Question: Are there only seven centres?
  • Question: Where is the third eye located?
  • Question: How does one open the third eye?
  • Question: Is there a particular centre I can meditate on in order to control my thoughts?
  • Question: Is the third eye the best place to focus one's attention during meditation, or can this lead to problems?
  • Question: When the third eye is open, what can you do?
  • Question: What is the relationship between the third eye and the heart centre?
  • Question: Once when you meditated, I noticed that you touched your third eye first and then your heart centre. What does this mean?
  • Question: What is the difference between the ordinary human heart and the spiritual heart or anahata chakra?
  • Question: Would you say that the heart center sometimes opens for a few seconds end then closes again?
  • Question: How do you know that the heart centre is open?
  • Question: If you feel warmth in the region of the heart, is this a sign of the heart centre opening up?
  • Question: What is the relationship between the spiritual heart and the kundalini power in terms of relative importance?
  • Question: I have been meditating on my navel centre. Do you feel this is wise?
  • Question: Then what about meditating on the base of the spine and trying to raise the kundalini from there. Is that also unsafe?
  • Question: Could you please speak about the fifth chakra?
  • Question: They say that there are eighty-six thousand subtle nerves. Are they distinct enough to actually be counted?
  • Question: Do the nerves of the subtle body have any correlation with the nerves of the physical body?
