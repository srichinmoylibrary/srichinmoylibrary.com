Question: Must one have humility in order to be surrendered?

Sri Chinmoy: Surrender and humility cannot be separated, although they are not one and the same thing. They can never be separated because one feeds the other. If you are really surrendered, then you are humble. If you are really humble, then you must also be surrendered.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My self-giving is my God-becoming, Agni Press, 1974
