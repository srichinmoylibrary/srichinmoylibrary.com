4843 A Reconciliation With God

Do not suspect the capacity
Of the outer world.
Do not doubt the beauty
Of the inner world.
Your long-lost spiritual breath
Will reveal itself to you
So that you can have
A reconciliation with God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 49, Agni Press, 1982
