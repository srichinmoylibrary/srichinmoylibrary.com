50. I need you

O hiding hands of love,
I need you.
O smiling eyes of love,
I need you.
O illumining heart of love,
I need you.
O revealing soul of love,
I need you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
