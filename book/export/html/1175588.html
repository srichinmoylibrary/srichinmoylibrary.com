2639 Make Your Choice

Make your choice:
Either cherish anger towards others
And perish,
Or liberate yourself totally
From others.
If you make the right choice,
You are bound to be happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
