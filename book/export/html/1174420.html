1483 I Sincerely Like You

I sincerely like you
Because you are discriminative.

I unreservedly admire you
Because you are inquisitive.

I sleeplessly love you
Because you are intuitive.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 15, Agni Press, 1981
