16 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only when I love You only
Do You show me how
We belong to each other eternally.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 July 2000, 7:50 a.m. Before practising for 740-lb. dumbbell bench press.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
