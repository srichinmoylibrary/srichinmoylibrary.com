14844

God does not mind
If you do not read His Suggestion List.
But your soul does mind,
And your soul will force you
To read God's Suggestion List,
For inside God's Suggestion List
Is the brightest beginning of your life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 149, Agni Press, 1991
