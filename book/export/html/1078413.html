766 My Armour...

MY ARMOUR

My faith in God
Was my yesterday's armour
Against world-temptation.

My patience in God
Is my today's armour
Against world-aggression.

My surrender to God
Shall be my tomorrow's armour
Against world-destruction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
