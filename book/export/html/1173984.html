1051 Keep Aloof

What pleases your heart?
Meditation.
Then keep aloof from those
Who scoff at your meditation,
And also from those
Who unpardonably doubt
Your sincerity-heart
And purity-mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 11, Agni Press, 1981
