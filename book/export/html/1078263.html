616 When I Wait For God...

WHEN I WAIT FOR GOD

When I wait for God
He may delay and delay,
But finally He comes;
He at least comes.
But when I wait for man
He simply never comes,
And he never will.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
