23.

My Lord,
If You really love me,
How is it I cannot prove it
To the world at large?
"My child,
My Love for you
Is not for your demonstration,
But for your life's illumination
And nature's transformation."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 3, Agni Press, 1998
