11 My Knowledge-Light ...

My knowledge-light
Is the quantity-beggar.
My wisdom-delight
Is the quality-chooser.
My Lord Supreme tells me
Not to worry.
He will gladly take care of
The beggar in me
And the chooser in me.
I just have to love Him only
And need Him only.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 14, Agni Press, 2002
