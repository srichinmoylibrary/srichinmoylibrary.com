62. Father, who was I

Father, who was I?
Daughter, you were the frankness
Of desire-life.

Father, who am I?
Daughter, you are the seriousness
Of aspiration-soul.

Father, what shall I be?
Daughter, you will be the sweetness,
Fruitfulness of Perfectiveness-Goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
