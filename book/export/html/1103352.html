28 March 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You love me now,
But You will love me
Infinitely, infinitely, infinitely more
If I love You only
And if I do not beg You
To tell me who You truly are.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 March 1999, 10:30 p.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously (Total: 400 lbs.) Lift number seven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
