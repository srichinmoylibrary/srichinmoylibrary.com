26 When I give to the world ...

When I give to the world

Far beyond my intended willingness,
God immediately embraces me
And tells me:
“My child, you are, indeed,
My tomorrow’s
Golden Dream-Boat-passenger.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 39, Agni Press, 2003
