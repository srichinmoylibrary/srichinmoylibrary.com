527. My Deep Heart Is Full

My deep heart is full.
I shall smile today,
Heaven's blue smile.
I shall study once more
In my inner school
And scatter God-Light all around,
For a while.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
