97 Anything Divine, Either In Our Inner...

Anything divine, either in our inner life
Or in our outer life,
Can never be exhausted.
It can only be astronomically increased.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 3, Agni Press, 2000
