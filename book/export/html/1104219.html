1 May 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In sacred secrecy supreme
You are telling me
That You want from my life
The fastest progress
Both in the inner world
And in the outer world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 May 2000, 7:40 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 300 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
