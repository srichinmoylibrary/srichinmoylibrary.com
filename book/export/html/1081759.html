200. Play your own role

Do your own part.
Don't be disturbed by your inferiors.
Your superiors will
Convince your inferiors
To adore you.

Play your own role.
Don't be afraid of your superiors.
Your inferiors will
Inspire your superiors
To admire you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
