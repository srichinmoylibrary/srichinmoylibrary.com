14472

My eagerness,
Just wait quietly.
God is bringing you
Some very special gifts
To expedite your journey.
Just wait for His blessingful
And blissful Arrival.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 15, Agni Press, 1999
