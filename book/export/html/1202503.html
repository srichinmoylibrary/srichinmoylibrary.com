Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 168

16701

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
May I be one of those
Who pray to You
To have an infinite Compassion-Heart
Like Yours.

16702

There is no darkness,
There cannot be any darkness
That cannot be illumined
By the power of a oneness-heart.

16703

Progress-towers
Are founded upon
The meditations
Of Eternity's silence-souls.

16704

Each soulful seeker
Will someday regain
His heart's lost dream-worlds.

16705

A God-realised person
Is he who brings
The aspiration-dedication-earth
Back to God's God-Heights.

16706

Even in our heart's suffering-garden
Divinity's flower-fragrance-joy
Will eventually blossom.

16707

A heart of confidence
Lovingly makes friends
With divinity's inevitabilities.

16708

A oneness-friend is he
Who cheerfully gives us
What he sleeplessly is.

16709

Divinity's Smiles and humanity's tears
Perfectly mingle
In Infinity's silence-light.

16710

My heart needs new friends:
Aspiration-cries,
Dedication-smiles
And gratitude-breaths.

16711

Every day I must climb up
My soul's patience-tree
To erase the consciousness
Of disturbing and disturbed humanity.

16712

When your mind frowns
At humanity,
Your heart pitifully cries
In its silence-nest.

16713

God deliberately deletes
Countless experiences
From His Heart-Record
So that He can forgive
And expedite
The evolution of mankind.

16714

No matter how compassionate you are
To the sleeping universe,
You are mercilessly misunderstood.

16715

Man loves
A fleeting moment of greatness,
And not
An abiding day of goodness.

16716

Step by step
We must conquer
The long distance
Of doubt and disbelief.

16717

The supreme gift of Mother-Earth
To each human being
Is her measureless patience.

16718

To alter the course of history
Every day you must devotedly feed
Your heart's soulful dream-hunger.

16719

Impatience-fire
Consumes
The aspiration-cries
Of mankind.

16720

If you do not aspire,
You are bound to have a barren heart.
Inside your barren heart
How can you expect to have
Love-oneness-peace-blossoms?

16721

When the mind enjoys
The confusion-hurricane,
The flower-beauty of the heart
Immediately fades away.

16722

My mind-tenant lives
In the house of doubts.
My heart-tenant lives
In the house of prayerful
And soulful tears.

16723

Do not surrender,
O seeker-friend,
To the shameless tyranny
Of ignorance-night!

16724

Only a man of transcendental faith
Can eventually become
A man of universal forgiveness.

16725

Alas, alas,
Self-styled hero-leaders
Are misleading
Ignorant and helpless humanity.

16726

Alas, I do not know why
I pursue instead of chasing away
The life of unreality.

16727

Each good thought
Is, indeed, a pioneer-light
Of a peaceful world.

16728

Man's unending ingratitude-life,
Ingratitude-heart and ingratitude-breath
God is accustomed to experiencing
Every day.

16729

Alas, when shall we see the beauty
And feel the purity
Of our heart's surrender-flames?

16730

Never claim
Your mind's world-information
As wisdom-truth from God!

16731

Alas, my mind-road
Is always full of imperfection,
And my heart-road
Is always under construction.

16732

God has no faith
In your mind-promises.
He has faith only
In your heart-hopes.

16733

You have all along
Given ignorance-night
A chance to make you happy.
Can you not give God's
Vision-promise
A chance to make you happy?

16734

No matter how many times a day
God telephones my mind,
He never receives an answer.

16735

God and God's sunlit Road
Are waiting for you.
How long do you want them to wait,
O ignorant humanity?

16736

Life is, indeed, a two-way street.
Man's aspiration goes out
To welcome
God's Satisfaction-Smile.

16737

O my mind,
When will you be awakened
From your ignorance-hallucination-dreams?

16738

My Lord, may I sing every day
The most melodious
Oneness-heart-song
To please and fulfil You
In Your own Way.

16739

A heart of magnanimity
Every day receives
The world-transforming message-light
From God Himself.

16740

Eagerness and enthusiasm
Are the two fastest
Self-transcendence-climbers
In God's Satisfaction-Sky.

16741

A tiny prayer
Can invoke Heaven's
Brighter-than-the-brightest
Satisfaction-Smile.

16742

Each individual, alas,
Gives a very slow response
To God's blessingful Requests.

16743

He whose heart
Is a purity-smile
Makes faster-than-the-fastest progress
In the inner life.

16744

Self-giving
In aspiration-life
Is an obvious sign
Of satisfying God.

16745

Each unaspiring individual
Carries generations of insecurity,
Stupidity, plus impurity.

16746

Alas, I do not know
When and how I shall stop
My undying preoccupation
With my little mind.

16747

No, God never approves
Of humanity's stupid
Self-pity-sufferings.

16748

Each dedication-life
Will eventually
Be brightened powerfully
By inner happiness.

16749

O seeker,
Every day try to develop
A sincere longing
For God-pleasing, soulful spontaneity.

16750

Indeed, each individual
Has inherited imperfection-night.
Again, each individual
Can eventually achieve perfection-delight.

16751

My Lord Supreme,
I soulfully pray to You
To liberate me and my mind
From dark doubt-dominated existence.

16752

My heart's soulful smile
Will definitely illumine
My mind's present clouded reality-sky.

16753

If we surrender
When doubt attacks our mind ruthlessly,
There is no way to avoid
Living in the dark shadow
Of excruciating pangs.

16754

Irregularity, insincerity and uncertainty:
These are definitely clumsy attempts
To please our Lord Beloved Supreme.

16755

A clarity-mind and a purity-heart
Can definitely put any seeker
Once again on the right track
Of spirituality.

16756

O my mind,
You have been teaching me all along
Self-deception-game.
But now yon have to learn from me
Self-illumination-game.

16757

My Lord,
May my divinely inspired thoughts
Be the creators
Of Your fulfilled Hopes.

16758

Is there any day,
My Lord Supreme,
When I do not trespass
On Your Kindness and Compassion-Heart?

16759

A soulful aspiration
Will always help us
To remain distant
From desire's powerful attacks.

16760

O ruthless ignorance-tyrant,
I am determined
To part company with you!

16761

Each person should feel
In the depths of his heart
His sleepless need for
A God-loving and God-serving life.

16762

When you do not live in the heart,
There is every possibility
That you will be forced to live
A disaster-prone doubt-life.

16763

My Lord,
May I pray to You every day
For a surrender-dawn
And a gratitude-eve.

16764

The heart knows
What to choose.
The body, vital and mind know
How to lose.

16765

The human in us
Hopes to exist.
The divine in us
Dares to live.

16766

To change
Is to make friends
With the unknown beyond,
Which is the source
Of constant opportunity.

16767

A man of belief
Compels the impossibility-tiger
To sit at his feet.

16768

Circumstances
Cannot change man's life.
His attitude
Can and does.

16769

A sincere and powerful smile
Is undoubtedly
A problem-solving capacity.

16770

True happiness is achieved
Only when the body, vital, mind and heart
Have completely surrendered
To the express will of the soul.

16771

To increase our inner progress
What we need
Is constructive encouragement
And not destructive criticism.

16772

Confidence
Is success-strength.
Oneness
Is progress-length.

16773

The physical in us
Touches the moon-land.
Indeed, this is man's greatness.
The spiritual in us
Loves the moon-consciousness.
Indeed, this is man's goodness.

16774

Self-acceptance
Is the dawn of satisfaction.
Self-confidence
Is the noon of perfection.

16775

Human imagination
Will eventually fail,
But divine aspiration
Can easily sail —
Sail to the Golden Shore.

16776

Unconditionally meditate
On God's infinite Compassion.
God will ask you to wait
For His choice Hour.
His choice Hour will, without fail
Liberate you from
Your age-old weaknesses.

16777

An iota of gratitude
Can keep a seeker
On the right path
In his aspiration-life.

16778

Out of His infinite Bounty
My Beloved Lord Supreme has transformed
My mind's anthill-frustration
Into my heart's star-high aspiration.

16779

Not before me,
O my mind,
But before my Lord Supreme,
You must be able to say
That you have all along
Been quite sincere with my heart.

16780

May a sleepless
Gratitude-heart
Be my life's
Chief attribute.

16781

My aspiring heart's astute observation
Is that my doubting mind
Has ruthlessly deceived me
Limitless times.

16782

Where were you
When God told your mind
That His creation
Is full of suffering?
Where were you
When God told you
That His Heart alone
Is the real sufferer?

16783

God is not surprised
By your undeserved deceit.
But He is surprised
By your unwillingness
To change your mind's loyalty
To the command of ignorance.

16784

Just imagine
How much more effective
Your dedication could have been
In the world
Without your insecurity, jealousy
And pride!

16785

Truth
Is the heart's
Absolutely strongest conviction.

16786

God the Eye is Reality.
God the Heart is Reality
Plus necessity,
My only necessity.

16787

Truth-beauty
And
Happiness-fragrance
Are inseparable.

16788

It is simply impossible for me
To live on earth
Without having God the Witness
And God the Advisor
On my side.

16789

God's Affection-Garland
He has received from God
Because he makes
His enemies happy.

16790

My Lord Supreme
Most proudly enjoys
My heart-garden-songs.

16791

My Lord Supreme has decided
My aspiration-flight will never descend.
It will only ascend higher and higher
Every day.

16792

My soul's master plan for us
Is to make
My Lord Supreme
Happy in His own Way.

16793

My Lord,
Do expose my secretly cherished
Imperfection-life
So that I can awaken
To Your Light supreme.

16794

The aspiration-heart
Is bound to win eventually,
Even against all odds.

16795

My soul is miserable
When it sees that
My desire-possession-mind
Is overloaded.

16796

My Lord,
Do make me a permanent
Peace-mission-server of Yours
On earth.

16797

Each soulful seeker
Is a member of God's
Oneness-Heart-Choir.

16798

O my mind,
It is high time for you
To start aspiring.
If you continue this way,
You will become a hostage
To your animal vital.

16799

Heaven's customs inspection
Demands our mind's
Sincerity-declaration.

16800

My Lord,
May I live every day
In between
Your unconditional Compassion
And Your unconditional Protection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 168, Agni Press, 1992
