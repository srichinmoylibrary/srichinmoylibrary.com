219. Three songs

The song of Heaven-light
I immensely enjoy.

The song of earth-night
I boldly reject.

The song of God-Delight
My heart accepts,
My vital rejects,
And my mind,
In this case,
Wants to remain neutral.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
