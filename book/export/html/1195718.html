9983

My heart is crying
Not because you are failing,
Not because you are not trying,
But because you are all the time
Loving the wrong person:
Ignorance-prince.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 100, Agni Press, 1984
