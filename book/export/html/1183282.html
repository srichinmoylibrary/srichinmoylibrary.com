146. O worshippers!

O kneeling worshippers,
Be sincere.
What you precisely need
Is sincerity.
O adoring worshippers,
Be pure.
What you unmistakably need
Is purity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
