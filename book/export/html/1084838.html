47. An extra soul

It is an extra eye
That sees God's Beauty,
        And
That eye is my heart.

It is an extra heart
That feels God's Satisfaction,
        And
That heart is my soul.

It is an extra soul
That becomes God's Perfection,
        And
That soul is God

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 17, Agni Press, 1974
