7.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

This morning I find myself
In Your Golden Boat.
I am right beside You.
You are carrying me
To the Golden Shore.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 December 2005, 4:23 a.m. Pangkor Island, Perak, Malaysia. Non-lifting day.

Sri Chinmoy the Eternal Beginner (Blue violin):
Ami jagibona ami kandibona

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 19, Agni Press, 2006
