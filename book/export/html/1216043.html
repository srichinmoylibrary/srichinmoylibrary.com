415. Dance and dine

When my weary night
Struggles into day,
God and I together dance
   In the garden of Love-light.

When my fiery day
Slips into night,
Satan and the King of death
   Together dine in the Kitchen of Surprise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
