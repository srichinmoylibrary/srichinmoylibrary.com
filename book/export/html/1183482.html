346. I am a fool

They say I am a fool.
I tell the world everything I know.

But what can I do?
How can I be mean?
I must share my knowledge with others.
Alter all I am practising
What I have been taught by the Supreme.

Did He not empty His Knowledge-Sun
Before me?

Whom shall I follow
If not my Father Supreme?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
