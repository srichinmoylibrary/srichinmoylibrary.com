834. Four prayers

The tree prays:
O Lord Supreme, see my height.

The branch prays:
O Lord Supreme, eat my life-fruit.

The flower prays:
O Lord Supreme, appreciate my beauty.

The root prays:
O Lord Supreme, bless my duty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 17, Agni Press, 1974
