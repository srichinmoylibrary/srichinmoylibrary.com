861 Don't Deny, Don't Forget...

DON'T DENY, DON'T FORGET

Don't speak to him.
He is all evil.
Don't go near him.
He is all evil.

But don't deny
He, too, is of God.
But don't forget
He, too, is for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
