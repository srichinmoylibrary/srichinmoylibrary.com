First in Inspiration-Light to the President

First
  In inspiration-light
  To the President.
First
  In aspiration-height
  With the President.
First
  In encouragement-might
  For the President.
First
  Russian child
  Admired, adored and loved
  By the four corners
  Of the globe.
[Dedicated to Raisa Gorbachev]

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: The home of oneness-peace-dream-world songbook, , 2001
