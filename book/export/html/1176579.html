3620 My Mind's Confusion

My life's imperfection
Is nothing but my mind's confusion.
My mind's confusion
Is nothing but my heart's
Lack of communication
With the Compassion-Flood
Of my Beloved Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
