11 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have come to realise
That when I take pride in thinking
And feeling
That You love me infinitely more
Than anybody else,
That is the time I create
An insurmountable barrier
Between You and me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 September 1999, 8:17 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 540 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
