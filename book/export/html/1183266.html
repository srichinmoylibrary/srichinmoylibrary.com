130. Three teachers

My mind can teach
The world all about the heart's
Clouded weather.

My heart can teach
The world all about the mind's
Threatening weather.

My soul can teach
The world all about the soul's
Inspiring weather.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
