363. The wings of friendship

The wings of earth-friendship
Fly very fast,
Sooner than at once.
We see them
But
They fly beyond, far beyond our ken.

The wings of Heaven comradeship
Slowly,
steadily,
unerringly fly
And
We can follow them easily
To the shores of Infinity's
Peace,
Light
and
Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
