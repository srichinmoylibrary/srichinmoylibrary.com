617. I forget, I remember

I forget
All my sad failures.

I remember
All my great triumphs.

By forgetting
All my failures,
I empty my mind.

By remembering
All my triumphs,
I feed my plenitude.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 13, Agni Press, 1973
