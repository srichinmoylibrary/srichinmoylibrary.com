My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 49

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Kijal and Penang, Malaysia in January and February 2006.

24 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

1 We Need Both ...

We need both
The tears of the heart
And the smiles of the soul
To perfect our lives.

2 Nothing ...

Nothing
Can be compared
To God’s sweet Whispers.

3 If You Are Not ...

If you are not
Firmly on the path,
How can you succeed?

4 God’s Closeness ...

God’s Closeness
Depends on
Our heart’s openness.

5 A Child’s Heart ...

A child’s heart
Draws immediate attention
From God’s Eye and Heart.

6 Meditation Has The Capacity ...

Meditation has the capacity
To take us far beyond the domain
Of sorrows and sufferings.

7 My Lord ...

My Lord,
One good look
Is all that I need
From You.

8 To Follow The Mind ...

To follow the mind
Is the height of stupidity.

9 To Follow The Heart ...

To follow the heart
Is to sing ecstasy-songs.

10 A Fountain-Heart ...

A fountain-heart
Will forever remain
Blossoming.

25 January 2006 ...

25 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

11 Opportunities ...

Opportunities
Are God’s Blessings.
Do not miss them!

12 I Beg You, My Lord ...

I beg You, my Lord,
With all my heart,
To scold me mercilessly
When I do something wrong.

13 Each Prayer ...

Each prayer
Has a special connection
To God’s Heart.

14 Each Meditation ...

Each meditation
Has a special connection
To God’s Eye.

15 My Lord’s Peace-Sea ...

My Lord’s Peace-Sea
Every day welcomes me
To come and swim.

16 My Mind ...

My mind,
How long will you sleep?
When will you wake up
To God’s Light?

17 Unless We Close ...

Unless we close
All the desire-doors and windows
Of the mind,
We will not be able to please God
In His own Way.

18 When God’s Victory-Sun ...

When God’s Victory-Sun
Shines,
Alas, I am fast asleep.

19 My Lord Every Day Blesses Me ...

My Lord every day blesses me
With a new work
To increase my capacity
In various ways.

20 God Awakens Us ...

God awakens us
From ignorance-sleep.
He energises us to walk along
Life’s wisdom-path.

28 January 2006 ...

28 January 2006

Penang, Malaysia

21 My Mind ...

My mind,
Stop dictating
To my heart!

22 My Lord’s Encouragement ...

My Lord’s encouragement
Expedites
My spiritual journey.

23 There Shall Come A Time ...

There shall come a time
When God will unveil
Our lofty visions.

24 Who Is Crying ...

Who is crying
Inside my heart,
If not my Lord?

25 If You Want To Find ...

If you want to find
Your true self,
Then the only place
Is your diving heart.

26 Do Not Allow Uncertainty ...

Do not allow uncertainty
To obscure your heart.

27 My Love Of God ...

My love of God
And my gratitude to God
Are keeping me alive.

28 God Gets Tremendous Satisfaction ...

God gets tremendous satisfaction
When we pay attention
To His Messages.

29 God-Willingness ...

God-willingness
Is needed
At every moment of life.

30 The Necessity ...

The necessity
Of God-obedience
Can never be exaggerated.

29 January 2006 ...

29 January 2006

Penang, Malaysia

31 I Wish To Have ...

I wish to have
God-surrender
As my life-partner.

32 Action! Action! ...

Action! Action!
No more feeble words.

33 God Loves ...

God loves
To obey
His own iron Laws.

34 The Creator Enjoys ...

The Creator enjoys
His Cosmic Game
Seated on His golden Throne.

35 God’s Cosmic Law ...

God’s Cosmic Law
Admits exception.

36 Conquer Temptation ...

Conquer temptation,
If perfection
You sincerely need.

37 Each Heart-Cry ...

Each heart-cry
Takes us away
From the meshes of ignorance.

38 Alas ...

Alas,
We are apt to invoke
Heaven’s Power
And not
Heaven’s Light.

39 Ignorance-Night ...

Ignorance-night
Again and again
Loves to fight
Against Heaven’s Light.

40 The Beauty And The Fragrance ...

The beauty and the fragrance
Of happiness
Lead and guide us
To the Higher Worlds.

30 January 2006 ...

30 January 2006

Penang, Malaysia

41 Wrong Thinking ...

Wrong thinking
Is nothing short of
Poison-drinking.

42 God ...

God
Protects my heart
From wrong feeling.

43 Life ...

Life
Has to be surcharged
With gratitude
Every day.

44 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
Do not surrender to despair!
Start your spirituality
All over again.

45 We Must Put ...

We must put
A strong accent
On self-giving.

46 We Must Not Be Bound ...

We must not be bound
By our mind’s
Self-styled whims.

47 My Happy Moments ...

My happy moments
Are
God’s dancing Hours.

48 May My Sweet Hopes ...

May my sweet hopes
Be faithful.

49 May My Powerful Promises ...

May my powerful promises
Be fruitful.

50 The Price ...

The price
Of name and fame
Is astronomical.

51 The Mind Needs ...

The mind needs
Flexibility
More than anything else.

31 January 2006 ...

31 January 2006

Penang, Malaysia

52 Every Morning And Evening ...

Every morning and evening
I go to God’s Surrender-School
To study.

53 My Soul Always Passes ...

My soul always passes
God’s Examinations
With flying colours.

54 O My Heart ...

O my heart,
See fear
Nowhere!

55 I Must Bring ...

I must bring
Tomorrow’s aspiration
Into my today’s life.

56 Stop Impatience ...

Stop impatience
At the very root.

57 The Forward March ...

The forward march
Is a challenge —
Only the brave succeed.

58 There Is No Substitute ...

There is no substitute
For a God-gratitude-heart.

59 Fight We Must ...

Fight we must
To establish God’s Kingdom
Here on earth.

60 Eternity Breathes ...

Eternity breathes
In each moment.

61 Each Dawn Inspires Us ...

Each dawn inspires us
To be the golden flames
Of the rising sun.

1 February 2006 ...

1 February 2006

Penang, Malaysia

62 My Lord ...

My Lord,
May my gratitude to You
Come first,
Before I do anything.

63 God’s Bountiful Message ...

God’s bountiful Message
Of my transformation
Reverberates in my depths.

64 From Now On ...

From now on,
I shall see
Only the good
In all human beings.

65 Today Is The Day ...

Today is the day
That I have smashed asunder
My doubt-prison cell.

66 My Lord, What Can I Do ...

My Lord, what can I do,
What can I do?
“My child, what can you not do,
What can you not do?
You can easily realise God!”

67 When We Do Not ...

When we do not
Believe in God,
We believe in
God-empty nothingness.

68 Each Day ...

Each day
Is a new adventure
Towards our Destined Goal.

69 My Lord ...

My Lord,
To my greatest joy,
Your Thoughts pervade my mind
All the time.

70 My Lord ...

My Lord,
May Your sacred Feet
Adorn my heart-garden.

71 My Lord ...

My Lord,
My whole life depends on
Your Smiles and Tears.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 49, Agni Press, 2006
