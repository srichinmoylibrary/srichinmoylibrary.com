1. Union and oneness

A happy union reigns supreme
When beauty's light
And duty's delight
   Meet together.

A happy oneness reigns supreme
When earth's cry
And Heaven's smile
   To each other lose.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Union and oneness, Agni Press, 1976
