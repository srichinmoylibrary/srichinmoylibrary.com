3.

My Lord,
I am planning to write
My autobiography.
Do You have any objection?
"My child,
I do have an objection."
Why, my Lord, why?
"I shall be terribly jealous
Of you.
I want you to write
My Biography first."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 3, Agni Press, 1998
