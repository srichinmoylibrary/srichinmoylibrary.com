5887 When I Want To Change

When I want to change
My outer life,
God uses His Vision-Eye
To smile at me.

When I want to change
My inner life,
God uses His Reality-Heart
To embrace me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 59, Agni Press, 1983
