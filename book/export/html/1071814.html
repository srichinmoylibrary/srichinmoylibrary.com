May 4

God the Poet sings and dreams. God the Philosopher thinks and drinks. God the Lover loves and liberates.
The Poet-God sings the Song of Beauty. He dreams the Dream of Duty.
The Philosopher-God thinks the Thought of Height. He drinks the Vastness of Light.
The Lover-God loves the world as it is. He liberates the world from what it is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
