19326

What we need is soulful oneness,
Not outer mastery.
We must offer our oneness-height
To the God-lovers and truth-seekers
Who are looking for peace
In their lives.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 194, Agni Press, 1993
