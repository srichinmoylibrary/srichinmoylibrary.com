238. Do you need me?

I do not know
What to say to you.
You have been so kind to me
Do tell me one thing:
Do you need me?
"I need you and I shall always need you
Because your need of me
Is well-founded and well-informed."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
