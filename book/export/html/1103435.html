7 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart's silver songs
Carry me
To the highest height of Heaven
And place me
At Your Compassion-Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 May 1999, 4:13 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 5, Agni Press, 1999
