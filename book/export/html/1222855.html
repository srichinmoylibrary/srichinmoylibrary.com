Bindu Ami Sindhu Hate

Bindu ami sindhu hate
Chahina je kabhu
Chahi shudhu tomar charan
Thakte dhare prabhu

Translation:

I am a tiny drop.
I do not want to become the ocean, ever.
My Lord, I want to stay
At Your Feet, clinging.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giti mala, , 2000
