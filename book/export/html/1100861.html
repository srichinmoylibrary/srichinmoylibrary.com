7.

It is the mental hallucination
Of a human being
To think that
He can deliberately ignore
God's Compassion
If he wants to.
God's Compassion
Is not only omniscient
And omnipotent,
But also omnipresent.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My gratitude-heart-garden, Agni Press, 1993
