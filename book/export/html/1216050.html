422. How hard I work

O beloved pride,
How hard I work
To build you up.

O beloved doubt,
How hard I work
To defend your cause.

O beloved fear,
How hard I work
To treasure your body.

O beloved depression,
How hard I work
To fathom your realisation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
