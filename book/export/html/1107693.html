Serve and love

Columbia University; New York, New York, USA
St. Paul’s Chapel
23 July 1971

To serve God I need only one thing:
     Joy.
To serve mankind I need only one thing:
     Patience.
To love God I need only one thing:
     Purity’s Breath.
To love mankind I need only one thing:
     Humility’s Soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The oneness of the Eastern heart and the Western mind, part 2, Agni Press, 2003
