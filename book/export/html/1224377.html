Jiban Maran Hasi Bedan

Jiban maran
Hasi bedan
Ke janena ke janena
Ashar prabhat
Sanjher aghat
Ke janena ke janena
Moher bandhan
Mukti sadhan
Ke janena ke janena

Translation:

Life and death, smiles and tears —
Who is not aware of them?
Morning hopes and evening blows —
Who is not aware of them?
The bondage of attachment
And liberation-practice —
Who is not aware of them?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 4, , 1981
