Kolre Dar Kol Bali Ese Kalir Prane Dite

Kolre duar kol bali ese kalir prane
Dite chahe dol
Swarge haowa ese tuliche hillol
Phule phale sthale jale laglo se dal
Kolre nayan kol
Sagar paner nadi chale
Shunna kallol

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
