21. New breath

When I think
Of my body,
I breathe a new breath
Of frustration.
When I think
Of my vital,
I breathe a new breath
Of destruction.
When I think
Of my mind,
I breathe a new breath
Of hallucination.
When I think
Of my heart,
I breathe a new breath
Of consolation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 10, Agni Press, 1974
