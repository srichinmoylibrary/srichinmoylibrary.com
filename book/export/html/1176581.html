3622 To Say Something Deeper

To say something deeper,
Be someone higher.
To be someone higher,
Have something purer.
To have something purer,
Sleeplessly and unconditionally
Remain a self-giver.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
