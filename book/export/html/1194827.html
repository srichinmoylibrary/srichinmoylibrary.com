Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 92

9101

I am so happy
Now that I am beginning
  To give my all
To my Beloved Supreme.

9102

The darkness within you
Will die
Only when the devotedness within you
Invokes illumination from Above.

9103

Each soul is special
Because each soul is made
Of God's Vision-Reality.

9104

If you are a God-lover,
That means God has already baptised you
  In His Satisfaction-Sea.

9105

A selfless man
Sees in everything
His perfection's God-song.

9106

Impurity caught his mind
  Off guard,
But the purity of his heart
Has come to his mind's rescue.

9107

My Lord Supreme,
Where are Your chosen instruments?
"Alas, My chosen instruments
Are enjoying ignorance-sleep
Instead of fulfilling
Their promised tasks."

9108

If you do not preserve
Your heart's purity-flower,
Nobody, not even your dear ones,
  Can save you
In your life of aspiration.

9109

Slowly and steadily
You have to silence your mind
So that the peace-dove
Can nest in it.

9110

My heart's gratitude-tears
Are the only jewels
That my Lord Supreme
Most powerfully appreciates.

9111

Since you are
  Your closed mind,
You will have
  Open enemies.

9112

He is happy
Because every day he tries
  And actually does
A little more than he thinks he can.

9113

  Surrender
Is the cornerstone
  Of man's
God-manifesting palace.

9114

Aspiration is a vow you took.
Now you should take a dedication-vow.
Then only will God be able
  To plow your heart-field
     Most satisfactorily.

9115

Faith has been chosen
  By God Himself
To be the campaign manager
  For God's Victory.

9116

If you do not silence
  Your self-indulgence supreme,
How can God feed you
To immortalise your heart's inner hunger?

9117

  Never say no
To God's Compassion-Eye-Request
If you want to have a life
  Of sleepless ecstasy.

9118

Try to see only God's Satisfaction-Face.
Then only will you be able to face
This ingratitude-world every day.

9119

Satisfaction in giving,
Satisfaction in receiving,
Satisfaction in transcending,
Satisfaction, satisfaction, satisfaction
Is the only God for me.

9120

  Great achievements
Do not demand great sacrifices.
  Great achievements
Need only a Compassion-Flood
From the Absolute Lord Supreme.

9121

Cultivate purity in your heart.
You will be able to rediscover
  Before long
The Kingdom of Heaven.

9122

O unconscious mind,
By any chance
Have you ever read
God's unconditional Compassion-Book?

9123

The life of freedom
You will be able to enjoy
Only after you have climbed
The tower of wisdom.

9124

God definitely wants you
To buy stock
  To your heart's content
In His Compassion-Company.

9125

If you do not want to recognise
The seeds of doubt
  Inside your mind,
God will not grant you
The fruits of faith
  Inside your heart.

9126

His heart's simplicity-life
  Was badly misunderstood
Even by one of his immediate possessions:
  His mind.

9127

  "Does God exist?"
This is your question.
God does exist.
He exists not only for you
But also for everybody else.
See if this answer can satisfy you.

9128

Unless and until your desire-life
Is reduced to nothingness,
You cannot become a member
Of God's universal Fulness-Society.

9129

He has run his aspiration-life
  So well
That God is all appreciation
  And satisfaction.

9130

If you live in an atmosphere
  Of aspiration,
Perfection will be
  Your God-given name.

9131

  Alas,
Only his ego-life
  Is progressing.
Everything else
  Is drastically regressing.

9132

Do not waste
  Your God-service-hours
Thinking mundane thoughts.

9133

  Death is compulsory
Because God wants the soldiers of life
  To take rest
At the end of each battle.

9134

  Each heart-path
Is strewn with purity-flowers,
  But the human mind
Does not want to believe it.

9135

Why are you so blind
  That you cannot see
Your stupendous pride is only splashing
  In your mind's ego-lake?

9136

I am not sure whether humanity
  Is a half-formed divinity
Or an ill-formed stupidity.

9137

Although he is notorious
For his insincerity-life,
  He is vigorous
In trying to transform his life,
  Once and for all.

9138

You are bound to win always
In the inner world
If you do not allow your mind
To analyse the outer world.

9139

When you are angry with God,
Your soul, with its strictness,
And your Lord, with His Compassion,
Make arrangements for an interview.

9140

Your soulful dedication
  To God
Will determine
God's fruitful Affection
  For you.

9141

If you open your heart,
  You will feel that God
Can never be strict with you,
  Never,
For His Compassion-Height
  Is infinitely stronger
Than His Justice-Light.

9142

Others may not know,
  But I do know
One of my outstanding faults:
I feel that the world
  Badly needs me.

9143

Early in the morning
  Every day
You must do one thing
Before you do anything else:
Strike your lethargy-infested mind.

9144

When I look into God's Eye,
  My life becomes
My heart's unhorizoned delight.

9145

The only wish of my life
  Is to discover
My cheerful and unconditional surrender
  To God's Justice-Light.

9146

  On principle
Mortals do not like to learn
Their hearts' aspiration-songs.

9147

You must realise that every day
  You enjoy
Your self-created crucifixion.
Why do you then blame God
  For not granting you
His Heart's Ecstasy-Ocean?

9148

If you want to make yourself happy,
If you want to make God happy,
Then self-giving has to be
The motto of your life.

9149

I do not mind
If all my other promises
  Remain unfulfilled,
But my God-realisation-promise
In this lifetime
  I must fulfil.

9150

Give what you have
And what you are.
Then every day God will observe
Your supreme victory-anniversary.

9151

Time hangs heavy on your life
Because you do not want God
To use your time
On your behalf.

9152

God at times may shut His Ear,
But He never shuts His Eye,
And never, never, never
Does He shut His Heart!

9153

Develop your inner confidence-power.
You will be able to become
  A perfect builder
Of God's Manifestation-Temple on earth.

9154

Unless you are a rank fool,
  You will always
Take the side of your soul.

9155

If you can be soulfully happy
  And remain happy,
God considers it
Your real meditation.

9156

Once you have established
  A solid inner link
With your spiritual Master,
Yesterday's Master, today's Master
  And tomorrow's Master
Will all become one in your life.

9157

From his desire-life
He has inherited nothing
But an endless series of failures.

9158

If you have to go slowly
In your aspiration-life,
  Go slowly.
But I don't want to see
  Your aspiration-life
On the brink of extinction.

9159

  My Lord Supreme,
At the end of my life
Will I remember
That when newness knocked
  At my door
I chose to remain closeted
Inside my old, tiny self?

9160

Life itself is always an opportunity.
If you are in the animal life,
You have the opportunity
  To become human.
If you are in the human life,
You have the opportunity
  To become divine,
     Plus perfect.

9161

What is your old self?
Your old self is
  An unsatisfactory self
That you have created
By not emptying your life-vessel
  Of its brooding imperfection.

9162

The new year comes
To give you another opportunity,
  Another hope.
But the new year will come and go.
So begin your self-transformation-play
  Immediately!

9163

The doubt-cancer in your mind
Is spreading every day.
  Be careful!
Faith-radiation is of immediate need.

9164

Is there anybody
Who does not indulge
  In opinions?
Is there anybody
Who does not wish to adhere
  To his convictions?

9165

He who can soulfully smile at God
Can definitely sail fast
On life's progress-river.

9166

When your dedication becomes irregular,
  Your faith in yourself
Cannot do anything else except wane.

9167

An impure mind
Quite often desires to use
  Its volcano-irritation
To destroy the world.

9168

Keep aside three special minutes a day.
The first minute you will use
  To realise God,
The second minute, to manifest God,
The third minute, to become
  God's sleepless Satisfaction-Delight.

9169

When the suspicious mind
And the dynamic vital
  Engage in a tug-of-war,
The heart invariably
Takes the side of the vital.

9170

The simpler the life, the better.
The purer the mind, the better.
The deeper the heart, the better.
The higher the self-giving, the better.
The quicker the God-realisation, the better.
The stronger the God-oneness, the better.

9171

Everyone loves to blame somebody else.
But who is that somebody else
  If not a portion of
The unrealised universal Self?

9172

His new inner boss, faith,
Dispensed with his old colleague, doubt,
The moment faith assumed
  Legitimate office.

9173

How long can you cling
To the shore of hope
Without using your heart's
  Determination-strength
To win in the battlefield of life?

9174

  If you see
Through the heart's eye,
  You will see
That you have already started
Your life's perfection-race.

9175

By defying doubt,
His mind is flying
In the sky of God's
Satisfaction-Delight.

9176

Behold, his old friends,
  Jealousy and insecurity,
Have left him quite unexpectedly.
Therefore, his delight
Is beyond the flight of his imagination.

9177

When you meditate,
Try to disconnect the thought-current.
  You will see and feel
A torrent of God-Delight
  Descending from Above
Into your aspiration-heart.

9178

I open my heart-door
Only in order to lock
  My Lord's Faith
Inside my heart.

9179

I have given God what I have:
  A self-indulgence-life.
God has given me what He is:
  His Effulgence-Heart.

9180

After a long time,
Today he is having
  A happy reunion
With his self-giving life.

9181

Your mind is enmeshed
In imperfection.
  Therefore
How can it see the perfection
In God's Perfection-Vision?

9182

I truly felt miserable
When I could not save my mind
With my heart's light
From drowning in selfishness-sea.

9183

Familiarity can take away all charm.
  But we must be wise,
For inside familiarity is also housed
Tremendously illumining
  And fulfilling opportunity.

9184

God the Beloved Supreme
Is waiting for you.
Never be afraid
Of entering the heart's
Cheerfulness-territory.

9185

Be careful!
You will be taunted
  By temptation
Even when you are on the verge
  Of God-realisation.

9186

Who says that being a mortal
Means you are insignificant?
Your heart's gratitude-flowers
Are the feasts of Infinity.

9187

Ambition comes not
  From the hungry heart
But from the disproportionate greed
  Of the vital.

9188

  Yesterday
He was at one
     With unwillingness.
  Today
Willingness has conquered
     His aspiration-heart.

9189

Only the unconditional souls
  Can sing with God
When He sings,
  And carry the melody perfectly.

9190

God is performing His Task.
He is keeping your heart-door open.
  Are you performing your task?
Are you keeping your mind-door closed?

9191

If you do not believe
  In your inner purity,
How can you be divinely satisfied
  With your outer beauty?

9192

Two eternal rivals:
A doubting mind,
The possession of this world,
And a climbing heart,
An inheritance from the higher worlds.

9193

He who has civilised his savage vital
  Has an added advantage
In reaching the final destination.

9194

The man who depends on his mind
  All the time
Has a life filled with futility.

9195

God definitely wants you
To follow in His Footsteps
And enter into the blossoming satisfaction
  Of His Heart-Garden.

9196

He prepared himself anew each day
For his Supreme Lord's arrival
With his heart's soulful prayer
And his life's peaceful meditation.

9197

True, Truth and God are one,
  But I love God more.
True, God and God-Ecstasy are one,
  But I need God more.

9198

Unless God's Compassion nourishes you
  And strengthens you,
You cannot stand on your own two feet
   In the inner world.

9199

Although he came to God,
Because of his Himalayan pride,
He did not accept anything from God.
He went away empty-handed!

9200

  I wish to surrender,
But how to surrender?
  I wish to be perfect,
But how to be perfect?
I can surrender my existence-life
  To God's Will
And I can become perfect
  In God's Heart
Only when I can sincerely claim
  My Lord Supreme
     As my own, very own.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 92, Agni Press, 1984
