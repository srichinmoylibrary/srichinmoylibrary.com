418. Silence and sound

The sound in silence
   Talks much,
   Says less.

The silence in sound
   Talks less,
   Says more.

The God in sound
   Speaks first,
   Then acts.

The God in silence
   Speaks not,
   Just acts.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
