443. Their opinions differ

The mind says:
Love is sex.

The vital says:
Sex is love.

The heart says:
Love is love.

The body says:
There is no such thing as love.

The soul says:
There is nothing but love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 9, Agni Press, 1973
