341. Let us go into the silence-room

O my mind,
Let us go into the silence-room.
I assure you,
You will be happy,
You will be illumined,
You will be fulfilled.

O my mind,
Let us go into the silence-room.
It is the silence-room that can give you
What you have always wanted:
Peace, peace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
