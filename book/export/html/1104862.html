14.

My Lord, my Lord,
You are reminding me
Again and again
Of the undeniable fact
That a sleeplessly self-giving seeker
Is absolutely the fastest runner
In the worlds of aspiration
And dedication.

25 March 2000
New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 2004
