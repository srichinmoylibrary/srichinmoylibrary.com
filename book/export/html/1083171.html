Boxer

Soul the boxer fights for humanity's aspiration. God the Boxer fights for humanity's salvation. Faith the boxer fights for humanity's perfection and glorification. Man the boxer fights for humanity's appreciation and admiration. Doubt the boxer fights for humanity's total destruction

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Orchestra, Agni Press, 1973
