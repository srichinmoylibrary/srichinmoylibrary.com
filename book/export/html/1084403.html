20. Try again

Nowhere to progress;
Therefore,
You are unhappy.
Nothing to express;
Therefore,
You are unhappy.
No one to impress;
Therefore,
You are unhappy.
None to suppress;
Therefore,
You are unhappy.
If you progress
Your soul will love you;
If you express
Your heart will love you;
If you impress
Your mind will love you;
If you suppress
Your vital will love you.
Therefore,
Try and try again.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
