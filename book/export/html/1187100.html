1450

A God-seeker is he
Who first surrenders
And then becomes
And finally wins the race.

A God-lover is he
Who first becomes
And then surrenders
And finally wins the race.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 15, Agni Press, 1983
