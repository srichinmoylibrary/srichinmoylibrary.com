Question: Is man's conscience more important than the outer law?

Sri Chinmoy: From the strict spiritual point of view, conscience is always infinitely more important than the outer law or manmade law because conscience represents the Light from above. And this Light was already operating long before the outer life came into existence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Justice-light and satisfaction-delight, Agni Press, 1976
