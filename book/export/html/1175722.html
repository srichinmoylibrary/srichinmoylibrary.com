2772 The Satisfaction-Sun

Think of the past,
Think of the present,
Think of the future.
Alas,
The thing that you are looking for
Is hiding.
You are looking for
The satisfaction-sun,
But this satisfaction-sun
Comes only after
The self-giving aspiration-dawn.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 28, Agni Press, 1982
