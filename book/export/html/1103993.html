13 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall not allow You
To overstrain
Your Compassion-Eye for me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 December 1999, 8:27 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before doing seventy one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (29 right, 41 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
