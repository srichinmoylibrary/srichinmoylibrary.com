43.

Limited are our lives.
Unlimited are our problems
And struggles.
Yet our Lord says to us,
“My children, fear not.
Move on, march on.
I am with you all.
I am in you all.
I am for you all.
You are bound to arrive
At your destined Goal.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My early morning heart-climbing prayers, Agni Press, 2006
