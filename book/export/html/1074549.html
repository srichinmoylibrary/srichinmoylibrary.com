Give what you have

Do you want
Himalayan joy?
Then give to God
What you have:
Your heart's aspiration-cry.

Do you want
Pacific peace?
Then give to man
What you have:
Your life's dedication-smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Cry within: yours is the Goal, Agni Press, 1974
