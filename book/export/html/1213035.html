127 Obstreperous

Madal: Chinmoy, it is said that God is extremely fond of children. To me, all children seem obstreperous. Hence, I literally hate them. Do you care for children?

Chinmoy: Madal, I do care for children. I like them, I love them, I adore them. You hate unruly, boisterous children and my budding disciples hate their unruly thoughts. So you and my disciples can be in the same boat sailing towards the same destination â€” nowhere.
