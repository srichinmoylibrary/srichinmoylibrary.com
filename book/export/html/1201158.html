15369

No matter how much complexity
Your mind cherishes
Either consciously or unconsciously,
God will bless your mind one day
With a childlike simplicity,
For that is the only way
Your mind will be able to enjoy
Delight infinite.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 154, Agni Press, 1991
