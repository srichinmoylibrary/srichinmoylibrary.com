My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 22

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Singapore and Solo, Indonesia in November 2003.

24 November 2003 ...

24 November 2003

Singapore

1 My Heart ...

My heart
Shall no more remain
A short-lived God-hunger.

2 God’s Face ...

God’s Face
May at times be invisible,
But His Heart
Is always visible.

3 God’s Closeness ...

God’s Closeness
Is my life’s fulness-smile.

4 Alas, God’s Heart ...

Alas, God’s Heart
Is a sleepless Tear for me.

5 Doubt Is Dangerous ...

Doubt is dangerous,
And self-doubt
Is infinitely more dangerous.

6 By Nature, Man Is Not ...

By nature, man is not
A God-Conversation-attention-ear.

7 My Heart’s Solemn Promise ...

My heart’s solemn promise
To God
Is my aspiration-return.

8 My God-Unwillingness ...

My God-unwillingness
Does not become me.

9 Today I Am My Heart’s ...

Today I am my heart’s
Aspiration-candle.
Tomorrow I shall become my soul’s
Illumination-sun.

10 My Mind Never Opens ...

My mind never opens.
God’s Eye never closes.

11 I Need Only One Thing ...

I need only one thing:
I need a tireless
God-surrender-heart.

25 November 2003 ...

25 November 2003

Singapore

12 When God’s Compassion-Eye Appears ...

When God’s Compassion-Eye appears,
My sufferings start to disappear.

13 When God’s Forgiveness-Heart Appears ...

When God’s Forgiveness-Heart appears,
My sufferings completely disappear.

14 Heaven’s Beauty ...

Heaven’s Beauty
And earth’s duty
I equally love.

15 Heaven’s Beauty ...

Heaven’s Beauty
Awakens my heart.

16 Earth’s Duty ...

Earth’s duty
Energises my life.

17 Oneness And Fear ...

Oneness and fear
Are perfect strangers
To each other.

18 My Lord ...

My Lord,
May I come close
To Your Feet?
“No, My child,
I want you to come close
To My Heart.”

19 Even The Abysmal Abyss ...

Even the abysmal abyss
Has more light
Than our conscious and deliberate
God-disobedience.

20 No God-Loving Meditation ...

No God-loving meditation
Can ever be an escape
From the world.

21 My Constant Self-Offering ...

My constant self-offering
To the world
Is a local call
To my Lord Beloved Supreme.

22 My Hesitant Self-Offering ...

My hesitant self-offering
To the world
Is the longest-distance call
To God.

23 My Mind Is Determined ...

My mind is determined
To possess the world.

24 My Soul Is Determined ...

My soul is determined
To address the world.

25 My Heart Is Determined ...

My heart is determined
To caress the world.

26 I Am Determined ...

I am determined
To become the world.

26 November 2003 ...

26 November 2003

Singapore

27 God The Eye Beckons Me ...

God the Eye beckons me.
God the Heart speaks to me.
God the Breath embraces me.

28 When God The Eye ...

When God the Eye
Beckons me,
I offer my heart’s gratitude-tears.

29 When God The Heart ...

When God the Heart
Speaks to me,
I offer my life’s gratitude-smiles.

30 When God The Breath ...

When God the Breath
Embraces me,
I become the flood
Of oneness-gratitude-delight.

31 Who Is Safe In Today’s World? ...

Who is safe in today’s world?
No, not even multibillionaires,
Needless to say
The poorer than the poorest.

32 Who Is Safe In Today’s World? ...

Who is safe in today’s world?
Not he who is wallowing
In the supremacy-pleasures
Of unbounded power.

33 Who Is Safe In Today’s World? ...

Who is safe in today’s world?
He who is endowed
With God’s Protection-Assurance
Here, there and all-where.

34 What Do I Learn From God’s Tears? ...

What do I learn from God’s Tears?
I learn from God’s Tears
That He has infinite concern for me.

35 What Do I Learn From God’s Smiles? ...

What do I learn from God’s Smiles?
I learn from God’s Smiles
That He has confidence in me
In measureless measure.

36 My Lord, Please Tell Me ...

My Lord, please tell me,
Why am I so unkind to You?
Why, why?
Why do I keep You waiting for me
For Eternity?

37 My Child, If You Come To Me ...

“My child, if you come to Me
Even beyond Eternity’s Eye and Heart,
I shall not mind,
As long as you come to Me,
Enter into My Heart
And become the Beauty of My Dream
And the Duty of My Reality.”

38 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That He is not at all worried
About my transformation,
Since He has no fixed schedule for me.

39 O Ignorance-Night ...

O ignorance-night,
I am not afraid of conversing with you,
But I am terribly afraid
Of your unthinkable vibration
That indefinitely lasts.
I am preparing myself
To conquer your vibration-power,
So that I can inundate my Mother Earth
With Infinity’s Delight.

40 No More ...

No more
My mind’s outer eye!
No more
My mind’s outer ear!

41 From Now On ...

From now on,
My heart’s inner eye and inner ear
I shall immediately welcome
And eternally treasure.

27 November 2003 ...

27 November 2003

Singapore

42 In The Small Hours Of The Morning ...

In the small hours of the morning
God comes to me
Not to preach, not to teach,
But to love me, my entire being.

43 God Comes To Me In The Evening ...

God comes to me in the evening
Not to bless, not to smile,
But to examine my love, devotion
And surrender.

44 God Comes To Me At Night ...

God comes to me at night
Only for one thing:
He comes to dream in and through me
His Infinity’s Dream.

45 When I Run Forward ...

When I run forward,
I easily receive God’s Smile.
When I climb upward,
I more easily receive God’s Smile.
When I dive inward,
I most easily receive God’s Smile.

46 My Meditation ...

My meditation
Is my God-invocation
In the heart of my silence-smile.

47 My Meditation ...

My meditation
Is my animal life-purification.
My meditation
Is my human life-transformation.
My meditation
Is my divine life-manifestation.

48 No Mind-Emptiness ...

No mind-emptiness,
No heart-oneness,
No God-fulness.

49 My Streaming Heart-Tears ...

My streaming heart-tears
Strengthen my prayer-life.

50 My Beaming Soul-Smiles ...

My beaming soul-smiles
Lengthen my silence-meditation.

51 My Madness-Mind ...

My madness-mind
Challenges God.

52 My Gladness-Heart ...

My gladness-heart
Embraces God.

53 God Is All The Time Crying ...

God is all the time crying
For the illumination
Of the doubting and suspecting mind.

54 The Doubting And Suspecting Mind ...

The doubting and suspecting mind
Is spying all the time on God
To discover some imperfection in God.

28 November 2003 ...

28 November 2003

Singapore

55 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That every eager effort
Of my heart
He deeply values.

56 An Intense God-Love ...

An intense God-love
Means a desire-conquered life.

57 Alas ...

Alas,
The fleeting moments of my life
Are empty of God’s Light.

58 God’s Flute ...

God’s Flute
Awakens my heart.
God’s Gong
Awakens my mind.
God’s Drum
Awakens my life.

59 I Have Millions Of Flowers ...

I have millions of flowers
In my heart-garden,
But I love the silence-flower
Infinitely more than any other flower.

60 Detachment — Not Out Of Shock ...

Detachment — not out of shock,
But out of wisdom-light —
Permanently lasts.

61 A God-Unwillingness-Mind ...

A God-unwillingness-mind
Is, indeed,
A real God-failure-life.

62 What I Sleeplessly Need ...

What I sleeplessly need
Is a Heaven-ascending heart.

63 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Every day my love increases for You.
How is it that Your Love for me
Does not increase even once a year?
“My child,
I really do not know
How to answer your question.”

29 November 2003 ...

29 November 2003

Solo, Indonesia

64 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Please, please free me
With Your Compassion-Eye.

65 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Please, please, please bind me
With Your Affection-Heart.

66 When I Arrive ...

When I arrive
At my Lord’s Palace,
He immediately embraces me
Most fondly and most proudly.

67 When My Lord Arrives ...

When my Lord arrives
At my cottage,
I immediately place myself
At His Feet
And devour the dust of His Feet.

68 My Heart’s Gladness ...

My heart’s gladness
And my life’s sadness
Both hear my Lord’s choice Voice.

69 Impossibility-Accomplishments ...

Impossibility-accomplishments:
This is what my Lord
Every day expects from me.

70 Each Time I Prayerfully Cry ...

Each time I prayerfully cry
Or soulfully smile,
My Lord invites me
To enter into
His Heaven-Heart-Garden.

71 The Divine Grace ...

The divine Grace
Comes down from Above
Not when I want it,
But only when I desperately need it.

72 Not God’s Eye ...

Not God’s Eye,
But God’s Heart takes the timing
In all our Heavenward races.

73 A Gratitude-God-Lover ...

A gratitude-God-lover
And a devoted God-seeker
Are God’s most favourite children.

74 God Has A Very Big Desire ...

God has a very big Desire:
His Desire is to see me happy
Twenty-four hours a day.

75 What Is My Mission? ...

What is my mission?
My mission is the outer manifestation
Of my Lord’s inner Vision.

76 Heaven Is Nowhere Else ...

Heaven is nowhere else
Save and except
In my Lord’s Compassion-flooded Eye.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 22, Agni Press, 2004
