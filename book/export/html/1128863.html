15449

You, too, can invent
Something absolutely new
Which nobody else
  Has invented.
What is that thing?
You will give and give
To humanity and to God,
But you will not ask
For anything in return.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 16, Agni Press, 1999
