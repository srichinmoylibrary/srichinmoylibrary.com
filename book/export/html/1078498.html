851 Even My Ignorance Knows...

EVEN MY IGNORANCE KNOWS

Why you love me
Is really far above
My earthly reasoning mind.

Why I need you
My heart-love knows,
My life-dedication knows,
Even my stupid ignorance blind knows.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
