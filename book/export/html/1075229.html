654 Why Am I So Unhappy?...

WHY AM I SO UNHAPPY?

Why am I so unhappy?
I am unhappy
Because I am ungrateful to my highest Self,
God.

Why am I so unhappy?
I am unhappy
Because I am not willing to teach my lowest self,
Temptation,
How to swim in the Sea of Illumination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 14, Agni Press, 1973
