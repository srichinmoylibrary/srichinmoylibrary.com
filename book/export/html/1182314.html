9299 Now He Is Running Fast

He started his inner journey
With no love,
No devotion,
No surrender -
Only sheer curiosity.
But now, years later,
He is running fast
Along his inner road
Of love, devotion and surrender.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 93, Agni Press, 1983
