715. Do anything you want

My soul I will pour
Into You:
Do anything You want to do with it.
Make me into either
The beauty of the morning
Or the ugliness of imperfection-night.
Do anything You want.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
