959 No Compromise...

NO COMPROMISE

No compromise!
Either glowing light
Or brooding night.
Make up your mind!

No compromise!
Either perfection-sky
Or frustration-sigh.
Make up your mind!

No compromise!
Either God-glance
Or death-lance.
Make up your mind!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
