9947 Determination-Fire

Determination-fire:
This is what my mind
Precisely needs.

Illumination-sun:
This is what my heart
Soulfully needs.

Satisfaction-God:
This is what my life
Sleeplessly needs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 100, Agni Press, 1983
