16 December 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My soul-enlightenment-Maker
Is
My heart-attachment-breaker.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 December 1999, 8:01 a.m. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Before lifting 350 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 700 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
