6337 Inside My Heart-Garden

Inside my heart-garden
I see the purity
Of humanity's cry
And the beauty
Of Divinity's Smile.
Also I see something else:
The ceaseless and sleepless flow
Of God's Heart-Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 64, Agni Press, 1983
