21. Our First And Last Miracle

God's first and last miracle:
He became many
To fulfil Himself.
My first and last miracle:
I became one
To enjoy myself.
And I must say
We both knew what we were doing.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Dreams that fly, Agni Press, 1975
