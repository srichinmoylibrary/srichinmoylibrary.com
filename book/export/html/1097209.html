64 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I send an invitation card
To my Lord Supreme.
He reads the card immediately
And says that He is not ready
Nor am I ready
To meet together.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 42, Agni Press, 2006
