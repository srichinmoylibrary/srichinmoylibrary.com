436. Earth-soul, where are you?

Earth-nature,
Indeed you are very bad.
Yet
I shall love you.

Earth-life,
Indeed you are very base.
Yet
I shall utilise you.

Earth-soul,
Where are you?
Do you really exist?
Have you ever existed?
Will you ever exist?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
