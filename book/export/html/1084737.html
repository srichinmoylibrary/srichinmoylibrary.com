48 I Speak...

I SPEAK

I speak to God
Because
God understands me.
I speak to Heaven
Because
Heaven appreciates me.
I speak not to man
Because
Man neither understands me
Nor appreciates me
To my wide surprise,
He does not even recognise me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
