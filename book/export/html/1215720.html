99. Love is all

The animal in me says:
Destruction is above all,
Destruction is all.

The human in me says:
Sensation is above all,
Sensation is all.

The divine in me says:
Liberation is above all,
Liberation is all.

The angel in me says:
Beauty is above all,
Beauty is all.

The God in me says:
Love is above all,
Love is all.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 2, Agni Press, 1974
