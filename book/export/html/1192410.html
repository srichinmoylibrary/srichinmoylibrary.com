6707

The difference
Between the desire-life
And the aspiration-life
Is this:
The desire-life wants
Countless gifts from God.
The aspiration-life longs
Only for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 68, Agni Press, 1984
