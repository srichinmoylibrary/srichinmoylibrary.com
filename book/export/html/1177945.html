4973 My Life Is Not A Transient Dream

My life is not a transient dream,
But my death is.
My God-realisation-light
Is not a transient reality,
But my struggle through ignorance-night was.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 50, Agni Press, 1982
