1009

God will not be there to receive you
If you hurriedly reach
Your life's finish line.
He will not only receive you
But also garland you and embrace you
If you slowly, steadily and soulfully reach
Your life's finish line.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 11, Agni Press, 1983
