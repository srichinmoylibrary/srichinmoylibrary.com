5279 What God Cares For

What is it that I need
And God does not need?
Determination-fire.
What is it that God
Constantly cares for
And I never care for?
My own self-transcendence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 53, Agni Press, 1983
