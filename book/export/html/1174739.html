1799 Perfection Blossoms In Obedience

Perfection
Blossoms in obedience.
Obedience
Glows in cheerfulness.
Cheerfulness
Is fulfilled in oneness.
Oneness is at once
A birthless smile
And
A deathless dance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 18, Agni Press, 1981
