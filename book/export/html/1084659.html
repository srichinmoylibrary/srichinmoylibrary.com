21. I need You eternally

O Compassion-God,
I needed You yesterday.
O Perfection-God,
I shall need You tomorrow.
O Liberation-God,
I need You now.
O Love-God,
I need You immediately,
I need You eternally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 14, Agni Press, 1974
