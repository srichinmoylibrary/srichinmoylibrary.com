31. My vital and my heart

My vital tells me:
   Love me or I will go away
   Never to return.

My heart tells me:
   Whether you love me or not
   I shall stay in you, for you.
   I shall never return
To the Heaven-Delight
Without you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 17, Agni Press, 1974
