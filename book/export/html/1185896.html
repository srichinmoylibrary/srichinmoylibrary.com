258

Before my heart,
My mind does not want to admit
Its inferiority.

Before my soul,
My heart does not want to admit
Its insecurity.

Before God,
I do not want to admit
My stupidity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 3, Agni Press, 1983
