1400 The Life Of Man Is Love

The life of man is love.
The love of man is perfection.
The perfection of man is satisfaction -
Satisfaction in God,
Satisfaction with God,
Satisfaction for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 14, Agni Press, 1981
