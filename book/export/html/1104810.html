13. When I think

When I think,
God shouts at me from a distance:
"Stop thinking."
When I concentrate,
God runs to me and says:
"Continue, continue, My son."
When I meditate,
God says:
"I am so happy that you know
the supreme secret."
When I contemplate,
God says:
"For both of us nothing can ever
remain undone."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My promise to God, Agni Press, 1975
