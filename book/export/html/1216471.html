835. Twice I enjoy my life

Twice I enjoy my life:
I enjoy my life
When I stand
Between
My man-ascent
And
My God-descent.
I enjoy my life
When my heart cries with the finite
And
My soul smiles with the Infinite.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 17, Agni Press, 1974
