Jagite Chahina Kandite Chahina

Jagite chahina kandite chahina
Pujite chahina amar hridaya gahane

Translation:

In the depths of my heart
I do not want to wake up,
I do not want to cry,
I do not want to worship.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Chander deshe, , 1991
