569 Satan And God...

SATAN AND GOD

When the human 'i' enters,
Satan enters, too.

When the divine 'I' enters,
God enters, too.

Who is Satan?
Satan is the grandson of imperfection
And
The son of destruction.

Who is God?
God is the Grandfather of life
And
The Father of love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 12, Agni Press, 1973
