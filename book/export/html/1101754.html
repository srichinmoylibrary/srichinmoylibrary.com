49.

My Lord,
I am proud
Of my gratitude-heart-tears.
Do You have anything
To be proud of?
"My child,
I have nothing
To be proud of.
From time immemorial
I have been performing
My self-imposed Task:
I love you.
I care for you."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 1, Agni Press, 1998
