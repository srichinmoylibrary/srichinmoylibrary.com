Ke Chahe Go

Ke chahe go ke chahe
Amar jiban ke chahe
Ke gahe go ke gahe
Amar bijoy ke gahe

Translation:

Who cares to have me?
Who cares to have my life?
Who cares to sing?
Who cares to sing my victory-song?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bela chale jai, , 1979
