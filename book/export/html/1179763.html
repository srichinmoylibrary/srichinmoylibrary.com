6773 A Miserable Failure

You are a Vision-Reality of God.
Alas, you are completely unaware of it.
What is worse,
You never have any longing
For God's eternal Satisfaction-Smile.
Therefore, are you not a miserable failure
In God's creation-awakening Vision?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 68, Agni Press, 1983
