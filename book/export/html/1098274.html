65 I Came Into The World ...

I came into the world
To serve God
Lovingly and self-givingly,
And not to experience
His highest Height
And His deepest Depth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 55, Agni Press, 2007
