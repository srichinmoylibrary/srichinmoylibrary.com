165. When wisdom resigns

You hurt knowledge
When you spare ignorance.

When
Knowledge is hurt,
Wisdom resigns.

When
Wisdom resigns,
God's Smile fades
Into earth-sadness,
Earth-helplessness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 4, Agni Press, 1974
