EASY LESSONS

When we deny God,
When we betray God,
He teaches us no easy lesson.
When we love God,
When we serve God,
He teaches us all easy lessons.
How?
Just by making all lessons easy,
Including even
Our God-realisation lesson.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
