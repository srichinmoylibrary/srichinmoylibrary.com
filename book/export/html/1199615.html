13841

My Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
I came into the world
To make You happy.
At the end of my journey's close,
You will take me back into Yourself
To make me happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 139, Agni Press, 1991
