6 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My God expresses
His extreme bewilderment
When I tell Him
That I am not inspired any more
To do His God-manifestation-work.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 March 2000, 12:32 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously six times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 13, Agni Press, 2000
