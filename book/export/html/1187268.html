1616

I have two teachers:
God and ignorance.
I have so many questions.
God the Teacher
Does not answer my questions.
Ignorance the teacher
Is unable to answer my questions.
Alas, what am I going to do now?
I am totally lost.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 17, Agni Press, 1983
