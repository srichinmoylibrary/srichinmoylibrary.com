15 August 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please, please bless me
With the capacity to place
Every day
My devotion-heart-blossoms
At Your Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 August 2000, 9:42 a.m. Before doing 800-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
