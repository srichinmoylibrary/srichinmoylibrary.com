1648 You Can Transform

You can transform
The hurtful pressures of your life
Into delightful pleasures
Just by telling yourself
That the world around you
Can easily exist
And even prosper
Without you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 17, Agni Press, 1981
