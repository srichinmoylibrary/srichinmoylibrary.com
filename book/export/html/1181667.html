8658 Just Because I Know

Just because I know
I am God's child,
I can be happy.

Just because I know
God loves me,
I should be kind to myself.

Just because I know
God is playing
His Satisfaction-Game
In and through me,
I must make myself
Cheerfully perfect.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 87, Agni Press, 1983
