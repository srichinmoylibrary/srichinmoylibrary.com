25. Leave me not alone

O earth, leave me not alone.
I need you.
I need your heart's
Aspiration-cry
To see the Face of God.
O Heaven, leave me not alone.
I need you.
I need your soul's
Divinity-smile
To discover the Beauty of God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
