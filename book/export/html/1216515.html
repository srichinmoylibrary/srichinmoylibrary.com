878. Your God, my God

You stay with your God;
Let me stay with mine.
Your God tells you
That I am unrealised,
That I am undivine
And
That I am imperfect.

My God tells me
That I am realised;
More than realised,
That I am divine;
More than divine,
That I am perfect.
My God tells me something more:
"My son, you are My
Only successor."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 18, Agni Press, 1974
