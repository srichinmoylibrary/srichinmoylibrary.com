7 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Even if You do not love me
Anymore,
Even if You do not speak to me
Anymore,
I shall not leave You.
"My child, My child, My child,
You will not be able to leave Me.
You will not be able to run away
From Me.
You will not be able to escape
From My Infinity's Heart-Home,
My child, My child, My child!"
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 7 January 1999, 11:05 a.m. Before lifting 220 lbs. twenty times with one arm. (10 right, 10 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
