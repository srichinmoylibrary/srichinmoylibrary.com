269. The best thing to do

To doubt God's existence
Is a fatal flaw.

To doubt one's own existence
Is an unforgivable flaw.

To love oneself divinely
Is a real thing to do.

To love God before one loves oneself
Is by far the best thing to do.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
