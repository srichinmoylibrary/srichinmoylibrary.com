29. Every message

Every secret message
Is a Thought of God.

Every sacred message
Is a Will of God.

Every divine message
Is a Consciousness-Sun of God.

Every supreme message
Is a Perfection —
        Manifestation —
            Reality
                 Of God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 4, Agni Press, 1974
