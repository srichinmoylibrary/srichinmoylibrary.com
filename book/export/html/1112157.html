The soul's connection

Every month X makes me a special cake or pie, which I give out as prasad. But this month I was out of town, so she did not give me any.

Early in the morning, just as I was going out to run, I was thinking, "Just because I was not here, she did not give me prasad."

Then, when I came back, she and Y were waiting there in the car. This is called the soul's connection.
