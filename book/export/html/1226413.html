Jiban Bhore Karbo Tomai Baran

Jiban bhore karbo tomai baran
Jiban sheshe karbo tomai haran

Translation:

I shall accept You
At the very dawn of my life.
I shall steal You
In the evening of my life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 8a, , 1981
