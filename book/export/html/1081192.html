261. You have given

Father,
You have given him
Your Pen.
You have given her
Your Ball.
Father,
What about poor me?
"My son, just wait
At My Heart-Gate.
I shall give you My All."

From: Sri Chinmoy, From the Source to the Source, Agni Press, 1974
