IN THE ASSEMBLIES

In the assembly
Of the lower gods,
His love-presence
Was welcome.

In the assembly
Of the higher gods,
His devotion-presence
Was necessary.

In the palace
Of the absolute Supreme,
His surrender-presence
Was indispensable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
