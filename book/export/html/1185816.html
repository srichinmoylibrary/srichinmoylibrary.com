179

Earth, my earth,
What do I owe you,
If not my concern?

Heaven, my Heaven,
What do I owe you,
If not my compassion?

God, my God,
What do I owe You,
If not my satisfaction?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 2, Agni Press, 1983
