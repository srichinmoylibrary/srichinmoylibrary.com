324. If you love to become one

If you like to obey,
Then you can go far.
If you love to surrender,
Then you will go very far.
If you love to become consciously,
constantly
and inseparably one
with God-Light,
Then not only will you reach the farthest
But you have already reached
The farthest of all.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
