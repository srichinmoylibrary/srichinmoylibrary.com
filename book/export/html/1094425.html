14 Infinitely More Concern Has To Bloom ...

Infinitely more concern has to bloom
In the heart of Heaven.
Then only
Peace will blossom
On the lap of Mother Earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 10, Agni Press, 2002
