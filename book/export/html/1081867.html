308. God fulfils my needs

I needed a beautiful soul;
Therefore
God gave me one.

I need a powerful soul;
Therefore
God has given me one.

I shall need a fruitful soul;
Therefore
God will give me one.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
