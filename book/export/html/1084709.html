20 He Is Still Alive...

HE IS STILL ALIVE

His life is based on hope-joy;
Therefore
He is still alive.
His mind is based on plan-might;
Therefore
He is still alive.
His heart is based on surrender-light;
Therefore
He is still alive.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
