37.

O my mind,
Do you not remember
That there was a time
When you loved God
Most dearly?
If you do not
Remember the time,
Let me tell you.
It was before the time
When you made friends
With fear, doubt,
Anxiety and despair.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My God-commitments, Agni Press, 1992
