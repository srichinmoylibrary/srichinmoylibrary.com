414. I am known to surrender

You are happy
     Because
You are unknown to doubt.

He is happy
     Because
He is unknown to failure.

Heaven is happy
     Because
It is known to tomorrow.

Earth is happy
     Because
It is known to joy.

Finally, I am happy
     Because
I am known to surrender, constant surrender, unconditional surrender.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
