150. My plan

Man, mar not
My perfect plan:
My aspiration-flame
And God's Compassion-moon
Ere long will get married.

Man, foil not
My secret plan:
My gold faith in God
Is at last ready
To bury my doubt-tiger
In its roaring den.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
