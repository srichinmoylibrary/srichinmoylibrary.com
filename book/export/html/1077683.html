36. The right person

Heaven-born freedom
I know not
Whom to share with.
Ah, I know the right person.
I shall share with my brother, man.

Earth-born bondage
I know not
Whom to share with.
Ah, I know the right person.
I shall share with my Father, God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
