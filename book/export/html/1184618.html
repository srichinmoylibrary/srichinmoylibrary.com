290. Those who never sleep

Many are there
Who sleep too much.
Very few are there
Who do not sleep enough.
Again, there are those
Who never sleep at all.
Theirs is the continuous search
For God-oneness,
For God-perfection,
For Eternity's God-embrace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
