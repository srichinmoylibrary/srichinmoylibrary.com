12470

Doubt has infected
Your mind.
Fear has invaded
Your vital.
But you must challenge
Both doubt and fear
So theirs is a short (life.) (fn:1)

(fn:1) The word (life) has been added by the editor. In the first edition of the book the verse was incomplete.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 125, Agni Press, 1989
