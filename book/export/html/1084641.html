3. My faith-flower

O humanity,
Doubt is my little self.
Don't pay any attention to it.
It will die away very soon.
O divinity,
Faith is my big self.
Do not give up hope,
Be a little more patient.
My faith-flower is blossoming
Petal by petal
For your manifestation-light
Here on earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 14, Agni Press, 1974
