412. The favourite

He is Heaven's favourite lover.
        Therefore
He loves the world
        Devotedly.

He is earth's favourite server.
        Therefore
He serves the world
        Soulfully.

He is God's favourite hero.
        Therefore
He defends the world
        Gratefully.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
