Tomai Jahara Kare Amanya

Tomai jahara kare amanya
tader rakhibo dure
Tahara na pabe kabhu ashroy
mama antara pure

Translation:

I shall keep those at a distance
Who disobey You.
They will never get shelter
In my heart-citadel.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giti mala, , 2000
