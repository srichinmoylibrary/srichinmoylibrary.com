309. He just barks

He barks
But
He does not bite.
What I am telling is true.
But, if he ever bites,
His bite will be no match for his bark.

He is clever
Therefore he does not bite.
I tell you,
This is the only reason
Why he does not bite.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
