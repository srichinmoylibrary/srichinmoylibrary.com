383. Never

        Never
He thinks out of fear;
        Never
He fears to think.
        Never
He loves out of fear;
        Never
He fears to love.
        Never
He becomes one out of fear;
        Never
He fears to become one.
   He thinks only of his life divine,
   He fears only his ignorance,
   He loves only his soul,
   He becomes only his goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 8, Agni Press, 1973
