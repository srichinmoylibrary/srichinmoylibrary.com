17.

O my mind,
Invoke God the
Forgiveness first,
And then invoke God the Compassion.
Then God the Justice
Will send you this message:
"My child,
I am glad to inform you
That you can surely escape my visit."

From: Sri Chinmoy, O my mind, Agni Press, 1988
