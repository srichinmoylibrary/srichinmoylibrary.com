326. Live where you are

Live where you are.
I shall not try to convert you.
I shall not inject fear into you.

Live where you are.
Only grow confidence indomitable within yourself
So that in you can loom large
God-necessity
And
God-satisfaction Reality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
