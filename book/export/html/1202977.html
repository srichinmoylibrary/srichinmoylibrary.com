17170

When I live inside my heart,
I see that my life has become
A source of inspiration
To all human beings.
When I live inside my mind,
I see that my life has become
Nothing but confrontation-desert
And confusion-jungle.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 172, Agni Press, 1992
