My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 41

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Pangkor Island, Malaysia in November and December 2005.

30 November 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

1 I Can And I Must Learn ...

I can and I must learn
How to live a perfect life
Inside my Lord’s Heart.

2 The Fresh Morning Dew ...

The fresh morning dew
Awakens
My sleeping aspiration-heart.

3 God Wants Only One Thing ...

God wants only one thing
From me:
A ceaseless and breathless
God-aspiration-heart.

4 At A Distance ...

At a distance
I imagine God’s Feet
Until the day arrives
When I can place myself
At God’s Feet.

5 God Wants Me To Please Him ...

God wants me to please Him
In a prayerful and soulful way,
And not in an astounding way.

6 God’s Compassion-Rain ...

God’s Compassion-Rain
Falls
To enthral my heart.

7 No Mind-Ignorance ...

No mind-ignorance
Can stop
My Heaven-climbing
Journey’s start.

8 A Happy Heart ...

A happy heart
Is
A God-given capacity
To spread God’s Light
Freely and ceaselessly.

9 This Time When God Sends Away ...

This time when God sends away
My ignorance-pride,
I shall definitely not
Bring it back.

10 I Pray And Meditate ...

I pray and meditate
Inside my heart’s
Sweetness-devotion-moon.

1 December 2005 ...

1 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

11 God Wants My Peace, My Love ...

God wants my peace, my love,
My joy and my self-giving life
For the universal benefit.

12 In The Morning ...

In the morning
I devour the sun-flames
To make the fastest progress
In my spiritual life.

13 In The Evening ...

In the evening
I devour the moon-beams
To make the fastest progress
In my spiritual life.

14 Not God’s Infinite Power ...

Not God’s infinite Power
But God’s Compassion-Tears
Have transformed my life.

15 God’s Compassion-Smiles ...

God’s Compassion-Smiles
Are keeping me
In the world of abundant bliss.

16 God Wants All The Aspiring Hearts ...

God wants all the aspiring hearts
Around the world
To be united in the inner world.

17 May The World I Live In ...

May the world I live in
Have only newness,
Oneness and fulness.

18 What My Body, Vital ...

What my body, vital,
Mind and heart need
Is the life of spirituality.

19 God Wants His Compassion ...

God wants His Compassion
And my aspiration
To spread out
Here, there and everywhere.

20 Only In Deep Meditation ...

Only in deep meditation
Does my aspiration-sun rise high,
Very high, unimaginably high.

21 I Love To Sit Devotedly ...

I love to sit devotedly
At the Feet
Of God the One.

22 I Love To Follow Eagerly ...

I love to follow eagerly
In the Footsteps
Of God the Many.

23 I Love To Be Constantly Blessed ...

I love to be constantly blessed
By God the Universal.

24 All My Heart-Tears ...

All my heart-tears
And all my soul-smiles
I sleeplessly dedicate
To God the Universal.

2 December 2005 ...

2 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

25 O My Heart ...

O my heart,
I want you to be the nest
Of my aspiration-bird.

26 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Do give me a heart
That will not find any difficulty
In remembering You
All the time.

27 Do Not Shake Hands ...

Do not shake hands
With ignorance-night!
Use your third eye
To destroy its exorbitant pride.

28 When Light Leads Us ...

When Light leads us,
Power helps us
And never stands
In Light’s way.

29 Each Heart ...

Each heart
Is
An aspiration-mountain.

30 Each Life ...

Each life
Is
A dedication-fountain.

31 The Mind That Instigates Me ...

The mind that instigates me
To make a serious blunder
Is the same mind
That later on blames me.

32 God Enters ...

God enters
Into my heart
On tiptoe.

33 I Enter ...

I enter
Into God’s Heart
Striking a thunder-drum.

34 The World May Forget ...

The world may forget
My face one day,
But it will never forget
My heart.

3 December 2005 ...

3 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

35 Silence, Silence, Silence! ...

Silence, silence, silence!
Silence was born
Before the Birth
Of our Lord Beloved Supreme.

36 Each Earth-Born Seeker ...

Each earth-born seeker
Will one day be caught
By the Cosmic Embrace
Of our Lord Supreme.

37 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
Be always careful!
Carelessness is a serious
And contagious disease
In the spiritual life.

38 I Rise From My Invisible Heart ...

I rise from my invisible heart
To my visible eye
To be of service
To mankind.

39 This Morning ...

This morning
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Was most compassionately
Watching me
While I was bathing in His Sea
Of infinite Peace and Bliss.

40 A Moment ...

A moment
To see,
But a lifetime
To become!

41 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I most prayerfully and soulfully
Offer my Lord
My heart’s aspiration-beauty-blossoms.

42 The Seekers Must Every Day ...

The seekers must every day
Practise one thing faithfully:
Self-sacrifice.

43 Every Child-Heart ...

Every child-heart
Radiates
The Beauty of the Unknowable.

44 There Is Only One Reality ...

There is only one reality:
God’s birthless and deathless
Compassion-Eye-Revelation.

4 December 2005 ...

4 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

45 With Kindness ...

With kindness
We start.
With oneness
We stop.

46 Be A Self-Giving Life ...

Be a self-giving life.
God Himself
Will sculpture you.

47 I Never Care For ...

I never care for
A hermit-cave.

48 I Only Care For ...

I only care for
A seeker’s prayerful cottage.

49 May My Heart Be A Nest ...

May my heart be a nest
Of self-giving
Affection-sweetness.

50 God Wants Me To Have ...

God wants me to have
Compassion-plants
In my heart-garden
More than anything else.

51 What Do I Need? ...

What do I need?
I desperately need
A more self-giving life.

52 What Else Do I Need? ...

What else do I need?
I sleeplessly need
A little more Heaven-climbing heart.

53 Each And Every Hope ...

Each and every hope
Of the aspiration-heart
Will one day fully blossom.

54 My Heart ...

My heart
Is an earth-born hope,
And my soul
Is a Heaven-born promise.

5 December 2005 ...

5 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

55 Always More God ...

Always more God,
Infinitely more God
Than the human beings!

56 Keep Quiet ...

Keep quiet,
My mind and my vital!
Our Lord is speaking.

57 My Earthly Life ...

My earthly life
Counts
My earthly years.

58 My Heavenly Soul ...

My Heavenly soul
Counts
My Heavenly hours.

59 Who Can Count ...

Who can count
Earth’s gratitude-tears
And Heaven’s sunburst-smiles,
Who can?

60 The Mind Should Know ...

The mind should know
That disobedience is not
A simple headache
But infinitely more painful
Than a migraine.

61 Do Not Allow Sorrow ...

Do not allow sorrow
To shadow your path.

62 Alas ...

Alas,
So late, so late —
I have just started
My God-manifestation-service!

63 At Long Last ...

At long last
Today I am enjoying
The expiration
Of my desire-world.

6 December 2005 ...

6 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

64 My Way ...

My way:
I love
Human beings
Every day less and less.

65 God’s Way ...

God’s Way:
God loves
Not only human beings
But His entire Creation
Every day more and more.

66 As Soon As I Wake Up ...

As soon as I wake up,
I watch my Lord’s Channel first.

67 The Second Thing That I Do ...

The second thing that I do:
I take love-devotion-surrender
Exercises.

68 The Mind ...

The mind
Is the most
Unreliable companion.

69 Doubt ...

Doubt
Is the most powerful tyrant
Here on earth.

70 The Ancient Path ...

The ancient path
Was to see God
Face to face.

71 The Modern Path ...

The modern path
Is to debate voraciously
About religion and scripture.
What a stupid path!

72 This Morning ...

This morning
God’s Compassion-Eye
Wiped away the sad differences
Between my heart-approach
And my mind-approach
To God-realisation.

73 The Mind’s Way ...

The mind’s way
Is to possess God.

74 The Heart’s Way ...

The heart’s way
Is to be possessed by God.

75 My Soul Has Banished ...

My soul has banished
Doubt and suspicion,
Jealousy and insecurity
From my heart-kingdom.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 41, Agni Press, 2005
