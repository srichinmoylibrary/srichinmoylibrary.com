LOUD QUESTIONS, SILENT ANSWERS

Earth's loud question:
Does God exist?

My loud question:
Is God alive?

Heaven's silent answer to earth:
God does exist,
But
Not for you.

Heaven's silent answer to me:
God is alive,
But
Not in you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
