446

As they want the Buddha
To be for the East
And the Christ
To be for the West,
Even so they want your one shoulder
To liberate them
And your other shoulder
To save them.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 5, Agni Press, 1983
