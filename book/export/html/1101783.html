25.

My Lord,
My heart breaks into millions
Of pieces
When I do not feel You
Inside my heart.
"My child,
You are not alone in your boat.
How is it that you do not see Me
Beside you?"

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 2, Agni Press, 1998
