19.

His body-temple offers
Purity's smile.
His vital-temple offers
Agility's smile.
His mind-temple offers
Sincerity's smile.
His heart-temple offers
Humility's smile.
His soul-temple offers
Luminosity's smile.
And he himself offers
Perfection's smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My heart-temple, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1999
