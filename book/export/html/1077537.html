15. Your life

           Yesterday
Your life was your
     Incessant expectation.           Today
Your life is your
     Incessant frustration.           Tomorrow
Your life shall be God's
     Incessant Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eternity's Silence-Heart, Agni Press, 1974
