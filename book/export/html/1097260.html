38 If You Really Want To Do ...

If you really want to do
Something good for God,
Then do not plan ahead.
God’s Will will blossom
In God’s Way
Inside your heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 43, Agni Press, 2006
