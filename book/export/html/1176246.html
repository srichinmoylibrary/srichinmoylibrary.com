3291 My Body Needs Calm Confidence

My body needs calm confidence,
My vital needs sublime confidence,
My mind needs resolute confidence,
My heart needs abiding confidence,
My life needs invincible confidence
So that I can become
My infinite Lord's
Eternally perfect instrument.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 33, Agni Press, 1982
