38. I am happy

I am happy
Because
My body is obedience.

I am happy
Because
My vital is confidence.

I am happy
Because
My mind is concentration.

I am happy
Because
My heart is dedication.

I am happy
Because
My soul is satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Vision-Promise, Agni Press, 1974
