92. God is always ready

In the world of form
God is always ready
To play with you
With His arms of Duty.
Do you know that?

In the world without form
You must remain always ready
To play with God
With your eyes of beauty.
Do you know that?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
