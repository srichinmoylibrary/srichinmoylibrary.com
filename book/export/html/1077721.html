74. Love is nectar

Insecurity
Is a subtle poison.

Doubt
Is slow poison.

Jealousy,
Is fatal poison.

Courage
Is without poison.

Love
Is nectar,
Infinity's Smile,
Eternity's Face,
Immortality's Race.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
