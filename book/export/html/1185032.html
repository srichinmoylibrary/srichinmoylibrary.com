704. The depth of my heart

The flame of Light —
Where is it?
In the depth of my heart.

The beauty of Light —
Where is it?
In the depth of my heart.

The perfection of Light —
Where is it?
In the depth of my heart.

The satisfaction of Light —
Where is it?
In the depth of my heart.

The depth of my heart
Houses all the Light
In all its forms and shapes,
In all its beauties and delights.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
