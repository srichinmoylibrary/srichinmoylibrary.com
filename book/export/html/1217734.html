Delight you need

Delight you need?
Sacrifice you feed.
Precious is love
Below, above.
And precious more
Surrender's Door,
Breathe in the One
Won Golden Sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, AUM -- Vol. 1, No.12, July 27, 1966, Boro Park Printers -- Brooklyn, N. Y, 1966
