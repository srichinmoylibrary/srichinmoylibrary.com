30. A solemn promise

I have made a solemn promise
To myself:
Next time
I see my Beloved Supreme,
I shall beg Him
To build me a new little heart
And totally destroy
My proud, giant mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, part 1, Agni Press, 1979
