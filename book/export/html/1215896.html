271. Don't be a fool

Don't be a fool.
You need aspiration
In your animal life.

Don't be a fool.
You need perfection
In your human life.

Don't be a fool.
You see frustration
In your divine life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 6, Agni Press, 1974
