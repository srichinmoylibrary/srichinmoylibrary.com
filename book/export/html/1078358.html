DESIRES UNFULFILLED

Desires unfulfilled,
He dared to fling defiance at God.

Desires unfulfilled,
He trained himself in the denial of God.

Desires unfulfilled,
He at once became
God-indifference
And
Man-indifference.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
