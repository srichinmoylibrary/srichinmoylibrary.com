259. Imperfect and perfect gifts

Three imperfect gifts
From the core of earth:
Desire-life
Ignorance-night
Doubt-poison.

Three perfect gifts
From the core of earth:
Aspiration-cry
Realisation-smile
Perfection-light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
