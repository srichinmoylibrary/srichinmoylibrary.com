Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 240

23901

The non-attachment of the mind
Is definitely good,
But the concern of the heart
Is infinitely better.

23902

My Lord does not approve of
My mind's superiority.
He does not approve of
My life's inferiority either.

23903

If we want to close our mind-door,
Who can prevent us?
But we allow our minds to make us feel
That we are too tired.

23904

Alas,
I do not seem to know
The difference between
My mind's hallucination
And my heart's exhilaration.

23905

O my Lord,
Like my soul,
May my heart, mind,
Vital and body
Become ready servants
Of Your supreme Will.

23906

My Lord,
Do give me back
My heart's ancient peace-morning-days
And peace-evening-nights.

23907

A cheerful mind, a cheerful heart,
A cheerful life —
These are, indeed, Heaven-products.

23908

God never wants me to celebrate
My journey's close.
He wants me to enjoy only
My journey's constant self-transcendence.

23909

Even the beating of a thousand drums
Will not be able
To awaken the sleeping mind
If it wants to enjoy
Its ignorance-pleasure-life.

23910

I have come to realise
That my best compatriot
In my aspiration-life
Is my God-obedience.

23911

In the long run, I know,
Everybody will be
A chosen instrument of God,
And everybody will fulfil God
In God's own Way.
But I believe only in the importance
Of here and now.

23912

My Lord,
I pray to You to grant me
A gratitude-heart
So that when I pass by You,
I will not foolishly ignore You.

23913

May each and every thought of mine
Be redolent with the purity of
My heart's God-surrender-bliss.

23914

I believed, I believe
And I shall forever believe
That God manifests Himself
Through each individual
In a unique way.

23915

God always takes for granted
Man's insincerity-mind
And his ingratitude-heart.

23916

My mind's lack of readiness
And my heart's lack of willingness
Can never be acceptable to God.

23917

God loves me,
Not because of what I say and do,
But because of what I truly am.

23918

When you are determined
To face bravely
The difficulty ahead of you,
The difficulty itself surrenders to you —
To your wide surprise.

23919

The mind-river is terribly afraid
Of flowing onward.
The heart-river enjoys
Flowing onward.
Therefore, it arrives unmistakably sooner
At the destination-ocean.

23920

From possession-hunger
To renunciation-meal
Is, indeed, an extraordinary distance.

23921

My Lord,
May the beauty of my heart
Forever remain
And not disappear
The way a rainbow disappears.

23922

A hero-warrior
Achieves extraordinary things
In his life
Because he does not allow himself
To be pushed by destiny's wind.

23923

The sword of time
Does not destroy us
Or punish us.
It only helps us transform our nature.

23924

O my mind,
Try sincerely to love silence
And not be inspired to broadcast
Your love.

23925

Who can save you,
Who can help you,
O my mind,
If you do not step aside
From doubt-suspicion-world?

23926

He is extremely happy
Because silence garlands
His morning prayers
And his evening meditations
With tremendous pride.

23927

He is his indomitable will-power.
He has said his final good-bye
To ignorance-night,
To breathe in the light and delight
Of Heaven.

23928

If you do not
Soulfully pray and meditate daily,
Then you will be overwhelmed
By a tidal wave of self-doubt.

23929

No mighty soul
Can ever be enslaved
By the tyranny
Of endless desires.

23930

There is a God-manifestation-dream
In each aspiration-heart.
This dream is, alas, imprisoned
And has to be liberated.

23931

We have inside us
A miniature world.
Let us not hide
But illumine
This small world
Of imperfection.

23932

Infinity multiplied by Infinity
Are the aspiration-flowers
That you are offering
To the Supreme.

23933

Today
God's Name is Happiness
Because His children have proved
Their loving oneness
With His creation.

23934

Each divine Word from God
Is a most powerful aspiration-flame
To illumine and immortalise us
In the Heart of our Beloved Supreme.

23935

The secret of secrets
Of the dearest child of God:
God is dearest
Not because He is omniscient,
Omnipotent and omnipresent,
But because He is all Love.

23936

Just remember:
If you really love the Supreme
In your Master,
You will not do anything
To disappoint or displease Him.

23937

God's loving Life-Breath is all yours,
For the manifestation of what you are:
The unmanifested God.

23938

When I meditate soulfully,
I make myself five years old.
When I do not meditate,
I become eighty years old.

23939

When you listen to
And obey
The Master's divine wish,
You are fulfilling
The Divine Itself.

23940

Disobedience
Is much worse than falsehood,
For it breaks the divine law.

23941

The Supreme is offering you
His continuous Blessing,
So you can learn how to bring forward
The eternal child inside you.

23942

The real gift
That God deeply cherishes
And treasures
Is your soulful smile.

23943

Humanity's appreciation
Of God's creation
Is its true gift
To the Creator.

23944

Love is the greatest force;
Who can dare to deny it?
But God's Command-Force
Is also the greatest Love.

23945

Each good thought
That you have encouraged
And nourished
Is your life's true work of art.

23946

The good will
That you offer to others
Comes to the fore
To manifest your own divinity.

23947

The moment you are about
To forget God's Dictates,
Soulfully think about
Your morning prayer
And your morning meditation.

23948

Although you have already seen
Thousands of days,
O seeker,
When tomorrow dawns,
You can create something absolutely new
In your life.

23949

You can constantly transcend yourself
Only by discovering
And maintaining your inner joy.

23950

When you have realised the Supreme
In His own Way,
You will never, never manifest Him
In a human way.

23951

Even when ignorance captures
The Supreme's dearest children,
He never accepts defeat.

23952

Just do the right thing
In the inner world and in the outer world.
Lo, you will realise the Highest
In the way that is meant for you.

23953

Physical brothers
Appear and disappear,
But spiritual brothers
Follow the path
Of eternal friends.

23954

O children of the Supreme,
You are not the children of the past,
But the children of the eternal Now.

23955

Our true satisfaction
Entirely depends
On our own sincere inner cry.

23956

If you work hard inwardly,
Automatically you will be blessed
With gratitude.

23957

Our inner vision sees
That silence
Not only has creativity inside it,
But is creativity itself.

23958

A God-lover's true discoveries:
God the Inner Pilot
Is all Beauty;
God the Inner Pilot
Is all Sweetness.

23959

Inspiring poetry is spirituality
Revealed and manifested
In a soulfully transcending
And fulfilling way.

23960

There is no neutrality in life.
Either you are on God's Side,
Or you are on the side of ignorance.

23961

It is humanity's receptivity
That can create the Kingdom of Heaven
Here on earth.

23962

To become a divine warrior,
Just develop and develop
Your sincerity-muscle.

23963

Wisdom
Is to obey the Message
Of the Inner Pilot.
There is no greater wisdom.

23964

If you want to handle your world
With wisdom,
What you need is oneness with God's Will.

23965

Aspiration, dedication,
Devotion and surrender
Are the divine jewels
That comprise your inner treasure.

23966

O God-dreamer,
God's Palace Supreme
Will always house and protect you
Inwardly and outwardly.

23967

What is perfection?
Perfection is liberation.
What is perfection?
Perfection is the manifestation of divinity.

23968

An unaspiring vital,
What is it?
A swinging pendulum
Of feeble insecurity.

23969

The day I surrendered myself completely
To God's Will,
God immediately made me
His Universal Joy-collector.

23970

Like my heart-garden-flowers,
I shall offer gratitude
To my Lord Supreme
Just for being.

23971

Alas,
It is not that I do not want
To please God.
I do want to please Him
In His own Way,
But I do not know how.

23972

No matter where I go,
My willingness-heart
And my Lord's Compassion
I see together.

23973

Our willingness, eagerness
And selflessness
Thrill and please God
Far beyond our imagination's flight.

23974

Our tears are beautiful,
More beautiful, most beautiful —
And sweeter than the sweetest —
When we place them at the Feet
Of our Lord Beloved Supreme.

23975

On the spiritual journey,
Our very best guide
Is our own devotion-heart.

23976

To make oneself happy,
The easiest and most effective way
Is to find every day
A new beginning.

23977

Those who think
That there will always be
A tomorrow for them
May finish the race,
But will not be able to win the race.

23978

I hired my mind to work
In my heart-garden,
But my Lord Supreme,
My Superboss,
Dismissed my mind summarily.

23979

Because of our stupid mind's
Blind preconceptions,
We go neither forward nor backward;
We remain a stagnant pool.

23980

My soul commands me
To teach my mind privately
So that it can study
In my heart-school.

23981

The ultimate teacher
Is not an experiment,
Not even an experience,
But our own self-discovery.

23982

O my setting sun-mind,
I shall never allow you
To rise again anywhere.

23983

The dreamer in me
Is my God-readiness.
The lover in me
Is my God-willingness.
The server in me
Is my God-eagerness.

23984

My Lord, my Lord,
Do not allow
My mind-heaviness-darkness-cloud
To eclipse
My heart-delight-illumination-sun.

23985

My Lord,
How can I have perfection-lustre in my life?
My child,
Walk along the surrender-road
Smilingly and unconditionally.

23986

Sadness disappears
Like a ghost
When I see my Master smiling.

23987

There is only one thing I must do.
I must mail away the sorrows of my heart
To Heaven.

23988

We must be very careful
To guard against depression.
Sooner than the soonest
Depression can approach us
At an unguarded moment.

23989

When a divine smile grows
Inside our heart,
We breathe in God's own Breath.

23990

Each inspiring dream
Comes from a higher world
With a special significance
To awaken and illumine this earth.

23991

Eternity beckons
The fleeting mind.
Infinity beckons
The tiny heart.
Immortality beckons
The fruitful life.

23992

When I live in the desire-world,
I feel that I came into the world
Only to make myself happy.
When I live in the aspiration-world,
I feel that I came into the world
Only to make myself perfect
For the Satisfaction of my Lord Supreme.

23993

Only one thing matters to me:
That I never disobey my Lord Supreme
Even for a fleeting moment.

23994

Pray to God
For God's boundless Compassion
To avoid stumbling on the stones
Of misfortune.

23995

My Lord, I am blessed
To have felt Your Heart.
My Lord, I am infinitely more blessed
To sit at Your Feet.

23996

When we examine
Our self-deception,
Our life-illumination
Speedily approaches us.

23997

My mind's silence
Is my God-nearness.
My God-nearness
Is a forward step for humanity
To arrive at God's Feet.

23998

You do not have to go to anybody
To receive answers
To satisfy your mind.
Just enter into your higher self.
All the answers are already there.

23999

The fragrance of spirituality
Is, indeed, the life-breath
Of universal music.

24000

God tells me:
"My child, you can have as many faces
As you want to,
But I love your simplicity-face the best.
My child, you can have as many hearts
As you want to,
But I love your surrender-heart the best."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 240, Agni Press, 1997
