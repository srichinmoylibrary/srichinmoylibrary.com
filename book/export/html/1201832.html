16036

My vital,
You have studied for a long time
At insecurity-school.
Now stop studying!

My mind,
You have studied for a long time
At insincerity-college.
Now stop studying!

My heart,
It is high time for you
To study at purity-university.
Start studying!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 161, Agni Press, 1991
