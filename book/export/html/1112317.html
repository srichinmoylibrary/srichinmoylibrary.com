Question: When will you go to Russia?

Sri Chinmoy: I am dying to go to Russia, but because of inner reasons it is not possible right now. At least you can come and see me here. Also, I have already sent my heart, my inner realisation and my Eternity's oneness to the inner existence and the outer existence of Russia.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Russia and Russia's God-Blossoming Heart, Agni Press, 1991
