19648

Alas, why do I allow
The outer world
To compel me to think
That God does not love me,
And why do I allow
The inner world
To compel me to feel
That God does not need me?
Why? Why?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 197, Agni Press, 1994
