23183

God feels His Presence
Inside your heart.
God feels your presence
Inside His Heart.
Therefore, He is happy.
If you want to be happy,
Then bring God and keep God
Inside your mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 232, Agni Press, 1996
