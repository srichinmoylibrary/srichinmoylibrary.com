Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 177

17601

When the loving heart
And the doubting mind
Collide,
The compassionate soul
Immediately comes
To rescue the heart.

17602

I do not want to be trapped
By my mind's idea-prison.
I want to be liberated
By my heart's ideal-sky.

17603

Only one song to sing:
My heart's oneness-song.
Only one dance to dance:
My mind's surrender-dance.

17604

The beauty of self-giving,
The purity of self-giving
And the divinity of self-giving
Can only be felt
And never expressed.

17605

Man's imperfect question:
How far away is God?
God's perfect answer:
He is Eternity's Here and Now.

17606

I am disgusted with my mind,
For it suspects even God's
Unconditionally self-giving Heart.

17607

O my vital,
When will you stop
Being hampered
By strangling thoughts?

17608

Each seeker has to realise
That he is destined
To be a traveller
To the ever-transcending Goal.

17609

O my stupid mind,
How long will you allow yourself
To live in constant fear
Of tomorrow?

17610

When a sincere seeker
Does not aspire,
He feels that he is drowning
In his mind's darkness-oblivion-sea.

17611

Each time I please myself
In my own way,
I enter into
My life's frustration-market.

17612

You are asking me
What I have done
With my mind's doubts.
To my extreme joy,
I have donated them
To the remote past.

17613

Each sincere heart-teardrop
Is a remedy
For the deplorable mistakes
Of the past.

17614

Today, not tomorrow,
I must sing the song
Of my heart's sky-climbing aspiration.

17615

When my heart's gratitude-bud
Blossomed,
Darkness-eyed doubt
Was illumined for good.

17616

O my ego,
Are you not embarrassed
That you are never invited
To enjoy the oneness-heart-feast?

17617

There are only two steps —
The self-giving heart-step
And the God-becoming life-step —
To reach the ultimate Goal.

17618

God-lovers and God-servers
Are the only ones
Who are entitled to become members
Of God's future-building team.

17619

Alas, will I ever be able
To fathom the depth
Of my emptiness-fulness-heart?

17620

Only a God-obedient
Happiness-heart
Changes and fulfils
Our lives.

17621

To me there is no difference
Between my mind's philosophy
And my life's thought-prison.

17622

Alas, is there nothing better
For me to do
Than waste my God-given life
Riding in world-criticism-train?

17623

The beauty of a sincerity-prayer
Is always welcomed
By the fragrance of God's Heart-Door.

17624

When we sincerely pray and meditate
We come to realise
That God's unconditional Love
Is proudly stamped
Upon our aspiration-heart
And dedication-life.

17625

It is through aspiration
That we transform
Earth-bound enemy-time
Into
Heaven-free friend-time.

17626

We suffer from one foolish malady,
And that malady is
Our God-search without
Instead of within.

17627

You may not cherish
God's Compassion-Eye,
But God cherishes each and every
Great gratitude-breath of yours.

17628

Alas, I do not want
To remain a stark fool.
I shall not allow my life-car
To be stalled anymore
By the teeming doubts
Of my mind.

17629

Because you have become
A genuine self-giver,
God is proudly and openly
Applauding you.

17630

God not only showed me
His Heart,
But also gave me
His Heart
When He first saw my heart's
Surrender-smile.

17631

I gave my Lord
My heart's self-perfection-flower.
God gave me
His Heart's Self-Satisfaction-Fragrance.

17632

My Lord,
Do give me the capacity
To claim You
To feed my heart's aspiration-flames.

17633

When God's Compassion-Love
Descends,
Even my imperfection-life
Gets the capacity
To ascend.

17634

There can be no gratitude-prayer
Which will remain unanswered
From Above.

17635

There are many boats
That sail fast, very fast,
But the heart's surrender-boat
Always sails the fastest.

17636

Beautiful
Is my heart's morning cry.
Fruitful
Is my life's evening smile.

17637

There was a time,
But not now,
When I was forced to surrender
My very existence
To ignorance-indulgence.

17638

Infinity's Power
Is housed
By the God-inspired silence-mind.

17639

If your life is founded
On faith,
Then God will claim your heart
As His favourite Home.

17640

Willingness-melodies
Are always haunting
Plus illumining.

17641

Neither God's Forgiveness
Nor even God's Compassion
Needs to ask us
To reciprocate His Love.

17642

No matter how hard you try,
If it is not God's Will,
You will not gain God's
Attention-Affection-Eye.

17643

Each smile of my soul
Is my Lord Supreme's
Chosen Homeland.

17644

The silence of the mind
Can and does
Break all thought-barriers.

17645

You must consciously and deliberately
Forget your own bondage-nights
To run towards
Your God-ordained destination.

17646

When self-discipline is lost,
You will lose your soul's
Illumination-breath.

17647

There is not a single soul on earth
Who can dare to say
That he has never been overwhelmed
By desire-illusion-frustrations.

17648

Our heart's oneness
Is indeed
A peace-bridge-builder.

17649

We must love
Not only beyond what we see,
But also beyond what we feel,
To sing the song
Of our own self-transcendence.

17650

The moment my heart
Appreciates a thing,
My mind immediately wants
To appropriate it.

17651

Alas, a most devastating shortage
Of faith
Is the order of the day.

17652

Alas, desire-dust
Has completely blinded
Your God-aspiration-eye.

17653

How can you succeed
If you allow doubt to brood
While offering your prayer-heart
To God?

17654

As every day
My life grows shorter,
Even so every day
My gratitude-heart becomes longer.

17655

Today's ego-emperor-smiles
Are tomorrow's
Ego-beggar-tears.

17656

The life of ego
And the breath of death
Are inseparable.

17657

Yours is a mind
Of countless contradictions
Because you do not
Daily feed your mind
With simplicity, sincerity
And purity.

17658

I do not think
There has been any time
In his life
When he has not suffered from
A tragic aspiration-shortage.

17659

Our minds suffer
Because our minds
Never take advice from
Our age-old wisdom-light-souls.

17660

May my heart's aspiration-cry
Always remain
A one-way sunlit road
Without any U-turn.

17661

I simply do not know
How my mind ever dares
To ask God
What He thinks about my mind.

17662

God will never stop carrying
Sweet hopes
In His Rainbow-Heart.

17663

God can bless me
With His Divinity's Smile
Only when I remain far beyond
My ego-confines.

17664

To be truly happy
I must live
Either inside my soul's silence-dreams
Or inside my heart's
God-fulfilling aspiration-reality.

17665

God, strike me hard!
If not,
I shall never try
To correct myself.

17666

An eagerness-heart
Can never be hostile
To changing and perfecting
Its life.

17667

Confront your doubting mind.
It is bound
To surrender to you.

17668

I do not allow my mind
To study
At loneliness-school.

17669

For a human being
The most important thing
Is to try every day
To gravitate towards
The Ultimate Truth.

17670

The small-minded
Are noisy
In the outer life
And clumsy
In the inner life.

17671

A divine promise
Is the pioneer
Of earth's newness-life.

17672

Fear hates.
Love forgives.
Wisdom forgets.

17673

His soul is unhappy
That it has employed
The unwilling mind
To manifest God.

17674

A heart-pilgrimage
Without devotion
Will definitely end
In utter frustration.

17675

My heart's surrender
Is the one-way bridge
To my golden tomorrow.

17676

Only a God-yearning cry
Can remove loneliness
From our minds
And insecurity from our hearts.

17677

You can travel
With sweet newness-fragrance
If you do not carry with you
Your thought-loaded mind.

17678

O my aspiration-heart,
Never surrender to your vital's
Unjust screams and shouts.

17679

The true message
Of all the spiritual Masters:
"Love only the Real in all:
God the Love."

17680

It is you and nobody else
Who can build
The funeral pyre
For your desire-life.

17681

There is no difference
Between my desire-capacity
And my smile-incapacity.

17682

Along the way
If you delay,
You may lose
Your God-desire-cry.

17683

In the morning
I reserve my heart
Only for God's Compassion.
And in the evening
I reserve my heart
Only for God's Forgiveness.

17684

God's Transcendental Eye
Is for my admiration.
God's Universal Heart
Is for my love.

17685

Your insecurity-heart
You can conquer
Only by being hospitable
To positive thoughts.

17686

God is always ready
To accept me.
But am I
Or will I ever be ready
To receive Him?

17687

God's Compassion-Feet
Are for only one thing:
To forgive me
And my life.

17688

God has a confidential message
For you:
He needs you,
He desperately needs you,
He always needs you.

17689

A God-seeker
Without devotion
Is on a fruitless God-search.

17690

Only a cheerful heart
Is entitled to receive
God's Compassion
In abundance.

17691

How can God fulfil you,
When your faith in Him
Is so fragile?

17692

Alas, my own mind's doubts
And my own mind's fears
Have rendered me completely helpless.

17693

My Lord,
Let me surprise You
By placing my frustration-mind
At Your Feet.

17694

The disappearance
Of the heart's happiness
Makes us feel how ruthless
The unhappiness of the mind
Can be.

17695

My Lord,
I may be able to live
Without Your Compassion-Eye,
But I shall never be able to live
Without Your Forgiveness-Heart.

17696

When God wants to give me something,
He just gives.
He does not care
Whether I deserve it or not.

17697

I love a really sincere
Atheist
More than a constantly false
Theist.

17698

May my aspiration-heart
See nothing but perfection
In everybody and in everything.

17699

There was a time
When my heart was crippled
By teeming fears.
But now my heart is singing
And dancing
With divinity's freedom-courage
To love and manifest my Absolute Lord.

17700

What I need is a heart
That embodies all hearts.
What I need is a soul
That reveals all souls.
What I need is a God
Who alone is everybody's All.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 177, Agni Press, 1993
