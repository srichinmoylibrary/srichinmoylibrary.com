337. God's treasures

Light, my Light,
What do you see?
"I see God's Transcendental Height."

Peace, my Peace,
What do you feel?
"I feel God's Universal Silence."

Bliss, my Bliss,
What do you distribute?
"I distribute the Life-Glory
Of God's Immortality."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
