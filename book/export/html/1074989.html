419. No and yes

No is not
A rejection
From a woman's heart.

Yes is not
An acceptance
Of a man's heart.

Cosmic Law is not
A rejection
From God's heart.

Forced Smile is not
The fulfilment
Of God's Promise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 9, Agni Press, 1973
