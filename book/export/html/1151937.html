Oh, My Journey's Half!
You and I are
the silver tears
Of a God-crying heart
And
the gold smiles
Of a God-serving life.

  1. ST 38500. This poem marks the halfway point in the Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees. Sri Chinmoy began this marathon literary journey on January 24th, 1998. In the first edition, the poem was reproduced on a pale blue page, in Sri Chinmoy's handwriting, with birds drawn all around it, and it was signed:
