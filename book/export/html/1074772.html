206. Don't worry!

   O Body, don't worry!
I have not yet
Begun to rest.   O Vital, don't worry!
I have not yet
Begun to fight.   O Mind, don't worry!
I have not yet
Begun to think.   O Heart, don't worry!
I have not yet
Begun to love.   O Soul, don't worry!
I have not yet
Begun to manifest.

My dear friends,
When the hour strikes
I shall play my roles
In your inspiration-flowers,
With your aspiration-flames,
And for your realisation-Sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 5, Agni Press, 1973
