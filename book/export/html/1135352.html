21932

To reach your goal is difficult.
  But to tell others
About reaching your goal
  Before you have,
And then to try to reach the goal,
  Is infinitely more difficult.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 22, Agni Press, 2001
