125. Just to see

Just to see
If my stupidity-friend was here on earth
I came all the way to earth
From Heaven.

Just to see
If my wisdom-friend is there in Heaven
I shall go all the way from earth
To Heaven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
