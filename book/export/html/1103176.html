15 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My heart-door is wide open.
Please do not stay outside like a dream,
But do come in and become a reality
In my life-breath.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 November 1998, 2:09 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
