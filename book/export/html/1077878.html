231. If you love me

If you love me,
Then why do you cry?
Am I not all-smile to you?

If you love the Supreme in me,
Then why do you sleep?
Has He not been knocking at your heart-door
All the time?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
