2. A beggar

Yesterday
I acted like a beggar
By asking God
To give me a smile.

Today
I am acting like a beggar
By asking God
To give me His Power.

Tomorrow
I shall be acting like a beggar
By asking God
To make me His only perfect instrument.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
