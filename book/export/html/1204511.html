18689

Before we accepted the spiritual life,
Our mind was the boss of our life.
Since the mind could not satisfy us,
We accepted the spiritual life
And got a new boss: the heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 187, Agni Press, 1993
