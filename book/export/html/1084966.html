22. I never want to forget

I never want to forget
How ruthlessly I have ignored God.

I never want to forget
How compassionately God has forgiven me,
  My darkness-pride
        And
My ignorance-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
