96

God was resolved that we all
Should go farther and farther,
Fly higher and higher
And dive deeper and deeper.
To our own great surprise,
We gave Him complete success.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A fourteen hour peace concert Immensity-Peace-Sea-Experiences, Agni Press, 1995
