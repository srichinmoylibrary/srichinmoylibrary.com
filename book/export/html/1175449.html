2501 Explore New Heights

Explore new heights,
Pursue new goals!
Now your heart
Is waving victory-banners,
Your mind
Is blowing victory-bugles,
Your eye
Is singing victory-songs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 26, Agni Press, 1982
