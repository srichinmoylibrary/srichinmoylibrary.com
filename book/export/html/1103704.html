30 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do make me Your most perfect
Satisfaction-Smile-stamp.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 August 1999, 8:02 a.m.

Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
