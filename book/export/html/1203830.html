18014

From the desiring ego
I have learned
How to cry.
From the surrendering ego
I have learned
Not only how to smile
But also how to proceed fast
Towards my goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 181, Agni Press, 1993
