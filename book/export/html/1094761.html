76 My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
You have made me
A mountain-success.
Now I pray to You to make me
A fountain-progress.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 13, Agni Press, 2002
