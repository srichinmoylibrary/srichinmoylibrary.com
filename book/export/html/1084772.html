32 What I Need...

WHAT I NEED

What I need
Is duty's body.
What I need
Is humility's vital.
What I need
Is serenity's mind.
What I need
Is purity's heart.
What I need
Is beauty's soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 16, Agni Press, 1974
