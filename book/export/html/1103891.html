18 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today my aspiring heart
Is compelling me to compose
   God-Victory-songs.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 October 1999, 9:22 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred and six times. (52 right, 54 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
