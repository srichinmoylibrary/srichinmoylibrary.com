839 Favours...

FAVOURS

Heaven,
I shall do you a favour.
I shall hide your deliberate indifference
Inside my mind-cave.
Now you do me a favour:
Place my heart-tears
Inside your eternally sailing
Dream-boat.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
