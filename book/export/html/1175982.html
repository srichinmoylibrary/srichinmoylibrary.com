3029 A Nagging Sense Of Emptiness

You are suffering
From a nagging sense of emptiness.
Do you know why?
Because you have renounced
The silence-moon and the sound-sun
Of your soul-bird.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 31, Agni Press, 1982
