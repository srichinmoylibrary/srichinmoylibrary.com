937 Absorb Good...

ABSORB GOOD

Absorb good.
Evil-prince will leave you.

Absorb good.
Frustration-hound will leave you.

Absorb good.
Failure-experience will leave you.

Absorb good.
Lo, God-realisation
Will dawn on you,
Your devoted heart
And
Your surrendered life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
