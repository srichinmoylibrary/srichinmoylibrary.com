My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 34

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Xiamen, China in December 2004.

20 December 2004

Xiamen, China

1 Anything That Is Human ...

Anything that is human
Is fleeting.

2 Anything That Is Divine ...

Anything that is divine
Is everlasting.

3 When There Is A Battle ...

When there is a battle
Between truth and falsehood,
Rest assured,
The ultimate victory
Goes to the truth.

4 Alas ...

Alas,
Unworthiness
Secretly and cleverly
Destroys our love of God.

5 We Open Our Mind-Door ...

We open our mind-door
To temptation-arrival.

6 We Open Our Heart-Home ...

We open our heart-home
To God’s Compassion-Arrival.

7 If God Is Not Sufficient ...

If God is not sufficient
In our life,
Who else can be?

8 God Never Wants ...

God never wants
To attract seekers
By performing miracles.

9 God Wants ...

God wants
To attract seekers
By singing the song
Of self-offering.

10 The Mind Doubts ...

The mind doubts
God’s Smiles.

11 The Heart Devours ...

The heart devours
God’s Smiles.

12 I Do Not Need ...

I do not need
A free world-wandering mind.

13 I Need ...

I need
A free world-loving heart.

14 The Mind Is Empty ...

The mind is empty
Of the river-beauty.

15 The Heart Is Full ...

The heart is full
Of the garden-fragrance.

16 A God-Gratitude-Heart ...

A God-gratitude-heart
Never wastes time in thinking
Of God-Satisfaction-reward.

17 The Hour Has Struck ...

The hour has struck
When self-transcendence-joy
Will meet with no obstruction.

18 God’s Living Presence ...

God’s living Presence
Means
Man’s ultimate transformation.

19 Those Who Look For God ...

Those who look for God
In a book
Will have to continue
Eternally.

20 Those Who Look For God ...

Those who look for God
In the heart,
God fails to hide
From them.

21 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I thrive on God’s
Compassion-Eye.

22 During The Day ...

During the day
I thrive on God’s
Encouragement-Heart.

23 At Night ...

At night
I thrive on God’s
Forgiveness-Feet.

24 Imagination Expedites ...

Imagination expedites
God’s Arrival
In our hearts.

25 My Lord ...

My Lord,
No matter where You throw me
Out of Your Life,
I always see
That I am deep inside Your Heart.

26 May My Aspiration-Heart-Sun ...

May my aspiration-heart-sun
Never set.

27 May My Disobedience-Mind-Clouds ...

May my disobedience-mind-clouds
Be dispersed
Sooner than at once.

28 Goodness ...

Goodness
Always surrounds
Our hearts.

29 The Love Of The Heart ...

The love of the heart
Is accepted
By the four corners
Of the globe.

30 Not Imperfection ...

Not imperfection
But perfection
Hides deep within us.

31 God Does Not Want ...

God does not want
To capture us
With His temptation-snare.

32 God Wants ...

God wants
To capture us
With His Eye’s Compassion-Care.

33 O My Mind ...

O my mind,
Why do you try
To find fault with others?
You will gain nothing,
Absolutely nothing.

34 My Heart ...

My heart
Has gained everything
Precisely because of
Its cheerful oneness with all.

35 I Cannot Imagine ...

I cannot imagine
How God still has hopes
For me!

36 My Prayers ...

My prayers
Cry and cry
To see God’s Face.

37 My Meditations ...

My meditations
Dive and dive
To feel God’s Heart
And become one with God.

38 Mind-Confusion ...

Mind-confusion
Destroys
Our purity-hearts.

39 Complexity And The Mind ...

Complexity and the mind
Never want to remain alone.

40 We Search For Everything ...

We search for everything
But not for our spirit —
The only Real in us.

41 Hope Tells Us ...

Hope tells us
To look upwards.

42 Faith Tells Us ...

Faith tells us
To look all around.

43 Promise Tells Us ...

Promise tells us
To look up first
And then look down
For our God-manifestation.

44 My Lord’s Light ...

My Lord’s Light
Is repairing my mind, vital
And body,
Which are all out of order.

45 Silence-Light ...

Silence-light
Is everywhere,
All-pervading.

46 In The Morning ...

In the morning
I hear my Lord’s
Budding Whispers.

47 During The Day ...

During the day
I hear my Lord’s
Blossoming Whispers.

48 At Night ...

At night
I fulfil my Lord’s
Budding, blossoming
And blossomed Whispers.
I become absolutely one
With my Lord’s Will.

49 When I Choose My Soul ...

When I choose my soul,
Only my soul,
My desire-life ceases.

50 When I Choose My Heart ...

When I choose my heart,
Only my heart,
I feel my Lord
Is next door.

51 When I Choose My Mind ...

When I choose my mind,
Only my mind,
I immediately become blind.
Darkness I feel all-where.

52 My Aspiring Realities ...

My aspiring realities
Originated
In the Heart of my Lord Supreme.

53 My Desiring Realities ...

My desiring realities
Originated
Inside my mind.

54 When I Run Towards My Lord ...

When I run towards my Lord
For protection,
I give my Lord’s Heart
Immense joy.

55 God Most Affectionately ...

God most affectionately
Embraces
All our positive thoughts.

56 God Gives ...

God gives
A Thunder-Kick
To our negative thoughts.

57 I Am ...

I am
A devoted partner
Of my soul-fragrance.

58 I Am ...

I am
An affectionate partner
Of my heart-beauty.

59 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I am praying to You
To give me a clear path
And an easy way to go forward.

60 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
Dive inward
Bravely.

61 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
Go forward
Self-givingly.

62 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
Go upward
Eagerly.

63 The Unruly Mind ...

The unruly mind
Is apt to destroy
My heart-blossoms.

64 Be Faithful ...

Be faithful
When you feel
That God is not near you.

65 Be Soulful ...

Be soulful
When God
Is right in front of you.

66 Be Prayerful ...

Be prayerful
When you feel
That God is right inside you.

67 If Anything Is Destined To Succeed ...

If anything is destined to succeed
In my life,
Then it is my God-faith.

68 Failure Is Quite Normal ...

Failure is quite normal
And natural.
Why do we then
Pay so much attention
To failure?

69 Every Day God Gives Us ...

Every day God gives us
Ample opportunity and capacity
To unearth our divinity’s life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 34, Agni Press, 2005
