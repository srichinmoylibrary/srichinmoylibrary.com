150 God Will Never Allow ...

God will never allow
Our God-hunger-heart
To starve.
God will definitely feed
Our God-hunger-heart sumptuously
At His choice Hour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 7, Agni Press, 2002
