35. My silence

Soul, my soul,
You are my sunlit silence.

Heart, my heart,
You are my half-lit silence.

Mind, my mind,
You are my unlit silence.

Life, my life,
You are my totally unconscious silence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
