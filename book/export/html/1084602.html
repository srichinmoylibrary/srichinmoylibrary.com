15. I shall maintain my love

With humanity's cry
I shall maintain
My inseparable love.
With divinity's smile
I shall maintain
My undivided love.
With God's Dream
I shall maintain
My constant love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
