May 5

Death was born when I was born. And it will die when I die. But I will leave my actions' deathless fruit to the earth. Blind death, beggar death will never have a just claim upon the gratitude of the earth. I will be deified and death will be defied by the same wise earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flame-Goal, Agni Press, 1973
