916. Who is he?

Day: what is it?
Day is my hopeful life.

Night: what is it?
Night is my peaceful life.

Man: who is he?
He is my stranger-friend.

God: who is He?
He is my soul's only choice,
He is my Goal's only voice.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
