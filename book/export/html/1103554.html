5 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I must devotedly practise
More and more and more and more.
Only then shall I be able
To accomplish my goals.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

5 July 1999, 8:43 a.m. Before lifting 350 lbs. fifty-six times with one arm. (27 right, 29 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
