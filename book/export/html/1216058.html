430. God and I meet together

   In the morning
God and I meet together
   To appreciate our beauty's birth.   In the evening
God and I meet together
   To admire our duty's end.   At night
God and I meet together
   To immortalise our necessity's life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
