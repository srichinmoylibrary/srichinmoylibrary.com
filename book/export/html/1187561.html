1906

My Lord Supreme,
Whom in this world
Should I obey?

"My sweet child,
Obey only the one who shows you
All kindness, all compassion
And all forgiveness.
Only he deserves
Your constant obedience."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 20, Agni Press, 1983
