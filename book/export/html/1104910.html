62.

Alas, I cannot remember
The last time when
My heart most sincerely,
Most soulfully and most self-givingly
Said to God,
“My Lord Beloved Supreme,
I love You only,
And I need You only.”

11 October 2003
New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 2004
