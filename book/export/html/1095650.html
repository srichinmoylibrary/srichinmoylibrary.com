71 As Long As My Lord Supreme ...

As long as my Lord Supreme
Knows and sees
That my heart-cries are sincere,
I do not have to prove anything
To the world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 23, Agni Press, 2004
