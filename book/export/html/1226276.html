Inspiration Takes Me to God

Inspiration takes me to
God the Length.
Aspiration takes me to
God the Height.
Realisation takes me to
God the Omnipotent.
Manifestation takes me to God —
Where He dances
With earth’s penury
And with Heaven’s Eldorado.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 6, , 1981
