641 Hoisting The Banner...

HOISTING THE BANNER

When I hoist
The red banner of power,
I am great.

When I hoist
The green banner of life,
I am great.

And when I hoist
The blue-white-gold
Banner of love,
I am vast,
I am divine,
I am perfect.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
