June 15

Human love says to God: "Father, I can live neither with you nor without you." Divine love says to God: "Father, if you live, I shall live for you. If I live, you must not take the trouble of living for me."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
