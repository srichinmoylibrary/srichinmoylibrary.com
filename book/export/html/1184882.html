554. The world will need you

Stand and wait;
The world will ignore you.
Walk and run;
The world will watch you.
Love and serve;
The world will admire you.
Pray and meditate;
The world will need you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
