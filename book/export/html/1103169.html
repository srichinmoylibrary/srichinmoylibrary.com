7 November 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May my life-boat ply
Between my singleness-mind
And
My oneness-heart
Every day.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 November 1998, 4:03 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs with each arm four times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
