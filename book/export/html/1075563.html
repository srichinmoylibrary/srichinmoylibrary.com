982. My Lord, forgive me

My Lord,
Forgive me.
Forgive my body.

My Lord,
Control me.
Control my vital.

My Lord,
Warn me.
Warn my mind.

My Lord,
Illumine me,
Illumine my heart.

My Lord,
Fulfil me,
Fulfil my soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 20, Agni Press, 1973
