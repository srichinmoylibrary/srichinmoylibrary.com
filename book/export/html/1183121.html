36 A fool and a rogue

He was such a fool.
Not saying a word to Father-Heaven
He left for Mother-Earth.

He is such a rogue.
Not saying a word to Mother-Earth
He is leaving for Father-Heaven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, This is God's Home, Agni Press, 1975
