15. The hour

The hour came
   When God blessed me
        And sent me down to earth.

The hour has come
   When I have to tell the world
        What the world is,
            And what the world will be.

What is the world?
   A self-giving seed.
What will the world be?
   A God-fulfilling tree.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 17, Agni Press, 1974
