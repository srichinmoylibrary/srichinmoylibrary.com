3807 God Will Deny You

Be not afraid of inner obedience
And outer obedience.
If you are afraid
Of inner obedience,
God will deny you
His Satisfaction-Heart.
If you are afraid
Of outer obedience,
God will deny you
His Protection-Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 39, Agni Press, 1982
