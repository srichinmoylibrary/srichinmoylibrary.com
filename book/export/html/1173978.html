1045 The City Of &quot;God And Yes&quot;

There was a time
When he lived
In the city of "man and yes."
There was a time
When he lived
In the city of "God and yes."
Alas, now he lives
In the city of "man and no"
And in the city of "God and no."
Alas, now his "I-ness" reigns supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 11, Agni Press, 1981
