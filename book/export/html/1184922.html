594. Justice and compassion

Justice is not the opposite of Compassion;
Compassion is not the opposite of justice.
Justice feels justified in perfecting imperfection.
Compassion feels justified in giving
A new chance,
A new hope,
A new dawn,
To the culprit in weakness-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
