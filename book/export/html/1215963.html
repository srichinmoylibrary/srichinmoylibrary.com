337. The transcendence

His eyes are the depths
Of the Life Divine.

His mind is the promise
Of the Life Divine.

His heart is the assurance
Of the Life Divine.

His soul is the transcendence
Of the Life Divine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 7, Agni Press, 1974
