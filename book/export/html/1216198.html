567. That's all

Lord, I just don't have the time
To think of You.
That's all.

"Daughter, I just think of you
Unreservedly.
That's all."

Lord, I just forget to love You
From time to time.
That's all.

"Daughter, I just happen to know
How to forgive you,
How to love you,
How to illumine you.
That's all."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 12, Agni Press, 1974
