705 I Need You...

I NEED YOU

O deathless Beauty's Core,
I need you.
Oh, I need you.

O sleepless Divinity's Door,
I need you.
Oh, I need you.

O blue Infinity's Shore,
I need you.
Oh, I need you.

O red Eternity's Roar,
I need you.
Oh, I need you.

O green Immortality's Life,
I need you.
Oh, I need you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
