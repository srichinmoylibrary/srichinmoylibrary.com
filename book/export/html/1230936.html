Kukur Atmatyage Nitya Tumi

Kukur kukur kukur kukur kukur kukur
Atmatyage nitya tumi baroi bharapur
Prabhur jiban rakhta karite nijere karuk dan
Tomar tyager nai je tulana prabhu anugata pran

Translation:

In self-offering you live,
Full to the brim.
To save your Master you are always ready
To give your own life.
No comparison between your sacrifice
And the sacrifice of others.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sundaratama dekha dilo aji, part 2, , 2006
