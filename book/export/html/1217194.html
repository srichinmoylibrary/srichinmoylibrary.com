19. You know one thing

   You know one thing:
Your human love
   Fails to grasp anything.   You will know something else:
Your divine love
   Will far exceed
        All earth-Heaven grasp.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Yesterday I was a crying dream, Agni Press, 1975
