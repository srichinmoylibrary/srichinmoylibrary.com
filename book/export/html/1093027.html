My aspiration-heart cycles, part 2

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers each time before he started to exercise on his stationary bicycle.

1.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
I am my doubtful mind.
God is God's tearful Heart.

1 January 2000

8:00 p.m.

Curitiba, Brazil

2.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
I wish to be the golden dust
Of Your Feet,
But You want me to be
The Himalayan pride
Of Your Heart.

2 January 2000

5:44 a.m.

Curitiba, Brazil

3.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The deeper our heart-roots,
The stronger our love of God.

3 January 2000

7:05 a.m.

Curitiba, Brazil

4.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
Each happy moment
In my inner life
Builds a new castle-glory
In my outer life.

5 January 2000

10:21 a.m.

Curitiba, Brazil

5.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
Every day at sunrise
I shall aim at
A new horizon.

7 January 2000

7:59 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

6.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The desire-days of my mind
Are invariably followed by
The worry-nights of my heart.

8 January 2000

8:13 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

7.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The strength of the mind
Fails to disarm
The hope-power of the heart.

9 January 2000

8:07 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

8.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
This morning You came down
To awaken me
To Your Infinity's Silence.

10 January 2000

6:26 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

9.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
I have tamed my outer life
To remain still.
I have trained my inner heart
To enjoy the thrill.

13 January 2000

6:35 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

10.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
My forgiveness-smile
Is the golden vastness
Of my heart-sky.

13 January 2000

6:35 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

11.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The tears of my heart
And
The smiles of my soul
Every day play hide-and-seek
To entertain You.

14 January 2000

7:43 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

12.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
Whenever angels see
An unconditionally
God-surrendered seeker,
They immediately start trumpeting
God's supreme Victory.

15 January 2000

9:56 a.m.

Foz do Iguau, Brazil

13.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
Man-made friendship
Is a foolish hardship.
God-made friendship
Is a rainbow boat trip.

Asuncin, Paraguay

My Absolute Lord Supreme!

To use His iron rod
So that my mind can fly
In His boundless Sky.

18 January 2000

6:21 a.m.

Asuncin, Paraguay

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
I must develop
Intense homesickness
For my Beloved Supreme.

19 January 2000

8:15 a.m.

Asuncin, Paraguay

I have only one aspiration.
I have only one aim.
I have only one goal.
I wish to become
My Master's choicest slave.

20 January 2000

5:24 a.m.

Asuncin, Paraguay

17.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
Impossible dreams are afraid of
My sleepless and breathless
Faith in God.

21 January 2000

6:18 a.m.

Asuncin, Paraguay

18.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
May my heart dance and dance
With new hope-promise-blossoms
Every morning.

23 January 2000

6:46 a.m.

Asuncin, Paraguay

My Absolute Lord Supreme!

Hide inside my mind-jungle,
Only my God the Father
And God the Mother
Searches for me
Full of worries
And full of anxieties.

26 January 2000

Brasilia, Brazil

20.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
God says to my doubting mind
And my strangling vital,
"No more freedom!
No more freedom!"

27 January 2000

8:38 a.m.

Brasilia, Brazil

21.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
May my early morning prayer
Be fragrant with
My God-gratitude-heart-tears.

28 January 2000

Brasilia, Brazil

My Absolute Lord Supreme!

The beauty of my heart
And
The fragrance of my soul.

New York

"My child, I am just wiping
The tears from your face.
I am just removing
The thorns from your eyes."

Eternity's Love.

14 February 2000

9:25 a.m.

New York

30.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
When I look at the world,
I am all sadness.
When I look at myself,
I am all madness.
My Supreme,
Save me from my sadness,
Save me from my madness!

"My child, My child,
Throw your sadness into My Heart,
Throw your madness into My Feet.
My Heart and My Feet
Will take care of your sadness
And madness.
My child, do not forget
That I depend on you,
I depend on you."

My Supreme, my Lord, my Beloved,
My Absolute Supreme,
Do accept the sea
Of my gratitude-heart-tears.

16 February 2000

8:54 a.m.

New York

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
My mind is a member
Of the God-seeker committee.
My heart is a member
Of the God-lover committee.
My life is a member
Of the God-server committee.

New York

To my God-loving
