12819

I may not know
Where my mind of simplicity is,
But I do know
Where my heart of sincerity eternally is.
It is inside the Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 129, Agni Press, 1990
