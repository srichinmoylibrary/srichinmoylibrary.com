10761

The ancient question is:
Can God ever be seen?
The modern answer is:
God can be seen
And played with,
But He is not loved.
Perhaps He does not have to be loved.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 108, Agni Press, 1987
