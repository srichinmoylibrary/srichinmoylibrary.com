Devotion

Devotion
     Is the cry
           That compels God
To seize the aspirant
With the very madness of Love.

A piece of candy,
     Lo, the child is won.
An iota of devotion,
God, the true and real God
Is captured.

Sweeter than honey,
     Purer than flowers.
           Deeper than love,
The flame-waves of devotion-light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, AUM -- Vol. 2, No.10,11, May -- June 27, 1967, Boro Park Printers -- Brooklyn, N. Y, 1967
