19604

Do not look into the past
For solutions to your problems.
Do not look into the future
For solutions to your problems.
But look inside the aspiration-flames
Of your today's heart.
All solutions are easily attainable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 197, Agni Press, 1994
