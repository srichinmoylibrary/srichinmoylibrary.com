30 June 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I need only two things
In this life:
I need the Beauty
Of Your Compassion-Eye;
I need the Fragrance
Of Your Forgiveness-Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 June 1999, 9:04 a.m. Before lifting 350 lbs. with one arm.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 6, Agni Press, 1999
