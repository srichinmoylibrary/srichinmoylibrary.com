268. Do not give up

Do not give up.
Tomorrow you will pluck
Love-stars from above.
You will become within, God-dove.
You will be the connection-tie
Between humanity's sigh
And divinity's sky.

From: Sri Chinmoy, From the Source to the Source, Agni Press, 1974
