September 5

My mind tells me that my God seldom thinks of me. My heart tells me that my God often thinks of me. My soul tells me that my God always thinks of me. I tell my soul to forgive my ignorance. I tell my heart that we must forgive our joint ignorance. I tell my mind that I have the capacity to forgive its ignorance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
