8. Trophies and smiles

I love the trophies
Of a world-conqueror.
They show me
What God the Beggar
Needs and gets.
I love the smiles
Of a world-lover.
They show me
What God the Emperor
Gives and is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 6, Agni Press, 1974
