3561 At The Mercy Of Time

Enthusiasm I had.
Alas, time has cleverly cooled
My enthusiasm.
Determination I had.
Alas, time has secretly stolen it away.
And now where am l?
I am at the merciless mercy of time.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 36, Agni Press, 1982
