17056

My soul's progress-report to God
About my mind
Has completely shattered its pride.

My soul's progress-report to God
About my heart
Has thrown my heart
Into a sea of ecstasy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 171, Agni Press, 1992
