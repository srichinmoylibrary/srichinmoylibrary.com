565 First Things First....

First things first.
You do not have to know
The secret of peace.
You just have to know
The secret of self-transcendence.
Your self-transcendence
Will surround your entire being
With peace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Peace-Blossom-Fragrance, part 6, Agni Press, 1994
