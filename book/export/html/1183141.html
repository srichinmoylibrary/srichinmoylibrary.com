5. What are they

What is earth,
If not the splendour of sacrifice-might?
What is Heaven,
If not the splendour of vision-light?
What is humanity,
If not the splendour of expectation-soul?
What is divinity,
If not the splendour of ecstasy-sea?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
