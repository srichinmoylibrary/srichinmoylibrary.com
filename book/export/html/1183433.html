297. You pray to God

You pray to God
In a time of necessity.
You do not want to learn
The lessons offered
By regularity and punctuality.
I tell you what regularity
Can and will do for you:
Regularity will show you
Your victory's crown
And punctuality will crown you,
Your achievement's glory.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
