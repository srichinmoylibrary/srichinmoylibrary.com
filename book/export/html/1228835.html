Bhuta Nata Bhava Mahadev Shiva Bibhola

Bhuta nata bhava Mahadev Shiva bibhola
Niye jao aj maner asim jamela

Translation:

O Lord of the ghosts,
O Lord Shiva, self-entranced!
Do take away from my mind
My endless problems.

From: Sri Chinmoy, One thousand lotus petals, part 1, , 2000
