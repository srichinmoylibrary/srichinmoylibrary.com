13.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

I know, I know
That ignorance-night
Is bound to betray us,
Sooner or later.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

Songs:
1. Dibya premik hate chahi aji
2. Dipti kothai shanti kothai
The Eternal Beginner (Blue violin): He dharani pal he dharani pal

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 18, Agni Press, 2005
