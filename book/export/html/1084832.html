41. Not wise enough

I am wise enough
To tell the world
That God is my might.

I am not wise enough
To feel that what I do
Is nothing but a constant manifestation
Of God-Light
   In me,
        For me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 17, Agni Press, 1974
