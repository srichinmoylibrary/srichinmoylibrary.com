4. I am happy

My sweet Lord,
  I am happy
           Because
     I am always in Your world.
  I am happier
           Because
     You are always in charge of my life.
  I am happiest
           Because
     I need You only.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
