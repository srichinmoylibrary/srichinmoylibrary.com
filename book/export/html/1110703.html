Part II

RF 117-127. On 6 September 1999, Sri Chinmoy invited disciples who had been on his path for under two years to offer him a spiritual word, on which he then elaborated.

Surrender

There are many roads that lead to God-realisation, but the surrender-road is the shortest and, at the same time, the safest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Rainbow-Flowers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
