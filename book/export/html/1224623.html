Theke Maner Gopan Dale Pathar Antar

Theke maner gopan dale
Pathar antar ale diner viday kale
Keno pakhi dake
Khunji theche jena kake
Priya jantar ashe ni phire
Tai bujhi bhase nayana nire
Amar apan kothai ekhan
Kothai jhariye kon maya jale
Pakhir matan keno nahi kandi
Syandha kale

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
