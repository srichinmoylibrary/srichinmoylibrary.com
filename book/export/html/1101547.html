14. We touch

Lord, I touch You
Only when I feel that You are proud of me.
Lord, You touch me always,
Even when You are not
Proud of me at all.
Lord, Your kindness
Fascinates my heart
And
Baffles my mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My life-tree, Agni Press, 1975
