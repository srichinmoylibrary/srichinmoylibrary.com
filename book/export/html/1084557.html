21. My evening song

My morning song was:
How far to go?
Far have I to go!
My afternoon song was:
Can I reach my Goal?
I can and I shall
Reach my Goal!
My evening song is:
Have I reached my Goal?
I have not only reached my Goal
But
Far transcended my Goal!
I desired to touch His Feet
But
My Lord has made me feel His Heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 12, Agni Press, 1974
