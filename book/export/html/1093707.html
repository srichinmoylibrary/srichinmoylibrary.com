42 My Lord, Please, Please Devour...

My Lord, please, please devour
Everything that I have
And everything that I am.
Be pleased to start with
My abominable pride.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 3, Agni Press, 2000
