The moment I please God in God's own Way

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God gives my eyes
The Beauty of His Eye
And gives my heart
The Fragrance of His Heart.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He makes my life
His Heart's own
Ambrosial Affection.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He takes me as an urgent need
Before He does anything.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My life becomes
An immortal protection-cradle.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My heart becomes
A flaming and climbing
Aspiration-flower-blossom.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
The purity-goddess
Starts living permanently
Inside my heart-temple.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
Each thought of mine
Grows into
An abiding affinity
With all the human beings
Of the world.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God compels me to forget
All my human foibles
And frailties.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
A failure-life becomes
A total impossibility.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He blesses me
With His transcendental
Vision-Light-Award.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
The doubt-deluge of the entire world
Does not dare approach
My life's progress-harvest.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He becomes my heart
And I become His Heartbeat.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
The first thing He does
Is to illumine
Bright, brighter, brightest
The dark alley of my mind.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He and I immediately become
Two mutually enthusiastic
Oneness-offerings.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I feel inspired from deep within
To write God every morning
A gratitude-heart-letter.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He makes me an excellent player
In His Cosmic Game.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He commands me
Not to ever ride
My mind's ego-horse.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I see nowhere
Temptation-snares.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child, happily and proudly
I shall become
The slave of your slave."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, I am burying
All your past
Evil deeds
And hurrying your future
Divine deeds."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child,
I have conquered your head;
You have conquered My Crown."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, you have given Me
What you presently have.
I am giving you
What I eternally am."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child,
Now that you have pleased Me,
Ask Me for a boon.
I shall immediately fulfil you."

My Lord,
Please give me the capacity
To love You infinitely more
Than You love me.
"My child, ask for another boon.
How can I allow you to steal
My own most precious desire?"

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child,
You have played your first role
As My lover most successfully.
Now I want you to succeed
In exactly the same way
As My Eternity's partner."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, I am placing
The animal in you
Before My Eye,
The human in you
Inside My Heart,
The divine in you
At My Feet."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, as it is true
That I am your shrine,
Even so it is equally true
That you are My temple."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child,
From today on I shall be
Your dearer than the dearest
And better than the best Messenger."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, you and I
Have cried for each other
For a very, very long time.
Now let us start smiling
At each other
Throughout Eternity."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He whispers
Enlightenment
And I whisper
Astonishment.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I remain far beyond the reach
Of the random arrows
Of hostile fate.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
Both my Mother Earth
And my Father Heaven
Become extremely proud of me.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My God-loving, God-serving
And God-manifesting capacities
Become the pride
Of God's Infinity.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My mind does not remain
Any more
An avalanche of impurity.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God welcomes me to study
At His Perfect Perfection-Satisfaction-School.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me
That from now on
I shall have to follow only
A one-way road
To my Destination.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God asks me
If I am sincerely interested
In His manifestation on earth.
If so, He will bless me
With a two-way road
To my Destination.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He asks me to drink deep,
Very deep
His Nectar-flooded Will.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I see nowhere
My mind's
God-unwillingness-confines.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He asks me to sprint
For His world-astonishing
And world-fulfilling
Peace award.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, stop watching Me.
Start participating
In My Cosmic Lila (Play)."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child, now is the time
For you to spread
The transcendental glories
Of My Vision-Light
Upon My creation."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God puts my life's desire-night
To eternal sleep.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God awakens my heart's
Aspiration-days
To play with
His Eternity's Silence
And
His Infinity's Sound.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God says to me,
"My child,
Claim My Immortality's
Ever-transcending Smile.
It is all yours."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I clearly see
My very earth-existence-life
Submerged
In an ocean of bliss.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God asks me to row
And He provides me with the oars
To arrive at the Golden Shore
Of the Beyond.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God puts an end
To my life's endless rounds
Of mistakes.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God asks my heart-quality
And my life-quantity
To smile at each other.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My self-glory-desire
Surrenders
To my self-discovery-aspiration.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I become
My gratitude-heart-presentation
And God becomes
His Infinitude-Heart-Compassion.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child, I am sure,
Like Me,
You will never take
A leave of absence."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My Lord promises me
That every morning
He will take a pleasure-walk
In my heart-garden.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God teaches me the supreme art
Of tuning my heart
To His universal Heart.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I am shocked to see God
Taking me as another God.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God immediately wants me
To enjoy
His thunderous applause.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God opens the door
Of His Eternity's Silence.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My heart-sky starts ringing
With the music of angels.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me that from now on
I will be able to touch
His Feet
At any moment I want to.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me to draw in
The fragrance
Of His Eternity's Breath.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God blesses me
With the blissful waves
Of His Touch.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God starts defending me
Against both earth and Heaven's
Accusations.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I become the 'News of the Day'
Both in Heaven and on earth.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My heart throbs
With devotion-tears
And God's Heart throbs
With Pride-Smiles.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My life becomes
A fully blossomed lotus-heart
For God-worship.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My heart becomes
A sleepless aspiration-boat
And my life becomes
A breathless dedication-train.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He immediately asks me
To decorate
His Heart-Home
In my own way.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I tell Him, "My Lord,
I do not need
Even Your infinite Delight.
I need only
Your constant Command."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I devour the dust
Of God's
Sweetness-Oneness-Fulness-Will.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God gives me the capacity
To fulfil Him
At every moment
On a very colossal scale.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He tells me to become
An everlasting contribution
To His Mission.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I compel the long expedition
Of ignorance-night
To halt.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
My heart-gratitude-smiles
Thrill God's Heart
Far more
Than I can ever imagine.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I revel in God's
Peaceful Heart
And
Blissful Embrace.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
He tells me that
Each heartbeat of mine
Has to be His own
Manifestation-life's watchword.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I dance in the core
Of God's
Compassion-flooded Fondness.

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child, from now on
You will be
The doctor of your mind."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child,
I am inhaling
Your life-tears.
You inhale
My Heart-Smiles."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child,
Start immediately
Energising yourself
With self-mastery
And God-discovery."

The Moment ...

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
I come to realise that
Heaven-Beauty is God's Eye

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,

I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child, no more rest!
Rest is a luxury

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child, from now on
Yours will be a life of heart-living

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child, from now on
Watch and listen to your heart-sky.

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child, from now on

The moment
I please God
In God's own Way,
God tells me,
"My child,
Although life has dealt you

Infinity's Joy

God immediately quickens
