15779

God has a very special Concern
For the newly-arrived souls.
He does not want them to mix freely
With the old souls,
Who have surrendered to
ignorance-night.
He wants these newly-arrived souls
To fight right from the beginning
Against ignorance-night
And establish His supreme Victory
Here on earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 158, Agni Press, 1991
