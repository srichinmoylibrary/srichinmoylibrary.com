6562 Do Spare Me From Name And Fame

My Lord Supreme,
Do spare me from name and fame.
My heart consciously, soulfully
And devotedly
Longs to play only with You
In Your Cosmic Game.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 66, Agni Press, 1983
