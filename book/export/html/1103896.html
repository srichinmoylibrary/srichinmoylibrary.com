19 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God the Mother always worries
   About me and my safety.
God the Father always tells me,
"Hurry up, My child, hurry up!
You and I must start walking
   Along Eternity's Road."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 October 1999, 8:29 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
