295. Do not slay yourself

Do not slay yourself.
Do not kill yourself.
Your Lord will not only save you
But illumine and immortalise you.

Do not slay yourself.
Do not kill yourself.
Your Lord has given you your life.
Your body and soul are of His own Silence-Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
