24. Two unanswered questions

I asked God
Two questions.
Strangely enough,
He could not answer
Even one.
My questions were:
How and why
Am I inferior to Him?
How does He know
That I cannot perfect Him?
My Lord of Compassion
Proved that He can be
The Lord of Silence, too.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 6, Agni Press, 1974
