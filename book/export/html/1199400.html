13628

As our life-book
Does not start with frustration,
Even so,
It does not end with frustration.
As our life-book
Starts with aspiration,
Even so,
It will continue with aspiration.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 137, Agni Press, 1990
