31. What can God do?

A thousand lives
And no aspiration!
   What can God do?

A thousand bodies
And no service!
   What can God do?

A thousand souls
And no promise!
   What can God do?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 11, Agni Press, 1974
