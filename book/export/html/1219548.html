Sonar Harin

Sonar harin hiyar harin
      Tibra tomar gati
Ashar pradip jwalai prane
      Premer unnati

Translation:

O golden deer, O psychic deer
      Of my heart,
Your fast, faster, fastest speed
Kindles the hope-lamp
      In my progress-love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bela chale jai, , 1979
