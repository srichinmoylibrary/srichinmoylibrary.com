42. Ages in his face

When I saw
Age in his face
I cried and cried
     Because
It was I who was responsible
For the age in his face.

When I saw
Ages in his face
I danced and danced
     Because
It was I who had helped him
And made him the possessor of ages.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, receive this little undying cry, Agni Press, 1975
