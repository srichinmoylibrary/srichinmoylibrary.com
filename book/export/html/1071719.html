January 29

Since God does not want to enjoy His Transcendental Delight always and everywhere, He has graciously granted liberation to some souls so that they can enjoy His Transcendental Delight. Rest assured, God will grant liberation to many more today and tomorrow to the rest of mankind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
