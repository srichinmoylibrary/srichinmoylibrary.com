November 9

When I think God is mine, temptation may torture me and frustration may devour me. When I think I am God's, aspiration awakens me, realisation liberates me and revelation fulfils me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
