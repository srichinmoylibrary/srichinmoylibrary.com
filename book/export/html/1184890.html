562. All gates to Heaven are open

He is an honest mind.
He is a pure heart.
He is an illumining soul.
All gates to Heaven
Are wide open for him.
Sleepless he will sit
At the Feet of the Absolute Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
