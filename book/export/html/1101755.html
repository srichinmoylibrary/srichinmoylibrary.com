50.

My Lord,
You have sent for me
And I am now here.
"But, My child,
I have asked your whole family
To come.
You and your body only are here.
Where is the rest of your family-
Vital, mind and heart?
How is it
That you have come to Me
Leaving them behind?
Alas, I wanted to take
Your whole family
Together with Me
To My new Home:
Satisfaction."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 1, Agni Press, 1998
