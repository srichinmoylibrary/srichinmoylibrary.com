137. Look at Her

Look at Her Feet,
It is all salvation.
Look at Her Eyes,
It is all illumination.
Look at Her Heart,
It is all perfection.
Look at Her Soul,
It is all satisfaction,
God-satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
