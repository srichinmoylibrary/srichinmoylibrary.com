E Jagate Keu Habena

E jagate keu habena
     Jakhan apan
Ashar byetha kena kari
     Kebal srijan

Translation:

When I know perfectly well
That there can be
Nobody on earth
Whom I can call my own,
Very own,
Why do I then
Create pangs,
Feeding teeming hopes?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 1, , 1978
