12.

My Lord,
What do You think of palmistry?
"My child,
My Heart
Is infinitely more beautiful
Than My Palm.
Therefore, I want to spend
All My Life
Appreciating, admiring, adoring
And loving My Heart."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 3, Agni Press, 1998
