2104 His Mind's Astonishment

Before he realised God
He was his mind's
Fearful and doubtful
Astonishment.

After he realised God
He became his heart's
Cheerful and soulful
Astonishment.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 22, Agni Press, 1981
