Questions and answers on the soul Part I

  1. Recorded by Mrs. Barbara Callen, New York City↩

  2. These questions were presented in writing to Sri Chinmoy by Mrs. Barbara Callen. A few days later, on January 4, 1967, the Guru answered them orally during an interview with her. Mrs. Callen recorded Sri Chinmoy's exact words, taking everything down in shorthand. With some minor revisions, we are printing the interview as it was recorded.↩
