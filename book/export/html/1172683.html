Sympathy

1.

Sympathy is the heart's oneness-cry.

2.

Sympathy is the beauty of a sweetness-smile.

3.

Sympathy is the fragrance of a fullness-love.

4.

Sympathy is the fresh happiness-dawn-bringer to humanity.

5.

Sympathy is the manifestation of oneness-wisdom.

6.

Sympathy is infinitely stronger and sweeter than kindness.

7.

Sympathy is the perfect revelation of divinity.

8.

Sympathy is Heaven's sunshine.

9.

Sympathy is by far the best in drying up others' tears.

10.

Sympathy is sorrow's unparalleled friend.

11.

Sympathy is the nectar-delight of a bleeding heart.

12.

Sympathy's inner name is sacrifice.

13.

Sympathy is the illumining light of compassion-tears.

14.

Sympathy is a fountain-heart of concern.

15.

Sympathy is the spontaneous comfort-giver to the heart, mind, vital and body.

16.

Sympathy is the oneness-lover of tears.

17.

Sympathy does not think. It acts. It acts to remove the ceaseless sufferings of the world.

18.

Sympathy is the joy of self-giving strength.

19.

A heart of sympathy knows no division. It only knows union.

20.

Sympathy is an unconditional hope-bestower.

21.

Sympathy quenches the thirst of hope.

22.

Sympathy most successfully consoles the streaming tears.

23.

Sympathy arrives. Sorrow disappears.

24.

A heart of sympathy is a very special pride of God.

25.

A life of sympathy-sea has no equal.

26.

Sympathy brings down Heaven into our bleeding heart.

27.

Sympathy is the greatest of all virtues.

28.

Sympathy gives immediate shelter to grief.

29.

Sympathy is the flow and glow of goodness.

30.

Sympathy is a God-representative on earth.

31.

A sympathetic heart is a God-fulness-life.

32.

Sympathy turns sorrows into roses.

33.

Sympathy blooms. Relief blossoms.

34.

Sympathy comes to sorrow unconditionally to heal.

35.

Sympathy is supremely blessed not by God's Hands, not by God's Heart, but by God's Breath.

36.

May my mind be a sympathy-river.

37.

May my heart be a sympathy-sea.

38.

May my life be a sympathy-kingdom.

39.

Sympathy is God's own desire-fulfilling action performed by man.

40.

Sympathy comes from God the Creator and goes to God the creation.

41.

The sorrow-sea and the sympathy-sky were born together.

42.

Sympathy is a world-pain-concern traveler.

43.

Sorrow thinks it is incurable. Sympathy does not agree. Sympathy cures the heart of sorrow.

44.

As this world is a malady, even so sympathy is a remedy.

45.

A sympathising heart gladly and unreservedly welcomes a complaining mind.

46.

Sympathy is the heart-expansion, mind-illumination and oneness-satisfaction.

47.

Pain saddens us. Sympathy gladdens us.

48.

Sympathy is the mother-heart of humanity.

49.

Tears purify our life. Sympathy illumines our heart.

50.

When pain attacks us, sympathy runs towards us to save us.

51.

As long as sympathy remains alive, God does not have to worry about His creation.

52.

Sympathy receives God's Heart from God Himself and gives it back to God's Body.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sympathy, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1998
