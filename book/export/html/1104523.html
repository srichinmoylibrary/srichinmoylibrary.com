22.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

You are telling me that
My world-indifference-desire
Is the height of my stupidity.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

26 January 2005, 6:01 a.m. Huangshan, China. Non-lifting day.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 18, Agni Press, 2005
