581 To Steer Humanity And Divinity...

TO STEER HUMANITY AND DIVINITY

To steer humanity
I need God's Reality-boat.
Ah, where is it?
Where is it?

To steer divinity
I need God's Dream-boat.
Ah, it has been waiting for us
For such a long time.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
