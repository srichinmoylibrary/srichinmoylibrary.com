1 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Why am I so ungrateful to You?
I clearly see that
Your Heart is drawn out towards me
To shelter me
And be my strongest support.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 11, Agni Press, 2002
