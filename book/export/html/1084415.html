32. A better way

A better way
To pass the morning:
I shall meditate every morning
For fifteen minutes
To love God in His own Way,
Plus cheerfully.

A better way
To pass the afternoon:
I shall meditate every afternoon
For fifteen minutes
To please God in His own Way,
Plus cheerfully.

A better way
To pass the evening:
I shall meditate every evening
For fifteen minutes
To fulfil God in His own Way,
Plus cheerfully.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
