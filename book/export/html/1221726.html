Dibanishi Prabhur Paye

Dibanishi prabhur paye
Karbo ami krira
Chale jabe amar maner
Sakal jatil pira

Translation:

Day and night I shall play
With my Lord Supreme.
Then all my serious mental
And vital ailments
Will disappear.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eso eso prabhu, part 1, , 1998
