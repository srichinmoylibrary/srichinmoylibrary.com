167. Four rooms

Quick instruction
Ends in the life-room.

Slow education
Ends in the school-room.

Great realisation
Ends in the heart-room.

Perfect manifestation
Begins and never ends in the body-room.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 4, Agni Press, 1974
