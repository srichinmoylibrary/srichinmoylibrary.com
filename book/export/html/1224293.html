Maraner Banshi Jibane Bajate Chahi

Maraner banshi jibane bajate chahi
Hatashai bhara pran
Asha pakhi mriyaman
Binasher gan bhore sandhyai gahi

Translation:

I want to play the flute of death
In my reality-life.
Day in and day out,
I sing the song of utter destruction-night.
Despondency is another name for my heart.
My hope-bird is almost dead.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 2, , 1976
