6 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I wish to cling only
To Your Protection-Feet.
I wish to cling only
To Your Compassion-Eye.
I wish to cling only
To Your Forgiveness-Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 August 1999, 6:41 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 90 lbs. (10 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
