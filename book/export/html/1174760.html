1819 What I Have Been Preserving For You

My Beloved Supreme,
I am now giving You
What I have been preserving for You
Throughout Eternity:
My Infinity's constant Satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 19, Agni Press, 1981
