5483 Inside Your Flaming Love

What you have now
Is a flaming temper.
What you need
Is a flaming love.
Inside your flaming love
One day, to your surprise,
You will see a growing and glowing God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 55, Agni Press, 1983
