12785

When I do something undivine,
I hope that God
Is not looking at me.
When I do something divine,
I pray to God
Not only to look at me,
But also to applaud me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 128, Agni Press, 1990
