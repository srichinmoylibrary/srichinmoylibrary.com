5. Slowly and quickly

        Slowly
Descend to the level of dust.        Quickly
Ascend to the heights of Heaven.        Carefully
Run to the farthest corner of the outer world.        Smilingly
Dive into the deepest depths of the inner world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 11, Agni Press, 1974
