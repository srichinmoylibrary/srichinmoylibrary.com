49. Four men

We blame
The living man
   Because
He is impure.

We forgive
The dying man
   Because
He tried to remain pure.

We admire
The dead man
   Because
His is the life that will not worry.

We need
The immortal man
   Because
His is the life
That can and will fulfil us.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 18, Agni Press, 1974
