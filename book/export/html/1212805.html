Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 270

26901

God reads my life's
Love-devotion-surrender-book
Every day, without fail,
Blessingfully.

26902

God reads my life's
Gratitude-heart-book
Every day, without fail,
Proudly.

26903

Each Heaven-climbing
Aspiration-heart
Is a God-treasury.

26904

My division-mind does not dare,
But my aspiration-heart
Dares to imagine
A world without suffering.

26905

Criticism
Is nothing short of
A wisdom-barren mind.

26906

Alas,
My heart is always
Painfully haunted
By the absence of God.

26907

A confusion-mind
Never knows
What the heart-prosperity is.

26908

It is the God-lovers
And not the God-speakers
Who eternally live for God.

26909

My aspiration
Is
My God-foundation-certainty.

26910

My obedience-heart
Is
My God-perfection-life.

26911

O soul of mine,
Can you not see
How often I pine
For you?

26912

A purity-thought
Is the ecstasy
Of sweetness.

26913

Not your bravery
But your stupidity
Says that it can live
Without God.

26914

My thinking
And God's twinkling
Always live together.

26915

Impossibility-trees
Do not grow
In my God-surrender-heart-garden.

26916

No aspiration-sun
Must suffer
From a sunset.

26917

Total satisfaction
Will be all ours
If we allow our hearts
To guide us.

26918

O my mind,
If you do not leave doubt,
Faith will never come to you
To give you happiness.

26919

O my aspiration-heart,
You are the only one
To please God
In His own Way.

26920

Can you imagine what God does?
When I tell God
That I shall never be as good as He is,
He just bursts out laughing.

26921

O my heart,
Do not be bashful before God.
Just be prayerful and soulful.
He is all for you.

26922

A doubting mind
Feeds on its stupid discovery
Of others' weaknesses.

26923

The self-doubting life
Is a self-disguised
Demon.

26924

The stupid and audacious mind
Enjoys
Disobedience-outbreaks.

26925

Miracles
Do happen
In every human life.

26926

God's Compassion-Life
And His Fragrance-Breath
Are inseparable.

26927

Sooner or later
We all must demolish
Our ego-towers.

26928

No devotion,
No quick and convincing
Satisfaction-progress.

26929

In the land of spirituality,
An unaspiring mind
Is more dead than alive.

26930

The doubting mind
Can never possess
A compassion-umbrella.

26931

No patience-practice,
No perfection-fulfilment-smile.

26932

Is there any human mind
That does not pass through
The valleys of uncertainty?

26933

God loves my heart dearly
Because it solely depends
On God.

26934

The mind's newness
And the heart's fulness
Must always be kept
In very good condition.

26935

An unconditional surrender
Is
A thriving fulfilment-smile.

26936

Expectation
Often flounders
In barren frustration-lands.

26937

The sterling faith-investment
Knows no
Ignorance-life-night.

26938

Detachment
Knows no
Failure-frustration-torture.

26939

Without a determination-mind,
Nobody can succeed
In his outer life.

26940

Without an aspiration-heart,
Nobody can proceed
In his God-discovery
On earth.

26941

Satisfaction loves to live
Only in
A God-surrender-heart.

26942

O seeker,
Every day you must tame
The anger of your vital.

26943

God's Compassion-Eye
And
God's Forgiveness-Heart
Are extremely fond of each other.

26944

Like the heart,
The mind too can invoke
Silence-delight
If it wants to.

26945

Because of his universal
Oneness-heart,
His mind-pride-tower
Has collapsed.

26946

A self-giving heart
Sees
No stop sign.

26947

A purity-heart
Lives far beyond
The snares
Of jealousy and insecurity.

26948

To decorate my heart-room,
My aspiration-heart-tears
Are of paramount importance.

26949

The reasoning mind shall doubt God,
Even if He stands right before
The reasoning mind.

26950

The aspiration-heart
Does not have to depend
On God's physical Presence
For authenticity.

26951

The gentle whispers
Of God's Compassion-Eye
Have transformed
My God-doubting mind.

26952

My mind wants to see God
Face to face
For its satisfaction.

26953

My life wants to live inside
God's Heart-Garden
For its satisfaction.

26954

My heart longs to live
At God's Lotus-Feet
For its satisfaction.

26955

Divinity may withdraw
By the time humanity
Awakens to Light.

26956

Each failure
Has a special significance
Behind it.

26957

I tell God
That He does not have to love me
As long as I can love Him.

26958

God tells me
That He abides only
By a give and take policy.

26959

Each self-giving thought
Is, indeed,
A world-peace-pioneer.

26960

Each day I pray to God
To give me the capacity
To declare
As the last day of my desire-life.

26961

My heart's God-gratitude-songs
Are the only ones
Flooded with beauty, purity,
Illumination and perfection.

26962

My life and I feel safe
Only at one place,
And that place is my Lord's
Ever-expanding Eye.

26963

A tiny hope-blossom
Is all I need every morning
To be a choice instrument
Of my Lord Supreme.

26964

If you look at desire,
No matter from which direction,
Your aspiration-heart-eye
Will definitely be blinded.

26965

Either God's Forgiveness-Feet
Or God's Compassion-Eye
Can alone feed
My aspiration-heart-cries.

26966

Without the descent
Of an unconditional Grace
From Above,
We can never clear
Our inner stormy skies.

26967

We may succeed in stopping
The speed of outer light,
But we shall never succeed in stopping
The speed of inner light,
For the inner light and its destination
Are immortal friends.

26968

We must shun anything
That tempts our aspiration-heart
And dedication-life —
The sooner the better!

26969

Where do I live?
I live inside the hope-fragrance
Of my heart-garden.

26970

My obedience-life is every day
Beckoned by
Eternity's newness
And
Infinity's fulness.

26971

What an ingratitude-life needs
Is an immediate and everlasting
Replacement.

26972

My gratitude-heart
Has a very special place
Deep inside
My Lord's own Satisfaction-Breath.

26973

It is our wisdom-light
That teaches us
How to cling to
Each God-moment.

26974

I have only one prayer:
May my heart charm prayerfully
My Lord's Forgiveness-Feet.

26975

O my lethargy-body,
Arise! Arise!
Just once open your eyes.
Look who is standing
Right in front of you —
God Himself!

26976

When we try to measure
God's Love,
We are forced to enjoy
An ever-puzzling creation-game.

26977

Alas, my dreams are fading,
My realities are hiding.
I find myself inside
My collapsing God-search.

26978

God tells me that
Each self-giving thought of mine
Can easily outshine
Every star in His Heart-Sky.

26979

My heart's sorrowful song
And I
Cherish each other.

26980

My soul's blissful song
And I
Fulfil each other.

26981

O my mind,
Heaven does not care
To know
Your mighty name.

26982

O my mind,
Heaven does not have anything to do
With your vast fame.

26983

O my mind,
Heaven is eagerly waiting
For the tiniest aspiration-bloom
Inside your desert-dryness.

26984

My Lord,
Do smile at me once more
To reassure me
That You do love me.

26985

Each time I tell my heart
That it is not aspiring
To my satisfaction,
It just bursts into sobs.

26986

When my heart satisfies God
In God's own Way,
I immediately see the blooming blossom
Of an ecstasy-rose
Inside my heart-garden.

26987

My mind,
So many divine thoughts
Are trying to enter into you.
Why are you so inhospitable?

26988

We must be ruthless
With our
Troublemaker doubts.

26989

To kindle the fire
Of the heart,
We need an incessant flow
Of tears.

26990

If I can offer
Only for a brief moment
My gratitude-heart to my Lord Supreme,
My joy will know no bounds.

26991

To bypass the doubting mind
We all must try
And we all must succeed.

26992

God tells me that
If I am unwilling to play with Him,
Then He will not allow me
To pray to Him.

26993

My mind procrastinates.
My heart hesitates.
Alas,
Who is going to come to liberate me?

26994

Attention, my mind, attention!
Come and be with me
Inside my heart-garden
Before brooding doubts
Start attacking you.

26995

My mind, I am pleading
With my aspiration-heart
And dedication-life
To turn towards Light.

26996

I tell you,
Very soon the story will change.
The beggar in me will play the role
Of an emperor.

26997

God is Compassion.
God is Forgiveness.
God is everything.
But He prefers to be
Oneness.

26998

Self-giving
Is the sacred secret
Of our happiness-life.

26999

If you are one hundred per cent
For God,
Only then can God teach you
The sweetest melody
Of His Love.

27000

My Twenty-Seven Thousand
Aspiration-Plants,
My Lord Beloved Absolute Supreme
Has given me to give you
The Sun-Smile-Blossoms
Of His Heart.

My Twenty-Seven Thousand
Aspiration-Plants,
You have given humanity
A sleepless God-thirst
And
A breathless God-hunger.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 270, Agni Press, 1998
