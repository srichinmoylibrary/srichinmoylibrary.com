Chokher Jale Pujle Tomai

Chokher jale pujle tomai
Dekha nahi pabo
Hiyar bale tushle tomai
Charan tale jabo

Translation:

Just with the tears of our eyes
We shall not be able
To see our Lord Supreme.
But on the strength of our aspiration-heart,
If we can satisfy Him,
Then we shall be able
To place ourselves at His Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
