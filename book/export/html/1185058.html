730. Who knows man's fate?

The soul in the soul
Immortal becomes.
The man in the man
Mortal becomes.
Who knows man's fate?
Who knows God's fate?
I know each man
Is tomorrow's blossoming God
And each God
Is tomorrow's manifesting man.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
