652. O my silence

O my silence-heart,
I love you and love you.

O my silence-life,
I need you and need you.

O my silence-soul,
How near are you, how near?

O my silence-goal,
How far are you, how far?

O my silence-God,
Where are You, where are You?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
