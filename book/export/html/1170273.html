177. The life of failure

Yours is the life
Of bombastic failure.
I feel sorry for you.
Mine is the life
Of soundless failure.
You may not feel sorry for me,
But God does.
He always feels sorry for me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sound becomes, silence is, Agni Press, 1975
