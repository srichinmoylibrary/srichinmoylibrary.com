Ami Kandibona Ami Hasibona

Ami kandibona ami hasibona
Shudhui tomare dakibo
Agyanatar dhrira bandhan tomar
Charane bhangibo

Translation:

I shall not cry, I shall not smile any more.
I shall only invoke You.
I shall smash asunder my taut ignorance-life
      At Your Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Swadeshe bideshe ghuriya berai, , 2007
