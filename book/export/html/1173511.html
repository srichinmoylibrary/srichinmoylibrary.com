583 My Immortal Tear-Drops

Totally exhausted,
My blue-gold dreams
Desire to sleep.
Never mind, no harm!
From now on
My heart shall treasure
My Eternity's immortal tear-drops.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 6, Agni Press, 1979
