Krishna Prabhur Jhumur Jhumur

Krishna prabhur jhumur jhumur
Nupur baje
Chinta harai amar gabhir
Dhyaner majhe

Translation:

Lord Krishna is dancing and I am hearing
His ankle bells ringing.
This happens only when
My mind is absolutely silent

When I am having my deepest meditation.
