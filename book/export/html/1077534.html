12. Fear-role

Do you know
     Why
     He hates himself?
     He hates himself
           Because
           Fear plays the most damaging role
           In his unconscious life.
           I am sure sooner or later
           He will feel it,
           And then he will admire
           My wisdom-light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eternity's Silence-Heart, Agni Press, 1974
