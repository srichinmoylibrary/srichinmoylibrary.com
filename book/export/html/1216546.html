908. I am a fool

I am a fool
When I blindly imitate.

I am a fool
When I unnecessarily hesitate.

I am a fool
When I helplessly cry.

I am a fool
When I all alone
Greedily and shamelessly fly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
