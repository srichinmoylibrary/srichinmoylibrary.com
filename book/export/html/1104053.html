8 January 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The dreamer in me
Is God’s Newness-Eye.
The dream-fulfiller
Is God’s Sweetness-Heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 January 2000, 5:47 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
