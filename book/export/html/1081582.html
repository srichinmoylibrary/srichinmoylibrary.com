23. I live for him and die for him

Secretly
I live for him
Who loves God.

Bravely
I die for him
Who loves God.

Happily
I live for him
Who knows not how to love.

Unconditionally
I die for him
Who knows not how to love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
