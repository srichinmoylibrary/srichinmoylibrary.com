80. He found his place

Sloth and vagary
Found no place in him.
Fear and doubt
Found no place in him.
Worry and despair
Found no place in him.
God's Love and Pride
Found place in him.
He found his place,
His Eternal Place
On the Lap of Singing God,
In the Heart of Dancing God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 2, Agni Press, 1973
