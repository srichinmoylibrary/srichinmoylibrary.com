Peregrination

(pilgrimage)

Be not afraid
Of the inner peregrination;
Your Inner Pilot will definitely
Inspire you, encourage you
And guide you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
