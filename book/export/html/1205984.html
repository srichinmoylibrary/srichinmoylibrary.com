20147

I must force my eyes to see
That God's Beauty
Is the only thing to look at.

I must force my heart to feel
That God's Compassion
Is the only thing to live for.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 202, Agni Press, 1994
