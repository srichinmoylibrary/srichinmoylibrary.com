Although God Loves Him

Although God loves him,
He is his heart’s sorrow.
Although God blesses him,
He is his life’s failure.
     Why? Why? Why?
Because he has not
Changed his name into
Love-devotion-surrender.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flower-flames, part 1, , 1987
