25 April 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Command is
My golden opportunity
To become all Yours
In the twinkling of an eye!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 April 1999, 8:46 p.m. Before lifting 240 lbs. fifty-five times with one arm. (27 right, 26 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
