45.

Jiber srashta brahmare jini
Karen srijan age
Bhakti arghya rakhitechi mora
Se deber purobhage

You are the Source
Of Brahma the Creator.
Before You we place
Our supplication, devotion
And adoration-surrender.

From: Sri Chinmoy, August 15 1945, Agni Press, 1996
