4507 Never Afraid

The mind is never afraid
To doubt and suffer.
The heart is never afraid
To give and receive.
The soul is never afraid
To watch and enjoy.
God is never afraid
To smile and transcend.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 46, Agni Press, 1982
