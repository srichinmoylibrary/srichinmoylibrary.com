18147

Alas, my mind does not want to know
That I can think
Beyond what it thinks.
Alas, my heart does not want to know
That I can feel
Beyond what it feels.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 182, Agni Press, 1993
