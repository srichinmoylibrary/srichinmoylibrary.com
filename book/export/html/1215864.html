240. Why am I here?

Why am I here
In the land of the Gurus?
Why am I here
In the land of the disciples?

I am in the land of the Gurus
Because
God wants to share with me
His Realisation-Light.

I am in the land of the disciples
Because
God wants to share with me
His ignorance-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
