What can I do

Heaven is afraid of me.
     What can I do?
I fly beyond its glee,
     Piercing the Blue.

Earth is afraid of me.
     What can I do?
I fell its Ignorance-Tree
     To plant the true.

Hell is afraid of me.
     What can I do?
My compassion frees its pangs
     From the racking screw.

My soul is afraid of me.
     What can I do?
Body's eternity
     I needs must hue.

﻿
