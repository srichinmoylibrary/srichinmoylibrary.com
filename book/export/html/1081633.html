74. God knew it

Although
He lived in his dream-reality,
He really was great.
God and Heaven knew it;
Also I and my devoted heart.

Although
He died in his reality-dream,
He really was good.
God and earth knew it;
Also I and my surrendered heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
