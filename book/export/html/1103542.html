28 June 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
From life to death,
From death to life
I travel,
Carrying the message
Of Your universal Victory.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 June 1999, 7:46 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 6, Agni Press, 1999
