37. Truth

A painful truth
Is worse than a lie.
Truth that hurts,
Truth that destroys,
Is no truth.
Truth that illumines,
Truth that reveals,
Truth that perfects,
Truth that transforms the animal in us
And
Satisfies the human in us
And
Fulfils the divine in us
Is the only Truth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Dedication-drops, Agni Press, 1976
