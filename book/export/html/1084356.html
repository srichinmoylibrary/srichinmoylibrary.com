24. Real God, false God

His dreaming perfection
Who has made God?
He Himself.
Who has made the small gods?
Man has made them.
Who is a real God?
A silence-lover
And
A sound-performer.
Who is a false God?
A builder of temptation-palace
And
A destroyer of love-nest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 8, Agni Press, 1974
