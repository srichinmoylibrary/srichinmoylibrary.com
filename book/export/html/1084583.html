47. All are eager (II)

My rabbit is eager
To jump for me.
My tiger is eager
To devour for me.
My giraffe is eager
To reach for me.
My turtle is eager
To die for me.
My monkey is eager
To climb for me.
My swan is eager
To realise for me.
My cat is eager
To cry for me.
My camel is eager
To carry for me.
My elephant is eager
To brave for me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 12, Agni Press, 1974
