26 February 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The god of sincerity
And
The goddess of purity
Always live inside my heart-temple.
They never, never live inside
My unlit mind-room.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 February 2000, 6:51 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
