2054 God Is In Full Control

Let your heart
Tell your mind
That God is in full control
Of your life.
You will immediately see
That your tension has ceased
And your confidence
Has powerfully returned.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 21, Agni Press, 1981
