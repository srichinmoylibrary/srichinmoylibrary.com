5872 Thoughts A Seeker Can Cherish

"God is all Love."
"I am all for Him."
These are the thoughts
A seeker can cherish.
All other thoughts
He should consider as
Sheer distractions.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 59, Agni Press, 1983
