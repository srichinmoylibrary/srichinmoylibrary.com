48. Run evermore

Sigh no more.
Your brave days
Are dawning fast.
Cry no more.
God's Smile
Is drawing nigh.
Run evermore,
Dive evermore,
Fly evermore.
Why?
Because God the Inspiration
Has Himself become your aspiration,
And
God the Aspiration
Has Himself become your realisation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 14, Agni Press, 1974
