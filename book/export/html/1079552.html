April 17

We have a dislike for our soul because we think that its mission will never be fulfilled on earth. But our soul has a liking for us, because it knows that if it excludes us it will never be able to dive into the sea of integrality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flame-Goal, Agni Press, 1973
