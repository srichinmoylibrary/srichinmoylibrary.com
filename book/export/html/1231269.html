Ami Amar Dhyane Tanmoy

Ami amar dhyane tanmoy
Ami akkhoy atma pujai tanmoy
Antare mor dibya swarup
Bahire kebali malinata stup
Mithya branti parajoy
Ami amari dhyane tanmoy
