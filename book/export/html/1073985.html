60.

My sweet children, do not hide anything from me. If you want to increase my faith in you, do not hide anything from me.

My children, you have many, many, many desires, but I have only one desire, only one desire, and that desire is to make you supremely chosen instruments of my Beloved Supreme. This is my only desire. Do help me fulfil my only desire.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Compassion-sea and satisfaction-waves, Agni Press, 1992
