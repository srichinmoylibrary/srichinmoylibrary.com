39. Your beauty

Temptation-cry:
   What is it?
That is your personal beauty.

Illusion-sleep:
   What is it?
That is your impersonal beauty.

Aspiration-might:
   What is it?
That is your divine beauty.

Realisation-light:
   What is it?
That is your supreme beauty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 18, Agni Press, 1974
