Tomar Paye Rekhe Aji

Tomar paye rekhe aji amar hiyar padda phul
Patha hara habona ar peyechi aj mukti kul

Translation:

I am placing at Your Feet my heart-lotus.
Now I have reached my liberation-destination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bhorer pakhi, part 3, ,
