15796

If you feel that God's Compassion-Eye
Is a stranger to you,
Then your aspiration-heart
Will not see the beauty
And the fragrance
Of God's universal Sunrise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 158, Agni Press, 1991
