17.

My Lord,
They tell me that
When everything else falls,
Try prayers.
My Lord,
Is it true?
No, it is not.
When everything else fails,
Start blaming Me.
My Lord, why?
Why?
Because My world-happiness-machine
Is always out of order.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My child, you and I are in the same boat, Agni Press, 1991
