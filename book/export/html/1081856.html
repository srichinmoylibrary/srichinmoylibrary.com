297. Earth-peace, Heaven-bliss

Peace, Peace,
Earth has given me
Its precious Peace
Borrowed from God.

Bliss, Bliss,
Heaven has given me
Its cherished Bliss
Borrowed from God.

Peace tells me what
I can become on earth.
Bliss tells me what
I was in Heaven
Long before my earth-sojourn.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
