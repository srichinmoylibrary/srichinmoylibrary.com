26376

My Lord,
What do I need most
From You?
Please let me know.

"My child,
Only one thing
You need from Me:
Advice, constant advice.
And you must obey My Advice!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 264, Agni Press, 1998
