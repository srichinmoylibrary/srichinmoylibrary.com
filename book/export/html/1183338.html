202. Three messages

Death has a message
For you:
"Do not try. Just cry."

Life has a message
For you:
"Do not give up. Continue."

God has a message
For you:
"Smile, always smile."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
