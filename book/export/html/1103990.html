9 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am telling the truth
That my heart is as beautiful
As Your Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/9 December 1999, 6:51 a.m. Nova Friburgo, Brazil. Before doing one-arm pushes with 500 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
