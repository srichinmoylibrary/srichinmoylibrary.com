Samyer Jug Aji

Samyer jug aji
Keha rahibena parer adhin
Utheche danka baji
Sukhi aj keha nai
Nahi raje alo thai
Samyer jug aji

Translation:

This is the era of equality.
Nobody can dare
To subjugate
And lord it over others.
Equalityâ€™s trumpet must awaken
The somnolent human hearts.
Happiness is what we all
Want and need;
But where can we see
The face of happiness,
If not in the Haven-Light
Of the Absolute Supreme?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 1, , 1978
