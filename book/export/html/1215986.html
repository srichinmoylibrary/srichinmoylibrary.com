359. How can I have it?

A world of diamond silence:
Where is it?
A world of golden sound:
What is it?
A world of silver bliss:
How far is it?
A world of copper peace:
Alas, how can I have it?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 8, Agni Press, 1974
