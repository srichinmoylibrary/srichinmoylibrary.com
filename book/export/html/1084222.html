43. To say the least

"Good-bye, Father,
I shall see You tomorrow."

Good-bye, son,
I shall see you in a minute.

"Why and how, Father?
I am leaving, Father.
I am tired, really tired,
To say the least."

I am proud of you,
I am one with you.
I share your glory's light
Equally and unmistakably,
To say the least.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 5, Agni Press, 1974
