357. God alone knows

Talk to others,
You will learn.
Talk to yourself,
You will learn more.
Stop talking,
Remain silent.
God will talk and God will learn
For you.
You will learn everything
That God alone knows.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
