1090 Each Thought

Each thought is either
A smiling mastery
Or
A crying slavery.

Each idea is either
A swimming satisfaction
Or
A sinking frustration.

Each ideal is either
A challenging reality
Or
A surrendering unreality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 11, Agni Press, 1981
