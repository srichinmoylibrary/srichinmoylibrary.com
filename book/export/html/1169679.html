324. I need a place

Lord, Lord, You run too fast,
I cannot keep pace.

Lord, Lord give me
A small room,
A solitary space,
In Your Compassion-Heart.

I need a place
For my days.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
