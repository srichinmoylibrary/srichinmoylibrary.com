344. Four men

The desiring man
In me declares:
"Action, action."

The aspiring man
In me declares:
"Intention, intention."

The surrendering man
In me declares:
"God-satisfaction,
God-satisfaction."

The realised man
In me declares:
"Eternal-perfection,
Eternal-perfection."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
