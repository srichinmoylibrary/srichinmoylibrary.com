275. Lord, I love your Sovereignty

Body, I love your necessity.
Vital, I love your temerity.
Mind, I love your clarity.
Heart, I love your spontaneity.
Soul, I love your divinity.
Lord, I love Your Sovereignty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
