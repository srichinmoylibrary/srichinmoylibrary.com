895. Your sweet imagination and your bold will

Here is the difference
Between
Your sweet imagination
And
Your bold will:
Your imagination
Lovingly and soulfully
Speaks to God,
Speaks about God;
Your will
Consciously and unmistakably
Becomes God,
Fulfils God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 18, Agni Press, 1974
