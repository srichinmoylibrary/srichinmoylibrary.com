234. Your Master has become

Today your Master
Has become a living dynamo.
Don't delay!
He will go on without you.

Today your Master
Has become a sea of Peace.
Stay where you are!
Lose not even a moment's Bliss.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
