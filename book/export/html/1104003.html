16 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
That in our spiritual life
All our heart-ultramarathons
Are sponsored by God Himself.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 December 1999, 8:06 a.m. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Before doing sixty-eight one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (30 right, 38 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
