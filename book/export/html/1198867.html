Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 132

13101

Every morning
I bring my heart's gratitude-tears
And my life's sun-smiles
To my soul's God-fondness-home.

13102

My Lord Supreme,
May I find myself always
In between
My heart's aspiration-beauty
And my life's dedication-duty.

13103

When God is left alone,
He starts counting
The number of gratitude-hearts
On earth.

13104

Now more than ever before,
I am loving God unconditionally,
And God is depending on me
Happily and proudly.

13105

If one is really sincere,
Then he is bound to find
More reasons to love God
Than to doubt Him.

13106

What I need
Is constant willingness
To cure my life's
Unhappiness-headache.

13107

I never enjoy the collisions
Between my mind's doubts
And my heart's faith.

13108

This morning
My soul and my heart met together
And agreed to throw away
My mind's stupidity-crown.

13109

If my aspiration is genuine,
Then God is bound to give me
The capacity to triumph
Over all adversities.

13110

If you yourself do not silence
Your doubt-mind,
Then nothing and nobody -
No, not even God's Compassion -
Will do it for you.

13111

Every morning
God the hope and man the promise
Together meet
For their mutual satisfaction.

13112

I do not know, I do not know
Why God should accept
My desert-dry surrender-life.

13113

I take each and every pure thought
As the prophet
Of an unimaginable miracle-performance.

13114

My Lord Supreme,
How difficult it is
To pray to You,
To meditate on You
And to love You
With a non-expectation-heart!

13115

I feel I have only
One thing to do
Every morning:
My heart's gratitude must feed
My soul's meditation-sunrise.

13116

I am telling you that soon,
Very soon,
Your half-hearted aspiration-life
Will completely die.

13117

Hopelessness is one worker
That you must never employ
In your life-factory.

13118

What I need is
An unconditionally God-loving,
Brave heart
To cross my life's
Impossibility-frontiers.

13119

The fulfilment-perfection
Of God-manifestation
Will always be within the reach
Of sincere peace-lovers.

13120

If man remains always
Unwillingness-obstruction,
How can poor God
Fulfil His golden Dreams
Here on earth?

13121

Gratitude
Is the only admission fee
To God s universal Love-Land.

13122

Was there any time in world history
When doubt-disease did not threaten
The whole world?

13123

God's Forgiveness-Net
Always catches me
Before I fall and die.

13124

Man's insecurity-heart
Is usually heavier
Than his aspiration-dedication-world.

13125

I shall never allow my life
To be captured
Either by unwillingness
Or by ingratitude.

13126

Today's aspiration-heart-light
Must conquer
Tomorrow's ignorance-mind-night.

13127

Every morning
You must empty your heart
Of endless and useless
Anxieties and worries.

13128

God comes to you
With His Compassion-Smile
Long before your aspiration-heart dies.

13129

God is always hungry
For my unconditional
Surrender-food.

13130

My Lord, not one more scolding
But as many scoldings
As You want to give me!
I shall always take Your scoldings
As special blessings.

13131

My heart speaks
To my Lord Supreme
Only in gratitude-tears.

13132

O my vital,
I shall no longer allow you
To remain crippled
By self-indulgence.

13133

You are telling me
That you have nothing to do.
I am telling you
That you have many things to do.
At least do one thing:
Extinguish your mind's conflict-flames.

13134

In the small hours of the morning,
Daily my dreaming heart
Smilingly blossoms.

13135

My loving heart
Is my inexhaustible wealth
In my inner world.

13136

Be not afraid of singing
Eternity's infinite
Victory-songs.

13137

Never allow self-doubt
To paralyse
Your God-searching mind.

13138

I am asking my heart
Never to journey
Into the territory
Of my futility-mind.

13139

A God-inspired heart
Is bound to reach
God's Summit-Vision-Heights.

13140

My heart's most beautiful
And most powerful gift to mankind
Is my soulful oneness-smile.

13141

No more shall I allow my life
To be besmeared
With the ashes of futility-hopes.

13142

Aspiration-light
Is the satisfaction-delight
Of a lifelong God-seeker.

13143

Loneliness is the self-created
Treasured possession
Of an unconsciously clever mind.

13144

The pride of impossibility
Can easily be challenged
And smashed
By the lovers
Of the peace-loving world.

13145

If you can cheerfully accept
The unexpected,
Then God's open Heart-Door
Is bound to beckon you.

13146

O my vagabond mind,
Why do you allow yourself
In season and out of season
To be terrorised by your own
Savage doubt-creations?

13147

If you are clothed
In insincerity,
You will never receive
An invitation from God.

13148

If you deliberately escape
Into your fantasy-world,
You will never be able to sing
The song of self-discovery.

13149

My Lord,
To see Your Compassion-Eye
Is to see beyond my heart's
Each and every sorrow.

13150

Inside
My surrender and gratitude-smile
I see my heart's dreams
And my life's reality
Marching.

13151

With breathless willingness
Every morning
I offer my aspiration-flame-songs
To my Lord Supreme.

13152

I love and treasure
The rainbow-smile
Of my God-obedience-heart.

13153

Lethargy-blanket,
No more are you my bodyguard,
No more!

13154

O my frustration-mind-bank,
I am indomitably determined
To empty you completely.

13155

O my aggression-vital-fighter,
Prepare yourself
To receive my spirit's
Death-blow!

13156

When my peace-heart
Dreams,
My bliss-life
Sings.

13157

My heart's silver river-hope
Is fast approaching
Its fulfilment-satisfaction-destination.

13158

Cast off, cast off
Your doubt-shackles!
You are, without fail,
A chosen instrument of God Himself.

13159

This ignorance-incarnation of mine
Will soon respond
To God's Life-Transformation-Hour.

13160

O my complacency-pillow,
I am really happy
To say good-bye to you.

13161

God's Love-Compassion
Will not remain invisible
If our faith remains invincible.

13162

Your letters will not reach God
If you do not mail them
With your heart's aspiration
And your life's dedication-stamps.

13163

If you most soulfully beg God
To play on your heart-flute,
I am sure His Melodies
Will multiply your aspiration-light.

13164

If you do not want to enjoy
Your doubting mind's funeral,
Then rest assured,
There will be nobody on earth
To attend it.

13165

Although it is meant for all,
Very few people dare to take
The truth-examination.

13166

My sacred heart
Secretly works.
My clever mind
Openly takes the salary.

13167

Relax, relax!
God wants you to relax
On His own Compassion-Satisfaction-Chair.

13168

I know what I am not:
I am not my heart's
Ingratitude-life!

13169

God wants to see your
Aspiration-heart and dedication-life
From His Compassion and Satisfaction-Mountaintop.

13170

Because love is not available
In your heart-garden,
The world-orchestra of destruction-night
Is playing
Inside your mind-forest.

13171

If you want to be
A divine celebrity on earth,
Then never pay any attention
To the unaspiring earth-throngs.

13172

Faster than the speed of light
Is my surrendering heart's
Gratitude-breath.

13173

Your outer world
Of deathless restlessness
Must be replaced
By your soul's peace-resources.

13174

If you want to be perfect,
Then offer God your heart's purity,
Your mind's clarity,
Your vital's dynamism
And your body's alertness.

13175

My heart's aspiration-heroes
Have powerfully planted
God's eternal Victory-Banner.

13176

If you have
A fear of newness-dawn,
How can you have
The delight of fulness-day?

13177

Each seeker
Must have silence-peace
Before he can enter
Into the palace of truth.

13178

God's Compassion-Eye
Plays the role of a Oneness-Heart
Between two individuals or two nations.

13179

When your life becomes
A running river of God's Compassion-Heart,
God will allow you
To make collect calls to Him
Any time you want.

13180

An abundance of purity
In the spiritual life
Is the fulfilment of God's Vision-Light
Here on earth.

13181

The starvation of oneness-heart
Gives birth
To loneliness-destruction.

13182

I never allow my mind
To be a victim
To the world's tormenting complications.

13183

Ungratefulness
Is the incurable disease
Of millennia.

13184

To underestimate
The divine in us
Is the usual and common thing.

13185

From insecurity-cave
To security-palace
Is the heart's very, very long journey.

13186

Each human life
Is nothing but the breath
Of a flickering dream.

13187

I am ashamed of so many things.
Alas, how is it that I am not ashamed
Of my confusion-producing mind-factory?

13188

Unless you become
An unconditional God-lover,
You are bound to suffer
From the sorrows of a goalless man.

13189

A great man
Is he whose mind
Is no longer a sleeping lion.

A good man
Is he whose heart
Is no longer a division-life.

13190

You must give up
Your fragile earth-possession-ornaments
If you want to enjoy
A life of paradise here on earth.

13191

God enjoys
His earthly holiday
Only in humanity's
Complete heart-surrender-country.

13192

The God of the human mind
Has a very short
Existence-life.

13193

When I meditate soulfully,
I see only two things:
The slow ascent
Of my heart-hope
And the quick descent
Of my Lord's Compassion-Eye.

13194

There can be no desire-man
Who has never been a victim
To frustration-sighs.

13195

What I need is a soulful
Peace-promotion-heart
And not a powerful
Sound-declaration-mind.

13196

Man's soulful smile
Is indeed a perfect expression
Of his inner peace.

13197

What we all want
Is an outer expression
Of God's inner Recognition.

13198

An aspiring man
Is he who is trying to disentangle
The chains of untold centuries.

13199

If you exercise daily
Your own iron self-control,
Then everybody is bound to see
The aura of your confidence-light.

13200

If your mind is made of
Inspiration-dreams
And your heart is made of
Aspiration-realities,
Then God the Pilot will grant you
A special place
In His Compassion-Satisfaction-Boat.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 132, Agni Press, 1990
