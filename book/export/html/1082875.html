God is all for you

Fear nothing,
Not even the wild laughter
Of lightening.
Remember,
God the Compassion is all for you.

Fear nothing,
Not even the ceaseless roaring
Of ignorance-night.
Remember,
God the Compassion is all for you.

Fear nothing,
Not even the unalterable laws
Of the universe.
Remember,
God the Compassion is all for you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God-Life: is it a far cry?, Agni Press, 1974
