62.

"My Lord,
Is there any way for a seeker
To become spiritually rich overnight?"

"Yes, there is a way.
Just tell the seeker
To give up devotedly
  What he has
And to wake up immediately
  With what he is."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Secrets revealed, Agni Press, 1973
