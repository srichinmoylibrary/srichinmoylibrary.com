1388

Three travellers —
My mind,
My mind's stupidity
And I —
Travel together all the time,
Looking for only one thing:
The dance hall
Where ignorance-king
And ignorance-queen
Each day perform.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 14, Agni Press, 1983
