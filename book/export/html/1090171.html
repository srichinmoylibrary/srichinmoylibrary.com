37. We are great

Heaven has received
My aspiration;
Therefore
Heaven is great.

Earth has received
My dedication;
Therefore
Earth is great.

My life has received
My liberation;
Therefore
My life is great.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, receive this little undying cry, Agni Press, 1975
