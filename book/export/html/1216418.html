783. Watching

My earth is thirsty;
Alas, no nectar-sea within my reach.

My Heaven is hungry;
Alas, no nourishing-sky within my reach.

My human birth is crying;
Alas, no saving hand within my reach.

Yet God is watching,
Carefully watching.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 16, Agni Press, 1974
