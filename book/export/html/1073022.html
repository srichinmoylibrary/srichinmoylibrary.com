40. Patience

Patience
Is God's fulfilling
Length of time.

Patience
Is man's Eternity's
   Necessity,
   Reality,
   Divinity
     And
   Inevitability.

Patience
Is man's harbinger
Of perfect Perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
