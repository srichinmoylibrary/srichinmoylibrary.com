870 He...

HE

His actions
Interpret God.

His silence
Represents God.

He has reached
The Highest.
He stands beside
The Highest.

And you know
Where he started from.
He started from
The lowest,
The very
Lowest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
