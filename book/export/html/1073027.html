45. Joy

He is a fool
Who thinks
   That he can make
Others happy.

He is a greater fool
Who thinks
   That others can make
Him happy.

He is the greatest fool
Who feels
   That his earth-life
Is for his own joy
   And not for the Joy
Of his Beloved Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
