33 My Heart’s God-Obedience-Flower ...

My heart’s God-obedience-flower
Must forever last.
My life’s God-obedience-hour
Must forever last.
My mind’s God-obedience-tower
Must forever last.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 10, Agni Press, 2002
