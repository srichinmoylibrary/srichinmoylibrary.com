595 Forget Me Not...

FORGET ME NOT

A few fleeting years
Can take me
To the animal kingdom.
O Lord Supreme,
Save me, save me!

A few slow years
Can take me
To Love-Oneness-Perfection Kingdom.
O Lord Supreme,
Forget me not!
Forget me not!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
