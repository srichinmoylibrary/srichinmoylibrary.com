47.

Those who vaingloriously tell the world that perestroika is dead and buried in oblivion are totally mistaken, for perestroika is God-Immortality's own world-transformation-vision.Your perestroika -dream will blossom into reality-ecstasy. Perestroika will definitely be the fulfilment you your victory-vows.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sixty-one Gratitude-Blossoms from the World-Heart-Home-Garden, Agni Press, 1992
