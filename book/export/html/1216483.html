847. What I do

What I do:
I sow the seeds
Of higher life;
I kill the trees
Of lower life.

What else I do:
I build the kingdom
Of brighter life;
I destroy the home
Of darker souls.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 17, Agni Press, 1974
