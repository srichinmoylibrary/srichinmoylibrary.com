550 Just A Habit...

JUST A HABIT

Why do I speak to God?
It is just a habit
Of my perfection.

Why do I adore myself?
It is just a habit
Of my imperfection.

Why do I love man?
It is just a habit
Of my obligation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 11, Agni Press, 1973
