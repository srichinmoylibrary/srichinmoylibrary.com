233. I like, I love

Society and sanity:
I like society,
I love sanity.

Beauty and purity:
I like beauty,
I love purity.

Generosity and humility:
I like generosity,
I love humility.

Responsibility and duty:
I like responsibility,
I love duty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
