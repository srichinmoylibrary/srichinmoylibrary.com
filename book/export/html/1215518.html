11. Three apostles

Three apostles of atheism:
     A sleeping body,
     A strangling vital,
     A doubting mind.

Three apostles of theism:
     A believing heart,
     A loving life,
     A serving soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When God-Love descends, Agni Press, 1975
