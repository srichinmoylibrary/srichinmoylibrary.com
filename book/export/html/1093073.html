45.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
May my unconditional surrender
To God's Will
Be the only joy
In my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life.

25 March 2000

7:01 a.m.

New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My aspiration-heart cycles, part 2, Agni Press, 2000
