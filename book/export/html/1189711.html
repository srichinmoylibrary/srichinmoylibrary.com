4035

My mind's silver desire
Is to become
As powerful as God.

My heart's golden desire
Is to become
An unconditionally surrendered breath
Of God's Satisfaction-Will.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 41, Agni Press, 1984
