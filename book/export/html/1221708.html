Jabo Phire Jabo Phire

Jabo phire jabo phire
Hiya pure
Prabhur jayer gaibo giti
Madhur sure

Translation:

I shall return, I shall return
To my heart-citadel.
Sweetly and soulfully
I shall sing the Victory-Songs
Of my Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eso eso prabhu, part 1, , 1998
