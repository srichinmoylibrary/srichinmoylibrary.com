3 July

My evening descends
And tells me
That the easiest and safest way
Of winning God's infinite Love
Is to ply my life-boat
Between my heart's eagerness-shore
And my life's enthusiasm-shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My evening descends, Agni Press, 1996
