Khulechi Duar Neme Aso Alo Antare Mor

Khulechi duar neme aso alo
Antare mor homanal jwalo
Shakal kamana akuti pradani
Sampinu charane e jiban khani
Jiban jagate jyotir karuna dhalo
Rekhona kothao bindu matra kalo

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flame-roads, , 1981
