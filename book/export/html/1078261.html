614 Because God Needs You...

BECAUSE GOD NEEDS YOU

Because
You do not love God,
You are useless.

Because
You do not need God,
You are worse than useless.

Because
God loves you,
One day He will make you useful.

Because
God needs you,
One day He will make you more than useful.
And I tell you,
That golden day
Is fast approaching.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
