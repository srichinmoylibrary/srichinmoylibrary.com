408. O my world

   O my world,
Sleep not with a groaning age.   O my world,
Stay not with a moaning age.   O my world,
Live, live with a climbing face.   O my world.
Love, love with the dawning grace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
