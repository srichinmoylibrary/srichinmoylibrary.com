1441

Never, never tell a lie
To any human being!
If you tell a lie
To a human being,
He will ruthlessly insult you
And torture you,
And at the same time,
He will not forgive you.
But if you ever tell a lie to God —
Which you must not
And should not —
Then God will forgive you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 15, Agni Press, 1983
