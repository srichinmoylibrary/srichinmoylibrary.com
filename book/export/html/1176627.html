3668 Totally Lost

My vital wants excitement
But does not need it.
My mind needs enlightenment
But does not want it.
Such being the case,
I am totally lost between
The fruitlessness of the vital
And
The unawareness of the mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
