13 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I was born to please You
In Your own Way,
And even when I am dying,
I shall please You in Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 September 1999, 8:43 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
