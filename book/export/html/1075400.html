822. Lord, make me worthy

Lord,
Make me worthy
Of serving the world.
Of course
In Your own way.

Lord,
Make me worthy
Of loving the world.
Of course
In Your own way.

Lord,
Make me worthy
Of pleasing the world.
Of course
In Your own way.

Lord, Lord, Lord,
Make me worthy
Of living my life.
Of course
In Your own way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 17, Agni Press, 1973
