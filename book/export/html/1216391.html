756. They are left alone

His desire-life
And the prince of hell
Are left alone.

His aspiration-life
And the prince of Heaven
Are left alone.

His God-life
And here and now
Are left alone.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 16, Agni Press, 1974
