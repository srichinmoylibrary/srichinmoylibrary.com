20521

O empire-conqueror,
Who needs you?
Who wants you?
Not me!
Not in the least.

O heart-conqueror,
Where are you?
Do come and live with me
In my sweet little heart-nest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 206, Agni Press, 1994
