272. My earth-years

My earth-years have taught me
More than countless books.

My earth-years have taught me
More than Heaven's repeated instructions.

My earth-years have taught me
More than they actually promised.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
