3 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May I become every morning
A new gratitude-heart-flower
And place myself
At Your Golden Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 May 1999, 6:37 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. seven times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.) New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 5, Agni Press, 1999
