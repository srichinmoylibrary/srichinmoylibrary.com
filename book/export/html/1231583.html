Kena Ami Chahina Taba Priti Kena Ami

Kena ami chahina taba priti
Kena ami gahina taba giti
Tomare khunjite daogo shakati
Tomare pujite daogo bhakati
Jibaner pita maraner mita

From: Sri Chinmoy, The tears and smiles of a God-fragrance-heart, , 2001
