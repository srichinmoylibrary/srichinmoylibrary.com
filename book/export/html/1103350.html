28 March 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day,
In the small hours of the morning,
I speedily run to my shrine
To measure my devotion-heart
And treasure Your Compassion-Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 March 1999, 10:01 p.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with both arms simultaneously from a seated position.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
