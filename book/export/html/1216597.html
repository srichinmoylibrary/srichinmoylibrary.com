958. My achievements and accomplishments

The inner sun
Is responsible for all
My achievements.   The outer sun
   Smiles on all
   My unexpected achievements.

The inner Master
Is responsible for all
My accomplishments.   The outer Master
   Smiles on all
   My unbelievable accomplishments.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
