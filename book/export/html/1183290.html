154. Revive

O my heart,
Revive your old devotion.
O my life,
Revive your old determination.
O my world,
Revive your old satisfaction.
O my God,
Revive Your old Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
