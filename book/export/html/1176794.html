3833 God's Ecstasy-Heart

If you can show God
Your hard-earned liberated mind,
God will show you
His Infinity's Ecstasy-Heart.
He will also tell you
That you can have His Heart
For the asking.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 39, Agni Press, 1982
