12. Selflessness

Selflessness is:
I am of God,
I am for God,
Him to please,
Him to serve
In His own way
At every moment
Is my heart's selflessness.

Selflessness is
Heart-giving,
Life-giving.
What we call selflessness today
Becomes God-awareness tomorrow,
        And
   The day after, that very thing
   Becomes God-oneness,
   Eternity's God-oneness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Union and oneness, Agni Press, 1976
