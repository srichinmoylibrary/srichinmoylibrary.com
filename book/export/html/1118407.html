5003

Please, my Lord, please,
Do not delay anymore
  In giving me
  A mind that will adore You,
A heart that will love You
  And
  A life that will serve You
  Unreservedly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 6, Agni Press, 1998
