Purna Hate Chaho Jadi

Purna hate chaho jadi bhitar bahir purna
Chaowa paowar bishal prashad churna karo churna

Translation:

If you want to be supremely complete
Within and without,
Then demolish the huge palace
Of your desire-hunger
And your desire-fulfilment.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Khunjite chahigo, part 1, , 2003
