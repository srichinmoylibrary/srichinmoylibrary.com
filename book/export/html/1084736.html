47 I Know God...

I KNOW GOD

My love knows
Where God is.
My devotion knows
Who God is.
My surrender knows
Why God is.
Where is God?
Inside my heart-cry.
Who is God?
God is my Life-Lover.
Why is God?
God is for my satisfaction-smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
