21.

My Lord,
Is there any special reason
Why You have come to me
So late at night?
"My child,
It is the only time
I can catch your mind
And try to tame it."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 2, Agni Press, 1998
