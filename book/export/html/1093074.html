46.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The mind greedily wants to study
World-destruction-terrorism.
The heart prayerfully likes to study
World-oneness-heroism.

29 March 2000

8:50 a.m.

New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My aspiration-heart cycles, part 2, Agni Press, 2000
