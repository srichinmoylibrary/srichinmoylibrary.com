567 Secret And Sacred Truths

God told me
His secret Truth:
"My son, I love your heart's
Sincerity-cry."

I told God
My sacred truth:
"My Father, I love Your Eyes'
Immortality-Smile."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 6, Agni Press, 1979
