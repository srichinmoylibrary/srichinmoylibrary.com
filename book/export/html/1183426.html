290. Go out

Go out
If you want to widen your mind.
Stay inside
If you want to maintain
The purity of your heart.
Go out and come back soon
If you want to conquer the world
Yet sustain your God-like beauty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
