42. O my patience

O my patience-heart,
Because of you
Today I own Liberation-sky.
O my patience-life,
Because of you
Today I own Perfection-world.
O my patience-God,
Because of You
Today I own Satisfaction-goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 12, Agni Press, 1974
