22.

Yesterday
My aspiration was enough
To please God.
Today
Even my realisation
Is not enough to please God.
Today
I will be able to please Him
Only if I can manifest Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Command from God the Justice, Whisper from God the Compassion, Agni Press, 1994
