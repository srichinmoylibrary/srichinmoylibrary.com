291. My supreme secret

I tell you
My supreme secret:
I used God's Breath
To declare
My ignorant body's
Ignorance-death.

You can also try.
Your success is guaranteed
For two million years
At least.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
