20 October 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My soul has placed its victory-crown
At Your Lotus-Feet
Lovingly, prayerfully and self-givingly.
May my heart, mind, vital and body
Do exactly the same.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

[After completing the lift:]

My Supreme, at long last I have done it, with Your boundless Grace. It is not my capacity, it is Your Compassion that has enabled me to lift up 120 pounds. It is Your Compassion, Your Compassion, Your Compassion and not my capacity.

20 October 1998 11:25 p.m. Before successfully lifting 120 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
