16. Four messages from four worlds

"Dominate and dominate."
The animal world
Gave me this message.

"Survive to dominate."
The human world
Gave me this message.

"Offer and become."
The divine world
Gave me this message.

"Offer and offer."
The world of the Supreme
Gave me this message.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
