My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 58

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Varna, Bulgaria and Bangkok, Thailand in January 2007.

10 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

1 My Lord, Please Bless Me ...

My Lord, please bless me
With two far-sighted eyes
And one deep-digging heart.

2 The Mind’s Unwillingness ...

The mind’s unwillingness
Eventually creates
Catastrophes.

3 Weakness-Transformation ...

Weakness-transformation
Is a most difficult task,
But not impossible.

4 My Patience ...

My patience
Has stabbed the heart
Of my doubting mind.

5 Criticism ...

Criticism
Is
A dangerous disease.

6 Life Is In Between ...

Life is in between
Pure light
And impure mind.

7 The Waters ...

The waters
Of my heart-river
Are all peace.

8 If You Are Not Satisfied ...

If you are not satisfied
With your life,
How can God
Ever be satisfied
With you?

11 January 2007 ...

11 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

9 God-Invocation ...

God-invocation
Is
Life-exploration.

10 Medicine ...

Medicine
May cure my body,
But God alone
Can feed my heart.

11 Earth-Arrival-Journey ...

Earth-arrival-journey
Is beautiful.

12 Heaven-Arrival-Journey ...

Heaven-arrival-journey
Is peaceful.

13 We Cannot Hide ...

We cannot hide
Either from God
Or from time.

14 Heart-Flowers ...

Heart-flowers
God dearly treasures.

15 Mind-Knowledge ...

Mind-knowledge
Can be quite often
Uncertain.

16 Heart-Wisdom ...

Heart-wisdom
Is always
Absolutely certain.

12 January 2007 ...

12 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

17 Every Morning ...

Every morning
My soul, my heart and I
Arrive
At a higher destination.

18 Every Day ...

Every day
My soul reaches
A sublime height
Of the Unknown.

19 Every Day ...

Every day
My heart reaches
A deeper depth
Of the Unknown.

20 Every Day ...

Every day
My Supreme commands me
To meditate
On the Unknowable.

21 In The Outer Life ...

In the outer life
Ignorance acts like
A thunderbolt.

22 In The Inner Life ...

In the inner life
Ignorance acts like
A dangerous poison.

23 Desire Powerfully Locks ...

Desire powerfully locks
Our heart-door.

24 Aspiration Not Only Unlocks ...

Aspiration not only unlocks
Our heart-door,
But also prayerfully and soulfully
Invites God.

25 Earth’s Beauty ...

Earth’s beauty
My heart devours.

26 Heaven’s Delight ...

Heaven’s Delight
My soul becomes.

13 January 2007 ...

13 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

27 When We Seek ...

When we seek
Soulfully and self-givingly,
A new frontier welcomes us,
Garlands us and embraces us.

28 Those Who Do Not Pray ...

Those who do not pray
And meditate
Will undoubtedly have a future
With no destination.

29 Self-Doubt ...

Self-doubt
Is a very powerful member
Of the mind-society.

30 Non-Believers ...

Non-believers
And disbelievers
Are very rich in obscurity.

31 Self-Indulgence ...

Self-indulgence
Is a fatal flaw.

32 My Heart-Room ...

My heart-room
Is the place from where
I begin everything.

33 I Need The Gods ...

I need the gods
Of the present
And not the gods
Of the long-buried past
To guide and lead
My present life.

34 Every Day ...

Every day
Without fail
I go to God’s
Forgiveness-Feet.

14 January 2007 ...

14 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

35 Every Time My Aspiration ...

Every time my aspiration
Dies down,
God most compassionately
Revives it.

36 Not Every Day ...

Not every day
But every minute
My Lord examines
My surrender-breath.

37 The Miracle Of Miracles ...

The miracle of miracles
Is my constant
God-gratitude-heart.

38 Every Day ...

Every day
My aspiration climbs up
A more difficult mountain-height.

39 A Cheerful Obedience ...

A cheerful obedience
Is all my life needs.

40 A Delightful Surrender ...

A delightful surrender
Is all my heart needs.

41 Ambition Prays To God ...

Ambition prays to God
To be
A monarch.

42 Aspiration Prays To God ...

Aspiration prays to God
To be
A slave of God’s Feet.

15 January 2007 ...

15 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

43 When My Mind Sighs ...

When my mind sighs,
God does not give my life
His Wings to fly.

44 Today ...

Today
My Supreme Lord’s Compassion-Eye
Is calling me.

45 Tomorrow ...

Tomorrow
My Supreme Lord’s Forgiveness-Feet
Will call me.

46 The Day After Tomorrow ...

The day after tomorrow
My Supreme Lord’s Oneness-Heart
Will call me.

47 Our Prayers Save Us ...

Our prayers save us
From making
Small mistakes.

48 Our Meditations Save Us ...

Our meditations save us
From committing
Himalayan blunders.

49 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
How do you expect
Heart-flowers
From your mind-jungle?

50 War ...

War
Takes birth
In the mind-factory.

51 Peace ...

Peace
Takes birth
In the heart-sea.

16 January 2007 ...

16 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

52 I Am Chosen ...

I am chosen,
Divinely chosen,
Supremely chosen,
To be a sleeplessly God-hungry soul
To manifest the Light
Of the Absolute Supreme
Throughout the length and breadth
Of the world.

53 The Heart Has To Cry ...

The heart has to cry
Helplessly and ceaselessly.
Only then will God not remain
A far cry.

54 I Lived ...

I lived
In the desire-market
For many, many years.

55 I Am Now Living ...

I am now living
In the vastness
Of aspiration-sky.

56 The Mind ...

The mind
Wants to possess
Everything that it sees.

57 The Heart ...

The heart
Wants to embrace
Everything that it feels.

58 Mistakes ...

Mistakes
Are to be discarded,
And not to be dragged along.

59 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme,
Will there ever come a time
In my life
When I shall be able to say
That I am no longer ‘mine’?

60 When I Walk Along My Life-Road ...

When I walk along my life-road,
I suffer and suffer,
But when I walk along my heart-road,
I prosper and prosper.

61 My Aspiration-Journey ...

My aspiration-journey
And my dedication-journey
Will never come to a halt.

17 January 2007 ...

17 January 2007

Varna, Bulgaria

62 My Newborn Life ...

My newborn life
Sings only
God-gratitude-songs.

63 The Beyond ...

The Beyond
Is the only necessity
Of our earth-bound life.

64 Have No Complaints ...

Have no complaints.
Your life will be empty
Of regrets.

65 We Live In A World ...

We live in a world
Where ignorance every day
Torments us.

66 Mind-Closed People ...

Mind-closed people
Eventually turn into tyrants.

67 I Decorate ...

I decorate
My Lord’s Feet
With my heart’s
Streaming tears.

68 My Lord ...

My Lord
Decorates my heart
With His blossoming Smiles.

69 The Seeds ...

The seeds
Of genuine aspiration
Never fail us.

20 January 2007 ...

20 January 2007

Bangkok, Thailand

70 Philosophy ...

Philosophy
Is for the mind-dwellers.

71 Spirituality ...

Spirituality
Is for the heart-divers.

72 Austerity ...

Austerity
Is for the cave-lovers.

73 My Lord’s Beauty ...

My Lord’s Beauty
Illumines my heart.

74 My Lord’s Power ...

My Lord’s Power
Dazzles my eyes.

75 My Lord’s Love ...

My Lord’s Love
Embraces each and every breath
Of my life.

76 The Reasoning Mind ...

The reasoning mind
Is a revolving door
With no escape.

77 The Aspiring Heart ...

The aspiring heart
Circles God’s Feet
While enjoying
Divinity’s Freedom.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 58, Agni Press, 2008
