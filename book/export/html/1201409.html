15617

O tears of my mind,
I do not trust you,
I do not need you,
I want nothing to do with you!
You are false,
You are deceitful!
You want to feed me
With false satisfaction-promises.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 157, Agni Press, 1991
