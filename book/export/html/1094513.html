15 God Says To Me ...

God says to me,
“My child, true,
You have gone astray.
But do I not have Love-Power
To draw you back?
Lo, you are back
And once more safe
Inside My Heart-Home.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 11, Agni Press, 2002
