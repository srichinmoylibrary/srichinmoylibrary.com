My Gratitude-Heart Is Truly Fond Of

My Lord Supreme,
     My gratitude-heart
Is truly fond of
      Your Compassion-Eye.

Many Have Come and Many Have Gone

Many have come and many have gone.
Many will come and many will go.
But I shall stay inside the Boat Supreme
To crown my sky-vast and sun-high dream.

The Power of Love

of power, man will have a new name: God.

Then only you can have
Union with God.

Divinity’s Compassion

Divinity’s Compassion
     Expects
Humanity’s perfection.

God’s Vision-Manifestation-Team

I may not be or cannot be a member of
God’s Vision-Manifestation-Team on earth,
But I dearly love the Team’s God-oneness-heart,
And profoudly admire its God-fulness-hands.
But I do know and I do feel that
God will eventually make me a member of
His Vision-Manifestation-Team.

Every Aspiration-Day Begins (1)

Every aspiration-day begins
      With new possibilities.
Every aspiration-day ends
      With new achievements.

Morning Is the Time to Accept

Morning is the time
To accept God’s Love-Light.
Noon is the time
To become God’s Love-Height.
Evening is the time
To reveal God’s Love-Delight.

No Path Can Be Too Hard for You

No path can be too hard for you
If you have one God-Gift:
      Faith in yourself.

With My Human Songs

With my human songs
     I wash my Lord’s
Compassion-Forgiveness-Feet.

They Came Only to Go

They came only to go,
But I came to stay,
Forever to stay,
Inside the Golden Boat
Of my Beloved Lord Supreme.

God, If You Break My Head for Me

God, if You break my head for me,
     I shall break my heart for You.
God, since I shall not disappoint You,
     I cannot expect disappointment from You, either.
God, You start the game and I shall end it.

Many, Many Lives Ago

Many, many lives ago,
God taught me
How to love Him
And how to serve Him
      Unconditionally.

What Can Equal God’s Ecstasy?

What can equal God’s Ecstasy?
Man’s bold promise to Him
To become another God.

Let My Yesterday Peacefully Sleep

Let my yesterday peacefully sleep.
Let my today brilliantly dream.
Let my tomorrow powerfully awake.

     Sleeplessly wild.

     An uncertain wager.

     A sad failure-plan.
Guru Chinmoy, Guru Chinmoy,
     God and man’s broken toy.

     All cute and wild.

     An earth-climbing wager.

     God’s own Rainbow-Plan.

     God and man’s fondness-toy.

Happy Are My Eyes (1)

Happy are my eyes
To see You, my Lord.
Happy is my heart
To feel You, my Lord.
Happy is my life
To feel You, my Lord,
As my own, very own.

Be Not Afraid of God

Be not afraid of God,
      Be not afraid.
Your sweet reunion
      Need not be delayed.

Enthusiasm Is God’s God-Beauty

Enthusiasm is
God’s God-Beauty
In His seeker-children.

My Lord, Break Me

My Lord, break me.
  My vital is ready.
My Lord, melt me.
  My heart is ready.
My Lord, mould me.
  My mind is ready.
My Lord, make me.
  I am fully ready.

My Lord, Kick Me

My Lord, kick me.
That is the only way
I can move forward.

My Lord Supreme, Before I Die

My Lord Supreme,
Before I die
I shall leave only one legacy:
My devoted life’s gratitude-heart.

Do Make My Life Three Dimensional

My Lord Supreme,
Do make my life three dimensional:
“Try, cry, fly!”

My Lord Supreme, Now Is the Time

My Lord Supreme, now is the time
For me to give You
My inner life of gratitude.

Success-Day I Like

My Lord Supreme,
Success-day I like.
Progress-sun I love.

O My Pilot Supreme

Your outer Name is Concern-Sky.

What I Wanted

What I wanted was a flicker of hope,
But my Lord came and stood before me
To give me His Infinity’s All.

My Hero God-Loving Soul

  God-serving soul,

  The supreme Goal of Goals.

Rich God Knows How to Forgive

Rich God knows
How to forgive,
How to forget.
Poor human beings
     Know neither.

O My Heart, Your Fondest Dream

O my heart, your fondest dream
Is constant God-satisfaction.
And my bravest promise is
  God-manifestation,
     Here and now.

Concentration-Power

Concentration-power I had,
      And I still have.
Meditation-light I had,
      And I still have.
Contemplation-delight I had,
      And I still have.
But God-Manifestation-Smile I never had,
      And I still do not have.

Be Thou

Be Thou my all.
Be Thou my body
That I may wake.
Be Thou my vital
That I may run.
Be Thou my mind
That I may fly.
Be Thou my heart
That I may dive.
Be Thou my soul
That I may reveal.
Be Thou my Goal
That I may fulfil.
Be Thou my all
That I may be only Yours.
