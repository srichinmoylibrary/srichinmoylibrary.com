18. You are special, you are divine

You are gifted.
Therefore
The world appreciates you.

You are special.
Therefore
The world admires you.

You are divine.
Therefore
Both God and the world
Love you
And
Love you,
Forever
And
Forever.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 5, Agni Press, 1974
