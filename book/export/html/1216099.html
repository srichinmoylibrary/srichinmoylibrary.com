470. The heart of forgiveness

The Hand of blessing:
How far is it?
Very near.

The Eye of compassion:
Where is it?
It is in your self-giving.

The Heart of forgiveness:
What is it?
Perfect self-extension.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 10, Agni Press, 1974
