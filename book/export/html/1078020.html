373. The heart's happiness

The unaspiring heart
Gives us a fearful happiness.

The aspiring heart
Gives us a soulful happiness.

The realised heart
Gives us a fruitful,
Birthless
And
Deathless happiness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
