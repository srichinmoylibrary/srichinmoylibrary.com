Se Chai Tomar Hasi

Se chai tomar hasi
Sneha rasi
Ami chai amare
Hridaye tomar
Parane sabare
Herite khelite

Translation:

He desires Your Smile,
Your affection-wealth.
I desire myself
In the heart of all human beings
To sport and become one, indivisible.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, I sing only for You, , 1974
