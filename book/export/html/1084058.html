32. He hates and he loves

   He hates prosperity
        Because
Prosperity harmed his humility.   He hates success
        Because
Success ruined his progress.   He loves sincerity
        Because
Sincerity never deserted him.   He loves humility
        Because
Humility is God's universal
   Oneness-light,
   Oneness-might,
   Oneness-flight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
