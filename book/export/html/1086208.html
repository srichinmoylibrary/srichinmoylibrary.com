Editor's note

The following poems on the theme "Here and Now" were written by Sri Chinmoy during the six-month period from 29 May to 1 November 2000. He recited many of them at various Peace Concerts in America and abroad during this time. The date and location of each Peace Concert is mentioned before each group of poems.
