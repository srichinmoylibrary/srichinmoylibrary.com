1355 This Game Of Progress

May outer sun is progressing
By emptying itself.
Alas, some day
This game will be over.
My inner sun is progressing
By revealing itself -
An everlasting
And ever-transcending game.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 14, Agni Press, 1981
