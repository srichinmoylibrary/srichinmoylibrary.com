40.

My Lord,
I am fully awake.
Cheerfully and self-givingly
I am at Your Command.
"My child,
Are you sure?
I plead with you,
This time do not
Eat your promise
Like previous times."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 2, Agni Press, 1998
