46. Consciousness

Earth-consciousness
Slowly and steadily
Ascends
To the highest Truth.

Heaven-consciousness
Soulfully and speedily
Descends
To cure the malady
Of human life:
The imperfection of ignorance-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
