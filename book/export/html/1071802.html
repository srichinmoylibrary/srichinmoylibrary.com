April 22

How to conquer bondage? Tell your body that your name is Aspiration. Tell your vital that your name is Determination. Tell your mind that your name is Concentration. Tell your heart that your name is Compassion. Finally see eye to eye with your soul when it says that your name is Illumination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
