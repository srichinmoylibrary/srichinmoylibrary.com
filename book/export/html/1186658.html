1012

What is he doing?
He is sleeping with his rainbow-dream
Inside his silence-heart.
What else is he doing?
He is making the final revision
Of his surrender-life-script.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 11, Agni Press, 1983
