Jahara Khuleche Hiyar Duar

Jahara khuleche hiyar duar
Tomare tahara labhibe
Taba prema bani bishwa bhubane
Prachar tahara karibe

Translation:

Those who have opened
Their heart-doors to You
Will have You as their own.
Your Love-Message,
Throughout the length
And breadth of the world,
They will proclaim.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giti mala, , 2000
