628. I must not stay here

Where the world is quiet
I must go.
Where all troubles have ended
I must go.
Here life is death;
I must not stay here.
There, death is death, life is life.
I must go, I must go.
There, life is life
And not, like here, sleep.
No, I must not stay here.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
