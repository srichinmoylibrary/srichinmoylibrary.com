1. Thank you, my Lord

Thank you, my Lord,
For unwinding my vital life.

Thank you, my Lord,
For unlocking my mental life.

Thank you, my Lord,
For illumining my psychic life.

Thank you, my Lord,
For fulfilling my dreaming life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 1, Agni Press, 1974
