635 Matchless Treasure...

MATCHLESS TREASURE

Father, my aspiration-cry
Is my matchless treasure.
Your Satisfaction-smile
Is Your matchless Treasure.
One day my cry
Shall claim You.
One day Your Smile
Shall employ me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
