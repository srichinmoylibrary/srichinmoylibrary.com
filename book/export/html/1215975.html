349. The touch

In my world
Light is the touch
Of a fulfilling hand,
Delight is the touch
Of a vanishing hand,
Perfection is the touch
Of a vanished hand,
Peace is the touch
Of a broken hand,
Concern is the touch
Of a weak hand,
Compassion is the touch
Of an unusual hand.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 7, Agni Press, 1974
