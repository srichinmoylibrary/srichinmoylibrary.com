199. I love

I love the presence
of Mother Earth.
She tells me
She will give me
Everything she creates.

I love the presence
of Father Heaven.
He tells me
He will give me
His Light-Power
to equal Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
