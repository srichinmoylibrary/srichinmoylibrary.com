11436

God does not create problems for us.
It is we who create problems
  For ourselves.
  God comes to us as an opportunity
To solve all the problems
  Of our life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 12, Agni Press, 1999
