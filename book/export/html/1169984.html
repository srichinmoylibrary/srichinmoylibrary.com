629. Lock Me, O Lord

Lock me, O Lord, and then throw away the key,
To please Thy Heart's Will.
Yet
I shall love Thee,
Only Thee,
For mine is the life
Only to please Thee.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
