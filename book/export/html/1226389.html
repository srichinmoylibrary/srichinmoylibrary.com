Abar Jabo Tomar Kachhe

Abar jabo tomar kachhe
Abar jabo
Kritagyatar hriday diye
Tomai pabo

Translation:

Once more I shall come to You.
Once more I shall come to You.
We shall get You
With our gratitude-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 8a, , 1981
