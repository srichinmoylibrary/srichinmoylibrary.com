16.

I do not enter into
My mind's hesitation-room.
Therefore, I make progress fast
I do not enter into
My heart's expectation-room.
Therefore, I make progress faster.
But I do enter into
My soul's satisfaction-room.
Therefore, I make progress fastest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Fast, faster, fastest progress, Agni Press, 1994
