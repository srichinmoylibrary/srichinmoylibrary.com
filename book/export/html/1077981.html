334. My God-Life on earth

My God-life on earth
Is not only a perpetual possibility
But also a
God-inevitability.

My animal-human life in Heaven
Is not only a rare possibility
But also a
God-impossibility.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
