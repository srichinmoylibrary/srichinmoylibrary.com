17598

O my heart,
Do not indulge in depression.
O my mind,
Do not indulge in exasperation.
You both
Have extraordinary things to do
To fulfil the Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 176, Agni Press, 1993
