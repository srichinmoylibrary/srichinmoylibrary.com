Death is the end, death is the beginning.

To the pleasure-lover,
  Death is the end.

To the joy-lover,
  Death is the beginning.

To the pleasure-lover,
  Death is the end
     Of his desire-life.

To the joy-lover,
  Death is the beginning
     Of his aspiration-inquiry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The silence of death, Agni Press, 1973
