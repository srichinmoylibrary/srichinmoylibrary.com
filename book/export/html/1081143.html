212. You teach

You teach
The world how to reach
God-Height.
I tell
The world to enjoy
Ego-night.

You teach
The world how to be
Perfection-tree.
I tell
The world to drink
Ignorance-sea.

From: Sri Chinmoy, From the Source to the Source, Agni Press, 1974
