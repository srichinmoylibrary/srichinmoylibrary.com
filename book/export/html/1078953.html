5.

O my insecurity-heart,
Leave me alone!
I want to make progress fast.
O my impurity-mind,
Leave me alone!
I want to make progress faster.
O my complexity-life,
Leave me alone!
I want to make progress fastest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Fast, faster, fastest progress, Agni Press, 1994
