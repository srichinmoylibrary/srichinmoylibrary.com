In my letter to God
I sent only one short message:
"My Lord, I love You only."
God immediately sent me
An express telegram:
"I am coming. I need you badly."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 34, Agni Press, 1984
