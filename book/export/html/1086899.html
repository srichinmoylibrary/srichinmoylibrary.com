I have a beautiful smile, I am a soulful cry

1.

I love God constantly.

2.

I need God constantly.

3.

I pray to God every morning to give me joy.

4.

I meditate on God every evening to give me peace.

5.

I get so much joy from my prayer and meditation.

6.

I sing a soulful song in the morning to make my heart happy.

7.

I sing a prayerful song in the morning to make my mind happy.

8.

I sing a powerful song in the morning to make my body happy.

9.

I sing a beautiful song in the morning to make my eyes happy.

10.

I sing a song of surrender to God in the morning to make God happy.

11.

Every day I exercise for at least ten minutes to keep my body perfectly fit.

12.

Every day with my hands I make at least one beautiful thing.

13.

Every day inside my mind I create at least one beautiful thought.

14.

My mind wants to have quantity-bushes.

15.

My heart wants to have quality-roses.

16.

My mind wants greatness-height.

17.

My heart wants goodness-depth.

18.

I thank my heart every day.

19.

Every I pray to God to teach me how to smile with my heart beautifully, more beautifully and most beautifully.

20.

I have two regular teachers: my heart and my mind. My heart teaches me how to hope, and my mind teaches me how to promise.

21.

My mind becomes tired after praying to God for three minutes.

22.

My heart does not become tired even after it has meditated on God for fifteen minutes.

23.

Day in, day out, my mind asks me to speak to God.

24.

My heart begs me only to listen to God.

25.

My heart wants me to be perfect in everything.

26.

My soul wants me to be excellent in everything.

27.

I may not know what perfection looks like, but I do know what excellence looks like. Therefore, for the time being, I pray to God for excellence and not perfection.

28.

My mind tells me that I need only God the Power.

29.

My heart tells me that I need only God the Compassion.

30.

I tell myself that I need only God the Smile.

31.

I sincerely respect my teachers.

32.

To my friends I give gifts which they never expected.

33.

When kids of my age fight in the street, I ask God what I should do. He says, "Look at them and pray to Me. Do not do anything else."

34.

When pray to God to make me the tallest person, He cries and cries. When I pray to God to make me the purest person, He smiles and smiles, and also says, "My child, I am fulfilling your prayer."

35.

When I pray to God for stupid things, He cries and cries. When I pray to God for simplicity, honesty and purity, He smiles and smiles. He says to me, "My child, you already have them, but I shall give you more, infinitely more. "

36.

Nobody has to force me to confess my mistakes.

37.

I have made many mistakes, but God tells me not to worry. He just asks me not to repeat them. He also tells me to be careful not to make any new mistakes.

38.

I am so fortunate to be a member of a very spiritual family. I have such wonderful parents: they never quarrel.

39.

I have such wonderful spiritual brothers and sisters: they also never quarrel and fight.

40.

I have such wonderful grandparents. They pray and meditate at least eight hours a day. When I am of their age, I shall do the same.

41.

Every day I learn so much by listening to my elders.

42.

I do not need appreciation. I need satisfaction.

43.

I have already read quite a few prayer books, and I am sure I shall read countless prayer books in this lifetime.

44.

When I grow up, I shall start reading spiritual books of a serious type.

45.

I admire my mother's affection.

46.

I admire my father's wisdom.

47.

I am proud of myself because my parents trust me.

48.

My parents are proud of me because I obey them.

49.

My father tells me that inside each serious problem there is a golden opportunity.

50.

My mother tells me that I must never wait for a second chance. I must do immediately whatever has to be done.

51.

My mother tells me not to criticise anybody.

52.

My father tells me not to ignore anybody.

53.

My father tells me never to gossip.

54.

My mother tells me never to irritate anybody.

55.

I have learned from my parents not to hate anybody. Therefore, I try to like each and every human being.

56.

My mind has a special love for my father because he is very famous.

57.

My heart has a very special love for my mother because she is very precious.

58.

My father tells me that Heaven loves me for what I have: a beautiful smile.

59.

My mother tells me that earth loves me for what I am: a soulful cry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I have a beautiful smile, I am a soulful cry, Agni Press, 1992
