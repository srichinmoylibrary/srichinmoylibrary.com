9393 If You Want To Give

If the world wants to give you
In small measure,
Don't be a fool.
Just take it.

If you want to give to the world
Without measure,
Don't be a fool.
Just do it.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 94, Agni Press, 1983
