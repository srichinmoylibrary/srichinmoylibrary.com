10 We Must Not Ask God ...

We must not ask God
Why, how, when and where.
We must tell God
That we are sleeplessly ready
With our eagerness-heart
To please Him in His own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 10, Agni Press, 2002
