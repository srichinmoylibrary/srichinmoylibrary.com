578 Practice Makes Perfect...

PRACTICE MAKES PERFECT

Practice makes perfect.
God believes in this theory;
Therefore
He practised Compassion.
Lo, He has become Compassion,
Unconditional Compassion.

Practice makes perfect.
God believes in this theory;
Therefore
He practised surrender.
Lo, He has become perfect Surrender,
Surrender to man's countless desires.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 12, Agni Press, 1973
