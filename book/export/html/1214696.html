1295.

When we quote
Our Lord Beloved Supreme perfectly,
His Joy knows no bounds.
When we misquote
Our Lord Supreme consciously and deliberately,
His unhappiness, too, knows no bounds.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, Part 13, Agni Press, 1996
