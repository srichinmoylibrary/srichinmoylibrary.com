523. Any time

Any time of the year
I can see God.

Any time of the month
I can love God.

Any time of the week
I can please God.

Any time of the day
I can own God.

Any minute of the hour
I can become God.

Any second of the minute
I can unburden myself
Of my self-imposed responsibility.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 11, Agni Press, 1974
