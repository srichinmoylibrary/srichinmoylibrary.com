Ogo Narayan Tomar Charan Amar Bhuban

Ogo narayan tomar charan
Amar bhuban jenechi ekata jenechi
Tomare pujite tomare tushite
Tomar lagiya darashan taba peyechi

Bajaye Mor Hriday Bina

Bajaye mor hriday bina
Karbo tomar chitta joy
Tomar khamai mukti pabe
Jiban maran bhoy

Translation:

Playing my heart-violin,
I shall conquer Your Heart.
In Your Forgiveness,
I shall achieve liberation
From my fear of life and fear of death.

Taba Karunai Phutite Chahigo

Taba karunai phutite chahigo
Nahe mama mahimai
Amar jiban amar maran
Shanti labhite chai

Translation:

My Lord,
With Your Compassion I wish to blossom
And not with my glories.
My life and my death desperately need
The peace of silence.

Basanar Jal Bhitare Bahire

Basanar jal bhitare bahire
Eshana bahni nai
Tai nahi pai prabhur charane
Nimesher tare thai

Translation:

I see aspiration-flames nowhere.
I see a desire-net within and without me.
Therefore, not even for a fleeting second,
I see myself seated
At the Feet of my Lord Supreme.

Dake Dake Amai Dake

Dake dake amai dake premer Brindaban
Shanto habe amar chapal paran tanu man

Translation:

The Love Divine from Lord Krishna's Brindaban
Time and again is beckoning me.
Therefore, my restlessness-vital, restlessness-body
      And restlessness-mind
Will soon be inundated with peace.

Jiban Amar Maran Amar

Jiban amar maran amar
Sabar tushite chahe
Bidhatar joy sabar hridaye
Paramanande gahe

Translation:

Both my life and death are eager to please
Each and every human being on earth,
And my life and death are singing
In ecstasy supreme
My Lord’s Victory in the heart of humanity.

Dure Dure Shanti Dure

Dure dure shanti dure bahu dure
Britha kandi shanti lagi hiya pure

Translation:

Far, very far: peace is, far, very far.
Alas, in vain, in my heart-citadel
I am crying for peace.

Shunyata Sei Je Purnata Hasi

Shunyata sei je purnata hasi
Ananda bichi mala rashi rashi

Translation:

Emptiness is nothing other than
The smile of plenitude.
Therein abide the dancing waves of ecstasy.

Marta Dharai Pagol Sama Ghure Berai

Marta dharai pagol sama ghure berai
Timir maner bane
Dure dure bahu dure rekhechi hai
Mor hiya tapane

Translation:

I am roaming in my darkness-mind-jungle
Carrying the insanity of the world insane.
I have kept aside, away from me, my own heart-sun.
