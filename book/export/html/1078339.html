692 No Disguise...

NO DISGUISE

No disguise;
I love You.
My heart loves You only.

No disguise;
I need You.
My life needs You only.

No disguise;
You and You alone:
My Eternity's all,
My sweet beloved Supreme!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
