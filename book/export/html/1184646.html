318. What to do?

What to do?
Don't you know?
Stick to one thing:
Love God.
Stick to something more:
Obey God.
Finally, one thing more:
Think that God is for you to use at every moment
In the march of changes
In ageless time.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
