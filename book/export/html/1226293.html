Lift Up Your Eyes

Lift up your eyes.
Beautiful they are
From near, afar.
Lift up your arms.
Powerful they are,
Duty’s tower.
Lift up your head.
Watch Heaven’s dance,
A oneness-trance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 7a, , 1981
