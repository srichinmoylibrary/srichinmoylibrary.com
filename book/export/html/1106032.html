4.

My Lord Supreme,
May my curiosity about spirituality
Come to an end.
May my heart's sleepless hunger for You
Come to the fore
And make You feel that I am all ready
For You, only for You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My sweet Father-Lord, where are you?, Agni Press, 1999
