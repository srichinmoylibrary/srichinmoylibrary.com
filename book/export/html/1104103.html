8 February 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your unconditional Grace
Is the only way for me to make
Any progress in any field.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 February 2000, 7:29 a.m. Before lifting 450 lbs. with each arm simultaneously (Total: 800 lbs.) New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
