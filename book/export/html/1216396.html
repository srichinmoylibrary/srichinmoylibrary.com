761. My peerless discovery

I am not God;
That all of you know.
I am the future God;
That you do not know.
But
I love you all;
Therefore
I gladly share with you
My peerless discovery.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 16, Agni Press, 1974
