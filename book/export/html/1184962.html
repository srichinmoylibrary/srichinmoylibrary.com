634. Not knowing

Not knowing what to do
He made friends
With deep doubts.

Not knowing what to say
He invited the ugly looking fear.

Now knowing whom to love
He loved his own self-form
Dearly and deeply.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
