5037

He thinks that he is too poor,
And his poverty is not allowing him
To think of God.

You think that you are too rich,
And therefore your riches
Are not allowing you
To think of God.

I think that I am neither poor nor rich.
Therefore God the Compassion
Has given me the capacity
To become man the aspiration.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 51, Agni Press, 1984
