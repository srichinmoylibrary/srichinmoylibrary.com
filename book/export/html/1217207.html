32. The difference

What is the difference
   Between
The life of art
   And
The art of life?

The life of art
Is man-aspiration
   And
Man-dedication.

The art of life
Is God-realisation
   And
God-manifestation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Yesterday I was a crying dream, Agni Press, 1975
