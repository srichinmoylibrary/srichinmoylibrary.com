September 20

Who comes first, God or man? If man comes first, I have to give up God. If God comes first, I have not to give up any man. For man is God's conscious life-breath.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
