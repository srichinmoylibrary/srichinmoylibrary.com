42. Don't forget

O my puny body,
You need me
     And
My aspiration
To realise God.
Don't forget
This supremely important fact.

O my large soul,
You need me
     And
My dedication
To manifest God.
Don't forget
This eternally significant fact.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
