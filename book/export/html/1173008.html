85. Only three strides ahead

Only one stride ahead:
God's Compassion.

Only two strides ahead:
My transformation.

Only three strides ahead:
God's Satisfaction-Smile
And
My gratitude-cry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, part 1, Agni Press, 1979
