27 April 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I need Your blessingful,
   Infinite Grace
To change my inner life
   Completely
And my outer life
   Immediately.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 7:45 a.m. Before lifting 220 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 440 lbs.) New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
