30. His body's shallow strength

   When he thinks,
He thinks from his mind's
   Pygmy height.   When he speaks,
He speaks from his vital's
   Unmeasured length.   When he acts,
He acts from his body's
   Shallow strength.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 4, Agni Press, 1974
