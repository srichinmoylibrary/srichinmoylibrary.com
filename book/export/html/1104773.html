37.

My Lord Supreme
Has repeatedly told me
That He will not be able
To treasure my meditation
As long as I have
A complexity-mind,
An insecurity-heart,
A hostility-vital
And
An impurity-body.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My prayer-life my meditation-heart, Agni Press, 1999
