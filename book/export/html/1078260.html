COMPLAINTS AGAINST HUMANITY

A poet's complaint:
He is not appreciated
By humanity.

A philosopher's complaint:
He is not understood
By humanity.

A seeker's complaint:
He is ignored
By humanity.

A lover's complaint:
He is criticised
By humanity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
