329. Do you know?

Do you know that my heart
Is a sacred island?
Perhaps you know.

Do you know that my mind
Is a secret island?
Perhaps you know.

Do you know that my vital
Is an ignored island?
Perhaps you know.

Do you know that my body
Is a treasure-island?
Ah, you didn't know?

Then learn it once and for all.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 7, Agni Press, 1974
