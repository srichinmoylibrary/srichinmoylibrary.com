931 Not Really Lost...

NOT REALLY LOST

Lost opportunities
Are not really lost.
Just dive deep within.
You can and shall discover them
Once again.

Lost God
Is not really lost.
Just try to go beyond the domain
Of your doubt-bound head.
You can and shall discover God
Once again.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
