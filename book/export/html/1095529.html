37 My Child, If You Come To Me ...

“My child, if you come to Me
Even beyond Eternity’s Eye and Heart,
I shall not mind,
As long as you come to Me,
Enter into My Heart
And become the Beauty of My Dream
And the Duty of My Reality.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 22, Agni Press, 2004
