Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 139

13801

My heart's eagerness-power
Brings me
My life's victory-crown.

13802

O my soul,
Do remove my life's
Heaviness-despair!

13803

God tells me that
My heart's enthusiasm-role
Pleases Him most.

13804

My Lord Supreme,
Do save me, save me, save me!
Save my life, save my life
From success-traps!

13805

If I do not aspire
More soulfully,
More cheerfully
And more self-givingly,
Then my lethargy-disease
Will remain incurable.

13806

O my mind-dictator,
Look, look!
My heart-liberator
Is fast approaching me.

13807

I am absolutely sure
That I have been chosen
By my Lord Beloved Supreme
To do something very special for Him.

13808

Self-advertisement
Is the worst kind of investment
In my life's perfection-dream.

13809

My Lord Supreme,
I am never afraid
Of my failure-burden,
But I am terribly afraid
Of Your Displeasure-Eye.

13810

I start each day
With my heart's morning beauty,
And I end each day
With my soul's evening fragrance.

13811

Only my heart's silence-smile
Is invited by my Lord Supreme
To strike His Victory-Gong.

13812

Every morning
My soul's silence-light
Unconditionally feeds
My heart's mounting faith-flames.

13813

Centuries pass by
Before God's golden Dreams
Become man's silver realities.

13814

Eternally beautiful
Is my God-realisation-smile.
Infinitely fruitful
Is my God-manifestation-cry.

13815

Every day
My Lord Supreme blesses me
With His Compassion-Smile,
So that I will make a monumental attempt
To go beyond
My daily frustration-horizon.

13816

A pure humility-heart
Is indeed
A sure God-intoxication-life.

13817

God Himself
Is lengthening your progress-strides
Because you are cheerfully welcoming
Each and every new God-seeker.

13818

God does not care for you:
This is your giant
Mental hallucination!

13819

Immortality is beckoning you
Because you are sleeplessly singing
The oneness-song
Inside God's Heart-Garden.

13820

Aspiration-lovers
And determination-warriors
Are bound to win,
Bound to win
The supreme victory for God
In the battlefield of life
Here on earth.

13821

Without Eternity's direct,
Secret and sacred help,
Nobody has the chance to become
A supremely choice instrument of God.

13822

My Lord Supreme tells me
That He wants to live
Only inside my heart's
Silence-fragrance-blossoms.

13823

There is always a long distance
Between my mind's loud voice
And my heart's hallowed
God-fulfilment-choice.

13824

You chose yourself
Foolishly
Only to lose God
Miserably.

13825

An unconditional God-lover
Never wants to be
Off-duty.

13826

Perfection-choice
Is the very beginning
Of a God-satisfying life.

13827

My morning promise to God
Is that I shall never measure
But only treasure
His Compassion-Light.

13828

Since the very beginning of creation
There has always been
A most deplorable shortage of
Compassion-eyes, forgiveness-hearts
And gratitude-lives.

13829

How can you become
An aspiration-specialist
If you do not have
A gratitude-heart
And a surrender-life?

13830

Nobody told me,
But I definitely know
That my purity-heart's gratitude-life
Is the only shortcut
To my God-realisation.

13831

What can a God-Smile do?
A God-Smile can glorify
The human in me
And manifest the divine in me.

13832

Unlike us,
God's Heart does not believe in
"Do not disturb" signs.

13833

My enthusiasm-heart
Quickly and unmistakably sows
Perfection-satisfaction-seeds.

13834

Each human being
Is a fulfilment-blossoming
Hope-dream-promise of God.

13835

I love to live
Either inside my heart's hunger-cry
Or inside my soul's feast-smile.

13836

At every moment
God wants us
To smile at Him outwardly
And cry for Him inwardly.

13837

Every day
My Lord Supreme proudly plays
With my mind-ball —
Simplicity,
And my heart-ball —
Purity.

13838

God sleeplessly treasures
The purity-smile
Of my heart
And the gratitude-cry
Of my life.

13839

Expect many changes,
Especially in your inner life,
If you want to make progress
Faster than the fastest.

13840

Even an iota
Of love-peace and oneness-power
From God
Can save not only today's fearful world
But also tomorrow's doubtful world.

13841

My Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
I came into the world
To make You happy.
At the end of my journey's close,
You will take me back into Yourself
To make me happy.

13842

My heart,
I am so proud of you —
That you can see God
Even when He is far away.
My mind,
I am so ashamed of you —
That you cannot see God
Even when He is right in front of you.

13843

My Lord's Whisper-Voice
Is my life's
Only illumination-perfection-choice.

13844

My heart lovingly
And gratefully
Claims the world.
My mind constantly
And endlessly
Blames the world.

13845

Every day
God blessingfully offers a message
To each and every earth-dweller:
Self-giving.

13846

A seeker's heart
Is a rainbow-hope
And a seeker's life
Is a sun-vast promise.

13847

I am illumining
My life's ignorance-night
With my soul-eternity's
Silence-light.

13848

Alas, today despair-tears
Have blinded not only my heart
But also my entire earth-existence.

13849

Alas,
When am I going to stop using
My criticism-microscope
To observe others' dwarf-flaws?

13850

Only a self-giving soul
Can sing the songs
Of world-peace-dreams.

13851

At last,
Today for the first time,
God and I are dreaming of singing
Our oneness-heart-song.

13852

My Lord,
I cannot imagine why You care
For my countless imperfections.
"My child,
I cannot imagine how you can live
Without My Heart's Satisfaction-Smile."

13853

Extremely sweet
Is my surrender-heart.
Exceedingly beautiful
Is my gratitude-life.

13854

Each soulful meditation
Has the capacity to show me
Where God is hiding.

13855

God enjoys
His mightiest Freedom
Only in my heart's
Silence-inundated kingdom.

13856

I have decided
That I shall never touch
The foreign shore
Of insecurity-timidity.

13857

Self-doubt-poison
Is infinitely more dangerous
And more destructive
Than you can ever imagine!

13858

My soul does not need
Any interpreter
When it speaks to my heart.
But my soul badly needs
An interpreter
When it wants to speak to my mind.

13859

May each and every human being
Grow into an immortal
Oneness-peace-heart.

13860

The spirituality-road
Is an uncertainty-road
Only for those who do not listen
To the soulful whispers
Of their blessingful soul.

13861

Good-bye, good-bye,
My old ignorance-friend!
My Heavenward illumination-journey
Begins today, here and now.

13862

Eternal aspiration-lovers
And infinite manifestation-heroes
Are unquestionably first-class instruments
Of the Supreme.

13863

Shatter, shatter
Your mind's insecurity-cage.
Come out, come out
Of your heart's impurity-cave.

13864

Attachment-cries
Are forced by God Himself
To swim in frustration-seas.

13865

Sincerity and purity
Are absolutely necessary
To explore the heart-land.

13866

My Lord's Compassion-Eye
And my own gratitude-breath
Complete each other.

13867

Man does not want
To change.
But poor God is always ready
To change Himself
To create a new world
Of soulful peace and fruitful delight.

13868

O seeker,
Do not be a fool!
Do not stand before the open arms
Of attachment-darkness.

13869

My Lord Supreme,
Your Compassion-Eye
Far surpasses everybody and everything.
Because of it I can still live
In my heart-home.

13870

My surrender-heart
Will be crowned by God Himself
Exactly the way a rainbow
Crowns the earth.

13871

Gratefully
My earth-bound life
Must cry.
Soulfully
My Heaven-free heart
Must fly.

13872

Even when the world sees
Illumination-bloom in your eyes,
It may not recognise you
As its pathfinder.

13873

Precious are peace-moments
In my inner life.
Prosperous are peace-hours
In my outer life.

13874

The self-indulgence-marathon
Of a human being
Is bound to reach its
Destruction-destination
Eventually.

13875

My heart accepts everything.
My mind expects everything.
My vital rejects everything.
My body neglects everything.

13876

My mind,
Are you not aware of the fact
That you have been roaming
In expectation-frustration-country
For a very long time?

13877

In my
aspiration-determination-surrender
To God's Will
I see the blossoming of
My life's perfection.

13878

When I still my mind,
I fulfil my first promise
To God.

13879

My life's soulful satisfaction-song
Is the only song
That God is never tired of hearing.

13880

O seeker,
Never allow your doubting mind
To climb up your heart's
Meditation-mountain!

13881

My Lord's Manifestation-Dream-Song
Is the only song
That I love to sing
Every morning and every evening.

13882

A purity-heart
And a humility-mind
Will discover God everywhere.

13883

Be patient!
Go slowly, steadily and unerringly.
You do not have to worry.
God is not in a hurry.

13884

To treasure loneliness
In order to make fast progress
In the spiritual life
Is utterly absurd.

13885

Long for dynamism!
Your soul will bless you
With a depression-shattering smile.

13886

A life of humility
Is the easiest and most effective way
To spread God's Light
On earth.

13887

My soul and my heart
Are begging
My mind, vital and body
To ride on aspiration-wings
In the small hours of the morning.

13888

O seeker,
Your doubtful mind
Is nothing other than
Confusion-destruction-storm.

13889

Alas,
Neither my heart's aspiration-clock
Nor my life's dedication-clock
Is keeping proper time.

13890

God's Compassion-Eye
Wants to purify me.
God's Forgiveness-Heart
Wants to immortalise me.

13891

If you miss your heart's
Hope-boat,
Then how will you reach your life's
Promise-destination?

13892

What I need
Is a pure heart of newness.
What I need
Is a sure life of fulness.

13893

O my lethargy-body,
You rightly deserve a death-sentence
From my Lord Supreme!

13894

Do you want to do God
A big favour?
Then force yourself
To give up self-importance.

13895

O my mind,
Do not allow yourself
To remain in hesitation-maze
If you want to become
A worthy instrument of God.

13896

It is not possible
For the mechanical life
Of the mind
To care for the heart's
Perfection-promotion.

13897

My sleepless faith-heart
Is my peerless
Confidence-life-builder.

13898

God was waiting,
God is waiting
And God will always wait
For my life's
Perfection-satisfaction-sunrise.

13899

Is there any human being on earth
Who has never suffered
From expectation-headache?

13900

My joy knows no bounds
Because today, for a change,
I am thinking of God only,
I am meditating on God only
And I am depending on God only.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 139, Agni Press, 1991
