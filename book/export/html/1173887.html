955 An Inner Task

I know, I know
Why I have been given an inner task
To perform.
An inner task I have been given
Because it is more than possible
For me to succeed and proceed:
Succeed to please myself
And
Proceed to please
My Eternity's Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 10, Agni Press, 1981
