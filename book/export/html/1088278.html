115.

My Lord Supreme,
I know how to fly,
but I do not know how to cry.
Is it because flying upward is infinitely easier than diving inward? I am not at all in a rush, but I do expect an answer from You, either in the near future or in the distant future.

Nibir Cole

From: Sri Chinmoy, In the amusement-enlightenment Heart-Garden of the Lord Supreme, Agni Press, 1995
