The Only News I Know

The only news I know
Is Bulletins all Day
From Immortality.
The only Shows I see —
Tomorrow and Today —
Perchance Eternity —
The only one I meet
Is God — The only Street
Existence — This traversed
If other news there be —
Or Admirabler Show —
I’ll tell it You —
[Words of Emily Dickinson]

From: Sri Chinmoy, The heart-home of the immortals, , 1979
