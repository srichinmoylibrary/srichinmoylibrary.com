May 9

My morning begins
By prayerfully asking God
Where I should go to please Him.
God says:
"Nowhere!
Just stay inside your heart-garden
And smell the fragrance of your soul-rose
To please Me."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My morning begins, Agni Press, 1996
