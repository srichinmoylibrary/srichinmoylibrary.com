My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 33

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Sanya and Xiamen, China in December 2004.

7 December 2004

Sanya, China

1 The Secret Facts ...

The secret facts
Must be revealed
For the betterment of the world.

2 The Sacred Truth ...

The sacred Truth
Must remain unknown,
If so is the Will of God.

3 In This Mortal Existence ...

In this mortal existence,
The more we can pray,
The more we can meditate,

The sooner we shall be able to arrive
At the Feet of God.

4 Nobody Can Estimate ...

Nobody can estimate
How much damage
The seeker’s disobedience-downfall
Can cause.

5 God Is Personally ...

God is personally
And compassionately concerned
About my each and every heart-breath
In my spiritual life.

6 When God Looks Straight ...

When God looks straight
Into my mind,
My mind fearfully cries.

7 When God Looks Straight ...

When God looks straight
Into my heart,
My heart immediately starts
Singing and dancing.

8 An Unconditional God-Obedience ...

An unconditional God-obedience
Is the longest stride
In our spiritual life.

9 Speaking About God ...

Speaking about God
Is good, very good.

10 Praying To God ...

Praying to God
Is, indeed, better.

11 Surrendering Oneself ...

Surrendering oneself
Totally and unconditionally
To God’s Will
Is infinitely the best.

12 Now That My Lord Is Teaching Me ...

Now that my Lord is teaching me
How to deal with my defeats
And failures,
All my sorrows and all my sufferings
Have totally vanished.

13 Our Inner Freedom-Choice ...

Our inner freedom-choice
Comes directly from God.

14 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That I need no outer degrees
To become
His choicest instrument.

15 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That my invocation
Of His Compassion-Eye
Is absolutely the highest degree
That God will give me
At His choice Hour.

16 Whenever I Forget God’s Presence ...

Whenever I forget God’s Presence
Inside my heart,
My breath becomes
Empty of peace.

17 My Mind-Prayers ...

My mind-prayers
Have a very, very short breath.

18 My Heart-Meditations ...

My heart-meditations
Have a longer than the longest breath.

19 When I Pray And Meditate Regularly ...

When I pray and meditate regularly,
God comes down
And charms my eyes and my heart.

20 When I Do Not Pray And Meditate ...

When I do not pray and meditate
Regularly,
God’s Power-Eye alarms me.

21 At My Master’s Express Command ...

At my Master’s express command,
I have entered into
The God-manifestation-field.

22 God Does Not Mind ...

God does not mind
When my mind takes full credit
For our success,
But my soul and my heart
Dislike it vehemently.

23 To Shorten My Doubt ...

To shorten my doubt
And lengthen my faith,
Every day I pray
To my Lord Supreme.

24 Each Approach ...

Each approach
To God’s Heart-Home
Is special,
Plus unique.

25 God’s Promise-Forgetfulness ...

God’s Promise-forgetfulness
Is a zero possibility.

26 God’s Late Arrival ...

God’s late Arrival
Is an absurdity.

27 My Mind Thinks ...

My mind thinks
That God’s Love for my mind
Is not true.

28 My Heart Feels ...

My heart feels
That God’s Love for my heart
Is Truth itself.

29 Today My Lord ...

Today my Lord
Is asking my heart
To be with Him
In creating a happy world.

30 When The Time Arrives ...

When the time arrives,
My Lord says
That I must be with Him
While He creates
A perfect world.

9 December 2004 ...

9 December 2004

Xiamen, China

31 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I do not want to be
Your worshipper.

32 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I do not want to be
Your adorer.

33 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I do not want to be
Your lover.

34 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I do not want to be
Your server.

35 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I do not want to be
Your soldier.

36 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I do not want to be
Your partner.

37 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I want to be always
Your Compassion-Feet-dreamer.

10 December 2004 ...

10 December 2004

Xiamen, China

38 Every Morning ...

Every morning
My Lord expects from me
A great surprise.
At noon He expects
A greater surprise.
In the evening He expects
The greatest surprise.
The first surprise:
Sincere tears.
The second surprise:
Sincere smiles.
The third surprise:
Sincere surrender.

39 God’s Flute-Heart-Sweetness ...

God’s Flute-Heart-Sweetness
Failed to transform
My life.

40 God’s Thunder-Eye-Kicks ...

God’s Thunder-Eye-Kicks
Have transformed
My life.

41 My Prayers ...

My prayers
Sweeten
My God-crying heart.

42 My Meditations ...

My meditations
Awaken
My sleeping life.

43 My God-Surrender Expedites ...

My God-surrender expedites
My God-journey
To my God-Destination.

12 December 2004 ...

12 December 2004

Xiamen, China

44 May Each Thought Of Mine ...

May each thought of mine
Embody
Rainbow-beauty.

45 Never Wait ...

Never wait
For the next aspiration-train —
Never!

46 O My Mind ...

O my mind,
You must not try to correct
Others’ way of life.

47 For Centuries And Centuries ...

For centuries and centuries,
I depended on human beings.
From now on I shall depend
Only on my Lord Beloved Supreme.

48 The Higher Visions ...

The higher visions
Are fast approaching
The God-lovers and God-servers.

49 Every Day God Reveals Himself ...

Every day God reveals Himself
In a most striking manner
To the sincere God-seekers.

50 Every Day ...

Every day
Is a new heart-cry
Of poor Mother Earth.

51 Any Evil Thought ...

Any evil thought
Can extinguish
Our heart’s climbing flames.

52 I Prayerfully Clasp ...

I prayerfully clasp
My Lord’s Compassion-Feet.

53 My Lord ...

My Lord
Most powerfully grasps
My earthbound life.

54 My Lord Invites Me ...

My Lord invites me
To ride with Him
In His Heart-Car
Only when He sees that
I am no longer interested
In my ignorance-desire-life.

13 December 2004 ...

13 December 2004

Xiamen, China

55 Faith Shows Surrender ...

Faith shows surrender
The way to God.

56 The Heart And Hope ...

The heart and hope
Are inseparable.
The soul and promise
Are inseparable.

57 In The Inner World ...

In the inner world,
A God-crying heart
Has the fastest speed.

58 The Soul Translates ...

The soul translates
God’s Transcendental Messages
To the heart.
The heart translates
The soul’s lofty messages
To the life.

59 God Is All Love ...

God is all Love,
But alas,
God’s Creation
Is empty of love.

60 An Unconditional Surrender ...

An unconditional surrender
Is the solid foundation
Of God-realisation.

61 God Looks Here, There ...

God looks here, there
And everywhere
To find a tear-flooded
Heart-seeker.

17 December 2004 ...

17 December 2004

Xiamen, China

62 My God-Obedience ...

My God-obedience
And my heart-happiness
Always stay together.

63 My Heart ...

My heart
Always longs to be
At my Lord’s Haven-Feet.

64 God Loves To Parade ...

God loves to parade
The seeker’s fragrance-soul
And beauty-heart.

65 Each Self-Giving Thought ...

Each self-giving thought
Is a sacred heart-shrine.

66 Self-Admiration ...

Self-admiration
Is quite common.

67 God-Glorification ...

God-glorification
Is quite uncommon.

68 Today God Has Sent My Soul ...

Today God has sent my soul
To sing and dance
On my desire-grave.

69 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That nobody has the right
To challenge
My God-gratitude-heart.

70 My Inner God-Oneness-Freedom ...

My inner God-oneness-freedom
In the midst of
Multifarious outer activities
Draws God’s highest admiration.

71 God Has Invited Me ...

God has invited me
To be always beside Him
With my unfailing love.

72 Unless I Give Myself Totally ...

Unless I give myself totally
To God,
How can I become
My true divine self?

73 I Did Not Come Into The World ...

I did not come into the world
To prove anything.
I came into the world
To love everyone
And everything.

74 My Earthly Source ...

My earthly source
Is Eternity’s Joy.
My Heavenly Source
Is Infinity’s Peace.

75 I Follow My Heart ...

I follow my heart
Lovingly.

76 My Heart Follows My Soul ...

My heart follows my soul
Faithfully.

77 My Soul Follows My Lord ...

My soul follows my Lord
Self-givingly.

78 A Divine Traveller ...

A divine traveller
Travels only at the Command
Of his Inner Pilot.

79 Stop Thinking! ...

Stop thinking!
The problems will
Eventually surrender.

80 Be Patient! ...

Be patient!
Adversity cannot
Forever last.

81 O My Mind ...

O my mind,
There is no such thing
As ‘unlovable’.

82 To Forgive Those ...

To forgive those
Who wrong us
Is to gain our own
God-Satisfaction-advantage.

83 A Self-Giving Man ...

A self-giving man
Finds himself
In the very Heart-Breath
Of God.

84 God Is Never Alone ...

God is never alone.
His self-giving Heart
Always accompanies Him.

85 The Fools Know ...

The fools know
Only one thing:
How to complain.

86 The Wise Ones Know ...

The wise ones know
The real thing:
How to remain silent.

87 God’s Love-Ocean ...

God’s Love-Ocean
Is only for those
Who promise to God
That He is their only aspiration,
Their only dedication
And their only realisation.

88 God Is Always Ready ...

God is always ready
And even eager
To help us discover
Our own uniqueness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 33, Agni Press, 2005
