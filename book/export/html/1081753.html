194. Allow us

Allow time
To direct your thoughts.
You will be divinely happy.

Allow God
To govern your will.
You will be supremely happy.

Allow me
To cry for your stupidity.
You will be eternally happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
