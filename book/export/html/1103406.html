22 April 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Mine are the self-giving dreams
To manifest You in Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 April 1999, 6:51 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.) Lifts number thirty-six and thirty-seven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
