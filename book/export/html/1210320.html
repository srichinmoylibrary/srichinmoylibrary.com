24440

Every day, without fail,
Early in the morning,
God invites His chosen instruments
To go for a joy-ride in His Heart-Boat.
Alas, even His choice instruments
Often fail to obey Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 245, Agni Press, 1998
