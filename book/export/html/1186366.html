723

O my soul,
Can you please tell me a few words
About God's Eye and God's Heart?
"God's Eye
Is His Eternity's Newness.
God's Heart
Is His Eternity's Fulness."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 8, Agni Press, 1983
