Tomai Mago Bujhte Giye Paran Halo Chup

Tomai mago bujhte giye paran halo chup
Hriday bane rakhbo shudhu pushpa malya dhup
Arati ma karini hai tomai pabar lagi
Sanpi amar khudra jiban ashish taba magi

From: Sri Chinmoy, Freedom-heights, part 1, , 1978
