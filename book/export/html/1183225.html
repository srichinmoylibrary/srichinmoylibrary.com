89. Three believers

My heart is a good believer,
Therefore
My soul loves my heart.

My soul is a better believer,
Therefore
My God loves my soul.

My God is the best believer,
Therefore
I always love God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
