36. Truth

At last I know the difference
Between Truth and God:
Truth tells me
One day, at God's choice Hour,
I shall realise God;
God tells me smilingly and proudly,
Who else is God if not I,
The Eternal I?

From: Sri Chinmoy, My California Redwoods, Agni Press, 1974
