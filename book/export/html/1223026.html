I Selected My Soul

I selected my soul
To love God on my behalf.
I selected my heart
To feel God on my behalf.
I selected my mind
To seek God on my behalf.
I selected my vital
To challenge God on my behalf.
I selected my body
To serve God on my behalf.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The goal is won, , 1994
