25 September 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart and my life,
Like my soul-bird,
Fly in Infinity's Sky,
Singing the Victory-Songs
Of my Lord Absolute Beloved Supreme.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 September 2000, 9:41 a.m. Before doing 940-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
