24. Alertness

   Alertness
Is the perfection
Of earth-bound reality.   Alertness
Is the upward, forward
   And inward movement
        Of God-revealing Light,
        God-illumining Compassion
              And
        God-fulfilling Satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
