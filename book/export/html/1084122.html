45. Then and now

There was a time
When I was the sadness
Of a saddening thought.

There was a time
When I was the madness
Of a maddening thought.

But now
I am the vastness
Of an unhorizoned thought,
I am the brightness
Of the solar thought.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 3, Agni Press, 1974
