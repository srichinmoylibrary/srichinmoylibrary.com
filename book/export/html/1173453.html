525 Life Is Nothing But This

My day tells me
That life is nothing but
A worrying mind.

My night tells me
That life is nothing but
A crying heart.

My God tells me
That life is nothing but
A loving smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 6, Agni Press, 1979
