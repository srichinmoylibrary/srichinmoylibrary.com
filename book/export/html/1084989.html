45. Let me face world-problems

Let me face
     All world-problems.
After all, they are all
        Temporary.

Let me not be over-eager
     To receive Heaven-Delight.
After all, it will eventually come
                 And
     Once I receive it
     I will drink it
           Eternally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
