10849

If you can bravely pray
Inside your mind-forest,
Then God will be proud of you.

If you can soulfully meditate
Inside your heart-garden,
Then God will be fond of you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 109, Agni Press, 1987
