219. They do not bother God

God is lazy.
Therefore
My body does not bother God.

God is weak.
Therefore
My vital does not bother God.

God is ignorant.
Therefore
My mind does not bother God.

God is shy.
Therefore
My heart does not bother God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
