Life and death

     What men call life
I call ambition's fight
     Against the strife
Of blind Ignorance-Night.     What men call death
I call the soul's repose
     In God's secret Breath
At the long journey's close.

From: Sri Chinmoy, AUM -- Vol. 1, No. 9, April 27, 1966, Boro Park Printers -- Brooklyn, N. Y, 1966
