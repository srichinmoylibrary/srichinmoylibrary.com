Dictator

When the body is the dictator, man becomes a corpse. When the vital is the dictator, man becomes suppression. When the mind is the dictator, man becomes insecurity. When the heart is the dictator, man becomes possibility. When the soul is the dictator, man becomes certainty. When God is the Dictator, man becomes Reality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Orchestra, Agni Press, 1973
