6395

My heart tells my mind,
"O my searching mind,
Do not be satisfied
With your present achievement.
You want to love the Inner Pilot most,
But that is not enough.
You have to love
The Inner Pilot only."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 64, Agni Press, 1984
