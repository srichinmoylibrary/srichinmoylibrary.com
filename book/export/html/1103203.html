23 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Do give me the strength of self-giving.
In self-giving alone lies
My peace of mind
And
My heart of fulfilment.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 November 1998, 6:07 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
