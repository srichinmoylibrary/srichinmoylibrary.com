928. The sad story

When our eyeless body
   Is in the foreground,
Our clever vital
   Recedes into the background.

When our aggressive vital
   Is in the foreground,
Our uncertain mind
   Recedes into the background.

When our unlit mind
   Is in the foreground,
Our insecure heart
   Recedes into the background.
   And there, there alone,
        The sad story ends.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
