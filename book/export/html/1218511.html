Ahangkarer Patan Chahi

Ahangkarer patan chahi ahangkarer patan
Ta na hale dibe na je dharte tomar charan

Translation:

My Lord, I beg of You for one thing:
I want the downfall of my ego and pride.
If not, You will never allow me to touch Your Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ashar duar, part 1, , 2004
