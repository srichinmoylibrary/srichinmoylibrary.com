PROMISES AND HOPES

My past promises
Are important.
My future hopes
Are more important.
My present cries
Are infinitely more important
Than my unfed promises
And
My hungry hopes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
