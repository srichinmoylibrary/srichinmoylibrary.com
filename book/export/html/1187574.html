1919

O seeker-artist,
How do you get ideas
For your paintings?
"I do not get any ideas
For my paintings.
I just get an inspiration-message
From within,
And then I begin to paint."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 20, Agni Press, 1983
