6.

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
My heart's mounting cries
And
My life's spreading smiles
Are my sacred gifts
Which I am placing
At Your Compassion-Feet
So that at every moment
I can please You
And fulfil You
In Your own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My sweet Father-Lord, where are you?, Agni Press, 1999
