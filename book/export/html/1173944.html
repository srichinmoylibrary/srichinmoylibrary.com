1011 The Golden Disc Of The Beyond

Believe it or not,
A wild darkness-night
Has unconsciously
Yet unquestionably
Helped me to fly
Towards the golden disc
Of the Reality-Beyond.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 11, Agni Press, 1981
