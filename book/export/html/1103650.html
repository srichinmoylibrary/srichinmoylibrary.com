14 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O my mind, it is true
That you have not pleased God.
But please make a brave, soulful
And prayerful attempt
To please God.
I am sure that you will succeed,
My mind,
You will succeed.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 August 1999, 6:47 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. seventeen times with one arm. (9 right, 8 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
