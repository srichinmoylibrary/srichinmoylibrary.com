Mane Prane Dehe Jakhan

Mane prane dehe jakhan
Kari kabhu bhul
Disha hara sarba hara
Nahi heri kul

Translation:

When I commit any mistake
In my mind, in my vital,
Or in my earthly physical existence,
Then I lose my vision, I lose everything.
I see no destination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bahir jagate, part 2, , 2002
