20086

O my Lord Supreme,
Do give me an iota
Of Your Tolerance-Ear
And an iota
Of Your Compassion-Heart
To love You in the inner world
And serve You in the outer world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 201, Agni Press, 1994
