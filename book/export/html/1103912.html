25 October 1999 (XI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord, today You are
   Most gloriously teaching me
How to write the songs
   Of our souls' ever-transcending
Newness, oneness and fulness-tree.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 October 1999, 10:35 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (104 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
