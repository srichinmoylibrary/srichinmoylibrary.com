349 Two Advisers...

TWO ADVISERS

God's advice to Man:
"Don't lie!"

Man's advice to God:
"Don't sigh!"

God's advice to Man:
"Don't hide!
Are you a thief?"

Man's advice to God:
"Don't chide!
Am I a thief?"

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 7, Agni Press, 1973
