667 And It Is

My mind thinks
That the world is against it -
And it is.

My heart feels
That the world is for it -
And it is.

And I know
That the world is indifferent to me -
And it is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 7, Agni Press, 1980
