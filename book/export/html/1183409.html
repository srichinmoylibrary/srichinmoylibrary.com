273. Your best friend

Your best friend is conviction.
Her best friend is discussion.
My best friend is hesitation.
Earth's best friend is dedication.
Heaven's best friend is illumination.
God's best friend is Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
