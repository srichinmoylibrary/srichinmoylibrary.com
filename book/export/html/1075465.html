886 What You Want, What You Need...

WHAT YOU WANT, WHAT YOU NEED What you want
Is an admirer.
What you need
Is an instructor.

What you want
Is a life-giver.
What you need
Is a life-liberator.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
