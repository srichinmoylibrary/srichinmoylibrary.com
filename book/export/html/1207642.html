21789

How painful it is to see
That each time a God-representative
Comes into the world
To raise the consciousness
Of humanity,
He is not only misunderstood
But also reviled
By those whom he came to liberate
From the meshes of ignorance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 218, Agni Press, 1994
