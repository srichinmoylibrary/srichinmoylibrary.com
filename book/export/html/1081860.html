301. Let us see who wins

Let us see
Who wins:
Your vital life
Or
Your Master's forgiveness-height.

Let us see
Who wins:
Your mental doubts
Or
Your Master's tolerance-light.

Let us see
Who wins:
Your body's ignorance-sleep
Or
Your Master's compassion-depth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
