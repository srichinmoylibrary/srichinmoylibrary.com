Peace: God's Fragrance-Heart, part 2

1.

In Heaven
I do not know what
I shall precisely need.
But on earth
I do know what
I desperately need:
Peace, an iota of peace.

2.

Your heart's oneness-peace
Is not real
If it cannot fly
Across the vastness-ocean.

3.

I call it
My heart's peace.
My Lord calls it
My perfection-life.

4.

My peace is
My heart's unconditional
Self-offering.

5.

The divine in me tells me
That peace is infinitely
More powerful
Than anything else.
The human in me
Asks the divine in me:
"But, brother,
Tell me where it is."

6.

Peace in stone statues
Has inspired and helped the world
Infinitely more
Than the present-day
Self-acclaimed peace-possessors.

7.

My peace is my heart's
Highest education-diploma
From my Lord Beloved Supreme.

8.

My satisfaction-peace
Is not the success
Of my outer smile
But the progress
Of my inner cry.

9.

How can you have
A wisdom-mind
If you do not have
A peace-heart?

10.

Great human beings may not be
Always wise,
But a heart of peace
Is unmistakably
And perpetually wise.

11.

My Lord does not visit
My heart-garden.
Do you know why?
Alas, it is because
My heart-garden does not have
A single peace-plant.

12.

I call it
My peace-heart.
But my Beloved Supreme calls it
His Treasure-Kingdom.

13.

A real man of peace
Is he who works sleeplessly
With God the creation
And eats hungrily
With God the Creator.

14.

First believe
That there is something called peace,
And then try to feel it.
Indeed, this is the proper way,
The only way.

15.

Mine is the heart
That cannot conceal peace.
Mine is the mind
That cannot reveal peace.

16.

A heart of peace
Has the supreme authority
To predict the immortal birth of peace
In the outer life.

17.

Peace is not
Property-prosperity-achievement.
Peace is
Self-enlightenment-attainment.

18.

A life of peace
Is the perfect perfection
Of the heart.

19.

God has not forgotten
To give us peace.
He is just waiting for us
To ask for it.

20.

Peace is
My heart's sacred book.
Oneness is
My life's devoted reader.

21.

Peace has no imperfection.
It never prompts us
To do anything wrong.
On the contrary, it only helps us
To do everything
Soulfully, unconditionally
And perfectly.

22.

God has always been extremely proud
Of the human beings
Who have peace,
And He always will be.

23.

Poor is he
Who does not have peace.
Poorer is he
Who does not need peace.
Poorest is he
Who does not want to hear
People talking about peace.

24.

Nothing is as high as peace.
Nothing is as deep as peace.
Nothing is as God-pleasing as peace.

25.

Peace is the only silence-power
That I need
To live happily with mankind
Without any fear.

26.

Peace is
The only divine wisdom-light
That successfully operates
In human affairs.

27.

I love you
Not because you have tremendous power,
But because
You are all peace.

28.

Because you have peace,
You are divinely great.
Because you want to share your peace
Lovingly and cheerfully with me,
You are supremely great, plus perfect.

29.

Power is the most difficult thing
To work with.
Peace is the easiest thing
To work with
And also the most rewarding.

30.

The wings of peace
Carry my heart
High, very high.
The shoulders of peace
Carry my mind
Far, very far.

31.

When we pray for peace,
God tells us
That we rightly deserve to be heard.

32.

Patience and sacrifice
Are my life's newly discovered
Peace-allies.

33.

Defy the existence of fear.
You will have peace.
Deny the existence of doubt.
You will have more peace.
Embrace the existence of oneness.
You will have Infinity's Peace.

34.

O my ingratitude-mind-sovereign,
You and my peace-heart
Will remain perfect strangers
To each other.

35.

A man of boundless inner peace
Is indeed
A self-discoverer.

36.

When you kneel
To a higher authority,
You unmistakably receive
Needed peace.

37.

At every moment
Celebrate the Hour of God
Inside your gratitude-heart.
Then you are bound to feel peace.

38.

There are many different approaches
You can make to God
For peace in your mind.
If you fail in one,
Then keep making new approaches.
Sooner or later fulfilment-peace
Will inundate your entire being.

39.

Do not allow the past
To impose itself on you.
Do not allow the future
To come to you unduly fast.
Just try to live
In your today's peace-world.

40.

Responsibility-evading human beings
Will never be blessed with
God's Joy-Peace-Satisfaction.

41.

Do not allow your mind-fort
To be invaded by doubt-suspicion-devils.
You will be blessed
With faith-blossomed peace.

42.

An iota of peace-satisfaction-result
Is infinitely better
Than thousands of imaginary expectations.

43.

Name-fame-hunger-fascination
Cannot have even a glimpse
Of satisfaction-peace-sea.

44.

With a serenity-mind
And a purity-heart
If you can look in the God-mirror,
God will give you
What you need most:
Peace.

45.

Never entertain your insecurity, jealousy
And impurity-acquaintances.
They will definitely destroy
Your peace-cottage-mind.

46.

The heart definitely wants
To make a peace-deal,
But the mind
Vehemently refuses.

47.

My aspiration-heart's
Oneness-responsibility
Is my dedication-life's
Fulness-peace.

48.

If I want peace,
Then there should be no space
Between
My unconditional surrender-heart
And God's Compassion-Satisfaction-Eye.

49.

I feel tremendous peace
Inside my heart
Not when I answer the question
"Who am I?"
But when I answer the question
"Whose am I?"

50.

I can have immediate peace
If I allow others
To be the rulers of their lives
The way I like to rule
My own life.

51.

Do not try to experience others
In your own way.
Just try to experience yourself
In your own way
And, if possible, in God's Way.
Then you will have real peace,
God-created peace.

52.

I gave God
My gratitude-heart.
God gave me
His Plenitude-Peace.

53.

A continuously blossoming peace
Is the fragrance-heart
Of my silence-mind.

54.

God blessingfully visits
My heart's meditation-peace-garden
Every day.

55.

No peace in austerity-cave.
Peace is in temptation-grave.

56.

If not today,
Then in the distant future,
The voice of peace will conquer
Insurmountable disharmony-obstacles.

57.

Aspiration-exercises strengthen
Our peace-loving heart-muscles.

58.

Doubt says to peace,
"Peace, I can easily erase you."
Peace says to doubt,
"No, you cannot erase me.
Can you not see that I am embracing you
To transform you totally?"

59.

God does not allow
My peace-heart
To hear anything
About my doubt-mind.

60.

My heart's
Enlightenment-peace-progress
Is my life's
Detachment-revelation-success.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God's Fragrance-Heart, part 2, Agni Press, 1992
