3124

Your disbelief
Can never be the real judge
Of your Master’s God-realisation.
If you believe in his God-realisation,
  Then be in his boat.
If not, find your own boat.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 4, Agni Press, 1998
