13. Don't you know that?

The animal in you
Is fond of destruction-night.
Don't you know that?
The human in you
Is fond of suspicion-might
Don't you know that?
The divine in you
Is fond of perfection-light.
Don't you know that?
The Supreme in you
Is fond of satisfaction height.
Don't you know that?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Immortality's dance, Agni Press, 1974
