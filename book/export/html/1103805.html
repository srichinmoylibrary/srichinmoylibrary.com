26 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
If I can have God-closeness,
God-faithfulness and God-devotedness,
Then I have everything that I need.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 September 1999, 6:27 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
