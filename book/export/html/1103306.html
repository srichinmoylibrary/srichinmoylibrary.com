16 February 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I need peace, I need peace,
I need peace.
Today I must put an end
To all my frustrations, irritations
And exasperations.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 February 1999, 6:04 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. twelve times with one arm. (5 right, 5 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
