336. God's God-Decision

My
Desire-friend
What can I do?
My Lord wants me to play with you.
He wants me to win,
He wants you to lose.
If not, I would have easily allowed you
To win at times, at least.

As you know I have taken
A solemn vow:
"God's God-Decision is always final."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
