8 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My prayer-life teaches me
How to realise God.
My meditation-heart teaches me
How to manifest God
On earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 September 2000, 9:22 a.m. Before doing 800-lb. dumbbell bench press.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
