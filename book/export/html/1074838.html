271. Where do you sleep?

O Electricity,
Where do you sleep?
"I sleep in the clouds."

O Beauty,
Where do you sleep?
"I sleep in God's descending Grace."

O God,
Where do You sleep?
"I sleep between
Earth's ingratitude
And
Heaven's indifference."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 6, Agni Press, 1973
