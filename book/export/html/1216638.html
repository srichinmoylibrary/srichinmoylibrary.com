999. Our manifestation-perfection hour

   O austere Angel,
I like you
        But
I don't love you.
        Why?
Because God does not care
For your austerity-tears.   O smiling and dancing Angel,
I love you
        Because
God loves you.
I need you
        Because
God needs you.
        Do you know why God and I love you?
Because you are our Power.
        Do you know why God and I need you?
Because in you alone
   Is our manifestation-perfection Hour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
