43. What do you do?

Father, O Father, what do You do
Here in my heart?
"I dream My cosmic Dream."
"Daughter, O daughter, what do you do
Here in My Mind?"
I plead with You again and again
To keep me always in mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Immortality's dance, Agni Press, 1974
