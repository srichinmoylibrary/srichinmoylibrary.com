4826 To Conquer Its Doubting Life

Why does the mind want to meditate?
The mind wants to meditate
Because it wants to conquer
Its doubting life.
The mind knows that meditation
Is the answer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 49, Agni Press, 1982
