What can I do?

What can I do?
I can think of
My Lord's constant
Forgiveness
In my life.

What else can I do?
I can feel
My Lord's constant Love
Inside my heart.

What can I see?
I can see every morning
A beautiful sunrise
Upon my eyes.

What else can I see?
I can see every evening
A moonlit night
Upon my head.

What can I give and receive?
I can give
My ant-tiny gratitude
To my Lord Supreme
And receive from Him
His Himalayan-high Satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Aurora-Flora, Agni Press, 1982
