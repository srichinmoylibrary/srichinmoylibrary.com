247. What dies, what lives?

What dies?
A thinking mind.

What lives?
An aspiring heart.

What will die and never be reborn?
Earth's depression-night.

What lives and will forever live?
Heaven's Perfection-smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
