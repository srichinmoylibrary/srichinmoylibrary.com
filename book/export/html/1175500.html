2552 I Know That You Care For Me

Mine is the mind
That is bewildered.
Mine is the heart
That is broken.
Mine is the vital
That is bruised.
Mine is the soul
That is aching.
My Lord,
Yet I feel
That You love me,
Yet I know
That You care for me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 26, Agni Press, 1982
