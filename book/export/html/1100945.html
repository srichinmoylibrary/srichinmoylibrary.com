Question: Guru, do you remember when you gave us the daily prayers,<a href="#fn1" class="footnoteRef" id="fnref1"><sup>1</sup></a> you said that you were giving us an approach that was most compatible with the Western mind, which is &quot;I must conquer, I must conquer.&quot;

Sri Chinmoy: We must conquer fear, doubt, jealousy — everything. Those prayers that I gave a few years ago, alas, alas, many of you do not do. Once again I am sincerely requesting you to offer those prayers on a daily basis.
