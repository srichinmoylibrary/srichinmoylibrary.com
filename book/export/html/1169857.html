502. The world loves me

Because I create,
The world loves me, my soul.
Because they receive,
Them I give cheerfully
My gratitude-heart
Pure and sweet,
Also
Soulfully
My oneness-all.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
