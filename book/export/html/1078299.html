THE TORCH OF LOVE

God made me
The torch of silence
To feed His celestial Height.

God is making me
The torch of life
To feed His terrestrial Space.

God shall make me
The torch of love
To feed His Heaven-beauty,
To feed His earth-duty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
