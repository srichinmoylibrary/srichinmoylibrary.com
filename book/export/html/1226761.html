Mukti Jadi Chaho Tumi

Mukti jadi chaho tumi
Jukti rakho dure
Bhakti jadi chaho tumi
Thako hiya pure

Translation:

If you desire liberation,
Then keep aside the reasoning mind.
If you want devotion,
Then always remain inside
Your heart-citadel.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
