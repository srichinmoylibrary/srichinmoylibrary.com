U Thant

U Thant, U Thant,
We love your silence-soul,
We loved your compassion-heart.
Your world-harmony role
Challenged ignorance-dart.
U.N. Pilot supreme,
You treasured perfection-dream.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Blue waves of the ocean-source, , 1979
