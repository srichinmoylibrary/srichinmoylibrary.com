211. Where is he

Where is he?
I know not.

Where is his body?
In the land of death.

Where is his life?
In the land of the immortals.

Where is his soul?
In the gratitude-breath of humanity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
