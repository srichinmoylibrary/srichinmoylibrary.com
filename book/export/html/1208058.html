Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 223

22201

When two God-seekers
Talk and talk about God,
God's Heart
Becomes all Pride
In them.

22202

Your heart-sky
Can be lit
Only with your own
Aspiration-cries
And realisation-smiles.

22203

Just try again and again!
All your failures
Will eventually fade away.

22204

Today's life
Is nothing but a journey
From one ruthless struggle
To another.

22205

How can we have
Peace of mind
If we do not release
The iron grip
Of our intolerant ego?

22206

The mind knows
What it has:
A series of complications.
The heart knows
What it is:
A streaming flow of compassion.

22207

Alas, when shall we learn
That each undivine action
Ends in endless anxieties?

22208

Soulful opportunities
Knock at our heart-door.
We just have to open the door
To love and please God
In His own Way.

22209

When we think we cannot climb
Any higher,
There begins the death
Of our inner journey.

22210

The absence
Of undivine thoughts
Is the presence-proof
Of purity.

22211

We have perplexities
In our mind-life
Just because quite often
We consciously cherish
Our mind's confusion-perplexities.

22212

Alas, what am I doing?
Why am I not using fruitfully
My life's earth-bound time
To enter into
My soul's Heaven-free time?

22213

No soul can travel
Along the path of Eternity
Without experiencing
The formidable strength
Of human tears and sighs.

22214

The day I do not
Pray and meditate,
I invariably find myself crushed
Beneath the heavy weight
Of helplessness.

22215

The tireless and breathless
Aspiration of the heart
Is always far above
The mind's poor understanding.

22216

Not because my mind
At times denies the existence of God
Am I a failure,
But because I have no time
To kindle the flame of aspiration
In my sleeping heart-room.

22217

The conflict between
The mind and the heart
Can be solved only
By the express illumination-presence
Of the soul.

22218

I have come to realise
That only God
Or His own personal Peace
Can comfort
My heart's streaming tears.

22219

Everything else fails
Or will fail,
But not self-giving love.

22220

To receive joy
In infinite measure,
I always choose God's Feet,
And not God's Face.

22221

A heart that is completely dedicated
To God-service
Will never be able to appreciate
The noise of the mind-factory.

22222

Each sincere seeker
Eventually becomes
His own life-transforming
Peace-apostle.

22223

The rarest gift to God
From the twentieth century
Will be humanity's
Satisfaction-smile.

22224

When temptation knocks
At your mind-door,
Immediately say, "No!"
And continue nourishing
Your "No!"

22225

A seeker's rise and fall
Depend entirely on
The mind's sincerity
And the heart's purity.

22226

When I am in the mind,
I foolishly desire
Satisfaction.
When I am in the heart,
I wisely long for
Perfection.

22227

What is the end?
The end is nothing but
The beginning
Of a new and unfulfilled
Dream-reality.

22228

Winning
Is not everything.
Self-giving
Is more than everything.

22229

May my heart's purity-smile
Always have
A soulful access
To every seeker's heart.

22230

To serve the soul
Of the world-body,
We need the fragrance
Of Heaven's Beauty.

22231

Each thought is an action.
Each action is either
A bloom of satisfaction
Or a doom of frustration.

22232

The power
Of a peace-dream
Can radically transform
The face of reality.

22233

The secret
Of earth-transformation
Lies in the breath
Of lovers of God the creation.

22234

A life ruled
By a doubting mind
Does not or cannot
Dare to attempt the impossible.

22235

A life
Without faults and failings
Is a life
Founded upon dreams
And not upon reality.

22236

Unconditional surrender
Is the supreme height
Of a seeker's aspiration.

22237

God's immediate Response
To our sincerity-cry
Helps us claim Him
As our very own.

22238

My mind
Loves to cherish
Possession-dreams.
My life
Wants to cherish
Satisfaction-realities.

22239

God's Unhappiness-Sound
We have heard.
God's Happiness-Silence
We shall become.

22240

Fools are those who,
Even unconsciously,
Allow their desire-life
To contaminate
Their aspiration-heart.

22241

A life dominated by
Pleasure-desires
Is heading towards
Destruction-realities.

22242

God chooses to remain concealed
In our desire-forest.
God loves to be revealed
In our aspiration-garden.

22243

Surrender without devotion
Can never reach
The ultimate Goal.

22244

The reasoning mind
Pretends to be very strong.
But in reality
It is nothing, if not fragile.

22245

Alas, how long
Is my body going to suffer
From the tyranny of my mind?

22246

It seems that
The more laws there are
In the outer life,
The more offenders
Are easily available.

22247

For me it is quite easy
To please others,
But, alas, quite difficult
To please my mind.

22248

My heart does not need
To read even one book
To become wise.
But my mind,
No matter how many books it reads,
Never becomes wise.

22249

To keep friendship alive,
We require
Both time and concern.

22250

Nothing really belongs to us.
No, not even
Our boundless desire-hunger.

22251

It is by virtue
Of our inner freedom
That we can make
True outer progress.

22252

True spiritual life
Will never need
Any outer understanding.

22253

If we have
A commitment-bridge to God,
Then only can we have
An enlightenment-destination.

22254

The ego of my mind
And the pride of my heart
Always fight for prominence.

22255

When I am in my mind,
I cannot separate myself
From confusion-jungle.
When I am in my vital,
I find it so difficult
To stay out of frustration-fire.

22256

The resistance of the mind
I have.
The surrender of the heart
I am.

22257

Alas,
The human mind lives
Between vanishing doubt
And constantly reappearing doubt.

22258

For a long time
I have been begging my mind
In vain
To take a detachment-course.

22259

Each time
My mind does something wrong,
My heart and I feel a sharp blade
Piercing our existence.

22260

You can arm yourself
With excuses,
But your perfection will still remain
A far cry.

22261

If you have receptivity
In abundant measure,
Then you will eventually become
A perfect truth-finder.

22262

A soul-made choice
Is what we need
To hasten our arrival
At our ultimate destination.

22263

The perfection of human nature
Cannot be achieved
By the unaspiring,
Ordinary run of mortals.

22264

Today's willingness-giver
Becomes
Tomorrow's fulness-achiever.

22265

The absence of prayer and meditation
From our lives
Ultimately makes us
Self-indulgence-victims.

22266

Only a seeker
Who is willing to be schooled
In the art of self-offering
Can slowly and steadily become
A perfect instrument of God.

22267

O my mind,
If you do not search for light,
You will be destined to travel
Unlit and frightening life-roads.

22268

Self-confidence-crisis
Each and every human being faces
In life.

22269

O my inner faith,
Come to the fore!
I am tired of fighting
With my mind-frustration-foe.

22270

God wants me to run
Into the illumining future,
But I love to watch again and again
My dismal past.

22271

Only a faith-follower
Can become
A perfection-achiever.

22272

If you are abandoned by hope,
You will feel that
There can be nothing worthwhile
In your life.

22273

When God's Compassion
Reigns supreme in us,
We become frequent fliers
On satisfaction-flights.

22274

O my mind,
Stop collecting
Your worthless and useless memories
Of the past.

22275

Attachment-diploma
I had.
Detachment-education
I now enjoy.

22276

Do not allow loneliness
To touch you.
If you do, it may one day
Devour you.

22277

The fulfilment of possibilities
Cannot be imagined,
But can be obtained
Through sleepless self-discipline.

22278

He who welcomes suspicion
Is doomed to be sneered at
By suspicion itself.

22279

We were not born
For human death,
But for divine Immortality.

22280

If we do not shun
The noise of our ego-mind,
How can we achieve
God's all-illumining Smile?

22281

No human being
Can ever become wise
By cherishing self-doubts.

22282

I am asking God
To be sincere.
God is asking me
To be receptive.

22283

Alas,
When will I learn
That pleasing God
Is indeed a delightful task?

22284

I pray to God every day
To extol my heart-life
And exile my mind-life.

22285

Self-giving to God's Will
Is, without fail,
A slow-ripening
But most delicious fruit.

22286

Our gratitude-heart
Is bound to multiply
The goodness of our life.

22287

My Lord,
The greatest of all Blessings
From You
Is my willingness-heart.

22288

A doubting mind
And an anxiety-life
Are generally inseparable.

22289

When I consciously speak ill of God,
I simply double God's Tears
And triple His Sighs.

22290

No matter what happens today,
I am determined
To meet the heart of tomorrow
Cheerfully and smilingly.

22291

The past
Has tortured you enough.
Now do not allow the future
To disturb you.

22292

The suspicion of the mind
Is indeed
A colossal self-defeat.

22293

Each individual
Is a runner along Eternity's Road,
Passing on
The inspiration-torch of life.

22294

The path of the reasoning mind
Leads us
To an untimely grave.

22295

He who desires
To see others happy
Has already covered a long distance
On the satisfaction-road.

22296

In the mind,
Sincerity and magnanimity
Are two rare virtues.

22297

My Lord Supreme,
Your blessingful Advice
Is Your fruitful Assistance.

22298

O my mind,
Do develop a great aptitude
For patience
To make my life happy.

22299

Anything that is good and divine
Does not have to be long-standing
To prove its perfection.

22300

When I say
That I love God only,
My ignorance-night leaves me,
Not only quickly
But also permanently.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 223, Agni Press, 1995
