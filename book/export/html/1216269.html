637. Be careful!

O insincere seeker,
You may swerve
From the path of truth.
Be careful! I tell you
It can easily happen.

O impure seeker,
You may drown
In the sea of ignorance.
Be careful! I tell you
It can easily happen.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
