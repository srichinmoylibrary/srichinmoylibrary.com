27 July 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
A purity-flooded heart
Knows no obstructions,
Knows no ignorance-night.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

27 July 1999, 2:02 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
