15252

Give yourself,
Widen yourself,
Receive the vast world
And be received
By the vast world:
These are the messages
A divine heart gets
From the higher worlds
And offers to the outer world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 16, Agni Press, 1999
