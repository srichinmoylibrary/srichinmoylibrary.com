266. My meal with God

Lord, my food is ignorance.
Do You want to eat with me?
"Son, on one condition:
We must eat everything,
And we shall not leave
Even a morsel."
Father, that is what
I need from You.
"Son, that is what
I want from you."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
