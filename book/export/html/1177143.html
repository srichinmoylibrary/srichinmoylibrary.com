4179 Faith And Life-Energy

Faith and the increase of life-energy
Must go together
If a seeker wants to succeed triumphantly
In his outer life
And proceed untiringly
In his inner life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 42, Agni Press, 1982
