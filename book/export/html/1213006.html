99 Imbroglio

Madal: Chinmoy, alas, when will my life be free from teeming imbroglios?

Chinmoy: Madal, your life will be free from confusing and complicated situations and misunderstandings the day you can remember that God always thinks of you, meditates on you and loves you unconditionally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two brothers: Madal and Chinmoy, part 2, Agni Press, 1982
