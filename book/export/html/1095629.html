51 God Surrenders To Me ...

God surrenders to me.
God says to me,
“My child, I have been leading you
For centuries after centuries.
Now I feel you can lead Me.”
“My Lord,
You must always lead me.
The child has always to follow
The Father.
Please do not break Your Cosmic Law
For me.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 23, Agni Press, 2004
