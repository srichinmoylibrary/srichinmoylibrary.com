11356

When your mind's fear
And your heart's courage wrestle,
You must immediately help your heart
So that you and your heart can quickly reach
Your soul's promised Divinity-Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 114, Agni Press, 1987
