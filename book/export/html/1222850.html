Larai Kari Maner Praner Sathe

Larai kari maner praner sathe
Tushta kari amar param nathe

Translation:

I constantly fight, I constantly wrestle
With my mind and vital.
By doing this, I am pleasing
My Absolute Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giti mala, , 2000
