INCOMPLETE, COMPLETE AND PERFECT

A fighting vital
Is an incomplete animal.

A doubting mind
Is an incomplete animal.

A loving heart
Is a complete man.

An all-giving life
Is a perfect man.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
