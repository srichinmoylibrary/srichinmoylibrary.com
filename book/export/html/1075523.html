943. Who can cure?

Defects of the heart
Love can cure.
Defects of the vital
Power can cure.
Defects of the body
Compassion can cure.
Defects of the mind
Nothing can cure,
None can cure,
Only God can cure.
And
He does it cheerfully,
He does it unconditionally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 19, Agni Press, 1973
