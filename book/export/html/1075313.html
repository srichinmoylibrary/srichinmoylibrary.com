737 When He Died...

WHEN HE DIED

When he died,
Both earth and Heaven
Held condolence meetings.

Earth-Children said:
"Alas, we shall miss
Our sweet brother,
The real God-distributor
Whose heart cried to illumine us."

Heaven-Members said:
"Alas, he is coming back,
The real rascal, the shameless
God-seller on earth.
Let us protest his arrival
At the inner Palace
Of our Heaven."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
