27 September 1999 (IX)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God tells me that my mind-voice
Hurts Him badly
Because it is so sharp.
He also tells me that my heart-voice
Gives Him tremendous joy
Because it is so sweet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 September 1999. Before using seated calf raise machine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
