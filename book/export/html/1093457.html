53 My Lord Has Repeatedly Told Me ...

My Lord has repeatedly told me
That there is no such thing
As a negligible mistake.
Each mistake can be fatal
In the spiritual life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 1, Agni Press, 2000
