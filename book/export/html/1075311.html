735 Unafraid...

UNAFRAID

Unafraid
Of fear:
Therefore, cheerfully I feel.

Unafraid
Of doubt:
Therefore, effectively I think.

Unafraid
Of darkness:
Therefore, clearly I see.

Unafraid
Of death:
Therefore, bravely I live.

Unafraid
Of life:
Therefore, supremely I enjoy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
