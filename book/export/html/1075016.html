446. I become perfect

Lord, I am sufficient
Only when I have You
   On my side.

"Son, I am sufficient
Only when I take
   Your side.

"When I add Peace
To your achievements,
   I become complete.

"When I add Light
To your achievements,
   I become perfect."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 9, Agni Press, 1973
