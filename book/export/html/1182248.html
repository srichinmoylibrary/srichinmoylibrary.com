9233 See Yourself

If doubt enters your inner life,
See yourself as a little child
Who is all faith.

If impurity enters your inner life,
See yourself as a beautiful flower
Which is all purity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 93, Agni Press, 1983
