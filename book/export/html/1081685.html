126. Big news today!

Big news!
This morning I thought of God.
Indeed
I am proud of myself,
And I bless myself.

Big news!
This evening God will make me another God.
God, I am all gratitude to You.
God, I am all fondness for You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
