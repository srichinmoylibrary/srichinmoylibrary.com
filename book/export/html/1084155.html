27. Forgetfulness-monarch

O stormy vital emotion,
From now on my name shall be
Forgetfulness-Prince.

O sombre mental frustration,
From now on my name shall be
Forgetfulness-King.

O foolish psychic destruction,
From now on my name shall be
Forgetfulness-Emperor.   Yet
I shall run,
But with a vital new,
     with a mind new,
     with a heart new,
Beyond the length
Of extremity's song,

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 4, Agni Press, 1974
