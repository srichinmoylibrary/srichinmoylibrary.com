12898

My Lord Supreme,
No matter what happens,
Either in my inner life
  Of aspiration
Or in my outer life
  Of dedication,
I shall obey You immediately
  And implicitly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 13, Agni Press, 1999
