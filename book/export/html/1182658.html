9639 A Mere Suggestion

To you, O earth,
A mere suggestion:
Be constantly conscious
Of what you are not receiving soulfully.

To you, O Heaven,
A mere suggestion:
Be constantly conscious
Of what you are not giving cheerfully.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 97, Agni Press, 1983
