5318 As Long As You Are Willing

As long as you are willing
To muster your inner courage
To achieve a perfect life,
God's Compassion-Flood
Will, without fail,
Wait for you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 54, Agni Press, 1983
