31 May 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my life becomes
A desire-beggar,
I would like You to immediately be
A denial-emperor.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 May 1999, 6:29 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm fifty-four times. (27 right, 27 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 5, Agni Press, 1999
