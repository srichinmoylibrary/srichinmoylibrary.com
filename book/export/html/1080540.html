76. They say and I say

They say: the morning is
Beautiful.
I say: your blossoming
Eyes are infinitely
More beautiful.

They say: the evening is
Beautiful.
I say: your falling
Tears are infinitely
More beautiful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Four hundred Gratitude-Flower-Hearts, Agni Press, 1979
