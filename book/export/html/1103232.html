18 December 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Onward my body marches,
Inward my heart dives,
Upward my soul flies
To be at Your Feet,
To be Your choice instrument,
To please You and fulfil You
In Your own Way,
In Your own Way,
In Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Singapore. 18 December 1998, 5:37 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. fifty times with two arms and one-hundred times with one arm. (50 right, 50 left in five sets)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
