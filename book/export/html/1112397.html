34.

Supreme!

You want
Your creation
To please You.
I do not know
How You imagine
Your desire
To be fulfilled
As long as You keep
Your creation
Not only
Unconsciously,
But consciously
Weak, helpless
And hopeless.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sadness-heart-silence. Madness-mind-eloquence, Agni Press, 1998
