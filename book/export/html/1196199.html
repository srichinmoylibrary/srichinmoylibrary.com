10459

When I keep myself
Under the control of my mind,
I clearly see
That I am a tiny ant.

But when I keep my life
Under God's Control,
I unmistakably see
That I am a budding God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 105, Agni Press, 1986
