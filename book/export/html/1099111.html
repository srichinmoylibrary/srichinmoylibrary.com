32.

When I pray
And climb up
High, higher, highest,
God blesses me
With the Beauty of His Heart.
When I meditate
And dive deep, deeper, deepest,
God blesses me
With the Fragrance of His Life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My early morning heart-climbing prayers, Agni Press, 2006
