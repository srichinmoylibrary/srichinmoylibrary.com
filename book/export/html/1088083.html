28. The deceivers

You are a deceiver.
You have deceived
God in man.
He is a deceiver.
He has deceived
Man in God.
I am a deceiver.
I have deceived
Both God and man.
How?
Ask God the Compassion.
Ask man the innocence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Immortality's dance, Agni Press, 1974
