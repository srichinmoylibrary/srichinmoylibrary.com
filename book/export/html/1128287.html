14874

The difference between
The human in me
And the divine in me
Is this:
The human in me wants
To prove to the world
That it is self-sufficient.
The divine in me wants
To prove to the world
That it is sleeplessly
And breathlessly God-dependent.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 15, Agni Press, 1999
