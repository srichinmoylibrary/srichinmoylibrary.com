225. Our eternal company

Lord, Your absence
Makes me see
Whom I have neglected.

Lord, my absence
Makes You feel
Why I am rejected.

Lord, let us become
Once more good friends.
Let us never part
Our eternal company.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
