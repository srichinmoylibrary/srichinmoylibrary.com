5817 A Very Good Combination

The Japanese nature
Is a very good combination.
The outer nature
Is like a lion resting.
The inner nature
Is like a lion roaring.
Both are extremely powerful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 59, Agni Press, 1983
