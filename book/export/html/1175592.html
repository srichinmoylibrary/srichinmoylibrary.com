2643 The Fountain Of Forgiveness

God has already given you
A most precious thing:
The fountain of goodness.

Now He will be giving you
The most precious thing:
The fountain of forgiveness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
