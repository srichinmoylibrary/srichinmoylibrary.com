My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 55

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Antalya, Turkey in December 2006.

11 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

1 Earth-Tears ...

Earth-tears
And Heaven-Smiles
Are God’s childhood friends.

2 Value Your Self-Discipline ...

Value your self-discipline.
Self-discipline is the way.
Self-discipline will bring you
To God’s Heart-Door.

3 We Obey God ...

We obey God.
God obeys our love,
Unconditional love,
For Him.

4 When The Gratitude-Heart ...

When the gratitude-heart
Fully blossoms,
The transformation of life
Begins.

5 My Lord Is My Friend ...

My Lord is my Friend,
Only Friend.
I do not need
A multitude of friends.

6 Soulfully And Self-Givingly ...

Soulfully and self-givingly
I sing and sing
At the top of my voice
To become a choice instrument
Of God.

7 The Outer Success ...

The outer success
We can easily measure,
But the inner progress
Is extremely difficult to measure.

8 Today’s God-Disobedience ...

Today’s God-disobedience
Tomorrow becomes
Life’s worst possible catastrophe.

9 He Who Dreams Of God ...

He who dreams of God
Quite often
In the small hours of the morning
Becomes a God-lover
Of the highest magnitude.

10 I Love God ...

I love God,
Not because He is
Divinely great
And not because He is
Supremely good,
But because He is my All,
Very All.

12 December 2006 ...

12 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

11 God-Obedience-Lessons ...

God-obedience-lessons
Are compulsory
From the journey’s start
To the journey’s close.

12 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Please, please,
Give me another chance
To please You
In Your own Way.

13 God Wants Me ...

God wants me
To fly with Him
With my ever-blossoming
Heart-songs.

14 My Soul, My Heart And I ...

My soul, my heart and I
Will never be found
In the thick of desire-forest.

15 Constant God-Choice ...

Constant God-choice
Is the only antidote
To self-doubt.

16 The Inner Joy ...

The inner joy
Gives more.
The inner joy
Receives more.
The inner joy
Becomes more.

17 In Silence-Secrecy ...

In silence-secrecy
My heart talks
To my Lord Supreme.

18 Our Sleepless God-Surrender ...

Our sleepless God-surrender
Is the architect
Of God’s new world.

13 December 2006 ...

13 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

19 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That my ever-blossoming
Gratitude-heart
Is His most favourite song.

20 My Heart’s Bliss ...

My heart’s bliss
Is the announcer
Of God’s express Arrival.

21 Earth Says To Heaven ...

Earth says to Heaven,
“Why are you
So beautiful?”

22 Heaven Says To Earth ...

Heaven says to earth,
“Why are you
So useful?”

23 Aspiration ...

Aspiration
Is
My heart-song.

24 Dedication ...

Dedication
Is
My life-story.

25 When I Desire ...

When I desire,
I become a body-cage.

26 When I Aspire ...

When I aspire,
I become a soul-bird
Flying in Infinity’s Sky.

14 December 2006 ...

14 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

27 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That all my promises to Him
Are just exquisite.

28 God’s Eye ...

God’s Eye
Is my most powerful
Heart-attraction.

29 Each Fleeting Moment ...

Each fleeting moment
I try to run at full speed
Towards my Lord Supreme.

30 Our Genuine Prayers ...

Our genuine prayers
Must bring us to our knees,
And not our endless
Worries and anxieties.

31 My Life’s Sufferings ...

My life’s sufferings
Can be measured,
But my soul’s bliss
Forever shall remain
Immeasurable.

32 My God-Obedience ...

My God-obedience
And God’s Pride in me
Are extremely fond of each other.

33 Impossible ...

Impossible —
A permanent God-failure-life
Is impossible!

34 When The Inner Heart ...

When the inner heart
Is all sweetness,
The outer life
Becomes all fulness.

15 December 2006 ...

15 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

35 Our Aspiration-Eagerness-Intensity ...

Our aspiration-eagerness-intensity
Helps us find our way back
To our Absolute Lord Supreme.

36 Day And Night ...

Day and night
My heart and I
Walk along
Our soul-paved road.

37 My Hope-Beauty ...

My hope-beauty
Comes from God’s Heart.

38 My Promise-Fragrance ...

My promise-fragrance
Comes from God’s Breath.

39 My Morning Meditation ...

My morning meditation
Is my soul’s
God-invocation.

40 My Morning Meditation ...

My morning meditation
Is my life’s
God-Dream-Delight.

41 My Morning Meditation Tells Me ...

My morning meditation tells me
That God-vision
Is not enough,
God-realisation
Is not enough,
Even God-manifestation
Is not enough.
My life’s complete transformation
Is my journey’s final halt.

42 May Each And Every Thought ...

May each and every thought
Of mine
Be always Heaven-bound.

16 December 2006 ...

16 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

43 When God Is On My Side ...

When God is on my side,
I become my life’s sad failure.
When I am on God’s side,
God gives me His Victory-Drum.

44 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I sing for my Lord
With my gratitude-heart-tears
And my gratitude-eye-smiles.

45 Wherever I Go ...

Wherever I go,
I carry with me
A newness-mind
And a soulfulness-heart.

46 Each Time I Pray ...

Each time I pray,
I must try to reach
A higher height.

47 Each Time I Concentrate ...

Each time I concentrate,
I must try to develop
A stronger will.

48 Each Time I Meditate ...

Each time I meditate,
I must try to enter into
A deeper depth.

49 Each Time I Contemplate ...

Each time I contemplate,
I must try to get
Closer, closer and closer
To God.

50 My Devotion ...

My devotion
Is my only
God-Satisfaction-
Fulfilment-necessity.

17 December 2006 ...

17 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

51 The Tears Of My Heart ...

The tears of my heart
Feed on our Lord’s
Compassion-Temptation-Feet.

52 Every Day ...

Every day
God wants me to accomplish
Something absolutely new,
Inspiring and aspiring.

53 Alas ...

Alas,
Will I ever be worthy
Of mingling
With the dust of my Lord’s Feet?

54 Determination, Determination ...

Determination, determination —
Stoic determination!
If not,
God-realisation
Will remain a far cry.

55 When Everything Is Gone ...

When everything is gone,
The tears of my heart
And I
Shall remain.

56 My Heart And I ...

My heart and I
Get tremendous joy
When my Lord Beloved Supreme
Checks and checks
On my surrender-life.

57 Our Lord Beloved Supreme ...

Our Lord Beloved Supreme
Expects all true
And self-giving seekers
To be madly in love
With Him alone.

58 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Every day,
For at least an hour,
Cradles my life
With His Compassion-Eye.

18 December 2006 ...

18 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

59 My Peaceful Heart ...

My peaceful heart
Prayerfully welcomes
My Lord’s blissful Eye
And His powerful Feet.

19 December 2006 ...

19 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

60 My Heart’s Cries And Tears ...

My heart’s cries and tears
Purify and fortify my life
Most significantly.

61 My Soul’s Smiles ...

My soul’s smiles
Feed my heart
To run the fastest
For God-realisation.

62 My Heart’s Performances ...

My heart’s performances
Are mightily appreciated
By earth’s heart
And Heaven’s Eye.

63 Do Not Allow ...

Do not allow
Lethargy-sleep
To prevent you from seeing
God’s morning Smile.

64 Morning Is The Time ...

Morning is the time
To receive from God
His torrential Blessings,
Affection and Concern-Rain.

65 I Came Into The World ...

I came into the world
To serve God
Lovingly and self-givingly,
And not to experience
His highest Height
And His deepest Depth.

66 Each Heart-Song ...

Each heart-song
Is a very long step forward
Towards God.

67 Sleep Like A Child ...

Sleep like a child,
Dream like a child,
Act like a child.
Lo, God is granting you
A free access
To His Heart-Home.

20 December 2006 ...

20 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

68 Poor God ...

Poor God
Is all the time calling us,
But we deliberately
Do not respond.

69 My Lord Supreme Returns ...

My Lord Supreme returns
My love-devotion-surrender-calls
Immediately.

70 God-Disobedience-Train ...

God-disobedience-train —
Once it starts,
It never stops.

71 The Ancient God Blessed Me ...

The ancient God blessed me
With the message
Of unity in multiplicity.

72 The Modern God Is Blessing Me ...

The modern God is blessing me
With the message
Of diversity and complexity.

73 In Our Spiritual Life ...

In our spiritual life,
Eagerness-speed
Knows no equal.

74 No Human Heart ...

No human heart
Can ever dare to resist
God’s Heart-Attraction.

75 God Never Sanctions ...

God never sanctions
Any seeker’s
Retirement-enjoyment.

76 Nothing In My Life ...

Nothing in my life
Is as sacred
As my heart’s
Sleepless and breathless
God-devotion.

77 Never ...

Never,
Never become tired
Of your inward dive!

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 55, Agni Press, 2007
