257. The only answer

O God-seeker,
A life in a Himalayan cave
Is no answer.

O God-lover,
A life of world-renunciation
Is no answer.

O God-fulfiller,
A life of selfless surrender
Is the only answer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
