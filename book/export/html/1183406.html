270. My body-life

My body-life
And
Earth-consciousness
Enjoy mutual recognition.
His heart-life
And
Heaven-consciousness
Enjoy simultaneous recognition.
My soul-life
And
God-Consciousness
Enjoy constant recognition.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
