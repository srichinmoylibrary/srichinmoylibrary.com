2611 Duty Comes First

My Lord, duty comes first
In Your Life.
Therefore, I love You
And I am dying to make You
My own.

My Lord, pleasure comes first
In my life.
Therefore, I hate myself
And I am desperately trying
To disown myself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
