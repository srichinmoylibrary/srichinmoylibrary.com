14791

On this very special day
My Lord Supreme
Is in an excellent mood
To answer any questions
I can possibly ask.
Alas, alas!
Where are my life-illumining questions?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 148, Agni Press, 1991
