I Play Not Alone

I play not alone.
When I play, the sun and the moon join me.
I sing not alone.
When I sing, light and delight join me.
I dance not alone.
When I dance, the sky and the sea join me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My complete God-surrender, , 1995
