18. For your sake

Father, it is for Your sake
     And not for my sake
That I need Perfection-Light.

"Son, it is true, it is true.
And it is for your sake
     And not for My sake
That I need Satisfaction-Height."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 18, Agni Press, 1974
