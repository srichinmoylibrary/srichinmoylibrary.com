Let it be

1.

Let it be the time for us to dream of world-peace.

2.

Let it be the time for us to dream of a world without war.

3.

Let it be the time for us to truly appreciate nature’s beauty.

4.

Let it be the time for all of us to give up negative feelings about each other.

5.

Let it be the time for us to become good human beings.

6.

Let there be hope for us to have an interdependence-bridge between people.

7.

Let it be my solemn promise to see only the good qualities in each and every human being.

8.

Let it be known that I truly love all the god-seekers on earth.

9.

Let it be the bounden responsibility of all human beings to love and help one another.

10.

Let it be my aim to remove sufferings from the world — of course, according to my capacity. Let me try. Let me try.

11.

Let it be the last time for me to exploit another human being.

12.

Let it be my daily prayer to see a better world and be of service to it.

13.

Let it be everybody’s sincere concern to protect the animal kingdom here on earth.

14.

Let it be everybody’s sincere concern for the poor and the needy.

15.

Let it be a collective oneness-effort to change the fate of this deplorable world.

16.

Let it be a regular practice for us to look at the world with sympathy.

17.

Let it be a world where the children can look forward with freshness and newness in their hearts.

18.

Let it be our task to teach the children the love of truth.

19.

Let it be our task to offer the inner education to the children — the love of the heart.

20.

Let it be the time for me to throw out all the doubts from my mind.

21.

Let the children live the simple life which gives them pure joy.

22.

Let the children know that we strive together with a clear mind for peace.

23.

Let it be a new age in which dawns a clear mind in all.

24.

Let it be the new age of sincere heart in everybody.

25.

Let it be known that the children are all God's sweet d reams.

26.

Let the war-mongers know that they achieve nothing fruitful by fighting.

27.

Let it be humanity's collective determination to stop war forever.

28.

Let it be humanity's collective determination to illumine the minds of the politicians who sincerely and proudly enjoy engaging in war.

29.

Let it be the choice of each and every human being to have our earth planet be flooded with peace.

30.

Let it be the voice of each and every human being to prevent world war.

31.

Let it be known that our service to our fellow human beings is indispensable.

32.

Let it be our chance to experience the joy of oneness.

33.

Let it be our choice to make a world where everyone helps each other.

34.

Let it be our choice to share God's Love with all.

35.

Let everyone be a hero.

36.

Let each person have the capacity to turn his insecurity to zero.

37.

Let it be my task to pray every day for the end of world-suffering.

38.

Let us understand that each desire betrays the Divine in us.

39.

Let each of us do our duty to sow hope-seeds in the world.

40.

Let it be today that we establish a world with no conflicts, no tension and no misunderstandings.

41.

Let us each reside in a world-oneness-heart.

42.

Let us be free of the mind of confusion and the life of destruction.

43.

Let it be the last time that ignorance can lord it over me.

44.

Let it be our sincere, strong desire to be a better person.

45.

Let it be our solemn promise to God that we shall not fail Him anymore.

46.

Let it be our solemn promise to Mother Earth that from now on we shall take very good care of her.

47.

Let us understand that the inner, spiritual world guides the material world.

48.

Let it be the choice of humanity to do everything for the manifestation of God's Light on earth.

49.

Let it be a world where inner faith tells us what we should do outwardly.

50.

Let it be a daily concern of the world to put an end to all world-misunderstandings.

51.

Let there be a morning song that will keep my heart beautiful throughout the day.

52.

Let there be a morning prayer that will keep my mind clear throughout the day.

53.

Let there be a morning sunrise to keep me happy throughout the day.

54.

Let me see a morning rose blooming inside my heart each day.

55.

Let me change the face and fate of mankind. Let it be my task.

56.

Let the evening sky breathe peace into my mind.

57.

Let the twinkling stars breathe joy into my life.

58.

Let our mind of sincerity come forward to help the world.

59.

Let the beauty of a rainbow give us new hope.

60.

Let our heart of purity love the world.

61.

Let our simplicity-life help to unburden the world.

62.

Let us have pure thoughts to nourish the mind.

63.

Let us have a strong will to energise the body.

64.

Let us offer a sincere smile to help strengthen the world.

65.

Let our heart-tears help to purify the world.

66.

Let us see the beauty in our unity and multiplicity being found together.

67.

Let it be a God-Victory-Song that we hear in the morning.

68.

Let it be the God-Compassion-Eye that I see every evening.

69.

Let the fragrance of our souls unify the whole world.

70.

Let there be a peace-tree to give me and my mind shelter.

71.

Let everyone enjoy his own heart-bliss garden.

72.

Let it be my choice to be a sleepless service-life.

73.

Let the smiles of our souls invite God to earth.

74.

Let the sincerity of our prayers and the tears of our hearts water earth-transformation seeds. Let it be. Let it be.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Let it be, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1999
