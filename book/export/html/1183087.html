2 You know not how

You know not how to add;
Therefore
You can never be glad.

You know not how to subtract;
Therefore
You shall never attract.

You know not how to multiply;
Therefore
You shall fail to fly.

You know not how to divide;
Therefore
Your face is doomed to hide.

From: Sri Chinmoy, This is God's Home, Agni Press, 1975
