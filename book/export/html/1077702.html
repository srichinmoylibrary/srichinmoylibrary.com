55. Ecstasy's sky

Ecstasy's sky,
How to climb?
Give up your desire-life.
Lo, you have climbed up!

Perfection-sun,
How to achieve?
Illumine the heart-cave
Of your aspiration-life.
Lo, you have achieved it!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
