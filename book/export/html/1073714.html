Fulminate

(thunder)

Because of its own
Ocean-vast stupidity,
The mind fulminates against
The God-fondness-innocence
Of the heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
