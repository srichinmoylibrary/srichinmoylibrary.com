228. Don't tell me a lie

Don't tell me a deliberate lie:
God loves you.

Don't tell me a half lie:
I clearly see
Your love of God is not totally
sincere.

Don't tell me a complete lie:
I tell you,
God does have the time
In measureless measure
To think of you,
To love you
And immortalise you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
