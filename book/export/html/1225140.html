Tomar Ashish Tomar Sneha

Tomar ashish tomar sneha
Rekhe amar mathe
Chalbo ami gaibo ami
Tomar sathe sathe

Translation:

My Lord, Your Blessings
And Your Affection,
Placing on my head,
I shall walk with You,
I shall sing with You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Jibaner bhor, , 1995
