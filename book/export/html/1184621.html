293. Don't sail in the doubt-boat

Don't sail
In the doubt-boat.
If you do, you are bound
To reach the soundless shore.

Don't sail
In the doubt-boat.
If you do, you are bound
To meet with nadir-darkness
And slumbering inconscience-cry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
