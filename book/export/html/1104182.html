27 March 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
No more impurity-mind,
Insecurity-heart
And inferiority-life
Can darken my meditation-room.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 March 2000, 8:38 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 13, Agni Press, 2000
