133. Big questions, big answers

Two big questions:
Is God alive?
If so, where is He?

Two big questions rightly deserve
Two big answers:
God is alive.
He is all-where;
Therefore
Where is He not?

Is God alive?
Yes, He is.
Where is He?
He is inside my stupidity's query;
He is inside my necessity's discovery.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
