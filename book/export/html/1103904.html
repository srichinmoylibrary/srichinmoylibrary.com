22 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You do not want me to be
   A cave-dweller-heart.
You do not want me to be
   A palace-dweller-mind.
You want me only to be
   A temple-dweller-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 October 1999, 9:28 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
