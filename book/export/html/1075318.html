742 Six Little Ones...

SIX LITTLE ONES

Two
Little ears
Hear the news
Of the whole world.

Two
Little eyes
See the beauty
Of the whole world.

One
Little heart
Houses the love
Of the whole world.

One
Little head
Understands the meaning
Of the whole world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
