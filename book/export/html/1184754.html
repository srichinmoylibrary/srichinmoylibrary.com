426. O Heavenly bodies

O twinkling stars,
Do take me home!
I am all ready.

O sweet moon,
Where is my Eternal Father?
I am dying to see Him.

O brave sun,
Can you fight for me?
I must conquer ignorance-night
With your unparalleled might.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
