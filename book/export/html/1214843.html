50. Light

In our life of aspiration
With the outer light
   We see,
With the inner light
   We become
        And
With the Light of the Supreme
We know what we eternally are:
        God's Dream-Boat
            And
        God's Reality-Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Union and oneness, Agni Press, 1976
