7. Vastness

Vastness
Is
The expansion of purity
In the human being.
Vastness
Is
The song of oneness
In the life of the seeker.
It is
The life of oneness
With the world of love
In the life of a genuine seeker.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
