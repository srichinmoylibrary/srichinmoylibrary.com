I treasure

A sweet and divine heart of prayer
   I treasure openly.

A pure and supreme heart of meditation
   I treasure secretly.

A perfect and absolute life of God
   I treasure sleeplessly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Meditation: humanity's race and Divinity's Grace, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
