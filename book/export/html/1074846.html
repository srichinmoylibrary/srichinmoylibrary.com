279. O Armies

O Armies of Night,
You were the strength
Of my eyeless yesterdays.

O Armies of Light,
You are the strength
Of my soulful todays.

O Armies of Bliss,
You shall be the strength
Of my immortal tomorrows.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 6, Agni Press, 1973
