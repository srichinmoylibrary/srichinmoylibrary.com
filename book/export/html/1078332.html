685 Your Golden Today...

YOUR GOLDEN TODAY

Don't be a liar!
Your yesterday was never golden.

Don't be a fool!
Your tomorrow can never be golden.

Be sincere, be wise!
Your today is unmistakably
And supremely golden.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
