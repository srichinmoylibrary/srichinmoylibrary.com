2211 I Am Divinely Proud

I am divinely proud of myself
Because I know how
To identify myself
With my soaring hopes.

I am supremely proud of myself
Because I know how
To identify myself
With my nourishing dreams.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 23, Agni Press, 1982
