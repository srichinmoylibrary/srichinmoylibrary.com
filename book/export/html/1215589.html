31. Alas

You are a complicated man;
Therefore
I do not go near you,
Alas!
He is a shallow man;
Therefore
I do not go near him,
Alas, alas!
I am an uncultured man;
Therefore
You two do not come near me,
Alas, alas, alas!

From: Sri Chinmoy, When I left God in Heaven, Agni Press, 1975
