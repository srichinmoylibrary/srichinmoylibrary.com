483

My Lord,
Give me speed!
Give me purity!
With speed I shall win
My long-lost inner race.
With purity I will be able to see
Once more
Your Infinity's immortal Face.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 5, Agni Press, 1983
