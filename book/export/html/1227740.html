My Heart’s Obedience-Joy

My heart’s obedience-joy
Is the shortest way
To my God-realisation.

An Obedience-Heart

An obedience-heart
Has a free access
To God’s Heart-Home.

At the Root of All Our Suffering

At the root of all our suffering
Is the lack
Of our inner God-obedience.

The Path of God-Obedience

The path of God-obedience
Is the only path
That I sleeplessly and breathlessly need.

God-Obedience Expedites

God-obedience expedites
Our life-boat-journey
Far, far beyond our imagination.

How I Wish I Could Earn

How I wish I could earn
A constant God-obedience-certificate.

I Am So Happy

I am so happy that my life has made
A permanent contract with God-obedience.

My Only Freedom

My only freedom
Is my constant obedience
To my Inner Pilot.

If You Can Have Obedience

If you can have obedience,
Then you can have a heart-home of peace.

I Bow and Obey

I bow and obey.
Therefore, I am happy.

Nothing as Important

There was nothing, there is nothing,
And there can be nothing as important
In a seeker’s life as obedience.

Believe and Obey

Believe and obey,
Obey and believe.
This is the only way to manifest
The Lord’s Day every day.

Only One Gift of Yours

Only one gift of yours God will treasure
And that gift is your inner and outer obedience.

I Love and Treasure the Rainbow-Smile

I love and treasure the rainbow-smile
Of my God-obedience-heart.

A God-Obedience-Mind

A God-obedience-mind
         Is
A most precious achievement.

My Heart-Fragrance

My heart-fragrance is
My God-obedience.

Take the Path of God-Obedience

Take the path of God-obedience.
All your resistance-barriers will soon disappear.

My Heart’s Divine Duty

My heart’s divine duty is to obey.
My life’s supreme duty is to surrender.

The God-Hour Has Arrived

The God-hour has arrived.
Are you ready with your obedience-power?

My Lord, If I Obey You

My Lord, if I obey You
The way You want me to obey You,
What will You give me?
“My child, I shall give you My Wings
To reach My Self-Transcendence-Height.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My God-obedience, my God-disobedience, , 1995
