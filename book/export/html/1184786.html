458. His is the Soul

His is the Eye
That watches the world
With Compassion.

His is the Heart
That loves the world
With benediction.

His is the Soul
That fulfils the world
With salvation's realisation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
