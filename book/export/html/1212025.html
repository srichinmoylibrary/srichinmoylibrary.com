26128

O my heart,
Do not cry.
O my heart,
Do not feel that you are lost.
O my heart,
Do not think that nobody cares for you.
Just move on and move on!
You will see that everybody and everything
Is right in its own way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 262, Agni Press, 1998
