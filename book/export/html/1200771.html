My mind says to my heart:
"O heart, tell me
What you have learned
From God's Satisfaction-Smile."

My heart says to my mind:
"O mind, do not search
For earth-explanation,
But long for Heaven-illumination!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 150, Agni Press, 1991
