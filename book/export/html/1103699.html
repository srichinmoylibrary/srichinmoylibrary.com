29 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A soulful smile
Is a mind-purification-guarantee.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

29 August 1999, 8:33 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
