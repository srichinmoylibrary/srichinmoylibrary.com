32. Melancholy days have gone

Melancholy days have gone.
There is a lustre divine
In his heart-sky.
No more his mind
Is crowded with doubt-forests.
He builds a bridge
   Between
Earth's nothingness
   And
Heaven's endlessness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 18, Agni Press, 1974
