270. My needs

The silence of the morning sun
My body needs.

The sound of the midday sun
My vital needs.

The surrender of the setting sun
My mind needs.

The peace of the loving night
My heart needs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
