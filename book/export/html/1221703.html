Eso Eso Param Pita

Eso eso param pita
Hiya pure eso
Asan pete rekhechi je
Ese aji baso

Translation:

My Lord Supreme,
Come, come today.
I have reserved a special seat for You.
Please come and take Your seat.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eso eso prabhu, part 1, , 1998
