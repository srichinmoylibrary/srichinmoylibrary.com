18.

I know, scores of times
I have broken
My peace-offering-promises
To the world at large.
HERE and NOW
I resume my promise,
And this time
My ceaseless love for humanity
And
My adamantine will-power
Are bound to succeed.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Here and now, Agni Press, 2001
