891 Perfect Certainty...

PERFECT CERTAINTY

Doubt-life
Is always
Worse than uncertainty.

Faith-life
Is at times
Black uncertainty.

Will-life
Expects certainty.

Surrender-life
Dances with perfect certainty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
