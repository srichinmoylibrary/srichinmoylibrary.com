31.

My Lord,
I need from You
The capacity to cultivate
More love, more devotion
And more surrender.
Do You need anything from me,
My Lord?
"Yes, My child:
More prayerful tears
And
More soulful smiles."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 3, Agni Press, 1998
