678 Who Believes?...

WHO BELIEVES?

Who believes
In his God-life?
Nobody!

Who believes
In his God-realisation?
Nobody!

Who believes
In his God-realising aspiration?
Nobody!

Indeed, this is fate.
Needless to say
God's Pride is for him,
For him alone.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
