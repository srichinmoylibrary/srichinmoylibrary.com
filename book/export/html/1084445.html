11. His happiness

In his animal life
He was happy
Because
There was no human observer.
In his human life
He is happy
Because
He is always at the Feet
Of God the constant Forgiver.
In his divine life
He will be happy
Because
He will always play
With God the unconditional Lover.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 10, Agni Press, 1974
