15146

God tells me that He will teach me
His own Heart-Language,
Which is most secret and most sacred,
Only when my life becomes
The unconditional surrender
Of a gratitude-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 152, Agni Press, 1991
