Part V: Poems

The spiritual message of tennis

The spiritual message of tennis:
Love, serve
And finally surrender
To the Supreme in the winner.
The loser who cheerfully
surrenders
To the Supreme in the winner
Becomes himself a real winner
In the inner world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I play tennis every day, Agni Press, 1994
