48. Oneness

What we call consecrated oneness
In the morning,
We call that very thing
Inseparable oneness
In the afternoon.
What we call inseparable oneness
In the afternoon,
We call that very thing
Perfection-oneness
In the evening.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Dedication-drops, Agni Press, 1976
