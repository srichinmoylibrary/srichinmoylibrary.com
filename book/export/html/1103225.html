7 December 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have dispelled doubt from my mind.
I have invoked faith from my heart.
The Victory of my Lord's Compassion-Eye
I now am.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Hawaii. 7 December 1998, 5:22 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred times with two arms and one-hundred and four times with one arm. (51 right, 53 left in five sets)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
