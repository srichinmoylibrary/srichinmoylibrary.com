10765

What man's mind demands,
God's Heart will not supply.
What man's heart invokes,
God's Heart will supply
In infinitely greater measure
Than man's heart can ever need.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 108, Agni Press, 1987
