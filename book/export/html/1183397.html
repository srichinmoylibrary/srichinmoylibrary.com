261. Do you want power

Do you want power?
Then go to the outer temple.

Do you want peace?
Then go to the inner temple.

Do you want love?
Then go to God-temple.
Where is it?
It is inside
The sea of your climbing eyes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
