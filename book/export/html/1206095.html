20257

Do not abhor ignorance.
Do not ignore ignorance.
Challenge ignorance!
Conquer ignorance
Once and for all!
This is the absolutely
Right way
To make yourself perfect
And happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 203, Agni Press, 1994
