Doubt we drink

     We sport with doubt,
And thus our strength we kill.
     Ah, doubt the poison
We drink in utter thrill.     Our ego shows
Our heart the path of lies,
     And leads us all
Where eyeless darkness flies.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My first friendship with the muse, Agni Press, 1973
