25. Life is a quest

Life is a quest
And
Not a conquest.
Love is a conquest
And
Not a quest.
Earth is aspiration
And
Not illumination.
Heaven is illumination
And
Not aspiration.
God is a smile
And
Not a cry.
Man is a cry
And
Not a smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 10, Agni Press, 1974
