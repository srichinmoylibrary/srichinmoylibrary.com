I am flying and flying and flying

1

I am flying and flying and flying
On my new God-obedience-wings
In Infinity s Sky.

2.

Aspiration is singular.
Manifestation is plural.

Doubt rightly deserves
To be
A homeless beggar.

Love is
Mistake-proof.

Bury your impatience
Here, there and
All-where.

God asks for volunteers
And not for soldiers.

Man-teacher preaches.
God-Teacher teaches.

I am looking for
A permanent doubt-remedy.

God accompanies
My opportunities.

The outer smile is beautiful.
The inner smile is perfect.

Cancel your
Hesitation-performance.

Aspiration-decline
Does not become you.

Negativity's bloom
Is
Negativity's doom.

God does not command,
But expects
An eager audience.

Effort needed.
Grace indispensable.

I am bettering myself
To increase humanity's
Divine pride.

Soulfulness-barrenness
Is
Terribly shocking.

Nobody can outlive
His usefulness on earth.

There is a sempiternal bond
Between
My nothingness
And
God’s fulness.

What is God doing?
God is looking
For a self-giving,
God-hungry seeker.

I must trust
The peace-dreamer,
I must.

I must trust
The peace-bringer,
I must.

I must trust
The peace-receiver,
I must.

I am giving you
The happiest-news:
Your long alienation
From your soul
Is just over!

The perfection-soul is by far
The best mediator
Between
The two secret rivals:
The mind and the vital.

Imitation is an unforeseen
Failure frustration.

I keep only four books
In my heart library:
Simplicity, Sincerity, Humility
And Purity.

May each life become
A flower-blossom-beauty.

May each heart become
A fountain-God-obedience.

Man's outer life
Is
A fading experience.

Man's inner life
Is
An ever-heightening
And
Ever-deepening Realization.

One flight can NEVER reach
The God-realization-height.
Countless stopovers
Are absolutely needed.

Miracle-Masters are fated
To deplorable disciple-conditions
And
Blessed with a miracle-audience.

Devotion-superfluity
Is an absurdity
Plus impossibility.

The perfection-song
Hides
In the imperfection-gong.

The King of Light
Lovingly expects
My heart.

The king of darkness
Ruthlessly demands
My blood.

A delight-heart
Is the tailwind
Of a surrender-life.

Our God-aspiration-heart
Does not sleep.

Our God-dedication-life
Does not sleep.

When I claim God
To be my own, very own,
I do not need sleep.

My doubting mind
Wants to be
A self-illumination-questioner.

My aspiring heart
Wants to be
Self-illumination-billionaire.

My utter disaster
Is only a hair’s breadth away
From my God-disobedience.

Do not wait
For God to approach you.
He may deliberately
Forget you.

There is no
Quick and sudden journey
To perfection.

I want my heart-compass
To turn towards
God's Polestar-Eye.

Our suffering does not pay
The admission fee to Heaven
But our God-surrender
Happily does.

A surrender life
Is a perfect
God-answer.

A great many times
My life has been saved
By a wee iota
Of God-obedience.

You fool!
Grace descends
For your immediate acceptance
And not for your total rejection.

O my God-searching mind,
Fly and fly
Far beyond the domain
Of world-complaint-criticism-torture.

My Lord,
Please give me anything else —
But not freedom.
Freedom I misuse;
Destruction I invite.

Every day God loves to have
A letter from me.
Even my volcano-anger-letter
Gives Him true joy.

God and I disagree
Only on one point:
He emphatically tells me
That I am another God.

My Lord Supreme,
I just love
Your high Commands.
They are at once
Extremely challenging
And extremely inspiring.

My Lord Supreme,
I quite dislike
Your mild Requests.
They are so weak
And completely empty
Of inspiration.

My mind,
I have already chosen
My heart
And not you
To be my destiny-maker.
You can remain silent.

The longer I sit
On my knees,
The sooner God comes down
To kiss my eyes.

The ignorance-thief
Does not know
How
To stop stealing.

My pride is
My self-applause
Thunder.

Immortality loves
To live
In the heart
Of fleeting moment.

God’s Face quenches
My eye-thirst.
God’s Feet feed
My heart-hunger.

Not out of dire compulsion
But out of sterling affection
I always say YES
To my soul.

Change of work
Is rest.
This is my God-lesson.

The aspiration-elevator
Is available
Only in the heart-palace
And not in
The mind-cottage.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I am flying and flying and flying, Agni Press, 1998
