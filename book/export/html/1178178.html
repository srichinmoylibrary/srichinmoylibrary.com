5203 My First Decision

My first decision:
I shall not criticise the world
Any more.
My last decision:
I shall love the world
Exactly the same way
My Lord Supreme loves the world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 53, Agni Press, 1983
