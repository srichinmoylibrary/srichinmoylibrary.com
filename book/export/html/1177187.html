4222 When My Love Of God Comes In

When my love of God comes in
And starts working for Him
In the inner world,
My faith in God goes out
And starts working for Him
In the outer world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 43, Agni Press, 1982
