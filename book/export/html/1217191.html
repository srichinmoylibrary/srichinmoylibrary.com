16. Allow me not to lose one thing

Young I was;
I am now old.

Beautiful I was;
Ugly now I am.

Lord, I have lost everything.
But one thing, Lord,
Allow me not to lose:
   My love of You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Yesterday I was a crying dream, Agni Press, 1975
