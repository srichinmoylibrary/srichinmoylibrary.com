April 10

Tomorrow: "Yesterday, I will not permit you to disturb the happiness of my golden children."
Yesterday: "Sorry, Tomorrow, I have left your dear children for good. It is they who fail to live without brooding over the deeds that they committed on my breast."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flame-Goal, Agni Press, 1973
