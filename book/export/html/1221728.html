Jagat Guru Param Guru

Jagat guru param guru
Amar guru
Mukti pelam tomar kripai
Duru duru

Translation:

O my Guru,
O Guru of the whole world
By your delicate, tingling touch
Today I have got my liberation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eso eso prabhu, part 1, , 1998
