207. Eternity's fastest train

You have snapped
Your vital chain.
Lo, you are in the kingdom
of supreme gain.

You have snapped
Your mental chain.
Lo, your life is in
Eternity's fastest train.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
