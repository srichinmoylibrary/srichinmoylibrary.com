Savyasachi's compassion

I was at the Indian market in Lima for such a long time. The shopkeepers would say one price and I would say another.

Savyasachi has more compassion than I do. I wanted to buy something, but I could not get the price I wanted. So I went away without buying it. But Savyasachi thought I liked it, so he went back and got it for me.

-13 January 1987

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
