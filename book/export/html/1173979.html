1046 Who Says?

Who says that I have a bad mind?
My mind appreciates God.
Who says that I have a bad heart?
My heart loves God.
Who says that I have a bad life?
My life needs God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 11, Agni Press, 1981
