1241

My mind thinks
That it is really great.
My heart feels
That it can be really great
If so is the Will of God.
My soul knows
Only one thing:
My Inner Pilot is everything.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 13, Agni Press, 1983
