738 I Weep...

I WEEP

I weep for man's sake
Because
Man never learns,
Man never surrenders.

I weep for God's sake
Because
He has no other choice
Than to confide in me
The failure of His earth-promise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
