1381 In The Abyss Of The Five Senses

In the abyss of the five senses,
The animal life does not know
The right thing.
The human life knows
But does not do
The right thing.
The divine life knows
And always does
The right thing.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 14, Agni Press, 1981
