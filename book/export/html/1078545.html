898 Beneath Your Soul's Dignity...

BENEATH YOUR SOUL'S DIGNITY

I have seen God.
You do not believe it,
You cannot believe it.
I tell you a supreme secret:
Your conduct is beneath
Your soul's dignity.
I have forgiven you.
But
I am afraid
Your soul will take some time
To forgive you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
