5185 You Are Afraid To Befriend

You are afraid to befriend
A sound-life.
That means you do not have
An all-loving heart.

You are afraid to befriend
A silence-life.
That means you do not have
An all-knowing mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 52, Agni Press, 1983
