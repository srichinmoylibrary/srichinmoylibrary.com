293. Things worth seeing

I shall see
Two things before I die:
Earth-sufficiency
And
Earth-efficiency.

I have seen
Two things worth seeing:
God cares more
For earth
Than for Heaven;
God will be fulfilled
Infinitely more by earth
Than by Heaven.
Of course, at God's choice Hour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
