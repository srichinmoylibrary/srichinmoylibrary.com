70. Love and hate

Love yourself.
Lo, you have begun
Your lifelong romance.

Hate yourself.
Lo, you have begun
Your lifelong battle.

Love God.
Lo, you have established
Your supremacy on earth.

Hate God.
Lo, the fool in you has caught
The fool you are.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 2, Agni Press, 1974
