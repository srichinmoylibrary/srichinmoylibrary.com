3 January 2005 (I)

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

I have travelled far and wide
And yet I have not seen
A seeker of soul-beauty.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

3 January 2005, 4:03 a.m. Qingdao, China. Seated Double-Dumbbell Lift: 1,006 lbs.
