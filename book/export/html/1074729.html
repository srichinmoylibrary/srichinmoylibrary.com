164. A drop of tears

A drop of my tears
Is enough to make my Lord
Embrace my ageless
   Inner poverty.

A drop of my Lord's tears
Is enough to transform my life of ignorance-dream
Into Reality's
   Divinity-Love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 4, Agni Press, 1973
