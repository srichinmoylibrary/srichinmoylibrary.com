My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 32

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Sanya, China in November and December 2004.

29 November 2004

Sanya, China

1 My Own Inner Capacities ...

My own inner capacities
Frighten me.
My own outer capacities
Sadden me.

2 My Lord Is Telling Me ...

My Lord is telling me
That He feeds on my aspiring,
Climbing and flaming heart.

3 To Transform The World ...

To transform the world,
The God-messengers do bring down
The nectar-flooded Messages
From God
Most perfectly.

4 Alas ...

Alas,
I do not know when and where
I shall start my gratitude-heart
To my Lord Beloved Supreme.

5 My Lord Has Promised Me ...

My Lord has promised me
His Golden Shore.
Therefore, I do not mind in the least
Travelling through endless
Darkness-nights.

6 My World-Attachment-Addiction-Licence ...

My world-attachment-addiction-licence
Has just expired.
I shall never, never, never renew it.

7 No Seeker ...

No seeker
Will ever be able to escape
From his giant ego-enemy.
The seeker must conquer
This enemy.

8 Each Seeker ...

Each seeker
Has to be endowed
With an indomitable faith
In God.

30 November 2004 ...

30 November 2004

Sanya, China

9 Each Prayer ...

Each prayer
Is a flame of my heart
Climbing towards
The highest Heaven.

10 Each Meditation ...

Each meditation
Is a unique opportunity
For me to establish
My conscious oneness
With my Absolute Lord Supreme.

11 If I Am Not Absolutely Sincere ...

If I am not absolutely sincere
In my prayers
And in my meditations,
How can my soul set fire
To my desire-life-house?

12 If I Really Want To Make ...

If I really want to make
The fastest progress
In my spiritual life,
My Lord’s Compassion-Eye
Is bound to help me
To attempt the impossible task
And be victorious.

13 Before I Pray And Meditate ...

Before I pray and meditate,
My soul feeds my heart, my mind,
My vital and my body
With a dynamic enthusiasm
And intense eagerness.

14 Each Self-Giving Action Of Mine ...

Each self-giving action of mine
Immediately becomes
A most beautiful flower
In my Lord’s Victory-Garland.

15 Each Time I Obey My Lord Supreme ...

Each time I obey my Lord Supreme
Happily and unconditionally,
He comes nearer and nearer,
Proudly and triumphantly,
Towards me.

16 First I Must Become ...

First I must become
A sleepless and breathless God-lover.
Only then can I become
An unmistakable God-winner.

17 Each Life ...

Each life
Is the Beauty
Of God’s Love.

18 Each Heart ...

Each heart
Is the Fragrance
Of God’s Delight.

19 Nothing Puzzles My Lord ...

Nothing puzzles my Lord —
No, not even my ingratitude-mind.
But it shocks my heart
Most powerfully.

20 My Beloved Lord Says To Me ...

My Beloved Lord says to me,
“Do not come to Me, My child,
For your perfection.
Just come to Me only
For My own Satisfaction.”

21 When I Claim God ...

When I claim God
As my own, very own,
I give myself
Ten out of one hundred.

22 When God Claims Me ...

When God claims me
As His own, very own,
I give myself
One hundred out of one hundred.
My Compassion-flooded
Lord Beloved Supreme
Does not agree with me.

1 December 2004 ...

1 December 2004

Sanya, China

23 Desire Kills The Seeker ...

Desire kills the seeker.
Aspiration revives the seeker.
Aspiration says to the seeker,
“O seeker,
If you really love me,
Then stop loving desire!”

24 I Must Stop ...

I must stop
My life’s desire-train
Immediately.

25 I Must Fly ...

I must fly
My aspiration-plane
Every day without fail.

26 Doubt ...

Doubt
Is world-torture.
Faith
Is God-rapture.

27 For A True God-Seeker ...

For a true God-seeker,
Aspiration-world
Is the only world.

28 We Are Promoted And Honoured ...

We are promoted and honoured
In the inner world
Only when we stop expecting
From the outer world.

29 My Mind Cares ...

My mind cares
Only for power —
God-Power.

30 My Heart Cares ...

My heart cares
Only for the things
That are about God.

2 December 2004 ...

2 December 2004

Sanya, China

31 My Desire ...

My desire
Is my God-lost life.
My aspiration
Is my God-found life.

32 Alas ...

Alas,
My Lord is still waiting for me,
Even at the eleventh hour.

33 All My God-Dreams ...

All my God-dreams
Are flooded
With my God-sweetness-fondness-smiles.

34 My Heart Hears Constantly ...

My heart hears constantly
God-Whispers:
“Go forward, My child, go forward!”

35 The Heart’s God-Devotion ...

The heart’s God-devotion
And the life’s God-sweetness
Are inseparable.

36 The Body Binds Us ...

The body binds us
Unconsciously and unintentionally.

37 We Bind The Body ...

We bind the body
Consciously and purposely.

38 Many, Many, Many Times ...

Many, many, many times
I have come and gone.
Alas, my life is still
A God-forgetfulness-sleep.

39 Each Soul ...

Each soul
Is a special God-Dream-Beauty
And God-Reality-Fragrance.

3 December 2004 ...

3 December 2004

Sanya, China

40 God Wants Me To Pave ...

God wants me to pave
My life-road to my destination
With the tears of my heart
And the smiles of my soul.

41 I Have Come Into The World ...

I have come into the world
To establish my friendship
And not my lordship.

42 In The Dedication-World ...

In the dedication-world,
Our swift feet and our strong arms
Receive very special Blessings
From God.

43 A True Seeker ...

A true seeker
Feels sad and miserable
When he is away from
His God-silence-heart-home.

44 We Must Cover Countless Miles ...

We must cover countless miles
Of aspiration-dedication-road
Before we can arrive
At our God-realisation-destination.

45 God For God’s Sake ...

God for God’s sake —
This is, indeed,
The only perfect choice
In a seeker’s life.

46 An Eagerness-God-Server ...

An eagerness-God-server
Gets more and more work
From God’s proud Heart.

47 My Desire-Fulfilment-Hunger ...

My desire-fulfilment-hunger
Has finally surrendered
To my God-needs.

48 I Must Focus Only ...

I must focus only
On my destination,
And not on my mind-hesitation.

49 God Deliberately Ignores ...

God deliberately ignores
Our curiosity-mind-questions.

4 December 2004 ...

4 December 2004

Sanya, China

50 We Have No Idea ...

We have no idea
When the time will arrive.
Time keeps it a top secret.

51 Every Seeker Has The Capacity ...

Every seeker has the capacity
To be tall, very tall,
In inner height.

52 God Does Not Want To Remember ...

God does not want to remember
The names
Of proud seekers.

53 God Says To A Humble Seeker ...

God says to a humble seeker,
“My child,
I want you to use
My own Name.”

54 The Foundation Of Realisation ...

The foundation of realisation
Is unconditional surrender
To God’s Will.

55 Those Who Sincerely Pray ...

Those who sincerely pray
Will never want to be
On their own.

56 I Came Into The World ...

I came into the world
To pull Heaven down quickly
And push earth up very carefully.

57 Every Morning I Meet And Greet ...

Every morning I meet and greet
God’s Compassion-Eye
Most soulfully.

58 Do Not Wait ...

Do not wait
For a new year.
Here and now
Make a fresh start
To realise God.

59 The Heart ...

The heart
Of a true disciple
Is always original.

60 Do Not Care For ...

I do not care for
The name of a seeker,
But I do care for
The God-loving and God-caring soul.

61 It Is The Hard-Earned Progress ...

It is the hard-earned progress
That endures.

62 To Be Independent ...

To be independent
Of God’s Will
Is an absurdity.

63 At Times ...

At times
God’s Silence
Is the very best answer.

5 December 2004 ...

5 December 2004

Sanya, China

64 May My Life Be Fed ...

May my life be fed
By my heart’s
Sunrise-beauty.

65 The Most Effective Medicine ...

The most effective medicine
Here on earth
Is love unconditional.

66 Those Who Live In The Desire-Forest ...

Those who live in the desire-forest
Will not be allowed to live
In God’s Heart-Nest.

67 The Outer Books ...

The outer books
Cannot cure the world.
The inner book can.
What is the inner book?
Our faith in God and in ourselves.

68 God Chose Me ...

God chose me
To be a lamb, His lamb,
In the inner world.
Now God is preparing me
To be a lion, His lion,
In the outer world.

69 God Wants From Me ...

God wants from me
An action-answer
And not a speech-answer.

70 Adversity Can Be ...

Adversity can be
A unique education-helper.

71 Do Not Curse The Darkness-Night ...

Do not curse the darkness-night.
Try to live always
In the Heart of Light.

72 Not When I Die ...

Not when I die,
But when I dive deep within,
Will I arrive at God’s Heart-Door.

73 My Lord ...

My Lord,
To my heart
You are the Lord of Compassion.

74 My Lord ...

My Lord,
To my mind
You are the Lord of Indifference.

75 My Lord ...

My Lord,
To my eyes
You are the Lord
Of ever-transcending Beauty.

76 My Lord ...

My Lord,
To my life
You are the Lord
Of ever-self-giving Duty.

77 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I worship the Beauty unparalleled
Of Your Eye.

78 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I worship the Fragrance exquisite
Of Your Heart.

79 First Dream And Dream ...

First dream and dream
And then act and act,
Ceaselessly and unconditionally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 32, Agni Press, 2005
