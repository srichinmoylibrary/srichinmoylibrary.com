166. Days of darkness-night

Days of darkness-night
Are short-lived.
I knew it.
My soul-height taught me
This truth sublime.

Days of darkness-night
Are short-lived.
I knew it.
My heart-depth taught me
This wisdom sublime.

Days of darkness-night
Are short-lived.
I knew it.
My smile-breadth taught me
This God-lesson unparalleled.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
