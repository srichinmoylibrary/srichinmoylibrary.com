50.

There is a secret and sacred God-society
That thinks and feels
That e-mail is synonymous
     With evil mail.
Fortunately or unfortunately,
I am a solid member
     Of that society.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-vacation aspiration-prayers, part 6, Agni Press, 2001
