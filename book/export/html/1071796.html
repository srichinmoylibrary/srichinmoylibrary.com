April 16

How to conquer ego? Start chasing your ego, the thief, early in the morning. In the evening you will be able to catch it. Once you catch it, cut it into pieces with your naked inner sword of universal oneness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
