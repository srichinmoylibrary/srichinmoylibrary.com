Three most significant things

Just go to sleep
  And don't complain.
God will think of you.

Just go to sleep
  And don't complain.
God will meditate on you.

Just go to sleep
  And don't complain.
God will do everything for you.

He has already done
  Three most
Significant things for you:
  He calls you 'son'.
     He has given you
     The message of Liberation.
        He has given you the key
        To unlock the Palace of Perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Meditation: humanity's race and Divinity's Grace, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
