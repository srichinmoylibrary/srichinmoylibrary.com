33. Welcome change

Don't resist;
   Be flexible.
Don't be rigid;
   Be supple.
Welcome change!
Each change
   Is an illumining opportunity
To knock at the door
   Of fulfilling progress sweet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Yesterday I was a crying dream, Agni Press, 1975
