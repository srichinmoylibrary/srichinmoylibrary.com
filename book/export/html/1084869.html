27. God's eternal Choice

Earth-grief
   burned his
        body's frame.

Earth-jealousy
   burned his
        life-fame.

Earth-ignorance
   burned his
        God-Vision.

Yet his soul
   loves art,
        treasures art,
For art is
   God's eternal Choice,
        His ultimate Voice.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 18, Agni Press, 1974
