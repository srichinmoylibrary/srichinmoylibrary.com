3700 Your Heart's Gratitude-Plant

Give what you truly
Never want to give:
Your heart's secret and sacred joy.

Keep what you truly
Never want to keep:
Your heart's tiny, innocent gratitude-plant.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
