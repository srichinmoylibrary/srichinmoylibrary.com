Ar Sab Jai Jak

Ar sab jai jak ar sab jai jak
Sabiy chale jak
Prana pane satya nishtha
Nitya dhare thak

Translation:

Let everything else go,
Let everything else go.
Always hold fast to Truth
For your very existence-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy rosebuds, , 1987
