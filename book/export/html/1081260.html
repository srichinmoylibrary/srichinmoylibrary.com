329. I know the secret desires of your heart

I know
The secret desires of your heart.
You want to bind,
You want to blind
This world of beauty's glow.
Good luck!
Indeed, you are so smart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, From the Source to the Source, Agni Press, 1974
