26 August 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
No hesitation-mind,
No suspicion-mind,
No God-doubt-mind
Will ever be able to arrive
At God's Palace.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 August 1999, 10:14 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 200 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
