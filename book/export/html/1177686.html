4716 If Your Heart Pines To Discover

If your heart pines to discover
Life's mystic fount,
Then discontinue
Your long-standing association
With the frowns of ignorance-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 48, Agni Press, 1982
