50 I

I

I was defeated
Because
I stayed with my little i,
Ignorance-eye.
I am defeated
Because
I do not feed
My big I,
Universal I.
I shall succeed
Because
I shall claim God-I
As my only I.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
