140. Who announces?

Who announces God's arrival?
The seeker in me.

Who announces God's victory?
The lover in me.

Who announces my arrival?
The dreamer in me.

Who announces my victory?
The sleeper in me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
