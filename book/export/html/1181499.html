8492 The Right Approach

He made the wrong approach:
He wanted to conquer the world
With words of hate.
He is now making the right approach:
He is conquering the world
With acts of love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 85, Agni Press, 1983
