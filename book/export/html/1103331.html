16 March 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only one thing is indispensable
In my spiritual life
For God-realisation:
My obedience to my Master's will,
My obedience to my Master's will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 March 1999, 6:52 a.m. Before using double-arm machine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
