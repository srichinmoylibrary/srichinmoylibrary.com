435. What does it matter?

What does it matter
If God loves me or not?
What does it matter
If God cares for me or not?
Do I love God?
If I answer this question in the affirmative,
Then there can be no reason
Why God will not love me
Or will not care for me.
God always does infinitely more for us
Than we do for Him.
He does according to His Necessity's capacity.
We do at our capacity's necessity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
