Chokher Jale Khunjechi

Chokher jale khunjechi
Hiyar jale pujechi
Premer bale dekhechi
Tyager bale peyechi

Translation:

With tears in my eyes
I have searched for You.
With tears in my heart
I have worshiped You.
With love in my life
I have invoked You.
With my self-giving renunciation
I have discovered You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
