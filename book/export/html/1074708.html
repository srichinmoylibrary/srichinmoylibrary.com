144. Be Thou

Be Thou my body
That I may wake.
Be Thou my vital
That I may run.
Be Thou my mind
That I may fly.
Be Thou my heart
That I may dive.
Be Thou my soul
That I may reveal.
Be Thou my goal
That I may fulfil.
Be Thou my all
That I may only be Yours.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 3, Agni Press, 1973
