16 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
The divine within
Welcomes and embraces
The divine without.
The undivine within
Challenges
The undivine without,
And they fight and destroy each other.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
