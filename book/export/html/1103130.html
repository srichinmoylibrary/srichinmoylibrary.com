3 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May I prayerfully breathe in Love
From Your Heart
And soulfully breathe out peace
To all my brothers and sisters
Of the world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 October 1998, 4:27 a.m. Before lifting 93 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
