27.

God whispers,
“My child,
I love you, I need you,
I thank you, I thank you.”
God,
I thank You, I thank You,
I thank You.
You are my heart’s
Dream-Boat-Reality-Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My early morning heart-climbing prayers, Agni Press, 2006
