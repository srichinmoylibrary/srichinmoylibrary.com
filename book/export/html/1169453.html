98. I shall not fail

I may constantly wail,
But I shall not fail.
My body is surprisingly frail,
But I shall not fail.
Although I step not on wisdom-scale,
No, never shall I fail.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
