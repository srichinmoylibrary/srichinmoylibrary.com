983. My Lord, the teacher

My Lord taught me
How to pray and expect
Good answers.

My Lord teaches me
How to pray and welcome
Sad answers.

My Lord shall teach me
How to pray and expect
No answer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 20, Agni Press, 1973
