552. Life's night, life's day

Each life has a night;
Each life has a day.
The night of a life
Has silence in it.
The day of a life
Has sound in it.
Life's night, life's day
Complete and fulfil
God's stay on earth,
God's stay in Heaven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
