337. Dear Doubt

Dear Doubt, after I have passed
Behind the curtain of eternity,
I might visit your country.
But now, during my earth-sojourn,
I shall stay only with my bosom friend
Faith.
Once more, if you
Darken my mind-door,
I shall never leave
My Faith's immortal shore.
So, be careful,
Doubt, my dear.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
