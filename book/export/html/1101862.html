16.

My sweet Lord,
How many steps are there
To reach God?
"My child,
There is only one step."
Only one step!
Please tell me
The name of the step.
"It is called
Your life's union-surrender-step."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord, make me Your happiness-child, Agni Press, 1992
