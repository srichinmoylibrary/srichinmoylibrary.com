16.

Tagore loved Subhas.
Tagore chided him.
And, in the evening of Tagore's life,
Tagore became fully aware of the undeniable fact
That Subhas was the man of the hour,
The man of the century
And the man of India's destiny.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Mother, Your 50th Independence-Anniversary! I am come. Ever in Your Eternity's Cries and Your Infinity's Smiles, Subhas, Agni Press, 1997
