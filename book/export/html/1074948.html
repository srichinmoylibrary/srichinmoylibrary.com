379. Addition and subtraction

I wanted to be really rich;
   Therefore
Constantly I wanted to add
   To my possessions.

God wanted me to be supremely rich;
   Therefore
He most carefully subtracted
   My possessions
           From
   My desire-life,
           And
Most carefully added
   My renunciation
   To my aspiration-soul
           And
   Realisation-Goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 8, Agni Press, 1973
