656. My wishes

My dear wish
Is to love God.
My heart is the witness.

My dearer wish
Is to serve God.
My soul is the witness.

My dearest wish
Is to fulfil God.
I have two witnesses:
Heaven and earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
