185 A Common Ability

The ability to hope,
Not everyone has.
The ability to smile,
Not everyone has.
But
The ability to cry,
Everyone has.

The following fifteen poems were written on 27 October 1979 on a plane trip from Osaka to Tokyo.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 2, Agni Press, 1979
