237. I have a hungry heart

I have a hungry heart;
Therefore
God loves me.

I have a weary mind;
Therefore
I love myself.

I have a fiery vital;
Therefore
The world hates me.

I have a drowsy body;
Therefore
I hate myself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
