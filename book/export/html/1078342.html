695 Chaos Grows Thick...

CHAOS GROWS THICK

Chaos grows thick
As my body snores.

Chaos grows thicker
As my vital roars.

Chaos grows thickest
As my mind doubts.

Chaos grows thicker than the thickest
As my heart shouts.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
