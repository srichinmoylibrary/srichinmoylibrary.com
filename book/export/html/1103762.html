12 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When my mind believes,
My heart receives.
And when my heart receives,
My life achieves.
What does my life achieve?
It achieves Your Compassion,
It achieves Your Satisfaction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 September 1999, 8:28 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 540 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
