25894

We are doomed to disappointment
When we see someone
Has equalled us,
But in God's case,
He gets Infinity's Joy
And Immortality's Delight
When we just make an attempt
To equal Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 259, Agni Press, 1998
