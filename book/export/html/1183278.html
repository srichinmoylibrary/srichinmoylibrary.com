142. What can I do

You have two timid feet.
Alas, what can I do?
What can my feet do for you?
Indeed, it is a helpless case.

You have two timid eyes.
Alas, what can I do?
What can my eyes do for you?
Indeed, it is a hopeless case.

You have a timid heart.
Alas, what can my heart do for you?
Indeed, it is a useless case.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
