10484

"O ignorance,
You can take back
Your destruction-weapons
And use them,
Since I do not use them
Anymore."
This is the lesson
That the illumined mind teaches.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 105, Agni Press, 1986
