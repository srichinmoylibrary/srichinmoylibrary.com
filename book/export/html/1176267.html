3311 My Inexorable Fate

Not to think of God
Is my tragic fate.
Not to pray to God
Is my hostile fate.
Not to meditate on God
Is my unthinkable fate.
Not to love God
Is my unforgivable fate.
But to become eventually another God
Is my inexorable fate.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 34, Agni Press, 1982
