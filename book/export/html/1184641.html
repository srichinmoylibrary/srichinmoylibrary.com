313. Conflicting philosophies

The body is just a thing;
Don't waste your time on it.
My philosopher-friend has taught me this.

The body is a temple in which to worship God;
Appreciate it, admire it, love it.
My Yogi-friend has taught me this.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
