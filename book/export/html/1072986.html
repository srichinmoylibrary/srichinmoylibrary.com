4. Transcendence

The human in us
Ascends for success,
   Greater success
        And
            Greatest success.

The divine in us
Transcends for self-giving,
   More self-giving,
     And
        Most self-giving.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
