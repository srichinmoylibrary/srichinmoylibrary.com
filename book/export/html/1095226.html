My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 19

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Australia in January 2003.

7 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

1 The Only Way To Free Oneself ...

The only way to free oneself
From the mind’s prison
Is to forgive others.

2 Hesitation, Doubt And Fear ...

Hesitation, doubt and fear
Leave me
Only when I stay
On the God-navigated course.

3 Like My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

Like my Lord Beloved Supreme,
I also must take
A full-time service:
Loving unreservedly
And unconditionally
Each and every human being.

4 My Meditation-Depth ...

My meditation-depth
Is my Lord’s
Ecstasy-Height-Pride.

5 My Lord’s ...

My Lord’s
Compassion-Concern-Heart-Door
Never has any lock.

6 Our Mind-Maladies ...

Our mind-maladies
Can be cured
Only by repeating constantly
God’s sacred Name.

7 Morning Devotion-Singers ...

Morning devotion-singers
Give tremendous joy
To their Lord Supreme.

8 Music Of The Heart-Tears ...

Music of the heart-tears
And heart-smiles
Is bound to thrill
All sincere seekers of the world.

9 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I pray to my Lord Supreme
To bless me
With a rainbow-heart-beauty-smile.

10 Just Yesterday ...

Just yesterday
My Lord Beloved Supreme scolded me
Most powerfully.
He said, “My child,
You must take My express Permission
Before you do something
Or say something important.”

8 January 2003 ...

8 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

11 When I Give God ...

When I give God
More than I promised,
He gives me
Seven transcendental Smiles.

12 Dreams Come True ...

Dreams come true
Only if we dearly love them
And proudly treasure them.

13 Humanity Is Not Interested ...

Humanity is not interested
In the light.
It is interested only
In the power-supremacy.

14 My Acceptance Of Spirituality ...

My acceptance of spirituality
Is neither my obligation
Nor my responsibility,
But a golden opportunity
To love God and be loved by God.

15 Life Has No Dead End ....

Life has no dead end.
Life carries the promise
Of endless progress-delight.

16 When My Mind Is Happy, ...

When my mind is happy,
Everything is pure.
When my heart is happy,
Everything is perfect
And everybody is perfect.
When I am happy,
My Lord Supreme
Immediately embraces me.

17 My Lord, May I Offer You ...

My Lord, may I offer You
A true story?
“No!”
My Lord, may I offer You
A beautiful poem?
“No!”
My Lord, may I offer You
A soulful song?
“No!”
My Lord, may I offer You
My gratitude-heart-tears?
“Yes, yes, yes!
My child, start offering.”

9 January 2003 ...

9 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

18 The Songs Of My Voice ...

The songs of my voice
Are pure and sweet.
The songs of my heart
Can touch God’s Feet.

19 The Self-Mastery-Education ...

The self-mastery-education
Nobody can ever complete.

20 To Pay Even The Slightest Attention ...

To pay even the slightest attention
To my insecurity-life
Is the height of my stupidity.

21 I Always Approach ...

I always approach
My Lord Beloved Supreme
On my bended knees
And not just with my folded hands.

22 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I place my sincerity-heart-tears
And my sincerity-heart-smiles
At the Feet of my Lord Supreme
Before I enter into my meditation.

23 My Lord Beloved Supreme Himself ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme Himself
Uses
My God-devotion-heart-magnet.

24 My Constant God-Dependence ...

My constant God-dependence
Is the safest and surest
God-discovery-road.

25 My God-Aspiration-Questions ...

My God-aspiration-questions
And answers of the past
Are of no avail.
My God-aspiration-questions
And answers of the future
Are simply ridiculous.
My God-aspiration-questions
And answers of my present life
Are of supreme importance.

26 My Lord, ...

My Lord,
My only prayer to You
Is this:
Do continue to remain
Fully in charge of my life.

10 January 2003 ...

10 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

27 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I become
My heart’s God-worshipping joy.

28 Each Happy Thought ...

Each happy thought
And each happy feeling
Gives me the strength
Of a roaring lion.

29 Delight ...

Delight
Is bound to permeate
A God-surrender-heart.

30 Spirituality Alone Succeeds ...

Spirituality alone succeeds
In giving the God-seekers
A God-oneness-home.

31 Be Brave, Divinely Brave, ...

Be brave, divinely brave,
Supremely brave!
Do not surrender
To despair.

32 Kneeling Prayers ...

Kneeling prayers
Receive immediately
Illumining Smiles
From God’s Eye.

33 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I memorise four soul-stirring
And heart-elevating Messages
From my Lord Supreme.

34 God Wants My Life To Be ...

God wants my life to be
The beauty of my heart
And
The fragrance of my soul.

35 My Only Need ...

My only need
Is what my Lord
Blessingfully gives me.

11 January 2003 ...

11 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

36 Each New Moment ...

Each new moment
Is a new bloom
Of my aspiration-heart.

37 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That my God-gratitude-heart
Is my promise-fulfilled life.

38 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That the surrender of my mind
Is His prized possession.

39 My Desire Abruptly Closes ...

My desire abruptly closes
My heart-door.
My aspiration slowly, steadily
And unerringly opens
My heart-door
And keeps it open.

40 The Golden Age Has Decided ...

The Golden Age has decided
Not to sleep any more.
It is about to wake up
To illumine the world
Once again.

41 Falsehood ...

Falsehood
Is sensation.
Truth
Is compassion.

42 Falsehood ...

Falsehood
Is fearless.
Truth
Is deathless.

43 Falsehood ...

Falsehood
Ultimately fails.
Truth
Sleeplessly sails.

44 Falsehood ...

Falsehood
Attracts.
Truth
Rejects.

45 My Lord Supreme Himself ...

My Lord Supreme Himself
Proudly hoists
My obedience-flag.

13 January 2003 ...

13 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

46 No Streaming Heart-Tears, ...

No streaming heart-tears,
No express God-Arrival.

47 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Visits
My sleeping body.

48 God’s Pride-Head ...

God’s Pride-Head
Visits
My serving life.

49 God’s Delight-Heart ...

God’s Delight-Heart
Visits
My surrendering breath.

50 My God-Thoughts Dream And Dream ...

My God-thoughts dream and dream
Inside the silence
Of my sweet and deep sleep.

51 My God-Actions ...

My God-actions
Are invited by God Himself
To fly and fly
In His Infinity’s Sky.

52 God Is Not Displeased ...

God is not displeased
With my feeble weakness-body.

53 God Is Extremely Displeased ...

God is extremely displeased
With my strong, bold and wild
Disobedience-vital.

54 God Is At Once ...

God is at once
A Compassion-Teacher
And an Illumination-Student.

55 The Beauty Of A Devotion-Heart ...

The beauty of a devotion-heart
And the fragrance of a surrender-breath
Are the supremely choice Blessings
From the Unknowable.

56 Alas, ...

Alas,
There is not a single day
That our doubting minds
Do not torture us
And our Lord Beloved Supreme.

14 January 2003 ...

14 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

57 I Love My Soul-Commands — ...

I love my soul-commands —
I really do!

58 I Love My Heart-Whispers — ...

I love my heart-whispers —
I really do!

59 I Am Not A Singer, ...

I am not a singer,
But the angels come and sing for me.
Why?
Because they have been asked
By my Lord Beloved Supreme.

60 I Am Not A Seeker, ...

I am not a seeker,
But the cosmic gods come and bless me.
Why?
Because they have been asked
By my Lord Beloved Supreme.

61 My Lord’s Feet ...

My Lord’s Feet
Beckon
My prayer-tears.

62 My Lord’s Heart ...

My Lord’s Heart
Beckons
My meditation-silence.

63 We Must Not Anticipate ...

We must not anticipate.
At God’s choice Hour,
God will definitely
Do the needful.

64 Those Who Pray ...

Those who pray
For a world-oneness-heart
Are the harbingers
Of tomorrow’s golden dawn.

65 My Hope-Heart-Bloom ...

My hope-heart-bloom
Takes me to God.

66 My Promise-Soul-Blossom ...

My promise-soul-blossom
Brings God to me.

15 January 2003 ...

15 JANUARY 2003

CAIRNS, AUSTRALIA

67 All That Lives ...

All that lives
Is my heart’s beauty.
All that dies
Is my mind’s ugliness.

68 One Forgiveness-Prayer-Tear ...

One forgiveness-prayer-tear
Can stand against
Ten serious blunders.

69 We Can Not Only Practise Meditation, ...

We can not only practise meditation,
But also can become
The Inner Pilot of meditation.

70 My Thought Can Be Displeased ...

My thought can be displeased
With God,
But my feeling cannot.

71 When I Talk To God, ...

When I talk to God,
Man becomes jealous.
When I talk to man,
I receive a very powerful Embrace
From my Lord Supreme.

72 Life Is A Golden Opportunity ....

Life is a golden opportunity.
The sooner we realise it,
The better.

73 My Self-Transcendence Is Blessed ...

My self-transcendence is blessed
By Eternity’s Silence,
Infinity’s Sound
And Immortality’s Delight.

74 My God-Revelation ...

My God-revelation
Is the beauty of my realisation.
My God-manifestation
Is the fragrance of my realisation.

75 The Tears Of My Heart ...

The tears of my heart
And the smiles of my soul
Are the Footsteps
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

76 My Hope-Heart ...

My hope-heart
Starts.
My promise-breath
Completes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 19, Agni Press, 2003
