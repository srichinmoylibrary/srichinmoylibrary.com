9 September 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do occupy my mind's desire-house
Exactly the way You have occupied
My heart's aspiration-temple.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 September 1999, 8:55 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred and five times. (53 right, 52 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
