May 8

Reason was nectar while we marched along the path of morality. Now that we have accepted spirituality reason is nothing but poison. And what is nectar now? Faith. Faith in whom? Faith in the Supreme and faith in ourselves. It is a proven fact that we can never have faith in ourselves unless we have faith in the Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flame-Goal, Agni Press, 1973
