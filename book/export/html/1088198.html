35.

My Lord Supreme,
Your earth-family always cries for You.
Your Heaven-family
always sings and dances with You.
Is it fair?
What are You doing
to Your poor earth-family?

Surashri Paradis

From: Sri Chinmoy, In the amusement-enlightenment Heart-Garden of the Lord Supreme, Agni Press, 1995
