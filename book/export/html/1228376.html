April 13th, 1964

April 13th, 1964 —
Khuliyache mor hiya dor.
April 13th, 1964 —
Dure bahu dure ama nisha ghor.
April 13th, 1964 —
Tumi mor maha swapaner bhor.
April 13th, 1964 —
America’s oneness-core.
April 13th, 1964 —
Arrived at the world-power-shore.
April 13th, 1964!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Namo namo namo shakti pujari, , 1997
