21403

This morning I gave my Lord Supreme
My most prayerful message:
"My Lord,
As long as I remain on earth,
My heart's gratitude-plant for You
Will every day grow tall,
Taller and tallest."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 215, Agni Press, 1994
