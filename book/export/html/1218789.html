Jagater Byatha Simar Bandhan Ei Hiya Jadi Na

Jagater byatha simar bandhan
Ei hiya jadi na kare piran
Shanti mukti karina kamana ar
Sarba jiber muktir lagi
Tapasya kare nishidin jagi
Toma lagi ek gantibo hirak har

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ashar duar, part 2, , 2004
