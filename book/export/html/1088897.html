<em>Donna Halper:</em> So on a personal basis, then, our relationships are not an accident? We don't just randomly find each other, but the people who find each other are finding each other because God wants them to find each other?

Sri Chinmoy: Not in every case. There is something called God's Hour. When God's Hour strikes, the persons who are needed will come. Otherwise, every person we meet is not sent by God.
