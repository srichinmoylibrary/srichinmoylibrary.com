My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 28

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Yogyakarta and Bali, Indonesia in December 2003 and January 2004.

28 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

1 My Lord Takes Care ...

My Lord takes care
Not only of my divine qualities,
But also of my undivine qualities.

2 My Lord Takes Care ...

My Lord takes care
Not only of my success-mind
And progress-heart,
But also of my failure-life.

3 My Lord Takes Care ...

My Lord takes care
Not only of my soul’s
Strengthening smiles,
But also of my life’s
Tearing fears.

4 My Lord Loves ...

My Lord loves
My body’s baby steps
And my soul’s giant steps
Equally.

5 I Love ...

I love
My Lord’s Thunder-Kicks
More than I love
My Lord’s Sweetness-Heart.
Why, why, why?
Because I desire
The fastest progress.

6 I Love ...

I love
My Lord’s Volcano-Eye
More than I love
My Lord’s soft and tender Caresses.
Why, why, why?
Because I desire
The fastest progress.

7 Now That I Am At The Lotus-Feet ...

Now that I am at the Lotus-Feet
Of my Lord Supreme,
My desire-life has come
To a complete and permanent halt.

8 Today I Am So Happy ...

Today I am so happy
Precisely because
My age-old stubborn mind
Is running towards
My Lord’s Heart-Home.

9 Today I Am So Happy ...

Today I am so happy
Because my heart-tears
And God’s Heart-Smiles
Are singing and dancing together.

10 Today I Am So Happy To See ...

Today I am so happy to see
That my infinite Lord
Has chosen my finite life
To play with Him.

29 December 2003 ...

29 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

11 Nobody Knows ...

Nobody knows
Which came into existence first:
Love or the heart.

12 May My Aspiration-Heart-River ...

May my aspiration-heart-river
Run, singing,
Towards the Ocean Unknowable.

13 May My Dedication-Life ...

May my dedication-life
Fly, dancing,
Towards the Skies Unknowable.

14 The Tears Of My Heart ...

The tears of my heart
Are always ascending
Towards Heaven.

15 The Smiles Of My Soul ...

The smiles of my soul
Are always descending
Towards the earth.

16 God Has Chosen ...

God has chosen
His Cosmic Eye
To be the sole Conductor
Of His Cosmic Orchestra.

17 My Devotion-Tears ...

My devotion-tears
My Lord Supreme enjoys
As His most delicious feast.

18 My Cheerfulness-Temptation ...

My cheerfulness-temptation
God cannot resist.
He comes running
To grab it!

19 My God-Gratitude-Heart ...

My God-gratitude-heart
Is, indeed, a fact,
Not a legend.

20 God Created Each Human Being ...

God created each human being
In a unique way
So that He can be pleased
By each human being
In a unique way.

30 December 2003 ...

30 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

21 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Takes His Compassion-Exercises
Twenty-four hours a day.

22 Love The World ...

Love the world
And serve the world
Unconditionally.
You will be the golden bridge
Between today’s world
And the years ahead of you.

23 My Lord ...

My Lord,
It is Your flaming Breath
Inside my heart
That is keeping me alive
Here on earth.

24 No More Shallow Promises ...

No more shallow promises,
My Lord!
This time I am determined
To fulfil my promise.
I shall please You at every moment
In Your own Way.

25 The Silence Of The Mind ...

The silence of the mind
Embodies
The Power of the Infinite.

26 A Wasted Moment ...

A wasted moment
Is the very beginning
Of my failure-life.

27 I Was The Worst Possible Fool ...

I was the worst possible fool.
I took my Lord’s
Compassion-flooded Eye
For granted.

28 Each And Every Surrender-Breath ...

Each and every surrender-breath
Of my heart
Prayerfully watches my Lord
Smiling, singing and dancing.

29 My Sleepless Surrender ...

My sleepless surrender
To God’s Will
Must never falter,
Never!

30 Every Morning And Every Evening ...

Every morning and every evening
My Beloved Supreme sumptuously feeds
My aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
To strengthen them
So that they can perform
Their respective tasks
Most successfully and most gloriously.

31 December 2003 ...

31 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

31 The Difference Between ...

The difference between
God’s Eye and God’s Heart
Is this:
God’s Eye promises;
God’s Heart fulfils.

32 God-Existence ...

God-Existence
In my life
Is my heart’s faith-flame.

33 Silence Speaks ...

Silence speaks.
Silence speaks
Very, very quietly.

34 Silence Listens ...

Silence listens.
Silence listens
Very, very carefully.

35 As The Prison Door ...

As the prison door
Of my doubting mind
Is wide open,
Even so the Heart-Door
Of my Lord Supreme
Is sleeplessly and completely open.

36 My Heart-Bird Flies And Flies ...

My heart-bird flies and flies
And then encircles
My soul-sky.

37 My Mind Knows ...

My mind knows
How to impose
Limitation-boundaries.

38 My Soul And I Know ...

My soul and I know
How to smash asunder
All the limitation-boundaries
Created by the mind.

39 Newness Glows ...

Newness glows
In God’s Eye
Breathtakingly.

40 Fulness Grows ...

Fulness grows
In God’s Heart
Sempiternally.

41 I Place ...

I place
My God-gratitude-heart-garland
At my Lord’s Lotus-Feet.

42 My Lord Picks Up My Garland ...

My Lord picks up my garland
From His Feet
And puts it around my neck
With His Transcendental Smile
And Pride.

1 January 2004 ...

1 January 2004

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

43 This Morning ...

This morning
My Inner Pilot said to me,
“My child,
Let your heart and My Heart
Exchange their gratitude-beauty,
Gratitude-fragrance
And gratitude-delight.”

44 Today The Talker In Me ...

Today the talker in me
Has surrendered completely
To the God-server in me.

45 Today Something Absolutely New ...

Today something absolutely new
Must take place
In my spiritual life.

46 Today My Soul, My Heart ...

Today my soul, my heart,
My mind, my vital,
My body and I
Together must do something
Unprecedented.

47 Today Without Fail ...

Today without fail
I shall make God,
My only Master-Lord,
Happy in His own Way.

48 An Endless ...

An endless
Desire-darkness-life
I was.

49 A Sleepless And Breathless ...

A sleepless and breathless
Aspiration-light-heart
I now am.

50 God Does Not Want To Know ...

God does not want to know
What I do
As long as I keep His Vision-Eye
In each and every action of mine.

51 Readiness, Soulfulness ...

Readiness, soulfulness,
Willingness, eagerness and I
Have decided to make oneness
Our team captain.

52 If God Thinks I Can ...

If God thinks I can,
Then definitely I can.
I must do it!
Lo, I have done it:
My God-surrender unconditional.

53 My Mind ...

My mind,
Do you really want to be happy?
Then start thinking,
Start doing,
Start becoming
Only in God’s own Way.

54 Today ...

Today
I have completely liberated myself
From the fetters
Of my doubting and suspecting mind.

55 Today I Am Swimming ...

Today I am swimming
In the sea of delight,
For I am able to run
At long last
The full length
Of my Lord’s commanding Will.

2 January 2004 ...

2 January 2004

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

56 My God-Hungry Eyes ...

My God-hungry eyes,
I deeply love you.

57 My God-Hungry Heart ...

My God-hungry heart,
I desperately need you.

58 Beautiful ...

Beautiful
When my Lord’s Eye
Beckons me.

59 Blissful ...

Blissful
When my Lord’s Heart
Beckons me.

60 Godful ...

Godful
When my Lord’s Feet
Beckon me.

61 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Most carefully watches
My sleeping heart, my sleeping mind,
My sleeping vital and my sleeping body.

62 Smiling, Singing And Dancing ...

Smiling, singing and dancing,
My Lord meets with
My surrender’s every need.

63 My Outer God-Obedience-Life ...

My outer God-obedience-life
Garners
Abundant inner wealth.

64 My Inner God-Obedience-Heart ...

My inner God-obedience-heart
Feeds and nourishes unreservedly
My outer life.

65 My Lord Dances ...

My Lord dances
His Ecstasy-Dance
When my heart’s inner day
Begins to break.

66 O God-Obedience-Sky ...

O God-obedience-sky,
My aspiration-heart-flames
Are climbing fast, very fast,
To reach you
And be an inner member
Of your family.

3 January 2004 ...

3 January 2004

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

67 From The Higher Worlds ...

From the higher worlds
The spiritual Masters,
Out of their infinite bounty,
Come down to visit me,
Bless me most powerfully,
Speak to me most affectionately
And appreciate my God-service
To humanity
Most unreservedly.

4 January 2004 ...

4 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

68 I Must Fulfil ...

I must fulfil
The needs of my soul —
I must!
What are the needs of my soul?
My sleepless God-hunger
And my breathless God-service.

69 The Desire-Life-Stories ...

The desire-life-stories
Are extremely painful.

70 The Aspiration-Heart-Songs ...

The aspiration-heart-songs
Are exceedingly haunting.

71 I Love And Bow To ...

I love and bow to,
I bow to and love the God-seekers
Who are infinitely more advanced
Than I am
In the spiritual life.

72 My Heart, My Mind ...

My heart, my mind,
My vital, my body and I
Are fully prepared
To be unconditionally surrendered
God-instruments.

73 Our Heart-Days ...

Our heart-days
Are
Our God-promise-fulfilment-days.

74 No Rest ...

No rest
After God-realisation.
No rest
Even after God-manifestation —
God comes with a new Dream.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 28, Agni Press, 2004
