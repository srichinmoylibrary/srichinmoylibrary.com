74 No Matter What The World ...

No matter what the world
Does to me,
No matter what the world
Thinks of me,
No matter what the world
Says about me,
I shall always be for the world,
For the betterment of the world,
As long as I am in the world of the living.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 8, Agni Press, 2002
