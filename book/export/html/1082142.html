God’s Glance and God’s Grace

God’s Glance is the arrow
     Of brightest flame.
It is the birth of love
     In human frame.

And ever largest in Him
     Is His Sea of Grace
For souls to be the waves
     Upon His Face.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God’s Glance and God’s Grace, Agni Press, 2006
