211. Greatness and goodness

Greatness is not big,
Greatness is small.
Greatness is short,

Greatness is never tall.

It is goodness
That is big and tall.
It is goodness
That is God's treasured doll.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
