11. Rivers of sorrow

Your life is a river of sorrow
Because
Nobody loves you.

Your life is a hundred rivers of sorrow
Because
Nobody needs you.

Your life is a thousand rivers of sorrow
Because
Man-confidence,
Self-confidence,
God-confidence,
Are all strangers to you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
