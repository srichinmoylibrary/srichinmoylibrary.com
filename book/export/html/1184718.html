390. This I know for sure

Your hope for me
Shall sustain me.
This I know for sure.

You will guide me
On my way to God.
This I know for sure.

My life has now become
A trial fatal.
My life's only true need
Is Your Love,
Your Love alone.
This I know for sure.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
