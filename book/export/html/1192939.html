7231

The soul knows
How to wait.
The heart does not know
How to wait.
The mind does not want to know
How to wait.
God the Emperor
Does not wait.
God the Lover
Is always ready to wait.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 73, Agni Press, 1984
