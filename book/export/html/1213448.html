59.

I do not have to convince God
That I do not know anything.
I do not have to convince God
That I know everything.
I have only to convince myself
That I love God and I need God alone.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, part 1, Agni Press, 1996
