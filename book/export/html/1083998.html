23. No one

No one has a kind word
        To tell you.
           Yet
You think of them devotedly.

No one has a kind heart
        To love you.
           Yet
You treasure them soulfully.

No one has a kind soul
        To please you.
           Yet
You bless them lovingly.

I alone know
How great you are.
     God alone knows
How good you are.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
