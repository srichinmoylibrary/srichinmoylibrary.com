45. Two smiles

O height of understanding,
I want you not.
O height of becoming,
I want you.
O height of oneness.
I am you.
In me you see the smile of
Here.
In you I see the smile of
Now.
Here eventually becomes.
Now eternally is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 1, Agni Press, 1973
