783. Who is called, who is chosen?

Who is called?
He alone is called
Who is ready
To fight,
Fight against teeming ignorance.

Who is chosen?
He alone is chosen
Who wants
To win,
Win the Victory supreme.

Who is called?
He alone is called
Who is prepared
To love,
Love the entire world.

Who is chosen?
He alone is chosen
Who embraces,
Embraces the life
Of universal oneness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 16, Agni Press, 1973
