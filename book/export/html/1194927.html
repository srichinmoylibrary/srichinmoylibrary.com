9200

  I wish to surrender,
But how to surrender?
  I wish to be perfect,
But how to be perfect?
I can surrender my existence-life
  To God's Will
And I can become perfect
  In God's Heart
Only when I can sincerely claim
  My Lord Supreme
     As my own, very own.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 92, Agni Press, 1984
