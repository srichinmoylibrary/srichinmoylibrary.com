16300

God loves you
For your heart's
Aspiration-purity.
God loves you
For your life's
Dedication-intensity.
God loves you
For your soul's
Omnipresent luminosity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 163, Agni Press, 1991
