Nata Janu Haye Pujibo Tomare

Nata janu haye pujibo tomare
      Nata janu
Tomar kripai phutibe amar
      Hiya bhanu

Translation:

Kneeling down, I shall worship You,
      Kneeling down.
By Your Grace the sun of my heart
      Will blossom.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
