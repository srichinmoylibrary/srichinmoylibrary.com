15175

God will not only
Stand right in front of you,
But also bless you
With tremendous Joy and Pride,
When He sees your life
As a single-minded resolve
To please Him in His own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 152, Agni Press, 1991
