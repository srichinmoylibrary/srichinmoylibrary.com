10. In common

Earth, I need you,
     Although
We do not have anything
     In common.

Heaven, I love you,
     Although
We do not have anything
     In common.

God, I need you,
  I love You
     Since
You and I have many things
     In common.

Alas, where are You?
And where am I?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
