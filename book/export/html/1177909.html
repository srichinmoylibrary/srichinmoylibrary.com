4937 You Are Completely Wrong

If you think God is displeased with you,
You are right.
But if you think you have
The rare capacity to displease God
Forever and forever,
You are wrong,
Completely wrong.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 50, Agni Press, 1982
