Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 128

12701

My Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
May my gratitude-heart sing and dance
Inside the cathedral of my soul
Every day and every hour!

12702

Perfection is something
That I want to achieve,
But something within me is telling me
That perfection is something
That I must receive
From Above.

12703

One,
Just one
Earth-desire-drop
Has poisoned
My whole Heaven-aspiration-pool.

12704

You have failed,
You are failing
And you will always fail
Because you are not listening
To your heart's silence-voice.

12705

God Himself wants to be
The Advocate of your aspiration-heart.
Let God, and nobody else,
Perform that task for you.

12706

Your heart's day-by-day aspiration
Is God's first choice.
And something else of yours He takes
As His first choice:
Your life's day-by-day dedication.

12707

A day of aspiration
Is a peace-blossoming day.
A day of expectation
Is a peace-breaking night.

12708

It is not enough
That your name is mentioned
On the God-adorers list.
Your name must be mentioned
On the God-lovers
And God-servers list
As well.

12709

God possesses you
Immediately
When you give up the desire
To possess Him
And His creation.

12710

My Lord,
Now that I am accepting
My defeat and my failure
As part of Your cosmic Vision-Plan,
I am exceedingly happy.

12711

Every day
I pray to my Lord Supreme
To give me the capacity
To build up my heart-temple
And tear down my mind-castle.

12712

Every day
I pray to my Lord Supreme
To teach me that His Will
Is my only perfection-freedom.

12713

The gratitude
Of my meditation-heart
And the Smile
Of my Lord's Satisfaction-Heart
Will forever and forever
Live together.

12714

When I cheerfully do
What I can,
My Lord Supreme
Unconditionally does for me
What I cannot.

12715

Every morning before I meditate,
My Beloved Supreme blessingfully gives me
His seven-minute
Compassion-Chariot Ride.
Every morning after I meditate,
My Beloved Supreme proudly gives me
His seven-minute
Satisfaction-Chariot Ride.

12716

My Lord Supreme,
Are You not tired of
My self-deception-meditations?
"My child,
Are you not tired of
My unconditional Forgiveness-Heart?"
No, my Lord!
"Then, no, My child!"

12717

This morning
My Lord Supreme told me
That my soulful prayers
And my powerful meditations
Can and will give me
Ignorance-free meals.

12718

Only the inner hunger-climber
Will reach God's
Satisfaction-Transcendence-Summit.

12719

Of all the roads
That lead to God's Palace,
The gratitude-road is shortest.

12720

Only heart-travellers
Are invited
To take the peace-pilgrimage.

12721

Those who love
The life of discipline
Will definitely be beckoned
By the Wisdom-Light of the Beyond.

12722

Our heart's silence-love
Speaks the most
To those whom we love the best.

12723

It finally dawned on me
That I could also try to become
A choice instrument
Of my Lord Supreme.

12724

God asks you to drink
His Oneness-Delight,
But you are drinking
Gossip-poison instead.

12725

Regularity takes me
To God's Palace.
Punctuality brings God
To my little home.

12726

O my mind,
How long will you remain caught
In the mire of disobedience-night?

12727

We tell the world
That ignorance has invaded us.
Alas, we do not tell the world
That it is we
Who have invited ignorance.

12728

You want peace.
Then why do you live inside
Your mind's attachment-cave?

12729

If you do not use your meditation-hour
Soulfully and sleeplessly,
Then you will be bankrupt
In the world of meditation-light.

12730

Your disappointing enthusiasm
Will definitely take you
Into the world of failure and defeat.

12731

My willingness becomes my cheerfulness.
My cheerfulness becomes my victory
And the Victory of my Lord Supreme.

12732

My desire-life, you are too old!
I do not like you
Anymore.
I do not want to live with you
Anymore.

12733

I am totally lost
Between my mind's sadness-night
And my heart's cheerfulness-sun.

12734

May my heart become
A precious receptivity.
May my life become
A spacious activity.

12735

When he sincerely prays
And soulfully meditates on God,
God broadcasts his spiritual life
On God's TV station.

12736

God right now believes
In my self-giving
And not in my world-illumining.

12737

Today you become
An aspiration-runner.
Tomorrow you are bound to become
A realisation-sprinter.

12738

If God decides to stop using me,
I shall not mind in the least,
For I know that He always knows
What is best for me.

12739

Alas, nobody in Heaven
Missed my illumining soul
When my soul left Heaven for earth.
Alas, alas,
Uninvited my soul came to earth!
Alas, nobody on earth
Missed my loving heart
When my heart left earth for Heaven.
Alas, alas,
Uninvited my heart came to Heaven!

12740

My body,
I really don't know
What my soul sees in you.
My vital,
I really don't want to know
What my soul thinks of you.
My mind,
I really can't tell you
What my soul tells others about you.
My heart,
I must tell you in all sincerity
That my soul loves you infinitely more
Than you can ever feel.

12741

It will be an unusual
And unprecedented
Earth-event-experience
If all the seekers
Form a soulful gratitude-committee.

12742

I always knew
That perfection is the choice
Of the divinely brave
And supremely chosen few.

12743

You cannot enjoy in your outer life
The lion-roar
If you do not have in your inner life
The lion-poise.

12744

My heart,
You are a God-made lover.
My mind,
You are a self-made prisoner.

12745

An unaspiring heart
And an uninspiring mind
Are two sad tragedies
Of the soul.

12746

Do not be a fool;
Start aspiring!
If you do not aspire,
Your soul will soon start
Living without you.

12747

No fragrance
Is more nourishing
Than life's
Self-sacrifice-fragrance.

12748

The mind needs the forest-calm
To be a choice instrument of God.
The heart needs the mountain-height
To be the perfect voice of God.

12749

Each human life
Is God's God-preparation on earth.
Each divine life
Is God's God-Satisfaction
Both on earth and in Heaven.

12750

Dear humanity, can you not see
That early in the morning
Reason limits you,
Early in the evening
It blinds you
And in the middle of the night
It strangles you?

12751

God's greatest
Blessing-Gift
Is my life's
Crying core.

12752

My earth-host
Is my oneness-heart.
My Heaven-guest
Is my fulness-smile.

12753

Start renouncing!
Do you know
That you have to renounce
Only one thing?
That thing is your mind's stupidity.

12754

Determination
Illumines me.
Aspiration
Fulfils me.
Perfection
Satisfies both God and me.

12755

Dear humanity,
I love to be
One of your sufferers.
Dear divinity,
I love to be
One of your self-givers.

12756

My aspiration-heart
Is indeed a gift from God.
My dedication-life
Is indeed my gift to God.

12757

A man of sacrifice
Is the thunderous applause
Of multiplying centuries.

12758

Because God is
His own Compassion-flooded Justice,
Man will eventually become
A perfect God-man.

12759

In the spiritual life,
An eagerness-mind
And a cheerfulness-heart
Are their own immediate rewards.

12760

If you present
Your unwillingness to God,
Then God will remain indifferent
To your mind's confusion-forest.

12761

Every day
You must muster courage
To make a firm commitment
To your obedience-life.

12762

You say that
You have nothing important to do.
But I am telling you,
Try at least to break
Your life's unworthiness-shackles.

12763

Slowly, steadily and unerringly
My inner faith has overthrown
My outer doubt-dictator.

12764

Alas,
I cannot even remember
The last time God smiled at me.

12765

Destiny's decisive hour
Has arrived.
Man's inner freedom
Has fully and fruitfully
Come to the fore.

12766

O my heart's hope-boat,
When will you cross
My life's promise-sea?

12767

Every morning and every evening
God wants me to pay
My perfection-toll
At His Satisfaction-Bridge.

12768

God has a very special fondness
For the rising
Aspiration-flame-songs
Of my heart.

12769

My mind's impossibility-thoughts
Must surrender
To my heart's confidence-will.

12770

A God-lover
Must welcome every day
A newness-flower-dawn.

12771

My prayer is the devotion-escalator
That takes me to God.
My meditation is the love-elevator
That brings God to me.

12772

Alas,
Will there ever be a day
When the human mind
Will not run in worry-circles?

12773

My Lord always, always opens
And never returns unopened
My ignorance-letters.

12774

Nothing
Can be beyond the reach
Of a oneness-peace-lover.

12775

The constant unwillingness
Of the mind
Cripples the heart's
God-vision-plans.

12776

The mind's simplicity
And the heart's sincerity
Are unparalleled partners
To the end.

12777

O Lord, O Lord, O Lord,
When will You silence
My war with myself?
When?

12778

Your false aspiration-flames
Will never be able to illumine
Your mind's teeming doubt-clouds.

12779

I am glad that my expectations
Have been forced to walk
The path of disappointment
By my compassionate Lord Himself,
And not by any human being.

12780

When we keep our eyes open,
We see that our jealous minds
Are heavier than the heaviest.
When we keep our hearts closed,
We feel that our insecure lives
Are heavier than the heaviest.
Alas, alas!

12781

Alas, how can I ever be
A choice instrument of God
When I allow my mind
To be swamped by greed,
And my heart by ingratitude?

12782

Enthusiasm means striking achievements.
Never lose enthusiasm!
Never allow your enthusiasm to depart,
Even when disappointment forces you
To your life's rock bottom.

12783

No ecstasy
Can be deeper
Than my heart's
Silence-ecstasy.

12784

Faith is always progressive.
It does not have to be
Either offensive or defensive.

12785

When I do something undivine,
I hope that God
Is not looking at me.
When I do something divine,
I pray to God
Not only to look at me,
But also to applaud me.

12786

Today my Lord Supreme is telling me
That His Compassion
Is absolutely free of charge
And His Justice borders on indulgence.
I am all eagerness
To have them both
In abundant measure.

12787

This morning God asked me
If I could please Him unconditionally
Only for today.
My immediate answer was
A resounding "Yes!"

12788

A global man
Devotedly serves the world.
He is afraid of neither
The world's stark hostility
Nor the world's abysmal indifference.

12789

A global man is he
Who keeps climbing his soul-heights,
Diving his heart-depths
And marching his mind-lengths.

12790

A global man is a pilgrim-soul
Who comes into the world
With a very special message-light
From beyond the future.

12791

There is no way to escape
A global man's
Vastness-oneness-fulness-heart.

12792

If you are a divine global server,
Then you have no time
To pay any attention
To the world's ingratitude-storms.

12793

The global man knows
That if he ever forgets
His world-progress-dream-promise,
Then he is bound to lose everything.

12794

Give up, give up
Your desire-prayers!
Satisfaction-paradise
Will beckon you.

12795

Since you know that you have a heart,
Can you not make it into a temple?
Since you know that you have a heart-temple,
Can you not turn it into
A sleepless prayer-life?

12796

Do you want to make tangible progress
In your mind's longing for peace?
If so, then every day, every hour,
Offer a prayerful thought
To your heart's all-loving rising sun.

12797

Choose, quickly choose,
The heart of endless faith.
Otherwise, your life will be drenched
By doubt-downpour.

12798

When I gave God my opinion
About ignorance-night,
He said: "My child,
How interesting
Your great observation is!"

12799

Alas,
Hurtful thoughts are flocking
To my mind-door,
And powerful death is knocking
At my heart-door!

12800

May my heart's soulful gratitude-pilgrim
Always walk along
My life's beautiful surrender-road.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 128, Agni Press, 1990
