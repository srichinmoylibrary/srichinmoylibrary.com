527. We do not need your help

God and I do not need your help.
You do not have to
Interpret God.
God is His own perfect interpreter.
You do not have to
Love me.
I am my own matchless lover.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
