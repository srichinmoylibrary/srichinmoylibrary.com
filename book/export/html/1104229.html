11 May 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must not picture my future.
I must live in my promise-fulfilment
Of the Eternal Now.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

11 May 2000, 8:57 p.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
