28.

My Lord,
I have called You
At least a thousand times.
But alas, no reply.
"My child,
You and I are playing
The same game.
I have called you
At least a million times.
But alas, no reply."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 3, Agni Press, 1998
