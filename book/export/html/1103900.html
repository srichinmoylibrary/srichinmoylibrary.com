20 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my life-breath be
The beauty and purity
Of a prayerful and soulful
   Rose.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 October 1999, 9:49 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred and four times. (52 right, 52 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
