Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 200

19901

God's Compassion-Eye
Is the only medicine
That can cure our shattered
Aspiration-hearts.

19902

A genuine hope
Is indeed a fort
To protect us from doubt-attacks.

19903

To fly
In the sky of freedom-happiness,
What we need
Is a faith-foundation
For our life.

19904

Our heart's tears
Must be far beyond the reach
Of our mind's thoughts.

19905

When God descends
With His smiling Eye,
We are supposed to ascend
With our crying heart.

19906

O seeker,
Inside your gratitude-heart
There will always be ample room
For your love, devotion and surrender
To grow and glow.

19907

There is only one way
For us to conquer
Our desire-madness,
And that way is the way
Of our aspiration-gladness.

19908

God is pitifully crying.
Do you know why?
Not because
You are not surrendering
To His Will,
But because
You are not allowing your heart
To claim Him as its very own.

19909

The insecurity-mask
Is larger than the largest.
Therefore, it easily hides
Our heart's Godward aspiration-flames.

19910

The hesitation and frustration
Of the mind
Always try to devour
The aspiration-flames and dedication-drops
Of the heart.

19911

We can grow old
With our desire-mind,
But we always remain young
And ageless
With our aspiration-heart.

19912

I have not one, but two places
To divinely enjoy myself:
Inside my God-manifesting soul
And inside my God-dreaming heart.

19913

Your ignorance-sleep
Will stop
When your genuine God-need
Starts.

19914

You are a victim
To constant helplessness
Because you are unwilling to swim
In God's infinite Compassion-Sea.

19915

A deceptive mind
Is not to be revealed.
It has to be concealed first,
And then illumined
By virtue of our prayer and meditation,
For this is the only way
God's Dream-manifestation can take place.

19916

To climb illumination-peak,
Lighten your mind-load
And brighten your heart-smile.

19917

To accompany God
On His universal Flight,
What we need is
The beauty of daily prayers
And the fragrance of daily meditations.

19918

When I live
Inside my heart's thought-free zone,
God and His Promise-Power
Befriend me.

19919

O my mind,
God wanted you to be
A purity-distributor.
Alas, you have become
An impurity-collector
Instead.

19920

No matter how strong
The mind is,
It can never deceive
The aspiration-heart
Nor force the heart to deviate
From the path of light.

19921

If it is divinity's silence-peace,
Then it can easily silence
The roaming and roaring mind.

19922

The beauty and fragrance
Of surrender-meditation
Have no equal.
Matchless they always remain.

19923

The mind
Is fond of success.
The heart
Is fond of progress.
The soul
Is fond of God-manifestation-promises.

19924

The power of world-meditation
Informs God
About the worldwide distrust
Of Him.

19925

The resentment of the mind
Quite often compels
The God-hungry heart to starve.

19926

Do not be a fool!
Your self-love has no wings.
It can never fly anywhere.
It can only remain
Inside its dark prison cell.

19927

If you run
With a God-revelation-sprinter,
Then easily
You will be able to leave for good
Your mind's ignorance-neighbourhood.

19928

My heart's
Confidence in God,
My heart's
Conference with God,
Even my heart's
Inference of God
Can definitely help me
To cross far beyond
All my doubt-obstacles.

19929

My soul is telling my heart
To bravely face the mind-storm.
The mind-storm is bound to subside.

19930

In your life-garden
Try to grow
As many aspiration-plants as possible,
For they will never produce
Frustration-fruits.

19931

Are you a fool?
Why are you enjoying
Your aspiration-famine
And self-hate-feast?

19932

If aspiration is missing,
Then hope will eventually split
Into a thousand pieces.

19933

Somehow, somewhere,
I always feel there are
Truth-seekers and God-lovers
Who will manifest God on earth
Lovingly, self-givingly and unconditionally.

19934

He who lives only in the heart
And only for his Lord Supreme
Can alone pave the way
For the rest of humanity.

19935

Our predecessors left us
Their blessing-light.
We must leave behind for our successors
Our aspiration-hearts.

19936

When aspiration disappears,
Life becomes
Its endless declining slopes.

19937

You can revive
Your pristine faith
By virtue of
Your heart's childlike cries.

19938

Alas, will there ever come a time
When God will discover
Inside my heart
A precious and valuable possibility?

19939

It seems to me
That my soul's forgiveness
Has no limit,
But its patience has.

19940

A life
Without an aspiration-heart
Is the very beginning
Of God-Satisfaction-famine.

19941

When your disobedience-strides
Are bold and indifferent,
Your heart sinks
In the sea of nowhere.

19942

This morning
I was simply amazed to see
That God was bathing
In my heart's gratitude-tears.

19943

Doubt
Is the inner sovereign;
Pride
Is the outer sovereign
In our unaspiring life.

19944

When aspiration-bird flies away
From our heart-nest,
Our mind's desire-tiger
Starts devouring us.

19945

My Lord,
What shall I do
To make myself happy?
"My child,
Walk beside Me,
Run with Me,
Cry and smile with Me
And for Me."

19946

Each self-giving thought
Is indeed
A perfection-breath-promise
Of our Lord Supreme.

19947

The soul likes to talk to us
In our own voice,
So that we can be more understanding
And more self-giving.

19948

Each soul has its unique way
To spread God's Light
Throughout the length and breadth
Of the world.

19949

A heart of peace
Has no equal
And will always remain
Unchallenged.

19950

When I come first in my life,
And not God,
There can be no room
For my self-improvement.

19951

Heaven
Is compassionate.
Earth
Is affectionate.
May my mind
Be always dispassionate.

19952

If you ever want to meet
Silence,
Then never ride,
Even by mistake,
The temptation-train.

19953

May my life be found
Inside my heart's gratitude-flowers
And not inside
My mind's ingratitude-thorns.

19954

Alas, why do you enjoy
Doubt-downpour
And not faith-sea
Inside your aspiration-heart?

19955

Each self-giving thought
Is indeed
A God-locating compass.

19956

O body,
How long do you want to remain
Inside your self-chosen lethargy-prison?

19957

May my happiness-heart
Decorate my life
With purity-flower-fragrance
Every day.

19958

If we do not follow
Our heart-guide,
Then our mind is bound to be flooded
With disappointment.

19959

The tears of my heart
Are too weak to receive
The smiles of my soul.

19960

You want to know
About my inner journey.
My inner journey,
Right from the beginning,
Is to my mind-transformation-land.

19961

May silence be
My mind's constant companion.
May gratitude be
My heart's constant companion.
May service be
My life's constant companion.

19962

Doubt and hesitation
Are the two most deplorable experiences
In our human life.

19963

I have discovered in God
My perfection-smile.
God has discovered in me
His Promise-Blossom.

19964

Each disobedience-stride
Is leading us
To our unmistakable destruction.

19965

My surrender-heart
Has quickly paved my way
To my Lord's Compassion-Eye
And Protection-Feet.

19966

Not God's Compassion-Eye
But God's Forgiveness-Promise
Will watch over me always.

19967

Be careful,
O truth-seeker and God-lover.
Every day carefully watch
Your heart's aspiration-plant
And do the needful,
For it can any day wither.

19968

The human life
Becomes not only meaningful
But also fruitful
Only when we sing
The deathless song
Of our aspiration-heart.

19969

An unaspiring day
Is nothing short of
A hollow emptiness-cry.

19970

A heart of devotion
Is a soul of satisfaction
In the fulfilment
Of God's Promise on earth.

19971

Where is my heart's peace-home?
Where is it?
How long can I remain
Inside the unfathomable sea
Of excruciating pangs?

19972

Doubt and suspicion
Always arrive unsolicited
And even unwelcomed.

19973

When I look at God's Eye,
I clearly see
That His Eye is made of
Compassion-Sun.
When I look at God's Heart,
I clearly see
That His Heart is made of
Forgiveness-Ocean.

19974

There are so many things
In this world
That drain my heart's joy.
Yet I have no sincere eagerness
To dissociate my life
From them.

19975

Whatever
Our aspiration gives us
Is infinitely generous
And eternally free.

19976

Each good thought
Is an inner promise
To reach God's ultimate Height.

19977

Which heart is the bravest?
Undoubtedly
The silence-flooded peace-heart
Is the bravest of all.

19978

God and God's Compassion
May be invisible,
But they do not always remain
Unattainable.

19979

God's Dream-lovers
Are those enlightened human beings
With an extraordinary
God-fulfilment-promise.

19980

Each human being has to transcend
The strong barriers of praise and scorn
To see the Face of God
And feel the Heart of God.

19981

The mind's certainty
Is not only vulnerable
But also fragile.

19982

O suspicion-mind,
How do you dare
To confine the dream
Of a soaring heart?

19983

Nothing is as essential
As the fragrance
Of a God-manifestation-thought.

19984

Do not enter into the past
To solve today's problems,
But enter into the silence-heart
Of the present.
Then all your problems will be solved.

19985

My heart of aspiration-tears
Will eventually prove to be
The source of my life's
Satisfaction-sun.

19986

To make my heart's
Aspiration-joy everlasting,
I try to keep an unbreakable link
With the inner worlds.

19987

Prayer and meditation
Are definitely two God-instruments.
Prayer manifests its divinity
Through cries.
Meditation manifests its divinity
Through smiles.

19988

No truth-seeker
And no God-lover
Can ever be lost
Once they have entered
Into the realm of aspiration.

19989

The thorns
Try to protect the rose.
The rose
Tries to liberate the thorns.
Similarly, fear
Tries to caution faith.
Faith
Tries to illumine fear.

19990

God will hear
Your mind-prayers
If they are sincere.
God will fulfil
Your heart-meditations
If they are pure.

19991

Sleepless love, breathless devotion
And deathless surrender
Are the secret and sacred combination
To my heart-safe.

19992

How I wish I could earn
A constant
God-obedience-certificate.

19993

It is absolutely certain
That ultimately
The ignorance-lover
And the ignorance-runner in me
Will collapse.

19994

I have discarded all my old desires
Except one:
To become an unconditional world-lover
And an unconditional man-server.

19995

We have been suffering
From ignorance-imperfection-illness
For millions of years.
Now God is descending
With His unconditional Love-Medicine
For our use.

19996

O my mind, how much longer
Can you lead a counterfeit life?
When will you allow simplicity
And sincerity
To enter into you?

19997

When inspiration knocks
At your mind-door,
If you delay in opening the door,
You are a fool.
When aspiration knocks
At your heart-door,
If you delay in opening the door,
You are worse than useless.

19998

Unlike us,
God does not believe in
Our life's inspiration-famine.
He only believes in one thing:
Our heart's inspiration-mountain.

19999

No other power,
Only purity's oneness-power,
Can erase the deplorable
And even irrevocable past.

20000

Each soul
Is divinely special,
Each heart
Is divinely special,
Each life
Is divinely special
Only if we know
That we are growing into
An irresistible God-Satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 200, Agni Press, 1994
