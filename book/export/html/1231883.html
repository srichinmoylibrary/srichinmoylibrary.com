Manab Jiban Manab Maran

Manab jiban manab maran
Chira paraner maha swapaner
Puta abiram lila

Translation:

Human life and human death —
The hallowed beauty-game
Of the Absolute Lord’s Transcendental Dream.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Your face is my dream, , 2009
