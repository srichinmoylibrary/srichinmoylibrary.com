Ami Chai Nahi Pai Kabhu Ami

Ami chai ami chai
     Ami chai
Nahi pai kabhu ami
     Nahi pai
Asha lagi kandi ami
     Asha lagi
Byatha sathe rahi ami
     Sada jagi

Translation:

I want, I want, I want.
I never get, I never get, I never get.
I cry for my old friend, hope.
Alas, now my only friend
Is an undying life of sorrow.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 2, , 1976
