Amar Puta Antare

Amar puta antare
Prabhur hasi santare
Amar madhu swapane
Prabhu nache gopane

Translation:

In my hallowed heart
The smile of my Beloved Supreme
      swims.
In my honey-dream,
In silence, ever dances
My Beloved Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My green adoration-gifts, part 2, , 1977
