Unfeigned ...

(genuine)

Your love of God
And surrender to God
Must be unfeigned
At every moment.

I am glad to notice that,
Like me,
You, too, have an unfeigned love
For nature's beauty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
