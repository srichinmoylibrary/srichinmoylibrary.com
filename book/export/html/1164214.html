Silence speaks, part 5

1.

Each day your snow-white heart-life
Can claim a blue soul-dream.
Each day your sweet Lord is building
In you another Supreme.

2.

To wear the bright crown
Of Immortality,
You must challenge the strength
Of fatality.

3.

O, trust and trust
Your heart of love.
It is your own soul's
Precious dove.

4.

In your fire is
Nowhere oneness.
Only your ego's
Deathless darkness!

5.

With all my might
I shall plunder
My blighting mind's
Destruction-thunder.

6.

Yesterday you were awaiting
God's Compassion-Arrival.
Today you are enjoying
God's Peace-Bliss-Festival.

7.

Pray not: you live
In the sombre lap of death.
Do pray: you grow
Into Immortality's Breath.

8.

Your purity-heart gives thanks to God,
Your purity-heart gives thanks,
Because you are a stranger to
Your mind's impurity-pranks.

9.

Your heart has to make
Only one commitment:
To spread the divinity
Of your soul's enlightenment.

10.

If you heed not your soul's
Determination-call,
You will soon suffer
A bad failure-fall.

11.

Inside your Lord's
Protection-Ring
His Victory-Song
Sing and sing.

12.

Live inside your heart's
Gratitude-tower.
You will be the hero
Of God's God-Hour.

13.

Your mind is unready;
Your body is asleep.
God's Nectar-Delight
How can you drink deep?

14.

Love more and more
Your heart's morning hopes
If you want to untie
Your life's binding ropes.

15.

Your first
Aspiration-preparation:
To doubt
Give simply no reception.

16.

The time has not yet come
To see God's Face.
But the time has come and gone
To clasp God's Grace.

17.

In the Heart of God
Daily my life is growing.
To the Light of God
Daily my mind is surrendering.

18.

Squander not
Good mornings
If you want
God-Blessings.

19.

You are at home
In the sea of self-pity.
Therefore you are
Your life's futility.

20.

My heart once more
Has touched God's Feet today.
My life once more
Is learning gratitude-play.

21.

Between abyss and summit
My life travels cheerfully.
I think and hope and dream
Of perfection eternally.

22.

Since you are building
Your own monument,
You cannot expect God
And His Enlightenment.

23.

Every day my good friend
Purity I hire
To extinguish regularly
My desire-fire.

24.

Live not alone.
Live not alone.
Live in the world's
Oneness-zone.

25.

I shall calmly welcome
Even serious pain
If I need it to ride
On the progress-train.

26.

In our mind-jungle
Doubt-plants grow.
In our heart-garden
Beauty-smiles flow.

27.

I must daily send my heart
To shop for oneness-light.
I must daily send my mind
To shop for newness-height.

28.

Tell me,
Why do you worry needlessly?
Tell me,
Why do you not cry sleeplessly?

29.

In his inner life
He is a oneness-lover.
In his outer life
He is a doubt-conqueror.

30.

Ask your soul to teach your heart
To become a sincerity-flow.
Ask your soul to teach your mind
To become a purity-glow.

31.

Arrest your mind
With its doubt-blindness.
Teach it to love
Your soul's bright fulness.

32.

You are your heart's
Oneness-prayer,
And not your mind's
Eyeless player.

33.

O sing, my heart
Of silence, sing,
And claim today
Your oneness-ring.

34.

Surrender not
To cries and sighs.
You are your soul's
Gold promise-skies.

35.

If your mind takes
A purity-bath,
You will escape
The dark doubt-path.

36.

Who is your leader,
Who is your chief?
No, not your sombre
Vital-thief.

37.

My seeker-friend,
There is nothing to fear.
Your dark mind-jungle
Your Lord will clear.

38.

A doubting mind surrenders
To devouring night.
A seeking heart drinks deep
God's God-Delight.

39.

His aspiration-heart
Is no longer alive.
His dark suspicion-mind
Will forever thrive.

40.

You may not be
A peace-creator,
But you can be
A peace-promoter.

41.

I begin again
With each ending line,
To make my life
God's Choice divine.

42.

To do something great,
Your mind must cry.
To be something good,
Your heart must try.

43.

No difference between your mind
And destruction-blow.
No difference between your heart
And God-oneness-glow.

44.

O seeker-friend of mine,
To know your soul's blue nest,
You must dauntlessly climb
The inner Everest.

45.

Because you are not one with
Your Pilot's Oneness-Plan,
Within, without you still remain
Eternity's unlit man.

46.

To doubt is to drink
From poison-cup.
No more doubt-sleep.
Wake up, wake up!

47.

Run, run, daily run
On aspiration-road!
Your new life will discard
Your old life's ignorance-load.

48.

Since your surrender-list
Is very long,
Your Lord will teach you
His Smile-Song.

49.

I live, I live,
In my ignorance-tree.
God lives, God lives,
In His Forgiveness-Sea.

50.

My hopes shall not fail,
My dreams shall not fade,
Because the right choice
My heart and I have made.

51.

God will grant you
His brightest Satisfaction-Look
If every day you study
His Earth-Transformation-Book.

52.

You and you alone
Can determine your earthly life
By challenging every day
The world's destruction-knife.

53.

God will descend
Every day from Above
If you will accept
His Gift: Oneness-Love.

54.

To receive the best
From the coming day,
Become God's partner
In self-giving play.

55.

Remember, you are not
Your mind's ego-mountain.
What you are is your heart's
Constant oneness-fountain.

56.

Have patience, have joy
And have determination.
You will be visited
By God the Liberation.

57.

If you want to see
God's Cosmic Ray,
Then sing a song of gratitude
Every new day.

58.

Our deepest longings
Will be answered someday.
Let us soulfully join
Our Lord's Cosmic Play.

59.

My seeker-friend,
Keep the Goal always in sight.
Do not be afraid
Of ignorance-darkness-fight.

60.

Nothing can be achieved
Without God's Grace.
This I have learned
From my life's inner race.

61.

If your life can become
One long stillness-prayer,
God will teach you to be
An ignorance-slayer.

62.

Every long step I take
Brings my Lord nearer.
Every God-thought I think
Makes my mind clearer.

63.

Start each day
With determination-fire.
Your high sun-goal
The world will admire.

64.

If you purely love
Below, Above,
God will make you
His fondness-dove.

65.

Even an infant-step
Of progress-light
Can bring you eventually
To God's ultimate Height.

66.

Earth's final song
Is surrender-light.
Heaven's first song
Is Compassion-Delight.

67.

Oneness is the name
Of the seeker-soul.
God's Love-Fulfilment
Is its supreme goal.

68.

If you can find
No time to pray,
How can God save you
From ignorance-clay?

69.

Do not roam
In the futile fields of yesterday.
God has come
To teach you His Perfection-Play.

70.

Within and without
You pretend not to see
Your own creation:
The ignorance-tree.

71.

When your heart learns
Aspiration-skill,
Your life will enjoy
Perfection-thrill.

72.

God's Compassion is searching for me.
What am I doing?
God's Forgiveness is looking for me.
Why am I hiding?

73.

My soul is my God's dream-seed,
My life His reality-tree.
Therefore, within and without
I am a blue bird ever free!

74.

O seeker, be not
A promise-breaker.
You are your world's
Destiny-maker.

75.

Never attend
Ego-exhibits, never!
Enjoy only
Oneness-delight forever!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Silence speaks, part 5, Agni Press, 1994
