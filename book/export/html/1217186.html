11. The art of life

   Self-illumining
Is the art of divine life.   Self-binding
Is the art of human life.   Self-giving
Is the art of Eastern life.   Self-transcending
Is the art of Western life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Yesterday I was a crying dream, Agni Press, 1975
