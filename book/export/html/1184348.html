20. Advice of a God-realised soul

Only one word of advice from a truly God-realised soul:
"O disciples of mine,
In stupidity
I have surpassed all donkeys
In God's creation put together
By becoming
A Guru.
I pray to you
Do not play this role of stupidity-sea."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
