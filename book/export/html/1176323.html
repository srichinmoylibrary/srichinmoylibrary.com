3367 Who Is On Your Side?

Who is on your side
Early in the morning?
God's Oneness-Life.
Who is on your side
During the entire day?
God's Compassion-Eye.
Who is on your side
During the entire night?
God's Forgiveness-Heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 34, Agni Press, 1982
