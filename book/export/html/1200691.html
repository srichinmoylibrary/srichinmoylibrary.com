14906

Simplicity in the outer life
Cannot do everything
To please us.
Purity in the inner life
Cannot do everything
To please us.
But once we have surrender
To God's Will,
Both simplicity and purity
Will be of unimaginable assistance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 150, Agni Press, 1991
