15102

Concentration-power I had
And I still have.
Meditation-heart I had
And I still have.
Contemplation-delight I had
And I still have.
God-manifestation-smile I didn't have
And I still don't have.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 152, Agni Press, 1991
