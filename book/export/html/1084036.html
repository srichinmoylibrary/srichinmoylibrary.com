10. Therefore you failed

It was a poor preparation.
        Therefore
You failed.

It was a bad calculation.
        Therefore
You failed.

It was an abrupt depression.
        Therefore
You failed.

It was an unwarranted surrender.
        Therefore
You failed.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
