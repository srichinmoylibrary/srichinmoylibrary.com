981 The Throne Of Love...

THE THRONE OF LOVE

I sat on the throne of truth.
My soul enjoyed it.

I sat on the throne of light.
My heart enjoyed it.

I sat on the throne of love.
My entire life enjoyed it.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
