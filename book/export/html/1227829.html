Jiban Amar Shaman

Jiban amar shaman
Shaman amar maran
Maran amar swapan

Translation:

My life is a constant battle.
My battle is my death.
My death is my constant
Inspiring and illumining dream.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My green adoration-gifts, part 1, , 1977
