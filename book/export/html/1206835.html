20990

God is near
When I live inside
My sweetness-heart.
God is nearer
When I live inside
My eagerness-heart.
God is nearest
When I live inside
My selflessness-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 210, Agni Press, 1994
