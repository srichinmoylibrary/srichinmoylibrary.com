My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 8

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Hawaii in November-December 2001.

24 November 2001 ...

24 NOVEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII

1 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God is God's
Compassion-Light.

2 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Man is man's
Rejection-pride.

3 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God is God's
Mother-Advice.

4 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Children are children's
Disobedience-pleasure.

5 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God is God's
Illumination-Boon.

6 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Children are children's
Ignorance-choice.

7 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God's Eye and God's Heart
Are streaming tears.

8 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Humanity is humanity's
Uncaring mind.

9 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God is God's
Sweetness-Whisper.

10 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
The world is the world's
Deafness-ears.

11 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God is God's
Forward-marching Will.

12 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Mankind is mankind's
Backward-crawling desire.

13 I Am Convinced ...

I am convinced
That God is always
Pleased with me.

14 Alas, ...

Alas,
I am never pleased
With myself.

15 God Takes Pride ...

God takes pride
In having me
As His partner.

16 I Take Pride ...

I take pride
In being
God's servitor.

17 God Tells Me, ...

God tells me,
"My child,
I have plenty of time.
Take your own time.
Tell Me what you have to say."

18 I Tell God, ...

I tell God,
"My Lord,
I am in a hurry.
Please forgive me.
My ignorance-friend
Is waiting for me."

19 God Tells Me, ...

God tells me,
"My child,
Until you take Me
As your only Friend,
Neither you nor I will have
Even an iota of peace."

20 I Tell God, ...

I tell God,
"My Lord, I surrender.
You have won the race.
I shall fly with You,
I shall dive with You,
I shall run with You,
I shall always be with You,
In You and for You."

21 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Man is afraid
Of God's Anger-Eye.

22 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God is afraid
Of man's sorrow-heart.

23 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God wonders why
Man does not pray to Him
Even once a month.

24 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Man wonders why
God hides all the time.

25 November 2001 ...

25 NOVEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII

25 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Only the world-awakening souls,
The God-aspiring hearts
And the God-serving lives
Are invited to participate
In God's morning Meals
And evening Meals.

26 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Finally I have stopped criticising God
For everything that happens
In my life.

27 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God is asking me
Not to blame Him any more
For the world-crisis.
God tells me,
"My child,
I am wide awake,
I am wide awake."

28 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
In my outer life
I have become a lamb
To charm You.
In my inner life
I have become a lion
To manifest You.

29 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
God tells me
That if I unreservedly love Him,
Then I must bury my past
In oblivion-cave.

26 November 2001 ...

26 NOVEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII

30 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Heaven bountifully descends
Only when earth cheerfully offers
Its bleeding tears to God.

31 Hope And Faith ...

Hope and faith
From deep within
Can challenge and defeat
The outer cruel fate.

32 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
He who rejects his Master's advice
Summarily
Loses immediately
His God-identification-life.

33 The Inner God-Surrender-Heart ...

The inner God-surrender-heart
Has to become
The outer world-prosperity-life.

34 The Mind-Admirer ...

The mind-admirer
And the mind-enjoyer
Cannot be
The heart-sky-climber.

35 My Prayer-Life ...

My prayer-life
Tells my mind
Where it must go.

36 My Meditation-Life ...

My meditation-life
Tells my heart
What it must become.

37 Aspire, Aspire! ...

Aspire, aspire!
God is extremely eager
To share with you
All His supreme Secrets.

38 My Prayer Loves ...

My prayer loves
My bended knees
Inside my mind-cave.

39 My Meditation Loves ...

My meditation loves
My deepening heart
Inside my soul-temple.

28 November 2001 ...

28 NOVEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII

40 Wherever I Go, ...

Wherever I go,
God's Compassion
Walks ahead of me
And His Protection
Walks side by side with me.

41 There Is Only ...

There is only
One Source:
God-Infinity's Delight.

42 My Faith In God ...

My faith in God
Is my inner freedom.
My faith in God
Is my outer confidence.

43 My God-Obedience ...

My God-obedience
Is my God-centred life.

44 God And I Always Speak ...

God and I always speak
The language of heart-fondness
And life-oneness.

45 The Love Of Power ...

The love of power
Dominates the outer world.
The power of love
Liberates the inner world.

46 Alas, ...

Alas,
When the mind's tunnel invites me,
I immediately accept the invitation
And run towards the tunnel.
But when the heart's palace invites me,
I get frightened. I hesitate.
Finally, I decline the invitation.

47 My Prayer-Tears ...

My prayer-tears
Quickly take me to God.
My meditation-smiles
Immediately bring God to me.

48 I Must Keep My Aspiration-Heart ...

I must keep my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
Far beyond
The limitations of my fate.

49 The Sweetness Of The Heart ...

The sweetness of the heart
Unmistakably nullifies
The bitterness of the mind.

30 November 2001 ...

30 NOVEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII

50 My Heart-Tears ...

My heart-tears
Are my thunders
To demolish my mind's
Pride-walls.

51 I Am Hopeless, ...

I am hopeless,
Worthless and useless
Until I sincerely feel
That I am of God
And I am for God.

52 My Heart-Flower ...

My heart-flower
Is God-Beauty.
My life-hour
Is God-Duty.

53 I Am My ...

I am my
Three hundred and sixty-five
Tearful days,
Yet my Lord Supreme
Is far, far away
From me.

54 My Lord Supreme Tells Me ...

My Lord Supreme tells me
That my sincere nothingness
Embodies His complete Fulness.

55 To Have The Mind ...

To have the mind
As the sole master
Of my life
Is to invite confusion-disaster.

56 With My Heart's ...

With my heart's
Sleepless devotion-tears
I drink deep the Beauty
Of my Beloved Lord's Feet.

1 December 2001 ...

1 DECEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII

57 Supreme! ...

Supreme!
Each and every citizen
Of this world
Must cry and smile
In love and trust.

58 My Lord, ...

My Lord,
At every moment
I need Your supreme Guidance
To solve all my inner
And outer problems.

59 My Lord, ...

My Lord,
I must not allow my self-doubt
To mar my heart's inner peace.

60 My Lord, ...

My Lord,
I must not allow
Anxieties and worries
To eclipse my heart-sun.

61 Every Day The Compassion-Eye ...

Every day the Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Supreme
Leads me
In so many unexpected ways.

62 Every Day My Lord Supreme, ...

Every day my Lord Supreme,
Out of His infinite Bounty,
Commands me
To say things and do things
Challenging, daring,
Surprising, astonishing
And startling.

63 My Lord Tells Me, ...

My Lord tells me,
"My child,
Start praying and meditating
For you to reach the Summit-Goal.
I shall do the rest."

64 God Tells Me, ...

God tells me,
"My child,
He who is obedient
To My Voice
Is My immediate Choice."

65 My Love Of God ...

My love of God
Is my only religion.
I must break through
All my mind-occupied
Religion-curtains.

66 For Me ...

For me
There is only one way
To reach my Goal,
And that way
Is by raising the standard
Of my self-discipline
And my God-dedication.

67 I Must Keep Quiet....

I must keep quiet.
I must keep my mind-road
Absolutely clear.
God is on His way
To come and speak to me.

2 December 2001 ...

2 DECEMBER 2001

KAUAI, HAWAII

68 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That even if I do not see Him,
Even if I do not feel Him,
I must believe in Him
When He says
That His Compassion-Eye
Is upon me all the time
In loving watchfulness.

69 My Lord Tells Me, ...

My Lord tells me,
"My child,
Be not overcharged
With worries and fears.
I shall gladly be
Your heavy burden-bearer."

70 I Must Realise ...

I must realise
That God's Heart-Eye
Is always open
To my tears.

71 I Must Realise ...

I must realise
That God's Heart-Ear
Is always open
To my cries.

72 My Lord Tells Me, ...

My Lord tells me,
"My child,
Do not think
Of your tomorrow's needs.
Like today,
Tomorrow also I shall be
Your cheerful and sure supplies."

73 My Lord Tells Me, ...

My Lord tells me,
"My child,
Have implicit faith in Me.
What am I here on earth for,
If I am not going to deliver you
From all your imaginary and real
Trials and tribulations?"

74 No Matter What The World ...

No matter what the world
Does to me,
No matter what the world
Thinks of me,
No matter what the world
Says about me,
I shall always be for the world,
For the betterment of the world,
As long as I am in the world of the living.

75 My Lord Tells Me, ...

My Lord tells me,
"My child,
Be not afraid of your life's dire crisis.
I shall be your life's
All-conquering Strength.
You just carry My Victory-Banner
Throughout the length and breadth
Of the world."

76 My Lord, ...

My Lord,
From now on
I shall always abide
By Your supreme Plans.
Whenever I plan anything,
I only create disaster.
Therefore, my planning
Is nothing short of my interference.
I know You are the supreme Player
And I am Your divine instrument.

77 Why Does God Have To Tell Me...

Why does God have to tell me
What He is going to do
In and through my life?
Why?
Let Him accomplish His Purpose
In my life.
I must always walk along the road
Of ever-increasing faith.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 8, Agni Press, 2002
