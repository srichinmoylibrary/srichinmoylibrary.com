7. O self-complacency

O self-complacency,
Be not unconscious
Of your stark inferiority.
Lo, the phantom-hour
Is mocking you.
Terminate your groundless
Satisfaction.
Stop, stop your prolonged
Clever navigation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 3, Agni Press, 1974
