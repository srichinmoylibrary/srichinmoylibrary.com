Ajike Ei Sanya Bela

Ajike ei sanya bela
Roder stahe megher khela
Marichika jal phela
Katin milan mela
Asa niye chale ekela
E je kator biraha mela
Bhaber khela sangya habe sanya bela
Bhaber khela sur habe
Nutan alo name habe
Byartha habe maya moher jal phela

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
