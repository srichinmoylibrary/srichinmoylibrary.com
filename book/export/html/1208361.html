Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 226

22501

Gratitude
Will indeed forever remain
My heart's most valuable
God-blessed asset.

22502

God-realisation
Will definitely be a clear vision
In my days to come.

22503

In the beginning
Hope swims hesitantly
In my heart's
Ever-widening sea.

22504

Disobedience can never
Near God's Throne.
Neither can impurity.

22505

The hour of death
And the arrival
Of ignorance-night
Are unavoidable.

22506

God does not want
My heart to be heavy with secrets,
But He wants my life
To be flooded with smiles.

22507

Like the Heart of God,
I need a heart
That will enjoy
Ignorance-refusal.

22508

My newly blossomed
Gratitude-heart
Has made God eager
To return to my life.

22509

The unaspiring mind
May look sincere,
But is not really so.

22510

O my aspiration-heart,
This world will remember you
For a long time.

O my surrender-life,
This world will remember you
Forever.

22511

Nothing can help me more
Than my gratitude-heart
To reap
My aspiration-harvest.

22512

In the spiritual life
No aspirant must take leave
Of the path of hope
And the path of promise.

22513

God-Satisfaction
In God's own Way:
This message only
My aspiration-heart
Can offer to God.

22514

Nothing remains so distant
As my mind's
Satisfactory transformation.

22515

May my prayer to God
To return my divine and positive qualities
Be crowned with
A fast-culminating success.

22516

My heart is progressing very fast
Because God-study
Is so fascinating to my heart.

22517

The unaspiring mind
And the rush-hour on the road
Are fond of choosing each other
As partners.

22518

Will there ever come a time
When ignorance-night
Will exhaust
All its darkness-heaviness?

22519

An insecure mind
Will never, never be sure
Of anything on earth.

22520

What I need:
A God-hunger-cry
To challenge
My aspiration-unwillingness-mind.

22521

Centuries roll by;
Yet the human mind
Does not want to know
What aspiration-hunger is.

22522

My heart desperately wants
My life to surrender to God.
God's Heart desperately wants
To feed my life
In God's own Way.

22523

No, not even an aspiration-heart
Can ever complete
The God-absorption-perfection.

22524

O my negativity-mind,
You do not expect it,
But you are bound to meet with
An all-destruction-death.

22525

God is delighted
To be with me in my heart's
Aspiration-garden.

22526

My inner life needs
Tolerance and patience;
My outer life needs
Promise and determination
To make my Lord happy
In His own Way.

22527

The simplest truth is:
"God is my All."
In it abides God
And His infinite Smiles.

22528

Those who approach life spiritually
Will eventually have
A heart of ecstasy
And a life of fulfilment.

22529

The ingratitude-lifestyle
Of my mind
Is something that God
Is unfortunately familiar with.

22530

God is always eager
To tell me all about Himself.
Alas,
Will I ever be ready to hear Him,
Even unwillingly?

22531

Alas, how long
Shall I deliberately remain a stranger
To my soul's illumination-perfection?

22532

True, death cannot be
Perpetually cancelled,
But death can be
Indefinitely delayed.

22533

I need only one voice:
God's Forgiveness-Voice.
I need only one choice:
God's Satisfaction-Choice.

22534

Not the head, but the heart
Has to be the spokesman of the family.
Then there can be no problem.

22535

Each good thought
Is indeed a real passport
To enter into
The perfection-realm.

22536

When I see my soul,
I invariably see it
With a magic smile,
Sweeter than the sweetest.

22537

My heart's hospitality
Is the only hospitality
My Lord Supreme
Happily and unreservedly trusts.

22538

Yes and No
Can never be friends.
They can, at best,
Be two untiring rivals.

22539

My soul tells me
Not to be ready in the least
When death knocks
At my life's door.

22540

May my mind's ignorance-volcano
Not only today and tomorrow,
But forever,
Remain fast asleep.

22541

Only when patience knocks
At peace-door
Does peace open up the door.

22542

If you are prepared
To live only for God,
Then God will have time
To be always at your disposal.

22543

O my restless mind,
Do not ignore the beckoning hands
Of my silence-heart.

22544

Each self-giving thought of mine
Has God's proud Vision
As its source.

22545

May my heart
Every day be ready
To be humanity's God-Feast.

22546

I am a dreamer.
What I love most about my dreams
Is their whisper-smiles.

22547

Unlike us,
God never takes
Forgiveness-vacations.

22548

Compromise
Is not satisfaction.
Compromise
Is not perfection.
Compromise
Is just a clever way
Of justice-light-evasion.

22549

We dream of many things,
But ego dreams of only one thing:
Sovereignty.

22550

God can be happy
Not with what we have,
But with what we are:
A sleepless gratitude-heart-smile.

22551

I am beautiful
When I am filled
With my heart's tears.
I am perfect
When I become
The smile of my soul.

22552

God appreciates my tears,
God admires my vows,
God loves my conscious and constant
Surrender-life.

22553

If you do not command your mind
To surrender to you,
You will not be able to see
Your heart's all-loving rising sun.

22554

Conviction does not come
From self-affirmation.
Conviction comes only
From self-perfection.

22555

To be absolutely perfect,
What I need
Is a God-believer-mind
And a God-lover-heart.

22556

O my heart's climbing tears,
You have no idea how beautiful,
Significant and fruitful
You are.

22557

With doubt,
Nobody will be able
To climb to the summit
Of silence-mountain.

22558

When I live inside my mind,
I am all exasperation.
When I live inside my heart,
I am all preparation.
When I live inside my soul,
I am all action-fulfilled satisfaction.

22559

When I please God
In God's own Way,
My life becomes
Ecstasy's perpetual journey.

22560

Human independence
Is the joint product
Of the mind's absurdity
And the life's futility.

22561

A newness-life
Inspires us.
A oneness-heart
Fulfils us.

22562

An expectation-mind
Ruins the beauty
Of self-giving
In our spiritual life.

22563

A real seeker
Must take everything in his life
Not only devotedly,
But also cheerfully.

22564

There are many ways
To live on earth.
I have chosen the way
Of God-intoxication-life.

22565

Intelligence
Is the beauty of the mind.
Wisdom
Is the illumination of the soul.

22566

The stars do not tell me
What I should do.
I tell the stars
To inspire the world
In and through me.

22567

I do not tell my mind
To do anything good,
For my mind does not listen to me.
I do not tell my heart
To do anything good,
For my heart does not wait for me —
It just does everything
Lovingly and self-givingly.

22568

How do you expect
To approach your Master
If you are not happily agreeable
To his reproaches?

22569

There is no difference between
An unaspiring mind
And a solid wall of doubt.

22570

God's infinite Compassion
And His continued Forgiveness
Are two insoluble mysteries.

22571

If your heart
Makes your soul shine,
Then God will invite you
To dine with Him.

22572

God's Compassion-Light
And His Justice-Height
Both have equal rights
To my life.

22573

More than anything else,
God's Forgiveness-Feet
I sleeplessly treasure.

22574

An ignorance-life-lover
I was.
Now an ignorance-life-fighter
I am.

22575

It is not possible to be both
A truth-defiance-life
And a God-reliance-life.

22576

May my aspiration-heart
Every day
Chase away my frustration-mind.

22577

My heart-garden
Is not only a beautiful place,
But also a cheerful place
For my Lord to work.

22578

God does not plan my future
In every detail
Unless I love Him only
And need Him only.

22579

O my mind,
Do tell me
Why you complicate my life.
O my soul,
Do tell me
How you liberate my life.

22580

My life's perfection-progress-hunger
Always receives
God's proud Attention.

22581

Not only the entire aspiration-life,
But each and every moment's
Aspiration-breath
Has a very special significance.

22582

O my mind,
Your independence-existence-hunger
Has turned my life
Into a complete failure.

22583

Quickly the mind distorts
The truth.
Slowly the heart feels and fulfils
The truth.

22584

God's Compassion-Eye
Is constantly blessing me
With the privilege of discovering Him
In and through my life.

22585

O my mind,
Be not too rigid.
O my heart,
Be not too timid.

22586

Not only does the mind not know,
But also it does not want to know
What divine love truly is.

22587

Alas, how can a human life be happy
When it has two bad bosses:
A dictating mind
And a dominating vital?

22588

God does not question
My disobedience-mind.
He just avoids and ignores it.

22589

O my mind,
My heart is the only
God-messenger of hope.
How do you dare
To crucify my heart?

22590

What is my desire-life?
My desire-life is something
That deserves constant rejection
From the divine in me.

22591

If you constantly live
In the aspiration-heart,
Then your fear and self-doubt
Will have no meaning.

22592

Every day
God wants everybody's heart
To reach into
Spanless vastness.

22593

God wants us to live
Inside our heart's faith-palace
And not
Inside our mind's doubt-cave.

22594

Just speak to God.
He is all eagerness
To listen to you.

22595

If you want to be happy,
Then at every moment listen
To the whispering child
In your heart.

22596

The hope of the heart
And the smile of life
Can and must
Live and work together.

22597

When self-doubt ends,
We feel that we have come out of
The mind's darkest abyss.

22598

The despair-journey
Is infinitely more fatiguing
Than any other journey.

22599

Alas, will there be a time
When my life will surrender
As beautifully and charmingly
As God originally intended?

22600

God-realisation is the only task
That my life has to bravely
And unmistakably accomplish.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 226, Agni Press, 1996
