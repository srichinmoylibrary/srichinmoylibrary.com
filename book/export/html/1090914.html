I play

When I meditate
Inside a mountain cave,
I play with God's secret Soul.

When I meditate
At the foot of a banyan tree,
I play with God's vast Sky.

When I meditate
Inside my heart,
I play with God's sacred Breath.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Meditation: humanity's race and Divinity's Grace, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
