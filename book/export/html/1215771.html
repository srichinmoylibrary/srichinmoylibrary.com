149. The story of the human world

Human help
Waxes to wane.
Human love
Grows to decay.
Human life
Lives to die.
Human death
Rests to re-enter.

This is indeed the story
Of the human world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 3, Agni Press, 1974
