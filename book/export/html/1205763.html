19928

My heart's
Confidence in God,
My heart's
Conference with God,
Even my heart's
Inference of God
Can definitely help me
To cross far beyond
All my doubt-obstacles.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 200, Agni Press, 1994
