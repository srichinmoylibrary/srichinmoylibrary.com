Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 86

8501 No Need Of Miracles!

No need of miracles!
Ask man and God for nothing.
Let the beauty of silence
Reign supreme in your life.

8502 Do Not Try To Bind Love

Do not try to bind love,
For love alone
Can change the face
Of this ill-fated earth.

8503 He Is Not The Loser

He is not the loser
And he can never be the loser,
For his heart's surrender-flower
And his life's gratitude-fruit
God has proudly accepted.

8504 Two Questions

Your first question is:
Is God-realisation
Everybody's birthright?

Your second and last question is:
How can the human cry
Be transformed into a divine smile?

8505 A Sacred Thirst

If your life has a sacred thirst,
Then God is bound to grant you
His own secret Well to quench it.

8506 Love Your Heart Ever More

Love your heart ever more.
And do something further:
Reawaken your imprisoned heart-flames!

8507 Stay Where You Are

My desire,
Stay where you are.
I am running towards you.
What for?
To reduce your life's
Loud clatterings.

8508 Sleepless Perfection

Sleepless perfection,
Will you ever be mine?
Sleepless satisfaction,
Can I be yours, only yours?

8509 I Once Promised Myself

I once promised myself
That I would not try to escape
Any appropriate punishment
If I misbehaved in my inner life.
Yet my promise remains unfulfilled.

8510 Beautiful Is His Heart's Loyalty

Beautiful is his heart's loyalty.
Soulful is his mind's enthusiasm.
Fruitful is his life's obedience.

8511 The Mind's Barrier

Unless I cross the mind's barrier,
How can I see beauty's face,
Purity's heart
And divinity's soul?

8512 What Can My Little Hands Do?

What can my little hands do?
They can paint the Face
Of God the Beauty.
What can my heart's soulful will do?
It can liberate man
The bondage-slave.

8513 A Purity-Heart Has The Capacity

A purity-heart
Has the capacity
And is the capacity
To live inside
The heart of Infinity's Love-Light.

8514 My Mind's Ignorance-Dreams

If I can keep my life safe
From my mind's ignorance-dreams,
Then I do not see why and how
I cannot become a perfect instrument
Of my Beloved Supreme.

8515 Gratitude Is All-Powerful

If humility is mighty,
Then purity is almighty.
If surrender is powerful
Then gratitude is all-powerful.

8516 The Scorn Of Multitude-Tongues

No matter how divine and how perfect
A human being is,
He is subject to the scorn
Of multitude-tongues.

8517 If God's Mind Is Subtle

If God's Mind is subtle,
His Heart is simple.
If God's Compassion-Eye is powerful,
His Satisfaction-Life is blessingful.

8518 Each Human Desire

Each human desire
Is an arrow
Which is quite often apt
To miss the target.

8519 Each Human Being Is Blessed

As a flower is blessed
With fragrance and beauty,
Even so each human being is blessed
With inner purity and divinity.

8520 His Unparalleled Inheritance

Because he has divine thoughts
As his unparalleled inheritance,
His life is a constant expression
Of his heart's intense gratitude.

8521 His Firm Belief

His firm belief:
Unless his entire being cries
For fulness-splendour in life,
Tears will drain
His aspiration-heart dry.

8522 When Imagination Fails You

When imagination fails you,
Do not feel sad.
Just smile and remain serene.
From above you will get another chance
To catch the imagination-bird.

8523 Refuse To Sing With Your Mind

Just refuse to sing with your mind.
Behold!
Your heart is all ready
To teach you its own soulful songs.

8524 God Will Treasure You

If you can cherish
What you have already received from God,
God will, without fail,
Grant you His Eternity's Treasure-Smile.

8525 What Hurts You More

Be sure to know
What hurts you more:
To teach the world
Or to learn from the world.

8526 Inside Your Heart

No matter what your physical appearance,
There is always a most beautiful person
Inside your heart
Who can make your face truly beautiful.

8527 There Is No Perfect Desire

As there is no such thing
As a perfect desire,
Even so there is no such thing
As imperfect aspiration-surrender.

8528 The Causes Of Sudden Death

A doubtful mind,
A fearful heart
And a hateful life
Are the unmistakable and powerful causes
Of sudden death in the spiritual life.

8529 We Need Many Things From God

We need many, many things from God
To become His divine instruments.
But what we need most of all
Is to keep our heart's door
Sleeplessly open to Him.

8530 If You Do Not Know How To Smile

If you do not know how to smile
When you descend,
Then God in His Infinite Compassion
May not appear
To help you ascend once again.

8531 Your Imagination-Bird Is Locked Up

You say you are
A genuine God-lover.
If so, how is it
That your imagination-bird
Is mercilessly locked up
In a tiny cage,
And your aspiration-bird
Is tightly locked up
In a tiny cave?

8532 God Is Always For You

You can love the world dauntlessly
Because God is always for you.
You can serve the world unconditionally
Because God is always with you.

8533 My Unaspiring Life Is Aware

As my doubting mind is aware
Of God's Compassion-Eye,
Even so my unaspiring life is aware
Of God's Forgiveness-Heart.

8534 As God Intended

When I use my heart
As God intended,
Only then can I say
That I live for God's Love alone.

8535 Do You Not See?

Do you not see
That God is unfolding Himself
Before your very eyes?

Do you not see
That your heart and God's Heart
Are two open books to each other?

8536 You Say That You Pray

You say that you pray every day.
But if you really prayed,
Your prayer would definitely sink into
Your aspiration-heart and gratitude- life.

8537 His Heart Is Crying

His heart is crying
For God's Supreme Love.
His mind is crying
For man's lofty appreciation.
Alas, what is man doing?
Man is dying
To see his fall.
And what is God doing?
God is unveiling Himself
To be claimed powerfully by him.

8538 The Curse Of The Mind

The mind tells the heart
That it will never see
The Face of God.
The heart receives Blessing-Light
From the soul,
Which buries the curse of the mind
In oblivion-night.

8539 When My Soul Invites Me

When my soul invites me,
It is all in perfect silence-light.
When I invite my soul,
It is all in imperfect sound-might.

8540 If Thoughts Are Flocking

When you meditate,
If thoughts are flocking,
Then try to imagine
That God Himself is rocking
Your heart-cradle.

8541 When The Mind Reigns Over Matter

When the mind reigns over matter,
You can conquer the outer heights.
When the inner depths conquer the mind,
Your aspiration-dedication-life
Becomes unparalleled.

8542 My Lord Is Pleased

My Lord is not pleased
When I say I am not worthy of Him.
My Lord is pleased
Only when I say
I am definitely going to be
His choice instrument.

8543 Nothing Is Worth Believing

Nothing is worth believing:
This is what the human mind
Tells us.

Doubt is worth nothing:
This is what the divine heart
Tells us.

8544 The Cynical Eye Distrusts

The cynical eye distrusts.
What does it distrust?
It distrusts man,
The God-representative on earth.

8545 Doubt Immediately Surrendered

His heart called out,
"Doubt, the bandit,
Must be seized and imprisoned!"
Doubt immediately surrendered,
And longed to be transformed
Into the heart's choice instrument.

8546 My Dedication To The World

My dedication to the world
Should be a beautiful gift
And not a demanding expectation.

8547 His Mind Has Unparalleled Poise

His mind has unparalleled poise.
Do you know why?
Because his heart is in constant touch
With Eternity's Silence-Smile.

8548 Your Fallen Self-Expectation-Tree

Do you know what has crushed
Your life's pride?
It is your huge,
Fallen self-expectation-tree.

8549 What The Weeping Multitudes Need

What the weeping multitudes need
Is a divine strength
Beyond human hope.

8550 The Supreme Guest Will Come

Although you are not aspiring
To His Satisfaction,
Just because you are still aspiring,
The unexpected Supreme Guest
Will come to you
With His inexplicable Splendour.

8551 Sorrow-Rain Will Fall

Sorrow-rain will eventually
Fall heavily on you
If you always remain
Self-enamoured.

8552 Aspiration Is The Beginning

Aspiration is the beginning
Of self-mastery.
Self-mastery is the halfway point
To God-discovery.

8553 My Lord's Earth-Born Child

My heart's sleepless cry
Is my Lord's earth-born child.
My soul's matchless smile
Is my Lord's Heaven-born child.

8554 Hope Is My Heart's God

Determination is my mind's God.
Hope is my heart's God.
Promise is my soul's God.
Surrender is my life's God.

8555 Why Do I Choose To Hide?

Why do I choose to hide?
Not because I am a thief,
But because the God-Hour
Has not yet struck
For me to reveal myself.

8556 My Search

My search for satisfaction within
Is yet unfinished.
My search for perfection without
Is yet to begin.

8557 Hint Of Surrender-Light

At your slightest hint
Of surrender-light
God will come to you
With His Infinity's Satisfaction-Delight.

8558 Expand Your Heart

Expand your heart,
Transcend your mind!
Lo, your ego-life
Is all gone.

8559 The Treacherous Queen

Open the door
Of your gratitude-heart.
Anxiety, the treacherous queen,
Will no longer be able
To rule your life.

8560 Only One Pure Heart

To serve mankind
You need only two clean hands.
To serve God
You need only one pure heart.

8561 Two Monsters Of Waste

A doubtful mind
And a fearful heart
Are indeed
Two monsters of waste.

8562 Your Earthly Role

Self-contempt will cling to you
Unless you become aware
Of your earthly role:
You are Eternity's pilgrim
On Immortality's road.

8563 God's Choice Instrument

If you allow your mind
To be chained to time,
How can you be God's choice instrument
In the heart of mankind
Or even in the Heart of God?

8564 Only One God-Trodden Path

There is only one God-trodden path,
And that path lies
Inside your heart,
Not before your eyes.

8565 If You Keep Your Heart-Eye Veiled

If you keep your heart-eye veiled,
Naturally you will be pierced
By the wild shafts
Of darkness-night.

8566 His Inner Glimpse Of God's Smile

His heart of love
And his life of service
Are always guided
By his inner glimpse
Of God's outer Smile.

8567 A Ruined Paradise

When the heart does not listen
To the dictates of the soul,
The heart is bound to become
A ruined paradise.

8568 Laugh At Yesterday's Despair-Night

How can you proceed today
If you do not laugh
At your yesterday's despair-night?

8569 A Visible Aspiration-Flame

Unless I become
A visible aspiration-flame
In the depth of my heart,
An invisible frustration
Will torture my life.

8570 God Will Cheerfully Allow You

If you are silent
And uncomplaining,
God will cheerfully allow you
To join Him
In His own eternal Hunger
For self-transcendence.

8571 Family Of Sighs

Fear, doubt and worry
Will have to leave eventually
With their family of sighs.

8572 The Universal Burden-Bearer

You do not have to run away
From yourself.
God not only will carry your burden
But He will also carry you,
For He knows that He alone
Is the universal Burden-Bearer.

8573 A Questioning Mind

A questioning mind
Does not want to perceive.
A questioning heart
Does not want to receive.
A questioning life
Does not want to achieve.

8574 O Fearful Heart

O fearful heart, believe me!
This world is not as undivine
As you think.
This mind is not as important
As you think.
Heaven is not as remote
As you think.

8575 How Can Your Heart Be Pleased?

How can your heart be pleased
With anything here on earth
When your mind is critical
Of everything there in Heaven?

8576 A Splendid Victory

Your outer service to mankind
Will give your inner faith
A splendid victory.

8577 Because Your Life Enjoys

Because your life enjoys
Trivial and worthless activities,
Your God-loving heart
Is still locked
In an amazingly cruel prison.

8578 Who God Is

O unbelieving mind,
I shall show you
Where God is.

O unaspiring heart,
I shall tell you
Who God is.

8579 His Heaven-Life Saw

His Heaven-life saw
A mountain of promise.
His earth-life is now dying for
A flicker of hope.

8580 Your Soul Has Planned Your Life

Because your soul has planned your life
From the beginning of time,
You are eager to extinguish
Your earth-desire-flames.

8581 Who Wants To Have You?

O fearful freedom,
Who wants to have you?
No, not my crying heart.

O soulful freedom,
Who wants to have you?
No, not my doubting mind.

8582 When I Live In My Heart

When I live in the very depth
Of my heart,
I run towards my destined goal.
When I live inside my mind,
My desire-life runs towards me
Fast, very fast.

8583 My Mind Is Not Satisfied

My mind is not satisfied
With newness.
Therefore, I am unhappy.

My heart is not satisfied
With fulness.
Therefore, I am unhappy.

My Lord and I are satisfied
With our oneness.
Therefore, we are eternally happy.

8584 On The Wrong Train

A money-minded seeker
Is on the wrong train.
His train will have no destination.
No, never!

8585 Only For You

My Lord,
My dream-sky exists
Only for You.
My reality-clouds exist
Only for You.

8586 Self-Confessed Insecurity

You are a broken-hearted seeker
Just because your heart has become
A self-confessed insecurity.

8587 The Presence Of Peace

If your outer liking
And your inner disliking
Of the world
Keep you always upset,
Then invoke the presence
Of peace;
It will grant you
Eternity's oneness-reality.

8588 A Fountain Of Joy-Tears

Since you are no longer
The slave of fear,
I shall shed a fountain
Of joy-tears.

8589 Eternity's Peace-Distributing Self

If you want to become
Eternity's peace-distributing self,
Then never give in
To the falsehood-power of neutrality.

8590 The Chains Of My Sinful Life

My Lord,
You do not have to break
The chains of my sinful life.
Just show me how I can chain myself
To Your Compassion-Feet.

8591 The Sunlit Frontier

Do not allow your hope
To disappoint you.
Let your hope be
The sunlit frontier
Of your God-searching mind.

8592 Few Will Equal You

If you can become
An unconditionally surrendered seeker,
You will see that few
Will take birth on earth
To equal you
And your unparalleled achievement.

8593 A Universal Favourite

Because of your beauty's mind,
Because of your purity's heart,
Because of your divinity's life,
You have become a universal favourite.

8594 Perfection Means

Perfection means constant rejection
Of your desire-life.
Perfection means constant acceptance
Of your aspiration-heart.

8595 His Life's Doubt-Clouds

Each time a new wave of faith
Enters into him,
His mind marshals
His life's doubt-clouds.

8596 The Beginning Of Death

Disobedience is
The beginning of death
In the spiritual life.
Ingratitude is
Permanent death
In the spiritual life.

8597 The Day Of My Heart

I pray and meditate
To lengthen the day
Of my heart.

I pray and meditate
Not only to delay but also to cancel
The arrival of ignorance-night.

8598 A Spiritually Full Man

A spiritually full man
Is he who fans the flame
Of his aspiration
Each new day
More cheerfully and more powerfully.

8599 God Helps The Seeker

God helps the seeker
Create his tomorrow's world today
When the seeker's life becomes
A surrender-moon and a gratitude-sun.

8600 You Have My Tomorrow

My Lord,
You have my tomorrow
Inside Your Heart.
Can I not have Your Today
Inside my heart?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 86, Agni Press, 1983
