420. The loneliness of the body

The loneliness of the body
Tells me:
"Unless you love me,
I shall leave you.
Unless you admire me
I shall leave you.
Unless you put the soul and me
On the same footing,
I shall leave you."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
