40 O My Desire-Beggar-Mind, ...

O my desire-beggar-mind,
Step back from the public eye,
Step back from the spotlight.
Lo and behold,
God's boundless Pride-Heart
Is beckoning you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 2, Agni Press, 2000
