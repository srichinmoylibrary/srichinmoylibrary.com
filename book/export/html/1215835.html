211. When he represents

He is one
When he represents
Truth and Light.

He is one
When he represents
Love and Life.

He is two
When he represents
His Inner Faith
And
His Outer Doubt.

He is many
When he represents
Earth-Bondage-Night
And
Heaven-Freedom-Bliss.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
