81.

Smiling,
The breath of the New Year
Has just entered into my heart
And has given me
Three spiritual names:
Excitement, enlightenment
And fulfilment.

31 December 2003
Yogyakarta, Indonesia

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 2004
