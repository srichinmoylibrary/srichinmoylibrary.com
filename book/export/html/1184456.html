128. Ask God!

Ask God!
He will tell you
How divinely beautiful you are,
Since you have decided
Not to believe me.

Ask God!
He will tell you
How supremely fruitful you are,
Since you have decided
Not to believe me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
