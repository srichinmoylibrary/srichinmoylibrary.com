151. He is really divine

He thinks of God;
Therefore
He is really divine.
He meditates on God;
Therefore
He is three times unmistakably
More divine.
He has surrendered
His Heaven-dream
And
Earth-reality to God;
Therefore
He is seven times supremely more divine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
