12. Can he ever be good?

He is great.
He loves God
At others' expense.

He is great.
He reveals God
At others' expense.

He is great.
He manifests God
At others' expense.

Can he ever be good?
Certainly he can.
But when?
Only when he totally
Unlearns his mind-inventions.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Vision-Promise, Agni Press, 1974
