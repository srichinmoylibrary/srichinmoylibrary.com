198. I shall not hide from you

I shall not hide from you.
Why should I,
And how can I?
I shall make you
My oneness-cry,
My oneness-sigh,
So that a day shall come
When I shall become
Your oneness-sky,
And your oneness-light,
And your oneness-kite.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
