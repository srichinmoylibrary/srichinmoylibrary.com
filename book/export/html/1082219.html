27.

God, my determination-mind
Looks at Your GREATNESS-Eye
With folded hands
And prays to You
For Your Blessings.
God, my devotion-heart
Sits at Your Goodness-Feet
And prays to You
For Your Forgiveness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Greatness and God's Goodness, Agni Press, 1998
