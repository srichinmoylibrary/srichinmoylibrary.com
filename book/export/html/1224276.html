Dyuloke Bhuloke Jhalake Jhalake

Dyuloke bhuloke
Jhalake jhalake
Tomar parash
Amar harash
Tomar charan
Amar jiban
Ananda banshi
Asimer hasi

Translation:

In Heaven and on our earth,
In a twinkling, Your magic touch
Inundates my life with bliss.
Your Feet give me an ever-transcending life
And Immortality’s Flute and Infinity’s Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 2, , 1976
