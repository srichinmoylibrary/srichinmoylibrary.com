77. Clear answers

Your dear question:
"Where is God?"
His clear answer:
"God is in your dream-life."

Your dear question:
"Who is God?"
His clear answer:
"God is humanity's beggar-starvation
And
Divinity's Emperor-feast."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
