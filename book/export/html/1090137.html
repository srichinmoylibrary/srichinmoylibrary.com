3. Do you want to be

Do you want to be a ruler?
Then be just.

Do you want to be a lover?
Then be pure.

Do you want to be a teacher?
Then be sure.

Do you want to be another God?
Then be perfect.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, receive this little undying cry, Agni Press, 1975
