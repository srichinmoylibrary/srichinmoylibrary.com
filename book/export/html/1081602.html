43. What are you doing?

God, what are You doing
In Heaven today?
"Son, today I have decided
To please you in your own way.
Son, I am sincere."

"Son, what are you doing
On earth today?"
Father, today I have decided
To challenge You,
To equal You.
Father, I am serious.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
