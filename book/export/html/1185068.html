740. The easiest thing

To call you my Master
Is the easiest thing,
And I have done it already.
To call you my all
Is also the easiest thing,
And I have done it already.
But to call you my human friend
Is the most difficult thing,
And I will never succeed in doing it,
Never!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
