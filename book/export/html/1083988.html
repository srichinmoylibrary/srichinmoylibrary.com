13. Alternatives

You exhausted
Your Heaven-free
     Alternative.
Therefore death-frown
Wanted to devour you.

You have exhausted
Your earth-bound
     Alternative.
Therefore God's Crown
Shines in you,
God's Palace
Treasures you,
God's Kingdom
Embraces you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
