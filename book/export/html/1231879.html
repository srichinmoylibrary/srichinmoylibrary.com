Sure Sure Hiya Pure

Sure sure hiya pure
Mor amire dhire dhire
Bandhbo nashbo aj

Translation:

Singing sweet and melodious songs
   Inside my heart-citadel
Today I shall slowly and slowly
   Bind and destroy my ego-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Your face is my dream, , 2009
