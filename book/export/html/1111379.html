To be like you

After I stopped running last night, I was watching the rest of the 33-mile race. A little boy came up to me and. He was very nice to me and said, "How I wish I could be like you." Then he said, "Did you hear me calling you 'Guru'?" He was only ten years old.

12 July 1980

From: Sri Chinmoy, Run and become, become and run, part 4, Agni Press, 1981
