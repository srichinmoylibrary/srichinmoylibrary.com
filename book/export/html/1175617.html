2668 Fill The Heart

Empty the mind!
Lo and behold,
Right in front of you
There is a fountain of peace.

Fill the heart!
Lo and behold,
Right in front of you
There is a fountain of bliss.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
