967. When they knock at your door

        When
Animal the child
Knocks at your door,
Feed him with your wisdom.
   He is hungry.        When
Man the child
Knocks at your door,
Bless him with your power.
   He is weak.        When
God the child
Knocks at your door,
Embrace Him with your love.
   He is lonely.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
