906 A Life Untouched...

A LIFE UNTOUCHED

Two eyes untouched
By beauty I have.

One heart untouched
By love I have.

One mind untouched
By light I have.

One life untouched
By meditation-prayers I have.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
