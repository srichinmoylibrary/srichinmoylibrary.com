330. Inside my heart

Inside my heart I feel
Nameless sadness.
Inside my heart I feel
A strong barrenness.
Inside my heart I feel
A sense of soullessness.
Inside my heart I feel
The beginning, absolute beginning
Of an untarnished newness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
