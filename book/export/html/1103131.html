5 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
O God-faith-heart-telescope,
I love you, I love you, I love you.
O God-doubt-mind-microscope,
I detest you, I detest you, I detest you.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 October 1998, 1:31 a.m. Before lifting 180 lbs with each arm three times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
