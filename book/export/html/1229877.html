Father, Do Organise My Shapeless World

Father, do organise
     My shapeless world.
Son, do feed
     My hungry world.
Father, my world
     Needs You desperately.
Son, My world
     Loves you unreservedly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Silence, please! God is coming, , 1999
