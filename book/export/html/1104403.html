8 September 2000 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart always live
In the rainbow-beauty
Of glowing hopes.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 September 2000, 8:32 p.m. Before doing 630-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
