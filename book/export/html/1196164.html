10424

It is an old story
When I tell the world
That I am going to perfect it.
It is a new story
When I tell not only the world
But also my mind
That I shall improve my own life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 105, Agni Press, 1986
