1493 The Conscious Steps Of Purity

Your life is futility,
Your heart is empty,
And your mind is a stranger
To satisfaction.
Why, why, why?
Because you are not familiar
With the conscious steps
Of illumining and fulfilling purity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 15, Agni Press, 1981
