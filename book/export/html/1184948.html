620. Everything is mortal

Everything is mortal here.
Love is mortal,
Joy is mortal,
Concern is mortal,
Everything is mortal;
Therefore
Troubled is my Lord.
But a little surrender to God's Will
Makes everything immortal:
Love, Joy, Concern.
A little surrender to God's Will
Becomes Immortality itself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
