13. No news

No news from death-mire
Is always good news.
No news from life-fire
Is always sad news.
No news from earth-temptation
Is always hope-news.
No news from Heaven-illumination
Is always death-news.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
