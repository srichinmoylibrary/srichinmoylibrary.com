213.

Yesterday
God passed His Peace-Book to me
And asked me to read it
Page by page,
Lovingly, devotedly and self-givingly,
To humanity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, part 3, Agni Press, 1996
