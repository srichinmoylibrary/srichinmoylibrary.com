587 Do Me A Favour...

DO ME A FAVOUR

Sweet Lord,
Please do me a favour.
Please steal my heart's sorrow.

Sweet Lord,
Please do me a favour.
Please steal my mind's suspicion.

Sweet Lord,
Please do me a favour.
Please steal my vital's audacity.

Sweet Lord,
Please do me a favour.
Please steal my body's timidity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
