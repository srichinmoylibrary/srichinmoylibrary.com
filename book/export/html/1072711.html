Question: “From Delight we came into existence. In Delight we grow. At the end of our journey’s close, into Delight we retire.” Could you comment on the above mantra?

Sri Chinmoy: God has written an open letter to His human children. His letter runs: “My sweetest children, you are the only Delight of My universal Existence.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, At the doors of time and delight opportunity knocks, Agni Press, 1974
