185. Three days and three nights

Three good days:
A day of true aspiration.
A day of pure dedication.
A day of total surrender.
Three good nights:
A night of sound sleep.
A night of sweet dreams.
A night of august silence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
