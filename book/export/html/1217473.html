Part I — Questions and answers

LPDD 1e1. Sri Chinmoy and a group of his students from various parts of the world visited Viet Nam in December 1992. This book contains the transcripts of Sri Chinmoy's answers to questions asked by his students and his remarks at a visit to the Mekong Delta.

Viet Nam

Viet Nam, Viet Nam, Viet Nam, Viet Nam!
You are your life's progress-joy-drum.
Your crying heart's streaming tears no more.
Your boat is touching the Golden Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, You are your life's progress-joy-drum, Agni Press, 1993
