6 April 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Not even for one breath
Do I belong to ignorance-hunger
And ignorance-pride.
I belong only to You and Your Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 April 2000, 7:53 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 13, Agni Press, 2000
