25. My school lesson

From the school
Of simplicity
I learned:
   God is simple
   And within my easy reach.

From the school
Of diversity
I learned:
   Incomplete uncertainty
   Is God's entire creation.

From the school
Of complexity
I learned:
   God is extremity's
   Futility.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
