434. Don't just exist

Don't just exist. Live!
Don't just live. See!
Don't just see. Feel!
Don't just feel. Become!
   And what happens
   When you become?
When you become,
You really exist
Both on earth
   And in Heaven.
You proudly live
In God's Heart.
You use God's Eye to see.
You use God's Heart to feel.
When you see,
   You see your own omnipotence.
When you feel,
   You feel your own omnipresence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 9, Agni Press, 1973
