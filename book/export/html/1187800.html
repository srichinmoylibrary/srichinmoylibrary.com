2143

In vain I knocked
At your mind's door
For admittance.
In vain God knocked
At my heart's door
For admittance.
Oh, open your mind's door soon
And teach me
How I can open my heart's door
To God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 22, Agni Press, 1984
