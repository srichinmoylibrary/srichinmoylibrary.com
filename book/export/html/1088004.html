Immediately stop!

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Hating yourself.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Belittling
Others' lofty achievements.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Wallowing in the pleasures
Of ignorance-life.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Advising the world
What it should do
And what it should not do.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Flying your mind's ego-kite
High, higher, highest.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Ignoring each precious moment,
For each moment is the source
Of a unique fulfilment.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Making friends
With chronic insecurity
And obsessive individuality.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Hurting, criticising and disgracing
Those very few
Who love God only.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Being the possessor
Of an ingratitude-heart.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Staying inside
The doubt-insecurity-domain.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Limiting
Your silence-oneness-peace-life.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Trusting
Your surrender-fractured-heart.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Doubting and criticising God-lovers.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Remembering the huge failures
Of the deplorable past.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Rejecting God's choice, illumining
And fulfilling Voice
In every sound.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Withdrawing from the outer world
Of God-ordained responsibility.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Avoiding the Inner Pilot,
Who so compassionately steers
Your life-boat.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Masking your inner fears
With your outer smiles.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Underestimating the unparalleled values
Of a gratitude-heart and a surrender-life.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Predicting the outer world-perfection-day
And the inner world-satisfaction-day.
World-perfection and world-satisfaction
Will definitely take place
At God's choice Hour.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Disregarding
Your aspiring heart's ecstasy-flames.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Cancelling foolishly or roguishly
Your God-appointments.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Lingering
In yesterday's, today's and tomorrow's
Desire-storms.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Discovering
Man-perfection and God-satisfaction
On television.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Looking for the satisfaction-dance
In temptation-lance.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Cherishing the despair-desert
Of your heart's streaming tears.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Mimicking secretly
God-dependent seekers.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Discouraging
The impossibility-challenger-hearts.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Dancing
With your hallucination-mind.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Neglecting
Your ancient wisdom-light.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Visiting
The rumour-clamour-parlour.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Multiplying
Your imaginary heart-fears.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Ignoring
God's Silence-Peace-Dictates.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Ignoring the climbing flames
Of your soul's beauty.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Singing the tearful songs
Of your self-styled loneliness.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Covering your mind-sky
With your insincerity-impurity-clouds.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Forgetting your heart's aspiration-beauty
And your life's dedication-fragrance.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Denying
Your universal oneness-heart.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Dissecting
Your heart's hope-song-flowers.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Paying attention to your past
Failure and discouragement-life.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Turning your face towards
Your mind's ignorance-abyss.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Being a rebellious mind,
A ferocious vital
And a duty-escaping life.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Starving your heart's
God-pleasing feelings.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Doubting God's compassionate descent
Into the aspiration-heart
Of every human being.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Cherishing
Even a fleeting desire-smile.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Living in your mind's
Ever-lengthening confusion-chains.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Being ignorant of the futility
Of fear and doubt.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Ridiculing
Your heart-ennobling ideals.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Being instigated
By your sophisticated mind.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Ignoring the goodwill
Of your flower-heart-fragrance.

Immediately ...

Immediately
Stop
Hiding.
Come out and bravely fight.
God wants you to be a hero supreme
On the battlefield of life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Immediately stop!, Agni Press, 1993
