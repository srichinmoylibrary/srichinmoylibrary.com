570 Thanks To Heaven, Thanks To Earth

Thanks to Heaven,
I know how to serve devotedly
And smile soulfully.

Thanks to earth,
I know how to aspire sleeplessly
And surrender unconditionally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 6, Agni Press, 1979
