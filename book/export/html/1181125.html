8121 Your Uncertain Life

Your old mind
Is tired of living.
Your new heart
Is afraid of dying.
Your uncertain life
Is crying one moment
And smiling the next -
Crying for possession
And smiling with renunciation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 82, Agni Press, 1983
