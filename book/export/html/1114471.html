1071

My Lord, the world blames me
  For many things.
Will You not tell the world
  Once and for all
That You are not only my Creator
But also my life-violin-Player?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 2, Agni Press, 1998
