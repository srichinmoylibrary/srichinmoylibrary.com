3188 The Only Remedy

In the mental world
There is only one disease:
"I know everything."

In the psychic world
Lies the only remedy:
"God knows everything in me and for me."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 32, Agni Press, 1982
