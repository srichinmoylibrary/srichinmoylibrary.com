Welkin

(sky)

I am dauntlessly determined
To make my inner welkin ring
With my heart's sleepless
Aspiration-cry.

Live in your heart-welkin
To be ever happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
