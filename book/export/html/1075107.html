THE MASTER-KEY

Life has two significant aspects:
A changeful aspect
And
A changeless aspect.
God is undoubtedly the master-key
To both the aspects.

Life has two stories:
The story of destruction
And
The story of perfection.
God is undoubtedly the master-key
To both the stories.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 11, Agni Press, 1973
