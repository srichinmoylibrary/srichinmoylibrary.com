9281 Two Questions Have Stung Me

Two questions have stung me:
Why have I not told God
The entire truth?
Is there anything
That I have learned
Which it would not be better
If I unlearned?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 93, Agni Press, 1983
