20. A brief oblivion

My earth-life:
A brief oblivion.
My Heaven-life:
A brief oblivion, too.
My earth-oblivion
Prevents me from seeing
The Face of God.
My Heaven-oblivion
Prevents me from touching
The Feet of God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
