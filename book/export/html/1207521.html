21669

I do not want to know
What they do in Heaven.
I do not even want to know
What others do on earth.
I just want to do the things
That will keep my Lord Supreme smiling.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 217, Agni Press, 1994
