21 March 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My implicit faith in You
Is the smile-beauty
Of my heart-garden.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 March 1999, 7:56 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
