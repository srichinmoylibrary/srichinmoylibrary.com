3288 What My Mind Needs

What my mind needs
Is an unwavering faith.
What my heart needs
Is an abiding faith.
What my life needs
Is a pure, blind faith.
And what I need
Is an unfeigned faith.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 33, Agni Press, 1982
