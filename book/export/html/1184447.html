119. I have a new friend

To make a very long story
Very short,
I wish to tell you
I have now a new friend,
a real friend,
Unlike all my previous friends.
Do you want to know
My best and only real friend's name?
Hope-flame.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
