3679

Is there any special reason
Why you cannot accept
God’s philosophy as your own?
Is it too demanding,
  Or is it too lenient?

“It is not that I do not want
To accept God’s philosophy.
  I want to, but alas,
My love-devotion-surrender
  I myself buried
In my self-oblivion.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 4, Agni Press, 1998
