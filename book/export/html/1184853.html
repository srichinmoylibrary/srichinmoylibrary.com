525. My birthday song

My birthday song:
I plan to soar,
I plan to roar.
The bird in me
Will teach me how to soar.
The lion in me
Will teach me how to roar.
I plan to love,
Below, above.
The lover in me will teach me
How to love
Within,
Without,
Below,
Above.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
