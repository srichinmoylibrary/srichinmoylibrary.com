Saral's brother

Yesterday I went to a store in Santiago, Chile, and bought an umbrella. The man in the store talked and looked so much like Saral that I couldn't believe it. He was showing me such devotion-just like Saral. He was one hundred per cent Saral's brother!

-23 December 1986

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
