Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 85

8401

Once upon a time
Your life was claimed
By God Himself
Because you felt
That there was nobody to claim
But God.

8402

God treasures
More than anything else
The sweetness
Of a satisfied soul.

8403

If you are a good man,
Then every day you must offer
Gratitude-gifts
To your supremely compassionate soul.

8404

Current news from Heaven:
God is going to give another chance
To humanity
For its perfect perfection.

8405

He was surprised to see
That there are so many professors
Available
At the university of self-deception.

8406

My heart's willingness
Constantly widens
God's Satisfaction-Road.

8407

He is prepared to fight
For the illumination
Of ignorance-night
Although he is attacked mercilessly
By the heavy clouds
Of ignorance itself.

8408

From aspiration-river
We enter into perfection-sea.
From perfection-sea
We enter into God's
Satisfaction-Ocean.

8409

Your love of God
Is extremely beautiful,
And your love of God
Is extremely powerful.
Do not allow it to mix
With the expectations
Of your stupid mind.

8410

You are telling me
That you are looking for
An inner shelter.
You fool!
Can you not see
That your soul is always ready
To grant you its sanctuary-shelter?

8411

When I look at my mind,
I unfortunately see
That darkness has defeated me.

When I look into my heart,
I clearly see
That darkness will before long perish.

8412

My mind tells me
That there is nothing as valuable
As success-march.
My heart tells me
That there is nothing as beautiful,
Meaningful and fruitful
As progress-walk.

8413

Not to speak of human beings,
Even God is spellbound
By your heart's aspiration-songs.

8414

His doubts are falling
From his mind-tree.
His fears are falling
From his heart-tree.
His imperfections are falling
From his life-tree.

8415

Open your unrealised heart
To realise God.
Again, seal your unillumined lips
To realise God.

8416

If you want to quench
Your heart's inner thirst,
You need your life's
Dedication-drink every day.

8417

Do you not see
That your entire being is caught
Mercilessly
In the web of a dark desire-spider?

8418

Each temptation-thought
Is undoubtedly the beginning
Of a destruction-action.

8419

Where there is hope,
There is peace.
Where there is peace,
There is God's Satisfaction-Dance.

8420

Alas,
No matter what he does,
No matter how he tries to meditate,
He is lost
Between his self-centred mind
And his God-centred heart.

8421

I may not know
Who God is,
I may not know
Where God is,
But I do know
And my heart does feel
That God wants me to be
A perfect instrument of His.

8422

I dream of God's Power-Hand
To make me great
So that I can dominate the world.

God dreams of my satisfaction-heart
To make me a perfect God-lover
So that I can become a perfect world-helper
On earth.

8423

In the spiritual life
If you don't illumine your mind
With your heart's faith-flames,
You will be totally lost
In the maze of self-doubts.

8424

Every day
If you water your faith-plant
Inside your heart-garden,
Then you will be
Out of destruction's reach.

8425

I shall be happy
To be of help to you
If you come to me
With a broken mind
And not a broken heart.

8426

His heart of purity
Came from nowhere,
But now it belongs
To all-where.

8427

My vital,
You have done absolutely
The right thing
By breaking an appointment
With temptation.

8428

True,
Ignorance-downpour has drenched you.
But behold,
Wisdom-sun is beckoning you!

8429

Unbelievable,
Although it is true:
He allows himself
To be engulfed
In a vast sea of complaints.

8430

He is expert
In sailing his life-boat
On his mind's confusion-river.
Alas, what is worse,
He enjoys it immensely!

8431

O my heart,
Reject, reject!
It is high time
For you to reject
Your old insecurity-culprit-friend.

8432

His Inner Pilot
Is always pleased with him
And proud of him
Because at every moment
He breathes in and out
Only the clear air
Of innocence-light.

8433

God will definitely satisfy you
One day
Because you are always
Fond of living
Under the canopy
Of hope-delight.

8434

Alas,
Your mind is nothing
But the wild activity
Of uncomely thoughts.
In vain
I try to help you,
All in vain!

8435

His desire-cancer
Has progressed so rapidly,
Even his soul
Is alarmed at it.

8436

Every day
God enjoys reading
Your love-flooded
Heart-letters.

8437

Tomorrow's realisation
Shall never come to you.
In vain you are waiting for it.

8438

Not earth-bound
But Heaven-climbing questions
Should absorb your mind.

8439

Each ugly thought
Is the builder
Of deathless death-pillar.

8440

If you long to help the world,
Then help with your sleepless eyes
And undivided heart.

8441

Someday
The inner soul
Will claim its outer property.

8442

If you want to live,
Then dream.
If you want to dream,
Then love.
If you want to love,
Then become.
If you want to become,
Then cry with the heart of humanity
And smile with the Eye of God.

8443

To be animal
Is to sadly fail.
To be human
Is to doubtfully hope.
To be divine
Is to soulfully manifest
God the Dream on earth.

8444

You do not need any human admirer,
For God Himself is admiring you
Every day, all the time,
For your tireless and selfless efforts.

8445

My mind has worries,
Teeming worries.
My heart has prayers,
Climbing prayers.
My soul has promises,
God-fulfilling promises.

8446

When surrender-plants start growing
In your aspiring heart-field,
God will bless you with a new name:
Perfection.

8447

Do not challenge ignorance-strength
With your mind's very limited capacity.
You can challenge ignorance-strength
Only with your soul's unlimited,
All-conquering will power.

8448

Not your gratitude-hands
But your gratitude-heart
Can and shall
Reach God's Feet
Of Satisfaction-Delight.

8449

He who prays to God
Soulfully
And he who meditates on God
Devotedly —
For him and only for him
His Beloved Supreme
Will always be on duty.

8450

How long will you be playing
Foolishly and unnecessarily
With your ugly desire-toys?

8451

All undivine thoughts
Must fade away
Under the perfection-light
Of the heart's morning sun.

8452

Choose your way
Among teeming ways.
No matter which way you go,
The destination will be the same.

8453

Your unwillingness
Has disfigured you,
Including your inner beauty's
Heart-flower.

8454

Your God-invoked heart-will
Will stand far above
Your mind's volcano-will.

8455

The Immortal
Dances with the hopes,
Frustrations
And sighs
Of mankind.

8456

An unthinking will
Is responsible
For the untold sufferings
Of this world.

8457

The Compassion-Depth
Of God's Heart-Sea
Always remains unfathomable.

8458

His life's lethargy-lease
Has expired.
Therefore, his heart and soul
Are extremely happy
And his vital and mind
Are extremely unhappy.

8459

Alas, in life
Nothing is as transient
As satisfaction-spring.

8460

Earth's sound-life
Will accompany him
Until God's Silence-Light
Replaces it.

8461

Alas,
Every day I try so hard
To civilise my senses.
Alas,
I am nowhere near
My satisfaction.

8462

Where does
A man of hesitation live?
He lives
In the house of bitter failure.

8463

Your pleasure-life
And God's Indifference-Eye
Are always found together.

8464

If you want to be
Divinely great and supremely good,
Then you must explore
Your heart's gratitude-territory.

8465

I have only one home,
And that is my hope-home.
When I abandon this home
I have nowhere to go.
I am all alone
In misery's land.

8466

A realisation-soul
Is a freedom-bird
That flies
In God's Bliss-dancing Sky.

8467

You want proclamations from God,
But God can and will give you
Only emancipation.

8468

God is ready to give you
What He has:
His fruitful Heart.

Are you ready to give God
What you have:
Your doubtful mind?

8469

No, not because I know,
No, not because I have,
But because I am,
My sweet Lord is
His supreme Satisfaction.

8470

If you do not want
To accept the world,
God's own world,
That means
God has already rejected you!

8471

What I soulfully see
Is my Lord's Compassion-Eye.
What I sleeplessly feel
Is my Lord's Oneness-Heart.
Therefore, I am on my way
To self-realisation.

8472

What am I doing?
I am waiting for my aspiration-heart.
What is my aspiration-heart doing?
My aspiration-heart is waiting
For an interview with our Beloved Supreme.

8473

My Lord Supreme,
Do make me conscious
Of the divine, supreme fact
That every day
You blessingfully keep in touch
With my heart's aspiration-cry
And my soul's illumination-smile.

8474

I forget,
That God is an old man
And that He can easily advise me
In everything I do and say.

I forget
That I am a little child
And that I can play with Him
Any time I want to.

8475

God is at once shocked
And embarrassed
When you say that you have
Nothing to do with
The ignorance-night of the world.

8476

God is His Head
To protect me.
God is His Eye
To illumine me.
God is His Heart
To love me.
God is His Breath
To immortalise me.

8477

When I pray devotedly,
God teaches me His Perfection-Song.

When I meditate soulfully,
God teaches me His Satisfaction-Dance.

When I surrender unconditionally,
God invites me to drink deep
His Nectar-Delight.

8478

When my aspiration descends,
I become my expecting mind
And my demanding vital.

When my aspiration ascends,
My Lord becomes my heart's Dream-Boat
And my life's Reality-Shore.

8479

Two Himalayan mistakes
You make almost every day:
One when you say
That you are not meant for
The spiritual path;
One when you think
That you are indispensable.

8480

If your aspiration
Does not long for perfection,
Then what kind of aspiration
Is it?

8481

God's Compassion-Emergency Exit
Has saved your life
Time and again
From impurity-destruction attacks.
Yet you do not have
An iota of gratitude
To offer God.

8482

Anything that is unlit
Is unwanted.
Our doubting mind tops the list
Of unwanted things
In our life.

8483

You do not have to slow down
The speed of your spiritual life,
For yours is at once
A God-protected and God-perfected life.

8484

You do not have to resist,
You do not have to insist,
You just have to exist on earth
And love God's Compassion-Eye
More and more
So that you can have
An abiding satisfaction-heart.

8485

Although you are
An inexperienced seeker,
You are going to run fast,
Very fast,
In your spiritual life,
For you are a genuine God-lover.

8486

Since my heart is of God
I shall succeed.
Since my life is for God
I shall proceed.
Since I am one with God
I shall always lead.

8487

Every day
God does something very special
For humanity:
He most compassionately listens
To humanity's heart-radio.

8488

Every day we take
A number of ego-exercises.
That means every day
We dig our graves.

8489

If you do not pray to God
Soulfully,
And if you do not meditate on God
Unconditionally,
How can you ever expect
The ultimate defeat of death?

8490

We never ask God
The right question,
Yet we expect from Him
The right answer.
Is it not absurd?

8491

We must be extremely careful
Of our shortcomings,
For our shortcomings are long-lived.
Not only that,
But they may remain on earth permanently
Even after we have all left
This earth-planet.

8492

Today I have chosen
To see God's Compassion-Eye
And His Satisfaction-Heart
Inside a human being.

8493

This morning I have destroyed
My expectation-mind.
Lo and behold,
My Beloved Supreme has given me
His own Satisfaction-Heart!

8494

From the world
Of a tornado-mind
He jumps into the heart
Of tranquillity's sea.

8495

God started the Cosmic Game.
Now it is you
Who have to continue it.

8496

A life of sleepless surrender
Has a free pass
To God's celestial Abode.

8497

Just a simple iota of love
Is all that is needed
To please our Beloved Supreme
Every day, every hour, every second,
And for this
No prerequisite is needed.

8498

O my mind,
You are praying reluctantly.
Therefore, for you
There is no salvation.

O my heart,
You are praying hesitantly.
Therefore, for you
There is no liberation.

O my life,
You are praying fearfully.
Therefore, for you
There is no realisation.

8499

Since you are not
Spiritually rich enough
To take a non-stop aspiration-flight,
How can you expect to see
God's immediate Satisfaction-Face?

8500

May your earth-life become
Immortality's
Goodness-Perfection.

May your Heaven-life become
Divinity's
Transcendence-Satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 85, Agni Press, 1984
