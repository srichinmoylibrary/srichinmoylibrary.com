4667 My Mind's Desire-Life Thrives

My mind's desire-life thrives
On success-night.
My heart's aspiration-life thrives
On progress-light.
And I thrive
On God's Compassion-Height.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 47, Agni Press, 1982
