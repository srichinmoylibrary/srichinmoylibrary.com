18 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My Beloved Supreme,
Only when Your unconditional Grace
Descends,
My heart, my life
And everything that I have and am
Can ascend.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 October 1998, 10:50 a.m. Before unsuccessfully attempting to lift 120 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
