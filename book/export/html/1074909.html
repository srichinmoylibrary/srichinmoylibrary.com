341 Two Teachers...

TWO TEACHERS

As a dance-teacher,
God teaches me
How to dance soulfully
Before the cosmic gods.

As an ignorance-teacher,
I teach God
How to cry pitifully
Before the human multitudes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 7, Agni Press, 1973
