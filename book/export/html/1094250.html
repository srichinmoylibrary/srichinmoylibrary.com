20 I Tell God, ...

I tell God,
"My Lord, I surrender.
You have won the race.
I shall fly with You,
I shall dive with You,
I shall run with You,
I shall always be with You,
In You and for You."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 8, Agni Press, 2002
