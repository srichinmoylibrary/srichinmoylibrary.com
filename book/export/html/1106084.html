55.

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
With every breath of my heart
I enter into Your Heart
To love You only in Your own Way.

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
With every breath of my heart
I sit at Your Feet
To need You only in Your own Way.

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
With every breath of my heart
I look at Your Eye
To fulfil You only in Your own Way.
