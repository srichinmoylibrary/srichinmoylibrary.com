He was a businessman who sold vegetable cutters. He thought that since the Master had quite a few disciples, he would be able to sell his vegetable cutters to them. He gave a free demonstration of his machine. The Master felt sorry for him and bought four or five sets which he presented to his favourite disciples. But the man was not satisfied.
