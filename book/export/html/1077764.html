117. The soul of Paradise

You have thought
Good thoughts.
Lo, you have become
The body of Paradise.

You have meditated
Silently and soulfully.
Lo, you have become
The soul of Paradise.

You have dedicated
Your life unconditionally.
Lo, you have become
The goal of Paradise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
