406

There was a time
When you used to feel
God's loving Heart,
But now you are only seeing
God's ruling Hand.
I tell you,
Do not be confused or frightened.
God's ruling Hand is only showing you
How to return home
Safely, soulfully and richly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 5, Agni Press, 1983
