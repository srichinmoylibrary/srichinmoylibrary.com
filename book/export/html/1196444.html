10701

I may not be or cannot be a member
Of God's Vision-Manifestation-team
On earth,
But I dearly love the team's
God-oneness-heart
And profoundly admire
Its God-fulness-hands.
But I do know and I do feel
That God will eventually make me
A member of His Vision-Manifestation-team.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 108, Agni Press, 1987
