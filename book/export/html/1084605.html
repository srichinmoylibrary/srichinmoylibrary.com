18. Good friends

Expectation and I
Were once good friends.
Surrender and I
Have become good friends.
Eternity's Infinity,
Infinity's Immortality
And I
Are soon going to be good friends.
Who says so?
God!
Who believes it?
The God in Heaven,
The God in my heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
