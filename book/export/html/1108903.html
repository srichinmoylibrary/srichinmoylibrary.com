51.

Do not try to experience others
In your own way.
Just try to experience yourself
In your own way
And, if possible, in God's Way.
Then you will have real peace,
God-created peace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God's Fragrance-Heart, part 2, Agni Press, 1992
