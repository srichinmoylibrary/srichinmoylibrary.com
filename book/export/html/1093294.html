16. Love

I love God
Because He alone loves me.
I love God
Because He alone claims me.
I love God
Because He alone needs me.
I love God
Because He alone tells me
That I am none other than Himself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My California Redwoods, Agni Press, 1974
