1676 A New Idol

How can you have
Improvement-satisfaction
When you have been copying
Your clever mind
For such a long time?
Have a new idol.
Call it the fountain-heart
Of your wisdom-art.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 17, Agni Press, 1981
