5678 Your Life Of Perfection

If your mind can be
Brutally sincere
To your heart,
And if your heart can be
Sleeplessly sincere
To your soul,
Then your life of perfection
Can never, never remain a far cry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 57, Agni Press, 1983
