11.

Do you want love?

You can feel love.

Try to tend a garden and see your loving concern blossom. You can show your concern and you can see it blossoming.

Lo. You feel love.

Lo. You give love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Love, joy, happiness, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1996
