47. Never and ever

God has chosen me.
I shall not fail Him.
Never!
Man has chosen me.
I shall love him.
Ever!
Heaven has chosen me.
I shall never speak ill of Heaven.
Never!
Earth has chosen me.
I shall carry earth's burden.
Ever!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
