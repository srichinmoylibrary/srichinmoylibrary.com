30. You are great you are good

The psychoses and neuroses
Of the outer world
Have not been able
     To plague you.
           Therefore
You are unmistakably great.

The realisation and perfection
Of the inner world
Have found
Their complete satisfaction
In your heart-cave.
           Therefore
You are supremely good.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
