8986 My Heart Is Suffering

My heart is suffering
From chronic pain,
Not because my mind
Does not love God,
Not because my mind
Does not need God,
But because my Lord Supreme
Has tried in vain, time and again,
To illumine and immortalise my mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 90, Agni Press, 1983
