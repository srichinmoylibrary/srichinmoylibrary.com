My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 53

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Istanbul and Belek, Turkey in November and December 2006.

21 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

1 I Pray And Pray ...

I pray and pray
And pray
To see God’s Feet.

2 I Meditate And Meditate ...

I meditate and meditate
And meditate
To feel God’s Heart.

3 I Contemplate, Contemplate ...

I contemplate, contemplate
And contemplate
To become one with God’s Will.

4 When My Prayer ...

When my prayer
Is sincere,
God blesses my head.

5 When My Meditation ...

When my meditation
Is deep,
God embraces my heart.

6 What Do I Need? ...

What do I need?
A heart-throbbing prayer.

7 What Do I Need? ...

What do I need?
A time-stopping meditation.

22 November 2006 ...

22 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

8 I Was Born ...

I was born
With a heart
Of God-beauty.

9 I Was Born ...

I was born
With a life
Of God-duty.

10 The Desire-Life ...

The desire-life
Is always busy —
It has no free time.

11 God Wants Our Heart’s Hopes ...

God wants our heart’s hopes
Always to be hopeful,
And not hopeless.

12 I Ask God ...

I ask God
How He spends His time.
He says to me,
“My child, I spend My time
Without thinking of Me.”

13 God-Realisation ...

God-realisation
Is
A solo run.

14 God-Manifestation ...

God-manifestation
Is
A relay run.

23 November 2006 ...

23 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

15 Every Breath Of My Heart ...

Every breath of my heart
Is needed
To worship the golden dust
Of God’s Feet.

16 Every Day ...

Every day
The Godward speed
Of my heart
Baffles my mind.

17 The Aspiration-Heart ...

The aspiration-heart
Knows no sunset years,
But the doubtful mind does.

18 There Is Not A Single Day ...

There is not a single day
That God does not show
His red caution-flag
To our disobedient mind and vital.

19 Our Heart’s God-Devotion-Tears ...

Our heart’s God-devotion-tears
Are full of
God-Touch-thrills.

20 God The Creator ...

God the Creator
Blesses
My climbing aspiration.

21 God The Creation ...

God the Creation
Embraces
My spreading dedication.

24 November 2006 ...

24 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

22 The Human In Me Wants ...

The human in me wants
The world’s thunderous praise
All the time.

23 The Divine In Me Needs ...

The divine in me needs
Blessingful Grace from God
All the time.

24 To Meditate ...

To meditate
Is to have a quick dive
Into God’s Heart-Swimming Pool.

25 Standing, I Can See ...

Standing, I can see
The Golden Shore
From my wide-open
Heart’s door.

26 Limitless Delight I Enjoy ...

Limitless delight I enjoy
When I remain nameless
In my God-love,
In my God-devotion
And
In my God-surrender-service.

27 Pride ...

Pride
Openly challenges.
Soulfulness
Secretly wins.

28 Every Morning When I Meditate ...

Every morning when I meditate,
The singing flowers,
The dancing branches
And the climbing trees
Join me in my meditation.

25 November 2006 ...

25 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

29 God’s Outer Presence ...

God’s outer Presence
Is
Definitely needed.

30 God’s Inner Presence ...

God’s inner Presence
Is
Absolutely indispensable.

31 The Mind Thinks Only ...

The mind thinks only
Of pleasing itself.

32 The Heart Dreams ...

The heart dreams
Of pleasing its own
Absolute Lord Beloved Supreme.

33 The Tears Of My Heart ...

The tears of my heart
Are most devotedly following
The Footsteps
Of my Lord Supreme.

34 I Wish My Heart ...

I wish my heart
To be
A ceaseless God-hunger-cry.

35 I Wish My Life ...

I wish my life
To be
A breathless God-Touch-thrill.

26 November 2006 ...

26 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

36 I Have Made ...

I have made
My heart-cottage
With my tears.

37 I Have Built ...

I have built
My soul-palace
With God’s Smiles.

38 When My Meditation Is Deep ...

When my meditation is deep,
Very deep,
My heart breathes in
God-hunger-breaths.

39 We Must Brave ...

We must brave
All our difficulties,
Problems and failures
In our lives.

40 My Lord ...

My Lord
And my Lord’s Forgiveness
Are inseparable.

41 Alas ...

Alas,
I always overestimate
The sincerity
Of my prayers.

42 Alas ...

Alas,
I always underestimate
The infinite Power
Of God’s Grace.

27 November 2006 ...

27 November 2006

Istanbul, Turkey

43 With Silence-Beauty ...

With Silence-Beauty
And Sweetness-Fragrance-Steps,
My Lord enters into
My tiny human heart.

44 When I Cheerfully ...

When I cheerfully
And unconditionally
Obey God,
I become immediately
His favourite toy.

45 Conscience ...

Conscience
Is the aspiration-heart’s
Clock-alarm.

46 Alas ...

Alas,
The way to God-realisation
Is very, very long.

47 The Desire-Life ...

The desire-life
Is utterly ignorant
Of hidden dangers.

48 Out Of His Infinite Bounty ...

Out of His infinite Bounty,
My Lord tells me
That my gratitude-heart
Has the capacity to drown
All my life’s blunders.

49 Sleeplessly ...

Sleeplessly,
My heart-eye is focused
On God’s Compassion-Eye-Star.

29 November 2006 ...

29 November 2006

Belek, Turkey

50 God-Realisation-Trees ...

God-realisation-trees
Come into existence
From God-surrender-seeds.

51 Hope-Sun ...

Hope-sun
Does not set.
Hope-sun
Is immortal.

52 To Walk With Life ...

To walk with life
Is beautiful.

53 To Sleep With Death ...

To sleep with death
Is peaceful.

54 My God-Surrender ...

My God-surrender
Means
A life of constant surprises.

55 Heaven Beckons Only ...

Heaven beckons only
The sleepless self-givers.

56 God Sings And Sings; ...

God sings and sings;
I sleep and sleep.
I sing and sing;
God dreams and dreams.

30 November 2006 ...

30 November 2006

Belek, Turkey

57 Each Additional Responsibility ...

Each additional responsibility
Is another golden opportunity
To please the Inner Pilot
In His own Way.

58 An Aspiration-Heart ...

An aspiration-heart
Without fail embodies
Many divine qualities.

59 Each Seeker’s Life ...

Each seeker’s life
Must fly into
The highest aspiration-sky.

60 When I Pray ...

When I pray,
I use my humility-knees.

61 When I Meditate ...

When I meditate,
I use my world-oneness-heart.

62 A Readiness-Life ...

A readiness-life
Needs
An eagerness-start.

63 Every Day ...

Every day
The Master is
His torrential Heaven-blessings,
His Heaven-love
And his Heaven-concern
For his disciples.

64 The Master Keeps ...

The Master keeps
His disciples’ sorrows
Not on his shoulders,
But deep inside
His compassion-heart.

1 December 2006 ...

1 December 2006

Belek, Turkey

65 Centuries Ago, My Lord ...

Centuries ago, my Lord,
You created me.
Since then,
You have been taking care of me
With Your infinite Compassion,
Infinite Affection
And infinite Concern.
My Lord,
I wish You to take rest!
From now on
I shall most lovingly,
Most soulfully
And most self-givingly
Look after You.

66 Immeasurable Is My Joy ...

Immeasurable is my joy
When God asks me
To devour the dust
Of His Feet.

67 In The Morning ...

In the morning
God blesses me with the Joys
Of His Higher World.
In the evening
God blesses me with the Peace
Of His Inner World.

68 In The Small Hours Of The Morning ...

In the small hours of the morning,
My Lord checks and checks
And checks
My love, devotion and surrender.

69 Every Day, Unmistakably ...

Every day, unmistakably,
God’s Eye cradles
My aspiration-heart
So lovingly and so fondly.

70 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye,
God’s Affection-Heart
And
God’s Concern-Life
Are renovating my old life.

71 God’s Unconditional Compassion ...

God’s unconditional Compassion
Has drawn my life close, very close,
To His Heart.

72 May My Whole Life ...

May my whole life
Be inundated
With God-obedience-
Fulfilment-promises.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 53, Agni Press, 2007
