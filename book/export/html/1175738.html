2788 Taste And Renounce

They say
Taste the world and then renounce it.
I say
You cannot do that.
Your taste of the world
Will never end,
And your renunciation of the world
Will never begin.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 28, Agni Press, 1982
