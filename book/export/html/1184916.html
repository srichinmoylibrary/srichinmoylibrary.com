588. I expect

I expect humanity to learn
From Heaven
The message of God-Justice
On earth.

I expect divinity to learn
From earth
The message of God-Compassion
And
The power of prevailing peace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
