530. Birthday expectations

I expect nothing
From the birthday
Of my earth-body
Because
My earth-body
Is my frustration-sea.

I expect everything
From the birthday
Of my Heaven-soul
Because
My Heaven-soul
Is God's God-promise to me,
God's God-satisfaction in me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
