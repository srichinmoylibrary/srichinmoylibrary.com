January 30

If you do not deceive yourself, your liberation is certain. If you do not find faults with yourself, your liberation is certain. If you can make your heart the cow of love, your body the dog of devotion, and your mind the deer of surrender, your liberation is absolutely certain.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
