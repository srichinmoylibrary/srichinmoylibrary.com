11976

I guard my mind
So that no uncomely thoughts
Can enter into it.
My heart guides my life
So that my life can
Cheerfully, soulfully and devotedly
Play with God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 120, Agni Press, 1988
