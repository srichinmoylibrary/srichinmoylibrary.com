36. Our commitment

Life is our commitment
        To equality.
Love is our commitment
        To reality.
Peace is our commitment
        To liberty.
Power is our commitment
        To divinity.
God is our commitment
        To Immortality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 4, Agni Press, 1974
