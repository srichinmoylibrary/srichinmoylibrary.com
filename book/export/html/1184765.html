437. What he does and what he says

What he does
Only God and he know.
What he says
He knows and his friends know,
Both earth and Heaven know.

His action bears fruit
Inside the Heart of God.
His speech bears fruit
Inside the heart of aspiring and glowing humanity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
