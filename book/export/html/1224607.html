Keno.. Bijan Ghater Pane Takiye Achi

Keno keno keno bijan ghater pane
Takiye achi ke jane
Ke jena oi ase ase
Baka nadir pase pase
Oi bujhi oi elo kather majhi
Jahar lagi eka base achi
Elo kina kejane sab cherechi
Jar tane cheye achi jar pane
Se je aj dakche khane

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
