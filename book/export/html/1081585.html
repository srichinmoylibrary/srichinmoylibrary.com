26. Why they are chosen

Do you want to know
Why she is chosen?
She is chosen
Because
She loves nobody else
But God.

Do you want to know
Why he is chosen?
He is chosen
Because
He wants to challenge nobody else
But God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
