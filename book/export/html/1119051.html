5647

O my mind,
Every morning you take
A full breath of optimism;
  O my heart,
Every morning you take
A full breath of dynamism —
To please my Lord Supreme
  In His own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 6, Agni Press, 1998
