Earth-bound hearts

Vain are the ways
Of human cries.
Strange are the moulds
Of earthly hopes.
Queer is the dream
Of climbing skies.
In earth bound hearts
Divinity gropes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, AUM -- Vol. 1, No.11, June 27, 1966, Boro Park Printers -- Brooklyn, N. Y, 1966
