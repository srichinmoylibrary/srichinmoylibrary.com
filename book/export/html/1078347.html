THE DARLINGS

Two are the darlings of Heaven:
Light and Delight.
Two are the darlings of earth:
Cry and Surrender.
Two are the darlings of God:
You and I.
But you must never breathe
This secret supreme to anyone.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
