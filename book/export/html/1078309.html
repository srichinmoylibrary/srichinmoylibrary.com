662 Out Of Sheer Consideration...

OUT OF SHEER CONSIDERATION

Out of His infinite Compassion
God has transformed
My earth-tears
Into
My Heaven-smiles.

Out of sheer consideration
I shall convert
God-Dream
Into
God-Reality.
I promise I shall.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
