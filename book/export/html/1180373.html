7377 Did You Say Hello To God?

Did you say hello to God this morning?
"I did not remember."
Did you thank God this morning?
"I did not remember."
Did you criticise God this morning?
"Yes, I did, I did."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 74, Agni Press, 1983
