Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 146

14501

When I tell God
That I am ready,
He tells me
That this is the very beginning
Of His Dream-fulfilment-dawn.

14502

My blossoming gratitude-heart
Can easily speak
Any earth-language.

14503

May I become an ever-blossoming
Service-life, surrender-heart
And perfection-cry.

14504

God tells me
That only my heart-inspired devotion
Can reach His Compassion-Feet.

14505

My earth-bound desire-steps,
Finally I am taking leave of you
For good!

14506

The melody of God-Compassion
Has rescued my life
Time and again
From imminent catastrophe.

14507

The ungrateful world
Will never applaud
No matter how high and deep
The inspiration and aspiration
Of God-loving, God-serving seekers.

14508

My mind secretly appreciates,
Admires and even adores
All the divine qualities
Of my aspiration-heart.

14509

Do you want to know
My new address?
My new address is my Lord's
Unconditional Compassion-Eye.

14510

O my heart,
Are you not tired
Of loving my strangling vital
And my sleeping body?

14511

O artificial
Love-devotion-surrender-seeker,
Your dreams
Will never be transformed
Into realities!

14512

My Lord tells me
That it is I,
And nobody else,
Who have to smash
My mind's chaos-cage.

14513

If I share
My heart's confusion-kingdom
With humanity,
Then my Lord Supreme
Will not grant me
His Himalayan Satisfaction-Smile.

14514

Forgetfulness was the way
I used to walk.
Wakefulness is the way
I now walk.
Willingness multiplied by surrender
Will be the way
I shall walk in the future.

14515

Do your work every day:
Write to God!
God will definitely
Answer your letters,
Provided you use your heart-language.

14516

No confusion-clouds of the mind
Can ever greet
My heart's aspiration-dawn.

14517

My mind,
This is your last chance to choose:
Either desire-life to cry
Or aspiration-life to fly.

14518

An unconditional surrender-heart
Is more precious
Than all my earthly cries
And Heavenly smiles.

14519

Today I have made my Lord Supreme
Extremely happy
And extremely proud of me
By telling Him
Mine will be the life
Of non-stop service
From now to Eternity.

14520

On principle
God wants to speak to us
In pin-drop silence.
But we force God to speak to us loudly,
Especially when we become victims
Of suffering.

14521

Do you know what God's
First morning thought is?
He wants to see a soulful smile
From each and every human being.

14522

God-centred activities
Eventually blossom
Into God-satisfaction-realities.

14523

Any student of silence
Will be taught by God Himself
God's manifestation-lesson on earth.

14524

A telex from Heaven
Has come today.
God Himself has announced
My heart's sleepless love for Him.

14525

My Lord, I do not want to be
A miracle-performer.
I would like to be
A self-giving lover of God
Inside the heart of humanity.

14526

Every day
I add my heart's flower-fragrance
To my divinity-treasury.

14527

Even God's infinite Compassion-Heart
Could not tolerate him anymore.
Therefore, God revoked
His doubt-license.

14528

What God wants to see
In your eyes
Is your heart's purity-cry.

14529

The doubt-manufacturing mind
Is now out of business
Because the aspiration-heart-garden
Is blooming very fast.

14530

Two different kinds of insecurity
Torture him:
A fearful heart
And an impure mind.

14531

Do not be afraid
Of your mind's ruthless division-sword.
The sword will ultimately surrender
To your hero-warrior-heart.

14532

Your outer face
Is bound to show
The pace of your inner life.

14533

Alas, when will
Unwillingness-majority-minds
Be vehemently defeated
By willingness-minority-hearts?

14534

Eagerness is absolutely necessary
To speed up the seeker's
God-manifestation-life.

14535

Every day you must succeed
In building up your soul's strength
Inside your heart-cries.

14536

Only a purity-heart
Knows how to jump into action
Speedily and successfully.

14537

Every day the Lord Supreme
Is sending His genuine servers
From Heaven to earth
On peace missions.

14538

One peace-dove every morning
Flies from my gratitude-heart
To my surrender-life.

14539

Open the inner
Receptivity-door
And close the outer
Insecurity-door.

14540

Today God is
Extremely proud of you
Because you have burnt your mind's
Ignorance-built boat.

14541

Arrest, arrest
All your mind's uncomely
And speeding thoughts
To become a supremely chosen
Instrument of God.

14542

O God-lover,
You must not slow down
Before you reach
Your life's complete
Transformation-finish line.

14543

Only your heart's
Unconditional surrender
Can and will help you
Anchor your life-boat
In peace-harbour.

14544

Is there any special thing
That You are looking for today,
My Lord?
"My child,
As usual, I am searching and searching
For your willingness-heart."

14545

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me the capacity
To play on all
Peace-heart-instruments
Available in the world.

14546

Every morning,
Before I wake up,
My Beloved Lord Supreme blesses me
With His whispered Rainbow-Hopes.

14547

Every morning I invite
My earthly and Heavenly friends
To come and have breakfast with me.
My celestial friends
Come and eat with me,
And deeply appreciate my generosity-heart.
My earthly friends
Do not accept my invitation.

14548

O my aspiration-friend,
Your goal is God-realisation.
Do not settle for
A lesser realisation!

14549

Inner freedom
Is the only doorway
To outer satisfaction.

14550

Instead of creating a reason
Why you cannot love the world,
Try to create a reason
Why you should and must
Love the world.

14551

To keep your life God-centred,
You do not have to value
The proud opinion of the world.

14552

Your implicit faith in God
Should be your only guidebook
For your skyward journey.

14553

He who loves God
In his outer life of dedication
Has already been loved
By God
In his inner life of aspiration.

14554

Every day I have made it a point
To practise soulfully
The inner law of self-giving.

14555

Only our oneness-heart can be chosen
To be the custodian
Of our peace of mind.

14556

The silence-smile
Of your God-directed life
Can easily ignore the thunder
Of your unleashed mind.

14557

God's smiling Eyes,
God's loving Heart
And God's self-giving Life
Are your immediate neighbours.

14558

The first note of my heart's soulful song
Starts with God-invocation,
And the last note ends
With God-manifestation.

14559

When the supreme Hour dawns,
God Himself will give you
The capacity
To be equal to the task
Of God-realisation.

14560

Your love-devotion-surrender-qualities
Are so precious.
How is it that you have allowed them
To be frightened by your mind's
Ignorance-night-thunderstorm ?

14561

God's Compassion-Fondness-children
Are those
Whose hearts are made of
Sweetness-fragrance-flowers.

14562

O self-criticism-arrows,
No more do I need you
Or your company.
We are parting for good.

14563

The inner battle
Will definitely end
The day I turn my entire life
Into a God-surrendered song.

14564

My mind, can you not see
That you are helplessly lost
In your own self-created
Doubt-wilderness?

14565

O my mind,
Stop thinking of your
Earth-born ideas.
Can you not see that
You have reached a dead end?

14566

God wants me to join Him
While He is singing
The world-transformation-song
Inside my heart's silence-temple.

14567

Be wise!
Instead of wrestling
With the reasoning mind,
Just remain faithful
To your God-loving heart.

14568

God asks me to take
A seven-minute prayer-test
In the morning
And a seven-minute meditation-test
In the evening.

14569

Compromise is no solution
To eternal problems.
All problems can be solved
By the enlargement of our oneness-heart.

14570

God wants to be your partner
Only when you are willing to sing
About God the universal Beauty
And God the transcendental Purity.

14571

God's Mind
Is very carefully watching
Your steps.
God's Heart
Is compassionately waiting
For you,
And for you alone.

14572

I take each good thought
As a shrine
Inside my heart's
Sacred silence-temple.

14573

Self-giving
Is truth-learning.
Truth-learning
Is God-finding.

14574

Truth that we speak of
Comes from God's Eye.
Truth that we become
Comes from God's Heart.

14575

God gives us always another chance.
This other chance will definitely
Save us and liberate us.

14576

God enriches us
With countless Blessings daily.
If we can count only a few
And devotedly use them,
Ours will be a life
Of measureless satisfaction.

14577

Poor God cries in silence
When He sees
That we are turning a deaf ear
To His Opportunity-Knocking.

14578

I am so surprised to learn
That God-obedience
Is such a sweet and simple thing!

14579

How can we appease God
With our imagined or self-inflicted pain?
Impossible!

14580

Both in the inner world
And in the outer world
I am noticing only two things:
God's Justice-Light shortage
And Compassion-Height surplus.

14581

When I write love-letters
To God,
He becomes happy with me.

When I write devotion-letters
To God,
He becomes proud of me.

When I write surrender-letters
To God,
He invites me to come and visit
His celestial Palace.

When I write gratitude-letters
To God,
He tells me to come and stay
At His celestial Palace permanently.

14582

If you wait and watch,
You will definitely see
A rush on the God-journey-road.
Therefore, start aspiring now,
Immediately.

14583

You want me to tell you what to do?
Just descend
Into your mind's insecurity-cave
With your heart's effulgent light.

14584

My Lord,
Like Your Compassion-Flood,
May my gratitude-drop
Never be out of season.

14585

Alas! I never realised
That protecting me from myself
Would be such an ordeal.

14586

Cherish not your doubting mind.
You will be forced to live
In ignorance-prison.

14587

God wants each and every sincere seeker
To enter into His Heart-Palace
Through the front door,
Since there is no other entrance.

14588

My seeker friend,
You can fool me,
You can fool others,
But you cannot fool God
With your wild inner insolence
And clever outer humility.

14589

This morning I dreamt of God.
When I told Him about my dream,
He said to me
That He had had the same Dream
Last night.

14590

O my heart's aspiration-army,
Can you not see that
You are under attack?
Can you not fight against
Your stark foes?
Can you not conquer them
In the Name of
Your Lord Beloved Supreme?

14591

Until you can align yourself
With God the Justice-Light,
You will not be able to prove
To the world
Your own divine worth.

14592

If you desperately need God,
Then force your ignorance-dreams
To be shorter than the shortest.

14593

Silence does not have to shout
To prove its authenticity,
For silence is always supported
By the Absolute Lord Supreme.

14594

The Sweetness-Smiles
Of my Lord Supreme
I treasure more than anything else.

14595

To break down the mind's
Ego-wall,
Use your heart's
Oneness-illumination-smile.

14596

My Lord Supreme,
In my inner life
I constantly feel Your Presence.
Why is it that in my outer life
I am doomed to helplessness?

14597

When I have the spiritual
Power-failure-experience,
I laugh and laugh.
When I have the spiritual
Power-success-experience,
I become my gratitude-heart
With tearful eyes.

14598

If you are determined to run every day
An unfaltering life-race,
Then soulfully multiply
Your heart's mounting flames.

14599

My soul's smile and my heart's cry
Together sail in the sea
Of God's infinite Compassion.

14600

Each Compassion-flooded day
Is a most powerful Blessing
From the Lord Supreme.
Let us not squander this Blessing.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 146, Agni Press, 1991
