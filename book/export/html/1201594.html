Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 159

15801

My Lord Supreme,
Your Satisfaction supreme
Is my life's
Sweeter-than-the-sweetest dream.

15802

My heart's faith
Is my life's master-key
To open up not only
Everybody's heart of love
But also everybody's mind of willingness.

15803

If you have
A childlike enthusiasm,
Then this enthusiasm
Will put an end
To your anxiety-mind.

15804

The last time I saw God,
He told me to be careful
Of my mind's complacent life.
He said, "Make friends
With your heart's climbing cries
To make Me happy
And to make yourself fully satisfied."

15805

In a moment of silence
God brings to the fore
My gratitude-heart,
And inside that gratitude-heart
I see God's all-illumining
Fountain-Smile.

15806

When it is a matter of man's aspiration,
There is a single lane of traffic.
But when it is a matter of surrender,
There is a double lane of traffic
Between man and God.

15807

An unfortunate report
Has come from Heaven
That right now
I am not needed at all.

15808

So many great obstacles
Have crossed my path,
But my soul's adamantine will
Has surmounted them all.

15809

I am supremely happy
To have two genuine friends:
My clarity-mind
And my magnanimity-heart.

15810

When I strive for perfection,
God drives me
In His Satisfaction-Limousine.

15811

I love to live
In three special places:
Inside my humility-mind,
Inside my purity-heart,
Inside my simplicity-life.

15812

I do not see much difference
Between an aggressive vital
And a lethargic body.
They both ultimately lead
A human being
To destruction.

15813

Time used undivinely
Brings destruction.
Time used divinely
Brings not only perfection
But also satisfaction.

15814

Jealousy quite often
Helplessly surrenders
To despair.

15815

Never extinguish
Your heart's hope-flames
If you want to see
The fulfilment-promise
Of your soul on earth.

15816

True, ignorant people
Do not suffer so much
From mental conflicts,
But their lives are utterly empty
Of peace, joy and bliss.

15817

I am divinely and supremely happy
Because my blue aspiration-bird
Is flying
On two beautiful and powerful wings.

15818

Nothing is permanent,
Not even my jealous
And voracious mind-doubts.

15819

There can be
No security-guarantee
In man's possession-life.

15820

O my mind,
I feel extremely sorry for you.
Your vision is very limited
And you are only a gossip-expert.

15821

Every morning
I prayerfully touch
The Compassion-Feet
Of my Lord Supreme
With my heart's gratitude-tears.

15822

No resentment-shackles
Of my mind
Can ever bind
My God-willingness-heart.

15823

My Heaven-climbing heart
Every day
Is energised by a fresh supply
Of energy-bliss.

15824

My rainbow-heart-hope
Has illumined
My mind's doubt-cave.

15825

I have only one fond dream:
I long to see
The Feet of my Lord Supreme,
And prayerfully cry and cry
And majestically smile and smile.

15826

One desire-free moment
Is as blissful
As flying in my Lord's
Delight-flooded Plane.

15827

An aspiration-heart
Is bound to melt eventually
In God-Eternity's Nectar-Sea.

15828

If you cannot push aside
All your obstructions,
Then invoke your soul's
Blessingful presence.
Your soul will help you
To do the needful.

15829

It is not so easy,
But not impossible,
To avoid
Ignorance-deception-trap.

15830

Every day
My heart's miracle-hopes
Soar and fly
With my soul-bird
In God's Nectar-flooded Sky.

15831

Silence blessingfully touched
My thought-chained mind
And liberated it.

15832

You just need an iota
Of heart-energising faith
To clear away
All your mind-doubt-debris.

15833

A soulful seeker has the capacity
To increase his inner ultramarathon distance
Every day
During his God-intoxicated meditation.

15834

Nothing is more beautiful,
Nothing is more meaningful,
Nothing is more fruitful
Than my heart's God-hunger-dream.

15835

I do have
The confidence-capacity
To march far beyond
My mind's insecurity-prison.

15836

No matter how fast
The mind runs,
Very easily
Fear, doubt and insecurity
Can overtake it.

15837

It is so strange
That the human mind
Likes to live
Under the hesitation-influence.

15838

I am so proud of my heart!
It sleeplessly keeps
The God-manifestation-dream burning
Bright, brighter, brightest.

15839

Never allow
Your mind's confusion-clouds
To eclipse your climbing heart's
Aspiration-summit.

15840

No unconditional life
Ever reaches a dead end.
It will always run
Faster than the fastest
Along Eternity's
God-Satisfaction-Road.

15841

When stark falsehood
Prevails over truth,
The human life suffers
Its most humiliating defeat.

15842

I love the mind that challenges
The pride of impossibility.
But I dislike the mind
That deliberately enjoys
Absurdity.

15843

In my life
Nothing can be as sweet
As my life's unconditional
Surrender-fruits.

15844

I am sleeplessly
And breathlessly fond of
My heart's gratitude-blossoming glow.

15845

O my mind,
You are such a fool!
Can you not see
That you are chasing
The shadow of insecurity?

15846

Give what you have:
Insecurity.
Become what you are:
Wisdom.
Then you are ready
To reach for the summit
Of your soul's bliss transcendental.

15847

O my purity-heart,
I am so fond of you,
I am so proud of you!
I shall always remain
Inside you,
Not above or below you.

15848

When my mind predicts
My uncertainty-flooded future,
My loving heart
Cries and cries
And my illumining soul
Laughs and laughs.

15849

Alas, alas!
I do not know
Why and how
The mind of humanity
Has become so fatality-prone.

15850

If you are a believer,
Then immediately I love you.
If you are a doer,
Not only do I love you
But also I need you,
And broadcast and extol you
To the skies.

15851

Because your mind
Is totally empty
Of divine grace,
Your imprisoned life
Is full of disgrace.

15852

Nothing was certain
In my life
Until I met my Lord's
Forgiveness-flooded Feet
And Compassion-flooded Eyes.

15853

O my mind,
I am absolutely certain
That it is not beyond your capacity
To appreciate and admire
My all-loving heart.

15854

When your mind
Is short on sermons
And your heart
Is long on dedication-service,
Then your life is ready
For God's Perfection-Manifestation.

15855

I am so happy
That my heart has now
Two extremely divine friends:
A God-loving tear
And a God-fulfilling smile.

15856

There is only one thing
That I never want to lose:
My heart's perfection-cry
In my Lord's Satisfaction-Smile.

15857

My Lord,
Nowadays You do not fulfil my desires.
Therefore, I am leaving You.
"My child,
Do you not think
That I have the capacity
To haunt you and chase you
And bring you back?"

15858

A sleepless and breathless
God-oneness-heart
Is larger
Than ten thousand climes.

15859

If you can learn
The gratitude-heart-language,
Then God will give you
His own Infinity's Wisdom-Bliss.

15860

Finally,
His mind and vital
Have done away with
Temptation-attachment-emptiness.

15861

Alas,
Why am I so stupid?
Why do I have to be
A prisoner
Of my self-created doubt?

15862

At long last
I have unchained
My anxiety-life.
I am now enjoying
My Lord's
Rainbow-Satisfaction-Smile.

15863

What the world
Desperately needs
Is a sincerity-heart's
Purity-flower-fragrance.

15864

O my mind-child,
Come to me!
Come, leave behind you
Your self-imposed insecurity-torture.
Come, come to me!
I am all for you,
And I am all concern for you.

15865

The doubt-mind
Maliciously, without reason,
Sues the faith-heart
Only to lose the case
Badly.

15866

Last night
My mind's ego-thief
I caught red-handed.
The ego-thief was stealing
My heart-ecstasy.

15867

My Lord,
Do tell me,
What would You like me
To talk about?
"My child,
I wish to hear from you
About your sincerity-mind-cry
And about your purity-heart-smile."

15868

Neither God's Compassion-Eye
Nor I
Can convince my mind
To do the right thing
And pray to God
For sleepless willingness.
Therefore,
My Lord's Compassion-Eye and I
Have begged God's Justice-Light
To come to our assistance.

15869

I have only two questions
To ask God:
Am I pleasing Him
In His own Way?
Is there any way
I can expedite earth-evolution?

15870

God is always ready
To bless us with rewards,
But only when we deserve the rewards
Do we become
Dependable and self-giving
Instruments of His.

15871

What is a human mind?
A human mind is something
That always likes to narrate
The story of God's
Excessive Pride in it.

15872

Do not think
Of yesterday's misfortunes.
Think of today's fortune-smiles,
And think of the day after tomorrow's
God-Satisfaction-Pride
In you.

15873

God does not want us
To take steps
Into the Unknowable.
He wants us only
To be perfect instruments
Of the Knowable.

15874

Wait! Wait! Wait!
Still there is a good chance
That your Lord Supreme
Will enter into your mind
To create God-hunger there.

15875

Today's unhappiness
Must not be kept for removal
Until tomorrow.
Tomorrow will be another day
For me to either suffer new unhappiness
Or enjoy a flood of delight.
Today's game I must play today
Wisely and perfectly.

15876

Go slowly, go steadily,
Go unerringly
Towards your
Destination supreme.
But never go back into your mind's
Unlit uncertainty-cave.

15877

God is extremely unhappy
That man thinks and feels
That he has only fragile ties
With God.

15878

The human mind is cramped
By confusion-night
Because it does not believe in
The fragrance of the heart's
Ever-new life.

15879

O my mind,
Do not give up!
You must continue chasing
Your unruly and wild thoughts.
You must capture them
And tame them.

15880

It is so strange
That the mind allows itself
To be bitten
By the doubt-mosquito.

15881

The human mind
Can also enjoy ecstasy-sea
If it makes friends with faith
The way the human heart
Has established its friendship
With faith.

15882

Only a God-representative
Can sumptuously feed
Your God-hungry heart.

15883

Each time my soul sings
A soul-song,
My life and I enjoy
The fastest Heaven-climbing flights.

15884

Self-hatred
Is a fatal disease.
Even the soul-doctor
Finds it difficult to cure.

15885

Two supreme necessities
Of today's world:
A universal peace-blossomed heart
And the perfection-beauty
Of God's Transcendental Smile.

15886

Nobody wants to hear
Your mind's empty words.
Everyone wants to feel
Your heart's self-offering feelings.

15887

An unwillingness-mind
And a failure-life
Are destined to remain
Inseparable.

15888

No matter how big
Or how small
The ego is,
It will have to surrender
To the vastness-heart.

15889

Once you come out
From behind your mind-barrier,
You can enter into the world
Of silence-ecstasy-dreams.

15890

God will definitely amplify
Your heart's aspiration
And your life's dedication.
He is just waiting
For the right moment
And the right place,
And also for the right audience.

15891

My heart was not born
To be a weeping citizen
Of an indifferent world.
My heart was born
To be a member
Of a God-blossoming
And God-fulfilling world.

15892

How to pass
God's purity-examination?
Just develop more purity
In the mind,
More simplicity
In the life
And more self-giving
In the heart.
You will easily pass
God's purity-examination.

15893

You will come to know of God
Last, very last,
If you allow your doubting mind
To betray your world-oneness-heart.

15894

If your mind is listed
In the doubt-directory,
That marks the final end
Of your life's spiritual journey.

15895

O my mind,
I am tired
Of everything that I do
And everything that I say.
In effect,
I am tired of everything
And of every human being.
When are you going to become tired
Of your intimate friendship
With your ignorance-friend?

15896

It is not at all difficult
To find faith-medicine
In the heart-shop.
Again,
It is not at all difficult
To use it to cure
The mind's doubt-disease.

15897

If your heart has the will
To struggle for
Perfect perfection in life,
I am absolutely sure
That God will bless your heart
With tremendous success.

15898

A simple life
And a pure heart
Are two God-servers
That keep man always happy.

15899

You cannot escape ignorance-attack
On your own.
You can escape ignorance-attack
Only when God's
Unconditional Compassion-Eye
Plays the role of your Saviour.

15900

My Lord
Compassionately and blessingfully
Tells me that
He needs me
Infinitely more than ignorance needs me
Or than I need ignorance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 159, Agni Press, 1991
