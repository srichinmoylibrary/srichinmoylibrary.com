19. Alas!

        Alas!
My brave Divinity-day
Has sunk in the chasm of night.        Alas!
My years have hastened
To their destined end.        Alas!
The Teacher of all teachers,
My inner faith,
Forgets to teach me
        Regularly,
        Divinely
            And
        Supremely.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
