42137

Now we are afraid of fear,
But there shall come a time
When fear will be afraid of us.
How and when?
Let us pray to God to bless us
  With a oneness-will.
This oneness-will will destroy the fear
  That so often attacks us.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 43, Agni Press, 2005
