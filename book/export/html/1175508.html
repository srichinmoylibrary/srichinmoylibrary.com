2560 A Suspicious Mind

A suspicious mind
Is its own punishment.
An auspicious heart
Is its own reward.
A strangling vital
Is its own punishment.
A serving body
Is its own reward.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 26, Agni Press, 1982
