704

Every day my soul visits
My Lord Supreme
And brings down faith-medicine
From His Salvation-Pharmacy
To cure my insincerity-mind,
Impurity-heart
And insecurity-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 8, Agni Press, 1983
