938. Two places to live

An injured lover
Lives in hell.
A doubtful seeker
Lives in hell.
A misunderstood Master
Lives in hell.
A demanding beginner
Lives in hell.
An unsought adviser
Lives in hell.
Let you and me
Live in Heaven.
Where is Heaven?
Heaven is just
Beside hell.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 19, Agni Press, 1973
