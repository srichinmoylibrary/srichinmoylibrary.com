354. You are unknown

You are unknown
To doubt.
Who can be as great
As you are?

You are unknown
To hate.
Who can be as good
As you are?

You are unknown
To failure.
Who can be as brave
As you are?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
