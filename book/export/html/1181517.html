8509 I Once Promised Myself

I once promised myself
That I would not try to escape
Any appropriate punishment
If I misbehaved in my inner life.
Yet my promise remains unfulfilled.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 86, Agni Press, 1983
