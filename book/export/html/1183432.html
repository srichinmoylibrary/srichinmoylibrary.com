296. Fruitful is God-Duty

Hopeful is sunrise.
Thoughtful is moon-beauty.
Soulful is love-purity.
Truthful is life-necessity.
Fruitful is God-Duty
Beyond sunrise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
