85. My first and last commitment

My first and last commitment to man:
I shall love him,
I shall always love him.

My first and last commitment to God:
I shall always love, serve
And fulfil Him
In His own way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
