18600

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Already many times
I have broken Your Heart
With my false promises.
Do give me a new heart
This time to please You,
Only You,
In Your own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 186, Agni Press, 1993
