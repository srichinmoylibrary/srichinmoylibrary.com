307. Nothing uncommon

Frustration:
It is nothing uncommon
To my mind.

Destruction:
It is nothing unnatural
To my vital.

Depression:
It is nothing unusual
To my body.

Consecration:
It is nothing unheard of
To my heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
