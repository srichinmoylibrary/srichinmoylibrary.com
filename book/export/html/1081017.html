86. The night is darkening

The night is darkening
Around my mind.
The day is breaking
Within my heart.
God-Truth is dawning
Inside my life.
God-Light is clearing
My journey's start.

From: Sri Chinmoy, From the Source to the Source, Agni Press, 1974
