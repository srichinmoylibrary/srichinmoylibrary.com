889 My Friends...

MY FRIENDS

Striking Success
Befriends the hero in me.

Illumining Progress
Befriends the seeker in me.

Fulfilling Perfection
Befriends the traveller in me.

Glowing Immortality
Befriends the lover in me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
