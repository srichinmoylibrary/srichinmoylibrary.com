13. No other choice

I have no other choice
Save Yours, my Lord!
I have no other voice
Save Yours, my Lord!
I have no other bondage-night
Save Yours, my Lord!
I have no other freedom-light
Save Yours, my Lord!
I have no other Perfection-Depth
or
Satisfaction-Height
Save Yours, my Lord!

From: Sri Chinmoy, My life-tree, Agni Press, 1975
