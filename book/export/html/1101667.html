12.

My Absolute Lord Supreme
Who, if not You,
Can and will liberate me
From the meshes
Of ignorance-night?
My Lord,
When, if not now
Will You grant me
Your Eternity's Closeness,
Your Infinity's Oneness
And Your Immortality's Fulness?

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord, I pray to You, Agni Press, 1994
