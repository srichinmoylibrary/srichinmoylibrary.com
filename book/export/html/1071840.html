May 30

Sincerity can never disgrace any seeker's aspiration. Humility can never disgrace any man's realisation. Sincerity is the fruit. Aspiration is the tree. Humility is the tree. Realisation is the fruit.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
