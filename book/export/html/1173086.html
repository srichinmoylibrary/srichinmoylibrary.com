162 Eternal Hunger

Eternal hunger, where is it?
In man's aspiring heart?
No!

Eternal hunger, where is it?
In man's soaring soul?
No!

Eternal hunger, where is it?
In God's and man's
Mutual self-giving roles.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 2, Agni Press, 1979
