1. What do I do?

What do I do?
I meditate.
  On whom?
     On God.
  For whom?
     For God.

And what do I do
In my spare time?
  I write love-letters to God.
  I compose love-songs for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
