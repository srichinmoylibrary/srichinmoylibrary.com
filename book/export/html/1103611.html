27 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Please, please never allow me
To act like an unaspiring human being
Who is all the time rushing
And rushing and rushing
With no destination.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

27 July 1999, 1:22 a.m. Before lifting 260 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 520 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
