3673 Your Worried Morning

Your worried morning
Is the result of
The inadequacy of your aspiration-life.

Your defeated evening
Is the result of
Your constantly unwilling attitude
Towards your self-perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
