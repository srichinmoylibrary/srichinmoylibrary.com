292. The door

I saw the Door.
Therefore
God loved me.

I am at the Door.
Therefore
God embraces me.

I shall be the Door.
Therefore
God and I shall claim
Each other
As our very own.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 6, Agni Press, 1974
