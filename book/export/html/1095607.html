31 God Tells My Aspiration-Heart ...

God tells my aspiration-heart
Immediately to devour
All my desires,
For they can at any moment
Attack my aspiration-heart
And destroy it totally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 23, Agni Press, 2004
