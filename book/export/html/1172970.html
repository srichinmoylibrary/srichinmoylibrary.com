48. Slowly and steadily

Slowly the mind
Becomes clean.
Steadily the heart
Becomes pure.
Unconditionally the soul
Manifests the Supreme.
Supremely our Beloved Supreme
Fulfils Himself
In and through His creation-child.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, part 1, Agni Press, 1979
