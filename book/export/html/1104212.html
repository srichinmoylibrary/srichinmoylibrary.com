22 April 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
No matter where I roam,
Your Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Heart
Follow me unconditionally.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 April 2000, 8:37 a.m. Before doing fifty-two one-arm pushes with 700 lbs. (25 right, 27 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 13, Agni Press, 2000
