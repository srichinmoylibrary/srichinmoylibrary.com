616. Love has realised him

Love, true I have not
Realised You,
But
What can I do?

"Child, be happy
That I have realised you
On your behalf.
You are your forgotten Infinity,
You are your remembered nothingness."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
