13 At Every Moment ...

At every moment
We must be careful!
Ruthless and baseless
Worries and anxieties
Invade our lives
When we enter into
Our mind-jungles.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 13, Agni Press, 2002
