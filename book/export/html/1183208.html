72. Why do I need God

Why do I need God?
Just because He is great?
No.
Why do I need God?
Just because He is good?
No.
Why do I need God?
Just because He is for me?
No.
Why do I need God?
Just because He is of me?
Yes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
