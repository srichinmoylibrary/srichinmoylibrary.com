102. Although

Although
My heart is small,
It is for all.
Although
My heart is weak,
It is for the meek.
Although
I have but one choice:
God-manifestation-light,
In my silence-poise,
I give a listening ear to the baseless voices
Of base-animal human demands.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
