1592 Greatness Gives Me

What does greatness give me?
Greatness gives me
The confidence of a lion.

What does goodness give me?
Goodness gives me
The satisfaction of a lamb.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 16, Agni Press, 1981
