Part I — confidence

TQCS 1-20. These twenty poems on confidence were written on 14 December 1981.

1.

Only a self-giving devotee
Can have ecstatic confidence
In the depths of his heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two divine qualities: confidence and sincerity, Agni Press, 1999
