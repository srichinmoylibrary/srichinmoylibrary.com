488. God's partners

God's visible partner:
His birthless Compassion-Light.
God's invisible partner:
His deathless Justice-Height.
Together they give God
Endless duty's
Endless delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
