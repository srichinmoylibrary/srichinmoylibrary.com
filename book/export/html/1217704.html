Classes

Classes by Chinmoy Kumar Ghose on philosophy, spiritual discipline and yoga are being held in New York City each Friday evening at 8:00 p.m.

The series began on Good Friday, April 8, 1966 with the topics of the classes as follows:

April 8 — Man and God

April 15 — Within Us is Our Goal

April 22 — Our Peace is Within

April 29 — Who is Fit for Yoga?

May 6 — The Strength of Surrender

May 13 — Meditation: Individual and Collective

May 20 — Has Your Soul a Special Mission?

May 27 — How Far Are We From Realisation?

Admission is free and all seekers are welcome. Please phone 633-8720 for information regarding location of classes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, AUM -- Vol. 1, No. 9, April 27, 1966, Boro Park Printers -- Brooklyn, N. Y, 1966
