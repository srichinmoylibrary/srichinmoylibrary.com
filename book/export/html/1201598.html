15804

The last time I saw God,
He told me to be careful
Of my mind's complacent life.
He said, "Make friends
With your heart's climbing cries
To make Me happy
And to make yourself fully satisfied."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 159, Agni Press, 1991
