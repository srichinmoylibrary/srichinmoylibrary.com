Brahma Vishnu Shib

Brahma, Vishnu, Shib
Ami achetan jib
Durga, Kamala, Kali
Kothai arghya thali
Kartik, Ganapati
Dao more dhrira mati

Translation:

Brahma, Vishnu, Shib!
An unconscious creatue am I.
Durga, Kamala, Kali!
Where is my aspiration-dedication-bowl?
Kartik, Ganapati!
Do surcharge my mind with firm determination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bela chale jai, , 1979
