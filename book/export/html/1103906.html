22 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To me an ingratitude-mind-arrogance
Is the most frightening nightmare.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 October 1999, 9:48 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 80 lbs. (89 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
