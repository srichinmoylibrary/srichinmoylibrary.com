19. God taught me

Before the first hour
Of my sleep,
God taught me
How to sleep.

During the second hour
Of my sleep,
God taught me
How to wake up.

In the last hour
Of my sleep,
God taught me
How to capture Him,
How to possess Him,
How to treasure Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 3, Agni Press, 1974
