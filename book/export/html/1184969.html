641. You are a dreamer

You are a dreamer,
But that doesn't mean you will become
A self-deceiving thought.

You are a dreamer,
But that doesn't mean you will become
A self-imposed authority.

You are a dreamer,
But that doesn't mean you will become
A God-fulfilling representative.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
