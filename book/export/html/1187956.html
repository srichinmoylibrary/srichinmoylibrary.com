2298

There was a time
When I knew nothing
Except my mind's business hours.
But now I know two things:
My heart's aspiration-hours
And
My life's dedication-hours.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 23, Agni Press, 1984
