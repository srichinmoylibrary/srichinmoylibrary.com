744 The Manager...

THE MANAGER

He knelt.
Therefore
God blessed him.

He wept.
Therefore
God embraced him.

He prayed.
Therefore
God employed him.

He surrendered.
Therefore
God made him the manager
Of His cosmic Factory.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
