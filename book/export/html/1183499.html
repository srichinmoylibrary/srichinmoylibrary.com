363. It never ends

It never ends
My ignorance never ends.

It never begins
My aspiration never begins.

It never cries
My stupidity never cries.

It never smiles
My fear never smiles.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
