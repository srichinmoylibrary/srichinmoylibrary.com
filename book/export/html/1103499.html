2 June 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My God-obedience must never be
Fearful and regretful.
My God-obedience has to be always
Prayerful, soulful, cheerful
And peaceful.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 June 1999, 7:22 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 190 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 6, Agni Press, 1999
