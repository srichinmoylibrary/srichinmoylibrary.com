9442 Your Mind Is Not At All Ready

Your mind is looking
For a shortcut.
Your mind is looking
For an easy way to realisation.
Alas, alas, your mind is not at all ready
For God-realisation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 95, Agni Press, 1983
