6 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please, please accept
My heart's gratitude-tears,
For today You have given me
The key
To open my heart-temple-door
To pray and meditate
Every morning and every evening.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 August 1999, 6:16 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
