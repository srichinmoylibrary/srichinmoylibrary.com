912. Pablo Casals

Pablo Casals:
He lived the life
Of a curious child
In the heart of Mother-nature.

He loved the life
Of a precious child
In the heart of Golden Skies.

He became the achievements
Of a stupendous child
In the heart of infinite Love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
