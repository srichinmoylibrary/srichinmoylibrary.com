124. Your life is all confusion

Do you know
Why your life is all confusion?
Because your life
Is not yours.
You are using a borrowed life.

Do you know
Why your life is all confusion?
Because your life
Is not yours.
Satan claims your life
As his very own.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
