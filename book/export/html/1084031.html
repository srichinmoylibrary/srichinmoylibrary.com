5. Although I sleep

Although I sleep
During my morning meditation,
God appreciates my attempt.
        Therefore
I thank God.

Although I sleep
During my evening meditation,
I am proud of my attempt.
        Therefore
I love myself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
