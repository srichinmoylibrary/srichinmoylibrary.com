Puji Bale Tomar Charan

Puji bale tomar charan
Diye rakta jaba
Hayegelam Mata Kali
Ami je suprabha

Translation:

Mother Kali, I worship You
With Your most favourite flower,
Which is red hibiscus.
Therefore, I have now become
All illumination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
