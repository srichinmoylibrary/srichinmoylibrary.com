178. Let them die

O sweet Lord,
Let my depression-night die
In its first moment
Of life.

O eternal Lord,
Let my frustration-sea die
In its first moment
Of life.

O beloved Lord,
Let my destruction-tornado die
In its first moment
Of life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
