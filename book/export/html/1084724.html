35 Because God...

BECAUSE GOD

You are beautiful
Not because
You love God sincerely,
But because
God loves you unconditionally.
You are divine
Not because
You need God desperately,
But because
God needs you eternally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
