17 December 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day
My mind concentrates to succeed,
My heart meditates to proceed
And
My soul contemplates to feed.
Whom? God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 December 1999, 7:43 a.m. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Before doing sixty-one one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (28 right, 33 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
