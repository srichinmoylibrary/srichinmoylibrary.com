33. Because

           Because
You meditated this morning
You have once more
           Become
Master of yourself today.
Don't forget this divinely adorable discovery.           Because
You smiled this morning
You have once more
           Become
The beloved friend of the whole world.
Don't forget this highly profitable discovery.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
