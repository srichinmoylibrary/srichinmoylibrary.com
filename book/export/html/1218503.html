Pran Gabhire Na Jwalale

Pran gabhire na jwalale bishwa premer bati
Bishwa prabhu nahi habe kabhu amar sathi

Translation:

In the depths of your heart,
If you do not kindle the flame of universal love,
God will never become your Companion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ashar duar, part 1, , 2004
