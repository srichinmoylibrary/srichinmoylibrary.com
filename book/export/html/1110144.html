8.

Father-mother, brother-sister,
Son-daughter of Sri Lanka,
Your soul is
Infinity's compassion-teacher
And your heart is
Eternity's dedication-student.

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Premadasa: Nectar-Bliss-Heart, Lion-Roar-Soul, Agni Press, 1990
