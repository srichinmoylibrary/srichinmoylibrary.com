9740 Purity's Oneness-Light

I have only one prayer,
And that prayer is simple and constant.
What do I pray for?
Purity's oneness-light
Within and without
At each human breath.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 98, Agni Press, 1983
