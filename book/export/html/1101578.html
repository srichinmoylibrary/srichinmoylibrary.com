45. What shall I do?

What shall I do with my leisure-life?
It has shortened my life.
What shall I do with my pleasure-life?
It is killing my life.
What shall I do with my doubt-life?
It has kept my Lord
Away from my life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My life-tree, Agni Press, 1975
