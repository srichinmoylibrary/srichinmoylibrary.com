Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 258

25701

My unconditional surrender-life to God
Is exactly what God has been waiting for
For centuries.

25702

When we are ready to please God
In God's own Way,
He blesses us with a panorama
Of opportunities.

25703

God tells me that
Those who sincerely aspire
Are His Divinity's treasured angels.

25704

It seems that a lifetime of longing
Is absolutely necessary
To liberate us
From our bottomless anxieties.

25705

Meditation
Is the only painkiller
For all our mind-ailments.

25706

God-reliance only
Has the capacity
To clip the flying wings
Of doubt.

25707

When we stop praying and meditating
Altogether,
Self-indulgence-abyss invites us,
And we immediately accept its invitation.

25708

The mind's obstinacy
Shuts the heart-door
Deliberately and ruthlessly.

25709

Oh, where is the God-Reality-Heart-Boat,
Where is it?
I am all eagerness to be a passenger.

25710

What we desperately need
Is a God-realisation-promise
And a lifetime surrender-heart.

25711

O self-infatuated mind,
God's Perfection-Touch
And God's Satisfaction-Smile
Will never be yours!

25712

What is self-doubt,
If not a lengthening chain
Of suffering?

25713

It is the blows of life
That will be embraced
By God's Compassion first.

25714

A cheerful surrender
Is the sunlit path
To the Source.

25715

God cannot close His Eyes
To the fact
That humanity on the whole
Does not care for Him
And does not feel it needs Him.

25716

All the divine blessings
Can easily be found
Inside a gratitude-heart-garden.

25717

You can efface destiny's stamp
On your forehead
If your life-breath becomes
God-reliance.

25718

To me,
God is His beautiful Eye,
God is His peaceful Heart,
God is His blessingful Feet.

25719

If we really want to be happy,
Then we must purify the mind
Of its surprisingly
And astonishingly limited
Understanding-power.

25720

When I have a deep meditation,
God Himself invites me
To be in the company
Of Heavenly stars.

25721

May my life
Be totally immersed
In God's Forgiveness-Fountain.

25722

The gratitude of the heart
Is itself the breath-gratitude
Of life.

25723

Because the mind is insincere
By nature,
Obscurity can come
And powerfully shake hands
With the mind.

25724

I need two things
To gladden my heart:
The beauty of tolerance
And the fragrance of patience.

25725

When all else fails, just repeat:
"My Lord, I am all Yours;
You are all mine."
Lo, you are completely saved
And utterly satisfied.

25726

My Lord, why is our inner journey
So arduous?
"My child, so that you will value
The supreme necessity of your destination:
The Golden Shore."

25727

I tell my Lord
That I am a shattered heart.
My Lord tells me,
"My child, then I am
My blessingful Smile for you."

25728

My Lord,
You know I am not doing at all well.
Forgive me.
"My child, I know, I know.
Just do not give up.
Everything will be all right again
At My choice Hour."

25729

My Lord of Compassion
Every day visits me
To cure my outer blindness
And inner illness.

25730

Unless you dare to say
A categorical "No!"
To your world-frustrations,
Your life is bound to be
Farther than the farthest
From satisfaction.

25731

O seeker, be careful!
At any moment fear and doubt
Can turn your heart-waves
Topsy-turvy.

25732

Nothing can be more accurate
And perfect
Than this realisation:
Today's God-seeker
Is tomorrow's God-discoverer.

25733

Alas, for the mind
The Hour God chooses
Comes and goes by.

25734

A sincere seeker does not allow
God's Compassion
To remain anonymous all the time.

25735

The divinity of my soul
Is my life's temple
And my heart's shrine.

25736

It is, indeed, an extremely difficult task
To cheerfully and proudly climb up
The tower of gratitude.

25737

When the mind speaks endlessly,
It sees nothing,
It feels nothing,
It remains nothing â€”
As usual.

25738

Insanity of the mind
Is the worst possible calamity
In the aspiration-life.

25739

When you are proud,
You can never contain the world.
You can only foolishly
Disdain the world.

25740

Love and devotion
Must cheerfully walk,
March and run
Along the beauty-flooded
Surrender-road.

25741

The doubting mind
Undoubtedly needs a cane,
While the aspiring heart
Desperately needs a plane.

25742

O intellectual mind,
Let us see how long you can remain
Without being dull
And without being gloomy.

25743

The body's austerity
Is nothing short of stupidity.
The heart's intensity
Is all divinity.

25744

O my heart's cheerful journey,
To me you are in no way inferior
To my blessingful Goal.

25745

God laughs hysterically
When I say that I am
Good for nothing.

25746

Do we ever realise
That because of our mind,
We have become virtually
Desire-victims?

25747

My expectation is my foolishness.
My foolishness does not allow me
To see the life-tree
Or its God-satisfaction-fruits.

25748

Illusion has found its home
Inside my mind.
Depression has found its home
Inside my vital.
And I have found my home, only home,
Inside my God-gratitude-heart.

25749

Even a faint-hearted seeker
Deserves my sincere appreciation
And admiration.

25750

Smile, smile as powerfully as possible
To relieve yourself
Of your mental tension.

25751

No human being
Is forever doomed
To swim in the sea
Of ignorance-night.

25752

May my heart's aspiration-train
And my soul's realisation-plane
Forever remain unstoppable.

25753

How I wish my doubting mind
To be pronounced guilty
And sentenced!

25754

The beauty of God's Compassion-Eye
Is now inexplicable,
But one day it will become
Quite transparent.

25755

If you develop harmony with all,
Then yours will be a heart
Totally free from sorrow.

25756

Everything you must transcend
If you want to become a God-fulfiller
And not just a well-wisher of God.

25757

God's Compassion has always been
The source of my aspiration-heart
And dedication-life.

25758

My Lord of Compassion,
I have finally decided
I shall not hide from You
And I shall not run away from You
Anymore.

25759

Never say good-bye
To the God-dreamers
Of peace, light and bliss.

25760

As earthly tears
Are irreplaceable,
Even so Heavenly smiles
Are irresistible.

25761

Do not allow your mind
To crucify your sincerity-heart
And simplicity-life.

25762

Do not give up your spiritual life.
If you do,
Eventually you will become
Heart-rending, flaming tears.

25763

God is God-Infinity's Compassion-Sea.
And what am I?
I am just a few tiny God-drops.

25764

Not my curiosity-mind
But my receptivity-heart
Is the shortest distance
To reach the height
Of the ever-transcending Beyond.

25765

You will be able to travel
In the company of stars
If your mind becomes kindness,
Your heart becomes affection
And your life becomes oneness.

25766

Abandon those thoughts immediately
That do not have the courage
To defend God.
Defending God is the only way
To arrive
At your luminosity-flooded Shore.

25767

My Lord,
Every day You come to me
As a Forgiveness-Fountain.
May I not become
A gratitude-mountain?

25768

What I need
Is an outer rainbow-smile
Of inner delight.

25769

My Lord Supreme,
I do not believe in
Humanity's proud opinion
Of Your Universal Life.

25770

Inside our aspiration-heart
There is always the beauty
And fragrance
Of the Unknowable.

25771

Alas, will my mind ever become
A flower-garden of purity-life
And aspiration-breath?

25772

God's biggest frustration
Is my mind's unwillingness
And my heart's unreadiness.

25773

My Lord of Compassion
Loudly appreciates
The prayer-breath
Of my heart-boat.

25774

O my tricky mind,
What you deserve is a thunder-kick
From my aspiration-heart
And dedication-life.

25775

My Lord Supreme,
I know if I do not obey You
Cheerfully and self-givingly,
Then my life will eventually become
A sorrow-flooded sea.

25776

O twinkling gratitude-moments
Of my heart,
How beautiful, how illumining
And how fulfilling you are!

25777

The human life is a market
Of desire-absurdities.
The divine life is a garden
Of supreme necessities.

25778

May my heart one day rise
To majestic authority
Over my audacity-mind.

25779

God wants each and every heart-train
To be completely full
Before it starts
Its God-Destination-journey.

25780

Not only does the heart know
But also it is heading towards
Immortality's blue-gold Shore.

25781

You want to know what I am doing.
I am just singing and playing
And playing and singing
In my soul's universal nest.

25782

Each dancing drop
Of God's Delight
Is quenching my Eternity's
God-thirst.

25783

God, You are my heart-beauty's
Divinity
And my soul-divinity's
Immortality.

25784

A happy, happier, happiest heart
Is needed
To awaken and mould man's smile.

25785

Every morning when I pray,
I keep my heart humming
The soulful beauty-melodies
Of light, peace and bliss.

25786

My self-indulgence
And
My ingratitude-life
Somehow
Always want to live together.

25787

A rainbow-smile from his heart
Has coloured the face
Of the entire universe.

25788

God's Compassion-Eye descends
To satisfy the hopes
Of countless human beings
In a twinkling.

25789

Your unwilling admiration of God
Is the product
Of your unendingly unillumined mind.

25790

O seeker, do not worry!
Your aspiration-heart-cries
Will eventually untie
The anxiety-worry-knots of your mind.

25791

My morning aspiration-eye
Brightens my inner life
And lightens my outer life.

25792

My mind prefers complexity,
My heart prefers simplicity
And I prefer unity.

25793

God will be hospitable
To your tears
If you are hospitable
To humanity's smiles.

25794

What I need is not
Human God-invention,
But divine God-discovery.

25795

I have decided that
I shall no longer remain
My mind-puppet-slave.

25796

When the divine in us
Fails to come to the fore,
The human in us
Starts disintegrating.

25797

Do not compromise,
If you really want to cherish
The bloom and blossom
Of your freedom-life.

25798

May my heart, my vital
And my body become
A constantly self-giving fountain.

25799

How beautiful and soul-stirring
Are the aspiration-eyes
That glisten with sleepless tears.

25800

The task of a perfect God-lover
Is to tell the whole world what God,
Out of His infinite Bounty,
Does for each human being,
And not what God is or where God is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 258, Agni Press, 1998
