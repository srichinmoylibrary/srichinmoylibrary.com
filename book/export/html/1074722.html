157. Please

O generous earth!
Pray for me, please.

O generous sky!
Think of me, please.

O generous heaven!
Meditate on me, please.

O generous God!
Love me, Love me, Love me, please.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 4, Agni Press, 1973
