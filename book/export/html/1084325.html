44. Earth's power and Heaven's power

A single earth-cry
Can bring down
God's transcendental Height.

A single Heaven-smile
Can illumine
Earth's ignorance-life of millennia.

A single earth-cry
Can empty
Eternity's Infinity.

A single Heaven-smile
Can spread
Divinity's Immortality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 7, Agni Press, 1974
