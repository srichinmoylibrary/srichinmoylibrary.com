More Tumi Karbe Khama

More tumi karbe khama
Ei bharashai achhi
More tumi karbe apan
Taito ami nachi

Translation:

I am alive only with the hope
That one day You will forgive me,
      My Supreme.
One day You will make me Your own;
Therefore, I am dancing.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
