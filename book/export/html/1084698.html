RENUNCIATION

Yesterday
You renounced
Your wild animosity;
Therefore
Heaven is unmistakably proud of you.
Today
You have renounced
Your blind stupidity;
Therefore
Earth is divinely proud of you.
Tomorrow
You will renounce
Your proud necessity;
Therefore
Both God and I
Will be supremely proud of you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
