April 15

How to conquer doubt? Observe the vow of inner silence; do inner meditation and inner selfless service. Your doubt will have no strength to shout at you. It has to die and it will for good.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
