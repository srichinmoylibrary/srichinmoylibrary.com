O my mind,
Are you trying to tell me something
That I do not know as yet?
"Yes! I am trying to tell you
That my very existence
At long last is caught
By God's Compassion-Smile."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 5, Agni Press, 1983
