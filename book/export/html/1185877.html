239

When I was a God-dreamer
My Lord said to me,
"Come, come, it is getting late!"

When I was a God-seeker
My Lord said to me,
"Run, run, I want you
To be on time!"

When I was a God-lover
My Lord said to me,
"Fly, fly, I have so many things
To tell you!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 3, Agni Press, 1983
