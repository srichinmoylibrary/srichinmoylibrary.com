Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 198

19701

O my aspiration-heart,
Are you deaf?
Can you not hear
My Lord Supreme's Compassion-Whispering?
Be wise! Be receptive!
Fulfilment will be yours.

19702

As my aspiration-heart needs
A blazing start,
Even so it needs
A blazing finish
To please my Lord Supreme
In His own Way.

19703

Although I have failed
Thousands of times
To please God in His own Way,
I am determined to continue
And be successful
In this lifetime, here on earth.

19704

Doubt
Is failure-seed.
Fear
Is failure-tree.
Jealousy
Is failure-fruit.

19705

God wants me to be
An aspiration-tower
And not
An expectation-collector.

19706

My Lord Supreme,
Do tell me when I will be able
To make the leap
From my vision-eye
To my action-life.

19707

Only when you become
A doubt-free mind
Can you feel
Your faith-cherished God.

19708

My heart-progress
Does not depend on
My life-success-performance.
My heart-progress
Depends on
My God-surrender-blossom.

19709

I do not want to be
A mind-competent hero.
I want to be
A God-supplicant servitor.

19710

Peace does not depend upon
My body's beauty.
Peace does not depend upon
My mind's brilliance.
Peace depends upon
My heart's fragrance.

19711

My Lord,
How can I feel You more?
"Think of Me as your life-boat.
Think of Me as your life-river.
Think of Me as your life-destination.
You will not only feel Me more,
But also be fulfilled
Far beyond your expectation."

19712

God will be proud
Of watching you.
God will be proud
Of teaching you.
Just become
A sleepless self-giver.

19713

The theoretical God-Eye
I wish to love.
The practical God-life
I wish to practise.

19714

When I use my mind-telephone
To speak to God,
I find God's Line
Is always busy.
When I use my heart-telephone
To speak to God,
Although the line is never busy,
I get no response.
When I use my soul-telephone
To speak to God,
God immediately answers
And both of us enjoy
Our marathon conversation.

19715

God feels sad
Not at my mind's deception
But at my mind's rejection.

19716

Heaven is so sad
That it has to remain
Always hidden
In almost every heart.

19717

The mind is always
On strike against God.
The heart is at times.
The soul is never.

19718

According to God,
An innocence-infant-heart
Is infinitely more important
Than anything else
Or anyone else.

19719

O my doubt-multiplication-mind,
Shame on you!
It is you and nobody else
Who is responsible for
My heart's aspiration-annihilation.

19720

Readiness-road
Is easy to travel.
Eagerness-road
Is easier to travel.
Soulfulness-road
Is the easiest to travel.

19721

O seeker, delay not.
Pray sleeplessly
To bring to the fore
Your inmost soul
To love, serve and illumine the world.

19722

When we aspire,
We come to realise that
There are no such things as
Disabilities and impossibilities.

19723

A true God-lover is destined
To go far beyond
The limitations of his earthly bondage.

19724

God is God's Confidence
Only inside
The aspiring and self-giving
God-seekers.

19725

Sincerity is
God-revelation.
Humility is
God-manifestation.
Divinity is
God-perfection.

19726

Alas,
When will I be able
To cast aside the heavy volumes
Of my mind's earth-bound knowledge?

19727

There are two ways
To reach the destination:
Either by becoming one
With the heart's cries,
Or by becoming one
With the soul's smiles.

19728

Each and every peace-dreamer on earth
Must pray and pray
For peace to wake up
From its long sleep.

19729

At the stroke of my God-Hour
I want to sit
At God's Forgiveness-Feet,
Not be inside
God's Compassion-Eye.

19730

When our determination
Is unshakeable and imperishable,
We shall reach
Our loftiest destination
Without fail.

19731

At the end of my life's bridge
I want to see
The beauty of my self-giving heart
And enjoy
The fragrance of my world-serving life.

19732

In his mind
There is one conviction:
There is no such thing as God.
In his heart
There is one conviction:
There is nothing but God.

19733

An old, haunting pain:
I do not pray to God,
I do not love God
And I do not try to please God
In God's own Way.

19734

How can you trust
The human mind,
Which fluctuates at every moment
From one belief to another?

19735

When we ceaselessly aspire,
Our heart can easily avoid
Insecurity and doubt-invasions.

19736

My Lord,
May the fount of my heart-tears
Never run dry,
For my heart-tears are my only friends
Who will help me feel You and see You.

19737

Freedom is not a dream.
Freedom is a reality
Yet to be revealed and manifested
On earth.

19738

When I pray,
I hear the Sound of the Beyond.
When I meditate,
Not only do I feel, but also I become
The Silence of the Beyond.

19739

Your dream-roads
Will be paved with gold
If you every day feed
Your God-satisfaction-promise
In God's own Way.

19740

May each and every God-loving soul
Start working once again
For the transformation of the human race
And for the perfection of the human face.

19741

My heart,
I have not consciously deceived you,
And you have not deceived me
Even unconsciously.
How is it that we are not
Two oneness-friends
To do God's work together
Here on earth?

19742

We can move forward
To a greater freedom
Only after we have established
Our oneness-peace
In the heart of the world.

19743

The seed of the aspiration-heart
And the fruit of the dedication-life
Can never be separated.

19744

When I pray and meditate,
I discover inside my heart
A unique gift:
Gratitude,
Which I can offer lovingly
And self-givingly
To my Lord Supreme.

19745

My prayer-heart
Has fully blossomed.
My meditation-soul
Has fully manifested itself
On earth
To sing God's all-inspiring Songs.

19746

My Lord Supreme,
May each action of mine
Be a perfect reflection
Of Your Compassion-Eye.

19747

The mind's true obedience-joy
Is nothing other than
The heart's pure oneness-love.

19748

My Lord,
Do give my heart the capacity
To pine for
My nature's transformation
And my life's perfection.

19749

Every day the clarion-call
From my Lord's Heart
Awakens me from my ignorance-sleep
And makes me long for
The all-fulfilling light
Of the inner sun.

19750

A sincere seeker's
Spiritual values
Can never be gotten
At a discount.

19751

Your God-longing cry
Will one day illumine the whole world,
And you will find yourself
At the diamond-centre
Of your aspiration-heart.

19752

There is not a single day
When I do not fight against my mind
With my illumining soul-smiles
And my streaming heart-tears.

19753

Needless to say,
The doubt of the mind is powerful.
But fortunately,
The faith of the heart
Is infinitely more powerful.

19754

The dormant faith
Must come to the fore
And remain alert
So that the dream of the heart
Can be manifested
As the reality of life.

19755

God is always trying
To keep me and my heart
As busy as possible
So that we do not have any time
To think of our doubting mind
And strangling vital.

19756

My prayerful eyes
And my soulful heart
Not only live
But also love
To do God's Will.

19757

The feelings of the heart
Cannot be forced.
The feelings of the heart
Can be awakened,
Can be liberated,
Can be fulfilled.

19758

God's Compassion
Picks up the fallen heart.
God's Satisfaction
Energises the aspiring heart.

19759

My Lord,
The more I bind my life to You,
The sooner I shall enjoy
The freedom of my heart.

19760

The human life
Is nothing other than
Struggling and smuggling.
The divine life
Is nothing other than
Self-giving and God-becoming.

19761

I have peace of mind
Only when my Lord Supreme
Places His Compassion-Feet
Upon my cheerful receptivity-heart.

19762

Love
Turns its radiance-eye,
Peace
Turns its satisfaction-eye
To the aspiration-heart-world.

19763

To serve the aspiring world,
May my eyes twinkle
With my soul's Heavenly concern
And sympathy.

19764

My Lord, do give me
A climbing aspiration-heart
Which will disentangle
My mind's fear-doubt-knot
On the way
To my soul's illumination-heights.

19765

The heart's satisfaction-room
Does not allow any resident
Of the mind's doubt-room
To knock at its door.

19766

A spiritual Master
Comes into the world
Anxious to intensify,
Not to verify,
Humanity's God-eagerness-hunger.

19767

God has made a promise
To my aspiration-heart
That there shall come a time
When His fully-blossomed Peace
Will be within my easy grasp.

19768

If you do not spend
A great deal of your time
Loving, serving and pleasing God,
Then how and why
Should God bestow upon you
His all-fulfilling Delight?

19769

When I do not sincerely aspire,
I see nothing but dark fingerprints
Of insecurity, jealousy, fear and doubt
On my life.

19770

Each human being
Is expert at playing
The old ingratitude-game
Inside his thick mind-forest.

19771

A man of constant self-giving
Is indeed a Heaven-sent boon
To humanity.

19772

Each self-giving breath
Is a direct representative
Of God's Dream-Fulfilment-Reality.

19773

You will never be seized
By a severe doubt-attack
If every day
You lovingly take care of
The faith-plant inside your heart.

19774

There shall come a time
When all feet will proudly tread
The oneness-peace-road.

19775

My aspiration-heart
Is really fond of
My discipline-mind
And my devotion-life.

19776

Just because
My heart's aspiration-dawn
Is blossoming,
My life's desire-day
Is now coming to an end.

19777

O seeker,
You must be always wary
Of your mind,
For it can cleverly thrust
Doubt and suspicion
Upon your aspiration-heart.

19778

If God's Grace is there,
Then your soul's promise
And your heart's satisfaction
Are bound to tread the same path.

19779

The mind's disobedience-nights
Are not only dark
But also careless, callous, ruthless
And destructive.

19780

My heart watches everything
With love and concern.
My mind not only does not want
To watch anything
But also wants to run away
From everything.

19781

In my unwillingness-mind
I always find
The darkest midnight.

19782

Desire-night
And aspiration-day
Always enjoy
Constant rivalry.

19783

May each human heart
Become an incarnation
Of God-gratitude
And God-obedience.

19784

May my life be found
Always
Either inside God's powerful Heart
Or inside His peaceful Breath.

19785

Every day
God loves to paint my heart,
But my heart disappoints Him
With its insecurity
And unwillingness-colours.

19786

The mind gets world-attention
More than it deserves.
The heart does not get even an iota
Of world-attention,
Although it rightly deserves
Infinite attention
From the world.

19787

It is already decreed
That our self-indulgence
Will before long
Destroy us.

19788

I was awe-struck
When I saw
That the goddess of peace
Was alone,
Waiting for me.

19789

God tells me,
"My child, at any time
Feel free to invoke peace.
I shall be always ready
To grant your prayer."

19790

The mind's peace-lamp
Can easily give light
If the aspiration-flame
Inside the heart
Is ready to inspire the mind.

19791

Yesterday my mind indulged
In pomp and ceremony.
Today my mind needs only
Simplicity's beauty and purity's divinity.

19792

God does not agree with me
When I tell Him that
The distance to my life's
Perfection-destination
Is long, very long.

19793

The cosmic gods
Do not charge us
When we register
At God's Compassion-Hotel.

19794

I do not want to see
My heart-friend, faith,
Ever in trouble!

19795

Each purity-breath
Is the fairest bud
In my aspiration-heart-garden.

19796

A mind of sincerity
And a heart of purity
Together
Always win the God-satisfaction-game.

19797

O my mind,
Consciously pray for patience-light.
Otherwise, impatience-dog
At any moment
Can attack you.

19798

I need a heart
That will always be watching
My mind-door
So that no doubt-thief
Can enter.

19799

I shall be able to drink
God's Nectar-Delight
Only when my life is charged
With God-belief.

19800

If you sleeplessly feed
Your heart's sacred aspiration-flame
With your meditation-peace,
Then your entire life will be
The Beauty of God's Eye
And the Lustre of God's Face.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 198, Agni Press, 1994
