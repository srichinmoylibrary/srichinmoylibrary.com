2231 If Your Heart Loves

If your heart loves and loves,
Then you will leave
An inspiring mark
On the world.

If your mind doubts and doubts,
Then the world will leave
A disgusting mark
On you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 23, Agni Press, 1982
