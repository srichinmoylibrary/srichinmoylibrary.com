255. In the soul's world

In the soul's world
He is quite healthy.
He never falls sick.
How?
Because there is no time
For him to be sick.
He keeps busy,
Devotedly busy,
Cheerfully busy,
Unconditionally busy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
