734 God Has Dawned...

GOD HAS DAWNED

God has dawned on my chaos-life;
Therefore,
From now on His cosmos
Shall remain perfection-light,
And my cry has found
A footpath to my world-peace,
My world-bliss.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
