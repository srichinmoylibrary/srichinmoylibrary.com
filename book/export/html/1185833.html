196

An ignorance-lover
Promptly closes his ignorance-eyes.
A God-lover
Not only immediately opens
His aspiration-eyes
But also silently begs God
To secretly open
His Eternity's Eye.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 2, Agni Press, 1983
