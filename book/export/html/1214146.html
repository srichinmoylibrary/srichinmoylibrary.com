750.

When I aspire sincerely and self-givingly,
My heart and I are given the opportunity
To swim in the sea of oneness
And arrive at God's Fulness-Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, part 8, Agni Press, 1996
