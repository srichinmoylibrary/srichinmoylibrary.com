3128 Roles

Once you have played
Your role,
God will, without fail,
Play His Role in you and for you.
He will give you
His transcendental Beatitude
And
His universal Feast.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 32, Agni Press, 1982
