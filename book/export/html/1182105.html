9092 If You Can Always Remember

If you can always remember
The Compassion, Concern and Love
That God has given you,
Then no hostile force can ever stand
Between you and your Beloved Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 91, Agni Press, 1983
