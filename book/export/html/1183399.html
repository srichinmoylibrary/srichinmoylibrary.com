263. He eats

He eats only twice a day.
His morning meal:
God's Compassion-Heart,
God's Meditation-Face.
His evening meal:
God's Perfection-Feet,
God's Satisfaction-Soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
