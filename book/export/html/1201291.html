Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 156

15501

To be lifted
By the Compassion-Forgiveness-Heart
Of my Lord Supreme,
I must increase
Consciously, soulfully and sleeplessly
The breath of my gratitude-heart.

15502

It is so painful
That God,
The Author of all Good,
Is still not acknowledged
By His entire creation!

15503

To recover from ignorance-night,
Make friends with wisdom-light
Consciously, sleeplessly
And self-givingly.

15504

Each time we embody
A soulful thought in our mind,
We ignite
A beautiful and powerful wisdom-flame.

15505

The deeper we dive
With our crying heart,
The higher we fly
With our smiling eye.

15506

If you want success
From your life,
Then pray and pray
Like a helpless orphan.

If you want progress
From your life,
Then meditate and meditate
Like a self-purifying saint.

15507

Thoughts that fight against age,
I need.
Thoughts that fight against imperfection,
I need.
Thoughts that fight against
God's Satisfaction-Smile on earth,
I do not need,
I do not need!

15508

Ultimately,
Only God's Compassion-Eye
Can cure us
From our centuries-old
Ignorance-life.

15509

The absence of inspiration today,
Tomorrow will be
The absence of aspiration,
And the day after, it will be
The absence of dedication.
No sincere seeker's soul
Can do without
Inspiration-mind,
Aspiration-heart
And dedication-life.

15510

If there is no willingness
In your life's
God-manifestation-responsibility,
Then your life is
Meaningless, worthless and useless.

15511

From a smaller-than-the-smallest
Impure thought,
An ignorance-giant can grow
Until it challenges the whole world.

15512

Each purity-breath
Leads us
To an all-illumining
And all-fulfilling horizon.

15513

We must soulfully
And gratefully accept
Each aspiration-flame
From the God-realisation-sun.

15514

The outer action
Without the inner joy
Will invariably lack
The life-fulfilling breath.

15515

The play of ignorance-night
Cannot, will not
And must not
Run for a long time.

15516

Who is extending and expanding
Our mind's ignorance-castle?
Not God the Compassion
But our desire-prince.

15517

Look up soulfully.
Dive deep prayerfully.
There is boundless joy,
And you can have it
For the asking.

15518

There are millions of ways
To satisfy God.
But God wants each of us
To choose only one way
To satisfy Him.
I have chosen
The unconditional life-surrender-way.

15519

At last
I have turned my mind
In a new direction
Where I can see that there is nothing
That cannot be done
To please God and fulfil God,
And thus to please myself
Truly and unmistakably.

15520

Truth-seekers
And self-givers
Are always in short supply.

15521

My mind,
Since you love to challenge,
Can you do me a favour?
Can you challenge
Your own ignorance-pride?
How I wish you could challenge
Your ignorance-pride
And show me your victory's crown.

15522

With my heart's soulful hopes
I am climbing up
Eternity's compassion-flooded
Self-giving-tree.

15523

Is there any mind-jungle
With no doubt-tiger?
No!
There can be no mind-jungle
In any part of the world
Where doubt-tiger
Is not found.

15524

My heart's happiness-bird
Soars
As soon as it hears
A word about God.

15525

God wants you to be proud,
Extremely proud,
Of hoisting your God-oneness-banner.

15526

There is only one way
To conquer your ego
And that way
Is to serve it aspiration-meals
With your humility-eyes,
Humility-mind and humility-heart.

15527

There was a time
When I prayed to God
To be a universal monarch.
But now I am praying to God
To be a universal server.

15528

Each good thought
And each good feeling
Help to decorate
Our heart-haven-room.

15529

God is extremely pleased
With his heart.
Therefore, God has placed on his head
God's divine Satisfaction-Crown.

15530

Do not neglect
Your aspiration-heart,
And do not neglect
Your dedication-life.
God is not going to wait for you
Endlessly!
When the God-Hour strikes,
You will find yourself
In the company
Of most deplorable failures.

15531

God's indifference-life-story
Is invented and written by man,
And nobody else.

15532

You have fractured
Your heart's aspiration-life
By making friends with
Your mind's undying doubt-life.

15533

An uncertainty-life
Certainly leads us
To a frustration-destination.

15534

My heart's gratitude-sunrise
Has cleared away
My mind's thunderstorms.

15535

If you do not revive
Your interest in God once more,
Your life's failure-cry
Will be helpless,
Hopeless and incurable.

15536

My Lord,
Please do me a big favour.
I need you in my life-illumining dreams
More than anywhere else in my life.

15537

To expedite your God-realisation-journey
You must take full advantage
Of every opportunity
That knocks at your heart-door.

15538

A sincerity-cry
Is the result
Of my year-round determination.

15539

To be
A world-class inner player,
Be a cheerful self-giver.

15540

God wants me
To keep Him up-to-date
With my sorrowful stories
Of the outer world
And my cheerful stories
Of the inner world.

15541

If you see a genuine peace-lover,
Then you are bound to see
A world-illumining face.

15542

His new-found shelter
Is inside his aspiration-heart
And no longer
In his division-mind.

15543

Each sincere confession
Feeds and strengthens our inner urge
To pray for our nature's transformation
And our life's satisfaction.

15544

The fragile mind-doubt
Is bound to be shattered
When the abiding peace-eye
Looks at it.

15545

An obscure mind
And murky thoughts:
These are the real impediments
On our way to the Golden Shore.

15546

The love of God
Is not and cannot be
My choice.
It is undoubtedly
His Choice
Operating in and through me.

15547

Every day
My Lord descends
Into my gratitude-heart
To purify and intensify it.

15548

You can forget
All other prayers
If you want to,
But do not forget
Your God-Forgiveness-prayer.

15549

This morning
God came to see you.
To His extreme sorrow,
He saw your world-devouring mind
And not your self-giving heart.

15550

Even God cries loudly
When He sees that humanity's desire-hunger
Is increasing enormously
Every day.

15551

God may forgive
My undivine deeds.
Even my soul may forgive
My misdeeds,
But I don't think I shall be able
To forgive them,
When my aspiration-hunger-eagerness
Is intensified.

15552

Remember the real reason
Why God's Compassion-Eye has left you.
His Compassion-Eye has left you
Only because
You have allowed your mind
To desert your gratitude-heart.

15553

Hope-flowers
Do descend from Above.
But unfortunately,
We do not treasure them.
Alas, we do not even value them.

15554

What I badly need
Is my ancient heart
Of aspiration-flames
To see the divine beauty
Of my God-oneness-life.

15555

My soul sang most powerfully
When my mind was fully awakened
To receive God's Light.

15556

The truth,
As I heard it then,
Was different from now.
God was supposed to come to me, then;
Now, I am supposed to go to Him.

15557

If you want to see
The beauty of Heaven,
Then use God's Eye.
If you want to see
The patience of earth,
Then use God's Heart.

15558

What your heart needs
Is a gentle, mild and delicate Touch
From the Compassion-Eye of God
To revive its sleepless aspiration-hunger.

15559

O God, save me!
Save me from the strangling rope
Of my own insecurity,
Anxiety and impurity.

15560

O my Lord Supreme,
May my mind become
Another obedience-angel,
Like my heart-angel.

15561

My fond memories
Of my Lord Supreme in Heaven
Are still vivid.
And it is these memories
That are sources of inspiration,
Aspiration and dedication-progress.

15562

If there is no devotion
In your meditation,
Then your meditation
Will not be able to reach
The perfection-height.

15563

Be a good inner runner.
You will be able to run away
From temptation-attacks
Easily, effectively and bravely.

15564

When God saw
My aspiration-sunset,
He cried and cried
Like a helpless child.

15565

It is not an uncommon reality.
God-representatives and Heaven-messengers
Are quite often badly misunderstood
By humanity's unlit mind.

15566

When my soul
Does something great for me
And I want to reward my soul,
My soul smilingly says,
"I don't need it!"

When my heart
Does something great for me
And I want to reward my heart,
My heart says,
"I don't expect it!"

When my mind
Does something great for me
And I want to reward my mind,
My mind takes the reward
And immediately demands something more.

15567

Alas!
My dark attachment-life
Has entangled my purity-longing
And world-oneness-longing heart.

15568

True, my past life
Did not surrender to God
And my present life
Is reluctant to surrender to God.
But my future life
Will not only surrender to God
But also promote God-Peace, God-Light
And God-Delight on earth.

15569

Your heart's gratitude-songs
Are so faint
That, sadly,
God fails to hear them.

15570

When my mind enjoys
Doubt-fiction,
My Lord Supreme gets irritated
And quickly disappears.

15571

If there is no enthusiasm
In your mind's God-search,
If there is no intensity
In your heart's surrender-life to God,
Then God-realisation will definitely remain
A far cry.

15572

When God sees my life of love
And my heart of gratitude,
He feels that His Manifestation-Dream
Is worth continuing.

15573

If your mind forgets to value God's Love,
Then you are bound to lose it, someday,
Perhaps forever.

15574

God's Compassion
Comes to inspect
My aspiration-start.

God's Compassion and God's Pride
Come to inspect
My realisation-goal.

15575

Only the willingness-mind
And the soulfulness-heart
Are considered to be God's great warriors
To fight against ignorance-titan
On earth.

15576

Your doubtful mind
And fearful heart
Are taking you away
From God's Compassion-Heart-Nest.

15577

I need my heart's streaming tears
To see the full Beauty
And full Divinity
Of my Lord Supreme.

15578

It is said
That everything is possible.
Today I have got
Firsthand proof of it.
My doubting mind
Has completely surrendered
To my God-loving and God-adoring heart.

15579

With a positive mind
And a positive heart
We are all expected
To play the game
Of world-harmony.

15580

When I place my head
At God's Compassion-Forgiveness-Feet,
He immediately gives me back
My heart's rainbow-smile.

15581

Humanity
And humanity's stupendous
Stupidity-mind
Have made it impossible for God to see
In this world
His Vision's hope-plants.

15582

My soul every day
Breathes in the secret breath
Of my heart's sacred
God-manifestation-promise.

15583

God-realisation-secrets
Are not secrets at all.
God-realisation is an open book,
Open study, open course and open school.
And God the Teacher
Is also sleeplessly open
To the students' requirements.

15584

Every day
My mind is on the lookout
For satisfaction.
But my mind does not realise
That this satisfaction comes
Only when it is empty
Of world-criticism.

15585

My selfless devotion
And God's Perfection-Manifestation:
These two things
Are Eternity's invaluable treasures.

15586

With my Heavenly friends
I walk, march and run
Along Eternity's Road
To manifest my Lord's
Transcendental Vision-Smiles
Here on earth.

15587

The only thing you need
Is a heart that expands,
So God can move in
Happily and proudly.
Everything else is of no consequence.

15588

May my daily aspiration-life
Become friends with
My soul's simple delight-waves.

15589

If you want to become a passenger
In God's Vision-Speedboat,
Then you must become a peace-dreamer,
Peace-receiver and peace-server.

15590

My Lord, my Lord,
You do not have to save me
From anything else
Or from anybody else,
If only You will free me
From my mind-prison!

15591

The invasion of doubt
Into our mind:
Does it ever end?
No!
It never ends,
It never does!

15592

God-lovers are loved and adored
By our God-searching
And God-invoking mind.

15593

You can establish your bond
With the rest of the world
By feeling its heart,
And not by examining its mind.

15594

Can you not see
That you have lost yourself
In your mind's illusion and delusion-sky?

15595

It is simply an impossible task
To escape from the mind's
Suspicion-downpour.

15596

God's Presence
In my mind-cave,
In my heart-room,
In my life-street
I see, I clearly see!
Therefore, I feel that
My God-realisation-days
Are fast approaching.

15597

God offers you
What He has and what He is.
He has infinite Love for you
And He is infinite Concern
For you.
Can you not give Him
What you have and what you are?
You have stupidity
And you are your present unwillingness.

15598

The heart of faith disappears
When the mind of doubt
Starts dancing.

15599

God is expecting every day
A break in my foul mind-weather.
He expects better weather —
Alas, all in vain!

15600

O my heart, do help me.
I want you to be the creator
Of my purity-mind.

O my mind, do help me.
I want you to be the preserver
Of my sincerity-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 156, Agni Press, 1991
