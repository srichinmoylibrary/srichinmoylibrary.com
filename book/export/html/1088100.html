45. Two fools

You are a living fool;
Therefore
Your hopeless fate has brought you
To me
To be my disciple.
I am an undying fool;
Therefore
My helpless fate has brought me
To you
To be your Master.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Immortality's dance, Agni Press, 1974
