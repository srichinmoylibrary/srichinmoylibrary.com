Ogo Prashanta Ogo Ananta

Ogo prashanta ogo ananta
Jago aji prane jago

Translation:

O depths unmeasured,
O Infinity unmeasurable,
Do open your vision-eyes
Within my heart today.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Four hundred blue-green-white-red soul-birds, part 1, , 1986
