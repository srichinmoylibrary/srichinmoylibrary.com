2. Are you not ashamed?

Hurry up, my body!
Are you not ashamed
Of your lethargy-night?

Hurry up, my vital!
Are you not ashamed
Of your aggression-fight?

Hurry up, my mind!
Are you not ashamed
Of your doubt-poison?

Hurry up, my heart!
Are you not ashamed
Of your timidity-insecurity?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, part 1, Agni Press, 1979
