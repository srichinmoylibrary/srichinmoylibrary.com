60. Four universes

What is really huge?
Material universe.
What is really powerful?
Spiritual universe.
What is always in the Goal?
Silent-universe.
What is always the Goal?
Transcending universe.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
