27 April 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I breathe in,
Do allow me to breathe in
   Gratitude-tears.
When I breathe out,
Do allow me to breathe out
   Surrender-smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 8:49 p.m. Before using standing double-arm machine — up to 180 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 210 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
