41. Love

I love God
   Because
I feel that with God's Love
I can create within me
        A new world — a world of beauty,
        A new consciousness,
        A new Kingdom of Heaven.

God loves me
   Precisely because
Without me
He feels He is
        Hopeless,
        Fruitless,
        Useless.
Without me
His existence has no meaning;
   Therefore
God loves me —
The seeker in me,
The lover in me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
