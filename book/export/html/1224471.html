Ke Tumi Karunamoy Jahar Charane Deb

Ke tumi karunamoy
Jahar charane deb debi ankhi
Pulake ghumaye roy
Rabindra shashi tara
Jahate hayeche hara
Jahar parashe guchi jai
Sab shak tap dukhra bhoy

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 6, , 1978
