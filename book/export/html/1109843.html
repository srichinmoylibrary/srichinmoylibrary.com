19. What annoys me

World interference
annoys me.
Heaven-indifference
annoys me.
My mind-preference
annoys me.
My heart-indulgence
annoys me.
My vital-arrogance
annoys me.
My body-complacence
annoys me.
My soul-assurance
annoys me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The prayer of the sky, Agni Press, 1974
