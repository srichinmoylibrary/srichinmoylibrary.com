2.

In the outer life
You make great sacrifices
To build a temple.
Similarly, in the inner life,
You can offer daily
Your inspiration, aspiration
And dedication
To make your body
A perfect temple
Of the Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My heart-temple, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1999
