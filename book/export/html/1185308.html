Tree of life

1.

The tree of life is the whisper of silence.

2.

The tree of life is the pride of sound.

3.

The tree of life is the stairway that leads everywhere.

4.

The tree of life is the miracle of silence, peace, love and oneness.

5.

Self-transcendence is the only goal of the tree of life.

6.

The tree of life smilingly looks into the eye of silence.

7.

The tree of life has unswerving faith in God the Creation.

8.

The tree of life is the hope of the hopeless world.

9.

I am completely lost when my tree of life sheds helpless tears.

10.

The tree of life is dearly loved by each and every praying heart.

11.

The tree of life has teeming surprises for each human life.

12.

The tree of life has freedom-light within and without in abundant measure.

13.

The faith-birds sings atop the tree of life everyday.

14.

On the tree of life God Himself blossoms slowly, steadily and divinely.

15.

The tree of life unconditionally reveals its beauty and fragrance to mankind.

16.

We claim to love the tree of life, but God loves to claim the tree of life.

17.

First and foremost, the tree of life is deeply rooted in truth.

18.

God touches and blesses the tree of life most affectionately and most proudly.

19.

The tree of life is the most beautiful child of God's Heart.

20.

The tree of life inwardly grows and outwardly glows.

21.

Every morning the tree of life awakens my heart and feeds my mind.

22.

The tree of life loves us not just unreservedly but also unconditionally.

23.

The tree of life blesses me with self-discovery-blossoms.

24.

The tree of life accepts all the sorrows of the world lovingly and smilingly.

25.

The tree of life has a permanent place in the self-giving heart.

26.

The tree of life has a free ascent to Heaven.

27.

The tree of life reveals a smile that perfectly satisfies both heaven and earth.

28.

At the foot of the tree of life I have long been a devoted student.

29.

Self-doubt blights the fruits of the tree of life.

30.

The tree of life has long been my guru. It is all illumination for me.

31.

The tree of life chooses silence as its unparalleled entertainment.

32.

The tree of life is extremely sad to watch the world collapsing under the heaviest weight of ignorance-night.

33.

The tree of life garners a succession of surprises for each human being.

34.

The tree of life teaches us how to search desperately for true love, divine love and immortal love.

35.

The tree of life has inexhaustible beauty-blossoms of self-giving.

36.

The tree of life helps us to reach up into the heart's glowing golden horizon.

37.

The tree of life gives us confidence-faith to conquer all doubt-invaders once and for all.

38.

The tree of life always expects a brighter than the brightest future for all human beings.

39.

The tree of life enjoys the highest meditation of ever-deepening and expanding oneness.

40.

Be not a fool. Resist not the blessing-light of the tree of life.

41.

The tree of life asks us not to read the mind-newspapers if we want to make the fastest progress in our aspiration-life.

42.

The tree of life always tries to lead our hearts, like a God-obedience-compass.

43.

The tree of life grows deep inside our oneness-fulness-heart.

44.

The tree of life sees the whole world through the compassion-eyes of a purity-heart.

45.

The tree of life does not allow fear and its shadow to touch our hearts.

46.

The soul-bird sings its songs of eternity alighting upon the tree of life.

47.

The tree of life teaches us to keep our mind's ego-ceilings low, extremely low.

48.

The tree of life is the map that shows us the shortest road to the Source.

49.

The tree of life compassionately teaches us progress-invocation and never teaches us success-intoxication.

50.

Lovingly and affectionately the tree of life encourages us to wake up, jump up, climb up with the all-illumining beauty of the rising sun.

51.

The tree of life is a God-messenger of hope and fulfilment.

52.

The fragrance of the tree of life grows in the beauty of silence-delight.

53.

The tree of life reveals to us that although there are many ways, there is but one Goal.

54.

The tree of life reveals to us that there is only one heart, one universal heart.

55.

The tree of life daily inspires me to offer the Supreme my blossoms, my gratitude-heart-blossoms.

56.

The tree of life tells me that outer association with God is good, but the inner union with God is infinitely better.

57.

The tree of life reveals to me that there is not a single heart-cry that cannot move our Lord's Compassion-Heart.

58.

Meditating at the foot of the tree of life, I am protected and illumined.

59.

The tree of life blossoms with heart-dream-smiles.

60.

The tree of life embodies the fragrance-heart of Mother Earth.

61.

The tree of life is the hermitage of my simplicity-heart.

62.

Every morning the tree of life blesses my meditative form with a gentle rain of love-devotion-surrender blossoms.

63.

The tree of life whispers in the breeze to me that we can truly be happy only when we sing our oneness-heart-songs.

64.

When I look at the tree of life, my aspiration-heart breathes in the vastness of the happiness-sky.

65.

I appreciate, admire, adore and love the soulful moonbeam eyes of the tree of life.

66.

The tree of life self-givingly sings and dances with the beauty of purity-stars, to light up my heart-sky.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Tree of life, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1997
