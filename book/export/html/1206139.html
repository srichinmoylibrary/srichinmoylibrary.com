Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 204

20301

In this century,
God-surrender is strange, unusual,
But never, never impossible!

20302

The unaspiring mind's unwillingness
Is nothing short of
Its worst possible stupidity.

20303

The aggression of the vital
Is not only powerfully negative
But also fruitlessly destructive.

20304

God's Tears
Immediately
Illumine my mind.
God's Smiles
Sooner than at once
Enrich my heart.

20305

My soul has many names.
I admire, adore and love
Its illumination-name
The most.

20306

O my stupid mind,
You have plenty of time
To indulge
In hundreds of indecisions,
But you have no time
For even one
Blossoming and fulfilling decision.

20307

We shall never realise
How much our willingness
And eagerness
Gladden and satisfy God.

20308

The teeming flaws
Of your yesterday
You can correct today
Only if you are planning to complete
Your aspiration-game
In God's own Way.

20309

God has not hidden
His Peace-Nest from us.
We are just not looking for it
In the right place.
The right place
Is our oneness-smile-heart.

20310

God is not asking me
Why I am here.
He is asking me
Why I am still
Unnecessarily delaying.

20311

Although
Spirituality sleeplessly needs
Enlightenment,
It does not have to shun
Child-heart-amusement
On its way to the destination.

20312

Alas, my Lord,
Why do I disappoint You every day?
"My child,
You disappoint Me every day
Because every day you faithfully keep
Your appointment with ignorance-night."

20313

God's Tears will never dry
Unless and until
You offer Him
Your heart's oneness-perfection-smiles.

20314

Each and every
Aspiration-heart-garden
Is bound to bloom,
But only when the God-Hour strikes.

20315

Since there is nothing
Unconquerable,
We must sleeplessly dare
To do the right thing
And be the right person.

20316

You have not dived deep enough.
Therefore you do not see
Your confusion-head
At God's Illumination-Feet.

20317

Everything is bound to go wrong
If you are enamoured of singing
Your own glory-songs
And not God's Victory-Songs.

20318

Ego is powerful,
But not fruitful.
Ego demands and commands,
Only to surrender eventually
To the illumination-soul.

20319

If you do not believe in
Your own prayer-life,
How can you ever thrive
In God's Compassion-Eye
And God's Satisfaction-Heart?

20320

God definitely loves me
No matter what I am doing,
But He will definitely love me
Infinitely more
If He sees me dreaming
Of becoming another God
Like Him.

20321

God wants me to show Him
My heart's aspiration-attempts
And not
My life's perfection-credentials.

20322

The setting sun
Blesses my life
With its purity.
The rising moon
Blesses my heart
With its beauty.

20323

The desire-boat,
The desire-boatman
And the desire-river
Will never be able to arrive
At the Golden Satisfaction-Shore.

20324

God's Satisfaction
Has not given up on me.
God's Compassion
Has not given up on me.
Why then do I have to be
Discouraged and disheartened
And make friends with darkness-negativity?

20325

My Lord,
I do not want to live anymore
In my mind-jungle-town.
Please help me move
Into my heart-garden-village.

20326

Be not afraid
Of the unfamiliar.
Be not afraid
Of the unknown.
Just cultivate your oneness-heart-soil.
You will see that
Nothing can remain unfamiliar
And nothing can remain unknown.

20327

In my outer life
I need my heart's immortal tears,
In my inner life
I need my soul's immortal smiles
To be constantly happy,
Constantly fulfilled.

20328

God is bound to introduce you
To His own new Life
If you just give Him
Your doubt-battered old mind.

20329

Each impurity-thought
Definitely deserves
A life-long exile.

20330

The mind needs
A pin-pointed concentration
To succeed.
The heart needs
A God-oriented dedication
To proceed.

20331

The mind has to change
To become receptive.
The heart has to remain the same
To be constantly productive.

20332

May Heaven's sunrise
Every day feed my outer life
Exactly the way
It feeds my inner breath.

20333

O my mind,
Do not destroy
The God-satisfaction-hope
Of my heart.

20334

An inner experience
Is infinitely more valuable
Than others' lofty wisdom
Thrust upon us.

20335

When sweetness
Shapes our thoughts
And humility
Moulds our lives,
Our inner speed
Becomes extremely fast.

20336

A minute of gratitude
To God
Can offer a man
Not merely hours
But days of joy.

20337

The outer doubting mind
Can either be silenced
By the heart's sleepless cries
Or illumined
By the soul's deathless smiles.

20338

Your spiritual life
Unmistakably ends
When your soul,
Being disappointed in you,
Gives up on you.

20339

God-manifestation
Is bound to bury
Humanity's frustrations and failures
In complete oblivion.

20340

A mind of silence
Is not only
A life of light
But also
A breath of delight.

20341

God-obedience-beauty
And God-obedience-fragrance
Expedite the seeker's journey
Faster than the fastest.

20342

If yours is a self-effacing life,
Then you will undoubtedly
Be the possessor
Of God's supreme Pride.

20343

The unwillingness
Of the mind
And the blossoming
Of the heart's dreams
Will always remain perfect strangers.

20344

The indulgence
Of the vital
And the carelessness
Of the mind
Are taking humanity away
Every day
From God's Compassion-Eye
And Satisfaction-Heart.

20345

My Lord,
You have already saved me
From my cravings
For name and fame.
Now do save me
From my self-importance.

20346

We are all bound
To be misjudged
As long as we allow the mind
To misguide us.

20347

Alas,
I sail my own boat
With my ego-pride.
Yet I expect
God's Protection-Eye.

20348

Aspiration
Is the cry of the heart.
Realisation
Is the smile of the soul.
Perfection
Is the dance of life.

20349

God does not want me to be
Curious all the time.
God does not want me to be
Serious all the time.
But God does want me to be
Spontaneous all the time.

20350

If we cannot have
A tension-free life,
Then we are at the mercy
Of eventual disaster.

20351

If you can resist
Your temptation-vital,
Then you can easily persist
In your aspiration-heart.

20352

Each good thought
Enhances our greatness-height.
Each good action
Enhances our goodness-light.

20353

I feel that my prayer is perfect
Only when I pray to God
Not for His Smile,
Not for His Satisfaction,
Not even for His Compassion,
But only for Him.

20354

God always has
A very marked preference
When He has to choose
Between
My expectation-mind
And my surrender-heart.

20355

Even though
I have many times failed
My God-examination,
God does not want to dismiss me,
For He wants His Compassion-Eye
To succeed eventually
In and through me.

20356

God's Satisfaction
Weeps inside me
Every morning and every evening.
God's Compassion
Does the same.
But God's Patience
Does not surrender to weeping.
His Patience knows
That it is destined to be
The eventual joy-bringer.

20357

The heart that is conscious
Of its incompleteness
Will definitely value
God's ceaseless Compassion
For its Heavenward perfection-journey.

20358

My heart
Has already discovered faith.
But my mind
Wants to invent faith.

20359

Twice God smiles:
Once when I do not want
To escape from my imperfections,
And once when I sincerely strive
For perfection.

20360

May my heart's aspiration-song
Remain endless
And breathless.

20361

Alas, I am helplessly tired
Of my mind's long slavery.
My mind, it is high time
For me to go beyond you
And your doubt-territory.

20362

My heart's prayerful tears
Always help me
To sail with peace-wind.

20363

I can and must thrill God
With my unconditional surrender-life
And gratitude-heart.

20364

If you can implant God's Name
And God's Compassion-Eye
Inside your heart,
You will be able to escape
A doubtful mind
And a hurtful vital.

20365

Pray and meditate
Again and again.
Your inner sun will once more
Come to the fore
And illumine your unlit mind
Once and for all.

20366

May my life-boat ply
Between the beauty
Of my heart-moon-shore
And the power
Of my soul-sun-shore.

20367

You will not be devoured
By shame and guilt
If you just kindle the flames
Of your aspiration-heart
With God's smiling Compassion-Eye.

20368

To revive
Your fading aspiration-heart
And dwindling dedication-life,
You must shed tears of gratitude
Sleeplessly
For God the Forgiveness-Heart.

20369

As long as you cherish
Your self-doubt,
God's Satisfaction-Victory
In and through you
Will remain a far cry.

20370

When the seeker's confidence
Is nothing but arrogance,
His heart loses all its beauty
And his life itself
Becomes futility.

20371

Do not forget that God
And God's Forgiveness-Heart-Fragrance
Live next door
To your aspiration-heart-cries.

20372

A life of surrender
And a heart of gratitude
Are definitely two shortcuts
To arrive at
The Destination supreme.

20373

Desire is not only greedy
But also eager
To devour your sweet surrender
To God's Will.

20374

If you cannot silence the mind,
Then you will always be lost
In the jungle
Of your confusion-thoughts.

20375

Attack ignorance!
Defeat ignorance!
These are the only things
Your soul wants you to accomplish.

20376

You will be allowed to enter
Into the heart-temple
Only when you have
Radically changed your mind.

20377

My soul wants me to accept
And not reject
God's earth-manifestation-Victory.

20378

Since God Himself
Is knocking at your heart-door,
Do let Him come in.
He only wants to make you
Truly, divinely and fruitfully happy.

20379

Do not dismiss
Your simplicity-life,
Purity-mind and divinity-heart
From your life
If you really want to become
The choicest instrument of God.

20380

If you want to please God
In His own Way,
Then you must allow
Simplicity to visit your mind,
Purity to visit your heart,
And surrender to visit your life.

20381

God has hidden
My aspiration-failure-breath
In secrecy supreme.
Alas! How can I ever be worthy
Of His Compassion-Sea-Eye?

20382

My heart's hope
Keeps me alive.
My soul's promise
Keeps me dynamic.

20383

Your mind's negativity
And your heart's creativity
Will always remain perfect strangers
To each other.

20384

Self-praise
Eventually becomes
Helplessly and hopelessly
Joyless.

20385

Be always
Prayerfully and soulfully receptive
To God's sacred Whispers.

20386

Unlike us,
God never finds anything tedious,
Even in our desire-bound life.

20387

When our pride makes us feel
That we are the doers,
And we do not need any help
From the inner world
Or the outer world,
That pride will be forced
To crumble into dust.

20388

If God wishes me to come last
In the Heavenward race,
Then cheerfully I am saying,
"So be it."

20389

If we unconditionally surrender
Our life to God's Will,
Then our destination itself
Will run towards us.

20390

God never wants us to cry.
He is ready to cry
On our behalf
All the time
For our perfection-life.

20391

The unwillingness-mind
And the insecurity-heart
Study in the same
Stupidity-school.

20392

The lethargy of the body
Is the killer of
The heart's enthusiasm-joy.

20393

My soul
Every day teaches my heart
A new and soulful
Tolerance-song.

20394

God will be satisfied with me
And God will be proud of me
Only when I climb up
Every day
My own self-transcendence-peak.

20395

My heart-gratitude
And my life-surrender
Have given me everything â€”
More than I deserve,
Even more than I need.

20396

A peace-dreamer and a self-giver
Are undoubtedly
God's supremely chosen children.

20397

Since every day you are sailing
Against God's Compassion-Current,
How do you even dream of
Happiness in your life?

20398

Today's streaming tears
Will be transformed into
Tomorrow's God-satisfying smiles.

20399

Every day
Each seeker should be determined
To drop impurity-pounds
From his mind.

20400

Nothing can postpone
The inevitable:
God's choice Hour
Can neither be ignored
Nor delayed.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 204, Agni Press, 1994
