41 When I Am In My Mind ...

When I am in my mind,
My mind gives me what it has:
Sighs.
When I am in my heart,
My heart gives me what it is:
Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 10, Agni Press, 2002
