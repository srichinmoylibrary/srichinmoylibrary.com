24341

Casting aside its usual indifference,
If Heaven sleeplessly weeps
For humanity's acceptance
Of God's Will,
Only then will humanity's tears
Be transformed into
Divinity's Smiles.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 244, Agni Press, 1998
