2785 A Special World Of God's

Each temple
Is a special world of God's.
Every day
In the morning,
At noon and in the evening
It broadcasts all the news,
But only about God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 28, Agni Press, 1982
