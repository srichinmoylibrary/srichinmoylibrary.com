Orphan

A child is an orphan
when he loses
his father and mother.
A man is an orphan
when be loses
his inner joy:
his Divine Mother,
and his inner peace:
his Divine Father.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Supreme and his four children: five spiritual dictionaries, Agni Press, 1973
