104 Incongruous

Madal: Chinmoy, my life is short, but my desires are as long as an endless train. My life and my desires are an incongruous pair.

Chinmoy: Madal, since you feel that your life and your desires are not harmonious in character, why not replace your long train of desires with a long train of aspiration? I assure you, God will flood your life with inner and outer harmony.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two brothers: Madal and Chinmoy, part 3, Agni Press, 1982
