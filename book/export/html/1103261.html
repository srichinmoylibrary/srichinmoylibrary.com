10 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Today my heart's God-revelation-hope
And
My life's God-manifestation-promise
Are so happily swimming together
In my soul's God-oneness-river.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Penang, Malaysia. 10 January 1999, 7:18 a.m. Before lifting 230 lbs. twenty-five times with one arm. (10 right, 13 left, plus once each arm to maximum extension)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
