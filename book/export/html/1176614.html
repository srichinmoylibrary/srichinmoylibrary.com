3655 It Can Never Be Too Late

It can never be too late
To free your mind
From the meshes of doubt.

It can never be too late
To free your heart
From the prison of fear.

It can never be too late
To free your life
From the fetters of ignorance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
