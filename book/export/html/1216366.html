732. He lived to make them happy

He lived unrevealed
To see the jealous earth happy.
He lived unmanifested
To see the callous Heaven happy.
He lived for Truth
To make God happy.
He lived for Love
To make himself happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 15, Agni Press, 1974
