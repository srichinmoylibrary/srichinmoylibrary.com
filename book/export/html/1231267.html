Ami Adihin Anta Bihin

Ami adihin anta bihin
Asimer ek bindu
Ami nai ar shudhu nirakar
Dakiche purna indu

Translation:

Beginningless am I and endless am I.
No more do I have form-life.
I am beckoned by the formless,
Infinite inner moon.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, teach me how to surrender, , 1975
