56.

The difference between
My desire-mind
And my aspiration-heart
     Is very simple:
My desire-mind
Is reward-expectation;
My aspiration-heart
Is God-satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-vacation aspiration-prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 2001
