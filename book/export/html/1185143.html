815. Merrily, merrily

Merrily, merrily
I shall spend my life.

Merrily, merrily
I shall enjoy myself in sleep.

Merrily, merrily
Immortal day within me shall grow.

Merrily, merrily
The ancient trees within my heart shall climb.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
