105. Concern-Lord, Compassion-Lord

Your earth-life
Has exhausted
All your cries and sighs;
Therefore
Your Concern-Lord is ready to accept you
As His own, very own.

Your Heaven-life
Has exhausted
All your beauty and divinity;
Therefore
Your Compassion-Lord is ready
To embrace you,
Energise you,
Perfect you
And
Manifest you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
