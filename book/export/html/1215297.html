33.

My Lord Beloved,
I thought You gave me the path
Of sweet, illumining
And fulfilling devotion.
But now I see
That the path You have given me
Is the path of birthless
And deathless frustration.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Volcano-agonies of the seekers, Agni Press, 1995
