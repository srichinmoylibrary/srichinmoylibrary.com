5563

When we do not march alongside
  Our fellow seekers,
God feels that we are not ready
  For the Path.
It is one family.
He wants all the members
To march along together.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 6, Agni Press, 1998
