8 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I soulfully pray,
I see myself seated
Inside God's Dream-Boat
And God Himself is piloting the Boat.
When I sleeplessly meditate,
I see myself singing and smiling
At God's Reality-Shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 September 2000, 9:36 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
