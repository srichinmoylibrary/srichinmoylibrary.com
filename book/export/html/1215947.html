321. My silver key, my golden key

My silver key
Opens the door
Of my wakeful and tearful heart
For God to come in
And my ignorance to go out.

My golden key
Opens the door
Of God's Nectar-Smile
For me to talk to God,
To sit on His Throne,
To become His Crown.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 7, Agni Press, 1974
