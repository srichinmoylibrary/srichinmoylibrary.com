24.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

The joy of my aspiration-heart
Is unimaginable.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

28 January 2005, 7:02 p.m. Huangshan, China. Seated Double-Dumbbell Lift: 1,006 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 18, Agni Press, 2005
