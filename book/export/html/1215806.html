183. Love is no labour

Thought is three-fourths labour.
Hope is half labour.
Joy is a quarter labour.
Love is no labour.

What

Love undertakes,
Love accomplishes
Sooner than at once.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 4, Agni Press, 1974
