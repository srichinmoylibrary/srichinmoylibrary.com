Jatra Pathe Naiba Pelam

Jatra pathe naiba pelam
Tomar prabhu dekha
Bhakti bhare karbo smaran
Tomar charan rekha

Translation:

My Lord, on my way to the Goal,
If I see You not, no harm, no harm, no harm.
With a devotion-heart I shall remember You
      And Your Footsteps.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Swadeshe bideshe ghuriya berai, , 2007
