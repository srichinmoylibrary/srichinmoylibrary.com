4185

The soul is all compassion,
But when it sees
That the mind has become
Total unwillingness
And the heart has become
Total cheerlessness,
Then the soul complains to God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 42, Agni Press, 1984
