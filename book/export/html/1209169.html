Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 234

23301

What I desperately need:
A life that never stops
Self-giving.

23302

When we are in our confusion-mind,
We are totally lost
In our life's blind and endless struggles.

23303

Only the heart's mounting cry
Can hear the Heavenly music
That echoes and re-echoes
In our heart-garden.

23304

The unaspiring mind
Is a self-appointed
World-saviour.
The aspiring heart
Is a God-appointed
World-server.

23305

I may not know when
My God-Hour will strike,
But I do know
That someday, somehow, somewhere
My God-Hour will strike,
And I shall be free, totally free
From my ignorance-bondage-night.

23306

Humility
Is our heart's sweet fragrance,
And this fragrance
Spreads tranquillity and peace
Everywhere.

23307

Eternity's Divinity
And Eternity's Perfection
Abide in the home of Silence.

23308

Because we ignore
God's Warning-Whispers,
We cannot see the new dawn,
We cannot feel the new awakening,
We cannot proceed towards our Source,
The Golden Shore of the Beyond.

23309

Each time I offer my gratitude-heart
To my Lord Beloved Supreme,
I immediately win a one-way trip
To celestial happiness.

23310

May the word "gratitude"
Be found on every page
Of my heart-book.

23311

There are millions of ways
To approach God's Heart,
But the easiest and shortest way
Is the cheerful, self-giving,
Sleepless devotion-way.

23312

Simplicity, sincerity, readiness,
Eagerness and willingness
Can easily terminate
Our age-old lethargy.

23313

As our suspicion-mind
Is the bearer of poison,
Even so our faith-heart
Is the bearer of nectar.

23314

He who loves God
Sleeplessly and breathlessly
Will never tremble
Before God's Thunder-Feet.

23315

We cannot arrive
At our own pinnacle-heights
If we cannot learn to love
The Silence of God.

23316

When we dive deep within,
We discover the wisdom-light
That is ancient
And at the same time new,
Ever-new.

23317

Slowly, steadily and unerringly
My aspiration-heart,
My dedication-life and I
Are arriving at our choice destination:
Satisfaction in perfection,
Perfection in satisfaction.

23318

I finally discovered
My Lord Supreme
In my heart's
Gratitude-village-home.

23319

Our mind's ignorance-exterminator
Is in our heart's aspiration-cry
And nowhere else.

23320

When we do not please
Our Master Lord Supreme
In His own Way,
Our soul suffers the deepest loss.

23321

The very presence
Of a surrender-life
Rings out God's Summit-Victory.

23322

Not the music of the mind,
Not even the music of the heart,
But the music of the soul only
Can liberate our earth-bound life.

23323

God's Compassion
Never stops flowing.
It is our heart's tears
That stop streaming.

23324

When God's Justice-Light descends,
It breaks our unwilling head
And embraces our willing heart.

23325

God never blames me,
Never,
For my uselessness,
But He does blame me
For my ceaseless unwillingness.

23326

How can I ever finish
The Godward race
If my mind
Is wanting in sincerity
And if my heart
Is wanting in purity?

23327

My mind longs
For God's constant Favour.
My heart pines
Only for a glance
From God's Compassion-Eye.

23328

If you are a true seeker,
Then every day God will bless you
Not only with a renewed promise,
But also with an extra supply
Of capacity.

23329

The aspiring heart knows
That there can never be
Any greater miracle
Than God's unconditional
Compassion-Sea.

23330

Challenge you must
Your heart's insecurity,
If you want to overcome it
Once and for all.

23331

O truth-seeker,
O God-lover,
A great celebration awaits you
When you reach the unparalleled
Heaven-summit.

23332

A seeker has only two genuine friends:
His aspiration-cry
And his dedication-smile.

23333

No more shall I use
My present address.
I shall use from now on
A totally new address:
God's Oneness-Heart-Home.

23334

I am definitely proud of
And pleased beyond measure with
My God-dreaming heart.

23335

God Himself
Most eagerly and most proudly
Protects
My heart's climbing aspiration-flames.

23336

Finally my mind's desperation
Has eagerly turned to
My heart's aspiration.

23337

He who prays
A parrot-prayer
Meets with eventual frustration.

23338

If you have a peace-hunger-heart,
Then God's Compassion-Eye
Will never fail you — never!

23339

God is extremely pleased with me,
For today I have made my heart
A sincere God-searchlight.

23340

The smiles of my soul
Are making my life divine.
The tears of my heart
Are making my life perfect.

23341

When desire takes my life
To the land of nowhere,
God blessingfully allows me
To make a U-turn
And arrive at my heart's
Aspiration-home.

23342

From time immemorial
Man has been building temples
To worship God.
But God made only one temple,
And that temple
Will forever and forever stand.
What is that temple?
Man's aspiration-heart.

23343

If our earth is still beautiful and pure,
It is all due to the tears
Of God's loving saints
And God's fulfilling servers.

23344

Everything else may betray you,
Everything else may fool you
And fail you,
But never, never
Your purity-flooded aspiration-heart.

23345

God's most favourite souls
Are those
Who sleeplessly and breathlessly
Pray to Him
To take rest.

23346

God's Compassion
And God's Forgiveness
We shall never be able to escape,
No matter how many ways
We try.

23347

Because I consciously wallow
In the pleasures of ignorance,
True happiness,
Illumining happiness,
Fulfilling happiness
Has left me far behind.

23348

Before my final curtain goes down,
I must once, in all sincerity,
Pray to God:
"Let Thy Will be done."

23349

Whenever I even think
Of making unconditional surrender,
God immediately applauds my resolution
To the skies.

23350

Our minds throng
To humanity's appreciation.
Our souls throng
To God's Compassion.

23351

O seeker,
Never, never, never slacken
In your God-realisation
And God-manifestation-determination.

23352

The depression of the vital
Is an early death.
The aspiration of the heart
Is the longest life.

23353

Finally I have come to realise
That I do not need a mind
To understand God.
What I need is a heart
That is always ready
To love and serve God
And to feel His Compassion-Delight
In His own Way.

23354

O my Lord Supreme,
Do bless me
With a tear-filled voice
That will never stop singing
Your Victory-Songs.

23355

My life's teeming imperfections
Are fading away
Precisely because I am ceaselessly praying
To my Lord Supreme to bless me
So that I can be a perfect instrument
Of His choice Will.

23356

Alas,
How little I know of God.
Alas, alas, alas,
How rarely I want to know
About God.

23357

All the world-conquerors
Have vanished in the dust of time,
But the world-lovers
Will forever and forever
Be cherished and treasured
By Mother-Earth.

23358

He who walks on the path
Of self-importance
Is diving into his own
Abysmal abyss.

23359

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
I want to bow not to You
But to the Illumination-Dust
Of Your Feet.

23360

What is a true prayer?
A true prayer
Is a heart-giving song
To the Lord Supreme.

23361

No sincere prayer
Will ever breathe in vain —
Never, never!

23362

There was a time
When I became a victim
To desire-night.
But now I am a faithful comrade
Of my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life.

23363

Your life has become God-fulness
Precisely because your heart
Is replete with God-thankfulness.

23364

Because of my constant self-offering
To the Will of my Lord Supreme,
I have now won
The fellowship of the immortals.

23365

Each and every aspiration-flame
Of my heart
Is an unparalleled
God-achievement-smile.

23366

When I feel
That I am not of God
And I am not for God,
I become not just a failure,
But total failure incarnate.

23367

A cry of Mother-Earth
And a smile of Father-Heaven
Will forever remain
My best friends.

23368

My soul tells me
That there is only one way
To soar through Eternity's infinite Silence,
And that is through
Conscious and constant self-offering.

23369

I have freed myself
From fate's firm grip
Just by becoming
A God-devotion-magnet.

23370

If your heart is deeply rooted
In humility,
Your life will be blessed
With Divinity's Prosperity.

23371

If you are a God-dreamer,
Then you are at God's Door.
If you are a God-lover,
Then you are inside God's Room.
If you are a God-server,
Lo and behold,
The world sees you conversing
With your Lord Beloved Supreme.

23372

My Lord Beloved Supreme tells me
That I am a beautiful flower
Of His Dream
And I am a most delicious fruit
Of His Reality.

23373

Only when my life is dressed
In silence-peace
Does God allow me to sing for Him
And to dance at His Feet.

23374

When I pray to God
And meditate on God
Happily and self-givingly,
Time proudly lengthens itself
For me.

23375

If it is a true aspiration-heart,
Then it is bound to embody
A series of surprises,
Both earth-illumining
And earth-fulfilling.

23376

My Lord Supreme
Finds my gratitude-heart
Singularly attractive.

23377

Abandon the ancient roads
That wanted to lead you to God
Through self-torture.
Accept the new road of life-acceptance
And God-service in man
To please God
And to please the Real in you.

23378

The breath of my gratitude-heart
Is the abode
Of my Lord's Promise supreme
For world-transformation.

23379

Millions of God-hungry hearts:
Where are they?
They are in God's own
Satisfaction-Heart-Pride.

23380

O my desire-mind
How do you dare to expect
My aspiration-heart
To play with you
On your muddy shore?

23381

God's Compassion-Station
Always invites aspiration
To broadcast its inspiring messages.

23382

Human life will forever remain
Lost in unsolved mysteries
If human life does not aspire
To please and fulfil God
In God's own Way.

23383

Before we entered into the spiritual life,
We had not the slightest idea
What role insecurity plays.
Now that we are in the spiritual life,
We see that insecurity
Is nothing short of a dictator
Who leads us to our destination:
Doom.

23384

There was a time
When I was an unfortunate
God-fearing thought,
But now I am a fortunate
God-loving, God-serving
And God-fulfilling will.

23385

My mind always cries to know.
My heart always cries to feel.
My soul always cries to become
One with God, one in God
And one for God.

23386

You have simply no idea
How much God loves
The beauty and fragrance
Of your heart-smiles.

23387

I have many friends
Here on earth and there in Heaven,
But my beloved soul-bird-friend
Shall forever and forever remain
Unparalleled.

23388

Do you know when my heart
Started praying to God?
My heart started praying to God
Only when my all-loving soul
Pinched my heart to wake up
And see the beauty
Of a new horizon.

23389

My soul is always thrilled
To see God,
No matter how many times a day
God allows my soul
To be in His Presence.

23390

O truth-seeker,
It is high time for you to see
That the race of the mind
Is undoubtedly a backward race.

23391

Although my mind daily,
Without fail,
Discourages my heart,
My heart sleeplessly
Continues to look for God.

23392

"Love everyone":
What does it mean?
It means
Love and love God,
Only God,
In everyone.

23393

My Lord Supreme,
I am praying to You
Not for a towering mind
But for a flowering heart.

23394

The day I do not see
My soul's rainbow-ecstasy,
I feel that day
Is completely lost.

23395

My mind sings many songs
To demand God's Appreciation.
My heart sings only one song —
Surrender —
But that song is treasured
By God Himself.

23396

Whenever I do something
Great and good,
God gives me immediately
A victory-trophy.
Can I not offer God
At least one trophy
To make Him happy?
And what is that trophy?
My cheerful gratitude-heart.

23397

Each aspirant will one day have to sit
For a God-satisfaction-examination.
Each examination is totally different,
But in the end,
The result-destination
Is always the same.

23398

It is never what the mind knows
That counts.
It is ever what the heart gives
That counts.

23399

There was a time
When nothing was able to change
My life-course.
Now, just a peace-drop
Has changed my entire life-course.

23400

God-realisation
For each and every human being
Is as certain
As the breaking of tomorrow's dawn.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 234, Agni Press, 1996
