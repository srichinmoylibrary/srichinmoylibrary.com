19. I need better thoughts

I need better thoughts.
I need the thought that loves God
Unconditionally.
I need the thought that loves man
Soulfully.
I need the thought that loves
The animal kingdom
Carefully.
I need the thought that loves
The plant world
Ceaselessly.
I need the thought that loves
The stone life
Compassionately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
