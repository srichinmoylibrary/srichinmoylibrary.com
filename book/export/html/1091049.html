January 17

Pray with fear. God sheds bitter tears. Pray with love. God smiles with the Beauty of the Golden Dawn. Pray with all that you have. God wings toward you to clasp you. Pray with all that are. God becomes your Liberation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Meditations: food for the soul, Agni Press, 1970
