407. Top secrets

   The biggest secret
Came out of my little mouth:
        God is still alive.   The sweetest secret
Came out of my little mouth:
        God loves me.   The purest secret
Came out of my little mouth:
        God is for all,
            Including me:
            My uselessness,
            My unwillingness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 9, Agni Press, 1973
