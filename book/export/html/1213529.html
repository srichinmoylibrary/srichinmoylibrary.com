139.

Let us go forward
Into the heart of the future
Bravely and self-givingly.
Lo, the Golden Destination-Shore
Is beckoning us.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, part 2, Agni Press, 1996
