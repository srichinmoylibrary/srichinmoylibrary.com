31. Divine friends

An unhurried mind
And
An unworried heart
Are divine friends.
I always enjoy
Their soulful company.

A surrendered life
And
A satisfied soul
Are divine friends
I always enjoy
Their fruitful company.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Vision-Promise, Agni Press, 1974
