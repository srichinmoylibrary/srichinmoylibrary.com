The broken wristwatch

I found a store in Valdivia that had a very peculiar and funny wristwatch. I said, "I will buy it for X."

But the wristwatch did not work. I knew that I could ask Y to fix it, but the man did not want to sell me a broken wristwatch.

-2 January 1987

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
