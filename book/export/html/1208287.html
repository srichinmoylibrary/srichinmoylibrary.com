22427

O my earthly life,
Why are you such a fool?
Why do you still long for more possessions?
Can you not see
That you are already ruthlessly possessed
By possession itself?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 225, Agni Press, 1995
