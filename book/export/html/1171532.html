SCA 1046-1056. In May 1994, Victory-Banner Productions, under the leadership of K, presented Sri Chinmoy's play entitled My Rama Is My All for several weeks in New York. On 12 May, Sri Chinmoy invited members of the cast to ask him questions about various aspects of the play. Stage personae of the questioners are given.

Performance of the play My Rama Is My All

Sri Chinmoy: Most sincerely, I am extolling you all to the skies. This is absolutely by far the best performance that you have done over the years. Now, if the performers want to say anything by way of self-criticism, then I can either agree or disagree.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sri Chinmoy answers, part 31, Agni Press, 2001
