Jani Jete Habe Thaki Bena Keha Bhabe

Jani jete habe thakibena keha bhabe
Jiban ki sab phanki
Maya diye thake dhaki
Jabo chale hasi mukhe bhule jabe sabe
Anmane katobela katanu bhabe
Tomar kajer bhar ke ekhan labhe
Khela phele chale jabo udar nabhe
Jadi balo puna khela khelibo bhabe

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
