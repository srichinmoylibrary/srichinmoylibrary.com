303. Him I gave my all

With eager speed I ran
Towards Heaven to see the Face
Of my Lord Supreme.

With eager cry
Him I saw,
Him I loved,
Him I gave my all:
Material world,
Spiritual world,
My realisation small
And aspiration tall.
My realisation small
And aspiration tall.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
