Question: You have been speaking of faith. Could you make it a bit easier for me?

Sri Chinmoy: Let me try. First of all, faith is not credulity or blind belief. It does not mean that you must constantly believe in the impossible. No, that is not faith. Faith is a spontaneous feeling. It does not care for human justification. It is the eye that visions the future, and it is always in tune with a Higher Truth. The door of faith is always open to the Truth Beyond, and by virtue of faith, we transcend ourselves.

From: Sri Chinmoy, AUM -- Vol. 1, No. 6, January 27, 1966, Boro Park Printers -- Brooklyn, N. Y, 1966
