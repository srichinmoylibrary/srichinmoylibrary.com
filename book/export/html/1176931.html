3969 Tell Your Mind

Tell your mind
What it needs
Is an inspiration-river.

Tell your heart
What it needs
Is an aspiration-fountain.

Tell your life
What it needs
Is a dedication-sky.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 40, Agni Press, 1982
