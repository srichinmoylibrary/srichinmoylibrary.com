ETERNITY'S COLOSSAL FAILURE

You have not obeyed God's Voice;
Therefore
You have failed.

You have not fulfilled God's Choice;
Therefore
You have failed.

You do not love God;
Therefore
Your name is Eternity's Colossal Failure.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
