My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 42

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Pangkor Island and Kuantan, Malaysia in December 2005.

7 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

1 The Outer Appearance ...

The outer appearance
Is not
The real life.

2 The Deeper Existence ...

The deeper existence
Is
The pure and real life.

3 Peace Of Mind Never Comes ...

Peace of mind never comes
By not accepting
The world.

4 Peace Of Mind Comes ...

Peace of mind comes
By loving the world
And by becoming the world.

5 An Unconditional God-Lover ...

An unconditional God-lover
And God-server
Is the real cynosure
Of all human eyes.

6 God’s Pride ...

God’s Pride
Is not
In the mind-book-volumes.

7 God’s Pride ...

God’s Pride
Is
In the heart-booklet.

8 Each Heart Has The Capacity ...

Each heart has the capacity
To be the joy and pride
Of the Universal Life.

9 I Always Love To Be ...

I always love to be
In the audience
And not an actor.

10 God-Actors ...

God-actors
Are born actors
And not man-made.

8 December 2005 ...

8 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

11 Nobody Can Go To God ...

Nobody can go to God
With weak faith.

12 This Moment ...

This moment
My Lord is my heart’s Pilot.
Next moment
My Lord is my life’s Charioteer.

13 I Keep My Curious ...

I keep my curious
And devoted ears
Always very close to God.

14 My Soul, My Heart And I ...

My soul, my heart and I
Every day
Plant hundreds of peace-trees
In my heart-garden.

15 When I Peacefully Sleep ...

When I peacefully sleep,
God lovingly watches
My sleep.

16 When It Is A Matter Of Strictness ...

When it is a matter of strictness,
God does not know
How to calculate His strictness.

17 Anything That Is Divine ...

Anything that is divine
Has to be lived and relived
Indefinitely.

18 I Love My Lord’s ...

I love my Lord’s
Compassion-Assurance
More than anything else.

19 We Are All ...

We are all
Heaven-sent first,
And then become
Earth-born realities.

20 A Life-Boat ...

A life-boat
Without aspiration-sails
Is a very sad
Failure-experience.

21 Ask Not ...

Ask not,
Want not —
Just become
Divinity’s child.

22 When I Look Down ...

When I look down
To see man’s head,
I descend.
When I look up
To see my Lord’s Feet,
My life at once ascends
And transcends.

23 My Lord’s Compassion-Eye ...

My Lord’s Compassion-Eye
Is an antidote
To my faith-starvation-life.

9 December 2005 ...

9 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

24 To Choose Obedience ...

To choose obedience
Is to choose the seeker’s
God-pleasing life.

25 To Choose Disobedience ...

To choose disobedience
Is the self-destruction
Of the spiritual life.

26 I Just Offer ...

I just offer
My God-pleasing willingness.
God does the rest
Most kindly, most affectionately
And most happily.

27 Ego Is Either ...

Ego is either
The world-satisfaction-beggar
Or the world-attention-commander.

28 If You Cherish ...

If you cherish
Wrong and undivine thoughts
In God’s Presence,
Then God can do nothing,
Absolutely nothing, for you.

29 Eagerness Can Easily ...

Eagerness can easily
Cross over the fence
Of the opposition parties.

30 After Joining The Spiritual Path ...

After joining the spiritual path,
If I cannot please my Master,
Then my Lord Supreme gets
The bitterest
Disappointment-experience.

31 Alas ...

Alas,
The outer sun
Has passed away.
To my greatest joy,
My inner sun will not leave me —
Never!

32 This Morning ...

This morning
I smiled and smiled
At my Lord Supreme.
He said to me,
“My child,
Today you have given Me
The most marvellous day
Of My Life!”

10 December 2005 ...

10 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

33 God The Heaven-Founder ...

God the Heaven-Founder
Says to me,
“My child,
Sleep with Me
And dream with Me.”

34 God The Earth-Founder ...

God the earth-Founder
Says to me,
“My child,
Work with Me
And work for Me.”

35 My Ego-Pride ...

My ego-pride
Is totally obsolete.

36 To My Greatest Joy ...

To my greatest joy,
My expectation-construction
Is collapsing.

37 The Fastest Way ...

The fastest way
To arrive at God’s Palace
Is to start with surrender.

38 Although The Mind ...

Although the mind
Is often dry,
It enjoys the gossip-world
Most surprisingly.

39 God’s Most Powerful Utterance ...

God’s most powerful Utterance
God will speak first.
Then my soul and my heart will speak.
Finally my mind will speak
Slowly, quietly and respectfully.

40 God Always Wants ...

God always wants
The heart of purity
To start His Cosmic Play.

41 O My Stupid Mind ...

O my stupid mind,
There is no roundabout way
To fool God.

42 Love God ...

Love God
Sleeplessly.
Sing God-songs for God
Untiringly.
An unimaginable God-receptivity
You will be granted from Above.

13 December 2005 ...

13 December 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

43 Today ...

Today
God’s every Breath
Is blessing my God-longing
Heart-cries.

44 Forever And Forever ...

Forever and forever
I have been
God-Infinity’s dream.

45 Today My Life ...

Today my life
Is forever changed!
How?
My Lord has placed
His Thunder-Feet
On my restless mind,
Wild vital and sleeping body.

46 Like God-Disobedience ...

Like God-disobedience,
God-deception also
Cannot be concealed.

47 God’s Forgiveness-Sun ...

God’s Forgiveness-Sun
Shines equally
For His good and bad children.

48 There Is Not ...

There is not
A single human being
Who does not need
God’s Compassion-Shelter.

49 Who Is Hungry ...

Who is hungry
For God’s Justice-Light?
Not the body,
Not the vital,
Not the mind,
But only the heart
Is ready and hungry
For God’s Justice-Light.

50 I Have Heard ...

I have heard
The Call
Of Infinity’s Silence.

51 A Blossoming Heart ...

A blossoming heart
Is
Exceedingly beautiful.

52 I Want The Mind To Live ...

I want the mind to live
In a desolate,
Thickest and darkest
Forest-night,
Which it rightly deserves.

14 December 2005 ...

14 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

53 Any Time Is The Best Time ...

Any time is the best time
To see God,
To be at God’s Feet
And to talk to God
In the world of silence.
But to realise God,
God chooses a special Hour.
At His choice Hour
We realise Him.

54 My Life’s Most Sacred Place ...

My life’s most sacred place
Is my heart-temple.
It is there,
Where I meet with God,
That I get infinite joy.

55 O Leisure-Class Seekers ...

O leisure-class seekers,
Each time you seek,
God sheds bitter tears.

56 I Sing Early In The Morning ...

I sing early in the morning.
My Lord says,
“Not enough, not enough!
Fly high
In the rainbow-morning-sky.”

57 Keep ...

Keep
Your aspiration-breath
Alive.

58 Keep ...

Keep
Your dedication-muscles
Active.

59 Keep ...

Keep
Your heart-prayers
Soulful.

60 My Joy Knows No Bounds ...

My joy knows no bounds
That today I have re-established
My heart-connection
With my Lord Supreme.

61 Today ...

Today
My Lord’s Love-Call
Is flooded
With Affection-Smiles.

62 When God Hears ...

When God hears
Our gratitude-songs,
He immediately starts
Smiling and dancing.

15 December 2005 ...

15 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

63 I Try To Please God ...

I try to please God
In Heaven.
I try to please God
On earth.
To please God on earth
Is infinitely more difficult
Than to please God in Heaven.

64 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I send an invitation card
To my Lord Supreme.
He reads the card immediately
And says that He is not ready
Nor am I ready
To meet together.

65 One God-Smile Is Enough ...

One God-Smile is enough
To change our life
If we really want it to.

66 Poor God! ...

Poor God!
He has nobody
To whom He can
Unburden His Heart.

67 Heart-Temples Teach Us ...

Heart-temples teach us
How to be pure,
How to be loving
And how to be self-giving.

68 To Be Perfect In Life ...

To be perfect in life
One does not need
A very, very long time.
One does not have to be on earth
Hundreds of years.

69 God Wants A Humility-Heart ...

God wants a humility-heart
To stand first,
Before anybody else.

70 I Spoke To The Mountains ...

I spoke to the mountains.
They told me
That they would immediately
Send me
Their mountain-deep peace.

71 The World-Peace-Lovers ...

The world-peace-lovers
Must accumulate
Their peace-dreams
And place them
At the Feet of God.

72 The Smiling Souls ...

The smiling souls
And the crying hearts
Start their journey together
And end their journey together.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 42, Agni Press, 2006
