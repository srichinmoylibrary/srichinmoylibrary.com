2622 Originality

There is another reason
Why I do not admire mankind.
Most people are afraid of originality.
They do not dare to embrace newness.
For me, originality
Is the blossoming and fulfilling reality
Of tomorrow's dawn,
Which embodies God-Satisfaction
Here on earth
And
There in Heaven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
