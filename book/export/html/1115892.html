2491

As it is true that I have spent
  Most of my life
Away from my Lord,
It is equally true that I shall spend
The rest of my life
  At my Lord’s
Compassion-Forgiveness-Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 3, Agni Press, 1998
