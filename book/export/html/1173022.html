99. Nothing is equal

Nothing is equal
To my God-need.
Nothing is equal
To my God-satisfaction.
My aspiration-world
Is sleeplessly crying
To enter into
My God-realisation-world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, part 1, Agni Press, 1979
