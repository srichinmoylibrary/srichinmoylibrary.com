5253 An Uncritical Friendship

An uncritical friendship:
Did it ever exist?
Does it exist now?
Will it ever exist in the future?
The answers are all unmistakable:
No, no, no!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 53, Agni Press, 1983
