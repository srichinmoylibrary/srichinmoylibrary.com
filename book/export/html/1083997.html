22. I refuse

I refuse to see
The animal face
        Because
It is cruel.

I refuse to see
The human face
        Because
It is ugly.

I refuse to see
The divine face
        Because
It is indifferent.

I refuse to see
The supreme Face
        Because
It is too slow.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
