19915

A deceptive mind
Is not to be revealed.
It has to be concealed first,
And then illumined
By virtue of our prayer and meditation,
For this is the only way
God's Dream-manifestation can take place.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 200, Agni Press, 1994
