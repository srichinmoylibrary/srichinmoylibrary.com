April 8

Oh Aspirant! Your heart's cry is a real treasure. Do not allow your obscure, unlit, discouraging and damaging vital to make light of it. Your heart's cry flies like an eagle to reach the Highest Goal of your purest soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
