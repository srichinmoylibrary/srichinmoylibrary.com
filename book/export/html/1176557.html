3599 Do Try To Be As Beautiful

O my heart,
Do try to be as beautiful
As the quick appearance
Of the rising sun.

O my mind,
Do try to be as beautiful
As the slow disappearance
Of the setting sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 36, Agni Press, 1982
