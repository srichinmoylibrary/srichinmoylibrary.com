303. Because

Because
He went to God
With a timid heart
God did not accept him.

Because
He went to man
With two timid eyes
Man did not accept him.

Because
He went to Heaven
With two timid feet
Heaven did not accept him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
