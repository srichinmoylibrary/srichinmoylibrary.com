Glorious is the path

   Adventurous
Is the path of prayer.
My body and I knew it.   Glorious
Is the path of meditation.
My heart and I know it.   Generous
Is the path of surrender.
My life and I shall know it.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Meditation: humanity's race and Divinity's Grace, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
