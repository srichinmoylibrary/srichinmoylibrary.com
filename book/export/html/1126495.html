13083

My Lord,
Which will be the better for me —
To try or to cry?
“My child,
If you want to try,
Try vehemently.
If you want to cry,
Cry ceaselessly.
Then both of them will be
Of equal importance.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 14, Agni Press, 1999
