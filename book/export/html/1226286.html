My Lord in Silence

My Lord in silence hears
     My helpless cry.
My Lord in silence helps
     My vision fly.
My Lord in silence kills
     My human pride.
My Lord in silence gives
     My heart a ride.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 7a, , 1981
