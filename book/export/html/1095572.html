My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 23

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Solo, Indonesia in November and December 2003.

30 November 2003 ...

30 November 2003

Solo, Indonesia

1 My Aspiration-Heart’s ...

My aspiration-heart’s
Most favourite task:
My God-prayer-tear-cries.

2 Rotating Worries And Anxieties ...

Rotating worries and anxieties —
Alas,
This is what the human mind is.

3 No ‘Why’, No ‘How’ ...

No ‘why’, no ‘how’,
No ‘when’, no ‘where’ —
My life’s God-surrender is.

4 Alas ...

Alas,
When will we realise
That our doubts
Are our mind-made products?

5 My Lord’s Love-Bridge ...

My Lord’s Love-Bridge
Spans my aspiration-cries
And my dedication-smiles.

6 From Now On ...

From now on,
No information-collection-mind,
But aspiration-ascension-heart.

7 Division ...

Division
Is the self-promoter.
Union
Is the God-prompter.

8 The Unaspiring Mind ...

The unaspiring mind
Is an over-hasty
Judgement-pronouncer.

9 When My Self-Dedication ...

When my self-dedication
Is absolutely unconditional,
My aspiration-cry skyrockets.

10 Only The Prayerful Receptivity-Hearts ...

Only the prayerful receptivity-hearts
Are able to watch
God’s hundreds of miracles every day.

11 My Lord Must Come First ...

My Lord must come first.
My Lord must come last as well,
And in between
There must be none.

12 A Sleepless ...

A sleepless,
Self-giving life
Is all sunshine.

1 December 2003 ...

1 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

13 My Lord Supreme Tells Me ...

My Lord Supreme tells me
To practise every day
Forward sprinting,
And never, never
Backward sprinting.

14 My Aspiration-Heart ...

My aspiration-heart
Is determined today
Not to allow the mind
To walk along
The godless shore.

15 My Lord, Do Capture Me! ...

My Lord, do capture me!
Can You not see
Each and every breath of mine
Is for You, only for You?

16 God And I Barter Every Day ...

God and I barter every day
My gratitude-tears
And His Pride-Smiles.

17 My Lord Loves To Hear From Me ...

My Lord loves to hear from me
My heart-songs
For hours and hours.

18 My Lord Has No Time ...

My Lord has no time
To hear even one story
Of my life.

19 Humanity’s Total Transformation ...

Humanity’s total transformation
Is only a local train,
And can never be
An express train.

20 I Devour The Dust ...

I devour the dust
Of my Lord’s Feet.

21 My Lord Devours The Sands ...

My Lord devours the sands
Of my life’s
Never-ending mistakes.

22 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
Be always on the alert!
At every moment
Try to please God in His own Way.
His Compassion-Eye
May not visit you again.

23 My Oneness With The Will ...

My oneness with the Will
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme,
Alas,
Is still at the beginning stage.

24 God Gives Me His Heart-Breath ...

God gives me His Heart-Breath
Far beyond my necessity.
Needless to say,
I deserve it not in the least.

2 December 2003 ...

2 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

25 My Aspiration-Heart ...

My aspiration-heart
Is not brittle.
Therefore,
It cannot be broken.

26 Darkness Cannot Surround Us ...

Darkness cannot surround us
If we can ever develop
True love of God.

27 Although It Is A Most Painful Task ...

Although it is a most painful task,
We must accept
The inevitable.

28 We Must Not Think ...

We must not think
Of escaping our past,
But we must dare
To challenge the past
And go far, far beyond
The domain of the past.

29 Every Day, Early In The Morning ...

Every day, early in the morning,
My Lord Himself enters into
My heart-garden
And collects all my prayerful, soulful
And beautiful heart-blossoms.

30 At Every Moment I Must And Must ...

At every moment I must and must
Soulfully pray to God
To illumine the darkness
Of my ignorance-night.

31 God Tells My Aspiration-Heart ...

God tells my aspiration-heart
Immediately to devour
All my desires,
For they can at any moment
Attack my aspiration-heart
And destroy it totally.

32 May My Enthusiasm-Heart ...

May my enthusiasm-heart
Be not only dauntless,
But also sleepless and breathless.

33 God Calls It ...

God calls it
My delight-heart-fountain.
I call it
My Lord’s unconditional
Compassion and Love-Mountain.

34 If The Tears Of Our Hearts ...

If the tears of our hearts
Are sincere,
Then God will definitely keep us
Inside His Compassion-Protection-
Perfection-Satisfaction-Ring.

35 My God-Obedience-Boat ...

My God-obedience-boat
My Lord always
Blessingfully and proudly keeps
On the right course.

36 A Prayerless Heart ...

A prayerless heart
Is, indeed,
A hopeless and useless life.

3 December 2003 ...

3 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

37 The Gratitude-Heart ...

The gratitude-heart
Is absolutely necessary
To live upon this whole earth.

38 Aspiration-Life Begins ...

Aspiration-life begins
The God-discovery-adventure.

39 Unless I Make My Lord Happy ...

Unless I make my Lord happy,
How can I be really happy?

40 My Heart’s Each New God-Song ...

My heart’s each new God-song
Is a forward step
Towards my Lord’s Heart-Home.

41 I Am My Life’s ...

I am my life’s
Inner tears.
God is His inner Heart’s
Unending smiles.

42 Nothing Is As Charming ...

Nothing is as charming,
Illumining and fulfilling
As my inner boat-ride
With my Lord Supreme.

43 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Is always desperately eager
To keep the genuine seekers happy.

44 With His Own Hands ...

With His own Hands
Our Lord Supreme feeds
His unconditionally surrendered
Children.

45 A Complete Surrender-Life ...

A complete surrender-life
Means a oneness-dance
Of God and the seeker.

46 Aspiration-Life Means ...

Aspiration-life means
Countless
God-fulfilment-opportunities.

47 All My Devotion-Heart-Letters ...

All my devotion-heart-letters
God’s Heart immediately reads
And His Hands immediately fulfil.

48 The Mind ...

The mind
Is the strength
Of only one individual.

49 The Heart ...

The heart
Is the power
Of multitudes.

4 December 2003 ...

4 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

50 I Must Walk, March, Run And Sprint ...

I must walk, march, run and sprint
Behind my Lord Beloved Supreme,
For He knows
Where He is taking me.

51 God Surrenders To Me ...

God surrenders to me.
God says to me,
“My child, I have been leading you
For centuries after centuries.
Now I feel you can lead Me.”
“My Lord,
You must always lead me.
The child has always to follow
The Father.
Please do not break Your Cosmic Law
For me.”

52 Life Needs Courage ...

Life needs courage,
Courage invincible.
Heart needs oneness,
Oneness universal.

53 Eternity Is Within ...

Eternity is within.
Infinity is without.
Immortality is at once
Within and without.

54 Man In General ...

Man in general:
A self-motivated plan.

55 Ultimately, Self-Importance ...

Ultimately, self-importance
Becomes homeless —
Nowhere to go.

56 God Has Only One Message ...

God has only one message
For me:
“Up, up, My child!
To the very top, My child!
Stop not
Until the Goal claims you
And your all.”

57 Do Not Think Unnecessarily ...

Do not think unnecessarily,
Do not think!
Your mind will completely be covered
With ink.

58 God Says To Me ...

God says to me,
“My child,
Seek Me, seek, seek!
Your heart’s tears and cries
Are still very weak.”

59 Alas, Alas, Alas! ...

Alas, alas, alas!
My life knows how to sleep,
My heart knows how to weep,
And I know how to cry and sigh
In vain.

60 I Know, I Know ...

I know, I know
Whom only I owe
For my ignorance-conquest-
Victory-smile:
He is none other than
My Inner Pilot.

61 My Lord’s Heart-Village-Invitation ...

My Lord’s Heart-Village-Invitation
Is the greatest privilege
And deepest delight
Of my life.

62 Who Saddens The Heart ...

Who saddens the Heart
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme?
Not me, not me, not me!
Who gladdens the Heart
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme?
Me, me, me!

5 December 2003 ...

5 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

63 O Foolish Mind ...

O foolish mind,
Do not dare to fight
With the Omnipotence-Silence!

64 Silence Embodies ...

Silence embodies,
Reveals and manifests
God’s Transcendental Dream
Upon earth.

65 Life Is Eternity’s ...

Life is Eternity’s
Silence-journey
Towards Infinity.

66 God Says To Me ...

God says to me,
“My child,
If you are ready to be responsible
For your today’s life,
Then I shall be fully responsible
For your entire future life.”

67 Do Not Try To Inform The World ...

Do not try to inform the world
Who you are.
Just try to reform the world
With the tears of your heart.

68 Each Soulful Prayer ...

Each soulful prayer
Touches God’s Feet.

69 Each Deep Meditation ...

Each deep meditation
Enters into God’s Heart.

70 God Has Made Me ...

God has made me
His Divinity’s partner,
Not because my mind
Is extremely powerful
And not because my heart
Is extremely beautiful,
But because I am of Him
And I am for Him.

71 As Long As My Lord Supreme ...

As long as my Lord Supreme
Knows and sees
That my heart-cries are sincere,
I do not have to prove anything
To the world.

72 Every Day My God-Surrender-Life ...

Every day my God-surrender-life
Has to increase in beauty and purity
If I really want God to love me more
And ever more.

73 When I Think ...

When I think
Of my Lord Supreme,
He is His Power infinite.

74 When I Pray ...

When I pray
To my Beloved Supreme,
He is His Compassion infinite.

75 When I Meditate ...

When I meditate
On my Absolute Supreme,
He is His Satisfaction-Heart infinite.

76 God Is His Immortality’s ...

God is His Immortality’s
Hope-Dream and Promise-Reality
For His full Manifestation
In each human being upon earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 23, Agni Press, 2004
