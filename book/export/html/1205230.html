Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 195

19401

Do not open the heart-door forcibly.
Open it lovingly and carefully;
You are bound to see
Your Inner Pilot
With all His Compassion, Affection
And Benediction.

19402

When I let God
Make the choice for me,
I see the Beauty of Heaven
Replacing the ugliness of earth.

19403

Do not try to expedite
The journey of the heart.
The heart knows better than anybody else
Its capacities
And its destined time of arrival
At the Goal.

19404

My step-by-step
Devotion and surrender-life
Will one day blossom
And become perfectly beautiful.

19405

Why are you not listening to God?
God has so many times asked you
To use your heart's
Surrender-freedom-wings
To fly in the sky of infinite Delight.

19406

If you just step up to a higher level,
You will find that your God-imagination
Will one day be transformed
Into God-realisation.

19407

When God bosses us around,
He does it with utmost Compassion
And utmost Concern -
Only for our perfection-realisation.

19408

My Lord's
First and foremost Command is:
"My child, please Me in Heaven
By fulfilling Me on earth."

19409

How can God possibly fulfil you
When the power of your receptivity-heart
Is almost negligible?

19410

God will not have to punish you
For your misdeeds.
That is your soul's task,
And your soul is bound to do it
In its own unmistakable way.

19411

Unless you change immediately,
Your life will bristle
With darkening, thickening
And life-destroying problems.

19412

My Lord's Compassion
Is always available.
My Lord's Forgiveness
Is always available.
What is not available
Is my gratitude-heart-tears.

19413

When I am in my soul,
I smile and smile
In the illumination-life
Of my soul.
When I am in my heart,
I cry and cry
In the despair-life
Of my soul.

19414

Alas,
My aspiration-room is unfinished,
My dedication-room is unfinished,
My surrender-room is unfinished.
But I am so happy that
My gratitude-room is unfinished,
And I hope my gratitude-room
Always remains the same.

19415

I do not want to remember
My negativity-mind.
I do not want to remember
My futility-life.
I want to remember only
My God-intensity-heart.

19416

Because your God-surrender
And your God-gratitude
Are still a far cry,
Your earth-life will eventually
Swim in a sea of tears.

19417

There can be no unlisted
Heart-number
For God's Compassion-Eye.

19418

The aspiring heart believes in
Only one road,
And that road is
Oneness-happiness-road.

19419

The invisible faith
Is often visible
To our heart's warrior-strength.

19420

Thought-batteries
Always supply the mind
With insignificant and useless
Incidents in life.

19421

The purity of the heart
And the sincerity of the mind
The soul carries
When it enters into
God's universal Oneness-Shrine.

19422

Anything positive
Will finally outlast
Anything negative.
Positivity is a God-made ocean;
Negativity is a man-made wave.

19423

If you treat your ignorance-night
Gently, compassionately and self-givingly,
Yours will be the life of
Quick destruction-arrow.

19424

The soul of each and every religion
Needs God's
All-fulfilling Compassion-Height
And not man's
Tempting ignorance-pleasure.

19425

Despondency can be fatal!
Enthusiasm is a divine spark
Within us
To challenge the pride of ignorance
Around us.

19426

Yesterday is too old.
Do not mix with the old reality.
Enter into today's
World-illumining
And world-fulfilling dream.

19427

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me a very special boon:
I would like to become stuck to You
As to a powerful magnet.

19428

His mind is the lion
Of dauntless power.
His heart is the God-fulfilling
And earth-illumining tower.

19429

If you want to breathe
With your Lord's own Breath,
Then do not breathe inside
The mind-desert
But inside the heart-garden.

19430

There are many keys
To open God's Gate,
But I always prefer my heart's
Unconditional surrender-key.

19431

I do not want my life to be
A play of possibilities
But a play of
God-manifestation-inevitabilities.

19432

Man speaks to God
With his brittle faith-prayers.
Yet he dares to expect
God's immediate Arrival.

19433

My heart's aspiration-life
Is quite often sweet.
My mind's desire-life
Is always bitter.

19434

It seems that
Each unaspiring human being
Has buried himself alive
In ignorance-night.

19435

Divinely beautiful
Is humanity's sincerity.
Supremely fruitful
Is humanity's humility.

19436

Start again with a new heart.
This time your mind's ego-wall
Will definitely crumble,
And your heart's oneness-smile
Will grow and glow.

19437

Every day I practise
My heart's hope-songs.
Therefore I have not given up
On man's ignorance-indulgence.

19438

Unwillingness in a seeker's life
Is by far
The greatest misfortune
And worst failure.

19439

There are innumerable
God-satisfaction-roads,
But I have chosen my heart's
Sleepless gratitude-road.

19440

The mind does not surrender
To God's compassionate Will
Even when the mind's troubles
Are at their worst.

19441

Everything that I have
Inspiring and aspiring
Is due to
God's Patience-Heart-Sea.

19442

My Lord of Compassion
Has followed me far and long.
Now it is my bounden duty
To follow Him in His own Way.

19443

God has asked me time and again
To give my ignorance
To His Compassion-Care.
But alas, I have sadly failed
To please Him in His own Way.

19444

Years and years ago
I made God happy in His own Way.
Alas, what am I doing now?
I am trying in vain
To please myself in my own way.

19445

It is so easy
Either to disgrace my life
With desire-night
Or to grace my life
With aspiration-sun.

19446

God tells me
That His faith in me
Is infinitely stronger
Than my self-doubt.

19447

Walk no more
On yesterday's dream-road,
But walk
On today's reality-road.

19448

Every day I pray to God
To bless my heart
With His Eternity's Hope.

19449

A godless mind
Is where
A heartless life
Abides.

19450

I am so fortunate
That God and God's Patience
Have followed me
So far and so long.

19451

God tolerates all my weaknesses
Except one:
My unwillingness.

19452

Years and years ago
I had the inner strength
To make my Lord Supreme happy.
Alas, I am now following the footpath
That my confusion-mind has chosen.

19453

God eagerly encourages me
To ask Him
Many life-illumining questions
Every day.

19454

If you are a man of a ready heart,
Then all the dreams of your soul
Will be fulfilled
Here on earth.

19455

Alas, I do not know why
My doubting mind,
My strangling vital
And my sleeping body
Are not convicted
By God the Justice-Light.

19456

A thought,
No matter how small it is,
Can easily be
My heart's poise-invader.

19457

God is
God's Fondness-Promise
Only when I am
My oneness-manifestation.

19458

Before you make a home
For God,
God wants you to share
Your compassion
With suffering humanity.

19459

As Heaven
Has most warmly recommended
Your soul to earth,
Even so earth should recommend
Your heart to Heaven.

19460

When it is a question of happiness,
You will find it inside
Your world-service-wisdom.

19461

God invites
A tiny drop of enthusiasm
From me
To become part and parcel
Of His Heart's Ecstasy-Sky.

19462

A reasoning mind
Is forced to play the role
Of a failed character
In God's Cosmic Play.

19463

God made forgiveness enough
For each and every human being,
But alas,
Nobody cares to use it.

19464

My Lord Supreme,
Your Heart of Forgiveness-Concern
Has removed my old human cravings.
My life shall no more fly on wings
Of desire-temptation-frustration.

19465

O my seeker-friend,
Be patient.
Your cloudy inner days
Cannot and will not last
For long.

19466

You can save yourself
From desire-dust
Only by releasing your heart-joy
From your mind-prison.

19467

I don't have to go far,
I don't have to go high,
But I have to go deep within
To satisfy my God-hunger.

19468

For my sake
My mind and I
Constantly want to be great.
For God's sake
My heart and I
Perpetually long to be good.

19469

If we can make
Unconditional surrender to God's Will,
Then all our burdens
Will turn into gains.

19470

God's Forgiveness
And my heart's secret and sacred joy
Together go
To accomplish something divinely great
And supremely good.

19471

Drink, drink
God's Compassion-Light
From His Blessing-Cup!

19472

No peace-traveller
In the aspiration-world
Will ever return home
Empty-handed.

19473

As I love
God's Compassion-Eye,
Even so I love
My gratitude-heart,
For together
They make me complete and perfect.

19474

Each soul
Flies from place to place
With God-manifestation-messages
Every day.

19475

Alas, I have fallen once again
Into my old mistake-life:
I constantly find fault
With the rest of the world.

19476

I shall definitely return
Once more
To my aspiration-heart
From my mind's desire-life.

19477

If you can practise
A life of sympathy and concern,
Then God Himself will sing
His Satisfaction-Song
Inside your heart-garden.

19478

We are completely lost
When we live a God-forgetful life
In spite of our aspiration-heart.

19479

God has no desire to offend you
In any way.
He has only the desire to illumine you
In every possible way.

19480

My heart and I
Every day soar
Far beyond my reasoning mind
To the end of my earthly life.

19481

God's personal Motto is:
"A Self-Transcendence-Life
In a Oneness-Heart."

19482

Knowing that God's Compassion-Eye
Is waiting for you,
Why are you so unwilling -
Even unprepared?

19483

May my unconditional surrender-life
Be always preserved
In the depth of my illumination-soul.

19484

Two things
Do not happen together:
The satisfaction of my desire-life
And the perfection of my aspiration-heart.

19485

We cannot and must not undermine
The sublime strength
Of each simple, God-loving thought.

19486

Beautiful, soulful and fruitful
Is each dream-blossom
Of my heart.

19487

My heart-tears
Will always keep my life's hope
Inspiring and illumining.

19488

Our true education
Comes from self-giving,
And self-giving
Comes from the cheerful acceptance
Of God's earth-illumining
And Heaven-fulfilling Will.

19489

It is not good
To doubt anybody or anything,
Except when it is a matter
Of doubting falsehood
And one's constant incapacities.

19490

I shall no longer roam
With a thirsty mind
And a hungry heart,
For I am climbing high, higher, highest
With my inner aspiration-flames.

19491

Experience is a solid bridge
Between
What our life has already achieved
And what our heart can eventually achieve.

19492

Whenever there is a struggle
Between my doubting mind
And my believing heart,
My wisdom-flooded soul
Always blessingfully and proudly
Takes the side of my heart.

19493

I am shocked twice:
Once when I see no God-eagerness
Inside my heart,
And once again
When I see no God-necessity
Inside my mind.

19494

I am totally lost
Between my mind's
Ignorance-pleasure-hunger
And my heart's
God-satisfaction-hunger.

19495

I want to create
A loving connection
Between my mind and my heart.
Alas, so far there has been none.

19496

O my attachment-mind,
It is you who have taken away
My heart's
Simplicity-sincerity-purity-flooded joy.

19497

It is a deplorable mistake
To think that
Our imagination, inspiration,
Aspiration and realisation
Can work overtime.
Overtime work for them
Is non-existent.

19498

If you cannot derive joy
From your constant God-obedience,
Then yours will be the life
Of ultimate failure.

19499

Two things you must do:
You must pray
For the breath of sincerity
In your searching mind
And for the death of impurity
In your aspiring heart.

19500

My Lord, when You think of me,
Do You know that You look
Beautiful?
My Lord, when You show
Your Compassion to me,
Do you realise that You look
More beautiful?
My Lord, when You forgive me,
Do You realise that You look
Infinitely more beautiful?
My Lord, I am not flattering You.
It is absolutely true.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 195, Agni Press, 1993
