17552

I do not want
To keep any longer
The tortured and fractured mind
Of a thinker.

I want to remain only
Inside the beautiful heart
Of a God-seeker, God-server
And God-lover.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 176, Agni Press, 1993
