10435

He is happy in the morning
When he offers
His heart's aspiring cry
To the rising sun.

He is happy at night
When he offers
His life's dedication-smile
To the beaming moon.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 105, Agni Press, 1986
