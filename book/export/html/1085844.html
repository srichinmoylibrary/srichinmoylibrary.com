71.

If I want to proceed
In my inner life,
Then I need confidence
At every moment.
It is by virtue of my confidence,
Divine confidence,
That I shall be able to make progress
In my inner life,
And this progress is all peace,
Peace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A heart of oneness-peace, Agni Press, 1999
