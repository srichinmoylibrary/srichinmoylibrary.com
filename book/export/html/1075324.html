748 When They Near Their End...

WHEN THEY NEAR THEIR END

When December-life
Nears its end,
Christmas comes.

When Doubt-fight
Nears its end,
Faith blossoms.

When Fear-night
Nears its end,
Courage roars.

When ignorance-sea
Nears its end,
Perfection glows.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
