373. The man of Eternity

   My aspiration-candle
Is the man of the hour.   My realisation-lamp
Is the man of the day.   My revelation-moon
Is the man of the year.   My manifestation-sun
Is the man of Eternity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 8, Agni Press, 1973
