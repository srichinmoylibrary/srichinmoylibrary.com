Amare Bandhiya Rekhechi Tomar

Amare bandhiya rekhechi tomar
Charana kamala tale
He param pita amare chhariya
Jeyona jeyona chale

Translation:

My Lord, I have bound myself
At Your Lotus-Feet.
O my Absolute Lord Supreme,
Do not go away leaving me alone.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eso eso prabhu, part 1, , 1998
