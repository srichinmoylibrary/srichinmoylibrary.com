3629 You Will No Longer Tolerate

Tell your vital
That you will no longer tolerate
Its outrageous demands.

Tell your mind
That you will no longer tolerate
Its poison-doubt.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
