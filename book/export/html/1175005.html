2062 If You Want Me To Think Of You

If you want me to think of you
In your own way,
Then you are
The most worthless human being.

If you want me to think of you
In my own way,
Then you are
A most perfect Truth-seeker
And God-lover.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 21, Agni Press, 1981
