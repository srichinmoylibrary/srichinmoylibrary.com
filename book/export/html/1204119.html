Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 184

18301

My Supreme Lord,
To spread Your Message-Light
All around the globe,
I shall make my heart pure.

18302

Silence I desire
In my vital and in my mind.
Without silence,
I am bound to be
A hopeless failure.

18303

O Beauty infinite,
O Love infinite,
Do come down, come down,
To transform my mind of clay.

18304

I long to fly
Like the birds in the freedom-sky.
No more do I want to remain below
In the attachment-world
In utter helplessness-sighs.

18305

My stupid mind loves
The desire-prison.
Therefore darkness, only darkness,
Within, without, I see.

18306

When I try to satisfy
Everybody's heart,
My soul-bird dances
Ecstasy's dance.

18307

Awake, awake, My Lord, awake!
Awake, awake today.
You I shall worship
With my devotion-heart.
You I shall give
My flower-casket.

18308

Into the world I came,
Myself to offer day and night.
This sweetness-message
I shall proclaim
With infinite delight.

18309

For peace I shall cry,
For peace I shall cry.
Cheerfully I shall bind
My little "I"
At Your Feet divine.

18310

O newness in Heaven,
O newness on earth,
Newness within, newness without,
Come, come, in silence come.
To my Lord Supreme
I desire to offer
My success-victory-light.

18311

In the world of the unknown I roam
In search of the God unknown.
One day I shall see
My Beloved Lord Supreme
In my meditation's deepest depth.

18312

I know, I know,
Ignorance does not want
To leave me.
To cast my ignorance aside,
Therefore,
I cry for the Compassion-Light
Of my Lord Supreme.

18313

Knowing perfectly well
The consequences,
In utter pleasure-life
Teeming mistakes I have made.
Therefore, my Lord,
The Golden Shore
Nowhere I see.

18314

Today I shall place my old life
Inside a cave completely sealed.
Fully blossomed I shall become,
And myself I shall raise
To the highest summits
Inside the dream of the ever-new.

18315

Day and night I roam and roam
With my mind
Inside the thick of the
thought-thorn-forest.
Therefore, I fail to see myself
Inside my own heart's wealth infinite.

18316

Each aspiration-heart
Carries in it
A special dream-fragrance
Of God's Vision-Light.

18317

I am determined
Not to sing anymore
The songs of man's
Imperfection-wrongs.

18318

Today's insincerity-mind
Will be held
In tomorrow's ruthless fear-grip.

18319

O seeker,
Do not stop to look back.
The goal is ahead.
Run as fast as God wants you to!

18320

If we want to reach
The ultimate Goal,
Then we must not allow
Our marathon enthusiasm-fire
Ever to be extinguished.

18321

If you are afraid of impossibility,
Then you can never be enriched
By the plenitude
Of your heart's inner power.

18322

Simplicity, sincerity and purity
Can easily silence
The complication-confusion-mind.

18323

If we wholeheartedly long for
Perfection-life,
Then God will come to us
As His whispering Wisdom-Fountain.

18324

If you want Peace infinite
In your mind,
Then you must pray and meditate
To go beyond, far beyond,
The realm of man-made
Division-thought-boundaries.

18325

An expectation-free commitment
Is undoubtedly the beginning
Of a life's enlightenment.

18326

If you want to be
Unfettered by desires,
Then pray to God
For the daily death
Of your mind's attachment-life.

18327

My heart feels
That there is only one thing dependable
On earth,
And that is God's Compassion-Shower.

18328

A choice God-runner
Smilingly, lovingly and unconditionally
Runs at least one inner marathon
Every day.

18329

Alas,
The tragedy of a complicated mind
Is that it paves the way
To utter suspicion-confusion.

18330

If your meditation-roots
Are not really deep,
Then God's smiling Eyes
And singing Heart
Will not be able to bless you.

18331

In the unhorizoned stillness
Of the mind
God's Perfection-Satisfaction-Divinity
Abides.

18332

I do not think
That there can be anything
Sweeter and more fragrant
Than the sweetness and fragrance
Of inner peace.

18333

The togetherness
Of our hearts
Is the fulness
Of God's Satisfaction-Soul.

18334

Alas,
Most human beings are blinded
By their own
Ingratitude-heart-nights.

18335

The heart's gratitude-journey
Is always beckoned
By the fulness
Of God's Golden Shore.

18336

When your heart and eyes radiate
With love divine,
Infinity's Peace comes and blesses
Your life's aspiration-journey.

18337

Self-doubt is bound to chase you
If God-faith within you
Does not become
Stronger than the strongest.

18338

Remain inside
The aspiration-fountain
If you want to climb up
To the realisation-mountain-summits.

18339

When I do not consciously
And prayerfully aspire,
I become the rise and fall
Of my own heart-kingdom.

18340

My everyday goal
Is to be of service
To every aspiring heart
And every serving life.

18341

What I need is
Deep harmony and sweet peace
To stop the rain
Of non-stop anxiety-blows.

18342

Only a self-giving heart
Has the right
To establish a direct contact
With its all-illumining soul.

18343

Study life's sincerity-lessons
And heart's purity-lessons.
God the Teacher will bless you
With His mighty Satisfaction-Pride.

18344

If you want to proceed
Quickly and perfectly
Towards the destination,
Then look at your life
From your soul's wisdom-height,
And not from your mind's intelligence-plain.

18345

You fool!
God does not want you to choose
The difficult over the easy
To arrive at His Heart-Home.

18346

My mind's newness-beauty
I love.
My heart's fulness-fragrance
I need.

18347

My Lord,
Repeated failures
Cannot paralyse me.
But Your displeasure-Face can.

18348

Each soulful thought of mine
Paves the way
To mankind's perfection-life.

18349

Every day
My gratitude-heart and I
Decorate the Feet
Of our Lord Supreme.

18350

O world's purity-flower-heart,
Where are you?
O world's impurity-thorn-mind,
Where are you not?

18351

No human mind
Can enter into
The thought-free Silence-Beyond.

18352

God I invoke
To visit my self-giving shore.
Me God invites
To His all-blossoming Shore.

18353

With my aspiration-heart
I sit at the Feet of God.
With my dedication-life
I become a member of God's Family.

18354

The whisper of the Silence-Beyond
Awakens humanity
From its death-bound sleep.

18355

A self-giving heart
Is the fastest
Upward climber.

18356

May my soul dream on
Until its dreams
Come perfectly true.

18357

May my soul's dance
Be Heaven's gift to earth.
May my heart's song
Be earth's gift to Heaven.

18358

May I not, my Lord,
Lose Your private phone number.
May I not, my Lord,
Lose Your home address.

18359

May I not receive
Old age
When it arrives for me.

18360

May I unconditionally bow,
My Lord,
To each and every Wish
Of Yours.

18361

May I walk with You,
My Lord,
Willingly, happily and self-givingly
Every morning.

18362

May my aspiration-attempts
Be not only quick,
But also enduring.

18363

May I not be a fool
To miss God's
Morning Meditation-Invitation.

18364

May I remove every day
Stark doubt-daggers
From my mind.

18365

May I always be found
Between the realisation-heart
And the God-manifestation-life.

18366

May I not be fool enough
To ever spring
An unwelcome surprise on God.

18367

May I every morning play
With my aspiration-heart
And its sunrise-smile.

18368

May my heart always remain
Wide open
For Love divine to enter.

18369

There was a time
When the poet in me
Prayerfully desired to roam and roam
Inside my heart-garden.
The poet in me now sleeplessly cries
To clasp the flower-beauty
Of my heart-garden.
And before long, the poet in me
Will meditatively grow into
The nectar-fragrance-delight
Of my heart-garden.

18370

My mind needs
Self-awareness.
My heart needs
God-fulness.

18371

Alas, alas, alas,
I am
My God-disobedience-downfall.

18372

My mind prays to God
For a greatness-life
To conquer the world.
My heart prays to God
For a goodness-life
To love the world.
And I pray to God
For a surrender-breath
To please God in His own Way.

18373

God says to me,
"Come in, My child,
My Heart's Rainbow-Wings
Are waiting for you."
God says to my heart,
"Come in, My child,
My Heart's Rainbow-Wings
Are waiting for you."
God says to my mind,
"I am sorry, My child,
There is no more room."

18374

Supreme, Supreme, my Lord Supreme,
You are lovingly, compassionately,
Untiringly and unconditionally
Preparing me
To be a choice instrument of Yours
For the fulfilment
Of Your Earth-Transformation-Dream.
And what do I do?
Every day I show You
My haughty mind
And give You
My ungrateful heart.

18375

My Lord Supreme, my Lord Supreme,
Please give me a new heart
To treasure Your infinite Tears.
Please give me two new eyes
To reveal Your immortal Smile.
Please give me a new mind
To please You sleeplessly
And breathlessly
In Your own Way.

18376

My Lord, my Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
I came not alone.
I came with Your Infinity's
Compassion-Blessing-Light.
My Lord, my Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
I shall go not alone.
I shall go with Your Immortality's
Perfection-Satisfaction-Delight.

18377

My Lord, my Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
I was Your Life-Infinity's
Vision-Flute-Melody.
My Lord, my Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
I am now Your Heart-Immortality's
Victory-Drum-Delight.

18378

My Lord, there was a time
When I was a spirituality-orphan.
I am now a spirituality-infant.
My Lord, what shall I be next?
"My child,
You will be a spirituality-giant,
And I shall make of you
My hero-warrior
To manifest My Transcendental Dreams
On earth."

18379

My Lord Supreme,
What a beautiful
God-blossoming morning!
Alas, my mind is too feeble
To embody my God-gratitude.
Alas, my heart is too stupid
To accept my Lord's God-Compassion.
Alas, my life is too blind
To see my Lord's God-Perfection.
My Lord Supreme,
Forgive me, forgive me, forgive me!

18380

My Lord, my Lord,
My life was once a long story
Of my mind's
Desire-reality-forest.
My Lord, my Lord,
My life is now a God-loving,
God-serving and God-pleasing song
Of my heart's
Aspiration-dream-garden.
My Lord, my Lord,
My name is now my gratitude-heart.

18381

My Lord Supreme,
Last night You had
A most significant Dream.
In Your Dream You made me
Your most perfect and choicest instrument.
My Lord Supreme,
Last night I, too, had
A most significant dream.
In my dream I clearly saw You happy,
Unimaginably happy,
For the first time in my entire life.

18382

Forgiveness
Is my soul's God-choice.
Gratitude
Is my heart's God-choice.
Surrender
Is my life's God-choice.
Perfection
Is my God-choice.
Self-transcendence
Is my Lord's God-choice.

18383

My Lord Supreme,
How can I realise
Your highest Divinity
And Your transcendental Heights?
"My child,
There is only one way —
The way of love, devotion
And surrender.
You can try other ways,
But you are bound to fail.
Happiness you want?
Mine is the only Way.
Fulfilment you want?
Mine is the only Way."

18384

My Lord, my Lord,
May Your Divinity's Beauty
And Your Immortality's Fragrance
Play hide-and-seek
Inside the aspiration-breath
And dedication-life
Of my Fountain-Art.

18385

My sleepless heart-hunger
Is my golden opportunity-sun
To love, serve and fulfil
My Lord Supreme
In His own Way.

18386

Only the smiling silence-life
Can bring down the Absolute Supreme
In His Transcendence-Delight.

18387

My soul is extremely proud
Of reading my heart's
Surrender-book.

18388

To get a satisfaction-trophy
From God,
Offer God
Your perfection-life.

18389

Who can live on earth
Without feeding his heart's
Hope-hunger?

18390

I went to God
For consolation.
He gave me
Illumination instead.

18391

The height of genuine devotion
Can never be reached
By frustration's arms.

18392

Your heart's silence-peace
Will not permit you
To lie to God.

18393

What I need to know
Is if God will ever
Be pleased with me.

18394

My Lord, my Lord,
Give me a doubt-free mind!
My Lord, my Lord,
Give me a fear-free heart!
My Lord, my Lord,
Give me a worry-free life!

18395

O God the Compassion-Eye,
Do not, do not
From me withdraw!

18396

My Lord, my Lord,
My heart breathlessly needs
To hear from Your Heart.

18397

In a moment's ticking,
God can make me
Another God.
Of course, if He wants to!

18398

My Lord,
Sulking I came to You.
Smiling I am returning.

18399

My Forgiveness-Lord
Is begging me
And commanding me
To steal into His Heart.

18400

I sacrifice
Only what is unreal:
My ignorance-possession.
I keep and I shall always keep
The things that are real:
My love of God,
My oneness with God
And my longing for God-Satisfaction
In God's own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 184, Agni Press, 1993
