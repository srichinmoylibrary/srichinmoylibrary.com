33. Yet God loves us

You are an artist
Who cannot draw;
Yet
God loves you.
Why?
Because you are pure.
He is a singer
Who cannot sing;
Yet
God loves him.
Why?
Because he is humble.
I am a seeker
Who cannot seek;
Yet
God loves me.
Why?
Because I am sincere.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
