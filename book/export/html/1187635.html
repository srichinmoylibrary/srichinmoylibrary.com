1980

How can you maintain purity
Throughout the day?
Just keep a flower with you
And look at it with a soulful smile
As soon as impurity
Tries to assail your mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 20, Agni Press, 1983
