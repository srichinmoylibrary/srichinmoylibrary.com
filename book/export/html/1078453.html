806 Just Wait And See...

JUST WAIT AND SEE

Yesterday
I was the devouring desire of my animal life.
Today
I am the frustrated desire of my human life.
Tomorrow
I shall be a desire-liberated cosmic god.
Just wait and see,
Just see and wait
For your own hour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
