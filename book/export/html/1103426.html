3 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please tell me how I can fly
Far beyond
My life's problem-mountains.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 May 1999, 6:20 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously and 240 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 5, Agni Press, 1999
