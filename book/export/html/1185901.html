263

My sweet Lord,
You are fond of me
Not because I have already become
Great and good,
Not because I shall become
Great and good,
But because You and Your Fondness
For me, Your creation,
Are always inseparable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 3, Agni Press, 1983
