Ai Ai Ai Ai Bela Jai Bela Jai

Ai ai ai ai
Bela jai bela jai
Sudurer sima nai
Jabo prati sandhyai

Translation:

O come, come!
Time is fast passing by.
Every twilight we shall proceed
Towards the Unknowable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 11b, , 1981
