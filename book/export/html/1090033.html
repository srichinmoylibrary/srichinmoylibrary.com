1. Reminders

Earth reminds me
Of my outer duty:
God-dedication.

Heaven reminds me
Of my inner beauty:
God-compassion.

God reminds me
Of my supreme necessity:
God-manifestation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, I ask you for one favour, Agni Press, 1975
