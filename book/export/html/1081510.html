2. A human being's story

To be a human being
Is to have a story.
The story I have
Is the story of my sad
Self-forgetfulness.

To be a human being
Is to be a story.
The story I am
Is the story of my glad
God-forgiveness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giving and becoming, Agni Press, 1974
