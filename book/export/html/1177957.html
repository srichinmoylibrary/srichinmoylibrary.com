4985 I Shall Feel Sad

My Lord Supreme,
I shall not feel sad if Your Compassion-Sky
Does not care for my heart's
Iota of aspiration.
But I shall definitely and sleeplessly feel sad
If Your Forgiveness-Sun
Does not care for my life's
Himalayan blunders.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 50, Agni Press, 1982
