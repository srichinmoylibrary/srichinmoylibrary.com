26316

I prayed
To the goddess of success.
I was at times successful and at times not.
Now I am praying
To the goddess of progress.
My very prayer gives me utmost satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 264, Agni Press, 1998
