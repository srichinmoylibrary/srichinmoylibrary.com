4.

My Supreme Lord,
When You make me happy
In my own way,
I feel that I have achieved
Something great.

My Supreme Lord,
When I make You happy
In Your own Way
What happens to You?

"My child,
I feel that I have made
Tremendous progress,
And I also feel that the Hour
Has struck for Me
To dream of a better, higher
And deeper creation
In and through you."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Morning prayers and poems on war, Agni Press, 1992
