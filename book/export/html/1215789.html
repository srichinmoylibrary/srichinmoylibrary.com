166. An education

What is defeat?
Nothing but a continuing education.

What is victory?
Nothing but an inspiring education.

What is progress?
Nothing but a fulfilling education.

What is perfection?
Nothing but a blossomed education.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 4, Agni Press, 1974
