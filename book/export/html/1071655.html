Dolphin: Sagacity

Dolphin, my dolphin,
Your advanced sagacity
Denies your inferiority
To forest animals.
You make man feel that your consciousness
Borders upon the extremity of human life
With ceaseless strife.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Animal Kingdom, Agni Press, 1973
