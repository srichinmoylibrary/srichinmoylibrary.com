855 I Am Tired, I Am Ready...

I AM TIRED, I AM READY

I am tired.
I am ready
To surrender to Ignorance.
But my Lord goes on
Asserting my cause.

I am fatigued.
I am ready
To surrender to Earth.
But my Lord goes on
Asserting my cause.

I am exhausted.
I am ready
To surrender to Heaven.
But my Lord goes on
Asserting my cause.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
