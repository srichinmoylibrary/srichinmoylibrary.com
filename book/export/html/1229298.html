Aro Byatha Keman Kare (2)

Aro byatha keman kare sahi balo mago
Tumi jadi hiya majhe kabu nahi jago
Jani amar dukher nishar habe abasan
Twara eso mago tomai dake chapal pran

Translation:

Mother, tell me,
How can I bear more pain?
If you never awake
Within my heart
I know the night of my sufferings
Will not come to an end.
O Mother, come quickly.
My impatient heart calls you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Patience-groves, , 1978
