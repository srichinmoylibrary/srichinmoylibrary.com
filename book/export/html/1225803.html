Phabhu Tumi Amar Shesh Ashroy Tumi

Prabhu tumi amar
Shesh ashroy
Tumi amar ghar
Ami tomate hoi amar
Tomar dhyane tomar gyane
Khele ananda lahar
Tomare pele kichu harabona
Harale toma pabar mato
Kichui pabona
Tumi amar ashroy
ami tomate hoi amar

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 13, , 1981
