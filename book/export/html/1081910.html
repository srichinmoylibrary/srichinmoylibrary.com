351. Bridges

My soul is the bridge
Between
Earth's abiding cry
And
Heaven's lasting Smile.

My heart is the bridge
Between
Earth's persistent failures
And
Heaven's constant success.

My life is the bridge
Between
My reality-torture
And
My God's Dream-rapture.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
