836. Food for the soul

The body's food
Is matter-made.
The soul's food
Is spirit-made.
Gratitude-life,
Gratitude-heart for God
Is food for the soul.
Perfection-cry
Is food for the soul.
Heart's awakening
Is food for the soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
