<em>Question</em> How can one protect oneself from doubt and remain always strong on the spiritual path?

Sri Chinmoy: We have to pray every day for God's Protection and Compassion; and every day we have to feel grateful to Him for blessing us with His Protection and Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Russia and Russia's God-Blossoming Heart, Agni Press, 1991
