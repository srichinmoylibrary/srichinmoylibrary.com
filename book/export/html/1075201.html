627. Pure and sure

You are pure;
Therefore, you are sure.

You are sure;
That does not mean
That you are pure.

In the heart of man's purity
God grows and glows.

In the vital of man's surety
His own destruction flows.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 13, Agni Press, 1973
