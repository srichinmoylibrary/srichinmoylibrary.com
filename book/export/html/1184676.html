348. Supreme Lord, save me

Supreme Lord,
Save me from all self-complacency.
Free me from all the invasions of fear.
Give me the capacity to tackle fear-night.
Lord Supreme,
To You I offer my Eternity's gratitude,
Because I am now all awake.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
