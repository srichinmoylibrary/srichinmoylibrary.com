Raisa, O Rainbow Queen

Raisa, O rainbow-queen
     Of culture-light,
The soul-wisdom-giver
     Of summit-height.
Indeed, a thunder-roar
     And flower-fragrance,
An effulgence-glow,
     Empty of ignorance.
Russia’s seed, Raisa,
     A cosmos-tree,
Snow-white fondness,
     Oneness and fulness free.

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: The home of oneness-peace-dream-world songbook, , 2001
