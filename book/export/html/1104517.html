16.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

Your visible Nearness-Presence
I love and I love,
My Supreme, my Supreme.
Your invisible Nearness-Presence
I worship and I worship.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

17 January 2005, 11:43 p.m. Xian, China. Seated Double-Dumbbell Lift: 1,006 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 18, Agni Press, 2005
