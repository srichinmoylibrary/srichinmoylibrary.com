God-search never ends

           You are an eternal search for God.
               Eternally you are searching
for the ever-blossoming Beauty of the Supreme.                  God-search never ends,
              even after God-realisation.
                    It never ends.

 

  1. HCEU 1. 10 January 2005, Qingdao, China↩

From: Sri Chinmoy, His Compassion is everything to us, Agni Press, 2013
