17 July 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Only my unconditional surrender
To Your Will
Will forever succeed.
Everyone else will fail me,
And I shall fail them as well.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

17 July 1999, 9:39 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 900 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
