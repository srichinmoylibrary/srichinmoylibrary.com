My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 44

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Kuantan, Malaysia in December 2005.

23 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

1 My Mind ...

My mind
Does not like duty
At all.

2 My Heart ...

My heart
Not only loves duty
But also is eager
To have every day
A newly added duty.

3 The Purity Of My Heart ...

The purity of my heart
And God’s smiling Eyes
Are always found together.

4 My Soul-Bird Sings And Sings ...

My soul-bird sings and sings
Only one song:
Love God and please God.

5 When The Heart Speaks ...

When the heart speaks,
The mind, the vital and the body
Must give full attention
To the heart.

6 Every Day My Heart’s Cries ...

Every day my heart’s cries
Must go up very speedily
And not lag behind
The speed of the previous day.

7 Selflessness ...

Selflessness
Awakens and feeds
All the time
The tears of the heart.

8 Only Silence And Peace ...

Only silence and peace
Can transform
Restless and useless mind-clamour.

9 My Lord’s Happiness-Eye ...

My Lord’s Happiness-Eye
At every moment
I try to devour.

10 My God-Hunger Was Born ...

My God-hunger was born
In my Lord’s soothing
Cradle-Smiles.

24 December 2005 ...

24 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

11 Ignorance-Mouth ...

Ignorance-mouth
Devours everything,
Including blazing fire.

12 Satisfaction ...

Satisfaction
Will never take birth
In the mind’s
Desire-forest.

13 Satisfaction ...

Satisfaction
Will gladly take birth
In the heart’s
God-smiling garden.

14 Most Dearly ...

Most dearly
Mother Earth’s tears
God’s Heart cherishes.

15 Meditation And Peace ...

Meditation and peace
Deeply enjoy
Their divine conversation.

16 We Are Not Compelled ...

We are not compelled
To lead a spiritual life,
But if happiness we want,
We must shun ignorance-night.

17 My Lord ...

My Lord,
You must keep Your Promise:
You will never leave my heart.

18 My Life-River ...

My life-river
And my heart-boat —
They live for each other.

19 My Heart ...

My heart
Is my soul’s
Joy-gathering nest.

20 Mother Earth ...

Mother Earth
Is
A pain-receiving heart.

21 Father Heaven ...

Father Heaven
Is
A joy-supplying Heart.

25 December 2005 ...

25 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

22 My God-Obedience-Boat ...

My God-obedience-boat
Has safely brought me
To God-Infinity’s God-Shore.

23 I Faithfully ...

I faithfully
Follow God’s Feet.

24 God’s Brightest Smile ...

God’s brightest Smile
Follows my heart.

25 When A Seeker Offers Even An Iota ...

When a seeker offers even an iota
Of self-giving effort,
He is bound to succeed.

26 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Do give me a Smile of Yours
So that I can pass through
My mind’s thickest
And darkest clouds.

27 No Mind ...

No mind
Can ever break through
The power of silence.

28 Every Day ...

Every day
May a deathless beauty and light
Dawn upon my aspiring heart.

29 My Gratitude-Heart ...

My gratitude-heart
Is first
On God’s priority list.

30 To Double My Happiness ...

To double my happiness,
I must become
A sleepless self-giver.

31 If You Do Not Obey God ...

If you do not obey God,
You as a traveller
Will never be able to enter
Into God’s Kingdom.

26 December 2005 ...

26 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

32 God Is Not ...

God is not
My occasional thought
But my sleepless thought.

33 Obedience ...

Obedience
Is an unmistakable way
To reach God’s Golden Shore.

34 Who Says ...

Who says
That God’s Heart
Is not accessible?
God’s Heart
Is a wide-open door.

35 My Question Is ...

My question is:
Who is God?
God’s Answer is:
“My child,
Some of you are budding gods,
While others
Are blossoming gods.”

36 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
God wants you to accompany
His Eye and His Heart
From dawn to eve
Wherever He goes.

37 Nobody Can Ever Dare To Separate ...

Nobody can ever dare to separate
God’s Compassion-Eye
From His Forgiveness-Heart.

38 The Soul’s Complete Manifestation ...

The soul’s complete manifestation
Of God’s Light
Takes place only
Here on earth.

39 God Never, Never Wants You ...

God never, never wants you
To be arrested
By ignorance-night!

40 God Wants You ...

God wants you
Only to be interested
In Him
All the time.

41 Again And Again ...

Again and again
God wants to hear from me
My gratitude-heart-songs.

27 December 2005 ...

27 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

42 The Christ ...

The Christ
Is not only for Christmas.
He is for three hundred and sixty-five days
A year.

43 Do Not Try To Hide ...

Do not try to hide
From God’s Eye.
You will sadly fail.

44 My Lord ...

My Lord,
You are taking care of
My soul and my heart.
Please take care of
Every moment of my life.

45 I Came ...

I came
From a higher world
To serve the world below.

46 Our God-Closeness ...

Our God-closeness
Makes our world
Extremely sweet.

47 The Privilege ...

The privilege
Of being God’s servant
Cannot be fathomed.

48 The Opinions ...

The opinions
Of the mind
Cannot be taken seriously.

49 My Heart Cannot Forget ...

My heart cannot forget
The sweeter than the sweetest
Memories of Heaven
While I am on earth.

50 God’s Breath ...

God’s Breath
Welcomes only
The devotion-heart.

51 Both The Outer And Inner Encouragements ...

Both the outer and inner encouragements
Are needed
To make the fastest progress
In spirituality.

28 December 2005 ...

28 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

52 When I Give Up ...

When I give up
In the battlefield of life,
God becomes His Madness-Eye
And Sadness-Heart.

53 My Gratitude-Heart-Tears ...

My gratitude-heart-tears
Every day wash
God’s omnipotent Feet.

54 When Aspiration Becomes ...

When aspiration becomes
Our true friend,
Life becomes
Spiritually meaningful.

55 Kindness ...

Kindness
Changes our fate.

56 Oneness ...

Oneness
Transforms our lives.

57 I Love To Live ...

I love to live
Between my heart’s unshed tears
And my life’s shed tears.

58 Tell The Mind ...

Tell the mind
Vehemently
That it has no room
In your life any more.

59 When I Truly Love God ...

When I truly love God,
My heart becomes
Peaceful.

60 When I Truly Need God ...

When I truly need God,
My life becomes
Fruitful.

61 When We Give Up Hope ...

When we give up hope,
Our promise
Starts to starve.

29 December 2005 ...

29 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

62 There Are So Many ...

There are so many
Secret and sacred ways
To go to God’s Palace.

63 Everything I Do ...

Everything I do
Takes root
In God’s Heart first.

64 There Are Many, Many, Many Seekers ...

There are many, many, many seekers
Here on earth
Who have given everything
To God.
I must try to mix with them.

65 I Want To Be Awake ...

I want to be awake
Around the clock
So that whenever God wants me,
I shall be all ready.

66 The Inner Cries ...

The inner cries
Have to be answered
Only by the inner world.

67 The God-Denying Mind ...

The God-denying mind
Builds and builds warships
To fight against
The divine forces.

68 The God-Believing Heart ...

The God-believing heart
Wants only a worship-boat
To invite God
To pilot the boat.

69 Earth Has ...

Earth has
Its stumbling feet.

70 Heaven Has ...

Heaven has
Its whispering Protection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 44, Agni Press, 2006
