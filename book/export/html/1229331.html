Ami Amar Chirantaner Abahela

Ami amar chirantaner abahela
Tumi amar mrityujayi sudha bela
Ami amar agyanatar kanna bedan
Tumi amar manas baner mukti jiban
Ami amar chinta sroter ghor parajay
Tumi amar hiya nabhe purnata nirbhay
