426. Danger-red, danger-white

Danger-red challenges
   Human courage.

Danger-blue welcomes
   Human faith.

Danger-green defies
   Earthly time.

Danger-white strengthens
   Our prayer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
