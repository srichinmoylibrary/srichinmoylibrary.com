1399

Where do I live?
I live with my Lord Supreme
In the tower of His Forgiveness.
What is the thing
That inspires me, illumines me
And fulfils me most?
The Breath of His universal
Breathless Life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 14, Agni Press, 1983
