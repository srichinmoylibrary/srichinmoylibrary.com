71 Unless I Have Convinced Myself ...

Unless I have convinced myself
That I am a supremely chosen child
Of my Master,
My Goal shall perpetually remain
A far cry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 10, Agni Press, 2002
