Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 232

23101

My heart's devotion
Every day carries my life
To the Feet of my Beloved Supreme.

23102

Our aspiring gratitude-heart
Always expands
As it rises.

23103

Peace
Will not take "no" for an answer
From the mind.

23104

God the Compassion
Sleeplessly keeps His Eye
On everything
That we say and do.

23105

Once we become seekers,
We must take each day
As a one-time opportunity
To realise and please God.

23106

Each smiling heart
Embodies
A God-willingness-breath.

23107

When my mind started searching
For God,
God immediately heaved a sigh
Of relief.

23108

My heart wants to be claimed
Only by my Lord's
Compassion-Eye.

23109

I want to be a conqueror
Of my ego
And a perfect slave
Of my soul.

23110

There was a time
When I used to weep
A river of tears,
But not now.
Why?
Because God's golden Smiles
Have enveloped my entire being.

23111

A beautiful heart-garden
Is a blessingful message
From the Lord Supreme.

23112

Every day
My soul is secretly and openly
Helping my heart
To expel my mind's
Doubt-ghosts.

23113

A seeker's self-giving life
To God
Can never grow old.

23114

Love in measureless measure
Results in
Universal oneness.

23115

The soulful music
Of the heart
Is the voice
Of divinity's perfection.

23116

Only a life of surrender
Can sit
On the throne of tranquillity.

23117

We treasure
The power of independence.
God treasures
The power of interdependence.

23118

My soul does not use
A loud voice.
It always speaks
In whispers.

23119

The fragrance
Of a gratitude-heart-blossom
Is unparalleled
In running the fastest
To God's Kingdom.

23120

The oneness
Of aspiring hearts
Embodies and reveals
The most haunting illumination-melody.

23121

True God-seekers
Are Heaven-pilgrim-souls
That throng before
God's Lotus-Feet.

23122

The difference between
Detachment and indifference
Is this:
Indifference blinds us;
Detachment illumines us.

23123

My life's happiest moments
Are those
When I am blessed
With brief glimpses
Of my Lord's Smile.

23124

If you do not forgive and forget
Your past unhealthy experiences,
God will not be able to bless you
With His Enthusiasm-Dynamism.

23125

When courage is founded
Upon oneness-love,
We can challenge the pride
Of ignorance-night.

23126

May my entire day be blessed
With the unparalleled beauty
Of the morning's rising sun!

23127

How do we regain
Our long-lost divinity?
Only through constant
God-obedience-willingness
And eagerness.

23128

Each time I have
A God-surrendering thought,
I immediately multiply
My heart's goodness.

23129

O seeker, be patient!
Sooner or later
You will definitely reach
Your sunlit aspiration-summit.

23130

My Lord,
The moment You smile at me,
My heart and I fly
A million miles above
This sorrow-filled earth.

23131

Do not cut down the mind,
But cut down the thick mind-forest
If you want to be divinely wise.

23132

If you want to live
In the world of peace,
Then keep tomorrow's thoughts
For tomorrow.

23133

He who is master
Of his thoughts
Is indeed the president
Of the universe.

23134

If you want constant happiness,
Then every day
Enlarge the circle of your love.

23135

When we dive deep within,
We definitely can hear
The whispers of Infinity.

23136

The simplest, the easiest
And the most effective way
To achieve God-realisation
Is to breathe the very breath
Of unconditional surrender.

23137

My crying heart
And my surrendering life
Have reawakened
My God-oneness-cry.

23138

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
I look and look
For Your Compassion-Eye
Inside my heart,
But alas,
It is not to be found there.

23139

God is unable
To keep back His Tears
When I doubt
My God-fulfilment-capacity.

23140

There is only one thing I cherish
At every moment,
And that is my tearful heart.

23141

Just go to God and give Him
What you have:
Your heart's cry.
God will immediately give you
What He has for you:
His Infinity's Smile.

23142

Love God
According to your limited capacity.
He will give you unlimited opportunities
To multiply your capacity.

23143

You are wrong when you think
The child inside you — your soul —
Has no power.
The soul has power
Far beyond your imagination.
But it uses its power
Only to please God
In God's own Way.

23144

It is never too late to start doing
The right thing.
You definitely can once again love and please
Your Beloved Lord Supreme.

23145

If you really want to see God smiling,
Then start doing the right thing
And becoming the right person
At this very moment.

23146

It is you who have to decide
Whether you want to be
A supremely chosen instrument
Of God
Or a willing instrument
Of ignorance-king.

23147

The harder the battle is,
The more faith God will have in us
When He sees we are winning
On the battlefield of life.

23148

God tells me
He does not want me to prove
My capacity.
He only wants me not to miss any opportunity
To love Him and please Him
In His own Way.

23149

Do not feel
You are doomed to disappointment,
Although your spiritual life
Has been a dry desert for years.
The golden days shall beckon you
Once again.
What you need is patience
And loving determination.

23150

You feel fear
Only when you doubt yourself
And doubt your own spiritual life.
Just identify yourself
With something higher and deeper.
Lo and behold,
Fear is nowhere to be found.

23151

Following a spiritual Master
Is like walking with someone
Who is infinitely stronger than you
And is always ready to help you
And make you happy in a divine way.

23152

Manifestation is divine
And manifestation is perfect
Only if it is done
With the heart's self-giving smile.

23153

At every moment
We have to feel our oneness
With humanity
If we want to be loved and embraced
By the universal Light.

23154

Alas, countless times
You are bringing thorns to the Supreme
By criticising mercilessly
Those who are trying to give Him roses.

23155

To make God really happy,
What your body needs
Is strength,
What your vital needs
Is dynamism,
What your mind needs
Is clarity,
What your heart needs
Is eagerness.

23156

Daily appreciate the beauty
Of your heart's forgiveness-flowers
And your life's tolerance-fruits.

23157

Just as unaspiring, negative
And doubt-filled thoughts
Separate you from God,
Even so, aspiring, positive
And faith-blossoming feelings
Take you speedily to God.

23158

May my mind, like my heart,
Be always eager
To place everything it has and is
At the Feet of my Lord Supreme.

23159

Bring your best to the fore
And keep it there.
You will unmistakably see
How close you are to God.

23160

God will definitely take care of you
And make you a happiness-king
If you just love your heart
Infinitely more than you do now.

23161

If you pray to God sincerely,
Is there anything
He cannot and will not
Do for you?

23162

You are bound to feel God's Love
Infinitely more
If you love Him, not in theory,
But in practical reality's intensity.

23163

My Lord,
This is my very special prayer to You:
Do give me the capacity every day
To offer You a new gratitude-heart.

23164

O seeker,
Why do you give God
Your worst possible spiritual defeat
When you have the capacity to give Him
Your supremely glorious victory?

23165

Today imagine
That God loves you
And God needs you.
Tomorrow you will see
That your imagination was nothing more
Than a reality unblossomed.

23166

Do not worry about the future.
The present moment is heavy enough
For you to carry.
How do you know that tomorrow
A bright and illumining dawn
Will not invite you?

23167

God wants you constantly to cherish
Your own aspiration
Exactly the way He enjoys
His own Satisfaction.

23168

You are bound to make progress
And make yourself really happy
If you take spirituality
As your real and only life.

23169

Only when we dive deep within
And meditate self-givingly
Does the cosmic sound reverberate
In our heart's aspiration-sky.

23170

God gives you the beauty
Of aspiration
To love Him.
God gives you the fragrance
Of dedication
To manifest Him.

23171

My God-surrender-blossoms
God Himself places
On His own Satisfaction-Heart-Shrine.

23172

Every day we must bring to the fore
The roaring lion in us
And manifest the divine Light,
To transform the face and fate
Of mankind.

23173

God always watches
Our heart-hunger
And, according to our heart-hunger,
His Compassion-Eye feeds us.

23174

God begs us for two things:
To give Him the opportunity
And the certainty
Of remaining permanently
Inside our life-breath.

23175

God does what He has to do:
He keeps us inside His Heart.
Unfortunately, we do not keep Him
Where He is supposed to be:
Inside our own heart.

23176

Just try always
To see God's Nectar-Smile.
Then, along your aspiration-road,
No wrong committed by you
Can seriously discourage you.
Dauntlessly you will run
Towards your destined goal.

23177

God is asking you to consider
Only three questions from Him
About your inner life:
In how many ways have you loved God?
In how many ways have you served God?
In how many ways have you fulfilled God
Over the years?

23178

Remember this once and for all:
Your heart's gratitude-smile
Has the fastest speed
To reach Infinity's Golden Shore.

23179

Do not think of how many times
You have disappointed God.
Only think of one thing:
Do you still love God?
If so, make a fresh attempt.
Lo, you have won the Goal.

23180

The human in you does not dare
To claim the divine in you.
The divine in you not only claims
The human in you,
But also helps the human in you
To enter into Infinity's Peace-Sea.

23181

God the Creator
Loves you.
God the creation
Needs you.
To God the Creator
Give your heart.
To God the creation
Give your life.

23182

Happiness is radiating on your face
From your heart.
Do you know why?
Because you have pleased God
In His own Way today,
And because you will please Him
Infinitely more tomorrow.

23183

God feels His Presence
Inside your heart.
God feels your presence
Inside His Heart.
Therefore, He is happy.
If you want to be happy,
Then bring God and keep God
Inside your mind.

23184

Do you want to know
The Will of God?
Then do two things:
Become and remain
Your heart's streaming tears.
You do not have to strive
To know the Will of God.
Your soul on its own will tell you
What God's Will is.
Your soul wants and needs from you
Only one thing:
An ever-deepening meditation.

23185

We all need fulfilment.
Therefore, why do we hesitate
To go to the right person
Who is all eagerness to bless us
With fulfilment?
Who is the right person?
None other than your own highest self!

23186

If you are true to yourself,
Only then will God remain
Your Eternity's only Boss.

23187

Whom can we obey?
Our Lord Beloved Supreme.
How can we obey Him?
Just by sleeplessly listening
To the dictates of our soul,
The direct representative of God.

23188

Real happiness can be found
Only in the world of dynamic
And self-giving activities.

23189

Our outer beauty
Feeds our eyes.
Our inner beauty
Feeds our Lord Supreme.

23190

God's greatest Blessing-Light
To man
Is a sleepless hunger
For God-satisfaction.

23191

I need a heart-cry
That will be satisfied
Only with an eternal God-union
And not with just a long-term God-union.

23192

O my heart,
Do not be discouraged.
God and God's Compassion-Eye
Will not be able to hide from you
Forever.

23193

He is unfortunate
Who descends from aspiration-tree.
But he who descends
From aspiration-tree
And does not recognise his descent
Is infinitely more unfortunate.

23194

I cry and smile.
I cry astonishingly
When I enter into the human mind.
I smile ceaselessly
When I enter into the human heart.

23195

To my extreme joy
My heart-tears
Are nourishing
My life-blossoms.

23196

If you choose your soul
Instead of anything else,
Only then will your suffering life's cries
Come to an end.

23197

Yesterday
I was on my Lord's
Forgiveness-List.

Today
I am on my Lord's
Compassion-List.

Tomorrow
I shall be on my Lord's
Satisfaction-List.

23198

God tells me
That there is no difference
Between
My heart's breathless cries
And His Eye's sleepless Smiles.

23199

Today
We painfully call it
Humiliation.

Tomorrow
We shall call that very thing
Illumination.

23200

Life is God's Universal Game.
The seekers join God's side
And play along with Him
Against the undivine and unlit forces.
God's side, without fail,
Will eventually win.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 232, Agni Press, 1996
