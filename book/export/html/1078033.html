386. Dance

My mind and I love
The dance of solitude.
Solitude-dance
Is really breathtaking.

My heart and I love
The dance of beatitude.
Beatitude-dance
Is really life-illumining.

My soul and I love
The dance of infinitude.
Infinitude-dance
Is really God-fulfilling.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
