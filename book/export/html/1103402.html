21 April 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your compassionate Heart
   Responds immediately
Only to those who need You
   Only, only, only.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 April 1999, 7:34 a.m. Before lifting 230 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
