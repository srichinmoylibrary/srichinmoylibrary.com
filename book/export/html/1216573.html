935. No more, no less

You are no more
Than a human being
When you fail to meditate.

You are no less
Than a cosmic god
When you soulfully meditate.

You are no more
        And
No less than God Himself
When you divinely love and serve.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
