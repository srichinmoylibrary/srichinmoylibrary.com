Prashphutila Taba Hasi

Prashphutila taba hasi
E shunya jibane
Ami nai tumi shudhu
Muktir plabane

Translation:

Your Smile divine has blossomed
In this barren life of mine.
No more do I exist;
You, only You, in the flood of liberation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Patience-groves, , 1978
