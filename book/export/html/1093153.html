18.

God claims me
as His own, very
own. Alas, when will
I be able to claim
God as my own, very
own? When?

Guru Sri Chinmoy
July 5th, 2007

From: Sri Chinmoy, My blessingful and pride-flooded dedication to the indomitable runners of the 3100-mile Self-Transcendence Race, 2007, Agni Press, 2007
