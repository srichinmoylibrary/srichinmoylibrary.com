188. Faith will change my future

Sincerity
Changed my past
Slowly and steadily.

Determination
Is changing my present
Powerfully and convincingly.

Faith
Will change my future
Amazingly and permanently.

From: Sri Chinmoy, 207 Flower-Flames, Agni Press,
