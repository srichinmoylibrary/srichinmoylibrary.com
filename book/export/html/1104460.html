12 December 2004 (I)

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

The God of Silence
Is blooming now
Inside my heart.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

12 December 2004, 4:18 a.m. Xiamen, China. Seated Double-Dumbbell Lift: 809 lbs.

1. Janani Mirar
2. Sri Aurobindo
