Alone he stood

Alone he stood
Above all storms of life.
He stood alone
To challenge pain and strife.
Alone he stood
To feed a blooming race.
He stood alone
To change earth's tearful face.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kennedy: the universal heart, Agni Press, 1973
