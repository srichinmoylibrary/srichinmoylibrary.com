29. Offerings

Love,
Offer your surrender.
Surrender,
Offer your love.
Beauty,
Offer your duty.
Duty,
Offer your beauty.
Aspiration,
Offer your transcendence.
Transcendence,
Offer your aspiration.
Promise,
Offer your goal.
Goal,
Offer your promise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The prayer of the sky, Agni Press, 1974
