25. Devotion

My devotion is sweet
   When I sit
At my Master's feet.

My devotion is sweeter
   When I devote myself
To my Master's
   Heaven-illumining vision.

My devotion is sweetest
   When I feel
I have nothing to show
To the world at large
Save my oneness,
Devoted oneness,
With my inner Pilot,
My Beloved Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
