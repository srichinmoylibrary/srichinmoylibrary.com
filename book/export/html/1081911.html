352. At least one person loves me

Man does not need me,
But
God needs me.
At least He says so.
So at least one person needs me.

Man does not love me,
But
God loves me.
At least He says so.
So at least one person loves me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
