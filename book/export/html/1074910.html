342 In My Past, Present And Future...

IN MY PAST, PRESENT AND FUTURE

There was a time
When I lived to eat.
I ate and ate and ate.

Now
I eat to live.
I regularly eat,
I devotedly eat.

There shall come a time
When I shall eat
At God's Will,
At God's Hour;
From God's Kitchen,
With God's Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 7, Agni Press, 1973
