9087

Although he is notorious
For his insincerity-life,
He is now disconnecting
His evil thought-current.
And all this he is doing
With the help of
God's unconditional Grace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 91, Agni Press, 1984
