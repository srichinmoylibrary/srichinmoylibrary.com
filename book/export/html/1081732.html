173. Dissatisfaction and satisfaction

Dissatisfaction
Will sap the energy
Of your heart-life
If you feed your mind's
Hungry abyss.

Satisfaction
Will expand the energy
Of your heart-life
If you love and serve
And serve and love
Your soul's Perfection-sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
