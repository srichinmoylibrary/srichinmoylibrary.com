Spirituality is a matter of belief.
You believe that your Master
Has realised God,
And your Master believes
One hundred per cent
That He will be able to make you realise
The Supreme, Absolute, Transcendental,
Beloved Father Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 187, Agni Press, 1993
