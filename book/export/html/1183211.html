75. You want to know

You want to know
What kind of mind I have.
Alas, I have a backward-walking mind.
You want to know
What kind of heart I have.
Ah, I have a forward-marching heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
