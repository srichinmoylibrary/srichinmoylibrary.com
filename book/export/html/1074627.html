64. A sin, a crime

A sin against man
I commit
When I love myself alone.
A crime against God
I commit
When I love only mankind.
A sin and a crime
Against myself I commit
When
I forget what I was, Truth,
And what I am, Love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 2, Agni Press, 1973
