121 Mulct

Madal: Chinmoy, very often I am mulcted of all my money by my deceitful friends. What shall I do with such friends?

Chinmoy: Madal, those who extract money from you by fraud or deceit can never be your friends. But you have one Friend who will never cheat you, and that Friend is God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two brothers: Madal and Chinmoy, part 3, Agni Press, 1982
