22. My needs

My Indian heart
Needs more compassion.

My American body
Needs more vitality.

My African legs
Need more speed.

My Chinese head
Needs more wisdom.

My European mind
Needs more nobility.

My German vital
Needs more determination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three soulful prayers, Agni Press, 1979
