761 One Step Ahead...

ONE STEP AHEAD

My aspiration-dreams
Have cancelled
My desire-dreams.
Therefore
I am happy with my life
At long last.
My Lord Supreme
Is one step ahead.
Behold, He is mightily proud of me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
