Amar Bhuban Majhe

Amar bhuban majhe
Tomar swapan raje
Tomar bhuban majhe
Ami sada mari laje
Amar bhuban majhe
Taba madhu banshi baje
Tomar bhuban majhe
Ami nachi ama saje

Translation:

Inside my world
Is Your Dream.
Inside Your world
I suffer and die
From ceaseless shame.
Inside my world
I hear You play Your Nectar-Flute.
Inside Your world
I dance the dance of ignorance-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Pole-star promise-light, part 1, , 1977
