8797

Why are you only appreciating
The flowers and fruits of the tree?
Where is your eagerness
To pluck the flowers and fruits
And have them for your own,
Very own?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 88, Agni Press, 1984
