43. King and Queen

You are Heaven's King;
Therefore
You are sure.
You are Heaven's Queen;
Therefore
You are pure.
You are earth's King;
Therefore
You have tried everything.
You are earth's Queen;
Therefore
You are everything.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 14, Agni Press, 1974
