July 24

When I am without God, I try and cry. When I am with God, I feel and fill. When I am in God, I think and bring. When I am for God, I row and sow. When I am of God, I really am.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
