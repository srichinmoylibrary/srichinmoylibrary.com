841. It's me, Father

It's me, Father.
For God's sake,
Do not be so alarmed.

It's me, Father.
For God's sake,
Do not be so disturbed.

"Son, I am not alarmed,
I am not disturbed.
I am only happy and amazed
That you are knocking at My Door
At long last.
Come in, My son, come in."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 17, Agni Press, 1973
