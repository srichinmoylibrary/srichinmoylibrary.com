10855

What you need
Is an unfailing faith.
What you need
Is a profound inspiration.
What you need
Is a sleepless inner urge
To conquer the storms of fear
And the volcanoes of doubt.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 109, Agni Press, 1987
