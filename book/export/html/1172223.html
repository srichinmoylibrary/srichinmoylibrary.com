Horst

"My Lord, You created me. Please tell me, who created You?"

"My child, I was created twice. The first time, I was created by My Eternity's, Infinity's and Immortality's Vision. The second time, I was created by your stupidity's giant ignorance-night."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sri Chinmoy with his upper-storey-weak students, Agni Press, 1993
