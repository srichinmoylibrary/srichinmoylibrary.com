4968 If You Have The Determination-Fire

If you have the determination-fire,
You can succeed in your outer life
Easily.

If you have the faith-glow,
You can proceed in your inner life
Amazingly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 50, Agni Press, 1982
