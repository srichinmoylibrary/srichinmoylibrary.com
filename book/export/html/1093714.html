49 Man's Promise To God: ...

Man's promise to God:
A very quick start.
God asks:
"Are you sure?"
God's Promise to man:
A very quick start.
Man says:
"Sorry, I am not interested."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 3, Agni Press, 2000
