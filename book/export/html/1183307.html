171. You want to know

You want to know
Why I love my heart.
I love my heart
Because of its intensity.
You want to know
Why I love my soul.
I love my soul
Because of its immensity.
You want to know
Why I love my God.
I love my God
Because
Of His earth-liberating Duty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
