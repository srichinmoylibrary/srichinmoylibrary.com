25 When My Mind ...

When my mind
Wants to speak to God,
God says to my mind,
“You are too close!
Go back at least
One hundred metres.
Do not worry —
I have two very big Ears.
I shall be able to hear you
Very easily.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 11, Agni Press, 2002
