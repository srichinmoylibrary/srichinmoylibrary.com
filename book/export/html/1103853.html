6 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The mind's freedom-march —
   No, no, no!
The heart's devotion-walk —
   Yes, yes, yes!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1999, 8:35 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
