3. Cover

My sweet Lord,
Do You cover
My falsehood-life,
Impurity-breath
And
Bondage-night?
"My sweet daughter,
I cover them not;
Again, I expose them not.
I claim your possession-world
As my reality's substance
And essence.
Therefore
I own your little world
And
I endeavour to change the face
Of your little world
To My Satisfaction-Light."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 6, Agni Press, 1974
