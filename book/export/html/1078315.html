668 Joining Hands With God...

JOINING HANDS WITH GOD

I joined hands
With God
To become His highest Silence-life.

I join hands
With God
To become His farthest Sound-life.

I shall join hands
With God
To become His deepest Love-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
