33. My companions

   Hope, my hope,
You are my conscious companion;
   Therefore
I always like you.   Patience, my patience,
You are my conscious and constant companion;
   Therefore
I always love you.   Surrender, my surrender,
You are my conscious, constant and illumining companion;
   Therefore
I always need you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 17, Agni Press, 1974
