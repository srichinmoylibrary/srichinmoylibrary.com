May 20

To love those who love us is to do the right thing. To love those who do not love us is to do the nice thing. To love God who loves us is to do the wise thing.When we do the right thing, we are free. When we do the nice thing, we are safe. When we do the wise thing, we are fulfilled.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
