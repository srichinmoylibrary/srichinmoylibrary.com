21891

The vital possessions
Have the capacity to destroy
The physical possessions.

The mental possessions
Have the capacity to destroy
The vital possessions.

The psychic possessions
Have the capacity to destroy
The mental possessions,
But the psychic possessions
Illumine the mental possessions
Instead.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 219, Agni Press, 1995
