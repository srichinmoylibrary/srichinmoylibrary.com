40. A king of perfection-love

You were a king
Of aspiration-flames;
Therefore,
You were a king
Of renunciation-life;
Therefore,
You were great.
BUT
Now you have become a king
Of Perfection-love;
Therefore,
You are good.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
