11 December 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I am crying for a new strength-discovery
Inside my body, vital, mind,
Heart and soul.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/Hawaii. 11 December 1998, 5:19 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. fifty times with two arms and one-hundred and twenty-eight times with one arm. (57 right, 71 left in six sets)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
