Silence speaks, part 3

1.

The Lord will draw
Your great life-plan
If you become
His greatest fan.

2.

An unwilling mind
Can nothing find.
An unwilling life
Is shockingly blind.

3.

I recognise
Impurity
By its face of
Insecurity.

4.

Earthly prosperity
Comes and goes.
Heavenly satisfaction
Comes and grows.

5.

Because you have erased your past
Without leaving a trace,
You are seeing within, without
God's Satisfaction-Face.

6.

You have the mind
That creates pains.
You are the soul
That breaks chains.

7.

A casual commitment
To spirituality
Is nothing other
Than useless futility.

8.

My ego-mind sings
My vital's attachment-song.
My oneness-heart strikes
My soul's enlightenment-gong.

9.

My prayer-life
Makes my heart divinely pure.
My surrender-life
Makes my world supremely sure.

10.

The fearful mind
Lives in a cave.
The doubting mind
Lives in a grave.

11.

His greatness wants to conquer
The truth to enjoy.
His goodness wants the truth
Him to employ.

12.

Where else can you find
Aspiration-flame
If not inside your
Inspiration-game?

13.

I call it
My heart's simplicity.
God calls it
His Life's Majesty.

14.

O leave the past!
Why do you care?
Live in the present
To smile and dare.

15.

No, no, you will
Get no award
If your mind looks
Always backward.

16.

The silence-voice
Of the inner life
Challenges daily
Ignorance-knife.

17.

You do not need
Your Lord's Fulness.
What you need now
Is His Forgiveness.

18.

Mine is a mind
Unpardonable.
Mine is a life
Deplorable.

19.

If I am
My mind's chain,
God is His
Forgiveness-Reign.

20.

God has now blessed you
With a visit unexpected.
O can you not show Him
Your earth-life perfected?

21.

God's powerful
Compassion-Lance
Teaches my heart
Every day to dance.

22.

Confine your doubtful mind,
Confine.
Refine your sordid life,
Refine.

23.

Not with your mind's
Narrowness-span
Can you change God's
Absolute Plan.

24.

Each man was born
For peace and bliss
And never for
Darkness-snake-kiss.

25.

A heart of promise-light
Must never die.
A life of hope-delight
Must never sigh.

26.

Religions, you are no longer
Teaching me how to fly.
You are forcing my heart and life
To cry and sigh and die.

27.

Because you are
Your heart's purity,
You are also God's
Real necessity.

28.

Find your forgotten
God-identity
If you desire His
Bliss-Eternity.

29.

If you are prone to
Indecision,
Never can you escape
Frustration.

30.

I may not be perfect
In seeking,
But my Lord is perfect
In finding.

31.

O doubting mind,
Do you not know
That you are your own
Unhappy foe?

32.

My mind and vital promise me
A life of excitement.
My soul and heart promise me
A life of enlightenment.

33.

Each divine thought is
Man's capacity
To transcend the boundaries
Of humanity.

34.

You want to show God
Your affluence.
God wants you to show
His Influence.

35.

When the mind
Wants to rest,
Always ready
The heart-nest.

36.

Desire,
You fascinate
Only
To assassinate.

37.

Life is man's
Expectation-night.
Life is God's
Experience-Delight.

38.

My earth-life's
Sleepless aspiration
Is my Lord's
Complete Satisfaction.

39.

In me God is God's
Eternal Play.
Therefore, my life-tree
Will know no decay.

40.

Give not way to
Life's hopelessness.
God is awaiting you
With His Fulness.

41.

You want your life
To be glorious.
Then make your heart
First victorious.

42.

If you keep not
Your personal choice,
God will not keep
His impersonal Voice.

43.

If you long only
For aspiration,
Then nothing can limit
Your perfection.

44.

My aspiration-heart's
Everyday choice
Is my sweet Supreme Lord's
Compassion-Voice.

45.

God has invited you
To run in His Race.
Therefore beg Him
To set the pace.

46.

God will give you
His Life's measureless Strength.
You just give Him
Your heart's purity-length.

47.

When he is with God,
He cries for God's Compassion.
When he is with man,
He cries for man's cooperation.

48.

Since you enjoy living
In your mind's jail,
Your poor heart's appeal
To God will fail.

49.

Devotion is something
That creates purity.
Surrender is something
That embodies divinity.

50.

My heart's self-giving song
Is for every hour.
My life's God-becoming dance
Is God's Delight-Tower.

51.

Perfection
Is my inner unfoldment.
Surrender
Is my God-endearment.

52.

If you enjoy the torrent
Of doubt-rain,
Your life is bound to become
A sleepless pain.

53.

Challenge the brutal strength
Of ignorance-frown.
The Lord Supreme will grant you
His own peerless Crown.

54.

If you are on
The path of desire,
Your service-life
God will never hire.

55.

God is extremely proud
Of your ignorance-fight,
And of your eagerness
To spread all-where His Light.

56.

O make my heart
A heart soulful.
O make my life
A life fruitful.

57.

To hear your soul's
Happiness-gong,
Sing not your mind's
Expectation-song.

58.

Many gods:
My mind's fabrication.
Only one God:
My heart's realisation.

59.

If you are cherishing
Your frightened heart,
You are also cherishing
An ignorance-dart.

60.

If you are not willing
To clean up your vital-mess,
God will not print
Your victory-name on His Press.

61.

How are You today,
My Beloved Lord Supreme?
Are You going to share with me
Your Satisfaction-Dream?

62.

Dive within.
You will feel perfection.
Serve without.
You will feel satisfaction.

63.

O my doubtful
And suspicious mind,
God's Vision-Light
How will you ever find?

64.

If your longing
Has no intensity,
Then your life
Is all futility.

65.

When Love divine
Begins to whisper,
The heart begins
To richly prosper.

66.

Beyond
The power of human reason
Is God's
Man-transforming season.

67.

Your mind is covered
With ignorance-mud.
Therefore, you see not
Your pure heart-bud.

68.

When I pray, I pray
For God's Greatness Transcendental.
When I meditate, I meditate
On God's Oneness Universal.

69.

If you are lost
In unwillingness-land,
How can you ever
Feel God's Hand?

70.

The dancing eye
Of a soaring imagination
Carries the mind
To the farthest destination.

71.

All you need to know about earth
Is its receptivity-heart.
All you need to know about Heaven
Is its ignorance-smashing dart.

72.

His human heart has now become
The possessor of boundless delight,
For he has cancelled his reservation
On the bondage-ignorance-flight.

73.

Make your choice:
God in His own Way,
Or continue living
In ignorance-clay.

74.

Do you not know
God's Love is unbounded?
Do you not know
Your fears are unfounded?

75.

In your mental world choose
Between possessing and renouncing.
In your psychic world choose
Between ruling and surrendering.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Silence speaks, part 3, Agni Press, 1994
