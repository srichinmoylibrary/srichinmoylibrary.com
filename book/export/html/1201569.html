15776

The mind oscillates
Between truth and falsehood.
Alas,
How can I free myself
From this mind-oscillation?
What I need is a mind
That permanently lives
In the heart of truth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 158, Agni Press, 1991
