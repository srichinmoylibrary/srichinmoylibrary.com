In your daily life

Where is your temple?
Is it not in your daily life?

Where is your shrine?
Is it not in your daily life?

Where is your duty?
Is it not in your daily life?

Where is your God?
Is He not in your daily life?

Who is God?
He who sings the song
Of self-transcendence
At each hush-gap.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God-Life: is it a far cry?, Agni Press, 1974
