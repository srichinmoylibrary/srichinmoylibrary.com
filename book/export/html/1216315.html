682. Bull, deer and lion

The Bull in me
Fights for the Freedom vast.

The Deer in me
Runs for the freedom of the Beyond.

The Lion in me
Enjoys the freedom of the ever-transcending
Goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
