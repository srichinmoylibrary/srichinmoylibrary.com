The disciples’ vibrations

Each morning when I come down the stairs to my weight room, I powerfully identify with the vibration of a particular disciple or a group of disciples. I collect the aspiring hearts of the disciples each day and, from that aspiration, I formulate the weightlifting message that I give. Sometimes it is the disciples’ souls actually speaking through me.

— August 1986

From: Sri Chinmoy, My weightlifting tears and smiles, part 1, Agni Press, 1986
