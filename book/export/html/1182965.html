9943 An Accident

My mind calls it
An accident.
My heart calls it
An experience.
My soul calls it
A life-transforming Touch of God.
My Beloved Supreme calls it
His inner Involution
And His outer Revelation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 100, Agni Press, 1983
