Amar Hiyar Gan Nahi Habe Abashan

Amar hiyar gan
Nahi habe abashan
Tomar kripar ban
Nahi habe abashan
Chetanar abhijan
Nahi habe abashan

Translation:

The songs of my heart
Will never come to an end.
The flood of Your Compassion
Will never stop.
The journey of consciousness-light
Will never come to an end.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 4, , 1981
