42. Those who believe

Those who believe, embrace.
What do they believe?
They believe that they are
Of God.

Those who embrace, believe.
What do they embrace?
They embrace
God's immanence,
God's permanence
And
God's transcendence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
