67. Teach me how to love

Father, do teach me
How to love.
Why, My daughter, why?
Father, if You teach me
How to love
Only then shall I be able
To love You
Unconditionally
Eternally
And
Supremely.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
