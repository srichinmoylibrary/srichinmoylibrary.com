22 April 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   Today do bless my mind
With a new inspiration-joy,
   Do bless my heart
With a new aspiration-joy
   And do bless my life
With a new dedication-joy.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 April 1999, 6:37 a.m. Before lifting 240 lbs. fifty-five times with one arm. (26 right, 27 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
