July 10

It is said that one cannot serve two masters. In my case I do serve two masters every day. I serve man the constant delusion and frustration. I serve God the eternal Compassion and Illumination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
