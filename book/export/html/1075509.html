929. The song of forgiveness

He is weak.
Therefore
He cannot afford to forgive.
You are strong.
Therefore
You can afford to forgive.
I am unwise.
Therefore
I know not how to forgive.
God is wise.
Therefore
He is all-Forgiveness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 19, Agni Press, 1973
