716 In Me...

IN ME

God saw in me
A poet.
Therefore
I loved nature's beauty.

God sees in me
A real man.
Therefore
I fulfil life's duty.

God shall see in me
A divine lover.
Therefore
I shall become Truth's Immortality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
