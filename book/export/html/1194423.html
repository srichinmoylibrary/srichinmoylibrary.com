Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 88

8701

The two rainbow-wings
Of his life's dedication
Are for the world to use.

8702

In the silence of your heart
Your life's perfection
Cannot remain a far cry.

8703

O my mind,
When are you going to be ashamed
Of your deathless, savage greed?

8704

His purity-mind
Is singing and dancing
On the altar of his heart's stars.

8705

The creation is the work,
The masterpiece,
Of only one Master-Artist Supreme.

8706

He is always seen
At one place:
In his Eternity's
Dream-filled boat.

8707

Turn the bold desires
Of your vital
Into the timid desires
Of your mind,
And then destroy them!

8708

Aspire, aspire!
Otherwise,
The whole world's emptiness-night
Will capture your entire being.

8709

Man's audacious mind
And man's treacherous mind
Are not far away
From man's God-intoxicated,
Self-giving heart.

8710

You must renounce
Servitude to
The ignorance-tiger in you.

8711

Go forward
With your volcanic will power!
Otherwise,
Yours will be a life
Of huge destruction.

8712

What is your heart's choice:
An excitement-man
Or an enlightenment-soul?

8713

Along with his soul,
His body, vital, mind and heart
Are journeying
Through unknowable Infinity.

8714

An illumined life
Is a God-cherished treasure
On earth.

8715

I can see that your inner fire
Is growing and glowing.
I urge you not to allow it
To be extinguished!

8716

He often wonders:
Does God really exist
In his stony ignorance-life?

8717

The secret of this world:
It wants to avoid wisdom-light.
The secret of the inner world:
It wants to claim God
As its very own.

8718

No human being —
No, not even one —
Is doomed to live
Inside the sea of ignorance-night.

8719

I definitely see
A divine change in you
Now that your mind has become
A perfect stranger to doubts.

8720

It is quite strange
That your vital enjoys
Remaining unhappy
In a tiny life-corner
Of the Infinite.

8721

Go to the inner school
Of silence-light
And start mastering
The mind's outer thoughts.

8722

If you are waiting
For the Dawn of God,
Then wait not only soulfully
But also unconditionally
To please Him in His own Way.

8723

Yours is a slow and confused
Self-search.
Therefore, how can you expect
Anything illumining and fulfilling
From your life?

8724

Nobody has compelled you
Or even begged you
To be fettered
By puny and silly thoughts.

8725

Your weaknesses
Will always chase you.
Do not run away from them,
But put up a brave fight
And defeat them once and for all!

8726

Suffering is not
A mere teacher,
But an unforgettable teacher!

8727

If you learn
The art of thanksgiving,
It will be so easy
For you to learn
The art of life-receiving.

8728

God wants you
To converse with Him
Only on one condition:
If you are ready to be
His Eternity's friend.

8729

When he secretly breathes
God-Breath,
He and God become
Earth's perfection-cry
And Heaven's satisfaction-smile.

8730

Each pure thought
Comes from Heaven
With its heavenly powerful promise.

8731

If your life is confined
By common and uninspiring things,
Your heavenward passage
Will untimely be closed
By death.

8732

If you are a genuine seeker,
Then you must always get approval
From your heart
Before you do anything.

8733

You fool!
How can you think
That God's unconditional
Compassion-Eye
Will remain impassive in your life?

8734

All doubtful thoughts
Are worse than sudden invasions
From the dark unknown.

8735

The heart's satisfaction-train
Runs only
On life's perfection-track.

8736

If you want to stop anything
In your life,
Then stop your mind's suspicion-train
Immediately!

8737

Set your mind's
Captive thoughts
Free.
Make your mind's
Oneness-will
Perfect.

8738

The mind's imperfections
Fade
At the heart's perfection-gate.

8739

Although my mind is not fully awake,
I shall break asunder
My mind's prison cell.

8740

For a God-loving
Cheerful heart
There can be no mask
Of fear.

8741

My sleepless hunger
Is supremely blessed
By the Compassion-Heart of Time.

8742

A gratitude-heart
Is man's most beautiful
And powerful gift to God.

8743

Today's problems are killing you.
Why do you want to be totally annihilated
By inviting tomorrow's problems?

8744

By cursing your mind's darkness-night
You cannot conquer it.
If you want to conquer it,
Then disperse it.

8745

I really do not know why and how
You have become a slave
To your desire-master.
Alas, the time has not yet come
For you to see
God's Perfection-Eye.

8746

God is taking care of His world.
If you do not find fault with it,
Then He will grant you the opportunity
To blow your own triumph-trumpet.

8747

God does not want me
To pitch His celestial Camp
In feeble earth-bound time.

8748

Although every day
God teaches you how to smile,
Your success
Still remains a far cry.
Alas, alas!

8749

O Lord,
Do give me the capacity
To love Your Silence-Sound
Infinitely more than I love
Anything else.

8750

Each God-seeker
Breathlessly reveals
The fountain-life
Of God's Compassion-Heart.

8751

God's Satisfaction-Smile
Can come
Only from your heart's
Perfection-peace.

8752

God will welcome your heart-smile,
The dreamer,
And not your vital-laughter,
The stranger.

8753

The mind-monkey
Quite often climbs up and sits
On the indifference-tree.

8754

Inside you I see nothing
Except a growing mountain
Of useless frustration.

8755

All your stupendous plans
Will be foiled
Because you do not want God
To be involved in your plans.

8756

Unless you make your soul
A prime candidate
For God-manifestation,
You will never have
Perfection-success
And
Progress-satisfaction.

8757

O my mind, how long
Are you going to allow ignorance
To strike you mercilessly?

8758

He thinks that he has learned
A great many God-inspired
And man-loving things
From his failure-life.

8759

Strangling doubts
Have destroyed
Your life's silver boat.

8760

When your mind is for God-denial,
Ask your heart immediately
To illumine your stupid mind.

8761

You fool!
How can your restless mind-boat sail
In God's Peace-Delight-Sea?

8762

Have you done enough for God?
If not,
Sleeplessly and breathlessly follow
Your soul's peerless guidance.

8763

You talk so much
About desire-poison.
When are you going to speak
About aspiration-antidote?

8764

You must know by this time
That there is no difference
Between your mind's advice
And a dangerous voice.

8765

Re-educate your mind,
But this time be very careful.
You must not allow your mind
To enter into the dark doubt-room.

8766

The supreme need for soulfulness
Will eventually be acknowledged
In the fulfilment of fulness.

8767

If you want to continue to climb
To meet the Infinite,
You must continually purify and divinise
Your finite life.

8768

The mind locks the life's door,
And then it completely forgets
Where it has kept the key.

8769

If you wish me to remember you
Every day,
Then every day learn from me
The lesson of non-expectation.

8770

If you want to build
Anything in life,
Then build every night
Beauty's birthless and deathless dreams.

8771

The human tries to limit
The divine.
The divine tries to inspire
The human
To enjoy the unlimited God-Delight.

8772

The depth
That responds to the height
And the height
That responds to the depth
Bring down the message
From the Unknowable.

8773

United lives
Have peace.
Liberated souls
Reveal and distribute peace.

8774

A devoted oneness-life
Is the seeker's fulness-perfection
For God.

8775

Do not discourage God
From building
His Peace-Pavilion
Inside your heart-garden.

8776

True,
Heaven is God's private property.
But He tells everyone
That it belongs to all.
Anybody can rightfully claim
To own it.

8777

My life of aspiration
May not have the capacity
To enter into Infinity,
But it does have the feeling
That one day it will find
Its safe place
In a small corner of Infinity.

8778

His life's inner ear
Makes him hear
The fast descent
Of God's Compassion-Footsteps.

8779

Faith-happiness
Is a very rare visitor
To the doubting mind.

8780

When darkness imitates light,
Can you not be more sincere
And more determined
To know the unreal from the real?

8781

My human mind
Thinks this world was created
By an illusion-maker.

8782

How can a heartless life ever have
An all-knowing
And earth-guiding
Eye?

8783

If you keep your dreams unfulfilled
For a long time,
They will be suffocated
Inside the mind-storm-room.

8784

Now that you have opened
Your heart's sealed door,
God will proudly invite you
Everywhere
To play with Him.

8785

His heart is beckoned
From Above
By the divinity of silence-peace.

8786

If you have
A true aspiration-heart,
Then you will have no problem
With the mystery of the inaccessible.

8787

A disobedient mind
Is a colossal failure
Of the soul.

8788

Only an illumined mind
Knows
What a liberated life
Does for God.

8789

It is not so easy
To accept one's own sufferings,
But it is quite easy
To accept and tolerate
The sufferings of others.

8790

Today
We call it frustration.
Tomorrow
That very thing we call
A huge destruction.

8791

While you are meditating,
Mental complacency
And vital restlessness
Are nothing but
Your immediate destruction.

8792

For a new beginning
Remain soulful, cheerful and beautiful
Inwardly and outwardly,
And remind yourself each day
Of the supreme necessity
Of your spiritual life,
Your God-life.

8793

Every day an inner flower
Is blossoming inside my heart.
Every day my heart
Is growing into
A new beauty's purity-flower.

8794

His unfortunate discovery:
Aspiration-life and realisation-life
Are real;
Manifestation-life is all false.

8795

He came to God
To see only a little bit
Of Height and Magnitude.
But God's Magnanimity
Made him a true member
Of God's chosen Family.

8796

By virtue of his heart's
Expansion-love,
Today he has become
Inseparably one
With God the Supreme Lover.

8797

Why are you only appreciating
The flowers and fruits of the tree?
Where is your eagerness
To pluck the flowers and fruits
And have them for your own,
Very own?

8798

Just because
You do not want to have
God's inner Wealth,
You do not see it,
Even while looking at
God's all-illumining inner Wealth
Face to face.

8799

It is your heart's inner volcano-intensity
That sees and feels
What your Inner Pilot is offering to you
And inspires you
To become inseparably one with Him.

8800

If you want to see God happy,
Then offer Him first
Your own happiness-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 88, Agni Press, 1984
