41 How can I be happy?

How can I be happy?
I am torn between
A misunderstanding mind
And
A misunderstood heart.

How can I be happy?
I am torn between
An ignoring Heaven
And
An ignored earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, This is God's Home, Agni Press, 1975
