4. She and the world

She plays merrily,
        Therefore
The world likes her.

She speaks sweetly,
        Therefore
The world loves her.

She sings melodiously,
        Therefore
The world adores her.

She blesses divinely,
        Therefore
The world constantly needs her.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 4, Agni Press, 1974
