Fisherman

A fisherman's necessity compels him to wait for a fish. His curiosity compels him to take the weight of the fish. With our patience let us wait for God. We shall be rewarded. With our gratitude let us try to weigh God's Love. We shall be awarded.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Orchestra, Agni Press, 1973
