The morning light in a seeker's eye
Is beautiful.
But the blazing sun inside a seeker's heart
Is not only infinitely more beautiful
But also infinitely more powerful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 239, Agni Press, 1997
