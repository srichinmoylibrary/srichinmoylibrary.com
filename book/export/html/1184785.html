457. Reality is near, reality is far

Reality is near,
Reality is far.
When I say God and I
Need each other,
Reality is near
And
I can touch it.
When I say I only need God
Or God only needs me,
Then reality is not only far,
But also hopelessly invisible.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
