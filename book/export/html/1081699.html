140. Who has it?

Stronger than the giant
Is faith.
Who has it?
Not I, not I,
I solemnly declare.
God smilingly says:
"Amen."
Needless to say, God is great.

Brighter than the sun
Is love.
Who has it?
Not I, not I,
I solemnly declare.
God lovingly says:
"Amen."
Needless to say, God is good.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
