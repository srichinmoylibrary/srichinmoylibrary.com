13. Do doubt, do question!

O my doubtful eyes,
When I think I am
Perfect perfection,
Do doubt my unprecedented discovery!

O my questioning mind,
When I act like a god,
Do question my authority!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
