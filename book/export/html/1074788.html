222. Lord and son

        Lord,
My service is at Your disposal.
Tell me what I can do for You.
        Son,
Empty your mind.        Lord,
My service is at Your disposal.
Tell me what I should do for You.
        Son,
Fill up your heart.        Lord,
My service is at Your disposal.
Tell me what I must do for You.
        Son,
Smile! Smile in the heart of the body,
Smile in the life of the soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 5, Agni Press, 1973
