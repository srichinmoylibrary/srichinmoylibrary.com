707. The sky is alive

The sky is alive
With trumpets.
The sky is alive
With stars and planets.
The sky is alive
With the sun and the moon.
The sky is alive
With something else:
My satisfaction-light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
