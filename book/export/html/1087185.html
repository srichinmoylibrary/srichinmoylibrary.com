The soundless flute

I went into a music store in Lima to buy a flute. I was trying a particular flute, but I could not make any sound.

Then the saleslady said she would show me how to play it. She was so happy and proud to show me, but she could not make any sound either.

-7 January 1987

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
