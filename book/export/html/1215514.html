7. What did God tell you?

When did I love you?
I loved you when God loved you.
Where did I see you?
I saw you where God saw you.
What did I tell you?
I told you what God told you.

What did God tell you?
God told you that
     He does not believe
           In the existence of death,
     He does not believe
           In the existence of ignorance.
     He does not believe
           In death-dart,
     He does not believe
           In ignorance-clay.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When God-Love descends, Agni Press, 1975
