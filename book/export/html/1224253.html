Taba Katha Chara

Taba katha chara
Mukhe kichu asena
Taba bani chara
Kan kichu shunena
Taba kripa chara
Man kichu bhabena
Taba hasi chara
Hiya kichu chahena

Translation:

Except Your thought,
I allow not my mind
To be occupied with anything.
Your message alone
   I listen to.
My mind thinks only of one thing
And that is Your infinite Compassion-Light.
My heart wants nothing, save and except
     Your blessingful Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 1, , 1978
