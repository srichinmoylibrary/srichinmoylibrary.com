871 As God's Daughter...

AS GOD'S DAUGHTER

You were born
As God's daughter.

You are living
As God's daughter.

You shall teach the world
As God's daughter.

You can and you shall
Transform
The face of the whole world
As God's daughter.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
