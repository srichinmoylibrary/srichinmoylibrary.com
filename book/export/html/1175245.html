2300 I Know

I know, I know,
Because I love God
The clouds of confusion
Will disappear today.

I know, I know,
Because I obey God
The skies of adversity
Will clear tomorrow.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 23, Agni Press, 1982
