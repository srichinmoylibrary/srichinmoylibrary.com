62. My heart knows

In the morning
My heart knows what emptiness is.

In the afternoon
My heart knows what vastness is.

In the evening
My heart knows what God-compassion is.

At night
My heart knows what God-satisfaction is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
