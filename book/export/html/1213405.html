16.

My mind claims that it knows
Many, many things,
If not everything.
My heart knows only one thing:
That day and night
My Lord's Forgiveness-River
Is flowing.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, part 1, Agni Press, 1996
