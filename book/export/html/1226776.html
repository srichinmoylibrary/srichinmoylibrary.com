Amar Jiban Amar Maran Mage Taba Pada

Amar jiban amar maran
Mage taba pada dhuli
Nimesher tare jibane marane
Tomare na jena bhuli

Translation:

My life and death are begging You,
O my Lord Supreme,
For the dust of Your Feet.
In life and death,
Even for a fleeting second,
Do not allow me to forget You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
