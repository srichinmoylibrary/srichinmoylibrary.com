1606 Awake, Arise!

Awake!
The beautiful and smiling Heaven
Is waiting for you.
It wants to give you
Its perfection-joy.

Arise!
The soulful and crying earth
Is waiting for you.
It desires
Your aspiration-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 17, Agni Press, 1981
