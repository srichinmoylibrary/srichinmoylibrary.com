A God-Man

Not what you receive,
But what you give
Makes you a God-Man.

Not what you conceive,
But what you achieve
Makes you a God-Man.

Not how many times you try,
But how soulfully you cry
Makes you a God-Man.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God-Life: is it a far cry?, Agni Press, 1974
