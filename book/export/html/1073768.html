Troglodyte

(cave-dweller)

To be a sleepless
Truth-seeker and God-lover
And not a mere troglodyte
Is the divine purpose
Of my life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
