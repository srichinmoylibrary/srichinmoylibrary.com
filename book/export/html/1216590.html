951. Outer and inner

        Water
We need for outer purity.
        Love
We need for inner purity.        Powder
We need for outer beauty.
        Humility
We need for inner beauty.        Effort
We need for outer success.
        Surrender
We need for inner success.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
