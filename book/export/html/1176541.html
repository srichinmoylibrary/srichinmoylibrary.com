3583 Do You Know?

Do you know what
You unmistakably want?
No!

Do you know who
Unconditionally loves you?
No!

Do you know if or when
You are going to re-establish
Your friendship with God?
No!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 36, Agni Press, 1982
