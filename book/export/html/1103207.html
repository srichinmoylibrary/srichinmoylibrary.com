24 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You want me to offer You
Pure devotion at every moment.
My Supreme, I am all ready.
My heart is ready,
My mind is ready,
My vital is ready
And
My body is ready
To offer You devotion
At every moment.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 November 1998, 5:23 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
