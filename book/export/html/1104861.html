13.

My Lord, my Lord,
My implicit faith
In my God-surrender-run
Is speedily and safely
Taking me
To my God-Destination
Of the ever-blossoming
And ever-heightening Beyond.

18 March 2000
New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 2004
