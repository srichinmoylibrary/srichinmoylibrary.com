3339

You fool!
Your earthly credentials
Will not change
Heaven's opinion of you.
For Heaven to appreciate
And admire you,
You need only one
Heavenly credential,
And that is your sleepless
Self-offering.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 34, Agni Press, 1984
