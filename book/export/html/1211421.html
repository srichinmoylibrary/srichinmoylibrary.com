25530

When science and spirituality
Receive an invitation-letter from God,
Spirituality immediately accepts it
With love, devotion and surrender,
But science does not even take the trouble
Of reading the invitation.
Science just discards it!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 256, Agni Press, 1998
