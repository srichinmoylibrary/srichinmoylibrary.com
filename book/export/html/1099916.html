81. God and the world

The world and God:
How to harmonise?
Difficult? No.
No wide surprise.

Our Father is God;
The world, our Mother.
This living truth
Our protecting cover.

Father is the Face,
Mother the Smile.
Without the one,
The other futile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Flute, Agni Press, 1972
