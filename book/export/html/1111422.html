She is ahead

At the start of the seven-mile race, I saw X go way ahead of me. I said, "Oh, she is so far ahead of me!" But she dropped dead before the one-mile mark. She had to stop running after one mile, but that I didn't see.

4 January 1981

From: Sri Chinmoy, Run and become, become and run, part 4, Agni Press, 1981
