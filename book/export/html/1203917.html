Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 182

18101

My Lord Supreme,
I know that You have
Countless secrets.
Please share with me
Only one particular secret.
What do You go through
To please
Each and every human being?

18102

I must remain
Inside my heart-fountain
So that I do not see
My mind's high barriers.

18103

God wants to tell you
His Life-Stories
Even if you do not want
To listen to Him.

18104

Two things God never does:
He does not stop
Speaking to me
And He does not stop
Waiting for me.

18105

My self-giving heart
Is the place
Where God's new Hopes
Will take birth.

18106

You can be proud of yourself
When your mind runs
Only towards God,
And not before.

18107

The beauty
Of sincere aspiration
And the purity
Of sincere dedication
Can and will
Definitely take the seeker
To his Source immortal.

18108

God tells me
That every time I move forward
I give Him
A prayerful and beautiful gift.

18109

Do not be afraid
Of going too far
With your dedication-life.
At one point
God's smiling Eyes
Will greet you.

Do not be afraid
Of going too deep
In your aspiration-life.
At one point
God's loving Heart
Will bless you.

18110

Do not try to understand God.
You will make Him cry.
Try to feel God.
You will make Him not only smile,
But also dance.

18111

When I pray,
I cry for God's Compassion-Sky.
When I meditate,
I long for God's Satisfaction-Sun.

18112

Alas, quite often
We desire so many things
That we do not actually need
At all.

18113

Before I make
Any complaints to God,
God closes not only
His Ears and Eyes
But even His Heart.

18114

With my heart's tearful songs
I try to please God,
But God wants me to please Him
With my life's cheerful stories.

18115

The heart-doorman
Always has the capacity
To detect
The clever mind-thief.

18116

In the morning
I devotedly cling
To God's Compassion.
In the evening
I self-givingly long
For God's Satisfaction.

18117

If we do not practise
Patience-game,
We will not be allowed to participate
In God's Satisfaction-Game.

18118

If you enjoy idle thoughts,
You will be forced to surrender
To ignorance-immobility.

18119

God wants me
To choose my thoughts
Wisely.
God wants me
To choose my friends
Self-givingly.

18120

When I am gracious
In my thoughts,
I become precious
In God's Eye.

18121

A purity-heart
And a sincerity-promise
Have no parallel.

18122

We must transcend
Our mind-boundaries
If we want to be
The choice of Heaven
And the voice of earth.

18123

You can cry
If you want to,
But God prefers smiling
If you want to be receptive
To His Blessings.

18124

Man's ingratitude
And God's Forgiveness,
Man's desire
And God's Compassion,
Are to be found everywhere.

18125

He who can face
The tragedies of life
Can challenge
The frowns of death.

18126

What my mind needs
Is illumination.
What my heart needs
Is satisfaction.
What I need
Is perfection.

18127

Again and again
My tricky mind
Tries to charm God.
Needless to say,
Again and again
It fails.

18128

When I am
A sincere lover of God,
God becomes
My sure employer.

18129

Simplicity, sincerity,
Purity and humility
Are the members
Of the heart-garden-family.

18130

Do not allow anybody
To touch your heart's
Enthusiasm-rose-petals.

18131

O seeker of the Lord Supreme,
Not only fast and faster
But fastest
Will be your progress
If you catch
Your pride-thief.

18132

Alas,
How can I make any progress
In my spiritual life
If I do not stop for good
Playing with
Earthly attachment-toys?

18133

If you enjoy your self-flattery
Inside your mind-kingdom,
Then you will be forced to participate
In the backward race.

18134

God does not want us
To sound the success-trumpet,
But to play
The progress-flute.

18135

Self-confidence
Is of paramount importance.
Otherwise, sooner or later
We will all be strangled
By the ever-increasing fear
Of the mind.

18136

My Lord Supreme,
I am so grateful to You,
For now I see and feel
My inner doubt-suspicion-storms
Subsiding.

18137

I take patience
As a golden opportunity
To illumine my unlit mind
And strengthen my weak heart.

18138

The tears of the heart
Are uncontrollable
When the life suffers from
Surrender-shortage.

18139

Be not in between
Your heart's soulful smile
And your mind's hurtful laughter
In your aspiration-dedication-world.

18140

Compassion is a gift
From God the Knowable.
Forgiveness is a gift
From God the Unknowable.

18141

May my life-boat sleeplessly ply
Between
My mind's inspiration-shore
And my heart's aspiration-shore.

18142

God does not blame you
If you are impure,
But God does blame you
If you do not want to offer
Your impurity to Him.

18143

The outer experiment-school
Is for everybody.
The inner experience-school
Is for the few.

18144

We may not all be candidates
For God's Compassion,
But we are all candidates
For God's Forgiveness.

18145

Do not worry about
Your belated gratitude to God,
For whenever you offer
Your gratitude to God,
God will be more than pleased
With you.

18146

Nobody can clip
Your hope-bird's wings
If you do not want them to.

18147

Alas, my mind does not want to know
That I can think
Beyond what it thinks.
Alas, my heart does not want to know
That I can feel
Beyond what it feels.

18148

You expect so much
From God.
How is it that you do not
Allow God
To expect a little more
From you?

18149

The sincere heart knows
How to appreciate.
The clever mind knows
How to appropriate.

18150

Alas, alas, I did not know
That my desire-dust
Would blind the Eyes
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

18151

If it is true
That I have disappointed
My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Then it is equally true
That I am starting my life
All over again today.

18152

My bondage-heart
And my freedom-mind
Have equally made me
Unhappy.

18153

As my life
Is growing shorter every day,
Even so my faith in God
Is growing brighter every day.

18154

This morning
My Lord Beloved Supreme told me
That He would like to give me
Two new names:
Readiness-humility
And
Willingness-purity.

18155

Only when I live inside my heart
Do I see
That my life's confidence-road
Has no obstruction.

18156

May I every day grow into
The unparalleled beauty
Of my heart's streaming tears.

18157

My Lord Supreme,
You have taught me many things.
Please teach me only one more thing:
Gratitude-multiplication.

18158

The human in us
Has to become divine
If it wants to greet the soul
For God-manifestation.

18159

I am lost between
My heart's confidence-light
And my mind's arrogance-night.

18160

The outer success-road
Is short
And full of apprehension.
The inner progress-road
Is long
And full of illumination.

18161

With each good thought
That we offer to mankind,
We perform a new miracle.

18162

Your self-giving heart
Is sweet,
Extremely sweet,
Breathlessly sweet to God.

18163

Cheerfulness, readiness and willingness
If we have,
Then we need nothing more
To please the Lord Supreme
In His own Way.

18164

O my Lord Supreme,
When will I realise
That I am not only in You
And with You,
But also always for You,
Only You?

18165

In the spiritual life,
Anything divine
Cannot be overdone.

18166

If gratitude cannot satisfy
Your God-joy-thirst,
Nothing and nobody can do it.

18167

My Lord,
Do blessingfully govern my life
As You do with the heart
Of the Heaven-life.

18168

Doubt we measure
Before we conquer.
Faith we measure
Before we offer to God.

18169

God tells me
That my earth-responsibility-shoulders
Can always carry more,
Infinitely more.

18170

Love divine
Definitely has the capacity
To conquer and illumine
Imperfection-life.

18171

The divine love
Can never withdraw
From any human being
Who aspires.

18172

By faith you can not only
Seek your way
But definitely arrive
At your destination.

18173

The dust of self-doubt
Can easily blind
The heart of faith.

18174

I never economise
On my concern-life
For aspiring humanity.

18175

When self-offering
Is sleepless,
Nothing fruitful
Can remain unattained.

18176

Each aspiration-blossom
Of man
Will be immortalised
By God
With His Pride divine.

18177

What we need
At every moment
Is an iota of humility-peace
To make us happy.

18178

It takes great courage
To become sincere.
It takes greater courage
And greatest courage
To become holy.

18179

God loves two things:
Our eagerness-preparation
And our soulfulness-dedication.

18180

In the morning, inner runners
Running along the Road of Eternity
Offer their prayerful and soulful salutation
To the rising Illumination-Sun.

18181

My Lord Supreme,
Do help me reclaim
My heart's long-forgotten
Aspiration-ground.

18182

Alas,
Will it ever be over,
The doubting mind's
Suspicion-night?

18183

O my mind,
Believe me,
God is not going to jump
Into your empty excuse-pool
To save you.

18184

If you walk
On the aspiration-road,
Dedication-travellers
Will accompany you.

18185

If you are a true seeker,
You must bravely remain
Above the smoke
Of past experiences.

18186

My unconditional surrender
Is nothing other than
My heart's confidence-freedom
In God.

18187

I am a God-believer.
At God's choice Hour,
The answers to all my life-problems
Will descend from Above.

18188

When I pray,
God gives me
The Joy of His Eye.
When I meditate,
God gives me
The Love of His Heart.

18189

If we do not pray,
Then desire will lead us
To an unattainable shoreless shore.

18190

Prayers
Are the beautiful wings
And meditations
Are the soulful birds
Of my heart.

18191

The journey's end of my heart
I may know someday.
The destination of my soul
Will remain forever unknowable
To me.

18192

The message of heart-expansion
Can never be false.
The heart has received this message
From the ever-transcending Beyond.

18193

God's Compassion-Sunshine
Will always bless
Our aspiration-heart-garden.

18194

There shall come a time
When the doubting mind
Will fall
At the illumining feet
Of faith.

18195

Do you want to know
What lethargy has done to you?
Lethargy has made your mind
Unthinkably impure,
And your heart
Unbearably insecure.

18196

God does not need
Your mind's explanation.
He needs only
Your heart's aspiration
To make Him happy.

18197

My Lord,
To better
My heart-life,
Do shatter
My mind-pride.

18198

Anger
Loudly demands justice.
But it is poise
That happily receives justice
From the Lord Supreme.

18199

God tells me
That if I do not study
At humility-school,
Then I will not be able
To go to Divinity-College
And Immortality-University.

18200

My Lord Supreme,
Today You have not given me something
For my own good.
But I know You will give it to me
When I am ready,
For You are all Wisdom-Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 182, Agni Press, 1993
