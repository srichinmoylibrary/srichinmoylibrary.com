5. My four points

     Crying
Was my starting point.
     Smiling
Is my finishing point.
     Loving
Shall be my ever-becoming point.
     Surrendering
Shall be my ever-transcending point.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
