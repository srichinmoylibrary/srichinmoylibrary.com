Destruction’s rod

O north, O South!
O east, O West!
I have no cry,
I have no nest.

O ceaseless fall,
An unfed hope,
A fearful sigh,
A tightened rope.

A living hush,
My imagined God,
Without, within,
Destruction’s rod.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God’s Glance and God’s Grace, Agni Press, 2006
