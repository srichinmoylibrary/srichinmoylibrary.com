Friendship

Friendship

1.

Friendship must needs be cherished by two great and good friends as God's divinely blessingful and supremely fruitful gift.

2.

Friendship is a supremely beautiful gift that God blessingfully gives to His fond seeker-children.

3.

Friendship is the flower-fragrance-bounty in the oneness-heart-garden of two self-giving seeker-souls.

4.

Friendship is the butterfly-play in the life-dance of today's Truth-seekers and tomorrow's God-lovers.

5.

Friendship is the perfection-bridge between earth's seeker-children and Heaven's lover-parents.

6.

Our friendship-bridge we cannot build with our own capacity. Our friendship-bridge is built by god Himself to fulfil His own supreme Necessity.

7.

Friendship means a perfection-foretaste-glimpse of Heaven.

8.

Friendship means two completely open heart-doors for each other to enjoy God's Fulness on earth.

9.

My true friend is he who loves me for what I inwardly am and not for what I outwardly have.

10.

To me, a seeker-friend is a rich supply of spiritual energy. When I help my friends, I am happy. When I serve my friends, I am fulfilled.

11.

When I help my friends, I am happy. When I serve my friends, I am fulfilled.

12.

My heart will never outgrow its need for friendship.

13.

My friends in silence remind me that I have infinitely more significant things to do here on earth than making complaints against my enemies.

14.

The vine that binds two friends together is adorned with gratitude-heart-blossoms.

15.

My friend is my victory-announcement-trumpet.

16.

Every day my heart cries for a new friend: God-seeking, God-loving, and God-serving.

17.

My friendship is the happy result of my heart's spontaneous, God-manifesting deci-sion.

18.

Friendship is not the greatness of our minds. Friendship is the goodness of our hearts.

19.

Forgive your friends. They will love infinitely more.

20.

What your friend needs most from you is your fondness-heart-power.

21.

A true friend is a miracle performed by God Himself.

22.

Because of our inner oneness, our outer friendship is so beautiful and so fruitful.

23.

I call it my friendship-expression. But God calls it His Heaven-expansion.

24.

When I see an aspiring friend of mine, I see his divinity around his entire being. Something more, I gloriously see my own divinity coming to the fore.

25.

Friendship sacredly breathes in this world. Therefore God has absolutely no intention of withdrawing from this world.

26.

Because of self-giving friendship this world of ours is exceedingly beautiful.

27.

My friend, do you know that God has given you these two beautiful and silence-flooded eyes to make me and keep me Heavenly and sleeplessly happy?

28.

Friendship means invitation. God blessingfully and cheerfully invites His sweet children to play inside His Satisfaction-Heart-Garden.

29.

Friendship of the mind sees no destination. Friendship of the heart sees destination everywhere.

30.

Friendship expands our sweet dream-horizons.

31.

A real friendship cannot be a frail and wavering bridge.

32.

Friendship is the soil where happiness proudly grows.

33.

Heaven blessingfully loves my soul-friendship. Earth affectionately loves my heart-friendship. I proudly love my life-friendship.

34.

The beauty of newness-friendship I have.
The fragrance of oneness-friendship I need.

35.

Self-possessive friendship eventually strangles itself.
Oneness-assertive friendship fulfils itself.

36.

Friendship disappears when selfishness invades.

37.

Suspect not, if you want your friendship to last long.
Expect not, if you want your friendship to last not only the longest, but forever.

38.

If you want to maintain your friendship, be careful of your suspicion-volcano.

39.

If you want to maintain your friendship, never build the supremacy-tower.

40.

If you want to maintain your friendship, readiness has to be your mind, willingness has to be your heart.

41.

Only a true friendship-ship can weather each and every storm.

42.

Friendship is not a secret life, but a sacred breath.

43.

Friendship must never try to escape from mutuality-responsibility.

44.

Friendship does not live in a division-mind.

45.

Friendship lives in a multiplication-heart.

46.

My friendship with Heaven depends on what I am.

47.

My friendship with earth depends on what I give.

48.

Friendship gives me nectar-bliss-drink.

49.

Friendship can weave the garland of joy in no time.

50.

When friendship and happiness converse, permit not doubt to interrupt.

51.

Friendship must never be buried under the weight of misunderstanding.

52.

Friendship is a heart-climber that arrives at the delight-peak.

53.

Friendship is a secret and sacred wisdom of millennia.

54.

Friendship must realise that the doubt-boat will always be on the verge of sinking.

55.

Friendship is a shower of joy-petals from Heaven.

56.

The mind-friendship is a fleeting mortal.

57.

The heart-friendship is an everlasting immortal.

58.

To have a solid friendship is to walk along peace-road.

59.

Friendship is the equal cheerfulness of two minds.

60.

Friendship is the equal fulness of two hearts.

61.

Friendship lasts when there is no struggle between the doubt of the mind and the faith of the heart.

62.

Friendship is the triumphant smile of the oneness-heart.

63.

The human life and its few years cannot afford to lose a real friendship.

64.

Friendship is the beginning of oneness-wisdom-light.

65.

Life is a complicated door, but friendship has the effective key.

66.

Do not lose friendship. Who wants to suffer a heart-shattering pain?

67.

I call it my friendship, but God calls it a beautiful rose in His Heart-Garden.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Friendship, Agni Press, 1995
