17 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
What is the meaning of disobedience?
"My child, My child,
Disobedience means an unavoidable
Disaster-life."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 November 1998, 6:24 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times. (50 right, 50 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
