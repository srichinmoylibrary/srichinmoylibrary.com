30 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Time and again I have broken
My God-fulfilment-promises,
Yet my Lord's Compassion,
Love and Affection
In infinite measure
Remain the same for me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 June 2000, 9:10 a.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
