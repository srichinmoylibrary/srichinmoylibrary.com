10 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall never, never, never allow
My disbelief-enemy
To enter into
My God-loving, God-searching
And God-crying mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 September 1999, 7:19 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm twenty-five times. (12 right, 13 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
