395. Yet my children love me

How little do I serve,
Yet my children love me dearly.
How little do I love,
Yet my children need me constantly.
Their humility-life and oneness-necessity
Feed me daily and still keep me alive.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
