879 In Love With God...

IN LOVE WITH GOD

In Heaven her soul
Is in love
With God's Dream.

On earth her life
Is in love
With God's Beauty.

In her heart
She is in love
With God's Infinity.

In God's Heart
She is in love
With God's Reality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
