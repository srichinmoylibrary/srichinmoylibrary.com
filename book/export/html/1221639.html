Tomar Kripa Karbo Adai

Tomar kripa karbo adai
Tomar charan dhariya
Shanti plaban pabo kebal
Tomar paye mariya

Translation:

Touching Your Feet,
I shall collect Your Compassion.
A flood of peace I shall get
Only when I die at Your Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eso eso prabhu, part 1, , 1998
