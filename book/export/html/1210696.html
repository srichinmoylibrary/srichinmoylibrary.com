24812

If there is anybody,
Either in Heaven or on earth,
As furious with me as myself
For wasting so much of God's Time
On earth,
Then that person is none other
Than my own soul!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 249, Agni Press, 1998
