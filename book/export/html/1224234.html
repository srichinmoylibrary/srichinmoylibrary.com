Nai Kona Ar Byetha

Nai kona ar byetha
Eseche namiya man pran dehe
     Prashanti nirabata
Ami mile gechi sajare
Dui mile sadare
Charidike mor ananda ghana
Madhumoy amarata

Translation:

No more suffering.
A sky of peace has descended.
My body, vital and mind
Are now in tranquillity-sea.
Within and without
I supremely enjoy
     Infinity’s infinite Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 1, , 1978
