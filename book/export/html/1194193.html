8473

My Lord Supreme,
Do make me conscious
Of the divine, supreme fact
That every day
You blessingfully keep in touch
With my heart's aspiration-cry
And my soul's illumination-smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 85, Agni Press, 1984
