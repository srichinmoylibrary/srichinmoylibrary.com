8 November 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must first have sleepless
And breathless love for You.
Only then can I have
Abiding faith in You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 November 1999, 7:47 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
