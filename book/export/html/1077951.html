304. Silence reigned

When he died
Silence reigned in Heaven,
Silence reigned on earth.

Earth-silence
Was another name
For earth's ignorance-indifference.
Heaven-silence
Was another name
For Heaven's gratitude-pride.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
