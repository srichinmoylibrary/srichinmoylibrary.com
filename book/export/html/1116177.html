2776

We can become
Choice instruments of God
  Only by
Thinking of Him alone,
Loving Him alone,
Devoting ourselves to Him alone
And surrendering our very existence
  To Him alone,
Sleeplessly and breathlessly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 3, Agni Press, 1998
