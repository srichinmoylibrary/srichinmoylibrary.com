5627

Before the race
They are all surrender.
They are praying for God's Victory.
But during the race
And after the race
They are thinking of
Nothing but their own victory!
I do not want you
To be in their company.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 57, Agni Press, 1984
