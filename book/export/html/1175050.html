2106 The God-Like Wish

How can you have
The God-like wish
To transform mankind
If you are your mind's
Impurity,
If you are your heart's
Insecurity and
If you are your life's
Uncertainty?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 22, Agni Press, 1981
