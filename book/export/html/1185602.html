20.

My Pilot Supreme most powerfully loves me
Because my soul knows
Who my Pilot Supreme is
And where my Pilot Supreme is.

My Pilot Supreme most affectionately
loves me
Because He knows
That soon my heart will start loving Him
Unconditionally.
