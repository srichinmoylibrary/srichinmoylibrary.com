2 Don't Be Aloof...

DON'T BE ALOOF

Don't be aloof.
God does not want that.
Don't be aloof.
The world cannot brook that.
Don't be aloof.
Your soul does not understand that.
Don't be aloof.
Your life may revolt
Against
Man, the future God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 16, Agni Press, 1974
