36 Twice I Am Thrilled ...

Twice I am thrilled
Beyond measure:
Once when I soulfully
Clasp God’s Eye,
And once when I hungrily
Devour the dust
Of God’s Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 56, Agni Press, 2008
