23268

With my mind-stories
I try to please God.
But He tells me that
He will never be satisfied
With my mind-stories.
He will be satisfied
With my heart-songs only.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 233, Agni Press, 1996
