12. Love, serve and surrender

        Love God.
You will be able
   To go to God.        Serve God.
God will definitely
   Come to you.        Surrender to God.
God will offer you
   Unmistakably
His transcendental Throne.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 11, Agni Press, 1974
