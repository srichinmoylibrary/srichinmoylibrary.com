27. The report

God, You want
The report on the planet earth?
Not so good.
I am afraid
It is dying.

Man, you want
The report on the star sun?
Quite good.
I am happy to say
It is descending
Into man's ignorance-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
