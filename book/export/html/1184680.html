352. If so is Thy Will

I am not mine own.
I know not what to say.
I move around with my beggarly heart.
I forgive myself,
My stupidity,
My existence-night
Only for Thee
To please Thee,
If so is Thy Will.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
