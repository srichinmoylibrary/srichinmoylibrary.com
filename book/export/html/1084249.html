19. No time

I love and love;
No time to hate!
I build and build;
No time to break!
I promise and promise;
No time to withdraw!
I become and become;
No time to see!
I am and am;
No time to dream!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 6, Agni Press, 1974
