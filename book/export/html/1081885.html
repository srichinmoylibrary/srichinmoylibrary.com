326. My lessons

Contention-lesson
I always get from my body.

Confusion-lesson
I always get from my vital.

Contradiction-lesson
I always get from my mind.

Illumination-lesson
I always get from my heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
