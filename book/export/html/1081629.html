70. O my teachers

O my mind,
My isolation-teacher,
No more do I need you.

O my heart,
My integration-teacher,
I need you ever-more.

O my vital,
My depression-teacher,
You have taught me
Far beyond my capacity.

O my body,
My humiliation-teacher,
You have taught me
Far beyond my necessity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
