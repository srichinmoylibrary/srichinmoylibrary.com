<em>Mr. George Riddell:</em> Do you practise celibacy?

Sri Chinmoy: Of course. Without celibacy, there can be no true spiritual life, not to speak of God-realisation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Earth's cry meets Heaven's smile, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
