173. Forget and forgive

What to forget?
Nothing.
Whom to forget?
None.
What to forgive?
Everything.
Whom to forgive?
Everyone.
What to illumine?
Ignorance.
Whom to illumine?
Your own self.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
