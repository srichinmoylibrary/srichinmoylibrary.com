3. Abandoned

Your body
You have abandoned to idleness.
His vital
He has abandoned to restlessness.
My mind
I have abandoned to forgetfulness.
God's Heart
God has abandoned to forgiveness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When I left God in Heaven, Agni Press, 1975
