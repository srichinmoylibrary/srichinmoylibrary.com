13.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

You are Your Kindness infinite.
Every morning
I receive a handwritten
Compassion-Inspiration-Letter
From You.
Every evening
I receive a handwritten
Satisfaction-Pride-Letter
From You.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/8 December 2005, 3:58 a.m. Pangkor Island. Perak, Malaysia. Seated Double-Dumbbell Lift: 500 lbs./

Sri Chinmoy the Eternal Beginner: (Blue violin):
1. Proem
2. I Came to Your Lotus-Feet

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 19, Agni Press, 2006
