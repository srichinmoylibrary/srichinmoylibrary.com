My soul tells my heart:
"Follow me, I am your leader."
My mind tells my heart:
"No, no, follow me, I am your leader."
My poor heart does not know
What to do.
My poor heart is completely lost.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 179, Agni Press, 1993
