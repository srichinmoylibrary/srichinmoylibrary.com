45 This Morning My Lord Supreme ...

This morning my Lord Supreme
Came to me and said,
“My child, I am now making you
A permanent member
Of My Universal Heart-Society.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 16, Agni Press, 2003
