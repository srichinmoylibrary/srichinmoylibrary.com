WARRIORS

Life is a battle
Between
Desire-warriors
And
Aspiration-warriors.
God
Does not want
Desire-warriors
To win
Because
They want to dominate
The world.
God
Desperately wants
Aspiration-warriors
To win
Because
They want to liberate
The world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
