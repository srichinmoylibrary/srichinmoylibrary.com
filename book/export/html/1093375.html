5.

My Lord,
Are You aware
Of the bad things
That I do?
My child,
No, I am not aware,
And I don't want to be.
My Lord, why?
Just because
You don't care to know
How many good things I do.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My child, you and I are in the same boat, Agni Press, 1991
