30. Just breathe a prayer

Just breathe a prayer.
God shall be
Your Impurity-chaser.
Just breathe a prayer.
God shall be
Your Duty-performer.
Just breathe a prayer.
God shall be
Your eternal Partner.
Just breathe a prayer.
God shall be
Your unconditional Lover.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 8, Agni Press, 1974
