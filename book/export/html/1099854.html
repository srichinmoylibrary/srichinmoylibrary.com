19. Never to meet again

Never to meet again:
     My yesterday's face,
     My backward race,
Never to meet again.

Never to meet again:
     Swift fear the thief,
     Wild doubt the chief,
Never to meet again.

Never to meet again:
     The clasp of death
     And Satan's breath,
Never to meet again.

Never to meet again:
     Chinmoy the failure,
     Ignorance pure,
Never to meet again.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Flute, Agni Press, 1972
