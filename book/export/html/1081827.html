268. Two machines

Father, I need
From You only two machines:
Concern-machine
And
Compassion-machine.

"Son, you need two
Machines from me,
But
I need only one machine
From you:
Surrender-machine.
Let us give and take
And be all even."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
