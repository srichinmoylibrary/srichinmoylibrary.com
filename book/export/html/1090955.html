God is proud of him

He is devoted;
  Therefore
God's Heart of Love
  And
God's Soul of Light
Are fond of him.

He is concentrated;
  Therefore
God the Creator
  And
God the creation
Are exceedingly proud of him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Meditation: man's choice and God's Voice, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
