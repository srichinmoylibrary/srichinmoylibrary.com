18 April 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Who can inspire You
   And encourage You,
If not my heart's absolutely sincere
   And pure promises?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 April 1999, 6:47 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.) Lift number thirty-two.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
