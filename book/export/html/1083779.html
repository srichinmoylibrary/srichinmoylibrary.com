6. His own world

Unlike others
He has only two
Theoretical problems:
Nobody cares for him;
God does not love him.

Unlike others
He has only two
Practical problems:
Since he does not
Belong either to this world
Or to any world,
He needs a world created by himself
And not those
Already created by God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Vision-Promise, Agni Press, 1974
