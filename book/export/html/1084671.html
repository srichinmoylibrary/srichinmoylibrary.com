33. O sleepless ones

O sleepless silence,
My heart desires
Your beauty's height.
O sleepless sound,
My body desires
Your duty's joy.
O sleepless God,
My life needs
Your Perfection-light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 14, Agni Press, 1974
