87 My Life's Perfection-Manifestation ...

My life's perfection-manifestation
Is not more of God
And less of me.
My life's perfection-manifestation
Is all in God, all with God
And all for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 1, Agni Press, 2000
