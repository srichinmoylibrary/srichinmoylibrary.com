266. Alas

Alas,
My native seas tell me:
I have lost my heart's depth.

Alas,
My native skies tell me:
I have lost my mind's vastness.

Alas,
My native sun tells me:
I have lost my life's divinity.

Alas,
My native gods tell me:
I have lost my soul's immortality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
