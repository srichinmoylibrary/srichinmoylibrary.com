Pechan Pane Chai Na Phire Age Jali

Pechan pane chai na phire
Age jali dhire dhire
Path je amai nitya dake
Haowa tenelai amake
Chala amar sahoj khela
Hoini amar asan mela
Ghor ban dhini nadir tire
Jiban bhela bhasai nire
Jadi dube asbena ar phire
Bhoi kari na mayer kripa ache amai ghire

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
