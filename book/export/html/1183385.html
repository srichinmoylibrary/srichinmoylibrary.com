249. Confusion and aspiration

To him,
Confusion worse than depression.
Confusion worse than frustration.
Confusion worse than destruction.

To him,
Aspiration better than realisation.
Aspiration better than revelation.
Aspiration better than manifestation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
