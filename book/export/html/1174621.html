1682 I Imitate

I imitate.
I want my mind to imitate.
What is the thing
That we are going to imitate?
The thing we are going to imitate
Is the fragrance-rose
Of my purity-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 17, Agni Press, 1981
