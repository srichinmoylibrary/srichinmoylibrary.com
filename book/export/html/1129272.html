15858

At every moment
Countless miracles take place
  In the inner world,
But we call them mere incidents.
Yet when something unusual takes place
  In the outer world,
We call it a miracle!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 16, Agni Press, 1999
