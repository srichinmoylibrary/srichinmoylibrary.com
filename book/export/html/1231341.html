Satya Swarup Bhagaban

Satya swarup bhagaban
Satyer dhyane tanmoy karo
Darashan karo dan

Translation:

O Lord, O Truth Supreme,
May I lose myself at every moment
Contemplating You,
Your Compassion-Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, teach me how to surrender, , 1975
