298

Heaven is indifferent to me.
That is no reason
Why my soul shall not go back
To its celestial abode.

The world does not appreciate me.
That does not mean
I shall not unreservedly
Give to the world
What I have
And what I am.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 3, Agni Press, 1983
