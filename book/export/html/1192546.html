6842

His spiritual life has become
Nothing but a stagnant pool.
He will undoubtedly be washed away
When new seekers come
With a new hope
And a new promise
To manifest the Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 69, Agni Press, 1984
