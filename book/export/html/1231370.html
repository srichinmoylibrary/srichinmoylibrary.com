Tomar Sebai Jiban Amar

Tomar sebai jiban amar tushta hate chai
Anya sakal amar chaowa dure jeno dhai

Translation:

By serving You alone,
My life wants to be satisfied.
All the desires have flown away.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Swadeshe bideshe ghuriya berai, , 2007
