21 November 1998 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May my heart aspire
To Your complete Satisfaction.
May my life serve
To Your complete Satisfaction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 November 1998, 5:03 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs one-hundred times — (50 right, 50 left) — for a new record of 2 mins 13 secs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
