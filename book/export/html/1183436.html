300. Hopeless and useless

Father, I know I am hopeless
And useless.
But since You have taken me
Into Your inner circle
Will You not help me to be worthy
Of that intimate circle?
I feel You certainly can.
Daughter, I certainly must.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
