923. Like you I wish to be

Matter, my dear,
Like you I wish to be explicit.

"Ah, that's easy,
Spirit dear.
Just touch my feet.
   Difficult?
Then sit at my feet.
   That's all!"

Spirit, my dear,
Like you I want to be implicit.

"Ah, that's very easy,
Matter dear.
Just enter into my heart
        And
Live there forever.
   Difficult?
Then feel my heart.
   That's all!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
