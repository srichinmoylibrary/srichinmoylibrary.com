839. You have all

O mind,
You have all the crowning thoughts.
O heart,
You have all the soulful thoughts.
O life,
You have everything:
Suffering-world,
Struggling-world,
Striving-world,
Imperfection-world,
Ingratitude-world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
