23185

We all need fulfilment.
Therefore, why do we hesitate
To go to the right person
Who is all eagerness to bless us
With fulfilment?
Who is the right person?
None other than your own highest self!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 232, Agni Press, 1996
