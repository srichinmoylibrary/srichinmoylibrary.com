Jara Jete Chai

Jara jete chai
Jete de tadere
Tora ai tora ai
Tader lagiya amar swapan
Hridayer anginai
Tora ai tora ai
Niye jabo ami tader sabare
Asimer simanai

Translation:

Let them go, those who want to go;
You come, you come.
It is for you that I have
Garnered my sweet dreams
In my garden-heart.
You come, you come.
I shall, without fail,
Carry you all
To the shores
Of the infinite Beyond.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 4, , 1981
