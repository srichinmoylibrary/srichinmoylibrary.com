157. Please accept me

Please accept me
As your disciple.
I shall not disappoint you.
I was born with sincerity.
Please accept me
As your Master.
I shall not deceive you.
I shall die with sincerity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sound becomes, silence is, Agni Press, 1975
