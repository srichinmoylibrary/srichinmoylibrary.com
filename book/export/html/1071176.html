34. Whether You like it or not

My Lord Supreme,
Whether You like it or not,
I shall come to You daily.
Whether You like it or not,
I shall tease You smilingly.
Whether You like it or not,
I shall place You on my shoulders surprisingly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, 207 Flower-Flames, Agni Press,
