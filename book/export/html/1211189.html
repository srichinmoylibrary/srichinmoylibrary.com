Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 254

25301

Because I love the only God only,
I also love inside all human beings
The same God only.

25302

A single-minded God-seeker
Will, without fail, arrive
At his destined Goal.

25303

If you are a God-surrender-failure-seeker,
Then how can you hope to have
An iota of peace, light and bliss
Either in your inner life
Or in your outer life?

25304

If you have an undisciplined vital,
Then definitely you are rushing towards
A volcano-eruption-disaster.

25305

When you descend in your spiritual life,
If you do not feel a sense of real loss,
Then you have gone far away
From your own divinity-heart-home.

25306

I wish to be
A perpetual student
Of the world-unity university.

25307

My world-oneness-heart
Is cherished dearly
By God's own Fulness-Heart.

25308

We can change the course of our life-river
For the better
If we create in ourselves
A constant God-receptivity-hunger.

25309

Alas,
The whole world
Is at the mercy
Of the devouring freedom-mind.

25310

My Lord Supreme,
Encircle my entire life
With the Smiles
Of Your Compassion-Eye.

25311

What you need every day
Is a new wave
Of God-fulfilment-promises.

25312

The only mission of my heart-life
Is God's full manifestation
Here on earth.

25313

I can easily deal with
The whole world,
But not with my God-doubting mind.

25314

If you want to make God smile,
Then you must appreciate, admire
And adore your soul.

25315

God came and touched
My sinking heart.
Lo, my heart is now rising
High, higher, highest!

25316

My Lord, You have possessed
The whole world.
You can easily possess
My confusion-jungle-mind
And save me from its ruthless torture.

25317

The only food of our ignorance-mind
Is the multiplicity
Of self-doubts.

25318

Until now
If you have not believed
That your Master suffers for you,
Then start believing right from now!
Your Master does suffer for you —
Far beyond the flight of your imagination.

25319

Nothing can remain
Permanently futile,
Since God compassionately breathes
In everything.

25320

The glowing purpose of your life
Is to run fast, faster, fastest
Towards the farthest and brightest Goal.

25321

Aspiration starts the Cosmic Game,
And illumination ends the Game.
God-Satisfaction continues the Game.

25322

My Lord,
I am going to give You
Everything that is good in me
Completely.
"My child,
Unless you give Me everything bad as well,
I am not going to accept it!"

25323

My soul asks my heart, mind,
Vital and body,
"My children, how long will you continue
Enjoying your bondage-night?"
Alas, not even one person answers!
They are all silent.

25324

Since today is not tomorrow,
I must fulfil first
The dreams that I have today,
And pay no attention
To tomorrow's dreams.

25325

God invites each and every human being
To come to study
At transformation-school.
But most of us are frightened to death.
Therefore, we do not accept
God's Invitation.

25326

One positive thought
Can be of tremendous help
To clear our mind-jungle.

25327

We must always hold fast
To higher ideals.
If we lower our ideals,
We shall meet with nothing
But real frustration
And false satisfaction.

25328

To make me feel that God
Is close to my heart and my life,
Every day I keep a heart-diary.

25329

A child is all heart.
Therefore, he is God's
Proudest Smile.

25330

Perfection dawns
Through regular practice
In a punctual manner.

25331

The moment I say and feel
That I am an eternal beginner,
I become the possessor
Of boundless joy.

25332

Alas, why do I not pray to God
Every day
To soulfully grow into something
Infinitely more divine?
Alas, why?

25333

God's beloved is he
Who loves to cry and cry
For God's constant Satisfaction
In his life.

25334

Do not think that whatever is inside you
Is all yours.
No, no, no!
It all belongs to God,
And to God-Compassion
And God-Forgiveness.

25335

When your inner consciousness awakens,
You will at once become the beauty
Of your mounting aspiration-flames.

25336

My aspiration-heart
Is definitely my life's
God-surrender-smile.

25337

Willingness is of paramount importance
To have a free access to God's
Satisfaction-Smile-Kingdom.

25338

What can purify
My impurity-mind
Is my heart's
Ever-increasing tears.

25339

"We obey because we love."
This is so beautiful.
But "we love because we obey"
Is infinitely more beautiful
And infinitely more perfect.

25340

Do not depend on others.
Depend only on God's Compassion-Eye.
It will immediately tell you
'Yes' or 'no'.

25341

May my life every day glow and grow
With the Compassion-flooded Silence-Smile
Of my Lord Supreme.

25342

Whenever I cultivate
Unconditional surrender
To God's Will,
I see God and God's Pride
Dancing together.

25343

My Lord never takes any break
From the world's anxieties and worries.
Why then do I beg God
Even for a short break?

25344

The day I miss my morning meditation,
My heart suffers from darkest fears
For the entire day.

25345

God-reliance
Is the only specialist
To cure every kind of human suffering.

25346

May my life become
A tree
Full of God-devotion-flowers.

25347

Each child is definitely
A new hope-heart
Of God.

25348

My soul is homesick.
It wants to go back
And live in the Heart-Home
Of our Lord Beloved Supreme.

25349

We must play the Cosmic Game
According to the rules
Set by our Lord.
There is no other way.

25350

Self-giving
Is self-mastery,
And not an insoluble mystery.

25351

God is always ready to bless us
With more peace than we ever need.
But alas, where is our receptivity?

25352

We need the peace-happiness
That defies all the divisions
Of our mind.

25353

God always feeds not only our soul,
But also our heart, mind, vital and body
With great importance.

25354

God wants us to increase every day
Our life's loveliness
And our heart's eagerness
For His divine Fulfilment on earth.

25355

May my eagerness-heart
Every day
Grow in immense intensity.

25356

I love watching my soul-echoes
Swimming
In the sea of silence.

25357

We cannot change the circumstances,
But we can easily stop worrying
Over the inevitable.

25358

God's all-nourishing Feast
Always caters to
Our earthly and Heavenly tastes.

25359

God's Compassion-Flood
Will always remain far beyond
Mankind's imagination.

25360

A sincere seeker
Will never allow his God-obedience
Either to delay or to depart.

25361

God blows His Victory-Trumpet
When my mind's readiness,
My life's willingness
And my heart's eagerness
Meet together.

25362

Whenever I do something divine,
I see Heaven's
Enthusiastic cheers.

25363

Finally my mind's doubt-prosperity
Was removed
By my heart's faith-riches.

25364

Each good thought will rise
High, higher, highest
On the scale of inner prosperity.

25365

Slowly and steadily
I am sure my ignorance-life
Will die and disappear.

25366

The heart prayerfully admires
God's Compassion-Eye.
The mind cleverly desires to inspect
God's Compassion-Eye.

25367

A gratitude-heart
Has the capacity to distribute
Harmony, joy and peace.

25368

If you can embrace God's Will,
Then your aspiration-heart-flames
Will reach Heaven's satisfaction-summit.

25369

The God-Satisfaction-seed
Must be sown
Just here and right now.

25370

My open hands are no match
For my open heart
In satisfying my Lord Supreme.

25371

I do not pray to God
For security and comfort.
I pray to God to give me the capacity
For the manifestation
Of His Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Heart.

25372

Either in the remote future
Or in the immediacy of today,
My heart-flowers will definitely replace
My mind-weeds.

25373

The fragrance of my hopes
Has a special place
In my God-fulfilment-promise-life.

25374

I am fighting
My lethargy-body
With my Lord's
Compassion-Sword-Eye.

25375

You must expand every day
Your mind's vision-horizon
If constant God-satisfaction you need.

25376

Our heart-enthusiasm
Can easily carry our life
Far beyond
Our mind's depression-prison.

25377

My heart-sky
Is finally empty
Of all mind-clouds.

25378

O my eagerness-heart,
Your devotion-flames
Have beauty unparalleled!

25379

I do not vote for anybody else.
I always vote
For God-Satisfaction-candidate.

25380

The unwillingness-headache
Of the mind
Can only be cured by the heart's
Deepening meditation.

25381

I start my day
With my prayer-songs —
The songs that God Himself
Has taught me.

25382

To my extreme joy,
My heart is now growing
In dynamic selflessness-strength.

25383

Peace-singers automatically
Will get a soulful voice
From God the Supreme Musician.

25384

The silence of meditation-mountain
Gladly and proudly beckons
The God-seeker-heart.

25385

A God-dependence-life
Will be blessed
With God's God-Abundance-Possession.

25386

We shall never arrive
At a point
Where we shall remain
Forever satisfied.

25387

I have made myself
A nest
Only for prayerful
And soulful thoughts.

25388

In which way is Heaven
Better than earth
If Heaven does not hear
The tearful whispers of earth?

25389

Every day
For a truth-seeker and God-lover
Begins with
A God-manifestation-promise.

25390

A burning faith is the only answer
To end the nightmare
Of our mind's endless doubts.

25391

If you dare to care for humanity,
Then God will give you
His own Wings
To spread all around.

25392

Alas,
How long shall my heart remain
Hopelessly bound
By my mind's confusion-darkness?

25393

Because we are totally blind,
We do not see
The hour of our life's transformation
Not only has arrived,
But is compassionately waiting
For the readiness of our mind.

25394

May my mind
Build its future
On prayer-beauty.

25395

May my heart
Build its future
On meditation-fragrance.

25396

There can be no summit
Too high
For our aspiration-heart
To climb.

25397

Every aspiration-heart
And every dedication-life
Has to bloom and blossom
From within.

25398

A heart of sincerity
Is bound to carry
Innumerable joy-waves.

25399

My life is, indeed,
A God-Present
Entirely made of God's Grace.

25400

My Lord, yesterday I asked You
To give me the capacity
To expect less and give more.
Today I am asking You
To give me the capacity
To expect nothing and give You more,
Infinitely more.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 254, Agni Press, 1998
