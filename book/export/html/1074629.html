66. I become

No daring is fatal.
No loving is mortal.
No serving is fruitless.
In my daring I become
The Truth Transcendental.
In my loving I become
The soul supreme.
In my serving I become
The Oneness absolute.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 2, Agni Press, 1973
