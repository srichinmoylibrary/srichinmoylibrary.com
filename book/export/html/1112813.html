The secret of beauty

1.

Beauty is the pride of God’s Dreamland.

2.

Beauty is to live a God-enchanted life.

3.

Beauty is a revelation of God’s inner World.

4.

Beauty is to feel ecstasy-thrill in the core of my being.

5.

Beauty is the singing delight of God’s creation.

6.

Beauty is the reciprocal trust of two human beings.

7.

Beauty is the glowing light of a loving heart.

8.

Beauty is the Blessing-Compassion of God the Mother.

9.

Beauty is loving other human beings soulfully and unconditionally.

10.

Beauty is my life’s fruitful creativity-manifestation.

11.

Beauty is power concealed.

Delight is beauty revealed.

12.

Beautiful is the sound of the rising sun.

13.

Beautiful is the silence of the setting sun.

14.

Beauty is in my mind’s purity-prayers.

15.

Beauty is in my heart’s profundity-meditation.

16.

Beauty is the absence of the mind’s doubt-thorn.

17.

Beauty is the presence of the heart’s faith-flower.

18.

Beauty is beauty only when I give it with my heart-hand.

19.

Beauty is to have a free access to the world of ever-new dreams.

20.

The beauty of my mind I shall never stop cultivating.

21.

The beauty of my imagination-land I shall never stop loving.

22.

My inner beauty starves when I do not pray.

My inner beauty dies when I do not meditate.

23.

Beauty-realities a child gives me when I enter into his heart-life.

24.

Only beauty within can appreciate beauty without.

25.

Beautiful is God. But He is infinitely more beautiful when He sits on my heart-throne.

26.

Beauty unparalleled a compassion-flooded heart is.

27.

The faith-beauty of my purity-heart captures God sooner than at once.

28.

Our expectation-beauty wanes. Yet we cannot and do not stop expecting.

29.

The beauty of the heart builds ecstasy’s perfection-home.

30.

Yesterday I looked beautiful, because I thought of God.

31.

Today I am looking beautiful, because I am praying to God and meditating on God.

32.

Tomorrow I shall look beautiful, because I shall surrender my entire existence unconditionally to God.

33.

To radiate beauty, abundant beauty and infinite beauty, I do not need an all-knowing mind, but I do need an all-loving heart.

34.

God’s Dream-fulfilled Beauty the mind becomes when the mind does not compete with the heart, but just completes the heart.

35.

Beauty is the determination of the hero-mind. Beauty is the faith of the singer-heart.

36.

Sweetness-fulness-beauty is my soul’s silence-smile.

37.

Not to hide what I really am is indeed the real beauty.

38.

The inner beauty can never be destroyed by the world’s cynical sneer.

39.

He who has inner beauty is safe from the mind’s unhappiness.

40.

May beauty’s brightness transform the ugliness of my mind.

41.

The beauty of life blossoms when benediction-angels surround us.

42.

Self-giving is beautiful in its eye and fruitful in its heart.

43.

A life centred on oneness is the paragon of beauty.

44.

When you have a heart of beauty, it can and will simplify your life’s myriad complexities.

45.

If you have inner beauty, then you will not care for the company of your mind’s doubts.

46.

God gives me His Rainbow-Smile only when I give Him my soul’s rainbow-beauty.

47.

O my heart of aspiration-beauty, I shall cherish you to my last breath.

48.

When my heart becomes the beauty of my soul, even my mind needs no proof.

49.

The beauty of my soul is at once birthless and deathless.

50.

Greatness is the power of the mind. Goodness is the beauty of the soul.

51.

The heart of humility is a peerless beauty.

52.

Beauty disappears when anxiety-arrows pierce the mind.

53.

The hunger for beauty is not only an individual and a collective hunger, but also a universal hunger.

54.

Beauty and divinity love to be running partners, always.

55.

Be not emotional, be devotional. Your heart will be inundated with beauty.

56.

Be not suspicious, be auspicious. Your mind will be inundated with beauty.

57.

Be not aggressive, be progressive. Your vital will be inundated with beauty.

58.

Be not deplorable, be adorable. Your heart will be inundated with beauty.

59.

From God’s Compassion I get two eyes of beauty. From God’s Forgiveness I get one heart of beauty.

60.

Happiness is where your heart’s rainbow-beauty breathes.

61.

The perfection-beauty of self-giving can and shall save the world.

62.

I become the joy of my heart’s rainbow-beauty not when I see God’s Eye, but when I touch God’s Feet.

63.

May my soul’s beauty, my heart’s purity and my mind’s sincerity try to make the kind earth happy.

64.

Heaven-free time travels on the fastest wings of the rainbow-beauty.

65.

Infinite beauty rests in my heart’s unconditional surrender to God’s Will.

66.

If you allow your mind to be crushed by doubt’s will, how can your life have any beauty?

67.

The outer beauty is the greatest news. The inner beauty is the most perfect news.

68.

The outer beauty can be destroyed by the mind’s chaos and life’s catastrophes, while the inner beauty is immortal.

69.

The compassion-beauty of God the Creator cradles my soul. The sacrifice-beauty of God the creation feeds my soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The secret of beauty, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1995
