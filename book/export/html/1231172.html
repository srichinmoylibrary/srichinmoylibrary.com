Amar Nayane Nehari Tomar Swapane

Amar nayane
Nehari tomar swapane
Tomar nayane
Nehari amar gahane
Dyulok nayane
Nehari tomar ketane
Bhulok nayane
Nehari amar marane

Translation:

In my eyes I see
Your divine Dream supreme.
In Your Eyes I see
The core of my heart.
In the Eyes of Heaven I see
Your Victory-Banner.
In the eyes of earth I see
My imminent death.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, teach me how to cry, , 1975
