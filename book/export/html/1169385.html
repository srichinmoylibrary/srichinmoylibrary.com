30. Farewell

Farewell, farewell,
O ungrateful world.
So much I have done for you,
So highly I have thought of you.
To please you, your all,
I totally forgot
To respond to God's God-Call.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
