3677

God tells me
That He does not need to see
  My soul’s credentials.
But He wants to see
  My heart’s credentials,
And He does not want
To waste His time
  With my mind’s credentials.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 4, Agni Press, 1998
