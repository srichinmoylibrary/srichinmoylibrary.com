794 Be Careful!...

BE CAREFUL!

Be careful!
Doubt-power is contagious.

Be careful!
Jealousy-power is outrageous.

Be careful!
Depression-power is dangerous.

Be careful!
World-power is suspicious.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
