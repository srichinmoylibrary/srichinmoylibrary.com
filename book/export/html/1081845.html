286. They never go together

Earth's stupidity
And
My God's Divinity
Never go together.
O earth, please learn
This from me.

Heaven's indifference
And
My God's reverence
For His many-in-one life
Never go together.
O Heaven, please learn
This from me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
