10 April 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   Please bless me
With an ever-growing
   Surrender-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 April 1999, 8:10 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine — up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
