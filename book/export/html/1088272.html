109.

My Lord Supreme,
Is it because You have forgotten my telephone number, or are You deliberately ignoring me so that I can pray and meditate more lovingly and devotedly? I am pleading with You to be satisfied with me, since I have done everything in my capacity to love and please You in Your own Way.

Durjaya Pliske

From: Sri Chinmoy, In the amusement-enlightenment Heart-Garden of the Lord Supreme, Agni Press, 1995
