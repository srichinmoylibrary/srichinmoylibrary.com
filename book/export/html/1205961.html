20124

O my Lord,
Do give me another chance
So that I can plead
For Your Forgiveness.
"My child,
I shall definitely give you another chance
To remove the desire-list
Of your mind
And replace it with the aspiration-list
Of your heart."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 202, Agni Press, 1994
