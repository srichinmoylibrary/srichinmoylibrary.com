217. Try, you can

Alone you will emerge
From earth's teeming chaos.
Try, you can;
Try, you will.
Is not God your eternal Friend?

Alone you will emerge
From the throes of the buried past.
Try, you can;
Try, you will.
Is not God your Compassion-Friend?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
