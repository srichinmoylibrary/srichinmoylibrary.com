76. My deathless hope

My secret hope:
My Master will one day love me.

My sacred hope:
My Master will one day employ me.

My birthless hope:
My Master will one day claim me
As his very own.

My deathless hope:
My Master will one day
Be pleased with me
In this lifetime.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
