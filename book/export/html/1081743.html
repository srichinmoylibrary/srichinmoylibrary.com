184. They give love

My heart gives
As much love as it asks for.

My mind gives
Much less love than it asks for.

My soul does not ask for love;
It just gives.

My God just gives
And gives.
Lo, He always gives.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
