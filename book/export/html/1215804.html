181. He will see

He will see,
Who saw.
He will feel,
Who felt.
He will think,
Who thought.
He will serve,
Who served.
He will give,
Who gave.
But
He will have and be,
Who surrenders.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 4, Agni Press, 1974
