Tumi Na Balile Kemane Balibo Tomar

Tumi na balile kemane balibo
Tomar dukher bani
Tumi jai balecha shudhu tai bali
Ar kichu nahi jani
Sakaler pran kariya chayan
Charane sampinu ani
Tomar nami sakala charani
Dakhbo tarani naibo tani

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 6, , 1978
