2 October 1999 (VII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
This morning
I had a very, very long
   Walk with You.
You simply said to me
   Only one thing:
"Shut up! Shut up! Shut up!"
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1999, 11:50 p.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
