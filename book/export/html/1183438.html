302. I love you

Father, I love You.
Daughter, that is not enough.
Father, I love You only.
Daughter, that is not enough.
Father, I need You.
Daughter, that is not enough.
Father, I need You only.
Daughter, that is not enough.
Why do you love Me?
Why do you need Me?
I love You, I need You
Because You, You alone are
My Eternity's All.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
