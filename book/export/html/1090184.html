50. Be pure

Are you not sure
That God is everywhere?
Then just do one thing:
Try to be pure.
If you become pure,
Then you will see
That it is not just God
Who is everywhere
     But
You, too, are everywhere.
Be pure, like God.
Your existence-light
Shall shine everywhere.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, receive this little undying cry, Agni Press, 1975
