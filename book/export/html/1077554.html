32. Then and now

He was good;
     Therefore
He was always accessible
           Always reasonable
           Always amiable.

He is now great;
     Therefore
He is inaccessible,
     Unreasonable
           And
     Unbearable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eternity's Silence-Heart, Agni Press, 1974
