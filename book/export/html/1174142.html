1207 God's Way

If God's Way
Is the right way,
Then my way too
Can be the right way.
How?
Just by following sleeplessly
God's Way.

Since God's Way
Is not so difficult,
Then let me surrender
My way to God's Way.
Then my way immediately becomes
As easy as God's Way,
God's perfect Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 13, Agni Press, 1981
