311 She And He...

SHE AND HE

She is very rich
Because
She always gives,
She always gives.

He is very poor
Because
He always needs,
He always needs.

She is always beautiful.
She is always beautiful
Because
Her surrender divine
Drinks the Beauty of God.

He is always ugly.
He is always ugly
Because
His undivine autocracy devours
the freedom of man.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 7, Agni Press, 1973
