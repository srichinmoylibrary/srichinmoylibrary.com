189. God needs your heart

Your heart is all false,
Counterfeit.
I do not need it.
But alas, God needs it.
Why?
God needs it
Because you try and cry
And cry and try
To love God,
Although in your own way.
No harm, that is what
Everybody does in the beginning.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
