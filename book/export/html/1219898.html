Sweet, Sweeter, Sweetest

Sweet is my Lord.
Him I have realised as the Eternal Truth.
Sweeter is my Lord.
Him I have realised as the only Doer.
Sweetest is my Lord.
Him I have realised as the Enjoyer Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Chandelier, part 1, , 1980
