16. His is the life

In God's Eye
His is the life of God-experience.
In man's eye
His is the life of utter failure.
In his own eyes
His is the heart of perfection-light,
His is the vital of frustration-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Immortality's dance, Agni Press, 1974
