308. Bind me no more

O my doubt-mind,
Bind me no more, bind.

O my greedy vital,
Blind me no more, blind.

O my insecure heart,
Find me, find God,
Inside your existence-reality,
Find.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
