10 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Do transform my heart, my mind,
My vital and my body
In the Compassion-Flames
Of Your Love-Sun.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 October 1998, 3:57 a.m. Before lifting 210 lbs with left arm eight times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
