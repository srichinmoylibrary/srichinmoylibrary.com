15728

We suffer
When there is an outer catastrophe.
But do we really care
About our inner catastrophes:
Fear, doubt and anxiety?
Our inner catastrophes
At every moment
Are not only haunting us
But destroying our inner life-breath.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 158, Agni Press, 1991
