43. Favours

Why do I not like you?
I do not like you
Because
You ask favours.

Why do I not like him?
I do not like him
Because
He grants favours.

Why do I not like myself?
I do not like myself
Because
I do not know what favours
Actually mean.
Who needs favours?
Not I, definitely not I!

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Vision-Promise, Agni Press, 1974
