84. You ask me

You ask me
If God is really so great.
I tell you,
He is not and
He does not want to be
As greedy as your vital.

You ask me
If God is really so good.
I tell you,
He is not and
He does not want to be
As deceptive as your mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
