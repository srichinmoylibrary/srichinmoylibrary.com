16895

Even if the whole world
Does not think that I am serious
In my God-devotion,
I shall not mind.
I shall continue, my Lord,
The way I have been showing You
My devotion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 17, Agni Press, 2000
