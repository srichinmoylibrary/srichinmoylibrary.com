1 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My past is nothing but dust.
I shall satisfy You
   Here and now,
Totally, unreservedly
And unconditionally
   In Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 1999,﻿9:30 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (95 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
