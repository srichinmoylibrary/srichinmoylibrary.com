909 He Is Tired...

HE IS TIRED

His heart is tired
Of earth-tears;
Therefore
It cries for Heaven-smiles.

His soul is tired
Of Heaven-dreams;
Therefore
It longs for earth-realities.

He is tired
Of God-promises;
Therefore
He openly shuns God's company.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
