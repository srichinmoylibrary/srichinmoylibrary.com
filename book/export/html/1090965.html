My life

     Yesterday
My desire-life
Was the song of nothingness.     Today
My aspiration-life
Is the dance of progress.     Tomorrow
My realisation-life
Shall be the Heart of God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Meditation: man's choice and God's Voice, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
