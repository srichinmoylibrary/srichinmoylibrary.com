22426

My mind has no solace for me.
My mind does not even know
What solace is.
My heart not only knows
What solace is,
But also has solace for me
In measureless measure.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 225, Agni Press, 1995
