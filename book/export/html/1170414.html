The veteran to the Immortal

   Not out of fear
I weep when I of Thee do speak,
   But out of joy,
O Grace supreme, unmeasured Peak!   Though Death looms large,
Thou with the Immortal's ecstasy
   Holdest my heart.
I plunge in Thy Infinity.

  1. SAI 37. (1956)↩
