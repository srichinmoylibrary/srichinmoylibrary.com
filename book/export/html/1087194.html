A heavenly majesty

Recently I went to a store in Queens. The owner, who is an Indian, said, "I saw you playing music in Vancouver at the end of last year."

I was wearing my track suit, so I said, "Now I am in this uniform."

He said, "No matter what you wear, you have a Heavenly majesty."

-5 March 1987

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
