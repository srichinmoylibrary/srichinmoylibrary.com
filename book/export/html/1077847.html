200. Do not disturb me!

Man, I am tired,
I am taking rest.
Do not disturb me!
Please do not ruin my morning.

Man, I am sleeping,
I need sleep badly.
Do not disturb me!
Please do not ruin my afternoon.

Man, I am dreaming,
I am enjoying sweet dreams.
Do not disturb me!
Please do not ruin my evening.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
