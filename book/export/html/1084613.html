26. They long to follow you

O tears and beggars,
Where are you going?
My heart-life
And
My love-soul
Long to follow you.
O joys and kings,
Where are you going?
My earth-renunciation
And
My Heaven-determination
Long to follow you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
