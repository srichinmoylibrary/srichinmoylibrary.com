12.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

My aspiration-heart
Is
Your ever-blossoming Victory-Song.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

15 January 2005, 7:40 a.m. Xian, China. Seated Double-Dumbbell Lift: 1,006 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 18, Agni Press, 2005
