86. How to be

How to be a genuine fake:
His vital-lord is ready
To teach him.

How to be a genuine teacher:
His soul-comrade is eager
To show him.

How to be a genuine student:
His heart-sister has already
Taught him,
Lovingly,
Devotedly
And
Unconditionally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
