2001

Uncertainty-supremacy assails the world.
God’s secret and sacred Will
In the world of the unknowable prevails.

The New Millennium is humanity’s unhorizoned
God-surrender-victory
         And
Divinity’s most glorious earth-blossom-discovery.

From: Sri Chinmoy, New Year's Messages from Sri Chinmoy, 1995-2007, , 2015
