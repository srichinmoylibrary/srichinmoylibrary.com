402. Three eyes of God

Three Eyes of God:
Each one does something special.

He uses
The Eye that is inside His Head
To see if I have done
Anything wrong.

He uses
The Eye that is inside His Heart
To see if I have done
Everything perfectly.

He uses
The Eye that is at His Feet
To teach me how to do
Everything in perfect perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
