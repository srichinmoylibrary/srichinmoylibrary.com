September 1985

If your mind has sincerity,
Then only
The Supreme will tell you
What to do.

If your heart has determination,
Then only
The Supreme will teach you
How to do everything.

If your life has unconditional surrender,
Then only
The Supreme Himself
Will do everything and everything
For you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Gratitude-Flower-Hearts, Agni Press, 1993
