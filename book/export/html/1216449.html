813. Never stay away

O devouring Time,
For God's sake,
Stay away.

O nourishing Time,
For God's sake,
Come and shake hands with me.

O illumining Time,
For God's sake,
Just once embrace me.

O fulfilling Time,
For God's sake, for your sake, for my sake,
Never stay away.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 17, Agni Press, 1974
