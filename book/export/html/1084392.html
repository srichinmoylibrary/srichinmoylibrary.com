9. Father-Consciousness, Mother-Light

Father-Consciousness,
I need You
To claim You —
Your Vision's Reality.
Without it I am lost
In ignorance-night.

Mother-Light,
I need You
To claim You —
Your Reality's Perfection.
Without it I am captured
By pleasure-snares.

Therefore,
Father and Mother,
I need You two
More than You
Can possibly think.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
