115. How about a new style

Lord, how about a new style
In Your Compassion-Heart?
So far Your Compassion-Heart
Has been unreserved.
Can You not try to make it
Unconditional, too?
Try, Lord.
I assure You,
You will succeed.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
