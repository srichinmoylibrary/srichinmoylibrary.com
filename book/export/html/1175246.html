Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 24

2301 My Simplicity And My Sincerity

My simplicity and my sincerity
May not live together
In the same aspiration-house,
But they are immediate neighbours,
And they love each other deeply.

2302 My Purity And My Oneness

My purity and my feeling of oneness
May not live together
In the same aspiration-house,
But they are immediate neighbours,
And they love each other deeply.

2303 My Realisation And My God-Satisfaction

My realisation and my God-satisfaction
Always live together
In my aspiration-house.
They need each other,
They love each other
And
They immortalise each other.

2304 You Can Realise Me Today

My child,
You are giving Me all the reasons
Why you will not be able
To realise Me today.
I tell you, My child,
You are wrong, absolutely wrong!
You can easily
Realise Me today.

2305 God's Time

If you think and feel
That you belong to God,
Then you have to think and feel
That your time also belongs to God.
Such being the case,
You have no right to waste
God's Time.

2306 First Become A Follower

First become
A perfect lamb-like follower.
Then only can you become
A perfect lion-like leader.

2307 A Relationship

A relationship:
In your outer life
And
In your desire life,
What is it?
Is it a chain or oneness?
In your inner life
And
In your aspiration-life,
What is it?
Is it your oneness or a chain?

2308 Faith Means

Faith does not mean
That you should
Close your eyes to reality.
Faith means
That you should
Open your eyes widely and soulfully
So that you can dance with reality.

2309 Supreme Truths

Not yesterday but today
The world has discovered
A supreme truth:
You are a true God-lover.

Not today but tomorrow
The world will discover
Another supreme truth:
You are destined to become
A perfect God.

2310 Discipline Yourself!

Discipline yourself,
Discipline yourself!
Your disciplined life
Will glorify your capacity
And multiply God's Confidence in you.
Discipline yourself,
Discipline yourself!

2311 Do Not Surrender To Struggles

Do not surrender to struggles.
Your ultimate and only goal
Is peace in joy
And joy in peace.
Do not surrender to struggles.

2312 The Light Is Waiting

What are you waiting for?
The outer light is eagerly waiting
To show you the way.
The inner light is unconditionally waiting
To help you reach
The transcendental Goal.

2313 You Have Not Failed!

He is an unpardonable liar
Who tells you that you have failed.
She is an unpardonable liar
Who tells you that you have failed.
No, you have not failed!
You have only acquired
A necessary experience
While walking along Eternity's Road.

2314 Two Gods

The God in Heaven
And the God in man
Have the same Reality-Existence.
The God in Heaven
Sees, knows and wills.
The God in man
Grows, becomes and transcends.

2315 No Excuse

My Lord's sleepless Forgiveness-Assurance
Is no excuse
For my careless,
Thoughtless
And
Senseless living.

2316 Two Soulful Prayers

My Lord Supreme,
This morning I have
Two soulful prayers to offer You:
My Lord,
Let my aspiring, loving and self-giving heart
Speak to You
For the entire day;
My Lord
Let Your Blessing, Compassion and Forgiveness
Speak through my mind
For my entire life.

2317 Peace Above All Else

O my soulful peace,
O my fruitful peace,
Even if you are going to last
For only sixty fleeting seconds,
I shall value you and treasure you
Above all else.

2318 Expectation And Frustration

Expectation and frustration
Are two inseparable existence-realities.
We cannot accept one
And reject the other.
We have to either accept both
Or reject both.

2319 Your Own Unparalleled Reward

O my body's stupidity,
You do not need any other punishment.
You are your own pounding punishment.

O my vital's impurity,
You do not need any other punishment.
You are your own pounding punishment.

O my mind's insincerity,
You do not need any other punishment.
You are your own pounding punishment.

O my heart's insecurity,
You do not need any other punishment.
You are your own pounding punishment.

O my soul's luminosity,
You do not need any other reward.
You are your own unparalleled reward.

2320 My Life's Oneness

My Lord, my Lord,
My mind's greatness
Cannot satisfy me.
My heart's goodness
Cannot satisfy me.
Only my life's oneness with Your Will
Can satisfy me,
For my life's oneness with Your Will
Transcends at once
My mind's greatness
And
My heart's goodness.

2321 The Return-Journey

Greatness is a matter
Of a moment.
Goodness is the work
Of a lifetime.
Oneness is the return-journey home
Of Eternity.

2322 You Win God's Crown

When you show no interest
In ego-competition,
You at once win
God's Crown and God's Throne.

2323 A True God-Seeker

A true God-seeker
Is above using his divided life
Against his fellow seekers.
What he has for them
Is his soulful love,
And what he is to them
Is his fruitful oneness.

2324 Conscious Self-Examination

Conscious self-examination
Is the most auspicious beginning
Of God-realisation.

2325 When The Vital Challenges

Only when our dynamic vital
Challenges the wild emotional vital
Can our soul erase
Our deplorable past mistakes.
Only then, in our life of aspiration,
Shall we feel continuous success
And continuous progress.

2326 The Supreme Likes

The body likes
Convincing idlers.
The vital likes
Convincing flatterers.
The mind likes
Convincing doubters.
The heart likes
Convincing seekers.
The soul likes
Convincing followers.
The Supreme likes
Convincing self-givers.

2327 Cheerfulness Is Progress

Cheerfulness is progress.
Progress is satisfaction.
Satisfaction is God Himself.

2328 Disobedience Is Destructive

There is no such thing
As harmless disobedience
In the inner world.
Disobedience, in its very nature,
Is harmful and destructive,
And obedience, in its very nature,
Is beautiful, soulful, peaceful and fruitful.

2329 To Please My Lord Supreme

In order to please my Lord Supreme
In my outer life,
I have liberated myself
From the doubting mind.

In order to please my Beloved Supreme
In my inner life,
I have liberated my heart
From the nightmare of uncertainty.

2330 Beauty From Heaven

Beauty from Heaven
Descends
Only when purity from earth
Ascends.

2331 I Have Nothing To Tell

I have nothing to tell the world,
For the world never understands me.
I have nothing to tell God,
For I am all ignorance.

2332 My Beautiful Eyes Cannot See

My beautiful eyes cannot see
The flood of Delight.
Do you know why?
Because my doubting mind
Does not allow my beautiful eyes
To see the flood of Delight.

2333 My Beautiful Heart Cannot Feel

My beautiful heart cannot feel
The sea of Delight.
Do you know why?
Because my impure vital
Does not allow my beautiful heart
To feel the sea of Delight.

2334 My Beautiful Life Does Not Know

My beautiful life does not even know
That the sun of Delight exists.
Do you know why?
Because my sleeping body
Does not allow my beautiful life
To know that the sun of Delight exists.

2335 Three Enemies To Conquer

Not one, but three enemies
I have to conquer -
My sleeping body,
My impure vital
And my doubting mind -
If I really and truly want to grow
Into infinite Satisfaction-Delight.

2336 Each Prayer

On earth
Each prayer is a beautiful flower
Inside my heart-garden.

In Heaven
Each prayer is a powerful weapon
In God's Omnipotence-Armory.

2337 O Earth, O Heaven

O earth,
I do not know
And I do not want to know
The answers to your questions.

O Heaven,
I do not know
But I am most eager to know
The answers to your questions.

2338 My Three Teachers

I have three teachers:
My soul, my heart and my mind.
My soul-teacher tells me
That it is not a difficult task
To love God.
My heart-teacher tells me
That it is not a difficult task
To love mankind.
My mind-teacher tells me
That it is absolutely
The most difficult task
To love myself.

2339 Three Rare Achievements

You are the possessor
Of not one, not two, but three
Rare achievements:
Eternity's aspiration-seed,
Infinity's service-tree
And
Immortality's surrender-fruit.

2340 Do You Want To Be Happy?

Do you want to be happy,
At least temporarily,
On the practical plane?
Then try to do the things
God is asking you to do,
Starting with your body,
Vital and mind.

2341 Start Struggling With Your Body

Do you want to be happy?
Then start struggling
With your struggling body.

Do you want to be happy?
Then start serving
With your serving body.

2342 Start Tormenting Your Vital

Do you want to be happy?
Then start tormenting
Your tormenting vital.

Do you want to be happy?
Then start blinding
Your blinding vital.

2343 Start Doubting Your Mind

Do you want to be happy?
Then start doubting
Your doubting mind.

Do you want to be happy?
Then start questioning
Your questioning mind.

2344 Start Crying And Loving

Do you want to be permanently happy
On the spiritual plane?
Then start crying
With your crying heart,
And start loving
With your loving heart.

2345 Start Smiling And Flying

Do you want to be permanently happy
On the spiritual plane?
Then start smiling
At your smiling soul,
And start flying
With your flying soul.

2346 My Heart's Peace

My mind's excellence
Cannot be my heart's peace.
My life's dedication-flood
Is my heart's peace-sea.

2347 One Second Of Hope

One second of hope embodies
Eternity's infinite joy
And
Immortality's satisfaction-life.

2348 If Your Mind Is Thought-Bound

If your mind is thought-bound,
Then beauty-flower will not blossom
And purity-day will not dawn
Inside your heart-world.

2349 A Kind Of Strength

Each desire
Is a kind of strength.
But we cannot trust this strength
For it quite often fails us.
What is worse,
At times it even strangles us.

2350 My First Aspiration

My last desire:
I shall not expect anything
From God.

My first aspiration:
Mine will be the life
Of surrender unconditional.

2351 His Master's Unwritten Message

His Master's unwritten message:
The human life is only
A series of humours and rumours.

His Master's unmanifested message:
Only the divine hunger
Satisfies God.

2352 The Same Boat

O my cheerless body,
O my hopeless vital,
O my goalless mind,
O my soulless heart,
We are all sailing
In the boat
That is plying
Between
Nothingness-shore
And
Destruction-shore.

2353 A New Affair

For many years
He has been having an affair
With his temptation-vital.
But today is the beginning
Of a new affair,
An affair with his dedication-life.

2354 An Unfamiliar Hope

I love God.
Therefore
God will give me
Whatever I want.
This is a familiar hope.

God loves me.
Therefore
God will give me
Only the things that He wants.
This is an unfamiliar hope.

2355 Your Peculiar Realisation

Because of your peculiar realisation -
"Progress-life is limited" -
Today you are doomed
To a life of abysmal failure.

2356 God Gave Me Instead

I prayed to God
To grant me His Thunder-Foot
For my life-purification.
God gave me instead
His Compassion-Eye
For my life-liberation.

2357 The Balloons Of Your Pretense

Finally
The balloons of your pretense
Have all burst.

Before long
Your silver sincerity will multiply
Your achievement-trees.

2358 Your Volcano-Aspiration

God will forgive you
If you suspect
His Compassion-Sea.
But God will not forgive you
If you suspect
Your volcano-aspiration.

2359 Your Himalayan Self-Command

A loving past you had,
An aspiring present you have,
An inspiring future you will have
By virtue of your
Himalayan self-command.

2360 No Failure Is Final

No failure is final.
Therefore,
I shall try once again.

No despair is destined.
Therefore,
I am bound to succeed.

2361 His Mind Is Now Really Happy

His mind is now really happy.
Do you know why?
Because a stream of silver faith
Is flowing sweetly.

2362 His Heart Is Now Really Happy

His heart is now really happy.
Do you know why?
Because a most beautiful gratitude-flower
Is lovingly blossoming.

2363 Success Is Action

Success is action.
Therefore,
I am smiling at God.

Progress is action.
Therefore,
God and I are dancing together.

2364 His Life's Unexplained Mysteries

His life's two unexplained mysteries:
Why did he sing so powerfully
With ignorance-day?
Why did he dance so openly
With ignorance-night?

2365 He Knows When To Fight Back

Nothing can permanently defeat him,
For he knows when to fight back.
When?
In the immediacy of today.
Where will he get help?
From God's own two beautiful Eyes
And God's own two powerful Arms.

2366 A Symphony Of Prosperity

His mind is enjoying today
A symphony of prosperity.
How can it be true?
Look,
His mind has surrendered
To his heart,
And his heart has transformed
His vital and his body.

2367 The Company Of Silence

I enjoy the company of silence.
It is at once
Soulful and fruitful.

I enjoy the company of sound.
It is at once
Meaningful and powerful.

2368 Sincerity Rises

Sincerity rises
After each fall.
Purity listens
To the inner call.
Humility is
God's own fond doll.

2369 The Dependence Is Mutual

The dependence is mutual.
My mind depends on my heart
For its soulfulness.
My heart depends on my mind
For its alertness.

2370 A Perfect Leader

A great leader
Is a great servant.
A good leader
Is a good slave.
A perfect leader
Is earth's perfect smile.

2371 Dreamers And Lovers

As a dreamer,
He is loved by God
Constantly.

As a Lover,
God is loved by him
Unconditionally.

2372 A Tomorrow That Never Comes

A tomorrow that never comes
Shall come to him alone,
For he is determined
To transcend himself
Within and without.

2373 He Will Never Suffer From Defects

He will never suffer
From abiding defects,
For every defect he takes
As a pure and sure
Strengthening experience-light.

2374 Simplicity Has Solved My Problems

Two problems I had.
My simplicity has at last
Solved them.
My simplicity has transformed
My hesitating mind
Into a daring mind.
My simplicity has transformed
My fearing heart
Into a cheering heart.

2375 The Vision-Light Of Heaven

The vision-light of Heaven
Is subtle but not obscure.
The manifestation-might of earth
Is obscure but not subtle.

2376 My Self-Transcendence-Height

My Lord,
If I obey You
The way You want me to obey You,
What will You give me?

My child,
I shall give you
My Wings to reach
My Self-Transcendence-Height.

2377 Two Sure Homes

Do you know
What my mind is?
It is the sure home
Of hopeless thoughts.

Do you know
What my heart is?
It is the sure home
Of my blessingful God.

2378 Worries Of The Mind

Three most deplorable
Worries of the mind:
In the morning
It knows too little.
In the afternoon
It knows too much.
At night
It either forgets everything
Or misuses everything.

2379 They Never Fail

A Satisfaction-God
Never fails.
A perfection-soul
Never fails.
A purity-heart
Never fails.
A gratitude-life
Never fails.

2380 The Hour Of Patience

Surprisingly beautiful
Is the hour of patience.
Amazingly fruitful
Is the power of patience.

2381 Question Your Mind Dauntlessly

Question your mind dauntlessly,
Search your heart carefully.
You will be able to fathom Heaven
Inside your dedication-life.

2382 God's Compassion-Heights

God's Forgiveness-Depths
Cover my littleness
Secretly.

God's Compassion-Heights
Magnify and multiply my greatness
Openly.

2383 Saviour Of The Mind

How can the mind forever remain imprisoned
Even if it scatters darkness
All around?
Who does not know
That its saviour
Is its own all-forgiving soul?

2384 My Heart's Sacred Thoughts

My sweet Lord,
Quickly and proudly You lead me,
But only when I tell You
My secret heart's
Sacred thoughts.

2385 Three Special Freedoms

My mind enjoys
The freedom of speculation.

My heart enjoys
The freedom of perfection.

My soul enjoys
The freedom of satisfaction.

2386 Titan Eyes

His is the mind
That has
The titan eyes.
But
His is the heart
That has
The sun-melting rays.

2387 A Smile On His Blue-Gold Face

When a smile crept over
His blue-gold face
At last,
It immediately quenched
My long-tormenting thirst.

2388 An Armour Of Proof

What my mind needs
Is a strong armour of proof.
What my heart needs
Is a sweet ripple of belief.

2389 The Grand Palace Of Will-Power

O mind,
O my poor, blind mind!
Nobody can force you
To live inside
A dark, deep thought-cave,
Nobody.
Why not start living
From today on
In the grand palace of will-power?

2390 When I Look At Him

When I look at him,
I see in him an earthly host.
When I think of him,
I feel in him a Heavenly guest.

2391 More Changeful Than The Tide

It is not your mind
That is more changeful
Than the tide.

It is not even your thoughts
That are more changeful
Than the tide.

It is your love of God
That is more changeful
Than the tide.

2392 The Gates Of Laughter Wild

The gates of laughter wild
Opened themselves wide
When I promised that
My mind would visit God
Every day
And my life would invite God
Every day.

2393 A Flower In His Life

His mind has now become
A withering flower
In his life.

His heart forever remains
A blossoming and glowing flower
In his life.

2394 When I Unconditionally Meditate

When I do not soulfully meditate,
My mind becomes self-deceiving fears.
When I unconditionally meditate,
My heart becomes God-winning tears.

2395 If Your Heart Is Empty

If your mind is empty of desires,
Then God Himself will feed you
His morning manna.

If your heart is empty of insecurities,
Then God Himself will feed you
His evening manna.

2396 A Poor Beggar-Singer

His heart's blue bird flies away
The moment he starts singing.
Helpless as he is,
He has no other choice.
Alas, none can sympathise
With this poor beggar-singer.

2397 If Your Mind Surrenders

If your mind surrenders
To even one intruding thought,
Then you will, without fail, be caught
In the snare of ignorance-sleep.

2398 The Perfection-Dawn

What is doubt,
If not an imminent disaster?
What is faith,
If not the perfection-dawn
Of a satisfaction-day?

2399 Silence Succeeds

Silence succeeds
Quickly.
Silence-light proceeds
Steadily.
Silence-delight prevails
Eternally.

2400 One Idol, One Hero

I have only one idol
And that idol is my crying heart.
I have only one hero
And that hero is my smiling soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 24, Agni Press, 1982
