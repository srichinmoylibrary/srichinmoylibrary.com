903. I play no more

I play no more
With my dying friend: fear.

I play no more
With my dead friend: doubt.

I play no more
With my strange friend: insecurity.

I play no more
With my puny friend: ego.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
