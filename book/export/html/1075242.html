667 O Earth, O Heaven!...

O EARTH, O HEAVEN!

O Earth!
For God's sake,
Do not throw your ignorance-weight around.
I want to proceed.

O Heaven!
For God's sake,
Do not throw your indifference-weight around.
I want to succeed.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 14, Agni Press, 1973
