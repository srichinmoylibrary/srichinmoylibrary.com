406 O My Hope

O my hope,
Where are you?
Can you not see me
Inside the despair-grave?

O my hope,
Where are you?
Can you not grant me someday
The helmet of protection
And the crown of satisfaction?
Hope, my hope!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 5, Agni Press, 1979
