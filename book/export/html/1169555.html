200. I place at Your Lotus-Feet

The blunder
Of my mind,
The thunder
Of my vital,
The tower
Of my heart,
And the hour
Of my body
I place at Your Lotus-Feet,
To free myself from ignorance-night
Impure, unlit.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
