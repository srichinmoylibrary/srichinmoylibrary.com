320 Inside I Live

Inside I live
Soulfully and sleeplessly.
Outside I give
Generously and unconditionally.

The following fifteen poems were written on 28 October 1979 on the plane from Hyderabad to Madras.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 4, Agni Press, 1979
