5739 The Clutch Of Evil Thoughts

In the morning
The clutch of evil thoughts
Makes me blind, totally blind.

In the evening
The clutch of evil thoughts
Compels my death, unavoidable death.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 58, Agni Press, 1983
