393. Is your heart alive?

Is your heart alive?
If so, why don't you smile?

Is your soul alive?
If so, why don't you love?

Is your God alive?
If so, why don't you become
God-manifestation,
God-perfection,
God-satisfaction?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
