74 When We Expect Rush Service From God,...

When we expect rush service from God,
God complies.
When God expects rush service from us,
In the twinkling of an eye
We disappear, we hide
And we continue our ignorance-ride.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 3, Agni Press, 2000
