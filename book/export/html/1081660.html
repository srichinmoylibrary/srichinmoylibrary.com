101. Forward, upward, inward

Forward to the shore!
God will offer you
His silver Throne.

Upward to the stars!
God will offer you
His golden Crown.

Inward to the source!
God will offer you
His diamond Heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
