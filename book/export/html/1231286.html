Bhalobasi Ami Atal Baridhi

Bhalobasi ami atal baridhi
Asim sunil ambara
Bhalobasi ami khuliya dharite
Ruddha supta antara

Translation:

Fathomless white ocean
I love,
Boundless blue sky
I love.
I also love
To keep wide open
My long-closed heart-door.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, teach me how to surrender, , 1975
