6. O my mind

O my racing mind,
   Slow down.

O my shouting mind,
   Calm down.

O my suspecting mind,
   Stop your suspicion-flight
   Forever.
Then I shall claim you
   As my peerless treasure.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Yesterday I was a crying dream, Agni Press, 1975
