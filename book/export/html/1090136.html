2. I assure you

Love earth.
     I assure you,
     The world will not
     Disappoint you.

Love Heaven.
     I assure you,
     Heaven will not
     Desert you.

Love the inner man in yourself.
     I assure you,
     He alone can and will
     Fulfil you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, receive this little undying cry, Agni Press, 1975
