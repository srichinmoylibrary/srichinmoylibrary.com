May 8

You are really clever when you take the world of desire as it is and not as it ought to be. You are really wise when you take the world of aspiration as it ought to be and not as it is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
