145. I entered

   I entered
Into the sombre care of human thought.
   Nothing there was.   I entered
Into the illumined Palace of Thought divine.
   Something there was.   I entered
Into the conditionally surrendered
   Soul of human life.
   There I saw God smiling.   I entered
Into the unconditionally surrendered
   Soul of Life divine.
   There I saw God becoming.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 3, Agni Press, 1973
