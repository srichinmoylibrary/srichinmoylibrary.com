29 December 2004 (I)

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

Please give me the capacity
To fix my prayerful eyes
On Your Golden Feet
All the time.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

/29 December 2004, 3:53 a.m. Qingdao, China. Seated Double-Dumbbell Lift: 809 lbs.

1. Namo namo namo Buddha deber
2. Sachimata dake Nimai Nimai
