100 God Does Not Want Me To Think...

God does not want me to think
Of my life's sunset.
He wants me only to think
Of my heart's all-illumining sunrise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 7, Agni Press, 2002
