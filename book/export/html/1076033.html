49.

The difference between
God and me:
When I go to God for help,
He tells me,
"My child, My help
Is immediately available."
When God comes to me for help,
I say to God,
"My Lord, my help
Is approachable, no doubt,
But not guaranteed."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The difference between God and me, Agni Press, 1998
