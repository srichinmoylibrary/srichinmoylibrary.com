4540 A Saga Of Selfless Service

Illumination you want?
God will grant you.
Liberation you want?
God will grant you.
Realisation you want?
God will grant you.
Do you know when?
Only when your life of aspiration
Becomes a saga of selfless service.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 46, Agni Press, 1982
