14. Inseparable friends

A man of leisure
And
A man of failure
Are inseparable friends.
A man of pleasure
And
A man of destruction
Are inseparable friends.
A heart of purity
And
A mind of clarity
Are inseparable friends.
A vital of determination
And
A body of dedication
Are inseparable friends.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Immortality's dance, Agni Press, 1974
