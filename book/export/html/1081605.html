46. Do follow me

God, do follow me
To my earth-home.
I shall give You
My long-cherished ignorance
To eat.

"Man, do follow Me
To My Heaven-Home.
I shall give you
My all-immortalising Nectar
To drink."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
