419. Let us wager

Lord, let us wager
Our kingdoms.
Let Thy Kingdom come down,
Let my kingdom go up.
Let us see whose kingdom
Has a faster speed.
Lord, if You win
I shall give you immediately
My only wealth: surrender-light.
Lord, if I win
Will You give me
Your Perfection-height?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
