497. Look and overlook

After we look doubt over,
We overlook doubt.

After we overlook faith,
We look faith over.

After we look Satan over
When he smiles,
We overlook him
When he cries.

After we overlook God
When He cries,
We look Him over
When He smiles.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 10, Agni Press, 1973
