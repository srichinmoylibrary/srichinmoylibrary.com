14 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Am I correct when I say
That each human being
Is a winner in his own way
In the desire-hunger-race?
"Yes, My child, you are correct,
Absolutely correct."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
