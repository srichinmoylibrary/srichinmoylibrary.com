Frog: Enthusiasm

Frog, my frog,
Endlessly you jump and jump,
Cheerfully you cover distance,
Proudly you conquer the pride of
length.
Who could imagine
That your legs had such spring
and strength?
Continue jumping.
In the process of your evolution
And your silver-bright enthusiasm
You are bound to cover
Eternity s length
And sing the song of
salvation-perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Animal Kingdom, Agni Press, 1973
