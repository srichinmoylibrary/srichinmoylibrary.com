Two competitors

In the Vermont Inspiration Marathon, the first time I saw G., at sixteen miles, he was smiling. S. was behind him. The second time I saw them, S. was ahead of G.. S. was so happy that he was ahead and had taken the lead.

When I started running at the twenty-one mile mark, I saw S. again. He was very glad to see me and said, "Guru, Guru, Guru!" G. was nowhere near him. I was always waiting to see if I would hear G.'s footsteps, and I kept looking around. But the only footsteps I heard behind me were not a man's. It was P. G. was walking.
