Dure Aji Bahu Dure

Dure aji bahu dure
Bandhan krandan
Kache aji ati kache
Maha Shib nartan

Translation:

Today bondage and cry
   Are both far, very far.
Today near, very near
   Is the dance
Of the great Lord Shiva.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 11a, , 1981
