125. Doubt, over-confidence and indifference

Doubt,
I am ashamed
Of our former attachment.

Over-confidence,
I am ashamed
Of our present attachment.

Indifference,
I am ashamed
Of our future attachment.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
