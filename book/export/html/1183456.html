320. The mind-man and the heart-man

Lord, the mind-man in me
Is unwilling.
Shall I force him?
Or
Shall I leave him?

Lord, the heart-man in me
Is more than willing.
How shall I thank him?
And
How shall I pilot him?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
