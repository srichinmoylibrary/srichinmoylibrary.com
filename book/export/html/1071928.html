August 26

When I cleave to my own will, I expose all my ignorance. When I cleave to God's Will, not only do I know what God has, but also I freely reveal to the world what God is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
