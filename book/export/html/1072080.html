25. My name is Madra. Who is more important, the divine lover or the divine beloved?

Yogi: Acceding to the divine observer, the divine lover and the divine beloved are equally important. But the divine lover thinks that the divine beloved is more important and the divine beloved feels that the divine lover is more important.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
