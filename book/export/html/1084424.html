41. Who fears?

Who fears to follow?
Not the heart-man in me.
Who fears to follow?
The mind-man in me.

Who fears to lead?
Not the soul-man in me.
Who fears to lead?
The body-man in me.

Who fears to change?
Not the God-man in me.
Who fears to change?
The vital-man in me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
