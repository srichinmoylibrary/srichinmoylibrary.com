3649 God Will Definitely Blame You

If you have not done
Anything remarkable for yourself,
God will not blame you.
If you have not done
Anything soulful for God,
God will not blame you.
But if you have not done
Anything meaningful for the world,
God will definitely blame you,
For this was the promise
That you made to Him in Heaven
Before you left for this world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
