4722

If I have to choose
Between God's Grace and God's Love,
I will definitely choose God's Grace,
For I know perfectly well
That God's Love will follow God's Grace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 48, Agni Press, 1984
