My fount

   When
I live in the thought-world
   My fount
Is my slow end.   When
I live in the will-world
   My fount
Is my quick start,
      great concentration,
      good meditation
         and
      perfect realisation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Meditation: humanity's race and Divinity's Grace, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
