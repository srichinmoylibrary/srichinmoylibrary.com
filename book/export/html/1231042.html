Ebar Amai Tano Mago

Ebar amai tano mago
Ebar amai tano
Amar praner khudratare
Tibra bhabe hano
Phutbo ami phuler mato
Tomar paye abirata
Amar hiyar gopan byetha
Pabe tomar nirabata
Ghor timire paran mamo
Tumito ma jano
Ebar amai tano mago
Ebar amai tano

Translation:

Draw me near You.

The meanness of my life.
Like a flower
I shall always blossom at Your Feet.

Mother, draw me near You this time,
Draw me near You.
