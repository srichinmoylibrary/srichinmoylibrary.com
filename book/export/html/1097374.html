My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 45

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Kuantan and Kijal, Malaysia in December 2005 and January 2006.

30 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

1 My Heart’s Blooming Smile ...

My heart’s blooming smile
I always offer
To my Lord Supreme
First.

2 God-Dreamers ...

God-dreamers
Eventually become
God-manifestation-fulfillers.

3 The More We Travel ...

The more we travel
Within,
The faster we get good results
Without.

4 My God-Gratitude ...

My God-gratitude
Is
My true life-transformer.

5 How Do I Find Happiness? ...

How do I find happiness?
I find happiness
By loving the world more,
Ever more.

6 The Source ...

The source
Of my inspiration
Is
Mother Earth.

7 The Source ...

The source
Of my aspiration
Is
My inner sun.

8 The Source ...

The source
Of my success
Is
My mind-determination.

9 The Source ...

The source
Of my progress
Is
My surrender-life to God.

10 Every Hour ...

Every hour
God sows
A new seed of transformation
In my heart-garden.

31 December 2005 ...

31 December 2005

Kuantan, Malaysia

11 The Heart Is Always Ready ...

The heart is always ready
To answer the questions
Of the mind,
But the mind is not satisfied.

12 A Life Of Meditation ...

A life of meditation
Gives tremendous joy
To God.

13 My Mind ...

My mind,
If you stay with doubt,
Then I shall have
Nothing to do with you!

14 Hesitation ...

Hesitation
Slows down the speed
Of everything divine
That we have.

15 When We See God ...

When we see God
Within ourselves,
He is beautiful and powerful.
But when we see God
In others,
He is only powerful.

16 No Matter Where ...

No matter where
God’s Feet are,
God’s Heart
Has given me the capacity
To clasp Them.

17 Only The God-Listeners ...

Only the God-listeners
Are my true friends.

18 Spirituality ...

Spirituality
Without surrender
Will always remain
Incomplete.

19 God Equally Loves ...

God equally loves
Those who aspire soulfully
And those who serve devotedly.

20 God Does Not Love ...

God does not love
The sleepers.
He has concern
For the sleepers,
But not love.

1 January 2006 ...

1 January 2006

Kuantan, Malaysia

21 When God Introduces Himself ...

When God introduces Himself
To me,
The finite in me
Immediately becomes the Infinite.

22 When I Introduce Myself ...

When I introduce myself
To God,
The Infinite in Him
Immediately becomes the finite.

23 I Am Enamoured ...

I am enamoured
Of each moment that I get
At the Feet
Of my Lord Supreme.

24 My Gratitude-Heart ...

My gratitude-heart
Has the capacity
To speak to my Lord Supreme
At every moment.

25 O Lord ...

O Lord,
Do purify my outer voice
And immortalise my inner voice.

26 My Lord ...

My Lord,
When will my mind
Realise You?
“My child,
I am more eager than you are
For your mind to realise Me.”

27 Ceaselessly I Ring ...

Ceaselessly I ring
Most devotedly
My God-gratitude-heart-bell.

28 Each Life Has ...

Each life has
Countless ascending steps
Before it arrives
In Heaven.

29 Be Brave! ...

Be brave!
Be not stunned
By the present stark reality.

30 If I Really Wish ...

If I really wish
To be a God-devotion-singer,
Then God will give me
The sweetest range of voice.

2 January 2006 ...

2 January 2006

Kuantan, Malaysia

31 God Enters Into ...

God enters into
The mind-jungle
With His Light, Peace and Bliss.
The mind hides.
After a while the mind comes
And touches God’s Feet.
God immediately transforms
The mind-jungle
Into the Garden of Eden.

32 Desire Sees The Moon ...

Desire sees the moon
But wants to remain
In the mud and clay
Of the earth.

33 The Doubting Mind ...

The doubting mind
Has no entry form
To enter into
God’s Heart-Palace.

34 Spiritual Progress ...

Spiritual progress
Ends
The moment the desire-life
Succeeds.

35 Age Has Nothing To Do ...

Age has nothing to do
With a sage life.

36 God Is Starving And Starving ...

God is starving and starving
For an iota
Of my inner progress.

37 Prayer ...

Prayer
Protects us
From ignorance-night.

38 Meditation ...

Meditation
Is
Ignorance-night-illumination.

39 Do Not Waste Time ...

Do not waste time
Even for a moment.
God is eagerly waiting
For you.

40 No Backward Step ...

No backward step
With doubt,
But forward,
Ever forward steps
With faith.

41 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I have only one prayer:
Please give me
Two Heaven-flying
God-obedience-wings.

42 Earth-Bound ...

Earth-bound
The mind travels.

43 Heaven-Free ...

Heaven-free
The heart flies.

3 January 2006 ...

3 January 2006

Kuantan, Malaysia

44 May My Aspiration-Heart ...

May my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
Become
Inseparable friends.

45 God Forgives ...

God forgives
Our ill-disciplined life,
But He never forgets it.

46 A Sincere Seeker ...

A sincere seeker
Never asks God
For a leave of absence.

47 If Unwillingness ...

If unwillingness
Does not accept opportunity,
Life becomes a failure.

48 When My Mind ...

When my mind
Does not obey
My Lord Supreme,
The tears of my soul
Flood my heart.

49 The Seeker’s First ...

The seeker’s first
And foremost duty:
God-acceptance
In God’s own Way
At every moment.

50 My Hopes ...

My hopes
And my dreams
Adorn my heart-garden.

51 A God-Feet-Surrender-Life ...

A God-Feet-surrender-life
Is compulsory
Until we breathe our last.

52 To Oversleep God’s Special Hour ...

To oversleep God’s special Hour
Is, indeed, a painful
But common experience
In a seeker’s life.

53 God Applauds ...

God applauds
When we cry.
God applauds
When we smile.
Is there any time
When God does not applaud,
No matter what we do?

4 January 2006 ...

4 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

54 We Must Brave ...

We must brave
The dire challenges of life
To become God’s
Most perfect instruments.

55 Our Powerful Meditation ...

Our powerful meditation
Is the only food
That God appreciates
Most sincerely
And most blessingfully.

56 There Is Always A Better Way ...

There is always a better way
To love God and serve God,
And we must try to discover
That particular way.

57 My Meditation ...

My meditation
Is my heart’s
God-love-sanctification.

58 Our Oneness-Heart ...

Our oneness-heart
Is God’s
Ecstasy-Dance.

59 God Wants Me To Win ...

God wants me to win
The inner world first
And then to think
Of the outer world.

60 Heaven’s Blessings And Concern ...

Heaven’s Blessings and Concern
I have brought down with me
To be of service
To the earth-planet.

61 Not The Mind’s Fantasy ...

Not the mind’s fantasy,
But the heart’s intimacy
I need.

62 The Privilege ...

The privilege
Of serving God
On a daily basis
In the inner world
And in the outer world
Is unimaginable.

63 My Devotion-Heart ...

My devotion-heart
And God’s Breath
Sing perfectly in tune.

64 If We Love God ...

If we love God
In God’s own Way,
There will be no stop
On our God-journey.

65 The Mind ...

The mind
Takes pride
In its high opinions.

66 The Heart ...

The heart
Surrenders its pride
Utterly and unconditionally
At the Feet of God.

67 If We Want To Work On Earth ...

If we want to work on earth
Soulfully and perfectly,
Then we must keep in touch
With Heaven’s
Constant Compassion and Concern.

68 Our God-Closeness ...

Our God-closeness
We must every day use
To increase
The world-oneness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 45, Agni Press, 2006
