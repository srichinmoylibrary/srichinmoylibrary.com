287. You are really great

Your intuition-mind
Is really great.
Your sacrifice-heart
Is really great.
Your perfection-concern
Is really great.
Your oneness-life with the world at large
Is really great.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
