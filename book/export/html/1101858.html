12.

My Beloved Supreme,
Is there any right time for me
To do the right thing?
"My child,
Not tomorrow,
Not even today!
"The right time for you
To do the right thing
Is now,
At this very moment,
Plus
Here and nowhere else."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord, make me Your happiness-child, Agni Press, 1992
