49.

Somewhere, somewhere, somewhere,
man will be Immortality's pride —
        My Lord tells me
        that man will be Immortality's pride
        when he discovers the supreme secret
           that God's Compassion reigns supreme
              all-where.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Something, somehow, somewhere, someday, Agni Press, 1973
