35. Flower-songs of morning

Flower-songs of morning
Make my life pure.
Fire-songs of noon
Make my life sure.
Rainbow-songs of evening
Make my life leap.
Star-songs of night
Make my life fly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
