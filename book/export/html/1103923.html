28 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My doubting mind
And God's compassionate Eye
   Challenge each other.
My God-loving heart
And God's all-illumining Heart
   Embrace each other.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 October 1999, 7:27 p.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
