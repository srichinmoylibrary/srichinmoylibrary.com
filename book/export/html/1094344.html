27 To Please God ...

To please God
In His own Way,
I must become a giant personality
In spirituality.
I must become unique
In God-achievements
And a real asset to humanity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 9, Agni Press, 2002
