32.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

I do excellent meditations
Only when I meditate
In my God-heart-temple.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

9 February 2005, 4:17 a.m. Nanjing, China. Seated Double-Dumbbell Lift: 1,006 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 18, Agni Press, 2005
