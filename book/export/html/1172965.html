43. Although God loves him

Although
God loves him,
He is his heart's sorrow.
Although
God blesses him,
He is his life's failure.

Why? Why? Why?
Because he has not
Changed his name
Into love-devotion-surrender.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, part 1, Agni Press, 1979
