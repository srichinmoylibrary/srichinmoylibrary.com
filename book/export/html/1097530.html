My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 47

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Kijal, Malaysia in January 2006.

10 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

1 Before Our Transformation ...

Before our transformation
Takes place,
God keeps it
A top secret.

2 To Listen To The Mind ...

To listen to the mind
All the time
Is, indeed, a good way
To go crazy.

3 God’s Grace-Heart ...

God’s Grace-Heart
Never stops.

4 I Never Miss A Chance ...

I never miss a chance
To fulfil God
In His own Way.

5 The Mind ...

The mind
Concerns itself
With things mundane.

6 The Heart ...

The heart
Concerns itself
With Godís Compassion,
Protection and Forgiveness.

7 Anything That Helps Us Evolve ...

Anything that helps us evolve
We must accept,
And be grateful.

8 O My Mind ...

O my mind,
Do not delay!
Set your prayers
In perfection-motion.

9 Constant Awareness ...

Constant awareness
Leads us
To full wakefulness.

10 The Mind ...

The mind
Is the reservoir
Of worries and anxieties.

11 The Heart ...

The heart
Is the reservoir
Of Godís Beauty
And Godís Forgiveness.

11 January 2006 ...

11 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

12 Whenever People Tell Me ...

Whenever people tell me
That God has special Love
For me,
I am overwhelmed
With God-gratitude.

13 I Came Into The World ...

I came into the world
To feed Godís chosen children
With my heartís love.

14 Whatever Comes Into My Life ...

Whatever comes into my life,
I shall never make friends
With ignorance-night.

15 The Past ...

The past
Must not discourage
Our forward march.

16 The Past ...

The past
Must not discourage
Our inward dive.

17 My Prayer ...

My prayer
Is the loving breath
Of my heart.

18 Undivine Thoughts ...

Undivine thoughts
Imprison
Our mind.

19 Divine Thoughts ...

Divine thoughts
Liberate
Our mind.

20 The Traveller In Me ...

The traveller in me
Carries hope
Wherever it goes.

21 Concentration-Training ...

Concentration-training
Is absolutely needed.

22 The Aspiring Heart ...

The aspiring heart
Has no enemy.
It sees and feels
Friends everywhere.

12 January 2006 ...

12 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

23 Everything ...

Everything
Sings in the heart
Of a genuine seeker.

24 Not Only Godís Dreams ...

Not only Godís Dreams
But also His Realities
At times change.

25 I Have Only ...

I have only
One thing to do:
To stay peacefully
Inside my heart.

26 God-Intoxicated Souls ...

God-intoxicated souls
Are the ones
Who will manifest God
Here on earth.

27 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Whether my heart is good or bad
Is for You to judge,
But my heart is all Yours.

28 As Soon As I Entered ...

As soon as I entered
Into Godís Heart-Room,
I saw that God
Had already prepared the Room
For my arrival.

29 Wherever I Go ...

Wherever I go,
I see that my Lord
Has preceded me.

30 My Mind ...

My mind,
Why do you think of the things
That blight your own vision?

31 God-Seekers, God-Lovers ...

God-seekers, God-lovers
And God-servers
Carry the message
Of world-transformation.

32 My Lord ...

My Lord,
You are Your overwhelming Love,
And
I am my heartís streaming tears.

13 January 2006 ...

13 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

33 Oneness Means ...

Oneness means
God-expansion
In a seekerís life.

34 Alas ...

Alas,
What can a seeker do
With his overfed pride?

35 No More Shall I Live ...

No more shall I live
With my torn hopes.
I shall live only
In the land of cheerfulness.

36 Each Seeker Must Bury ...

Each seeker must bury
His anxieties and worries
In his sterling faith.

37 Will-Power Has To Be Used ...

Will-power has to be used
All the time
To save us from wrongdoings.

38 Soulfulness And Eagerness ...

Soulfulness and eagerness
Are extremely needed
In the spiritual life.

39 We Do Not Need ...

We do not need
Insecurity-thorns.
We need only
Beautyís security-roses.

40 Doubt, Doubt, Doubt ...

Doubt, doubt, doubtó
I am fully prepared
Bravely to meet you head on.

41 God The Helper ...

God the Helper
And God the Supporter
We need
When we want to achieve
The impossible.

42 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Give me tears in my heart.
Give me cheers in my life.
Give me divine freedom in my thought
To make me a perfect instrument
Of Yours.

43 I Shall Never, Never Go ...

I shall never, never go
To the expectation-world.
Time and again
It has failed me.

44 Exorbitant Pride ...

Exorbitant pride
Is
The God-renouncer.

45 The Heart Can Protect ...

The heart can protect
The outer life,
But the outer life cannot protect
The inner life.

46 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I love Your Thunder-Kick-Anger
To make the fastest progress
In my inner life.

47 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I love Your Strictness
The way I love Your Kindness.

14 January 2006 ...

14 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

48 Everything Divine ...

Everything divine
Begins in the heart
And then spreads.

49 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Anything that I have in my lifeó
Divine or undivineó
Rest assured, it is all Yours.

50 My Lord Tells Me That ...

My Lord tells me that,
To love Him more,
I shall have to shed soulful tears
In abundant measure every day.

51 My Mind No Longer ...

My mind no longer
Dares to block
My fastest progress.

52 Every God-Created Heart ...

Every God-created heart
Consciously or unconsciously sings
Godís Victory-Songs.

53 To My Greatest Surprise And Joy ...

To my greatest surprise and joy,
My stone heart
God melted long ago.

54 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Give me not greatness,
Not goodness,
But oneness with Your Will
In everything that I say and do.

55 My Life Is All God-Service ...

My life is all God-service.
I do things not to magnify myself.
God says to magnify me
Is His Task.

56 We Have The Right To Choose ...

We have the right to choose.
We must choose
Wisely.

57 Our Inner Reality ...

Our inner reality
Is the Smile
Of God-Beautyís Dream.

15 January 2006 ...

15 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

58 I Must Know That It Is ...

I must know that it is
Only for my good
That God does not answer
My questions.

59 My Irregular Devotion ...

My irregular devotion
God does not appreciate.

60 My Mind And My Vital ...

My mind and my vital,
Please do not make
My Lord Supreme angry.
He is all Love for you.

61 Every Day I Pray To God ...

Every day I pray to God
To be fully in control
Of my life.

62 Forgetfulness ...

Forgetfulness
Is, at times,
A great blessing.

63 This World ...

This world
Is not interesting
But instigating.

64 Everything I Do ...

Everything I do
With Godís complete
Permission and Assurance.

65 Do Not Try ...

Do not try
To perfect the world,
But try to love the world
More.

66 I Call Myself ...

I call myself
A God-lover,
But God calls me
His constant partner.

67 God Does Not Mind ...

God does not mind
If I criticise Him
Without any rhyme or reason,
But He does mind
When I do not pray every day
Prayerfully and soulfully.

16 January 2006 ...

16 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

68 My Life Began ...

My life began
With my heartís
Tear-beauty.

69 My Life Shall End ...

My life shall end
With my soulís
Smile-fragrance.

70 When I Think ...

When I think
Of the world situation,
I find myself
In the heartbeat of tears.

71 An Atom Of Inner Peace ...

An atom of inner peace
Is infinitely more important
Than all the successes
That we have accumulated
Over the years.

72 O My Mind ...

O my mind,
Obey my heart
The way my heart obeys my soul,
If you really want to have peace.

73 I Want To Be ...

I want to be
A faith-hero
And no longer remain
A doubt-slave.

74 Faith And Doubt ...

Faith and doubt
Should be kept apartó
Poles apart.

75 To Please God ...

To please God
My heart-smile has to be
Brighter than the brightest.

76 Anything That God Gives Me ...

Anything that God gives me
Is beautiful within
And useful without.

77 God ...

God,
Please give me
A sky-vast and sun-high eagerness
To please You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 47, Agni Press, 2006
