August 5

When I climbed the hills of doubt, I saw the prince of confusion. When I climbed the hills of ignorance, I saw the prince of destruction. When I climbed the hills of life, I saw the prince of liberation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
