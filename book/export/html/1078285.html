638 Love-Light...

LOVE-LIGHT

Life-truth chisels me.
Therefore
I am allowed
To sit at the Feet of God.

Love-light chisels me.
Therefore
I am allowed
To see the Eye of God;
Also,
I am allowed
To sleep in the Heart of God;
Also,
I am allowed
To claim and proclaim God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
