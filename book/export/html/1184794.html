466. Now he is inseparably one

Man's most desired light
Is in his heart's inner cry.
There his wisdom rules;
His eyes have the healing touch.
His is the life that was
Once-upon-a-time
A weak human cry.
But now he is inseparably one
With endless height
And
With endless delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
