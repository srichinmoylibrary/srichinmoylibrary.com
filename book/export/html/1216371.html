737. Nothing is real

Nothing is real!
No, not even my heart.
Therefore I do not pray.
Nothing is real!
No, not even my soul.
Therefore I do not meditate.
Nothing is real!
No, not even God.
Therefore I do not love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 15, Agni Press, 1974
