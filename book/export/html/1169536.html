181. How to get back

How to get back
Life's lost charm?
You can get back
Life's lost charm
If you do not harm
Your childlike inner cry
And childlike outer smile.

Life's lost charm
Easily you can regain
If you refrain
From thought-temptation-chain.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
