11. I challenge

Trance of the sky,
I challenge your beauty.

Trance of the night,
I challenge your ignorance.

Trance of God,
I challenge your Duty.

Trance of man,
I challenge your unreality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 3, Agni Press, 1974
