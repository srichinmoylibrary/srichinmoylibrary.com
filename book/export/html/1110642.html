Surrender

Surrender means immediate progress. Surrender means spontaneous joy. Surrender means the matchless wisdom of the aspiring soul. Surrender means the perfection of human aspiration.

Man's unreserved surrender means God's unconditional acceptance. Surrender is the brightest and most effective key to open the Supreme's Heart's door at every moment in the all-aspiring life of a true seeker.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Rainbow-Flowers, part 1, Agni Press, 1973
