175. The waves of temptation, frustration and destruction

   Temptation-waves
Are the armies of the day.
   In vain my vital fights with them,
            In vain.   Frustration-waves
Are the armies of the night.
   In vain my heart fights with them,
            In vain.   Destruction-waves
Are the armies of tomorrow.
   In vain my soul fights with them,
            In vain.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 4, Agni Press, 1973
