Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 267

26601

When the Master Lord Supreme
Plays on His cosmic Harp,
His transcendental Life-Heart
Simply explodes with Delight.

26602

Every morning God comes down
And blesses
The sacred altar of my self-offering
With His divine Pride.

26603

Only the beauty and love
Of a childlike heart
Can transform the nature of the world.

26604

There are many people on earth
Who love to live
With their delusion-fantasies.

26605

The soul is every day,
Every hour and every minute
Asking the body, vital, mind and heart
To enter into the soul
And become as enriched as the soul is.

26606

Even the worst possible beggar
Can have a loftier than the loftiest aim
For the transformation of mankind.

26607

If you do not have inner strength,
Then God will not welcome you
To be on His Path.

26608

Among the spiritual qualities,
Humility's role can never be
Adequately described.
It can only be felt.

26609

Fear, doubt, jealousy
And insecurity
Have such power to ruin
Our God-blessed happiness.

26610

It is useless to ask the doubting mind
And the strangling vital
To stop fighting.
Let us deal only with the heart â€”
The heart that aspires
And
The heart that wants to love God.

26611

The soul touches the Feet
Of the Lord Supreme
Every morning and evening.
It is the heart, vital, mind and body
That have no time even to look at Him.

26612

No matter how many ways
We try to escape from the reality-world,
We shall never be able to.
Therefore, it is advisable
To be in the reality-world
And become true citizens
Of the reality-world.

26613

The soul silently tells us
What to do.
The soul secretly tells us
What to do.
But alas, we do not pay any attention
To the silent and secret messages
Of our soul.

26614

The mind-train
May perpetually move forward,
But it has no destination.

26615

The soul-driver of my life
Is always careful and blessingful.
The heart-driver of my life
Is careful and prayerful.
The mind-driver of my life
Is never careful.

26616

A world-peace-dreamer,
A world-peace-lover
And
A world-peace-server
Is the most dedicated exponent
And highest expression
Of the Absolute Lord Supreme.

26617

Unless we transcend
Our earthly speech and earthly thought,
We shall not be able to arrive
At the Golden Shore
Of God-Perfection and God-Satisfaction.

26618

Either consciously or unconsciously
We are searching for the highest Self,
Which is at once eternal and infinite.

26619

The experience of God's Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Heart
Can never be compared
With any other experience.

26620

Definitely the Real in us
Is always eager to come to the fore,
But we do not allow, we do not allow!

26621

To bring about the harmony
Of all humanity,
We must not allow ourselves
To feed the quenchless thirst
Of ego.

26622

To love the Supreme's Way
Of doing things
Is to become the Supreme's Way
Of Divinity and Immortality.

26623

Unless and until
Oneness with the Supreme
Is achieved,
Nothing is beautiful,
Nothing is encouraging,
Nothing is illumining,
Nothing is fulfilling.

26624

My Lord's blessingful Eye
Is my ecstasy's height,
Ecstasy's depth
And ecstasy's length.

26625

Along with spiritual food,
Earthly food also is necessary
To make the journey of the spirit
Towards the Absolute Height.

26626

I am advancing my simplicity-mind,
Purity-heart and self-giving life
Each day.

26627

Alas, I do not know
How I can get out of the disorder
Of the mind,
Which I have cherished
For a great many years.

26628

My awareness of my spiritual life
Is doomed
Because I deliberately shook hands
With the pride of ignorance-night.

26629

Oneness is complete
Only when compassion
Is infinite.

26630

Our Lord Absolute
Is always ready
To rescue us from danger,
If we just let Him.

26631

How can I have peace in my world
Amid the turmoil of the outer world?
My Lord says to me:
"Just feel that there is only one world,
And that is My world,
My Divinity's all-illumining world."

26632

You are looking for the centre
Of the universe.
I am saying to you that the true centre
Of the universe
Is inside the little room
Of your God-worshipping heart.

26633

May my morning lotus-prayers
Awaken my sleeping mind
To join me.

26634

My faith in God
Is my heart's beauty
And my soul's fragrance.

26635

If you want to love and serve someone,
Then love and serve that person
With your newness-mind,
With your oneness-heart
And
With your fulness-hands.

26636

To tear our mind's worries
Into shreds,
We need very special Blessings,
Very special Love
And
Very special Compassion
From our Lord Supreme.

26637

Gratitude, only gratitude,
Is what we need
To have a receptivity-heart.

26638

May my mind also cherish,
The way my heart cherishes,
My God-dedication-life.

26639

Our sweet, sweeter, sweetest
Gratitude-tear-drops
Expedite our Godward journey
Far beyond our imagination.

26640

Our determination-power
Will eventually bring in
Our life's transformation-satisfaction.

26641

God-dreamers live inside the heart.
God-lovers live inside the heart.
But God-examiners live inside the mind.

26642

The eagerness of the heart
And willingness of the mind
Have discovered the shortest road
To arrive at God's Feet.

26643

My morning God-obedience-songs
And
My evening God-obedience-songs
Are the only realities in my life
That I can truly be proud of.

26644

To hoist our faith-banner,
We must every day
Increase our love for God.

26645

My Lord, why are You crying?
"My child, I am crying
Because you are not smiling."

26646

There are different types of tears,
But the tears that we shed
To develop our unconditional love
For God
Are the only tears that I really need.

26647

To make the fastest progress,
Every day I pray to God
To give me the sweetness-innocence
Of a child-heart.

26648

Do not mock any God-lover
Or God-server,
For everybody at one time
Has to play the role of a beginner.

26649

Be extremely careful.
If you criticise others,
Yours will be the spiritual doom
Sooner than the soonest.

26650

My mind,
Believe me when I tell you
That the time is fast approaching
For you to surrender to God's Will.

26651

Your temporary failure
Is not the end.
It is the beginning
Of a new illumining hope
And the beginning
Of a new fulfilling progress.

26652

When I give something
And I remain unrecognised,
Then only my self-giving
Is true and perfect.

26653

I do not know how many more heartaches
I must suffer
Because my desires are not fulfilled
In my own way.

26654

God never appreciates
My cleverness-mind.
He appreciates and admires
Only my sacredness-heart.

26655

O my mind,
Every day you are making
Feeble attempts at obedience.
How can you then ever succeed?

26656

Next time I get a chance
To speak to God,
I shall ask Him, without fail,
To tell me the secret
Of unconditional surrender.

26657

God loves me most
When I cheerfully agree
To play with
His ill-fated children.

26658

For me there is no day,
No night!
I follow my Lord's express Will.

26659

My Lord, if You wish to use me,
I shall be grateful.
If You do not wish to use me,
I shall be equally grateful.
Only to please You in Your own Way
I saw the light of day.

26660

It is God who creates opportunities for us
In boundless measure.
And what else?
He also appreciates us for using them.

26661

When I try to fool someone
In the spiritual life,
I do not realise that I am dealing with
The Real in him â€” God.

26662

When I give my Lord Supreme
A small quantity of my love and faith,
He blessingfully and proudly tells me:
"My child, I have come.
Receive from Me My All."

26663

I am happy when I see
First my Lord's Eye and then His Feet.
But I am infinitely happier when I see
His Feet first and then His Eye.
God's Eye is my Protection.
God's Feet are my Salvation.

26664

My Lord Supreme,
Do tell me where I am supposed to live
Every day.
"My child,
I want you to live every day
Inside your heart-garden of self-giving."

26665

Each time I disobey God,
Either consciously or unconsciously,
I am forced to take a giant step
Away from God.

26666

Every day I face
My heart-faith-challenges,
And every day I succeed.

26667

Every day increase your love for God.
Then you will see
That the darkness of your mind
Will, without fail,
Be completely dissolved.

26668

What is my heart?
My heart is a dream of God
That every day is increasing
Its beauty, its lustre and its divinity.

26669

My Lord,
I need my heart-freedom first
For the establishment
Of my mind-illumination.

26670

What I need is a mind of patience.
What I need is a heart of poise.
What I need is a life of surrender,
Constant God-surrender.

26671

Secretly the angels guide us,
And proudly they tell God
That we are quite receptive
To God's Will.

26672

You have to compel your mind
To take a long vacation
So that you and your heart
Can work together
For the manifestation of God's Will
On earth.

26673

In the mind-jungle we notice
That gratitude is out of season
All the time.

26674

All your secrets are safe
When you keep them
In your aspiration-heart
And dedication-life.

26675

When I offer an aspiration-rose
To my Lord Supreme,
He blesses me with a bouquet
Of His Compassion-Blossoms.

26676

The climbing earth-cries
Breathe the Heavenly fragrance
Of Delight.

26677

When we live
In the unsearching mind,
We live in the age
Of total uncertainty.

26678

My mind does not believe in
World-information anymore.
It wants only world-illumination,
And for that it is going to study
At God's God-Surrender-School.

26679

What is it
That touches my heart?
My Lord's Compassion-Eye-Delight.

26680

Faith and doubt
Course through me.
I am so happy
Faith always wins.

26681

My mind's complexities
Are totally destroying
My simplicity-sincerity-heart.

26682

My body lives inside
The purity of my heart-smiles.
My life lives inside
The divinity of my soul-smiles.

26683

After a long time,
My long-lost inspiration-mind
And aspiration-heart
Have come back to me.

26684

I tremendously fear
Only one thing:
My God-unwillingness-mind.

26685

O seeker, do not forget
That you entered into the spiritual life
For inner accomplishments
And not for outer fulfilments.

26686

When our mind is flooded
With inner silence,
Then our mind will have
Free access to God
The way our heart of aspiration
Already has.

26687

At the last moment of our life,
If we pray to God for His Will to be done,
Then we are fooling ourselves.
We must pray for His Fulfilment
Throughout our life.

26688

Every day God is blessing us
With God-manifestation-possibilities
Of the most startling nature.

26689

At every moment we need
Tremendous self-confidence,
Founded upon God's Compassion-Light,
To fulfil God
And thus fulfil ourselves.

26690

If our journey's goal is success,
Then we will be totally fooled.
If our journey's goal is progress,
Then God will be proud of us
And we shall be proud of ourselves.

26691

In the spiritual life,
Nothing is of minor importance.
Everything
Is of supreme importance.

26692

Sometimes when we take
Our spiritual life
With utmost seriousness
And give no relaxation to the mind,
We are not able to enjoy the Playfulness
Of the Absolute Supreme.

26693

At every moment
We need the heart-nurturing
And life-giving springs
Of devotion.

26694

We must destroy the prison-house
Of our name and fame
To be inside Divinity's
Immortality-Palace.

26695

The mind's screen
Has ever-flickering images â€”
Nothing permanent,
Nothing useful,
Nothing fruitful!

26696

This morning when I went to see God,
His Compassion-Heart gave me
A broad Smile of Acceptance.

26697

Every day
I keep my appointment with God
Early in the morning.
I give myself no way to miss
My regular duty.

26698

My mind is roaring with determination.
My heart is flying with aspiration.
Therefore, I know my Goal
Is just next door.

26699

My soul wants me to enjoy
The brilliance and radiance-ecstasy
Of my God-seeker-victory
Over ignorance.

26700

When we really love God
With our every breath,
Then God gives us
His Dream-fulfilled, sumptuous
Reality-Feast.
