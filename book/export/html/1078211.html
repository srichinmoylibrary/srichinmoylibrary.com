GOD'S COMPASSION

God's Compassion
Has awakened my body's sleep.

God's Concern
Has sweetened my vital's madness.

God's Concentration
Has illumined my mind's blindness.

God's Satisfaction
Has immortalised my heart's selflessness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
