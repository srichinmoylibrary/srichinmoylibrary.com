23 June 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You come and go,
You come and go,
You come and go.
But this time when You come, I shall,
On the strength of my heart-tears,
Not allow You to go.
I shall keep You permanently
Inside my heart, my mind, my vital
And my body
Without fail!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

23 June 1999, 7:35 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 6, Agni Press, 1999
