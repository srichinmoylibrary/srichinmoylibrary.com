27. My outer school and my inner school

My outer school
Is the perfect bridge
     Between
My information-education
     And
Frustration-destruction.

My inner school
Is the perfect bridge
     Between
My illumination-education
     And
Realisation-perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
