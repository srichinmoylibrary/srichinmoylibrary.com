50. No difference

Love of power:
What is the difference
Between
You and a ferocious tiger?
No difference, none!

Power of love:
What is the difference
Between
You and God?
No difference, none!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
