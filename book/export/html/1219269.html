Agyanatar Timir Guhai Ami Thaki Supta

Agyanatar timir guhai ami thaki supta
Bangshidhari dao go dekha karo amai mukta

Translation:

In the darkness-cave of ignorance
   I remain fast asleep.
O Lord Krishna, do appear before me
   And liberate me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bahir jagate, part 1, , 2002
