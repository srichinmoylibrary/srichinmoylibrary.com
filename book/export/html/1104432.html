28 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May I have a new name: Grace,
And this name will be all Yours.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 September 2000, 8:51 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
