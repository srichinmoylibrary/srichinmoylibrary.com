984 Look At Me...

LOOK AT ME

You looked at me
With a hurried glance;
Therefore
I could not give you anything
In spite of my best intention.

Look at me
With your heart-eye
Slowly, steadily and unerringly,
And I shall give you
My Eternity's treasure
Unreservedly
And
Ceaselessly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
