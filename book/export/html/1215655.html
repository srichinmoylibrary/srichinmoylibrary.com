35. A great fog

There is a great fog
Between
Human love and Pride divine.

There is a great fog
Between
Human desire and Aspiration divine.

But

There is no fog,
No, none at all,
Between
Human surrender and Perfection divine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 1, Agni Press, 1974
