6. Surrender

Let us surrender to God
   Not only what we have
But also what we are.
What we have
   Is a real inner cry for God.
What we are
   Is our conscious or unconscious oneness
        With ignorance.
Let us offer both
   To the Pilot within.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Union and oneness, Agni Press, 1976
