353. Before Time kills me

I shall think of Time
   Before
Time kills me.

I shall love Time
   Before
Time kills me.

I shall use Time
   Before
Time kills me.

I shall reveal God
   Before
Time kills me.

I shall fulfil God
   Before
Time kills me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 8, Agni Press, 1973
