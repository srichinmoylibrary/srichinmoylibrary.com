8033 A New Dream

My Lord,
When I tell You that I am a hopeless case,
What do You think of me?

"My child,
I think of giving you immediately
A new dream:
The dream of God-Satisfaction
In your giant mind."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 81, Agni Press, 1983
