Ajanare Achenare

Ajanare achenare
Ami basi bhalo
Tai mor puta pran
Sada nashe kalo

Translation:

I love the unknown
And the unseen.
      Therefore
My hallowed heart
      Always
Destroys darkness-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bela chale jai, , 1979
