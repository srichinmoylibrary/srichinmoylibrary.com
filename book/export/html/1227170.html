Chalak Hale Chalbena Go Joga Pathe

Chalak hale chalbena go joga pathe
Thai pabena bishwa prabhur bijoy rathe

Translation:

Along the path of spirituality,
Cleverness will be of no avail.
You will not have a seat inside
The Victory-Chariot of the Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Khunjite chahigo, part 1, , 2003
