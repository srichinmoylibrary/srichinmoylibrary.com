Phanki Tomar Dhaka Dhaki

Phanki phanki phanki
Tomar dhaka dhaki
Saibo keman kare
Amar jiban bhare

Translation:

More deception, deception, deception.
How can I endure all my life
Your thunderous noise?

From: Sri Chinmoy, My green adoration-gifts, part 2, , 1977
