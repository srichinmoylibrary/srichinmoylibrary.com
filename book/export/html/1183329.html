193. Perfect strangers

Since you and sincerity
Are perfect strangers
You have to climb
A greased pole
Before you see the face of God.

Since you and sincerity
Are perfect strangers
Your God-realisation
Will entirely depend
On God's infinite Bounty
And not your incapacity's capacity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
