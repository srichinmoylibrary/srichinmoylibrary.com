32 I Need You Only...

I NEED YOU ONLY

O morning of beauty!
I love you.
I always love you.
O evening of silence!
I love you.
I always love you.
O time of Compassion-perfection!
I need you.
I always need you.
I need you only.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
