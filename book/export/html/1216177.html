547. Still it is not too late

O heart, my heart,
Do not cry.
Still it is not
Too late to heal.

O mind, my mind,
Do not cry.
Still it is not
Too late to expand.

O vital, my vital,
Do not cry.
Still it is not
Too late to surrender.

O body, my body,
Do not cry.
Still it is not
Too late to start.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 11, Agni Press, 1974
