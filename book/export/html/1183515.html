12. Do not imagine

Do not imagine!
The animal in you
Becomes jealous
If you imagine.
Do not think!
The divine in you feels sad
That you are a hopeless case
If you think.
Just act and become,
And become and act.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three soulful prayers, Agni Press, 1979
