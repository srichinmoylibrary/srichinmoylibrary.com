1 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May I be a heart-cry in my inner life
For You
And may I be a life-smile in my outer life
For You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 September 2000, 10:18 a.m. Before doing 600-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
