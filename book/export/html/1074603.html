41. I need

The human in me
Needs Crucifixion-night.

The Divine in me
Needs Ascension-day.

The Immortal in me
Needs Perfection-noon.

And whom do I need?

I need God.
I need Ishwara.
I need Adonai.
I need Lord.
I need Ahurmazda.
I need Allah.
I need Amitabha.

Many more, all, all
Inside the boundless Net
of the Absolute Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 1, Agni Press, 1973
