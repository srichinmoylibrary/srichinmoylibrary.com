5. The prayer of the sky

The prayer of the sky
Is for the increase
Of vastness-light.
The prayer of the moon
Is for the increase
Of beauty's right.
The prayer of the sun
Is for the increase
Of duty's delight.
The prayer of God
Is for the increase
Of Compassion's might.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The prayer of the sky, Agni Press, 1974
