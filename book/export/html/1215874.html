250. Nobody came, all came

Light for sale.
Nobody came
Because
Light is too powerful.

Truth for sale.
Nobody came
Because
Truth is too hurtful.

Ignorance for sale.
All came
Because
Ignorance teaches them how to sing.

Bondage for sale.
All came
Because
Bondage teaches them how to dance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
