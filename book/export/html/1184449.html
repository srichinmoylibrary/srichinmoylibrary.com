121. Here we are again

Here we are on earth again.
God says to me,
"Son, be brave enough to please Me
This time."

Here we are in Heaven again.
I say to God,
"Father, be kind enough to leave me alone
This time
And
For all time."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
