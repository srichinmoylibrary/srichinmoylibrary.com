5. Light is precious

Precious,
Light is precious.
Spacious,
Life is spacious.
Gracious,
Truth is gracious.
Voracious,
Falsehood is voracious.
Sagacious,
Heart is sagacious.
Glorious,
Faith is glorious.
Dangerous,
Hate is dangerous.
Conscious,
Love is conscious.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 12, Agni Press, 1974
