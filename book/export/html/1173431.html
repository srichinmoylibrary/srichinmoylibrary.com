503 O My Desire-Life

O my desire-life,
Why do you force me
Always
To carry this heavy load
Of constant grief?
Why? Why?

O my aspiration-life,
I am dying for you.
I really am.
Where do you hide?
How do you hide?
Why do you hide?
Why? Why?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 6, Agni Press, 1979
