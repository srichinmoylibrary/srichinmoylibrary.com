913 When You Conquer Temptation...

WHEN YOU CONQUER TEMPTATION

When temptation
Conquers you,
Evil smiles at you
And evil smites you.

When you
Conquer temptation,
You become a channel
For God-power,
And God supremely treasures you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
