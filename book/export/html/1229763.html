Param Pita Chhoto Seje

Param pita chhoto seje
Aso amar kachhe
Dekhe tomar bishal swarup
Bhaye mari pachhe

Translation:

O Absolute Father Supreme,
You have come to me
With a finite, tiny, little form,
In case I get frightened
When I see Your Universal Form.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy rosebuds, , 1987
