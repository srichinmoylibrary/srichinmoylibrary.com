620 Too Weak...

TOO WEAK

Too weak to watch
God-Smile,
Too weak to feel
God-Love,
Too weak to receive
God-Compassion,
Too weak to achieve
Inner cry.
Yet
I proudly claim
I am of God
And
I am for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
