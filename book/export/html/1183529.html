26. Lord, I love you

Lord, I love You
Because You are great;
I love You
Because You are good.
Your greatness inspires me
To be great like You.
Your goodness elevates me
To be good like You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three soulful prayers, Agni Press, 1979
