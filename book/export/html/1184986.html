658. Entirely dependent on you

I am entirely dependent on You.
I shall become the air of Your immortal Bliss.
I shall become Your unconditional follower divine.
I shall maintain my unbroken hope.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
