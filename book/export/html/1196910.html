11163

You may allow all your other possessions
To be taken away,
But do not allow one possession of yours
To be stolen,
And that possession is
Your Eternity's hope-boat.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 112, Agni Press, 1987
