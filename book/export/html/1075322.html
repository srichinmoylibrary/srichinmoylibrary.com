746 I Am Following You...

I AM FOLLOWING YOU

O good intentions,
I am following you all
With good deeds.

O good deeds,
I am following you all
With a good life.

O good life,
I am following you
With God-Manifestation.

O God-Manifestation,
I am following you
With God-Perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
