15857

My Lord,
Nowadays You do not fulfil my desires.
Therefore, I am leaving You.
"My child,
Do you not think
That I have the capacity
To haunt you and chase you
And bring you back?"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 159, Agni Press, 1991
