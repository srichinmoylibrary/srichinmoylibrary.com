13

My Lord, I do so many things for You.
How many things do You do
For me?
"My child, since I am an old man,
I do only one thing for you.
Every day, for twenty-four hours,
I cradle you inside My Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Heart."

From: Sri Chinmoy, I am my life's God-hunger-heart, part 3, Agni Press, 1994
