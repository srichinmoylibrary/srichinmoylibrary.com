493

My mind is waiting
For my heart's absurdity-dream to end.
My heart is waiting
For my mind's hallucination-dream to end.
My life is waiting
For my impossibility-dream to end.
My soul is waiting
For my ignorance-dream to end.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 5, Agni Press, 1983
