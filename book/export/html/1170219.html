123. Ascend, descend

Ascend, ascend!
At the end of your Heaven-bound journey
You will grow into
God the Eternal Lover.
Descend, descend!
At the end of your earth-bound journey
You will grow into
God the Eternal Beloved.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sound becomes, silence is, Agni Press, 1975
