106. I know the root

I know,
I know the root of things:
Beauty sublime.

I know,
I know the root of man:
Divinity supernal.

I know,
I know the root of God:
Necessity eternal.

I know,
I know the root of myself:
Reality immortal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
