27. I love death

I love death
Because
I am renewed by death.

I love life
Because
I am inspired by life.

I love love
Because
I am energised by love.

I love God
Because
I am examined,
Perfected
And
Fulfilled by God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
