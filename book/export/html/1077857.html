210. Always remember

You have four rooms of your own:
Temptation-room,
Tempest-room,
Suspicion-room,
Salvation-room.
Your body loves temptation-room;
Don't forget that!
Your vital loves tempest-room;
Don't forget that!
Your mind loves suspicion-room;
Don't forget that!
Your heart loves salvation-room;
Always remember that!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
