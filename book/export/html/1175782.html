2831 Because My Game Was A Pleasure-Life

Because my game was a pleasure-life,
Today I see nothing but pressure
Inside the heart
Of the inner world
And inside the body
Of the outer world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 29, Agni Press, 1982
