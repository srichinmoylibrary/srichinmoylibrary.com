15. I shall not fail

I shall not fail,
I shall not fail.

My soul shall sail,
My heart shall sail.

I shall succeed,
I shall succeed.

My life shall take
The glorious lead.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
