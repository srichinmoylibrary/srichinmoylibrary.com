163. The age of achievement

This is not the age of triumph.
This is the age of sacrifice.
This is the age of service.
This is the age of love-achievement,
truth-achievement
and
perfection-achievement,
Here, there, everywhere.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
