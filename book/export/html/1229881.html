Faith, My Faith

Faith, my faith,
Never leave me.
You know, without you,
I am a rudderless boat.
Surrender, my surrender,
Never leave me.
You know, without you,
I shall reach a goalless shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Silence, please! God is coming, , 1999
