36. In themselves

Desire in itself is
Hurtful.

Aspiration in itself is
Fruitful.

Grace in itself is
Success-might.

Surrender in itself is
Progress-light.

God in Himself is
Perfection-Height.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 3, Agni Press, 1974
