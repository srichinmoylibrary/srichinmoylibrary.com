36. Oneness

Where is oneness?
On earth?
   Impossible!

Where is oneness?
In Heaven?
   Impossible!

Where is oneness?
   Oneness is there
   Where I see
   The Face of my Beloved
           And
   Where I become
The Grace of my Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
