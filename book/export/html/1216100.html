471. When I weep

Sometimes I wake to weep,
Sometimes I weep to wake.
My heart loves me
When I weep to wake.
My life loves me
When I wake to weep.
And I love myself
When I weep, only weep.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 10, Agni Press, 1974
