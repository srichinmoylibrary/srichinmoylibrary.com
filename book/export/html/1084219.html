40. Nothing else to think about

Lord, I think of You
Not because You are
Kind to me,
But because I have
Nothing else to think about.

"Son, I think of you
Not because I have
Nothing else to think about,
But because when
I think of you
I get the joy of a child,
The strength of a youth
And the wisdom of an old man."

Lord, from now on
I shall think of You
And not remain indebted to You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 5, Agni Press, 1974
