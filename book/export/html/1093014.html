37.

My Absolute Lord Supreme!
The unaspiring mind
Is a powerful magnet
For nonsense.
The aspiring heart
Is the most powerful magnet
For universal oneness.

14 December 1999

5:21 a.m.

Nova Friburgo, Brazil

From: Sri Chinmoy, My aspiration-heart cycles, part 1, Agni Press, 2000
