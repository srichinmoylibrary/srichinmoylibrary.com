26. His life-chronicle

His body is a village
Of the sleepers.
His vital is a town
Of the fighters.
His mind is a city
Of the dreamers.
His heart is a province
Of the lovers.
His life is a country
Of the sufferers.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
