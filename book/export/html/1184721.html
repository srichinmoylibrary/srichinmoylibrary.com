393. Thy Will is my peace

Lord,
Today at long last
I have lost
The fear of failure;
Therefore
I have become Thy Will.
Thy Will alone is my peace,
My Eternity's peace,
My Immortality's peace,
My Reality's peace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
