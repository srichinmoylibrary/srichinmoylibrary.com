17184

The difference between
Your path and my path
Is this:
Your path thinks
That one has to do everything good
To please God;
My path thinks
That nobody can please God
Unless God wants to please Himself
In and through us.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 172, Agni Press, 1992
