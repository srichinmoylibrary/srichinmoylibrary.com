727. O my aspiring heart

Heart,
In you is my home;
In you is my all.
You are the preserver
Of all that is real in me.
You are my God-hunger,
You are my God-nourishment,
O my aspiring heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
