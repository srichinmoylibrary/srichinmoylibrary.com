Be Thou

Be Thou my all.
Be Thou my body
That I may wake.
Be Thou my vital
That I may run.
Be Thou my mind
That I may fly.
Be Thou my heart
That I may dive.
Be Thou my soul
That I may reveal.
Be Thou my Goal
That I may fulfil.
Be Thou my all
That I may be only Yours.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Song-flowers, part 8, , 1992
