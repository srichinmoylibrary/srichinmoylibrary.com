10 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
My Lord Beloved Supreme,
My sleepless devotion to You
Is my mind's newness,
My heart's sweetness
And my life's fulness.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

10 July 1999, 8:24 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
