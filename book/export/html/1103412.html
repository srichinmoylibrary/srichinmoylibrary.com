27 April 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart's streaming tears
   Shorten
My God-Destination-distance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 7:31 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 180 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
