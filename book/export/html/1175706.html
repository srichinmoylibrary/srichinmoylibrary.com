2756 The Beginning

Self-observation is the beginning
Of self-perfection.
Self-perfection is the beginning
Of God-Satisfaction.
God-Satisfaction is the beginning
Of God's new Dream.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 28, Agni Press, 1982
