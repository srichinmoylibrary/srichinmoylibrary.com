217. Open

Open my body.
You will see engraved
        "Service-Life."

Open my vital.
You will see engraved
        "Compassion-Life."

Open my mind.
You will see engraved
        "Will-Life."

Open my heart.
You will see engraved
        "Love-Life."

Open my life.
You will see engraved
        "Promise-Life.
        Failure-Life.
        Surrender-Life.
        Perfection-Life."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 5, Agni Press, 1973
