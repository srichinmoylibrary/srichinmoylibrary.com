Tomare Cheyechi Tomare Pujechi Tomare

Tomare cheyechhi
Tomare pujechhi
Tomare peyechhi
Tomare bendhechhi

Translation:

I have desired You.
I have worshipped You.
I have received You.
I have bound You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 8a, , 1981
