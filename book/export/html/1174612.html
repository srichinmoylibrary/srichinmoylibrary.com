1673 Satisfaction Wants Immortality

Ignorance in man
Wants superiority.
Knowledge in man
Wants equality.
Wisdom in man
Wants liberty.
Satisfaction in man
Wants immortality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 17, Agni Press, 1981
