21. No ordinary human being

You are no ordinary
Human being.
In you I see
A star of illumination blue.
He is no ordinary
Human being.
In him I see
A moon of compassion green.
And
I am no ordinary
Human being.
In myself I see
A sun of perfection gold.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
