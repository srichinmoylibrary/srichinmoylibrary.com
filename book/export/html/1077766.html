119. The Lord

Light supreme
Is the Lord of the immortals.

Love supreme
Is the Lord of the mortals.

Preparation supreme
Is the Lord of my earth-life.

Perfection supreme
Is the Lord of my Heaven-soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
