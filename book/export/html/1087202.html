The advantage of being short

Two days ago X and I went shopping. At one spot, they were unloading things from a truck, and there was a rail blocking the sidewalk. I was about to go out into the street to walk around the rail.

X was smiling at me. Then he just walked right under the rail.

I said, "See, there is an advantage to being short!"

-30 October 1987

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
