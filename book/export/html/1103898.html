20 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my inner life love God-dream s
   Sleeplessly.
May my outer life serve God-realities
   Unconditionally.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 October 1999, 9:37 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
