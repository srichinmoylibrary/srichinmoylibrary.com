Dyuloke Purna Bhuloke Shunya Amar

Dyuloke purna bhuloke shunya
Amar hriday khani
Kemane bidhata karecho srijan
Keno taha nahi jani
Ogo jadukar dyuloke bhuloke
Bichitra taba khela
Janina bujhina taito tomai
Kari ami abahela

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 9b, , 1981
