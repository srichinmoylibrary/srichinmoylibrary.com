TOUCHES

God-Touch
Has given me
The wings of morn.

Man-touch
Has given me
The wings of eve.

Faith-touch
Has given me
The breath of life.

Doubt-touch
Has given me
The buffets of death.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
