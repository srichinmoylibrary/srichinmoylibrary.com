6944

Do not worry
If you have lost God's Message
In your mind's ignorance-mire.
Just ask your heart
To beg God
To grant you another Message.
But be sure, this time,
Not to lose it!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 70, Agni Press, 1984
