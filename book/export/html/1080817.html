THE PEACE CONCERT

It was held at Public School 117 in Briarwood, New York, on Saturday, 25 November 1995.

It started at 9:05 in the morning and continued until 11:20 at night. There were six sessions with a brief intermission between each one.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A fourteen hour peace concert Immensity-Peace-Sea-Experiences, Agni Press, 1995
