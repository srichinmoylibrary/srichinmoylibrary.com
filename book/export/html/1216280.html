648. I am a child

I am a child of Heaven.
Therefore
I dream.

I am a child of earth.
Therefore
I serve.

I am a child of sound.
Therefore
I grow.

I am a child of silence.
Therefore
I glow.

I am a child of God.
Therefore
I really am.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
