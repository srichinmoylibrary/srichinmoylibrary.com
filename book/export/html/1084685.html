47. When sound was my Lord

When God
Was my Lord,
I was misunderstood.
When I
Was my Lord,
All alone I stood.
When Silence
Was my Lord,
I was deified.
When Sound
Was my Lord,
I was crucified.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 14, Agni Press, 1974
