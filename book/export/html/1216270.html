638. Be cheerful

O sincere seeker,
You may realise God
Even in this lifetime. Who knows?
So be cheerful. I tell you,
This can easily happen.

O pure seeker,
God may choose you to be
His matchless instrument
To fulfil Him on earth. Who knows?
So be cheerful. I tell you,
This can easily happen.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
