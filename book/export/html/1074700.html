136. When I do

   When I do
One thing at a time,
        I am successful.   When I do
Two things at a time,
        I am doubtful.   When I do
Three things at a time,
        I am fearful.   When I do
Four things at a time,
        God smiles at me.   When I do
Five things at a time,
        God cries with me.   When I do
Six things at a time,
        God dies with me.   When I do
Seven things at a time,
        God does them all for me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 3, Agni Press, 1973
