451. Everything kills him

Worry kills him,
Anxiety kills him
Fear kills him,
Doubt kills him,
Everything kills him.
But
He can be saved
Not only from one thing
But from everything,
Provided he has the willingness
To follow the dictates
Of the inner Source.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
