10951

Man wants only one thing
In life and from life:
A comfortable complacency-life.
God wants from man only one thing:
He wants man to learn every day
A new song of self-transcendence
From Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 110, Agni Press, 1987
