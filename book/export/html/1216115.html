486. You will be loved

Bind not!
You will be bound.
Lo, even the boundless time
Is bound by a tiny clock.

Love!
You will be loved.
Lo, even a tiny dew drop
Is loved by the morning God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 10, Agni Press, 1974
