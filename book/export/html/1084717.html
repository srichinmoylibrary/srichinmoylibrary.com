28 Humanity Misunderstood...

HUMANITY MISUNDERSTOOD

Humanity
Misunderstood me
When
I stood an inch higher.
Humanity
Misunderstood me
When
I stood an inch lower.
Humanity
Misunderstood me
When
I stood parallel.
Therefore
My frustration-life
Is pining to enter
Into the world-chasm of nowhere.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
