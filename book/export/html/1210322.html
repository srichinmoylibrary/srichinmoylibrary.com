24442

Am I blind?
Why can I not see
That my Lord Supreme
Is piloting my life-boat
To His choice Destination:
The oneness and fulness
Of humanity's divinity?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 245, Agni Press, 1998
