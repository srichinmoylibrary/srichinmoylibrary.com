51.

I soulfully pray
To my Absolute Lord Supreme
That each and every Russian,
Plus each and every world-citizen,
Sleeplessly adore and breathlessly love
President Gorbachev —
Which he so unmistakably deserves —
Long before he sails his life-boat
To the Golden Shore of the Beyond.

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: the home of Oneness-Peace-Dream-World, Agni Press, 2001
