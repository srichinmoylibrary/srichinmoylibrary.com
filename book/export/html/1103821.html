28 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall never, never try
To understand You.
This is my sole promise to You.
My Supreme, do increase my faith
At every moment
In Your Compassion-Eye,
Forgiveness-Heart
And Protection-Feet.
This is my sole prayer to You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 September 1999, 8:16 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 400 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
