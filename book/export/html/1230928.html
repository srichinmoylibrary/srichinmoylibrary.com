Haste Pari Gaite Pari Nachte Pari

Haste pari gaite pari nachte pari
Dak eseche rakhal rajar bangshidhari

Translation:

Call has come from the cowherd king,
The flute player supreme.
Therefore, I can smile, I can sing,
I can dance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sundaratama dekha dilo aji, part 2, , 2006
