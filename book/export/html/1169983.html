628. Only You

Father, You love not only me
But my ignorance too.
Who can and who will go so far?
Father,
It is You,
Only You,
Who can not only go so far
But infinitely farther.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
