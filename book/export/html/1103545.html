30 June 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
That if I start laughing,
Then You will start smashing
One by one
All my problems.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 June 1999, 8:52 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 6, Agni Press, 1999
