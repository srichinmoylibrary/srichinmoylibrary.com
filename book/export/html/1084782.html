42 Everything Happens To Please Me...

EVERYTHING HAPPENS TO PLEASE ME

When the time is ripe,
Everything happens to please me.
Lo and behold!
Earth-cry needs me,
My dedicated service,
To realise God.
Heaven-smile needs me,
My dedicated service,
For its manifestation
On earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 16, Agni Press, 1974
