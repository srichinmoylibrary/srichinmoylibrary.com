20387

When our pride makes us feel
That we are the doers,
And we do not need any help
From the inner world
Or the outer world,
That pride will be forced
To crumble into dust.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 204, Agni Press, 1994
