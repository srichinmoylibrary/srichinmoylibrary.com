Ke Uthilo Daki

Ke uthilo daki
Hriday gahane thaki
O je mor pran o pakhi
Se jage ekaki
Cheye thake abiram
Analas ankhi

Translation:

Who is awakening my heart today?
Ah, it is my soul-bird.
My soul-bird looks around with its all-loving
     And all-illumining vision
Where purity reigns supreme.

Translation:

I do not want life.
I do not want death.
I want to have only Your Feet
That destroy all my fear.
I want to have a crying heart.
May Your Will always be fulfilled.

Taba Pada Renu Hiyar Kusume

Taba pada renu hiyar kusume
Sajabo param jatane
Tumi prananath anadi tiyasa
Amar jibane marane

Translation:

With the dust of Your Feet
I shall carefully and soulfully
Decorate my flower-heart.
You are the Lord of my life,
You are my eternal thirst
In my life and in my death.
