156 You Are Not

You are not
The breath of your pride.

You are not
The life of your jealousy.

You are not
The body of your ignorance.

You are
The heart of God's Vision-Love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 2, Agni Press, 1979
