Shunya Hate Shunya Hiyai

Shunya hate shunya hiyai
Purnatare dakbo
Ei je amar pan
Bedan rate maran buke
Purnatare bandhbo
Amar ashar dhan

Translation:

With empty hands and with an empty heart
I shall invoke the life of Infinityâ€™s perfection.
