141 Someday, Somehow, Somewhere

His confiscated body,
His frustrated vital,
His humiliated head,
His elevated heart,
His unlimited soul
Will grant him
Someday, somehow, somewhere
An emancipated life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 2, Agni Press, 1979
