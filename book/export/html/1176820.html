3859 The Transformation Of Man's Nature

The transformation of man's nature
Is not an easy task.
Everybody knows that.
But is it not true
That there are many who really enjoy
Their animal nature?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 39, Agni Press, 1982
