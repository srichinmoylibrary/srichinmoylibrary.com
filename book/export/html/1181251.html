8246 Tomorrow Try To Be Satisfied

Today be satisfied
With what you have.
Tomorrow try to be satisfied
Not only with what God has
For you
But also with what God is
For Himself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 83, Agni Press, 1983
