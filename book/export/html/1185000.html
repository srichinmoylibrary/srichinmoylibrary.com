672. Lord, I thank You

Lord, I thank You
For Your Smile.

I thank You
For my heart's cry.

I thank You
For all that You have done for me
And all that You have not done for me.

Lord, I thank You
Because You have given me
A sure heart,
A brave vital,
A pure mind
And a strong body.

Lord, finally, I love You
Because You have made me
Your blessing-love incarnate.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
