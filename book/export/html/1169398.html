43. The power of life

The power of life
I badly need.
The life of power
Who wants?
Not me, not me.
Although
Power wants to bind me,
I shall remain
At easy ease,
Eternally free.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
