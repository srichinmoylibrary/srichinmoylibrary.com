HESITATION

Where do I hesitate?
In my mind-room.
When do I hesitate?
The moment I shake hands
With falsehood.
Why do I hesitate?
I hesitate
Because
Still I have not claimed myself
As God's supremely chosen child.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
