41. Are you not ashamed?

O my mind,
Are you not ashamed
Of your darkness blind?
O my heart,
Are you not ashamed
Of your deplorable insecurity?
O my life,
Are you not ashamed
Of your undying stupidity?

From: Sri Chinmoy, When I left God in Heaven, Agni Press, 1975
