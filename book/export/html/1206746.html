20901

O seeker,
If you want to remain alive on earth,
Then every day make a solemn promise
To yourself
That you are going to remain alive
Only with your heart's aspiration-cries.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 210, Agni Press, 1994
