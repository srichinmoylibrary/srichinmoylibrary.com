181. Songs of the stars

The songs of the morning stars
Teach me:
"Be brave and great."
O morning stars,
I am of you.

The songs of the evening stars
Teach me:
"Be patient and good."
O evening stars,
I am for you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
