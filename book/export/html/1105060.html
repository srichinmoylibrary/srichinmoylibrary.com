8.

My Lord,
I run and run and run and run
To make You happy.
“My child,
You are the smile of My Eye,
You are the beauty of My Heart
And you are the pride of My Life.”

11 June 2006
New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 2008
