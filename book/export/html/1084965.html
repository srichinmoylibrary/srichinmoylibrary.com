21. I am grateful to God

In the physical world
I am grateful to God
     Because
He has forgiven me
     And
My over-indulgence.

In the vital world
I am grateful to God
     Because
He has forgiven me
     And
My over-confidence.

In the mental world
I am grateful to God
     Because
He has forgiven me
     And
My self-absorption.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
