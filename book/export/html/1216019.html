392. They offer

His body-temple offers
Purity's smile.
His vital-temple offers
Agility's smile.
His mind-temple offers
Sincerity's smile.
His heart-temple offers
Humility's smile.
His soul-temple offers
Luminosity's smile.
And he himself offers
Perfection's Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 8, Agni Press, 1974
