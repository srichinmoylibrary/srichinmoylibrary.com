39. Safety

Heart-cry is my body's
Only safe spot.
God-smile is my life's
Only safe shore.
Earth-sacrifice is my vital's
Only safe promise.
Heaven-oneness is my soul's
Only safe perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 6, Agni Press, 1974
