10.

My Lord,
Will You allow me
To be as good as You are?
Certainly.
Do you know why,
My child?
Why, my Lord?
Because,
If you become as good as I am,
Then I will be able
To enjoy My retirement,
Which I so desperately need.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My child, you and I are in the same boat, Agni Press, 1991
