6.

I do not pray
To an unknown God.

I do not meditate
On an unknowable God.

I pray to the God
Whose Transcendental Eye
Is extremely familiar.

I meditate on the God
Whose universal Heart
Is extremely popular.

His Transcendental Eye
Tells me to make Him happy
Only in His own Way.

His Universal Heart
Tells me to become perfect
Only in His own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Morning prayers and poems on war, Agni Press, 1992
