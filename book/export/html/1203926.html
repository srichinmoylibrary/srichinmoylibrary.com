18109

Do not be afraid
Of going too far
With your dedication-life.
At one point
God's smiling Eyes
Will greet you.

Do not be afraid
Of going too deep
In your aspiration-life.
At one point
God's loving Heart
Will bless you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 182, Agni Press, 1993
