826. Why do you need me?

Why do You need me, Lord?
"Son, I need you because
With you I shall face
My ever-transcending Beyond,
For you I shall play
My cosmic Game,
In you I shall see
My Dream-Reality and My Reality-Dream."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
