18 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must come back to Your Feet,
To Your Lotus-Feet,
To Your Nectar-Feet.
I must come back immediately,
I must come back breathlessly,
I must come back unconditionally.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 May 1999, 7:39 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. sixty-four times with one arm. (32 right, 32 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 5, Agni Press, 1999
