4.

My Lord says to me,
“My child,
Take your outer pain
As your inner gain.
Take your outer failures
As your inner triumphs.
Take your outer life
As an experience.
Take your inner life
As your path-finder.”

20 May 2006
New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 2008
