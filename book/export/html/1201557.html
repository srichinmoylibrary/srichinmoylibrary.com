15764

If you are reluctantly ready
To change your life,
Then God does not want it.
He cannot appreciate
Your reluctance-transformation.
What He needs and wants from you
Is a cheerful and willing
Transformation-beauty,
Transformation-perfection and
Transformation-satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 158, Agni Press, 1991
