14. His descending sun

   I saw
His ascending sun.
It was so pure and sure;
Yet it has failed.   I saw
His descending sun.
Earth and Heaven alike
Did not care for it.
And, to my surprise,
Neither did he himself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Yesterday I was a crying dream, Agni Press, 1975
