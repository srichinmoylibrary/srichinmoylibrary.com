Question: Both my husband and I have seen you in your previous incarnations with different clothes and different ornaments. It was perhaps a few hundred years back or maybe a thousand years back. Once you were giving a concert. Afterwards, my husband asked me, &quot;Did you see what I saw?&quot; Then I said, &quot;You're talking about Guru's new earrings?&quot; &quot;Yes,&quot; he said. &quot;Yes.&quot; Then when you came out, when the concert was over, you appeared as normal. But throughout the concert you were wearing a different costume from a different time period. That was amazing.

Sri Chinmoy: It is absolutely true!
