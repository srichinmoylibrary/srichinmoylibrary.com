10 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I shall pray to You,
I shall meditate on You,
I shall love You
And I shall serve You
Even long after I have made
My surrender complete.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

10 July 1999, 8:16 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
