997. Consciousness-sea, light-sky

O Consciousness-God,
I need Your Power.
Don't forget me.
If You forget me
I shall die.
I tell You,
You will be embarrassed.
For Your sake, for my sake,
Give me Your Consciousness-sea.

O Light-Goddess,
I need Your Love.
Don't forget me.
If You forget me
I shall die.
I tell You,
You will be embarrassed.
For Your sake, for my sake,
Give me Your Light-sky.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
