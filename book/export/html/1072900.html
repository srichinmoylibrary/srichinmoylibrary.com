Beautiful is my whispering soul

1.

Beautiful is my whispering soul.
Beautiful is my climbing heart.
Beautiful is my willing mind.
Beautiful is my uncomplaining vital.
Beautiful is my serving body.

2.

I look beautiful
When I accept
Impossibility-challenges.

3.

I look beautiful
When I play with
My silence-mind.

4.

I look beautiful
When I am not tortured
By jealousy, insecurity
And doubt.

5.

I look beautiful
When my mind divinely enjoys
The endless process
Of transformation.

6.

I look beautiful
When each thought of mine
Grows into angelic perfection.

7.

I am supremely beautiful
Only when I become
The nothingness of my own life.

8.

I am supremely beautiful
Only when I become
The fulness of my soul.

9.

10.

I am challenging
The haughtiness
Of untruth.

11.

I am challenging
The tyranny
Of impurity.

12.

I am challenging
My mind's
Confusion-jungle-thoughts.

13.

I am challenging
My ingratitude-mind.

14.

I am challenging
My long-standing helplessness-life,
Hopelessness-life
And fruitlessness-life.

15.

I am challenging
My failure-pangs
And pathos-sighs.

16.

I am challenging
My worst enemy:
Self-indulgence.

17.

I am challenging,
On God's behalf,
His oldest enemy:
My ignorance-night.

18.

I am challenging
The death
Of my God-manifestation-dream.

19.

I am challenging
My heart's
Anxiety-chains.

20.

I am challenging
The fruitless desert
Of my mind.

21.

I am challenging
The bite
Of my loneliness-vital.

22.

I am challenging
My desire-snakes.

23.

I am challenging
My mind's
Unwillingness-supremacy.

24.

I am challenging
My satisfaction-punctured heart.

25.

I am challenging
The failure of my soul's mission
On earth.

26.

I am challenging
My expectation-frustration-graveyard.

27.

I am challenging
The inevitable doom
Of human life.

28.

I am challenging
The wasteful regret
Of my failure-life.

29.

I am challenging
My mind's sincerity:
Does it really love God?
Does it really need God?
Can it really live without God?
Can it really dare to doubt God?

30.

31.

No more
Shall I suffer
From binding and blinding anxieties.

32.

No more
Shall I enjoy
Aspiration-vacation.

33.

No more
Shall I indulge
In dedication-retirement.

34.

No more
Shall I suffer
From an incomplete heart.

35.

No more
Shall I tolerate
My mind's surrender-bankruptcy.

36.

No more
Shall I be afraid
Of my heavy responsibilities.

37.

No more
Shall I be with those
Who tell me that I am not
God's choice instrument.

38.

No more
Shall I ignore
My heart's peace-hunger.

39.

No more
Shall I allow my mind
To take the expectation-frustration-course.

40.

No more
Shall I allow my mind
To think of any human being
As a dangerous stranger.

41.

No more
Shall I allow my vital
To love suspicion-wrestling.

42.

No more
Shall I allow my mind
To feed on doubt-boxing.

43.

No more
Shall I cherish
My mind's ego-feast
And my heart's oneness-starvation.

44.

45.

I am more.
I am more
Than my mind's sincerity.

46.

I am more.
I am more
Than my heart's purity.

47.

I am more.
I am more
Than my life's spontaneity.

48.

I am more.
I am more
Than my outer appearances.

49.

I am more.
I am more
Than my inner experiences.

50.

I am more.
I am more
Than my readiness.

51.

I am more.
I am more
Than my willingness.

52.

I am more.
I am more
Than my fulness.

53.

Who am I actually, then?
I am my Eternity's Dream-Boat
Heading towards
My Immortality's Reality-Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beautiful is my whispering soul, Agni Press, 1992
