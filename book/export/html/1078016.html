369. I love

I love the eyes of my morning
Because
They are beautiful.

I love the arms of my noon
Because
They are powerful.

I love the feet of my evening
Because
They are peaceful.

I love the heart of my night
Because
It is soulful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
