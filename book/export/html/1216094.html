465. In my case

Everybody needs something.
Therefore,
In my case I need
My old friend, the Himalayan cave.

Everybody needs someone.
Therefore,
In my case I need
God the dream-boat Sailor.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 10, Agni Press, 1974
