5715 I Need Only One Thing

During the day
I need only one thing:
God's Vision-Eye
To guide me.

During the night
I need only one thing:
God's Compassion-Heart
To illumine me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 58, Agni Press, 1983
