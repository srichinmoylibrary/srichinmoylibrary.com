Dugdha Dayini Pushti Dayini

Dugdha dayini pushti dayini tushti dayini gabhi
Sahas anane atmatyagini nahi karo kichu dabi
[Dedicated to the cow]

Translation:

She who gives milk,
And nourishes and satisfies with the milk,
Self-giving with a smiling face,
She never asks for anything.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sundaratama dekha dilo aji, part 2, , 2006
