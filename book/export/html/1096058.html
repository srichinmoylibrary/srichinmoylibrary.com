My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 29

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Bali, Indonesia in January 2004.

5 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

1 God-Realisation ...

God-realisation
Is not an impossible task —
No, not even
The most difficult task.
So says my soul to my mind.

2 To Be A Supremely Chosen Instrument ...

To be a supremely chosen instrument
Of God
Has to be the aim
Of each and every God-seeker.

3 Nothing Forever Remains The Same ...

Nothing forever remains the same.
This darkest night of my mind
Shall pass —
I know, I know.

4 I Do Not Pray To God ...

I do not pray to God
To weaken my enemies.
I pray to God
To illumine them.

5 God Wants Me To Build ...

God wants me to build
A special Heaven
For me and my oneness-heart-friends
Here on earth
And nowhere else.

6 No Heart ...

No heart
Is too weak
To receive Love
From God.

7 No Life ...

No life
Is too insignificant
To love
And serve God.

8 The Mind’s Proudest Verdict: ...

The mind’s proudest verdict:
God does not know
What the mind does.

9 The Heart’s Humblest Opinion: ...

The heart’s humblest opinion:
God not only knows
What the heart does
In every detail,
But also inspires the heart
To do everything
Lovingly, soulfully
And self-givingly.

10 My Heart-Breath ...

My heart-breath
Secretly and sacredly treasures
My sleepless love of God
And my breathless service to God.

11 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Is not at all interested
In listening to my life’s
Worthless and useless stories,
But He is extremely eager
To hear my heart-songs
And also extremely eager
To watch my life’s
Surrender-dance.

6 January 2004 ...

6 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

12 I Use My Vision-Eye ...

I use my vision-eye
When I am in
The inner world.

13 I Use My Mission-Heart ...

I use my mission-heart
When I am in
The outer world.

14 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Please, please, please
Do not allow
My gratitude-heart-flower
Ever to fade!

15 When I Most Sincerely Pray ...

When I most sincerely pray,
My Lord says to me,
“My child,
You are, indeed,
My Beauty’s child.”

16 During My Very Deep Meditation ...

During my very deep meditation,
God comes to me and says,
“My child,
You are, indeed,
My Duty’s hero-warrior.”

17 Love And Serve ...

Love and serve.
Love God the Creator;
Serve God the Creation.
Your life-breath
In God’s Heart-World
Will never cease.

18 I Am Eagerly Waiting For The Day ...

I am eagerly waiting for the day
When my God-love,
My God-devotion
And my God-surrender
Will be in full bloom.

19 My New Inner Name ...

My new inner name
Is
God-Blessing-abundance.

20 My New Outer Name ...

My new outer name
Is
God-surrender-ecstasy.

21 To God ...

To God,
Nothing is more important
Than my tearful heart
And my blissful eyes.

7 January 2004 ...

7 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

22 To Increase My Love Of God ...

To increase my love of God
Every day
Is the only way for me
To live divinely on earth.

23 My Mind And I — ...

My mind and I —
When we depend on each other,
We miserably fail.

24 My Heart And I — ...

My heart and I —
When we depend on each other,
We invariably succeed.

25 My God-Obedience ...

My God-obedience
Is my God-given
Golden flute-melody-life.

26 My God-Disobedience ...

My God-disobedience
Is the thunder-drum-sound
Cherished by the hostile forces.

27 My Ego-Mind ...

My ego-mind
Tries to overrule
My soul’s solemn
Judgement-light-utterances
In vain.

28 My Master Reveals ...

My Master reveals
The unseen God to me
At the very right moment.

29 I Must Seriously Take Care ...

I must seriously take care
Of my inner health
And my outer life.

30 The Tears Of My Lord’s ...

The Tears of my Lord’s
Compassion-Eye
Strengthen my life
And lengthen my breath.

8 January 2004 ...

8 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

31 My Love, My Devotion ...

My love, my devotion
And my surrender —
God has taken them
As His confidants.

32 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Do make me
Your selfless slave
To make me really happy
In this lifetime.

33 We All Must Discover ...

We all must discover
The secret and sacred magnitude
Of each divine thought.

34 God Is Ready To Discuss With Me ...

God is ready to discuss with me,
Not about my past,
Not about my future,
But about today.

35 My Real Homeland ...

My real homeland
Is
My God-aspiration-heart.

36 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Do give me another chance!
I am ready for the climb.
This time I shall not fail You.

37 Long, Long Ago ...

Long, long ago
You lost God.
You are now
Rediscovering Him.
God is helping you.

38 In The Spiritual Life ...

In the spiritual life,
Every day, every hour,
Every minute
I must long for
God’s inner Guidance
And God’s outer Guidance.

9 January 2004 ...

9 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

39 God’s Compassion-Eye ...

God’s Compassion-Eye
Blessingfully teaches me
How to cry.

40 God’s Forgiveness-Heart ...

God’s Forgiveness-Heart
Blessingfully teaches me
How to smile.

41 I Make No Mistake ...

I make no mistake
When I tell the world
That I am all God’s.
I make no mistake
When I tell the world
That God is all mine.

42 My Lord Says To Me ...

My Lord says to me,
“My child,
You are totally mistaken.
You must say
That you are all God
And I am all you, only you.”

43 With Every Single Breath ...

With every single breath,
My heart and I
Are rising towards
Our Lord Beloved Supreme.

44 At Long Last ...

At long last
I am able to enter
Into my life’s
Beauty-fragrance-inundated horizon.

45 Transcendence And Expansion ...

Transcendence and expansion
Work together
Happily and proudly.

46 When I Offer ...

When I offer
My life’s power-of-attorney
To my Lord Supreme,
He immediately embraces me
Lovingly, powerfully and self-givingly.

47 A Sleepless Self-Giver-Seeker ...

A sleepless self-giver-seeker
To humankind
Has no time for himself.

48 During My Deep Meditation ...

During my deep meditation,
When God-ecstasy appears,
My inefficiency-life
Immediately disappears.

10 January 2004 ...

10 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

49 From This Moment On ...

From this moment on,
My Lord shall take His seat
Inside my stupidity-flooded mind.

50 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Never says ‘no’
To me,
But I do say ‘no’
To Him.

51 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Never doubts me,
But I do doubt Him.

52 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme
Never insists —
But I do.

53 Where Do I Live? ...

Where do I live?
I live in between
My life’s eagerness-heart
And my life’s intensity-breath
To fulfil my Lord Beloved Supreme.

54 Self-Indulgence ...

Self-indulgence,
I have nothing to do with you.
I shall see you no more.

55 Every Day My Lord Supreme ...

Every day my Lord Supreme
Most seriously examines
My mind-purity-capacities.

56 Every Day My Beloved Supreme ...

Every day my Beloved Supreme
Most affectionately feeds
All my necessities.

57 Desires ...

Desires
Dominate my life.
Aspiration
Elevates my heart.

58 Every Morning ...

Every morning
And every evening
I wash my Supreme Lord’s Feet
With my devotion-heart-tears,
And then I kiss His Feet
Self-givingly.

11 January 2004 ...

11 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

59 Determination ...

Determination
Of the utmost strength
Is needed
For God-realisation.

60 I Do Not Want To Be ...

I do not want to be
A book-knowledge-smart
Man.

61 I Want To Be ...

I want to be
A heart-wisdom-smart
Seeker.

62 We All Must Be ...

We all must be
Very, very careful.
Doubt can at any moment plunder
All our divine qualities.

63 Faith-Flower-Fragrance ...

Faith-flower-fragrance
Is exquisite.

64 I Must Make ...

I must make
My God-dedication-strides
Longer than the longest.

65 The Mind Gets Tired ...

The mind gets tired
Right after
A very short prayer.

66 The Heart Does Not Get Tired ...

The heart does not get tired
Even after
A very long meditation.

67 The Cyclone-Violences ...

The cyclone-violences
In various parts of the world
Can be stopped
By soulful prayers
And powerful meditations.

68 We Need A Failure-Ignoring ...

We need a failure-ignoring
And self-doubt-shunning aspiration
To arrive
At our destined Goal.

69 Today ...

Today
My God-worship-shrine-heart
Is exceedingly happy and excited,
For soon
My Lord will arrive.

70 My Lord Is Telling Me ...

My Lord is telling me
That all my God-dreams
Will definitely become
Reality-blossoms
At His choice Hour.

12 January 2004 ...

12 January 2004

Legian, Bali, Indonesia

71 A True Seeker ...

A true seeker
Is a Heaven-inspired
God-messenger
On earth.

72 God’s Invisible Blows ...

God’s invisible blows
Awaken
My life.

73 God’s Visible Blows ...

God’s visible blows
Expedite
My Godward journey.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 29, Agni Press, 2004
