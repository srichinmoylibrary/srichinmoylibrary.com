20 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My aspiration-heart-sky
Is the place
Of my rainbow-sunrise.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 December 1999, 6:30 a.m. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Before doing fifty-two one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (26 right, 26 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
