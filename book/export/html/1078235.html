588 My Heart's Family...

MY HEART'S FAMILY

My heart's best brother:
God-determination.

My heart's best sister:
God-dedication.

My heart's best Father:
God-liberation.

My heart's best Mother:
God-perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
