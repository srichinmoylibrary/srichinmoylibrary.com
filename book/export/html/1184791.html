463. He is pleased with everything

He was
Pleased with nothing;
Therefore
God was not pleased with him.

He is now
Pleased with everything;
Therefore
God has given him two things:
Satisfaction-throne,
Perfection-crown.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
