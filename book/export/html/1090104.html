21. The eternal Master

I do not know
Who I am.
I do not know
Why I am not another God.
But I do know
That I can really love and serve God
If I want to.
From now on
Let me love my God,
The eternal Beloved,
And
Serve my God,
The eternal Master.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, I need you, Agni Press, 1975
