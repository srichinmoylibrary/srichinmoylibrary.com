Asbe Tara Phire Amai Asbe Tara Phire Hese

Asbe tara phire amai
Asbe tara phire
Hese hese neche neche
Raibe amai ghire

Translation:

They will come back
Again and again.
Smiling and dancing,
They will come back
And move around me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
