475. You will be happy

Open the gates of a new life.
You will be happy.
Close the gates of your old life.
You will be happy.
Offer your service-light to God in man.
You will be happy.
Offer your satisfaction-promise to man in God.
You will be happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
