2 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Your Compassion-Eye
Spoke a Nectar-Word
To my aspiration-heart
And changed my life entirely.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1998, 7:08 p.m. Before lifting 160 lbs with each arm twice.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
