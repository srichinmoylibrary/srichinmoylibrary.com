1050 I Purify Myself

I purify my body
By chanting God's Name.
I purify my vital
By serving God.
I purify my mind
By emptying my mind for God.
I purify my heart
By meditating on God's Compassion-Love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 11, Agni Press, 1981
