Asha Tari Chalo

Asha tari chalo
Chetanar pal tule
Niye jabe more
Mahanirban kule

Translation:

O hope-boat, carry me along
With your consciousness-sail
To the great silence-bound shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 11b, , 1981
