49. His persistent life

Calm persistent vision
Is his soul-life.

Sweet persistent mission
Is his heart-life.

Quick persistent illusion
Is his mind-life.

Strong persistent frustration
Is his vital-life.

Slow persistent destruction
Is his body-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
