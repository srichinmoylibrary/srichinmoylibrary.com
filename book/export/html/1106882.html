Chidananda's mother

Dec. 12, 1975

Dear Guru:

Thank you very much for the lovely luncheon, corsage and gifts.

It was nice meeting David's friends and their mothers, and I enjoyed the afternoon very much.

Sincerely,

Elsie Burke
