Who still remains?

Death is dead!
  Who still remains?
   Time,
  Time survives.

Life is dead!
  Who still remains?
     Light,
  Light survives.

Love is dead!
  Who still remains?
     Aspiration,
  Aspiration survives.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The silence of death, Agni Press, 1973
