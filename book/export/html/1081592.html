33. Broken promises

I do not trust God
Because
He has not kept His Promise:
I am nowhere near Him.

God does not trust me
Because
I have not kept my promise:
I still love the company
Of ignorance-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
