4155 Your Soul Knows How To Play

Your soul knows how to play
With God's Smiles.
Your heart knows how to play
With man's cries.
Now you should try
To know how to play
With your life's perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 42, Agni Press, 1982
