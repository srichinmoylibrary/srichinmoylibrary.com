9371

He was such a fool!
He thought his insecurity
Was a tiny object
That he could easily crush.
But as soon as he tried to grab it,
He discovered that it was
Larger than the largest
And stronger than the strongest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 94, Agni Press, 1984
