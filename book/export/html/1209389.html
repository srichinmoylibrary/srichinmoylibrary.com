23518

Remember, just remember
Your own divinity-manifestations:
In how many countless ways
You have served the Supreme,
Loved the Supreme
And fulfilled the Supreme.
These are your true divine possessions.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 236, Agni Press, 1997
