326. Be pure, be sure

Your thoughts are base,
Therefore nobody endeavours to embrace
You and your thought-reality.

Be pure, be sure.
In you is awaiting
God-freedom,
God-perfection and
God-satisfaction-unity's multiplicity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
