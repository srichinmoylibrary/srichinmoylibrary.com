992. When you surrender

When you desire,
You do not get
What you expect.

When you aspire,
Your achievement
Far transcends
Your expectation.

When you surrender,
God gives you
What He Himself
   Eternally treasures:
        Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
