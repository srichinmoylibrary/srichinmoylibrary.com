19908

God is pitifully crying.
Do you know why?
Not because
You are not surrendering
To His Will,
But because
You are not allowing your heart
To claim Him as its very own.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 200, Agni Press, 1994
