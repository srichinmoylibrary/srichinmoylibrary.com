857. I shall offer

To the sun-power
I shall offer my newly acquired power:
Aspiration.

To the moon-beauty
I shall offer my long treasured beauty:
Heart.

To the God-light
I shall offer my ever-deepening light:
Surrender.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 18, Agni Press, 1974
