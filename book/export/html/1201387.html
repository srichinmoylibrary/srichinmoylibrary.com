15596

God's Presence
In my mind-cave,
In my heart-room,
In my life-street
I see, I clearly see!
Therefore, I feel that
My God-realisation-days
Are fast approaching.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 156, Agni Press, 1991
