30. Mind-night and heart-light

O mind of night,
I am tired of seeing
Your ugly face.

O heart of light,
Where are you?
Do you really live?
Or is it all my imagination?
O soul of perfection,
Do you really carry
The message of perfection-light?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
