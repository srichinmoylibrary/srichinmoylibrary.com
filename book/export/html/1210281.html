24401

When I place my life
In the Hand of God,
I am happy, unimaginably happy.
When I place my heart
At the Feet of God,
I am not only happy
But also perfect.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 245, Agni Press, 1998
