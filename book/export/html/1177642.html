4673 The Unparalleled Winner

Give yourself away
To your greedy mind:
Lo, you are totally lost.

Give yourself away
To your hungry heart:
Behold, you are the unparalleled winner.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 47, Agni Press, 1982
