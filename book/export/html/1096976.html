My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 40

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Pangkor Island, Malaysia in November 2005.

23 November 2005

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

1 God’s Heart-Doors ...

God’s Heart-Doors
Do not believe in
Even one single lock.

2 The Mind’s Brilliance ...

The mind’s brilliance
Dazzles.
The heart’s aspiration
Radiates.

3 To My Greatest Joy ...

To my greatest joy,
I am not a beginner
But an adept
In the world-service-game.

4 Only To The Sleepless Self-Givers ...

Only to the sleepless self-givers
God gives the golden opportunity
To shoulder His Responsibilities.

5 I Need Help ...

I need help,
And I get inner Help from God
And outer help from man.
Left to myself,
I shall not succeed
In any field of God-manifestation
Here on earth.

6 Every Morning ...

Every morning
And every evening
I pray to be a foot soldier
Of my Lord Supreme.

7 The Mind Envies ...

The mind envies
The success of the world.
The heart embraces
The progress of the world.

8 My Powerful Smiles ...

My powerful smiles
Please God.
My helpless tears
Please God equally.

9 Each God-Lover ...

Each God-lover
Is a sunrise-heart
And not a sunset-life.

24 November 2005 ...

24 November 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

10 I Am A God-Beggar ...

I am a God-beggar
At God’s Feet.

11 I Am Not A Man-Beggar ...

I am not a man-beggar
At man’s door.

12 Early Retirement-Enjoyment ...

Early retirement-enjoyment
Is the height of stupidity.

13 My Heart, Do Not Despair! ...

My heart, do not despair!
God’s Compassion-Concern-Heart
Is fast approaching you.

14 God Says To Me ...

God says to me,
“My child,
Your aspiration deserves
A round of applause.”

15 God Says To Me ...

God says to me,
“My child,
Your dedication deserves
A standing ovation.”

16 God Says To Me ...

God says to me,
“My child,
Your manifestation of My Light
Deserves man’s greatest admiration
And My proudest Blessings.”

17 My Love-Devotion-Surrender-Life ...

My love-devotion-surrender-life
With no gratitude
Is my God-failure-ineptitude.

18 The Giant Mind ...

The giant mind
Eventually topples.
The tiny heart
Eventually triumphs.

19 In The Spiritual Life ...

In the spiritual life,
God-faith is the armour
And God-surrender is the warrior.

25 November 2005 ...

25 November 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

20 God Says, “No!” ...

God says, “No!”
God’s Eye says, “Yes!”
God’s Heart says,
“Come in, My child, come in!”

21 This Morning ...

This morning
My heart’s love-affection-stadium
Has invited all God-seekers
And all God-lovers.

22 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I am
Because Your Heart
Affectionately and sleeplessly is.

23 Truth-Telling People ...

Truth-telling people
I love.
Truth-seeking people
I need.

24 Each Good Thought ...

Each good thought
Is
A new vision-pathfinder.

25 Life Is Not ...

Life is not
The supremacy-power of man.
Life is
The Compassion-Shower of God.

26 I Am True To Myself ...

I am true to myself.
Therefore I am exceedingly happy.
I am true to God.
Therefore I am a perfection-bud.
If I continue
To remain true to God,
Then there shall come a time
When I shall grow into
A perfect perfection-blossom.

26 November 2005 ...

26 November 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

27 My Morning Dawns ...

My morning dawns
With a hope-panorama.

28 Each Soul Starts ...

Each soul starts
From a different plane
Of consciousness,
But at the end of the journey,
All arrive at the same destination.

29 My Mind ...

My mind,
I command you —
Stop at once what you are doing
And follow God!

30 When We Do Not Try ...

When we do not try
Our very best,
We are compelled to have
A God-empty life-road.

31 God Picks ...

God picks
Our gratitude-flowers
From our ingratitude-thorns.

32 My Lord, Can I? ...

My Lord, can I?
“No!”
My Lord, shall I?
“No!”
My Lord, must I?
“Yes!
You must, from this very moment,
Embark
On the God-realisation-journey.”

33 At God’s Feet ...

At God’s Feet
The mind not only survives
But also thrives.

27 November 2005 ...

27 November 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

34 A True Devotion-Tree ...

A true devotion-tree
Is bound to produce
Nectar-fruits.

35 My Lord Supreme ...

My Lord Supreme,
I need from You
A receptivity-plant
In my heart-garden,
And not a receptivity-implant
Inside my body
From the world-admired doctors.

36 The Moment I Conquer ...

The moment I conquer
An ignorance-night-thought,
God comes to me immediately
And asks me to join Him
In His triumphant March.

37 My Reading, My Talking ...

My reading, my talking
And my lecturing
Help me very little
In comparison to
My praying, my meditating
And my self-giving.

38 God Will Definitely Come To You ...

God will definitely come to you
And bless you
If you try your utmost
To please Him
To your very last breath.

39 My Life-Long Mission ...

My life-long mission
Is not in missionising the world,
But in serving the world
So it can climb Heavenward.

40 God Tells Each And Every God-Seeker ...

God tells each and every God-seeker
To quickly take the nearest seat
To His Heart-Home.

28 November 2005 ...

28 November 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

41 Each Moment ...

Each moment
I get an opportunity-flower
From the inner world
To offer to my Lord Supreme
In the outer world.

42 World-Attachment ...

World-attachment
Is
Extremely painful.

43 God-Attachment ...

God-attachment
Is
Exceedingly blissful.

44 The Earth-Founder ...

The earth-founder
Is
My smiling soul.

45 The Heaven-Founder ...

The Heaven-founder
Is
My crying heart.

46 The Beyond-Founder ...

The Beyond-founder
Will be my God-aspiration,
God-dedication
And God-manifestation.

47 The Mind, The Vital ...

The mind, the vital
And the body
Enjoy the gossip-world.

48 The Soul, The Heart ...

The soul, the heart
And the breath
Enjoy the God-Whisper-world.

49 God Gives Purity ...

God gives purity
The most important part
In His Cosmic Play.

50 No Fear, No Doubt ...

No fear, no doubt,
No suspicion
Can ever dare to touch
My soul-smiles.

51 Those Who Know Well ...

Those who know well
The art of forgiveness
Are God’s chosen children.

52 I Call It My Surrender-Life ...

I call it my surrender-life.
God calls that very thing
His peerless treasure.

53 God Gets Tremendous Delight ...

God gets tremendous delight
Whenever I do anything
With a sincere heart.

54 If You Are A Sincere Seeker ...

If you are a sincere seeker,
Then your ego
Is now totally obsolete.

55 If You Want To Open ...

If you want to open
God’s Heart-Door,
Surrender
Is the only key.

56 Time ...

Time
Does not believe in
A slow pace.

57 My Heart And My Soul ...

My heart and my soul
At long last
Held a farewell party
For my mind.

58 A Real Seeker ...

A real seeker
Has to treasure time
As if it were all diamond.

59 My Lord Wants Me To Retrace ...

My Lord wants me to retrace
The ancient heart-path
And not to follow
The modern mind-path.

60 My Faith In God ...

My faith in God
Is my only reliable
Life-companion.

61 My Heart Is Desperate ...

My heart is desperate
Only for one thing:
My Lord’s Compassion-flooded Feet.

29 November 2005 ...

29 November 2005

Pangkor Island, Malaysia

62 My Heart-Tears ...

My heart-tears
Weaken
My Lord Beloved Supreme.

63 My Soul-Smiles ...

My soul-smiles
Strengthen
My Lord Beloved Supreme.

64 I Always Keep Wide Open ...

I always keep wide open
My life-house
And my heart-home.

65 The Seeker May Lose ...

The seeker may lose
To God’s Eye
But never
To God’s Heart.

66 My Mind Has Many Stories ...

My mind has many stories
To tell God,
But God has only one Song
To sing for my mind:
Forgiveness.

67 Alas ...

Alas,
God’s Shoulders accept
All the problems of humanity,
But I do not want to fulfil
My little life’s little responsibility.

68 Mother Earth Blesses Me ...

Mother Earth blesses me
With hopes.
Father Heaven blesses me
With promises.
I must fulfil my Parents’
Hopes and promises.

69 In My Aspiration-Heart-Home ...

In my aspiration-heart-home,
There is no entry and no exit
For thoughts.

70 Poor Mind! ...

Poor mind!
It has no choice.
It is not allowed to hear
My soul’s voice.

71 This Morning ...

This morning —
I do not know how, why
And wherefrom —
A new joy is emboldening
My heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 40, Agni Press, 2005
