675 You Are Ready...

YOU ARE READY

You are ready
For enormity.
Therefore
God has given you
A big mind.

You are ready
For immensity.
Therefore
God has given you
A big heart.

You are ready
For divinity.
Therefore
God has given you
An aspiring life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
