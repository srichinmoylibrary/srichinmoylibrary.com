Hai Hai Jader Kari Upakar

Hai hai hai hai jader kari upakar
Barai nithur tader jani byabahar

Translation:

Alas, alas,
All those who are helped by me
Are now hurling their misbehaviour at me.
Their misbehaviour is very cruel.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bahir jagate, part 2, , 2002
