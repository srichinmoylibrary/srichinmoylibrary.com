3511 To Know More

To know more,
You have to love more.
To love more,
You have to discover more.
To discover more,
You have to not only surrender more,
But also surrender constantly
To the Will of God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 36, Agni Press, 1982
