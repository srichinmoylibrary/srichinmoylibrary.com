685. Why they love God

My mind loves God
Because
God can.
He can illumine the mind.

My heart loves God
Because
God has.
He has all love for the heart.

My soul loves God
Because
God is.
God is all.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
