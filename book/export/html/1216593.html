954. Who dares to defeat you?

Your life is selfless;
   Therefore
Earth dreams of you,
Heaven dreams for you,
God works through you.
Who dares to defeat your
   Death? Never!
Who dares to challenge you?
   Satan ? Never!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
