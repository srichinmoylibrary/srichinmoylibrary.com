Joseph Haydn

In any field of life, he who offers compassion and encouragement is indeed a great mind and a good heart.

Speaking to a child violinist, Haydn states: "No need to be scared of me, my boy. I'm a bad player myself."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Music: Ecstasy's heart-hunger, Agni Press, 1994
