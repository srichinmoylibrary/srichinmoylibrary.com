1031

O my body and vital,
Be not afraid of my mind's greatness.
O my mind,
Be not afraid of my heart's goodness.
O my heart,
Be not afraid of my soul's oneness.
O my soul,
You are never afraid of my Lord's Fulness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 11, Agni Press, 1983
