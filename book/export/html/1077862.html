215. I need a perfection-sky

Father, the more You ignore me
The more I need You.
"Why, daughter, why?"
Because I need
A Satisfaction-smile from You.

Father, the more You scold me
The more I love You.
"Why, daughter, why?"
Because I need
A Perfection-sky from You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
