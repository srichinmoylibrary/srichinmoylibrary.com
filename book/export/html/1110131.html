67.

The seer in President Gorbachev
Graciously illumines us:
"We are all passengers aboard one ship,
The Earth,
And we must not allow it to be wrecked."
Sail we must together in a oneness-heart
And in a fulness-life
Towards the Golden Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: the home of Oneness-Peace-Dream-World, Agni Press, 2001
