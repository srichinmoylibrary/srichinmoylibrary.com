669 A Divine Life...

A DIVINE LIFE

Divine thoughts he thinks.
Therefore
Nectar-light he drinks.

A divine life he leads.
Therefore
God-necessity on earth,
God-necessity in Heaven,
He tirelessly feeds.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
