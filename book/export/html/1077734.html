87. Ancient and modern splendours

Three are the splendours
Of the ancient world:
Faith-moon,
Concern-sky,
Sacrifice-sun.

Three are the splendours
Of the modern world:
Indifference-house,
Doubt-walls,
Suspicion-gate.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
