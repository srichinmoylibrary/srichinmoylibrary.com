907. What I need

   On earth
What I need is peace.   In Heaven
What I need is progress.   For humanity
What I need is love.   For divinity
What I need is service.   From the Supreme
What I need is a sun-vast Smile.   From myself
What I need is willingness,
And its bosom friend,
   Readiness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
