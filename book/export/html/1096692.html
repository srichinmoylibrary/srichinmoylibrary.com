My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 37

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Qingdao and Xian, China in December 2004 and January 2005.

29 December 2004

Qingdao, China

1 The Desire-Experiences ...

The desire-experiences
Of my life
Come and go.

2 The Aspiration-Experiences ...

The aspiration-experiences
Of my life
Always remain.

3 My Heart Is Well Acquainted ...

My heart is well acquainted
With my Lord’s
Satisfaction-Heart.

4 I Adore ...

I adore
My Lord’s
Beautiful Eye.

5 I Worship ...

I worship
My Lord’s
Merciful Feet.

6 If You Want To Explain ...

If you want to explain
God’s Will,
You are bound to fail.

7 God’s Heart And My Heart ...

God’s Heart and my heart
Always live
A very, very, very simple life.

8 My God-Surrender-Life ...

My God-surrender-life
Is not
My mental hallucination —
It is Reality itself.

9 Alas ...

Alas,
There are so many things
In life
That remain
Insoluble mysteries.

10 My Purity-Heart ...

My purity-heart
Is exactly the same as it was
Half a century ago.

11 Sweet ...

Sweet
Is my Lord’s Eye.

12 Sweeter ...

Sweeter
Is my Lord’s Heart.

13 Sweetest ...

Sweetest
Are my Lord’s Feet.

14 To Say That I Will Try ...

To say that I will try
Is nothing short of a joke.

15 To Say That I Must ...

To say that I must
Is, indeed, the brave utterance
Of a real seeker.

16 It Seems That ...

It seems that
To have an enduring bliss
On earth
Is almost an impossible task.

17 Peace ...

Peace
Is founded upon
Forgiveness.

18 When My Heart ...

When my heart
Starts blooming,
My Absolute Lord Supreme
Starts dancing.

19 When We Harmonise Anything ...

When we harmonise anything,
We divinise it
At the same time.

20 What Have I Been Doing? ...

What have I been doing?
I have been taking shelter
At my Lord’s Nectar-Feet.

21 Pleasure-Comfort ...

Pleasure-comfort
Is fleeting.

22 Sacrifice-Joy ...

Sacrifice-joy
Is everlasting.

23 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our sleep-body.

24 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our aggression-vital.

25 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our suspicion-mind.

26 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our insecurity-heart.

27 Nothing To Hide ...

Nothing to hide,
Save and except
Our pride-life.

28 The Mind Is Extremely Afraid ...

The mind is extremely afraid
Of raising itself
Upward.

29 Lengthen And Strengthen ...

Lengthen and strengthen
Your heart-wings
If you want to fly
All over the world.

30 Satisfaction Lies ...

Satisfaction lies
Only between my heart’s cries
And my Lord’s Smiles.

1 January 2005 ...

1 January 2005

Qingdao, China

31 The Fragrance Of My Heart ...

The fragrance of my heart
Is
My morning God-satisfaction.

32 No More Do I Have ...

No more do I have
The mind-knowledge-thirst.
I am all my heart-hunger
For the golden dust
Of my Lord’s Feet.

33 We Can Open Up Earth’s Heart ...

We can open up earth’s heart
And Heaven’s Heart
All at once
If we can breathe in God’s Victory
At every moment.

34 Fear Hides ...

Fear hides,
Only to arrest and capture us
At its choice time.

35 May My Heart Long ...

May my heart long
For one brief glimpse
Of my Lord Supreme.

36 Never Forget ...

Never forget
Your patience-exercise.
All other exercises
You can forget,
But not your patience-exercise.

37 Justice-Light ...

Justice-Light
Is God’s
Absolutely last Choice.

38 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That He is eager
To be the slave
Of my surrender-joy.

39 To Be A Genuine ...

To be a genuine
God-seeker,
No price is too great.

40 A Path-Finder ...

A path-finder
Is, indeed,
A God-binder.

3 January 2005 ...

3 January 2005

Qingdao, China

41 God’s Blissful Eye ...

God’s blissful Eye
God unreservedly shares
With the world.

42 Only Sincere Heart-Seekers ...

Only sincere heart-seekers
Can see
God’s golden Throne.

43 A Limitation-Mind ...

A limitation-mind
Is
A frustration-life.

44 My Inner Life ...

My inner life
Is
An indomitable power.

45 I Never Cared ...

I never cared
And I will never care
For countless sounds.

46 I Care Only ...

I care only
For boundless silence.

47 Only Silence-Joy ...

Only silence-joy
Can show us the way
To God-realisation.

48 When I Pray To God ...

When I pray to God
To come down,
He tells me,
“Come up, come up,
My child!
This place
Is infinitely safer.”

49 Every Day ...

Every day
God wants me
To spring a new surprise
On Him.

50 The Ingratitude ...

The ingratitude
Of the mind
Is ruthlessly shocking.

6 January 2005 ...

6 January 2005

Qingdao, China

51 Sleeplessly I Keep ...

Sleeplessly I keep
My heart-room open
For my Lord Supreme.

52 I Search ...

I search
For God-closeness
Everywhere.

53 I Search ...

I search
For God-oneness
Deep within.

54 Heaven Showers Blessings ...

Heaven showers Blessings
Upon me
Every morning.

55 Every Morning ...

Every morning
I treasure my Lord’s Feet
More than I treasure
My Lord’s Eye.

56 When I Am All Aspiration ...

When I am all aspiration,
My Lord is all action
In and through me.

57 The Mind Wants Me ...

The mind wants me
To be a member
Of its world-division-club.

58 The Heart Wants Me ...

The heart wants me
To be a member
Of its world-oneness-assembly.

59 O Sorrow ...

O sorrow,
Do not shadow
My heart!

60 O Joy ...

O joy,
Do make me
Your cherished toy!

8 January 2005 ...

8 January 2005

Qingdao, China

61 Each Human Being ...

Each human being
Is either God’s
Recognised representative
Or God’s
Unrecognised representative.

62 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That I must give myself away —
Not to everybody,
But to the chosen few.

63 The Moment Divinity ...

The moment Divinity
Touches the earth,
Humanity either remains
Fast asleep
Or wakes up slowly,
Very slowly.

64 Be Careful ...

Be careful
Of the doubting mind!
It can spread like wildfire
Everywhere.

65 When I Do Not Pray To God ...

When I do not pray to God
For devotion,
I feel myself to be
A poor street-beggar.

66 I Must Climb Up ...

I must climb up
The aspiration-mountain-summit
During my lifetime.

67 Anything That Is Simple ...

Anything that is simple
Embodies the natural Beauty
Of God.

68 The Supremacy Of The Mind ...

The supremacy of the mind
Will without fail
One day collapse.

69 May My Inner Courage ...

May my inner courage
Never desert me.

70 I Do Not Want My Faith ...

I do not want my faith
To be fruitful.
I want my faith
To be Godful.

9 January 2005 ...

9 January 2005

Qingdao, China

71 We Cannot Really ...

We cannot really
Surprise God,
But He pretends
To be surprised
Even at our minor success.

72 I Long Only To Have ...

I long only to have
God’s Compassion-Eye
And God’s Forgiveness-Heart
In my heart-home.

73 The Mind Shocks God ...

The mind shocks God
By not accepting immediately
God’s Light.

74 I Have Closed The Long Distance ...

I have closed the long distance
Between me and my Lord
With my sleepless and breathless
Surrender.

75 I Must Always Keep ...

I must always keep
My aspiration-heart-standard
As high as possible.

76 There Is No Other Way ...

There is no other way
To develop my closeness to God
Than with my prayerful
And tearful eyes.

77 When I Touch ...

When I touch
God’s Forgiveness-Feet,
God immediately opens up
His Compassion-Eye.

78 The Modern Life ...

The modern life
Is blindly racing
Towards a goalless goal.

79 When I Pray To God ...

When I pray to God For His Victory, He immediately blesses me With two inspiration-wings And one aspiration-heart.

80 When The Heart, The Mind And The Vital ...

When the heart, the mind and the vital
Become real friends,
The soul dances ecstasy’s dance.

14 January 2005 ...

14 January 2005

Xian, China

81 Self-Effacement ...

Self-effacement
Is the first step
In our self-exploration.

82 Alas ...

Alas,
My heart and my mind
Have extreme
Opinion-differences.

83 I See The Authenticity ...

I see the authenticity
Of light
Even in the darkest night.

84 Not “Go Alone” ...

Not “Go alone”
But “Go together with all”
Should be our last
And lasting experience.

85 I Love My Heart’s Service ...

I love my heart’s service
And not
My life’s servitude.

86 Infinity, Eternity And Immortality ...

Infinity, Eternity and Immortality
Live together most amicably.
They are divinely and supremely
Bound to one another.

87 Difficulties Arise ...

Difficulties arise,
But we must make them feel
That we are destined
To reach a destination
Where they will not be able
To accompany us.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 37, Agni Press, 2005
