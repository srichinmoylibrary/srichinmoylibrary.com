3.

Sunday was a delightful experience. Not only was it so nice to be invited by my daughter to a luncheon like this, but it was a meaningful experience to see that she is surrounded by such warm, loving friends.

Mrs. B. H.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Fourteen American mothers and fourteen American daughters with Sri Chinmoy, Agni Press, 1975
