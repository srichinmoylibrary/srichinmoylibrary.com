Jare Ami Bhabi Apan Se Jai Dure Chale

Jare ami bhabi apan se jai dure chale
Tare ami rakhte chahi tomar charan tale

Translation:

My Lord, whomever I take as my own goes away.
But I want only one thing:
I wish to place him at Your Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Swadeshe bideshe ghuriya berai, , 2007
