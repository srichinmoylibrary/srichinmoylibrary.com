177. My earth-friends and my Heaven-friends

My earth-friends warn me
Not to mix with Heaven-lovers.
They say: Heaven-lovers are all rogues.
I do not know what to do.
My Heaven-friends warn me
Not to mix with Heaven-doubters.
They say: Heaven-doubters
Are all impossible fools.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
