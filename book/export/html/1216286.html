653. Your turn

Willing or unwilling,
You must go to God.

Willing or unwilling,
You have to realise God.

Willing or unwilling,
God will make you
Another God
Before long.
Ah, He has already resigned.
Now yours is the undisputed turn.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
