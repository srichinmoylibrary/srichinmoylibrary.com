May 1986

Some seekers have failed.
That does not mean you also will fail.
No!
You can and you must succeed
In the spiritual battlefield
Of your divine life.
If you are simple,
If you are sincere,
If you are humble,
If you are pure,
If you are devoted,
If you are disciplined,
If you are determined
And if you are surrendered
To your Lord Beloved Supreme,
Then you are bound to succeed.
The Absolute Supreme needs your heart
And wants your life
To manifest His transcendental Vision
Here on earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Gratitude-Flower-Hearts, Agni Press, 1993
