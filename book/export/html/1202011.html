16213

My mind tells my heart:
"Look what I can do
For you."
My heart tells my mind:
"Look what I have given you
And also
What I am going to give you
In a twinkling."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 163, Agni Press, 1991
