Delayed Arrival

Before my brother received the copy of the programme for the German concert that we sent him, a lady in the Sri Aurobindo Ashram gave him a copy. Had she attended the concert, or did somebody send her a programme from Germany? Who knows — but this is how my brother got it!

— 7 April 1984

From: Sri Chinmoy, The World-Experience-Tree-Climber, Part 3, Agni Press, 1993
