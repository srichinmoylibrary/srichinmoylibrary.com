131. Each day

Each day
I dream my God
Anew
To increase my love-flames.
Lo, I am successful.

Each day
I face the world-ignorance
Anew
To increase my light-power.
Lo, I am more than successful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
