35. Learn to conquer

Learn to conquer.
Conquer what?
Conquer the animal in you.

Learn to fight.
Against what?
Against the human in you.

Learn to surrender.
To what?
To the divine in you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
