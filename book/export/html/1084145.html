17. Like it or not

Whether you like it
        Or not,
God will tell you many things.

Whether he likes it
        Or not,
God has no time to talk to him at all.

Whether I like it
        Or not,
God wants to embarrass me
   By placing me on
        His Transcendental Throne.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 4, Agni Press, 1974
