177. Life's roads

When I walk along life's
Inspiration-road,
I feel I have to reach God.

When I walk along life's
Aspiration-road,
I feel I shall, without fail, reach God.

When I walk along life's
Realisation-road,
I see, I feel, I know
God has already reached me,
Soulfully,
Devotedly
And
Unconditionally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
