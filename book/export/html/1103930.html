1 November 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I am triumphantly enjoying
The ashes of my ego's unmistakable
And permanent death.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1999, 9:31 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
