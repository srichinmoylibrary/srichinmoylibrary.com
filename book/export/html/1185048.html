720. The kind of person I am

If you do not know the kind of person I am,
Then I wish to tell you.
I am a Truth-seeker,
A God-lover,
A God-revealer
And a God-fulfiller
On earth;
A God-dreamer
And a God-bestower
In Heaven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
