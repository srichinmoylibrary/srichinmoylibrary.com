43. Transformation

Transformation
Of the physical consciousness
Is by far the most difficult task
For us.
Once the physical consciousness
Is transformed,
There is nothing,
Either on earth
Or in Heaven,
That one has to acquire.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God-Compassion and God-Justice, Agni Press, 1975
