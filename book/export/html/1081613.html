54. Endless lessons

My earth-life has taught me
Endless imitation.

My Heaven-life has taught me
Endless perfection.

My thought-life has taught me
Endless frustration.

My will-life has taught me
Endless emancipation.

My body-life has taught me
Endless limitation.

My soul-life has taught me
Endless progression.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
