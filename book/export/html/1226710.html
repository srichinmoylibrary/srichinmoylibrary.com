Jiban Samare Bhiti Das Haye

Jiban samare bhiti das haye
   Jara kare palayan
Kapurush bhiru pabe ki go tara
   Taba pada darashan

Translation:

In the battlefield of life,
Those who have become slaves
To fear, cowardice and helplessness
Will never get even a glimpse
Of Your Feet, O my Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
