991 The Thick Forest Of Solitude

With his tears of repentance,
His mind has entered
Into the thick forest of solitude.
Before long he and his life
Will swim in the sea
Of satisfaction-ecstasy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 10, Agni Press, 1981
