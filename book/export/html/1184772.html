444. He who trusts his heart

He who trusts his mind is a fool.
Indeed, he has to pay the penalty for his stupidity.
He who trusts his heart
Is not only a wise man
But the wise man,
For his heart always,
Like a magnet,
Pulls Wisdom-Light
From the Delight-Source of the soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
