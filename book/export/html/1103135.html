6 October 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May my past life
Not remain in me as a thorn.
May my present life
Be the beauty of my heart.
May my future life
Be the fragrance of my soul.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1998, 7:10 a.m. Before lifting 110 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
