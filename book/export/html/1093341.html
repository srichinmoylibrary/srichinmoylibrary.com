23. Beginning to end

Let me begin my day with
     God's Compassion.
I shall end my day with
     My concern for man's
           Insecurity-night.

Let me begin my night with
     God's Love.
I shall end my night with
     My service to man's
           Necessity- height.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Canadian fruits, Bhakti Press, 1974
