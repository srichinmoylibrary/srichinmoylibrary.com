Mane Andhiyar Prane Andhiyar

Mane andhiyar prane andhiyar
Mor dehe andhiyar
Jani ami tai tomare pujite
Nahi mor adhikar

Translation:

My mind is full of darkness,
My vital is full of darkness,
My body is full of darkness, I know.
Therefore, I have no right to worship You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giti mala, , 2000
