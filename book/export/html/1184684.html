356. Impurity has robbed your life

Impurity has robbed your life.
Go back to your aspiration-source
Slowly and steadily.

Think of God, dream of God.
He will catch your impurity-thief
Quickly and readily.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
