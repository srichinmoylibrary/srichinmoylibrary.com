20155

O temptation-face,
You have brought tremendous disgrace
To my climbing aspiration-heart.
You are forcing me
To run backwards
Fast, very fast,
To my total destruction-shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 202, Agni Press, 1994
