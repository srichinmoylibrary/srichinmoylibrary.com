275.

If you are not thrilled
By God's Presence,
How can you dare to think
You will run the fastest
In the God-realisation-race?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, part 3, Agni Press, 1996
