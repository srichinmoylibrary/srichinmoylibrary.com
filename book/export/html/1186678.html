1032

When a true God-lover
Cannot decide what to do
In his outer life,
God, out of His infinite Bounty,
Takes it as His bounden Duty
To help that person choose
The right thing to do.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 11, Agni Press, 1983
