Jiban Amar Sanga Habe

Jiban amar sanga habe
      Maran nadir tire
Chaoa paoar bhari bojha
      Rakhbona ar shire

Translation:

My life shall end
At the rim of death-river.
I shall not carry any more
The heavy load of my desire-night
And fulfilment-day.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bela chale jai, , 1979
