23. False and true

False!
What is false?
A power-hungry life.
False!
Who is false?
A love-demanding man.
True!
What is true?
A self-giving life.
True!
Who is true?
A God-becoming man.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
