Aji Hate Thakbo Kebal Nirabatar Buke

Aji hate thakbo kebal nirabatar buke
Agyanatar mukharata thakbena mor mukhe

Translation:

[Translation for the first line]
From today on, I shall dwell in the heart of silence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Jatra amar shesh habe aj, , 2006
