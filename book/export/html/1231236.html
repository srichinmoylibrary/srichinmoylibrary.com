Tumi Kanda Bhul Kareo

Tumi kando bhul kareo
  Jakhan nahi dhari
Tumi haso ami jakhan
  Tomai bichar kari
Tumi nacho jakhan tomai
  Barek ami smari

Translation:

You cry even when
I do not catch You by mistake.
You smile when
I try to judge You.
You dance if even once
I invoke You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, teach me how to cry, , 1975
