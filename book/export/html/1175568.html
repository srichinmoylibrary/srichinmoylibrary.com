2619 Two Needed Friends

Purity, my needed friend,
Where have you been for so long?
Humility, my needed friend,
Where have you been for so long?
Now that you two have come back to me,
Please do not desert me.
I shall in every way
Feed your inner hunger.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
