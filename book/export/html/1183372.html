236. Not alone

I play not alone.
When I play
The sun and the moon join me.

I sing not alone.
When I sing
Light and Delight join me.

I dance not alone.
When I dance
The sky and the sea join me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
