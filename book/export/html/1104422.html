20 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Whenever I do anything unconditionally
For You,
I take You by surprise.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 September 2000, 6:35 p.m. Before doing 710-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
