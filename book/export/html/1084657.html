19. Eternal Time

Nature's total transformation:
Let me leave it
For the moment.
After all, am I not living
In Eternal Time?
God's perfect manifestation:
Let me leave it
For the moment.
After all, why worry,
Since I am living
In God's own Eternal Time?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 14, Agni Press, 1974
