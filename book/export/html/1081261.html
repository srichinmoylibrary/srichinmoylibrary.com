330. Arise to victory

Arise to victory unknown.
Your life will dine
With a lofty height.
Devour the surrender-fruits
Unknowable.
God will come to you Himself
With His infinite Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, From the Source to the Source, Agni Press, 1974
