58.

God's GREATNESS,
At times the divine Avatars
Play their roles in a human way
To lift up humanity and thus
Please You only, in Your own Way.
God's Goodness,
The Avatars cry for You,
Cry with You and cry in You;
Smile for You, smile with You
And smile in You,
Only to please You in Your own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Greatness and God's Goodness, Agni Press, 1998
