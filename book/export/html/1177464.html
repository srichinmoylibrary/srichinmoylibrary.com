4497 The Creation Of Light

The creation of Light
Knows what it has:
A cry for Divinity's unknown Love.

The creation of Delight
Knows what it has:
A cry for Immortality's
Unknowable Oneness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 45, Agni Press, 1982
