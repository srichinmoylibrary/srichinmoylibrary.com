Tumi Amar Banshi

Tumi amar banshi
Hiyar kanna hasi
Ami tomar banshi
Kripa rashi rashi
Tumi amar bhor
Ami tomar dor

Translation:

You are my flute.
You are my heart’s joy and sorrow.
I am Your flute
Which invokes Your endless
Compassion-sea.
You are my Eternity’s dawn
And I am Your door
For humanity to enter into
Your Palace supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, make my life a gratitude-flood, , 1976
