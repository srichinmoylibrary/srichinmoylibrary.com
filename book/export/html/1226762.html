Maner Byatha Praner Byatha

Maner byatha praner byatha
Parbona ar saite
Bishwa pitar bijoy dhwaja
Parbo ki hai baite

Translation:

I shall no longer be able to bear
The sufferings and pangs
Of my mind and of my heart.
Alas, will I ever be able to carry
The Victory-Banner of the World Father?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
