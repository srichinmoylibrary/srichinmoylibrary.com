41.

My Lord Supreme,
I have only two friends:
Your Compassion-Eye
And Your Forgiveness-Heart.
"My child,
I also have only two friends:
Your morning's smiling face
And your evening's singing heart."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord, I pray to You, Agni Press, 1994
