Taruner Asha Purna Haibe

Taruner asha purna haibe
E katha janabo

Translation:

I shall let the entire world know
That the green hope of the green generation
Will, without fail, be fulfilled.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 10a, , 1981
