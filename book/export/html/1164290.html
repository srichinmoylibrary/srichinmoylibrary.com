Silence speaks, part 6

1.

No more failure,
No more remorse,
Because I enjoy
The self-study course.

2.

God is not a foreigner;
God is not a stranger.
God is your own Love,
Your heart's Oneness-Player.

3.

The doubt-doctrine
Is for the mind to believe.
The faith-diploma
Is for the heart to receive.

4.

For a world of peace,
O seeker, pave the way.
Practise oneness-life
Every night and every day.

5.

Be pure, be sure
At the moment of choice.
God the Supreme
Will be your fulness-voice.

6.

Practise your inner life's
Discipline-drill.
Soon you will discover
Ecstasy's thrill.

7.

My seeker-friend,
The desire-train
Carries only
The bondage-chain.

8.

To live in your heart's
Peace-flooded nest,
You must first win
God's Satisfaction-Contest.

9.

If you remain
On the ego-hill,
Nowhere for you
God-oneness-thrill.

10.

O seeker, for perfection-light
Always take a bold stand.
Behold! Within, without
God's Liberation-Hand.

11.

To own the crown and throne
Of immortality,
Become the oneness-cry and smile
Of silence-purity.

12.

The world without is shamelessly proud
Of its destruction-knife.
The world within is divinely proud
Of its compassion-life.

13.

What I call
My aspiration-flame,
God calls
His Satisfaction-Game.

14.

Be not afraid
Of death's stark lance.
Dance your life's
God-oneness-dance.

15.

Live not, live not
In the lap of death.
Fear not, fear not
God's Perfection-Breath.

16.

To illumine
My ancient mind,
My soul-bird
I shall quickly find.

17.

I am learning the language
Of Eternity's silence-time.
I am forgetting the experience
Of truth-abandoned clime.

18.

Never expect
From this world of ours, never!
You will find it is
Incredibly clever!

19.

An aspiration-prize
From God you mill receive
If your doubting mind at last
Can dare to believe.

20.

Be not your mind's
Giant ego-mountain.
Become your heart's
World-love-fountain.

21.

Where are you,
My aspiration-cannon?
Do destroy
My desire-demon!

22.

Do not lose
Your ingratitude-life.
It is your
Self-destruction-knife.

23.

Purity is beauty's
Natural face.
Perfection is divinity's
Natural grace.

24.

What you need is
Aspiration-skill
Before God grants you
Ecstasy's thrill.

25.

I know, my Lord's
Compassion-Presence
Is my earth-life's
Divinity-essence.

26.

The outer man
With his orphan-life cries.
The inner man
Speedily Godward flies.

27.

I try, I try,
I always try
To love and please
My inner cry.

28.

The perfume
Of your aspiration
Awakens
My determination.

29.

God is sought;
Therefore, He is found.
God is loved;
Therefore, He is bound.

30.

God wants to increase
Our value immensely.
Therefore, He tells us
To love Him intensely.

31.

Deep I dive, and see!
My Lord supplies my needs.
High I fly, and see!
My bondage-mind He frees.

32.

Negative thoughts
My heart-prayers withstand.
Positive thoughts
Take me to God's Peace-Land.

33.

You may not know,
But it is absolutely true —
You are a member
Of God's manifestation-crew.

34.

I was engulfed
In ignorance-night.
And who appeared?
My Lord of Light!

35.

Wear your life's strong
Obedience-chain
To cure your severe
Disobedience-pain.

36.

Will God not care for me
If I love Him deeply?
Will God not love me
If I cry for Him sleeplessly?

37.

To reverse your life's
Powerful death-trend,
Become your soul's
Constant servitor-friend.

38.

My heart shall cast aside
My mind's sharp ignorance-knife
To invoke my Lord Supreme
At once to light my life.

39.

Your body is buried
In lethargy-graveyard,
Yet you want to be
The world-awakening bard.

40.

Faithfully and sleeplessly
A Christlike life I live.
Openly and lovingly
My oneness-heart I give.

41.

Nobody can laugh at me,
Nobody can frown,
If I keep wearing
My wisdom-crown.

42.

If by you
Ignorance-life is sought,
By your Lord's Justice
You will be caught.

43.

This world of ours
Is full of eyeless, heartless fools.
Do you know why?
They study not in inner schools.

44.

My Lord shall light
My heart-pathway
If I give up playing
In ignorance-clay.

45.

Come out of your mind-room!
Prepare yourself to greet
Your Lord Supreme today.
He is on your heart-street.

46.

Carry not your mind's
Past-misery-briefcase
If you desire to see
God's golden, trance-bound Face.

47.

Each new attempt
Is the beginning of perfection.
Each new surrender
Is the smile of satisfaction.

48.

The simplicity-beauty
Of hope-heart
Can easily challenge
Ignorance-dart.

49.

Who can alone
To God ascend?
He whose vision-flames
Never descend.

50.

How can you lose
If you go on trying?
Who can challenge you
If your oneness is flying?

51.

If you can think of planting,
And not of the harvest,
God will grant you His sacred
And secret Heart-Nest.

52.

Behold, your Lord Supreme
Is fondly whispering,
"Wake up, stand up, jump —
Smiling and loving!"

53.

No speed limits
A self-giving God-lover knows,
For his life-tree
In God's Satisfaction-Heart grows.

54.

Obedience-light
Is the path to God's Heart-Breath.
Disobedience-night
Is the path to imminent death.

55.

The mind human creates.
The heart divine transforms.
The truth-seeker sees.
The God-lover performs.

56.

Because your mind-city
Is fortified with purity,
You are now collecting
The harvest of divinity.

57.

Something is fast approaching.
Perhaps it is my God-Hour.
Something is fast declining.
Perhaps it is my pride-tower.

58.

A silence-mind
Is the strongest determination.
A oneness-heart
Is the highest aspiration.

59.

O seeker-friend,
Learn the science of sacrifice.
God will grant you
His Heart's Fondness-Paradise.

60.

O seeker, your humility
Will quench your soulful thirst.
Your dedication will make you
Unmistakably first.

61.

When I am in the mind,
God sends me His Question long.
When I am in the heart,
He gives me His Answer-Song.

62.

Confidence-gift
Is always powerful.
Surrender-gift
Is always fruitful.

63.

My Lord gave me
Sincerity-strength.
I am offering Him
My progress-length.

64.

To believe without seeing
Is to hear the Message of the Unknown.
To see and not believe
Is the Truth to foolishly disown.

65.

How can you, O seeker-heart,
Speak of lasting joy
If your life has not become
God's true surrender-toy?

66.

Your soul's melody
You will easily hear
When from your mind
You bravely stamp out fear.

67.

If you are ready to obey,
God will give you more.
If you are ready to surrender,
God will show you the Shore.

68.

Because you are a truth-seeker,
Your mind will ever expand.
Because you are a God-lover,
You can touch God's Fulness-Hand.

69.

Although you look
Like a soulful man,
A stranger you are
To God's Vision-Plan.

70.

Because you are
A material mind,
An ethereal heart
How can you find?

71.

Today is meant
For your life-preparation.
Tomorrow is meant
For your God-satisfaction.

72.

My hope is the oasis
Where my heart flies.
My doubt is the desert
Where my heart dies.

73.

Never allow
Dark depression-knife
To butcher
Your aspiration-life.

74.

Foolishly feed not
Your mind's temptation-snake.
Wisely you must swim
In your heart's aspiration-lake.

75.

Yesterday, alas,
I was an empty-handed truth-seeker.
Today, I am worse:
I am an empty-hearted God-lover.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Silence speaks, part 6, Agni Press, 1994
