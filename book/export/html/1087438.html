I go to God twice a day

I go to God twice a day.
In the morning
I go to my Beloved Supreme
With the surrender
of a tennis ball.
He tells me I am on my way
to perfection.
In the evening
I go to my Beloved Supreme
With a basket of
gratitude-flowers.
He tells me I am all perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I play tennis every day, Agni Press, 1994
