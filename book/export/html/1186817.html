1170

Yesterday
My Master's silver advice
To me was:
Love all in God.

Today
My Master's golden advice
To me is:
Serve all for God.

Tomorrow
My Master's diamond advice
To me will be:
Become one with all in God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 12, Agni Press, 1983
