152.

Not only the light of your future,
But also the light of your present
Will shine all-where
If you have a God-surrendered heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, part 2, Agni Press, 1996
