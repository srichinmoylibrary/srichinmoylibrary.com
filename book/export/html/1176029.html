3076 Sail On Your Aspiration-Voyage

Sail on your imagination-voyage.
Your discoveries will be beautiful.
Sail on your aspiration-voyage.
Your discoveries will be beautiful
Plus fruitful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 31, Agni Press, 1982
