15934

God does not mind
Even if you have serious flaws.
But He does mind
If you do not have faith
That He can transform your flaws
Into His Perfection-Smiles.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 160, Agni Press, 1991
