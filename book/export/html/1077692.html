45. We are really great

Yours is the soul
Of mysterious bliss;
Therefore
You are really great.

His is the heart
Of generous peace;
Therefore
He is really great.

Mine is the life
Of prosperous surrender;
Therefore
I am really great.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
