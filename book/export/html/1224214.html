Bhalobasa Bhalobasa

Bhalobasa bhalobasa
Ananda alo bhasha
Nijere bilaye diye
Amiya sagare bhasa

Translation:

What is love?
What is love?
Love is Delight’s message-light.
Love is the distribution of one’s self-giving
In the sea of Nectar-Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 1, , 1978
