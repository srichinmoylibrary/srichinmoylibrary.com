Jegechi Aj Nabin Ushar

Jegechi aj nabin ushar
Mukti dake
Pelam ami apan kare
Bishwa make

Translation:

Today a new liberation-dawn
Has awakened me.
Therefore, today I can claim
The World-Mother as my own,
   Very own.

From: Sri Chinmoy, One thousand lotus petals, part 2, , 2000
