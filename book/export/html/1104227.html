9 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
A desire-life is, indeed,
A long run of expectation,
Frustration and destruction.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 May 2000, 1:45 p.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
