651. The ear of the world

The ear of the world
Is very big.
It hears everything.

The heart of the world
Is very small.
It does not contain anything.

The mouth of the world
Is big, big.
It devours everything,
Even though at times unnecessarily.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
