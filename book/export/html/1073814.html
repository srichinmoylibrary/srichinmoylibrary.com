21.

Yesterday
I thought
That God created my enemy
To humiliate and destroy me.
Today
I clearly see
And strongly feel
That God created my enemies
To warn and perfect me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Command from God the Justice, Whisper from God the Compassion, Agni Press, 1994
