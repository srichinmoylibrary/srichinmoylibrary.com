PASSPORTS

Money is the superlative passport
In the country of
Deception and corruption.

Conscience is the most effective passport
In the country of
Truth and justice.

Surrender divine is the only passport
In the country of
Realisation and Perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 12, Agni Press, 1973
