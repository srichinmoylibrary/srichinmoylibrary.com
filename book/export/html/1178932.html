5950 What Will You Do For Me?

My Lord Supreme,
What will You do for me
If I give You my heart's blossoming faith?
"My child,
I shall take away
Your mind's brooding doubts."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 60, Agni Press, 1983
