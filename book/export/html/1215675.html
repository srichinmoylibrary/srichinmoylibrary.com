54. Big dreams, small dreams

His small head
Had big dreams.
Now
His big head
Has small dreams.
He is waiting
For his head
To become larger than the largest
So that his small dreams
Can die down
Secretly, unnoticed,
And his reality-friends
Can stand living with him
Openly and fruitfully.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 2, Agni Press, 1974
