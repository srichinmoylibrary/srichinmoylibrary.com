279. Progress you want?

Progress you want?
Remember then,
Your black doubt
Is your first and last limit.

Progress you want?
Remember then,
Your courage
Is your last limit.

Progress you want?
Remember then,
Your faith
Is your endless limit.

Progress you want?
Remember then,
Your love of Light
Is your birthless start
And your deathless limit.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 6, Agni Press, 1974
