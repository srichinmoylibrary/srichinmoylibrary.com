8191 My Mind Knows

My heart may not know
How to love God soulfully.
My life may not know
How to serve God cheerfully.
But my mind knows
How to delay God's appearance
Indefinitely.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 82, Agni Press, 1983
