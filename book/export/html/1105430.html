My Rose-Road. My Lotus-Home

1.

My rose-road.
      My lotus-home.

2.

The rose-smile
            of the West
         I have.

3.

The lotus-tears
              of the East
           I am.

4.

      The beauty
         of a rose
purifies my
     mind-jungle.

5.

The fragrance
     of a rose
     beautifies my
         heart-garden.

6.

In the morning,
           the roses
are the lightning smiles
         of my soul.

7.

              In the evening,
                 the roses
are the bleeding tears
      of my heart.

8.

      When I see a rose
with my aspiration heart,
   I immediately feel
           that I am
      very close to God.

9.

   When I see a rose
with my dedication life,
   I spontaneously feel
         that I am
   a choice child of God.

10.

May my
heart-rose
never be
        surrounded by
     my mind-thorns.

11.

Each rose petal
                  is at once
  the eagerness of my heart
     and the enthusiasm
            of my life.

12.

Thorns do their duty
           powerfully:
they protect the roses.

13.

Roses do their duty
        blessingfully:
they liberate the thorns

14.

The purity life
a rose has.

15.

The divinity breath
      a rose is.

16.

      Indeed,
     a rose and a lotus
   are extremely beautiful,
   but the gratitude-tears
           of our hearts
are infinitely more beautiful.

17.

Each rose has
      three special names:
God's Vision-Eye,
  God's Compassion-Heart,
God's Satisfaction-Life.

18.

The outer rose
      blesses my eyes
         immediately.

19.

The inner rose
illumines
        my heart
   sleeplessly.

20.

Unlike human beings,
      no rose needs
qualification or
   recommendation.

21.

Each rose
      is a
master-key
to God's Heart-Kingdom.

22.

My desire-mind
      has no access
to my heart-garden.

23.

My aspiration heart is
far beyond the snare
  of my mind-forest.

24.

   When I pray,
                     I become
a hope-fountain rose.

25.

   When I meditate,
         I become
a promise-mountain rose.

26.

   Twice my stupidity
reigns supreme:
              once when
           I look for a rose
                  inside my
              mind-desert,
           and once when
     I neglect to look
        for a rose
inside my heart-garden.

27.

  My mind’s
               doubt-weight
     I lose completely
   the moment I enter
into my heart's rose-garden.

28.

O my lotus-heart,
   only because of you
I see God's smiling Face
           everywhere.

29.

     O my lotus-heart,
      because of
your exquisite fragrance,
  I am able to become
     a God-dreamer.

30.

      My heart-garden
        deeply enjoys
  the company of
        two guests:
compassion and sympathy.

31.

As beauty and fragrance
     are inseparable,
even so my heart-lotus
  and my soul-breath
are inseparable.

32.

      Alas,
will my Indian heart
      ever be as pure
  as an Indian lotus?

33.

      Alas,
     will my Indian heart
        ever be able to see
   the compassion-beauty
  of an Indian lotus
      in each human being?

34.

Each lotus
  is the
fondest child,
     greatest pride
and sweetest smile
      of an Indian goddess.

35.

The discovery
   of my
  Indian heart:
        no Indian lotus,
     no Indian goddess.

36.

My heart lotus tells me
              that I am not
     my mind's dark and
      starless night.

37.

My heart-lotus tells me
                 that I am
     my soul's
illumination-adorer.

38.

   Each petal
      of my heart-lotus
reveals a unique message
        from the Beyond.

39.

Each petal
of my heart-lotus
               receives
  a special boon from
God's Compassion-Eye.

40.

Each petal
of my heart-lotus
     is a humble prayer
  of my heart.

41.

      My mind's
ingratitude-weeds
      disappear
  the moment
         I enter into
   my heart's lotus-garden.

42.

The lotus-life
   sings
      inside
my tearful heart-nest.

43.

      The lotus-life
              dances
           inside
      my blissful
soul-kingdom

#+ENDVERSE.

44.

My lotus-heart bloom
         expedites
the arrival of God's Hour.

45.

  I call it
my lotus-life.
     God calls it
my heart's purity-breath.

46.

     The
   aspiration heart-lotus
     knows
no venom-desire-fruits.

47.

My
aspiration heart-lotus
     loves me immensely.
Therefore I do not
     have to go
  to the desire-market
              anymore.

48.

  A great
mind-tower
     is empty
  of both flower-beauty
      and flower-fragrance.

49.

  A pure
heart-flower
is the height of beauty
            and the depth
  of fragrance.

50.

Today
   is my
  today's
   hope-flower.

51.

Tomorrow
   will be
  my tomorrow's
   promise-fruit.

52.

God the Lover
and God the Beloved
   sleeplessly love to play
        hide and seek
inside humanity's
     aspiration-garden.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Rose-Road. My Lotus-Home, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1995
