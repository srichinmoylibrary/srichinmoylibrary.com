16.

My Lord,
Do You not think
It is high time
For You to create
A better world?
"My child,
I thought you are supposed
To do that.
"Please, My child,
Do not thrust upon
My two weak Shoulders
Your heavy responsibility.
You may not believe Me,
My child,
But I am already overburdened."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 3, Agni Press, 1998
