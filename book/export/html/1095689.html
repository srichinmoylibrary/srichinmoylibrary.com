29 The Earth-Time Ticks And Ticks ...

The earth-time ticks and ticks
To awaken me.
The Heaven-time beckons
And beckons me
To raise me high, higher, highest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 24, Agni Press, 2004
