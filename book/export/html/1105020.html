70.

When I think of God,
God says to me,
“I am not fully satisfied with you.”
When I pray to God,
God says to me,
“I am not fully satisfied with you.”
When I meditate on God,
God says to me,
“I am not fully satisfied with you.”
When I say to God,
“My Lord, I love You only
And I need You only,”
God says to me,
“My child, come to Me
And be with Me
All the time, all the time.”

30 November 2005
Pangkor Island, Malaysia

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 2006
