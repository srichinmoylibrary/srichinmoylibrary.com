COMPLAINTS

You complain
Because
Your greatness
Is not acknowledged.
He complains
Because
His smallness
Is well exposed.
God complains
Because
His goodness
Is not claimed by humanity.
Alas,
I know not what to do
With you people.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
