76 My Mind Must Live ...

My mind must live
In prayers.
My heart must live
In meditations.
Only then shall I be able to have
A full life of God-Beauty
And God-Fragrance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 10, Agni Press, 2002
