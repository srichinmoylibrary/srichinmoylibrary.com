176. Do better things, say better things

Do better things.
Prove to Divinity
That your earth-life is something.
Say better things.
Prove to humanity
That your Heaven-realisation is something.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
