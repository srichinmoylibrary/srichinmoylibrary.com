694. No higher pride, no greater joy

No higher pride
Mother Earth contains
Than the man
Whom Divinity maintains.

No greater joy
Father Heaven owns
Than the man
Whom ignorance disowns.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
