Insipid

(tasteless, dull)

If you do not have
An inspiration-mind
And an aspiration-heart,
Then you are bound to have
An insipid life.

O my mind,
I am sick of your
Constant, insipid doubt-talk.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
