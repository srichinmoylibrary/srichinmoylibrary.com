9 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must conquer my mind's frustrations.
I must conquer my heart's depressions.
I must conquer my life's failures.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 September 2000, 6:39 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
