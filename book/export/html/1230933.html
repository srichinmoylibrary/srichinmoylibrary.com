Ami Simahin Biram Bihin

Ami simahin biram bihin sasimer karagare
Bhagna swapan bhagna jiban andhiyar charidhare

Translation:

Sleeplessly endless I was.
I now find myself in the prison of the finite.
Broken is my dream, broken is my life.
Tenebrous existence-reality all around me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sundaratama dekha dilo aji, part 2, , 2006
