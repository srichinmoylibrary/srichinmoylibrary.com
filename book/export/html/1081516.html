8. My Lord Supreme spoke to me

In the morning
My Lord Supreme spoke to me
With His Compassion-Heart.
I did not care;

Therefore
In the afternoon
My Lord Supreme spoke to me
With His Attention-Eye.
I did not care;

Therefore
In the evening
My Lord Supreme spoke to me
With His Justice-Hands.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giving and becoming, Agni Press, 1974
