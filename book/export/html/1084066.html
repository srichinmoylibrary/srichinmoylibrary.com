40. World plans

Your head houses
   World-shaking plans.

Your eyes contain
   World-devouring plans.

Your heart embraces
   World-illumining plans.

Your soul treasures
   World-surrendering plans.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
