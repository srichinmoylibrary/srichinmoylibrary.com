4 June 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I must increase
My mind's simplicity,
My heart's sincerity,
My life's purity
And my God-necessity
In incredible measure.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 June 1999, 8:53 a.m.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 6, Agni Press, 1999
