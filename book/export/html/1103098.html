December 6

My morning begins,
And to my great joy and relief,
Doubts do not dare to bark at my mind
And insecurities do not dare
To torture my heart.
I now have become high, higher, highest-flying
Aspiration-delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My morning begins, Agni Press, 1996
