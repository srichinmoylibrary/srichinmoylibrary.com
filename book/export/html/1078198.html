551 The Inner Sun...

THE INNER SUN

The rainbow comes and goes
With a charming body.
The outer sun comes and goes
With a vast body.
The inner sun stays and stays
With its boundless
And deathless
Love-light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
