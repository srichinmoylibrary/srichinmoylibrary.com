FRIENDS OF MINE

You are a celestial friend of mine;
Therefore
I need your smile,
You need my love.

You are a celestial friend of mine;
Therefore
I do not need your impurity,
You do not need my stupidity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
