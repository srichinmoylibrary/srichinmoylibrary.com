He Ishwari Pale Pale Tomai Smari

He Ishwari he Ishwari
Pale pale tomai smari
He Ishwari he Ishwari
Dibarati puja kari
He Ishwari he Ishwari
Ratul tomar charan dhari

Translation:

O Goddess Absolute Supreme,
O Goddess Absolute Supreme,
Sleeplessly I think of You.
Day and night I worship You.
I have placed my life at Your
   Infinite power-red Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bahir jagate, part 1, , 2002
