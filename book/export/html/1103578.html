14 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Do inundate my entire life
With Your infinite Compassion
So that I can soften the sorrows
And decrease the sufferings
Of the world.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

14 July 1999, 6:55 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
