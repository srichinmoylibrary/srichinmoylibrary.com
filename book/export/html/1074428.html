44.

Iccha-sakti

Will-power

Will-power reaches the goal not because it is the possessor of an adamantine heart, but because it is the boundless lover of an illumining and fulfilling goal.No will-power, no success. No will-power, no progress. For success, be careful and be brave. For progress, be conscious and be self-giving.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Core Of India's Light, Part 3, Agni Press, 1992
