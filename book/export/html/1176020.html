3067 If Your Mind Develops

If your mind develops
A bad opinion of your pure heart
Even by mistake,
Then it will never, never be able
To see the vision-eye
Of heavenly Blue.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 31, Agni Press, 1982
