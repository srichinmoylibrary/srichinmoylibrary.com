20039

The fastest outer sprinter
And the fastest inner sprinter
Are not rivals.
They are two complementary souls.
The outer sprinter
Charms the inner sprinter.
The inner sprinter
Illumines the outer sprinter.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 201, Agni Press, 1994
