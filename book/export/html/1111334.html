Incognito

The other day in Hawaii, we were eating at the Pancake House. A man and his wife were sitting in the next booth. The man was talking about the Sri Chinmoy Marathon.

I looked at Ranjana to say that I was facing him, but he didn't know it was me. Ranjana was whispering to Lavanya about it, but the man still didn't recognise me. He must have been reading someone's T-shirt.

4 January 1980

From: Sri Chinmoy, Run and become, become and run, part 3, Agni Press, 1980
