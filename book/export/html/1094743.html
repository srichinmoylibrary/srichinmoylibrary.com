60 My Imagination-Mind ...

My imagination-mind,
Wake up, wake up!
Sleep no more.
My aspiration-heart,
Climb up, climb up!
Delay no more, delay no more!
My dedication-life,
God Himself is watching.
Be careful, be careful!

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 13, Agni Press, 2002
