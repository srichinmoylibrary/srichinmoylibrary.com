My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 24

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Solo, Indonesia in December 2003.

6 December 2003 ...

6 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

1 My Love Of God ...

My love of God
Must immensely increase
From this very moment.

2 My God-Feet-Devotion ...

My God-Feet-devotion
Must immensely increase
From this very moment.

3 My God-Heart-Surrender ...

My God-heart-surrender
Must immensely increase
From this very moment.

4 With My Aspiration-Cries ...

With my aspiration-cries
And my dedication-smiles,
I am desperately trying
To make up for lost time.

5 My Lord’s Compassion-Eye ...

My Lord’s Compassion-Eye
Loves to play all the time
With the tears and smiles
Of my heart.

6 My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme
Is extremely fond
Of my heart-whispers.

7 My Lord Wants ...

My Lord wants
The beauty of my smiles
To be invincible.

8 My Lord Wants ...

My Lord wants
The fragrance of my tears
To be indestructible.

9 God Begs My Doubting Mind ...

God begs my doubting mind
To mingle
With my aspiring heart.
He also tells my aspiring heart
Not to worry.

10 Every Single God-Seeker ...

Every single God-seeker
Embodies
God’s Vision-Fulfilment-Eye.

11 The Desire-Life ...

The desire-life
That I once stupidly chose
I am now wisely
Casting aside.

12 Each Moment I Can Apply ...

Each moment I can apply
For my conscious ascent
To the highest.
Alas,
What prevents me,
If not my unwillingness-mind?

13 Every Day ...

Every day
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Drinks and drinks and drinks
From my gratitude-heart-fountain.

7 December 2003 ...

7 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

14 My Lord Is Always Eager ...

My Lord is always eager
To receive the tears
Of my heart.

15 Sooner Or Later ...

Sooner or later
We needs must journey
Towards the Unknowable.

16 My Joys Know No Bounds ...

My joys know no bounds
When I am
My heart-country-traveller.

17 We Must Realise ...

We must realise
That the river of time
Does not wait
For anyone.

18 To My Greatest Joy ...

To my greatest joy,
Inside my Lord’s Heart-Garden
My gratitude-heart-flowers
Keep growing and growing.

19 O My Mind ...

O my mind,
I am begging you
To come with me only once
Into the silence-world.

20 I Must Never, Never Suffer ...

I must never, never suffer
From a broken heart-relationship
With my Eternity’s Beloved Supreme.

21 Alas ...

Alas,
Most of us love
Only emergency prayers.

22 My Lord, Chain Me! ...

My Lord, chain me!
Chain my heart, chain my life
And chain my all
To Your Compassion-flooded Will.

23 May My Life’s ...

May my life’s
God-surrender-shrine-worship
Be fulfilled
In this lifetime.

24 My Lord ...

My Lord,
As long as You care
For my heart-garden,
I shall not mind
If You do not care
For my mind-jungle
And my life-market.

25 With Every God-Gratitude-Breath ...

With every God-gratitude-breath
Of mine,
I come closer and closer
To my Lord Supreme.

26 My Lord ...

My Lord,
You have endless Names,
But I call You
And I shall forever call You
By only one Name:
Forgiveness.

8 December 2003 ...

8 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

27 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That He secretly and proudly treasures
My prayer-life
And my meditation-heart.

28 The Sleepless And Selfless ...

The sleepless and selfless
Heart-giver
Expects no return.

29 The Earth-Time Ticks And Ticks ...

The earth-time ticks and ticks
To awaken me.
The Heaven-time beckons
And beckons me
To raise me high, higher, highest.

30 Who Is My Mother Earth ...

Who is my Mother Earth,
If not a ceaseless flood
Of God-Tears?

31 Who Is My Father Heaven ...

Who is my Father Heaven,
If not an ocean
Of God-Smiles?

32 God Quickly Comes ...

God quickly comes
To the seeker
Who does not claim anything.

33 God Proudly Comes Running ...

God proudly comes running
To the seeker
Who lovingly, devotedly and soulfully
Claims God.

34 I Wish Every Step Of My Life ...

I wish every step of my life
To be guided
By the Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

35 God Is Exceedingly ...

God is exceedingly
And equally pleased
With my sleepless heart-tears
And my breathless soul-smiles.

36 What Was I Doing? ...

What was I doing?
I was repeating and chanting
God’s Forgiveness-Name.

37 What Am I Doing? ...

What am I doing?
I am repeating and chanting
God’s Compassion-Name.

38 What Will I Be Doing? ...

What will I be doing?
I will be repeating and chanting
God’s Inspiration, Encouragement
And Enthusiasm-Names.

39 Centuries Ago ...

Centuries ago
I started devouring
The golden dust
Of my Lord’s Feet,
And I have not stopped
Even for a fleeting moment.

9 December 2003 ...

9 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

40 Since My Lord ...

Since my Lord
Does not think of my future,
Why do I have to think
Of my future?
Do I not know
That my future is eternally safe
In my Lord’s Hands?

41 Alas ...

Alas,
Very few people enjoy
Walking along life’s
Transformation-road.

42 When My Heart Suffers ...

When my heart suffers,
My soul comes
With an overwhelming flood
Of sympathy.

43 Move Away, My Mind! ...

Move away, my mind!
My God wants to enter
Into my heart.

44 How Can A Seeker ...

How can a seeker
Succeed
Without his faith-breath?

45 Self-Doubt Is, Indeed ...

Self-doubt is, indeed,
The very beginning
Of a dangerous self-threat.

46 May My Heart ...

May my heart
Always remain enraptured
By God’s Sweetness-Eye.

47 Every Day We Should Increase ...

Every day we should increase
Our love-devotion-surrender-speed
On our God-fulfilment-road.

48 Alas ...

Alas,
I do not know how and when
I have lost contact
With my Beloved Supreme.

49 When I Cry ...

When I cry,
I see myself above earth.
When I smile,
I see myself below Heaven.

50 My Aspiration-Mountain ...

My aspiration-mountain
And my dedication-fountain
Must work together.

51 My Life ...

My life,
Continue to pray!
My heart,
Continue to meditate!
Ignorance-forces before long
Will retire and surrender.

52 My Love, My Devotion ...

My love, my devotion
And my surrender
Have only one Source:
My Lord’s Compassion-Eye.

11 December 2003 ...

11 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

53 Where Is God? ...

Where is God?
God is just behind me,
Watching me very carefully.

54 What Is God Doing? ...

What is God doing?
God is merrily dancing
Inside my singing.

55 God’s Will ...

God’s Will
Is not my master.
God’s Will
Is my Eternity’s partner.

56 Our Prayers ...

Our prayers
Inch by inch
Take us to God’s Feet.

57 Our Meditations ...

Our meditations
Mile by mile
Take us to God’s Heart.

58 As We Want To Be Loved ...

As we want to be loved
By God,
God also wants to be loved
By us —
Even more so.

59 Hard To Believe ...

Hard to believe,
But it is true:
God loves not only
My aspiration-heart,
But also
My recreation-life.

60 Mother Earth ...

Mother Earth
Dearly loves
Her running children.

61 Father Heaven ...

Father Heaven
Proudly loves
His meditating children.

62 Mother Earth Expects ...

Mother Earth expects
Smile-lustre
From her children.

63 Father Heaven Expects ...

Father Heaven expects
Peace-hunger
From His children.

64 Be Neither A Clever Seeker ...

Be neither a clever seeker
Nor a stupid seeker.
Publicly you smile;
Privately you sigh.

65 All Sincere Seekers ...

All sincere seekers
Agree on one thing:
We need God the Love
More than God the Power.

12 December 2003 ...

12 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

66 My Singing Heart ...

My singing heart
Is God’s proud Eye.

67 There Was A Time ...

There was a time
When I was a lover
Of God’s Feet,
But now I am a devourer
Of God’s ambrosial Feet-dust.

68 Sleepless Self-Giving ...

Sleepless self-giving
Knows no equal.

69 The Mind Thinks ...

The mind thinks
That it can easily live
Without God’s Love.

70 The Heart Feels And Knows ...

The heart feels and knows
That it cannot live
Even a fleeting second
Without God’s Love.

71 My Lord’s Compassion-Eye ...

My Lord’s Compassion-Eye
And my heart-cries
Can never be separated.

72 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That He does not want
To hear from me
My sad news and bad news
Any more.

73 God’s Pride-Eye ...

God’s Pride-Eye
And my love-heart
Celebrate elaborately
Our mutual arrivals.

74 Insecurity-Heart Disappears ...

Insecurity-heart disappears
Only when
God’s Compassion-Eye appears.

75 My Heart-Tears ...

My heart-tears
Have a free access
To my Lord’s Smile-Eye.

76 Outwardly God Does Not Tell Me ...

Outwardly God does not tell me
What He thinks of my visits
To His Heart-Home,
But inwardly He does.
I know it, I can feel it —
It is all His blessingful Love
And Joy.

77 The Mind ...

The mind
Does not love God.
The mind
Does not need God.
It enjoys only
Its self-importance.

78 I Have Only One Desire ...

I have only one desire:
I wish to be
A breathless, heart-diving
God-lover.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 24, Agni Press, 2004
