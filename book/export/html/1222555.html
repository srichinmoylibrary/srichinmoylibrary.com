Kandite Chahina Hasite Chahina

Kandite chahina hasite chahina chahi je kebal pujite
Dharai esechi he param prabhu tomare satata tushite

Translation:

I do not want to cry, I do not want to smile,
I only want to worship You.
I have come into this world, O my Beloved Supreme,
   Only to please You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Gahite chahi, , 1995
