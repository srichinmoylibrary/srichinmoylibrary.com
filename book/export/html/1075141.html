568 He Needs More...

HE NEEDS MORE

The more an earth-bound man has,
The more he needs.

He has doubt;
He needs more.

He has despair;
He needs more.

He has failure;
He needs more.

The more a heaven-soaring man has,
The more he needs.

He has faith, faith in himself;
He needs more.

He has courage, courage to brave ignorance;
He needs more.

He has love, love to feed the world;
He needs more.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 12, Agni Press, 1973
