Encouragement

1.

Encouragement is the helper of enlightenment.

2.

When we encourage others, we increase our own divine qualities.

3.

Encouragement is the heart of a new journey.

4.

Encouragement shortens the distance to the Golden Shore.

5.

Encouragement expedites our achievements.

6.

Encouragement is the power-transmission.

7.

Encouragement is of constant need both in the inner world of progress and in the outer world of success.

8.

Encouragement awakens the body, energises the vital, strengthens the mind and widens the heart.

9.

Encouragement is the revelation and manifestation of our inner joy.

10.

Through encouragement, we offer our divinity to mankind.

11.

Encouragement-givers are the choice instruments of God.

12.

Encouragement is the beauty of selfless-service.

13.

Encouragement is the source of God-satisfaction right from the beginning.

14.

Encouragement is the fragrance of our soul.

15.

Encouragement is our love-manifestation to gladden the heart of humanity.

16.

While I am encouraging others, I feel that I am soaring and singing in the vastness-sky.

17.

Encouragement increases others' faith in themselves to reach their destined goals.

18.

Encouragement is the supplier of a new heart, a new mind and a new life.

19.

Encouragement is as quick as lightning in joy-giving.

20.

Encouragement can flood the whole world with its light.

21.

The delight of encouragement will never fade from memory.

22.

Encouragement rises from light to light and from delight to delight.

23.

Encouragement is an immediate smile-producer.

24.

Encouragement is a dark despair-shooter.

25.

Encouragement has the capacity, in ample measure, to unfold its own beauty.

26.

Encouragement is genuine eagerness to help the world.

27.

Encouragement is compassion and concern in action.

28.

Encouragement unimaginably helps us to reach our destination.

29.

Encouragement is the needle pointing to the pole-star.

30.

Encouragement is the light that spreads our heart's divine qualities.

31.

Encouragement does not believe in a failure-life.

32.

Encouragement is the jubilee of our inner light.

33.

Encouragement is the heart in tune with God's appreciation.

34.

Encouragement is a hope-bird that flies on promise-wings.

35.

Encouragement is a resplendent faith-bestower.

36.

Encouragement tells us how to communicate with the sorrowful world.

37.

Without encouragement the self-will may forever remain dormant.

38.

Encouragement is a very special light that is born of self-offering.

39.

God encourages me to be perfect. I encourage God to give me a little more time.

40.

Encouragement is the courage-supplier to become victorious in life.

41.

Encouragement is the doubt-fear-anxiety conqueror.

42.

Encouragement is conscious and illumining oneness-heart-expansion.

43.

Encouragement radiates love, concern and the feeling of oneness.

44.

Encouragement is the performance of oneness-responsibility.

45.

My encouragement is the cheerful offering of my heart and hand.

46.

Encouragement is the beauty and fragrance of the heart-spring.

47.

My encouragement is my self-dedication for the improvement of the whole world.

48.

Each time I encourage the world, God helps me to run faster, dive deeper and fly higher.

49.

Everybody needs encouragement, specially the world peace-dreamers.

50.

O encouragement-fountain, you are my inner joy and my outer pride.

51.

The source of encouragement is the oneness-heart of the beyond.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Encouragement, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1998
