53 I Do Not Know Why, ...

I do not know why,
But it is absolutely true
That God loves my God-claiming heart
Infinitely more than He loves
My God-loving and God-serving life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 3, Agni Press, 2000
