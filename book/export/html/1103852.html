6 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have prayerfully and soulfully
   Established
My heart, my mind, my vital,
   My body and my life
Upon my God-realisation-foundation.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1999, 8:29 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
