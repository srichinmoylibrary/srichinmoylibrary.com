1228 If You Come To Me Now

If you come to me now,
I shall immediately show you
The Vision-Eye of God.
If you do not come,
I shall show you
The orphan-tears
Of your complete failure-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 13, Agni Press, 1981
