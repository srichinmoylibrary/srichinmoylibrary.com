Mata Mata Mata

Mata mata mata
Saraswati mata
Karo prane shuddha
Karo mane buddha
Jibana samare
Mama antare
Dao dao mata
Asim purnata

Translation:

Mother, Mother, Mother,
      Mother Saraswati,
Do purify my vital.
Do enlighten my mind.
In the battlefield of my life,
      O Mother divine,
Do grant my heart
      Your fulfilment infinite.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bela chale jai, , 1979
