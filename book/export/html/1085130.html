June 1985

My Beloved Lord Supreme,
You have forgiven me limitless times.
Will You not forgive me this time?

"My sweet child,
There was a time
When you used to please Me
In My own Way.
But alas, those golden days
Are buried in oblivion.
Will you not start pleasing Me once again
In My own Way?"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Gratitude-Flower-Hearts, Agni Press, 1993
