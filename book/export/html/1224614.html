Megh Je Deke Jai Ai.. Ghare Thaka Dai

Megh je deke jai ai ai ai
Ghare thaka dai rob utheche tai
Jai ai jai
Megher tari neche chale
Oi dakiche sabai
Bare bare megh je deke jai
Nacher nesha laglo gacher patai
Sedake kar pran na matai

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
