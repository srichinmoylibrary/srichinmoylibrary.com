304 I Can't Follow...

I CAN'T FOLLOW

I can't follow God
Because
He wants, instead, to follow me most faithfully.

I follow man
Because
He wants to lead me constantly.

Staying behind,
God inspires me
To reach my sun-world.

Staying ahead,
Man instigates me
To ignore my aspiration-world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 7, Agni Press, 1973
