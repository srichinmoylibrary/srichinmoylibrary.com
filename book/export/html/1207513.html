21661

Let us not stop.
Let us continue and continue.
If we have an iota of peace,
Then we have to get more peace,
Abundant peace, boundless peace.
This is our way
Of transcending and becoming,
Becoming and transcending.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 217, Agni Press, 1994
