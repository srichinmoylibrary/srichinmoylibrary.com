332. Will God allow it?

Who can fail?
Will God ever tolerate
Such nonsense?
No, never!

Who can fail?
Will God allow Himself
To remain asleep?
No, never!

Who can fail?
Will God dare to forget
His promise to Himself?
No, never!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
