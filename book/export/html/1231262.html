Alor Sathe Alo Haye

Alor sathe alo haye
Aloi chali mishe
Bahur buke dodul dule
Berai dasha dishe

Translation:

With the Light divine,
One I have become.
I have perfectly blended
In the Light divine.
All-where in the heart of the many
I swim and sport
With boundless joy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, teach me how to surrender, , 1975
