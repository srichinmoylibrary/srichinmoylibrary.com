1427

If your heart loves God
In God's own Way,
If your life serves God
In God's own Way,
I cannot understand
How you can ever suffer from
An inner energy crisis.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 15, Agni Press, 1983
