16 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   From now on
I shall not waste
Even a fleeting second
With my self-doubts.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 October 1999, 9:57 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
