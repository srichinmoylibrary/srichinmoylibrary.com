April 12

How to conquer jealousy? Conceal it consciously. To your wide surprise, one day you will not be able to trace it at all. It will all be gone, a story of the past.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
