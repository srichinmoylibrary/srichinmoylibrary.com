This Morning My Lord Said to Me

This morning my Lord said to me,
“My child, if you once more weep
At your unworthiness,
Never shall I visit you.
As I claim you to be My all,
Even so, you must claim Me
To be your All.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, Enthusiasm, part 5, , 2003
