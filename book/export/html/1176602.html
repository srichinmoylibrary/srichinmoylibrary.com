3643 Live Up To Your Himalayan Heights

Live up to your Himalayan heights.
Let the world criticism
Bark at you and bite you.
You will not be affected.
Before the world barks at you and bites you,
You will be carried by God Himself
To the remotest and safest haven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
