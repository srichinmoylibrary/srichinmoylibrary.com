722 O Rama!...

O RAMA!

O Rama! O Rama!
Tell me,
Why
Were you so unkind
To Sita, your consort?
Why? Why?

"I wanted
To prove
To the world
At large
That my Sita's
Sacrifice-Light
Far surpassed
My morality's
Judgment-Might."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
