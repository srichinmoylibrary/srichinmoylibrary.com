91. When I think of God

When I think of God,
God tells me
He will give me all that He has.

When I meditate on God,
I clearly see that He has given me
All that He has been treasuring
From time immemorial
And all that He eternally is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
