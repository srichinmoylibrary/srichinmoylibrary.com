78. When

O earth-body,
When
Will you be receptive?

O earth-vital,
When
Will you be perceptive?

O earth-mind,
When
Will you be concentrative?

O earth-heart,
When
Will you be contemplative?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
