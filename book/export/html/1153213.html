39775

My Lord, no matter what kind of hell
  I create for myself,
Your boundless Affection
And boundless Concern
  Will save me,
Provided I keep my heart-door open
  To You, only to You,
And to nobody else.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 40, Agni Press, 2004
