193. Just once

You will recover
Full confidence in yourself -
Just feel once
That God will do everything for you.

You will discover
Full God-realisation in yourself -
Just believe only once
That your source
And
God's source
Are always the same.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
