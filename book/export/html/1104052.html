7 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please bless me
With abundant capacities
So that my spiritual children
Do not make daily complaints to You
Against me.
They are telling You that
They are unsought, unasked, unassisted,
Unappreciated, unadmired, unloved
And even unnoticed by me.
I wish to put an end
To this slogan of theirs.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 January 2000, 9:07 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously twice. (Total: 800 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
