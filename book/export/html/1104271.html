21 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Forgiveness ends
My helpless days.
Your Compassion begins
My hopeful days.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 June 2000, 8:30 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
