14371

Yesterday my aspiration-account
Was short of funds.
Today my dedication-account
Is short of funds.
Tomorrow my surrender-account
Will be short of funds.
Alas! Alas! Alas!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 144, Agni Press, 1991
