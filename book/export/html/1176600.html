3641 You Can Expect Nothing

Since your vital is sense-ridden,
You can expect nothing
From the world of joy.

Since your heart is grief-stricken,
The world of satisfaction
Can expect nothing from you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
