18. Two things he does

Two things, only two things,
He does:
He breathes in the perfume
Of God-promise
To Mother-Earth;
He breathes out the odour
Of frustration-reality
To Father-Heaven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 12, Agni Press, 1974
