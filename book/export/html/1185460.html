<em>Caller:</em> I was trying to give some healing to my father who was not too well, and I felt some energy moving in my hands. Am I right to feel that this and other kinds of experiences that I've had may be coming from God?

Steve Powers: You're asking about healing? I guess first we have to establish whether you believe that there is a force of healing.

Sri Chinmoy: I do believe in the force of healing.

Steve Powers: And the source of the force?

Sri Chinmoy: The source of the force is God, the all-Good. God is omnipotent; again, He is all Love. So it is from His Love that the healing power comes into existence.
