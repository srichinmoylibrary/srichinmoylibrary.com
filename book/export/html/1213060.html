151 Ribald

Madal: Chinmoy, I am sorry to tell you that many athletes enjoy ribald jokes. Needless to say, you do not sail in their boat.

Chinmoy: Madal, it is true that I do not indulge in course and vulgar jokes, but I do indulge in sports, and I am always proud to be called an athlete.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two brothers: Madal and Chinmoy, part 4, Agni Press, 1993
