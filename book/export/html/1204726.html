18901

You have an invisible Friend
Who is always eager to help you.
You may not see Him
With your human eyes,
But you can feel
His compassionate Presence
Inside your aspiring heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 190, Agni Press, 1993
