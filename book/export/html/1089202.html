China and Japan

China and Japan
in friendship-love
Is the gift supreme from
high above.
Man-power and mind-power
Have become a heart-flower,
With beauty's light and
purity's might,
The oneness-song
in fulness-height.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Japan: soul-beauty's heart-garden, Agni Press, 1993
