20. Don't worry, my friend

Your vital is the victim
Of acute depression.
        Don't worry, my friend.
I shall stay
With your depressed vital.

Your mind is the victim
Of wild frustration.
        Don't worry, my friend.
I shall stay
With your frustrated mind.

Your heart is the victim
Of cruel desertion.
        Don't worry, my friend.
I shall stay
With your deserted heart.

Your body is the victim
Of constant temptation.
        Don't worry, my friend.
I shall stay
With your tempted body.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
