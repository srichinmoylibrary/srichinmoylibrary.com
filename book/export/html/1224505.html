Ami Tomare Bhalobasi Tai Peyechi Taba

Ami tomare bhalobasi
Tai peyechi taba hasi
Ami sabare bhalobasi
Tai peyechi taba banshi
Ami amare bhalobasi
Tai peyechi andhar rashi rashi
