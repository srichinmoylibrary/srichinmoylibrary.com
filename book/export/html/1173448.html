520 A New Name

At last my searching mind
Has a new name:
Emptiness.

At last my crying heart
Has a new name:
Selflessness.

At last my smiling life
Has a new name:
Oneness.

At last my loving soul
Has a new name:
Fulness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 6, Agni Press, 1979
