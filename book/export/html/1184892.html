564. He loves his death

He loves his death.
He feels that death
Will give him some rest.
He feels that death
Will care more for him than life.
He feels that the other shore is greener.
He feels that a new experience
Will gladden him and fulfil him;
Therefore
His love for death
He treasures,
Expands,
Distributes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
