Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 250

24901

Every morning
God asks me to give Him
My aspiration-report.
Every evening
He asks me to give Him
My dedication-report.

24902

My Lord Supreme,
If You really love me,
Then kidnap my heart
Here and now!

24903

Every God-aspiration-attempt
Will eventually grow into
A God-satisfaction-fulfilment.

24904

The modern world
Desperately needs
The beauty, purity and divinity
Of hermitage.

24905

God is both
The Ideal and the Real.
When I am in my mind,
He is the Ideal Divine.
When I am in my heart,
He is the Real Supreme.

24906

May my mind become
An aspiration-student,
A dedication-teacher
And a surrender-professor.

24907

You may quarrel
With your desiring mind,
But never quarrel
With your aspiring heart,
For your aspiring heart
Is treasured by God Himself.

24908

Worldly fame
Is nothing but a fleeting flickering
Of the restlessness-eyes.

24909

I am sure my Lord is there
Inside my heart
To wait compassionately
Until I come.

24910

God showers His choicest Blessings
And Pride
Upon those who have
Happiness-wings-hearts.

24911

My soul wants me
To run the fastest.
Therefore, it is extremely sorry
To see that I have kept
My inner life
Always under construction.

24912

Our faith in God
Has to be indomitable.
Then only do we not have to stop
At the doubt-fear-hesitation-roadblocks.

24913

There was a time when I was
An aspiration-army-soldier.
Now my Lord Supreme
Wants me to volunteer
For His Divinity-manifestation-army.

24914

The beauty of peace
And the fragrance of silence
Are not only interdependent
But also inseparable.

24915

God asks me
To count the daily Blessings
That I receive from Him.
My mind wants me
To count all the frustrations
That I treasure every day.

24916

God's Compassion-Eye
Has dried all my tears.
Now He expects from me
A beautiful, powerful
And sky-soaring smile.

24917

Alas, by nature
The human mind is prone
To a deception-life of ease.

24918

Alas, so often my doubting mind
And my aggressive vital
Bring disgrace
To my aspiring heart.

24919

My Lord Supreme,
Do bless me with the Smile
Of Your Compassion-Eye,
So that I can liberate myself
From the ruthless doubt-prison.

24920

If your life is only
For God-Satisfaction on earth,
Then you can heroically and easily
Stand against overwhelming odds.

24921

The seeker who has nerves of iron
Can transform
His abysmal abyss-fate
Into God-Satisfaction's
Highest heights.

24922

My Lord Supreme,
Every day I shall beg You
For a new favour.
For today, do free my mind
From stark world-illusion-temptation.

24923

Because the human mind
Does not aspire,
It is always burdened
By uninspiring
And unnecessary questions.

24924

If we can prayerfully
And self-givingly cling
To God's Nectar-Feet,
Then we can easily commune
With the Ineffable.

24925

Each individual
Has come into the world
To cross beyond the snares
Of limitation,
And each individual
Will eventually succeed.

24926

An excellent God-instrument
Is he whose every breath on earth
Is a God-fulfilment-promise.

24927

If you want to run unaided
Towards your Goal,
Then yours will be a blunder
Of Himalayan heights.

24928

No desire-prompted action
Will ever be able to please God
In His own Way.

24929

He has received
From the Lord Supreme
His most coveted Prize:
Satisfaction.

24930

If perfection is what you need,
Then God-Satisfaction will one day be
At your disposal.

24931

The outer education
Is for the mind.
The inner education
Is for the heart.
The soul needs no education,
For the soul has always been
In perfect union
With God's Will.

24932

I have come to realise
That as long as I walk inside
My mind-jungle,
God is not going to walk inside
My heart-garden.

24933

When God comes to us,
He immediately looks
For the silence of our heart,
And nothing else.

24934

The real aspiration-heart-sun
Can never, never set.
It is always on the rise.

24935

If the name of your life-boat
Is obedience,
Then God will bless you
With His ever-blossoming Smile.

24936

Those who come to God
As a last resort
Will have to cover a very long distance.

24937

Gratitude-prayer-garland
Is made of
Sweetness-heart-flowers.

24938

Your brooding heaviness-mind
Will never find
The all-clearing Light
To show you the way
To your satisfaction-goal.

24939

Our heart-garden is the only place
Where we proclaim the first thing first:
God for God's sake only.

24940

My Lord,
As long as I have
Your Compassion-Eye
In front of me,
I shall never lose my way.

24941

Every day
Invariably we make friends
With frustrations.
Therefore, every day
We are drowning
In a whirlpool of frustrations.

24942

There is only one freedom-road
That my life has,
And that road is my constant oneness
With God's Will.

24943

I have only one goal,
And that goal
Is to climb up
My God-obedience-summit.

24944

Alas,
There is always a tug-of-war going on
Between man's frustration-mind
And God's Compassion-Eye.

24945

What my soul expects from me
Is nothing unusual.
It is something absolutely normal
And natural:
My selfless love for God.

24946

Self-admiration-disease
I have.
God-adoration-medicine
I need.

24947

If we settle for less than the highest,
The human in us may be satisfied,
But never the divine in us.

24948

My life-boat plies between
God the Compassion-Eye-Shore
And God the Forgiveness-Heart-Shore.

24949

The modern world needs
The simplicity of the ancient mind
And the purity of the ancient heart.

24950

To be happy and perfect,
I must look at myself lovingly,
And I must not only look at
But also enter into
My Lord Supreme self-givingly.

24951

To be sleeplessly self-giving
Is to reach the summit
Of all virtues.

24952

The human mind
Is nothing short of
A God-unwillingness-rock.

24953

If I am in my mind,
I am forced to accept
The cross of life.
If I am in my heart,
I am invited to be part of
The sweetness of life.

24954

There is nothing as sweet,
Beautiful and fruitful
As losing ourselves
In the very Compassion-Heart of God.

24955

No more shall I enjoy
The raging torrents
Of depression and frustration.
From now on, I shall enjoy
The loftiest victory
Of my inner faith.

24956

Each human mind,
Either knowingly or unknowingly,
Is a humour-rumour-columnist.

24957

What you need
Is a converted mind.
What you need
Is an unconverted heart.

24958

Every day
God comes down to blessingfully offer
His Pride-Reward
Only to those who rightly deserve it.

24959

No matter how long it takes,
My aspiring heart
Is ready to stay in darkness
While it is waiting for God.

24960

God's Compassion-Eye says:
"Give more, give more, give more!"
Man's receptivity-mind says:
"No more, no more, no more!"

24961

The highest world cannot ignore
Even one drop
Of man's self-offering love.

24962

Have patience, my seeker-friend!
To expect anything good
In the twinkling of an eye
Is nothing but utter stupidity.

24963

Only for the genuine God-lover
Is the Nectar-Heartbeat
Of Heaven.

24964

The very nature of the mind
Is to vehemently resist
God's Arrival.

24965

I am absolutely certain
That God suffers
From my careless neglect,
Although He never tells me.

24966

Alas,
It seems that the desert-days
Of my aspiration-life
Know no end.

24967

My Lord,
Can You not see
How sincerely I am praying to You
To save me from my shallow living?

24968

God does not wait,
Even for a minute,
To announce my unconditional love
For Him.

24969

To limit God
Only to the scriptures
Is the height of our stupidity.

24970

At long last,
My Lord Supreme has rescued me
From my blinding and binding mind.

24971

Only he who surrenders to God
Unconditionally
Can enjoy the beauty and fragrance
Of God-life.

24972

Most human beings
Find it extremely difficult
To lose even one pound
Of ignorance-weight.

24973

The beauty
Of our hope-dreams
Can never be fathomed.

24974

The fragrance
Of our promise-realities
Can never be measured.

24975

For years and years
I have been trying to listen
To my Lord's
Compassion-Sweetness-Whispers.

24976

Every day
I soulfully meditate
To strengthen my love-bond
With my Beloved Supreme.

24977

No matter what happens in my life,
I shall always steer my life-boat
To the God-Heart-Shore.

24978

There was a time when I was lost
In a world of depression.
Now that world
Is a perfect stranger to me.

24979

At long last,
I have caught the unhappiness-thieves
Red-handed!

24980

No joy can equal
The joy that is in
Full aspiration-swing.

24981

Alas,
How desperately I am trying
To enter into my heart's faith-cottage
Once more.

24982

There is always time
For a genuine seeker
To bury his ignorance-pleasure-life.

24983

Solid and abundant happiness abides
Only when we are surrounded
By beautiful inspiration
And powerful aspiration.

24984

The spiritual life
Is nothing other than
Successive attempts
At God-satisfaction.

24985

Every day
I simply die for
The affection-flooded Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

24986

To avoid
My aspiration-heart
And suspicion-mind-collision,
I always pay extra attention.

24987

I must examine my life
Only through the Beauty
Of my Lord's Compassion-Eye.

24988

My Lord Supreme,
Every day do save me
From the narrow confines
Of my stupid absurdity-mind.

24989

Each soulful meditation
Is, indeed,
A Heavenly host of delight.

24990

We are all eager to receive
Our realisation-diploma from God.
Our Lord of Compassion tells us:
"My children, just wait a while!"

24991

Each spiritual Master of the highest height
Is a flag-bearer
Of a fast-approaching new generation.

24992

Each illumining and fulfilling meditation
Has the capacity
To unlock the door of our heart-shrine.

24993

O my mind,
How can you ever be satisfied
By betraying the immortal will
Of my all-illumining soul?

24994

My happy heart is, indeed,
The secret and sacred sign
Of my Lord's Affection infinite.

24995

O my mind,
Your stupidity knows no bounds!
Every day you are exaggerating
The torture of your impurity-struggles.

24996

Alas, quite often
Our human life likes to satisfy itself
With the intrusion
Of the confusion-mind.

24997

O seeker, be careful!
Darkness knows how to grow
Inside your heart
Unnoticed.

24998

Nothing can be
As hurtful or destructive
As the tyranny of the suspicion-mind.

24999

God's Joy knows no bounds
When He hears the blissful voice
Of a seeker's prayerful devotion.

25000

There is no prayer
In God's entire creation
That can never be answered
When our life becomes
The beauty and fragrance
Of sleepless tears.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 250, Agni Press, 1998
