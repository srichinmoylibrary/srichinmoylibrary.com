2660 Unconditional Forgiveness

My heart knows,
My mind knows not.
My heart knows
When it is disobedient to God.
My mind does not know
That God can be unconditional Forgiveness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
