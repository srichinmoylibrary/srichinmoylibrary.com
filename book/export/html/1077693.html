46. A mounting possibility

A mounting possibility:
Your God-realisation.

A mounting possibility:
Your life-transformation.

A mounting possibility:
Your oneness-manifestation.

A mounting possibility:
Your God-satisfaction.

Therefore
Lose not hope, try.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
