19331

With our soulful prayers
Let us enter into the consciousness
Of the truth-seekers and God-lovers,
Together to create a new life and a new world
For a new generation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 194, Agni Press, 1993
