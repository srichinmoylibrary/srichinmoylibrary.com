268. I love a holy tree

I love a holy tree.
It inspires me to shelter
My earth-friends:
Impurity and insecurity.

I love a holy man.
He inspires me to invite
My Heaven-friends:
The cosmic gods.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
