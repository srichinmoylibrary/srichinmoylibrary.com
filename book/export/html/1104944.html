96.

No age will replace
Our Master’s tears
And our Master’s smiles.

No age will replace
My children’s love for me
And their faith in me.

No age will replace
My gratitude to their hearts
And their gratitude to my life.

6 March 2004
New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 2004
