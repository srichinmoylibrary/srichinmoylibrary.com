209. What I love

My eyes love
The smile of the morning-light.

My arms love
The power of the midday-height.

My ears love
The song of the evening-life.

My heart loves
The silence of the night-splendour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
