19. Hope

Sweet is my hope.
Pure is the life of my hope.

With my sweet hope
     I try to reach the higher worlds.
With my pure hope
     I try to fathom my inner worlds.     But alas,
In neither way do I succeed.
     I fail,
           I miserably fail.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, receive this little undying cry, Agni Press, 1975
