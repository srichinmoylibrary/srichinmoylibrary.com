20102

When I mix with earth-wallowers,
I feel that it is too late
For me to aspire.
When I mix with Heaven-followers,
I feel that it is too soon
For me; I am not ready.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 202, Agni Press, 1994
