16.

For Carl Lewis, unlike others, there is no such thing as confidence-overdose. In his case, we call it confidence-light. This confidence-light has come to the fore from the Compassion-Satisfaction-flooded Blessings of his Inner Pilot.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Carl Lewis: the champion inner runner, part 1, Agni Press, 1991
