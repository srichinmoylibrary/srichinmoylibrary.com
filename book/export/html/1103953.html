8 November 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
Every day You allow me
To have a lovely and lively
Conversation with You
At least for an hour.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 November 1999, 7:52 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
