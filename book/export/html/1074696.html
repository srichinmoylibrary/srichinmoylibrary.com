132. Yet you are fond of me

God, I am absent-minded;
Yet You are fond of me.

God, I am negligent;
Yet You are fond of me.

God, I am insecure;
Yet You are fond of me.

God, I am impure;
Yet You are fond of me.

God, why are You so fond of me?
"My child, I am fond of you,
        Because
            You try,
        Because
            You cry."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 3, Agni Press, 1973
