63.

His soulful admirers and faithful adorers
Treasure him in the depths
Of their gratitude-pride-heart,
For his life's continuous
Dynamism-speed-lead
In the present-day world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: the home of Oneness-Peace-Dream-World, Agni Press, 2001
