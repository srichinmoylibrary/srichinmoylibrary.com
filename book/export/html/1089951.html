Ignorance

Who was I?
Ignorance-sea.
My Lord Supreme says,
"Definitely not."
Who am I?
Ignorance-wave.
My Lord Supreme says
"I hold a different opinion."
Who shall I be?
Ignorance-drop.
My Lord Supreme says,
"You are all wrong."
He tells me I shall be
The sea of His Consciousness-Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Light-delight-journeys, Agni Press, 1974
