3339

All you do is pretend
That you are excellent in everything
  That you say and do.
Poor God is working so hard
  To inject sincerity
Into your heart, mind and life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 4, Agni Press, 1998
