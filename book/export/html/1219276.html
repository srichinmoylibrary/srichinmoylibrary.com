Bandhan Khandan Halo Aji

Bandhan khandan halo aji
Tai bujhi dekha dilo hiya majhi

Translation:

All the bondages of my life
   Are sundered today.
Therefore, my heart’s Boatman
   Appears before me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bahir jagate, part 1, , 2002
