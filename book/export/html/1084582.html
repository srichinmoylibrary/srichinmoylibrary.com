46. All are eager (I)

My deer is eager
To win for me.
My horse is eager
To dive for me.
My bull is eager
To fight for me.
My dog is eager
To wait for me.
My cow is eager
To illumine for me.
My lion is eager
To roar for me.
My God is eager
To smile for me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 12, Agni Press, 1974
