7 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am absolutely sure
That my heart's God-search-pilgrimage
Shall be crowned with success.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

7 July 2000, 8:24 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift four times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
