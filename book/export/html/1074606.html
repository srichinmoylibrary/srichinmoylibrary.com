44. Another beggar

There is something.
What is it?
Realisation?
I had it.

There is something.
What is it?
Revelation?
I am achieving it.

There is something.
What is it?
Manifestation?
I don't have it.
I need it.

Like me,
Another beggar needs it.
Who is He?

He, the Father of the Big Heart.
He, the Son of the Big Eye.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 1, Agni Press, 1973
