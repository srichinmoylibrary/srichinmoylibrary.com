3079 See If I Am Correct

You can be happy
If you say good-bye
To useless thoughts.
Do it, and see if I am correct.

You can be happy
If you say good-bye
To the soulless mind.
Just try it, and see if I am correct.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 31, Agni Press, 1982
