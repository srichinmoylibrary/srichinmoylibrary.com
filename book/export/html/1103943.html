4 November 1999﻿ (VIII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What I desperately need
Is a God-surrender-heart
And not
A world-computer-mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 November 1999, 9:25 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
