557 Heaven's Promise, Earth's Promise

Heaven has promised
To wait for me.
It will not
Permit this incarnation
To be a colossal failure.

Earth has promised
To keep me here
Until perfection becomes
My real name,
My only name.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 6, Agni Press, 1979
