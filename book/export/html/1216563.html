925. God-realisation

God-realisation
   Is your birthright.
   Fantasy, it is not.
   Obstinacy, it is not.
   Autocracy, it is not.
   Accuracy of your life Divine,
        It is.
   Supremacy of your Reality,
        It always is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
