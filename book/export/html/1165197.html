H — represents humility. Without humility my spirituality is curiosity, my spirituality is futility.

E — represents eternity. With faith my spirituality is eternity's wealth, my spirituality is eternity's delight.

R — represents revelation. Without devotion to God's self-revelation in me, my life is a breathless hush, my life is the song of death.

E — represents evolution. With my surrender's light I am God's ever-blooming and expanding evolution. I become the selfless link between His Infinity and His Immortality, I become His Vision's Reality and His Reality's Vision.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Songs of the Soul, Agni Press, 1971
