22 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Happiness I create
By silencing my mind.
Happiness I create
By bringing my heart to the fore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 May 2000, 8:45 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
