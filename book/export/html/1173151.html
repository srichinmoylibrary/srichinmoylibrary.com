226 I Know You Will Wait For Me

O my Lord Supreme,
I know You will wait for me
No matter how long I delay in offering
My timidity-heart,
My futility-mind,
My stupidity-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 3, Agni Press, 1979
