Question: Does divine opportunity come more than once in one lifetime?

Sri Chinmoy: Divine opportunity comes many times in our life. If we avail ourselves of each opportunity, then we run the fastest toward our destined goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, At the doors of time and delight opportunity knocks, Agni Press, 1974
