7732 Why Are You So Angry?

My Lord,
Why are You so angry with me?
"I am not angry with you.
I am angry with Myself."
Why? Why?
"Because still I have not been able
To perfect you."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 78, Agni Press, 1983
