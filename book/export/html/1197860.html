12103

Yesterday my life was
The full growth of supreme greatness.
Alas, today I have become
Unforeseen frustration-night
And the hurtful sighs
Of ceaseless sorrows.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 122, Agni Press, 1989
