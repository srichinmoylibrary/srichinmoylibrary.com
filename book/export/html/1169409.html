54. My friend-enemies

Hopeless imperfection,
Helpless situation,
Lifeless perfection,
Godless satisfaction.
These friend-enemies
Of mine;
Compelled always
With them to dine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
