5 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When the mind unjustly prospers,
The poor heart terribly suffers.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 July 2000, 6:54 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift four times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
