17. A God-failure

When I remove my earth-divinity
I am a God-failure.
     I see it
           But
     I ignore it.

When I remove my Heaven-reality
I am a God-failure,
           But
     I deny it.

When I remove my God-necessity
I am a God-failure.
     I don't deny it.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eternity's Silence-Heart, Agni Press, 1974
