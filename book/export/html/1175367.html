2420 My Soul And I

O my soul,
You and I
Shall go up together
To give Heaven
Earth's tearing cry.

O my soul,
You and I
Shall go down together
To give earth
Heaven's immortalising Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 25, Agni Press, 1982
