455

If you are a saint,
You will discover purity
Inside nothingness.

If you are a yogi,
You will discover fulness
Inside nothingness.

If you are another God,
You will discover that nothingness-dream
And oneness-reality
Are extremely fond of each other.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 5, Agni Press, 1983
