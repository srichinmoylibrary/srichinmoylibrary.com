416 To Make Yourself Happy

To make yourself happy,
Be what you have:
Aspiration-flame.

To make God happy,
Be what you are:
Dedication-lamp.

To make both yourself and God happy,
Become what you have to become:
Illumination-sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 5, Agni Press, 1979
