25. Your unwanted guest

In the past
When God came to you
You were deeply sceptical.

This morning
God came to you again.
You doubted Him openly.

God will come to you
Again and again
Even if you go
To the length of hating Him ruthlessly.           Why? Why?
Because
Poor God cannot dare
To live without you.
You are His Soul.
You are His Goal.
You are His All.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
