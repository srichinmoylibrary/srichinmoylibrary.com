Amar Swapan Tomai Diye Ebar

Amar swapan tomai diye ebar habo shanto
Satya katha balchhi mago ebar ami klanto
Bastabata jachi mago jago amar prane jago
Ar katokal raibo mago swapan niye mane
Satya shikha jwalao mago amar hriday bane

Translation:

I shall now offer You my dream-world
And then become totally silent.
I am telling You frankly
That I am completely exhausted.
What I want from You is reality-light
And not a dream-world.
How long have I to keep the dream-world
In my mind?
Realityâ€™s truth-world, do awaken
Inside the forest of my heart.
