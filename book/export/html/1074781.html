215. Listen to me

Sleep, my body, sleep,
It is a gentle thing.

March, my vital, march.
It is a brave thing.

Think, my mind, think.
It is an important thing.

Feel, my heart, feel.
It is a noble thing.

Sacrifice, my soul, sacrifice.
It is the perfect thing.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 5, Agni Press, 1973
