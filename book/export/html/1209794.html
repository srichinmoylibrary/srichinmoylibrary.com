23919

The mind-river is terribly afraid
Of flowing onward.
The heart-river enjoys
Flowing onward.
Therefore, it arrives unmistakably sooner
At the destination-ocean.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 240, Agni Press, 1997
