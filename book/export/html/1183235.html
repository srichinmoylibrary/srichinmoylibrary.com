99. Soul’s eye, ear and arm

Your soul has an ear.
With it you hear all about
Earth-failure.

Your soul has an eye.
With it you see
Heaven-satisfaction.

Your soul has an arm.
With it you create
World-perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
