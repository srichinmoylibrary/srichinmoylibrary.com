243. I have placed myself in You

My dream-boat
I have placed
In front of Your Eyes.

My reality-shore
I have placed
At Your Feet.

My earth-life
I have placed
Inside the Compassion-smile
Of Your Heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
