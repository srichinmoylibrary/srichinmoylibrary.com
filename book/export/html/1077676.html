29. You are the goal of everything

Your life tells me
You are the end of everything,
The final man.
I feel it is unmistakably true.

Your soul tells me
You are the goal of everything,
The universal God.
I know it is supremely true.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
