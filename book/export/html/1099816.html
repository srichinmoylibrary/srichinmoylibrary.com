Desires of mine

     Desires of mine
Limit my possibility.
     They allow me not
To hasten my Lord's Infinity.     The darkness blind
In secret covers my Knowledge-Sun.
     Alas, in me
The game of death has now begun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My first friendship with the muse, Agni Press, 1973
