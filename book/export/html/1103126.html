1 October 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
When I cry for You,
The Beauty of Your Eye
Embraces my heart.
When I smile at You,
The Fragrance of Your Heart
Pilots my life-boat
To Your own Golden Shore
Of the ever-transcending Beyond.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 1998, 2:33 a.m. Before lifting 83 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
