19. Perfection-banner you offer

Truth-banner you own;
Therefore
You are great.
Light-banner you hold;
Therefore
You are great.
Perfection-banner you offer;
Therefore
You are great,
You are good,
You are divine,
You are supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 12, Agni Press, 1974
