14. The Supreme

Father, You are the Grace.
           "No."
Father, You are the Law.
           "No."
Father, You are the Birth
           and
     Death of Creation.
           "No."
Father, You are the Child
           of
     Your DREAM.
           "Yes."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Flute, Agni Press, 1972
