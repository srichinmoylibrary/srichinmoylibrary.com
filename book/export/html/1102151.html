98.

"My Lord,
I am sure You are now tired.
You are now tired
  Of my ceaseless questions.
You are now tired
  Of our marathon talk.
You are tired
  Of my endless stupidity."

"My child,
I get Joy only when you talk to Me.
My Joy is rest, ever-nourishing
And ever-fulfilling."

"My Lord, I am a clever fellow.
I was just being modest and polite.
It is I who am actually tired."

"I see.
Then, My child, sleep.
Sleep inside My Life's Depth,
Inside My Soul's Breath,
Inside My Vision's Glow,
Inside My Reality's Flow.
Sleep, My child, sleep."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Secrets revealed, Agni Press, 1973
