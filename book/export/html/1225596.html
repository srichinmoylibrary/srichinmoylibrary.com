Rater Bhore Premer Dore

Rater bhore premer dore
Tomai ami bandhbo
Tomar paye mor amire
Rakhbo ami rakhbo

Translation:

When the night ends
And the day dawns,
I shall bind You
With my divine love-rope.
At Your Feet I shall place
My little ego-‘I’.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 11a, , 1981
