1401 My Dream-Boat

Do you know how often
You have sailed in my precious dream-boat?
My boat is not only beautiful
But also unconditional.
It will love you
Even if you do not care for it
And sail in another boat.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 15, Agni Press, 1981
