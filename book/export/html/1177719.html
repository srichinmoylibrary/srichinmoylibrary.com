4749 Quarreling With Happiness

You are quarreling
With your own happiness
When you allow your mind
To feed its doubts
And when you allow your heart
To feed its insecurities.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 48, Agni Press, 1982
