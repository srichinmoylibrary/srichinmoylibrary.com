69. They are fond of

Body is fond of
The snoring hour.
Vital is fond of
The roaring hour.
Mind is fond of
The doubting hour.
Heart is fond of
The loving hour.
Soul is fond of
The dreaming hour.
God is fond of
The promising hour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
