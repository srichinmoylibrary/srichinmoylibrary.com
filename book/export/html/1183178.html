42. You want to know

You want to know
Where I was born.
My sincere heart speaks:
I was born in the field of toil.
You want to know
Where I now live.
My sincerity speaks:
I live on prostrate soil.
You want to know
Where will be my future home.
My sincerity speaks:
My future home will be
In my earth-bound foil.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
