48. Never can I repay

Earth-support
I have already paid.
Earth has
My flowing concern.
Heaven-Smile
I have repaid.
Lo, my glowing dedication
Is in Heaven-Soul.
God-Compassion
Never can I repay,
Never

From: Sri Chinmoy, The prayer of the sky, Agni Press, 1974
