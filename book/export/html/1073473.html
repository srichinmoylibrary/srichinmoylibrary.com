13.

His heart’s gratitude-boat every day, without fail, plies between his father’s soul in Heaven and his mother’s life on earth. The beauty, purity and divinity of this boat send ecstasy-thrills through all those who know how rare is the existence of gratitude in this world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Carl Lewis: the champion inner runner, part 1, Agni Press, 1991
