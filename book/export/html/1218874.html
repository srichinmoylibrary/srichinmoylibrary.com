Chalbo Ami Bhore Sanjhe

Chalbo ami bhore sanjhe tomar charan anusari
Asim kripai dake amai mago tomar alor tari

Translation:

I shall follow Your Footsteps
In the morn and the eve.
Mother, Your Light-flooded Boat
Is beckoning me
With Compassion infinite.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ashar duar, part 2, , 2004
