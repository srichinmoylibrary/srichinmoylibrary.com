2834

God will ask you
To write the preface to His Autobiography
If you can dwell far above
  Your surface mind
And sleeplessly sing the song
  Of self-transcendence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 3, Agni Press, 1998
