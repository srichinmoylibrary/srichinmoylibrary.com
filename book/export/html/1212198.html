26300

When I prayerfully look at my Lord's Face,
He embraces my eyes.
When I prayerfully look at my Lord's Feet,
He blessingfully and proudly
Blesses my head with His Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 263, Agni Press, 1998
