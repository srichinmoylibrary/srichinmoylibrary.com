183. Don't argue

Don't argue with truth.
You will be compelled
To heave a sigh
Of an unreasonable mind.

Don't argue with love.
You will be compelled
To heave a sigh
Of an unthinkable heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
