1972

You want to know
What I am doing now.
I am sleeping
With my heart's silver dreams.
I am also doing something else:
I am inviting you
To come and enjoy with me
My unprecedented experience-ecstasy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 20, Agni Press, 1983
