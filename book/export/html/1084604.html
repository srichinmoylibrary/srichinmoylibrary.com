17. Yes, I have one

Yes, I have a soul,
But right now
It is all shattered.
Yes, I have a heart,
But right now
It is all battered.
Yes, I have a life,
But right now
It is worse than a sheep.
Yes, I have a God,
But right now
He is fast asleep.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
