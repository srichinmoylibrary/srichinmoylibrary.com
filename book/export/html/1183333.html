197. Father and daughter

From now on, Father, I shall think of You
More than You think of me.

Daughter, I am happy to hear that.
But do you want to make Me happier?

Certainly, Father, but how?

Just do not think of yourself.
I shall gladly and devotedly
Think of you on your behalf.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
