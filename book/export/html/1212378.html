26478

It is not necessary to be first
In the outer race.
It is not necessary to be first
In the inner race.
But it is absolutely necessary
To have the race-speed
To arrive at the destination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 265, Agni Press, 1998
