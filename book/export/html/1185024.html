696. The river of my heart's tears

The river of my heart's tears
Makes me feel
That this hour is nothing
But destruction-sigh.

The river of my soul's smile
Makes me feel
That indeed, this is the world of beauty,
This is the world of ecstasy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
