GREAT SINGERS

Swans sing
Before they leave the world.

Men sing
After they have left
Ignorance-sea.

Human doubt sings
Before the dawn of faith.

Human despair sings
After it has embraced
The life of Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
