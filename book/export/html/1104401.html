8 September 2000 (II)

My weightlifting is my prayer-life
To inspire my sisters and brothers
Of the entire world.

8 September 2000, 11:07 a.m. A prayerful message for television.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
