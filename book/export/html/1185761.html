124

Your life of noise must love
The Voice of God.
Your life of noise must love
The Life of God.
If not, you are bound to see
Your life unfinished,
And therefore unrecognised
Here on earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 2, Agni Press, 1983
