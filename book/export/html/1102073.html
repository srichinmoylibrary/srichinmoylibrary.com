20.

Yesterday
I got my first lesson from God
  On Divinity.

Today
I am getting my second lesson from God
  On Eternity and Infinity.

Tomorrow
I shall get my third lesson from God
  On Immortality.

The day after tomorrow
I shall get my fourth
And last lesson from God
  On Reality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Secrets revealed, Agni Press, 1973
