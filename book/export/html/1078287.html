640 What I Need...

WHAT I NEED

Imagination
I need to think of God.

Inspiration
I need to meditate on God.

Aspiration
I need to realise God.

Realisation
I need to proclaim God.

Perfection
I need to become another God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
