45.

My Lord,
Every day I sing
Your Victory-Songs
For at least four hours.
May I know from You
What You do for me?
"My child,
You do your work
And I do My Work.
I energise your mind
And inspire your heart."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 1, Agni Press, 1998
