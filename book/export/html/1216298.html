665. I know not why

To be exact,
God loves you more than
He loves me.

To be accurate,
God has all eternity for you
But
For me He does not have
Even a minute to spare.

To be precise,
God is always and ever
Partial to you,
Yet I love God.
Why?
I know not why,
And never shall I know.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
