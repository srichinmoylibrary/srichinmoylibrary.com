X-Ray

The medical science has no other choice
than to cherish
a limited and imperfect x-ray machine.
Three cheers for the Yogic science,
for it has discovered God,
the unlimited and perfect x-ray machine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Supreme and his four children: five spiritual dictionaries, Agni Press, 1973
