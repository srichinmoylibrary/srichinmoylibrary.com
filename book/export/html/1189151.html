3481

Every day I am watching
A most soulful and powerful game.
It is a game that is played
By my heart's consciousness-flames
And my Lord's Consciousness-Sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 35, Agni Press, 1984
