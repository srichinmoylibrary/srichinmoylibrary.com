267. His days slip by

All his days slip by
In lethargy-black.
All his days slip by
In incapacity-brown.
All his promise-joy slips by
In oblivion-swoon.
Yet he cherishes the expectancy
Of God's arrival.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
