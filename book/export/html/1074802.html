236. Man, God and angel

I am man;
   I need forgiveness.
        God has it for me.

I am God;
   I need indifference.
        Man has it for me.

I am man;
   I need beauty.
        An angel has it for me.

I am an angel;
   I need duty.
        Man has it for me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 5, Agni Press, 1973
