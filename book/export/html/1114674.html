1274

My mind wants God
  Unmistakably
To be proud of my mind.
My heart wants God
  Throbbingly
To be fond of my heart.
My life wants God
  Desperately
To be the only Ruler of my life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 2, Agni Press, 1998
