April 17

How to conquer pride? Early in the morning think a few times of the ones who are infinitely superior to you in Love and Power. In the evening try to feel that you are sleeping in the lap of the Supreme's Supreme Love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
