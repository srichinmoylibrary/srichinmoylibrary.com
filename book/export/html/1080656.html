5.

There are many forms of goodness in the world and when one finds oneself in the presence of one, it is the highest form of spirituality to recognise it and enjoy it.

Mrs. H. R.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Fourteen American mothers and fourteen American daughters with Sri Chinmoy, Agni Press, 1975
