16094

The mind's mantra is:
"I can speak on God-subject
Twenty-four hours a day."

The heart's mantra is:
"My Lord,
Speak and speak!
Do speak to me
Twenty-four hours a day.
I will just listen,
Seated at Your Feet."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 161, Agni Press, 1991
