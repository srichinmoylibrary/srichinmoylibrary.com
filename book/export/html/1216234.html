602. Integrity

You struggle and struggle
For integrity.
Yet
You have no integrity,
You say.
I tell you,
You don't have to cry
For integrity!
Integrity will run
To embrace you
If you just request
Your heart-friend
To look after it.
Just speak to your heart-friend.
Lo, all integrity is yours.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
