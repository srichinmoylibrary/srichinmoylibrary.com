16051

I have been on the pinnacle-peak
Of success.
Alas! Happiness was not
To be found there.
Now I am on the solid ground
Of progress,
And I am all delight,
All fulfilment.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 161, Agni Press, 1991
