298. Counsel

Smiling counsel
Cuts off one foe.
Friendly counsel
Cuts off ten foes.
Loving counsel
Cuts off ninety foes.
Sacrificing counsel
Cuts off three hundred foes.
Oneness counsel
Cuts off all foes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 6, Agni Press, 1973
