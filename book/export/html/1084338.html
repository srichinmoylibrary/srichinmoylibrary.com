6. When I think

When I think of myself
I know
What an invading pleasure is.
When I think of God
I know
What a perishing pleasure is.
When I think not,
I know not
Where pleasure-treasure is,
And
When I judge not
I know
Where perfection-satisfaction is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 8, Agni Press, 1974
