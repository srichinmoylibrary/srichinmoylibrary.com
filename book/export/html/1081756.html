197. Why are you wedded to worry?

Why are you
Wedded to worry?
He who ranks above
Your life-plan
Is bound to protect you.
Don't you know that
This is your God's only business?
Don't worry.
Let me see what more
I can do for you.
Ah, I have a splendid idea.
On behalf of God
I shall liberate you,
If you want me to,
And
If such is the Will of God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
