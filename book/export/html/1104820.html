23. I am flexible

O earth, I am flexible.
I can be what you want me to be.
Just tell me what I should do.
O Heaven, I am alive.
I am eagerly waiting
To hear from you
How to please you
In your own way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My promise to God, Agni Press, 1975
