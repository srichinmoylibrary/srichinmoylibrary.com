19 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must either destroy
Or give a determined chase to
All the ruthless and shameless doubts
That attack my poor mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 August 1999, 9:05 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. thirty-one times with one arm. (13 right, 18 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
