522

In his outer life
He is happy because he keeps pace
With his mind's inspiration-rabbit.
In his inner life
He is happy because he keeps pace
With his heart's aspiration-bird.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 6, Agni Press, 1983
