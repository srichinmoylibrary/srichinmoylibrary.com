The affectionate dog

X, Y and I went to a store. There was a very nice dog in the store, but it didn't pay any attention to either X or me. It immediately went over to Y and started licking his feet very affectionately.

-18 September 1987

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
