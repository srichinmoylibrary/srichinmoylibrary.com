289. Two experts

The difference
Between
God and me is this:
He is an expert in hiding Himself
And
I am an expert in exposing myself.
Since each of us has
A good quality worth learning,
We are both equal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
