I am my life's God-hunger-heart, part 3

1

I am
My life's
God-hunger-heart.

2

My soul's highest promise
And my Lord's deepest Satisfaction
Are two immortal travellers,
Walking side by side
Along Eternity's Road.

3

A perfect Truth-seeker and God-lover
Is not Heaven-born.
He is earth-born
From earth's sleepless and breathless
Tears and cries.

4

Each and every God-thought
In my mind
Comes from the peace-fragrance
Of my heart.

5

Ignorance-night invades us
Only when our hearts
Are empty of devotion.

6

Aspiration
Is how I breathlessly feel
And not
What I ceaselessly talk about.

7

My mind's fog and clouds
Cannot hide
The sunlit path of my heart.

8

My aspiration-heart dearly loves me
Because I love God only.
My God-realisation-soul proudly loves me
Because God is my All.

9

Today my mind's desire-night
Is over.
My heart's aspiration-dawn
Is blossoming.

10

God cannot help feeling shocked
When He hears from me
That I am an utter failure.

11

Peace-seed
Is my heart's inner name.
Bliss-harvest
Is my life's outer name.

12

Each and every pure thought of mine
Is a direct and perfect representative
Of God's purest Dream
Here on earth.

13

My Lord, I do so many things for You.
How many things do You do
For me?
"My child, since I am an old man,
I do only one thing for you.
Every day, for twenty-four hours,
I cradle you inside My Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Heart."

14

Our oneness-inspired actions
Are the only actions
That God treasures.

15

May the beauty, purity and fragrance
Of my heart
Fly beyond time and space.

16

During the day I amuse my Lord
With my life-stories.
At night I feed my Lord
With my soul-songs.

17

God will tell the whole world
That I am a supremely choice instrument
Of His
Only when my eyes are filled
With the beauty of silence
And my heart
With the divinity of peace.

18

I can clearly see that each time
I shake hands with desire,
The beauty of my aspiration-life
Is immediately marred.

19

My aspiring heart is immortalised
By God Himself
Just because I am no longer
Victimised by my doubting mind.

20

I was born of hope-seed
To fly
In Infinity's Promise-Sky.

21

The farther I move away
From my own expectation-prison,
The closer my Lord Beloved Supreme
Comes to me
With His immortality's Joy, Love
And Pride.

22

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
When You bless me with Your Heart's
Compassion-Ocean,
What can I give You in return
To make You happy?
"My child, only a gratitude-drop
From your heart-cup."

23

My Lord,
Is there any single piece of advice
You can give me
That will immediately illumine me
And satisfy me completely?
"Yes, My child, I have one.
At every moment listen
To your dreaming heart-whispers
And never listen
To your roaming mind-roar."

24

I pray to sit
At God's Compassion-Feet.
I meditate to see
God's Illumination-Heart.

25

Humanity's most deplorable audacity
Is its declaration:
"No oneness-heart-exercise!"

26

Since you know that your mind
Is conscience-guilty,
Why do you hesitate
To correct your mind?

27

As the desire-mind-attack is severe,
Even so is
The aspiration-starvation-attack.

28

May my soul's rainbow-beauty
Brighten my heart-sky
Every day.

29

Mine is the heart
That feeds on gratitude-blossoms.
Mine is the life
That feeds on surrender-blooms.

30

The mind that thirsts
For the Newness-God-Beauty
I have.
The heart that hungers
For the Oneness-God-Fragrance
I am.

31

My desire-mind wants to bind
God's powerful Hands.
My aspiration-heart wishes to touch
God's beautiful Feet.

32

O my mind,
Stop broadcasting your ego-balloon-news!
God wants to hear
My heart's sweetness-oneness-flute-melody.

33

God Himself has brought me
To the path of spirituality.
Therefore, it is my bounden duty
To love Him, please Him and fulfil Him
Only in His own Way.

34

Each time I dive deep within,
My Lord Supreme blesses me
With the paradise-fragrance
Of the ever-transcending Beyond.

35

Each man
Is a helpless victim-slave
To his own self-importance.

36

Today I am a supreme enjoyer
Of two realities:
My fast-decaying doubt-mind
And my fast-blossoming faith-heart.

37

Your progress-delight
Is guaranteed
The moment your heart can sing
A prayerful and tearful God-song.

38

Beauty incarnate
Is a God-lover.
Delight incarnate
Is a God-server.

39

A twenty-four-hour God-lover
Can never have a vulnerable point:
So says God.

40

My mind is happy because
My mind is choosing God's Victory
Over my mind's victory.

My heart is happy because
My heart is choosing God's Needs
Over my heart's needs.

41

Desire-mind-tragedy
I was.
Aspiration-heart-melody
I am.

42

A stubborn unwillingness-mind
I was.
A docile willingness-heart
I am.

43

Spirituality without purity
Is like
A boat without the boatman.

44

Only a peace-bird-heart
Can fly
In God's own Rainbow-Sky.

45

Only a peace-bird-heart
Can dance
To God's universal Oneness-Melody.

46

Eagerness-sincerity
And aspiration-intensity
Always love to be
In each other's company.

47

Forgiveness
And
Happiness
Are twins.

48

Choose we must!
Desire's destruction-dance
Or
Aspiration's liberation-song.

49

God Himself nurtures
My heart's fondest hopes
And
My life's bravest promises.

50

No one told me;
I just know that God's Eye
And His Compassion-Heart
Will help me hold
His flaming universal Peace-Torch.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I am my life's God-hunger-heart, part 3, Agni Press, 1994
