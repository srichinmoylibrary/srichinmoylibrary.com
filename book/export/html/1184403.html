75. Reputation

Reputation is a great hindrance.
My disproportionate success-life
Knows it.

Reputation is not at all a hindrance.
My timely, proportionate progress-life
Knows it and will always remember it.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
