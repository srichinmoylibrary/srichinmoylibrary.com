25 December 2004 (I)

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

You have given my soul the capacity
To thrive on inner challenges.
You have given my eyes the capacity
To thrive on outer challenges.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

/25 December 2004, 4:03 a.m. Qingdao, China. Non-lifting day.

Songs:
1. Khule dao ankhi khule dao hiya

Sri Chinmoy played The Invocation on his synthesizer and read aloud in Sanskrit from the first chapter of the Bhagavad Gita.

2. Ogo bholanath ogo mahadev

The Eternal Beginner (Blue violin): Akarane prabhu chitte tomar
