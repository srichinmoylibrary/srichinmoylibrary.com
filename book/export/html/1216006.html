379. Without knowing why

Sometimes
We say something
Without knowing why.

Sometimes
We do something
Without knowing why.

Sometimes
We become something
Without knowing why.

But
We never love Truth
Without knowing why.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 8, Agni Press, 1974
