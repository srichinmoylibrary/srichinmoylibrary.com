6420

In vain
He tries and cries
And cries and tries
To be touched
By the sunset-smile of peace.
Why does he fail?
He fails because
He deliberately ignores
The heart's sunrise-cries.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 65, Agni Press, 1984
