40. Bliss

Sweet is bliss
When I see the Face of my Beloved
The way I want to see it.
Sweeter is bliss
When I feel the Heart of my Beloved
Supreme
The way I want to feel it.
Sweetest is bliss
When I see His Face
And
Feel His Heart
The way my Beloved Supreme
Wants me to.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Dedication-drops, Agni Press, 1976
