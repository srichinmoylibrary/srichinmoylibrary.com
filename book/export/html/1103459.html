17 May 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Encouragement-Heart
Is the only fulness
In my dedication-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 May 1999, 7:47 a.m.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 5, Agni Press, 1999
