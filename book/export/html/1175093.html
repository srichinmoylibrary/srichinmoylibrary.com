2149 Your Love

Lord,
Your Love
Has awakened my body.
Your Love
Has disciplined my vital.
Your Love
Has illumined my mind.
Your Love
Has liberated my heart.
Your Love
Has bound my soul.
Your Love
Has selected my life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 22, Agni Press, 1981
