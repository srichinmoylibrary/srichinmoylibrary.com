Kebal Pather Parichoy Apan Keha Noy

Kebal pather parichoy
Apan keha noy
Jari tumi karu bhoy
Se kareni maron joy
Miche amar amar bale je koy
Se to kebal andhakare roy
Hari bina apan keha noy
Apan shudhu hari dayamoy

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 6, , 1978
