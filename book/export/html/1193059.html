7350

You want me to help you
In God-realisation?
Certainly I can!
But before that,
You have to tell me
That you will have nothing to do
With your mind's confusion-circles.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 74, Agni Press, 1984
