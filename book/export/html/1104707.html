19.

My earth-responsibilities
Are my Heaven-sent
Opportunities
To bring to the fore
My divinity's prosperity
And offer it
To the heart of humanity
For the manifestation
Of God's Peace,
God's Bliss
And God's Satisfaction
On earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My perfection-promise to God, Agni Press, 1993
