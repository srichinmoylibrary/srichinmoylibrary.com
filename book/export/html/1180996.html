7994 One Life, One Message

For a genuine truth-seeker
The outer life and the inner life
Are one,
And that one life
Has only one message:
God comes first,
Spirituality comes first.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 80, Agni Press, 1983
