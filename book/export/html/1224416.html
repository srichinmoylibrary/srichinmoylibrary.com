Sabai Amar Bhai

Sabai amar bhai
Mukti giti dibas rate
Amra sabai gai
Sabai amar mita
Amra nachi alor gane
Hase bishwa pita

Translation:

Everyone is my brother.
We sing the song of liberation together
All day and night.
All are my friends.
We dance the dance of light.
The Lord Supreme of the creation vast
Smiles at us.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 4, , 1981
