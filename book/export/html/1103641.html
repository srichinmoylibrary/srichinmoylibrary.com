10 August 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I pray soulfully,
You feed me.
When I meditate self-givingly,
You ask me to feed You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 August 1999, 8:22 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 120 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
