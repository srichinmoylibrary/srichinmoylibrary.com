1.

Brother Jesus,
Where are you?
"Why?
Am I not on earth?"
No, you are not.

Brother Jesus,
Where are you?
"Why?
Am I not in Heaven?"
No, you are not.
"Where do you think
Then, I am?"
I do not know.

"Brother C,
The beauty of
Your self-chosen ignorance-eye
I am deeply enjoying.
But mind you,
I am not going to treasure it.
Never!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Brother Jesus, Agni Press, 1975
