Bill Pearl’s receptivity

One does not have to become a disciple to identify himself with me. Bill Pearl is the proof. On the strength of his oneness with me, my inner creativity he sees and feels. When we say that there are people on earth who are not my disciples but who are extremely receptive, here is the proof.

— 18 June 1986

From: Sri Chinmoy, My weightlifting tears and smiles, part 1, Agni Press, 1986
