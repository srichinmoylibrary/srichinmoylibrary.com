45. Preach and practise

In the inner world
My Lord preaches the necessity
Of sound-life
And I practise it
In the outer world.
In the outer world
I preach the necessity
Of perfection-life
And my Lord practises it
In the inner world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sound becomes, silence is, Agni Press, 1975
