140. The liberal

Liberal was he then
   In constant advice.
Liberal is he now
   In continuous promise.
Liberal will he be
   In ceaseless self-giving.
        His advice: Be divine.
        His promise: You I shall make divine.
        His self-giving: Lo, you are divine!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 3, Agni Press, 1973
