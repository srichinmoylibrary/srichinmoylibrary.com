At Last I Know

At last I know my name.
My name is God’s eternal Game.
     At last I know my name.
     At last I know my age.
My age is Infinity’s page.
     At last I know my age.
     At last I know my home.
My home is where my flame-worlds roam.
     At last I know my home.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Chandelier, part 1, , 1980
