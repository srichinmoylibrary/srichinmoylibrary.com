Flattery pays off

In another store I wanted to buy a pillow case. The lady said 65 and I said 40.

Then I flattered her and said, "You speak such good English!"

The lady was so happy that she gave me the pillow case for 40.

-13 January 1987

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
