760. Homesick

My soul is homesick.
It wants to go back
To its Fulfilment-Home.

My heart is homesick.
It wants to go back
To its Oneness-Home.

My mind is homesick.
It wants to go back
To its Vastness-Home.

My vital is homesick.
It wants to go back
To its Dynamism-Home.

My body is homesick.
It wants to go back
To its Ignorance-Home.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 16, Agni Press, 1973
