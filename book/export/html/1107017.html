Tagore appreciates

Our history teacher,
The late Sisir Kumar Mitra,
On being asked,
Told me that Tagore himself
Had told him at Shanti Niketan:
"Nolini Kanta's contribution
To Bengali literature
Is unique."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Nolini: Sri Aurobindo's unparalleled friend-son-disciple, Agni Press, 1984
