608. A diamond universe

Science-prince had
A silver universe.
His mind used it and used it.

He has now
A golden universe.
His heart is now trying and trying to use it.

He soon shall have
A diamond universe.
Once achieved,
His soul will use it
Smilingly,
Lovingly
And
Unreservedly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
