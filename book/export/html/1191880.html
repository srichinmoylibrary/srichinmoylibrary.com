6183

You are telling me
That you have nothing to do.
I am telling you
That you have not yet done
At least this particular thing:
You have not built a celestial home
For your Inner Pilot
Deep inside your heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 62, Agni Press, 1984
