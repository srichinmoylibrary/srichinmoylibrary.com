10 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
To realise You,
What I need is inner wealth:
Aspiration, and nothing else.
The outer wealth
Is infinitely worse than useless.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

10 July 1999, 8:35 a.m. Before lifting 350 lbs. fifty-four times with one arm. (27 right, 27 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
