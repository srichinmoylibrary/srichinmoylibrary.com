My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 26

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Yogyakarta, Indonesia in December 2003.

17 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

1 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That He treasures
My happy heart’s
Gratitude-blossoms.

2 A Child-Heart Is Always Eager ...

A child-heart is always eager
To play life’s
Transformation-satisfaction-game.

3 My Intense Prayers ...

My intense prayers
Soar high,
Very high.

4 My Profound Meditations ...

My profound meditations
Roar,
Triumphantly roar.

5 Each Sincere Prayer ...

Each sincere prayer
Is a newness-smile
Of my life.

6 Each Deep Meditation ...

Each deep meditation
Is a soulfulness-song
Of my heart.

7 I Dearly Love ...

I dearly love
My God-worship.

8 God Proudly Loves ...

God proudly loves
My God-partnership
And God-friendship.

9 Each Good Thought ...

Each good thought
Of mine
Is a rainbow-beauty.

10 Each Dedicated Service ...

Each dedicated service
Of mine
Is my God-satisfying duty.

11 Gratitude ...

Gratitude
Never takes anything
For granted,
But ingratitude does.

12 Love ...

Love
Is never afraid
Of fear.

13 Fear ...

Fear
Is always afraid
Of love.

14 Every Day I Want My Life ...

Every day I want my life
To be a most soulful
God-surrender-song.

15 Peace And Love ...

Peace and love
Shelter each other
And fulfil each other.

16 Sweetness ...

Sweetness
Is the most powerful strength
Of the heart.

17 When I Look For Silence, ...

When I look for silence,
I find silence
Inside my soul.

18 When I Look For My Soul, ...

When I look for my soul,
I find my soul
Inside my silence.

18 December 2003 ...

18 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

19 Each God-Seeker ...

Each God-seeker
Is a special breath
Of God’s Heart.

20 Each God-Seeker ...

Each God-seeker
Is a special flower-beauty
And fragrance
In God’s Heart-Garden.

21 Each God-Seeker ...

Each God-seeker
Is a special player
In God’s Cosmic Drama.

22 Each God-Seeker ...

Each God-seeker
Is chosen by God Himself
To be a song of His Heart
For the perfection of humankind.

23 The Spiritual Life ...

The spiritual life
Is an endless
God-discovery-adventure.

24 I Am Now Going Back ...

I am now going back
To my childhood’s
Bliss-flooded mornings.

25 God Lovingly And Proudly Preserves ...

God lovingly and proudly preserves
All my heart-songs
In His own Heart-Safe.

26 My Love Of God ...

My love of God
Is building a new hope-temple
And making a new promise-shrine.

27 My Desire-Life Has Received ...

My desire-life has received
A very short-lived Blessing
From God.

28 My Aspiration-Heart Has Received ...

My aspiration-heart has received
An everlasting Blessing
From God.

29 Hope ...

Hope
Knows no fear.

30 Hope Dares To Blossom ...

Hope dares to blossom
Even inside the abysmal abyss.

31 A Seeker’s Hope-Heart ...

A seeker’s hope-heart
Gladdens God’s Heart
Most significantly.

32 Hope Secretly Feeds ...

Hope secretly feeds
And strengthens
Promise.

33 God The Supreme Artist ...

God the Supreme Artist
Has given me another chance
To make my face, eyes and heart
Exceedingly beautiful.

34 To Avoid God ...

To avoid God,
Either consciously
Or even unconsciously,
Is to enter into the chasm
Of nothingness.

35 The Mind Becomes Old ...

The mind becomes old
And withered,
But the heart can remain young
Forever.

36 God Wants My Love For Him ...

God wants my love for Him
Lovingly, soulfully and self-givingly
To encircle the globe.

19 December 2003 ...

19 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

37 A Child-Heart Knows Not ...

A child-heart knows not
How to doubt
God’s Affection.

38 No Matter How Undivine We Are ...

No matter how undivine we are,
God’s Compassion-Funds
Never run low.

39 If You Want To Be ...

If you want to be
Close to God,
Then eagerly learn
As many things as possible
About God.

40 Each Thought ...

Each thought
Is the creator
Of a new experience.

41 When A Seeker Fails ...

When a seeker fails,
God takes the full blame.

42 Alas ...

Alas,
In vain God expects
More love and more gratitude
From mankind.

43 True ...

True,
My life-boat is slow,
But it is extremely reliable.

44 My Mind-Library Houses ...

My mind-library houses
Neither God-written books
Nor books written about God.

45 Wisdom And Age ...

Wisdom and age
Do not depend on each other.

46 The Difference Between Infant Seekers ...

The difference between infant seekers
And adult seekers:
Infant seekers love God
Only from the heart;
Adult seekers love God
This moment from the heart,
Next moment from the mind
And the following moment
From the physical consciousness.

47 My Life Must Become ...

My life must become
The beauty
Of my soul’s smile.

48 My God-Aspiration ...

My God-aspiration,
God-dedication and God-absorption
Have to be sleepless.

49 I Light My God-Heart-Candle ...

I light my God-heart-candle
With my devotion-breath.

50 Every Day ...

Every day
My heart collects Compassion
And Forgiveness-Flowers
From my Lord’s Heart-Garden.

51 When I Measure God’s Blessings ...

When I measure God’s Blessings
With my mind,
I only add more miseries
To my life.

52 When I Measure God’s Blessings ...

When I measure God’s Blessings
With my heart,
My life is inundated
With bliss.

20 December 2003 ...

20 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

53 God-Dreamers ...

God-dreamers
Will eventually be
God’s Victory-announcers.

54 God-Dreamers ...

God-dreamers
Know no obstacles.

55 I Am Not Afraid Of Storms ...

I am not afraid of storms,
For God
Is my life’s Boatman.

56 I Must Bloom ...

I must bloom
Where my Lord plants me,
And nowhere else.

57 When I Am A Member ...

When I am a member
Of the outer world,
I myself
Make all my decisions.

58 When I Am A Member ...

When I am a member
Of the inner world,
God not only makes
All my decisions,
But also gladly and proudly
Executes them.

59 God Does Not Want Me ...

God does not want me
To be
An invisible life.

60 God Wants Me ...

God wants me
To be
An indispensable heart.

61 Courage Is Needed ...

Courage is needed
For the outer victory.

62 Unconditional Surrender ...

Unconditional surrender
To God’s Will
Is needed
For the inner victory.

63 Everybody Has ...

Everybody has
An absolute secret.
Mine is an open secret:
I know the Will
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

64 Always The Inner Cries ...

Always the inner cries
And the inner tears
Have to be kept
In good health.

65 Sound ...

Sound
Is extremely eager
To be heard.

66 Silence ...

Silence
Is majestically eager
To raise the consciousness
Of the world.

67 I Have Come To Learn ...

I have come to learn
At long last
That there is nothing as fragile
As my self-confidence.

68 Alas ...

Alas,
Humanity is blindly speeding
Towards dire uncertainty.

69 I Have Heard, I Have Heard ...

I have heard, I have heard
The clarion call
Of my Transcendental Self —
I have heard, I have heard.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 26, Agni Press, 2003
