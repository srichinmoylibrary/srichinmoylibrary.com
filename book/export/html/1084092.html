15. They know not

Reason and passion
Know not how to converse.
Therefore,
Each other they hate.

Reason and faith
Know not how to love.
Therefore,
Each other they hate.

Reason and doubt
Know not how to look.
Therefore,
Each other they hate.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 3, Agni Press, 1974
