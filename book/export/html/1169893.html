538. Soul Of Endless Beauty

O soul of endless beauty!
Do teach me the song:
Unity versus multiplicity.
You are the only one
Who knows the song.
Therefore all souls
To you must throng.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
