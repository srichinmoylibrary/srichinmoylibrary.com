Taba Pada Renu Hiyar Kusume

Taba pada renu hiyar kusume
Sajabo param jatane
Tumi prananath anadi tiyasa
Amar jibane marane

Translation:

With the dust of Your Feet
I shall carefully and soulfully
Decorate my flower-heart.
You are the Lord of my life,
You are my eternal thirst
In my life and in my death.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 12b, , 1981
