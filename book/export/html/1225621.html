Asim Amai Base Bhalo Sasim Base Bhalo

Asim amai base bhalo
Sasim base bhalo
Karo prane nahi heri
Kabhu kurup kalo

Translation:

The infinite loves me.
The finite loves me.
In no heart do I ever see
Sordid darkness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 11b, , 1981
