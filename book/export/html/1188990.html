3321

God has no pride;
Therefore
He can enter into your heart
Uninvited.
But once He has entered
Into your heart,
Will you be ready
To offer Him His due Throne?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 34, Agni Press, 1984
