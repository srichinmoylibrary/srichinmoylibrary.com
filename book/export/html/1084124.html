47. Love-flower, ingratitude-soul, depression-life

All things eventually fade
Save and except
A love-flower.

All things ultimately live
Save and except
An ingratitude-soul.

All things ultimately win
Save and except
A depression-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 3, Agni Press, 1974
