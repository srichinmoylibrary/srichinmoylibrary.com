Beauty Is Beauty

Beauty is beauty
Only when it is sanctioned
And guaranteed
By God’s Eye.
Purity is purity
Only when it is sanctioned
And guaranteed
By God’s Heart.
Divinity is divinity
Only when it is sanctioned
And guaranteed
By God’s Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flower-flames, part 2, , 1988
