256. Four sisters

Four harmonious sisters:
Purity and luminosity,
Beauty and duty.

Great is purity's breath.
Tall is luminosity's heart.
Inspiring is beauty's soul.
Aspiring is duty's role.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
