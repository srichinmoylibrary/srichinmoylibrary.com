622. The Hardest Miracle

This is the hardest miracle
That I have ever seen:
Man talks of God
Without knowing Him,
Man meditates on God
Without seeing Him,
Man prays to God
Without loving Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
