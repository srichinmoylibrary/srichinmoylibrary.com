349. The purpose of my existence

God, my heart
Was born to love.
Do You believe it?
"Son, I do, I certainly do."

God, my body
Was born to serve.
Do You believe it?
"Son, I do, I certainly do.
I also believe something
More:
Today you love,
Today you serve;
Tomorrow you will guide,
Tomorrow you will lead."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
