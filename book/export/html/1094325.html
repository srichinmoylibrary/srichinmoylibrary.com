10 God Tells Me ...

God tells me,
“My child,
I am eager to make you
An exact prototype of Myself.
Are you ready?”
My Lord,
I am ready.
I am at Your express Command.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 9, Agni Press, 2002
