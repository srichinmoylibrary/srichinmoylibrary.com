Satya Mor Dak Diyechhe

Satya mor dak diyechhe mithya deshe ese
Chalechhi ami sakashe tar madhur hasi hese
Satya kahe samoi hale sabare ami daki
Bishal mor pran gabhire sabare bendhi rakhi

Translation:

Truth has come
Through falsehood-world
And now truth is calling me.
I am appearing before truth
With my heart’s soulful smile.
Truth tells me:
“When the time comes,
I arrive at the right person.
I bind each and every one
With my freedom-light.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, Pole-star promise-light, part 3, , 1977
