Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 10

901 The Real In Me

Purity awakens the real in me:
Oneness.
Humility strengthens the real in me:
Perfection.
Gratitude enlightens the real in me:
Satisfaction.

902 The Heart-Land

Now that I have
Become an explorer,
I shall explore
A new land:
The heart-land.

903 Your Right Of Self-Encouragement

My Lord Supreme,
I need self-newness.
How can I have it?
"My child,
You can have self-newness
Only by exercising your right
Of self-encouragement."

904 The Defeat Of My Life

There is a defeat
That is more glorious
Than victory,
And that defeat
Is the defeat
Of my entire life
By Truth.

905 Since You Do Not Play

Since you do not play
The foul game
Of blind negatives,
Yours will be the Consciousness-delight,
Here, there and
All-where.

906 Something Exists Above

Something exists above
Our usual desire-life,
And that something
Is our aspiration-life.

Something exists far above
Our rare aspiration-life,
And that something
Is our realisation-goal.

907 What I Shall Have

Today what I have
Is a tiny aspiration-seed.
Tomorrow what I shall have
Is a powerfully developed realisation-blossom.

908 My Aspiration-Heart Tells Me

My confusion-mind tells me
That life is nothing
But a dreadful nightmare.
My aspiration-heart tells me
That life can be
A lovely dream.
Why not let me give a chance
To my self-giving heart?

909 My Soul Has Already Become

My mind does not know
What it must know.
My heart knows
What it must know:
The beauty of gratitude-flames.
My soul not only knows
What it must know,
But it has already become
Satisfaction-sun.

910 A God-Smile

As a vigorous shake
Can awaken a sleeping man,
Even so, a God-Smile
Can inspire an unaspiring seeker.

911 The Happy Result

Unreservedly strengthen the part of you
That wants God's Love
Above everything else,
And soulfully see
The happy result.

912 My Supreme Realisation

Darkness is light:
This is my human realisation.

Light is for darkness:
This is my divine realisation.

Darkness is for light:
This is my supreme realisation.

913 Helpless And Senseless

Lord, is it because
I am helpless
That I am senseless?
Or is it because
I am senseless
That I am helpless?
"No, no, you are helpless because
You do not cry,
Cry for Light.
You are senseless because
You do not need Light."

914 God Sees Me Differently

God sees me
In a totally different way
Than the way I see myself.
He sees me
As another God.

915 Because God Loves Me More

Precisely because
God loves me infinitely more
Than I love myself,
God cannot afford to be
As careless with my life
As I am.

916 If We Can Raise Ourselves

On earth
If we can raise ourselves,
In Heaven
We shall rise
With the cosmic gods.

917 Man Loves His Confusion-Fog

Alas, man loves
His confusion-fog
More than he loves
His sure capacity,
Which can easily illumine
His confusion-fog.

918 Your Foolish Mind

No wonder you have failed
In the battlefield of life!
You have failed because
Your foolish mind
Is full of frail opinions.

919 Pains Follow Us

Pains, physical or otherwise,
Follow us into tomorrow
Because we do not cancel them today
With our soul-power.

920 You Must Listen

My Lord says to me:
"My child, since you cannot see
The things I can see,
You must needs listen to Me,
Although you fail to understand."

921 Wisdom-Light Dawned On Me

Wisdom-light dawned on me
The day I recommended the Truth
To my own little
Receptivity-self.

922 The Answer

Wait and see.
The temporary emptiness
Of not discovering
The answers of life
Cannot last,
For the Answer itself
Is blossoming
Petal by petal
For you.

923 Self-Glory

Self-glory:
What is it
If not deceitful?

Self-discovery:
What is it
If not fruitful?

924 If You Have The Courage

If you have the sincere courage
To declare that you are
Totally lost,
Then God has the unreserved Compassion
To show you the way
To the Satisfaction-Goal.

925 When You Are Liberated

When you are liberated
From the attraction-vital
And the repulsion-mind,
You will be able to enjoy
A perfection-life
And
A satisfaction-heart.

926 Mine Are The Eyes

Self-exploitation,
Mine are the eyes
That dislike you.

Self-deception,
Mine is the heart
That hates you.

927 Let Me Follow My Heart

O world,
I know not why
My heart does not want
To go the way you want to go.
Let me at least
Follow my heart
For a while.

928 When I Pray To The False God

When I pray
To the false god,
My praying is not enough.
I have to run towards him.
When I pray
To the real God,
He immediately runs towards me.

929 If You Long For Peace

In your outer life
If you long for peace,
Then in your inner life
You must become energy-action.

930 Beyond The Inner Barriers

Are you aware
Of your inner barriers?
If so, fear not,
For your awareness itself
Will carry you beyond
All the inner barriers.

931 If God And Truth Communicate

If God and Truth can communicate
In perfect silence,
Then what is wrong with me?
I must try and succeed
In joining them.

932 Counterfeit Victory

You must decide
Whether you want
An uncompromising victory over falsehood
Or the counterfeit version of victory:
Truce.

933 Remain Unseen

Remain unseen,
Remain unapplauded.
Faster than the fastest
You alone, and nobody else,
Will be able to run.

934 Two Enemies Have Left Me

You are asking me,
Why am I so happy?
I am happy because
At long last
My two unparalleled enemies,
Submission-life and domination-vital,
Have left me.

935 I Am Not Needed

I am not needed
To say the right thing.
I am only required
To be the right thing.

936 You Will Hurt Yourself

I tell you,
You will hit and hurt yourself
If you fail to learn
How to bend.

937 Divinity Fails To Hide

Divinity fails to hide
When generosity shines in the heart,
Sincerity shines in the mind
And
Purity shines in the vital.

938 Truth Is Paralyzing

Truth is paralyzing
Your insincerity-smile.
You are paralyzing
The perfection-beauty of Truth.

939 Old God, New God

The old God
Taught me how to fight
Against ignorance-night.

The new God
Is teaching me how to surrender
To His Vision-Light.

940 Those Who Are Always Late

Those who are always late
Will never have
Teeming aspiration-flames
To win the satisfaction-smile
Of the inner sun.

941 Each Heart Is The Beauty

Each heart is the beauty
Of a God-dreamer.
Each life is the delight
Of a God-lover.

942 Three Tear-Drops

The doubt-beast left me
The moment God discovered
At His Feet
Three tear-drops
Of my heart.

943 Special Attention

Because I receive
Special attention from You,
My Lord,
Everybody is jealous of me.
Because You bless me,
My Lord,
With Your special Love,
Everybody shuns my company.
"My child, that is fine with Me.
I hope that is fine with you, too."

944 An Excellent Boss

Have you ever worked with God?
He is an excellent Boss.
He has taught me the secret
Of creating freedom-delight.

945 Mine Is The Vision-Eye

Mine is the vision-eye
That unlocks the door
Leading to God-satisfaction in man
And
Man-perfection in God.

946 The Habit Of Trusting Yourself

You are in the habit
Of trusting yourself,
But I wish to tell you
To trust the real nature in you,
Which is God-Nature,
And not your usual self,
Which is ignorance-hunger.

947 Real Confidence

False confidence we get
From blind imitation.
Real confidence we get
From self-cultivation,
Self-perfection,
God-Compassion
And
God-Satisfaction.

948 A Great Encouragement

Always it is a great encouragement
To feel and realise
That the ultimate Truth
Can never, never tolerate
Human deception-night.

949 I Shall Depend

I do not depend
And will never depend
On the supposed and borrowed courage
Of others.
I shall depend only on my
Certainty-soul
And
Compassion-Immortality-God.

950 I Shall Relax

I shall relax,
For relaxation is the only way
To watch my mind flow
With its plenitude-resources.
I shall relax, and I shall always relax.

951 I Shall Not Hide From Truth

I shall not hide from Truth,
For I know Truth loves me.
Truth loves me
Only to perfect me.
Truth loves me
Only to fulfil the Real in me,
And the Real in me is God-Vision,
And nothing else.

952 Prison Of Misunderstanding

Each human being is doomed to stay
In a prison of misunderstanding.
Alas, when will the day dawn
When misunderstanding will be replaced
By heart-understanding
And soul-fulfilling
Summit-oneness?

953 Your Goal

There are many things on the road
That fight and frighten you,
But remember,
Those things are all totally false.
Ignore them mercilessly
And
March forward!
Fly upward and dive inward
Speedily and sleeplessly.
Your Goal: The Truth Ultimate.

954 When I Understand You And Him

When I understand you and him,
I understand the entire universe.
When I understand the entire universe,
I become a conscious, sleepless
And self-giving representative
Of my Beloved Supreme.

955 An Inner Task

I know, I know
Why I have been given an inner task
To perform.
An inner task I have been given
Because it is more than possible
For me to succeed and proceed:
Succeed to please myself
And
Proceed to please
My Eternity's Lord Supreme.

956 Today And Tomorrow

What you are today
May be a concealed frustration,
But tomorrow you will definitely be
A revealed satisfaction.

957 Whom Shall My Soul Believe?

Whom shall my soul believe?
Not him who tells the world
That I am useless,
Not him who tells the world
That I can try to aspire,
But him who tells the world
That I am not only aspiring
But succeeding.

958 The Flower Of A Reality

The fragrance of a dream
Has a short-lived breath.
The flower of a reality
Forever lasts.

959 The Science Of Poison-Doubt

The science of poison-doubt
Has at last discovered
That God is not strong
And
Man is not weak.

960 A Zone Of Indifference

Your very life
Is a zone of indifference.
Nothing ever grows within you.
Nothing!
Never!

961 No Recommendation

The beauty of the rose
Needs no recommendation.
The fragrance of my gratitude-heart
Needs no recommendation either.

962 My Human Life Knows

My animal life did not know
How to ascend or descend.
But my human life knows
How to ascend and descend.
It can go higher than the height
Of the cosmic gods.
It can descend below
My animal life.

963 Time Bridges

Time bridges
The purity of my heart's cry
And
The beauty of my life's smile.

964 The Key

The key to the world
Is wisdom-light.
The key to wisdom-light
Is surrender-delight.

965 Where Is The Strength?

Where is the paint
To improve
The beauty of a flower?
Where is the strength
To increase
The love of my heart?

966 The Root Of Prosperity

The root of poverty
Is in the immediate fear
Of myself.
The root of prosperity
Is in the constant love
Of God.

967 The Gift Of Gifts

Peace is the gift of gifts
That God bestowed
Upon
His choice children.

968 Time Silences The Animal In Me

Time silences the animal in me.
Time enlightens the human in me.
Time quickens the divine in me.

969 A Master Key

A gratitude-heart
Is a master key.
It can open
Any heart.

970 Time Awakens

Time silences
The duty of life.
Time awakens
The beauty of death.

971 Never For Sale

The aspiration-cry
Can never be borrowed.
The gratitude-smile
Is never for sale.

972 If Your Heart Does Not Know

If your heart does not know
How to cry,
How do you expect
Your life to smile?

973 Two Neighbours

The mind's impurity
And the heart's purity
Are two neighbours.
But each is afraid
Of losing its identity.
Therefore,
They never meet.

974 I Shall Outlast

The sublime days of the perfect past
Are crowding fast.
I shall, therefore, outlast
The chain of my present miseries.

975 A Reality Of Love

Because you live
On a limitless scale
Of vastness,
Your life is a stupendous
Reality of love.

976 The Mystery Of Mysteries

The mystery of mysteries
Is why anyone flatters the desire-life.
The mystery of mysteries
Is why anyone ridicules the aspiration-life.

977 To Increase Faith

Be on the right track,
So that you can increase faith
Not only in yourself
But also in others.

978 The Roots Of Your Doubts

Be on the right track,
So that you can destroy
The roots of your doubts
And those of others.

979 The Unbelievable

You want to believe
The unbelievable:
That your mind is pure.
You want to make me believe
The unbelievable:
That my heart is poor.

980 You Are A Rogue

You are a rogue.
You want ready-made
God-realisation.
You are a fool.
You want ready-made
God-manifestation.

981 Choice, Not Chance

It is choice, not chance,
That tells me
Where to go,
How to go,
Why to go
And, finally,
Whom to realise.

982 He Who Compromises

He who compromises
Deliberately forgets to remember
What he cried for
Previously.

983 The Forced Duty

My Lord tells me:
"My son,
If you know the Truth,
Then never compromise.
Compromise is the forced duty
Of mutual weakness."

984 My Victory, My Defeat

My victory comes only when
God sanctions it.
My defeat comes only when
I admit it.

985 Nobody Can Defeat You

Nobody can perfect you
Except God.
Nobody can defeat you
Except your own
Stupid willingness.

986 Each Difficulty

Each difficulty
Is a hard examination:
So says my truth-fearing mind.
Each difficulty
Is an increased strength:
So says my God-loving heart.

987 You Are Lost

You are lost
When you believe your doubts.
You are dead
When you doubt your beliefs.

988 My Doubts Have Died

In the morning
I feed my faith-flames.
In the evening
I see something quite astonishing:
All my doubts have died
Out of starvation.

989 A Direct Gift

Education we can buy.
Satisfaction we cannot,
For satisfaction is
A direct gift from God.

990 My Enthusiasm

My enthusiasm needs
No imagination-flight,
For it is always
A rich harvest.

991 The Thick Forest Of Solitude

With his tears of repentance,
His mind has entered
Into the thick forest of solitude.
Before long he and his life
Will swim in the sea
Of satisfaction-ecstasy.

992 Visitor

Stay in the lap of pleasure, you fool!
God may not come to see you,
But somebody definitely
Will come to visit you
Without a whisper.
Do you know who?
Your own destruction-tiger.

993 The Inevitability

For months I have not prayed
To God.
I have totally forgotten
God's Love-Compassion-Forgiveness.
Within and without
I see the omnipotence of oblivion.
And now I am the inevitability
Of stark disappointment.

994 What I Have Become

What I see
Is a disturbing sunset.
What I feel
Is a trembling hope.
What I have become
Is a heart-piercing cry.

995 A Train Of Sadness

My Lord, do protect me
From my friends
Who are urging me
To follow the crowds.
They are unconsciously forcing me
Into a train of sadness.

996 A Fountain Of God-Thought

A fountain of God-thought
Tells me that mine is not
A weak and mortal birth.

997 I Am Helpless As Ever

When will You return,
My Beloved Supreme?
My heart-cave is all empty.
I am helpless as ever
In my throes.

998 The Great Authority

My wild thoughts were chasing me
Like beasts in the dense forest.
But now at last I have renounced
The great authority
Of my own life.

999 The Days Of My Slavery

Yesterday I was a slave
Of my own power.
Today I am a slave
Of my own doubt.
The days of my slavery
Will tomorrow be over.
I shall become the master-friend
Of my life.

1000 My Heart's Challenging Life

The achievement
Of my heart's challenging life
Is an aspiring flame,
An illumining lamp
And
A fulfilling sun
Every day.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 10, Agni Press, 1981
