8996 His Only Rival

There was a time
When adversity
Was his heart's only rival.
Now prosperity
Is his life's only rival.
In the future he himself
Will be his only rival.
Himself he will transcend
Not in his own way,
But in God's Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 90, Agni Press, 1983
