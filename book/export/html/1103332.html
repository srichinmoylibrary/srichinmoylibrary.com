17 March 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I am a humility-mind,
A sincerity-heart
And a purity-life,
I fly and fly and fly
In Infinity's Delight-Sky.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 March 1999, 5:10 a.m. Before using double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously from a seated position.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
