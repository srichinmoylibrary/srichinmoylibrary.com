147. Yesterday, today and tomorrow

Yesterday
I saw the sleeping triumph
Of my total doom.

Today
I see the glorious triumph
Of my dedication-life.

Tomorrow
I shall see the precious triumph
Of my all illumining perfection-soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
