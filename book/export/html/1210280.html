Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 245

24401

When I place my life
In the Hand of God,
I am happy, unimaginably happy.
When I place my heart
At the Feet of God,
I am not only happy
But also perfect.

24402

Every day
My soul begs my mind
To rise and run
With the blossoming dawn.

24403

We cannot read the entire book of life,
We cannot.
We can only read
As many pages as we are allowed to
By our Lord Supreme.

24404

Those who sincerely seek God
Will inevitably find God
At God's choice Hour.

24405

God teaches us
Only what we need to learn
And nothing more,
Nothing less.

24406

The most immediate priority
In my life
Is my sleepless and breathless surrender
To God's Will.

24407

We give God a plethora of excuses
Why it is impossible for us
To love God in God's own Way.
Poor God just smiles at us
With His Infinity's Compassion
And Forgiveness.

24408

O seeker, be extremely careful!
Never allow your aspiration-heart-river
To run dry
If you really need God in your life.

24409

When I am in my mind,
I feel that God has run away.
But when I am in my heart,
I feel and clearly see
That God never runs away from me.

24410

If we can dive deep within
On the strength of our prayers
And meditations,
We shall clearly see
That peace comes to us
From all directions.

24411

Once, twice, even a thousand times
God has appreciated our aspiration-heart
And dedication-life,
But we feel that it is not enough.
We expect and we demand today
More Satisfaction-Smiles from God.

24412

Love of God
Must come from within.
It can never be adequately taught
By an outer force.

24413

For God to take human form
Is, indeed, a reality
That is far beyond
Even our sweetest dreams.

24414

Our surrender to God
Is our prayerful and powerful
God-expansion
Inside our heart-garden.

24415

He who has become
A victim of failure
In the inner life
Has already separated his very existence
From the boundless Love of God.

24416

Just because
It has never happened before
That I have loved God
For God's sake,
That does not mean
I shall never be able
To love Him in His own Way.

24417

When I go down on my knees
And ask God for help,
He says to me
Most compassionately and affectionately,
"My child,
What have I been doing all along?"

24418

During my deep meditation,
I clearly see
That I am participating with the sky
In singing its freedom-songs.

24419

My gratitude-heart
Always has a free access
To God's
Peace and Bliss-Treasures.

24420

God tells me I need only
My old faith-friend.
I really do not need
Any other friend,
Specially not my new friend,
Self-doubt.

24421

Each God-lover
Has entered into the world
To propagate as fast as possible,
On a large scale,
God's Views with regard to His creation.

24422

My soul believes
In only one pilgrimage,
And that pilgrimage
Is to God's Compassion-flooded Feet.

24423

What is surrender?
Surrender is something
In which gratitude
Can take birth.

24424

My Lord,
Do give me the capacity
With my prayerful smiles
To wipe the Tears
From Your Compassion-Eye.

24425

Spirituality
Will never be out of date
If Mother Earth always has
Even a few thousand God-lovers.

24426

It is our outer lives
And never our inner lives
That contradict one another.
In our inner lives
We are all one,
All trying to illumine the world
And fulfil God's Message-Light
Here on earth.

24427

Our obedience-mind
Gets the greatest opportunity
To become one, inseparably one
With the gratitude-breath
Of our heart.

24428

O earth-bound worldly knowledge,
Can you not see
Once and for all
That I am suffocated
By your ignorance-darkness-night?

24429

What God has been trying to tell me
Over the years
Is that I can become
A choice and perfect instrument
Of His.

24430

God's Patience and Compassion-Stories
No human being on earth
Will be able to finish reading.

24431

God does not ask a spiritual Master
To do two things.
He asks him to do only one thing:
To transform humanity's
Ignorance-intoxicated animal nature
Into Divinity's oneness-fulness-love.

24432

To doubt God's Existence-Life on earth
Is, indeed, a universal disease
Of millennia.

24433

I am so happy when I watch
The swiftness of my mind-clouds'
Disappearance.

24434

Why does man fail God
Again and again?
Because he enjoys
An undercurrent of deep unwillingness.

24435

Old desires
Are bound to raise their heads
When we do not feed
Our fresh aspiration-heart-flames
Regularly.

24436

What is temptation?
Temptation is something
That examines
My God-realisation-resolve.

24437

Even the smallest glimmer of hope
Will definitely expedite
Our aspiration-heart-journey
Towards the destined Goal.

24438

What is unwillingness?
Unwillingness is something
That not only delays but also slays
Our God-aspiration, God-realisation
And God-manifestation-need.

24439

My Lord, my Lord,
Save me, save me
From brooding doubts!
My doubts have compelled me
To be on the verge of self-extinction.

24440

Every day, without fail,
Early in the morning,
God invites His chosen instruments
To go for a joy-ride in His Heart-Boat.
Alas, even His choice instruments
Often fail to obey Him.

24441

Only when I live inside my heart
Does God teach me
His never-ageing
Oneness-Fulness-Song.

24442

Am I blind?
Why can I not see
That my Lord Supreme
Is piloting my life-boat
To His choice Destination:
The oneness and fulness
Of humanity's divinity?

24443

My heart,
You do have the authority
To reclaim your capacity
To inspire mankind.

24444

My mind's disobedience
And my mind's resistance
Can never see the danger signs
All around me.

24445

I have written my Lord Supreme
A ten-page letter
Explaining why I fail again and again
To please Him in His own Way.
To my wide astonishment,
I see that God
Holds nothing against me.
He just smiles and smiles.

24446

What is the mind's wisdom?
It is nothing other than
A volley of follies
In our life.

24447

Unless our God-aspiration
And God-fulfilment-prayers
Become extremely powerful,
We are bound to see
That our shortcomings
Are overpowering us.

24448

God and God's Forgiveness
Are always ready
To empty us of all our sorrows,
But our receptivity is never ready.

24449

Alas, our demanding
And self-aggrandising vital
Fails to see
That it is heading towards
Self-destruction.

24450

My Lord,
You are Your Compassion-Eye;
You are Your Forgiveness-Feet.
Therefore,
Even before I could search for You,
You have come to me.

24451

There can be no sweeter inner music
Than my soulful, cheerful surrender
To my Lord's Will.

24452

It is only my self-giving life
That can and shall reach
My Lord's Bliss-Shore.

24453

It is humanity's sincere aspiration
And Divinity's sincere Concern
That link
Heaven and earth.

24454

It is really surprising
That anything undivine in us
Is always so punctual!

24455

My Lord Supreme always expects
Gratitude-tears and surrender-smiles
From my aspiration-heart.

24456

O seeker,
God is inside
Your own flower-garden-heart.
You need look nowhere else.

24457

We must choose everything divine,
Not only what is within our reach
But also what is far beyond our reach
At this moment.

24458

It seems that
In each human being
Faith is yet in its infancy.

24459

The day I do not aspire,
I just fall
Into the sea of desires.

24460

When I am in my deep meditation,
I clearly hear
The whispering Heart
Of my Lord Supreme.

24461

All men are truth-seekers.
All men are God-lovers.
All men are God-worshippers.
This sublime truth
Today they do not know,
But tomorrow they will.
They must!

24462

My Lord, how is it
That I can please You
Anytime I want to,
Whereas I cannot please any human being
No matter how hard I try?

24463

Every morning I observe
A serious conflict
Between the aspiring man in me
And the desiring man in me.

24464

My Lord,
I never want to have a heart
Empty of aspiration-cries.

24465

Every day I lose
In the battlefield of life
In my fight against ignorance-night.
Again, every night I watch
The rising of hope-moon
Inside my heart.

24466

O my mind,
O my vital,
O my body,
God's Door is wide open.
Where are you?
Come, come — just come in!

24467

There was a time
When I used to shop on a regular basis
At my mind's confusion-store.
But now I only move around
Inside my heart-garden.

24468

If you want to liberate yourself
From the meshes of ignorance,
Never, never hesitate
To meditate.

24469

I never knew
That God was an old friend of mine.
What is more,
He has always been
My only true friend.

24470

My Lord, You have blessed me
With many capacities.
Do grant me one more capacity:
The capacity to stamp out
Disobedience-night
From my mind.

24471

What has changed my life
Totally?
Just a sorrowful glance
From my Master.

24472

You want to know
What I am doing now.
I am enjoying the farewell speech
Of my ignorance-night.

24473

God is not interested
In what mankind thinks.
He only wants mankind to believe in
What it is going to become eventually:
A supremely choice instrument of God.

24474

A ceaseless God-obedience-life
Is an infinite waterfall
Of God's Delight.

24475

Every morning God comes to me
To bless me,
And I never let Him leave me
With empty Hands.
I give Him the beauty
Of my heart-cries.

24476

My Lord tells me
That since He is not tired of listening,
I can continue telling Him
All the grievances I have
Against humanity.

24477

Yesterday God asked me
To purify my mind.
Today He is asking me
To simplify my life.
Tomorrow He will ask me
To beautify my heart.

24478

Although my dissatisfied mind
And dissatisfied vital
Quite often disagree,
Unfortunately
They often work together.

24479

God is extremely hungry
For humanity's total
God-manifestation-commitment.

24480

Self-doubt
Is a most serious obstacle
To God-aspiration, God-realisation
And God-manifestation.

24481

God has already planned my future
In every detail.
Therefore, for me
There is nothing to worry about.

24482

Only a doubting mind
Does not want to make friends
With God
To make itself happy.

24483

Two most significant things
The mind does not know:
What to love
And how to love.

24484

My doubting mind
Does not bother God.
It is my unwilling heart
That bothers God,
For God expects everything positive
In abundant measure
From my heart.

24485

My Lord, I have forgotten
What I started out to say.
"My child, never mind!
Only remember
That I am truly and sleeplessly for you."

24486

In spite of all my attempts
To shake off lethargy
From my life,
I am nowhere near success.

24487

My doubting mind
Does not know
Whether peace exists
At all.

24488

There can be
No exemption
From my God-manifestation-responsibility.

24489

No matter how hard
The doubting mind tries
To oust
My loving faith in God,
It will always fail.

24490

Two things only are indispensable
In my life:
My God-obedience-mind
And my God-surrender-heart.

24491

Poor God!
Every day He suffers and suffers
When He reads
Mother Earth's newspapers.

24492

I am so happy and proud to know
That my heart-temple is blessed
By God's Peace
And God's Delight.

24493

Alas, I do not know
Why my mind and my heart
Have to remain always
Polar opposites.

24494

Help me, my Lord Supreme,
I am falling!
"Help Me, My sweet child!
I am starving for your smile."

24495

We must be all ready
To receive God
Every morning,
For His Visit is not only swift
But also brief.

24496

God always
Blessingfully, warmly and proudly
Welcomes
My gratitude-heart.

24497

Alas, I do not know how long
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Will have to wait
To see my mind swimming
In the beauty and fragrance
Of silence-sea.

24498

O my mind, surrender!
Surrender everything
To God's Compassion-Eye
If you want to liberate yourself
From the tyranny of obligations.

24499

The doubting mind
Deserves to be punished by God.
The aspiring heart
Deserves to be embraced by God.

24500

My first and last lesson in spirituality
Is my sleepless and breathless oneness
With the Will of my Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 245, Agni Press, 1998
