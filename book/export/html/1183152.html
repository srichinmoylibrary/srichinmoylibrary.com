16. God-Power is always within me

I have no money-power
Yet
I am unmistakably happy.

I have no earth-power
Yet
I am exceedingly happy.

I have no friend-power
Yet
I am constantly happy.
How?
Just because God-Power is always within me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
