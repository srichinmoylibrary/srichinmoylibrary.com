93. God is proud of him

He is devoted;
Therefore
God's Heart of Love
And
God's Soul of Light
Are fond of him.

He is concentrated;
Therefore
God the Creator
And
God the Creation
Are exceedingly proud of him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
