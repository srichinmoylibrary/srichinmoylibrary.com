15 November 1998 (VII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I am so grateful to You for allowing me
To watch the hide-and-seek game
That is being played by my purity-mind
And my sweetness-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 November 1998, 6:43 p.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
