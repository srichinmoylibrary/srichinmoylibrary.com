350. I have compared

I have compared myself
With God.
He has only two things
More than I have:
Compassion
And
Forgiveness.

I have compared myself
With man.
I have only two things
More than he has:
Self-deception
And
Self-gratification.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
