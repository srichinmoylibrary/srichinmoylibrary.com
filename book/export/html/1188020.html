2361

Do not hide your heart
Inside your mind.
Keep your heart always
Before your soul,
For this is the only way
You can see the truth
At its summit
And play with God
Inside His own Heart-Garden.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 24, Agni Press, 1984
