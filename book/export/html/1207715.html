21861

When I see myself
Through my own eyes,
I see and feel
That my life is not an inch better
Than the life of a beast.

When I see myself
Through God's Eye,
I see and feel
That I am on the fair road
To Godhood.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 219, Agni Press, 1995
