Your beautiful Eye,
   My life loves
Your blessingful Feet
   And I love
Your Heart's Forgiveness-Smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 October 1999, 10:18 p.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

26 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My life's dedication-service
Is my heart's fastest progress-run
   Towards You.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 October 1999, 10:26 p.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm one-hundred and four times. (52 right, 52 left)

26 October 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not know whether or not
   To enjoy a roaring laughter
When my mind says that it is
   The only God-knower,
When my heart says that it is
   The only God-lover
And when my life says that I am
   The only God-fulfiller.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 October 1999, 10:45 p.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 80 lbs. (81 reps)

28 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   We must realise
That our confession to God
   Is our best lover
And not our worst torturer.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 October 1999, 6:33 p.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 160 lbs. with each arm separately.

28 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Finally I have ended
My self-doubt-torture-dance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 October 1999, 6:49 p.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

28 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, when will I realise the fact
That my ingratitude-thoughts
Ruthlessly hurt Your Ears?
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 October 1999, 6:55 p.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

28 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Out of Your infinite Compassion,
   You are telling me
That my heart's God-gratitude-songs
Make Your Heart dance
   With boundless joy.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 October 1999, 6:57 p.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

28 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My doubting mind
And God's compassionate Eye
   Challenge each other.
My God-loving heart
And God's all-illumining Heart
   Embrace each other.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

28 October 1999, 7:27 p.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

Editor's preface to the first edition

These morning prayers were offered by Sri Chinmoy during his weight-lifting sessions at his home in New York in October 1999.
