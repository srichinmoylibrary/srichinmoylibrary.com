20426

Disobedience-destruction,
Disobedience-danger,
Disobedience-disaster
Is lowering the consciousness
Of the loving, aspiring, self-giving
And peace-dreaming humanity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 205, Agni Press, 1994
