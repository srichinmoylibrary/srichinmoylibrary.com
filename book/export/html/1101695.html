40.

My Lord Supreme,
When I tell You
That I have been thinking of You,
I sincerely feel that I am offering You
A piece of good news.
My Lord Supreme,
When You tell me
That You have been thinking of me,
I immediately feel
That I have made
Tremendous progress
In my life of aspiration,
And I clearly see
That my life's transformation-goal
Is not a far cry,
But is within my easy reach.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord, I pray to You, Agni Press, 1994
