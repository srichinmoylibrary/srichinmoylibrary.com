101. I shall not stop

I shall not stop.
I must gallop.
I am God-duty's
Truth-serving,
Life-awakening,
Lustre-horse.
In tune with God's cosmic muse,
Rhythmic dance,
My heart shall hop.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
