28.

Jate amader chirakal shake
Nitya nutan teal
Tari tare chalo sajatane chali
Amara tarun dal

We, the ever-blossoming souls,
Must be extremely careful
In preserving
Our newness-strength indomitable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, August 15 1945, Agni Press, 1996
