235 No Longer Necessary

Despair, no longer necessary.
Hope-moon is blossoming.

Hope-moon, no longer necessary.
Promise-sun is dawning.

Promise-sun, no longer necessary.
My victory-smile
God has already granted.

The following twenty-five poems were written on 27 October 1979 en route from Hong Kong to Bangkok.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 3, Agni Press, 1979
