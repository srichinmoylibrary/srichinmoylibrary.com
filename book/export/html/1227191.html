Swapan Kusum Chayan Karibo

Swapan kusum chayan karibo taba pada tale rakhite
Jiban maran rahasya giti paribo janite shikhite

Translation:

My Lord, I shall collect all my dream-flowers
To place at Your Feet.
And I shall learn from You
The mystery-song of my life and death.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Khunjite chahigo, part 1, , 2003
