April 23

How to conquer ignorance? Look downward! Now say, "My Lord, I am coming. I am sure that You are ready to receive me with your infinite Compassion."

Look forward! Now say, "My Lord, I am come. I am sure that You are ready to receive me with your Infinite Benediction."

Look upward! Now say, "My Lord, I have come. I am sure that You are ready to receive me with Your Infinite Realisation."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
