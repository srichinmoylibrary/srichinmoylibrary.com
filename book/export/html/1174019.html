1086 I Thank You

My Lord,
I thank You
Because You have given me
A small part in Your cosmic Play.
I thank You
Because You have given me
The capacity to admire
Your great Dreams for Your universe.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 11, Agni Press, 1981
