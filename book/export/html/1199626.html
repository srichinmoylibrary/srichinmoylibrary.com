13852

My Lord,
I cannot imagine why You care
For my countless imperfections.
"My child,
I cannot imagine how you can live
Without My Heart's Satisfaction-Smile."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 139, Agni Press, 1991
