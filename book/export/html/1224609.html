Ki Peyechi Tari Hisha Milate Pran Mor

Ki peyechi tari hisha milate
Pran mor nahe raji
Ki pai ni tari hishab nilate
Pran pakhi mor byastha ai
Bhalobasi nai ei dharanire
Sei byatha mane ase phire phire
Kato phul ache nadi tire tire
Bharini amar saji

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
