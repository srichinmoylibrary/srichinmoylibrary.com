914. Her vital, mind and body

Her vital triumphed
Over her heart,
But failed
To conquer her heart.
Her mind confused
Her heart,
But failed
To weaken her heart.
Her body forgot to love
Her heart,
But failed
To ignore her heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 19, Agni Press, 1973
