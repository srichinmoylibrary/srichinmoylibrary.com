645 Where Is Your Partner?

Aspiration,
Where is your partner:
Dedication?
Without it,
You cannot have realisation.

Dedication,
Where is your partner:
Grace?
Without it,
You cannot have the satisfaction of perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 7, Agni Press, 1980
