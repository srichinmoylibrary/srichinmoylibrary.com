589 I Enjoy His Heart's Hospitality...

I ENJOY HIS HEART'S HOSPITALITY

I enjoy
The pure hospitality
Of his heart.

I fear
The sheer curiosity
Of his mind.

I avoid
The wild vitality
Of his vital.

I dislike
The dark impurity
Of his body.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
