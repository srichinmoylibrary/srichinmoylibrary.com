2. My Lord's favour

My Lord's favour
Has caused me tremendous worries.
I am afraid
At any moment I may lose His favour.

My Lord's disfavour
Has given me tremendous determination.
I am certain
I shall someday win His favour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
