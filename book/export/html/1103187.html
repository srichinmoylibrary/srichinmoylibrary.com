17 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I know, I know, I know,
No mountain-heart-aspiration,
No progress-life-happiness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

17 November 1998, 6:14 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
