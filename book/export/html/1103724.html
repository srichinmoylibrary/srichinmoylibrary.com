2 September 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
Every morning
You write something encouraging,
Inspiring, illumining and fulfilling
On the tablet of my aspiration-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 September 1999, 7:52 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
