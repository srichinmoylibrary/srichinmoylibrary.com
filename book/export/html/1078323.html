676 When This Life Dies...

WHEN THIS LIFE DIES

You are wrong
If you think
That this life immediately flies to God
When it dies.

You are wrong
If you think
That God comes down and immediately
Visits this life
When it dies.

You are right
If you feel
That this life gains an experience
Of God-Light
In the heart of God-Universality
When it dies.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
