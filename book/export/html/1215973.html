347. The magnet

A rich smile of God
Is the magnet
That draws my heart,
My life, my all
To God's ever-transcending Heights.

A rich cry of mine
Is the magnet
That draws God's Heart,
God's Soul, God's Goal
To my ever-sinking nadir.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 7, Agni Press, 1974
