10749

Have you lost the boarding pass
For your Heaven-flight?
If so,
Enter into your heart-garden
And collect some beauty-flowers
And offer them to the ticket-checker.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 108, Agni Press, 1987
