Devotion

Devotion
     Is the cry
           That compels God
To seize the aspirant
With the very madness of Love.

A piece of candy,
     Lo, the child is won.
An iota of devotion,
God, the true and real God,
Is captured.

Sweeter than honey,
     Purer than flowers,
           Deeper than love:
The flame-waves of devotion-light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God’s Glance and God’s Grace, Agni Press, 2006
