602. My Creator's Praise

My Creator's praise
On my dedication-life
I treasure.

My Creator's infinite Light
I distribute soulfully
And devotedly
And thus humanity's consciousness
I raise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
