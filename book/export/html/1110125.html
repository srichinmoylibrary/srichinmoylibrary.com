61.

President Gorbachev warns
The human chaos on the rise:
Behold! The sunrise-rainbow-beauty,
Harmony, peace and bliss
Are slowly but convincingly arriving.

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: the home of Oneness-Peace-Dream-World, Agni Press, 2001
