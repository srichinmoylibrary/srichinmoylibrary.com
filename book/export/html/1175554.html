2605 Who Says That God Forgets?

Who says that God remembers
Everything?
Does He remember
My animal past?

Who says that God forgets
Everything?
Does He forget
My heart's gratitude-bud?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
