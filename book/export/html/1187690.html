2034

O desire-man,
Your heart's door
Has a very strong lock.
No man-made key can open it.
Therefore, I am praying
To God's Vision-Eye
Not only to open your heart
But also to help me enter
Into your heart
And play with you there
God's Satisfaction-Game.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 21, Agni Press, 1984
