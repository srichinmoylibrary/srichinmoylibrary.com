19 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My soul's
Perfection-freedom-delight
Is my divinity.
My life's
Imperfection-bondage-unhappiness
Is my humanity.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/Surabaya, Indonesia. 19 January 1999. 6:43 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and eighty-six times with one arm. (95 right, 91 left in five sets)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
