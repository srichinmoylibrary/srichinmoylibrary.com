51. Who can save me?

Who can save me
From the problem-game of birth?
Nobody, save and except
The God-lover in my heart.

Who can save me
From the problem-game of death?
Nobody, save and except
The God-seeker in my life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
