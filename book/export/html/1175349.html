2402 My Adamantine Determination

Who can whitewash
My filthy and ugly mind?
Nobody!

What can whitewash
My filthy and ugly mind?
My adamantine determination
And my silver sincerity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 25, Agni Press, 1982
