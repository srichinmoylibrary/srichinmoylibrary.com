159. The shortest route

Self-doubt
Is the shortest route
From the cradle
To the grave.

Self-faith
Is the shortest route
From the grave
To the cradle.

God
Is the shortest and safest route
From the earth-cradle
To the Heaven-cradle.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 4, Agni Press, 1974
