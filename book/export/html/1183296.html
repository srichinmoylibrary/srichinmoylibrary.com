160. They enjoy

My earth-life enjoys
Infant slumber,
Infant cry.

My Heaven-life enjoys
Infant awakening,
Infant smile.

My God-life enjoys
Infant song,
Infant love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
