976 My Needs...

MY NEEDS

World-comfort not for me.
I do not need it.
World-pleasure not for me.
I do not need it.

World-salvation all for me;
I need it and I need it.
World-perfection all for me;
I need it and I need it.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
