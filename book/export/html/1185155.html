827. Their sorrows

The artist's sorrow:
Nobody appreciates his painting.

The poet's sorrow:
Nobody reads his book.

The philosopher's sorrow:
Nobody accepts his philosophy.

The seeker's sorrow:
Nobody takes him seriously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
