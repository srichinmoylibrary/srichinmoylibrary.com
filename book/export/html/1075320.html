THE OLD ROAD AND THE NEW ROAD

At the end of the old road
He was warned by God:
"No entry!
One way only!
Go back, go back!"

At the beginning of the new road
He was inspired by God:
"Son, run,
Always run.
Prove your worth.
If not, the Goal Itself
Will come and touch you
And thus embarrass you."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
