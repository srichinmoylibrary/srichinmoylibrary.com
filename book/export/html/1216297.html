664. Who will read my poems?

Who will read my poems?
Man? Never!
Since he has so many
Important things to do,
I just forgive him.

Who will read my poems?
God? Why not?
He has to,
Since it is He who
Has written all these poems
In and through me.

Naturally He has to
And
He always does,
Carefully,
Lovingly,
Cheerfully.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
