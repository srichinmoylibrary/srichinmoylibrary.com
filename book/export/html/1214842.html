49. Purity

   Purity
Is the real divinity
In the heart of a human soul.
   This divinity
Ultimately makes us feel
That the Absolute
Is not only the Absolute
   But also
The Supreme Beloved
   In us,
   For us.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Union and oneness, Agni Press, 1976
