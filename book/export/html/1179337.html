6351 My Pain

My past pain was:
I did not want to see
God's Face.

My present pain is:
I do not want to live
Inside God's Heart.

My future pain will be:
I shall not need God
For God's sake.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 64, Agni Press, 1983
