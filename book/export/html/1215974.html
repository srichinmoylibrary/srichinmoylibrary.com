348. Will you barter with me?

O martyr-sun,
Will you barter with me
My martyr-candle?

O martyr-ocean,
Will you barter with me
My martyr-drop?

O Martyr-Immortality,
Will you barter with me
My martyr-death?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 7, Agni Press, 1974
