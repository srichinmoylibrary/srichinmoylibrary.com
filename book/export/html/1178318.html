5342 My Soul Powerfully Celebrates

On earth
My heart soulfully celebrates
The victory of
God's Compassion-Height.

In Heaven
My soul powerfully celebrates
The victory of
God's Justice-Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 54, Agni Press, 1983
