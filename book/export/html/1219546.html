Tomar Sathe

Tomar sathe khelbo jedin
Sabar sathe khelbo
Tomar prane milbo jedin
Sabar prane milbo

Translation:

I shall play with everyone
On the day I play with You.
I shall blend in every heart
On the day I blend in You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bela chale jai, , 1979
