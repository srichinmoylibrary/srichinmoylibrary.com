31.

Sei sumahan labheche janan
Banga mater buke
Sukhi habo more tahariy sukhe
Dukhi habo tar dukhe

He has taken human form
In the heart of Mother Bengal.
In His happiness
Our hearts must rejoice.
In His sadness
Our hearts must suffer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, August 15 1945, Agni Press, 1996
