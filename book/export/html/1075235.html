660 Thank You...

THANK YOU

The world knows
How to ask me
For favours.

God immediately
Tells me:
"Thank you."

The world's helplessness
Makes me feel
That I am supremely great.

God's loving gratitude
Makes me feel
That I am divinely good.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 14, Agni Press, 1973
