351. O Giver of Thyself

O Giver of Thyself,
You have given me
More than I deserve,
Infinitely more than I deserve.
To You I have given
My aspiration-cry,
My realisation-smile,
And
My determination-perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
