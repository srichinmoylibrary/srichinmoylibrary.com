446. The message of meditation

The meditation of my soul
Says to me:
God is Beauty.

The meditation of my heart
Says to me:
God is Goodness.

The meditation of my mind
Says to me:
God is Greatness.

The meditation of my life
Says to me:
God is Duty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
