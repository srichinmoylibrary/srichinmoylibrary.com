3889 If You Have A Prayerful Heart

If you have a doubtful mind,
Then give it to God
For its illumination.

If you have a prayerful heart,
Then give it to me
For my perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 39, Agni Press, 1982
