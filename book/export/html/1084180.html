1. Do the gods feel sorry?

Do the gods feel sorry?
Yes, they do.
Why?
Because men surpass them.

Does God feel sorry?
Yes, He does.
Why?
Because most men
Do not dare to play with Him,
Let alone compete with
Or surpass
Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 5, Agni Press, 1974
