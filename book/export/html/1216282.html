650. God knows the art of compensation

Because
I am a timid deer,
God has granted me
The message of speed
To reach speedily my goal.
I told you, my world,
God knows the art
Of compensation.

Because
I am a timid man,
God has granted me
The message of love
To found His Kingdom on earth.
I told you, my world,
God knows the art
Of compensation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
