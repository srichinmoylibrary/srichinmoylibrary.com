Pather Parichoy Sthayi Kichui Noy

Pather parichoy sthayi kichui noy
Jatrir habe joy
Egiye chal egiye chal
Charidike shudhu chhal
Pete habe amar parichoy
Hok bidhatar joy
Andhakarer khoy

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 7, , 1981
