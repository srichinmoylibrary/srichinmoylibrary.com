135. What do we treasure

Eagerness
My sound-life always treasures.
Soulfulness
My silence-heart always treasures.
And what do I treasure?
I treasure the attentiveness
And perfectiveness of my Master Pilot
Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
