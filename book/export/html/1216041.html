413. To please God, to please man

   To please God
I offer my life's little lamp.
   And God is unreservedly satisfied.   To please man
I offer my soul's vast sun.
   Yet man is unquestionably dissatisfied.   Who can please God?
Whoever wants to.   Who can please man?
No one, not even God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
