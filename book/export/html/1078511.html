864 Lost And Found...

LOST AND FOUND

Lost satisfaction-beauty found
At aspiration-door.

Lost perfection-lustre found
In surrender-cave.

The same way
Lost God will be found, too,
Somewhere, somehow,
Someday.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
