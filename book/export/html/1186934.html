1286

There are five tollbooths
On the highway to Heaven.
The first one is
Surrender-plant-tollbooth.
The second one is
Gratitude-tree-tollbooth.
The third one is
Perfection-flower-tollbooth.
The fourth one is
Satisfaction-fruit-tollbooth.
The fifth one is
God's Oneness-Embrace-Tollbooth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 13, Agni Press, 1983
