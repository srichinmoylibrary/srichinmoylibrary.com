1114

Your oneness-heart
Does not have to say anything,
For it at once embodies
Both earth's ascending aspiration-hope
And Heaven's descending
Compassion-promise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 12, Agni Press, 1983
