3929 When My Lord Pulls Me

When my Lord pulls me,
I feel that my desire-life
Is extremely heavy.

When my Lord pushes me,
I feel that my aspiration-life
Is extremely light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 40, Agni Press, 1982
