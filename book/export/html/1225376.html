O Sun of My Soul

O sun of my soul,
I devotedly love your duty.
O moon of my heart,
I soulfully love your beauty.
O star of my life,
I surprisingly love your purity.
O sky of my search,
I love your generosity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 1, , 1977
