I Think of God Because He Is So Kind

I think of God
     Because
He is so kind.
I think of man
     Because
He is so sad.
I think of me
     Because
I am so blind.
I think of you
     Because
You are so mad.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 1, , 1977
