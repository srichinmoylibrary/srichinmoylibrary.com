43. Marriage

What is marriage?
     The smile of love
That allows two souls
     To soar above.

What is marriage?
     The curse of night.
A tug-of-war,
     No escape to Light.

What is marriage?
     God's fulfilment true.
In Silence and Power
     His Vision's due.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Flute, Agni Press, 1972
