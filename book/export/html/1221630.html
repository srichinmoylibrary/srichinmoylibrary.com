Ar Katokal Karbo Larai

Ar katokal karbo larai
Maner sathe
Ei kathati sudhai ami
Bishwanathe

Translation:

How long do I have to continue
Fighting with my mind?
This is the question that I am asking
The Lord of the Universe.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eso eso prabhu, part 1, , 1998
