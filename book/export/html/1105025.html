75.

My Lord,
This morning You are asking me
To join Nature
In her prayer to You:
“My Lord,
You have given me
Beauty infinite,
But You have not given me
My duty.”
“My child,
Your duty
Is to climb up incessantly
To clasp My Feet
And devour the golden dust
Of My Feet.”

17 December 2005
Kuantan, Malaysia

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 2006
