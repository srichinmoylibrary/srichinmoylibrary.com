2656

Aspiration is the beginning
Of perfection.
Perfection is the beginning
Of satisfaction.
Satisfaction is the beginning
Of nothingness nullified
And fulness multiplied.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 27, Agni Press, 1984
