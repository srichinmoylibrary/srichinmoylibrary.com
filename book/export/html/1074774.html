208. Command and obey

Faith to command,
Doubt to obey.

Purity to command,
Impurity to obey.

Light to command,
Darkness to obey.        But
Soul-Love to command,
Soul-Love to obey.

God-Will to command,
God-Will to obey.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 5, Agni Press, 1973
