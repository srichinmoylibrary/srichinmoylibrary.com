4708 The Supreme In Me

The animal in me
Is all ignorance-night.
The human in me
Is all knowledge-light.
The divine in me
Is all wisdom-delight.
The Supreme in me
Is all Self-Transcendence-Height.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 48, Agni Press, 1982
