12274

Do you not think
That God does not appreciate
Your heart's sleepless
Insecurity-stories?
Alas, the soulful heart
Is quite often a captive
Of our ego-mind.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 123, Agni Press, 1989
