2953 Three Kinds Of Love

I have three kinds of love:
My thoughtful love,
My soulful love and
My fruitful love.
My thoughtful love
Does everything.
My soulful love
Has everything.
My fruitful love
Is everything.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 30, Agni Press, 1982
