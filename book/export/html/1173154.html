229 Soulful Happiness Is My Escort

Soulful happiness is my escort.
Therefore
I shall go very far.

Fruitful oneness is my escort.
Therefore
I am reaching my Perfection-Goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 3, Agni Press, 1979
