45. Believing or not believing

Believing nothing
You go to death-world
As an invited guest.

Believing something
You stay in your own world
Of merciless caution.

Believing everything
           Without concentrating
                 And
           Without meditating
You enter into the frustration-world
As a tempted guest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
