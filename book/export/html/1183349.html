213. Mother and father

Who is Heaven's mother?
Light-sea.

Who is Heaven's father?
Consciousness-sky.

Who is earth's mother?
Patience-moon.

Who is earth's father?
Knowledge-sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
