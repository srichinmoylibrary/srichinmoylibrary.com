668. His youth remains

His youth remains,
Although
He is ninety years old.

His youth remains,
Because
He loves the quintessence of life:
Oneness-light.

His youth remains,
Because
God has always much work
To accomplish in him,
Through him, on earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
