38. Pleased with me

     Sound-life
Was pleased with me
When I said
     God is great.     Silence-life
Was pleased with me
When I said
     God is good.     Perfection-life
Was pleased with me
When I said
     God is becoming.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When God-Love descends, Agni Press, 1975
