218. This is my fate

This is my fate.
Indeed,
This is my fate:
I have forgotten
To open
My own heart-gate.

This is my fate.
Indeed,
This is my fate:
I have forgotten
My last
God-interview date.

From: Sri Chinmoy, From the Source to the Source, Agni Press, 1974
