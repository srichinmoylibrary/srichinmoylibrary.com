Questions on food and cooking

SIW 40-63. The following questions on food were answered by Sri Chinmoy on February 7, 1979.

Question: What is the most important aspect of cooking?

Sri Chinmoy: The most important aspect of cooking is its life-energising reality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Secrets of the inner world, Agni Press, 1980
