Jago Rater Sathi Ghumer Sathi

Jago jago jago jago
Rater sathi ghumer sathi swapan sathi
Premer bhati khamar bale kripar bale
Phutao hiyar shatadale

Translation:

[Partial translation]
Arise, awake,
O friend of my dream-sleep.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Consciousness-sky, , 1983
