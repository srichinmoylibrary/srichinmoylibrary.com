22740

Although I do not have
An iota of peace in my mind,
I am sure God will not allow my mind
To remain in agony.
God will one day illumine my mind
And bless my mind
With Infinity's Peace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 228, Agni Press, 1996
