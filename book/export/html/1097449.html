My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 46

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Kijal, Malaysia in January 2006.

5 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

1 O Seeker ...

O seeker,
Do not slow down!
God’s Heart
Is eagerly waiting
For you.

2 We Think Of God ...

We think of God.
We speak about God.
But, alas,
How rarely
We stand up for God!

3 God Firmly Says To My Body ...

God firmly says to my body,
“You must never think
Of your
Old-age retirement-pleasure!”

4 Peace-Gong ...

Peace-gong
The mind cannot hear.
Peace-flute
The heart hears,
And the melody of the flute
The heart shares lovingly
And self-givingly
With the rest of the world.

5 My Devotion-Heart-Eye ...

My devotion-heart-eye
Is the only eye
That can see
My Lord’s golden Feet.

6 Alas ...

Alas,
The human mind
At times enjoys confusion,
Even dissatisfaction.

7 I Came Into The World ...

I came into the world
To see God’s real Smile.
Alas,
God’s real Smile
Is nowhere to be found.

8 Believe Me ...

Believe me,
The Divine Power
Is much greater
Than the power of evil.

9 We Long ...

We long
For reality.
Reality longs
For a divine dream.

10 Despair Weakens ...

Despair weakens
Sooner than the soonest
Our aspiration-heart.

11 Only A Stark Fool ...

Only a stark fool
Neither cries
Nor smiles.

12 Until Hope ...

Until hope
Becomes a promise,
Hope is all brittle.

13 Even A Giant Ego ...

Even a giant ego
Suffers a terrible defeat
When the God-Hour dawns.

14 Every Day ...

Every day
God’s Compassion-Eye
Softly, tenderly
And blessingfully awakens
All God-dreamers.

15 The Outer Freedom ...

The outer freedom
Misguides and misleads
The world.

16 The Inner Freedom ...

The inner freedom
Can play the role
Of a saviour.

17 I Wish To Be ...

I wish to be
Blissfully blind
In following my Lord’s
Express Commands.

6 January 2006 ...

6 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

18 Our Progress-Speed ...

Our progress-speed
Is in God’s Hand
And never in
Our own hands.

19 Meditation ...

Meditation
Is a gradual
Godward journey.

20 When We Do Something ...

When we do something
Seriously wrong,
Our souls shed
Bitter tears.

21 Aspiration Is At Once ...

Aspiration is at once
Our heart’s divine arrow
And our life’s divine protection.

22 When My Aspiration-Flames ...

When my aspiration-flames
And my meditation-sun
Play together,
It is unimaginably beautiful.

23 If I Live ...

If I live
Only for God,
Then nothing can frighten me,
Nobody can frighten me.

24 Imagination ...

Imagination
Sometimes can be
Mental hallucination.

25 My Devotion-Bird ...

My devotion-bird
Has a very special nest:
My heart.

26 My Lord ...

My Lord,
How shall I address You?
“My child,
You can address Me
By any name
Or by any form.”

27 Spirituality ...

Spirituality
Has many privileges.
The first and foremost
Is God-closeness.

28 A Delayer ...

A delayer
Will never be chosen by God
To be a player
In His Cosmic Game.

29 The Sweet Whispers ...

The sweet whispers
Of my soul
Give my heart
Tremendous joy.

30 My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Please tell me
If there is a place
In Your entire Creation
Where I can live in peace.

31 Each Doubt-Invasion ...

Each doubt-invasion
Must be challenged and conquered
By our most powerful faith
Deep within.

32 The Unaspiring Human Beings ...

The unaspiring human beings
Sooner or later will drown
In the vast sea
Of ignorance-night.

33 I Belong Solely To God ...

I belong solely to God
In the inner world of silence
And
In the outer world of sound.

34 The Seeker’s Aspiration-Life ...

The seeker’s aspiration-life
Ends
When he allows the mind
To push him
And the vital
To pull him.

7 January 2006 ...

7 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

35 Every Morning ...

Every morning
My heart becomes
A rainbow-dream-lover.

36 A Desire-Life ...

A desire-life
Is nothing short of
A weakness-mind.

37 Happiness-Stars ...

Happiness-stars
Are found only
In the heart-sky.

38 The Mind Divides ...

The mind divides.
The heart brings
Everything together.

39 My God-Obedience ...

My God-obedience
Is a resounding success
And progress
In my spiritual life.

40 Every Day ...

Every day
There will be new challenges,
And again,
There will be the brightest sun
From deep within
To help us brave the challenges.

41 The Seeker Does Everything ...

The seeker does everything
To build a spiritual shrine.
The Master gives
The finishing touches.

42 This Moment ...

This moment
The mind is a jungle.
Next moment
The same mind becomes
A desert.
Finally it becomes
A useless nothingness.

43 No Matter What We Do Wrong ...

No matter what we do wrong,
God is eager to see us
Again and again.

44 Even ...

Even
Self-transcendence-attempts
Are most remarkable.

8 January 2006 ...

8 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

45 Every Day ...

Every day
I must dive deep within,
Deeper and deeper,
To become a choice
Student-instrument
Of God.

46 Self-Deception ...

Self-deception
Can easily be
Contagious.

47 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I am sacrificing
My long-lost self
To You.

48 Surrender Has To Be ...

Surrender has to be
Active and dynamic,
Not merely fascinating
Or dreaming.

49 God’s Victory-Songs ...

God’s Victory-Songs
The angels sing
Untiringly and self-givingly.

50 Like The Prisoner’s ...

Like the prisoner’s
Hunger pangs for light,
We needs must develop
Deep, inner hunger pangs
To see the Face of God.

51 God’s Chosen Children ...

God’s chosen children
Are always on the alert
So that undivine forces
Cannot invade
God’s Inner Circle.

52 Always And Always ...

Always and always
There is an unknown Source
Of love, devotion and surrender
Deep within us.

53 Earth Has ...

Earth has
Its multiple and multiplying
Desperation-cries.

54 Heaven Has ...

Heaven has
Its multiple and multiplying
Compassion-expansion-Smiles.

9 January 2006 ...

9 January 2006

Kijal, Malaysia

55 My Aspiration-Heartbeat ...

My aspiration-heartbeat
Quickens
At the very mention of God.

56 In My Spiritual Life ...

In my spiritual life,
Many, many, many more
Supremely divine things
Are yet to come.

57 Poor God! ...

Poor God!
He sincerely tries
Not to see
The darkness of the world,
But unfortunately
He sees it.

58 There Are Some Things ...

There are some things
That must be done
Before others —
Such as my aspiration
And my dedication.

59 Ignorance-Night ...

Ignorance-night
Is dauntless,
Ruthless,
Godless.
Therefore it is useless.

60 Affection, Love ...

Affection, Love,
Sweetness, Fondness
God has for me.
Every day I live and grow
Under His Protection-Tree.

61 God With His Smile ...

God with His Smile
Has descended upon my head
To shatter my ego-pride
Permanently.

62 There Is Only One Thing ...

There is only one thing
That I do not like about God:
He likes to hide.

63 There Are Many Nice Things ...

There are many nice things
That I can say about God,
But one thing I would like to say:
His Love for us
Has neither beginning
Nor end.

64 Quite Often ...

Quite often
The mind is empty
Of concern.

65 I Must Not Give Up Hope ...

I must not give up hope.
I must carry it with me
Far into the distant future.

66 Pay No Attention ...

Pay no attention
When ignorance-night
Begs to visit you.

67 Whatever God Tells My Heart ...

Whatever God tells my heart
Is absolutely true —
Forever true!

68 To Meditate Soulfully ...

To meditate soulfully
And powerfully
Is to start our homeward journey
To the Absolute Supreme.

69 My Lord Has In Mind ...

My Lord has in mind
A full service-schedule for me
Every day.

70 Medicine We Need ...

Medicine we need
For the body.
Aspiration we need
For the heart.
Surrender we need
For God.

71 Out Of His Infinite Bounty ...

Out of His infinite Bounty,
My Lord every day lifts
My heavier than the heaviest
Mind-doubt-weights.

72 I Came From God ...

I came from God
To see.

73 I Am Returning To God ...

I am returning to God
To be.

74 Divinity ...

Divinity,
With You
I want to be.

75 Humanity ...

Humanity,
I know not who you are,
And I have no desire
To know!

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 46, Agni Press, 2006
