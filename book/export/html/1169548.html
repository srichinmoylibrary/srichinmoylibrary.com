193. Easy, not easy

Easy dreaming,
Easy climbing,
Easy smiling,
But not easy loving,
Not easy surrendering,
Not easy fulfilling
God, the Dream,
God, the Dreamer
And God, the Dreamland.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
