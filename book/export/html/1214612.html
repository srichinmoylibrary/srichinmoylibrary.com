1211.

My Lord Supreme,
Our lack of aspiration displeases You.
Our lack of dedication displeases You.
My Lord, is there anything we have
That does please You?
"Yes, My child,
Your heart's soul-flooded smile."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, Part 13, Agni Press, 1996
