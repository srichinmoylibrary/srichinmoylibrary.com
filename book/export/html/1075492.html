912. The palace of falsehood, the palace of truth

Pure overstatement
Lives in the Palace
Of Falsehood.
Gross understatement
Lives in the Palace
Of Falsehood.
Self-discovery
Lives in the Palace
Of Truth.
God-mastery
Lives in the Palace
Of Truth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 19, Agni Press, 1973
