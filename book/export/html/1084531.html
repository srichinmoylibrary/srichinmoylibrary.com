46. Follow them

Follow the stars;
They will inspire you.
You do need the stars.

Follow the moon;
She will feed you.
You do need the moon.

Follow the sun;
He will guide you.
You do need the sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 11, Agni Press, 1974
