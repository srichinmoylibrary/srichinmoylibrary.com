260 You Are Not A Man

You are not a man
Although you look like one.
Your very presence
Is nothing short of a nightmare.
Upon seeing you,
My whole world shall die
Before it hopes to dare.

The following twenty-five poems were written on 27 October 1979 en route to Calcutta from Bangkok.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 3, Agni Press, 1979
