Kato Dhan Je Pare Ache Shyama Mayer

Kato dhan je pare ache
Shyama mayer charan tale
Tabu ami dhaner lobhe
Jai kena ma dure chale
Dhanto paina bhasi nayan jale
Jedike jai sabai amai chhale
Chhalana noi chhele khela
Dube jabe jibana bhela
Kabe ami thakbo shudhu
Shyama mayer charan tale
Jiban amar dhanya habe
Nirabadhi ma bale

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
