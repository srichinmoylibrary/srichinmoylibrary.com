Maner Bojha Kotai Namai Kichutai Jaina

Maner bojha kothai namai kichutai jaina
Chintari bhut chepeche mathai
Charite je ta chaina
Jatobar jai ghure phire ase
Sei bhut jena akashe batase
Cheretare ekebare
Mano shanti paina
Ashantite jale mare
Shanti tabu chaina

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
