618 God's Heart...

GOD'S HEART

God's Heart
May be big for humanity,
But not for me.
I tell you why:
I have easily concealed it,
I can easily reveal it.
Something more
(This time I shall have to tell
You in a whispering voice):
I shall reveal
God's Heart to you
Only to perfect you,
Fulfil you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
