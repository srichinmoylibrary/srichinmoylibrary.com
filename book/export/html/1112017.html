The challenge

In today's two-mile race, I saw Kritagyata and Pratyaya so far ahead of me during the first mile.

Then I challenged Lavanya and Nilima. I was a few metres behind them, but I felt that I would be able to catch them at the one-mile point. Then, after I passed them, I started walking.
