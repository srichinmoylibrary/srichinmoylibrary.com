10881

Year after year
Your sincerity compels you
To tell God about your weaknesses.
God tries His best to help you,
But, alas, your determination is not there
To conquer those devouring tiger-forces.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 109, Agni Press, 1987
