312. My God is many

Your God is but one God.
He is great;
I do not deny it.

My God is many.
They are not only great
but also good.

Because of their greatness,
In Heaven they dance and dance.
Because of their goodness,
On earth they cry and cry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
