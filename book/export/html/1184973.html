645. God-language

Do you want to learn God-language?
God-language is
Aspire and give,
Give and aspire;
Delight achieve,
Delight distribute.
This is God-ecstasy immaculate;
This is God-language-delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
