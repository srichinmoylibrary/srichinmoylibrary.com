39. Something remains untold

Something remained untold.
What was it?
God's Compassion
For humanity.

Something remains untold.
What is it?
God's Aspiration
In humanity.

Something shall remain untold.
What is it?
God's Perfection
Of humanity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
