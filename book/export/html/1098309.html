18 Why Am I So Proud ...

Why am I so proud
Of my heart?
I am proud of my heart
Because it is always eager
To take giant steps
Towards the Destination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 56, Agni Press, 2008
