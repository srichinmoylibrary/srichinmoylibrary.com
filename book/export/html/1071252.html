110. Two supreme realities

Do not descend from Heaven
After me.
My heart will feel miserable.

Do not descend from Heaven
Before me.
Your soul will not appreciate it.

God has two supreme Realities:
Togetherness-life
And
Oneness-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, 207 Flower-Flames, Agni Press,
