Madal the Child, Sleeplessly Wild

Madal the child,
     Sleeplessly wild.
Chinmoy the teenager,
     An uncertain wager.
Sri Chinmoy the man,
     A sad failure-plan.
Guru Chinmoy, Guru Chinmoy,
     God and man’s broken toy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Song-flowers, part 8, , 1992
