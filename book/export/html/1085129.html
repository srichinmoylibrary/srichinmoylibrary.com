May 1985

Darkness-temptation there is.
That does not mean
I shall have to surrender
To darkness-temptation.

Failure-frustration there is.
That does not mean
I shall have to become
A giant failure-frustration.

God-Perfection there is.
Who dares to tell me
That I cannot become
Another God-Perfection?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Gratitude-Flower-Hearts, Agni Press, 1993
