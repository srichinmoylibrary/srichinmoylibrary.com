20 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart's patience-light
Is my life's wisdom-delight
In my God-fulfilment-task.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 October 1999, 9:18 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
