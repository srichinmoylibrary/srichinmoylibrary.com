Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 227

22601

I cry for
God the Runner.
I die for
God the Sprinter.

22602

Chaos is the real name
For the mind.
Catastrophe is the real name
For the vital.

22603

God tells me that
He knows of no better sight
Than my tearful eyes.

22604

Pray to God sleeplessly.
He will definitely give you
Sovereignty over yourself.

22605

When I live inside
A tiny expectation-circle,
I die.

22606

Impurity has not found
And will never find
The soul.

22607

When God shares
His supreme Secrets with me,
The human in me proudly smiles.

22608

O slave of pride,
You are, indeed,
A self-imposed sufferer.

22609

Alas, I pride myself
On my success-mind,
Not on my progress-heart.

22610

Thank God it is all over!
I do not have to be stung
By self-doubt-mind anymore.

22611

Spirituality
Is the supreme necessity
Of the awakened heart.

22612

I tire God out in many ways,
Consciously or unconsciously,
But my eagerness to love and serve Him
Can never tire Him out.

22613

When I tell God that I shall become
An unconditional instrument of His,
It delights Him immediately
Plus immensely.

22614

O doubt,
Why are you so cruel to me?
When you die
Nobody will attend your funeral —
No, not even my mind.

22615

No matter how bad and useless we are,
We cannot ultimately interfere
With God's infinite Happiness.

22616

Unlike us, God never fails,
Even for a fleeting second,
To appreciate openly
Each and every human being
In His creation.

22617

Each aspiring and climbing sun-flame
In my heart
Is dearer than the dearest
To the Heart of my Lord Supreme.

22618

Even God finds it hard
To accept those human beings
As His own
Who are unused to working for Him.

22619

I shall no more allow
My mind to attend
The jealousy, insecurity
And negativity-meeting
That takes place every day.

22620

An unaspiring vital
Quite often locks itself up
In its own torments.

22621

God never forces anybody.
He only speaks to those and for those
Who give Him a willing ear.

22622

My Lord,
I have broken Your Heart many times.
From now on,
I give You my soul-promise
That I shall love You, serve You and fulfil You
In Your own Way
Until I breathe my last.

22623

The heart-lamb
Is so adorable.
The mind-horse is, alas,
Always ungovernable.

22624

Alas, my heart's gratitude-hours
For God to cherish
Are so surprisingly few.

22625

There is only one message
We receive from peace,
And that message is:
God is come.

22626

God could hardly believe His Eyes
When He saw me crying so soulfully
For my nature's transformation.

22627

I do not know
Anything about others.
But I do know
That God always insists
On knowing the details of my life.

22628

My Lord,
I may not love You
The way You want me to love You:
Unconditionally.
But I assure You,
You will never be neglected,
You will never be rejected
By me.

22629

God has good reason for asking me
Why I am not ready for God-realisation,
Since I accepted the spiritual life
Centuries ago.

22630

Unlike us,
God never enjoys quarrelling with us.
On the contrary,
When we quarrel with Him,
He suffers more than we can ever imagine.

22631

When I make
Even some small progress,
God comes and dances
Inside my heart-garden.

22632

God's Compassion does not allow Him
To listen to our bitter complaints
And our unthinkable accusations
Against Him.
But we must know that
Nothing is out of God's Earshot.

22633

Even the heart of suspicion
Is ugly.
Even the breath of suspicion
Is destructive.

22634

The beauty and fragrance of peace
Is neither young nor old —
It is immortal.

22635

For the sake of God within,
My heart cries.
For the sake of God without,
My life smiles.

22636

Life is not to be thrown away.
But life's unwillingness-demon
Must be thrown away
Sooner than the soonest.

22637

I do God a great wrong
When I say that neither God
Nor God's Compassion
Is for me.

22638

God laughs and laughs
When I tell Him
That I am not yet ready
To be His Eternity's comrade.

22639

My mind's inconsistency,
My mind's carelessness,
My mind's callousness —
These things can never
Be approved by God.

22640

How can a doubting mind
Ever be certain of anything,
Either in the outer world
Or in the inner world?

22641

God knows the art
Of multiplication so well
That when I offer Him
An iota of perfection,
In return He blesses me
With His infinite Affection.

22642

As my destiny unfolds itself,
I feel that my heart shall have
A quenchless thirst
For God's Compassion-Eye.

22643

God is so compassionate
That He never accepts our failure
As something final.

22644

God's special friend
Is humour.
God's worst enemy
Is rumour.

22645

He who has no peace of mind
Does not like to follow
Or to be followed.

22646

Very old was my mind.
Therefore, it had to die —
Needless to say,
Not willingly.

22647

The world does not forgive me.
I do not forgive the world.
Poor God is being strangled
Because of His Oneness with me
And the world.

22648

My Lord Supreme has forgiven me
Time and again.
How is it that I never even dream
Of forgiving
Anybody here on earth?

22649

Do you know what my fear does?
My fear has the capacity
To chase my Lord Supreme away.

22650

Do you know what my doubt does?
My doubt does not allow my Lord Supreme
To enter into me.

22651

Do you know what my jealousy does?
My jealousy does not allow my Lord Supreme
To smile at me.

22652

Do you know what my insecurity does?
My insecurity stabs
My Lord Supreme.

22653

My Lord's Heart is for expansion.
My Lord's Mind is for perfection.
My Lord's Life is for transformation.

22654

There is nothing of God's
That I do not love,
But His Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Feet
I love sleeplessly and breathlessly.

22655

Alas, I do not know
Why I allow my desire-mind
And not my aspiration-heart
To catch a glimpse of God's Smile.

22656

I may not know
What is in God's Mind,
But I do know
What is in God's Heart:
Unconditional Love for me.

22657

I want God to fulfil
My heart's hopes.
God wants me to fulfil
My life's promises.

22658

The human in me prays to God
For comfort from God.
The divine in me prays to God
For God's Advice.

22659

I did not know
That I was making a horrible mistake
When I started giving so much importance
To my doubting mind.

22660

Alas, there is not a single human being
Who is spared the experiences
Of physical pain, mental agony
And vital frustration.

22661

My aspiration-heart
Is regaining its strength once more
Under God's tender Handling.

22662

I am amazed to see
How my soul keeps God up-to-date
On everything in my life.

22663

In spite of my repeated
Himalayan blunders,
God's Compassion has concluded
That I am not past correction.

22664

The difference between God and me
Is this:
I do not allow God to run fast
Inside my heart.
He not only allows me, but also begs me,
To run the fastest inside His Heart.

22665

I am nothing.
I can do nothing.
Alas, will You not accept
My life's failure-tears
As my only gifts to You?

22666

Alas, I do not know
When and how
My mind became
The towering rock of pride.

22667

My Lord,
If You once come into
My little heart-room,
I assure You,
You will not only like it,
But love it.

22668

Both God and I
Desperately need speed.
I need speed in my God-hunger.
God needs speed
In my nature-transformation.

22669

Human life can become perfect
Only when the life-breath of the heart
Self-givingly loves the Lord Supreme.

22670

God's Compassion-Eye
Never pierces anybody,
But illumines and enlightens
Everybody.

22671

How can your heart find peace,
When you allow your mind
To roam everywhere?

22672

My Lord,
The Compassion-Smile
Of Your Compassion-Eye
Has fed my heart
Beyond my imagination.

22673

My Lord,
I want to love You unconditionally
Every day.
If I miss even one day,
Then that very day I want to die
And be buried,
Along with my ignorance.

22674

God the Justice
Either cannot or does not want to
Plumb the depths
Of God the Compassion.

22675

My Lord,
Do not allow my life to die
Without significance
And mingle with the dust.
I really want to love You
In Your own Way.
I really want to please You
In Your own Way.

22676

To my extreme sorrow,
My unconditional love for God
Is of a very short duration.

22677

Repeat, repeat
The Name of the Lord Supreme
Countless times, unceasingly.
This will eventually absolve you
Of all your countless sins.

22678

Life is given to each human being
For a very special purpose.
This secret each human being
Must discover for himself.

22679

God's infinite Beauty
Is His own Eternity's
Silence-Bliss.

22680

Fight! Fight again and again
To break down your mind's
Unwillingness-wall!

22681

Be always willing and eager
If you want to brighten your mind
And sweeten your heart.

22682

If there is true willingness,
Then there can be no iota
Of hesitation.

22683

O my mind,
Prayerfully let us together explore
The galaxies of Heaven.

22684

The generosity of the mind
And the purity of the heart
Are interdependent.

22685

Gratitude-tears are extremely quick
In erasing the mind's
Foolish dictation.

22686

God watches my mind
Early in the morning
And weeps
During the entire day.

22687

When I met God
For the first time,
He shared with me
Compassionately and blessingfully
A wealth of sweet anecdotes.

22688

It is becoming more and more clear
To God
That I love only the human in me
And not the divine in me.

22689

Finally my mind is ready
To announce its latest news:
It is all ready to surrender
To my life's oneness-heart.

22690

When we achieve something great,
God smiles and smiles.
When we achieve something good,
God tells us,
"Stop not, stop not!
This is just the beginning."

22691

O my Lord Supreme,
Do shelter my life
Every day, every hour,
Every minute and every second.

22692

Alas, I do not know how and when
Our human mind will be ready
For radical changes
And illumining improvements.

22693

It is always a good time
To talk to God.
But the very best time
To talk to God
Is in the small hours of the morning,
Between three and four.

22694

If you can offer gratitude to God
Before your meditation,
During your meditation
And after your meditation,
Then you will feel your progress-light
Clearing your mind-clouds.

22695

Who can ever determine
The age of earth's aspiration
And the age of Heaven's Compassion?

22696

To receive joy infinite,
I shall always walk along the path
Of a peace-pilgrim.

22697

I try to hold God spellbound
With my sound-victories.
God always holds me spellbound
With His Peace-Songs.

22698

The voice of Truth
Will speak to us
Only when our heart becomes
A sacred reservoir.

22699

Each time we offer
Our prayerful gratitude to God,
He blesses us immediately
With a newly awakened
Satisfaction life.

22700

When my mind becomes
A sea of tranquility,
God blessingfully greets my mind
With a most beautiful victory-garland.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 227, Agni Press, 1996
