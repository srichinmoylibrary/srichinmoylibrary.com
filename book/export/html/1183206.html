70. Live

Live in the heart-rose,
You will learn the song
Of sinless progress.
Live in the soul-lotus,
You will learn the dance
Of shadowless progress.
Live in the God-Fragrance,
You will learn the secret
Of endless progress.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
