39. God is there to see

God is there to see.
Why do you worry?

God has asked you to start.
Why do you tarry?

God has asked you to comply.
Why do you not use your own
Heart's oneness high?

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
