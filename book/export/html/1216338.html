704. One thing I shall know

Three things I knew:
How to announce myself,
How to multiply myself,
How to entangle myself.
Two things I know:
How to give,
How to receive.
One thing I shall know:
How to become God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 15, Agni Press, 1974
