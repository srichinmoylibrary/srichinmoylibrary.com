7249 No Time To Criticise

Those who serve the world constantly
Do not have time
To criticise others,
While those who do not serve
Others selflessly
Have endless time
To criticise the whole world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 73, Agni Press, 1983
