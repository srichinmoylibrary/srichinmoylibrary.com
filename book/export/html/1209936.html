24060

God has no time
To build a road
Inside my mind
To reach Him.
But God is busy
Every day
Building a new road
Inside my heart
So that I can arrive
At my Destination
And forever live there.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 241, Agni Press, 1998
