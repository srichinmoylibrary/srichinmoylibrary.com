21 April 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
What I need is more intensity
   In my aspiring heart.
What I need is more immensity
   In my God-searching mind.
What I need is more humility
   In my God-serving life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 April 1999, 7:25 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine — up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
