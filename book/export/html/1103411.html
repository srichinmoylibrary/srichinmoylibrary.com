27 April 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart's soulful smile
Is my God-satisfying
   Sacred secret.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 7:21 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
