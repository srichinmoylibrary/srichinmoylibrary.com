961 God's Promise-Filled Days...

GOD'S PROMISE-FILLED DAYS

Earth's pleasure-filled days
Have disappointed me
Badly.

Heaven's treasure-filled days
Have deserted me
Secretly.

God's promise-filled days
Have forgotten me
Unmistakably.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
