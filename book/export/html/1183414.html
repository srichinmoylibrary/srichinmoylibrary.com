278. Do you know

Do you know why God has patience
In boundless measure?
Just to make me His representative on earth.
Just to make me His prototype.
Just to make me His equal.
Just to make of me another God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
