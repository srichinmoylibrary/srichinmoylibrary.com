325. Who says

Who says
You have to become
A God-believer first
If you want to be
A conscientious objector?
It is not at all true.
You just need firm faith
In your discovery
Of wisdom-light.
If that is done,
You do not have to bring God into the picture.
After all, poor God,
He has many more important things to do.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
