26360

When I love my Lord Supreme
As a drowning man or as a starving man,
I please myself.
But when I love my Lord Supreme
As a self-giving man,
I please my Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 264, Agni Press, 1998
