758 Claims...

CLAIMS

Day-king claims my eyes
To be of service to him.
Night-queen claims my heart
To be of service to her.
I claim my life
To be of service to God-Dream
And
Man-transformation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
