42.

Mornings come to me
With the sound-power
Of the Unknown.
Evenings come to me
With the silence-peace
Of the Unknowable.
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Comes to me both in the mornings
And in the evenings
With what He has and what He is:
Concern.

21 July 2001
New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 2004
