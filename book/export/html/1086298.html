I have given you

I have given you,
  Already given you,
     And
  I am ready to give you even more
     In case you need more,
        My dear friend.
Strangely enough,
You have never shown
  Any sign of gratitude
     For my God-manifestation,
        God-perfection
           Gifts.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A hundred years from now, Agni Press, 1974
