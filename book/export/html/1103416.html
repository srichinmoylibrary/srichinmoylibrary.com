27 April 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My prayerful and soulful determination
Has a special name given by You:
You call my determination
   Your Heart-hero.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 9:01 p.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
