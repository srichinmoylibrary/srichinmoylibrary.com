100. The exiles

Yesterday
His insincerity was exiled
From the face of light.

Today
His impurity is exiled
From the heart of light.

Tomorrow
His insecurity will be exiled
From the life of light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
