492. All are unhappy

You are unhappy
That you are not God.

He is unhappy
That he is not a divine man.

I am unhappy
That still I do not have
The necessary capacity
To escape the embrace
Of the animal-kingdom.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
