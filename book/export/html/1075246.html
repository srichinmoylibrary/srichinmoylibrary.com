671 Two Things I Kill...

TWO THINGS I KILL

I kill two things to survive:
My useless doubt
And
My hopeless fear.

God loves two things to survive:
His ceaseless Compassion
And
His sleepless Forgiveness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 14, Agni Press, 1973
