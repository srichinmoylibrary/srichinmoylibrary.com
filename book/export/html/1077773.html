126. Your soul is adored

Your mind
Lives in the doubt-world;
Therefore
Your mind is pitied.

Your body
Lives in the ignorance-world;
Therefore
Your body is ignored.

Your vital
Lives in hell;
Therefore
Your vital is harried.

Your heart
Lives in Heaven;
Therefore
Your heart is hallowed.

Your soul
Lives on earth;
Therefore
Your soul is adored.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
