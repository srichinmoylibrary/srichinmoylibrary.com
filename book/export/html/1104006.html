17 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May each day the tears and smiles
Of my heart
Develop a deeper, stronger and greater
God-hunger.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/17 December 1999, 7:37 a.m. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Before lifting 350 lbs. with each arm simultaneously four times. (Total: 700 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
