16 I Shall Not Control ...

I shall not control
And dominate anybody.
I shall not lead
And guide anybody.
I shall only love everybody
And be for everybody.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 56, Agni Press, 2008
