1. What I shall tell

I know what I shall
Have to tell the world:
"I have cried."

I know what I shall
Have to tell myself:
"I have failed."

I know what I shall
Have to tell Heaven:
"I have smiled."

I know what I shall
Have to tell God:
"I have succeeded."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giving and becoming, Agni Press, 1974
