13 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My dreamboat-life
Is sailing and sailing and sailing
In my Lord's
Compassion-Heart-Dream-Sea.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 September 1999, 8:20 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 70 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
