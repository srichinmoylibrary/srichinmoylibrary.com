1237.

The difference between
My mind and my heart is this:
My mind questions God's Justice;
My heart bathes in God's Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, Part 13, Agni Press, 1996
