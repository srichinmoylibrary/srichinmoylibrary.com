A Time to Love

There is always
A time to love,
A time to serve.
When the morning dawns,
Love, love,
Become the breath of love.
When evening starts,
Serve, serve,
And be the breath, fulfilling and fulfilled,
Of God above.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 6, , 1981
