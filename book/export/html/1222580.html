Ore Amar Deha Ashru Nadir Geha

Ore amar deha
Ashru nadir geha
Ore amar pran
Pashan purir gan
Ore amar man
Gabhir nishar ban
Ore amar hiya
Bishwa prabhur priya

Translation:

O my body,
You are the home of the river of tears.
O my vital,
You are the song of heartless city.
O my mind,
You are the forest of tenebrous night.
O my heart,
You are the love of the World-Lord.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Garden of love-light, part 1, , 1974
