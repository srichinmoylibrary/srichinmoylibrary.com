409 A God-Man

A desire-man
Is a dreamer.

An aspiration-man
Is a builder.

A realisation-man
Is a God-lover.

A God-man
Is the bringer of
A new creation,
A new harmony,
A new perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 5, Agni Press, 1979
