Amar Lagi Tumi Ano

Amar lagi tumi ano
Tomar madhu banshi
Tomar lagi ami ani
Amar chapal hasi

Translation:

You bring into my heart
Your sweet, haunting,
And melodious flute
For me to play.
I enter into Your Heart
With my uncontrolled
And restive smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My green adoration-gifts, part 1, , 1977
