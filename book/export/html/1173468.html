540 It Will Help

Cry, My child,
It will help your heart.
Smile, My child,
It will help your soul.
Serve, My child,
It will help your life.
Love, My child,
It will help not only your goal
But also My Role.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 6, Agni Press, 1979
