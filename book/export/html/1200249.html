14469

The difference between
A doer and a watcher
Is that the doer is on
His safe and certain highway
To God's Infinity,
Whereas the watcher can be
Either tomorrow's starter
Or tomorrow's sleeper.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 145, Agni Press, 1991
