3 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
That the more concern I have
For humanity,
The more confidence You will have
In me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 August 1999, 1:00 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
