Havis

Offering

When we give to God as an offering both our desire-life and our aspiration-life, God tells us that the offering of our desire-life helps us run toward Him, and the offering of our aspiration-life helps Him run toward us.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Core Of India's Light, Part 3, Agni Press, 1992
