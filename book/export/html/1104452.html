9 October 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
With God-loving tears
I came into the world.
With God-serving smiles
I shall depart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 October 2000, 10:20 a.m. Before doing 800-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
