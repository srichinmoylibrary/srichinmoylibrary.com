3801 True Freedom Will Come

True freedom will come,
But where will it come from?
Not from the height
Of my mind-tower,
But from the bliss
Of God's unconditional
Compassion-Shower.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 39, Agni Press, 1982
