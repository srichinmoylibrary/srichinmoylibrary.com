Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 144

14301

Even before I start
My morning prayers,
I see a stream of blessings
Descending into my heart.

14302

What your life needs
Is illumination
And never ovation.

14303

If your idol is mental idleness,
Then psychic happiness and fulness
Will never come into your existence.

14304

Perfection-performance is the result
Of a slow, steady and unerring
Preparation.

14305

In the new edition of your life-book,
There should be a chapter
On your unconditional surrender.

14306

You may not expect ingratitude
From the world.
Nevertheless,
The world will bless you
With ingratitude,
Without fail.

14307

How is it
That you do not notice
You have a small mind
But a big ego?

14308

When God asks you to do something,
He does not set any deadline.
But if you want to satisfy Him
In His own Way,
Then do everything for Him
Faster than the fastest!

14309

Unlike earth's rules and regulations,
God's rules and regulations
Are always easy to follow.

14310

If you can please God
With your aspiration-flames,
Then the realisation-sun
Is within your easy reach.

14311

To kill time
Is to force the soul
To forget about
Its God-manifestation-promise.

14312

If you take meditation
As an obligation,
Then your life's emancipation
Will always remain a far cry.

14313

Take the inner, soulful initiative.
The outer success
Will fast approach you.

14314

What can substitute for
The inner satisfaction?
Nothing, absolutely nothing!

14315

Your forward surrender-steps
Make God happy
Far beyond your imagination!

14316

God is ready to give you
His blessingful Consciousness-Crown.
Just show ignorance
Your life's disgust-frown.

14317

Your God-manifestation-awareness
And eagerness
Do not underestimate!

14318

Alas, three promises we forget
When we enter into the world:
God-realisation,
God-manifestation
And earth-transformation.

14319

If you are not a genuine seeker,
God will definitely not allow you
To enter into His Heart-Temple.

14320

O my mind,
In so many years on earth
You have learned so little,
And what's more,
Even that was too late.

14321

Your mind's desire-dust
Has polluted your heart's
Aspiration-cries.

14322

May my mind every morning
Become as beautiful
As hope-dawn-rays.

14323

Your life-boat is sinking.
But even now
Your heart is not bringing God
Into the picture.

14324

Constant silent prayer
Receives a dream-boat
With golden sails
From Above.

14325

What are you searching for?
I am searching for my heart's
Birthless and deathless sun.

14326

When you have done away with
Your frustration-life,
You are blessed with
Aspiration-acceleration.

14327

If you are serious
About your self-transformation,
Then God will make
Your self-examination-course
Extremely easy.

14328

It is in the unknown sea
Of the Beyond
That I feel my permanent home.

14329

Unseal the treasures of your soul.
Your sadness-mind and madness-vital
Will no longer function.

14330

Small is humanity's aspiration-cry.
Smaller is humanity's realisation-smile.
Smallest is humanity's perfection-dance.

14331

Every day God hopes to have
A new, illumining, fulfilling member
In His universal Oneness-Family.

14332

When your heart learns to weep
Only for God,
Your ignorance-sleep
Will come to an end.

14333

I would like to play a game
Where there will be
No winner, no loser,
But only the enjoyer
Of the game itself.

14334

God will always provide us
With opportunities
To find the real path
To reach the perfection-shore.

14335

God wants to create
A compassion-continent
In your sleeplessly self-giving life.

14336

Use this moment
To empty your mind,
Enlarge your heart
And invoke God's Compassion-Rain.

14337

To live is to obey;
To obey is to fulfil
Both the receiver and the giver
In the world of inner aspiration
And outer dedication.

14338

My heart is a regular subscriber
To God's Heaven-singing
And earth-crying Newspaper.

14339

God never asked me
To change anybody's mind.
He asked me only
To enlarge my heart.

14340

My human pride tells me
God is complete
Only with me.
My divine wisdom tells me
That out of His infinite Compassion
God invites me to play with Him
To complete His Game.

14341

How can you be so cruel
As to ignore your soul-child's
Helpless cries?

14342

The outer adventure
Makes us happy.
The inner adventure
Makes us perfect.

14343

Is there any day
When we are not imprisoned
By our ego's hurtful hallucinations?

14344

You do not have to blame temptation
When it comes.
Just resist it!

14345

God will become
The Director of your life-movie
Only after you have prayed to Him
To become an unconditional, earth-loving,
Heaven-fulfilling actor.

14346

To doubt one's own inner capacities
Is nothing short of drinking poison
Made by oneself.

14347

God may wish to remain anonymous
When He does something for us.
But He never wants us to remain anonymous
When we cry to sing His Victory-Song.

14348

God, will there be a day
When I do not have to face
The torture of my mind's
Shameless tyranny?

14349

What keeps
A human being alive?
His progress-rainbow-hope!

14350

Don't waste your time
Thinking of Eternity.
Do immediately
What you are supposed to do.
Since Eternity exists,
It will definitely bring you the reward
For your today's self-giving.

14351

The mind's ignorance-night
Threatens the beauty and purity
Of the heart's day.

14352

The clever ignorance-enemy
Has taken
My innocent progress-friend.

14353

I left my God-manifestation-dream
In Heaven.
I lost my God-realisation-dream
On earth.

14354

If you do not have
A sincere heart-cry,
You will not be able to fight
Against the hostile forces
In the inner world.

14355

When I live inside
My gratitude-heart-home,
God's Compassion-Eye
Is my immediate neighbour.

14356

Since God does not sell peace
In His Universal Shop,
I shall have to make it
Inside my heart-home.

14357

Nothing can ever eclipse
My life's
Purity-heart-moon.

14358

Insecurity and humiliation
Are unconsciously
Inseparable friends.

14359

Your heart-whisper
Is tremendously loud.
Even the distant God
Can hear it.

14360

A perfect disciple's life
Is worth all the trouble
Previously created by him.

14361

A calculating mind
God never appreciates.
A self-giving heart
God not only loves
But also treasures.

14362

According to God,
Everybody has natural,
Illumining and fulfilling
Aspiration-capacity.

14363

The inner doctor
Always finds
That the outer patients
Are incurable
Precisely because
They are too impatient.

14364

Nothing beautiful,
Nothing meaningful,
Nothing fruitful
Can be produced
By the fear-factory
Of the human mind.

14365

My Lord,
I really do not want to wait
For Eternity
For the transformation
Of my human nature.

14366

O my mind,
When will you start running forward
Instead of walking backward?

14367

Every day my soul and I
Are desperately looking
For a burial ground
For my past blunders.

14368

There can be no dead end
On purity's
Happiness-progress-highway.

14369

O my mind, it is you
Who are deliberately enjoying
A self-imposed exile
From God's Compassion-Kingdom.

14370

God wants my soul to know
Who is who
In the aspiration-world.
God wants my heart to know
Who is who
In the dedication-world.

14371

Yesterday my aspiration-account
Was short of funds.
Today my dedication-account
Is short of funds.
Tomorrow my surrender-account
Will be short of funds.
Alas! Alas! Alas!

14372

A genuine seeker
Will definitely be found
Between his life's greatness
And his heart's goodness.

14373

He who wants to make
The fastest progress
Should have this motto:
"Now is the only time!"

14374

Alas,
Is there any day
When my mind's enthusiasm-flag
Is not at half-mast?

14375

Aspiration-road and dedication-road
Are never bound
By any speed limit.

14376

Only an unconditional seeker
Sits for the final
God-realisation-examination.

14377

My Lord's Protection-Eye
Has always played the role
Of my life's protection-armour.

14378

God is immensely surprised
When the human mind
Follows the divine heart.

14379

When my Lord sees a soulful thought
Inside my mind,
He asks His Soul-Bird to dance
To my heartbeat.

14380

To speak with divine authority
You have to cry like an orphan
For luminosity.

14381

If you do not light your heart-candle,
Then peace in your human mind
Is nothing short of impossibility.

14382

Is there any earthly problem
That does not have
A purifying and illumining purpose?

14383

God-manifestation
Is the fulfilment of my life's
Beginningless and deathless dream.

14384

Your fears are unfounded.
When the opportune time arrives,
Both God the Compassion
And God the Perfection
Will come to you.

14385

The human mind asks,
"Why? Why? Why?"
The divine heart asks,
"Why not? Why not? Why not?"

14386

Fastest progress-privilege
Is meant only for those
Who can cheerfully
And unconditionally wait
For God's choice Hour.

14387

The giant-self inside my heart
Is always amused
When it sees the dwarf-self
Inside my mind.

14388

When God gives
His Renunciation-Delight-Sermon,
Immediately the church
Becomes empty!

14389

No self-giving here on earth
Will remain unrecognised,
Unapplauded
And unfulfilled.

14390

Run with your longest
Enthusiasm-strides.
God will grant you
His Ocean-Heart-Love.

14391

My sincere aspiration-heart
Shortens the life span
Of my doubting mind.

14392

Intensify your heart's
Aspiration-cries.
Realisation-sunrise
Will beckon you.

14393

My fondness
For my doubting mind
Pollutes the soulfulness
Of my heart.

14394

God sings the song of perfection
Only when I become
The voiceless voice
Of my silence-heart.

14395

God's earth-planet
Is very fragile,
But His peace-planet
Is His Infinity's Power.

14396

In the inner world I call it
My heart's aspiration-entrance.
In the outer world I call it
My mind's desire-exit.

14397

O my vital,
I am so tired of listening
To your never-ending
Failure-sadness-stories.

14398

The fastest wings
Of the time-bird
No mortal can ever dare
To challenge.

14399

God's Justice-Light
May not wait for you,
But His Compassion-Height
Will definitely wait for you,
No matter how late you are.

14400

Slowly and steadily
If you walk with gratitude-steps,
Then lovingly and convincingly
God will come toward you
With plenitude-treasures.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 144, Agni Press, 1991
