214. Say not anything against me

O life,
I love you.
I love your sound-wave.
Therefore,
Say not anything against me.

O death,
I love you.
I love your silence-peace.
Therefore,
Say not anything against me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
