19 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
It is only when the tears of my heart
   Place me at God's Feet
That God accepts me unreservedly.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 October 1999, 7:04 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 150 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
