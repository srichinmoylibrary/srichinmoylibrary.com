9961 My Mind Admires God

My mind admires God
Because He is powerful.
My heart loves God
Because He is beautiful.
My life needs God
Because He is merciful.
My soul reveals God
Because He is fruitful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 100, Agni Press, 1983
