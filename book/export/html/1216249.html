617. My desire stays on

Morning comes and morning goes,
Evening comes and evening goes.
My aspiration comes,
My aspiration goes.
But
My desire neither comes
Nor goes;
It just stays on and on.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
