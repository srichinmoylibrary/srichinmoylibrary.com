290. Leave me, love me

O my thought-life,
Leave me, leave me alone.
I am sick of you.

O my soul-will,
Love me, guide me.
I need you alone.
Don't you see and feel
I am all fondness
And
All admiration for you?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
