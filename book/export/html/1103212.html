25 November 1998 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You are compassionately asking me
To correct and perfect
My unbelief and disbelief-mind,
So that I can be sleeplessly
And breathlessly happy.
My Supreme,
I shall fulfil Your kind Request.
I shall fulfil Your supreme Command.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

25 November 1998, 5:14 a.m. Before lifting 145 lbs with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
