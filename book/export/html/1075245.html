THE DIVINE CHINA SHOP

Simplicity
Sincerity
Humility
Purity

He sells in his divine China Shop.
His prices are very low.

Alas,
Doubt, the cosmic bull,
Very often
Upsets everything,
Breaks everything,
Destroys everything.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 14, Agni Press, 1973
