211. You, too, have a place

You are the doubter
And
The teeming doubts;
Therefore
Nobody needs you.
But I love you
Because you too
Have a place at God's Feet
To sing your transformation-song.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
