598

Someday the body will give to the soul
What it has:
Its aspiration-hunger.
Someday the soul will give to the body
What it has:
Its realisation-smile,
A divine meal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 6, Agni Press, 1983
