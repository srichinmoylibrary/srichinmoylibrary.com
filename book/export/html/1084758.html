18 Do You Love?...

DO YOU LOVE?

Do you love man?
Then give him a cheerful smile.
Do you love God?
Then give him a soulful cry.
Do you love yourself?
Then give yourself a powerful assurance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 16, Agni Press, 1974
