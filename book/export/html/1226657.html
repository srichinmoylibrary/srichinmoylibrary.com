Shatek Juger Basana Chhariya

Shatek juger basana chhariya
Nijere sampinu aji
Ogo dayamoy ogo premamoy
Eso hiya tari majhi

Translation:

Abandoning my desire-life
Of millions of years,
I have placed myself today at Your Feet.
O my Lord of Compassion,
O my Lord of Love, do come.
O my heart-Boatman, do come.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
