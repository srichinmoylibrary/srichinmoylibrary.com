May 21

When one desires, one appropriates.
When he aspires, he appropriates more than he gives. When he realises God, he just gives.
Yet, he sacrifices nothing because he knows that his gifts of today are his receipts of yesterday.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flame-Goal, Agni Press, 1973
