6. Because of you

Lord, You love me proudly;
   Therefore
You are on my lips
   Always.

Lord, I love You devotedly;
   Therefore
I am on Your Lips
   Always.

Lord, because of me
Your Boat may sink.
"Son, because of you
   My Boat will sail
        Steadily,
        Unerringly,
            And
        Supremely."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 11, Agni Press, 1974
