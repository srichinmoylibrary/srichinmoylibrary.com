388. When

When doubt ceases,
Faith begins.
When fear ceases,
Courage begins.
When hatred ceases,
Love begins.
When jealousy ceases,
Oneness begins.

When faith ceases,
   I am lost.
When courage ceases,
   I am caught.
When love ceases,
   I am useless.
When oneness ceases,
   Everything ceases.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 8, Agni Press, 1973
