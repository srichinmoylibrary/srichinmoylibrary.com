2670 Sleep And Dream

Sleep and dream.
If you sleep,
You will go beyond hope's reach.
If you dream,
You will expedite God's Manifestation-Promise
Here on earth
And God's Satisfaction-Delight
There in Heaven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
