Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 268

26701

The human life is, indeed,
A golden
God-serving opportunity.

26702

A soulful smile is, indeed,
A messenger descended from
God's celestial Palace.

26703

Self-giving is, indeed,
An indispensable piece
Of God-Wisdom in us.

26704

When God hears that His dear ones
Have given up the spiritual life,
He deeply sighs and mourns.

26705

When God sees that His dear ones
Have increased their aspiration,
He congratulates them
Far beyond their imagination.

26706

God the Compassion only
Connects the road between
My determination
And God-Satisfaction.

26707

O disobedience-mind,
Be careful!
You will soon be imprisoned
By my aspiration-heart â€”
Indefinitely!

26708

My faith in the world
Goes far beyond
The deplorable deeds
Of the world.

26709

My heart-bird
Sleeplessly enjoys
Soaring aspiration-flight.

26710

Each time I soulfully pray,
I become the beauty
Of my heart-blossom.

26711

A God-crying heart
Is
A God-manifesting smile.

26712

A desire-bondage-freed chain
Our life becomes
When our aspiration reaches
Its zenith-height.

26713

What is humility,
If not our soul's nobility
Revealed in and through
Our lives?

26714

May each human being become
A peace-loving God-dreamer,
God-lover and God-server.

26715

I am so proud of myself
That I am captured by
My Lord's
Compassion-Protection-Feet.

26716

If we are chosen instruments
Of God,
Then we must only support
The good qualities of others
And deliberately connive
At their weaknesses.

26717

I call it disobedience.
My Lord calls it
My life-disaster.

26718

An unconditional surrender
Is
A God-blossoming beauty.

26719

An unconditional surrender
Is
A God-becoming fragrance.

26720

There is no fixed hour
For our aspiration-heart-journey.
We can at any moment start.

26721

When we pray and meditate well,
We are blessed by God
With His Satisfaction-Pride-Surprise.

26722

May my heart and I
Live always
In our God-gratitude-life.

26723

How stupid we are!
We are seeking security
In weak man-made
Weaker sanctuaries.

26724

When all else is taken away
From our life,
We see God's Compassion-Eye
Is still with us and for us.

26725

Alas, our fear and doubt
Are infinitely more powerful
Than our faith in God.

26726

Each heart-experience
Is an expansion
Of God-Fulfilment
On earth.

26727

Frustration frowns at us
When we do not regularly feed
Our heart's
Aspiration-cries.

26728

Never regret the past.
Just go forward,
Fly upward,
Dive inward.
Be a man of wisdom-delight.

26729

Freedom, freedom!
Freedom within, freedom without,
To allow
Beautiful, soulful
And powerful creativity
To take birth in us.

26730

God's Compassion-Eye
Is the driving force
Behind man's goodness-actions.

26731

My Lord,
Since You love me so dearly,
How is it that You do not shelter me
From life's constant ups and downs?

26732

In my case,
The absence of God
Is not only heartbreaking,
But also God-Delight-denying.

26733

Every morning we must charge
Our aspiration-heart
To inspire our
God-manifestation-service-life.

26734

No miracle
Can ever truly feed
Our genuine God-hunger.

26735

God loves us
Just the way we are,
But He can and He will love us
Infinitely more
If we become what He wants us
To be.

26736

God's Eye
Is
God's Compassion-Umbrella
Wide open to protect us.

26737

If you want to bring about peace,
Never pay any attention
To your imperfections,
But pay all attention
To your readiness, willingness
And eagerness
For world-transformation.

26738

Each godless dream
Ends in
Man-frustration-torture.

26739

No ardent aspiration-attempt
Will ever end
In failure.

26740

There is not a single seeker
On earth
Who has never passed through
The valley of uncertainty.

26741

Each day is my soul's
God-manifestation-commitment
On earth.

26742

If we keep our newness-mind alive,
Then our life will be
A Fulness-God-Blessing-delight.

26743

Our Lord Supreme asks our souls
To intercede
When the mind and the heart
Are not on good terms.

26744

May my God-satisfaction-hunger
Remain permanent
In my inner aspiration-life.

26745

Nobody can deny the fact
That his life has been
A long-standing friendship
With ignorance-night.

26746

Each time we are relaxed
In our spiritual life,
We are forced to suffer
God-disobedience-outbreak.

26747

If we do not take our aspiration-life
Seriously,
Then ours will be an unfulfilled
God-satisfaction-future.

26748

In everything else
We take risks,
But in our God-faith-investment
There can be no risk.

26749

God wants us to be
Aspiration-dreamers,
Aspiration-lovers
And, finally,
Aspiration-dispensers.

26750

What is doubt,
If not complete distortion
Of God-belief in us?

26751

Self-confidence is, indeed,
The most powerful result
Of our selflessness-life.

26752

God blesses humanity
Every time He smiles
From the very depth
Of His Heart.

26753

Perfection playfully
Hides behind
Our God-surrender-door.

26754

A fault-finding man
Eventually becomes
A truth-empty life.

26755

My only hope is that one day
My mind will more sincerely
Invoke the presence
Of silence.

26756

Impatience is nothing but
An unnoticed,
Self-defeating experience.

26757

The mind-bloated books
Are not worth reading â€”
No, not even worth opening!

26758

May my life be a tiny heartbeat
Of my Lord's
Constant Awareness.

26759

My Lord, do tell me,
When will I wake up
Once and for all
From the lap of ignorance-night?

26760

The stark stupidity
Of human beings
Even baffles
Divinity's Wisdom-Light.

26761

Our heart does not know
How our mind can be
So enamoured
Of stark ignorance-night.

26762

The tears of my heart
Smile through my Lord's Eye
Every day.

26763

Ignorance is so cruel
That it tells me
It has temptation
To intimidate me.

26764

Wisdom-heart tells me
That it has its purity-sword
To challenge ignorance.

26765

The happiness of the heart
Is the freedom of life,
Even when we live
In ignorance-prison.

26766

The human mind does not comprehend
Anything divine
That is shaping and moulding our lives
For our perfection.

26767

God is looking for you.
Can you not even for a second
Stay inside your heart-garden?

26768

There is no stop sign
On my
God-pleasing road.

26769

God may become tired at times
Of our stupidity,
But God-Forgiveness
Never becomes tired.

26770

The mind by nature
Is poverty-stricken
In the realm of spirituality.

26771

May my heart, mind, vital
And body
Always have plenty of hope
To live on.

26772

My Lord is telling me
Again and again
To give Him the opportunity
To worry about all my problems.

26773

Alas, I have given up everything
Save and except one thing:
My conscious God-dividing mind.

26774

I have decided to tell the world
That I have nothing else to do
But please God
At every moment.

26775

In a sea of silence,
The soul reprimands
The God-doubting life.

26776

A God-surrender-life
Is bound to win
In the battlefield of life.

26777

What you call
Your heart's gratitude,
God calls that very thing
A special banquet.

26778

When I repeatedly
Do something wrong,
God's sorrowful Tears
Inundate His Eyes.

26779

Every morning,
In the small hours of the day,
I am all eager
For my Lord's wake-up Call.

26780

The undivine doubt
Has nothing appreciable.
It interferes with
God's Peace-manifestation
On earth.

26781

Disobedience
Is always a fatal case
In a seeker's life.

26782

God has read my heart-book
Over and over again.
No page remains unread
By God.
Therefore, I am swimming
In ecstasy-sea.

26783

Be brave
Day in and day out
To meet with your destiny.

26784

Never avoid, O seeker,
The inner responsibility
To love God
In God's own Way.

26785

God's world-satisfaction-story
God Himself reads out
From time to time.

26786

If you are not good enough
To listen to God,
Then when will you become
A God-instrument
In your life?

26787

Every morning
My soul wants my heart
To enjoy the rising sun
Of peace, light and bliss.

26788

We must not wait for anything
Until tomorrow,
Never!

26789

My Lord's Will
Is my Lord's
Only abiding Satisfaction.

26790

Unlike us,
God never believes in
Authoritative pronouncements.

26791

Peace
Can never be something
Far beyond our reach.

26792

If we are true seekers,
Then we must put forth
Our own self-offering
To God's Will.

26793

The inner life
Of a spiritual seeker
Has to overflow
With dynamic energy.

26794

When we take even one step upward,
God the Smiler comes down
And greets us.

26795

Surrender, gratitude and bliss:
These are the three
Most powerful branches
Of our life-tree.

26796

My devotion-heart
Can live without anything,
As long as it can claim
God's Compassion-Eye
As its own, very own.

26797

There is only one place
For me to take shelter,
And that place is
My Lord's Compassion-Eye.

26798

No matter how or when
My heart sings
For my Lord Supreme,
My Lord is all appreciation,
All joy and gratitude.

26799

May my life every day
Live in the unfathomable depth
Of my soul-illumination.

26800

My soul has reserved
A special seat for my heart
At God's Nectar-flooded Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 268, Agni Press, 1998
