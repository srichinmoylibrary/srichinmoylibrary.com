Sun, moon, stars

1.

The outer sun is eternally, sleeplessly and breathlessly at work to accomplish something stupendous for the inner sun.

2.

The morning sun gives us inspiration-flood.

The evening sun gives us aspiration-sea.

3.

Every time I look at the full moon I receive the ecstasy-fragrance of love.

4.

The full moon immediately takes away my mind’s hostility towards my enemies.

5.

The silence of the setting sun so sweetly and compassionately speaks to my heart.

6.

The sun tells me to give in abundance and never to lend.

7.

When I pray and meditate, I see a self-transcendence-sun in my heart-sky.

8.

The sweet whisper of the evening sun calms my turbulent mind immediately.

9.

The morning sun weeps if I do not pray.

The evening sun sighs if I do not meditate.

10.

O evening sun, your infinite patience awakens the sleeping peace inside my heart.

11.

O blazing sun, you tell me that death can be delayed.

12.

Each star is nothing other than a magic and magnetic smile.

13.

May I be ready every day to appreciate the beauty of Mother Earth when the sun knocks at my heart-door.

14.

O sun, moon and stars, how I wish you also got some vacation every year, which you so rightly deserve.

15.

The sun loves dearly even a late riser.

16.

The sun helps to diminish suffering in our life.

The moon helps increase joy in our lives.

17.

I want the sun to be the governor of my life.

I want the moon to be the dreamer of my heart.

18.

The sun comes to me as a song-composer.

The moon comes to me as a storyteller.

19.

Neither the sun nor the moon is ever anxious to complete the endless race.

20.

When my mind needs power, I invoke the sun.

When my heart needs peace, I invoke the moon.

When my life needs illumination, I invoke the stars.

21.

If you want to shine like the sun, then start singing your life’s self-offering song.

22.

The flowers say to the morning sun, 'O sun, do give us your beauty.'

23.

The morning sun says to the flowers, 'O flowers, do give me your sweetness.'

24.

If it is true that the mind is ignorance-night, then it is equally true that the heart is wisdom-sun.

25.

I do not want to be the possessor of the sunrise, but I want to be the lover of the sunrise.

26.

I do not want to be just a lover of the moonlit night but I want to be a dreamer of the full moon night.

27.

A new sun is for a new hope.

A new hope is for a new promise.

28.

Every day the outer sun and the outer moon are the same, but not the inner sun and the inner moon.

29.

My struggle-night was. My struggle-night still is. But my struggle-night will surrender before long to my victory-sun.

30.

My mind’s readiness runs fast, very fast, towards the sun.

31.

My heart’s willingness runs fast, very fast, towards the moon.

32.

My mind’s eagerness runs fast, very fast, towards the stars.

33.

A commitment-flame gives birth to a contentment-sun.

34.

May my heart every day sing with the rising sun.

35.

May my heart every day dance with the rising moon.

36.

O morning sun, you give my mind what it does not have: joy.

37.

O setting sun, you give my heart what it does not have: peace.

38.

The morning stars tell me where I come from: the unknown.

39.

The evening stars tell me where I must go: the unknowable.

40.

My concentration-mind is a boon from the sun.

41.

My meditation-heart is a boon from the moon.

42.

When I give cheerfully, I become a rising star.

43.

When I receive reluctantly, I become a setting star.

44.

The morning star tells me not to think of the past.

45.

The evening star tells me to think only of the future.

46.

Achievement-promise I receive from the rising sun.

47.

Perfection-fulfilment I receive from the rising moon.

48.

My mind is an ascending cry to the sun.

49.

My heart is a descending smile from the sun.

50.

The sun tells me: 'Be not afraid of the past. My compassion will take care of you.'

51.

The moon tells me: 'Be not afraid of the future. My affection will take care of you.'

52.

My tormented mind is cradled by the soothing evening stars.

53.

The morning stars are promising me to bless me with my life’s transformation-blossoms.

54.

The stars command my mind to stop its restless and useless game.

55.

The stars command my heart to start its birthless and deathless game.

56.

To the morning sun I offer my praying heart cheerfully.

57.

To the evening sun I offer my loving life unconditionally.

58.

A peace-empty sky is a moon-empty night.

59.

How I wish I could be in my outer life as beautiful as my twinkling heart-stars.

60.

The silence-eye of the sun is indeed the delight-face of the universe.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sun, moon, stars, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1995
