3626 Resist The Power Of Darkness

Insist on bringing to the fore
What you inwardly have.
Resist the power of darkness within and without
That tells you
Yours is the long, longer, longest way
To reach the fulness of Perfection-Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
