1 January 2005 (II)

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

The message of self-transcendence
My soul receives from You every day,
My heart receives from my soul every day
And I receive from my heart every day.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 17, Agni Press, 2005
