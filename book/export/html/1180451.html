7454 Integrally Perfect

Yesterday
I desired to become a great man.
My Lord said,
"That is not enough."

Today
I desire to become a good man.
My Lord says,
"That is not enough."

I ask my Lord,
"Is there anything
Beyond greatness and goodness?"

My Lord says,
"Yes, I want you to be
Integrally perfect."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 75, Agni Press, 1983
