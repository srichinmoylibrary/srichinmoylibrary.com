13030

My aspiration-elevator is ascending fast,
Not because I have done something great
But because I have made
A solemn promise to God
That I am ready to make
My unconditional surrender to Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 131, Agni Press, 1990
