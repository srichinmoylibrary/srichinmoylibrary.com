4613 When Will You Free Me?

My Lord,
When will You free me
From the wild impatience of my mind,
When ?

My Lord,
When will You free me
From the blind ingratitude of my heart,
When?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 47, Agni Press, 1982
