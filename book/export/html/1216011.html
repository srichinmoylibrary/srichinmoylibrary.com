384. For your sake

O Lord,
For Your sake
I am doing the right thing.

For Your sake
I am loving everything and everyone.

For Your sake
I am becoming the pride of
Heaven and earth.

I want nothing,
I need nothing
For myself
Either from Heaven-city
Or from earth-village.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 8, Agni Press, 1974
