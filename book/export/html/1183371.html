235. Yesterday, today, tomorrow

Yesterday
I saw your soul-flames.
You cannot imagine
How beautiful they are.

Today
I am seeing your soul-fire.
You cannot imagine
How powerful it is.

Tomorrow
I shall be seeing your soul-sun.
Both you and I
Will fail to know
How fruitful it is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
