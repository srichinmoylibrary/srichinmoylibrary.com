164. Aspiration

In the morning,
God in me is the giver
Of aspiration.
At noon,
God in me is the liver
Of aspiration.
In the evening,
God in me is both lover
And fulfiller
Of aspiration.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
