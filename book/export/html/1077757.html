110. Where is Heaven?

Where is Heaven?
Not in my ignorant sound-life.

Where is Heaven?
Not in my undying life-tears.

Where is Heaven?
Heaven is in my God-building heart.

Where is Heaven?
Heaven is in my self-offering light
And
God-becoming delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
