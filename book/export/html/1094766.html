My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 14

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Malaysia in February 2002.

3 February 2002 ...

3 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

1 A Birthday ...

A birthday
Is the blossoming
Of a new
Consciousness-delight-dream.

2 The World Demands ...

The world demands
The very best from me.

3 My Lord Desires ...

My Lord desires
To have
Everything of mine.

4 The Final And Last Choice ...

The final and last choice
Has to be made here and now.
No more chances
Shall be granted.

5 Not My Outer Belief ...

Not my outer belief,
But my inner faith
That decides who God is,
Where God is
And what God actually expects
From my life.

6 The Outer Success ...

The outer success
Reaches the fleeting
Pride-crown-destination.

7 The Inner Progress ...

The inner progress
Reaches the abiding
Happiness-throne-goal.

8 What We Have ...

What we have
Is a false outer smile.
What we need
Is a sincere inner cry.

9 An Open Mind ...

An open mind
Is a very rare achievement.
A God-surrender-life
Is a God-achievement.

4 February 2002 ...

4 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

10 I Must Examine ...

I must examine
The beauty and fragrance
Of my own happiness-heart
Before I offer them
To humanity’s heart.

11 My Knowledge-Light ...

My knowledge-light
Is the quantity-beggar.
My wisdom-delight
Is the quality-chooser.
My Lord Supreme tells me
Not to worry.
He will gladly take care of
The beggar in me
And the chooser in me.
I just have to love Him only
And need Him only.

12 To Me, The Highest Height ...

To me, the highest height
Is not the happiest privilege,
But the greatest and heaviest
Responsibility.

13 My Lord Has Chosen ...

My Lord has chosen
My utterly insignificant life
To accomplish the impossible:
The full manifestation of God
Here on earth.

14 If My Life Really Desires ...

If my life really desires
To be a God-server,
Then God’s Heart will be
My life’s untiring supplier.

15 I Measure My Life-Boundaries ...

I measure my life-boundaries.
My Lord counts only
My heart-garden-flowers.

16 There Are God-Doubters ...

There are God-doubters.
There are God-believers as well.
Who can prevent me
From being a God-believer,
And who can prevent me
From being a God-lover,
A God-server
And, finally, a God-manifester?

17 In God’s Eye ...

In God’s Eye
I am never insignificant.
In God’s Heart
I am eternally and infinitely
More significant
Than I can ever imagine.

18 My Life-Book Begins With ...

My life-book begins with
The God-aspiration-chapter
And ends with
The God-realisation-chapter.

19 The Heart Has Another Name ...

The heart has another name:
Aspiration.
The mind has another name:
Suspicion.
The vital has another name:
Restlessness.
The body has another name:
Idleness.
Life has another name:
Uncertainty.
Man has another name:
Complexity.
The soul has another name:
Observer.
God has another Name:
Forgiver.

5 February 2002 ...

5 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

20 My Lord Blessingfully Whispers ...

My Lord blessingfully whispers.
Alas, I pay no attention.
My soul compassionately whispers.
Alas, I pay no attention.
My heart lovingly whispers.
Alas, I pay no attention.

21 My Mind Speaks To Me ...

My mind speaks to me.
I immediately obey.
My vital speaks to me.
I proudly obey.
My body speaks to me.
I lovingly obey.
Alas, when shall I stop
Obeying them, when?
Alas, when shall I be
The choice instrument
Of my Lord Supreme, when?

22 In This Life ...

In this life
I need only one qualification:
God-obedience.

23 Only One Thing ...

Only one thing
I shall never, never, never desire:
God-disobedience.

24 My God-Obedience ...

My God-obedience
Is my God-closeness.
My God-disobedience
Is my God-aloofness.

25 My God-Satisfaction ...

My God-satisfaction
Asks my God-obedience
To run side by side with it
All the time.

26 My God-Defiance ...

My God-defiance
Bravely marches along the road
That has no goal.
My God-disobedience
Proudly follows my God-defiance.

27 We Cannot Merit ...

We cannot merit
God’s Transcendental Consciousness
And His Universal Consciousness,
But we can inherit and do inherit
His Transcendental Consciousness
And His Universal Consciousness.

28 My Outer Beauty ...

My outer beauty
Attracts and welcomes
All the hostile forces of the world.
My inner beauty
Frightens and transforms
All the hostile forces of the world.

29 If I Am A Real God-Lover ...

If I am a real God-lover,
Then I must always remember
That God the Power infinite
Is my constant helper.

30 I love God ...

I love God.
This may be an iota of truth.
God loves me.
Indeed, this is the bloom of truth,
And this is the blossom of truth.

6 February 2002 ...

6 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

31 I Dare And Dare To Say ...

I dare and dare to say
That my Lord and I
Love each other intensely
And care for each other immensely.

32 God Wants Only ...

God wants only
The selflessness-hearts
To broadcast His most glorious
Life-Activities here on earth.

33 God Tells Me That Spirituality ...

God tells me that spirituality
Is not something fanatic,
Fantastic, eccentric and idiotic,
But really realistic and idealistic.

34 My Prayers And I ...

My prayers and I
Together
Climb and climb.

35 My Meditations And I ...

My meditations and I
Together
Dive and dive.

36 My Life Is Absolutely Safe ...

My life is absolutely safe
Only when I can remain seated
At the Feet of my Lord Supreme.

37 The Inner Worlds ...

The inner worlds
And the outer world
Are clashing and crashing together.
Yet my Beloved Lord Supreme
Has kept me
Absolutely unshaken.

38 Alas ...

Alas,
In an unknown and mysterious way,
We cherish our endless
Worries and anxieties.

39 In The Battlefield Of Life ...

In the battlefield of life,
My Lord has granted my heart-tears
The supreme victory
Over the darkest ignorance-night.

40 Only Inside The Heart Of Silence ...

Only inside the heart of silence
Does God give us
His Nectar-flooded Smile.

7 February 2002 ...

7 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

41 My Absolute Lord Supreme ...

My Absolute Lord Supreme,
I love You, I really do,
But the Cosmic Game
That You play with me
Is so confusing.

42 My Child ...

“My child,
I love you, I really do,
But the earthly game
That you play with Me
Is so uninspiring.”

43 I Prayerfully Offer My Lord Supreme ...

I prayerfully offer my Lord Supreme
The streaming tears of my heart.
My Lord Supreme blessingfully Offers me
The beaming Smiles of His Heart.

44 The Mind, The Vital And The Body ...

The mind, the vital and the body
Ruthlessly attack
The poor and pure heart,
But they eventually
And deplorably lose.
Why?
Because God always
Takes the side of the heart.

45 This Morning ...

This morning
My Lord Absolute
Compelled me to discard
My long-cherished ignorance-mask.

46 I Am The Owner ...

I am the owner
Of sincerity-weakness-tears.
God is the Owner
Of Forgiveness-Divinity-Smiles.
God is commanding me to barter
My tears for His Smiles,
And I immediately obey.

47 O Heaven, Do Accept ...

O Heaven, do accept
What I have to offer:
My prayers.
O earth, do accept
What I have to offer:
My service.

48 Spiritually My Soul Is Fully Grown ...

Spiritually my soul is fully grown
Precisely because for centuries
My soul has been sowing
God-Smiles and God-Ecstasies
In every heart.

49 My Outer Life Loves ...

My outer life loves
The success-dance.
My inner life loves
The progress-song.
I love my Lord’s
Victory-Banner.

8 February 2002 ...

8 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

50 My Only Desire Is To Obey ...

My only desire is to obey
My Lord Absolute Supreme
All the time
With a flower-pure heart.

51 God Tells Me That ...

God tells me that
I do not need His Permission
Before I trust others,
But I do need
His express Permission
Before I distrust others.

52 In The Spiritual Life ...

In the spiritual life,
To see humanity
Without the heart of beauty
And purity
Is the height of stupidity.

53 A Caring Heart ...

A caring heart
Is a daring glory.

54 My Life And I ...

My life and I
Faithfully follow
The tears
Of my ever-climbing heart.

55 There Is A Sure Way ...

There is a sure way
To make oneself happy,
And that way is to bury the past
In oblivion-cave.

56 The Aspiring Heart ...

The aspiring heart
Is always an open door
To a new God-hope
And a new God-promise.

57 Alas, It Is Not God ...

Alas, it is not God,
But it is I
Who have forsaken myself.

58 There Are Many Paths To Reach ...

There are many paths to reach
The God-Summit-Heights.
Each path is absolutely right
In its own way.
But my heart and I have chosen
The path of love, devotion
And surrender.

59 Our Heart-Aspirations ...

Our heart-aspirations
And
Our life-dedications
Are very carefully examined
By God Himself
Before He allows us to live
In His Infinity’s Heart-Home.

9 February 2002 ...

9 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

60 At Last ...

At last,
At the very peak,
I discover what I seek.

61 O Friendship-Flower ...

O friendship-flower,
O friendship-flower,
Do not wither,
Do not wither.

62 Come What May ...

Come what may,
Come what may,
My heart must pray,
My heart must pray.

63 No Dead End ...

No dead end,
No dead end.
Just a bend,
Just a bend.

64 My Lord Gives Me ...

My Lord gives me
A new lesson
At every hour
So that my heart can be
His Beauty’s flower.

65 God Is Not An Iron Rod ...

God is not an iron rod,
God is not an iron rod.
Let us all go quickly,
Go to the Shelter of God.

66 Meditation, Meditation ...

Meditation, meditation,
Meditation:
My God-preparation,
My God-preparation.

10 February 2002 ...

10 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

67 No Prayer, No Prayer ...

No prayer, no prayer,
No prayer:
Life is an ignorance-lair.

68 No Meditation, No Meditation ...

No meditation, no meditation:
Life is an utter imperfection.

69 Sound ...

Sound
Is man-cheering.
Silence
Is God-becoming.

70 Life-River Flows ...

Life-river flows.
Patience-tree grows.

71 Life Is A Thunder-Gong ...

Life is a thunder-gong.
Death is a silence-song.

72 My Life-Story ...

My life-story
Is painful.
My heart-song
Is Godful.

73 What Is Peace? ...

What is peace?
The absence of selfishness.
What is bliss?
The presence of oneness.

11 February 2002 ...

11 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

74 Wake Up, My Mind ...

Wake up, my mind,
Wake up
To our Lord’s
Sweetness-Presence-Smile.

75 God’s Nationality ...

God’s nationality
Is a Self-Transcendence-
Vision-Reality.

76 God Feels Me ...

God feels me
Only when
My heart kneels.

77 My Bleeding Heart-Tears ...

My bleeding heart-tears
Are my alarm clocks
To put an end
To my ignorance-slumber.

78 My Soul-Smiles Whisper ...

My soul-smiles whisper,
And I arrive at God’s Heart-Door
Always on time.

79 Only A God-Obedience-Life ...

Only a God-obedience-life
Receives from God
His real Reward:
His Heart of Infinity.

80 My Tears And My Smiles ...

My tears and my smiles
Have created my human life.
My God-promises
And my God-fulfilments
Are creating my divine life.

14 February 2002 ...

14 February 2002

Sabah, Malaysia

81 Meditation Means ...

Meditation means
The beginning of
A God-becoming-life.

82 Meditation-sun shines ...

Meditation-sun shines
Around the clock
Throughout Eternity.

83 Peace-Dreamers ...

Peace-dreamers
Are
Heart-givers.

84 God Warns Us Inwardly ...

God warns us inwardly
Long before darkness comes down
Upon our lives.

85 O My Ignorance-Mind ...

O my ignorance-mind,
You can cherish your proud opinions,
But I shall cherish only
My God-union-heart-hunger.

86 God Never Misses An Opportunity ...

God never misses an opportunity
To tell the aspiration-hearts
That He is all for them.

87 My Convictions ...

My convictions
Are always founded upon
God-Opinions.

88 God Wants Only ...

God wants only
My gratitude-heart-tears
To introduce me to Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 14, Agni Press, 2002
