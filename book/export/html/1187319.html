1667

O my doubtful mind,
Your life is ridiculous.
O my fearful heart,
Your life is ridiculous.
O my uncertain soul,
Your life is ridiculous.
O my sleeping body,
Your life is ridiculous.
O my strangling vital,
Your life is ridiculous.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 17, Agni Press, 1983
