Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 143

14201

When I live in my heart,
God tells me
That it is my first and foremost chance
To love and be loved by Him.
When I live in my mind,
God tells me
That it is my very last chance
To love Him.

14202

Do not waste your time
Looking for a truth-home
Inside your mind.
Your heart-home is surcharged
With peace.
Remain therein always!

14203

Each spiritual mistake
Is followed
By destruction-demand
From the world of ignorance.

14204

Everyone has missed the God-alarm,
Including my heart.
My soul is the only one
That has not missed
The God-alarm
And I am sure that
My soul will never miss it.

14205

Heaven is not my permanent home.
Earth is not my permanent home.
My permanent home is
My Lord's Compassion-Feet.

14206

As there is no
Heart-aspiration-contest,
Even so there is no
And there can be no
God-realisation-contest.

14207

God's Greatness is loved
By the possession-loving mind.
God's Goodness is treasured
By the liberation-loving heart.

14208

When a seeker is suffering
From Compassion-shortage,
His life will eventually become
A sorrowful, fruitless failure-cry.

14209

If you allow yourself to be attacked
By a swarm of uncomely thoughts,
God will not grant you
His Heart-Guest-Room.

14210

Every day God helps us
Lessen our mind's
Untold destruction-miseries.

14211

The human mind secretly loves
To read forbidden
Insecurity-impurity-insincerity-stories.

14212

Who knows where God is?
Who cares for God after all?
In the future these two questions
Will not be asked
By earth's aspiration-cry.

14213

Alas,
You do not have the time
To see your heart's sunrise,
But you have all the time
To see others' sunset.

14214

We do not know
Where we are heading,
But we do know
That God's Compassion-Forgiveness
Will follow us.

14215

A satisfaction-heart
Is the best
Life-acceleration-smile.

14216

From whose point of view
Is God unmindful?
You may treasure your proud opinion,
But from my point of view
God's Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Heart
Are perfect earth-transformation-guarantors.

14217

God's Compassion
Forgives us.
God's Justice
Illumines us.

14218

Do you want to know
What you should do
With your ignorance?
Just ignore it!
Do you want to know
What you should do
With your sadness-heart?
Just quench its inner thirst
And feed its inner hunger
Lovingly and unconditionally!

14219

Earth does not need
A world-shaking warrior.
But earth does need
A world-consoling liberator.

14220

You are a stark fool
If you think
Your wrongdoings
Will not eventually haunt you.

14221

How long will you remain
In your fear-frozen life?
When will you long for
God's Oneness-Love-Heart?

14222

Just for once,
Can you not have faith
In God's Compassion-Eye?
Just for once,
Can you not love God
For God's sake?

14223

Much have I thought.
Much have I said.
But alas,
Little have I done
And little have I become.

14224

My mind,
When will you stop playing
With your same old toys:
Insecurity, insincerity and impurity?

14225

I have decided
That I shall always remain
As beautiful and soulful
As my heart's faith-plant.

14226

I can never convince my mind
That its false friends —
Arrogance, jealousy and pride —
Are its unthinkable
And unchangeable foes.

14227

Be conscious within.
Be conscious without.
Stop at every moment
Your mind's ignorance-interruptions.

14228

What you call
Your heart's aspiration,
God affectionately calls
Your life's real contribution.

14229

If you want to be
Divinely happy,
Then you must station yourself
Far beyond sound-sight-territories.

14230

Heart's aspiration-path
And life's surrender-path:
These two paths are protected
By God's Compassion-Eye.

14231

Do you want to talk
To angels every day?
If so,
Lighten your mind,
Heighten your heart
And enlighten your life.

14232

What is human life
If not something composed
Of fragile dreams?
What is divine life
If not God's
Manifestation-seeking Vision?

14233

You do not need
An outer guide
So long as you have
Your heart's mounting cry
As your inner guide.

14234

He who compassionately tolerates
Frustration-volcano
Is indeed a superior soul.

14235

By running away,
Nobody can ever escape from
Life's serious and endless problems.

14236

What you need
Is a new vision of truth.
What you need
Is a new love of God.
This you can have
Only when you are able
To draw on your heart's
Silence-reservoir.

14237

What can poor God do?
Humanity's arrogance-disgrace-mind
Does not allow God to erase it!

14238

We are all lovers
Of opportunity,
But not fighters
Against calamity.

14239

To love God soulfully
Is difficult,
But to serve God sleeplessly
Is infinitely more difficult.

14240

God is never tired of
Giving the same message
Over and over again to humanity:
Never give up!

14241

Temptation and frustration
Know how to play well
Their favourite game:
Hide-and-seek.

14242

God does not blame me
For not working hard
For earth-transformation,
The world does not blame me either.
But I do blame myself vehemently
For not being an earth-transformer.

14243

Who is responsible
For the world's incurable flaw-diseases
If it is not you, not he?
Alas, doubtless I am the
Helpless culprit.

14244

God does not particularly care
For man's success-sound.
Man definitely does not care
For God's Progress-Whisper.

14245

If we think that God's Justice-Light
Wants us as badly
As God's Compassion-Height needs us,
Then we are obviously enjoying
Self-delusion.

14246

Freedom-fighters and
Tolerance-builders
Must always sail
In the same boat.

14247

If you see darkness-night
Here, there and everywhere,
It is because even by mistake
You do not give God
The right of way.

14248

If you continue doing
Your silence-meditation daily,
God will definitely strengthen
Your inner life-muscles.

14249

Each good thought
Can eventually be blessed
With two flying wings:
Surrender-flower
And gratitude-fragrance.

14250

Beautiful
Is the earth-illumining song
Of my heart.
Fruitful
Is the life-transforming dance
Of my soul.

14251

Intensify
Your God-satisfying hunger-cry
To be a conscious partner
Of God's Vision transcendental.

14252

Ambition-success
Wants to be visible and tangible.
Aspiration-progress
Wishes to be unnoticed and unacclaimed.

14253

My Lord Supreme tells me
That you can do anything
You want to do with your life,
Except one thing:
Do not cover your heart's gratitude-sun.

14254

Is there any human being on earth
Who has never experienced
Incredible self-indulgence-capacity?

14255

Smooth sailing
On obedience-ocean
My heart is learning
From my soul.

14256

Today I am most powerfully
And most convincingly
Putting an end to my mind's
Excessive devotion to reason.

14257

Alas, alas!
Today is another day for me
Without a glimpse
Of my Lord's Compassion-Smile.

14258

I shall never allow
My searching mind
To be stung
By the outer world's scorpion-doubt.

14259

If you do not have a searching mind,
If you do not have a climbing heart,
Then your life will be forced to descend
Into the confusion-spiral.

14260

My soul was shocked to notice
That my heart's sorrowful sigh
Could be so devastating.

14261

Can you not see
That God is in a special mood
To speak to you?
Immediately start listening to Him!

14262

My life's transcendence
Is indeed God's transcendence
For His complete manifestation
On earth.

14263

Every day
Our body, vital, mind and heart
Are being transformed automatically.
But if we want our heart, mind,
vital and body
To be transformed devotedly,
Soulfully and unconditionally
In God's own Way,
We must place our earth-bound cry
And Heaven-free smile
At every moment
At the Feet of our Beloved Lord Supreme.

14264

Every day
God starts a cry-change
In my aspiration-life.
But alas, I do not have
The heart-eye to see it.

14265

O earth,
You may not believe it,
O Heaven,
You may not believe it,
But my Lord told me this morning
That I am making satisfactory progress
Just by intensifying my heart-cry.

14266

Every morning,
Compassionately plus pitifully,
My Lord Supreme begs me
To empty my sordid mind-room.

14267

Do you know who is preparing
God's Peace-Sun-Arrival
Here on earth?
Definitely not my desire-life,
But my heart's sleeplessly mounting
Aspiration-cry.

14268

In silence supreme
Every morning
I see the golden sunrise
Of my inner perfection-beauty.

14269

Beautiful
Is my aspiration-life.
Powerful
Is my dedication-life.
Fruitful
Is my surrender-life.

14270

The quiet voice
Of my patience-heart
Every day is proudly fed
By my Lord Supreme.

14271

My Lord Supreme
Is extremely fond of the music
Of my sweetness-flooded
Surrender-soul.

14272

My Beloved Lord Supreme
Is celebrating today
The sunset of my desire-life.

14273

My Lord Supreme,
No matter where I go,
I see Your two most powerful
Forgiveness-Shelter-Wings.

14274

My Beloved Supreme,
I know, I know,
That my life will remain
Meaningless, fruitless and useless
Until I have sung
Your Eternity's, Infinity's
And Immortality's
Victory-Songs on earth.

14275

Alas, he has an aversion
To a slow and steady heart-progress,
But he has no aversion
To a fast and harmful mind-success.

14276

O seeker,
If you cannot capture
God's Compassion-Eye,
Try at least to capture God's
Forgiveness-Heart.

14277

God is willing to meet you,
But not in a secret place.
He wants to meet you
On your life's highway.

14278

Perfection-shore is indeed
A new frontier
For each and every
Truth-seeker and God-lover.

14279

Your devotion is full of commotion.
Therefore, God does not want
To ruin His most precious Ears
By listening to you.

14280

It is not true
That God has not created
Any human being
Without a tearful and painful sigh.

14281

My long-treasured depression-friend
And I
Have cleverly and forcefully
Put God to sleep.

14282

God the Creator finds it difficult
To tolerate me.
But God the creation
Not only tolerates me
But also tells me
That He will wait throughout Eternity
For wisdom-sun to dawn in my heart.

14283

Stand upon surrender-mountain.
Lo, the governor of your life
Has become the love within your heart.

14284

I asked God
If His Compassion-Eye
Was only for my imperfection-life.
God said to me:
"Definitely My Compassion-Eye
Is for your imperfection-life.
Also, My Compassion-Eye
Is for the acceleration
Of your soul's Heavenward speed."

14285

O seeker,
Right now you do not need
A huge Everest-experience.
What you need is
A tiny, self-giving thought.

14286

Are you searching for my mind
Or are you searching for my heart?
If you are searching for my mind,
You will never be able to find it.
If you are searching for my heart,
Lo and behold, it is there
Right in front of you!

14287

Quite often
Ignorance-night compels me to utter:
"God does not love me!"
"God does not need me!"

14288

O seeker,
Do you want to use your mind
To retaliate,
Or do you want to use your heart
To radiate?
Make your choice immediately.

14289

No matter how strong you are,
If it is not God's Will,
You will not be able to ring
God's Heart-Doorbell.
No matter how weak you are,
If it is God's Will,
You will easily be able to ring
God's Heart-Doorbell.

14290

Every morning God comes to me
And helps me weed
My mind's thought-garden.

14291

I am ready to fight
With the omnipotent God.
But a single tear
Of the compassionate God
Can easily destroy my life,
Inner and outer.

14292

You have simply no idea
How soulful, beautiful and fruitful
Your heart's ecstasy-cry is.

14293

It is good to know,
Both from Heaven and from earth,
That although God can live
Without me,
He prefers not only to live
With me
But also to live for me.

14294

How can you hear
Your aspiration-alarm-clock ringing
If you are not at all serious
In your aspiration-heart
And your dedication-life?

14295

Do you want to live longer
Or do you want to become
A God-lover?
If you want to become
A God-lover,
Then come and take service from me.
If you want to live longer,
Just tell death to postpone
Your Heaven-arrival!

14296

To me,
Desire is as young
As the mind of man
And aspiration is as old
As the heart of man.

14297

An insecurity-mind
Is, alas, condemned
To be a prisoner of itself.

14298

The future world
Will not belong
To the world-fighters
But to the God-dreamers only.

14299

The delay in your
Unconditional surrender
Is the final barrier
Between you and God.

14300

Illumine your mind's
Ignorance-night.
Lo, your life-dreams are flying
In God's Heart-Sky!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 143, Agni Press, 1991
