15665

God's Forgiveness-Feet
Invite
Not only my willingness-heart
But also my unwillingness-mind.
But my willingness-heart is invited
To be His hero-warrior
And my unwillingness-mind is invited
To be His fault-announcer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 157, Agni Press, 1991
