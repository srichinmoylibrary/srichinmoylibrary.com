4883 He Has To Surrender

He has experienced the beauty
Of his inner life.
Therefore he is happy.
He has now to surrender
The ugliness of his outer life
To his Beloved Supreme
So that both he and his Lord
Can be happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 49, Agni Press, 1982
