January 22

If you live in the body, you will be too weak to repress anything. If you live in the vital, you will be too authoritarian to allow anything. If you live in the mind, you will be too indifferent to love anything. If you live in the heart, you will be too indulgent to control anything. Live in the soul, God will say, do and become everything for you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
