The Upanishads have inspired all systems of Indian philosophy and even today guide the spiritual lives of millions of Truthseekers. God alone knows how many Upanishads once existed, but only 108 have been faithfully preserved. Lead us from Darkness unto Light,
Lead us from Death to Immortality.

(From the Brihad-aranyaka Upanishad: 1.3.28)
