335. Two complementary souls

I find it impossible to separate
Earth's beauty-cry
From
Heaven's Beauty-smile.
They are just
Two complementary souls.

I find it impossible to separate
Earth's aspiration
From
Heaven's Illumination.
They are just
Two complementary souls.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
