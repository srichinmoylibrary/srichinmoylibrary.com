17. Are you a seeker

Are you a seeker
Of silence-life?
Then come in, please.
Indeed,
This is the right place.

Are you a seeker
Of sound-life?
Then leave this place immediately.
Indeed,
This place can never welcome you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
