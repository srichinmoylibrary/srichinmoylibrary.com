2152

My Lord Supreme,
You have given me
Two weak hands.
"But My child,
I have given you also
My own powerful Heart."

My Lord Supreme,
You have given me
Two blind eyes.
"But My child,
I have given you also
My own Immortality's Vision-Life."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 22, Agni Press, 1984
