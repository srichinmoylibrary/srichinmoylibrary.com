The day of my father's death

The day my father died, he told my brother Chitta, "Give me anything you want me to eat." For quite a few days he had not eaten, and everybody was very concerned about him. But on the day he left the body, he told them to give him whatever they wanted him to eat.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Brother Chitta, Agni Press, 1998
