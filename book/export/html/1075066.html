495. Reincarnation

He is the clear proof
Of reincarnation.

In his previous incarnation
He spoke excessively
Ill of God.

In this incarnation
He is trying to compensate.

He is appreciating God
Beyond his sincerity.

He is admiring God
Beyond his capacity.

He is loving God
Beyond his necessity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 10, Agni Press, 1973
