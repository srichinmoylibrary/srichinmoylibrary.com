644. Three serious faults

He has just three
Serious faults,
That's all.
He can't stop talking,
He can't start listening.
He can,
But he does not want to
Learn the art of loving God
And thus perfect
His own human life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
