1 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
You are asking me
Not to discourage the world
And at the same time
Not to be discouraged by the world.
You are asking me
To encourage the world
And at the same time
To be encouraged by the world.
If I do this, then I shall please You
In Your own Way.
My Supreme, I shall fulfil
Your express Command.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

1 July 1999, 8:14 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
