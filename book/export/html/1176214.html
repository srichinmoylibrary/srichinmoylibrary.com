3259 Journey's End, Journey's Beginning

A God-seeker consciously ends his journey
In God-Satisfaction.
A God-lover unconditionally begins his journey
With God-Satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 33, Agni Press, 1982
