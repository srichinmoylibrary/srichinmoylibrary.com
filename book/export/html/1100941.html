41.

Sometimes I feel
That God does not know,
Even does not care to know,
What is happening
In my life.
But God tells me that
He does not have to know
What is happening in my life
If my life is only for myself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My gratitude-tears and God's Satisfaction-Smiles, Agni Press, 1993
