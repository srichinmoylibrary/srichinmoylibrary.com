Ami Kari Prarthana

Ami kari prarthana
Ami jachi santana
Ami kari bandana
Bhule gechi banchana
Ami kari kalpana
Habo taba ranjana

Translation:

I pray and pray.
I need Your Consolation-Light.
I worship You alone.
I have totally forgotten my deception-life.
I am now in the imagination-world;
I am hoping to be Your Delight supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 2, , 1976
