20411

My Beloved Supreme,
Do tell me
How I can please You most
In the New Year.
"My child, you will be able
To please Me most
If you just give Me two things
Once and for all:
Your mind's ceaseless deception-night
And your heart's sleepless aspiration-light."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 205, Agni Press, 1994
