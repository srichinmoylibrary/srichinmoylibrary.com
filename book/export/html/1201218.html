15428

There is Someone
To change your inner life.
There is Someone
To change your outer life.
Just dive deep within
To see His Compassion-Eye
And Forgiveness-Feet.
Indeed, He is
Your Eternity's Inner Pilot.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 155, Agni Press, 1991
