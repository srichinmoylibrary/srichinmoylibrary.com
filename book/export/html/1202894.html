17088

O suspicion-mind,
I do not need you.
O inspection-mind,
I do not need you.
O interrogation-mind,
I do not need you.
O illumination-mind,
I need you,
I have always needed you
And I shall always need you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 171, Agni Press, 1992
