2 March 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I say, “I do not,”
My Lord cries and cries and cries.
When I say, “I cannot,”
My Lord laughs and laughs and laughs.
When I say, “I can”
My Lord smiles and smiles and smiles.
When I say, “I have done”
My Lord immediately says,
“My child, I am making you
Another God, a future God.”
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 March 2000. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 13, Agni Press, 2000
