459. I am more than willing

Mind, my mind,
I am more than willing
To give you what you so desperately need:
Reality.

Heart, my heart,
I am more than willing
To give you what you so desperately need:
Divinity.

Life, my life,
I am more than willing
To give you what you so desperately need:
Perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
