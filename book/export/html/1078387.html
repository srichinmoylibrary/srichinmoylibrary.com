740 If Tomorrow Fails Me...

IF TOMORROW FAILS ME

Yesterday failed me.
Therefore
My eye was on today.

Today is failing me.
Therefore
My eye is on tomorrow.

If tomorrow also fails me,
Like yesterday and today,
Then my eye shall be
On the Eternal Now.

Who is whispering?
Ah, my sweet Lord.
What is He whispering about?
He is offering me His Confidence-assurance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
