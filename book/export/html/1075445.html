ABILITY, NECESSITY AND REALITY

My only ability
Is that I have recognised
God's capacity.

My only necessity
Is that I see the Supreme Lord
Smiling at me.

My only reality
Is that my soul is of God
And
My heart is for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
