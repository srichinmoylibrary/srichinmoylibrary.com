50. Found among Light-thieves

     Yesterday
I was found
     Among Light-thieves.     Today
I am found
     Among Light-distributors.     Tomorrow
I shall be found
     Among Light-owners,
           The real owners.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eternity's Silence-Heart, Agni Press, 1974
