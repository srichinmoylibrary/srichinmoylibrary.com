960 I Have Become Earth's Advocate...

I HAVE BECOME EARTH'S ADVOCATE

I have seen the tears of sorrow,
I have felt the pangs of heartache,
I have become
Earth's advocate for
Heaven-considerations
And
God-Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
