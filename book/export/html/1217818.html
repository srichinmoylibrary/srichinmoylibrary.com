My ego and my soul

My ego needs.
My soul has.

My ego tries.
My soul does.

My ego knows the problem that is.
My soul becomes the answer that

I am not alone.
Within my unlit self
My ego, my naked death.

I am not alone.
Within my snow-white heart
My soul and my Spirit's Flame.

From: Sri Chinmoy, AUM -- Vol. 2, No. 7,8, Feb. -- Mar. 27, 1967, Boro Park Printers -- Brooklyn, N. Y, 1967
