3.

Supreme!

I am eagerly waiting
For someone
To replace You,
So that
I can have someone
To swim with me
While I swim
In the sea of tears,
Frustrations
And failures.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sadness-heart-silence. Madness-mind-eloquence, Agni Press, 1998
