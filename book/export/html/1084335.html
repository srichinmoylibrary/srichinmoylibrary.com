3. No difference

No difference
Between
His earth-life
And
His arrows of dark desire-night.
No difference
Between
His Heaven-life
And
His stars of blue Perfection-Light.
No difference
Between
His God-life
And
His love-flames of Oneness-Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 8, Agni Press, 1974
