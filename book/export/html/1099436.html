12 May

My evening descends,
And I tell it
That my day blesses me
With worries
And my evening blesses me
With hopes.
My evening adds:
"And God blesses you
With both powerful promises
And fruitful fulfilments."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My evening descends, Agni Press, 1996
