21491

My God-obedience-mind
And my God-gratitude-heart
Aspire to live together
Gloriously and peacefully.

My God-disobedience-vital
And my man-destruction-life
Conspire to live together
Shamelessly and dauntlessly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 215, Agni Press, 1994
