Twenty-First Century,

Twenty-first Century
Oishi surabhi madhuri
Neme eso neme eso
Andhar biduri

Translation:

Twenty-first Century!
O Heaven's Beauty and Fragrance,
Do come down, come down,
Chasing darkness.
