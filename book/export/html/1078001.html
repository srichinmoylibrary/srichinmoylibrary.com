354. If you believe

Be careful
If you believe
In the power of the mind.
It will instigate you
To see night in Light.

Be not forgetful
If you believe
In the power of the heart.
It will inspire you
To transform night-cave into Light-palace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
