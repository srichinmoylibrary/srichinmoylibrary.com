831 A Sublime Truth

My despair,
My wild despair,
I have discovered
A sublime truth:
I can do something
Infinitely better
Than surrender to you -
Fight, fight!
I must fight against you
To bring to the fore
The Real in me:
My inspiration-sky
And aspiration-sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 9, Agni Press, 1981
