Hiya Nil Pakhi Karuna Nayane

Hiya nil pakhi karuna nayane
Dake more aji prashanti nabhe urite

Translation:

With the compassion-eye, my blue soul-bird
Is calling me to fly in the sky of peace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I long to quench my heart thirst, , 1993
