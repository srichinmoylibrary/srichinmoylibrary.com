15 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
It is Your infinite Patience
That enables me to achieve
   Momentous progress
In my inner life of aspiration
        And
   Tremendous success
In my outer life of dedication.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 October 1999, 10:29 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 40 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (96 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
