631 My Debts...

MY DEBTS

My aspiration-realisation-life
Is my personal debt to earth.

My vision-delight-life
Is my personal debt to Heaven.

My progress-perfection-life
Is my personal
Plus
Eternal debt to God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
