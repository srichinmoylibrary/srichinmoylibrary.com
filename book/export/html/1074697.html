133. He thinks, he feels, he knows

His vital thinks
To live with God the disciplinarian
        Is ridiculous.

His mind thinks
To live with God the simpleton
        Is ridiculous.

His body thinks
To live with God the inconsiderate
        Is ridiculous.

His heart feels
To live without God the Beloved
        Is ridiculous.

His soul knows
To live without God the Lover
        Is ridiculous.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 3, Agni Press, 1973
