10759

Live not in the heart
Of false hope.
Live not in the mind
Of false disappointment.
If you live in these two places,
You will be inducted into
The hall of stupidity-fame.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 108, Agni Press, 1987
