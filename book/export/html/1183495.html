359. Indeed

Indeed,
You are a real clever Master.
Just because I do not follow
Your path, you secretly
Tell your people
That I am not ready for the spiritual life.
Just because I have not taken you
As my Master, you openly say
That I am not meant to be your disciple.
Indeed,
You are a real clever Master.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
