2837 Yes And No

Do not switch from no to yes
Or from yes to no
Like an opportunist.
But to God-Satisfaction
Immediately say, "Yes!"
And to self-satisfaction
Definitely say, "No!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 29, Agni Press, 1982
