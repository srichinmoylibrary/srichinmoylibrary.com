26 July 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To please God on the largest scale,
My ceaselessly surrendered life
Is of paramount importance.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 July 2000, 6:22 p.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift four times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
