7497

I love God
  Not because He is great.
  I love God
  Not because He is good.
  I love God
  Because nothing else
Ever inspires my mind,
  Nothing else
  Ever feeds my heart,
  Nothing else
  Ever fulfils my life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 8, Agni Press, 1998
