Love, Devotion, Surrender, How Far?

Love, devotion, surrender, how far?
How far? How far?
I am crying, I am dying for you three.
Make me a oneness-seed.
Make me a oneness-plant.
Make me a oneness-tree.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Impossibility bows, , 1995
