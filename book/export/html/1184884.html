556. When I worship you

When
I worship You with folded hands
I get joy.

When
I love You with my heart's adoration-tears
I get greater joy.

When
I surrender to You
My desire-world
And
My aspiration-sun,
I get the greatest joy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
