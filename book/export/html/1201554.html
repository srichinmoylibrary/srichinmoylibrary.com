15761

There is no such thing
As an aspiration-overflow.
There is no such thing
As a dedication-overflow.
There is no such thing
As a manifestation-overflow.
There can be only one overflow:
A desire-overflow.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 158, Agni Press, 1991
