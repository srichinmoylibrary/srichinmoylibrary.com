30.

Carl Lewis, Sudhahota, O world’s fastest body-speed and heart-speed, we your genuine admirers are enjoying the Infinity’s happiness-feast that you have given us. Your heart and your life will eternally remain in our gratitude-heart-garden.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Carl Lewis: the champion inner runner, part 1, Agni Press, 1991
