77. Who means most to you?

Who means most to you?
If God,
Then give Him
What you are:
His Silence-Smile.

Who means most to you?
If man,
Then give him
What you have:
Constant hope, constant frustration.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
