624 Necessity-Flood...

NECESSITY-FLOOD

Good morning has given me
Its purity-flood;
Good evening has given me
Its sincerity-flood;
And
My beloved Supreme has given me
His Compassion-filled
Necessity-flood.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
