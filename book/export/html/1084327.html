46. Command and obey

I have commanded;
Therefore I have also obeyed.

Who has commanded?
The I-God in me.

Who has obeyed?
The I-man in me.

I have obeyed;
Therefore I shall also command.

I shall command
My Eternity's perfection
        Of dedication-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 7, Agni Press, 1974
