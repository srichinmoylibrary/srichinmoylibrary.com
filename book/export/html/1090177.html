43. Do you have anything else?

Oh earth,
Do you have anything else
Save and except
Your blind God?

Oh Heaven,
Do you have anything else
Save and except
Your callous indifference?

Oh God,
Do You have anything else
Save and except
Your unknowable Sacrifice-Life?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, receive this little undying cry, Agni Press, 1975
