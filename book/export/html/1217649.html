My dying soul

Two running ears,
     One howling tongue,
Two trembling knees,
     One bleeding heart,
Two weeping eyes,
     One struggling breath,
Two surrendering arms,
     One dying soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, AUM -- Vol. 1, No. 6, January 27, 1966, Boro Park Printers -- Brooklyn, N. Y, 1966
