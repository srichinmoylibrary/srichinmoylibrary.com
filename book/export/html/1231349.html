Sundara Shib Mrinmoya Jib

Sundara shib
Mrinmoya jib
Kare toma abahan
Shraddha bhakti
Amita shakti
Antare karo anayan

Translation:

O beautiful Lord Shiva,
Earth-children are invoking You,
Your Compassion-Presence.
Do come into my heart
Of love and service
With Your boundless Power-Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, teach me how to surrender, , 1975
