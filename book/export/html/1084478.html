44. Arise in faith!

Arise in faith!
Earth will give you
All its love.
Arise in faith!
Heaven will give you
All its joy.
Arise in faith!
God will give you
All His Freedom.
Arise in faith!
You will realise
That you are not only in all,
Of all and for all,
But also
You are ALL.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 10, Agni Press, 1974
