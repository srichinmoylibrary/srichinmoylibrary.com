19 September 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Shake me,
Wake me and shock me,
To make me
A faithful and choice instrument
Of Yours.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 September 1999, 8:04 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 50 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (67 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
