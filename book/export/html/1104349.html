6 August 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Impossibility is in man's dictionary.
Grace is in God's Dictionary.
Impossibility must surrender
To God's Grace unconditional.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 August 2000. Before doing 1,300-lb. seated calf raise. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
