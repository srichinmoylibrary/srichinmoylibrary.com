249. What they need

What my body needs
Is an endurance-contest.

What my vital needs
Is an appearance-contest.

What my body needs
Is an inconscience-conquest.

What my heart needs
Is an assurance-rest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
