Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 237

23601

When the mind's simplicity
And the heart's purity
Grow together,
They create unimaginable beauty.

23602

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me a heart of hope
And a life of promise
That will never die.

23603

My Lord is delighted beyond measure
When He sees that my gratitude-heart
Is never out of breath.

23604

God wants to heartily congratulate
My willingness-mind
On its eagerness-performance.

23605

If you give from the heart,
You win.
But if you take with the mind,
You lose.

23606

Success
Always intoxicates
My mind.

Progress
Always satisfies
My heart.

23607

There remain only two choices
In my life:
Either I look forward
Towards my destination,
Or I look backward
Towards the animal kingdom.

23608

There is no greater sorrow
For God
Than my constant unwillingness
To obey Him.

23609

I must reach
My God-Destination-Shore
Regardless of
My mind's inclement weather,
Relying upon
My heart's eternal Spring.

23610

God wants human beings
To be interdependent at least,
Since we do not depend on Him
Completely at every moment.

23611

Compassion
Is God's beginningless past
And God's endless future.

23612

My doubting mind's sincerity
Amuses God.
My loving heart's purity
Inspires God.

23613

The only reason
Why God is fond of you
Is because you are sleeplessly devoted
To Him.

23614

For want of inspiration-freedom,
His aspiration-life
Is descending and descending daily.

23615

Because He does not
See us smiling,
God every day sheds
Sorrowful tears.

23616

My Lord Supreme is planning
To write a new book —
Alas, a very short book —
On my obedience-mind.

23617

God long ago read my mind's
Disobedience-letters.
But He forgave both my mind
And its letters.

23618

Sincere willingness
Will sleeplessly and breathlessly
Advance
In God's direction.

23619

God takes
A God-gratitude-heart
And a self-giving life
As His most valuable Treasures.

23620

I rejoice
In God's Compassion-Sea.
God rejoices
In my gratitude-drop.

23621

If we take pride
In the noise of our popular acclaim,
Then down we fall
Into the abyss of self-disgrace.

23622

Simplicity's sweetness
And simplicity's fruitfulness,
Alas,
Always remain underestimated.

23623

If you want to make
Faster than the fastest progress
In the spiritual life,
Then your outer loss and outer gain
Must be less than minor considerations.

23624

O my soul-bird,
For me do not lower
Your vision's perfection-wings.

23625

Who is weakening me?
No, not any outer enemy,
But my own disobedience-mind.

23626

I beg my mind:
"Sleep and sleep and sleep."
I command my heart:
"Wake up, wake up, wake up!"

23627

My aspiration-heart
And God's Compassion-Eye
Have promised to meet
At least once a day.

23628

Aspiration
Heightens my God-journey.
Devotion
Quickens my God-journey.
Dedication
Enlightens my God-journey.

23629

My Lord, I know Your other Name
Is Compassion.
Do You have another name for me?
"Yes, My child,
I have one more name for you:
Hope."

23630

If you have inner competence
And outer courage,
Then your mind will be
A soulfulness-beauty,
And your life will be
A fulness-fragrance.

23631

O my God-surrender-life,
I owe you everything good
That I am.

23632

Alas, I never thought
That my mind's confidence-tower
Would tremble so helplessly.

23633

The true path
Leads upward and inward.
It leads me upward
To see God;
It leads me inward
To become another God.

23634

When I do not please
My Lord Supreme,
I feel that I am a soul-bird
With broken wings.

23635

If you are inwardly resolute,
Your God-realisation-destination
Cannot remain a far cry.

23636

A determination-mind
And a sincerity-heart
Are of paramount importance
To succeed in any sphere of life.

23637

What is aspiration?
It is something that fills my life
With beautiful hopes and sweet dreams.

23638

It is high time for me to believe
That I can offer my life totally
To serve God to His Satisfaction.

23639

If you give and give and give
Unconditionally,
Then only will God
Place the stamp of perfection
On your outer life.

23640

May my heart-room
Never allow
Any Godless thought
To enter.

23641

When I meditate
And dive deep within,
I hear the voiceless Voice
Louder than a thunder-roar.

23642

O my mind,
Are you not ashamed
Of what you are doing?
You have put God on hold
For such a long time!

23643

My Lord, what I am
Is Your Compassion,
And what I have
Is Your Forgiveness.

23644

The foolish mind
Looks at what is lost.
The wise heart
Looks at what is left.

23645

For every obstacle-mountain
There is
A God-Compassion-Fountain.

23646

Do not depend on tomorrow.
Do not depend even on today.
Just depend on now,
This very moment.

23647

Each seeker
Must develop a mind
That will be averse
To ignorance-food.

23648

I live in between
My heart's God-realisation-hope
And
My mind's God-manifestation-promise.

23649

What is faith?
Faith is a meteoric aspiration-rise.
And what is doubt?
Doubt is a meteoric aspiration-fall.

23650

We sadden God
When we rest
On our yesterday's laurels.

23651

How do you dare to think
That God is pleased with you,
When you allow yourself
To be buffeted
By desire-storms?

23652

My Lord Supreme,
May my life be non-stop
Service-delight.

23653

If you do not pray
Every morning and evening,
Then you must realise
That you are descending
Into spiritual poverty.

23654

Earth sleeplessly depends on
Heaven's Compassion.
Heaven ceaselessly depends on
Earth's dedication.

23655

My Lord,
I have done so many things
To displease You.
Do You still love me?
God's one-word reply:
"Yes."

23656

The mind's superiority-brilliance
Does not last.
The heart's unity-radiance
Forever lasts.

23657

Anything that is human
Is an invalid passport
To Heaven.

23658

Because our spiritual eyes
Are still shortsighted,
We do not see God
When He compassionately looks at us
From a distance.

23659

If the mind is filled with sincerity
And the heart is filled with purity,
Then there cannot be a collision
Between the mind and the heart.

23660

My heart's aspiration
And my life's dedication
Are perfect for each other.

23661

If you can graduate
From your God-obedience-school,
Then there will be nothing unattainable
For you.

23662

An unconditional service-life
Is at once gratifying
And edifying.

23663

O my mind,
Can you not see that my heart
Is always ready to purify you
With its sunshine-thoughts?

23664

To my extreme delight
My heart's Heaven-climbing tears
Have reached God's Satisfaction-Heart.

23665

Both our God-aspiration-record
And our God-dedication-record
Should remain unblemished forever.

23666

Alas, we mortals
Walk along so many roads;
But somehow we fail to walk along
The gratitude-path.

23667

An unconditional surrender
Is indeed
The achievement-pinnacle.

23668

Spirituality is not a part-time
But a full-time relationship
With divinity.

23669

I know
I have made considerable progress
In my spiritual life,
All due to my heart's
Indispensable assistance.

23670

Behold,
Because of God's
Unconditional Compassion-Descent,
My life's imperfection-day
Has drawn to an end.

23671

Although humanity has kept
Very few of its promises so far,
God still has perfect faith
In humanity.

23672

Make your mind
At every moment
Sweeter,
And make your heart
At every moment
Purer.

23673

Although I am totally lost within them,
My Lord Supreme tells me
That He has practically no interest
In my mind's archives.

23674

When I live in my uninspired mind,
I do not even want to know
Who God is and where God is.

23675

God's Feet may not be available
At every moment,
But God's Heart
Is always available.

23676

To serve God unconditionally
Is indeed
An exceptional privilege.

23677

If it is true
That my mind is in love
With God the creation,
Then it is equally true
That my heart is in love
With God the Creator.

23678

If your life is armed with spirituality,
Then in everything you do,
You will keep God first and foremost.

23679

My mind is my battlefield.
My heart is my life-garden
Plus my soul-fragrance.

23680

Open your eyes soulfully,
And trust what you see
Unmistakably.

23681

When I dream God-dreams,
I lovingly feed
My heart-garden.

23682

May my morning
Be decorated
By the beauty
Of golden silence.

23683

My Lord does not want me
To compromise
In anything whatsoever.

23684

I know, I know,
Gratitude has a safe shelter
In everybody's heart-home.

23685

God does not want you
To explain everything to Him.
He just wants you
To entertain Him.

23686

My Lord has come to me
With wonderful news!
From now on,
I shall be able to please Him
In His own Way.

23687

I challenged God's Pride
When I said that my useless mind
Is beyond transformation.

23688

My Lord,
Do tell me if there is anything
That far surpasses everything else
In beauty.
"My child, it is
Your own aspiration-heart."

23689

God's Blessings
Are always available,
But where is the beauty
Of my receptivity
And the fragrance
Of my gratitude?

23690

I am always to be found
Either in my mind's prayerful cries
Or in my heart's soulful smiles.

23691

The very nature of the mind
Is to watch carefully.
The very nature of the heart
Is to give freely.

23692

A God-centred life
Will, without fail,
Either today or tomorrow,
Arrive at the ultimate Destination.

23693

The human mind
Will always want more.
The divine heart
Will always give more.

23694

O my heart,
At every moment
Try to climb and climb
My life's aspiration-tree.

23695

My mind wants me to love
God the Mother and God the Father
Equally.
Indeed, this is a very difficult task
For the human mind.

23696

Each time
My heart does something great,
My soul celebrates
My heart's achievement.

23697

There are many entrance-ways
To God's Palace.
I have chosen my life's
Unconditional surrender-way.

23698

The expectation-messenger
Foolishly knocks
At the frustration-door.

23699

May my heart-dream-sky
Always remain free of clouds
So that I can fulfil the Will
Of my Lord Supreme.

23700

O my heart,
Every day I want to bathe
In the infinite joy of forgiveness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 237, Agni Press, 1997
