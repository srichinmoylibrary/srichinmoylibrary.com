718. Emptiness and fulness

Emptiness of the mind
Is necessary.
Emptiness of the heart
Is obligatory.
Fulness of life-sound
Is God-preparation.
Fulness of silence-life
Is God-perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 15, Agni Press, 1974
