Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 68

6701

Nothing means more to me
Than to become
And remain
A gratitude-life.

6702

When I see God with me
And feel God in me,
Even the human burden
Becomes an enjoyable gift.

6703

If you need
God's Forgiveness-Heart,
Then just say so,
And do not waste
God's most precious time.

6704

When it is time to leave this earth,
Say only one thing to yourself:
That Mother Earth
Was exceptionally kind to you.

6705

A true God-lover
Will not rest
Until he can rest
In the Satisfaction-Heart
Of his Beloved Supreme.

6706

How long will you remain
A shadow of your true self?
It is high time for you to become
Your true self itself!

6707

The difference
Between the desire-life
And the aspiration-life
Is this:
The desire-life wants
Countless gifts from God.
The aspiration-life longs
Only for God.

6708

How can your illumination-heart
Ever waver?
I cannot believe it!
It is simply impossible!

6709

Every daring heart
Is a fulfilling Message
From the Absolute Supreme.

6710

What is the difference
Between the mind's audacity
And the life's futility?

6711

Since you have
A soulful and powerful heart,
You can easily leap over
Life's futility-fence.

6712

Equanimity never loses.
It always wins
In the battlefield of life.

6713

God does not have to know
That you care for Him.
Just do the right thing
Every day.
Try to love Him consciously
Every day.

6714

Man's faithful life
And God's fruitful Heart
Are immediate neighbours.

6715

A perfectly God-realised man
Is undoubtedly
A human being in name only.

6716

Live inside the doubting mind.
You are bound to see difficulties
Even in golden opportunities.

Live inside the surrendering life.
You are bound to see opportunities
Even in dire difficulties.

6717

To keep your heart happy
All the time,
Ask your mind to receive
Daily faith-instructions
From your soul.

6718

God will always
Be ready to fulfil
The longing cries of your heart.
But He will never
Become a servant
To the demands of your vital!

6719

God's unconditional Compassion-Eye
Has been the biggest influence
In my life.

6720

Your mind-knowledge
Has sent you
To your confusion-market
To buy hurtful doubts!

6721

You may not be able to prevent
Your body
From getting old,
But you can certainly prevent
Your heart
From becoming impure.

6722

One soulful action
Of your aspiration-heart
Weighs infinitely more
Than your million words.

6723

The God-seeker
Who is only for God-manifestation
Here on earth
Has no time to think
Of his yesterday's failure-life.

6724

It is you
Who are totally responsible
For making your present life
A slave of your past.

6725

He may not know who God is,
But he is leading a life
That makes God
Supremely proud of him.

6726

Since self-deception
Has not pleased you,
Can you not try something else?
I mean, self-perfection.

6727

To smile
Soulfully and unconditionally
Is to carry
The satisfaction-delight of Heaven
Wherever you go.

6728

My soul has been waiting
Compassionately and sleeplessly
For my heart's full flowering.

6729

A God-seeker's purity-heart
And his oneness-soul
Can never lead an idle life.

6730

His sleepless faith
In his Beloved Supreme
Defies all fear
From both the inner and the outer world.

6731

If you have a purity-heart,
Then you will know
Where your secret and sacred
Heart-garden is.

6732

God has pierced your ego-mind
So that He can give
More love and joy
To your surrender-heart.

6733

Ask no more questions!
Even God
Does not want anything to do with
Your tireless, questioning mind!

6734

Do not allow your vital
To be caught in the impurity-jungle
If you want your entire being
To live in your heart's purity-garden.

6735

When are you going to recognise
That the fire of your desire-life
Has burned all your beauty,
Inner and outer?

6736

A truth-seeker and God-lover
Knows
That the Eternal Now
Is always the best time.

6737

My Beloved Lord Supreme,
Where are You going?
"I am disappearing
Because you do not want Me.
But I shall come back
For the asking."

6738

Today
Make your life hopeful.
Then tomorrow
Your life will automatically
Become fruitful.

6739

Do you think that God
Will make an exception for you
In everything you do and say
Just because you feel
That God is very close to you?

6740

If man's promise is not reliable,
Then try God's Compassion.
I am sure God's Compassion
Will always be reliable.

6741

Do not worry about
What grade God will give you.
Just continue studying
At God's School.

6742

Right from the very beginning
His desire-life has not been able
To come out of
Life's bottomless abyss.

6743

Do not examine
The possibilities in life.
Just accept them,
Better them
And, finally,
Satisfy the divine in you.

6744

How can the world forget you
Since you have shown the world
How to touch God's Feet
And how to clasp God's Eye?

6745

God may not be
Measurable,
But He is always
Loveable.

6746

If you always deal
With the ideal,
The Real will always
Love you.

6747

Who told you
That your problems are too heavy?
Just pack them in a small box!
God will come
And take them away from you.

6748

He who does not expect anything
Either from Heaven or from earth
Is a distinguished member
Of God's divine Society.

6749

Can you not see
That your life needs major renovations?
All your best intentions
Are of no avail.
What you need
Is sleepless aspiration.

6750

Your aspiration-heart knows
All the languages of the world.
It does not need a human interpreter
To speak to God.

6751

Right from the very beginning
The Compassion-Eye
Of his Beloved Supreme
Has been his protection-bodyguard.

6752

Your mind's
Exorbitant greed
Far exceeds
Your life's needs.

6753

Once you accept the spiritual life,
If you then do not continue,
Your life will be worse than useless.

6754

He who offers his life to mankind
Lovingly
Is a powerfully unfolding Dream
Of God.

6755

An intellectual mind
Is doomed to remain
An unripe fruit
In life's vast garden.

6756

The mind will have no peace
Until it has become unparalleled.
The heart will have no peace
Until it has become inseparable
And universal.

6757

How can a desire-animal
Embark
On life's divinity-journey?

6758

If you are a daring soul,
Then every day
You must participate
In life's concentration-contest.

6759

Your life's anxiety-flames
Will eventually become
Frustration-ashes.

6760

To awaken
The seeker's sleeping silence
The spiritual Master
Unconditionally knocks
At the seeker's heart-door.

6761

You fool!
Have you not realised
That your gratitude-plant
Has stopped growing completely
In God's Heart?

6762

Humility
Opens the heart-door.
Purity
Receives the Absolute Supreme.

6763

Who can be more miserable
Than a seeker who is a dropout
From God's Illumination-School?

6764

He struggled with his conscience
For a long time.
Alas, finally he surrendered it
To temptation-night.

6765

I simply do not know
How a human being can live on earth
Without having hope-plants
In his heart-garden.

6766

If your heart's cry
Is persuasive,
Then God's pervasive
Compassion-Smile
Shall be all yours.

6767

You can live without God,
But poor God does not
And cannot
Live without you.

6768

I get a pain in my heart
When my mind
Speaks ill of God's Compassion
And ignores God's Forgiveness.

6769

He lives
To do only one thing:
To forgive
The unaspiring being in himself
Every day.

6770

My first impression of God:
He is infinitely more indulgent
Than I thought.

6771

This choice moment of mine
I shall offer only to him
Who loves God infinitely more
Than I do.

6772

My thought-world
Prepares me.
My deed-world
Builds me.
My God-world
Immortalises me.

6773

There is not a single day
When his doubting mind
Does not enjoy
The fear-fabrication-game.

6774

Now that you have discarded
Your mind-rubbish,
Your life-prosperity will flourish
In God's Heart-Garden.

6775

Your heart
Is always on the alert.
It is your mind
That is fast asleep.
Therefore, the forces undivine
Attack your mind at every moment.

6776

He does not believe
In pleasure-trips.
He only believes
In his heart's progress-journey.

6777

Your heart-boat
Is meant for one thing
Very special:
It is supposed to dance
On your life's gratitude-waves.

6778

The Compassion-Wind
Of his Beloved Supreme
Sails his life-boat
Smilingly, lovingly
And blessingfully.

6779

You are telling the whole world
That you are unhappy,
And I am telling the whole world
That you are not suffering
From unhappiness
But from ingratitude.

6780

If your heart has genuine aspiration,
Then it will definitely know
How to dance
To God's Life-Transformation-Flute.

6781

Just because you are getting old,
Don't think that your vital
Will stop
Staging its unthinkable revolts
Against divinity.

6782

Man does not know
And does not want to know
That it is he who is responsible
For his self-created separation
From God.

6783

Do not talk about impurity.
It will blacken
Not only your face
But also your pure heart.

6784

If you can make your heart
Once more
Purity's gold,
Then easily you will be able to dream
Once more
God-dreams.

6785

The sole duty of a Master
Is to transfer his spiritual children
From the purity of the known
To the beauty of the Unknowable.

6786

If you are dealing
With God the Compassion,
Then even His Justice-Punishment-Light
Cannot be too severe.

6787

If you do not pray and meditate
Regularly,
Soon you will be stranded
In your unfulfilled desire-desert.

6788

If you cherish doubt
In your mind,
Peace will have no reason
To stay in your heart.

6789

You wanted to give
Your life-breath to God
For training.
Lo, God has accepted it
In a twinkling.

6790

Nobody has been granted
A gold medal
In both self-deception
And God-Perfection-sports.

6791

Ignorance-snake will bite you
Because you are not challenging
Your past desire-life.

6792

God the Compassion-Heart
Is a stranger
To a helpless, useless
And homeless human heart.

6793

The conservative mind
Wants to reach the mountain-height
Eventually.

The liberal heart
Wants to reach the mountain-height
Swiftly.

The radical soul
Wants to reach the mountain-height
Immediately.

6794

My Beloved Supreme
Every day
Weighs my life
On His Infinity's Vision-Scale.

6795

Pour out all your life's problems.
God's unconditional Compassion-Eye
Does not know how to ignore them.

6796

Everything will be tormenting
Since you are ignoring
The God-cries of the divine child
In your heart.

6797

If you do not survive
Your mind's ignorance-winter,
How will you see the face
Of your heart's wisdom-spring?

6798

Your heart's purity-music
Not only elevates
My unaspiring mind
But also tames
My unruly vital.

6799

I have heard many strange stories
About Heaven.
I can't believe them.

I see many pathetic realities
On earth.
I can't deny them.

6800

At the end of the world
The only question will be
Not who remains,
But will you remain for God,
Only for God?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 68, Agni Press, 1984
