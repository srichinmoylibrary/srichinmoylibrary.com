946. Unseen links

        Beauty
Has its unseen links
   With Heaven's smile.        Beauty
Has its unseen links
   With earth's cry.        Beauty
Has its unseen links
   With God's Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
