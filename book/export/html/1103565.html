9 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart grow into
An ever-blossoming devotion
To worship
Your Golden Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

9 July 1999, 8:39 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
