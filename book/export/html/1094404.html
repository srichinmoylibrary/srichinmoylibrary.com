82 I Have Learned ...

I have learned
Something absolutely new today.
God has given my mind
An ultimatum:
The mind has to choose
Either God or ignorance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 9, Agni Press, 2002
