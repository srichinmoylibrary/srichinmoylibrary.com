Sweet reminiscences

Just two days before I did my first “human mountain” lift with Prakash, I was telling some of the disciples how Prakash reminds me of my own father as a young man. Then I said that Agraha reminds me of my brother Chitta — his face, the way he moves his hands, the way he walks — in every way.

— 23 June 1986

From: Sri Chinmoy, My weightlifting tears and smiles, part 1, Agni Press, 1986
