Gratitude

If there is any shortcut in the spiritual life to arrive at the Golden Shore, then gratitude is the shortcut. If there is a secret and sacred way to love, please and fulfil God, then it is the way of gratitude. Gratitude embodies and reveals all our divine virtues. It is by virtue of gratitude that we can bring down God's infinite Power, infinite Light, infinite Love. Gratitude is the master key to open up all the doors of Divinity's Palace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Rainbow-Flowers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
