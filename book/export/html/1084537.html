1. Waiting for you

O body, my body,
Don't sleep anymore.
Look, Vision-God is waiting for you!
O vital, my vital,
Don't remain spiritless anymore.
Look, Energy-God is waiting for you!
O mind, my mind,
Don't doubt anymore.
Look, Perfection-God is waiting for you!
O heart, my heart,
Don't cry anymore.
Look, Satisfaction-God is waiting for you!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 12, Agni Press, 1974
