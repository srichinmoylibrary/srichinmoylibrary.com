Trying to buy sushi

Today I went to buy sushi for Lavanya's birthday. It took 15 minutes to make them understand what I wanted. My Indian English they didn't understand. What could I do?

-18 September 1987

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
