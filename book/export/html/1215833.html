209. O light, O trance

O light of trance,
Where are you?
O trance of night,
Where are you not?
O night of trance,
Where can I kill you?
O Light, O trance,
I am of you.
O night, O trance,
God is there for you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
