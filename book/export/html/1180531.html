7533 Tragic News

This morning my Lord Supreme
Gave me two pieces of tragic news:
My mind consciously loves
Self-deception,
And my heart consciously enjoys
Self-limitation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 76, Agni Press, 1983
