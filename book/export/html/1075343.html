766. He is suffering

He is suffering
Because
He knows not
His Mother's voice.

He is suffering
Because
He knows not
His Father's choice.

He is suffering
Because
He knows not
Where God is.

He is suffering
Because
He knows not
Who he is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 16, Agni Press, 1973
