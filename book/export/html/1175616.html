2667 One Unforgivable Mistake

Since you want to know the truth,
I am telling you that I have made
Only one unforgivable mistake
Which I am unable to rectify,
And that is:
I do not want to unlearn
The information my mind has given me
Over the years.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
