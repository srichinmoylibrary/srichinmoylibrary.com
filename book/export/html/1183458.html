322. Do you believe

Do you believe
In the life of reality?
If you believe
Then do not renounce life.
Do not indulge
In quitting activity
As long as you believe in reality;
God's omnipresent Reality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
