My solid goal

This morning I transcended myself in the one-arm lift by lifting 380 pounds. 390 pounds I am not going to do, because I don’t like the number 90. So, from today on, I am aiming at 400 pounds. This is my solid goal. Let it take even forty days if necessary!

— 19 September 1986

From: Sri Chinmoy, My weightlifting tears and smiles, part 1, Agni Press, 1986
