21 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Each heart carries
A very special Message from God
For the betterment of this world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 September 1999, 9:26 a.m. Before lifting 275 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 550 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
