15648

If you have a mind
Of great intentions,
If you have a heart
Of good aspiration,
And if you have a life
Of sleepless dedication,
Then your nature's perfection
Cannot remain a far cry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 157, Agni Press, 1991
