37. How do I forget?

How do I forget
My mental pains?
I forget my mental pains
By treasuring my psychic gains.

How do I forget
My earth-failures?
I forget my earth-failures
By remembering my Heaven-dreams
         And
My Heaven-promises.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 17, Agni Press, 1974
