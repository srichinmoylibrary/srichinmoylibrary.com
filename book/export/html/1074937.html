368. Thinking and thanking

I stopped thinking;
        Therefore
I don't have to thank any more.

I stopped thanking;
        Therefore
I don't have to love any more.

I stopped loving;
        Therefore
I don't have to repent any more.

I stopped repenting;
        Therefore
I don't have to sleep any more.
   I must act,
   I must become.
   I must act to become,
   I must become to act.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 8, Agni Press, 1973
