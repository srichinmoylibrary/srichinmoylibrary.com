Amare Diyecho Hasi

Amare diyecho hasi
Amar amire nashi
Amare diyecho kul
Ami taba puja phul
Taba madhu kripa rashi
Amare diyeche banshi

Translation:

You have given me
Your transcendental Smile
By having destroyed my little ‘i’.
You have given me
Your Haven-Feet.
I have now become
My heart’s worship-flower.
Your sweet Compassion-flood has granted me
My life’s transformation-flute.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 2, , 1976
