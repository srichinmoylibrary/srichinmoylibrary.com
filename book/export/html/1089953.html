Ignorance

Ignorance-smile,
Yesterday
You smiled at me;
Today I am smiling at you.
Yesterday you smashed
My God-oneness pride divine.
Today I have devoured you,
My ignorance-sea,
Unconditionally,
Unreservedly,
And supremely.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Light-delight-journeys, Agni Press, 1974
