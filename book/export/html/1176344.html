3388 I Know What God Has For Me

I do not know who God is.
I do not know where God is.
But I do know and feel
That God has for me
His Eternity's Love
And
His Immortality's Concern.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 34, Agni Press, 1982
