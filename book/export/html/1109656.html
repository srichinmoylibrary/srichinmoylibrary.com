3.

O body, my body,
Think of the soul,
For with the help of the soul
You will grow into
Eternity’s Poise, Peace, Light and Bliss.

O soul, my soul,
Think of the body,
For it is in and through the body
That you will have to manifest your divinity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Pioneer-runners of tomorrow’s world-peace-dawn Ultramarathon running and self-transcendence, Agni Press, 1998
