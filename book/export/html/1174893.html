1951 Yesterday I Was On Earth

Yesterday I was on earth
To be myself.

Today I am on earth
To rise above myself.

Tomorrow I shall be on earth
To surrender my new self
To God's Will.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 20, Agni Press, 1981
