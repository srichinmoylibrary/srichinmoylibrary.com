Goat: Desire

Goat, my goat,
No more do you care
For the desire-land.
Therefore
Truth-Light is descending into you
And
You shall before long become
A member divine of the aspiration-band.
And then through you will play
God's own Hand.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Animal Kingdom, Agni Press, 1973
