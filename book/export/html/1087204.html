The Pope's picture

The manager of the Marine Midland Bank told his wife that I had met the Pope and that I was going to present him with an autographed picture.

His wife didn't believe that I would sign the picture for him.

The manager begged me to autograph the picture so he could prove to his wife that we are friends.

-30 October 1987

From: Sri Chinmoy, I love shopping, part 4, Agni Press, 1994
