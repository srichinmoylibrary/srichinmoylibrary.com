10 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
How far my mind is away from God,
God alone knows.
But how far my heart is away from God,
Not only God knows but I also know.
The distance is most astonishingly
Negligible.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 September 2000, 7:01 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
