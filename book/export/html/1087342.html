23. I am not sure

I think my soul is uncertain
But I am not sure.

I think my heart is weak
But I am not sure.

I think my mind is blind
But I am not sure.

I think my vital is wild
But I am not sure.

I think my body is useless
But I am not sure.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I need only God, Agni Press, 1975
