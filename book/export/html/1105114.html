62.

Do not surrender to your fate.
Do not accept your fate.
Love God more and more,
Unconditionally.
God has two big Ears.
He will transform
Your earth-bound fate
Into the Heaven-free Bliss.

14 July 2007
New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 2008
