905. Failure and success

Defeat
Is a bad failure.
Despair
Is a worse failure.
Not to try again
Is the worst failure.
Victory
Is a good success.
Progress
Is a better success.
Surrender
Is the best success.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 19, Agni Press, 1973
