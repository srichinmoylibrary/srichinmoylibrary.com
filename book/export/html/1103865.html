12 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You teach me only when
   I am ready.
Your Compassion teaches me
   Even when I am not ready.
   Your Forgiveness teaches me
All the time —
   Alas, often in vain!
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 October 1999, 8:41 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 50 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (61 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
