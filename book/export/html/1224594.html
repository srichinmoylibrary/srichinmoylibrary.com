Chilam Akul Kul Meleche Amito Ar Nai

Chilam akul kul meleche
Amito ar nai
Ami ar kichu na chai
Phire jete chai
Swarup pete chai
Pareche dak chalechi aj tai
Shudhu pranam kare jai
Ashish karo swarup jena pai

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
