718. God's Rapture-fire

O mute despair,
I feel your suffering,
I feel your pangs.
Wait, have patience.
Your despair will be transformed
Into God's Rapture-fire.
Wait for God's choice Hour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
