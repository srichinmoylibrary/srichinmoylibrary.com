2057 Make Up Your Mind

Make up your mind
Once and for all!
Do you really want to come out
Of your insecurity-prison?
Indeed, you have reasons!
But your reasons are incorrect.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 21, Agni Press, 1981
