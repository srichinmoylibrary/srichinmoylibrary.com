4870

God could not understand
When he asked God
For a personal loan.
God said to him,
"The term 'loan' is not available
In My Heart-Dictionary.
Self-giving is the correct word
To be found in My Heart-Dictionary."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 49, Agni Press, 1984
