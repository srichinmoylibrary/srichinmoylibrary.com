41.

Supreme!

I fed You
With my heart's
Nectar-devotion.
I want the world
To continue
To have faith in You.
Therefore,
I am not telling
The world
What You are
Feeding me with.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sadness-heart-silence. Madness-mind-eloquence, Agni Press, 1998
