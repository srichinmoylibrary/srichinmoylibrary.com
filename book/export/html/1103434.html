5 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must sincerely admit
That I am a member
Of God's inner circle
Not because my aspiration-hunger
Is very deep,
But because God's Compassion-Meal
Is unconditional.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

5 May 1999, 7:01 a.m. Before lifting 280 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 560 lbs.) New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 5, Agni Press, 1999
