Yuri

"God, do You ever tell any lies?"

"Yes, I have to tell lies every day in order to digest My food. But the one that gives Me the most joy is this one: when I tell the cosmic gods and goddesses that I am extremely, extremely pleased with and proud of My human children."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sri Chinmoy with his upper-storey-weak students, Agni Press, 1993
