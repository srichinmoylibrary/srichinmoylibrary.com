Sacrifice

The animal in us demands sacrifice from others.
The human in us expects sacrifice from others.
The divine in us cheerfully and unconditionally sacrifices.
The Supreme in us says: "There is no such thing as sacrifice. Sacrifice is nothing but the conscious fulfilment of one's universal Oneness."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Colour Kingdom, Agni Press, 1974
