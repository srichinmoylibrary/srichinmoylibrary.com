Death says to life:
“My Brother,
I have been waiting for you
  For a long time.
Please come, come. Please come!”
Life says to death:
“I am sorry, Sister,
I am coming at God’s choice Hour.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 41, Agni Press, 2004
