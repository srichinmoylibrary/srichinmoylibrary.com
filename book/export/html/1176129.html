3175 My Mind Cries

My Lord,
My mind cries
For Your Voice.
My heart cries
For Your Touch.
My life cries
For Your Smile.
And I cry
For Your lasting Satisfaction in me -
If possible, for me, too.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 32, Agni Press, 1982
