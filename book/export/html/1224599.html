Janam Niyechi Dhulite Chahi Na Se Katha

Janam niyechi dhulite chahi na se katha bhulite
Dhanya janam dhulamoy dharanite
Charan parash dio
Phuler matan amare tulia niyo
Tomar pujai jena lagi
Toma tare jena sada jagi
Hriday amar toma pane
Chahe khulite
Diyona tomare bhulite

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
