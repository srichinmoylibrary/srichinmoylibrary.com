227. The human eye, the divine Eye

The human eye sleeps
In the room of dreams.

The divine Eye strikes
The Hour of Reality.

The human heart declares:
Love before you perish.

The divine Heart reveals:
Love and become,
Become and love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
