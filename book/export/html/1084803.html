12. No one can lose

God says:
No one must lose.

God says:
No one shall lose.

God says:
No one can lose.

No one must lose in the battlefield of life.
No one shall lose in the Heaven-winning race.
No one can lose in Eternity's cosmic Game.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 17, Agni Press, 1974
