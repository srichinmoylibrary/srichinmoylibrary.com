328. They declare

Silently
My soul declares
The Vision of the Supreme.

Tenderly
My heart declares
The Compassion of the Supreme.

Clearly
My mind declares
The Light of the Supreme.

Boldly
My vital declares
The Power of the Supreme.

Unerringly
My body declares
The Love of the Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 7, Agni Press, 1974
