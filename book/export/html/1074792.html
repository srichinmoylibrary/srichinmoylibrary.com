226. His heart

His heart of love
Became Krishna's Kadamba-tree.

His heart of wisdom
Became Buddha's Bodhi-tree.

His heart of cries
Became Ramakrishna's Panchavati-trees.

His heart of service
Became Chinmoy's Rupantar-tree.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 5, Agni Press, 1973
