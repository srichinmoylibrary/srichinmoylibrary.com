111.

Run, run, with your soul's
Dynamism-river-flow.
You are bound to succeed
In everything
That you want to do
And everything
That you want to become.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, part 2, Agni Press, 1996
