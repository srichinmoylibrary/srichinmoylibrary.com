45. A God-invoking work

A soulful work is the first reward.
A surrender-work is the best reward,
A God-invoking work
     Is the first and best dream-reward of God,
     The first and best reality-achievement of man.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When God-Love descends, Agni Press, 1975
