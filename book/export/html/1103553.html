5 July 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
If I am not for You only,
Sleeplessly and breathlessly,
Then why am I here on earth?
No reason for me
Even to stay for a second!
My Supreme, do give me the capacity
To live only for You.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

5 July 1999, 8:33 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
