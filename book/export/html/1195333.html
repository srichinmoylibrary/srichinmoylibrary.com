9601

If you are more than willing
To jump with God,
To run with God,
To fly with God,
You may not succeed outwardly,
But you have already succeeded inwardly
Beyond your imagination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 97, Agni Press, 1984
