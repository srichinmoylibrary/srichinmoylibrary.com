3023 On The Eve Of His Venture

On the eve of his hazardous venture
Three extremely intimate friends of his
Quite quietly deserted him:
His volcano-will,
His sky-peace,
His sun-joy.
Alas, alas, alas!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 31, Agni Press, 1982
