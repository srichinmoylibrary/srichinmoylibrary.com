6 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   This morning
Unconditionally You have shown me
The measureless depth
   Of Your Compassion-Eye.
Do accept the tears
   Of my gratitude-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 October 1999, 8:01 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
