7329

To delay in responding
To God's Compassion-Light
Is almost like refusing
God's Love.
To refuse God's Love
Is not only to feed
One's human desire-life,
But also to destroy
One's divine aspiration-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 74, Agni Press, 1984
