710 My Tasks...

MY TASKS

I have only four tasks
To perform:
Smile, fly, dive and run.

I have only three tasks
To perform:
Smile, fly and dive.

I have only two tasks
To perform:
Smile and fly.

I have only one task
To perform:
Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
