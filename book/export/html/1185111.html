783. The faithful and the faithless

He forgave the faithless ones;
Therefore
He is happy.

He loved the faithful ones:
Therefore
He is happy.

He became one with
The suffering of the faithless ones
And
The joy of the faithful ones;
Therefore
He is triumphantly happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
