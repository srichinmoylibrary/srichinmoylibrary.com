22 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Your Compassion-Eye awakens me.
Your Forgiveness-Heart feeds me.
Your Protection-Feet illumine me
And my all.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 September 2000, 10:02 a.m. Before doing 730-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
