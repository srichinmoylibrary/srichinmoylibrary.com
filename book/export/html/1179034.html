6051 A Wise Man Knows

A wise man knows
What to tell,
How to tell
And
When to tell.
A clever man foolishly tells
More than he knows
Or less than what
He is supposed to tell.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 61, Agni Press, 1983
