172. Everybody watched me

Everybody watched me
When I lost faith in God.
But now I have regained
All my faith in God.
But nobody comes to watch me.
They want me to be bound to ignorance-night.
And what do I want?
I want them only to be devoted
to the Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
