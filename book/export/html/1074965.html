396. The difference

O Lord Buddha,
   Please tell me the difference
        Between
Sujata's sweetmeat
            And
Chunda's venom-meat.

"Brother, the difference?
   Practically none!
Sujata's sweetmeat
Helped me openly in unlearning
The lessons of Ignorance.
Chunda's venom-meat
Helped me secretly in learning
The message of Nirvana."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 8, Agni Press, 1973
