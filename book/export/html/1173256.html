330 Time For Heaven To Descend

It is time for Heaven to descend.
Him to greet,
Fly with your soul
Bravely and immediately.
Him to serve,
Dive with your heart
Prayerfully and soulfully.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 4, Agni Press, 1979
