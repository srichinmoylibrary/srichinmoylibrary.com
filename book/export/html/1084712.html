23 When...

WHEN

When she concentrates,
The Sun-king gives her
Power infinite.
When she meditates,
The Moon-queen gives her
Love immortal.
When she contemplates,
The Sky-princess gives her
Concern eternal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
