506. Who will untie the knots?

The knots of my heart
Who will untie,
If not my forgiving soul?

The knots of my mind
Who will untie,
If not my loving heart?

The knots of my body
Who will untie,
If not my striving vital?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
