16824

O my Lord Supreme,
I am offering You
My heart's impurity,
I am offering You
My mind's insincerity,
I am offering You
My life's insecurity
So that I may become
A supremely choice instrument of yours.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 169, Agni Press, 1992
