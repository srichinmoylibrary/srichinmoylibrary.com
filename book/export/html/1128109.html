14696

My Lord, please allow me
To see the Light in Your own Way,
To feel the Light in Your own Way,
To become what You want me to be
In Your own Way,
Not my way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 15, Agni Press, 1999
