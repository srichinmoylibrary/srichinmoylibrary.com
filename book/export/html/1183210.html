74. Regularity and punctuality

Striking is the difference
Between
Regularity-power
And
Punctuality-light.
Regularity shows me
God’s Face
Eventually.
Punctuality makes me
God’s Heart
Immediately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
