87. The Indian love

The English language
Unites the whole world
But
Separates America from England.

The Indian love
Feeds the whole world
But
Strangles both the brothers:
Pakistan and India.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 2, Agni Press, 1974
