Gnome

(fairytale dwarf)

How can you be
Other than a gnome
In the world of spirituality
If you do not pray and meditate
Regularly plus soulfully?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
