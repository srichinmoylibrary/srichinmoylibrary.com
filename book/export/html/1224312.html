Diner Sheshe Pathe Base

Diner sheshe pathe base
Tomar pane chai
Se din prabhu balechhile
Pare pabo thai
Hasi mukhe chale gele
Jaoni prabhu abahele
Bhikkha magi tripti lagi
Jetha ami jai

Translation:

At the end of the day alone I stand
On the road looking for You.
Lord, the other day You told me
That You would grant me shelter
      at a later date.
You left me with a smile.
You did not ignore me.
Lord, wherever I go I beg only for
      one thing:
Satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 3, , 1976
