66.

"My Lord, is there anything which,
Once attained, is never lost?"

"Yes, My child, there is something.
There is something, and you already have it."

"I have it, yet I do not know what it is!
My Lord, for God's sake,
Do not torture my curiosity.
Please let me know what it is
That I have that will never be lost."

"My supremely stupid child,
You can never and will never lose
Your self-knowledge,
Your self-realisation."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Secrets revealed, Agni Press, 1973
