50. Three sublime things

He always does
Not one, but three sublime things:
     He loves God openly,
     He keeps God's Blessing-Fruit safely,
     He distributes God-Delight unreservedly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When God-Love descends, Agni Press, 1975
