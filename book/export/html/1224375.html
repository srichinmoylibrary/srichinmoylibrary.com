Mahiruha Mato

Mahiruha mato
Habo ami nato
Khama giri mato
Habo unnata
Jananir mato
Sada jagrato
Dharanir mato
Bandana rato

Translation:

Like the tree I shall bow down.
Like the mountain I shall forgive
And keep my head high.
Like the mother I shall always remain awake.
Like the heart I shall always worship.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 4, , 1981
