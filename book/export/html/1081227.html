296. I must retire

My Lord, if You want to fire me, fire;
I must retire.
I must enjoy some rest
In Your sleep-nourishing Nest.
I shall not be mad,
I shall not feel sad.
Fire me, my Lord, fire.
I needs must retire.

From: Sri Chinmoy, From the Source to the Source, Agni Press, 1974
