24018

My Lord Supreme,
I do not know how I dare to say
That I love You deeply
When I consciously and deliberately
Do not want to be one
With Your all-knowing and all-fulfilling Will.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 241, Agni Press, 1998
