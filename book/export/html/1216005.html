378. Sometimes I think

Sometimes
I think I am great.
Therefore
I dare to govern others.

Sometimes
I think I am good.
Therefore
I dare to love others.

Sometimes
I think I am God's.
Therefore
I dare to claim all
As my own,
My very own.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 8, Agni Press, 1974
