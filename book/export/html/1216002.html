375. My first choice

Believing, not seeing:
My fourth choice.

Feeling, not thinking:
My third choice.

Loving, not expecting:
My second choice.

Being, not becoming:
My first choice.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 8, Agni Press, 1974
