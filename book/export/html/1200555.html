14772

God, can You tell me
How many drops there are
In the ocean?
This is the wrong question.
God, will You make my heart's
Gratitude-tears
As countless as the drops
Of the ocean?
This is the right question.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 148, Agni Press, 1991
