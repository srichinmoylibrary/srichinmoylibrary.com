3.

The English language
Unites the whole world
But
Separates America from England.

The Indian love
Feeds the whole world
But
Fails to unite the brothers:
Pakistan and India.

From: Sri Chinmoy, India, my India. Mother India's summit-prides, Agni Press, 1997
