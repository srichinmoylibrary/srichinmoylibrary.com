Ashar Duar Khulechi Aj Ashar Duar

Ashar duar khuylechi aj ashar duar
Taito heri chaudike mor mukti jowar

Translation:

Hope-door I have opened up today.
Therefore, I see all around me
The flood of liberation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ashar duar, part 1, , 2004
