584. If you just look at me

My heart shall bloom
into adoration
If You just once look at me.

My life-tree shall bear
Nectar-fruit
If You just touch it
With Your Benediction-Compassion-Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
