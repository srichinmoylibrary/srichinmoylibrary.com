Balogo kemane premamoy dane
Hriday sagare pabo
Shripad sebibo das haye rabo
Tomar sathe sathe jabo
Jagater dhan hasi ananda
Lagibena mor kichu
Chhutibona ar alik swapan
Maya hariner pichu
