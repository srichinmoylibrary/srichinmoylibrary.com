76. When I talk to man

When I talk to man
I see that each discussion is
A public meeting
Without a microphone.

Naturally I shout,
I bark and
I do not even hesitate to bite
When necessity demands.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 2, Agni Press, 1974
