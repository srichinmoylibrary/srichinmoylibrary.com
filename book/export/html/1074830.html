263. I am of God and I am for God

Helpless is Time
When I sleep.

Useless is Time
When I doubt.

Hopeless is Time
When I despair.

Godless is Time
When I halt.

Measureless is Time
When I move.

Birthless is Time
When I am of God.

Deathless is Time
When I am for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 6, Agni Press, 1973
