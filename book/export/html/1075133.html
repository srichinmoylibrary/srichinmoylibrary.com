560 Before...

BEFORE

Duty before pleasure,
Compassion before justice,
Offering before receiving,
Heart before mind,
Faith-flame before doubt-net,
God-realisation before world-perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 12, Agni Press, 1973
