2615 My Meditation Tells Me

My short meditation tells me:
God can and does see me
All the time.

My long meditation tells me:
I can and will see God
If I really and desperately want to.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
