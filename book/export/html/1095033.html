70 We Can Overfeed Ourselves Easily ...

We can overfeed ourselves easily,
But we can never overfeed
Our Lord Supreme
With our love, devotion and surrender.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 16, Agni Press, 2003
