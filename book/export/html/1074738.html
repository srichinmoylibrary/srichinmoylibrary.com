173. I cry

        I cry
When my heart-sea
Is black with desires.        I cry
When my mind-sky
Is grey with desires.        I cry
When my vital-forest
Is green with desires.        I cry
When my body-desert
Is red with desires.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 4, Agni Press, 1973
