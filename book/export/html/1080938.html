7. I love

I love, O world,
No, not because I give.
I give because I love,
Because I live
In my Master's vision-sky
With my selfless glow.
Perfection now am I
In my breathless flow.

From: Sri Chinmoy, From the Source to the Source, Agni Press, 1974
