623 No Backward Life...

NO BACKWARD LIFE

No eyes to look backward!
Therefore
I am happy.

No feet to walk backward!
Therefore
I am happy.

No mind to think backward!
Therefore
I am happy.
My sweet Lord is happy, too.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
