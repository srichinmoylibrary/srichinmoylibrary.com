4. Sincerity

   Real sincerity
Is the sincerity
That sees reality
The way reality wants to be seen,
And that becomes the reality itself.
   Real sincerity
Does not see reality
With its own eye.
   Real sincerity
Sees reality
With the eye of reality itself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Union and oneness, Agni Press, 1976
