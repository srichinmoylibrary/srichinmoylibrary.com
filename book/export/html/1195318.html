My Beloved Supreme,
I know, I know,
When You ask me to do something
And when You ask others to do something
But do not ask me,
It is invariably for my own good.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 96, Agni Press, 1984
