24574

I have good news and bad news.
The good news is that I love God
With each and every breath of my heart.
The bad news is that I still care for
World-admiration and world-acclaim.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 246, Agni Press, 1998
