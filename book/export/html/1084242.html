12. God's bounden duty

O my infant eyes,
Don't worry!
God is nearing
Your vision-light.
O my orphan heart,
Don't worry!
God the Father
And
God the Mother
Are totally incomplete
Without you.
Therefore
It is Their bounden duty
To blossom within you
And
For you, for you alone.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 6, Agni Press, 1974
