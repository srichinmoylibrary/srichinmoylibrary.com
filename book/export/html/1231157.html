Ore Amar Man Agyanatar Dhan

Ore amar man
Agyanatar dhan
Ore amar man
Kanna sanatan
Ore amar man
Bandhan chirantan
Ore amar man
Mrityu anukkhan

Translation:

O my mind,
You are the treasure of ignorance-night.
O my mind,
You are my eternal cry.
O my mind,
You are my sempiternal bondage.
O my mind,
You are my constant death.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, teach me how to cry, , 1975
