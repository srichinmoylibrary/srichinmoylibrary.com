3 September 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Only when I sincerely
Love and serve the world
Do You allow my life to blossom
In Your own Heart-Garden.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 September 2000, 7:40 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
