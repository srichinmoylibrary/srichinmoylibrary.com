714. I have found your nest

O Lord,
In Your Eyes of Beauty
I have found Your Nest.
Delight is my name
When I live in Your Nest.
The heart of a singing bird
My life becomes
In song's world of silence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
