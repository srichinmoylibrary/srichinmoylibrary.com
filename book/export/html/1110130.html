66.

President Gorbachev's own nobility-mind,
Magnanimity-heart and luminosity-soul
Speak:
"Suppose we make mistakes. So what?
It is better to rectify them
Than sit and wait."
His compatriots, other politicians,
Have made no mistakes!
Never, never!! Not even in their
Imagination-worlds!!!

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: the home of Oneness-Peace-Dream-World, Agni Press, 2001
