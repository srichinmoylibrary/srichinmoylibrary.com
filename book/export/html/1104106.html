10 February 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do You really read my troubled heart
Every day?
“Yes, My child, I do.
In fact,
I pay first and foremost attention
To your troubled heart every day.”
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 February 2000, 8:17 a.m. Before lifting 450 lbs. with each arm simultaneously six times. (Total: 900 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
