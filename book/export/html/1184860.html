532. I am doing it for God

I flung my soul into the sky.
God saw it.
He enjoyed my courage.

He now asks me
To throw my soul
Into the fathomless abyss.

Lo, I am doing it,
His Eyes to entertain,
His Heart to feed.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
