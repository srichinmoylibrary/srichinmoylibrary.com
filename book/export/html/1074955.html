386. Hidden treasures

   Love is hidden
Even from those who express it.   Joy is hidden
Even from those who feel it.   Truth is hidden
Even from those who know it.   But
God is not hidden
Even from those
Who want Him not,
Who need Him not.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 8, Agni Press, 1973
