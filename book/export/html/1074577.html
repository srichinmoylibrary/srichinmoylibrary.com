15. I am happy

I am happy
Because
My mind has forgotten how to
Calculate.
I am happy
Because
My heart has forgotten how to
Hesitate.
I am happy
Because
My life always knows how to
Tolerate.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 1, Agni Press, 1973
