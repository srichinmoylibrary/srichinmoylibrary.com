8. Doubt-king, Faith-queen

O Doubt-king,
No room is left
In my mind-room.
Therefore
Go away,
Go away.
Who wants you?
Who needs you?
O Faith-queen,
Still there is room
In my heart to fill;
Therefore
Come in,
Do come in.
I need you.
God's entire creation needs you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 14, Agni Press, 1974
