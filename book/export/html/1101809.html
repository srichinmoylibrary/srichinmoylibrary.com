51.

My Lord,
Will my life's service-tree
Be able to inspire
One hundred seekers?
"My child,
Only one hundred?
What a shock!
What an insult to your soul
And to Me!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 2, Agni Press, 1998
