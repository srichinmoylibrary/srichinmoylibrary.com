First

First
In inspiration-light
To the President.

First
In aspiration-height
With the President.

First
In encouragement-might
For the President.

First
In fulfilment-delight
With the President.

First
Russian child
Admired, adored and loved
By the four corners
Of the globe.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Gorbachev: the master-key of the Universal Heart, Agni Press, 1990
