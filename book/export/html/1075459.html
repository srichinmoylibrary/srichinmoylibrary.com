880 I Saw Him, I Felt Him, I Knew Him...

I SAW HIM, I FELT HIM, I KNEW HIM

I saw Him
When He was building
Castles in the air.

I felt Him
When He was crying
For a piece of bread.

I knew Him
When I gave Him
An iota of authority.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
