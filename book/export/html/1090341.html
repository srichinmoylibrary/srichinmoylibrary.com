45.

"My child, do not worry.
I have already
Forgiven you.
I am now preparing Myself
To help you embark
On a new Journey
And watch your heart's
Blossoming, singing
And
Dancing
Rainbow-Sunrise."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Love, compassion, forgiveness, Agni Press, 1993
