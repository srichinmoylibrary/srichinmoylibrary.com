Amar Prane Mago Tumi Eso Sabar

Amar prane mago tumi eso sabar sheshe
Aghat karo prane amar madhur hasi hese
Bhalobasar aghat se je mor paraner chaowa
Sei aghate nritya kare amar sakal paowa

Translation:

Mother, come into my heart at last, at the end of all.
Strike my heart hard with your sweet Smile.
I want this torture and punishment of Yours.
My heartâ€™s choice is the torture of Your Love.
All my achievements dance in Your torture.
