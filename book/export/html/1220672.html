In the Darkness of My Mind-Night

In the darkness of my mind-night,
I call You ‘God’.
You smile at me.
In the darkness of my mind-night,
I call You ‘my Lord’.
You bless my head.
In the darkness of my mind-night,
I call You ‘Comrade’.
You shake my hands.
In the darkness of my mind-night,
I call You ‘my Beloved’.
You embrace me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Enthusiasm, part 11, , 2008
