370. Upward, always upward

My body endlessly sleeps.
My vital spreads
Its aggression in all directions.
My mind spreads
Its suspicion in four directions.
My heart spreads
Its insecurity
Only in one direction -
Upward, always
Upward.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 8, Agni Press, 1974
