9 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   O my doubting mind,
   O my suspicious mind,
   O my jealous mind,
You immediately need
The blooms and the blossoms
   Of a oneness-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 October 1999, 9:10 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously twice. (Total: 600 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
