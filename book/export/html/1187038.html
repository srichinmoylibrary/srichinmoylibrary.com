1389

My mind was looking for
A doubt-tree.
My heart was looking for
A faith-plant.
My life was looking for
A surrender-flower.
My soul was looking for
A perfection-fruit.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 14, Agni Press, 1983
