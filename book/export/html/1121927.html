8520

God will never be pleased
If you bury yourself
In the Himalayan caves
For God-realisation
  And
God-manifestation.
Those ancient God-practice-days
Are absolutely unnecessary
  For today’s man.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 9, Agni Press, 1998
