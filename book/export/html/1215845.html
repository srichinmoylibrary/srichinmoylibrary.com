221. Two are better, one is better

Two are better than one.
Therefore
I always stay
With the Compassion of the Supreme.

One is better than two.
Therefore
I never stay
With my personal efforts.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
