374. The prince of my morning

My hope is the prince
Of my morning.

My determination is the king
Of my noon.

My dedication is the emperor
Of my evening.

My realisation is the supreme monarch
Of my night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
