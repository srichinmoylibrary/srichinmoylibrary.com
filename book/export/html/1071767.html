March 18

Love mankind. This is an instruction of God. Love truth. This is the instruction of God. Grow into the silence-height and the manifestation-light. This is the only instruction of God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
