1 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Man is man's overdue
Gratitude-heart to God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 June 2000, 9:31 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,200 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
