19338

Fearful are my mind's tears.
Deceitful are my vital's tears.
Beautiful are my heart's tears.
Powerful are my soul's tears.
Fruitful are my life's tears.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 194, Agni Press, 1993
