7. No one

You are asking me
To open my eyes.
Tell me, who is there to appreciate
The God-beauty of my eyes?
No one, absolutely no one.

You are asking me
To use my arms.
Tell me, who is there to appreciate
The God-duty of my arms?
No one, eternally no one.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
