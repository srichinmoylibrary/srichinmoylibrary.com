68. Is God partial?

Is God partial?
Yes, He is.
He is partial to weak
Human beings.
Why?
Because this is the only way
He can elevate and illumine them.

Is God impartial?
Yes, He is.
He is impartial to strong
Cosmic gods.
Why?
Because this is the only way
He can train and utilise them.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
