4814

God is really sad
When He sees that He is
Your second-best friend.

God is not happy
When He sees that He happens to be
Only your best friend.

God will be happy and proud
Of Himself and of you
Only when you consider Him to be
Your only Friend.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 49, Agni Press, 1984
