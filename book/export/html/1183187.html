51. Teach me how to become

Father, do teach me
How to become.
Why, My daughter, why?
Father, if You teach me
How to become,
Only then shall I be able
To become
Your egoless child,
Your surrendered child,
Your perfect child.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
