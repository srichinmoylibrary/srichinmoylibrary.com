491. Don't mix with him!

Don't mix with him!
He enjoys spiritual fuss.

Don't mix with him!
He indulges in spiritual jugglery.

Don't mix with him!
He shamelessly takes pride
In belittling all Masters
Except his own.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
