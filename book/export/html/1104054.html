8 January 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Out of Your infinite Bounty,
You are replacing
My disappointment-tears
With Your Enlightenment-Smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 January 2000, 5:58 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before lifting 400 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 800 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
