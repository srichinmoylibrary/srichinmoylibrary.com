143. My realisations

My morning realisation:
Receive from God, receive.
My noon realisation:
Achieve perfection in your life, achieve.
My evening realisation:
Conceive God-satisfaction, conceive.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
