526 In Me They Need...

IN ME THEY NEED

The animal
In me needs
Freedom from aggression.

The human
In me needs
Freedom from fear.

The divine
In me needs
Freedom from ignorance.

The Supreme
In me needs
Freedom from Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 11, Agni Press, 1973
