Bidai Bela Balbo Ami

Bidai bela balbo ami ekti shudhu katha
Param prabhu dur karogo dharar badhirata

Translation:

At the end of my life journey,
I have only one prayer, one message:
O my Lord Supreme,
Remove the deafness of the world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Khunjite chahigo, part 1, , 2003
