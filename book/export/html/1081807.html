248. Something is better than nothing

O my vital,
Do you want to please my Lord
Unwillingly?
Then do it.
Something is better than nothing.
My God will definitely bless you.

O my mind,
Do you want to satisfy my God
Unconsciously?
Then do it.
Something is better than nothing.
My God will definitely bless you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
