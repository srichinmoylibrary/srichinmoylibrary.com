158. A great runner

He was a great runner.
In the outer world he ran
To satisfy his quenchless thirst
For name and fame.
In the inner world he ran
To feed and satisfy
The God-hunger in him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
