483. I shall lean on you

Lord Supreme,
With my human heart
I shall lean on Your Compassion-smile
And
Divine Heart.

Lord Supreme,
With my human life
I shall lean on Your Perfection-smile
And
Immortal Life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
