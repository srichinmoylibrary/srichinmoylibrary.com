30 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
May my soul's divinity-light
Permeate each and every action
Of my daily life.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

30 July 1999, 8:43 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 85 lbs. with one arm.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
