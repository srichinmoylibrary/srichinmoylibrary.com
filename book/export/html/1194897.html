9170

The simpler the life, the better.
The purer the mind, the better.
The deeper the heart, the better.
The higher the self-giving, the better.
The quicker the God-realisation, the better.
The stronger the God-oneness, the better.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 92, Agni Press, 1984
