4392 Don't Sleep

Don't sleep
If you are going to help others.
Don't sleep
While you are helping others.
Don't sleep
Even when you unconsciously accept
The responsibility of helping others.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 44, Agni Press, 1982
