533 He Will Wake Up...

HE WILL WAKE UP

He talks.
He is a man of empty talk.

He lives.
He lives a purposeless life.

He yields.
He constantly yields to cowardice.

He is.
He is hopelessly faint-hearted.

Yet one day
He will wake up.
He will wake up
And listen to the clarion call
Of his Inner Pilot.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 11, Agni Press, 1973
