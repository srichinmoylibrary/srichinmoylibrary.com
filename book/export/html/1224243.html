Ke Amare Chai

Ke amare chai
     Megher aral hate
     Dake ai ai
Ke jena amare tane
     Chali unmad sama
     Ke jane kothai

Translation:

Who wants to see me
Hiding inside the veil of teeming clouds?
Who calls me so lovingly and tenderly —
     “Come, come”?
Who pulls me so powerfully towards Him
Like a volcano?
Love.
I am now running
Fearless, free, towards Him alone.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 1, , 1978
