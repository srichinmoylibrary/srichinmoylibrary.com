5156 I Do Not Have To Remind God

I do not have to remind God
Of all His Promises,
For God sleeplessly
And unconditionally
Wants to reveal Himself
In and through
My tiny little heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 52, Agni Press, 1983
