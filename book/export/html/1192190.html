6490

Beyond my imagination!
Not only did God grant me
Illumination,
But He also remembered
My desire-life
So that He could purify it
And make it His Aspiration-Life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 65, Agni Press, 1984
