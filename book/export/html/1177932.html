4960 You Wear The Crown Of Miseries

You wear the crown of miseries,
Not because you have sadly failed
In your life,
But because you have not surrendered soulfully
To the Beauty of God's Omniscience-Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 50, Agni Press, 1982
