1367

Life is God's Game
And He has invited you
To play with Him.
Right now you are not
Scoring properly,
But do not worry.
Again and again
God Himself will teach you
How to score successfully.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 14, Agni Press, 1983
