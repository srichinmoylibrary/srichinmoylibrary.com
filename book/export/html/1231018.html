Nai Nai Nai Ami Ar Nai

Nai nai nai
Ami ar nai
Shudhu achhe ek chhaya
Sahasa sonali
Alor jhalake
Se o je harai kaya

Translation:

Nothing there is.
I no longer exist.
Only a shadow,
And even that shadow
Has lost its body-form
And is transformed
By the flash of golden light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, I sing only for You, , 1974
