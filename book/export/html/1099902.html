67. I need you not

O Time and Space,
I need you not.
My breath has caught
My Supreme's Face.

O Depth and Height,
I need you not.
My soul has bought
Reality's Light.

O Golden Goal,
I need you not.
My Lord has sought
My Journey's Soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Flute, Agni Press, 1972
