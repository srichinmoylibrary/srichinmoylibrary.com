175 His True Nature

His hatred of man
Is all in his head.
His love of God in his heart
Will inundate soon
His entire being.
For nothing
Save and except love
Is his true nature.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 2, Agni Press, 1979
