Amar Mato Tomai Hate Habe

Amar mato tomai hate habe
Tomar pane chahi ami jabe
Dekhi jena amar jyoti bhabe

Translation:

You shall have to become
An exact prototype
Of my soul-reality.
When I look at you
I want to see
My own world of Light supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My green adoration-gifts, part 1, , 1977
