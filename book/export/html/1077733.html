86. Tears

There was a time
When my tears filled my years;
Therefore
I clearly saw God's Heart
Of infinite Beauty.
But now
My tears have empty years;
Therefore
I carry my heavy life
Of inescapable duty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
