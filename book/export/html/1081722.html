163. Learn to unlearn

Think of doing the things
That give you success.

Learn to do the things
That offer you progress.

Learn to shun
And
Learn to unlearn
The things that bring about
Your immediate regress.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
