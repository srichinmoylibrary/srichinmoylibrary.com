965. Your climbing cry and spreading smile

The golden Shore
Will beckon you.

The golden Boat
Will inspire you.

The golden Boatman
Will carry you.

Just permit not time
To devour your climbing cry
        And
Your spreading smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
