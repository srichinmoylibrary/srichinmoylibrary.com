Mind preaches more than it actually teaches. Heart teaches more than it usually preaches. The soul neither preaches nor teaches. It only learns. It learns the meaning of creation from God Himself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
