989 I Shall Not Fail You...

I SHALL NOT FAIL YOU

Even
If you doubt me,
I shall not fail you.

Even
If you betray me,
I shall not fail you.

Even
If you deny me,
I shall not fail you.

But
I shall fail you
If ever you treasure self-pity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
