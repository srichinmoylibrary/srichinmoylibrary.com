1607

You are telling me
That you do not have any friend.
Nobody thinks of you,
Nobody likes you,
Nobody loves you,
Nobody treasures you.
Well, I clearly see that you have
An excellent friend.
Your ignorance-friend does think of you,
Does like you,
Does love you,
Does treasure you at every moment.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 17, Agni Press, 1983
