29 December 2004 (II)

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

You are telling me that
The depth of Your Forgiveness
And the height of my happiness
Are inseparable.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 17, Agni Press, 2005
