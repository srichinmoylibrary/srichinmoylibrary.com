236. Remember

Remember,
Your Master may be
Dissatisfied with your weak efforts,
But he can never be
Dissatisfied with your life.

Remember,
Your Master may
Scold you outwardly, ruthlessly,
But he loves you
Secretly,
Deeply,
Wholeheartedly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
