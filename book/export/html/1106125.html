Big “tumours”

I admire the huge muscles of bodybuilders like anything. I admire them, but I am not inspired to have them myself. When the bodybuilders show their biceps, here and there you see big tumours. I try so hard to have at least something in my own muscles, but they remain flat, whereas the bodybuilders have big tumours.

— 19 January 1986

From: Sri Chinmoy, My weightlifting tears and smiles, part 1, Agni Press, 1986
