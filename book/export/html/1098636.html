My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 60

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Cha-Am and Chiang Mai, Thailand in January and February 2007.

31 January 2007

Cha-Am, Thailand

1 God Watches Our Tears ...

God watches our tears,
But we do not have time to wait
For Him to comfort us.

2 This World Shall Remain ...

This world shall remain
Imperfect
Until our eyes meet
With God’s Eye.

3 Aspiration Is The Way ...

Aspiration is the way
To prove
That we need God.

4 Dedication Is The Way ...

Dedication is the way
To prove
That we love God.

5 Heart ...

Heart
Is self-giving.
Life
Is God-becoming.

6 Willingness ...

Willingness
Is
God-pride-servant.

7 My God-Gratitude-Heart ...

My God-gratitude-heart
Saves me
From my mind’s miserable days
And nights.

8 Only When I Ceaselessly Cry ...

Only when I ceaselessly cry
For God
Do I climb up my life’s
Perfection-tree.

9 When I Hear About ...

When I hear about
God coming,
My life thrills
With ecstasy’s delight.

10 The Server In Me Says ...

The server in me says,
“I am God’s.”

11 The Lover In Me Says ...

The lover in me says,
“God is mine.”

1 February 2007 ...

1 February 2007

Cha-Am, Thailand

12 God’s Complete Confidence ...

God’s complete confidence
Is my faith-heart.

13 If We Really Want To Run ...

If we really want to run
The fastest,
We must take God seriously.

14 Only One Thing I Have ...

Only one thing I have
In my possession:
God’s Compassion.

15 God-Obedience ...

God-obedience
Is the perfect key
To open God’s Heart-Door.

16 I Tell God ...

I tell God,
“No matter how far the journey is,
Do take me with You.”

17 Humanity Deserves ...

Humanity deserves
A final warning
For constant God-disobedience.

18 Every Soul ...

Every soul
Is an ever-blossoming
Dream of God.

19 Silence Is God’s ...

Silence is God’s
Most favourite messenger.

20 Uncover Yourself ...

Uncover yourself
To discover God.

21 If We Pray To God ...

If we pray to God
On our knees,
We immediately feel
God’s infinite Love.

2 February 2007 ...

2 February 2007

Cha-Am, Thailand

22 Like My Soul ...

Like my soul,
I, too, want to please
My Lord
Throughout Eternity.

23 My Aspiration-Heart ...

My aspiration-heart
Deepens every year.

24 My Dedication-Life ...

My dedication-life
Widens every year.

25 My Soul ...

My soul
Is absolutely perfect
Because it is God-made.

26 I Pray To God ...

I pray to God
To give me the capacity
To mingle
In all the tearing sorrows
And beaming joys
Of the world.

27 I Am Absolutely Certain ...

I am absolutely certain
That my Lord will never ask me
To do anything
Beyond my capacity.

28 I Fill My Days And Nights ...

I fill my days and nights
With my Lord’s
Compassion-Eye-Smiles.

29 My Heart’s Dream ...

My heart’s dream
Is
God-realisation.

30 My Life’s Dream ...

My life’s dream
Is
God-revelation.

31 My Soul’s Dream ...

My soul’s dream
Is
God-manifestation.

3 February 2007 ...

3 February 2007

Cha-Am, Thailand

32 When I Dive Deep Within ...

When I dive deep within,
I breathe the fresh air
Of a new dawn.

33 God Himself Plies My Life-Boat ...

God Himself plies my life-boat
Between two shores:
The known and the Unknown.

34 When I Veil My Mind ...

When I veil my mind,
I unveil Heaven.

35 My Only Choice ...

My only choice:
God’s earth-lifting
And Heaven-climbing
Voice.

36 God Includes ...

God includes
Each and every human being
In His Cosmic Play.

37 God Asks Us ...

God asks us
Direct questions.
He expects from us
Direct answers.

38 A Spiritual Master ...

A spiritual Master
Gladly gives himself away,
Although he badly suffers.

39 God Forgives Us ...

God forgives us,
Not because we deserve
His Forgiveness,
But because He surrenders
To His own Compassion-Heart.

5 February 2007 ...

5 February 2007

Cha-Am, Thailand

40 Even An Iota ...

Even an iota
Of our God-manifestation
Thrills God.

41 My Lord, May I Be Always ...

My Lord, may I be always
In between
Your Infinity’s Peace
And
Your Eternity’s Silence.

42 The Outer Beauty ...

The outer beauty
Is the charming beauty
Of a child —
It does not last for long.

43 The Inner Beauty ...

The inner beauty
Knows no death.

44 God Has Infinite Rooms ...

God has infinite rooms,
But our hearts
Are His favourite rooms.

45 I Wish To Be Found ...

I wish to be found
Always in the company
Of God-Feet-worshipping seekers.

46 I Love To Wake Up ...

I love to wake up
With the rising sun
And run against
The thickening clouds.

47 A Songful Heart ...

A songful heart
Is
A fruitful life.

6 February 2007 ...

6 February 2007

Chiang Mai, Thailand

48 Do Not Follow ...

Do not follow
The stars of the sky,
But follow
The sun of your heart.

49 Why Is Surrender So Difficult? ...

Why is surrender so difficult?
Because our heart-tears
Are not pure and sincere.

50 Each Seeker Needs ...

Each seeker needs
A totally different
Aspiration-road.

51 Prayer Embodies ...

Prayer embodies
The beauty of life.

52 Meditation Reveals ...

Meditation reveals
The fragrance of the heart.

53 Jealousy And Insecurity ...

Jealousy and insecurity
Are twin sisters.

54 God Wants Me ...

God wants me
To raise my consciousness
Instead of praising Him
On my knees.

55 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That I have come a long way
With Him —
Can I not go to the end?

7 February 2007 ...

7 February 2007

Chiang Mai, Thailand

56 Again And Again ...

Again and again,
God wants me to be
His brave partner
And not His mild follower.

57 No Earth-Born Human Being ...

No earth-born human being
Can fathom
The secrets of life and death.

58 Desire ...

Desire
Is a blazing fire
In the mind.

59 God’s Way ...

God’s Way
Is to act and act
And not to sit and wait.

60 Self-Styled Gods ...

Self-styled gods
Are everywhere, alas!

61 Life Has To Be ...

Life has to be
A God-fulfilling story
And not
Man-imagined history.

62 The Inner Oneness ...

The inner oneness
Has to be manifested
As the outer goodness
At least.

63 I Love And Treasure ...

I love and treasure
My God-ordained
Soul-authority.

8 February 2007 ...

8 February 2007

Chiang Mai, Thailand

64 Self-Giving ...

Self-giving
Is by far the best
Among our divine qualities.

65 Every Day ...

Every day,
God injects His Compassion
Into my heart-breath.

66 My Devotion ...

My devotion
Is
My God-Sweetness-intoxication.

67 Each Day ...

Each day
God wants me to dream
Of His high, higher, highest Heaven.

68 Alas, Our God-Ingratitude-Life ...

Alas, our God-ingratitude-life
Does not allow God
To feed us every day.

69 When My Heart Sings ...

When my heart sings,
Even my doubting mind
Enjoys the song.

70 My Lord Enjoys ...

My Lord enjoys
My surrender-life-moments
Mightily and proudly.

71 God’s Presence ...

God’s Presence
Embraces my soul.
God’s Presence
Thrills my heart.
God’s Presence
Disentangles my mind.
God’s Presence
Feeds my life.

9 February 2007 ...

9 February 2007

Chiang Mai, Thailand

72 An Ideal Seeker ...

An ideal seeker
Lives
On the God-cradled earth.

73 Surrender We Must ...

Surrender we must
Our earthly possessions
If we really want to receive
Smiles from God.

74 A Desire-Mind ...

A desire-mind
And a frustration-life
Together live.

75 A Prayer-Pond-Diver ...

A prayer-pond-diver
I was.

76 A Meditation-River-Diver ...

A meditation-river-diver
I now am.

77 A Surrender-Sea-Diver ...

A surrender-sea-diver
I shall become.

78 Even God Finds It Difficult ...

Even God finds it difficult
To recognise me
When I deliberately enjoy
My desire-forest-life.

79 God Wants Me To Remain ...

God wants me to remain
At my Himalayan aspiration-height
And not to come down
To my old anthill-height.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 60, Agni Press, 2008
