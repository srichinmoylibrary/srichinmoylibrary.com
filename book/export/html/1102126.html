73.

"My Lord,
Is there anything sweeter
Than forgiveness?"

"Yes, My child,
Gratitude is by far sweeter
Than forgiveness."

"My Lord,
Is there anything more fulfilling
Than the feeling of oneness?"

"Yes, My child,
When you feel that you are the Eternal Lover
And I am your Eternal Beloved,
And when you feel that I am the Eternal Lover
And you are My Eternal Beloved,
The feeling of oneness is supremely transcended
And immortally fulfilled."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Secrets revealed, Agni Press, 1973
