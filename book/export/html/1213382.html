40.

Sincerity,
I do love you and need you.
But I am telling you
In all sincerity
That I love and need
God's Will-Manifestation-Delight
On earth
Infinitely more.
Nay, God's Fulfilment-Smile
Is the only thing
That I sleeplessly need.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two divine qualities: confidence and sincerity, Agni Press, 1999
