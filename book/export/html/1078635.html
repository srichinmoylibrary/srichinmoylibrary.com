988 I Shall Never Fail You...

I SHALL NEVER FAIL YOU

I shall never fail you.
Never!
My promise-flood depends not
Upon your aspiration-drop,
Or your perfection-grain.
It depends only on my own
Love-thirsty and
Oneness-hungry
Necessity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
