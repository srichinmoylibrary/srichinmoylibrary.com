136. Nobody wants to confess

Light is the harbinger of God-Day.
Everybody knows that.
Night is the harbinger of world-death.
Nobody knows that.
Why?
Just because nobody dares to expose himself.
Nobody wants to confess
That he is the sole cause of night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
