Lord, I Am Tired

Lord, I am tired.
“Since you are tired, my son,
Even before your journey’s start,
I shall run for you.”
Lord, I am tired.
“Since you are tired, my son,
Even before your journey’s start,
I shall sail your dream-boat
And reach for you
Your golden Reality-Shore.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, The goal is won, , 1994
