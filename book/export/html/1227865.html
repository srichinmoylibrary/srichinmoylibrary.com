Bhikkha Magi Bhikkha

Bhikkha magi bhikkha
Dikkha dao aj dikkha
Bhikkha magi bhikkha
Nutan jiban shikkha
Bhikkha magi bhikkha
Hok shesh mor pratikkha

Translation:

I implore Your alms divine.
Do initiate me, my earth-bound life.
