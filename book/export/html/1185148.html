820. Behind all shadows of adversity

Behind all shadows of adversity
There is prosperity.
Wait and see!

Beauty's Infinity,
Infinity's Beauty
Will clasp you behind all shadows
Of adversity-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
