300. Invisible goal

Invisible goal:
The transcendence-light
Of silence.

Visible goal:
The immanence-might
Of sound.

Unknowable smile:
The Perfection-satisfaction
Of God.

Unknown smile:
The aspiration-liberation
Of man.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
