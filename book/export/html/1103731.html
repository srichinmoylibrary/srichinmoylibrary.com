4 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The pure prayer-heart of a seeker
Is bound to succeed.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 September 1999, 8:16 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with one arm forty-four times. (29 right, 15 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
