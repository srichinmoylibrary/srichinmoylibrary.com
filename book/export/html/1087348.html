29. He is extremely rich

In the inner world
He is extremely rich;
Therefore
He can easily afford
To cry every day for God.

In the outer world
He is extremely rich;
Therefore
He can easily afford
To smile every day at man.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I need only God, Agni Press, 1975
