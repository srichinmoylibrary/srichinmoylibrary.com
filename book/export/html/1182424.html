9407 I Am Divinely Happy

I am divinely happy
That my heart is now experiencing
Eternity's hunger.

I am supremely happy
That my soul is always ready
With Immortality's meal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 95, Agni Press, 1983
