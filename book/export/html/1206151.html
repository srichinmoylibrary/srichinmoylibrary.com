Alas, my Lord,
Why do I disappoint You every day?
"My child,
You disappoint Me every day
Because every day you faithfully keep
Your appointment with ignorance-night."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 204, Agni Press, 1994
