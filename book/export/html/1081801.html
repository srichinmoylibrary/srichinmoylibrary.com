242. Consider me something

Lord, consider me something,
At least.
You may call me Your enemy.
I shall not mind,
For without fail,
You shall perfect me.

Lord, consider me something,
At least.
You may, by mistake,
Call me Your best instrument.
I shall not mind,
For without fail,
You shall perfect me.

Lord, consider me something,
At least.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
