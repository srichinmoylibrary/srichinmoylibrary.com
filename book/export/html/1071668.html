Louse: Atrocity

Louse, my louse,
Why do you devour
With your atrocity-hunger
Man's purity-head and beauty-hair?
It is not fair,
It is not fair!
God's Compassion-flood
Shall save man and his innocence.
Man's angry hands
Shall destroy you, your
life-blood.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Animal Kingdom, Agni Press, 1973
