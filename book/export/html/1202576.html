16773

The physical in us
Touches the moon-land.
Indeed, this is man's greatness.
The spiritual in us
Loves the moon-consciousness.
Indeed, this is man's goodness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 168, Agni Press, 1992
