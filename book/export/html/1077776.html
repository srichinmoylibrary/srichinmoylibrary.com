129. The pride of life

The pride of life
Is the mortal triumph
Of a fleeting hour.
Your vital has at last accepted
This fact inevitable.

The lustre of life
Is the immortal triumph
Of an eternal day.
Your heart has now discovered
This truth sublime.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
