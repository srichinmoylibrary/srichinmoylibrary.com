4797 He Is Both Useless And Godless

He is prayerless.
That means
He is faithless.

He is faithless.
That means
He is loveless.

He is loveless.
That means
He is both useless and Godless.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 48, Agni Press, 1982
