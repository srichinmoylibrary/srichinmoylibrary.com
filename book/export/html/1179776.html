6786 How Can I Be So Stupid?

My thinking-power
Cannot understand you.
My understanding-power
Cannot love you.
My loving-power
Cannot transform you.
Therefore, how can I be so stupid
As to tell you
That I shall be responsible
For your perfection?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 68, Agni Press, 1983
