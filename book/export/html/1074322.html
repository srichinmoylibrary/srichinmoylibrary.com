60. Aparadha

We offend others; others offend us. But the worst possible offense is when we treasure our little “I”. It binds us inside the body-cage, strangles us inside the vital-den and kills us inside the mind-prison.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The core of India's Light, part 1, Agni Press, 1992
