27 June 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart be made of
God-gratitude-smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 June 2000, 5:45 p.m. Before doing 510 lbs. dumbbell bench press.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
