206. Only to God-lovers

Father,
I am yours.
That is all I know.

"Daughter,
You are God's.
That is all I know."

Father,
You are mine.
That is all I need.

"Daughter,
You are God's.
That is all you need."

Father,
You stay with your God,
Let me stay in your heart.

"Daughter,
I am sorry, but my heart-door is open
Only to God-lovers."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
