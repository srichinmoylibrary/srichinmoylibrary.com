66. The Dancer

Yesterday
I danced with Life.
Life does not know how to dance well.
Today
I am dancing with Death.
Death is a hopeless dancer.
Tomorrow
I shall dance with God.
We both will be able
To teach each other.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sound becomes, silence is, Agni Press, 1975
