164. Sow while reaping

The mind neglects to sow
Once more
While reaping.

The heart forgets to sow
Once more
While reaping.

The soul neither neglects
Nor
Forgets to sow
Once more
While reaping.
On the contrary,
The soul is inspired
To sow more and ever-more
While reaping.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
