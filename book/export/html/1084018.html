43. Where is the promised land

Where is the promised land?
Is it inside my greatness
or
Inside my I-lessness?

It is neither inside my greatness
nor
Inside my I-lessness.

The promised land
Is in man's constant acceptance-light
Of God's Perfection-Delight
On earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
