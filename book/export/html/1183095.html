10 Don't waste your time

Don't waste your time
In comparing your earth-capacities
With God's Heaven-capacities.
God does not need an equal.
He desperately needs you,
Your heart-beauty's lustre-smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, This is God's Home, Agni Press, 1975
