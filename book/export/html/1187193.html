1542

I desired to become great
On the human level.
My desires killed me.
I aspire to become good
On the divine level.
My aspirations are illumining me
And fulfilling me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 16, Agni Press, 1983
