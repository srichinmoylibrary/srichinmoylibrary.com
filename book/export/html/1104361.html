12 August 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Alas, my life's time-river
Is flowing by,
And I have accomplished nothing special
To please God
In His full manifestation on earth.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 August 2000, 9:21 a.m. Before practising for 500-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
