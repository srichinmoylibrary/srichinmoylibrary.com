693. O echoes of my heart

O echoes of my heart,
I love you.

O echoes of my heart,
I need you.

O echoes of my heart,
You make me feel
That I can challenge Eternity's Height,
Infinity's Light
And Immortality's Life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
