1 January 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Like my soul,
May my heart, my mind, my vital
And my body
Be inexorably drawn towards God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 January 2000, 8:31 a.m. Curitiba, Brazil. Before doing fifty-seven one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (28 right, 29 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
