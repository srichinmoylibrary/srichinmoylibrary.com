404. I sleep with God

From the golden morn
To the fiery noon
I pray to God.

From the fiery noon
To the retiring eve
I meditate on God.

From the retiring eve
To the starry night
I contemplate on God.

From the starry night
To the Golden Dawn
I sleep with God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
