474. My teacher

My soul of light was determined.
Therefore,
My heart of love dared.

My heart of love dared.
Therefore,
My life of duty did.

But I must tell the world
How I did all this.
I did it
Because
I had my teacher: God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 10, Agni Press, 1974
