48.

My outer life succeeds
And my inner life proceeds
Precisely because
My Beloved Supreme
Has kept my aspiration-heart
In between
His own Compassion-Heart
And His own Encouragement-Eye.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Fast, faster, fastest progress, Agni Press, 1994
