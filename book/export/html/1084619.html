32. Light and faith

Darkness has vanished
But
Where is light?
O where is light?
Light is in self-giving.
Doubt has vanished
But
Where is faith?
O where is faith?
Faith is in love-becoming.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
