216. Close the door

Close your outer door;
You will silence the storm.

Open your inner door;
You will shelter the storm-tossed sea.

Close your ignorance-door;
God will be happy.

Close your reality-door;
Nobody will be happy -
No, not even God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
