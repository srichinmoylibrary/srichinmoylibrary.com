10 September 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I do not want You to be on my side.
I want my very existence on earth
To be always on Your side.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 September 2000, 6:43 a.m. Before doing 650-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
