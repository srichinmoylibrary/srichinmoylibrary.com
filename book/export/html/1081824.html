265. God is pleased

Imagination-sky I had;
Therefore
God is pleased.

Inspiration-moon I have;
Therefore
God is pleased.

Aspiration-sun I am;
Therefore
God is pleased.

Ah, not in one,
But in three
Different and significant ways,
I have pleased God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
