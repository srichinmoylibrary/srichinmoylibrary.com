613. The beauties of New York City

The beauties
Of New York City:
It does not sleep
At night.
It has forgotten how to walk;
It just runs and runs.
It uses little of its necessity
To endear,
And much of its capacity
To steer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
