34. Mix with God

Don't mix with him.
He will end in frustration.
Don't mix with her.
She will end in suspicion.
Don't mix with me.
I shall end in destruction.
Mix with God.
He will begin with Compassion;
He will end with Satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Immortality's dance, Agni Press, 1974
