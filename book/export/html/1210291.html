24411

Once, twice, even a thousand times
God has appreciated our aspiration-heart
And dedication-life,
But we feel that it is not enough.
We expect and we demand today
More Satisfaction-Smiles from God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 245, Agni Press, 1998
