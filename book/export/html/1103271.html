16 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
The self-made man proudly thinks
That he can do and he can become.
The God-made man devotedly
And unmistakably sees
That God has already done
Everything for him
And
God Himself has chosen him
To be God's choicest instrument.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Surabaya, Indonesia. 16 January 1999, 8:43 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. two-hundred and three times with one arm. (97 right, 106 left in five sets)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
