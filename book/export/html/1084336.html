4. Slow trains

The world's slow train:
My love-cry.
Alas, what can I do?
The world's slower train:
My sacrifice-smile.
Alas, what have I done?
The world's slowest train:
My oneness-embrace.
Alas, what shall I do?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 8, Agni Press, 1974
