6.

"Brother C,
Do you have any special message
For me today?"
Yes, I have.
Unlike yours, it is a big one:
Eternity's gratitude-seed,
gratitude-plant,
gratitude-tree
Are glowing within me unreservedly,
Are growing within me unerringly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Brother Jesus, Agni Press, 1975
