520 My Needs...

MY NEEDS

I need a sleepless body
To see God.

I need a tireless vital
To play with God.

I need a fearless mind
To travel with God.

I need a selfless heart
To converse with God.

I need a deathless life
To dance with God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 11, Agni Press, 1973
