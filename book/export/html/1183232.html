96. Pyramids

A pyramid of beauty
My heart enjoys.

A pyramid of height
My mind enjoys.

A pyramid of fire
My vital enjoys.

A pyramid of slumber
My body enjoys.

A pyramid of light
My soul enjoys.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
