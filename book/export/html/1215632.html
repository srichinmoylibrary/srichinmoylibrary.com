12. Three questions answered

The first question:
Where from?

The second question:
Where to?

The third and last question:
How?

The first answer:
From God-sky.

The second answer:
To God-sea.

The third and last answer:
Through self-transcendence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 1, Agni Press, 1974
