527. I died for light

I died for beauty,
But beauty came not.

I died for joy,
But joy came not.

I died for peace,
But peace came not.

I died for Light,
Light came,
Love followed,
Oneness blossomed.
Divinity's Reality
And Reality's Divinity
Are in everlasting embrace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 11, Agni Press, 1974
