I prayed

I prayed without feeling.
     My Father slept.
I prayed without crying.
     My Father left.

I prayed without loving.
     My Father died.
I prayed without ceasing.
     My Father cried.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God’s Glance and God’s Grace, Agni Press, 2006
