530. Without my love

I feel God in my glowing dreams.
God sees me in my crying realities.

Without God's Love
I have nothing to live for.

Without my love
God does not want to continue
His cosmic Duty's cosmic Game.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
