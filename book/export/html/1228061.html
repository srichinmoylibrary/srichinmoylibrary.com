Mago Yogamaya Mago Yogamaya

Mago Yogamaya Mago Yogamaya
Mago Yogamaya Mago
Shatek ashish kusum aniya
Amar hiyai jago

Translation:

My Mother Yogamaya, Mother Yogamaya,
Mother Yogamaya,
Do bring down from Heaven
One hundred blessing-flowers
To awaken my heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Indian sunrise, , 1998
