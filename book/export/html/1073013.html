31. Light

Where is light?
In the mind?
   Yes, in the mind
   Light there is,
   But difficult to discover.

Where is light?
In the heart?
   Yes, just enter
   And claim your old friend:
   Your own forgotten reality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
