14 My Lord Beloved Supreme, ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
I am completely lost
Between Your Justice-Light
And Your Indifference-Might.
Do forgive me.
Do give me the capacity to please You
In Your own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I am my life's God-hunger-heart, part 1, Agni Press, 1994
