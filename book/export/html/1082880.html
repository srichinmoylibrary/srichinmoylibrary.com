We are happy

You are happy
Because
You share with God
All the happenings,
Little and big,
In your life.

He is happy
Because
He loves God
Untiringly and unconditionally.

I am happy
Because
I have a real need for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God-Life: is it a far cry?, Agni Press, 1974
