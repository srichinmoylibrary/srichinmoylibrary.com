April 13

I am the most loyal servant of the heart, mind and vital. My wage is sincerity. My master, heart, is always exact in payment. My master, mind, is at times exact. But I exact sincerity from my master, vital, with much severity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flame-Goal, Agni Press, 1973
