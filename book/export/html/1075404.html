826. I know

I know definitely
That a strong body
Is the Body of God.

I know unmistakably
That a daring vital
Is the Vital of God.

I know clearly
That a doubtless mind
Is the Mind of God.

I know devotedly
That a kind heart
Is the Heart of God.

I know soulfully
That a pure life
Is the Life of God.
I know, I know.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 17, Agni Press, 1973
