<em>Interviewer:</em> Would it be a good idea to meditate at least for a few minutes before you sit down to eat?

Sri Chinmoy: I do not advise my students to meditate seriously immediately before a meal. But they often meditate for a minute or so before they eat.

From: Sri Chinmoy, What I need from God, Agni Press, 1982
