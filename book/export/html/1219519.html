Nitya Amai Bahan Kari

Nitya amai bahan kari
Amar shire
Shanti pabo keman kare
Pran gabhire

Translation:

Every day I carry
My existence-life
      On my head.
How can I have satisfaction-peace
Inside my heart?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bela chale jai, , 1979
