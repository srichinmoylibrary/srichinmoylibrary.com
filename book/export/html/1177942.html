4970 May My Life Have The Capacity

May my doubting mind
Have the capacity to unlearn.

May my longing heart
Have the capacity to learn.

May my serving life
Have the capacity to return darkness-night
To its possessor:
Ignorance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 50, Agni Press, 1982
