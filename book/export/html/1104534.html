33.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

Every day I play
On the blue violin of my heart
To invoke the blessingful presence
Of my Goddess-Mother Saraswati.

My Supreme,
My Supreme!

Songs:
1. Jiban debata daki animesh
2. Gurudev tumi amar lagiya
The Eternal Beginner (Blue violin): Aruna swapan

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 18, Agni Press, 2005
