125. God gives me

God-faith
I needed;
Therefore
God gave me
In boundless measure.

God-love
I need;
Therefore
God gives me
In infinite measure.

God-Perfection
I shall need;
Therefore
God shall give me
In ever-increasing measure.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
