233. What do you want

What do you want?
I want a night without darkness.

What do you want?
I want a day with the sun.

Why? Why?
Because I want to hear
The song of independence,
I want to see
The dance of independence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
