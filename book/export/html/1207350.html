My Lord Supreme,
Is there anything I can do
To give You a few seconds of rest a day?
"Definitely, My child.
You can help Me if you try every day
To love your human brothers and sisters
Infinitely more than you do now."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 215, Agni Press, 1994
