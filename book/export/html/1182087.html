9074 A Fanatic

I am more than ready
To be called a fanatic
So long as I can remain
In the Supreme's Boat.
For it is only the Supreme's Boat
That can safely carry me
To my Satisfaction-Goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 91, Agni Press, 1983
