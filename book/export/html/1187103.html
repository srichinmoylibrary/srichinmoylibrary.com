1453

Although it is the most difficult
Choice to make —
God or me —
I have successfully solved
This most serious problem.
When I need something,
I shall say, "God, only God."
When I do not need anything,
I shall say, "Me! Who else can there be?"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 15, Agni Press, 1983
