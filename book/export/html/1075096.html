524 How Can I Attain God?...

HOW CAN I ATTAIN GOD?

Not by rituals
Can I attain God.

Not by austerity
Can I attain God.

Not by self-denial
Can I attain God.

I can attain God
With a love-heart,
With soul-bliss.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 11, Agni Press, 1973
