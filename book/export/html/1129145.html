15731

If you do not accept
The soul’s guidance,
It is like being all alone
In the street at night
In a heavy downpour.
Desperately you are trying
  To go home,
But you have lost your way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 16, Agni Press, 1999
