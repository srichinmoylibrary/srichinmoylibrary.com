41. Enough

My body declares:
A little is enough.

My vital declares:
What is enough?

My mind declares:
Where is enough?

My heart declares:
Here is enough.

My soul declares:
Nothing is enough.

My God declares:
My child, for Me
You are more than enough.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 11, Agni Press, 1974
