"The difference between You and me, my Lord, is this: I come to You when I need You; You come to me even when You don't need me."

"My child, it seems that I shall have to correct your philosophy all My Life! You come to Me outwardly when I inwardly need you. I come to you even when you unconsciously need Me."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Secrets revealed, Agni Press, 1973
