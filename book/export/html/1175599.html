2650 Two Supreme Realities

Do not descend from Heaven
After me.
My heart will feel miserable.

Do not descend from Heaven
Before me.
God will not appreciate it.

God has two supreme Realities:
Togetherness-life
And
Oneness-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 27, Agni Press, 1982
