21 June 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The bright sweetness of the heart
Has to be increased
In infinite measure by
The immortal forgiveness of the soul.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 June 1999, 9:25 a.m.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 6, Agni Press, 1999
