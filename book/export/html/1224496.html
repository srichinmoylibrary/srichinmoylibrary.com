Jibane Amar Jatoi Pulak Peyechi Dibas

Jibane amar jatoi pulak
Peyechi dibas rat
Se kripar lagi smaribo tomare
Pujibo niyata nath
Jakhan tomar srishti nirakhi
Harashe paran uthe je pulaki
Ami chai kritagyatar
Madhu ashru par

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 6, , 1978
