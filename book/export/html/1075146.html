573 Tomorrow Does Not Come...

TOMORROW DOES NOT COME

Tomorrow does not come.
Why?
Because
Yesterday was enough
And
Today is more than enough.

Tomorrow will never come.
Why?
Because
God has not given birth
To tomorrow
And
He does not want
To change His Plan.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 12, Agni Press, 1973
