My Soviet sisters

My Soviet sisters,
Come to the fore!
Hide not, hide not;
Run to the shore!

Lag not behind,
Lag not behind!
Treasures in us
The world must find.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Gorbachev: the master-key of the Universal Heart, Agni Press, 1990
