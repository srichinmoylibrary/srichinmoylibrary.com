181. My happiness explained

Do you know why
I am so happy?
I am happy
Because
I ignore the condemnation
Of my previous friend, doubt.

Do you know why
I am so happy?
I am happy
Because
I value the approval
Of my present friend, aspiration.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
