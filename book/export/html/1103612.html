27 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
I have seen the head and face
Of greatness.
I am not at all satisfied.
My Supreme, my Supreme,
I wish to feel the heart and breath
Of goodness
To be fully satisfied.
My Supreme, please fulfil
My long-cherished desire.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

27 July 1999, 1:31 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and two times with one arm. (50 right, 52 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
