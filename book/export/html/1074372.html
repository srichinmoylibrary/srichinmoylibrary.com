49. Brahma

Brahma created the world. If I can change my own thought-world, then the Creator of the world will give me what I do not have: satisfaction in self-giving and satisfaction in God-receiving.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Core Of India's Light, Part 2, Agni Press, 1992
