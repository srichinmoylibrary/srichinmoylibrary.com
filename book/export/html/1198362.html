Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 127

12601

My Lord Supreme,
Your Compassion-Eye
I adore,
Your Forgiveness-Heart
I implore.

12602

The moment I look within,
I see my climbing heart.
The moment I look without,
I see my descending mind.

12603

The supreme duty of the chosen ones
Is to please God in God's own Way
Without waiting for God's outer Command.

12604

My mind declared
Its perfect perfection.
Alas, my soul came and discovered
That my mind is full
Of imperfection-blunders.

12605

Each prayer goes up
To God the Power.
Each meditation brings down
God the Peace.

12606

The mind thinks
That it can never have a golden future.
The heart feels
That there is only one important thing:
Eternity's Now-satisfaction.

12607

You want to know how
You can increase your heart's joy?
I tell you, just become
The breath of ceaseless surrender!

12608

My searching mind
Is so happy
That my doubting mind
Has gone out on a long vacation.

12609

Every morning run
An extra inspiration-mile
To see God's extra
Satisfaction-Smile.

12610

Even God's Compassion-Boat
Does not carry
Hopeless lethargy-passengers.

12611

A gratitude-heart
Is every day fed
By God's own Satisfaction-Breath.

12612

If you want to leave the world
Better than you found it,
Then use your heart of compassion
And your life of concern.

12613

Even if you do not want
To say goodbye to your desire-friends,
One day, to your great surprise,
They will say goodbye to you,
For God's inner Pressure
Will be too much for them to endure.

12614

God has not created my mind
To live in the abysmal abyss.
He has created my mind
To live in the abode of peace.

12615

An aspiration-heart
Means an exceptional
God-realisation-opportunity.

12616

Every day countless Compassion-Books
Written by God Himself
Are given away by God
To truth-seekers and God-lovers.

12617

When man becomes
A God-serving heart,
God becomes
A man-guiding Eye.

12618

God wants my mind to specialise
In self-investigation
And not in earth-imperfection
Criticism.

12619

When I fail
In my life's examination,
I see my poor vital
Carrying my depression-banner.

12620

God never allows
A gratitude-heart
To be empty
Of His sweetest Ecstasy.

12621

I clearly see that my mind
Will soon start enjoying
God-Delight,
For all my doubts are going out
On a long vacation.

12622

Unless you have
A oneness-heart-passport,
How can you have a visa
For God's inner Perfection-Kingdom?

12623

My soul has decided
To stay at my heart's door
All the time
So that my mind cannot come
And steal my heart's poise.

12624

An extra enthusiasm-mile a day
Will chase away
The mind's frustration-cherishing night.

12625

The heart never files any false complaint
Against the mind.
Therefore, God grants the heart
His Satisfaction-Smile.

12626

My heart's soundless sound
Teaches my life
The supreme art of self-giving.

12627

A selfless action
Is far beyond the domain
Of expectation-grasp.

12628

If you continuously breathe in
The breath of your unworthiness,
Then your spiritual death
Is imminent.

12629

It is simply impossible
For a doubting mind
To live inside
The powerfully mounting aspiration-heart.

12630

If you want to realise God
And if you want to manifest God
In God's own Way,
Then your life has to walk always
On a one-way sunlit aspiration-road.

12631

I have warned my vital
Very seriously,
And it has promised me
That it will never visit
Despair-domain.

12632

Alas, you are totally lost
Between your selfishness-mind
And your selflessness-heart.

12633

The luminous and penetrating eyes
Of faith
Can easily save a seeker
From doubt-destruction's jaws.

12634

He who does not claim
God's Satisfaction
As his ultimate goal
Will undoubtedly be a spiritual failure.

12635

Look carefully once more!
I am sure the peace-guest
Is waiting for you
In the depths of your heart.

12636

You first have to open your accounts
At the aspiration and dedication-banks
If you ever hope to open your account
At the realisation-bank.

12637

The older the joy
Of the aspiration-heart,
The younger the seeker-eyes look.

12638

Alas, my cheerful surrender
To God's Will
Had an unbelievably short life!

12639

If you are your mind's resentment
Then God will never be able to increase
Your heart's oneness-receptivity.

12640

Can you not see
That your mind's expectation-dreams
Are torturing your aspiration-life ruthlessly?

12641

True aspiration
Is bound to fly
On the fastest inspiration-wings.

12642

Be always ready
With your heart's troops
To fight against
The armies of doubt.

12643

My heart feels
That it is not necessary
To keep its eye open
While walking along the path of faith.

12644

Do not allow your mind
Even for a fleeting second
To be blinded by dark doubt-night.

12645

God's unconditional Compassion-Mountain
Always remains infinitely higher
Than man's comprehension-mind-hill.

12646

Every day I must soulfully pray
To God
To show me the sunset
Of my mind's disobedience.

12647

Do you know why doubt
Appears ceaselessly?
It appears because it wants
To threaten, weaken
and finally dethrone
Your mind-king.

12648

Whenever and wherever
My aspiration-heart travels,
My Lord's Compassion-Eye
Lights the way.

12649

Every morning shake hands first
With your happiness-satisfaction-friend
Long before you enter into
Your daily life-activities.

12650

If you are
A sincere seeker and God-lover,
Then every day you must devotedly
Sweep away your mind's desire-dust.

12651

Unlike all our human friends,
God's Compassion-Eye
Is not a fair-weather friend.

12652

Our faith in God
Prescribes solutions
To each and every problem
That our mind creates.

12653

Aspiration-acceptance
And desire-rejection:
These two games must be played
Bravely and simultaneously.

12654

If you listen to your soul's
Meditation-message,
You can easily have a freer passage
To your life's ultimate Destination.

12655

If you cherish your mind's
Depression-night,
How can God bless you
With His Heart's Protection-Light?

12656

God always keeps His Heart-Lines open
For those who are destined to manifest
His Divinity's Light on earth.

12657

Insecurity,
You are the main barrier
I have to conquer
In order to make the fastest progress
In my conscious aspiration-life.

12658

Your hope-dreams have tremendous strength.
They will definitely be able to climb
Your perfection-realisation-heights.

12659

Progress always runs
Toward a more illumining
And satisfying destination.

12660

God's Compassion-Height
Always precedes
God's Justice-Light.

12661

God-realisation means God-discovery,
And God-discovery is nothing other than
The reclaiming of one's own
Ancient God-roots.

12662

If you are an obedient soldier today,
Then tomorrow God will make you
His Cosmic Game's perfection-captain.

12663

Do not cherish your mind's
Sound-drum,
But cherish your heart's
Gratitude-silence-songs.

12664

Once you pass your heart's
Faith-examination,
God takes you to watch
His Splendour-Exhibition.

12665

I see no difference
Between a negative mind
And a poisonous life.

12666

Have faith in God's Compassion-Power.
This Power can liberate you
From your centuries-old insecurity-heart.

12667

God's Confidence-Light
Can live in me
Only when I exercise
My determination-might.

12668

If you can maintain your mind's
Monumental indifference
To earthly demands and commands,
You are bound to succeed in your inner life
Faster, infinitely faster,
Than you can ever imagine.

12669

God is always ready to remove
Bitter frustration from my mind,
But my mind cleverly hides
From God's Compassion-Protection-Eye.

12670

If your heart suffers
From purity-failure,
Then it is also bound to fail
In achieving God-oneness-satisfaction.

12671

Run at full speed
To come out of
Your self-chosen ignorance-night.
Your Lord Supreme
Is eagerly awaiting your arrival.

12672

May our human mind enjoy
The beauty of inspiration-light
And not the battle of opinion-night.

12673

If you are your searching mind,
And if you are your aspiring heart,
Then your realising life
Cannot remain a far cry.

12674

God lovingly invites
Man's happiness-heart
To come and blow
His Satisfaction-Whistle.

12675

My unconditional surrender-life
Has immensely augmented
My heart's God-visibility.

12676

My soul's illumination-homeland
Is the ultimate goal
Of my aspiration-heart.

12677

I fervently hope
Someday and somehow
To see my heart's rising rainbow-sun.

12678

My soul's lustre-radiance
Has always lived in
My Lord's Nectar-Satisfaction-Heart.

12679

O my mind's desire-tears
Although you do not fulfil God
Alas, to me you are
Very beautiful to look at.

12680

Only a confidence-heart
Can enlarge the mind's
Kindness and concern-circles.

12681

O my mind,
Do not hide from my heart
When giant doubt
Captures your life.

12682

A prosperous faith-smile
Can easily cure
Your poisonous doubt-mind.

12683

Ultimately, a peaceful mind
And a happy heart
Will be two inseparable friends.

12684

My self-giving heart
Immediately widens
My mind-horizons.

12685

Two most loving and devoted friends:
Aspiration and meditation.
Aspiration helps meditation
Dive deeper than the deepest.
Meditation helps aspiration
Rise higher than the highest.

12686

A God-warrior receives
His victory-garland
Only after he has transformed
His attachment-mind
Into a detachment-life.

12687

The moment an uncomely desire
Nears you,
Just arrest it
With your soul's light
And then keep it under your mind's
Will-lock and key.

12688

Beautiful is your heart-home.
Now make it also fruitful
By illumining it
With your soul's divinity-light.

12689

Peace is the only one
That I can trust
To be my lifelong friend.

12690

Now that your mind is ready
To love God the creation,
God the Creator will proudly help you
Reach His Delight-Heights.

12691

May there be no
Misunderstanding-bomb-explosion
Between my truth-searching mind
And my God-loving heart.

12692

When I pray,
I pray to touch
The Feet of God.
When I meditate,
I meditate to become
The Heart of God.

12693

No, not even God the Compassion-Heart
Can liberate you
If you allow your mind
To enjoy thickening doubt-clouds.

12694

All the parades of the world
God has carefully watched.
Now He wants to watch proudly
Only one parade:
The parade of the peace-flooded
Oneness-world-family.

12695

My Lord Supreme,
I have a very special Christmas gift
For You.
My inspiration-mind,
My aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
I am cheerfully and soulfully placing
At Your Protection-Perfection-Feet.

12696

If I soulfully long
For an illumination-mind,
Then only will God grant me
His Protection-Umbrella
And His Satisfaction-Sun.

12697

Every day my heart's aspiration
Helps me embark upon
My
God-discovery-satisfaction-expedition.

12698

Your life has become
A daily ordeal
Because you please
Your pride-inundated mind
And not
Your humility-blossomed oneness-heart.

12699

What you desperately need
Is first
Your long-lost aspiration-rediscovery
And then
Your life's perfection-mastery.

12700

Into the world I came
To sit at God's Compassion-Feet
And serve Him sleeplessly.
This message from above
Is crystal clear to me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 127, Agni Press, 1989
