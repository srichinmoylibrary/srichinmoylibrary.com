Each Time I Pray, I See Inside My Heart

Each time I pray,
I see inside my heart
      A peace-tree growing.
Each time I pray,
I see inside my heart
      A peace-flower blossoming.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Greet the morning with your heart's aspiration-cry, , 1995
