3320 You Must Learn

O my body, you must learn
How to behave.
O my vital, you must learn
How not to crave.
O my mind, you must learn
How to brave.
O my heart, you must learn
How to pave.
O my soul, you must learn
How to save.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 34, Agni Press, 1982
