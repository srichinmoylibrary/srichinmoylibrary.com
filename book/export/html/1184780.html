452. Fastest he runs

His inner life
Is the loveliness of perfection-deeds.
His outer life
Is the flow of beauty's light
And duty's glow.
He never drags the broken mind;
Therefore
Fast, faster, fastest he runs
Towards his destination-smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
