517. The dream of bliss

The dream of Bliss
God and I
Together enjoy
In the heart of God's Silence-height.

The Bliss of dream
My heart and I
Together enjoy
In the life of earth's sound-flight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
