16 September 2000 (III

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God's Compassion-Eye
Thrills my heart.
God's Forgiveness-Feet
Thrill my heart abundantly more.
The Nectar-dust of God's Feet
Thrills my heart infinitely more.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 September 2000, 12:27 a.m. Before doing 900-lb. dumbbell bench press. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 16, Agni Press, 2000
