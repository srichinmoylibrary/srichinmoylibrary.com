285. Three messages

Faith has three messages to offer.
First message:
God is necessary.
Second message:
God-realisation is indispensable.
Third message:
God-Perfection is attainable
At God's choice Hour,
It is unmistakably attainable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
