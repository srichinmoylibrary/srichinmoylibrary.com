Prati Rate Chander Deshe

Prati rate chander deshe
Jete chahi hese hese
Sethai pabo bandhan hara
Shanti dhara

Translation:

Every evening, smiling,
I like to go to the moon lands.
There I shall receive peace boundless.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Chander deshe, , 1991
