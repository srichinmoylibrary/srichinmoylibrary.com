Kahar Sathe Kandbo Ami Kahar Sathe

Kahar sathe kandbo ami kahar sathe kandbo
Prabhu tomar sathe tomar sathe
Kahar sathe hasbo ami kahar sathe hasbo
Prabhu tomar sathe tomar sathe
Kahar sathe nachbo ami kahar sathe nachbo
Prabhu tomar sathe tomar sathe

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 3, , 1977
