9530 My Heart's Rekindled Faith

O my doubting mind,
I have a very special message
For you today:
My heart's rekindled faith
Is guaranteeing your immediate collapse
And permanent death.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 96, Agni Press, 1983
