44. Real and unreal

My desire-life
Made my earth-life
Unreal.

My aspiration-life
Is making my Heaven-life
Divinely and supremely
Real.

My realisation-life
Will make my death-life
Unreal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 3, Agni Press, 1974
