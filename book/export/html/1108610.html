567 You Must First Know...

You must first know
More precisely about yourself
Than about anybody else,
And then work
On your perfect perfection.
Lo, peace is standing
At your life's door,
Smiling and smiling.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Peace-Blossom-Fragrance, part 6, Agni Press, 1994
