115 My Lord, From Now On ...

My Lord, from now on
And for the rest of my life,
I shall need only
The Compassion-Beauty
Of Your Eye
And the Satisfaction-Fragrance
Of Your Heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 2, Agni Press, 2000
