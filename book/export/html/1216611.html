972. Those who are

Those who are before you
Will inspire you.

Those who are behind you
Will acclaim you.

Those who are around you
Will doubt you.

Those who are for you
Will devour you.

Those who are of you
Will revive you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
