1817

He has proved
Something unprecedented:
The outer man
Of unparalleled name and fame
Can soulfully bow
To the inner man,
Who is eternally the ambassador
Of the Absolute Supreme on earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 19, Agni Press, 1983
