952.

O insecurity,
O jealousy,
O impurity,
I am determined
To conquer you all
Before I make
My final God-satisfaction-journey.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Two God-Amusement-Rivals: My Heart-Song-Beauty and My Life-Dance-Fragrance, part 10, Agni Press, 1996
