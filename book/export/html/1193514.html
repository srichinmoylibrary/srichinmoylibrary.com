Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 79

7801

One high dream
I have all along dreamed:
God-Satisfaction in me.

7802

Because I still live
A desire-life,
All my offerings have been returned
By Heaven.

7803

His surrender-life
Is striding fast,
Very fast,
On the path of progress-delight.

7804

How can you,
Why should you
Blame the world
When you yourself
Are fully responsible
For your self-made misery?

7805

With his stupidity-life
He has turned to Eternity
To fulfil his desire-life.

7806

A seeker's heart
Sails towards the sea
Of tomorrow's delight.

7807

It takes a heroic heart
To say no
To the desire-world.

7808

What he enjoys most
Are his God-dreams,
Even when he is wide awake.

7809

Do not try to lead
Man's mind to Heaven.
You will fail!
Beg Heaven to descend
Into the human.
You will succeed!

7810

The desire-life
Is always afraid
Of missing or losing something.

7811

My soul has arrested
My doubting mind
For obstructing my Lord's
Compassion-Vision-Eye.

7812

What consoles his stupid mind?
Certainly not illumination,
But hallucination!

7813

Each high dream
Of man
Is God's own
Self-transcendence.

7814

His is a cry
Of his heart's
Lost world.

7815

With an eager heart
I am looking
For the full blossoming
Of my aspiration-life.

7816

The haven of self-giving-light
Is at once beautiful
And powerful.

7817

Silence calls me.
Therefore, I am on my way
To the perfection-life.

7818

Alas,
He is blinded
By earth's
Ignorance-cherished knowledge.

7819

His days are numbered.
Therefore
He longs to see
God's Forgiveness-Light
As an undeniable reality.

7820

You can cherish
The inner "you."
Who says it is
A most difficult task?

7821

His mind dies
Today
So that his heart
Can start living
From tomorrow.

7822

At last
His mind has started enjoying
The departure
Of illusion and delusion.

7823

Cry within and smile without:
This is the only message
That God gives to human beings.

7824

A heart of silence-delight
Is a fulfilled Dream
Of God.

7825

Is God a dream?
So does the human mind think.
Is God a reality?
So does the divine heart feel.

7826

Why do you want
To give the dying past
An everlasting life?

7827

In spite of his teeming weaknesses
He feels that God alone
Is his All.

7828

In Heaven
What God calls His Dream,
On earth
He calls it Truth.

7829

Your unconditional love
Of God
Is locked inside my heart.

7830

His longing for perfection
Is itself a sign
From the heart of his life.

7831

Every smile
Of our birthless and deathless journey
Is greeted by God's Compassion-Eye
And His Satisfaction-Heart.

7832

Ambition does not know
That it shows us the way
To an unsatisfied greatness.

7833

God's God-Smile
Is a secret hope
That a God-seeker invokes
To vanquish his shameless foes.

7834

A purity-heart enjoys
The express boat ride
To Divinity's Shore.

7835

You must know why
An impure thought
Is locked in your mind.

7836

Each sacred hope
Is a blessingful gift
From Heaven's heart.

7837

Your suspicion-mind
Meets with a sigh
From the heart of creation.

7838

A self-giving life
Is a special blessing-message
That lives forever on earth.

7839

Each aspiring heart
Has the perfection-capacity
Of infinite reach.

7840

My mind likes
Long journeys.
My heart loves
Uncharted shores.

7841

Your uncertain mind
Is fast asleep without.
Your certain heart
Is fully awake within.

7842

Unreality's message
Is what my mind learns.
Reality's message
Is what my heart learns.

7843

His God and his soul
Are coming to see
His heart's universal sunrise.

7844

All is changed
For the best.
I do not need myself
Anymore.
I need only God.

7845

The unreal mind
Does not know
What the real heart is.

7846

His divinity-eye
Is the evidence
Of one stupendous Truth.

7847

The mind's
Thought-house
Has no protection-roof.

7848

One solemn promise:
Every day I shall meditate
With my aspiration-heart.

7849

True,
You have mortal lips,
But that does not prevent you
From immortal utterances.

7850

You fool,
How long will you remain
In your mind's tiny,
Private cave?

7851

I am forcing my mind
To develop a special capacity
To appreciate my heart's
Silence-life.

7852

In my desire-life
What I actually need
Is an endless "No!"

7853

The coast of the mind,
Who wants to reach?
Who is that fool,
Who?

7854

Every day
Either I vehemently refuse pain
Or I long for a refuge
From pain.

7855

Every day he enjoys
The enlightening visits
Of unexpected souls
From the invisible worlds.

7856

Your tempted mind
Is now playing
On your heart's broken strings.

7857

Thought
Has its weak body.
Will
Has its strong soul.

7858

He is well established
On the path
Of self-effacement.

7859

To economise every moment
Is to consciously make friends
With Eternity's Infinity.

7860

Thorns and flowers
Grow together
So that flowers can be
Infinitely more appreciated.

7861

Only one
Great destruction-foe:
A suspicion-mind.

7862

The human life itself
Is a strong desire
To be led by the clever mind.

7863

If you are a chosen child
Of God,
Then you know how to laugh
At reason's dominion.

7864

Your fondness for loneliness
Is never appreciated
By God's Satisfaction-Heart.

7865

A hopeful life
Knows how to return,
Where to return
And
When to return.

7866

In spite of your stupidity-life,
I admire your love
For Divinity's Blessings.

7867

Each self-giving heart
Is a fulfilling Dream
Of God.

7868

How can you be wise
If you have not already
Prayed to God to claim you?

7869

A seer-poet
Daily enjoys the company
Of God's Vision.

7870

He is building
An Immortality-Tower
In the heart of human life.

7871

Inner courage is indispensable
To accept the challenge
Of grey days
In each human life.

7872

Two age-old questions:
Does God love me?
Do I need God?

7873

Oneness-delight
Builds its nest
Only for God-dreamers.

7874

Claim not
Recognition,
But long for
Perfection.

7875

What do you want to learn
From me?
I can only teach you
How to love God
Unconditionally.

7876

No more
Am I going to remain
With my mind's crowding thoughts.
I shall remain
Inside my heart's flaming will.

7877

He lives
In his searching mind
To be happy.

He lives
In his crying heart
To be perfect.

7878

You have all along seen
A struggling mind.
From now on you will see
A daring heart —
Your own.

7879

Fearless
If you remain within,
Spotless
You can remain without.

7880

May my heart-garden
Grow beauty's
Rose-hopes.

7881

Suspicion, the new God,
Has replaced
Illumination, the old God.

7882

Earth does not need my heart
To pray for its transformation.
Heaven does not need my life
To sacrifice for its satisfaction.

7883

Neither resolution
Nor revolution
But soulful evolution
Will transform the world.

7884

Each desire-man
Is a fallen dream,
Unfulfilled.

7885

His aspiration-heart,
Dedication-life
And God-satisfaction-mission
Shall remain beyond the pages
Of history.

7886

The Creator is looking for someone
Who has the willingness,
And not only the capacity,
To appreciate His creation.

7887

Make always
The right decision,
And carry it
To perfection.

7888

Lack of poise
Means lack of surrender
To the Will of the Inner Pilot.

7889

God Himself
Is smiling at you.
Then why do you cry?
For whom
Do you have to cry?

7890

God does not compel you
To follow His Aspiration-Way.
Never!
He only wants you
To become perfect
In His manifested world.

7891

Who am I?
In Heaven
I am a God-partner.
On earth
I am a God-worshipper.

7892

The world is stupid.
It does not need
Your dedication.
You are wise.
You need the world's
Aspiration.

7893

If you strongly feel
That you are going to pass
The temptation-examination,
Then you are not mistaken.

7894

Be a simple life.
Earth needs your help.
Be a pure heart.
Heaven needs your help.

7895

I simplify my mind
To be an instrument of God.
I purify my heart
To become a child of God.

7896

You want to succeed.
Therefore, you teach.
I want to proceed.
Therefore, I learn.

7897

Not what you give,
Not even what you are,
But only what you want to become
Counts in the long run.

7898

Prove to God
That you are for Him alone.
Then the visions of delight
Shall be within your easy reach.

7899

Now that he sincerely enjoys
The inner journey,
His life has become
Satisfaction-delight.

7900

His willingness-heart
Is every day blessed
With bright perfection-hints
From Heaven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 79, Agni Press, 1984
