1. Don't believe

My foolish heart,
Who told you
That I do not love you?
Don't believe insecurity.
I tell you,
Insecurity is a graceless liar.

My foolish mind,
Who told you
That I do not need you?
Don't believe doubt.
I tell you,
Doubt is a hopeless liar.

My foolish vital,
Who told you
That I hate you?
Don't believe impurity.
I tell you,
Impurity is a shameless liar.

My foolish body,
Who told you
That I force you?
Don't believe lethargy.
I tell you,
Lethargy is a useless liar.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
