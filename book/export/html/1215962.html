336. Five messages

Perfection
Is the message of my soul.

Compassion
Is the message of my heart.

Determination
Is the message of my mind.

Expansion
Is the message of my vital.

Revelation
Is the message of my body.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 7, Agni Press, 1974
