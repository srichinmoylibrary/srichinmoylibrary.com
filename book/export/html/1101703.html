48.

My Lord, my sweet Lord,
My Supreme Lord,
I do know that my life's
Unconditional surrender to You
Is fast asleep.
But I clearly see that my heart's
Most sincere gratitude to You
Is wide awake.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord, I pray to You, Agni Press, 1994
