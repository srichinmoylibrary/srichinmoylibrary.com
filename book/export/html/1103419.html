29 April 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Do give me the capacity
To bind You inwardly,
Prayerfully, soulfully
   And self-givingly
Only with my devotion
In my inner life of aspiration.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

29 April 1999, 7:25 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine — up to 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously, and 230 lbs. with each arm separately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
