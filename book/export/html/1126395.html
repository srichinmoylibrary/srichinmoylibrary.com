12984

When I work hard, very hard,
  For God-manifestation,
The hostile forces attack me
  More vehemently,
And my Lord Supreme
In secrecy supreme
Watches me carefully
To see whether I am truly
  Only for Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 13, Agni Press, 1999
