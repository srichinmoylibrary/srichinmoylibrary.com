Benignant

(gracious)

It is the benignant qualities
Of our heart
That endear others
And, at the same time,
Make us divinely beautiful
And supremely lovable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
