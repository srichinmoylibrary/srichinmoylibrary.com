81. Don't doubt!

Don't doubt!
When you doubt God
You lengthen your ignorance-journey,
You diminish the flow
Of your life-energy,
You destroy your
Fast-approaching sovereignty.
Therefore, don't doubt,
Never!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 2, Agni Press, 1974
