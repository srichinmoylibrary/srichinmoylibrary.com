3 March 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Eventually desire-life fades
And aspiration-heart
Succeeds and flourishes.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

/3 March 2000, 9:04 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously seven times (Total: 1,300 lbs.)/

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 13, Agni Press, 2000
