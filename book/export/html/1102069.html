16.

My Lord tells me
That I need both science and spirituality.

In my life of desire,
I need science
In order to relay my thoughts
To the furthest end of the globe.

In my life of aspiration,
I need spirituality
To bring me the message of the Unknown,
The ultimate Beyond.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Secrets revealed, Agni Press, 1973
