20137

My Lord Supreme,
There was a time
When I was afraid
Not only of Your eternal Justice
But also of Your infinite Compassion.
But now my surrender-heart
Needs them equally
For my life's complete transformation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 202, Agni Press, 1994
