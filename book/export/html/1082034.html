24. Guru

A Guru is he
Who asks God
For only one favour,
And that favour
Is for God to give him time,
Endless time,
Before God examines
His expansion-reality:
His spiritual children.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God-Compassion and God-Justice, Agni Press, 1975
