64.

No more my life will walk along
A God-empty road.
I am now blossoming
With a lifelong
God-Touch-Smile,
I am now blossoming
With a lifelong
God-Touch-Song,
And I am now blossoming
With a lifelong
God-Touch-Embrace.

1 October 2005
New York

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Race-Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 2006
