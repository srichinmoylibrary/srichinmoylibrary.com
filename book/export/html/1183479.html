343. He is my real Friend

God shook hands with me
Only to prove that He is my real Friend.

God blessed me on my head
Only to prove that He is my true Father.

God embraced me powerfully
Only to prove that He is my eternal Lover.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
