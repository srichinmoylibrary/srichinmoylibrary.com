45. Because he loves

Because
He loves the ascending earth,
God has given him
His Eternity's Strength.

Because
He loves the descending Heaven,
God has given him
His Infinity's Length.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, part 1, Agni Press, 1979
