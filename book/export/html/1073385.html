Question: What are the ways of God-realisation?

Sri Chinmoy: There are only two ways: prayer and meditation. In meditation there are three stages: concentration, meditation and contemplation. Aspiration houses both prayer and meditation. If you have aspiration, that is the most important thing you need. If you follow either path, you can reach your goal.
