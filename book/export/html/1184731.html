403. Lessons from God

Yesterday
God taught me
How to smile soulfully.

Today
God is teaching me
How to cry ceaselessly.

Tomorrow
God will teach me
How to surrender consciously,
Cheerfully,
Constantly
And unconditionally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
