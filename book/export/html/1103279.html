22 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I must climb beyond
All obstacle-mountains
To reach Your Life's Pride-Fountain
And
Your Heart's Satisfaction-Fountain.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Bandung, Indonesia. 22 January 1999. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
