Nayan Khule Dekhi Jakhan

Nayan khule dekhi jakhan
Dekhi sabiy bhul
Hriday khule heri jakhan
Heri jiban kul

Translation:

Opening my eyes,
When I look at the world,
I see everything wrong.
Opening my heart,
When I look at the world,
I see my life's destination-shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eso eso prabhu, part 1, , 1998
