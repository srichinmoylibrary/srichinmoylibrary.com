206. Don't be

Don't be
In the luxury of power.
Destruction-shower will greet you
Without fail.

Don't be
In the luxury of light.
Night-frown will torture you
Without fail.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
