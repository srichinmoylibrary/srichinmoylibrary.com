23730

If God forgets my phone number,
What can I say
And what can I do?
But if I lose God's phone number,
I should and will remain
Inexcusable and inconsolable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 238, Agni Press, 1997
