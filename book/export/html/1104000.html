15 December 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling
All the God-seekers and God-lovers
That, for them, there is no such thing
As a passing infatuation
With worldliness.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 December 1999, 7:52 a.m. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Before doing sixty-three one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (29 right, 34 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
