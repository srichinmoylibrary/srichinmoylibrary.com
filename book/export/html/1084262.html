32. Eavesdropper

O eavesdropper,
My mind employs you
To know what
God says about me.
O eavesdropper,
My heart employs you
To know what
Death thinks of me.
O eavesdropper,
My soul employs you
To illumine the furious man in me
And
To awaken the gracious God in me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 6, Agni Press, 1974
