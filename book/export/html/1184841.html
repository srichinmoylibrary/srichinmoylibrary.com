513. By far the greatest

Who shuns his earth-desires
Is great.

Who shuns his Heaven-possessions
Is greater.

Who accepts life,
Transforms life
And
Fulfils God
Is by far the greatest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
