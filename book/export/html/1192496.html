6793

The conservative mind
Wants to reach the mountain-height
Eventually.

The liberal heart
Wants to reach the mountain-height
Swiftly.

The radical soul
Wants to reach the mountain-height
Immediately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 68, Agni Press, 1984
