987. I await your command

   O Emperor-God,
How high have I to fly?
   I await Your supreme command.   O King-God,
How deep have I to dive?
   I await Your good command.   O Prince-God,
How far have I to walk
        On Eternity's Road?
   I await Your immediate command.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
