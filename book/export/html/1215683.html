62. Before and after

You use your wise eyes
Before and after
You buy anything.

He uses his proud nose
Before and after
He buys anything.

I use my clever ears
Before and after
I buy anything.

God uses His Oneness-Heart
Before and after
He buys anything.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 2, Agni Press, 1974
