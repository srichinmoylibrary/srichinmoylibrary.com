I ask God:
“Please tell me, how far do I have to go?”
God says to me:
“My child, this is the wrong question!
Instead you should ask Me,
‘Do give me the capacity,
My Lord, to be with You always,
Wherever You go.’”

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 43, Agni Press, 2005
