117

God loves me,
But I do not have the time
To love God.

God speaks to me,
But I do not have the time
To speak to God.

God knows who I am,
But I do not have the time
To know who I am,
Whose I am
Or why I am.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 2, Agni Press, 1983
