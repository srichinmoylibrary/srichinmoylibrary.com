My Lord,
How can I feel You more?
"Think of Me as your life-boat.
Think of Me as your life-river.
Think of Me as your life-destination.
You will not only feel Me more,
But also be fulfilled
Far beyond your expectation."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 198, Agni Press, 1994
