God and I Support Each Other

God and I support each other
      Soulfully.
I support God
With my adoration-leaf.
God supports me
With His Compassion-Tree.
God and I support each other
      Unerringly.
I support God
With my devotion-drop.
God supports me
With His Illumination-Sea.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Silence, please! God is coming, , 1999
