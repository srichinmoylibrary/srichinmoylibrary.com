Chalo Ebar Chali Mora Mayer Dake

Chalo ebar chali mora mayer dake
Praner mato bhai chere bhai kadin thake
Theke hridi shatadale ai bale oi mata dake
Sei dak shune keu ki kare dhare rakhe
