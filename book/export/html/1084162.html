34. When I look at him

When I look at him
   I see an aura of fear
        Behind his head.
When I look at him
   I see an aura of death
        Around his face.
A mere mortal
   He can never be.
An inner pain
   Gnaws at him
        Constantly.
His is a life of
   Ill-fortune
        And
   Hermit-existence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 4, Agni Press, 1974
