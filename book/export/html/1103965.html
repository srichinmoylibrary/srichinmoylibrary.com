15 November 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are telling me
That I have chatted enough
With You.
Now I must start chanting
Your Name.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 November 1999, 8:11 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with each arm simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
