39. You will be caught

Don't pretend
Without the truth;
You will be caught.

Don't expand
Without the light;
You will be caught.

Don't preach
Without the silence;
You will be caught.

Don't argue
With your inner effulgent light,
With your outer flickering candle;
You will be caught.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 5, Agni Press, 1974
