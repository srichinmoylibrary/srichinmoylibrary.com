8614 You Think Of God

You may not know
When I think of God,
But I know
When you think of God.
You think of God when God tells me
That He has two
Supremely chosen instruments.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 87, Agni Press, 1983
