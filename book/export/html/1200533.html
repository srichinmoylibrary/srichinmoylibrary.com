14750

Concentration-speed I need
To run forward.
Meditation-speed I need
To dive inward.
Contemplation-speed I need
To claim and be claimed
By the higher God,
The inner God
And the outer God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 148, Agni Press, 1991
