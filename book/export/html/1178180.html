5205 To Make Your Life Real

To make your life real
You must be more affectionate
To your heart
And more indifferent
To your mind;
You must be more strict
With your vital
And more patient
With your body.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 53, Agni Press, 1983
