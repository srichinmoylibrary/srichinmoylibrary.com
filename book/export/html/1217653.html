The Supreme

Father, I have Seen.
           "No."

Father, I have Known.
           "No."

Father, I have Felt.
           "No."

Father, I have Become.
           "No."
     Father, I AM.
           "Yes."

From: Sri Chinmoy, AUM -- Vol. 1, No. 6, January 27, 1966, Boro Park Printers -- Brooklyn, N. Y, 1966
