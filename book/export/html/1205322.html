19492

Whenever there is a struggle
Between my doubting mind
And my believing heart,
My wisdom-flooded soul
Always blessingfully and proudly
Takes the side of my heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 195, Agni Press, 1993
