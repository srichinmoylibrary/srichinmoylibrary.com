19 When God Came To Him ...

When God came to him
For the first time,
God saw his heart
Fast asleep.
Therefore, God went back
To His celestial Abode.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 15, Agni Press, 2003
