419. Your eye

My body-life
Is afraid of your fixed eye.

My vital-life
Is afraid of your fiery eye.

My mind-life
Is afraid of your tranquil eye.

But

My heart-life
Is not afraid of you,
Not even of your trance-eye.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
