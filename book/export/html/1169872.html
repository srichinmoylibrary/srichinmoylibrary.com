517. I kneel before Thee

I kneel before Thee in prayer
With devotion pure.
I lift my soul
For you to console.
I try to live chaste in thought
To enjoy my Godward eternal destiny.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
