9. I depend on God

I depend on you;
Therefore
I cry.
I depend on him;
Therefore
I sigh.
I depend on myself;
Therefore
I die.
I depend on God
Therefore
I am revived,
Transformed
And
Immortalised.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The prayer of the sky, Agni Press, 1974
