3443 The Supreme In Us Loves

The animal in us
Loves destruction.
The human in us
Loves separation.
The divine in us
Loves cooperation.
The Supreme in us
Loves satisfaction in perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 35, Agni Press, 1982
