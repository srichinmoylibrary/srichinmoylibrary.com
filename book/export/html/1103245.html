1 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May my outer service-life
Be the beauty of the New Year.
May my inner aspiration-heart
Be the fragrance of the New Year.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. 1 January 1999, 8:44 a.m. Before lifting 210 lbs. twice. (once with each arm)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
