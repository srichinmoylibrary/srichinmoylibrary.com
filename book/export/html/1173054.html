130 The Vision Of Freedom-Might

The vision of freedom-might
Man's outer life
Desperately desires.

The reality of oneness-delight
Man's inner life
Eternally is.

The following twenty poems were written at the Osaka Grand Hotel on 26 October 1979

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 2, Agni Press, 1979
