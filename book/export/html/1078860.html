14.

Yesterday I performed
Three real miracles,
And these three miracles
Lasted for the entire day:
I conquered my negativity-mind,
I proclaimed the victory
Of my willingness-heart,
And I became my life's
Unconditional surrender
To God's Will.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Every day a new chance, Agni Press, 1991
