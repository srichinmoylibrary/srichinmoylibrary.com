Nutan Jiban Kari Abahan Deba Jibaner

Nutan jiban kari abahan
Deba jibaner kari aradhan
Eso go prakhar komanal shikha
Jadi taha mor thake bhalo likha
Asha ankur karaho biloy
Ek matra chahi tomar bijoy
Eso sangram eso mahajoy
Maraner ar nahi kari bhoy
Eso go dibya jiban
Deba jibaner kari aradhan

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
