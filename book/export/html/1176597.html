3638 You Can Conquer

You can conquer the outer world
With what you know
And by doing what you say.

You can conquer the inner world
Only by becoming a faithful instrument
Of your lofty ideal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
