9093 Continue To Perform Soulfully

Continue to perform soulfully
The work which God has given you.
To serve God is the greatest honour,
Even if the world does not recognise it.
Then, one day, even the world will admit
That you, and nobody else,
Served God faithfully
In His own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 91, Agni Press, 1983
