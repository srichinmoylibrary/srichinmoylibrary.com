Premaloker Kanane Nirabatar Swapane

Premaloker kanane
Nirabatar swapane
Ogo chira sundara
E hiya alingane

Translation:

In the garden of Love-Light,
In silence-dream,
O Beauty Eternal!
This heart of mine is in Your embrace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Garden of love-light, part 1, , 1974
