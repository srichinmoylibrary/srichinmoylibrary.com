39.

Chetana jahar akashe batase
Himadri shiro pare
Sagarer jale gahar andhare
Miliya nritya kare

O Lord, Your Consciousness
Pervades the sky
And the Himaloy-pinnacle-heights.
In darkness-caves
And in ocean-expanse
It sings and dances
In perfect oneness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, August 15 1945, Agni Press, 1996
