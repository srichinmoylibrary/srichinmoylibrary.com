6. The collectors

Dear Heaven,
     You have collected
     The tears of my night.
Thank you, thank you.

Sweet earth,
     You have collected
     The smiles of my life.
Thank you, thank you.

Lord Supreme,
     You have collected
     My desire-night
           and
     My aspiration-sun.
Thank You, Thank You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Canadian fruits, Bhakti Press, 1974
