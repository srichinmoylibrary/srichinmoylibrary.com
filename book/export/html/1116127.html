2726

My Master asks me:
  “Where is your future?”
Master, how can I say
Where my future is?
  “My child, that is not your problem.
Leave it to me.
Happily I shall deal with it.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 3, Agni Press, 1998
