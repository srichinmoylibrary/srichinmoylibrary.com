241. They do not contradict me

A great man
Does not contradict me
Because
He knows
I am a senseless soul.

A good man
Does not contradict me
Because
He knows
I am loveless soul.

A God-man
Does not contradict me
Because
He knows
I am a helpless soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
