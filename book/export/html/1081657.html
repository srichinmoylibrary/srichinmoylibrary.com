98. God's magnet

I love God's magnet-Feet;
They pull me compassionately.

I love God's magnet-Heart;
It pulls me incessantly.

I love God's magnet-Soul;
It pulls me
Unconditionally,
Divinely
And
Supremely.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
