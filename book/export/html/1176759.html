3799 I Am Already Up!

Satan says to me,
"Hurry up, hurry up!"
I say to Satan,
"Shut up, shut up!"
My Lord says to me,
"Get up, my child, get up!"
I say to my Lord,
"I am already up, Father,
I am already up!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 38, Agni Press, 1982
