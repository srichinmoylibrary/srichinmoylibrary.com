913. God descended, I ascended

I unconsciously
Ascended from a monkey.
God consciously
Descended from the skies.
I unconsciously
Offered God
My only treasure:
Cry, heart's cry.
God consciously
Offered me
His transcendental Treasure:
Smile, Goal's Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 19, Agni Press, 1973
