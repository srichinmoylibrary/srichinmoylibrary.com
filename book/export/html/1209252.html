23383

Before we entered into the spiritual life,
We had not the slightest idea
What role insecurity plays.
Now that we are in the spiritual life,
We see that insecurity
Is nothing short of a dictator
Who leads us to our destination:
Doom.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 234, Agni Press, 1996
