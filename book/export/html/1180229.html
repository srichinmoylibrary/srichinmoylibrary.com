7234 I Met God

The other day I met God
Walking down the street.
To my extreme happiness
He said to me
That His Heart of Compassion-Ocean
Would wash away
All my teeming imperfections.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 73, Agni Press, 1983
