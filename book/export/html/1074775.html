209. My God, my God!

My God, my God,
Why have You forsaken me?
   Have I told a lie?
   "No, never!"
   Have I betrayed You?
   "No, never!"
   Have I condemned Your world?
   "No, never!"
   Have I tried to dethrone You?
   "No, never!"

My Lord, my Lord,
   Why have You then
        Forsaken me?
            Why?
   "Dive deep within,
   And discover the answer."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 5, Agni Press, 1973
