10 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
With my prayer-mind
I always try to please myself.
With my meditation-heart
I always long to please You
In Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 August 1999. 2:00 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and three times with one arm. (49 right, 54 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
