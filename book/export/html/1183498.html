362. Victory-speed

Supreme,
We try so hard,
We cry so often,
How is it that
We never succeed?

Daughter, My daughter,
Try not to succeed
But
Try only to proceed.
Lo, your victory-speed
Is fast approaching you,
My daughter sweet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
