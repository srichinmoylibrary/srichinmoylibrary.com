31.

My Lord,
Why are You so indifferent
To me today?
"My child,
I do not even know the meaning
Of indifference.
Therefore, your opinion of Me
Does not apply to Me."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 1, Agni Press, 1998
