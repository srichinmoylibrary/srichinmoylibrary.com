23719

O my mind,
I want you to be
A full-time God-seeker.
O my heart,
I want you to be
A full-time God-lover.
O my life,
I want you to be
A full-time God-server.
And I myself
Want to be
A full-time God-dreamer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 238, Agni Press, 1997
