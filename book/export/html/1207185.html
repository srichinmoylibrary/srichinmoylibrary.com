My Lord,
Is there any place
Where You enjoy my presence
And where You dislike my presence?

"My child,
I have a real liking
For your heart-garden-fragrance,
And I have a strong dislike
Of your mind-thorn-jungle."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 214, Agni Press, 1994
