250. Unwilling

You are
Unwilling to be fed
By God.

He is
Unwilling to be fed
By God.

I am
Unwilling to be seen
By God's Justice-light
But
I am
Eagerly longing to be seen
By God's Compassion-Might.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
