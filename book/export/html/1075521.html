941. A reasoning animal

He is.
He is a reasoning animal.
He is not.
He is not a reasonable animal.
He has.
He has the false capacity
To admire all.
He does not have.
He does not have the admirable capacity
To aspire at all.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 19, Agni Press, 1973
