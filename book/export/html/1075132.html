559 Boys And Girls...

BOYS AND GIRLS

Boys will be boys;
Girls will be girls.
Boys will reach their Goal
Singing and running.
Girls will reach their Goal
Dancing and diving.

Boys will be boys;
Girls will be girls.
Boys will realise God,
The Highest Realisation.
Girls will manifest God,
The Absolute Perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 12, Agni Press, 1973
