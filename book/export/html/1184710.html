382. You are bound to succeed

If you want to satisfy ambition,
You will fail.
If you want to satisfy your ideal,
You will fail.
If you want to satisfy God-necessity
In yourself,
In others
And in the heart of the world,
You will certainly succeed;
You are bound to succeed.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
