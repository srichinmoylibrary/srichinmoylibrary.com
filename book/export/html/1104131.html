26 February 2000

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
No more clamouring mind-confusion,
No more!
God-satisfaction more, infinitely more,
Eternally more.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 February 2000, 6:26 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously five times. (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
