83. Why

Body, why do you love
The moonless night?
Vital, why do you love
The sunless day?
Mind, why do you love
The fruitless sky?
Heart, why do you love
The goalless shore?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
