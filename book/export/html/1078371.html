724 We Are Happy...

WE ARE HAPPY

You are happy
Because
You share with God
All the happenings,
Little and big,
In your life.

He is happy
Because
He loves God
Untiringly and unconditionally.

I am happy
Because
I have a real need for God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
