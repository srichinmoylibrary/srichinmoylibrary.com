20056

The mind is always ready
To blame somebody else.
The heart is quite often ready
To blame itself.
The soul says:
"O mind,
By blaming others
You can never be happy.
O heart,
By blaming yourself
You will get nothing worthwhile.
O mind, O heart,
Stay not in the world of blame.
Pray to God, meditate on God
For illumination."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 201, Agni Press, 1994
