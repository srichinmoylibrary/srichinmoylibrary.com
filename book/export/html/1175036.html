2093 One And The Same

With my Lord,
My accomplishment
And
My relinquishment
Are one and the same thing
Constantly.

With my Lord,
My aspiration to give
And
My aspiration to receive
Are one and the same thing
Invariably.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 21, Agni Press, 1981
