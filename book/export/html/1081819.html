260. The poet and seeker in me

O poet in me,
I love your snow-white inspiration.
I simply love it,
And
I mean it.

O seeker in me,
I love your blue-gold aspiration.
I simply love it,
And
I mean it.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
