660. Awake and gather

Awake and gather,
Awake and gather,
Awake! The world needs you.
Gather! You need the world.
He who sees within
Discovers the reality
That God needs him.
He who sees without
Discovers the reality
That he needs God, God alone.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
