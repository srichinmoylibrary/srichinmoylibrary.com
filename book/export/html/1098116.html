My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 54

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Belek and Antalya, Turkey in December 2006.

2 December 2006

Belek, Turkey

1 We Must Never Give Up ...

We must never give up
Our God-realisation-hope
And our God-manifestation-hope.

2 God Likes The Silence-Blooms ...

God likes the silence-blooms
Of my outer world.
God admires the silence-blossoms
Of my inner world.

3 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Do You not have any Secret
To share with me?
“My child,
I have many, many Secrets
To share with you,
But the time has not come.”

4 There Is No Difference ...

There is no difference
Between a strong desire
And a wild, hungry fire.

5 God Himself Uses ...

God Himself uses
My aspiration-breath
When He sings
And plays music.

6 The Human In Us ...

The human in us
Wants power-victory.
The divine in us
Needs love-victory.

7 True ...

True,
My hand-violin
Has sadly failed me,
But my heart-violin
Has never failed me
And will never fail.

8 When My Silence-Heart ...

When my silence-heart
Invokes God,
God comes down
Sooner than at once.

3 December 2006 ...

3 December 2006

Belek, Turkey

9 My Entire Being Is Thrilled ...

My entire being is thrilled
To be a member
Of my Supreme Lord’s
Inner family.

10 My Lord ...

My Lord,
You are all the time
Inside my heart.
How is it
That I do not see myself
Inside Your Heart,
Even for a fleeting moment?

11 Who Are The True Heroes? ...

Who are the true heroes?
Not those who destroy
But those who try to build
God’s Heart-Home on earth.

12 I Simply Break ...

I simply break
My Lord’s Heart
When I feed my ego.

13 In My Inner Family ...

In my inner family,
Only my soul and my heart
Are real citizens
Of God’s Heart-Land.

14 God Tells Me Again And Again ...

God tells me again and again
That He needs
My devotion-heart
And not
My brilliance and my talents.

15 Enthusiasm ...

Enthusiasm
Is a divinely proud forward march
Towards God’s Heart-Home.

16 Whenever I Write ...

Whenever I write
Most devoted letters to God,
He immediately invites
All the cosmic gods and goddesses,
And then He reads out my letters.

4 December 2006 ...

4 December 2006

Belek, Turkey

17 The Desire-Life ...

The desire-life
Wants God the Power.
The aspiration-heart
Needs only God.

18 To Be A Sleepless God-Lover ...

To be a sleepless God-lover
Is to be
A great ignorance-conqueror.

19 I Began My Life ...

I began my life
With God the Beautiful.
I shall end my life
In God the Peaceful.

20 I Am Infinitely Happier ...

I am infinitely happier
When I feel
That I belong to God
Than when I feel
That God belongs to me.

21 The Genuine Devotion ...

The genuine devotion
Of the heart
Is a magnetic power.

22 I Shall Never Allow ...

I shall never allow
My aspiration-day
To expire.

23 A Gratitude-Blossom-Heart ...

A gratitude-blossom-heart
Is
Beyond compare.

24 Love-Devotion-Songs ...

Love-devotion-songs
God has already taught me.
Nowadays He teaches me
Only surrender-songs.

5 December 2006 ...

5 December 2006

Belek, Turkey

25 I Call My Sad Failure-Past ...

I call my sad failure-past
‘Past’.
I call my happy present
‘The Blue Eye
Of my Golden Supreme’.

26 When I Say To God ...

When I say to God,
“God, You are great, very great,”
God bursts into wild laughter.

27 When I Say To God ...

When I say to God,
“God, You are good, very good,”
God gives me
A very sweet Smile.

28 Some Make Their Godward Journey ...

Some make their Godward journey
Out of sheer curiosity,
While others,
Out of their absolute necessity.

29 God Himself Feeds ...

God Himself feeds
My climbing aspiration-heart
Seven times a day.

30 Every Breath ...

Every breath
Is
A God-fulfilment-
Opportunity-moment.

31 God Alone Knows ...

God alone knows
If humility will ever be able
To touch the haughty head
Of humanity.

32 Each Soulful Smile ...

Each soulful smile
Of a God-seeker
Is a special God-manifestation-joy
Here on earth.

33 The Higher I Go ...

The higher I go,
The stronger I feel the need
To feast only
On God’s Smiles.

34 True, I Am Not At All Important ...

True, I am not at all important
To the world,
But I am extremely important
To my Lord Absolute Supreme,
For He knows that my heart and I
Love Him only
And need Him only.

6 December 2006 ...

6 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

35 My Dedication-Life-Road ...

My dedication-life-road
Goes all the way
To God’s highest Abode.

36 The Doubting Mind ...

The doubting mind
Is a God-torturer.

37 The Aspiring Heart ...

The aspiring heart
Is a God-treasurer.

38 With My Self-Giving Services ...

With my self-giving services,
I feed earth.

39 With My Teeming Dreams ...

With my teeming dreams,
I feed Heaven.

40 Little By Little ...

Little by little,
We give to God
What we have.

41 Profusely And Unreservedly ...

Profusely and unreservedly,
God gives us
What He is.

42 Not A Giant Mind ...

Not a giant mind
But a little heart
Has a free access
To God’s Heart-Home.

7 December 2006 ...

7 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

43 When I See God’s Eye ...

When I see God’s Eye,
I get terribly frightened.
When I see God’s Heart,
I am immediately awakened.

44 Where Do I Live? ...

Where do I live?
I live under my Lord’s
Protection-Eye.

45 My Morning Begins ...

My morning begins
By devouring
God’s Nectar-Feet-Dust.

46 God’s Forgiveness-Embrace ...

God’s Forgiveness-Embrace
Has given my heart
A new hope-light
And my life
A new promise-delight.

47 Time-River Flows ...

Time-river flows
Slowly, very slowly,
When my meditation-heart
Glows.

48 To Be Victorious In Life ...

To be victorious in life,
I must surmount
Each and every
Difficulty-mountain.

49 To Make Our Beloved Lord Supreme ...

To make our Beloved Lord Supreme
Happy,
We must open not one
But all opportunity-doors.

50 Now Is The Time ...

Now is the time
And here is the place
To please God
In His own Way.

8 December 2006 ...

8 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

51 Today I Am A Lover ...

Today I am a lover
Of God’s soul-stirring Flute.
Tomorrow I shall be a lover
Of God’s Victory-Trumpet.

52 My Freedom ...

My freedom
Is
My God-obedience.

53 My Freedom ...

My freedom
Is
My desire-annihilation.

54 My Freedom ...

My freedom
Sees nowhere
Earth-bondage-chains.

55 Each God-Heart-Song ...

Each God-Heart-Song
Of mine
Has a God-Soul-Fragrance-Melody.

56 Yesterday My Life Was ...

Yesterday my life was
A God-reliance-dream-beauty,
But today my life has become
God-Reality-Fragrance.

57 A God-Gratitude-Heart ...

A God-gratitude-heart
Is
A God-plenitude-life.

58 When It Is A Physical Death ...

When it is a physical death,
We cry and cry.
When it is a spiritual death,
God cries and cries.

9 December 2006 ...

9 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

59 This Morning ...

This morning
My heart-flame reached
A very, very high height.

60 Aspiration ...

Aspiration
Is
Heaven-climbing joy.

61 Dedication ...

Dedication
Is
Earth-spreading joy.

62 At The Top Of The Life-Tree ...

At the top of the life-tree
Is liberation.

63 At The Foot Of The Life-Tree ...

At the foot of the life-tree
Is the beginning
Of God-hunger.

64 Life And Death ...

Life and death
All the time ask questions,
But they never answer
Each other.

65 The Silence Of My Heart ...

The silence of my heart
Does not stop,
But the sound of my life
Does.

66 Needless To Say ...

Needless to say,
My fate on earth
Is deplorable.
Alas,
So is God’s.

10 December 2006 ...

10 December 2006

Antalya, Turkey

67 My Lord ...

My Lord,
May all my desire-prayers
Remain unanswered.

68 Heaven Is Heaven’s ...

Heaven is Heaven’s
Compassion for me.
Earth is earth’s
Concern for me.
I must succeed
In my God-manifestation-task.

69 Nothing Divine ...

Nothing divine
Can ever decay.
Nothing divine
Can even fade.

70 I Need On Earth ...

I need on earth
Only one thing:
A God-devotion-breath.

71 Every Morning ...

Every morning
God blesses me
With a new sunshine-heart
To receive Him
With enormous joy.

72 Pay No Attention ...

Pay no attention
To the wild thunders
Of the world.
Pay all attention
Only to the sweet Whispers
Of your Inner Pilot.

73 We Can Fathom ...

We can fathom
Everything else here on earth
Save and except
The unfathomable depth
Of silence.

74 God’s Perfection-Dream ...

God’s Perfection-Dream
For my life
Shall prevail.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 54, Agni Press, 2007
