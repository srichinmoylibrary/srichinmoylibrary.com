3 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
You are telling me
That our belief in You is not sufficient.
We must make You a living Reality
On earth
If we want to prove
That we love You only
And we need You only.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. 3 January 1999, 7:33 a.m. Before lifting 210 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
