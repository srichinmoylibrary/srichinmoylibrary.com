23 When God Came To Him ...

When God came to him
For the fifth time,
He asked God
Why He continued coming.
He asked God if God was a beggar
Or a robber.

(23. continued)

God said to him,
“My child,
I am neither a beggar
Nor a robber.
I just cannot help seeing you,
For you are My Divinity’s
Self-Transcendence-Dream.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 15, Agni Press, 2003
