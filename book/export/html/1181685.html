8676 God Himself Commanded Me

You want to know
If I consulted God
Before advising my mind to be pure
And my heart to be sincere.
I must say
That I did not consult God,
But God Himself commanded me
To speak to my mind
About its purity
And to my heart
About its sincerity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 87, Agni Press, 1983
