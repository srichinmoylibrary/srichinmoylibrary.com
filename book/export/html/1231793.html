Je Vis Pour Toi Seul

Je vis pour Toi seul,
Mon Seigneur de Compassion Supreme.
Je vis en Toi seul,
Mon Seigneur de Satisfaction Supreme.

Translation

I live for You alone,
My Lord of Compassion Supreme.
I live in You alone,
My Lord of Satisfaction Supreme.

Ecoutez-Moi, Ecoutez-Moi

Ecoutez-moi, ecoutez-moi
Sabar hriday purnyatoa
Ecoutez-moi, ecoutez.
Animesh rishikesh pancha bhute
Ecoutez-moi, s’il vous plait
Dharani jagiche ese khele

Translation

Listen to me, listen to me.
The heart of each human being is a river of virtue.
Listen to me, listen.
Sleeplessly the Heart of the Lord abides
In man’s five prime elements.
Listen to me, please.
Smiling and sporting, the world is awakening.

Je Connais

Je connais, je connais, je connais,
Ghuri ami mana bane
L’amour divin n’existe pas ici
Hetha shudhu bibishika chechamichi

Translation

I know, I know, I know,
I roam inside my mind-forest.
Love divine exists not here.
Here is only
Frightening and strangling clamour.

Je Ne Sais Rien

Je ne sais rien, je ne sais rien, je ne sais rien.
Tai mama priyatama chale more sathe niye

Translation

I know nothing, I know nothing, I know nothing.
Therefore, my Beloved Supreme
Takes me with Him wherever He goes.

Je Ne Parle Pas

Je ne parle pas, je ne parle pas, je ne parle pas
Tai tanu prana mane dibanishi shanti prabha

Translation

I do not speak, I do not speak, I do not speak.
Therefore, my body, vital and mind
Are flooded with peace and light.

Pas Ici.. La-bas.. Hetha Shudhu Dhula

Pas ici, pas ici, pas ici,
La-bas, la-bas, la-bas.
Hetha shudhu dhula mati kantaka chai
Hotha paramer abha

Translation

Not here, not here, not here.
There, there, there.
Here is only dist and earth, thorn and ash,
There is the Light of the Absolute Supreme.

Bidai, Bidai, Au Revoir

Bidai, bidai, au revoir, bidai, bidai, au revoir
Bhitare sabar premer jowar
Bidai, bidai, au revoir, bidai, bidai, au revoir
Ghume jagarane mukta duar

Translation

Goodbye, goodbye, goodbye.
Inside our heart is
The mounting flow of love divine.
Goodbye, goodbye, goodbye.
In sleep and in awakening,
Wide open is our heart-door.

Translation

Truth is in all,

Aujourd’hui

Aujourd’hui, aujourd’hui, aujourd’hui
Je reste seul avec Dieu;
Demain et a jamais
Dans l’eclat de Sa Compassion.

Translation

Today, today, today
Alone with God I stay;
Tomorrow and forever
With His Compassion-Ray.

Lorsque le Pouvoir de L’Amour

Lorsque le pouvoir de l’amour
Remplacera l’amour du pouvoir,
L’homme aura un nom nouveau:
      Dieu.

Translation

When the power of love
Replaces the love of power,
Man will have a new name:
God.

Translation

Translation

The confidence of the heart proposes.

Le doute humain voyage vite,
Mais la grace divine le rattrape toujours.

Translation

Human doubt travels very fast,
Yet the divine Grace overtakes it.

L'Aspiration Apparait Rapidement

L'aspiration apparait rapidement tandis que

Translation

Aspiration’s existence quickly appears
When desire's existence slowly expires.

Sincerity is our life's protector.

Aimer Dieu Est Une Opportunite

Aimer Dieu est une opportunite.

Translation

Opportunity means to love God

Mon Coeur Aime Celui Qui Aime Dieu

Mon coeur aime celui qui aime Dieu.
Mon ame aime celui que Dieu aime.
Ma realisation aime celui
Qui aime Dieu sans en rien attendre.

Translation

My heart loves the man who loves God.
My soul loves the man whom God loves.
My realisation loves the man who loves God
Without expecting anything from Him.

Translation

Light destroys darkness.
Aspiration destroys bondage.
Realisation destroys ignorance.

Est ma soumission interieure.

Translation

My outer confidence
Is my inner surrender.

Je Parle a Dieu

Je parle a Dieu.
Voila ma seule capacite.
J'ecoute Dieu.

Est devenue synonyme de liberte parfaite.

Translation

I can talk to God.

Dormez

Dormez.
Dieu est avec vous.
Eveillez-vous.
Dieu est pour vous.
Marchez.
Dieu est en vous.
Courez.
Dieu est a vous.

Translation

Sleep.
God is with you.
Wake up.
God is for you.
Walk.
God is within you.
Run.
God is of you.

Bonne Nuit

Bonne nuit, bonne nuit, bonne nuit.
Nishite hutibe hiyar kanane pabitar jui
Bonne nuit, bonne nuit, bonne nuit.
Moder lagiya dyuloke bhuloke samatar ekatar bhui

Translation

Good night, good night, good night.
At night in our heart-garden,
Purity-jasmine will blossom.
Good night, good night, good night.
For us in Heaven, and on earth for us,
The land of equality and oneness.

Vive La France!

Vive la France! Vive la France!
Your wisdom-sea is your Himalayan dance.
Vive la France! Vive!
Vive la France! Vive!
Your renaissance-skies eternally shall live.
The wonder of wonders: your culture role.
Your “Marseillaise” has won the cosmos-soul.

Une Simple Verite: Dieu Existe

Une simple verite:
Dieu existe.
Un bref message:
Dieu m'aime.
Une profonde realisation:
Je suis a Dieu seul
Et pour Dieu seul.

Translation

A simple truth:
God is.
A brief message:
God loves me.
A deep realisation:
I am of God alone
And I am for God alone.

O Comment Pourrais-Je Vivre Seul Avec

O comment pourrais-je vivre seul avec moi-meme?
Comment, et pourquoi?
Sans le coeur d'Amour de mon Supreme,
Ma vie ne serait qu'une plainte amere.

Translation

O how can I live with myself alone?
O how can I, and why?
Without my Supreme's Heart of Love,
My life a barren sigh.

O Seigneur, Ou Est La Verite?

O Seigneur, ou est la verite?
   “La ou se trouve ton Bien-Aime.”
Qui est mon Bien-Aime, qui?
   “Celui en Qui ta vie n’est que paix.”

Translation

O Lord, where is the Truth?
   “Where your Beloved is.”
Who is my Beloved, who?
   “In Whom your life is peace.”

France: Revolution and Evolution

Revolution, revolution, revolution.
Evolution, evolution, evolution.
Revolution of the crying and daring France;
Evolution of her Heaven-climbing dance.
France gave the world-summit Spirit’s fire-power;
The world-heart became hallowed gratitude-tower.
Two hundred year-blossoms, you we proudly treasure;
Your splendour-sky nonpareil beyond measure.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Vive La France, , 1989
