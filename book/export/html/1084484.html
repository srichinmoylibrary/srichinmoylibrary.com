50. Perhaps we both are right

  You say
He has a sharpened mind.
  I say
He has a darkened mind.
Perhaps we both are right.  You say
His heart is all love for humanity.
  I say
His heart is all love for Divinity.
Perhaps we both are right.  You say
His soul is of God.
  I say
His soul is for God.
This time no "perhaps";
We both are unmistakably right.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 10, Agni Press, 1974
