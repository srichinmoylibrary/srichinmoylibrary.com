20 May 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart all the time
Dream of the Beauty and Delight
Of the highest Heavens.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 May 2000, 8:42 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,150 lbs. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
