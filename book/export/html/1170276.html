180. Believe it or not

Believe it or not,
I have seen God, the real God,
Inside you, inside
Your purity-body.
Believe it or not,
I have seen God, the real God,
Inside you, inside
Your beauty-eye.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sound becomes, silence is, Agni Press, 1975
