18 May 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The world-doubting mind
Is a torturer.
The soul-doubting mind
Is a sufferer.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

18 May 2000, 8:25 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 280 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
