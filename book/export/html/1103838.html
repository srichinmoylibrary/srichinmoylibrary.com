2 October 1999 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   May my life-boat
Today and every day
   Ply between
Your Compassion-Eye-Beauty-Shore
   And
Your Forgiveness-Heart-Fragrance-Shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 October 1999, 9:30 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 900 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
