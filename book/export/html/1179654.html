6665 Each Thought

True, each thought
Is not a striking thing,
But it is a living thing.

True, each man
Is not manifesting the truth,
But he is definitely embodying the truth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 67, Agni Press, 1983
