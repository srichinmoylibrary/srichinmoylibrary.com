570 There Was A Time...

THERE WAS A TIME

There was a time
When I loved desire-ground.

There was a time
When I loved aspiration-hill.

There was a time
When I loved realisation-mountain.

Now I love only one thing:
God-surrender.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
