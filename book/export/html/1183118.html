33 Who forgives me?

Who forgives me?
Not you,
But God.

Who tolerates me?
Not you,
But God.

Who has accepted me?
Not you,
But God.

Then how do you expect me
To be with you,
To be for you,
To be in you,
You stark, impossible fool!

From: Sri Chinmoy, This is God's Home, Agni Press, 1975
