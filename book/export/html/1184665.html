337. To know you a little better

To know you a little better
Is to make significant progress
In the inner life.
To know you a little better
Is to run towards the summit
Of success-hill.

To stay more in you, less in me:
This is the only way
I can see the face of
Perfection-delight.
There is no other way,
There is no other way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
