984. Only one complaint

In the City of God
Man has only one complaint
   Against God:
God does not love him anymore
   The way He used to.

In the city of man
God has only one complaint
   Against man:
Man does not think of Him anymore
   The way he used to.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
