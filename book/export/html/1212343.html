26443

We do not want to mould our life
According to God's Vision-Light.
We are only satisfied in begging
Or forcing God
To tolerate our self-chosen way of life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 265, Agni Press, 1998
