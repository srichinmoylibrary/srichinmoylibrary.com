913. God's new Dawn

I love the eyes
Of the morning rose.
In them I see
   The Beauty of God.

I love the ears
Of the evening rose.
In them I see
   The Patience of God.

I love the lips
Of the dying rose.
From them I hear
   The message of God's new Dawn.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
