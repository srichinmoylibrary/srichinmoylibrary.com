Question: Guru, is there any reason why black athletes are so good?

Sri Chinmoy: They still maintain some of the dynamic qualities of the animal kingdom. Here in athletics they use it in a constructive way. They still hold that kind of daring vital consciousness to reveal and manifest the Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Politics and spirituality: can they go together?, Agni Press, 1977
