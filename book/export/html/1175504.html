2556 Two Powerful Enemies

I have two powerful
And shameless enemies:
Anxiety and impurity.
To anxiety I say:
"I shall not think of you
Anymore."
To impurity I say:
"I shall not allow you
To think of me
Anymore."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 26, Agni Press, 1982
