15691

Because it is so easy
To betray the heart,
The mind does it every day.

Because it is so difficult
To love the mind,
The heart accepts the challenge
And does it daily,
Plus satisfactorily.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 157, Agni Press, 1991
