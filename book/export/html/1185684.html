48

My Lord, do not tell me
What Your Will is.
Without knowing Your Will
I shall try to fulfil it.
Why?
Because, my Lord,
If I first learn Your Will
And then execute it,
Who knows,
I may not be as happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 1, Agni Press, 1983
