8 January 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
An eagerness-stride of my heart
Is
A progress-mile of my life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 January 2000, 6:08 a.m. Foz do Iguacu, Brazil. Before doing sixty-one one-arm pushes with 500 lbs. (30 right, 31 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
