233. God's business

Earth-ascension:
God's business, not mine.

Heaven-descension:
God's business, not mine.

Creation-perfection:
God's business, not mine.

Yet why am I unhappy?
I am unhappy
Because I am a self-styled God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
