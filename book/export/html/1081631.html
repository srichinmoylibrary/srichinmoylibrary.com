72. Familiar faces

Doubt
Is familiar as an old and damaging error
Of mine.

Faith
Is familiar as an old and brave friend
Of mine.

Fear
Is familiar as an old and shameless enemy
Of mine.

Courage
Is familiar as an old and yet eternally bold captain
Of mine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
