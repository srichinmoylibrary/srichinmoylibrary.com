627 In Her Eyes...

IN HER EYES

In the morning
I see sun-beauty in her eyes.

At noon
I see sun-power in her eyes.

In the evening
I see sun-peace in her eyes.

At night
I see sun-bliss in her eyes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
