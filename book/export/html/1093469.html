64 The Desiring Mind Opens Its Door ...

The desiring mind opens its door
To the earthly amazements.
The aspiring heart opens its door
To the Heavenly enlightenments.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 1, Agni Press, 2000
