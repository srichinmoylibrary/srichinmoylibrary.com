13008

O mind,
How long will you remain
Chained to doubt?

O heart,
How long will you remain
Chained to insecurity?

O life,
How long will you remain
Chained to ignorance?

O world,
How long will you remain
Chained to indifference?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 131, Agni Press, 1990
