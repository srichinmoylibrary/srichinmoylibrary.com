48. Just the beginning

   To God
I have offered
A flood of tears.
I assure God
That there is nothing left.   To me
God has given
A sea of Joy.
He assures me
That this is just
The very beginning.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 18, Agni Press, 1974
