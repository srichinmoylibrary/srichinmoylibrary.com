6839

You are really good,
Yet you entertain
The lowest opinion of yourself.
He is really bad,
Yet he remains convinced
That he is extremely good.
What can poor God do?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 69, Agni Press, 1984
