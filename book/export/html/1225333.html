I Had a Dream, I Had a Dream

I had a dream,
     I had a dream.
God will make me
     His Compassion-stream.
I have a dream,
     I have a dream.
God needs my smile
     To support His team.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 1, , 1977
