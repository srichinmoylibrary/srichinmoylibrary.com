21 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day, in the small hours
Of the morning,
I devotedly send You an express telegram
With a prayerful gratitude-message-cry.
My Supreme, do You ever read
My express telegrams?
"My child, not only do I read them,
But I love them and I treasure them."
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 February 1999, 8:16 a.m. Before lifting 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 280 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
