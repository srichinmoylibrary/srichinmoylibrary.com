Angels

1.

Angels speak, they whisper more. Angels whisper, they inspire more. Angels inspire, they radiate more. Angels radiate, they protect more.

2.

Greatness angels conceal. Goodness angels reveal.

3.

Sweetness-fountain, radiance-mountain an angel is.

4.

An angel is Heaven’s God–descending vision–beauty. An angel is earth’s God–ascending mission-duty.

5.

An angel blesses the sleep-world with peace. An angel blesses the dream-world with delight.

6.

An angel is the beauty of an affectionate life and fragrance of a compassionate heart.

7.

An angel comes not alone to birth, joy comes too. An angel come not alone to death, peace comes too.

8.

When I meditate, the ancient peace of an angel cradles me. When I pray, the modern sound of an angel energizes me.

9.

An angel helps the human mind to be a God-seeking journey. An angel helps the human heart to be a God–arriving destination.

10.

The instant touch of an angel can be a lasting foundation of all heart–virtues of a seeker. The very touch of an angel dispels the agonizing headache of my bondage–slavery–mind.

11.

Not infant tears, not even orphan tears, but angel tears of my heart are the swiftest in conquering the Heart of God and pleasing God in God’s own Way.

12.

When I listen to an angel, I hasten my God–arrival.

13.

No matter how often I see an angel in my dreams, I am always thrilled to the core.

14.

O my guardian angel, I love to repeat countless times your flower-sweet name.

15.

The very presence of an angel can, and does still, answer all my untiring questions.

16.

It is your arrogance, and not your confidence that does not listen to the message of an angel.

17.

My guardian angel tells me that this is not the era of a cave-dweller but the era of a sky-flier.

18.

My guardian angel is by far the best tamer of my anxiety-tremors.

19.

From the invisible Beyond, the angels come down and make themselves visible to us.

20.

My life becomes a sea of delight the moment an angel plays on the chimes of my heart.

21.

You do not have to be a saint to be loved by an angel. An angel can love you no matter what you are or who you are.

22.

O my guardian angel, whether you stand very close to me, or at a distance, your illumination-eyes have a magnetic pull on me.

23.

Even the very word 'angel' charms my heart immeasurably.

24.

Angels just give in abundance. They do not expect anything from us in return.

25.

The very appearance of an angel is an immediate escalator ride to Divinity’s lofty heights.

26.

O my guardian angel, how I wish I could eagerly, sleeplessly and self-givingly run to meet your instant needs.

27.

The angels live between my heart-room and God’s Heart-room.

28.

If you can get a glimpse of an angel today, God Himself will be willing to be caught by you tomorrow.

29.

In my dreams, the angels will help me breathe with the blossoming dawn.

30.

Each time I empty my mind, I see an angel painting a rainbow in my heart-sky.

31.

When my prayer is sincere I hear an angel singing a song of immortality.

When my meditation is deep, I hear an angel playing the music of Infinity.

32.

I become the ecstasy of my heart the moment an angel appears before me.

33.

An angel always invites us not to wait for tomorrow’s future trains.

34.

The moment an angel descends, my weakness-desires disappear.

35.

When I see an angel, the first thing he does is open up my heart-door to hope.

36.

Only a stark fool will obey an angel grudgingly.

37.

When my prayers are simple, sincere and pure, God sends an angel to me with his rainbow-smile-flood.

38.

The greatest mistake we make in our life is when we disobey the sweet whispers of an angel.

39.

My guardian angel wants me to be a seeker and not a speaker.

40.

He who becomes glad after seeing an angel, gladdens others.

41.

When I telephone my Lord Supreme for news, He immediately answers the phone and then He passes it on to one of His special angels to speak to me on His behalf.

42.

Unlike on earth, angels are always in heavy demand in Heaven.

43.

We may not know it, but it is absolutely true that each human being is inextricably related to at least one angel.

44.

A great surprise is when we do not think that angels exist. A greater surprise is when we believe in their existence but we do not see them. The greatest surprise is when we believe in their existence and we see them, yet we do not listen to their advice.

45.

When God swiftly wants to offer me His blessingful Hand, He always employs an angel.

46.

No angel can really be old, either in earthly years or in Heavenly calendars.

47.

When my hope-bird disappears, I cry and cry. When my guardian angel appears, I fly and fly.

48.

I was a fool once when I said that angels are nothing short of our mental hallucination. I was a fool twice when I thought that angels can do nothing for the betterment of the world. I was a fool thrice when I said that this world of ours can easily live without the inspiration and protection of the angels.

49.

When my God-gratitude-tears rise and fall in my fountain-heart, I immediately see the world-illumination-dance of the angels.

50.

My guardian angel repeatedly tells me that I do not have to remain a prisoner of my fate.

51.

Not once, not twice but three times I make my guardian angel extremely sad: once when it sees that my mind is devoid of conscience; once when it sees that my heart is wanting in receptivity; once when it sees that my life is gratitude-famine.

52.

An angel never suffers, but we do suffer from our angel-existence-doubt-limitations.

53.

When God is sad, nobody can console God as quickly and as successfully as His angel-child does.

54.

The main task of an angel is to scatter joy-blossoms all-where.

55.

I feel I am indeed a unique seeker only when God grants me my victory and angels cheer my victory.

56.

When my meditation is extremely deep, I clearly see that my guardian angel chariots my heart from paradise to paradise.

57.

Each time I see an angel, my God-devotion-delight immediately increases.

58.

When an angel loves me, I smile and smile. When I love the world, the world smiles and smiles.

59.

My guardian angel is my most admirable and adorable medium of God-communication.

60.

When angels start dancing, my heart cannot stop applauding.

61.

My heart enjoys a holiday each time it is visited by an angel.

62.

Angels just ignore our doubting mind’s sneering.

63.

The touch of an angel is my passport to my heart’s freedom-joy.

64.

An angel not only goes around my head, but also enters into my heart.

65.

Bliss escorts my guardian angel when it wants to come and bless me.

66.

Without the express blessings from my guardian angel I can see very little of my inner world.

67.

Every morning, my guardian angel blessingfully invites me to occupy a treasured place in its heart.

68.

My guardian angel has a huge umbrella under which I can always find shelter.

69.

As my heart has nothing against God, even so, my guardian angel never has anything against me.

70.

The moment an angel touches my head, immediately I know that I am destined to do something great. The moment an angel touches my heart, immediately I feel that I am destined to be unique.

71.

My guardian angel does not command me to listen, but I command myself to learn.

72.

I do not know why at times I want to hide when I know perfectly well that my guardian angel is always proud of me.

73.

A moment’s touch of an angel can and does liberate me from the unpleasant mixture of fear and shame.

74.

I do not know why I need anybody else besides my guardian angel.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Angels, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1995
