573. Believe it or not

I am an angel.
Believe it or not,
I am a real angel.
Alas, nobody believes it.

I am a Yogi.
Believe it or not,
I am a real Yogi.
Alas, nobody believes it.

I am God.
Believe it or not,
I am really God.
Alas, nobody believes it.

Since nobody believes me
I shall no longer remain
An angel, a Yogi or God.

I shall go back
To my old life,
A simple and ordinary life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 12, Agni Press, 1974
