13. Three messages

He had only three messages to give:
One to Heaven,
One to earth,
One to hell.
"Dear Heaven,
Be careful of your indifference-power.
Dear earth,
Be careful of your ignorance-length.
Dear hell,
Be careful of Satan's autocracy."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 5, Agni Press, 1974
