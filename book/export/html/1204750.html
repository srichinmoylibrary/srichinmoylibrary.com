18925

There is nothing wrong
If we start our journey
With a question mark-mind.
But eventually we must discontinue
That friendship
And establish a new
And permanent friendship
With our God-seeking heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 190, Agni Press, 1993
