14 Union And Re-Union...

UNION AND RE-UNION

You want to know
The difference
Between
Man-realisation
And
God-realisation.
The difference is very simple:
Man-realisation is the union
Of illumining Light
And
Devouring darkness
In your mind-search.
God-realisation is the re-union
Of Universal Love
And
Transcendental Truth
In your heart-cry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 15, Agni Press, 1974
