Praner Thakur Ami Tomar Hater Putul

Praner thakur ami tomar hater putul
Kabhu hasi kabhu nachi na kare bhul

Translation:

O my Inner Pilot, I am Your doll.
At times I smile,
At times I dance.
I never make any mistake.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy rosebuds, , 1987
