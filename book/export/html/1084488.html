3. My ancient promise

Lord, You are with me;
Therefore,
I am not afraid
Of the darkening and frightening clouds
In my mind.

Lord, You are with me;
Therefore,
I am ready
To brave the storms
In my heart.

Lord, You are with me;
Therefore,
I shall be worthy
Of my ancient promise to You:
   Love-manifestation
   In the core of my body-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 11, Agni Press, 1974
