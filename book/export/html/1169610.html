255. Calamity, calamity

Calamity, calamity,
You are the true friend
Of uncertainty.

Calamity, calamity,
Away you take the essence
From sure reality.

Calamity, calamity,
In you I see
A failure-life's untold poverty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
