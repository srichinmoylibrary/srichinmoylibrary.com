31. They need silence

Soothing silence
My body needs.
Striving silence
My vital needs.
Searching silence
My mind needs.
Illumining silence
My heart needs.
Fulfilling silence
My soul needs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
