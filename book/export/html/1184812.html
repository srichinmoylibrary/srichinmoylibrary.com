484. He lives in his fears and doubts

In his world there is no plan.
In his life there is no idea.
In his heart there is no idea.
He lives in his baseless fears
And
In his graceless doubts.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
