47. Willpower

Willpower
Is the soul's light within us.
Willpower
Is the hero divine within us.
Willpower
Is the self-giving love in us
For the Reality in us.
And the Reality in us
Is the Inner Pilot,
The Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Dedication-drops, Agni Press, 1976
