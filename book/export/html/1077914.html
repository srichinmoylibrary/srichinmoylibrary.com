267. Where are you?

O glowing days of hope,
Where are you?

O flowing days of life,
Where are you?

O dreaming days of love,
Where are you?

O illumining days of God,
Where are you?

Oh, where are you?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
