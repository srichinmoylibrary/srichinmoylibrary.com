Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 150

14901

My soul, I need
Your ancient wisdom-light
To feed my heart.
My heart, I need
Your pure love
To feed my mind.

14902

O humanity's loving heart,
Your sweetness-smile
Captures God's Compassion-Eye.

14903

God asks for
Only one good quality,
Not even two,
Inside the heart-life
Of each and every human being.

14904

Unless you become
A self-giving server of God
In man,
Your complete happiness
Will not come into being.

14905

God does not want me
To create opinions.
He wants me only to create
Prayerful and soulful actions.

14906

Simplicity in the outer life
Cannot do everything
To please us.
Purity in the inner life
Cannot do everything
To please us.
But once we have surrender
To God's Will,
Both simplicity and purity
Will be of unimaginable assistance.

14907

Only a man of sterling faith
Is ready for the inner journey,
The journey that God treasures
More than anything else.

14908

Before you expect any favour from God,
Show Him your mind's determination,
Your heart's aspiration
And your life's willingness.

14909

I have entered many times
My heart-temple,
Which is so beautiful.
How I wish I could enter only once
My soul-temple
Which is infinitely more beautiful,
Plus fruitful!

14910

Say the right thing,
Do the right thing,
Become the right person,
Before it is too late
For you to feel
That God-Compassion is on your side.

14911

The mind says to the heart,
"Have you come here to destroy me,
Or have you come to illumine me?"
The heart says,
"I have come to illumine you,
And nothing else."
The mind says,
"Then I am at your disposal."

14912

A mere sadness-heart
Cannot expedite
Our Godward journey.
A mere sadness-heart
Is a deplorable failure
In a human life.

14913

Unless you become
A sleepless voyager,
The surges of ignorance-sea
Will just devour you!

14914

Be not at war
With your mind's imperfection.
Just take care of your heart.
God's Compassion
And your heart's aspiration
Will do everything for you.

14915

My meditation-fountain
Quenches my heart's
Aspiration-thirst.

14916

The outer world-voice
Is so easy to hear.
The inner world-voice
Is extremely difficult to hear
Because my body, vital and mind
Are at constant war
Inside my silence-heart-life.

14917

My Lord Supreme,
My willingness-capacity to serve You
Is the only good quality that I have
In my aspiration-life.

14918

Sweetness, soulfulness and fruitfulness
Have dawned in your eyes
Because you have made
Your unconditional surrender to God.

14919

The mind's complaint against the heart
Is a birthless and deathless story.
The heart's complaint against the mind
Is a rare experience-song.

14920

My heart's gratitude-flame
Has the capacity to hasten
My life's inner cry
And my life's outer smile.

14921

Do you know what God's
First morning thought is?
He wants to see a soulful smile
From each and every human being.

14922

My Lord is asking me
To devotedly wait
For the rainbow-dawn
Of a new world.

14923

God does not believe in
Slave-surrender.
He believes only in
Cheerfulness-surrender.

14924

A division-mind
Will imprison you.
An aspiration-heart
Will liberate you.
A perfection-life
Will immortalise you.

14925

Inner faith in God
Is the very beginning
Of stupendous victory-delight.

14926

A heart of sleepless surrender
Is the treasure-house
Of God's endless, sacred Blessings.

14927

Sweetness-forgiveness has dawned
Upon your life,
Because you are sleeplessly pleasing God
In God's own Way.

14928

God does not want you
To enjoy lethargy anymore.
He wants you to be dynamic
In outer self-giving
And inner God-becoming.

14929

I have made it a point
To soulfully brighten
My heart's self-giving lamp.

14930

God wants me to make the choice
Either to claim Him as my very own
Or to possess His world
And be possessed by His world.

14931

Every morning I try devotedly
To sail the life-boat
Of inner progress-delight.

14932

My life's hope
Blossoms
Only in my heart's
Surrender-garden.

14933

If you are an advanced seeker,
Then God's one-way soliloquy
Will illumine you and fulfil you.

14934

I shall remember
That my life's stupendous
Heavenward journey
Commenced with a fragile hope.

14935

How can you make any inner progress
If you allow yourself all the time
To be assailed
By blind impatience-night?

14936

Once you have established
Your inner contact
With your Inner Pilot,
Your life's transformation-victory
Will not remain a far cry.

14937

Immortality does love
The beautiful achievements
Of fleeting time.

14938

No matter how hard you try
To annoy or ignore God,
You will fail to escape
God's all-pervading Compassion-Eye.

14939

Man has only two duties:
To offer God an orphan-cry
And to offer God a prince-smile.

14940

Alas,
We unmistakably treasure
The boons
And cleverly forget
The Boon-Giver.

14941

God is impartial,
But the human mind
Fails to understand it.
It judges God's Wisdom-Light
In its own very limited way.

14942

God tells me
That I have many times given Him
My crying heart.
Now He wants me to give Him
My smiling life.

14943

If you pay attention
To God's timely inner Warnings,
Then in your outer life
You can avoid danger-accidents.

14944

It seems that most human beings
Secretly enjoy
An overdose of doubt.

14945

When you do not do the right thing,
Neither Heaven's compassion
Nor earth's concern
Will be responsible for you.
You will only be able to blame yourself.

14946

Each day we try to become
A little wiser.
Alas, instead of becoming wiser,
We become helplessly older.

14947

Any piece of good news
Is only meant for
Heaven-receiving and earth-inspiring
Human beings.

14948

A genuine prayer-life -
Believe it or not, my fellow seekers -
Is supremely simple and fruitful.

14949

How can you have
Full confidence
In your mind's
Temporary faith?

14950

Because of God's constantly
Unconditional Forgiveness,
Man can still hope
And still proceed forward.

14951

My doubting mind
And my strangling vital
Have joined the gathering
Of completely lost beings.

14952

If you are a stranger
To aspiration-summit,
How do you expect perfection-sun
To dawn upon you?

14953

Without
God's infinite Compassion-Light,
No human being can shatter
His life-binding shackles.

14954

Your life will not be able
To remove the dust of failure
If you do not make friends
With climbing hope and illumining promise.

14955

To pass through the dark corridor
Of ignorance-night,
You need a heart
Flooded with silence-light.

14956

My soul this morning blessed me
With very special advice:
Be happy, be happy, be happy
Only after you have accomplished
The impossible,
And not before!

14957

O sky-vast hope,
Where are you?
Will you not respond
To my heart's streaming tears'?

14958

My soul is asking my life
To live only inside
The breath of my gratitude-heart.

14959

O my mind,
I feel so sorry for you -
That you have allowed yourself
To be tortured by curiosity-eagerness.

14960

My soul repeatedly tells me
That there shall come a time
When my outer face will be surrounded
By my soul's all-illumining
And all-fulfilling aura.

14961

Today something completely new
Has awakened my mind.
My mind feels sincerely guilty
For its exorbitant self-praise.

14962

When my mind does something,
It wants to amplify
Its action-achievement-sound,
But when my heart does something,
It prayerfully waits
For God to do the needful.

14963

Alas, my heart's long-treasured
God-realisation-hope
Has been washed away
By a ruthless, giant disbelief.

14964

No blighted doubt can uproot
The sterling faith-plant
In the heart of human beings.

14965

Alas, there is a yawning gulf
Between hurtful depression
And powerful determination.

14966

The repetition of advice
I never enjoy,
Either when I give it
Or when I receive it.

14967

My heart of oneness-sympathy
Always tries to erase
The failure-fear of my mind.

14968

Inside the very depths
Of my mind's storm,
I always see a vast expanse
Of silence-peace.

14969

Not when you are forced
To find truth,
But when you are eager
To find truth,
Will you discover
The true meaning of life.

14970

This morning I was so surprised
To see my mind standing side by side
With my heart
At the faith-starting line,
Preparing to run
To the peerless satisfaction-finish.

14971

The illumining soul
And the crying heart
Always like to play
God-fulfilment-game.

14972

God has a special liking for me,
Not because
I have achieved something great,
But because
I have a God-faith-foundation-heart.

14973

The ignorance-giant
Ruthlessly drags my mind
Along the brooding,
Thickening doubt-road.

14974

O my mind,
I can tolerate you
Even when you do many, many
Things wrong.
But one aspect of yours
I dislike vehemently,
And that is your stubborn
Unwillingness-frown.

14975

Stark disappointment
Gave a hasty chase
To his fame-fed life.

14976

I have put my hope-friend
In my heart's aspiration-boat
To arrive at perfection-fulfilment-shore.

14977

My heart's abiding aspiration-cry
Is now being protected and perfected
And eventually will be liberated
By the watchful Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Supreme.

14978

Every morning I do
My heart's oneness-stretching exercises
So that I do not suffer
From my mind's insecurity-cramps.

14979

The sweetness-fruits
Of my surrender-life
My Lord Supreme
Dearly treasures.

14980

To gain confidence-might,
Use your imagination-mind
And aspiration-heart.
You are bound to be successful!

14981

To forgive humanity unconditionally
Is, indeed, to meet
An unparalleled challenge in life.

14982

O stupid mind,
When will you have the wisdom
To search for eternal Truth
And not for temporary, fleeting truth?

14983

Based on what I have observed
In my spiritual life,
I believe that nothing good
Can ever remain unachieved
By a sincere God-lover.

14984

Alas, I do not know how long
I shall have to hang on to
A tiny, feeble faith-thread
In my aspiration-life!

14985

I have never felt
Anything like this before!
One single purity-thought
Has kept my entire being
Extremely happy
For many long hours.

14986

My mind says to my heart:
"O heart, tell me
What you have learned
From God's Satisfaction-Smile."

My heart says to my mind:
"O mind, do not search
For earth-explanation,
But long for Heaven-illumination!"

14987

Alas, how many times
Does God have to tell me
That it is my aspiration-seed
That will one day grow into
A fully-blossomed manifestation-tree?

14988

Every morning
My soul teaches my aspiring heart
A new, self-giving happiness-song.

14989

The Eastern view of life:
Time will wait for you
As long as you want.

The Western view of life:
Alas, what was I doing
When the time disappeared?

14990

He who has not achieved
Perfection-patience in life,
Will not be blessed
By God's infinite Delight.

14991

To accept all human limitations
And then transcend or transform them
Is precisely the thing God wants
From each and every human being.

14992

Your gratitude-heart-fragrance-breath
And God's Satisfaction,
God wants to keep inseparable.

14993

Everything has to go wrong, alas,
The moment the mind
Becomes the sole judge!

14994

Before destruction has taken place,
Nobody thinks that destruction
Is attachment-forest in disguise.

14995

Every day
God gives a special Blessing-Message
To His intimate circle of believers.

14996

My heart's prayer-dreams
Are my life's
Reality-fulfilment-promises.

14997

God-adoring feelings
Are the peerless treasures
Of a seeker-heart.

14998

God does not use
His direct Justice-Light-Line
As often as He is entitled to.

14999

Do not ride the depression-train!
It will without fail, collide
With the destruction-train.

15000

God compassionately commissions
Each life-artist
To paint a picture
Of a peace-loving world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 150, Agni Press, 1991
