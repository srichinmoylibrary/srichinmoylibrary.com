24347

God wants you to become
Mankind's lighthouse
On the strength of your sleepless
And breathless self-offering.
And what will God do then?
God will give you a standing ovation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 244, Agni Press, 1998
