199. Why does he break?

Whom to blame?
No one.
Why blame?
No reason.
Who is blaming?
The deliberate breaker.
What does he break?
Oneness-vessel.
Why does he break?
Because he knows not what
Glowing
Flowing
Fulfilling
Immortalising
ECSTASY is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 4, Agni Press, 1974
