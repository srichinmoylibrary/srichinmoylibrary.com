3672 If You Can See The Beauty

If you can see the beauty
Of God's Compassion-Light
In God's Eye,
Then God also will see the purity
Inside the heart
Of your gratitude-smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
