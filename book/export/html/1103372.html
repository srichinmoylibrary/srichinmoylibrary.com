6 April 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You are so kind to me.
You are so compassionate to me.
Do allow the tears of my devotion-heart
   To flow
Only towards Your Compassion-Sea.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

6 April 1999, 9:43 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. fifty-two times with one arm. (25 right, 25 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
