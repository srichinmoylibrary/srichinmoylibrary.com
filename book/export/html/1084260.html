30. They long to consume

My heart's hunger
Longs to consume
The perfume of my soul.
My soul's hunger
Longs to consume
The Light of my Lord.
My Lord's hunger
Longs to consume
The imperfection of my bosom's pride.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 6, Agni Press, 1974
