Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 165

16401

My Lord,
Do give me the capacity
To sing every day
A few new love, devotion and
Surrender-songs
To make You happy.

16402

O my mind's
Enthusiasm-child,
Wake up!
Sleep not anymore!
It is already very late
For you to wake up.

16403

Is there anybody
Who has never suffered
From the stupid mind's
Ruthless inferiority-invasion?

16404

No!
Not God's Justice
But you alone
Have created
Your uselessness-life.

16405

Every day
God's Compassion
Teaches the seeker
How to discover
His life's deeper dimensions.

16406

O where are you,
My life's silence-speed?
O where are you,
My heart's gratitude-speed?

16407

My mind
Is now genuinely ashamed
Of its long-standing friendship
With doubt.

16408

My mind's strong convictions
Are no match
For my heart's pure aspirations.

16409

Every day
I force my ingratitude-mind
To go and visit
Its graveyard.

16410

I may not be sweet,
But my heart's gratitude-tears
Are always sweet,
Measurelessly sweet.

16411

Where do I live?
I live in my imperfection's
Despair-ridden world.

16412

A suspicion-mind
Is always a prelude
To a disaster-life.

16413

My Beloved Supreme,
Do You remember me?
Was I not once upon a time
A most favourite child
Of Yours?

16414

O my heart's peace-dove,
Fly, fly,
In Infinity's oneness-sky.

16415

My heart's hope-fragrance
Is the supreme inspirer
Of my life's promise-dreams.

16416

Today my soul
Has given me the capacity
To burn up my mind's
Unwillingness-arrogance.

16417

God's Satisfaction-Heart
Is the protection supreme
For all heart-travellers.

16418

Devotion's intensity
Multiplies
The heart's aspiration-flames.

16419

Obedience-heart-flowers
Have no
Disobedience-mind-thorns.

16420

May I find
My days and nights
Between my soul's dynamism
And my heart's eagerness.

16421

O my life's empty dreams,
Can you not see
That you are carrying me
To nothingness-land?

16422

Indeed,
There is an unbridgeable chasm
Between my mind's hallucination
And my heart's illumination.

16423

O my poor life,
Abandon, abandon immediately
All your failure-thoughts
And see
The first dawn of reality.

16424

Impossibility-frontiers,
Every day
I shall vehemently
Push you back
Farther and farther.

16425

Self-doubt
Will take you
To the danger-path,
And there you will meet
With stark disaster.

16426

Liberation you will get
At God's choice Hour.
Rest assured,
It will not come too late!

16427

A purity-heart can enjoy
A small foretaste
Of celestial peace-delight.

16428

Is there any day
When I am not embarrassed
By the harassment
Of my mind?

16429

I do not totally abhor darkness,
For there is definitely
Some light
Even in darkness.

16430

My mind,
I am not utterly displeased
With you,
For you are at least
A curable atheist.

16431

You are so fortunate that
God Himself has commissioned you
To reveal and manifest Him
In His earth-transformation-Promise.

16432

God wants
Your undivided attention.
Are you ready?
If not, you and your mind's
Bumpy road
Can go in your own way.

16433

A sleeplessly self-giving soul
Is not an excitement-elevator
But an enlightenment-elevator.

16434

I am so happy and blessed
That my very first attempt
To go beyond the mind
And enjoy the bliss of silence
Was completely successful.

16435

My Lord,
Your Justice-Light may not,
But Your Compassion-Height can easily
Tell me how
To separate my mind
From the whirlpool of worries.

16436

O how beautiful
Is desire's natural death!
O how fruitful
Is aspiration's spontaneous birth!

16437

Daily
Everybody has to capture
And tame
His monkey-mind and vital-horse.

16438

Compassion is the temple
And forgiveness is the shrine
Of all truly God-loving souls.

16439

My Lord,
Will You love me more
If I escape from my mind-jail?
"My child, infinitely more!"

16440

From now on,
Silence-peace
Will be my only
Conversation-partner.

16441

I am sure that
Right behind my life's teeming clouds
God's Compassion-Forgiveness-Sun
Shines.

16442

My Lord,
Your blessingful Nectar-Invitation
Even my unsearching mind
Loves.

16443

Your insincerity-mind
And your ingratitude-heart
Enjoy collusion.

16444

O my mind, delay not!
Sink, sink
Your doubt-arrogance-submarine!

16445

Because of your sleepless
Self-giving,
God Himself is striking
Your life's transformation-gong.

16446

My Lord Supreme,
Once, only once,
Will You think of me —
I mean
My poor and hopeless heart?

16447

You are
Your fountain-gratitude-heart.
Therefore,
God has come to you
With His Mountain-Satisfaction-Life.

16448

No life-surrender-substitute
Is accepted
By the Absolute Supreme.

16449

Is there any seeker
Who has not become
Part and parcel
Of the rising and falling
Of aspiration-river?

16450

Alas!
How many years
Do I have to live
On the fruitless shore
Of my God-forgotten dreams?

16451

Willingness, cheerfulness
And soulfulness
Are three inseparable
And unparalleled God-lovers.

16452

No human life
Can liberate itself
From responsibility's claims.

16453

Because
You are a true God-lover,
You are able to enjoy
Your heart's beautiful
Aspiration-flower
And your life's ripe
Dedication-fruits.

16454

A self-giving life
Does eventually reap
A huge happiness-harvest.

16455

Each promise-moment
Is the beginning
Of a new creation.

16456

O my mind,
When are you going to complete
Your higher purity-course?

16457

Today I shall strengthen
My heart's
Aspiration-cry-flames
And I shall weaken
My mind's
Unwillingness-resistance-frowns.

16458

Breathe, breathe,
Breathlessly breathe
God's Compassion-Heart-Fragrance.

16459

An unconditional life
Is the living story
Of God.

16460

My sleepless gratitude-heart
Is a first-class passenger
On God's Satisfaction-Boat.

16461

Each human mind
Is full of
Unreality-questions.

Each divine heart
Is full of
Reality-answers.

16462

I have commanded my mind
To change,
And it has devotedly
Obeyed me.

16463

My Lord, do tell me,
Where is Your hiding place?

"My child,
My hiding place is inside
Your unlit mind's thought-cave."

16464

How can anybody
Make any progress
If he lives in the land
Of mere chance?

16465

To live in thought-squalor
Is not an uncommon experience
For the human mind.

16466

O my doubting mind,
Do not misunderstand
My all-loving heart!

16467

If you yourself
Do not give serious thought
To your own spiritual life,
Why should anybody else
Do it for you?

16468

O my heart's aspiration-cry,
You are my only
Trustworthy friend.

16469

My heart
Lovingly and compassionately
Teaches my mind
How to read
The God-manifestation-map.

16470

Temptation-maker
Is caught.
Temptation-shooter
Is sought.

16471

A purity-heart
Is indeed
One of the supreme trophies
Of life.

16472

No matter how useless
A human life is,
It cannot exhaust
God's infinite Compassion.

16473

Of all my soulful gifts,
My heart's gratitude-garland
God likes most.

16474

There is no life
Without a sigh.
There is no heart
Without a cry.

16475

His eyes never forget
To show his heart's smile
To humanity.

16476

What is making me
So happy?
I am clearly seeing
My fading doubt's failure.

16477

A supremely
Peace-flooded oneness-heart
Knows no limits.

16478

If you are unrealistic,
Then the frustration-train
Will arrive at your mind-station
Much sooner.

16479

Each human being
Has to run
A compulsory ultramarathon
To arrive at perfection-truth.

16480

Alas,
How is it that
We human beings
Are not tired
Of a non-stop
Circus-life-performance?

16481

God's deadline:
Either today or any day!
Man's deadline:
Now or never!

16482

O my mind,
How and why do you enjoy
The same old failure-stories?

16483

Two things
I need at every moment:
A sleepless aspiration-life
And a breathless meditation-heart.

16484

O my mind,
When will you learn
That each good thought
Is a delight-spreading
Rainbow?

16485

Today I have liberated
My mind
From the world's
Comparison-prison.

16486

Good news
For all God-lovers:
God's Compassion-Fountain-Eye
Is fast approaching us.

16487

Weakness,
Why do you appear and reappear
So frequently
In my aspiration-life?

16488

Ignorance,
You have offended me
In so many ways!
But I shall offend you
In a special way.
I shall completely
Transform you.

16489

At last
Everybody in my family —
Soul, heart, mind,
Vital and body —
Has become a hero-climber
Of aspiration-mountain.

16490

How can I have
Admiration for you,
When you are just
A spiritual tourist?

16491

If you insist
On your own way,
Then God's Eye will sit
On the Judgement-Seat.

16492

If you breathe world-sympathy,
Then God will breathe
His own Heart-Pride
In you.

16493

A soft, sweet whisper
From within
Stirs my entire outer being.

16494

Nobody can fell
And nobody will ruin
My life's huge hope-tree.

16495

My seeker-friend,
Since when have you
Stopped drinking
Your life's darkness-poison?

16496

Your God-realisation-
Plane ticket
Can be purchased only by
Your sleepless surrender-guide.

16497

God wants me to run
On His Heart-Pride-Road
And not
On His Compassion-Eye-Road.

16498

God's Justice-Light-Bank
Never gives loans
To anybody.

16499

Alas,
It is so difficult
To escape from
Insecurity-mosquito bites!

16500

I ask God for many favours.
But God asks me
For only one favour:
"Claim Me, My child,
Just claim Me!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 165, Agni Press, 1992
