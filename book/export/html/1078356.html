709 Three Visions Of Christ...

THREE VISIONS OF CHRIST

In his Humility
I saw Father Joseph's little son.

In his Compassion
I saw Mother Mary's big son.

In his Perfection
I saw his Heaven-Father's Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
