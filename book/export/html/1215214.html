17.

My life's immense love
And
My heart's intense gratitude
Are inseparable friends.

"Your patience and your compassion will overcome all the odds of the world. They will all be illumined by your infinite patience and infinite compassion. This is my most sincere feeling."

17 March 1992

From: Sri Chinmoy, Vladimir Petrovsky: builder of a new heart-firmament, Agni Press, 1998
