12 December 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
May Thy supreme Victory be proclaimed
Even by my idle body, my restless vital,
My stupid mind and my weak heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Hawaii. 12 December 1998, 5:16 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. fifty times with two arms and one-hundred and eighteen times with one arm. (59 right, 59 left in six sets)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
