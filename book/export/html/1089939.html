Supreme

Supreme Father,
In Your Immortality's
Shelter-Grace,
I shall one day see
Your supreme Reality-Face.
And then I shall unconditionally be
What You have been
Sempiternally dreaming through me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Light-delight-journeys, Agni Press, 1974
