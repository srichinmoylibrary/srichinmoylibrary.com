He who loves God
In the aspiration-world
Has no responsibility of his own.
The Compassion of God
Does everything for him.

He who loves ignorance
In the desire-world
Has also no responsibility of his own.
The temptation of ignorance
Does everything for him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 21, Agni Press, 1984
