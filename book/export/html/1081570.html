11. Definitions

Definition of the soul:
God-manifestation.

Definition of the heart:
God-recognition.

Definition of the mind:
God-examination.

Definition of the vital:
God-separation.

Definition of the body:
God-frustration.

My definition of God:
Self-congratulation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
