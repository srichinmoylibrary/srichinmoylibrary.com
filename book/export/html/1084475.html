41. Who?

Who applauds you?
He who longs to imitate you.
Who stones you?
He who knows
That you are far beyond his imitation.
Who loves you?
He who wants to know you.
Who hates you?
He who wants you
To be ignorant of him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 10, Agni Press, 1974
