3 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Insecurity disappears
The moment the heart-singer
Starts singing only God-songs.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 July 2000, 8:59 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift four times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
