394. What is sacrifice?

Duty's beauty
God alone admires.
Beauty's purity
God alone admires.
Purity's sacrifice
God alone admires.
And what is sacrifice?
When the right hand
Devotedly gives
And the left hand
Devotedly receives.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 8, Agni Press, 1974
