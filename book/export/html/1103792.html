21 September 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
To my heart's greatest joy
And my life's greatest relief,
My doubting mind
Has completely surrendered
To my aspiring heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 September 1999, 9:32 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-five times. (33 right, 32 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
