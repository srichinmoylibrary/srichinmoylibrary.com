6448

In the outer life
You make great sacrifices
To build a perfect temple.
Similarly, in the inner life
You can offer daily
Your inspiration, aspiration and dedication
To make your body
A perfect temple of the Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 65, Agni Press, 1984
