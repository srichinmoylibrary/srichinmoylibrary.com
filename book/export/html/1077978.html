331. Which side to take

Night warns us
Of light's exaggerated claims
And
Impossible dreams.

Light reminds us
Of night's ignorance-poison
And
Destruction-prison.

Alas,
Our unawakened life
Knows not which side to take.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
