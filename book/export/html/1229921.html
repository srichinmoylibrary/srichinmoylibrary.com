Believe It or Not, I Have Seen God

Believe it or not,
I have seen God, the real God,
Inside you, inside your purity-body.
Believe it or not,
I have seen God, the real God,
Inside you, inside your beauty-eye.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Silence, please! God is coming, , 1999
