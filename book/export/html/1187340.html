1688

You have asked your mind
To drive your life-car.
Quite often the car
Is out of control.
Why do you have to blame
God the Driver,
Whom you have not invited
To drive your life-car?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 17, Agni Press, 1983
