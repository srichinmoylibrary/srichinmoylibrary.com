365 I Am Crying

My body is sleeping.
Therefore
I am crying.

My vital is fighting.
Therefore
I am crying.

My mind is doubting.
Therefore
I am crying.

My heart is hesitating.
Therefore
I am crying.

The following ten poems were written during a car ride from Madras to Pondicherry on 29 October 1979.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 4, Agni Press, 1979
