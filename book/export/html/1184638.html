310. Such is hope

Such is hope:
It tells us what to do,
But it does not
Teach us how to do it.

Such is hope:
It tells us that God is appearing,
But alas, who appears?
Frustration-monster.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
