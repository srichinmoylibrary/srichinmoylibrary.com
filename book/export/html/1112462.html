33. Detachment-songs

In the morning,
My life and my heart together sing
Detachment-songs
From world-castigation.
In the evening,
My life and my heart together sing
Detachment-songs
From world-ovation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sail my heartbeat sail, part 1, Agni Press, 1998
