1 June 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
For me, today is the day
Of my unconditional surrender
To Your express Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 June 2000, 7:14 p.m. Before doing standing double-dumbbell lift with 1,300 lbs. four times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
