1880 Look Beyond Appearances

Do not allow circumstances
To frighten you.
Do not allow situations
To torture you.
Look beyond appearances.
Yours will be
The unmistakable happiness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 19, Agni Press, 1981
