40. You feel, you hear, you see

You feel.
What do you feel?
You feel God's Presence.
You hear.
What do you hear?
You hear God's Voice.
You see.
What do you see?
You see God's Face.
When you feel,
God is pleased with you.
When you hear,
God is proud of you.
When you see,
God is ready to garland you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 10, Agni Press, 1974
