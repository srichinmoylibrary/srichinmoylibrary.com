354. Teeming spots

Even the world-illumining sun
Has teeming spots.
Why do you worry
About my life's blackspots,
Mother Earth?

Son, I want you to be
As perfect as Eternity's
Father-sun
And not the time-born
Planet-sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
