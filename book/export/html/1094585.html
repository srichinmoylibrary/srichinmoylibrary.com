My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 12

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Cambodia and Viet Nam in January 2002.

2 January 2002 ...

2 January 2002

Phnom Penh, Cambodia

1 I Know And I Feel ...

I know and I feel
That my Lord Beloved Supreme
Has an everlasting Love
For me.

2 God Does Not Hide Anything ...

God does not hide anything
From me.
Because of my blind eyes,
I do not see
What He does for me
Self-givingly and sleeplessly.

3 God Blesses Me ...

God blesses me
Out of the Bounties of His Heart.
Alas, where is my receptivity-heart,
Where?

4 If We Want To Walk ...

If we want to walk
Every day
In the newness of life,
Then we must have enthusiasm
In our mind
In abundant measure.

5 Every Morning And Evening ...

Every morning and evening
My Lord tells me
Only one thing:
Not to do,
But to be.

6 God Expects ...

God expects
The deepest affection-portion
From my aspiration-heart,
And no,
Never some random portion.

7 The Days Are Short ...

The days are short.
My desires are endless.
O climbing aspiration-flames,
Where are you?
In your absence
I am helpless,
I am hopeless,
I am useless.

3 January 2002 ...

3 January 2002

Phnom Penh, Cambodia

8 I Search ...

I search
For my inner food:
Life divine.

9 An Insecurity-Mind-Plant ...

An insecurity-mind-plant
Rapidly grows.

10 An Impurity-Life-River ...

An impurity-life-river
Dauntlessly flows.

11 God-Manifestation On Earth ...

God-manifestation on earth
Entirely depends
On humanity’s genuine love
For God.

12 A Faultless Heart ...

A faultless heart
Is possible,
But not
A faultless life.

13 The Human In Us ...

The human in us
Sleeplessly needs
Self-mastery.

14 The Divine In Us ...

The divine in us
Breathlessly needs
God-discovery.

15 How Can God Tame ...

How can God tame
My unruly earth-existence-life
If I every day blame God
Consciously and deliberately?

16 My Life Needs Nothing ...

My life needs nothing
From the future.
It needs everything
From the immediacy of today.

17 Every Day, Without Fail ...

Every day, without fail,
My Lord Himself breathes
My gratitude-heart-tear-breath.

18 Desire-Journey Ends ...

Desire-journey ends
In the land of nowhere.

19 Not To Entertain ...

Not to entertain
My mind, my vital and my body,
But to enlighten my life
I came into the world.

20 The Beauty Of My Prayer ...

The beauty of my prayer
And the fragrance of my meditation
Are carrying me
To my Lord’s Heart-Home.

21 My Heart’s ...

My heart’s
Devotion-temple
Is my life’s
Pole-star.

22 Look Forward ...

Look forward,
My life!
Never, never look back
At the sad failures
Of the past.

4 January 2002 ...

4 January 2002

Phnom Penh, Cambodia

23 I Must Hold Myself ...

I must hold myself
To the absolutely highest
God-satisfaction-standard.

24 I Must Brighten My Mind ...

I must brighten my mind,
Deepen my heart
And strengthen my life.

25 Be Fully Prepared! ...

Be fully prepared!
Lo and behold,
The unparalleled opportunity-train
Is come.

26 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That God-Satisfaction
Is my birthright.
I have only to claim it,
The sooner the better.

27 I Must Find My Inner Road

I must find my inner road
Myself,
Or I must beg God
To make one for me.

28 Every Divinely Blooming Thought ...

Every divinely blooming thought
Becomes
A supremely blossoming experience.

29 My Prayers And Meditations ...

My prayers and meditations
Are answered
Not when I am begging God,
But when I am loving
And serving God.

30 God’s Protection-Shelter-Home ...

God’s Protection-Shelter-Home
Is for all human beings,
Whether they are aspiring
Or desiring.

31 I Unmistakably See ...

I unmistakably see
That my desire-mind-cloud-sky
Is clearing.

32 May My Gratitude-Heart ...

May my gratitude-heart
Expand and expand
In every direction.

33 When My Meditation ...

When my meditation
Is very deep,
I breathe in perfect rhythm
With my Beloved Lord Supreme.

34 He Who Has Never Visited Hell ...

He who has never visited hell
Will never be able
To appreciate, admire,
Adore and love Heaven
Most adequately.

35 Only The Helpless Tears ...

Only the helpless tears
Of my heart
Are given the chance
By my Lord Supreme
To take a short-cut
To His Heart-Home.

5 January 2002 ...

5 January 2002

Phnom Penh, Cambodia

36 Every Morning ...

Every morning
The very first thing I do
Is to offer my gratitude-heart-tears
To my Lord Beloved Supreme.

37 A God-Surrender-Life Means ...

A God-surrender-life means
A life of ever-blossoming
God-Delight.

38 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That He will make me
A blue-gold star
In His Heart-Sky
If I meditate soulfully
And self-givingly.

39 Yesterday My Lord Gave Me ...

Yesterday my Lord gave me
The lightning speed of inspiration.
Today my Lord gives me
The lightning speed of aspiration.
Tomorrow my Lord will give me
The lightning speed of self-offering.

40 God comes to my surrender-angel ...

God comes to my surrender-angel
Inside my heart
On tiptoe
With His infinite Joy
And infinite Pride.

41 Every Day Our Inner Faith ...

Every day our inner faith
Needs newness-joy
To grow into
The Fulness of God.

42 Every Morning My Heart And I ...

Every morning my heart and I
Hoist our Supreme Lord’s
Victory-Flag.

6 January 2002 ...

6 January 2002

Phnom Penh, Cambodia

43 There Is Nothing ...

There is nothing
That a hope-heart
Cannot achieve.

44 Alas ...

Alas,
I fool myself miserably
By thinking that I know
Who I truly am.

45 I Must Replace The Pleasure-Life ...

I must replace the pleasure-life
Of my outer world
With the heart-joy
Of my inner world.

46 God Proudly Smiles At My Mind ...

God proudly smiles at my mind
When He sees that it has
Even an iota of peace.

47 If I Really Want Peace ...

If I really want peace
In my life,
Then I must not busy myself
With what others do and say.

48 To Be Perfect In My Outer Life ...

To be perfect in my outer life,
I must harmoniously live
With my inner
God-aspiration-heart.

49 A Famous Life ...

A famous life
Is no match
For a God-Pride-heart
And a God-faithfulness-life.

50 To See My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

To see my Lord Beloved Supreme
Smiling,
I am ready to sacrifice everything
In my life.

51 I Must Not Postpone ...

I must not postpone
Even for a single day
My unconditional surrender
To God’s Will.

52 Not What The Mind Says ...

Not what the mind says,
But what the heart feels
I must immediately believe
And accept.

53 My Heart’s Soulful ...

My heart’s soulful
Devotion-skill
Is my life’s momentous
Enthusiasm-thrill.

54 A Devotion-Heart ...

A devotion-heart
That has intensity
Is of paramount importance.

55 A Doubting Mind ...

A doubting mind
Can influence other minds,
But never an aspiring heart.

56 Every Morning I Receive ...

Every morning I receive
My Lord’s Blessing-Rain
With my gratitude-heart-tears.

9 January 2002 ...

9 January 2002

Ho Chi Minh City, Viet Nam

57 My Heart-Breath Descends ...

My heart-breath descends
From my Lord’s
Transcendental Delight.

58 I Must Multiply ...

I must multiply
My heart’s sweetness
And my life’s oneness.

59 When The Hour Of God Strikes ...

When the Hour of God strikes,
Do not delay!
Just run your fastest
To your final Destination.

60 Every Morning ...

Every morning
Prayerfully, soulfully and blissfully
I fly on the wings
Of my God-love.

61 Do Not Mind ...

Do not mind
If you fall.
Only allow God
To lift you up again.
He is all ready
To do you this big favour.

62 In The Marketplace Of Life ...

In the marketplace of life,
Nothing is essential
And nothing is permanent.

63 Sweet Is God’s ...

Sweet is God’s
Compassion-Eye.

64 Sweeter Are God’s ...

Sweeter are God’s
Affection-Arms.

65 Sweetest Is God’s ...

Sweetest is God’s
Fondness-Heart.

66 The Mind Gives Millions Of Reasons ...

The mind gives millions of reasons
Why it does not want to accept God
As its Creator.

67 The Heart Says ...

The heart says,
“I cannot live without God
Even for a fleeting moment.
No reason-snare
Can ever catch me.”

68 I Need ...

I need
An ever-progressing
God-gratitude-life.

69 A God-Eagerness-Heart ...

A God-eagerness-heart
Feeds on only
Now.

10 January 2002 ...

10 January 2002

Ho Chi Minh City, Viet Nam

70 My Lord Says To Me ...

My Lord says to me,
“My child,
I am infinitely more interested
In your aspiring heart
Than in your life’s
Mighty achievements.”

71 I Desperately And Sleeplessly Need ...

I desperately and sleeplessly need
Enthusiasm-thrill
In my dry mind.

72 God Says To Me ...

God says to me,
“My child,
I want to reverse the game.
My only desire is this:
From now on,
You will be indulgent to Me
And not to yourself.”

73 In Each Century ...

In each century,
God-realised souls
Can be counted
On the fingertips.

74 To Smile At God ...

To smile at God
Is a seeker’s
Very first requirement.

11 January 2002 ...

11 January 2002

Ho Chi Minh City, Viet Nam

75 I Dearly Love ...

I dearly love
My God-adoration
And
My God-manifestation-convictions.

76 Do Not Enjoy Your Friendship ...

Do not enjoy your friendship
With your unaspiring mind.
You will be a dejected
And rejected God-devotee.

77 When We Please God ...

When we please God
In His own Way,
He accepts us as true members
Of His inner Family.

78 Aspiration ...

Aspiration
Is the transcendence-assurance
Of our previous achievements.

79 God-Criticism-Acceptance ...

God-criticism-acceptance
In our spiritual life —
Not only unacceptable,
But also unpardonable.

80 God The Forgiveness-Heart ...

God the Forgiveness-Heart
And
God the Compassion-Eye
Pick us up
After each fall.

81 To Protect ...

To protect
His supremely chosen children,
God is God’s
Round-the-clock Concern.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 12, Agni Press, 2002
