Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 225

22401

Only one thing
Keeps my heart alive:
My God-oneness-hope.

22402

Alas, I do not know
Why my gratitude-heart
Begins to die
The moment it is born.

22403

God's frustration
Once I was.
God's Satisfaction
Now I am.

22404

My heart's purity-breath
God treasures
Beyond measure.

22405

Because of my intense
Aspiration-heart-fire,
My desire-life will
Before long expire.

22406

A life of desire
Is always accompanied
By the torture of fear.

22407

O my God-faith,
Beauty-flooded is your heart;
Purity-flooded is your breath.

22408

Only two things
A God-lover needs:
His nature's perfection
And his love for God-Satisfaction.

22409

The mind derives joy
From receiving something special.
The heart derives joy
From becoming something special.
The soul derives joy
Neither from receiving nor from becoming,
But from pleasing God
In God's own Way.

22410

Is there anybody who does not desire
To arrive at the destination
Long before God's choice Hour?

22411

In the battlefield of life
There is only one hero,
And that hero is
The self-offering-hero.

22412

My self-transcendence-song
Is the only treasure
That my Lord Supreme cherishes
And will forever cherish.

22413

Alas,
Will there ever be a time
When my life's lion-heart
Will roar and roar
For the full manifestation
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme?

22414

How can we ever expect
To arrive on time
At our inner destination
When we use our mind-vehicle
To take us on the rush-hour-road?

22415

Be a self-giver.
Everybody will follow
In your all-illumining footsteps.

22416

Nothing divine
Can either be achieved
Or purchased by anxiety.

22417

God's Cry awakens
The world-peace-dreamers.
God's Smile feeds
The world-peace-lovers.

22418

I am happy
When I do something good.
I am infinitely happier
When God's Compassion-Rain
From God's Heaven
Descends upon me.

22419

An adamantine will
Is a sure cure
For jealousy-meanness-life.

22420

True, God needs my pure heart,
But He needs my self-giving life
Infinitely more
To feed His Compassion-Eye.

22421

My Lord,
Do tell me if and when
My backward-running race
Is going to end.

22422

God's Compassion-Eye
Every day swims
In the tearful sea of my heart.

22423

My happiness is beyond measure
When I see God's smiling Heart
In God's Compassion-Eye.

22424

Each kind thought
Is a direct representative
Of God
To illumine the ignorance
Of the world.

22425

Perfection on earthly soil
Is not possible today,
But tomorrow it will be inevitable.

22426

My mind has no solace for me.
My mind does not even know
What solace is.
My heart not only knows
What solace is,
But also has solace for me
In measureless measure.

22427

O my earthly life,
Why are you such a fool?
Why do you still long for more possessions?
Can you not see
That you are already ruthlessly possessed
By possession itself?

22428

If you are a God-seeker,
Then you automatically become
A man-inspirer.

22429

Because of my heart's climbing
Aspiration-flames,
I am no longer anchored
By my old desire-life-chains.

22430

I call it
My heart's aspiration.
God calls it
His own Experience-Adventure.

22431

O my mind,
Start aspiring immediately
Before you are disgraced
In the world's eye.

22432

If you are a heart-citizen,
Your eyes will feed
The God-hunger-cry
Of the world.

22433

A heart that is
Completely dedicated to God
Is bound to feed God the man
And man the God.

22434

The God-touch
Can be either very gentle
Or very powerful,
As necessity demands.

22435

When I am completely alone
With God,
I feel that I am nothing
But a speck of dust,
Meaningless, worthless and useless.

22436

God does not want me to be
My mind's cave-dweller.
He wants me to be
My heart's palace-enjoyer.

22437

If you are a true seeker,
Then you must not participate
In unhealthy and barren
Intellect-tournaments.

22438

My wise head tells me
How I can be generous
In God's Eye.
My warm heart tells me
How I can be precious
In God's Heart.

22439

When I lose my gratitude-heart,
Whatever I say about God
Is stupid, ridiculous and absurd.

22440

My mind-doubts
Are small and ineffective storms
Inside my soul-eternity's still and calm,
Eternal and infinite peace-sea.

22441

God forgives me
To awaken my heart.
God blesses me
To fulfil my life.

22442

O my mind's insincerity,
Will you ever realise
How ruthlessly you torture
My Lord's Compassion-Eye-Sun?

22443

No soaring enthusiasm
In my heart,
No Satisfaction-Eye
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

22444

I cannot easily believe
That I will soon become
A choice instrument
Of my Lord Supreme.

22445

If my life does not live
In my heart-nest,
My Lord will have no chance
To rest.

22446

Inspiration-flames
I was.
Aspiration-fire
I now am.
Realisation-sun
I shall become.

22447

My Lord has left it
In humanity's hands
To build
The Kingdom of Heaven
On earth.

22448

It is not difficult for me
To tell the world
How undivine I am.

22449

The idleness of the mind
And the timidity of the heart
Are always found together.

22450

Alas,
My Lord is so little understood
In this world,
Yet I do nothing
To illumine the mind of the world
Or to feed the heart of the world.

22451

Peace
Is neither my new home
Nor my old home.
Peace
Is my only home.

22452

Do not try to make
The future visible.
Try to make the present
Not only as visible,
But also as useful as possible.

22453

My Lord of Compassion tells me
That my nature's perfection
Is now not too far off.

22454

Whenever I live in the mind,
I am forced to pass through
The depths of ignorance-night.

22455

The desire-life is the life
That knows
What thirst is,
But knows not
How to quench it.

22456

God starts laughing at me
Even long before I admit
That I am hopelessly useless.

22457

If ever you do something
Good for humanity,
You will be thanked
By God Himself.

22458

Only because of
God's unconditional Forgiveness-Light
Am I able to see Him,
Not for any other reason.

22459

May the eyes of my heart
See my Lord Supreme
In the very near future.

22460

O seeker,
If patience is not
One of your strong points in life,
You will never succeed.

22461

O seeker, remember
You are not
Your mind's stupidity,
And you are not
Your heart's timidity.

22462

We pride ourselves
On our outer success.
God prides Himself
On our inner progress.

22463

Like God,
I shall never refuse anybody
As a friend of my heart.

22464

Compassion, not compromise,
Can solve the teeming problems
Of human life.

22465

My smiling heart
Is the summit of my life's
Progress-mountain.

22466

Volcano-ego
And mountain-sovereignty
Can never part.

22467

I get infinitely more joy
From my aspiration-heart
Than from my satisfaction-life.

22468

I need only one thing:
A heart that is always ready
To command my mind.

22469

When I sing
With my heart's rising moon,
I am eternally happy.
When I dance
With my soul's rising sun,
I am totally fulfilled.

22470

God can be caught
Anywhere He is sought
By your heart-tears.

22471

To be a partner of the mind
Is to sigh.
To be a partner of the heart
Is to fly.

22472

The heart's ecstasy
Comes from
God's Oneness-Intimacy.

22473

God reveals Himself
Through my heart-tears.
God fulfils Himself
Through my soul-smiles.

22474

Your mind's narrow road
Is wide enough
For doubt and jealousy
To walk along.

22475

Conviction tells me
What I can eventually become.
Realisation tells me
What I eternally am.

22476

The beauty of my heart
And the fragrance of my soul
Always fly together.

22477

I do not know how powerful
The mind is,
But I do know how powerful
The fear of the mind is
For me.

22478

An unwillingness-mind
Will eventually have to fight
Against immense dangers.

22479

Nothing
Can always remain the same â€”
Alas,
Not even my intense love
For this earth-planet.

22480

Each time I have a new hope,
A new and illumining world
Lovingly welcomes me.

22481

O my mind,
Pray and pray to God,
So that you can escape
Ignorance-mind-impurity.

22482

What my searching mind
Propounds
My crying heart
Expounds.

22483

The attachment of the vital
Cannot escape from
Eventually sobbing and moaning.

22484

As long as God's Compassion
Is in charge,
Our stupidity will not meet
With its well-deserved
God-Justice-Light.

22485

Although our aspiration is ancient,
It can remain untouched
By earth-dangers.

22486

O ingratitude-mind,
It is high time
For you to leave the world
For good.

22487

When I am in a rage,
I find myself in my mind's
Inescapable cage.

22488

As an artist delights
In painting,
A seeker must delight
In aspiring.

22489

O sincerity-heart,
Where are you?
O stupidity-mind,
Where are you not?

22490

How fruitful
My silence-heart is.
How hurtful
My sound-mind is.

22491

How I wish I could enjoy
My God-manifestation-reality
The way I enjoy
My God-aspiration-dream.

22492

O my mind,
The time has come
To stop your wilderness-expedition
And begin your stillness-journey.

22493

The human in me shamelessly wants
To dominate the world.
The divine in me lovingly
And self-givingly wants
To liberate the world.

22494

It is the doubt-clouds
Of my mind
That want to separate me
From my sunshine-heart.

22495

No matter what I do,
No matter how hard I try,
I cannot run away
From the battlefield of despair.

22496

I ask God why
I am so undivine and unspiritual.
The Compassion-Eye of my Lord
Refuses to give an immediate answer.

22497

I fail
When I am not
The urgency of my mind.
I fail
When I am not
The intensity of my heart.

22498

For tilling the aspiration-ground,
What I need is a searching mind
And a self-giving heart.

22499

Not the doubting mind
But the faithful heart
Is always ready
For changes and improvements.

22500

O my Lord,
Do take from me every day
What I have for You
And what I am to You.
What I have
Is a heart of love,
And what I am
Is a soul of gratitude.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 225, Agni Press, 1995
