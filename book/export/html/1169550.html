195. Who is there?

Who is there?
Man?
Stay where you are.

Who is there?
God?
Do come where I am.

Who is there?
My desire-friend?
Go back. Go back,
To your dingy cage.

Who is there?
My aspiration-flame?
Come back. Come back,
We want you to learn
From Immortality's Infinity-page.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
