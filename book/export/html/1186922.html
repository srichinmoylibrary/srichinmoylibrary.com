1274

You want to know
What God's Compassion-Breath
Is doing for me.
I want to tell you
That His Compassion-Breath
Is every day, every hour saving me
From my spiritual death.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 13, Agni Press, 1983
