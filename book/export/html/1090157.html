23. Selflessness

I had a dream.
In my dream
I became selflessness.
My dream disappeared
     And
I became reality-story.
In my reality-story
I discovered myself
As the perfect perfection of
     Earth-possessiveness,
     Heaven-possessiveness,
     God-possessiveness,
           And
     Self-possessiveness.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Lord, receive this little undying cry, Agni Press, 1975
