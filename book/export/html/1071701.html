January 11

The renaissance of our body is the offering of our life's capacity to the world.
The renaissance of our vital is the fulfilling marriage of love and power.
The renaissance of our mind is the inner sincerity that cries to see the Face of Truth.
The renaissance of our heart is the constant devotedness to the Pilot Supreme.
The renaissance of our soul is the Breath of Paradise that grants us an Immortal Life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
