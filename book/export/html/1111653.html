The two rivals

X and I are two rivals. God is not satisfied with punishing me alone; He has to punish X also. I can't run; God has blessed me. Now I see that X can't run either. Usually one rival wins. In our case God has demolished both of us. For the last few weeks neither one of us has been running.

One day we shall be able to fight against our fate. This year in the New York Marathon we have to do well.
