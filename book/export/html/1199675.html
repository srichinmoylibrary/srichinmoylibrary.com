Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 140

13901

Let me do something
Absolutely new today:
I shall unconditionally fulfil God,
If even for a fleeting second.

13902

Hope shows the way.
Promise shortens the way.
Aspiration reaches the destination.

13903

If you hold fast
To your ideals,
God will definitely expedite
Your Heavenward journey.

13904

Humanity's perfection-dream
Comes from
The universal soul within.

13905

Aspiration can cover
The weaknesses of your heart
And also the imperfections
Of your mind.

13906

My life-prayer is
For humanity's God-realisation.
My heart-meditation is
For humanity's God-manifestation.

13907

O seeker,
What you need is dynamism.
O seeker,
What you need is radiance.
If you have both dynamism and radiance,
Then you can escape
Many impediments in life.

13908

A couple of minutes
Is more than enough
To intensify your heart's
Aspiration-cry
And multiply your life's
Dedication-smile.

13909

You do not need anything else
Save and except your inner faith
To navigate your way
To the Golden Shore.

13910

Fate never wrongs you.
It is you who do wrong things
To your fate.

13911

Gratitude-song is the only song
That the angels sing
At every minute
For the Absolute Lord Beloved Supreme.

13912

To fulfil
Your Eternity's dream
You need
Your Infinity's courage.

13913

God, I do need
Your Justice-Light.
But I need Your Compassion-Height
Infinitely more
To quench my sleepless thirst
For You.

13914

Uncertainty, insecurity and impurity
Decrease
As the aspiration-flames increase
In a seeker's life.

13915

God is all ready to shower
His choicest Compassion-Blessings
On you.
Why are you delaying
In coming to Him
With your weary heart?

13916

A twinkle in God's divine Eye
Can illumine your darker, darkest
Earth-journey
So you can reach
Heaven's immortal Satisfaction-Smile.

13917

My Lord Supreme,
The sweetness and fulness
Of Your divine Eye
Are illumining my long-treasured
Doubting mind.

13918

God does not smash our dreams,
For God Himself has created our dreams
To be the foundation-stones
Of our Himalayan realities.

13919

In the spiritual life
How can you be cruel
If you have a prayerful heart
And a soulful life
As your self-giving friends?

13920

My sweet Lord Supreme,
Save me, save me, save me
From stabbing doubt-agonies!

13921

My heart fondly tells me
To do the right thing.
My mind forcefully compels me
To be the wrong thing.

13922

When I walk along
The sincerity-road,
I become my life's
Capacity-sky.

13923

Every day
My Lord Supreme sits
At the foot of His own
Compassion-Tree,
And I sit
At the foot of my own
Gratitude-tree.

13924

Only the willingness-boat
Carries us
To the fulness-shore.

13925

Mine is the daily morning duty
To invoke
A devotion-prayer-life.

13926

Why are you so sure
That your mind-sound
Will never lose
To your heart-silence?

13927

My greatest gift
To humanity
Is my purity-light-fragrance.

13928

If you are a genuine seeker,
Then you know that
Your prayer-aspirations
Will be fulfilled in God's own Way.

13929

You can lose everything
If you have to.
But do not allow yourself to lose
One thing:
Your hope-heart-boat.

13930

My Supreme Lord,
You do not have to explain,
For I shall no more complain.

13931

When I go even one step beyond
My ego-mind,
God tells me
That my life is perfect.

13932

My Lord Supreme
Asks me only to transmit His
Forgiveness-Message-Light.

13933

My life's only serious mistake
Is that every day
My stupidity prevents me
From receiving Light
From God's Compassion-Eye.

13934

God wants me to
Take Him soulfully
And take my own life seriously
At every moment.

13935

My silence in action
Means my life is travelling
On the perfection-road.

13936

What I have
Is a daring mind,
And what I am
Is a weeping heart.

13937

My aspiration-heart,
Cry, cry!
I want to see you crying.
My dedication-life,
Smile, smile!
I want to see you smiling.

13938

God's Compassion-Smile
Is my life's only
Secret and sacred collaborator.

13939

Readiness runs,
Oneness flies,
Fulness dives.

13940

What today I call
My heart's devotion-plant,
Tomorrow I shall call that very thing
My life's perfection-tree.

13941

It is you who have chosen
Your misery-life.
What can poor God do
To save you or help you?

13942

My oneness-heart
Needs only God-lovers.
My supremacy-mind
Wants for itself
Not only admirers
But also adorers.

13943

If there is no purity
In your heart,
That means your spirituality-train
Is off the track.

13944

Devotion-offering
Is my soulful task.
Compassion-showering
Is my Lord's bountiful Task.

13945

Man has only a short-term
Aspiration-cry,
But he wants to have
A very long-term
God-Satisfaction-Smile.

13946

Although the human mind
Is hard of hearing,
God will someday, somehow
Sing His blessingful Faith-Songs
Through the human mind.

13947

What I need every day
Is a shuttle service
Between my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life.

13948

An abiding satisfaction
Blossoms
Even from a brief glimpse
Of God.

13949

O human mind,
I have always known and felt
That you are an orphan
Of divinity.

13950

For your disobedience-dart
God may forgive you,
But your soul will not —
Never!

13951

I must ask my mind
And all its problems
To evacuate my aspiration-heart.

13952

O my mind, come, come!
Come and see God.
Regardless of your past,
God is allowing you
To come and see Him.

13953

Every day in a very special way
My heart is cradled
By the silver light-moon.

13954

Only your soul-lot
Is safe.
Therefore, there alone
You can park
All your mind-problems.

13955

Listen, listen, O my mind!
God is singing
His Oneness-Satisfaction-Song.

13956

May my vision-eye
Self-givingly enjoy
Heaven's beauty and earth's purity.

13957

God's Compassion-Light
Always loses something precious
In translation.

13958

A seeker's life is a life
Of conscious and infinite
Possibilities.

13959

Meditate on God sleeplessly!
You will see that God's Compassion-Eye
Is not even a heartbeat away.

13960

O my stupid mind,
I am giving you
One more chance.
This time choose the right thing:
Faith, purity-faith.

13961

Without making
Unconditional surrender
One cannot study
God's complete Satisfaction-Subject.

13962

Only a child
Of purity-light-dawn
Is allowed to speak to God
Every morning.

13963

Behave yourself, O my mind!
Otherwise, God the Compassion-Height
May not like you,
And God the Justice-Light
May punish you.

13964

O my mind,
Your illusion-smoke
You mistake
For your aspiration-fire.

13965

My surrender is my passport
To Heaven.
My gratitude is my free access
To my Lord Supreme.

13966

In the deep silence-delight
Of your aspiration-heart
No temptation-tornado
Can torture you.

13967

At times I wonder
Why my heart has taken the risk
Of accepting my ungrateful mind.

13968

May my life live between
My gratitude-tears
And my surrender-smiles.

13969

One can always get a clear view
Of the satisfaction-shore
From the top of the aspiration-mountain.

13970

My Lord, I have seen You perform
Your disappearing tricks.
Please show me
Your reappearing tricks.

13971

My outer pain teaches me
How to be pure.
My inner pain teaches me
How to be perfect.

13972

I am indeed happy today
Because I have surrendered
My own feelings
To my Lord's all-fulfilling Will
Unconditionally.

13973

Yesterday I dreamt of aspiration.
Today I am dreaming of perfection.
Tomorrow how I wish I could dream
Of God-manifestation.

13974

If you want God to be responsible
For what you say,
Then beg God at every moment
To claim you as His very own.

13975

Soulfully, sleeplessly and breathlessly
I wish to be God-directed
In everything that I do
From now on.

13976

O my mind,
For so many years I made you happy
By allowing you to lead me.
Can you not now make me happy
By following me?

13977

Possessiveness and fruitlessness
Together live
And together die
In our mind-jungles.

13978

I do not have to lose my own divinity
To awaken others' sleeping hearts
And silence others' doubting minds.

13979

Do not depend on others.
Do not depend on yourself.
Just depend on the Sweetness-Whispers
Of your Inner Pilot.

13980

God wants me to be responsible
For His manifestation
Exactly the way He is responsible
For my aspiration.

13981

Say 'yes', say 'yes', say 'yes'
To the world
Only when you can be of service
To the world.
Otherwise, enjoy complete silence!

13982

God wants me to show Him
My life's humility
So that He can bless me
With His Eternity's Infinity.

13983

I have made many choices
In my life.
Alas, each stupid and greedy
Choice of mine
Has taken me farther and farther
Away from God.

13984

When the time comes,
God Himself will ask you
To take a break.
Meanwhile, continue manifesting
God's Love-Compassion-Light
Here on earth.

13985

I may be afraid of my relationship
With God's Vision-Eye,
But I am never afraid of my relationship
With God's Compassion-Heart.

13986

Every day I am enjoying
My dependence
On my soulful heart
And my independence
From my hurtful mind.

13987

God wants to know from you
When He has ever asked you
To please others' minds
Before pleasing His Heart.

13988

Keep no space
Between God's Forgiveness-Heart
And your own gratitude-heart.

13989

Who am I,
If not an easy-going
Amateur God?

13990

Surrender has been a friend of mine
For a long time.
Alas, still I do not know him very well!

13991

When Heaven helps you
Readily,
Can you not offer Heaven
Your gratitude-heart
Unreservedly?

13992

Why don't you listen to God?
He has asked your guilt-harbouring mind
So many times
To forgive and forget,
And proceed onward.

13993

Alas, I cannot control my own mind
Even for a fleeting second.
Then how do I dare to try to control
The mouth, face and eye of the world?

13994

Your God-defending thoughts
Can and will save you
From your self-defeating life.

13995

Life's tragedies have only one remedy:
God's Compassion-Eye
In God's Satisfaction-Heart.

13996

If you want to choose happiness in life,
Then choose willingness first;
Happiness will automatically follow.

13997

To lose material things
Is to lose the power of earth.
To lose spiritual things
Is to lose the beauty of Heaven.

13998

Alas, I did not know
That my mind's impatience-moments
Have the capacity to destroy
My life's God-approaching dream-boat-shore.

13999

Your confession of your mistakes
Is God's
Perfection-Preparation-Satisfaction
In and through you.

14000

My prayers know how
To touch God's Feet.
My meditations know how
To feel God's Heart.
My surrender knows how
To please God
In God's own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 140, Agni Press, 1991
