770. Your life of lustre

Smile, unreservedly smile!
Your life will be
The lustre of a rose.

Dance, unerringly dance!
Your life will be
The lustre of the moon.

Cry, soulfully cry!
Your life will be
The lustre of the Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 16, Agni Press, 1974
