14911

The mind says to the heart,
"Have you come here to destroy me,
Or have you come to illumine me?"
The heart says,
"I have come to illumine you,
And nothing else."
The mind says,
"Then I am at your disposal."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 150, Agni Press, 1991
