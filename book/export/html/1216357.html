723. Always one-way

From the Master,
Love-message in one ear,
Out the other.
From the Master,
Devotion-message in one ear,
Out the other.
But
God's Compassion-message once in,
No exit.
It is always one-way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 15, Agni Press, 1974
