5808

As a tree needs
All its countless branches,
Leaves and flowers,
Even so the world needs
All its multifarious human capacities.
This is why God may give one thing
To one child of His,
But to another,
Something entirely different.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 59, Agni Press, 1984
