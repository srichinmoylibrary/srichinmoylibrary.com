3 May 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Injustice-night, injustice-night,
Injustice-night-everywhere.
Justice-light, justice-light,
Justice-light-nowhere.
But, my Lord, do give my life
The capacity to grow into
Your universal Justice-Light.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

3 May 2000, 8:22 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 280 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 14, Agni Press, 2000
