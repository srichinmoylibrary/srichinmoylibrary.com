304. God-realisation

God-realisation
Is the most difficult attainment.
Who denies it?
Nobody.
Life-perfection
Is the most difficult achievement.
Alas, nobody wants to admit it.
Nobody wants to try it.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
