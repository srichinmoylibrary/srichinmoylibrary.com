21. In the morning

In the morning
My Lord has only two names:
Compassion and Love.

In the afternoon
My Lord has only one name:
Justice.

In the evening
My Lord has no name:
I shall have to give Him
A suitable name.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three soulful prayers, Agni Press, 1979
