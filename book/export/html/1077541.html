19. Spending my time

I have spent
Most of my time
     Looking and dreaming.

I am spending
Most of my time
     Looking and crying.

I shall spend
Most of my time
     Looking and smiling:
           Looking at the vision-tree,
           Smiling at the realisation-fruit.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eternity's Silence-Heart, Agni Press, 1974
