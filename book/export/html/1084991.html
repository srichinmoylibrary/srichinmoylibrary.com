47. A real fool

I was a real fool
     To come to earth.
Neither earth nor I need each other.

I am a real fool
     To go back to Heaven.
Heaven will plead with me
  To return to earth.
        What for?
  To sow the seeds of
  Earth-perfection
        And
  God-satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
