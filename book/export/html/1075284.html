708 I Command And I Obey...

I COMMAND AND I OBEY

I command
My will,
I obey
My thought.
Why?
Because my will
Is brave,
Because my thought
Is helpless.

I command
My heart,
I obey
My body.
Why?
Because my heart
Is pure,
Because my body
Is insecure.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
