16. Try this helper

When other helpers fail you,
Try this one:
His name is earth-patience.

When other helpers fail you,
Try this one:
His name is Heaven-Compassion.

When other helpers fail you,
Try this one:
His name is surrender-beauty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When God-Love descends, Agni Press, 1975
