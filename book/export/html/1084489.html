4. God's Dream-Reality

I think
I am an animal,
And I mean it.
God declares
I am another God.
Who is right?
We both are right.   God lives in the Dream-Reality
Of His Perfection-light.
   I live in the reality-dream
Of my destruction-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 11, Agni Press, 1974
