4467 I Am On My Way Back Home

With my desire-life
I went away from my home.
But now, with my aspiration-life
I am on my way back
To my Eternity's sweet and trance-bound home.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 45, Agni Press, 1982
