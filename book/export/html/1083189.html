Distributor

There is a slight difference between a communist distributor and God the Distributor. A communist distributor distributes equally the possessions of others, but his own possessions he keeps in perfect safety. But God the Distributor distributes constantly and unreservedly what He has and what He is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Orchestra, Agni Press, 1973
