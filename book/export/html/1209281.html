23411

It is always advisable
To preach and practise
At the same time.
If not, quite often
We spend so much time in preaching
That we have no time to practise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 235, Agni Press, 1996
