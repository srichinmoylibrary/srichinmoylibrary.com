314. His is the soul that loves

His is the vital that loves
The imperious waves.

His is the mind that loves
The spacious skies.

Hi is the heart that loves
The gracious sun.

His is the soul that loves
The precious moon.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 7, Agni Press, 1974
