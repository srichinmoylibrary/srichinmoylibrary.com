23911

In the long run, I know,
Everybody will be
A chosen instrument of God,
And everybody will fulfil God
In God's own Way.
But I believe only in the importance
Of here and now.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 240, Agni Press, 1997
