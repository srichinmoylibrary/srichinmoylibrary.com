My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 20

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Australia in January 2003.

16 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

1 My Mind ...

My mind
Never wants to change.
It is I who have to force
My mind to change.

2 Heaven Is Not Infinite Miles ...

Heaven is not infinite miles
Above my head.
Heaven is right here,
Deep inside my God-gratitude-heart.

3 God’s Desire And My Desire ...

God’s Desire and my desire
Have the same goal:
Love.

4 How Can My Limited Mind ...

How can my limited mind
Ever experience
The beauty and majesty
Of my Absolute Lord Supreme?

5 Nothingness And Infinity ...

Nothingness and Infinity
Are the two shores
Of God’s Life Universal.

6 To My Greatest Surprise ...

To my greatest surprise,
I seek freedom
And yet I love captivity.

7 Alas, We All Are In Love ...

Alas, we all are in love
With a real stranger-thief:
Ignorance.

8 In Vain ...

In vain
Death, the finite,
Tries to hide
Life, the Infinite.

9 The Acceptance Of Light ...

The acceptance of Light
And the rejection of darkness
Must be done
With utmost sincerity,
Willingness and eagerness.

10 My Heart Is No More Mine ...

My heart is no more mine.
My heart is all Yours,
My Lord Beloved Supreme.
“My Heart is also no more Mine,
My sweetest child.
It is all yours,
Yours it was,
And yours it will forever be.”

17 January 2003 ...

17 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

11 My Lord ...

My Lord,
My prayers
Do not touch Your Heart.
My meditations
Do not touch Your Heart.
My cries
Do not touch Your Heart.
My tears
Do not touch Your Heart.
What else can I do, my Lord?

“My sweet child,
Patience, patience!
Wait for My choice Hour.”

12 Never Give Up Hope! ...

Never give up hope!
Hope ultimately succeeds.

13 The Unlit Man ...

The unlit man
Is his mind.
The illumined man
Is his heart.
The perfect man
Is his soul.

14 Fight Against Mankind ...

Fight against mankind —
You are a fool.
Fight against yourself —
You are a saint.

15 My Life Belongs ...

My life belongs
To my faith.
My faith belongs
To God.

16 Each Pure Thought ...

Each pure thought
Is a strong bridge
To cross
To God’s Heart-Home.

17 I Wish To Be ...

I wish to be
A God-loving heart
And not
A world-interesting man.

18 Forget The Past ...

Forget the past.
Guide and lead the present.
Tomorrow’s golden dawn
Will bless you and embrace you.

19 Oneness From Above ...

Oneness from Above
Is sure illumination.
Oneness from below
Is sweet devotion.

18 January 2003 ...

18 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

20 Only The Aspiring Heart ...

Only the aspiring heart
Has the capacity to bury itself
In the ambrosial Lap
Of its Beloved Supreme.

21 Only The Unconditional ...

Only the unconditional
God-surrender-seekers
Receive very special lessons
From their Beloved Supreme.

22 The Unaspiring Mind-Land ...

The unaspiring mind-land
Is a dangerous place.

23 My Life’s Greatness ...

My life’s greatness
Has badly failed to please God.
My heart’s goodness
Has sadly failed to please God.
My sleepless gratitude-heart-tears
And my breathless surrender-life-smiles
Have unreservedly pleased God.

24 My Prayer-Life Began ...

My prayer-life began
With begging.
My prayer-life now is singing
Only God’s Glories.
My prayer-life shall end
With surrendering my all
To the Will of my Beloved Supreme.

25 God Is A Strange Matter ...

God is a strange matter
With the arid intellectuals.
My Lord, do give me
A simplicity-life and a purity-heart
To sing always
Your Victory-Songs.

26 Self-Lovers And Self-Servers ...

Self-lovers and self-servers
Are to be found all-where.
God-lovers and God-servers
Are extremely rare achievements.

27 Nobody Can Ever Dare ...

Nobody can ever dare
To take away from me
My God-Touch-Thrill.

19 January 2003 ...

19 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

28 Morning Begins ...

Morning begins.
I begin to pray.
God begins to give.
In silence supreme
He gives me His All.

29 When My Mind Thinks Of God ...

When my mind thinks of God,
God lightens
My life-burdens.

30 When My Heart Prays To God ...

When my heart prays to God,
God removes
All my life-burdens.

31 When I Meditate On God ...

When I meditate on God,
God says to me,
“My child,
All your burdens
Are self-imposed.”

32 My Sweet Lord ...

My sweet Lord,
May the sound of Your Footfall
Frighten my doubting mind.
May the sound of Your Footfall
Awaken my sleeping heart.
May the sound of Your Footfall
Enlighten my serving life.

33 My Beloved Lord ...

My Beloved Lord,
Today I have nothing to spare.
“My child,
Come, come closer to Me!
Empty your heart.
I have everything
To share with you.”

34 In The Morning I Sing ...

In the morning I sing
With the blissfully ascending waves
Of my heart-sea.

35 In The Evening I Sing ...

In the evening I sing
With the peacefully descending waves
Of my heart-sea.

36 My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Today You have made me
Immeasurably happy
By allowing my heartbeat
To mingle, sing and dance
With the golden dust
Of Your golden Feet.

20 January 2003 ...

20 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

37 I Know, I Know ...

I know, I know,
My heart-tears for God
Will never be lost
In the dust of the earth.

38 O My Mind-Doubts ...

O my mind-doubts,
I shall not cherish you any more!
You must leave me alone.

39 O My Heart-Fears ...

O my heart-fears,
I shall not shelter you any more!
You must leave me alone.

40 O My Life-Weaknesses ...

O my life-weaknesses,
I shall not carry you any more!
You must leave me alone.

41 Once More ...

Once more
I have won back my heart-kingdom
From the hostile forces.
From now on
My heart-kingdom and I
Shall forever remain together.

42 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Your Flute has stolen
The tears of my heart.

43 My Child ...

“My child,
Your songs have stolen
The Smiles of My Eye.”

44 God’s Choice Hour ...

God’s choice Hour
Has struck.
His Hunger-Heart
Has devoured my devotion-life.

45 This Morning ...

This morning
My Lord said to me,
“My child, if you once more weep
At your unworthiness,
Never shall I visit you!
As I claim you
To be My All,
Even so, you must claim Me
To be your All.”

21 January 2003 ...

21 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

46 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Please tell me
When Your Hour will strike
For You to place Your Feet
Inside my crying and bleeding heart.

47 Ignorance-Sleep ...

Ignorance-sleep
Attacks us
With no warning.

48 Like My Heart ...

Like my heart,
I must make my mind,
My vital and my body
Always available
To my Beloved Supreme.

49 My Lord ...

My Lord,
This time when You enter
Into my heart,
Please bring with You
Your Enthusiasm
And Dynamism infinite.

50 Each God-Lover ...

Each God-lover
Is a direct instrument of God
For God’s full manifestation
Here on earth.

51 My Lord Supreme Has Graced Me ...

My Lord Supreme has graced me
With His divine Presence,
But alas, I am not ready
To race against ignorance-night
And win the supreme Victory
For my Lord Supreme.

52 My Lord Never Disappoints Me ...

My Lord never disappoints me.
It is my expectation-cleverness
That disappoints me.

53 True ...

True,
I cannot sprint,
I cannot run,
I cannot march,
I cannot walk,
But I can easily crawl
Towards my Lord Beloved Supreme.

54 God’s Long Stories ...

God’s long Stories
Awaken me.
God’s beautiful Poems
Charm me.
God’s soulful Songs
Thrill me.
God’s Tears and Smiles
Perfect me and fulfil me.

22 January 2003 ...

22 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

55 When I Go To God ...

When I go to God
As a beggar,
He immediately closes
His Eyes and Ears.

56 When I Go To God ...

When I go to God
As His own child,
He immediately stands up,
Blesses me and embraces me.

57 Wisdom-Light ...

Wisdom-light
Is giving and giving.
Ignorance-night
Is self-styled and self-multiplying.

58 God Does Not Appreciate ...

God does not appreciate
My shyness.
He appreciates
My openness.

59 Earth Needs ...

Earth needs
God’s Descent.
Heaven needs
God’s Consent.

60 My Immediate ...

My immediate
And eternal need
Is God-fulfilment
In God’s own Way.

61 Every Second ...

Every second,
Many, many, many God-seekers
Are pleasing God in His own Way.
How I wish I could be
One of those fortunate ones!

62 God-Worshippers ...

God-worshippers
And
God-lovers
On any path
Are my only
Divine brothers and sisters.

63 Like My Soul ...

Like my soul,
How I wish I could spend
All my time
Only with my Lord Beloved Supreme!

64 To Realise God, Reveal God ...

To realise God, reveal God
And manifest God here on earth
Is the birthright
Of a selfless God-lover.

24 January 2003 ...

24 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

65 The Day My Lord ...

The day my Lord
Cannot come to see me,
He sends His Heart to me.

66 The Day My Lord’s Heart ...

The day my Lord’s Heart
Cannot come to see me,
He sends His Eye to me.

67 The Day My Lord’s Eye ...

The day my Lord’s Eye
Cannot come to see me,
He sends His Ear to me
To receive messages
From my soul, heart,
Mind, vital and body.
In this way, every day
My Lord keeps
A very, very special connection
With me.

68 Every Morning ...

Every morning
My Lord’s Heart
Says something very nice
To His Eye
About me.

69 God-Believers Are, Indeed ...

God-believers are, indeed,
God’s happiness-harbingers
To mankind.

70 Disappointment ...

Disappointment,
I do not know you
And I do not want to know!

71 When We Run ...

When we run
On the aspiration-track,
God says to us,
“My children, no slow speed
Is allowed!”

72 No Impurity-Bite ...

No impurity-bite
When we pray.
No mind-intrusion
When we meditate.

73 God Tells His Seeker-Children ...

God tells His seeker-children
That without the climbing
Aspiration-flames
From their hearts,
They will never be able
To satisfy Him in His own Way.

74 When I Am Able To Offer ...

When I am able to offer
Even an iota of satisfaction
To my Lord Beloved Supreme,
My heart immediately gets
Throbbing joy.

25 January 2003 ...

25 January 2003

Cairns, Australia

75 Forgiveness ...

Forgiveness
Is the only real happiness
That lasts.

76 To Become A Happiness-Fountain ...

To become a happiness-fountain,
I must forgive those
Who ruthlessly hurt me
Every day.

77 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Today I am praying to You
To think of me
And work in and through me
To the fullest.

78 My Lord ...

My Lord,
You have taught me
Many, many, many things
Right from my very birth.
Only two more things
Please teach me:
How to have tears in my heart
When I pray to You
And how to have poise in my heart
When I meditate on You.

79 My Lord Says To Me ...

My Lord says to me
That from today
Until I breathe my last,
He will decide for me
And choose for me
My life’s “yes” and “no”.

80 To My Soul ...

To my soul
My Lord has given the capacity
To be a maker of bliss,
My heart, to be a maker of peace
And me, a maker of gratitude.

81 Every Day ...

Every day
I must give myself
The time I need
To please my Lord Supreme,
Not only when I am fully awake,
But also when I am dreaming.

82 I Must Not Enjoy Any More ...

I must not enjoy any more
Unrealistic expectations
In my life.

83 Every Morning ...

Every morning
My Lord’s Compassion-Eye
Comes to me
To guide and lead me
On the golden path ahead.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 20, Agni Press, 2003
