9408 God's Satisfaction-Hour

His mind is made
Of flaming faith.
His heart is made
Of illumining love.
His life is made
Of sacrificing breath.
Therefore, God's Satisfaction-Hour
Is fast approaching him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 95, Agni Press, 1983
