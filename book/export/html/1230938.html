Barir Dulali Asim Sneher Patri Biral

Barir dulali asim sneher patri biral
Sabiy madhur kebal tomar chokkhu dharal

Translation:

You are the darling of the family.
You are the embodiment of infinite affection.
Everything of yours is sweet,
Save and except your sharp eyes.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sundaratama dekha dilo aji, part 2, , 2006
