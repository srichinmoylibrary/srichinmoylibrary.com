Today I Have Danced

Today I have danced
My final suspicion-mind-dance.
Today I have danced
My final hesitation-heart-dance.
Today I have danced
My final unwillingness-life-dance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Greet the morning with your heart's aspiration-cry, , 1995
