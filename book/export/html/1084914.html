21. The ladder of life

The ladder of humility
Leads us to God-Heights of
Man-emancipation
And
Man-perfection.
The ladder of pride
Is uncertainty's song,
Imperfection's dance
And
Futility's hush.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
