354. Who has freed me?

Who has freed me
From confusion-thought?
My eternal Friend, God.

Who has made to dawn on me
Possibilities of Divinity's life?
My old Friend, God.

Who has given me
The capacity to transcend
Every day, every hour,
My existence-reality?
My God, my Beloved Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
