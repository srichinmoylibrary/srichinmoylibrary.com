4 May 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please, please, open my heart-door
Wide
With the Light
Of Your Compassion-Eye.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

4 May 1999, 6:27 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. fifty-six times with one arm. (27 right, 27 left plus once each arm to maximum extension)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 5, Agni Press, 1999
