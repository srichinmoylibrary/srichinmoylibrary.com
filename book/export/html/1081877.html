318. Perhaps God, perhaps me

Who is accurate?
Not me.
Perhaps God.

Who is obstinate?
Not God.
Perhaps me.

Who is autocratic?
Not God.
Perhaps me.

Who is perfect?
Ah, that is a question
Which cannot be answered
For a few centuries.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
