Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 123

12201

A true truth-seeker's life
Every day ascends
With a rising rainbow
Of supreme delight.

12202

May my mind's thought-world
Peacefully rest
In my aspiring heart's
Silence-nest.

12203

You are your heart's
Breathless willingness.
Therefore, God will grant your life
Abundant fruitfulness.

12204

My mind is wrapped
In the ever-growing hope
That I will go beyond the domain
Of insecurity-pain.

12205

May my heart never stop dreaming
Every night
My life-transformation-dream.

12206

Because of my heart's
Humility-light,
I never come near my mind's
Ego-tree.

12207

There can be no hurtful conflicts
Between an aspiration-heart
And a dedication-life.

12208

If you do not pray
For your heart's oneness-courage,
Then yours will be a life
Of ceaselessly accumulated insecurity.

12209

O priceless moments
Of unconditional surrender-delight,
Do you really exist?
Or is your existence-light
The product
Of man's hallucination-mind?

12210

From my mind's obedience
My heart receives cheerfulness.
From my heart's cheerfulness
My Lord receives supreme Satisfaction.

12211

Surprisingly vast
Is my mind's sound-sea.
Astonishingly pure
Is my heart's silence-sun.

12212

Although lethargy carries iron weights,
It wants to roam
In the expectation-fulfilment-garden.

12213

You can neither fly high
Nor dive deep
With your aspiration-hobby.
You can never set your heart-sails
For your life's sunrise
With your aspiration-hobby.

12214

I surprisingly
Found God
Right where I
Deliberately left Him.

12215

There can be no choice
Between
My life's progress-light
And
My heart's satisfaction-delight.

12216

My heart's obedience-light
Flies with God.
My mind's disobedience-night
Crawls with man.

12217

Unless you fight against yourself
And win,
You cannot escape
Your doubtful and hurtful mind.

12218

Every morning God comes to me
To congratulate me
On my cheerfully surrendered
God-acceptance-life.

12219

You can be another God
The day you bravely challenge
The ceaseless pride
Of your unhappiness.

12220

Since God owns
My heart's aspiration-hunger,
I must disown
My mind's frustration-feast.

12221

Everything that is supremely fruitful
On earth
Is on temporary loan
From Heaven.

12222

Because of your humility-mind
And your purity-heart,
You will before long become
In the inner world
An ultradistance runner
With striking speed.

12223

He who enjoys self-doubt
Is indeed
An insecurity collector.

12224

Awaken
Your heart's faith-soldiers.
It is high time for them
To destroy
Your mind's doubt-demons.

12225

The seeker's
Aspiration-heart-cry-hunger
And God's
Satisfaction-Smile-Meal
Are a oneness-pair.

12226

A Ph.D. may not understand God,
But a kindergarten student
Can at once understand
And reveal God.

12227

Desire means
Emptiness-beggar.
Aspiration means
Fulness-emperor.

12228

O my mind,
Why are you such a fool?
When are you going to make God
Your very first resort?

12229

No soulful aspiration-heart-cry
Will ever fail to reach
God's Compassion-Eye.

12230

Aspiration-perfection achieved,
Lo, the bondage of self-will
Long deceased!

12231

Your sleepless unwillingness
Is bound to suffer from the fever
Of ruthless ego-frustration.

12232

O faith-deserter,
You have next to nothing,
Yet you expect everything,
Including God's supreme Pride
In you.

12233

Alas,
Your unused heart-capacities
Are decaying
Faster than your fastest
Imagination-flight!

12234

My aspiration-heart was born
To be daily fed
By God's Compassion-Height
And not to be ruthlessly tormented
By negative thoughts.

12235

Fight off your ego-fever.
Otherwise, you will suffer
From a dislocated mind.

12236

I loved my soul's
Ancient illumination-journey.
I love my life's
Modern perfection-goal.

12237

My mind,
I have accepted you
As you are.
How stupid you are
Not to accept yourself!

12238

I can never become
A God-pleaser
Without being blessed
By God's unconditional
Compassion-Sea.

12239

Yesterday I was
A God-chooser.
Today I am
A God-prayer.
Tomorrow I shall become
A God-surrenderer.

12240

Yesterday my heart
Was a God-invoker.
Today my heart
Is a God-praiser.
Tomorrow my heart
Will be a God-supplier.

12241

Yesterday my mind
Was a God-adorer.
Today my heart
Is a God-follower.
Tomorrow my life
Will be a God-lover.

12242

Yesterday I was
A God-invoker.
Today I am
A God-affirmer.
Tomorrow I shall be
A God-satisfier.

12243

Yesterday I was
A God-meditator.
Today I am
A God-embracer.
Tomorrow I shall become
A God-publiciser.

12244

Yesterday my soul
Was a God-dreamer.
Today my heart
Is a God-beginner.
Tomorrow my life
Will be a God-producer.

12245

It requires the wisdom-light
Of the heart
To enjoy the perfection-delight
Of the soul.

12246

It requires a giant exercise
Of faith
To manifest the oneness-bond
Of man with God.

12247

Because you are no longer
Restrained by self-doubt,
God's Satisfaction-Heart-Joy
Is swirling around your life.

12248

Each day is a new opportunity
For my mind's fear
To depart
And for my heart's strength
To arrive.

12249

To escape
The great grip of frustration,
Every day you and your heart
Must soulfully open new frontiers.

12250

Disobedience is happy
Only when
It astronomically multiplies
The ignorance-night
Of each and every human being.

12251

If you want to reach
Divinity's summit,
Then you must use sleeplessly
The wings of your heart's
Aspiration-bird.

12252

Devotion has the capacity
To purify the vital,
Illumine the mind
And
Simplify the life.

12253

You will be able to come out
Of the sea of insecurity
If you devotedly obey
The whisper-call
Of your Inner Pilot.

12254

How can your life's manifestation-tree
Have leaves
If your heart's inner cry
Is not genuine?

12255

Water your heart's faith-garden
Faithfully every day
If you do not want to develop
Doubt-blight
In your unaspiring mind.

12256

If you want
Your aspiration and expectation
To go together,
Then you must be prepared
To fight constantly
Against the attack of frustration-wolf.

12257

The inner life teaches us
How to be detached
From the desire-life
And how to be attached
Only to God,
The Compassion Supreme.

12258

Before you pray and meditate,
Try to see
That there is no failure-thought
In your mind's silence-room.

12259

I call it
My aspiration-heart.
God calls it
His Dream-Fulfilment-Smile.

12260

Since you are so attached
To your self-doubts,
Your soul withdraws its promise
To your heartless life.

12261

No seeker-soul
Will communicate with you
Unless you change your ego-address
To a oneness-address.

12262

Your mind's doubts
Create all problems for you,
But your heart's faith
Can offer you most adequate solutions.

12263

Daily
Use your heart's forgiveness-flames
To burn away
The jealousy-insecurity
Of the blind world.

12264

My Lord Supreme asks me
To smile powerfully every day
So that I do not remain any longer
His failure-child.

12265

The strange methods that fear offers
To save us
Are more useless than
A grain of sand.

12266

Each unhealthy thought
Can easily blow out
Your heart's purity-candle.

12267

Ask your mind every day
To go to your heart-school.
Ask your heart every day
To sit at your soul-shrine.

12268

No good thought can ever be homeless,
For God Himself treasures
The beauty, purity and divinity
Of each and every good thought.

12269

Before your lower self betrays you,
Use your higher self
To expedite your
God-manifestation-promise.

12270

Each time I think of my mental pain,
I think of myself as a good seeker.
Each time I think of my psychic gain,
I feel that I am a chosen instrument
Of God Himself.

12271

If you do not swim against
Your life's imperfection-current,
Then you will always find yourself
In life's inferiority-corner.

12272

His mind-room every day welcomes
The Compassion-Sun from Above
And aspiration-flames
From the entire world.

12273

In the sacred chambers
Of my heart,
I see only God
And the Grace of His Smile.

12274

Do you not think
That God does not appreciate
Your heart's sleepless
Insecurity-stories?
Alas, the soulful heart
Is quite often a captive
Of our ego-mind.

12275

No other place
But my heart-garden,
No other time
But God's choice Hour -
Here and now.

12276

How can the tides of desire
Disappear
If you do not invoke God
To bathe you
In His Compassion-Wisdom-Light?

12277

I have one constant friend
And one constant enemy:
My morning hope
And
My evening frustration.

12278

You may not know where
Your heart's purity is,
You may not know what
Your soul's divinity is,
But I see your heart's purity
And your soul's divinity
Right on your soulful and powerful face.

12279

Each life has to be a surrender-tree
So that God's
Love-Manifestation-Fruits
Can gladden the human life.

12280

Alas, when my mind does not
Accept the invitation from God,
It is my poor heart that suffers miserably,
And not my stupid mind.

12281

You have to keep
Your surrendered oneness-heart
Always pure
If you want to explore
The beauty and the divinity
Of the truth-world.

12282

God will make you
His Eternity's partner
Only when your human life
And your divine breath
Live for God alone.

12283

As soon as you see the doubt-thief
Entering your mind-room,
Sound your heart-alarm!
You will be able to catch the thief
With no difficulty.

12284

Long before my heart-flames
Flicker and die,
I must do something supremely significant,
Supremely fruitful,
For the affection-heart of Mother-Earth.

12285

God does not want to know
What you have done
With His Forgiveness-Delight.
God only wants to know
What you have done
With His Compassion-Height.

12286

The first and worst mistake
Of your life
Is that you have become
A self-ordained teacher
Of world-transformation.

12287

How can you have satisfaction-delight
If you allow yourself
Even unconsciously
To be held up by hesitation-hooligan?

12288

Do not remain standing all the time
On possibility-platform.
Come down and shake hands
With the aspiration-fulfilment-seekers.

12289

If you do not believe
In God's unconditional Compassion-Light,
Then in the inner world
You are undoubtedly an owner
Of useless nothingness.

12290

I want to monopolise
God's Life.
God wants to monopolise
My heart.
Who knows who is going to win?

12291

Can God keep track
Of all His children?
Yes, He can.
He keeps track of all His children
Exactly the same way I keep track
Of all my countless desires!

12292

The hesitation-heart says to God:
"If I enter into You,
Your Vastness infinite may frighten me.
Therefore, I am hesitating."
The reluctance-mind says to God:
"You may be supremely great,
But I can live without You."

12293

Your mind is digging up
Its desire-ruins.
Therefore, your soul-beauty's
Heart-flower is fading.

12294

Develop powerful oneness-light
To burst through the giant
Ignorance-night.
Try, you can do it!
Lo, you have done it!

12295

Nobody can
And nobody will ever be able to
Enter into God's Heart-Door
Without his own heart's purity-pass.

12296

If you have no devotion,
That means you do not have
Sweetness in your heart.
If you have no surrender,
That means you do not have
Fulness in your life.

12297

A single doubtful thought-wind
Is strong enough to capsize
Your heart's cheerfulness-boat.

12298

If you have your heart's
Hope-light,
Then God will give you His Eye's
Promise-Light,
Without fail.

12299

I do not think
There is any human soul
Who has not suffered
From expectation-ailments.

12300

My Lord Supreme,
Even if You do not allow me
To claim You as my own,
Very own,
Do at least allow me
To place every day
My heart's gratitude-blossoms
At Your Feet.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 123, Agni Press, 1989
