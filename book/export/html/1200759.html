14974

O my mind,
I can tolerate you
Even when you do many, many
Things wrong.
But one aspect of yours
I dislike vehemently,
And that is your stubborn
Unwillingness-frown.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 150, Agni Press, 1991
