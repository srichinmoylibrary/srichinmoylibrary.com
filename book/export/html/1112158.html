Oneness-sacrifice

I am so proud of X and Y. Twice I have seen them running together.

See X's oneness-sacrifice! Look at his speed and her speed, but still he runs with his wife.

But their daughter was not with them. I asked, "What is she doing?"

They told me, "She is sleeping!"
