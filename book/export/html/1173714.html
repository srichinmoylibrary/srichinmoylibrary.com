784 My Human Life Hangs Heavy

Because my human life
Hangs heavy upon me,
I try to maintain my friendship
With my divine life.
Alas, my divine life
Is in constant need of repair.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 8, Agni Press, 1981
