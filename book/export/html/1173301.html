375 A Secret Source

Self-conquest
And
God-quest
Love each other deeply
And live together happily.
O world, hear it
From me, a secret source.

The following twenty-five poems were written on 29 and 30 October at the home of Sri Chinmoy's family in Pondicherry.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 4, Agni Press, 1979
