4983 They Do Not Want To Remember

My mind does not want to remember
How much God has done for it
Although God blesses its breath
Time and again, unconditionally.

My heart does not want to remember
How much it has done for God
Although it serves God every day
Devotedly, soulfully and unreservedly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 50, Agni Press, 1982
