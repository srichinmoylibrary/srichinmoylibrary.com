21747

What I need
Is a dynamic vital
That will always strive
For God-manifestation.
What I need
Is a sympathetic heart
That will always endure sufferings
For the cause of God-manifestation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 218, Agni Press, 1994
