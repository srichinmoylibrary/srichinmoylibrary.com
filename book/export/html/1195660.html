9925

Yesterday's doubting mind
May become tired today,
But today's loving heart,
Today's aspiring heart,
Is not going to be tired tomorrow.
This is the conviction
I need from my soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 100, Agni Press, 1984
