51 God Tells Me That ...

God tells me that
I do not need His Permission
Before I trust others,
But I do need
His express Permission
Before I distrust others.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 14, Agni Press, 2002
