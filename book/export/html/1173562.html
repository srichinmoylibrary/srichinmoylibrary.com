633 A God-Nourishing Fruit

You talked
But
Did not act.
Therefore,
The world called you
A religious nut.
Now
You are acting and not talking.
Therefore,
We call you
A God-nourishing fruit.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 7, Agni Press, 1980
