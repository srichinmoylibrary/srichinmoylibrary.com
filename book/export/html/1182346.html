9330 We Shall Not Ultimately Fail You

O Lord Supreme,
We, Your children,
May unimaginably delay You,
But we shall not ultimately fail You
In Your God-Manifestation-Promise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 94, Agni Press, 1983
