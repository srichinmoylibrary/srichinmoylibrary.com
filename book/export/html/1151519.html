38082

When I pray to God,
I am, indeed, good.
When I meditate on God,
I am, indeed, better.
When I do both — pray and meditate —
I not only do the very best,
But also I am on the way
To perfect perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 39, Agni Press, 2004
