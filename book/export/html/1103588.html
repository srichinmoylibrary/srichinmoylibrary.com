17 July 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Today I shall have purity
In my thoughts,
I shall have beauty
In my speech
And I shall have sincerity
In my actions.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

17 July 1999, 8:50 a.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
