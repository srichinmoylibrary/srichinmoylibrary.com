458 Now What Is Happening?

There was a time
When my aspiration-life and I
Were misapprehended.
And now what is happening?
My realisation-life and I
Are being misrepresented.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 5, Agni Press, 1979
