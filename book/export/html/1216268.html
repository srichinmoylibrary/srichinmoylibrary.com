636. Who can tell me?

Who can tell me
Where God-Light is?
Perhaps my aspiration-friend can.

Who can tell me
Where God-Power is?
Perhaps my prayer-friend can.

Who can tell me
Where God-Delight is?
Perhaps my surrender-friend can.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 13, Agni Press, 1974
