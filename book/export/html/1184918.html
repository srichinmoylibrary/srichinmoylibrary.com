590. To raise humanity's faith

Humanity has fallen.
How can he raise humanity's faith?
Only by showing humanity how to govern its life:
How to be the possessor of its prayer-life,
How to be the regulator of its meditation-life,
How to claim the Beloved Supreme as its very own.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
