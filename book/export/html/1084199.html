20. Together they go

The hour of grace
And
The shower of glory
Together shine.

The hour of victory
And
The power of surrender
Together strike.

The Hour of God
And
The flower of gratitude
Together blossom.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 5, Agni Press, 1974
