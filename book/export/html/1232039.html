Shunya Hate Tomar Kache Esechilam

Shunya hate tomar kache esechilam
Purna haye bhitar bahir phire elam

Translation:

With empty hands I came to You.
My Lord, I have now come back.
Within, without I am full.
I am now fulfilled.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Your face is my dream, , 2009
