44. The hour of need

I helped God
In His Hour of need;
     Therefore
God is now able to sing and dance.

God helped me
In my hour of need;
     Therefore
In Heaven I am complete
     And
On earth I am perfect.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 17, Agni Press, 1974
