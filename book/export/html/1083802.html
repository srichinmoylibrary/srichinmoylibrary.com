29. As they see you

You do not see yourself
As Heaven sees you.
Heaven sees you
As a God-promise.

You do not see yourself
As earth sees you.
Earth sees you
As a God-failure.

You do not see yourself
As God sees you.
God sees you
As another God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Vision-Promise, Agni Press, 1974
