Asa Jaowar Mela Kato Ase Dato Chale Jai

Asa jaowar mela kato ase kato chale jai
Eito tomar khela
Shukano pata jhare pare
Kachi pata dala du bhare
Ei bhabe je shrote bhase jiban bhela
Hasi kanna abhinoy
Chele khela joy parajoy
Satya shudhu asa jaowa mela
Nitya pujai jena na hoy abahela

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
