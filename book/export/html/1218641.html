Kanur Banshi Chander Hasi

Kanur banshi chander hasi heri aji gagane
Chitta amar jiban amar gabhir dhyana magane

Translation:

The smile of the moon
And the flute of Lord Krishna
I see in the sky, together
   At the same time.
Therefore, my heart and my life
Are rapt in meditation-trance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ashar duar, part 1, , 2004
