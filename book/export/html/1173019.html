96. I do not interfere

I do not interfere in God's Affairs.
Every day I let Him
Create a new world.
God does not interfere in my affairs
Either.
Every day He lets me
Destroy an old world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, part 1, Agni Press, 1979
