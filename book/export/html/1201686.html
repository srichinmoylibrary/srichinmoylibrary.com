15892

How to pass
God's purity-examination?
Just develop more purity
In the mind,
More simplicity
In the life
And more self-giving
In the heart.
You will easily pass
God's purity-examination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 159, Agni Press, 1991
