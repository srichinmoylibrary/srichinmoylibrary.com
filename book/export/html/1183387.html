251. Life, love and God

Life is a challenging fact.
Both you and I know it.
Love is a revealing truth.
Both you and I know it.
God is an ever-transcending Reality.
Both you and I do not know it
And
Do not want to know it.
Alas! Alas!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
