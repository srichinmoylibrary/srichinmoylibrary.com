405

When I hear
The song of your eyes,
I enter into
The world of beauty.

When I hear
The song of your heart,
I enter into
The world of purity.

When I hear
The song of your soul,
I enter into
The world of sweetness unknowable.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 5, Agni Press, 1983
