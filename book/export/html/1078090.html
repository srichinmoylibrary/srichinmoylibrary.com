443. Dream not of any other world

Dream not of any other world;
This world really loves you.
Think not of Heaven-God;
Earth-God really loves you.
Who is Heaven-God?
Promise.
Who is earth-God?
Hope.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
