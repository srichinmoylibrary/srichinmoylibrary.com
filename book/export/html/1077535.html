13. Falsehood-role

Do you know
     Why
     I hate myself?
     I hate myself
           Because
           Falsehood plays the most dangerous role
           In my conscious life.
           I am sure sooner or later
           You and the whole world
           Will know it.
           And then you will admire
           My confession-might.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Eternity's Silence-Heart, Agni Press, 1974
