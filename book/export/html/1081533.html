25 Yesterday, today, tomorrow

Yesterday
You were a desire-thief;
Therefore
You suffered.

Today
You are a pleasure-lover;
Therefore
You are suffering.

Tomorrow
You will be a God-seeker;
Therefore
You will be
Smiling,
Singing
And
Dancing.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giving and becoming, Agni Press, 1974
