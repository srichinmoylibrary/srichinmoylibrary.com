24330

My Lord,
You have taken me
As Your own, very own.
But when will I
Be able to do the same?
When will I be able —
Consciously and constantly —
To take You as my very own?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 244, Agni Press, 1998
