434. I offer my capacity

O Lord, purify my heart,
Vivify my life.
I wish cheerfulness to preside over my existence.
Father, in Your Name
I offer my capacity
To the life of earth-beauty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
