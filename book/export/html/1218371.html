Bahir Jibane Prachurjya Diye

Bahir jibane prachurjya diye
Bhulaye rekhona more
Bhitar jibane akuti pradani
Rakhiya tomar krore

Translation:

O my Lord Supreme,
Do not allow me to forget You
By blessing me with plenitude
   In the outer life.
Only keep me with my heart’s aspiration-flames
   In my inner life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Asha phul, part 3, , 1995
