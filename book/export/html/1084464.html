30. Two boons

No more hurdles
Between me and my Lord Supreme!
He has given me two supreme boons:
His lotus Feet belong to my heart;
The tyranny of my ego-life
From now on is His concern,
And not mine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 10, Agni Press, 1974
