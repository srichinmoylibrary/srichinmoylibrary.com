6 July 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme!
Sincerity I need at every moment
In my mind
To please You only in Your own Way.
Purity I need at every moment
In my heart
To please You only in Your own Way.
Simplicity I need at every moment
In my life
To please You only in Your own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme!

6 July 1999, 7:34 a.m. Before lifting 350 lbs. fifty-seven times with one arm. (29 right, 28 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 7, Agni Press, 1999
