6729 God Will Run After You

By virtue of faith
If you want to see God's Face,
God will run after you
With His Heart of Love and Compassion
To speedily help you
In your inner journey.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 68, Agni Press, 1983
