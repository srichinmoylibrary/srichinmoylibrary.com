138. The temple of Eternity

When I wish to pray,
I enter into the temple
Of Eternity.

When I wish to meditate,
I enter into the sky
Of Infinity.

When I wish to realise,
I enter into the sun
Of Immortality.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
