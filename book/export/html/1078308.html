661 My Breathless Necessity...

MY BREATHLESS NECESSITY

God's Height
I have measured
Successfully and gloriously.
But
My two tiny feet
I fail to measure.
Why?
Just because I feed
Only the hunger
Of my breathless necessity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
