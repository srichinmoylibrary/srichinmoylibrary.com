26.

My Lord,
It is so easy for me to feel
That You love me,
But why is it so difficult
For me to feel
That I love You?
"My child,
Do not worry.
As long as you feel
My Love for you,
Your road will remain
Amazingly clear."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Lord's Lotus-Feet versus my devotion-heart, part 3, Agni Press, 1998
