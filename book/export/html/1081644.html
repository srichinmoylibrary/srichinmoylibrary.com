85. In their defense

In defense of God,
I must say
He always tries hard
To perfect me unconditionally
Through His Compassion-light.

In defense of man,
I must say
He always tries hard
To perfect me ceaselessly
Through his deception-height.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
