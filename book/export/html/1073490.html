28.

Not once, not twice, but thrice I have read his book Inside Track cover to cover with my mind’s relentless admiration and my heart’s ceaseless affection. The thing that inspires and impresses me most is this: how does he win so frequently here, there and all-where? He wins precisely because his is at once the enthusiasm-energy-boatman and the progress-dream-boat.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Carl Lewis: the champion inner runner, part 1, Agni Press, 1991
