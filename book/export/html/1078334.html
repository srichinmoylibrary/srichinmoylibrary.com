687 My Heart Is Enough...

MY HEART IS ENOUGH

I have no need
For a sweet tongue.
My heart is enough.

I have no need
For a brilliant mind.
My heart is enough.

I have no need
For two strong arms.
My heart is enough.

I have no need
For two seeing eyes.
My heart is enough.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
