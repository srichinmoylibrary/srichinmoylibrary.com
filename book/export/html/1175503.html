2555 Introductions

My Lord, my Lord,
Please liberate me
From my monologue-prayer.
Please feed the hunger
Of my prayer today.
Do come down
And let me introduce You
As my very dearest and fondest Friend
To my dear friends:
The weak heart,
The blind mind,
The lame vital,
The sleeping body.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 26, Agni Press, 1982
