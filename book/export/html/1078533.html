886 Honest Promise, Honest Dream...

HONEST PROMISE, HONEST DREAM

My honest promise:
I shall please God
In God's own way.

My honest dream:
God shall need me
To found
His Divinity in earth-clay.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
