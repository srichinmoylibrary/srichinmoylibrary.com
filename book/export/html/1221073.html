The Day I Do Not Meditate

May my blood pressure rise
The day I do not meditate.

The Day I Do Not Pray

I must eat not
The day I do not pray.

The Day I Doubt My Lord Supreme

I must sleep not
The day I doubt my Lord Supreme.

The Day I Doubt Myself

I must punch my nose
The day I doubt myself.

The Day I Do Not Care for the World

I must pull my ears
The day I do not care for the world.

The Day I Love Not, Serve Not, the World

I must bite my tongue
The day I love not,
Serve not, the world.

The Day I Lose Faith in Myself

I must hide and hide
The day I lose my faith in myself.

The Day I Love Not My Lord Supreme Only

May I get a heart attack
The day I love not my Lord Supreme only.

The Day I Speak Ill of Others

May I have a hell-experience
The day I speak ill of others.

The Day My Mind Is Empty of Thought

May I have a Heaven-experience
The day my mind is empty of thought.

The Day I Can Please My Lord Supreme

I shall sing and dance
The day I can please my Lord Supreme.

May My Love of God

May my love of God and God’s Love of me
Be proud of each other every day.

May I Find Myself Every Day

May I find myself every day
Praying and meditating
Between a peace-mountain
And a bliss-fountain.

My Heart-Beauty and My Soul-Fragrance

May my heart-beauty
And my soul-fragrance
Dine together every day.

The Outer Education

The outer education we complete.
Nobody can ever complete
The inner education.

Each Seeker Is an Unhorizoned Dream (1)

Each seeker is
An unhorizoned dream
Of God’s Vision-Eye.

The Heart of a Real God-Seeker

The heart of a real God-seeker
Is God-obedience.

A True Seeker Spends All His Time

A true seeker spends all his time
      On God’s needs
And does not spend even a second
      On his own needs.

God Has Opened My Eyes

God has opened my eyes.
I must open my heart.

All My Problems

All my problems are self-imposed, I know.
They all come from my life’s ignorance-flow.

Keep Aside Life-Destruction-Knife

Keep aside life-destruction-knife.
Your deceased heart will spring to life.

My Heart, I Am Watchful

My heart, my heart,
I am watchful.
You remain peaceful,
You remain blissful.

The Oldest Life upon Earth

God’s Love is the oldest life upon earth.
God’s Heart is Infinity’s birth.

Let Me Begin

Let me begin my love of God again.
Joy, joy all-where I shall be, empty of pain.

My Wisdom-Light

My wisdom-light ripens in my heart
To stop the pride of ignorance-dart.

New Year Comes; Old Year Goes

New year comes; old year goes.
Hope-promise-river flows.
Progress-delight certain!
I shall be the bliss-fountain.

Aspiration Wants to Go Up

Aspiration want to go up
And fly for its fulfilment.

Dedication Wants to Go Around

Dedication wants to go around
And search for its fulfilment.

My Body Sleeps and My Vital Wanders

My body sleeps
And my vital wanders.

My Mind Doubts and My Heart Dreams

My mind doubts
And my heart dreams.

My Soul Flies and Flies

My soul flies and flies.

My God Whispers and Whispers

My God whispers and whispers.

All That Lives (1)

All that lives is my heart’s beauty.
All that dies is my mind’s ugliness.

One Forgiveness-Prayer-Tear (1)

One forgiveness-prayer-tear
Can stand against ten serious blunders.

My Thought Can Be Displeased with God (1)

My thought can be displeased with God,
But my feeling cannot.

When I Talk to God (1)

When I talk to God,
Man becomes jealous.
When I talk to man,
I receive a very powerful Embrace
From my Lord Supreme.

My Hope-Heart Starts (1)

My hope-heart starts.
My promise-breath completes.

My Self-Transcendence Is Blessed

My self-transcendence is blessed by
Eternity’s Silence,
Infinity’s Sound
And Immortality’s Delight.

The Tears of My Heart (1)

The tears of my heart
And the smiles of my soul
Are the footsteps
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

I Love My Soul-Commands (1)

I love my soul-commands,
I really do.

I Love My Heart-Whispers (1)

I love my heart-whispers,
I really do.

My Lord’s Feet Beckon (1)

My Lord’s Feet beckon
My prayer-tears.

My Lord’s Heart Beckons (1)

My Lord’s Heart beckons
My meditation-silence.

My Hope-Heart-Bloom (1)

My hope-heart-bloom
Takes me to God.

My Promise-Soul-Blossom (1)

My promise-soul-blossom
Brings God to me.

No Streaming Heart-Tears

No streaming heart-tears,
No express God-Arrival.

The Self-Mastery-Education

The self-mastery-education
Nobody can ever complete.

I Always Approach My Lord Beloved Supreme

I always approach
My Lord Beloved Supreme
On my bended knees.

Life Has No Dead End

Life has no dead end.
Life carries the promise
Of endless progress-delight.

My Lord’s Compassion-Concern-Heart-Door

My Lord's Compassion-Concern-Heart-Door
      Never has any lock.

God’s Desire and My Desire

God’s Desire and my desire
Have the same goal: love.

Nothingness and Infinity

Nothingness and Infinity
Are the two shores
Of God’s Life Universal.

To My Greatest Surprise

To my greatest surprise,
I seek freedom
And yet I love captivity.

Alas, We All Are in Love

Alas, we all are in love
With the real stranger-thief:
Ignorance.

Death the Finite

In vain, death the finite
Tries to hide Life the Infinite.

One Forgiveness-Prayer-Tear (2)

One forgiveness-prayer-tear
Can stand against ten serious blunders.

My Thought Can Be Displeased with God (2)

My thought can be
Displeased with God,
But my feeling cannot.

When I Talk to God (2)

When I talk to God,
Man becomes jealous.
When I talk to man,
I receive a very powerful Embrace
From my Lord Supreme.

All That Lives (2)

All that lives is my heart’s beauty.
All that dies is my mind’s ugliness.

My Hope-Heart Starts (2)

My hope-heart starts.
My promise-breath completes.

The Tears of My Heart (2)

The tears of my heart
And the smiles of my soul
Are the Footsteps
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

My Lord’s Feet Beckon (2)

My Lord’s Feet beckon
My prayer-tears.

My Lord’s Heart Beckons (2)

My Lord’s Heart beckons
My meditation-silence.

My Hope-Heart-Bloom (2)

My hope-heart-bloom
Takes me to God.

My Promise-Soul-Blossom (2)

My promise-soul-blossom
Brings God to me.

I Love My Soul-Commands (2)

I love my soul-commands,
I really do.

I love My Heart-Whispers (2)

I love my heart-whispers,
I really do.

My Heart-Tears for God

I know, I know,
My heart-tears for God
Will never be lost
In the dust of the earth.

O My Mind-Doubts

O my mind-doubts,
I shall not cherish you any more.
You must leave me alone.

O My Heart-Fears

O my heart-fears,
I shall not shelter you any more.
You must leave me alone.

O My Life-Weaknesses

O my life-weaknesses,
I shall not carry you any more.
You must leave me alone.

My Lord, Your Flute Has Stolen

My Lord, Your Flute has stolen
The tears of my heart.

My Child, Your Songs Have Stolen

My child, your songs have stolen
The Smiles of My Eye.

God’s Choice Hour Has Struck

God’s choice Hour has struck.
His Hunger-Heart has devoured
My devotion-life.

This Morning My Lord Said to Me

This morning my Lord said to me,
“My child, if you once more weep
At your unworthiness,
Never shall I visit you.
As I claim you to be My all,
Even so, you must claim Me
To be your All.”

Morning Begins

Morning begins.
I begin to pray.
God begins to give.
In silence supreme,
He gives me His All.

When My Mind Thinks of God

When my mind thinks of God,
God lightens my life-burdens.

When My Heart Prays to God

When my heart prays to God,
God removes all my life-burdens.

All Your Burdens

When I meditate on God,
God says to me,
“My child, all your burdens
Were self-imposed.”

In the Morning I Sing

In the morning I sing
With the blissfully ascending waves
Of my heart-sea.

In the Evening I Sing

In the evening I sing
With the peacefully descending waves
Of my heart-sea.

The Unaspiring Mind-Land

The unaspiring mind-land
Is a dangerous place.

Unconditional God-Surrender-Seekers

Only the unconditional
God-surrender-seekers
Receive very special lessons
From their Beloved Supreme.

Nobody Can Ever Dare

Nobody can ever dare
To take away from me
My God-Touch-thrill.

My Outer Journey Feeds My Eyes

My outer journey feeds my eyes.
My inner journey feeds my heart.

My Lord Begs Me

My Lord begs me
To harmonise my life.
I beg my Lord
To monopolise my life.

I Fear Nothing

I fear nothing.
I fear nothing save and except
My Lord’s streaming Heart-Tears.

Man Is Man’s God-Forgetfulness

Man is man’s God-forgetfulness.
God is God’s man-wakefulness.

My Lord Is Asking Me

My Lord is asking me
To stop fooling myself.
He wants me to discover
My true self.

May My Lord’s Eye Awaken Me

May my Lord’s Eye awaken me.
May my Lord’s Heart enlighten me.

God Appoints My Life

God appoints my life.
I disappoint His Heart.

Destroy I Must

Destroy I must
My old mind-cage.
Destroy I must
My strong earth-bondage.

I Shall Not Retire

I shall not retire, I shall not retire,
I shall not retire,
Until I expire, until I expire,
Until I expire.
