Jogi Hate Chaho Jadi

Jogi hate chaho jadi
Jogi hate chaho
Atma priti bhule giye
Prabhur giti gaho

Translation:

If you want to become a yogi,
You must forget your self-love
And only sing God-songs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
