6432

Yesterday he said
You were bad,
For he saw all darkness
In your face.
But today he is saying
You are good,
For he is seeing your face
Flooded with sunshine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 65, Agni Press, 1984
