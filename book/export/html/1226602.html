Tomare Pujite Ekhano Samoi Ache

Tomare pujite ekhano samoi ache
Hiyar bagane tai hiya shikhi nache

Translation:

Still I have the time to worship You.
Therefore, inside my heart-garden
My heart-deer is dancing

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
