Ati Druta Gati Hridaya Kanane

Ati druta gati hridaya kanane
Vishwa prabhur khamar nayan nache

Translation:

Speedily and speedily, in my heart-garden,
The Forgiveness-Eye of the World-Lord
      Is dancing.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Khama karo ‘forgive me’, , 1987
