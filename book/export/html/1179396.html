6409 Nobody Is Safe!

Nobody is safe!
Even excellent instruments
Of the Lord Supreme,
Devoted pilgrims on the spiritual path,
Can become lame and crippled
And be totally devoured
By ignorance-forces.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 65, Agni Press, 1983
