Jago Jharna Jago

Jago jharna jago
Jago kala jago
Nai kichhu ar dainya
Kebal alor banya

Translation:

Awake, Fountain, awake!
Awake, Art, Awake!
Nothing poverty-stricken today.
All-where the flood of Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, One thousand lotus petals, part 2, , 2000
