653. What is it?

Joy, what is it?
It is its own security.

Security, what is it?
It is its own perfection.

Perfection, what is it?
It is the common satisfaction
Of humanity and divinity,
Of man and God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
