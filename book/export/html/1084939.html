46. Reality of love

Intensity of love
My heart of gratitude
Embodies.
Immensity of love
My life of fortitude
Reveals.
Divinity of love
My soul of plenitude
Manifests.
Reality of love
My God of infinitude
Eternally is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
