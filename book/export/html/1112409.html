46.

Supreme!

Openly,
You tell the doctors
To heal the patient.
Secretly,
You tell death
To kill the patient.
Since the beginning
Of Your creation,
You have been
Playing this game.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sadness-heart-silence. Madness-mind-eloquence, Agni Press, 1998
