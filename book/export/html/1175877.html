2925 Open My Eyes

My Lord Supreme,
Open my eyes
So that I can see Your Beauty.
Open my mind
So that I can appreciate Your Beauty.
Open my heart
So that I can surrender myself completely
To Your Beauty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 30, Agni Press, 1982
