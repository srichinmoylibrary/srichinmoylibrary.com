Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 167

16601

O my surrender-life,
O my gratitude-heart,
I desperately need you both
To be my Eternity's unparalleled friends.

16602

Illumine the inner road
To enjoy fully
The outer freedom.

16603

Dive deep within;
You are bound to hear
The whispering
Peace-sea-messages.

16604

An unconditional God-lover
Always runs the fastest
To reach God's Satisfaction-Feet.

16605

God expects
Each human being to be
His problem-liberator.

16606

Every day I chain my heart
Either to God's Compassion-Eye
Or to His Forgiveness-Heart.

16607

Alas, the cosmic gods
Do not help the human race
In God-manifestation!

16608

God wants
My aspiration-heart-cry
To be His very close companion.

16609

Only a self-giving seeker
Is entitled
To weave a God-song
Every day.

16610

At God's choice Hour
No soul
Will remain unawakened.

16611

O devotion-adoration-hearts,
Who can equal you
In sweetness-delight?
No one!

16612

I want humility-fragrance
To guide and lead
My life's outer journey.

16613

What you need is
Not a new mind.
What you need is
Not a new heart.
What you need is
A new God â€”
A God who will tell you
That you can and you will
Fulfil Him.

16614

Alas, why have you allowed yourself
To be imprisoned
By unworthiness-ruthlessness?

16615

I am so happy
That your inner beauty-seeds
Are growing so rapidly
In your outer life.

16616

My gratitude-flower
And God's Satisfaction-Hour
Keep my life-boat
Sailing smoothly.

16617

Every morning I send my mind
For humility-training
And I send my heart
For purity-training.

16618

How affectionately my soul
Is teaching my heart
To fly on the blue-gold silence-wings
Of Eternity!

16619

My prayerful eagerness
Can devotedly accomplish
Everything that God wants me
To accomplish.

16620

A true heart-hero
Pleases God at every moment
Of his life.

16621

Your mind's fiery will-power
I admire.
Your heart's tenderness-tears
I love.

16622

At last I have found
My Lord's Footprint
On the altar
Of my sacred heart's
Secret temple.

16623

My doubt-challenging confidence-soul,
Do take me with you
Wherever you go!

16624

Every day I enjoy watching
My rainbow-radiance-soul
From my gratitude-heart-garden.

16625

Because of my morning's
Delight-heart
My Lord is exceedingly pleased
With me.

16626

This morning
I forcefully evacuated
All the dark doubt-fog
From my mind.

16627

Every day I am supposed to renew
My heart's silence-licence.
I do it cheerfully and gratefully.

16628

Every morning
My soul compassionately teaches
My heart
How to run at top enthusiasm-speed.

16629

O my vital,
You must not scatter
Your frustration-thorns everywhere.
Don't make me suffer so helplessly!

16630

Every day receptivity-river
Beckons me to swim in it
Free of charge.

16631

If you ignore
The God without,
How can you adore
The God within?
Impossible!

16632

Your faith-heart
Is absolutely faultless.
Again, your doubt-faults
Are unpardonably countless.

16633

At the end of the inner race,
A goodness-heart
Is bound to be the winner.

16634

O my mind,
Because of your unruly thoughts,
I am now prepared to jump
Into an unknown abyss.

16635

Be supremely patient with yourself.
Say a cheerful 'yes'
All the time to your life!

16636

I must turn away from
My endless material needs.
It is high time for me to look at
My life's higher goals!

16637

The wheel of good fortune
Has spun once again
My way.
My aspiration-heart is back!

16638

How can you reach
Your soul's mountaintop
Without crying intensely
And self-givingly?

16639

Anything new
Is an excitement.
Anything perfect
Is an enlightenment.

16640

Human fulfilment
Is hopelessly confined.
Divine fulfilment
Never sets limits.

16641

At the aspiration-gate
We must consciously wait
And carefully listen
For God's blessingful Whispers.

16642

My Lord,
One last Smile from You
Today,
And I shall love You
Infinitely more
Tomorrow!

16643

If you really want to win,
There can be no
Division-thought-response
Either in the inner
Or in the outer fight.

16644

My Lord,
When I do not love You
Unconditionally,
Please bless me
With a heart-stopping fear!

16645

The divine in me
Loves everybody.
It does not know how
To blame anybody.

16646

Before, I was a punctual
God-expectation-dreamer.
But now I have become
A punctual God-surrender-lover.

16647

My soul and my heart
Every day exchange
One love-song each.

16648

My Lord, teach me only
Two most melodious lines:
"I love You only;
I need You only."

16649

God has finally allowed me
To become a citizen
Of His Satisfaction-Kingdom.

16650

My adoration-longing-tears,
Your beauty far transcends
My description-capacity!

16651

My surrender-heart
Is the kingdom
Of my life's Heavenward dreams.

16652

Alas, at times
I do sincerely feel
That I can live without God,
But God cannot live without me.

16653

I am not bragging
When I tell the world
That every day God tells me
In fondness-whispers
That I am another God.

16654

Determination minus ego
Is the beauty-smile
Of a perfection-life.

16655

Just try
A soulful cry.
Lo, God is
His powerful Smile!

16656

Silence-peace-poverty
Is overwhelming
Here, there and everywhere
On earth.

16657

My mind's sorrowful dreams
Are always followed by
My life's tearful realities.

16658

Alas, I am not looking for
Hope-trees,
Not even for hope-plants,
But only for a few
Tiny hope-seeds!

16659

Nobody can fathom
The beauty of your heart's
Sacred and secret tears.

16660

How can I make any progress
With my mountain-high
Monotony-life?

16661

Nothing attempted, nothing achieved.
Be supremely brave!
The Transcendental Goal is yours.

16662

Patience never loses for good.
Its ultimate victory
Is certain.

16663

This morning my heart and I
Celebrated the perfect end
Of my mind's tyranny.

16664

God has made my heart's cries
One, perfectly one,
With the colours of tomorrow's dawn.

16665

There is not
A single ego-mind
That is not
An exhibitionist.

16666

An unconditionally surrendered heart
Is to be found
In a galaxy
Of God-Fragrance-Breath.

16667

No more shall I permit my mind
To be the manager-director
Of my aspiration-life.

16668

O my heart,
I am praying to you
To become
My uncompromising boss
And to demote
My stupid ignorance-mind.

16669

My heart and I
And even my mind enjoyed
The swan song of my mind's despair.

16670

God-Bank accepts
Only our heart's
Purity-fragrance-cheques.

16671

Once you have joined
The God-manifestation-team,
There is no way to quit.

16672

My silence-flooded mind
Knows not even a single
Desire-whisper-breath.

16673

I can do it!
I have done it!
I have become the sleepless
Willingness-child
Of my Lord Supreme.

16674

A powerful mind
Can have enemies,
But not a peaceful heart.

16675

My heart is lovingly washing off
The impurity-dirt
Of my mind's unwillingness.

16676

God lovingly adds
His divine Dignity
To my heart's purity-smile.

16677

Why should I live anymore
With the dead ashes
Of fruitless loneliness?

16678

When God the Compassion
Speaks well of him,
He immediately turns a deaf ear
To God the Justice.

16679

My mind,
I am telling you
Once and for all,
Either please me
Or leave me alone!

16680

My Lord,
My secret heart-shrine
Is only for Your Compassion-Feet.

16681

When a oneness-heart
Runs for President,
Every citizen of the inner world
Will vote.

16682

O outer prosperity,
You are not beautiful
Or powerful enough
For me to cherish.

16683

It seems that by nature
The human mind
Does not like self-expansion.

16684

O my unwillingness-mind-balloon,
Do surrender to my soul-light!
Otherwise, before long,
You will be punctured.

16685

The power of the heart
Is secret, sacred,
Illumining and fulfilling.

16686

The doubting mind
Is the stuff of which
Life-disasters are made.

16687

At every moment
The heart needs happiness-news
To expedite
Its God-manifestation-journey.

16688

O my mind,
Start searching and aspiring.
Otherwise, you will be forced
To surrender to uselessness-nothingness.

16689

Be not lost in between
The pride of your mind
And the selflessness of your heart!

16690

Yesterday my mind was standing
And today my vital is standing
In line
For stupidity-throne.

16691

Your mind suspects
God-existence.
Your heart rejects
Your mind-stupidity.

16692

Every morning God comes to me
With His Perfection-Rainbow-Smiles
To feed my heart's
Aspiration-hunger-flames.

16693

There is no problem
That cannot be solved
By perseverance and persistence-light.

16694

Each human being is expected to be
A soulful ambassador of goodwill
To improve the standard of mankind.

16695

At times a little nostalgia
Is helpful
To intensify the heart's
Aspiration-cries.

16696

Good fortune
Will definitely smile on you
If you stand divinely firm
Behind your own supreme convictions.

16697

Start your self-discovery-journey
Toward your life's integral
And universal freedom.

16698

Challenges disappear
When you unconditionally depend
On God's Compassion-flooded Eye.

16699

Peace is not a compromise.
Peace is
A oneness-fulness-satisfaction-embrace.

16700

God does not want to hear from you
All your ignorance-experiences.
God wants to hear from you
How you are planning to realise Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 167, Agni Press, 1992
