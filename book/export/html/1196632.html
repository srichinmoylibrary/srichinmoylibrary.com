10888

You do not have
The capacity or willingness
To fulfil God's Request.
At the same time,
It is beneath your dignity
To ask others to help.
Alas, you are
Your stark stupidity-life!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 109, Agni Press, 1987
