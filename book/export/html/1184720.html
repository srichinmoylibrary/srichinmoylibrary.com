392. In supreme gratitude

Lord,
I wish to shine
In supreme gratitude;
Therefore
I am awake,
I am walking,
I am running,
Towards the golden Destination-dance.

Lord, many a time
You have forgiven me.
Just forgive me this one more time.
With my ever-undying gratitude
I shall leave You alone in peace,
For good.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
