104. Nonessential and essential

O my thought-life,
In my soul-sea
You shall always remain
Nonessential.

O my will-life,
In my heart-sky
You shall always remain
Essential,
Indispensable.
You are God's own Perfection-pride.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
