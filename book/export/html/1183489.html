353. He and she

He is a cloud-born man.
What can you really
Expect of him?

She is a sun-born soul.
Is there anything
That both on earth and in Heaven
You cannot expect of her?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
