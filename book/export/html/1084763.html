23 What Do You Want?...

WHAT DO YOU WANT?

What do you want?
God's Infinity?
It is inside the smile
Of your eyes.
What do you want?
God's Eternity?
It is inside the cry
Of your heart.
What do you want?
God's Immortality?
It is inside the silence-beauty
Of your soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 16, Agni Press, 1974
