15263

When you meditate,
You embody only good thoughts.
You will not find fault
With others;
You will not speak ill
Of them;
You will not be a hindrance
To their achievements;
You will not try
To surpass them.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 16, Agni Press, 1999
