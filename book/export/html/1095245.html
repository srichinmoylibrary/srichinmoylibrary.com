17 My Lord, May I Offer You ...

My Lord, may I offer You
A true story?
“No!”
My Lord, may I offer You
A beautiful poem?
“No!”
My Lord, may I offer You
A soulful song?
“No!”
My Lord, may I offer You
My gratitude-heart-tears?
“Yes, yes, yes!
My child, start offering.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 19, Agni Press, 2003
