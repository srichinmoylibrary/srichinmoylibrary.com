24816

If you love your Master
Unconditionally,
Then he becomes your heart's
Emperor supreme.
But if you love him conditionally,
Then you can at any time
Turn him into a veritable beggar.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 249, Agni Press, 1998
