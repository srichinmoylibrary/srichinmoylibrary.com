He Anurakti Jao Dure Chale

He anurakti jao dure chale jao dure chale
Chupi chupi chupi ati druta gati kathati na bale

Translation:

O attachment, away you go,
      Away you go,
In pindrop silence, fast, very fast,
      Without a single word.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Khunjite chahigo, part 1, , 2003
