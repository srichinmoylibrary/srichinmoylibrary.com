12.

Slowly, steadily, compassionately
And blessingfully
Lord Krishna inspired Arjuna
To become
His supremely choice instrument
For the establishment
Of Truth Transcendental
In the heart of Life Universal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Message-Light of the Bhagavad Gita, Agni Press, 1999
