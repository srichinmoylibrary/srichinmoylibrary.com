674 God Gave You

Power you wanted.
God gave you.
Love you wanted.
God gave you.
A few things more you want:
Illumination-perfection
And
Oneness-satisfaction.
These also God will undoubtedly give you
At His choice Hour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 7, Agni Press, 1980
