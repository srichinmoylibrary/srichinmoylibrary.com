Alone he stood

Alone he stood
Above all storms of life.
He stood alone
To challenge Pain and Strife.

Alone he stood
To feed a blooming race.
He stood alone
To change earth's tearful face.

﻿

  1. In commemoration of President John F. Kennedy, ... November 22nd, 1963..↩
