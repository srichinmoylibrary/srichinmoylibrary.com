798. Hope

Hope
Is kind.
Therefore
I mix with hope.

Hope
Is swift.
Therefore
I run with hope.

Hope
Is brave.
Therefore
I collect boundless strength
From hope.

Hope
Is sincere.
Therefore
I invite hope
To accompany me
To the Golden Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 16, Agni Press, 1973
