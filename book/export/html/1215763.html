141. God loves him

His prayer is his lip-homage,
Yet God loves him.

His prayer is his clever deception,
Yet God loves him.

God loves him because
He is of God.

He shall be for God because
He is in God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 3, Agni Press, 1974
