31 August 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my heart-garden be always full
Of God-surrender-blossoms.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

31 August 2000, 9:03 a.m. Before doing 570-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift. New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
