5909 When Are You Going To Stop?

Earth, O earth,
When are you going to stop
Hoisting the banner
Of ridiculous helplessness?

Heaven, O Heaven,
When are you going to stop
Hoisting the banner
Of cruel indifference?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 60, Agni Press, 1983
