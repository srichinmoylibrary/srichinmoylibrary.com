24 Thank you, my daughter

Father, I have brought You
My soul's willingness.
"Thank you, My daughter.
Anything more?"

Father, I have brought You
My life's usefulness.
"Thank you, My daughter.
Anything more?"

Father, I have brought You
My heart's love.
"Thank you, My daughter.
Nothing more do I need.
Your love is what
I eternally need."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giving and becoming, Agni Press, 1974
