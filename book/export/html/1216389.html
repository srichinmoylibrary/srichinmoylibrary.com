754. The supreme secret

Fear cannot please
His body anymore.
Depression cannot please
His vital anymore.
Doubt cannot please
His mind anymore.
Insecurity cannot please
His heart anymore.
Why?
Because he has discovered
The supreme secret:
That he is of God-Delight
And
He is for God-Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 16, Agni Press, 1974
