Phulero Dola

Phulero dola dola dola
Atma bhola atma bhola
Dhara adharar ananda rup
Jibanahutir pabitra dhup

Translation:

A swing of flowers,
God's sweetness showers.
Man's oneness-role,
No division-dole.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Chandelier, Part 2, , 1981
