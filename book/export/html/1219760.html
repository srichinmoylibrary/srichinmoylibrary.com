Ganga Jamuna Kalakala Rabe

Ganga jamuna kalakala rabe bandana taba kare
Maral bahini vidyadayini tomar kripar tare
Adikabi hate tarun kata
Haste rekheche kusum stabak
Janate chahe je tomar charane
Pabita bhakati nati
Rekheche sudure rekheche sudure
Man apaman basona jatana khati
Eso go sarada bastaba rupe
Heribo tomare aratir dhupe
Mangal dip jwalabo ajike bajabo shankha dhwani
Gahibo sakale ek sathe mile bharatir agamani

From: Sri Chinmoy, The bloom of my lotus-heart, , 2005
