Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 262

26101

Every day I have made it a point
To walk lovingly and faithfully
Along the path of gratitude.

26102

God lovingly and proudly
Tells my surrender-heart
To walk freely with Him.

26103

The first duty of a God-seeker
Every day
Is to use his smiles
To look at God the creation.

26104

When I am in my mind,
I become a contradiction-mind.
When I am in my vital,
I become a destruction-vital.

26105

No, not our body, vital, mind or heart —
Not even our soul —
But God Himself is the last authority
On spirituality.

26106

If you do not take your mind away
From the travail of daily life,
How can you ever receive
God's choicest Blessings?

26107

The heart knows
The direct road to God.
The mind knows
Only a roundabout route to God.

26108

Alas, I do not know
How my mind has become
A never-ending regret-sigh!

26109

The strength of self-effacement
Can never be measured
Adequately.

26110

Each thought is meant
To become a prayer
For God to smile at us.

26111

The mind's negativity
Quite often runs
From danger to destruction.

26112

God Himself wants to take
An immortal picture
Of my surrender-life
And gratitude-heart.

26113

God does give
Our heart the capacity
Every day
To lift us up to new heights.

26114

God and God's Silence
Are two inseparable and immortal
Oneness-Friends.

26115

Humanity's peace-prayers
And peace-meditations
Are treasured
By the Absolute Lord Himself.

26116

The team of God-servers
Must know that it is God's Will
That they are serving
In each and every human being.

26117

O seeker, do not be afraid
Of your insecurity-barriers.
Be brave!
To your wide surprise,
You will be able to cross
All the insecurity-barriers.

26118

The heart of God-realisation
Is progressive.
It always sings the song
Of self-transcendence.

26119

If you are determined
To resist the temptation
Of your ignorance-pursuing life,
Then you are bound to feel and see
That God is all for you.

26120

Self-adulation-disease
Is so common.
Yet it is very difficult
To free oneself from that disease.

26121

Each human being meets with
Countless desire-disappointments.
Yet nobody wants to give up
The desire-life.

26122

To become an overnight success,
You need to prepare
Inwardly and secretly
For a long time.

26123

God sends us joy
To be our first teacher.
When the joy-teacher fails,
He sends the pain-teacher
To teach us.

26124

If you are not eagerly willing
To jump into the Light,
Then you will always remain
In your mind's darkest night.

26125

Learn to live
With as few things as possible.
This is the only way you can have
Peace of mind.

26126

First learn to look at yourself
Lovingly and compassionately.
Then look at yourself
As strictly as possible
To run the fastest.

26127

Work with what you have.
God will bring you and give you
What He is.

26128

O my heart,
Do not cry.
O my heart,
Do not feel that you are lost.
O my heart,
Do not think that nobody cares for you.
Just move on and move on!
You will see that everybody and everything
Is right in its own way.

26129

We look for God's Crown
And God's Throne above.
We look for God's Crown
And God's Throne below.
But they are neither above nor below.
They are inside our heart-breath.

26130

Self-giving and self-transcendence
Are inseparable friends.
They always meet together.

26131

If the modern world had more simplicity,
Then the modern world
Would have infinitely more joy
Than it has now.

26132

The worst problem in our life
Is that the mind does not want
To doubt its own doubt.

26133

If you are afraid
Of your failure-life,
Then progress will never knock
At your heart-door.

26134

What is human life?
It is the heavy and strong bondage
Of unnecessary extravagance.

26135

The mind cannot, but the heart can
Distinguish the outer wants
From the inner needs.

26136

Do not waste any more time!
Just ask yourself in all earnest
What you actually need
From God.

26137

If there is sincerity in your prayer,
Then God's Answer
Will definitely be audible.
If there is no sincerity,
Then God
Will definitely turn a deaf ear to you.

26138

Does God give us another chance?
He does, but alas,
We misuse the chance —
Not only twice and thrice,
But time and again.

26139

Every day God comes to me
With new answers
To all my old questions.

26140

A life of constant surrender
Can constantly breathe in
God's own Breath.

26141

Alas, we are prone
To be receptive to absurdities
And not to reality.

26142

Man tells God:
"God, You do not know
How much I suffer from passion."

God tells man:
"My child, you do not know
How much I suffer from you
When you do not believe
In My Compassion."

26143

I tell you, my seeker-friend,
That there shall come a time
When you will laugh and laugh
At your past achievement-peaks.

26144

My utter stupidity
Is catching up with me.
I thought that I could live easily
Without God.

26145

O my heart,
Do not walk anymore
Under the clouds
Of your darkness-mind.
Allow your inner sun to burst forth!

26146

The human life
Is a life that enjoys
Deliberate falsehood
And bold doubts.

26147

God wants us
To spread our dreams
All-where
And not to hide them
From the rest of the world.

26148

My Lord, tell me,
Will You ever allow Your Forgiveness
To give up on me?
"My child, never!"

26149

My Lord, will You ever apply
Your Indifference-Justice
And give up on my life?
"My child, never!"

26150

My Lord,
When will You allow Yourself
To give up on me?

"My child,
The day you give up on yourself
And prevent Me from interfering
In your life!"

26151

God has many more
Important things to do
Than to console
Your constantly brooding
Heaviness-mind.

26152

Every day you must honour
Your own heart-cry
To make the fastest progress.

26153

If you do not dismiss
Your mind's absolute decisions,
Then you will never succeed.

26154

Do not forget that each heart
Is made of God's own
Sweetness-Heart.

26155

How is it that you are not aware
Of the thoughts that are hiding
Inside your mind-prison?

26156

Alas, I am fully responsible
For the failure
Of my soul's promises to God.

26157

Do one thing, my seeker-friend.
Every day make it a point
To spare poor God
From your countless complaints.

26158

All the mind's confusion-thoughts
Are always
Ill-founded.

26159

Only our tearful hearts
Can hear
God's Sweetness-Whispers.

26160

Your heart does not need
Your compassion.
What your heart needs from you
Is your constant willingness
To do the right thing
And become the right person.

26161

If you have adamantine will-power,
Then it will irresistibly lead you upward
To God's Kingdom-Light.

26162

Nobody wants to hear
The monologue of the mind.
It is for the mind to enjoy
Its own stupid absurdities.

26163

At your heart-shrine,
Imagine yourself
As the most beautiful angel
While you are praying.

26164

There is always time for us
To take spiritual exercises
To please our Lord Supreme.

26165

Each aspiring heart
Has a sweet and unfading yearning
To be in God's company.

26166

The soul's sunlight
Knows no shadow
Of the mind
And no darkness
Of the vital.

26167

Alas, it is we who confuse ourselves
With our mind's constant
World-enquiries.

26168

Your heart-eye must look straight
And travel faster than the fastest
To the Feet
Of your Lord Supreme.

26169

Only a flower-heart
Can be drawn easily and effectively
Towards the soul.

26170

Alas, the human mind
Cannot see
That its ingratitude is ever-increasing!

26171

This morning
God the Compassion Himself
Has come down to replenish
My faded heart-hope.

26172

If you have the sharp edge
Of intensity
In your heart, mind and vital,
Then who can prevent you
From making the fastest progress?

26173

May the light of the new millennium
Successfully spread
All over God the creation.

26174

Every day my soul begs
My heart, mind, vital and body
To hoist
Their aspiration-sails.

26175

The doubting mind
Is a daily problem-finder,
And the aspiring heart
Is a daily problem-shooter.

26176

If you are a fool,
You will fail to see.
If you are a fool,
You will fail to believe.
If you are a fool,
You will fail to climb, run and dive.
If you are a fool,
You will be empty
Of God-fulfilling opportunities.

26177

Each moment is flooded
With Divinity's Smiles
And humanity's tears.

26178

If you want to have
The fastest deer-progress,
Then do not think of your success-life.
Think only of God's
Ever-brightening Smile.

26179

Our heart-gratitude
Has the rare capacity
To illumine even the darkest corner
Of our mind.

26180

If you think that you will embark
On the spiritual life
When things get easier,
Then there will never be a time for you
To accept the spiritual life,
Either on earth or in Heaven.

26181

If you think and feel
That you need to please God only,
Then you are ready
For the real spiritual life.

26182

Shorten the tale
Of your complaints
And lengthen the beauty
Of your world-appreciation.
Lo, God is singing and dancing
Before you!

26183

Alas, human pride is something
That can never see itself
In its real activity.

26184

Who is God's right-hand man?
He whose life-breath
Is for constant God-fulfilment.

26185

My Lord, use me
Not only when You need me
But whenever You want me.

26186

If your love is only for God,
Then God will bless you
Not only with
God-manifestation-possibilities
But also with
God-manifestation-inevitabilities.

26187

What do you see
When you look at yourself?
Do you see yourself as another God
Preparing to complete
Your God-ordained task?
Then you are absolutely right!

26188

Each human mind always feels
That it is justified
In all its actions.

26189

Until you stand right in front of God,
You may not know
How much beauty you have
In your simplicity
And how much purity you have
In your sincerity.

26190

The unaspiring mind will never be able
To smile properly,
Not to speak of divinely.
It only pretends to smile.

26191

God says to the spiritual Master
That since He does not know
What retirement is,
He will not be able to grant retirement
To the spiritual Master.

26192

If God wants my life to be examined
Even by my worst foe,
I shall be more than eagerly ready,
For that is the way I will be able
To make the fastest progress.

26193

When my heart
Does not keep in touch with God,
I see that my monkey-mind
Pinches me infinitely harder.

26194

God, my donkey-back is really strong.
Please allow me to carry
Some of Your burden.
I assure You, I will not collapse.

26195

When aspiration declines,
Frustration becomes
An all-devouring hunger.

26196

My sound-life wants to be sovereign,
But my Lord Supreme
Wants only my God-aspiration-heart
To be my life's sovereign.

26197

God has sent you to earth
Not to collect your complacency-drop
But to harvest
Your God-willingness-thoughts.

26198

O seeker, choose God
As soon as possible.
Then God will definitely
Choose you
One day, without fail.

26199

God will richly reward you
If your heart's progress-life
Moves forward
One heartbeat at a time.

26200

God's Heart-Line
Is longer than the longest.
He needs no extension cord
To reach me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 262, Agni Press, 1998
