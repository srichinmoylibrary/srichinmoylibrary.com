1711

He wanted to go to hell
And see what it looked like.
Teeming doubts came to him
And said,
"You do not have to take the trouble
Of going to hell.
We have brought hell to you."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 18, Agni Press, 1983
