95. Live

Live in the heart-garden;
Yours will be the angelic inspiration.
Live in the vital-jungle;
Yours will be the Satanic temptation.
Live in the soul-sky;
Yours will be the dynamic aspiration.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
