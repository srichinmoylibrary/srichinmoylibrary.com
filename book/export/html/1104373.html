19 August 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
On the nineteenth of February
I started practising 1,300 pounds.
Today I complete five long months.
Alas, satisfaction is still a far cry.
My Supreme,
My heart is all gratitude to You
For You have inundated me
With Your Compassion-Patience-Light.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 August 2000, 7:37 a.m. Before doing 1,300-lb. standing double-dumbbell lift three times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
