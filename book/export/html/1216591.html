952. My four dreams

   I sacrificed my first dream
        To fulfil my second dream.   I sacrificed my second dream
        To fulfil my third dream.   I sacrificed my third dream
        To fulfil my fourth dream.        To become a scholar
   Was my first dream.        To become a poet
   Was my second dream.        To become a Yogi
   Was my third dream.

To become a God-server in humanity
Was my fourth and last Reality-filled dream.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
