1 May 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please, please give me
Sincerity and intensity
In my prayers
So that when I pray to You,
All my worries and anxieties
Disappear
In the infinite vastness of the sky.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 May 1999, 7:41 a.m. Before using standing double-arm machine up to 160 lbs. with both arms simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 5, Agni Press, 1999
