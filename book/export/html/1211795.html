Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 260

25901

To my heart's extreme joy,
My life has completely blended
With the shadow
Of my Lord's Feet.

25902

My Lord asks me every morning
To sing only one song:
My surrender-life-song.

25903

I have been begging my Lord
To make me once more
The child of His Dream.

25904

At times it is more difficult
To embrace the waves of joy
Than to confront the surges of pain.

25905

O my heart's aspiration-flames,
You are meant to climb up
And not to allow yourself
To be extinguished.

25906

Let the memories of miseries
In my aspiration-life
Drop dead here and now!

25907

My mind-disobedience
And my ignorance-pride
Live together, play together
And dine together.

25908

If your spiritual life is not
Powerful and bold,
Then your inner progress
Will be extremely, extremely slow.

25909

I have sung my soul's
Manifestation-song.
I have sung my heart's
Surrender-song.
I have sung my mind's
Disobedience-song.
I have sung my vital's
Arrogance-song.
I have sung my body's
Lethargy-song.

25910

My heart feels
All the sorrows of the world.
My soul activates
All the joys of life.

25911

My heart's surrender-joy
Is feeding
My Lord's Compassion-Eye.

25912

My Lord Supreme wants me to deliver
All His Peace-Dream-Messages
To every aspiration-heart-home.

25913

I never thought that my soul
Could be so tangible,
And always available.

25914

When I sing my Lord's
Compassion-Heart-Song,
I feel that I do not have to
Go to Heaven,
For I am already in Heaven.

25915

The ingratitude of the mind-world
Is desperately trying to destroy
The gratitude-heart of the world.

25916

There is only one thing
I would like to see.
I want to see my heart's
Peace-dream-horizon
All the time rising and rising
And rising.

25917

My Lord, either You sing for me
Or allow me to sing for You.
If not, my heart will surely die
Shortly.

25918

Prayerful is my heart-sky.
Doubtful is my mind-sky.
Powerful is my life-sky.
And alas,
I am my own uncertainty-sky.

25919

No matter how sincerely I try,
I am always crippled
By the jungle-confusion
Of my mind.

25920

Although we can start our life-journey
With self-doubt and God-doubt,
Eventually we will reverse the game
Completely.
We will have faith in God
And in ourselves.

25921

May my heart at every moment
Sing God-surrender-songs
To perfect my life.

25922

This time I am determined
To spend all my time
In my heart-garden
And not walk through
My desert-helplessness-life.

25923

Each and every heart
Must have a soulful, tearful
And blissful prayer-life.

25924

If my meditation-heart and prayer-life
Cannot please God,
Alas, then I have nothing
That can please my Lord Supreme,
Either in my own way
Or in His own Way.

25925

My Lord,
Will there be a day
When I shall be able to surprise You
In any way?

"My child,
Definitely there will be,
And that day is fast approaching!"

25926

Only a rank fool
Comes to conclusions
About God's Activities.

25927

My Lord,
Will I ever be
One of Your Heart-Satisfaction-Songs?

"My child,
You have already become one."

25928

My Lord,
What is needed
To be a permanent passenger
On Your Boat?

"My child,
You need to have
The beauty and the fragrance
Of a child-heart-rose."

25929

O my Master, I do not mind
If you have the longest road for me,
As long as you remain my guide,
Only guide.

25930

My Lord,
To swim in the sea of ecstasy,
What do I need?

"My child,
You need your heart-intimacy
With My Heart."

25931

I cannot run as fast as my heart does,
But when my heart runs the fastest,
My entire being is inundated with delight.

25932

You play your role:
You work hard, very hard.
God will also do His Work:
He will give you extraordinary results —
Far beyond your expectations!

25933

My Lord, if ever You grant me
The opportunity to come and visit You,
I assure You,
I shall not allow my desire-tiger
To accompany me.
I shall allow only my aspiration-deer
To accompany me.

25934

My Lord Supreme,
You have allowed me to do
And You have allowed Yourself to do
Many things for me.
One more thing I need from You:
To allow me, out of Your infinite Bounty,
To recognise You —
Who You are and what You are.

25935

My Lord, I am desperate to love You
In Your creation.
Do give me one more opportunity
To please You in Your own Way.

25936

My partnership with God
Does not depend on my capacity.
It depends on God's Compassion-Eye
And His Forgiveness-Feet.

25937

Although the mind is higher
Than the vital,
The vital does not find God-satisfaction
To be an impossible task,
The way the mind does.

25938

Let me realise the stupidity
And absurdity of my mind
Before it becomes too late.

25939

Every day God gives me
New lessons.
Today's new lesson is:
God only for God's sake!

25940

My Lord, the more You expect
Humility from my mind,
The more You receive
Audacity's absurdity from my mind.

25941

My Lord, I have no objection,
And I mean it,
To whomever You want to love.
Love whom You want to love.
Only give me the capacity
To love You only.

25942

My Lord,
Do You ever get disheartened?
"No, My child,
Because I never expect."

25943

To my great sorrow,
I have allowed my mind to guide me
For so many years.
Now I see that my mind
Has not guided me at all,
But has only misguided me.

25944

I did not know what I actually needed,
But now I do know what I need.
I need only my Lord's blessingful Feet
Inside my heart-garden.

25945

My Lord, do You ever
Get really angry with me?

"My child, what is wrong,
If that is the only way left
For Me to perfect you?"

25946

God wants you to tell Him
What price you are going to pay
To study the God-realisation-course.

25947

I shall prove to God time and again
That His Love is the only thing I need,
Not only in my heart-garden
But also in my mind-jungle.

25948

My Lord, You have been kind to me
For so many years.
Now it should be my turn
To be kind to You,
To be affectionate to You
And to fulfil You in Your own Way.

25949

My Lord,
Why should You be responsible
For the utter stupidity in my life?

"My child,
Since I have taken responsibility
For the wisdom in your spiritual life,
I feel that I am under obligation
To be responsible for your stark stupidity
As well."

25950

My Lord,
When I quarrel with You unnecessarily,
What do You think of me?

"My child,
I think of you
The way I have always thought of you —
As another God-representative —
And I still love you and need you
Unmistakably,
Lovingly, plus necessarily."

25951

May my life be completely transformed
Into a readiness-song
And a willingness-dance.

25952

If a garden-flower can look at the sun
And offer its gratitude to the sun,
What is wrong with me?
How is it that my heart-flower
Does not look at my inner sun
With ceaseless gratitude?

25953

My Lord,
I have spoken so much about the soul
At Your express Command.
Alas, my disciple-world
Misunderstands me badly.
How I wish You to either come directly
Or to send
Your direct representative on earth,
The soul,
To convince my disciple-world
That what I have said about the soul
Is absolutely true!

25954

My Lord, do You know at times
Why I deny You?
I deny You precisely because
I need more love for You,
And I also expect You
To take more love from me.

25955

My Lord, my happiness
Is Your immediate Satisfaction.
But why is it that Your Happiness
Is not my satisfaction?

25956

My Lord Supreme,
When I think of leaving You,
Can You not increase Your Love for me?

"My child, I do, I do!
Just because I increase My Love for you,
You are still with Me."

25957

My Lord,
What does my joy do to You?

"My child,
Your joy makes Me feel
That I am absolutely perfect,
According to you."

25958

My Lord,
What does my sorrow do to You?

"My child,
Your sorrow makes Me feel
That I am imperfection incarnate,
According to you."

25959

My Lord, do tell me
What You wanted from me,
What You want from me
And what You will want from me.

"My child, only one thing
I wanted, still want
And will always want from you:
Your satisfaction
In My own divine Way."

25960

My Lord,
You are so kind to me.
You are so compassionate to me.
All my heart's sorrows
And all my heart's smiles
You have allowed to find
Their perfection-satisfaction-home
Inside Your Heart.

25961

O seeker, be careful!
At any moment your spiritual life
May be destroyed
By a violent emotion-explosion.
Always pray to God for protection!

25962

Victory and defeat
Every day in our spiritual life
We must accept with equal equanimity.

25963

Definitely we can arrive
At the supreme height of the supreme Truth
Only by the Grace
Of the Absolute Lord Supreme.

25964

There shall come a time
When there will be
No country-boundaries,
And it will be in the near future,
Not the remote future.

25965

If you have a breathless urge
To speak with God,
God will definitely give you a chance.

25966

Our gratitude-heart
Is the best dwelling place
For our Lord Supreme.

25967

When the outer eyes are blinded
And wrong, undivine forces are not seen,
It is so easy to go away from
One's own divinity.

25968

Self-indulgence-days
Do not last for long.
This is what we must always learn.

25969

Our spiritual life becomes precious
Only when our aspiration-heart
Becomes consciously spacious.

25970

We must never give up our ascent
To the highest peak of perfection,
For that is what
We all have to ultimately reach.

25971

Everything is beautiful in life,
But one thing is most beautiful,
And that is our prayer-tears.

25972

Each important figure on earth
Has to be well prepared
To face the trials and tribulations
Of this world.

25973

Try to see yourself as a seeker
With no deplorable past.
Indulge not in the past,
Good or bad.
Only run towards the golden future.

25974

O my vital, your arrogance-ignorance
Has played its role.
Now I want you to play the role
Of dynamism-oneness in my life.

25975

There can be no heart-garden
Where there is no light.
A heart-garden is always inundated
With Divinity's Light.

25976

You believe in your own inadequacy
All the time.
But from now on,
You must try and succeed in having
An unmistakable belief
In your own adequacy.

25977

First you have to ascend
And reach a certain goal.
Only then will you get the inner message
To transcend and transcend.

25978

O seeker, you should be happy and proud
And not sad
That God has appointed the world
To crush your ego.
It is all for your ultimate victory
And infinite joy.

25979

The natural quality of the heart
Is to turn towards God
And hasten towards God.

25980

In the spiritual life,
The speed of eagerness
Far surpasses the speed
Of everything else.

25981

Centuries of slumber
Our earthly life has enjoyed.
Now it is high time for human beings
To open their hearts
To Divinity's supreme Light.

25982

Ecstasy's fleeting second
Can make us feel
That God is not only for us
But He is also with us,
In our living breath.

25983

When will God come?
God will come
When my gratitude and surrender
Are playing all the time
Inside my heart-garden.

25984

Have a heart-smile!
Your smile is the power
Of your outer success.
Your smile is the light
Of your inner progress.

25985

Love-devotion-surrender-seekers
And lovers
Are indeed very, very rare.

25986

Something that never wants to change:
Our unwillingness to be a member
Of God's world-transformation-game.

25987

Before the touch
Of ruthless sorrow,
Can we not go and touch
God's Forgiveness-Feet?

25988

Life came into existence
Not only to see and feel peace,
But to distribute peace
All over the globe.

25989

Give God a soulful smile.
To your wide surprise,
He will come
And dry all your tears.

25990

Every day
We must empty our mind
Of expectation-stupidity.

25991

If we can believe in the capacity
Of our all-illumining soul,
Then our soul will teach us
How to sail
Even through the treacherous seas
Of ignorance-night.

25992

I do not want anything in my life
To play the role of a scholar.
I want to remain an eternal student,
Even in my God-surrender-life.

25993

God does not want us to be
Desire-prisoners.
He wants us to be
Aspiration-distributors.

25994

The incredible wonder
Of my world
Is my tiny aspiration-heart-cry.

25995

I have been longing for many years
To return to my true religion.
My true religion
Is my Lord's only Satisfaction.

25996

How can I reach You, my Lord?
"My child, start climbing!
I shall be there to help you."

25997

If depression has found its home
Inside you,
Then you will always be deprived
Of God's Attention.

25998

I am not alone on my heart-pilgrimage.
God's Compassion-Eye-Smiles
I devotedly and proudly carry
With me.

25999

My Lord,
Do tell me one thing
That I must never forget.

"My child,
You are My Eternity's bloom,
You are My Infinity's blossom,
And you are My Immortality's
Dream-fulfilling reality."

26000

Do you want to see miracles
Performed by God?
Your sleepless love,
Your breathless devotion
And your fulness-surrender —
What are these,
If not three great miracles
Performed by God Himself
For you to watch?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 260, Agni Press, 1998
