May 11

How great is our God? Our God is greater than our imagination, purer than our inspiration, swifter than our aspiration, surer than our concentration, deeper than our meditation, higher than our contemplation and wiser than our realisation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
