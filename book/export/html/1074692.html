128. I become

Slowly and steadily
With silver tears
I see the Head of God.

Lovingly and silently
With gold tears
I breathe the Heart of God.

Constantly and unconditionally
With diamond tears
I become the Feet of God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 3, Agni Press, 1973
