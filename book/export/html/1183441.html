305. Choice Hour

Sacrifice-star
You have it.
You do not need any more.

Love-moon
You do not have.
You need it ever more.

Perfection-sun
God has kept aside for you.
He is just waiting
For His choice Hour.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
