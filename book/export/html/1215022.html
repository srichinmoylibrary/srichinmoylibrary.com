XII

Transliteration

Saha nau avatu, saha nau bhunaktu
Saha viryam karavavahai.

Translation

May He protect us together.
May He own us together.
May He make unto us vigour and virility.

Comment

God, Guru, and disciple:
God is Compassion and Protection;
Guru is Concern;
The disciple is dedication.
When these three work together, perfect Perfection shines, and it will shine through eternity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Upanishads: the Crown of India's Soul, Agni Press, 1974
