Ei Jibaner Pratikaje Tomar Parichoy Ma

Ei jibaner pratikaje
Tomar parichoy ma
Tomar kripai amai jena nahoi sangshoy
Amar shram je shweta shatadal
Dheke ache tomar padatal
Amar khudra praner pare
Sukher swapna biraj kare
Seito tomar kripar parichoy
Ma tomar tyage amar habe joy
Tomar dhyane karo amai
Niyata tanmoy
Ei jibane pratikhane
Tomari hok joy

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
