679. Take life easy

Take life easy,
For life is God's playground of transformation
And
Man's satisfaction-school.
Always take life easy.
This is the only way
To maintain
Serenity
In earth's mental asylum.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
