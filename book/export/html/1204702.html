18878

There is a special joy in waiting
For God's Arrival.
There is no end to its blessings.
Therefore, my Lord,
I shall not grudge
If my waiting for You
Has no end.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 189, Agni Press, 1993
