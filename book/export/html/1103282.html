23 January 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
Your Nearness-Presence
Is unimaginably blissful.
Your Remoteness-Presence
Is unforgettably painful.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Bandung, Indonesia. 23 January 1999, 7:04 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. two-hundred and sixteen times with one arm. (109 right, 107 left in five sets)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
