2713

Do not mislead yourself
Anymore.
If your heart is not fit
For the perfection-life,
How can you expect your mind,
Vital and body
To be fit for the perfection-life?
No! The heart has to be ready first.
Then only the mind, vital and body
Will follow it.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 28, Agni Press, 1984
