310 Death And God...

DEATH AND GOD

Only two persons
On earth and in heaven
Do not contradict.

These are Death and God.

Death-hush knows not
How to contradict.
God-Wisdom feels
It is not worthwhile to contradict.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 7, Agni Press, 1973
