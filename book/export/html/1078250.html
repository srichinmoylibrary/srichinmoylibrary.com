603 I Have Given You...

I HAVE GIVEN YOU

I have given you,
Already given you,
And
I am ready to give you even more,
In case you need more,
My dear friend.
Strangely enough,
You have never shown
Any sign of gratitude
For my God-manifestation,
God-perfection
Gifts.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
