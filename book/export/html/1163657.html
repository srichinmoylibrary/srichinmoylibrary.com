14. Swift progress

Swift is your progress
When you discover your soul's colour.
Swifter is your progress
When you discover your soul's mission
on earth.
Swiftest is your progress
When you discover that your soul
Is your earth-child and Heaven-Father.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The silence-song, Agni Press, 1975
