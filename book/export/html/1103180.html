15 November 1998 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I am asking You to bless me
With satisfaction.
You are telling me that right now
What I need from You
Is instruction,
And not satisfaction.
Satisfaction will come in its own way
At the right time.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

15 November 1998, 6:37 p.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
