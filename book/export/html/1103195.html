21 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
My Heaven
Is inside Your Compassion-Eye.
My Heaven's Heaven
Is at Your Forgiveness-Feet.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

21 November 1998, 4:52 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm once.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
