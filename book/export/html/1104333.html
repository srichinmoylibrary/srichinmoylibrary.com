24 July 2000 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Every day I prayerfully and soulfully
Celebrate God's Will
With my gratitude-heart-tears
And smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 July 2000, 9:09 a.m. Before practising for 500-lb. seated double-dumbbell lift.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
