What Can I Do?

What can I do?
With God I can converse.
What can I do?
In peace I can immerse.
What can I do?
I beckon the soul of feeble dole.
What can I do?
I produce God-soul
     And
Pave the way to perfection-goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 1, , 1977
