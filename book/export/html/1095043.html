My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 17

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to New Zealand in December 2002.

15 December 2002 ...

15 December 2002

Taupo, New Zealand

1 The Heart ...

The heart
Of every real God-seeker
Is God-obedience.

2 My Heart And I ...

My heart and I
Bow and bow and bow
To everything
That my Lord blessingfully says
And compassionately does.

3 A True Seeker ...

A true seeker
Spends all his time
On God’s Needs
And does not spend even a second
On his own needs.

4 Perfect Perfection ...

Perfect perfection
Is not a mental hallucination.
Perfect perfection
Is a distant tomorrow’s realisation
In every aspect of life.

5 My God-Surrender-Life ...

My God-surrender-life
Is the only proper way
For me to aspire.

6 Nobody ...

Nobody
Can ever complete
The inner education.

7 We All Must Develop ...

We all must develop
An intense and immense
Attraction to God.

8 Each Seeker ...

Each seeker
Is an unhorizoned dream
Of God’s Vision-Eye.

16 December 2002 ...

16 December 2002

Taupo, New Zealand

9 A Lion-Heart Is Indispensable ...

A lion-heart is indispensable
To win
Life’s battle-victory.

10 Sleepless And Breathless Tears ...

Sleepless and breathless tears
Show us the shortest way
To the highest Heaven of delight.

11 The Desire-Bound Human Beings ...

The desire-bound human beings
Suffer from chronic
Doubt-afflictions.

12 Every Day ...

Every day
I make a bold attempt
To extinguish my life’s
Desire-fire.

13 My Utter Desire-Failure ...

My utter desire-failure
Was the very beginning
Of my aspiration-success.

14 My Soul’s Inner Poise ...

My soul’s inner poise
I apply
In every disturbing situation.

15 May My Heart Remain ...

May my heart remain
Always hungry
For oneness-happiness.

16 God’s Silence-Eye ...

God’s Silence-Eye
Is
The world-awakening bell.

17 God Never Demands ...

God never demands
More than I can give Him
Cheerfully.

18 Our Response To God’s ...

Our response to God’s
Compassion-Eye-Invitation
Has to be compulsory
And not voluntary.

18 December 2002 ...

18 December 2002

Wellington, New Zealand

19 I Must Climb Up ...

I must climb up
The mountain-summit
Of God-worship.

20 Our Negativity-Mind ...

Our negativity-mind
Has to be crushed
At every moment.

21 Every Morning My Heart Feels ...

Every morning my heart feels
That my soul and my Lord Supreme
Are having most serious
And most intimate conversations.

22 The Greatest Joy And The Highest Pride ...

The greatest joy and the highest pride
I get
Only when I see my heart-tears
Are self-givingly mingling
With the golden dust of my Lord’s Feet.

23 Even A Moment ...

Even a moment
Of our unconditional surrender
To God’s Will
Brings us very close to God.

24 Today I Have Utterly Destroyed ...

Today I have utterly destroyed
My mind’s God-disobedience-tree —
Root and branch.

25 Let My Life’s Only Motto Be ...

Let my life’s only motto be
Aspiration —
Sleepless aspiration
And breathless aspiration.

26 May My Lord’s Absolute Victory ...

May my Lord’s absolute Victory
In my life
Be imminent.

20 December 2002 ...

20 December 2002

Christchurch, New Zealand

27 Every Morning I Receive ...

Every morning I receive
Most beautiful Smiles
From God’s Transcendental Eye.

28 Every Evening I Receive ...

Every evening I receive
Most powerful Blessings
From God’s Universal Heart.

29 God Himself ...

God Himself
Proudly beats
My surrender-victory-drum.

30 God’s Vision-Eye ...

God’s Vision-Eye
Is the home
Of all my sweet dreams.

31 God’s Compassion-Affection ...

God’s Compassion-Affection-
Love-Embrace
Is for all,
Born and yet unborn.

32 My Life-Road Is Absolutely Clear ...

My life-road is absolutely clear
Only when I faithfully listen
To the dictates of my aspiration-heart.

33 The Mind Does Not Believe ...

The mind does not believe
In God-gratitude,
But the heart does —
It always does.

34 When My Heart-Bird Sings ...

When my heart-bird sings,
God immediately stops
Whatever He is doing.

21 December 2002 ...

21 December 2002

Christchurch, New Zealand

35 God Tells Me ...

God tells me
That my God-devotion-magnet
Is not only most powerful,
But also most beautiful.

36 The Ego Does Not Want To Die ...

The ego does not want to die,
No matter what happens.
The soul does not want to give up
Its God-manifestation-promise,
No matter what happens.

37 When My Heart-Tears ...

When my heart-tears
Send an invitation
To my mind,
Even my mind does not dare
To ignore.

38 May Every Day ...

May every day
My earth-existence
Feel a very close connection
With God’s Heart-Home.

39 My Aspiration-Heart ...

My aspiration-heart
Responds only
To tomorrow’s rainbow-dawn.

40 God’s Name-Repetition ...

God’s Name-repetition
Embodies all my prayers
And all my meditations.

41 My Heart-Tears ...

My heart-tears
Come from my soul’s
Beginningless journey.

42 My Life-Smiles ...

My life-smiles
Come from my soul’s
Endless journey.

43 From Time Immemorial ...

From time immemorial,
This world of ours
Has been in bondage-night.
Alas, we have no idea
When it will be able to celebrate
Its independence-delight.

44 No God-Dream ...

No God-dream
Can ever be empty.

45 The Love Of The Heart ...

The love of the heart
Blooms.
The oneness of the soul
Blossoms.

22 December 2002 ...

22 December 2002

Christchurch, New Zealand

46 In The Morning ...

In the morning
My Beloved Supreme
Is lovable and adorable.
In the evening
My Absolute Supreme
Is incomparable and approachable.

47 My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
Please, please stop
Your separation-game!
Can You not see
That my eyes are crying
And my heart is bleeding for You?

48 May My God-Service-Joy ...

May my God-service-joy
Forever remain
Unparalleled.

49 The Divine In Us ...

The divine in us
Loves God-dictation.
The human in us
Loves man-quotation.

50 The Compassion-Eye ...

The Compassion-Eye
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme
Every morning clears the road
For my life’s progress-chariot.

51 Just Yesterday ...

Just yesterday
I was helpless and hopeless.
But today my Lord,
Out of His infinite Bounty,
Has made my heart useful
And my life fruitful.

52 God Is Asking Me ...

God is asking me
To make my pure devotion-dream
A sure manifestation-reality.

53 When I Am Without God ...

When I am without God,
My mind and I collect destruction-facts.
When I am with God,
My heart and I act and act and act
Lovingly, devotedly,
Self-givingly and breathlessly
For a new creation,
Empty of darkness.

54 A Sweet Surrender-Life-Joy ...

A sweet surrender-life-joy
Discovers Eternity’s Light
And Infinity’s Delight.

23 December 2002 ...

23 December 2002

Christchurch, New Zealand

55 The Beauty Of Love ...

The beauty of love,
The fragrance of devotion
And the sweetness of surrender
Are unimaginably exquisite.

56 My Wild ...

My wild
God-unwillingness-disobedience-
Extinction-hour
Is fast approaching.

57 God Desires My Life To Be ...

God desires my life to be
His Lotus-Heart-Home.

58 There Are Countless Things On Earth ...

There are countless things on earth
For us to feed,
But alas, why do we feed
Our desire-hunger-fire
That burns our God-aspiration,
God-dedication
And God-manifestation-life?

59 I Must Not Make ...

I must not make
My Lord’s Golden Boat heavier
With my expectation-frustration-
Heavy weights.

60 At Long Last ...

At long last
My age-old ignorance-prison
Is in flames.

61 The Master Teaches His Disciples ...

The Master teaches his disciples
How to hear
God’s Silence-Footsteps.

62 By Virtue Of My Prayers ...

By virtue of my prayers,
I increase immensely
My life-beauty.

63 On The Strength Of My Meditations ...

On the strength of my meditations,
I increase measurelessly
My heart-fragrance.

25 December 2002 ...

25 December 2002

Christchurch, New Zealand

64 The Morning-Devotion-Breath ...

The morning-devotion-breath
Of my heart
Awakens me and carries me
To the Heart-Home
Of my Lord Supreme.

65 I Must Not Do Anything ...

I must not do anything
Without having permission first
From my God-oneness-heart.

66 My Third Eye Sees Everything ...

My third eye sees everything.
My third eye knows everything.
My third eye becomes everything.
My third eye is everything.

67 Every Day ...

Every day
I must bask in the sunshine
Of my God-gratitude-heart.

68 My Eyes Are Wide Open ...

My eyes are wide open,
But alas,
My heart is fast asleep.

69 The Beauty Of My Tears ...

The beauty of my tears
Is in my God-adoration.

70 The Fragrance Of My Smiles ...

The fragrance of my smiles
Is in my God-surrender.

71 My Lord Beloved Supreme ...

My Lord Beloved Supreme
Will not allow me to know
What failure truly is.

72 My Mind Doubts ...

My mind doubts
My extraordinary capacities,
Yet I love my mind.
My wild vital kicks me day and night,
Yet I love my wild vital.
My body does not obey me,
Yet I love my body.
My Lord Supreme loves me,
Cares for me and protects me,
Yet I do not love my Lord Supreme.

26 December 2002 ...

26 December 2002

Christchurch, New Zealand

73 God Is At Once ...

God is at once
The sweetest Dream
Of my heart
And the farthest Reality
Of my eyes.

74 We Can Succeed ...

We can succeed
In transforming the world
Only with our love-smiles
And sympathy-tears.

75 I Ply My Life-Boat ...

I ply my life-boat
Between
Earth’s solemn duty-shore
And
Heaven’s ambrosial Beauty-Shore.

76 Heaven’s Nectar-Smiles ...

Heaven’s Nectar-Smiles
Bring us down.
Earth’s heart-tears
Return us.

77 My Life-Blood ...

My life-blood
Secretly embodies
All my prayers.

78 My Heart-Breath ...

My heart-breath
Silently embodies
All my meditations.

79 In The Inner World ...

In the inner world
I am a joy-living heart.

80 In The Outer World ...

In the outer world
I am a joy-giving life.

81 My Life Begs And Begs ...

My life begs and begs
My soul
To keep its eyes closed.

82 My Soul Begs And Begs ...

My soul begs and begs
My life
To keep its heart open.

83 Long Ago ...

Long ago
I far transcended
All my expectation-barriers.

84 Peace On Our Lips ...

Peace on our lips
We all have.
Peace in our hearts
We all sadly need.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 17, Agni Press, 2003
