My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 25

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Solo and Yogyakarta, Indonesia in December 2003.

13 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

1 Today I Am Determined ...

Today I am determined
To do everything
In God’s own Way.

2 To Have Purity ...

To have purity,
The seeker has to feel
That he is a child
Of the Eternal Now.

3 If You Are Searching For God ...

If you are searching for God
With a genuine thirst and hunger,
Then God will without fail
Appear before you.

4 My Life Is In Between ...

My life is in between
God’s Compassion-Arms
And His Forgiveness-Heart.

5 God’s Will Does Not Operate ...

God’s Will does not operate
In a doubting mind.
God’s Will operates only
In a self-giving life.

6 It Is Far Beyond The Capacity ...

It is far beyond the capacity
Of the mind
To think that God
Is all Forgiveness.

7 The Heart Feels ...

The heart feels
That God is sleepless
And unconditional Forgiveness.

8 Shut Up, My Mind! ...

Shut up, my mind!
My heart and I are ready
To fly
On our aspiration-wings.

9 Alas ...

Alas,
When God gives talks
On surrender,
The number of the seekers present
Is disappointing.

10 A Gratitude-Heart ...

A gratitude-heart
Is a very rare reality.

11 God-Closeness Depends Upon ...

God-closeness depends upon
The brightness and sincerity
Of our smile.

12 My Gratitude-Heart ...

My gratitude-heart
And my surrender-life
Are both needed
To fulfill God’s Dream on earth.

13 My Soul Allows My Heart ...

My soul allows my heart
To talk to God
Only on one condition:
My heart has to speak to God
Only about God.

14 December 2003 ...

14 December 2003

Solo, Indonesia

14 God Wants My Inner Experiences ...

God wants my inner experiences
To guide my outer life.

15 My Life ...

My life
Without fail
Tremendously improves
When I inspire others.

16 My Heart ...

My heart
Makes the fastest progress
When it establishes its oneness-delight
With the entire world.

17 Move Not Backward! ...

Move not backward!
Stark ignorance-night
Will once more tempt you
And devour you.

18 Move Forward! ...

Move forward!
God’s Compassion-Eye
And God’s Affection-Heart
Are beckoning you.

19 God Loves Me ...

God loves me
Not because I also love Him.
God loves me
Because I am ready,
I am willing
And I am eager
To love Him.

20 The Human ...

The human
In each human being
Always thinks differently.

21 The Divine In Us ...

The divine in us
Always thinks
Exactly the way
God wants us to think.

22 The Child-Heart-Loser ...

The child-heart-loser
With tremendous difficulties
Makes his spiritual progress
Inch by inch.

23 He Who Has Not Lost ...

He who has not lost
His child-heart
In his spiritual life
Is surprised by his own
Unimaginable progress.

24 When I Doubtfully Think ...

When I doubtfully think
That God loves me,
God immediately
Sheds tears of sorrow.

25 When I Unmistakably Feel ...

When I unmistakably feel
That God loves me,
God’s Eye immediately starts swimming
In His Infinity’s Ocean of Delight.

26 I Tell The World ...

I tell the world
That God dearly loves me.
The world asks me,
“Where is the proof?”

27 When The World Tells Me ...

When the world tells me
That God loves the world,
I tell the world,
“I need no proof,
For God has repeatedly told me
That loving
Is the only thing He knows.”

15 December 2003 ...

15 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

28 My Happy Heart ...

My happy heart
Is the only thing
That my Lord wants to have
From me.

29 Every Thought — ...

Every thought —
Encouraging or discouraging —
Has a magnetic power.

30 My Lord Has A Very Special Love ...

My Lord has a very special Love,
Affection and Fondness
For the heart-blossoms
Of my life.

31 Do Not Try To Escape Your Past ...

Do not try to escape your past.
Challenge and conquer your past
Once and for all,
So that you can march forward,
Dive inward and fly upward.

32 Every Day We Must Say ...

Every day we must say
Our God-surrender-prayers
Early in the morning
And in the evening.

33 My God-Gratitude-Thrill ...

My God-gratitude-thrill
Can never be described.

34 May I Be The Possessor ...

May I be the possessor
Of an enthusiasm-heart
And a sleepless prayer-life
To please my Lord
At every moment.

35 My Heart ...

My heart,
You must give your undivided attention
To the soul
When the soul comes down
To give you God-lessons.

36 Out Of His Infinite Bounty ...

Out of His infinite Bounty,
My Lord has taken me away,
Far beyond the domain
Of confusion-mind-darkness-night.

37 Desire Needs Us ...

Desire needs us
Desperately.
Aspiration we need
Sleeplessly.

38 If I Do Not Aspire ...

If I do not aspire,
I shall not be able to conquer
My ego’s self-advertisement-pride.

39 A God-Crying Heart ...

A God-crying heart
I am —
Two God-smiling eyes as well.

40 Be A Self-Giver ...

Be a self-giver
Before you become a God-dreamer.
Be a God-dreamer
Before you become a God-lover.

41 All Our Heart-Progress-Days ...

All our heart-progress-days
Shall be garlanded
By God Himself.

42 To Be An Atheist ...

To be an atheist
Is easier
Than believing in God.

43 To Believe In God ...

To believe in God
Is easier
Than thinking of God.

44 To Think Of God ...

To think of God
Is easier
Than praying to God.

45 To Pray To God ...

To pray to God
Is easier
Than loving God.

46 To Love God ...

To love God
Is easier
Than obeying God.

47 To Obey God ...

To obey God
Is easier
Than having a sleepless
God-oneness-meditation.

48 To Meditate On God ...

To meditate on God
Is easier
Than pleasing and fulfilling God
All the time.

49 To Please And Fulfill God ...

To please and fulfill God
Is easier
Than becoming a God-representative
On earth.

50 To Become A God-Representative ...

To become a God-representative
Is easier
Than manifesting God
Throughout the length and breadth
Of the world.

51 To Manifest God All-Where ...

To manifest God all-where
Is to put an end
To the Cosmic Game
Of comparison.

16 December 2003 ...

16 December 2003

Yogyakarta, Indonesia

52 Dream-Lover ...

Dream-lover
I was.

53 Action-Believer ...

Action-believer
I now am.

54 Self-Giver ...

Self-giver
I shall become.

55 God-Knower ...

God-knower
My Ultimate Goal is.

56 I Can Have True Love Of God ...

I can have true love of God
If I can go far beyond the frontiers
Of all man-made
And man-prophesised religions.

57 Self-Doubters ...

Self-doubters
Are bound to be
Their inner happiness-losers
Plus their goal-destroyers.

58 Each Seeker ...

Each seeker
Has to face ignorance-night
Undaunted.

59 Do Not Take The World ...

Do not take the world
As a meaningless, useless
Object-display.

60 Take The World ...

Take the world
As God’s full
Manifestation-Promise-Land.

61 My Lord ...

My Lord,
I have very powerfully clasped
Your Feet.
No ignorance-force
Can dare to take me away.

62 The Real Spiritual Life ...

The real spiritual life
Is a one-way
Progress-delight-road.

63 Each Heart ...

Each heart
Is
A God-singing song.

64 The Compassion-Eye ...

The Compassion-Eye
Of my Beloved Supreme
Thunders
At my disobedience.

65 I Must Pay All Attention ...

I must pay all attention
To the beauty of God’s Creation
And not indulge
In my self-creation.

66 I Really Love To Take Shelter ...

I really love to take shelter
Under the Sovereignty-Sky
Of my Beloved Supreme.

67 To My Greatest Joy ...

To my greatest joy,
My God-manifestation-hope-flood
Is coming back.

68 Physical Hunger-Nourishment ...

Physical hunger-nourishment
Is needed
To sustain the body.

69 Spiritual Hunger-Nourishment ...

Spiritual hunger-nourishment
Is indispensable
If God is to be realised.

70 Do Not Retire! ...

Do not retire!
If you retire,
God’s Pride
Will not hire you.

71 The Mind ...

The mind
Cannot come out of
The fact-factories.

72 The Heart ...

The heart
Can easily cross far beyond
The fact-boundaries.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 25, Agni Press, 2003
