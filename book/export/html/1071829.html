May 19

Think of God; your body's fleeting years will support you. Meditate on God; your soul's streaming years will transport you. When you are in your body, you are God's creation. When you are in your soul, you are God's realisation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
