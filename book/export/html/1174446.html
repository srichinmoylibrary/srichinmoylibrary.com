1508 My Nights Are Fractured

My nights are fractured
Because I do not
Sleeplessly pray and meditate
The entire night.

My days are fractured
Because I do not
See the beauty of man
And
The Duty of God
The entire day.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 16, Agni Press, 1981
