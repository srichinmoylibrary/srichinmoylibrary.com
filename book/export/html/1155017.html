41577

My Lord,
How do I know
That You really care for me?
“My child,
If you can shed helpless tears,
Each helpless tear will make you feel
How much I care for you.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 42, Agni Press, 2005
