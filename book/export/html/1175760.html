2809 What Have You Lost?

What have you lost
Since you have accepted the spiritual life?
Your mind has lost
Its stupidity.
Your heart has lost
Its impurity.
Your life has lost
Its insecurity.
Now tell me, would they have been
Really worthwhile to keep?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 29, Agni Press, 1982
