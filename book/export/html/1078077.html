430. Truth-Life begins

Is it in Heaven
That truth-life begins?
No, definitely not!

Is it in the sun
That Truth-life begins?
No, definitely not!

Is it in the moon
That truth-life begins?
No, definitely not!

Is it in the stars
That truth-life begins?
No, definitely not!

Is it in the sky
That truth-life begins?
No, definitely not!

Is it in my heart-cry
That truth-life begins?
Yes, a thousand times yes!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
