845. Beauty, purity, duty and divinity

Beauty glows
In the eyes of the man
Who sees.

Purity flows
In the heart of the man
Who feels.

Duty sings
In the arms of the man
Who works.

Divinity dances
In the soul of the man
Who loves.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 17, Agni Press, 1973
