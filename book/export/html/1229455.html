Hatashai Bhara E Jiban Mor

Hatashai bhara e jiban mor kahare dekibe aj
Ke parabe dehe asaktihin dhruba karmer saj
Ogo sundar laho nati mor dao more bishwas
Purnata ar amarata taba mor shudhu abhilash

Translation:

Doomed to disappointment
Is this life of mine.
Whom can I call today?
Who will help me don the robe of detachment?
Who will help me don the robe of truth-action?
O Beautiful One,
Do accept my heart’s obeisance.
Give me faith in boundless measure.
Your Infinitude and Your Immortality
Can alone please me,
      Within, without.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Pole-star promise-light, part 3, , 1977
