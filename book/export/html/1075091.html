519 Everyone Asks Me For A Favour...

EVERYONE ASKS ME FOR A FAVOUR

Doubt asks me
To cherish him
And boast of him.

Fear asks me
To nourish her
And boast of her.

Courage asks me
To feed him
And look after him.

Faith asks me
To strengthen her
And immortalise her.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 11, Agni Press, 1973
