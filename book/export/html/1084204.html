25. He wants both

He wants both
Humanity and Divinity:
Humanity to wash his feet,
Divinity to breathe its love.

He wants both
God and man:
God to pull him towards God,
Man to push him towards God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 5, Agni Press, 1974
