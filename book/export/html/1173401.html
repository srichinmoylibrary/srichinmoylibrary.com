474 If Not Grateful

Soulfully I am able
To place myself at Your Feet.
Therefore I am really happy,
If not grateful.

Constantly I am able
To think of You.
Therefore I am exceedingly happy,
If not grateful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 5, Agni Press, 1979
