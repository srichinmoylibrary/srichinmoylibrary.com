38. They listen to me

The morning-silence
Listens to me;
Therefore
I am so pure.
The noon-sound
Listens to me;
Therefore
I am so sure.
The evening-light
Listens to me;
Therefore
I am never obscure,
I am never neglected,
I am never rejected,
And
My heart-love has become
What my soul-life eternally is.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 12, Agni Press, 1974
