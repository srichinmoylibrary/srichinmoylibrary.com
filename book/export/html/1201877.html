16081

In the morning
When I offer my obedience
To my Lord Supreme,
I feel I am a real member
Of His Heart-Family.

In the evening
When I offer my soulful gratitude
To my Lord Supreme,
I feel I am a true member
Of His Inner Circle.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 161, Agni Press, 1991
