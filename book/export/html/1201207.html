15417

No one told me,
But from my personal experience I know
That God will be happy
If we all give up
Our earth-bound desire-life
And embrace Heaven-free aspiration-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 155, Agni Press, 1991
