33. Why am I so fond of you?

"Lord, is it because You are great
That I am fond of You?
Lord, is it because You are good
That I am so fond of You?"
No, your reasons are incorrect.
You are fond of Me for another reason.
You are fond of Me
Because in Me you see I
Your own fulfilled capacity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The silence-song, Agni Press, 1975
