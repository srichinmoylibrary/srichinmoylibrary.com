47 I Have The Time...

I HAVE THE TIME

I have no time
To think of great possibilities.
I have no time
To think of grand opportunities.
I have the time
Only for dedicated activities
And blossoming necessities.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 16, Agni Press, 1974
