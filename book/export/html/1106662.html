44.

God tells us
That He has already erased
His dissatisfaction-memories.
How is it that we
Are not doing the same?
Why? Why? Why?
Why do we have to wait
For the arrival of the next century?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The New Millennium, Agni Press, 1999
