8468 The Human Mind

The human mind
Does not want to know
Where God is.

The human heart
Does not know
What God looks like.

The human life
Does not understand
Why God delays.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 85, Agni Press, 1983
