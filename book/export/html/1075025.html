454. The same palace

Truth and dignity
Share the same palace.
Love and joy
Share the same palace.
Father and son
Share the same palace.
Faith and doubt
Share not the same palace.
Fear and courage
Share not the same palace.
Alas, ingratitude has no palace, none at all.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 10, Agni Press, 1973
