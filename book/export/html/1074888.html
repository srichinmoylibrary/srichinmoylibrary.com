320 Before Columbus Discovers You...

BEFORE COLUMBUS DISCOVERS YOU

Don't sleep,
Get up at least.
Don't stand still,
Walk a little at least.

Before Columbus
Dares to discover you,
Fly your wings
Into the unknowable Beyond
Where
Your doubt-Columbus,
Your fear-Columbus
Shall have no access.

There you can reign
Unseen,
Unchallenged,
Unfathomed,
Sovereign,
Absolute,
Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 7, Agni Press, 1973
