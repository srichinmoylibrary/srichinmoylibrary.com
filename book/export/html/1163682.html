39. His envy-life

His envy-life never dies!
There was a time
When he envied the world-champion athletes.
There was a time
When he envied the world-philosophers
and saints.
And now whom does he envy?
He envies his soul in its purest beauty.
He envies his God in His absolute Perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The silence-song, Agni Press, 1975
