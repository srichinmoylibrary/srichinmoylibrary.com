22. Father and son

Father, emptiness on Your Face.
How can I bear it?
Son, fruitlessness in your heart.
How can I bear it?
Father, I am sorry that I have
disappointed You.
Son, I am sorry that I have not appointed you
As a world-lover,
As a world-sufferer,
As a world-liberator.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When I left God in Heaven, Agni Press, 1975
