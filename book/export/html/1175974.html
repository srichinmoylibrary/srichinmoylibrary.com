3021 Tomorrow's Silence-Beauty

The moment he turned his back
On the past,
The golden dawn
Of tomorrow's silence-beauty
Invite him to ride
In the chariot of the transcendental Sun-God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 31, Agni Press, 1982
