117. My two worlds

Truth, where are you
In my vast world?
Falsehood, where are you not
In my tiny world?
Desire, how long will you remain
The bridge between
My tiny world
and
My vast world?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sound becomes, silence is, Agni Press, 1975
