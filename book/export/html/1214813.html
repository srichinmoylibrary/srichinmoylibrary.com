20. Aspiration

            Yesterday
   What we called aspiration
                 Today
We call that very thing realisation.
            And tomorrow
   We shall call that very thing
            God-satisfaction
                     In
        God-manifestation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Union and oneness, Agni Press, 1976
