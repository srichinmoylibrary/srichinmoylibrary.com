838 Today I Am A Little Tired...

TODAY I AM A LITTLE TIRED

Be careful,
Arms of falsehood!
Tomorrow I shall challenge
Your power.
Today I am a little tired.

Be careful,
Eyes of ignorance!
Tomorrow I shall challenge
Your vision.
Today I am a little tired.

Be careful,
Head of Satan!
Tomorrow I shall challenge
Your brain.
Today I am a little tired.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
