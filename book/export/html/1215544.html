37. Is your soul troubled?

Is your soul troubled?
Then I tell you,
Think of your claim:
     Your God's Greatness-Height.
Is your life troubled?
Then I tell you,
Think of your life's nothingness-might.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When God-Love descends, Agni Press, 1975
