9018 Two Messages

What I needed was an orphan-cry.
This was the message
That I received from my Lord Supreme
Last year.

What I need is an emperor-smile.
This is the message
That I have received from my Lord Supreme
This year.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 91, Agni Press, 1983
