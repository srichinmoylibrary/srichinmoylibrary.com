35.

If you know only
How to love yourself
And how to care
For yourself,
Then God will never
Invite you
To be a member
Of His Perfection-
Manifestation-Team
On earth.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My gratitude-tears and God's Satisfaction-Smiles, Agni Press, 1993
