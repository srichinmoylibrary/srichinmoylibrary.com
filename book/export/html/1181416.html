8409 Compete Only For Compassion-Light

O my mind,
Do not compete with my heart
For supremacy.
If you want to compete
With anything or anyone,
Then compete only for
God's constant Compassion-Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 85, Agni Press, 1983
