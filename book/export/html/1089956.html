Ignorance

Ignorance dear, I know who you were,
Who you are,
And who you will be.

Who were you?
You were my self-imposed lord.
Who are you?
You are my surrendered slave.
Who will you be?
You will be my God-perfected soldier divine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Light-delight-journeys, Agni Press, 1974
