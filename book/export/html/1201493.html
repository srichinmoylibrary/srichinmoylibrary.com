Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 158

15701

My life
Is not a passing pleasure.
It is a lasting treasure
Of God.

15702

Do not stand against
God's Compassion-Rule.
God's Justice-Light
Will not tolerate it
Indefinitely.

15703

Again and again
God's Compassion-Rain descends.
But alas, we do not value
Its all-loving, all-caring
Descent.

15704

You are your mind's disobedience.
You are your heart's disobedience.
You are your life's disobedience.
Therefore,
You and your sad defeat
Must live and breathe together
In failure-jungle.

15705

A powerful Smile
From my Lord Supreme
Is my heart's happiness-heritage.

15706

So many times
God has saved you
With His Compassion-Life-Preserver.
But you have never offered
Your gratitude-heart
To your Lord Supreme.

15707

God will allow me
To enter into
His Promised Peace-Land
Only after I have become a member
Of His Oneness-Heart-Garden.

15708

I surprised God
With my heart's genuine God-hunger.
God surprised me
With His Compassion's
Unconditional Nectar-Rain.

15709

If it is true
That God loves my reality-life,
Then He loves
My heart's dream-boat-journey
Infinitely more.

15710

Do you want to touch
God's Heart?
Then become either
The beauty and fragrance
Of a flower
Or the cry and smile
Of a child.

15711

My Lord is telling me
That the more I widen
My heart's happiness-smile,
The sooner He will be able
To manifest
His transcendental Divinity
In and through me.

15712

What can poor God do?
He will never be satisfied
With your mind's insincerity-cry.

15713

Do not travel temptation-road
Either with your mind
Or with your vital.
Pray to your soul
To transform your temptation-road
Into aspiration-road
For your safety
And for your perfection.
Your soul will do it for you.

15714

Every day
God the Creator
Sends His Smiles
To God the creation,
And God the creation
Sends its cries
To God the Creator.

15715

Everything that a human being does
Is too slow.
He has his own time,
His own rhythm.
Poor God has completely abandoned
His expectation-hour
When He deals with human beings.

15716

Everything
Has a beginning and an end.
In the case of doubt,
Its last stop is total destruction.

15717

With my heart-tears
Every day I wash the Feet
Of my Lord Supreme.
After I finish washing the Feet
Of my Lord Supreme,
He invites me to come
And dine with Him
In His own Heart-Garden.

15718

Anxiety and insincerity-attacks
Are so powerful.
Unfortunately, they do not overlook
Any mortal.

15719

An aspiration-seeker,
A dedication-server
And a perfection-lover:
God wants these three
To be inseparable
And immortal friends.

15720

When I visit
My Lord's Heart-Palace,
He blesses me
With His own Heart's
Sunrise-Smile and Rainbow-Dawn.

15721

Danger hovers over you
Due to your own carelessness,
Which is nothing short of
An unwanted hostile attack.

15722

When you stop being indulgent
To your mind and vital,
You are bound to see yourself
Swimming in the sea
Of sleepless delight.

15723

Everybody starts
With a life-dream.
Alas,
Very few life-dreams
Are fulfilled in any century.

15724

If you continue doubting,
Then one day
You will be forced to fear,
And your fear
Will surrender one day
To hopeless suffering.

15725

Each time I have
A sweet, encouraging
And inspiring hope
In my heart,
I feel that I am seated
At the foot of my life's peace-tree.

15726

God never allows
His selfless seekers
To cut short
Their heart-aspiration
And life-dedication
On earth.

15727

Every morning
My soul encourages me
And inspires me
To cross beyond the happiness-horizons
And bring light and delight
From there
To transform the face and fate
Of humanity.

15728

We suffer
When there is an outer catastrophe.
But do we really care
About our inner catastrophes:
Fear, doubt and anxiety?
Our inner catastrophes
At every moment
Are not only haunting us
But destroying our inner life-breath.

15729

The mind's hesitation,
The heart's frustration
And the life's destruction
Are forced to run
Towards an unknown destination.

15730

For so many years
I was enslaved
By my mind's insecurities,
Jealousies and impurities.
But now my Lord Supreme
Has unconditionally saved me
From my titan-captors.

15731

Every time a human being
Does something divinely great
And supremely good,
God immediately broadcasts it
On His Heart-Radio.

15732

My soul observes
My soul's birthday promise
Every day.
And what is that promise?
"God for God's sake,
God's Satisfaction for God's sake.
Everything on earth,
Everything in Heaven,
Only for God's sake."

15733

A desire-life
Will, without fail,
Need radical purification
Before it is allowed
To enter into the heart-beauty's
Aspiration-garden.

15734

If you are
Entirely dependent on God
In your inner life
And your outer life,
Then God is sleeplessly responsible
For the perfection of your outer life
And the satisfaction of your inner life.

15735

It is our psychic promises
That are eventually fulfilled.
Our mental promises,
Emotional promises
And all other promises
Quite often are not fulfilled.
Why?
Because they do not have
The necessary perseverance,
Their way of seeing the truth
Is not clear
And their goal itself
Is unclear.

15736

My Lord Supreme,
I have prayed to You
For many things in this life.
Now I would like You
To fulfil only one desire of mine.
My Lord,
Only save me from myself!

15737

When my mind-ego
Plays the role of a genuine seeker,
I see the aspiration-theatre
Is completely empty.

15738

I shall not allow
My soul-child
To be completely lost
In my mind's confusion-jungle.

15739

My desire to know,
My desire to love
And my desire to become divine and perfect
Are my only desires in life.

15740

Forgiveness
Is by far the best
Freedom-delight
In God's entire creation.

15741

In God's Eye
Your heart's aspiration-cry
Is as valuable as
Your soul's illumination-smile.

15742

O my human mind,
It is high time for you to realise
That your ancient desire-disease
Will lead you to utter destruction.

15743

My heart is always
An eager volunteer
In God's world-illumining
Activities.

15744

You do not have to face
A fruitless future.
It is you who can make
A fruitful, powerful
God-satisfaction-future
With your own self-giving life.

15745

There is not
A single book worth reading
In the ignorance-mind's
Stupidity-library.

15746

What each seeker needs
Is a spotless, faultless faith
So that he can run like a deer
Along the road
Of awakening spirituality-fulfilment.

15747

What does a desire-life do?
It only disappoints us,
Either at the very beginning
Of our journey,
Or in the middle, or at the end.
The desire-life-journey
Is always a disappointment
Either today or tomorrow
Or some other time.

15748

Each life is caught
By temptation-tentacles.
Again, each life can be liberated
By God's infinite Compassion
And man's sleepless aspiration.

15749

When his mind's teeming doubts
Said good-bye to him,
God immediately gave him
His adamantine Will
To manifest God in God's own Way
Here on earth.

15750

What is doubt
If not trouble,
Here, there and everywhere'?

15751

If you think that your mind
Is always a trouble-maker,
Then why don't you feel that your heart
Is always a problem-shooter?

15752

If you do not aspire,
Then you are bound to become
Sooner or later
An attachment, frustration
And destruction-hostage.

15753

There shall come a time
When it is you
Who will be doubting your doubts
And taking shelter
In your faith-flooded heart.

15754

Armed with the faith-sword,
Each truth-seeker and God-lover
Must run faster than the fastest
Along the road of God-manifestation.

15755

Instead of surrendering
To tomorrow's devouring hunger,
We must pray and meditate
To swim speedily
In the river of self-giving-delight.

15756

Alas, alas!
I clearly see that my mind
Has been totally and mercilessly attacked
By insincerity-insects.

15757

Who can measure
The ruthless offence
Of an ingratitude-mind
And a God-surrenderless life?

15758

Alas!
My self-indulgence
Consciously compels
My soul to starve.

15759

I want to become
A doer of God's Will
And not just remain
A hearer of God's Will.

15760

When we want to make
Enormous progress
In our spiritual life,
We must live in a thought-free zone.

15761

There is no such thing
As an aspiration-overflow.
There is no such thing
As a dedication-overflow.
There is no such thing
As a manifestation-overflow.
There can be only one overflow:
A desire-overflow.

15762

It is necessary
For each individual
To become wise, humble
And self-giving
If he wants to be
God's choice and perfect instrument.

15763

A good thought
Is always the starting point
Of our Heavenward journey.

15764

If you are reluctantly ready
To change your life,
Then God does not want it.
He cannot appreciate
Your reluctance-transformation.
What He needs and wants from you
Is a cheerful and willing
Transformation-beauty,
Transformation-perfection and
Transformation-satisfaction.

15765

In my case,
There is only one way
To make progress,
And that is through my life's
Unconditional surrender.

15766

God definitely deposits
His Blessing, Affection and Love
In your heart-bank.
But your mind
Secretly and stealthily withdraws them,
So that everything is depleted.

15767

God-lovers
Are always hired
By God Himself.
They never suffer from unemployment.

15768

My Lord,
My gratitude-heart
I should have offered to You
Long, long ago.
Now it is shamelessly overdue!

15769

I made some progress
In my inner life.
Then I became overconfident
And proud of myself.
Since then, it has all been downhill
In my spiritual life!

15770

When my heart's aspiration-flames
Were not burning properly,
I begged God to come.
He came only to tell me
That mine was not a serious case,
And that I would be cured.
And, in fact, He cured me
In the twinkling of an eye!
Now my aspiration-flames
Are burning bright, brighter, brightest.

15771

When I look at my soul
And my Lord Supreme
With my heart's oneness-eye,
I see that my soul is like a child
In the Lap of my Lord Supreme,
And that the child and the Father
Look most surprisingly alike!

15772

Our love for God
Should not be duty-bound.
Our willingness, soulfulness
And fruitfulness-love only
Is what God needs,
For that is the only way
We can eventually make ourselves happy.

15773

Our hope becomes
False and useless
Only when we make
Insincere and weak efforts.

15774

What will happen
If I forget God?
Then I will miss happiness,
I will miss perfection,
I will miss satisfaction.

15775

There will be a new world
Which will be created
By genuine truth-seekers and God-lovers.
In this new world,
Only God and His choice children
Will play, sing and dance.

15776

The mind oscillates
Between truth and falsehood.
Alas,
How can I free myself
From this mind-oscillation?
What I need is a mind
That permanently lives
In the heart of truth.

15777

To me, indecision is not something
Insignificant.
It is something significant,
Something destructive,
For it does not allow me
To go forward
To see the beauty of truth
And the light of joy.

15778

Alas,
When shall I be able
To liberate myself
From my mind's ever-thickening
Jungle-night?

15779

God has a very special Concern
For the newly-arrived souls.
He does not want them to mix freely
With the old souls,
Who have surrendered to
ignorance-night.
He wants these newly-arrived souls
To fight right from the beginning
Against ignorance-night
And establish His supreme Victory
Here on earth.

15780

Each human being by nature
Wants to know the truth.
But very few
Want to grow into the truth
And become truth-representative-messengers
On earth.

15781

God wants to see
This universe of His
Flooded not with equality-rivalry
But with equanimity-satisfaction.

15782

Man has turtle-speed.
Man has insect-ability.
Yet man is fond of being
Extolled to the skies
By the whole world.

15783

From our heart's aspiration-airport
We shall have to come
To our life's dedication-airport.
From our life's dedication-airport
We shall have to go to God's
Manifestation-Satisfaction-Airport.

15784

When I see the eagerness
Of a starving spiritual seeker,
I feel that the full manifestation
Of God on earth
Will soon take place.

15785

Do not allow your fear, doubt,
Anxiety and worry
To march before you
Proudly and shamelessly.
Capture them and transform them
Into choice instruments of God.

15786

Each human being
Should long for the time
When he will be flooded
With peace-prosperity.

15787

What has gone wrong
With your aspiration-cry?
I see there is no sincerity,
No purity, no intensity in it.
Alas,
Your aspiration-cry has lost
All its beauty, purity and divinity.

15788

My heart of unconditional surrender
Is God's supreme spokesman
On earth.

15789

Someday
No one will be born,
No one will live,
No one will die
In ignorance-prison-captivity.

15790

In the inner world
There is no substitute
For the deathless cry.
In the higher world
There is no substitute
For the infinite Satisfaction.

15791

When consciousness-calamity
Takes place,
Each man becomes
An expression of the animal world.

15792

My Lord,
Please do not bless me
With the outer-world riches.
I need only inner-world riches,
Which are aspiration-cries
And dedication-smiles.

15793

If you are a truth-seeker,
Eventually you will become
A God-lover.
Then you will see
That unless you become a God-server,
You will not be fully satisfied.

15794

Alas,
What rules my days and nights?
My days and nights are being ruled
By my mind's ego-extension,
Ego-dominion and ego-satisfaction.

15795

God is ready to offer you
His Compassion-Guidance.
But is your wild vital ready?
Will it ever be ready?

15796

If you feel that God's Compassion-Eye
Is a stranger to you,
Then your aspiration-heart
Will not see the beauty
And the fragrance
Of God's universal Sunrise.

15797

Where there is divine love,
There is a heart of oneness.
Where there is a heart of oneness,
There is a life of satisfaction.

15798

I pray to God
To take care of
My tiny aspiration-plants.
He tells me
That this is the only thing
He is well-qualified to do.

15799

No matter
What your critics say,
Pay no attention to them.
Just increase
Your own heart's cry
And your own soul's smile.

15800

I see the footsteps of unseen guests
Inside my heart.
I know, although they are unseen today,
Tomorrow they will be fully visible
To inspire me, help me and guide me
To the Reality supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 158, Agni Press, 1991
