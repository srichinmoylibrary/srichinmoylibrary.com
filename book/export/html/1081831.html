272. My power

My thinking-power
Is limited;
Therefore
I am saved.

My doubting-power
Is unlimited;
Therefore
I am encaged.

My loving-power
Is yet unmanifested;
Therefore
I am still frustrated.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
