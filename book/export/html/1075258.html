683 Quiet, Please!...

QUIET, PLEASE!

Quiet, please!
I wish to listen to Heaven.

Quiet, please!
I wish to speak to Earth.

Quiet, please!
I wish to worship God.

Quiet, please!
I wish to love humanity,

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 14, Agni Press, 1973
