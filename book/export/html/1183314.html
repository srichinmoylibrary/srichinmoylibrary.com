178. Do not suggest, just command

O my heart, do not suggest,
Just command.
I am ready, more than ready.
O my mind, do not command,
I allow you only to suggest.
I am not ready
And
I shall never be ready
to listen to your ignorance-commands.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
