9 November 1998 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
How can a true peace-dreamer
Be a stranger to me?
No, he is my friend,
He is my brother,
He is my oneness-heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

9 November 1998, 4:32 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs with each arm two times.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
