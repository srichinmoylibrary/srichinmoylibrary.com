44. Like yesterday

God cares for me;
Therefore,
Like yesterday,
Today for me
Is another aspiration-experience.
I care for God;
Therefore,
Like yesterday,
Today for God
Is another satisfaction-dawn.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
