26 October 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not know whether or not
   To enjoy a roaring laughter
When my mind says that it is
   The only God-knower,
When my heart says that it is
   The only God-lover
And when my life says that I am
   The only God-fulfiller.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 October 1999, 10:45 p.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 80 lbs. (81 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
