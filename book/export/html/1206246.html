20406

I must not destroy
My God-fulfilment-dream
With my self-doubt.
Reality is at God's Feet.
Dream is inside my heart.
Let me place my heart
At God's Feet,
Him to fulfil in His own Way
In and through me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 205, Agni Press, 1994
