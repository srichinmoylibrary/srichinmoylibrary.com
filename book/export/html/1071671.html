Mouse: Bearer

Mouse, my mouse,
You are the bearer of Ganapati,
The giver of realisation-plenitude.
Earth-consciousness knows
This truth sublime.
Therefore
To you, to you alone,
Earth-life is all gratitude.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Animal Kingdom, Agni Press, 1973
