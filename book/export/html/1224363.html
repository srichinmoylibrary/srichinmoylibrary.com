Ami Pathhara Pathik

Ami pathhara pathik
Ami dikhara nabik
Ami kajhara shramik
Ami ashahara premik

Translation:

I am a lost traveller.
I am the sailor
Who knows not where
The destination is.
I am the labourer
Who has lost his job.
I am the lover
Devoid of hope-flames.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 4, , 1981
