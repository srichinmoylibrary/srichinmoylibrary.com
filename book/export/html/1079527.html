March 24

Your mind is a prisoner. How can your prisoner-mind liberate his comrades: doubt, fear and suspicion? Impossible.
Your heart is a lover. How can your lover-heart bind her relatives: song, dance and smile? Impossible.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Flame-Goal, Agni Press, 1973
