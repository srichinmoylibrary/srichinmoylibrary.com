136. Thinking

You are too lazy to think.
He knows nothing but thinking.

The human world
Knows not how to think.
Therefore
It most deplorably sinks.

The divine world
Blesses the thought-sea,
Transforms the thought-sea,
And offers the will-sun
To the thought-sea.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 3, Agni Press, 1974
