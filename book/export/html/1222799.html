Keno Amai Nitya Nutan

Keno amai nitya nutan aghat diye jao
Bhenge amar hriday khani tripti bujhi pao

Translation:

Why do You strike me every day hard,
Very hard, in a new manner?
Do You get satisfaction
Smashing asunder my heart?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giti mala, , 2000
