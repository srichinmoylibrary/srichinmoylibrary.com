166. An impossible task

Imagine you know nothing.
I know this is a difficult task.
Imagine you have nothing.
I know this is a most difficult task.
Imagine you are nothing.
I know this is an impossible task.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
