7237

Where does he live?
He lives
In the green hills of hope.

Where did he live?
He lived
In the grey hills of doubt.

Where will he live?
He will live
In the blue hills of satisfaction.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 73, Agni Press, 1984
