182. He is a fool

He is a fool.
He lives in his determination-doubt.

He is a fool.
He forgets to treasure his surrendering breath.

He is a fool.
He tries to define
His ascending love of God
and
God's descending love of him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
