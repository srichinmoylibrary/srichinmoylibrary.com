20674

While looking at God's Eye,
I sing my first soulful song.
While placing myself at God's Feet,
I sing my second soulful song.
While entering into God's Heart-Garden,
I sing my third soulful song.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 207, Agni Press, 1994
