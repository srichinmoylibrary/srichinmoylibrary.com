2395 If Your Heart Is Empty

If your mind is empty of desires,
Then God Himself will feed you
His morning manna.

If your heart is empty of insecurities,
Then God Himself will feed you
His evening manna.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 24, Agni Press, 1982
