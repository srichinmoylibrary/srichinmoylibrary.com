18903

Our inalienable birthright
Is to claim God.
Our only goal is to become
His choicest and most perfect instrument
To love Him and serve Him
In His own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 190, Agni Press, 1993
