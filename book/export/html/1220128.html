Hridaye Kanna

Hridaye kanna
Manase kanna
Jibane kanna
Tai unnati ati druta gati

Translation:

Tearful cries in my mind,
Tearful cries in my heart,
Tearful cries in my life:
Therefore, my fastest progress.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Chander deshe, , 1991
