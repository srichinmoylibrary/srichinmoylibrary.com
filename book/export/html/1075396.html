818. Three opinions

What others think of me
I perfectly know —
"I am useless."

What God thinks of me
I always know —
"I am perfect."

What I think of myself
I do not know,
I shall not know,
Never.
Alas! Alas!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 17, Agni Press, 1973
