545. It's Too Late

It's too late.
I ask my soul to come.
I ask my heart to come
Personally.
Since time goes on
And waits for nobody,
I use the precious time
Here, right in front of me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
