54.

A TRUE disciple
Embarks on the spiritual journey
Thinking that his Master's way
Is by far the best way.
But while walking, marching, running
And sprinting
On his Master's way,
He realises that his Master's way
Is the only Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A true disciple, Agni Press, 1998
