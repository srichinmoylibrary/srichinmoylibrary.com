9139 Only One Breath

Between desire and faith
There is only one
Sweet and life-illumining breath.
When the mind desires
Something from God,
The heart feels
More faith in God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 92, Agni Press, 1983
