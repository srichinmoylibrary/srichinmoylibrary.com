My Soviet Sisters

My Soviet sisters,
Come to the fore!
Hide not, hide not;
Run to the Shore!
Lag not behind,
Lag not behind!
Treasures in us
The world must find.

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: The home of oneness-peace-dream-world songbook, , 2001
