30. The Inventors

Who invented life?
Man the lover.

Who invented death?
Man the thinker.

Who invented God?
Man the server.

Who invented Heaven?
Man the sufferer.

Who invented earth?
Man the dreamer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Dreams that fly, Agni Press, 1975
