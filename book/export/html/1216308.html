675. For God's sake, don't

O my mind,
For God's sake
Don't entangle yourself.

O my vital,
For God's sake
Don't strangle yourself.

O my heart,
For God's sake
Don't torture yourself.

O my body,
For God's sake
Don't belittle yourself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
