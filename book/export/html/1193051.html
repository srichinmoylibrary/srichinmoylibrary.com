7342

If you have a heart
Rich in divinity,
If you have a mind
Rich in sincerity,
And if you have a life
Rich in purity,
Then God will visit you
Long before you actually invoke
His Presence.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 74, Agni Press, 1984
