791. What I have left

Lord Supreme,
In the morning You counted my tears.
In the evening You heard my songs.
Now at night I give You what I have left:
A pure,
Serene
And spontaneous love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
