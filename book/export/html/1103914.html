26 October 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I am all gratitude to You,
For You have a very special
Secret and sacred Affection-Fondness
For my heart's silence-tears.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 October 1999, 10:08 p.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 400 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
