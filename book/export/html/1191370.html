5678

God the Creator,
With His infinite Vision,
His infinite Light and Delight,
Has created us.
We are part of His universe.
This universe is His
Dream-Reality's Satisfaction-Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 57, Agni Press, 1984
