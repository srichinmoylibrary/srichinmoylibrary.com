4.

Supreme!

You want me
To sacrifice
My life to You.
Never!
You do not value
My life,
To say the least.
Therefore,
I am buying a goat
For sacrifice
To satisfy You,
For I feel
The goat-sacrifice
Will be valued By You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sadness-heart-silence. Madness-mind-eloquence, Agni Press, 1998
