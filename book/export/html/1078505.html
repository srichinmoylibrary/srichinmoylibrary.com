858 What My Friends Tell Me...

WHAT MY FRIENDS TELL ME

My conscience-friend tells me
What to do.

My consciousness-friend tells me
What to become.

My God-friend tells me
Who I eternally am:
Another perfect God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
