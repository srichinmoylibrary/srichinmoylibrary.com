303. My fate

Body, you have bound me;
Yet I love you.
What can I do?
Indeed,
This is my fate.

Vital, you have weakened me;
Yet I love you.
What can I do?
Indeed,
This is my fate.

Mind, you have encaged me;
Yet I love you.
What can I do?
Indeed,
This is my fate.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
