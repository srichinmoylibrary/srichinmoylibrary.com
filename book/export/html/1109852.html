28. No explanation

Your speciality is:
You never complain.
His speciality is:
He never constrains.
My speciality is:
I never disdain.
God's speciality is:
He never explains.
Alas, God tells me
There is no necessity
For His explanation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The prayer of the sky, Agni Press, 1974
