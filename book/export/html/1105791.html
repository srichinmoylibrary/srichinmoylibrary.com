My spirituality

1.

My spirituality
Is
My heart's God-oneness-song.

2.

My spirituality
Is
My life's nature-transformation-gong.

3.

My spirituality
Is
My sleepless self-transcendence.

4.

My spirituality
Is
My breathless God-dependence.

5.

My spirituality
Is
My mind's emptiness-fulness.

6.

My spirituality
Is
My heart's divinity-universality.

7.

My spirituality
Is
My world-acceptance-beauty.

8.

My spirituality
Is
The fragrance of God-Forgiveness.

9.

My spirituality
Bravely accepts
The challenges of each new day.

10.

My spirituality
Knows that
My countless problem-solutions
Are my Lord's
Compassion-flooded uniqueness.

11.

My spirituality
Is
My constant yearning
For something beyond.

12.

My spirituality
Wants me to remember
Only one thing:
God answers.
He answers in His own
Inimitable Way.

13.

My body dies,
But not my spirituality,
Never!

14.

My spirituality
Plays hide-and-seek
Inside my sound-life
And
My silence-death.

15.

Although God loves hiding,
He never hides His Footsteps
From my spirituality-eye.

16.

My spirituality
Is
My readiness, willingness
And eagerness
For the satisfaction
Of those in need.

17.

My spirituality's sun
Knows only how to rise
And knows not
How to set.

18.

My spirituality
Has two unfailing friends:
Perseverance and patience.

19.

My spirituality-mind blooms
And
My spirituality-heart blossoms
In the same Heart-Garden
Of my Lord Beloved Supreme.

20.

In loving surrender,
My spirituality-head
I always keep low
At the Feet of my Lord Supreme.

21.

My spirituality-ear
I always keep close
To the disturbing and disheartening noise
Of the world.

22.

God tells me
That my spirituality-tears
And
My spirituality-smiles
Are the real angels on earth.

23.

My spirituality tells me
That each aspiring heart
Is God's hallowed shrine.

24.

My spirituality
Is
My God-embodiment-whisper
And
God-revelation-trumpet
And
God-manifestation-gong.

25.

My spirituality
Is
My heart's God-sword.

26.

My spirituality
Is
My life's God-shield.

27.

My spirituality
Remains always empty
Of desires.

28.

My spirituality
Is always replete
With aspirations.

29.

My spirituality-tears
Follow
The divine in me.

30.

My spirituality-smiles
Lead
The human in me.

31.

My spirituality-vital
Is
A perfect stranger
To frustration-aggression.

32.

My spirituality-body
Is
A pure flame
Of selflessness-life.

33.

My spirituality at once
Loves to become
An evil-fighter
And
A goodness-lover.

34.

Self-oblivion
Is
My spirituality's past name.

35.

Self-mastery
Is
My spirituality's present name.

36.

Self-discovery
Is
My spirituality's future name.

37.

My spirituality-boat
Shall never fail,
For it is endowed with
The capacity to sail,
Forever sail.

38.

My spirituality
Is
My heart's morning flute.

39.

My spirituality
Is
My life's evening bell.

40.

A pleasure-shunned life
My spirituality
Treasures.

41.

My spirituality does not live
Either in a praise-palace
Or
In a blame-cave.

42.

My spirituality
Remains far, very far,
From praise-rapture
And
Blame-torture.

43.

When I lose my spirituality,
Where do I find it?
I find it inside my heart-cradle
And not
Inside my mind-grave.

44 My Spirituality Means ...

My spirituality means
God-necessity,
Conscious and constant,
Sleepless and breathless.

45.

My spirituality
Is not
My mind's Himalayan supremacy.

46.

My spirituality
Is
My heart's Atlantic intimacy.

47.

My spirituality
Remains prostrated at God's Feet
Around the clock.

48.

Where is my spirituality?
My spirituality is resting
In the Heart-Peace-Room
Of my Lord Supreme.

49.

To my great joy,
My spirituality never gets lost
In my mind-hesitation-fog.

50.

Every day
My spirituality sports
In the hallowed territory
Of my Lord's Compassion-Eye.

51.

My spirituality
Grows younger and younger,
With more and more determination,
With each passing year.

52.

God,
Out of His infinite Bounty,
Invites my spirituality to share
His Infinity's good fortune.

53.

My spirituality
Is my daily one-pointed trust
In the Ultimate Divine.

54.

My spirituality
Longs to be the conqueror
Of the world's streaming tears.

55.

My spirituality
Longs to be the server
Of the world's beaming smiles.

56.

My Lord,
May my spirituality
Wave the flag of universal peace
Every day.

57.

My spirituality
Never walks along
The world-criticism-road.

58.

My spirituality
Every day walks along
The world-appreciation-shore.

59.

My spirituality
Does not
Authoritatively preach
In God's Name.

60.

My spirituality
Does not
Eloquently teach
In God's Name.

61.

My spirituality
Shows the world
What God unreservedly has
For the world.

62.

My spirituality
Is not
My worldwide mind-manifestation.

63.

My spirituality
Is
My worldwide heart-presentation.

64.

My spirituality
Is
My soul-pull from Above.

65.

My spirituality
Is
My heart-push from below.

66.

My spirituality's aspiration-tree
Is blossoming
With my heart's gratitude-rain.

67.

My spirituality-thoughts
Are my gratitude-birds
Spreading beautiful plumage.

68.

My spirituality
Is not
My futility-mind-jungle.

69.

My spirituality
Is
My fecundity-heart-garden.

70.

My spirituality
Is
A prayerful and cheerful traveller
Along Eternity's
Compassion-flooded Road.

71.

My spirituality
Is
Neither too old
Nor too young.
With its wisdom-maturity,
My spirituality runs and runs
Towards Infinity's Golden Shore.

72.

My spirituality-heart
Every day bustles
With God-Arrival-preparations.

73.

My Lord Supreme
Has blessed my spirituality
With an eye that speaks volumes
Without a single word.

74.

My spirituality
Knows that it has to conquer
At every moment
Difficult tests and stark challenges.

75.

My spirituality
Does not believe
In world-interference.

76.

My spirituality
Does not believe
In world-indifference.

77.

My spirituality
Believes only
In world-mind-illumination
And
In world-life-transformation
And
In world-heart-satisfaction.

78.

Needless to say,
My spirituality
Is not
My self-assertion.

79.

My spirituality
Is not
My self-abnegation.

80.

My spirituality
Is
My self-preparation.

81.

What does God want?
God wants my spirituality
To have intense and immense
Familiarity with me.

82.

My spirituality
Has settled all my problems
With the one and only solution:
An unconditional surrender
To God's Will
Once and for all.

83.

My spirituality
Is fully aware
Of the undeniable fact
That each individual has a task
That is most difficult to perform.

84.

My spirituality
Is
Normal, natural and moderate.

85.

My spirituality
Is not
A monk's penniless austerity.

86.

My spirituality
Is not
The smile and pride
Of a king's prosperity-wealth.

87.

My spirituality
Believes in having
Earthly necessities
And not
Earthly superfluities.

88.

My spirituality
Is
My unparalleled
Self-improvement-opportunity.

89.

My spirituality
Is
My constant God-fulfilment
Supreme necessity.

90.

My spirituality-tree
Climbs up to reach
The vault of Heaven.

91.

My spirituality-flower
Grows to rise and reach
The blossoming morning sun.

92.

In my outer life,
My spirituality
Is
A God-blessed sword.

93.

In my inner life,
My spirituality
Is
A God-blessed flute.

94.

My spirituality
Accepts one God
And many gods as well.
My spirituality
Equally believes in
A very special future God:
Man.

95.

My spirituality
Does not want me to become
A lion-king.

96.

My spirituality
Wants me to become
A God-lamb.

97.

My spirituality
Is not
My self-attainment.

98.

My spirituality
Is
My ever-fulfilling
God-commitment.

99.

My spirituality's code of life:
In the immediacy of today,
HERE AND NOW!

100.

My spirituality
Means
My unprecedented
God-manifestation-opportunity.

101.

My spirituality
Is
My world-perfection-
God-dreamer-sower.

102.

My spirituality
Is
My world-satisfaction-
God-warrior-revealer.

103.

My spirituality
Is not
My life insurance policy.

104.

My spirituality
Is
My God-assurance-ecstasy.

105.

My spirituality may accept
Refusals from earth,
But not from Heaven,
Never!

106.

My spirituality's only law
Is love
Below,
Above.

107.

My spirituality
Does not want to learn
How to suspect.

108.

My spirituality
Does not want to learn
How to doubt.

109.

My spirituality
Wants to learn
Only how to serve
The world in need.

110.

My spirituality
Is not
Austerity-torture-stupidity.

111.

My spirituality
Is
Normality-wisdom-journey
Forward, upward and inward.

112.

My spirituality
Is
A perfect stranger
To impossibility.

113.

Behold,
My spirituality has made God
A living Breath-Reality
On earth.

114.

My spirituality's speech
Has only one listener
In the audience:
Myself.

115.

My spirituality's silence
Has the entire world
As its audience.

116.

The questioning mind
Does not dare to knock
At my heart's spirituality-door.

117.

The searching mind
Tiptoes
To my heart's spirituality-door
Only to discover
That the door is wide open.

118.

My spirituality
Is
My heart's express train,
Whistling and speeding
Towards God-fulness-station.

119.

My spirituality
Is
Eternity's Hunger-Cry.

120.

My spirituality
Is
Infinity's Feast-Smile.

121.

My spirituality
Is
Immortality's Dream-Blossom.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My spirituality, Agni Press, 1999
