863 Something...

SOMETHING

Say something,
Do something,
Become something
At least.

I have already said
That I have realised
God fully.

I have already done
The work of total transformation
In myself.

Don't you see
That I have become another God,
A real God?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 18, Agni Press, 1973
