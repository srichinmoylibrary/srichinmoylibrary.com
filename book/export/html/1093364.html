46. God's reality-dream

The writing on my palm
     I easily effaced.

The writing on my forehead
     I laboriously effaced.

The writing on my head,
     I do not have to efface
Because it is God's
     Reality-Dream.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Canadian fruits, Bhakti Press, 1974
