Amare Je Base Bhalo

Amare je base bhalo
Kemane se rabe kalo
Ami jare basi bhalo
Prabhu tar prane sudha dhalo

Translation:

If anybody loves me
How can he remain in ignorance-night?
Impossible!
If I love someone,
O Lord Supreme,
Do pour inside his heart
Your Nectar-Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My green adoration-gifts, part 2, , 1977
