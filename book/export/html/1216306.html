673. What is next, please?

Father, I have seen Your Face.
What is next, please?
"Daughter, then love Me more."

Father, I am loving You more.
What is next, please?
"Daughter, promise you will always take part
In My Cosmic Game."

Father, I shall always take part
In Your Cosmic Game
Cheerfully,
Devotedly,
And unconditionally.
What is next, please?
"Nothing, absolutely nothing,
My sweetest child."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 14, Agni Press, 1974
