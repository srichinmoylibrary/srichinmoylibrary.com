18092

I can see that
God's Compassion-Eye
Has already arrived
At your heart-door.
But when are you planning to send
Your heart's gratitude-smiles to Him?
When?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 181, Agni Press, 1993
