37840

There is only one remedy
For all our anxieties
  And worries.
Just claim that God’s Love
Is for you, only for you.
But His Love for you has to be
  At His own Time
And in His own Way.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 38, Agni Press, 2004
