20 January 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I live in this world
Neither to possess the world
Nor to be possessed by the world.
I live in this world
To love the world,
To serve the world
And
To become the world —
The beautiful and breathtaking
Creation of God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Surabaya, Indonesia. 20 January 1999, 7:07 a.m. Before using double-arm machine — up to 140 lbs. with each arm simultaneously from a standing position.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
