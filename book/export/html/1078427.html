780 They Shield My Life...

THEY SHIELD MY LIFE

My God-faith shields my life
From despair-tornado.

My soul-faith shields my life
From doubt-clouds.

My heart-faith shields my life
From jealousy-frowns.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
