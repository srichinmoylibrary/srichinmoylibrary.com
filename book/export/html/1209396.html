23525

If you can feel that you are
A seven-year-old child,
Filled with enthusiasm, readiness,
Willingness and eagerness,
The spiritual life will never fail you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 236, Agni Press, 1997
