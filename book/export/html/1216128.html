499. I taught

I taught my soul
What to speak:
The power of love.

I taught my heart
How to speak:
Devotedly and soulfully.

I taught my life
Where to speak:
In the garden of Love-light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 10, Agni Press, 1974
