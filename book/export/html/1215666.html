46. What can God do? What can you do?

What can God do?
Just give Him a chance.
He can and will make
You
Another God.

What can you do?
Ah, you have already done it.
You have made God
Your perfect slave,
Perhaps unintentionally.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 1, Agni Press, 1974
