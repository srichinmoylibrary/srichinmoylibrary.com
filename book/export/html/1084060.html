34. My food

O desire-life,
Do you want
To eat my food?
Doubt-sighs are my food.

O aspiration-life,
Do you want
To eat my food?
Progress is my food.

O Realisation-life,
Do you want
To eat my food?
Oneness is my food,
   My only food.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 2, Agni Press, 1974
