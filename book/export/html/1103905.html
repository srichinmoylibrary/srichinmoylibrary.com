22 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Gratitude is not an imagination-word
   In my mind.
Gratitude is are reality-life
   In my heart.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 October 1999, 9:31 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 600 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
