10787

I have seen many things in my life,
And many more things I shall see.
But one thing I have not seen
And, alas, shall never see,
And that is my mind's
Climbing aspiration-steps.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 108, Agni Press, 1987
