1400

At long last
My heart is ready
To become Your Dream,
My Lord Supreme.

"Then, My child,
I am giving you back
Your completely forgotten
Oneness-realisation
With My transcendental Vision
And universal Mission."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 14, Agni Press, 1983
