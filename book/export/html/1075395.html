817. He who thinks

The philosopher in me speaks:
He who thinks
Is a stupid seeker.
He who fears
Is a sick seeker.
He who hesitates
Is a lame seeker.
He who doubts
Is not a seeker
At all.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 17, Agni Press, 1973
