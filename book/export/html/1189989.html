4310

You do not have to wait
For earthly fulfilment
To realise God.
To realise God
You do not have to wait
For anything.
Also, do not ask God
To wait for you.
Just keep your heart's door wide open
And let Him enter.
His Compassion-Presence
Is your earthly and heavenly fulfilment
All at once.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 44, Agni Press, 1984
