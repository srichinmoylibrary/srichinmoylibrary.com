He Ajana Tumi Amar

He ajana tumi amar
Sakal kabe habe
He achena apan bale
Chinbo tomai kabe
Kabe tumi amar prane
Sthayi habe robe
Kabe amai deke tumi
Tomar mahotsabe

Translation:

O Unknown,
When will You become
Everything to me?
O Unfamiliar One,
When shall I know You well as my own?
When will You become
True in my life-boat?
When will You call me
To attend to Your Victoryâ€™s
Festival supreme?
