4130

I surrender to the Supreme
Not because I feel I am helpless,
But because I feel
His Oneness-Delight is something
I have all along been craving
In my own life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 42, Agni Press, 1984
