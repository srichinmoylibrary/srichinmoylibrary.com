20104

O humanity,
I do not want to be
Even one step above you.
I want to be
On the same footing,
Not only to make you happy
But also to make myself really happy.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 202, Agni Press, 1994
