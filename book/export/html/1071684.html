Sparrow: Secrecy

Sparrow, my sparrow,
You embody future's high secrecy.
You reveal its wealth
With your psychic delicacy.
Your tiny body and your spanless
Foreseeing eye
Create in man bold contradiction
Of fascination-sky.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Animal Kingdom, Agni Press, 1973
