Ma Jani Tumi Asbe Phire Satya Habe

Ma jani tumi asbe phire
Satya habe bani
Tabu prane prabodh nahi mani
Japi dibanishi taba bani
Niyo more tomar kache tani
Taba bani dyaner malai gata habe
Ashru dharai nabin rabe
Ma jani tumi asbe phire jani

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 8, , 1981
