94 I Want Everything That Is Divine In...

I want everything that is divine in me
To grow and glow
And anything that is undivine in me
To shrink and fade.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 3, Agni Press, 2000
