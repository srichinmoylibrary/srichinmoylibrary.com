Amare Apan Karo

Amare apan karo
Amar duhat dharo
Amare karogo jantra
Dao more guru mantra
Ogo sundar daki
Khulo mor hiya ankhi

Translation:

Make me Your own, very own.
Only by Your Hand,
O Lord of Compassion,
Make me Your unconditional instrument.
Grant me Your Guru-mantra,
      Incantation-fire.
O Lord of Beauty, do open
      My heart’s vision-eye.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 2, , 1976
