31. Don't deny it!

Vital, don't deny it!
I saw you with your friend depression
This morning.
Mind, don't deny it!
I saw you with your friend suspicion
Last night.
Heart, don't deny it!
I saw you with your friend insecurity
Only yesterday.
You three are the least reliable friends.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My promise to God, Agni Press, 1975
