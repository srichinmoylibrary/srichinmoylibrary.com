367. Reveal your humility-life

O voice of my weeping night,
Be not silent.
Reveal, express your humility-life!
Oneness-delight will rule your life
When you have reached
The goal of your destination-dance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
