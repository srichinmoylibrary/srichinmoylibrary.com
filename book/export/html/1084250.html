20. Two boons

Two boons of my aspiration-life
I give to the world:
My transformation-body
And
My dedication-soul.
Two boons of my realisation-life
I give to the world:
My contemplation-flood
And
My oneness-blood.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 6, Agni Press, 1974
