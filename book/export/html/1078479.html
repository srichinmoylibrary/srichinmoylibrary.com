832 Lord Of My Heaven-Life...

LORD OF MY HEAVEN-LIFE

O Lord of my Heaven-life,
Can I have You
As my own, very own?
"Amen!"

O Lord of my earth-life,
Will You have me
As Your own, very own?
"Amen!"

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
