808 I Do Not Doubt...

I DO NOT DOUBT

I do not doubt man.
Why?
Am I a fool
To doubt my today's self?
No, I can't be that kind of fool.

I do not doubt God.
Why?
Am I a fool
To doubt my tomorrow's self?
No, I can't be that kind of fool.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
