I Test You Not, Never

I test you not, never,
      In the inner world.
I just show you my heart’s
      Sweet oneness-way.
I test you not, never,
      In the outer world.
I just tell you to claim
      My Eternal Day.

From: Sri Chinmoy, I shall forgive my past, , 1976
