10 August 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My prayers are my human life's
Helpless cries.
My meditations are my divine life's
Giant smiles.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

10 August 1999, 2:21 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 45 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (22 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
