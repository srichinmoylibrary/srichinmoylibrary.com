Supreme

Sweet Supreme Lord,
Do You care for my heart-food?
If not, my i-ness shall die
In You to see what food
You regularly eat
So that I can please You,
Your earth-tongue
And
Heaven-taste.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Light-delight-journeys, Agni Press, 1974
