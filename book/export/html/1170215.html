119. No matter what

A desire-man
Is bound to complain
No matter what he has
And
What he is.
An aspiration-man
Is bound to smile
His satisfaction-smile
No matter what he has not
And
What he is not.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sound becomes, silence is, Agni Press, 1975
