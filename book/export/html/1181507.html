8500 My Life's Devoted Receptivity

My life's devoted receptivity
Has and is the capacity
To make my heart feel
That it is not only close
To my Lord Supreme,
But closer than the closest.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 85, Agni Press, 1983
