July 1986

The strength of my sound-life
Surrenders to the power of my silence-life.
The power of my silence-life
Surrenders to the Vision-Eye
Of my Lord's Compassion-Heart.
My Lord's Compassion-Heart
Is now telling me,
"My child, My Immortality's Dream-child,
Speed up! The Goal is within your easy reach.
The Goal is all yours.
You and My Eternity's Liberation-Light
Will before long be transformed
Into My Infinity's
Absolute Satisfaction-Delight."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Gratitude-Flower-Hearts, Agni Press, 1993
