28.

"One should not come to America for the first time!"

Jawaharlal Nehru

O non-Americans, the heart and soul of America are welcoming you. Come, with your openness-heart, to be inseparably one with America's ascending heart of hope and manifesting soul of promise.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Choice Wisdom-Fountain-Souls, Agni Press, 2000
