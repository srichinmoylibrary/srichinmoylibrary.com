431. Three songs

I know only three songs:
Heart-song,
Soul-song,
Life-song.

With my heart-song,
I reveal God the Lover.

With my soul-song,
I manifest God the Beloved.

With my life-song,
I fulfil God the Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
