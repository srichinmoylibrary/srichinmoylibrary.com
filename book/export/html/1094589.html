2 God Does Not Hide Anything ...

God does not hide anything
From me.
Because of my blind eyes,
I do not see
What He does for me
Self-givingly and sleeplessly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 12, Agni Press, 2002
