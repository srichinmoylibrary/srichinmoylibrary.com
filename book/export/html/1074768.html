202. My soul is the player

My thought is the doubt,
My mind the doubter.

My feeling is the love,
My heart the lover.

My desire is the destruction,
My vital the destroyer.

My ignorance is the sleep,
My body the sleeper.

My will is the play,
My soul the player.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 5, Agni Press, 1973
