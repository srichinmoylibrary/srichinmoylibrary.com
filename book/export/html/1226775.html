Dibarati Namer Jasher Kangal Bale

Dibarati namer jasher kangal bale
Dao na je thai prabhu amai charan tale

Translation:

Day and night, I run after name and fame.
Therefore, You do not allow me
To place myself at Your Feet,
My Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
