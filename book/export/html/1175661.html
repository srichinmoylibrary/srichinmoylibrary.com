2711 Many Are The Ways

Many are the ways
To use simplicity, sincerity,
Purity and humility.
But there are only very few ways
To increase them
In our lifelong journey towards God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 28, Agni Press, 1982
