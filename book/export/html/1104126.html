24 February 2000 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my God-love, God-devotion
And God-surrender
Increase every day
At least by an inch.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

24 February 2000, 9:15 a.m. Before lifting 650 lbs. with each arm simultaneously three times (Total: 1,300 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 12, Agni Press, 2006
