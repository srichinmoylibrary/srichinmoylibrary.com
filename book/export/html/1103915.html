26 October 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I have many names,
But my most favourite name is
My heart's sleepless
   God-satisfaction-cry.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 October 1999, 10:14 p.m. Before lifting 250 lbs. with both arms simultaneously (Total: 500 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
