216. A transaction

The life human
Is at its best
A transaction
Between
Black depression
And
Brown frustration.

The life divine
Is at its worst
A transaction
Between
A hungry sky
And
A thirsty sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 5, Agni Press, 1974
