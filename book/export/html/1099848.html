13. The Supreme

Father, I have seen.
           "No."
Father, I have known.
           "No."
Father, I have felt.
           "No."
Father, I have become.
           "No."
Father, I Am.
           "Yes."

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Flute, Agni Press, 1972
