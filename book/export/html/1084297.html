16. You are and God is

You think,
   Therefore
You cry.

You cry,
   Therefore
You fly.

You fly,
   Therefore
You love.

You love,
   Therefore
You live.

You live,
   Therefore
You are
   And
God is.
        You are of God's Promise;
        God is for your love.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 7, Agni Press, 1974
