121. My confidence

My human confidence
Wants to see
The Face of God.

My divine confidence
Wants to feel
The Heart of God.

My supreme confidence
Wants to become
The Silence-smile of God,
The Sound-cry of God
And
The Perfection-realisation of God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
