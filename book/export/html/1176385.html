3428 You Just Have To Tolerate Yourself

You do not have to
Describe yourself.
You do not have to
Broadcast yourself.
You just have to
Tolerate yourself
And then illumine yourself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 35, Agni Press, 1982
