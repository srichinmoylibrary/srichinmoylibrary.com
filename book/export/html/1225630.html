Dekha Tomar Paina Kabhu

Dekha tomar paina kabhu
Amar hriday dware
Tabu kena nahi chali
Tomar abisare

Translation:

My Lord Supreme,
True, I do not see You.
But why do I not search for You?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 11b, , 1981
