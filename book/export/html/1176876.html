3914 You Do Not Have To Worry

You do not have to worry
About suspicion.
Just do not live
Inside your binding mind.

You do not have to worry
About your aspiring heart.
Just lead a faithful life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 40, Agni Press, 1982
