16 December 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I must love God the Creator
And serve God the creation
Simultaneously
If I want to please God completely
In His own Way.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

16 December 1999, 7:47 a.m. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.
