Alas, Alas, Even My Own Heart

Alas, alas, even my own heart
Will not wait for me.
Alas, alas, even my own soul
Does not care for me.
Alas, alas, even my own God
Has forsaken me.
Alas, alas, even my dream-world
Has deserted me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 6, , 1981
