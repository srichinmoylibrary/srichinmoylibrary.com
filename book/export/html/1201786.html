15991

My Lord,
Only one desire I had,
I still have
And I am sure
I will always have:
Wherever You go,
Take me along with You!
I am dying to accompany You
Everywhere.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 160, Agni Press, 1991
