90. I have more to give

I have more light,
Just wait and see.
I have more light to give,
Just empty your mind and receive.

I have more love,
Just dive within.
I have more love to give,
Just smile and achieve.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
