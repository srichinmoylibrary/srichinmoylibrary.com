118. On strike

Now that your heart-sun
Is in bed
Your vital life is on strike.
It demands more attention from you.

Now that your perfection-goal
Is asleep
Your desire-body is on strike.
It demands more compassion from you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
