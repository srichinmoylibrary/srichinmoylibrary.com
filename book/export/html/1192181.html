6481

He wanted once again to march along
The inner path he once followed.
But alas,
A baseless fear caught him,
And he felt that perhaps
Again he would fail,
So the best thing would be
Not to even try.
Look at the height
Of his stupidity!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 65, Agni Press, 1984
