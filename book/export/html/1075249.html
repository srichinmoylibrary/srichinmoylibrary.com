674 They All Export...

THEY ALL EXPORT

My heart exports
Delight
To my mind.

My mind exports
Light
To my heart.

My vital exports
Determination
To my body.

My body exports
Enthusiasm
To my vital.

My God exports
Pride
To my soul.

My soul exports
Gratitude
To my God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 14, Agni Press, 1973
