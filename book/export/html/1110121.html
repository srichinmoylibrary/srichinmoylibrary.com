57.

The German poet Goethe's immortal pen
Revealed to the world a new way
To the world-awakening consciousness:
"How can you come to know yourself?
Never by thinking; always by doing."
What are
The immortal action-achievements
Of President Gorbachev?
A self-awareness-flower-life,
A self-respect-beauty-mind,
A self-sacrifice-fragrance-heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: the home of Oneness-Peace-Dream-World, Agni Press, 2001
