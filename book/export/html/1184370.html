42. My days of sweetness

My days of sweetness
I have totally lost.

My days of sadness
I have somehow rediscovered.

My days of goodness
Will be found
Perhaps
Only in God's Compassion-Haven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
