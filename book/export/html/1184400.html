72. Yet God loves me

I have no boat.
Everybody else has one.
Yet God loves me.

I have no boatman.
Everybody else has one.
Yet God loves me.

I have no goal.
Everybody else has one.
Yet God loves me.

Why does God love me?
Because His is the Life
Of unconditional Compassion.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
