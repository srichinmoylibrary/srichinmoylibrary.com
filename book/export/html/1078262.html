615 I Have Two Hearts...

I HAVE TWO HEARTS

Unlike others,
I have two hearts:
A hiding heart
And
A crying heart.
My hiding heart shall feed
My earth-curiosity.
My crying heart shall feed
My Heaven-necessity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
