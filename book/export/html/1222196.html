Alor Pakhi Balte Habe

Alor pakhi balte habe
Kon sudure thako

Translation:

O bird of light, you must tell me
Where, where, where,
In which remotest land
  You live.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Four hundred blue-green-white-red soul-birds, part 1, , 1986
