220. Pride and power

My earth-friend,
Your pride of power
I can totally destroy
And I shall.

My earth-friend,
Your power of pride
Can easily destroy me,
My heart of aspiration
And
My life of dedication.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
