Arid

(dry)

You want to know
Why nobody likes you.
Nobody likes you
Because nobody wants to be
Affected by your arid mind
And impure vital.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
