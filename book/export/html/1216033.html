405. They make their own selections

The soul selects
Its own members.

The heart selects
Its own brothers and sisters.

The mind selects
Its own mentors.

The vital selects
Its own admirers.

The body selects
Its own fellow-dreamers.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 9, Agni Press, 1974
