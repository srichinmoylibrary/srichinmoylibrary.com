1 October 1999 (IV)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You want me to keep
   My heart-eye open
Sleeplessly, soulfully
   And self-givingly
To Your all-guiding Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 October 1999, 9:09 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-two times. (32 right, 30 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
