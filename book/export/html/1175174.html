2229 Love Your Master

Love your Master.
It is the short way
To spiritual progress.

Have faith in your Master.
It is the shorter way
To spiritual progress.

Obey your Master.
It is the shortest way
To spiritual progress.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 23, Agni Press, 1982
