Kabe Habe Hetha Purna Bidhatar Ash

Kabe habe hetha purna bidhatar ash
Kabe habe dharanir andhiyar nash
Kabe pabe sarba jib mahananda pran
Kabe pabe kripa surja mukti sandhan

Translation:

When will God’s
Desire-Hope be fulfilled
Here on earth?
When will the ceaseless
Darkness of this world
Be illumined?
When will all
Human beings enjoy
Immortality’s life-breath?
When will the Compassion-sun
Grant the world
Liberation-flood?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 1, , 1978
