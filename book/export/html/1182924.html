9902 Give Me A Large Heart

My Lord,
Give me a large heart
So that I can treasure
Your rich Smiles.

My Lord,
Give me a pure life
So that I can discover
The fountain of Your infinite Delight.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 100, Agni Press, 1983
