492. O my soul, o my body

O my soul,
Let me wing with you
On your Heaven-bound flight.

O my body,
Let me crawl with you, stumble with you
On your journey towards uncertainty's smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
