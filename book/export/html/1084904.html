11. In silence

In silence you suffered
Because
You were too great.
In silence you suffer
Because
You are too good.
In silence you will smile, sing and dance
Because
The Hour of God before long will strike.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
