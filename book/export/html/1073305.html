18.

Brother Jesus,
I am a Hindu.
I know nothing about
Your Judaism and Christianity.
Please tell me the difference
Between the two.
"Brother C,
Judaism knows how to strike;
Christianity knows how to envelop."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Brother Jesus, Agni Press, 1975
