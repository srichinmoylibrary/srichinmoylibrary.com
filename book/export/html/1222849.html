He Amar Man Nemona Nimne Ar

He amar man nemona nimne ar
Heribe haibe tumi ghor andhiyar

Translation:

O my mind, do not go down any more.
You will not only see, but you will become
The darkest darkness-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Giti mala, , 2000
