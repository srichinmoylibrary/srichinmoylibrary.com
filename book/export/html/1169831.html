476. A silly fellow

You are a silly fellow.
You cannot free yourself
From doubting.
I tell you, life is for those
Who are all-loving
In life-breath
And all-giving
In death-length.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
