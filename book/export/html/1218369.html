Bahir Jibane Prachurjya Diye

Bahir jibane prachurjya diye
Bhulaye rekhona more
Bhitar jibane akuti pradani
Rakhiya tomar krore

Translation:

O my Lord Supreme,
Do not allow me to forget You
By blessing me with plenitude
   In the outer life.
Only keep me with my heart’s aspiration-flames
   In my inner life.

Nutan Jiban Karecho Amare Dan

Nutan jiban karecho amare dan
Tumi je tomar daya aphuran

Translation:

My Lord, today You have granted me
   A new life.
You have proved to me that You are
   Your Compassion Infinite.
