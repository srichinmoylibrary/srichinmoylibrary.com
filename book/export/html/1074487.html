44.

Maya

Illusion

The desire-world is all illusion.

The aspiration-world is all illumination.

The realisation-world is all perfection.

The perfection-world is all satisfaction.

The satisfaction-world is all oneness and all newness: oneness universal and newness transcendental.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Core Of India's Light, Part 4, Agni Press, 1992
