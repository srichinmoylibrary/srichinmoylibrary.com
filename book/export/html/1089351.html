President

35th President, January 20, 1961

The One comes down
With His descending Grace.
Men come to Kennedy,
To hope's dynamic face.

Mother Earth offers
Her endless yoke of bondage.
Kennedy reveals
Heaven's all-transforming Message.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kennedy: the universal heart, Agni Press, 1973
