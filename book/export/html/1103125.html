30 September 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
I do not know what I am doing,
But I do know that You know
What You are doing
In and through me
For Your own Manifestation-Light
Here on earth
And for Your own Satisfaction-Delight
There in Heaven.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

30 September 1998, 4:53 a.m. Before lifting 127 lbs with each arm once.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 1, Agni Press, 1999
