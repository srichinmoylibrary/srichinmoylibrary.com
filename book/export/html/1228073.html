Namami Mata

Namami mata namami mata
Namami mata namami
Tumi amar swapan sudha
Amai laho pranami

Translation:

My Mother, to You I bow,
To You I bow,
To You I bow.
You are my Nectar-Dream.
Do accept my love-devotion-obeisance.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Indian sunrise, , 1998
