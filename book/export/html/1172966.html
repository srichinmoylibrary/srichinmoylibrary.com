44. Poor little heart

Poor little heart,
Do not cry.
I have come to you
With a rising rainbow.

Poor little heart,
Do not cry.
I have come to you
With my beloved Father's
Fountain-Flow.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, part 1, Agni Press, 1979
