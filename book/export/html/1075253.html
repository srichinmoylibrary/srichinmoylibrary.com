678 Two Different Moments...

TWO DIFFERENT MOMENTS

The unguarded moment
Secretly
Dragged me to Hell
And
Prevented my return.

The guarded moment
Openly
Carried me to Heaven
And
Offered me God's eternal Throne.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 14, Agni Press, 1973
