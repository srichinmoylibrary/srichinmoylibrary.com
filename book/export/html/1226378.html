Roar, Roar, My Lion-Heart

Roar, roar, my lion-heart.
The cosmic door is wide open.
Roar, roar, my lion-heart.
My eagle bird shall soar and soar
      Ever more.
Life is but a prosperity-store.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 7b, , 1981
