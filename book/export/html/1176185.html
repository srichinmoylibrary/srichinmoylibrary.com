3230 The Weight Of My Ignorance-Night

The full weight of my ignorance-night
Shall rest inside God's Forgiveness-Sun
And not upon my trembling shoulders,
If not every day,
At least for today.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 33, Agni Press, 1982
