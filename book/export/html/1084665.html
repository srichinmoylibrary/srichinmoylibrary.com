27. My banner-bearers

My inner world's
Banner-bearer
Is my ever-increasing poise.
My outer world's
Banner-bearer
Is my ever-roaring noise.
What is poise?
God-light of the Supreme
What is noise?
Man-might of mortality's futility.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 14, Agni Press, 1974
