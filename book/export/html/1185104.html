776. Painful it is to see

Painful it is to see
The empty heart's fear.

Painful it is to see
The insecure heart's pangs.

Painful it is to see
The weak heart's friendship with the base vital.

Painful it is to see
The unaspiring heart's slow movement
Towards the Golden Shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
