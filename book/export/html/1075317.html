741 Somewhere And Someone...

SOMEWHERE AND SOMEONE

Somewhere
There is someone
To think of you.

Somewhere
There is someone
To love you.

Somewhere
There is someone
To please you.

Somewhere
There is someone
To claim you.

Somewhere
There is someone
To inherit you.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
