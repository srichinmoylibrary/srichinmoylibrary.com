16392

When we do not aspire,
We fearfully see
That death is our breath-collector.
But when we aspire,
We clearly see
That death is actually
The supplier of a new life-breath.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 164, Agni Press, 1991
