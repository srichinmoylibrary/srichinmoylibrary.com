36. Our dreams

You belong to earth;
Therefore
You dream of fame and glory.

He belongs to Heaven;
Therefore
He dreams of love and service.

I belong to God;
Therefore
I dream of compassion and perfection.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God's Vision-Promise, Agni Press, 1974
