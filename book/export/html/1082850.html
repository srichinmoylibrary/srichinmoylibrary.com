My questions

My clever mind asks
The unanswered question:
Why do I love God?

My sincere heart asks
The unanswerable question:
Why does God love me?

My desire-life asks
The unanswered question:
Why do I need God?

My aspiration-life asks
The unanswerable question:
Why does God need me?

From: Sri Chinmoy, God-Life: is it a far cry?, Agni Press, 1974
