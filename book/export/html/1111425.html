The disciple surrenders

Before the first mile was over, I was ahead of Nirvik, and I was so delighted. Then, after two miles, he went ahead of me. I said, "Oh, he was fooling me."

After three and a half miles, I was only looking for Nirvik, but I couldn't see in. For five miles it went on like this. But later I surrendered. By the time we reached seven miles, he was following me.

January 1981

From: Sri Chinmoy, Run and become, become and run, part 4, Agni Press, 1981
