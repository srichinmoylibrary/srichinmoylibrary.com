Circumscribe

(limit)

I am the last person to ask you
To circumscribe your meditation-hours.
On the contrary,
I want you to meditate more,
But always cheerfully
And devotedly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
