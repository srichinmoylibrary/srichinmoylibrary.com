8785 Do Not Be Afraid To Act

Do not be afraid to act.
If you act and succeed,
You will be able to declare:
"I have done it."
If you act and fail,
At least you will be able to say:
"I tried."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 88, Agni Press, 1983
