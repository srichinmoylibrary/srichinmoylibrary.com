21 My Mind Speaks To Me ...

My mind speaks to me.
I immediately obey.
My vital speaks to me.
I proudly obey.
My body speaks to me.
I lovingly obey.
Alas, when shall I stop
Obeying them, when?
Alas, when shall I be
The choice instrument
Of my Lord Supreme, when?

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 14, Agni Press, 2002
