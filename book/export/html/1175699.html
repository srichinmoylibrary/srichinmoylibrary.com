2749 The Express Falsehood-Train

Don't pretend that you
Know the truth
Or insist that you
Embody the truth.
For, alas, truth does not want
To ride with you
In the express falsehood-train.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 28, Agni Press, 1982
