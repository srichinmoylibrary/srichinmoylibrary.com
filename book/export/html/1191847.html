6150

In the spiritual life
The first-class compartment
Is the compartment of my soul-delight,
And the second-class compartment
Is the compartment of my heart-light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 62, Agni Press, 1984
