When I pray, when I meditate

When I pray
  God embraces
  My crying heart,
  My surrendering heart,
  My dreaming heart.

When I meditate
  God blesses my life,
  God caresses my soul,
  God reveals my Goal.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Meditation: humanity's race and Divinity's Grace, part 1, Agni Press, 1974
