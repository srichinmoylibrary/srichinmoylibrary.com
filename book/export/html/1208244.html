The age-old questions:
Does God really love me?
Does God really need me?
Oh, where is the answer?
Who can give the answer,
If not the real in me,
My own God-love?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 224, Agni Press, 1995
