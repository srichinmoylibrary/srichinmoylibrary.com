558 Smile A Little More...

SMILE A LITTLE MORE

Father, smile a little more
In my heart.
"Daughter, cry a little more
In My Heart."

Father, I am sorry;
My heart tears are sleeping.
"Daughter, I am sorry;
My Soul-tears are not functioning."

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
