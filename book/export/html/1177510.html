4542 Do You Remember?

Do you remember
That once upon a time
You sang with God
In His own Heart-Garden?

Do you remember
That once upon a time
You played with God
In His own Life-Playground?

Do you remember
That once upon a time
God shared with you
His own Transcendental Throne
In His Universal Empire?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 46, Agni Press, 1982
