Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 249

24801

My Lord,
I can at most resist
Your Compassion-Eye,
But I shall never be able to resist
Your Forgiveness-Feet.

24802

If obedience
Is your life-boat,
Then in your life's uncharted journey
You are bound to succeed.

24803

Alas, our expectation-key
Is apt to open
Only the wrong door.

24804

Today's minute expectation
Can turn into tomorrow's
Frustration-volcano.

24805

I can completely empty
My life's dark problem-room
When the Name of God
Becomes the very breath of my heart.

24806

God tells me that I have become
A choice instrument of His
Precisely because
I have carpeted my entire being
With humility-grass.

24807

God can and will
Do the right thing for you
Only when your love for Him
Is pure and genuine.

24808

The mind craves attention,
The heart longs for reward.
Therefore, alas,
Neither the mind nor the heart is ready
To have unconditional love for God.

24809

How I wish I could always speak
To my Lord Supreme
Exactly the way my soul does:
In silence-whispers.

24810

We feel that God does not hear
Our cries,
But do we, even in our imagination,
Try to make our cries
Prayerful, soulful and self-giving?

24811

I ask my Lord
To come to me quickly.
My Lord asks me,
"Are you really ready
To receive Me?"

24812

If there is anybody,
Either in Heaven or on earth,
As furious with me as myself
For wasting so much of God's Time
On earth,
Then that person is none other
Than my own soul!

24813

A purity-mind,
A devotion-heart
And a surrender-life
Must rule my very existence
At every moment.

24814

My Lord Supreme,
How I adore You!
Do You know why?
Because You transform
My helpless despair
Into most powerful and intense delight.

24815

What saves my life
At every moment?
Only one thing:
My heart's gratitude-breath
To my Lord Supreme.

24816

If you love your Master
Unconditionally,
Then he becomes your heart's
Emperor supreme.
But if you love him conditionally,
Then you can at any time
Turn him into a veritable beggar.

24817

When I tell God
That I love Him only,
Alas, do I really mean it?

24818

I ask God,
"My Lord, when You tell me
That You desperately need me,
Do You really mean it?"
My Lord tells me,
"I mean it, I mean it,
I shall always mean it, My child."

24819

I ask my Lord Supreme,
"Do tell me
What is wrong with me."
My Lord Supreme tells me,
"My child, only one thing:
You must love Me infinitely more
Than you love Me now."

24820

"In God we trust."
This is an American motto.
Does God trust us?
Poor God tells us
That He has not yet learned
The art of distrust.

24821

Learn to look at yourself seriously.
Ask yourself,
"Do I really need God?"
Ask this question every day.

24822

Perfection will always long for
More perfection,
Abundant perfection
And infinite perfection.

24823

God-eagerness,
God-manifestation
And God-fulfilment:
Indeed, a supremely high trinity!

24824

My soul not only loves
My God-dreams,
But also shelters my God-dreams.

24825

Dream and dare,
Dare and dream
If you want to proceed fast
In your spiritual life.

24826

Do not expect anything spectacular
Either from God or from yourself.
Just give yourself to God
Happily and proudly.
The rest you must leave to God.

24827

Joy has failed to teach you.
Therefore, God has sent pain
To teach you.

24828

My Lord Supreme,
You have given me
A most difficult task:
You have asked me to count
All Your disappointments.

24829

My Lord Supreme is telling me
That He will enter into my heart
Only through my self-dedication-door.

24830

Be careful, O seeker,
Of your insecurity!
Today's insecurity
Will most likely turn into
Absurd haughtiness.

24831

My Lord Supreme,
Do give me
The beauty of love divine
And do give me
The fragrance of devotion divine
So that I do not have to remove
My prayerful gaze
From Your Compassion-flooded Feet.

24832

The more I am sincere
With my spiritual life,
The more it is clear to me
That I need only one thing:
God's ceaseless Compassion-Eye.

24833

Alas,
We do not believe
The undeniable fact
That our lives are full of
Divinity-manifestation-possibilities
And opportunities.

24834

God has made it a point
Every day
To take a new picture
Of my prayerful and tearful
Gratitude-heart.

24835

The beauty of my surrender
And the Power of God's Will
Are fond of living inseparably.

24836

God blessingfully and proudly
Accepts us
As His choice instruments
When we turn all our thoughts
Into prayers.

24837

O humanity,
Do not give up!
God's Patience
Has not yet given up.

24838

My Lord Supreme,
Do bless me
With an ever-expanding faith
To please You
With every breath of my heart.

24839

Only he who has surrendered,
And not he who says
That he is going to surrender,
Will be cradled
In God's ever-loving Embrace.

24840

Your Lord Beloved Supreme
Is eagerly waiting for you.
Just step inside your heart-temple.

24841

God the Love has invited me
To see His Heart, to feel His Heart
And to become His Heart.

24842

You are looking
For God's Throne.
Alas, you are looking
In the wrong place!
The right place is your own heart.

24843

My life-boat treasures
Many passengers,
But peace-passengers
It treasures the most.

24844

O seeker, can you not see
That you have lovingly and proudly kept
All-destroying doubt
As your beloved friend?

24845

Self-giving is, indeed,
An everlasting,
Self-transcending journey.

24846

The mind can doubt
Its own doubts,
But the heart always agrees
With its faith.

24847

If you are afraid
Of your failure-life,
Then this very failure-life
Will never allow you
To make any progress
In your spiritual life.

24848

Not what you think
Will make you ever happy,
But what you prayerfully
And soulfully give
Can and will make you happy.

24849

I am so grateful to my soul,
For it has come today
To excavate
All my heart's old buried dreams.

24850

The human mind does not realise
That there are
Infinitely more important things
In life
Than just increasing life's speed.

24851

Everything that my heart does
I enjoy immensely,
Specially when my heart
Prayerfully and soulfully weeps
For God.

24852

O my sunshine-hope-heart,
I sleeplessly and breathlessly
Depend upon you.

24853

When you aspire,
You realise that Heaven hears
All your heart-whispers.

24854

A sleeplessly self-offering heart
Will definitely have
The highest mastery
Over the unaspiring life.

24855

My Lord tells me
That my very existence on earth
Is worthy of His deepest Concern.

24856

A sleeplessly self-giving heart
Is, indeed, a God-lover
Of the first rank.

24857

A universal oneness-heart
Will always remain
A dire challenge
To modern science.

24858

The human mind has changed
Next to nothing
Over the course of centuries.

24859

Every morning
I enter into my heart-home
To pay my prayer-visit
To God's Compassion-Eye
And His Forgiveness-Feet.

24860

The beauty of love
And the fragrance of surrender
See each other everywhere.

24861

Each snow-white prayer
Is, indeed,
A God-inspired messenger.

24862

I want my life
Every day
To be ruled
By my soul-emperor.

24863

My life is running
Towards God's Feet.
God's Eye is soaring
Towards my heart.

24864

During my deep meditation
I clearly hear the voice of my soul
Ringing melodiously with celestial love.

24865

My God-given faith
I sleeplessly treasure.
My man-given doubt
I vehemently abhor.

24866

The latest news
From God's Universal Heart
Is this:
God's Forgiveness-Feet
Will never leave me,
And God's Compassion-Eye
Will always take care of me.

24867

May my future life
Be flooded with Divinity's
Immortality-Dreams.

24868

When I live inside my heart-home,
I clearly see that my faith
Can run infinitely faster
Than my doubt
To carry me to my destination.

24869

A soulful smile
Is definitely a sign
Of love-devotion-surrender-progress.

24870

God tells me
That it is definitely possible
To free my mind
From the tangle of brooding doubts.

24871

A selfless life is, indeed,
A beautiful and powerful experience
Of God Himself
Inside the seeker.

24872

There is not a single time
When the heart-flute does not play
Sweetness-melodies.

24873

God gladly and proudly
Adds beauty and fragrance
To each service-moment
Of my life.

24874

Where is abiding security?
It is only
Inside our oneness-heart.

24875

If your life becomes
A torrential rain of eagerness
And intensity,
Then you are bound to become
A tree of God-Satisfaction-fruits.

24876

Every day when I meditate,
God blesses me
With His own Heart-Silence-Whispers.

24877

To be an unaspiring mind
Is the beginning
Of self-destruction.

24878

The disobedience of your darkness-mind
Will disappear
When inside your heart
You discover your God-devotion-magnet.

24879

When we obey God's Call,
God blessingfully invites us
To come
To His Heart-Kingdom.

24880

My small heart
Is nothing but frustration
And exasperation.
My big heart
Is nothing but compassion
And illumination.

24881

If we can adorn
Our sacred heart-shrine
With love, devotion and surrender,
Then we shall never be blinded
By the desire-world.

24882

What is determination?
Determination is something
That conquers the undivine in us
For the full manifestation
Of the divine in us.

24883

Oneness is ultimately
The full victory
Of our aspiration-heart
And dedication-life.

24884

Our throbbing
Aspiration-heartbeats
Please God infinitely more
Than we can ever imagine.

24885

The mind never runs out of
Doubt-poison.
The heart never runs out of
Faith-nectar.

24886

Our love of God
Chases the unwillingness-thief
From our mind.

24887

Surrender, surrender to God's Will
Happily and proudly!
Lo, all your frustration-tears
Have completely disappeared.

24888

Each seeker has to be armed
With indomitable determination.
If not, he may sink
Into utter despair
Along the spirituality-road.

24889

The secret of sleepless aspiration
Lies in singing the songs
Of Heavenly glory.

24890

It is not my self-confidence
But my God-reliance
That feeds my heart
With Infinity's Joy.

24891

Finally,
My ignorance-vital-volcano
Has surrendered
To my wisdom-heart-garden.

24892

Each good thought of my mind
Immediately adds
To my perfection-qualities list.

24893

Every day I pray to God
To help me see
The beauty and divinity
Of my silence-soul.

24894

What is obedience,
If not the first requisite
Of God-aspiration, God-realisation
And God-manifestation?

24895

I can cheer up my sad heart.
But alas,
I can never tame my mad mind.

24896

Every day my Lord is eager
To help me grow
Aspiration-heart-wings,
But alas, I am never ready.

24897

If we do not possess
The full measure of obedience-light,
Then we shall never be able
To come out of ignorance-night.

24898

A God-server
Has to be a God-lover.
He cannot be otherwise!

24899

God tells me
If I want to see His Compassion-Eye,
Then I must sweep away
My mind's confusion-dust.

24900

A constantly cheerful self-giver
To God's Will
Is the sweetest God-singer.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 249, Agni Press, 1998
