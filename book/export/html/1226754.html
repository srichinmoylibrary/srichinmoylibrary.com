Dibarati Jara Amai

Dibarati jara amai
Kare upahas
Sujog pele tara amar
Karbe sarbanash

Translation:

Those who make fun of me,
Day in and day out,
If they get an opportunity,
Leave no stones unturned to destroy me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
