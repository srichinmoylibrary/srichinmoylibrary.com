14 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
You start singing, dancing
And flying
The moment You see
My willingness-mind,
Eagerness-heart
And enthusiasm-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

14 August 1999, 6:35 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred and twenty-two times with one arm. (53 right, 69 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
