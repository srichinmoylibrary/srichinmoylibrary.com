9029 Your Ultimate Vision And Reality

Every day offer yourself consciously
To your ultimate Vision and ultimate Reality.
Your ultimate Vision tells you
That you are of the Supreme.
Your ultimate Reality tells you
That you are for the Supreme,
The Supreme alone.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 91, Agni Press, 1983
