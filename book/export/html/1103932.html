1 November 1999 (VII)

Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My heart,
Cry and cry and cry!
Were born to cry for God.
My mind,
Smile and smile and smile!
You and I
Were born, to smile at God.
My life,
Surrender and surrender and surrender!
You and I
Were born to surrender to God's Will.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 November 1999, 10:08 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
