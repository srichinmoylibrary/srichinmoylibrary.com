12 August 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Age pitifully cries and cries
When it foolishly and greedily
Lives for itself.
Age brightly and powerfully
Smiles and smiles
When it lives in God, with God
And for God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

12 August 1999, 6:44 a.m. Before doing clean and press from the ground up to 90 lbs. (10 reps)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
