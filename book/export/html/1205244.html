19414

Alas,
My aspiration-room is unfinished,
My dedication-room is unfinished,
My surrender-room is unfinished.
But I am so happy that
My gratitude-room is unfinished,
And I hope my gratitude-room
Always remains the same.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 195, Agni Press, 1993
