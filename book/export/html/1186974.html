1325

Why did God aspire?
He aspired to transform His Vision
Into His Reality.
He was successful.
He aspired to transform His Revelation
Into His Manifestation.
He was again successful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 14, Agni Press, 1983
