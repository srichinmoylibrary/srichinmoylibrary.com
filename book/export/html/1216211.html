580. The only reality

Death, be not proud!
Who am I,
If not the Child of Immortality?

Life, be not stupid!
You and God
Each other need.

God, be not indifferent!
Your Smile is the only Reality
Of the universe.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 12, Agni Press, 1974
