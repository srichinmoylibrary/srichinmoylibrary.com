20 February 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please tell me what You want from me.
I shall immediately give it to You.
"My child, I need only one thing from you:
Your surrender-blossom-smile."
My Supreme, my surrender-blossom-smile
Is all Yours.
I place it at Your Feet.
Do make me Your own, very own.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

20 February 1999, 8:04 a.m. Before lifting 140 lbs. with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 280 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
