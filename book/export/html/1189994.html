4315

If you are lost
In your mind-confusion,
Just mention soulfully only once
The Name of your sweetest Lord.
Not only will you come out
Of your mind-confusion,
But also your mind-confusion itself
Will be illumined.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 44, Agni Press, 1984
