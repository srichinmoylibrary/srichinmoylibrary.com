Chal Sapta Rishir Dal

Chal chal chal sapta rishir dal
Chal chal chal chalre o bhai chal
Chal chal chal chalre o bon chal
Andhar guhai alor khulai
Amra prabhur bishwa premer bal
Chal chal chal
Agyanatar moher karar halo aji abasan
Ananda paramananda satyer purnata sandhan

From: Sri Chinmoy, Freedom-heights, part 1, , 1978
