15479

On the day of our birth,
The soul says,
“I am loving God
  Unconditionally.
My brother heart,
My brother mind,
My brother vital
And my brother body,
You also do the same.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 16, Agni Press, 1999
