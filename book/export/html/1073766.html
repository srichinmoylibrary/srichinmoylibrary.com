Thaumaturgist

(miracle-worker)

The Supreme Thaumaturgist
Has the incredible capacity
To transform Heaven's
Indifference-mind-frown
Into its concern-heart-smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
