19 December 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
The doubt of the human mind
Darkens and blights
The aspiration-heart.
The faith of the human mind
Brightens and strengthens
The dedication-life.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Singapore. 19 December 1998, 3:50 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. fifty times with two arms and one-hundred and two times with one arm. (52 right, 50 left in five sets)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
