19352

My Lord Supreme tells me
In strict confidence
That my life's perfection will dawn
Only when I see cheerfully
His blessingful and purposeful Presence
In all human beings with no exception.
Needless to say,
This includes my enemies.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 194, Agni Press, 1993
