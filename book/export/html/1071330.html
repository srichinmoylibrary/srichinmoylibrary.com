188. Faith will change his future

Sincerity
Changed his past
Slowly and steadily.

Determination
Is changing his present
Powerfully and convincingly.

Faith
Will change his future
Amazingly and permanently.

From: Sri Chinmoy, 207 Flower-Flames, Agni Press,
