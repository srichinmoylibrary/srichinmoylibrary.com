40. I am where I am

I am from above,
Believe it or not.
For God's sake I am on earth.

I am from below,
Believe it or not.
For man's sake I am in Heaven.
No preference have I
For the highest Height
Or the abysmal depth.
For God's sake,
For man's sake,
I am where I am.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When God-Love descends, Agni Press, 1975
