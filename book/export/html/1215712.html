91. Love increases

Love increases
By visiting friends.
Love increases more
By helping friends.
Love increases most
By loving friends.

God the Host
Loves a visiting friend.
God the Father
Loves more a helping friend.
God the Beloved
Loves most a loving friend.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 2, Agni Press, 1974
