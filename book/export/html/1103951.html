8 November 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
My life has to be rooted and grounded
In my sleepless love of God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 November 1999, 7:39 a.m. Before using seated double-arm machine up to 150 lbs. with each arm simultaneously.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
