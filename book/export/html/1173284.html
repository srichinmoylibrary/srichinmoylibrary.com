358 O My Foolish Body

O my foolish body,
Are you not ashamed
Of your most deplorable conduct?
Because of your lethargy,
Because of your unwillingness,
I am falling,
Falling all the time;
I am failing,
Failing everywhere.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 4, Agni Press, 1979
