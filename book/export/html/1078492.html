GOALS

To be
God's sound-son
Was the goal of my outer life.

To be
God's silence-son
Was the goal of my inner life.

To be
God's surrender-son
Is the goal of my God-realisation-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
