355. My added contribution

I have carefully read
God's Biography,
Therefore
I know practically everything
About God.
But one thing even now
I do not understand.
Is God responsible for this miserable creation
Or
Am I responsible for my added contribution?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
