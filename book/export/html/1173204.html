279 Make The Right Choice!

Yours is the faith
That can shatter mountain-obstructions.
Again,
Yours is the doubt
That can empty the ocean-vast Love of God.
Be wise -
Make the right choice!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 3, Agni Press, 1979
