940. Heart and soul, truth and peace

Heart offers its love,
It cannot be bought.
Soul offers its joy.
It cannot be caught.
Truth reveals itself.
It cannot be demanded.
Peace spreads itself.
It cannot be commanded.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 19, Agni Press, 1973
