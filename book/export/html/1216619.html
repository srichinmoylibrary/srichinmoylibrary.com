980. Painted dreams and God-dream

O my painted dreams,
Do not magnify me.
   I know who I am,
   I know what I am.
I need no help from you.

O my God-dream,
Do not forsake me,
Do not ignore me.
   I know how great you are,
   I know how real you are.
I need help from You,
   Only from You.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 20, Agni Press, 1974
