Ek Sajiyache Bahu Nijere Karite

Ek sajiyachhe bahu
Nijere karite aswadan
Bahu sajiyachhe ek
Nijere danite pramodan
Bahu ar ek jani
Abhinna hiya nibedan

Translation:

One has become many
For aspiration’s enjoyment supreme;
Many have become one
For manifestation’s enjoyment supreme.
Many and one are inseparably one
In Reality’s Perfection-Fulfilment-Light.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 1, , 1978
