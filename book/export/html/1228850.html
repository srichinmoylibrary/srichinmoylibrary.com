Kanur Lagiya Ganthinu E Mala

Kanur lagiya ganthinu e mala
Kanu rai asilona
Taito jiban nahi habe mor
Rupantarer sona

Translation:

I have made this garland for Kanu,
But Kanu has not come.
Therefore, my life will not become
Transformation-gold.

From: Sri Chinmoy, One thousand lotus petals, part 2, , 2000
