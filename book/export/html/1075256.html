MY FIVE TEACHERS

My poetry teacher taught me
How to cry.

My philosophy teacher taught me
How to dream.

My religion teacher taught me
How to bind life.

My yoga teacher taught me
How to find Love.

My liberation teacher taught me
How to create Peace.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 14, Agni Press, 1973
