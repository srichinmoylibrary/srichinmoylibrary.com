Dhakka Maro Lathi Maro

Dhakka maro lathi maro
Tabu tomai chharbona na na na
Kari shapath agyanatai
Tomai ami marbona na na na

Translation:

My Lord, You can push me aside.
You can kick me hard.
I shall not leave You any more.
My solemn promise to You:
I shall never hurt You in ignorance-night.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Bahir jagate, part 1, , 2002
