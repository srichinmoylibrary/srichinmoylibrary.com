Morning dawn

1.

The morning beauty becomes the prosperity of the day.

2.

Morning shows the peace-road to each and every human being.

3.

In the morning we bathe in sweet joy-silence.

4.

Sunrise inspires us to smile at ourselves.

5.

Morning is by far the best God-obedience-opportunity.

6.

As morning dawns, hope and determination dance together.

7.

Morning is the rising enthusiasm-river.

8.

The morning dawn helps us to make the truth-acceptance choice.

9.

At sunrise silence sings in the heart of purity.

10.

When morning dawns, readiness, willingness and eagerness rise to their Himalayan heights.

11.

At break of dawn our soul teaches us the lesson of perfection.

12.

In sunrise-silence, our waking mind becomes the lover of peace.

13.

The morning-confidence energises even a hopeless heart.

14.

Sunrise does not hear the ear-splitting doubt-thunder.

15.

Morning is the inspiration-spring and aspiration-summer.

16.

Morning loves to behold the ever-transcending beyond.

17.

In the morning our soul oversees God-manifestation with greater care.

18.

At dawn we are closely in touch with God's Heart-Embrace.

19.

At morning sunrise a self-giving heart radiates with the beauty of the entire creation.

20.

Every morning God comes to visit our heart-temples.

21.

In the morning sunrise the Way becomes most apparent to us.

22.

In the morning our hope-wind disperses our mind's doubt-fear-clouds.

23.

In the morning we must not swerve from our destined road.

24.

When morning dawns, Mother Earth blesses us with her heart of world-friendliness and world-oneness.

25.

Morning is the best time to achieve everything in the shortest amount of time.

26.

The light of morn brings its illumination-rays to the dreaming world-children.

27.

Morning announces the victory of light over darkness.

28.

Because of my morning peace, God tells me, "My child, you are Mine."

29.

At the light of dawn, our rainbow-dreams are transmuted into gleaming heart-treasures.

30.

In the morning our hearts open to the expanding light of the universe.

31.

At dawn, God comes to us in His form of Light to awaken our sleeping eyes.

32.

In the morning truth-marchers travel the highway to light-delight-destination.

33.

The morning-light shows the seeker a deeper and richer existence.

34.

The morning God not only appreciates and admires, but enjoys the tenderness of our heart-garden.

35.

The morning sun surrounds us with its cosmic embrace.

36.

The soulful morning smile is a treasure of the mind and heart alike.

37.

In the morning the greatness of the mind and the goodness of the heart are inseparable.

38.

In the morning our hearts dawn with sweetness-dewdrops.

39.

Each morning is the birth of a new opportunity.

40.

In the morning Heaven descends in all its glory to become the rising sun.

41.

The morning light feeds our hearts and guides our lives.

42.

In morning's silence-delight, the Supreme Himself visits my heart-abode.

43.

At the morning dawn, I hear the gentle whisper of my blessingful inner guide.

44.

Morning fills my heart-garden with its sweetness-fragrance.

45.

In the morning we begin our Godward journey through the life-forest.

46.

In the morning we find our heart playing on the swing of delight.

47.

Each morning is made of thousands of golden silences.

48.

In the morning opportunity-smiles are fond dreams in front of us.

49.

In the morning our heart soulfully studies at the God-obedience-university.

50.

Morning is the best time to leave the table of desire and sit at the table of aspiration.

51.

Morning is the whisper-voice of our inner beauty.

52.

In the morning our souls blossom in our dreaming eyes.

53.

With a sweet glance every morning, my Lord illumines my eyes and enlightens my heart.

54.

Morning is the silver sail of my life-boat.

55.

In the morning surround yourself with the fragrance of divine thoughts.

56.

Morning is the best time to reside within the Breath of the Supreme.

57.

In the morning my heart is one total embodiment of God-gratitude-offering.

58.

In the morning we breathe in the fragrance of living peace.

59.

At the break of day, my life-boat plies cheerfully upon the joy-swells of my heart.

60.

In the morning our eyes feast on nature's beauty.

61.

In the morning joy-light-beams touch our hearts.

62.

In the morning we share invaluable moments with the beauty of God the creation.

63.

In the morning our hearts cannot help singing and dancing wherever they go.

64.

In the morning everybody's heart is extra large, extra beautiful and extra generous.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Morning dawn, Jharna-Kala Card Co., 1997
