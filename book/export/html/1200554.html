14771

God, will You make my mind
As vast as the ocean?
This is the wrong question.
God, will You make my mind
As pure as a flower?
This is the right question.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 148, Agni Press, 1991
