457

The day I lost
My complaining mind,
Something extremely significant
Took place.
My Eternity's Beloved Supreme
Showed His explaining Heart
To the world at large
To reveal my perfect perfection-life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 5, Agni Press, 1983
