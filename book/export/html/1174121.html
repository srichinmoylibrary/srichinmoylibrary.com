1187 The Divine Always Wins

It is said that
Either the animal in us
Or the divine in us
Will ultimately win.
But it happens that
The divine always wins
At the end of our journey's course.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 12, Agni Press, 1981
