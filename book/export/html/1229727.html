Vishnu Debata Vishnu

Vishnu debata Vishnu
Tumi satata sahishnu
Apar kripa avatar
Taba khama srotadhar

Translation:

O Lord Vishnu, O Lord Vishnu!
You are Your Eternity’s Patience-Light.
O Compassion Incarnate!
You are Your Infinity’s Forgiveness-Flow.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Satisfaction-blossoms [songbook], , 1983
