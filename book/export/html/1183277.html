141. Alas, alas!

Alas, alas!
No one will believe me
If I say that God has
Employed me to represent Him
On earth.
Yet I perform my task most efficiently.

Alas, alas!
No one will believe me
If I say that God has
Employed me to advise Him
In Heaven.
Yet I perform my task most devotedly.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
