42. My secret sorrow

Lord, finally today
I am giving You
My secret sorrow.

Son, secret sorrow is sacred.

Ah, since You are my only Lord,
I must confess to You
        Or
Confide in You.
Why am I not another God?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 17, Agni Press, 1974
