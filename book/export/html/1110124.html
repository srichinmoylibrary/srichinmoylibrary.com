60.

To say that President Gorbachev
Is a most beautiful flower
And a most delicious fruit
Of Mother-Earth-Tree for mankind
Is to embrace him
As a cosmopolitan soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, President Gorbachev: the home of Oneness-Peace-Dream-World, Agni Press, 2001
