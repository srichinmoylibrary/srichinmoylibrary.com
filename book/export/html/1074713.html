149. Mutual love

I adore God
For what He has:
Yesterday's Promise,
Today's Assurance,
Tomorrow's Gift.

God loves me
For what I am:
Curiosity-Prince,
Stupidity-King,
Futility-Emperor.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 3, Agni Press, 1973
