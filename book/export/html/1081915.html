356. Visitor's identification

To visit God's Palace
I need no identification,
Because
Everybody knows that
I am God's son.

To visit man's hut
I need a series of identifications.
Otherwise,
Man would throw me out
Of the door of his hut.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
