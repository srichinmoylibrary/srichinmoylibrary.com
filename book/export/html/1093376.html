6.

My Lord,
Do tell me
When You are really
Pleased with me.
My child,
I am really pleased with you
Only when you hate Me.
My Lord,
How? How can it be true?
How, my child?
That is the only time
That you think of Me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My child, you and I are in the same boat, Agni Press, 1991
