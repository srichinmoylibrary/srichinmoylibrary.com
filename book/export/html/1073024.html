42. Silence

God's Silence-Light
   Created me.
My silence-light
   Annoys the world,
   Confuses the world,
   Puzzles the world.

God's Silence-Height
   Immortalises me.
My silence-height
   Is the conscious imperfection,
   Constant imperfection,
        Of my non-acceptance of God's
            All-encompassing,
                 All-embracing
                     Reality on earth,
                     Reality in Heaven.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Beauty-drops, Agni Press, 1975
