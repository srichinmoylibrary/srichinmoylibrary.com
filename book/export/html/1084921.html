28. They need her

She embodies
The Eye of Heaven;
Therefore
Earth constantly needs her.
She embodies
The heart of earth;
Therefore
Heaven unreservedly needs her.
She heralds
God-perfection on earth;
Therefore
God supremely needs her.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
