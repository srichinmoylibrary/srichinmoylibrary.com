26 March 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
Please bless me
With some divine capacities
So You can put them to use
Smilingly, lovingly
And compassionately.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

26 March 1999, 3:12 a.m. Before lifting 200 lbs. with both arms simultaneously (Total: 400 lbs.) Lift number five of the 200-lb. double-arm series. (Interim lifts not videoed.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 3, Agni Press, 1999
