2 August 1999

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
When I do something wrong,
Please, please scold me severely.
If You scold me severely,
Only then will I feel
That You truly love me
And Your Concern for me
Is genuine.
My Supreme, my Supreme,
Do scold me, do scold me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

2 August 1999, 10:11 a.m. Before lifting 270 lbs. twice with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 540 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 8, Agni Press, 1999
