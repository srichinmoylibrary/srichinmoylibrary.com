15937

My sincerity-mind and purity-heart
Are bound to seek God,
My Lord Supreme,
Here, there and everywhere,
Throughout the length and breadth
Of the world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 160, Agni Press, 1991
