26504

For many years I have tried
To please myself.
Alas, everything has been in vain.
From now on I shall perform
My God-assigned tasks.
I shall pray, meditate and serve Him
Every day.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 266, Agni Press, 1998
