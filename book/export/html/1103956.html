8 November 1999 (VI)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
In my spiritual life,
I shall have to be
A dauntless runner
With determined and measured strides
To arrive at my Destination.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

8 November 1999, 8:37 a.m. Before using seated calf raise machine up to 1,000 lbs.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 11, Agni Press, 2000
