1862

If you do not have the inner urge
To please your Supreme Pilot,
Then why do you stay in His Boat?
You are only forcing Him
To carry the weight
Of a dead elephant.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 19, Agni Press, 1983
