Dekha Dao Prabhu

Dekha dao prabhu hriday gahane shayane swapane
Danibo amare tushibo tomare chupi chupi sugopane

Translation:

My Lord, do appear before me.
Appear in the very depth of my heart.
Appear in my sleep and my dream.
I shall offer You my entire existence.
I shall please you in silence-secrecy supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ashar duar, part 2, , 2004
