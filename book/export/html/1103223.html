5 December 1998

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme,
The human life is not for fun.
The human life is for a constant
And continuous run
Towards the Golden Shore.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Hawaii. 5 December 1998, 6:16 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. one-hundred times with two arms and one-hundred and one times with one arm. (50 right, 51 left in five sets)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 2, Agni Press, 1999
