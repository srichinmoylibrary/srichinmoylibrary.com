411. My dry soul

My dry soul,
How can God manifest Himself
In and through you?

My dry heart,
How can God love you
And claim you as His very own?

My dry life,
How can God dare
To breathe in you?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
