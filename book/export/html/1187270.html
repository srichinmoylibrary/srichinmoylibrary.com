1618

You have been accepting
Temptation's invitation
For such a long time
And not accepting
God's Invitation.
Do you not think
That it would be nice of you
To accept God's Invitation
At least once?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 17, Agni Press, 1983
