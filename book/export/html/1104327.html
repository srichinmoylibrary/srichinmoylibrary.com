22 July 2000 (I)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
I do not strain every nerve
To please You.
I just surrender myself to You
Unconditionally all the time,
And You are fully satisfied with me
And mightily proud of me.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

22 July 2000, 8:11 a.m. Before practising for 800-lb. dumbbell bench press.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 15, Agni Press, 2000
