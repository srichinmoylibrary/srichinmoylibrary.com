566. I have much news

Faith, my faith,
Since you left
I have no news,
None at all.

Doubt, my doubt,
Since you left
I have much news.
God has come
And
Is staying with me.
He is even thinking of hiring me
To look after His vast creation.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 12, Agni Press, 1974
