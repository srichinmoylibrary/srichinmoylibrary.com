200. My play is done

My play is done.
Nothing more to give,
Nothing more to receive,
Nothing more to grow,
Nothing more to achieve.
My play is done.

My play is done.
I have become
What I was:
Freedom-sky,
Silence-sea.
My play is done.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 4, Agni Press, 1974
