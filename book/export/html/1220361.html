Nirabe Janabo Pranati

Nirabe janabo pranati
Nirabe phutabo bhakati
Nirabe sanpibo akuti
Shuno shuno mor minati

Translation:

[Partial translation]
In silence I shall bow to You.
In silence my devotion-flower
Will be blossomed...

From: Sri Chinmoy, Consciousness-sky, , 1983
