113. O sweet ones

O Sweet Lord,
   Forget me not
In my wanderings far and wide.

O sweet Heaven,
   Ignore not
The outpouring of my heart.

O sweet earth,
   Underestimate not
The eternal voyager in me.

Lord, we two are one.
Heaven, we are of each other's Dream-boat.
Earth, we are for each other's Reality-shore.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 3, Agni Press, 1973
