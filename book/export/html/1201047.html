15259

Why waste your time
By reasoning
With your darkness-mind?
Can you not spend your time
In a most effective way
By loving and utilising your heart
More, ever more?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 153, Agni Press, 1991
