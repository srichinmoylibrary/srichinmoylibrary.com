6. No sin, no virtue

When sin plagues him
He cries aloud.
When virtue plagues him
He cries aloud.
His Lord today tells him:
"No sin, no virtue, My son.
Only think that
I experience the life-breath
Of every moment
In a different way,
That's all."

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 5, Agni Press, 1974
