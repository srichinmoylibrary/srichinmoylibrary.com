3372

O my Lord Supreme,
What I have for You
Is only a tiny heart of gratitude.
Therefore
Do not expect much from me.
If You expect more from me,
I shall certainly fail You,
And I know You do not deserve that.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 34, Agni Press, 1984
