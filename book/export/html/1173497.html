569 Something Else, Something More

There was a time
When I was ignorance-night.
Something else:
Life's death-revolver.

Now I have grown
Into wisdom-day.
Something more:
Life's death-solver.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 6, Agni Press, 1979
