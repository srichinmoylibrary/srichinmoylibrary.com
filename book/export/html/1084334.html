2. What God has made me

My body desired to be
A great runner.
But God has made me
A God-lover.
My vital desired to be
A great warrior.
But God has made me
A God-lover.
My mind desired to be
A great thinker.
But God has made me
A God-lover.
My heart desired to be
A God-lover.
But God has made me
Another God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 8, Agni Press, 1974
