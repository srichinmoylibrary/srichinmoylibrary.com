7. Look at them!

Two greedy eyes
Want to devour
God's infinite Love.
Look at their stupid audacity!
Two speedy feet
Want to reach
God's endless Goal.
Look at their deplorable stupidity!
One needy heart
Wants to feel God's
Compassion-flood.
Look at its miserable incapacity!

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 13, Agni Press, 1974
