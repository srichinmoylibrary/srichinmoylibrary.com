21. I love patience

I love patience
Because
It is mild.
I love patience
Because
It is sweet.
I love patience
Because
It is subtle.
I love patience
Because
It is enduring.
I love patience
Because
It overcomes all the buffets of my life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My promise to God, Agni Press, 1975
