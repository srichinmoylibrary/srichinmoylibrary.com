73. Centuries have rolled away

Centuries have rolled away,
And still the outer man does not know
Where the inner man
Unmistakably is,
And the inner man does not know
What the outer man
Actually wants.

From: Sri Chinmoy, 207 Flower-Flames, Agni Press,
