Kande Hiya Toma Lagi

Kande hiya toma lagi
   Sei mor sukh
Khane khane dekha peye
   Ghuche jai dukh
Tumi achho ami achi
   Ar nai keha
Shudhu ek pran ar
   Ache dui deha

Translation:

My heart cries for You;
Therefore I am happy.
Your presence banishes all my sorrow’s night.
When I obey you, I alone exist
And nobody else.
Two outer frames with one
Soul-existence life.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Supreme, teach me how to surrender, , 1975
