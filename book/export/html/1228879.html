Kare Rakhi Dure

Kare rakhi dure
Kare rakhi kache janina
Jani shudhu hai
Param pitare manina

Translation:

Some I keep far away,
While others I keep very close.
I do not know why.
Alas, I only know that I do not obey
My Father Lord Supreme.

From: Sri Chinmoy, One thousand lotus petals, part 2, , 2000
