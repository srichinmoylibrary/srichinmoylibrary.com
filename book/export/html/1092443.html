2.

My problem is this:
I cannot think
Of God
Even for seven minutes a day.
God's problem is this:
He cannot stop thinking
Of me
Even for seven seconds a day.

My problem is this:
I do not want to know
How great God is,
As long as He is kind to me.

God's problem is this:
He does not want to know
How undivine I am,
As long as I call Him my Father.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Morning prayers and poems on war, Agni Press, 1992
