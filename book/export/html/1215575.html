17. On the hilltop of sanctity

Yesterday I stood
On the hilltop of sanctity,
But I fell down.
Today I am standing
On the hilltop of sanctity.
Alas, I am frightened to death.
Tomorrow I shall stand
On the hilltop of sanctity,
Only to ascend infinitely higher.

From: Sri Chinmoy, When I left God in Heaven, Agni Press, 1975
