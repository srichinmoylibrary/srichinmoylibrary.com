611. Let Me Sail

Once more let me sail
My life-boat,
Let me sail.
This time I can never fail
And I shall never fail.
Success will definitely be mine.
Through progress-light
I shall shine.

From: Sri Chinmoy, A soulful cry versus a fruitful smile, Agni Press, 1977
