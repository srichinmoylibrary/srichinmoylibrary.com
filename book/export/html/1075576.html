995. I am proud of You

Lord Jesus,
This is Mother-Earth speaking:
I am proud of You
Because
You showed me
Your God-Power
For three years.

I am grateful to You
Because
You suffered for me
On the Cross
For three hours.

I am full of You
Because
You stayed with me
For thirty-three years.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 20, Agni Press, 1973
