Spider: Patience

Spider, my spider,
You build your home
With patience-light.
Against the speedy time
You and your confidence-fight.
You teach the world
What patience can do:
Build perfection's kingdom
pure and true.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Animal Kingdom, Agni Press, 1973
