May 9

The body is loveless. The vital is shameless. The mind is faithless. The heart is lifeless. Nevertheless our journey is hopeful, our soul is meaningful and our goal is fruitful. This is possible because the Supreme Pilot is within us and for us.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Arise! Awake! Thoughts of a yogi, Frederick Fell, Inc., 1972
