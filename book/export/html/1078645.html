998 To Think Of God...

TO THINK OF GOD

To think of God
Is to live in God's Kingdom.

To pray to God
Is to live in His Palace.

To meditate on God
Is to sit on His Throne.

To love God
Is to sing and dance in His Heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
