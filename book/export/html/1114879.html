1479

My Lord, when I do not please You
  In Your own Way,
Do make me feel only one thing:
  That I am a mere nothing.
This feeling of nothingness
  Will wake me up.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 2, Agni Press, 1998
