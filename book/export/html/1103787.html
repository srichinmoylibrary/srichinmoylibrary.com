19 September 1999 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
The moment I pray to You
Soulfully and self-givingly
For world peace,
You immediately promise me
An undivided, happy, illumining
And fulfilling oneness-world.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

19 September 1999, 9:06 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. with both arms simultaneously three times. (Total: 600 lbs.)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 9, Agni Press, 1999
