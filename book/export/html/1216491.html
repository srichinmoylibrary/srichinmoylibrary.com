854. How it ended

My animal life,
How it ended:
It ended when mind
Knocked at its door.

My human life,
How it ended:
It ended when love
Knocked at its door.

My divine life,
How it began:
It began when oneness,
Universal oneness,
Was released from my
Transcendental Being.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 18, Agni Press, 1974
