3 My Lord ...

My Lord,
Do You not have any Secret
To share with me?
“My child,
I have many, many Secrets
To share with you,
But the time has not come.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 54, Agni Press, 2007
