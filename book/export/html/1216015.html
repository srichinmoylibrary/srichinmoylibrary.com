388. Where are you?

O Lamb of God,
Where are You?
My eyes say You are not on earth,
My mind says You are not in Heaven.

O Lamb of God,
Where are you?
My heart is fed by Your Promise-light,
My soul is fulfilled by Your Perfection-height.

O Lamb of God,
Where are You?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings Of Light, Part 8, Agni Press, 1974
