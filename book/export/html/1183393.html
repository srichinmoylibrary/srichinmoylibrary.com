257. The difference

The difference
Between
You and me is this:
You fast on earth
And
You feast in Heaven.

I feast on earth
And
I fast in Heaven.

I feel
Earth-food: fear, doubt, jealousy,
Are most delicious.
You feel
Heaven-food: peace, light and bliss
Are most delicious.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
