Death Has a Message for You

Death has a message for you:
“Do not try. Just cry.”
Life has a message for you:
“Do not give up. Continue.”
God has a message for you:
“Smile, smile, always smile.”

From: Sri Chinmoy, My complete God-surrender, , 1995
