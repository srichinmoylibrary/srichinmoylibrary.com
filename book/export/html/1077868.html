221. Pomp and wealth

Earth-friend,
Here is the difference
Between
Your pomp of wealth
And
Your wealth of pomp:
Your pomp of wealth
Frightens even an emperor;
Your wealth of pomp
Is not deserved even by a beggar.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
