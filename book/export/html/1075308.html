732 When...

WHEN

When
I look through
The eyes of life,
I feel cheerful.

When
I look through
The eyes of death,
I feel peaceful.

When
I look through
The eyes of earth,
I feel meaningful.

When
I look through
The eyes of Heaven,
I feel soulful.

Finally,
When
I look through
The Eyes of God,
I feel fruitful.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
