Prabhu Prabhu Bale

Prabhu prabhu bale
Jurai amar pran
Prabhu sathe gahi sada
Dharar mukti gan

Translation:

I sing my Lord’s Name.
I pray to Him.
He grants me,
My vital life,
Purity’s breath.
I celebrate the liberation-song
Of the world.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Illumination-song and liberation-dance, part 5, , 1976
