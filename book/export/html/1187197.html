1546

The chosen instruments
Of the Lord Supreme
Will always feel
That they are not indispensable.
The very moment
They feel that they are indispensable
Is the beginning
Of their spiritual downfall.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 16, Agni Press, 1983
