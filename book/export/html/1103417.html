27 April 1999 (VII)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
   My faith-life
You have lovingly, compassionately
   And unconditionally
Kept inside Your very Breath.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

27 April 1999, 9:12 p.m. Before lifting 230 lbs. three times with both arms simultaneously. (Total: 460 lbs.) New record.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 4, Agni Press, 1999
