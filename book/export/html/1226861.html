Bhitar Jibane Prashanti Parabar

Bhitar jibane prashanti parabar
Bahir jibane ahakar anibar

Translation:

In the inner life you have
The ocean of peace.
In the outer life
You are crying desperately.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kalpana rath (imagination chariot), , 1999
