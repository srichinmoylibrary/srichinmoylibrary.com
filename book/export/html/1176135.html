3181 I Am Determined

I am determined to see something
Behind me.
What is it?
My failure-life.

I am determined to see something
Before me.
What is it?
My purity-heart.

I am determined to see something
Beyond me.
What is it?
My perfect perfection-soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 32, Agni Press, 1982
