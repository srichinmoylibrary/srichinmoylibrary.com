My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 35

Editor's note

Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Qingdao, China in December 2004.

21 December 2004

Qingdao, China

1 Death Is Not ...

Death is not
The end.
Death is
Our silence-friend.

2 Death ...

Death
Has an end.
Life
Is an eternal flow.

3 My Lord Tells Me ...

My Lord tells me
That my God-obedience
Is far beyond all boundaries.

4 God’s Justice-Eye ...

God’s Justice-Eye
Quite often
Takes a vacation.

5 The Complacent Life ...

The complacent life
Of a spiritual seeker
Is extremely destructive.

6 Sincere Aspiration-Seekers ...

Sincere aspiration-seekers
Always succeed.

7 A God-Obedience-Life ...

A God-obedience-life
Is an inner beauty
And an outer beauty as well.

8 God’s Compassion-Train ...

God’s Compassion-Train
Carries to the Destination
Only those who have implicit faith
In God’s Compassion.

9 God Wants Every Seeker ...

God wants every seeker
To be for the whole world
And for all time.

10 My Cheerful Surrender ...

My cheerful surrender
To God’s Will
Is an express train
To God’s Feet.

11 Our Aspiration-Moments ...

Our aspiration-moments
Expedite
Our God-arrival.

12 Remember ...

Remember,
Your heart-beauty-smile
Is for all.

13 Cheerfulness ...

Cheerfulness
Is
A most remarkable beginning.

14 Never Allow ...

Never allow
Your aspiration-speed
To slow down.

15 A Heart-Love Finds Itself ...

A heart-love finds itself
In the heart
Of everybody’s love.

22 December 2004 ...

22 December 2004

Qingdao, China

16 Life Is Not ...

Life is not
A helpless cry.
Life is
A brave journey.

17 Inside My Self-Reliance ...

Inside my self-reliance,
Uncertainty
Looms large.

18 Inside My God-Reliance ...

Inside my God-reliance,
My soul rings
My victory-bell.

19 If You Do Not Want To Be Homeless ...

If you do not want to be homeless
In your spiritual life,
Then consciously place your heart
And your life-breath
In the Heart-Home
Of your Lord Supreme.

20 Our God-Security ...

Our God-security
Lives at two places:
Inside our soul-heights
And inside our heart-depths.

23 December 2004 ...

23 December 2004

Qingdao, China

21 God Says To My Heart ...

God says to my heart,
“Welcome, welcome,
My aspiration-child!”

22 My Faith Can Open Up ...

My faith can open up
God’s Heart-Door
At any time.

23 Do Not Blame Anybody ...

Do not blame anybody
For this world.
The world is entirely
God’s Creation.

24 Everything That Is Good ...

Everything that is good
I cherish.

25 Everything That Is Bad ...

Everything that is bad
I consciously and deliberately
Leave behind.

26 The Way Misfortune Dogs Us ...

The way misfortune dogs us,
Can we not dog the divine ideas
In exactly the same way?

27 The Desire-World ...

The desire-world
Is always empty
Of reality.

28 My realisation-elevator ...

My realisation-elevator

Reaches the highest floor,
Where God accumulates
All His world-transformation-Dreams.

29 I Am A Proud ...

I am a proud
God’s Compassion-Feet-beggar.

30 God Does Not Appreciate Me ...

God does not appreciate me
When I say that His Creation
Is beyond repair.

31 My Prayer ...

My prayer
Is
My life-song.

32 My Meditation ...

My meditation
Is
My heart-sunlight.

33 The World Is Full ...

The world is full
Of one thing:
Ignorance-prisons.

34 My Gratitude-Heart ...

My gratitude-heart
Is
My soul-beauty’s flower.

35 War Breaks ...

War breaks.
God cries.

36 God’s Favourite Child, Peace ...

God’s favourite child, Peace,
Consoles God
By promising a new world
Empty of war.

37 How Long Can My Mind Hide ...

How long can my mind hide
My Lord’s Victory-Banner —
How long?

38 My Desire ...

My desire
Wants
World-attractions.

39 My False Aspiration ...

My false aspiration
Wants
World-attention.

40 My Sincere Aspiration ...

My sincere aspiration
Desires
God-Affection.

41 I desire God’s Manifestation-fulfilment-determination ...

I desire God’s Manifestation-fulfilment-determination.

42 Aspiration Goes Up ...

Aspiration goes up
High, higher, highest.

43 Transformation Goes ...

Transformation goes
Around and around,
Here, there and everywhere.

44 Disobedience ...

Disobedience
Is a seeker’s
Spiritual death-cancer.

45 The Negativity-Mind ...

The negativity-mind
Rightly deserves
An immediate grave.

46 Do Not Worry ...

Do not worry —
God will return to your heart.
Just be careful this time!

47 I Dearly Love ...

I dearly love
My soul’s constant
God-fulfilling dreams.

48 How I Wish ...

How I wish
I could be
One choice dream
Of my Lord Supreme!

49 I Wish To Become ...

I wish to become
A genuine God-lover
And God-server.

50 God Wants Me To Be ...

God wants me to be
Nothing other than
His sleepless and breathless partner.

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, Part 35, Agni Press, 2005
