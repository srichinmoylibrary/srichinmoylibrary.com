4849

Only a spiritual Master
Of the highest height
Can tell you where you are,
  How far you will go
And how long it will take you
To arrive at your final Destination.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 5, Agni Press, 1998
