Silence speaks, part 8

1.

You say your mistakes
Are all unintentional.
Should God then grant you
Pardon unconditional?

2.

You alone have made
The desire-choice.
Now you alone have to
Silence its noise.

3.

Delight you will feel
Only in self-mastery.
Oneness-song you will sing
Only with God-discovery.

4.

About yourself care
Only for God's Opinion:
You are His loving
And faithful companion.

5.

Do not doubt yourself,
Do not, I say!
Go and join once more
God's Oneness-Play.

6.

Your climbing heart-cry,
O never neglect,
If your life-smile
You long to perfect.

7.

Impossible today,
Inevitable tomorrow.
This is how man's
God-dream-seeds grow.

8.

When you mind becomes one
With your heart's obedience-light,
Yours will be the fastest
Heavenward journey's flight.

9.

Self-doubt is poison-night.
Self-doubt is destruction-gun.
Therefore, do not take part in
Your mind's long self-doubt-run.

10.

Confess your blunders
Cheerfully.
God will reach out
Powerfully.

11.

God does not want to,
But He does withdraw
When your sad earth-life
Becomes a giant flaw.

12.

Inspiration-bird flies
With beauty's wings.
Aspiration-flames mount,
Progress-bell rings.

13.

What the future holds
I do not know,
But my heart foresees
God-oneness-glow.

14.

Opportunity-flowers
Are blossoming at every moment.
Pick them at once!
Delay not your heart's enlightenment.

15.

You are carrying your life's
Heavy lethargy-load.
Yet you want to walk
On God's Perfection-Road.

16.

If your mind dreams always
Of human desire,
How can your life expect
Liberation-fire?

17.

If you are a lover
Of innocence-light,
How can you travel
In ignorance-night?

18.

Live by your inmost
Soul-sincerity
To become your Lord's
Sun-Reality.

19.

He enjoys his life's
Self-giving marathon.
Therefore, God gives him
His Satisfaction-Dawn.

20.

When his life becomes
All luminosity,
The seeker becomes
God's Heart-profundity.

21.

Your inner faith, the Blessing-Grace
Of the Lord Supreme
To help you quickly grow into
His Self-Transcendence-Dream.

22.

Your wasted years are running out
Of aspiration-time.
Are you content to never see
God's Satisfaction-Clime?

23.

If you read every day
Your mind's guilt-record,
How will you ever think
Of pleasing your Lord?

24.

Patience and tolerance â€”
Two lessons in life
Which save human souls
From destruction-knife.

25.

A prayer-life
Is a Heavenly passage.
A silence-heart
Is God's Oneness-Message.

26.

O sail your God-hunger-boat,
Every day sail,
Until God's own Hand lifts your
Ignorance-veil.

27.

Alas, you are your clever mind's
Mere aspiration-act.
Therefore, you will ever miss
God's Sweetness-Smile-Contact.

28.

Your silence-heart
Will grant you peace of mind.
Your oneness-soul
Will conquer turmoil blind.

29.

When my mind sees the world,
It sees only God's Face.
When my heart feels the world,
It feels His quick Embrace.

30.

A prayer-heart
Is a divine opportunity.
A meditation-life
Is God's Silence-Continuity.

31.

What humility is
If you truly know,
Then your life and deeds
Are bound to glow.

32.

God smilingly loves
Even sad failures.
His powerful Smile
With them endures.

33.

I know, I know,
I am alive today
Because my heart
Enjoys devotion-play.

34.

O my mind's wild
Desire-fire,
Today at last
You must expire.

35.

Your Lord Supreme
Created your soul
To be a partner of His Dream.
Your heart must please Him
In His own Way
To satisfy His soulful Scheme.

36.

My Lord Supreme
Is His Compassion-Tower.
My life shall be
His choice Heart-Garden flower.

37.

Be a beautiful, soulful
Surrender-infant.
God will grant your prayers
His Answers instant.

38.

God has forgiven
Your shameless meanness.
Can you not give Him
Your whole life's oneness?

39.

God's Heart of Oneness-Love
You have refused.
Therefore, you and your life
Are all confused.

40.

The human life is short.
The human mind is insecure.
The divine life is eternal.
The divine mind is pure and sure.

41.

Think not of your days of success
Nor of your failure-nights.
Just meditate and meditate
On your progress-delights.

42.

Each and every sweet dream-smile
Increases fast our true God-love
And makes us better God-servers
Within, without, below, above.

43.

Do not give up, do not give up!
Fight until the last.
You and your soul will triumph
Even in the dust!

44.

The mind attacks others
And then tries to defend itself.
The heart surrenders to God
And becomes the Universal Self.

45.

With God you found
A precious oneness-moment.
The world will build
Your perfection-monument.

46.

If you want to serve
The life of humanity,
First feel the heart
Of Compassion-Divinity.

47.

If you do not climb up
The aspiration-tree,
How can you enjoy
Your heart-life's ecstasy?

48.

You want to know where
Is my Beloved Supreme?
He is living inside
My heart's innocence-dream.

49.

With your mind's dauntless strength
The outer world face.
With your heart's oneness-love
The inner world embrace.

50.

My Lord's Compassion-Eye
Patrols my life's heart-door.
Therefore, I never hear
The world's ignorance-roar.

51.

If you keep your dreams pure
Both by night and by day,
You will soon be invited
With Divinity to play.

52.

To please your soul,
Day and night pray.
To please your Lord,
Avoid ignorance-clay.

53.

To feel His Grace
In you, you must
In God keep always
A determined trust.

54.

I tell you, my friend,
You are inwardly blind.
Therefore, God's Love-Light
You never will find.

55.

Alas, you have neglected
The Lord for man.
Your ignorance has ruined
His Vision-Plan.

56.

If you follow your mind's
Dark and unclean thought-road,
You will have to carry
Futility's great load.

57.

God knows your capacity's
Ultimate length.
You must learn for yourself
Your present strength.

58.

The right time to enjoy
Aspiration-fast: never!
The right time to enjoy
Surrender-feast: ever!

59.

O my pure and powerful
Morning meditation,
You are my entire day's
Perfect salvation.

60.

Your true longing for
Purity's newness
Is the first step toward
Your Lord's Forgiveness.

61.

Every night God empties
Your darkness-mind.
To His Compassion-Light
Be not so blind!

62.

Confidence has to be
Strong and sure.
Surrender has to be
Sweet and pure.

63.

The mind's long road
Leads to God's Justice-Light.
The heart's short road
Leads to God's Oneness-Height.

64.

Before you and God
Meet face to face,
Your dark ego-life
You must erase.

65.

A man of compromise
Is a real fool!
Impossible for him
To attend God-School.

66.

What you wanted:
An expectation-road.
What you now have:
A frustration-load.

67.

All out of proportion
Is your dark ego-life.
Soon you will be stabbed
By self-destruction-knife.

68.

The strength of harmony
Is admirable.
The power of peace
Is unfathomable.

69.

You will someday escape from
Your taut bondage-chain
If you soulfully welcome
Enthusiasm-reign.

70.

From world-competition
If you bravely refrain,
You can ride constantly
The self-transcendence-train.

71.

Now that you have put God
In full command of your life,
You cannot be threatened
By the world's destruction-knife.

72.

Who can fulfil God's
Compassion-Messages
If he has not studied
God's Love-Book-pages?

73.

Do not follow
The desire-man.
He belongs to
The peril-clan.

74.

God the Compassion
Is always adorable.
God the Forgiveness
Is always approachable.

75.

Only one thing, O seeker,
Worth proving:
That you are constantly
Improving!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Silence speaks, part 8, Agni Press, 1994
