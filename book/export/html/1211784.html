Wisdom of the ancients:
Give up everything
For the sake of God.
Wisdom of the moderns:
Since God has everything
At His disposal,
Let me have a few minor things
From Him.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 259, Agni Press, 1998
