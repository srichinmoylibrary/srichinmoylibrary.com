244. A lavish doubter

When I was a lavish dreamer
God loved me.

When I was a lavish promiser
God loved me.

When I was a lavish lover
God loved me.

When I became a lavish doubter
God forgave me,
Earth hated me,
Heaven ignored me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
