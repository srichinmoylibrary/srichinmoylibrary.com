292. We grope

You grope to grasp;
Therefore
Your vital is excessively proud of you.

He gropes to last;
Therefore
His mind is supremely proud of him.

I grope to clasp;
Therefore
My heart is eternally proud of me.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
