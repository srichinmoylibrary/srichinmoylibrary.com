26. I keep sentinel

I keep sentinel
Over my loveless words;
Therefore
My heart is nearing
My Lord's Perfection-Door.
I keep sentinel
Over my black thoughts;
Therefore
My mind is nearing
My Lord's Perfection-Door.
I keep sentinel
Over my teeming desires;
Therefore
My vital is nearing
My Lord's Perfection-Door.
I keep sentinel
Over my Godless deeds;
Therefore
My body is nearing
My Lord's Perfection-Door.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 10, Agni Press, 1974
