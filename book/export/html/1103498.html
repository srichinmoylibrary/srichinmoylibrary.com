1 June 1999 (III)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
May my ever-blossoming faith-heart
Always remain inaccessible
To my doubting mind.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

1 June 1999, 7:07 a.m. Before lifting 300 lbs. fifty-seven times with one arm. (28 right, 29 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 6, Agni Press, 1999
