815 A Common Weakness...

A COMMON WEAKNESS

A common weakness:
To love ignorance.

A common weakness:
To doubt the existence of the soul.

A common weakness:
To forget God.

A common weakness:
To exaggerate Heaven-bliss.

A common weakness:
To ignore earth-beauty.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
