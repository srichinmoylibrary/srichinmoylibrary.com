15. O come and sing with me

O come and sing with me
O come and sing with me
I shall give You, I shall give You
My love and all my beauty free.
O come and dance with me
O come and dance with me
I shall give You, I shall give You
My breath and my life of nectar-sea.
O come and cry with me
Cry with me, cry with me
O come and cry with me
Cry with me, cry with me
O Beloved, O Beloved
O Lover, Lover sweet
We shall shine, we shall shine
Through Eternity.

From: Sri Chinmoy, God then, God now, Agni Press, 1971
