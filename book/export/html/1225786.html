Dibar Matan Kichui Karini Dan Tabu Ja

Dibar matan kichui karini dan
Tabu ja diyechi phiriye diyona prabhu
Phiriye diyona mor hasi bhara mukh
Phiriye diyona mor khaniker sukh
Phiriye diyona mor nayaner jal
Phiriye diyona mor praner garal

From: Sri Chinmoy, Journey's goal songbook, part 13, , 1981
