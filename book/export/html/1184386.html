58. Retrogression

Yesterday
You had the strong desire
To please God.

Today
You have the strong desire
To see God.

Tomorrow
You will have the strong desire
To become another God
Without pleasing, without seeing God,
Alas, alas!

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
