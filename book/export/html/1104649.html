9 February 2006 (II)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

Today I am extremely happy
Because I have pleased my Lord Supreme
In His own Way.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 21, Agni Press, 2006
