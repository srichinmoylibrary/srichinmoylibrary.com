1119

I prayed to my Lord Supreme
For power.
My Supreme Lord has given me Love
Instead of power.
His Love has not only made
My heart powerful
But also is making
My life perfect.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 12, Agni Press, 1983
