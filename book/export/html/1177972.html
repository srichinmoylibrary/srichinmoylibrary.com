5000 When I Want To Surrender

When I want to think
God grants my mind
A large room to think.

When I want to pray
God grants my heart
A larger room to pray.

When I want to surrender
God grants my life
The largest room to surrender.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 50, Agni Press, 1982
