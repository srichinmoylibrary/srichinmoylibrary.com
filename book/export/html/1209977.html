Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 242

24101

The real happiness
Is an insatiable hunger
For sleepless God-Satisfaction
In God's own Way.

24102

My mind waits
For the return of God's Power.
My heart waits
For the return of God's Peace.

24103

Our Lord Supreme tells us
That He wants to be sought
And not to be bought.

24104

The surrender beyond all surrenders
Is our constant willingness
To please God in His own Way.

24105

God-Compassion
Is necessary.
God-obedience
Is more than indispensable.

24106

Expectation and despair
Always move together,
Although they do not care for each other
At all.

24107

God has been waiting
For a long, long time
To greet my eagerness-heart.

24108

There are many ways
To make God smile,
But the easiest way
Is to tell God sincerely,
"You are my All,
My Eternity's All."

24109

There are many enemies I have,
But I feel that expectation
Is my worst enemy.

24110

A heart of faith
Is not a fairytale.
A heart of faith
Is the beauty and perfection
Of the angelic world.

24111

It is simply impossible to create
An anxiety-worry-free territory
Inside one's mind.

24112

A transformation-life
Is within the reach
Of every human being.

24113

Today we call it
The darkest night.
Tomorrow that very thing
We will call the brightest dawn.

24114

Gratitude
Is the very breath
Of our constant love of God.

24115

God asks us
Not to think of the Goal.
He just asks us
To take a first step.

24116

God has quite a few
Closely-guarded secrets.
The most important one
Is man's unexpected total transformation.

24117

A goalless seeker
Will eventually have frustration
As his sad finish.

24118

If you want to be another God,
Then renounce your life
Of pleasure-illusion.

24119

Each and every human being
Will realise God eventually.
But if one wants to expedite
One's God-journey,
Then one has to take God-choice
As the first and foremost choice.

24120

The art of self-giving
Is, indeed, a supreme promoter
Of God-manifestation
Here on earth.

24121

God the Compassion
Knows when to caress our hearts.
God the Justice
Knows when to smite our minds.

24122

God never justifies Himself,
For He knows that in the end
We are bound to justify Him ourselves.

24123

What is enmity?
Enmity is nothing other than
The inner wrestling of human beings.

24124

O my mind,
No matter how many times
You refuse to be transformed,
There shall come a time
When you are bound to be transformed —
Of course, at God's choice Hour!

24125

To strengthen our capacities
We need assistance from two divine helpers:
A sympathy-heart
And an encouragement-hand.

24126

My love of God
Has no need of Heaven's Bliss,
For my love of God
Itself is the highest Bliss.

24127

When we aspire
Most sincerely and most soulfully,
We come to realise
That there is no such thing
As an unattainable goal.

24128

God tells the human mind,
"My ignorance-child,
You are hating the world today,
But tomorrow I shall change your hatred
Into amusement-laughter."

24129

Although we may not agree,
According to God
Everything a genuine God-lover has within
Is lovable.

24130

Each good thought
Immediately opens
The gate of inner strength.

24131

The shortest shortcut
To Heaven's highest Height
Is our sleepless and breathless self-giving
To the Will of our Lord Supreme.

24132

My Lord tells me
That for the fastest progress
In my spiritual life
I have no need for the past or the future.

24133

When God clothes Himself
In a physical body,
He is not only misunderstood
But also ridiculed
By humanity.

24134

My Lord Supreme,
I pray to You to bless my gratitude-heart-bird
With the strongest wings
To fly at every moment
In the sky of Your Compassion-Light.

24135

Do not try to bribe Fate;
You will fail!
Do not try to chase Fate;
You will fail!
Try to conquer Fate;
You will succeed,
For success is your ultimate birthright.

24136

God within me
Is not the critic
But the audience,
Filled with curiosity and concern.

24137

Nobility in the mind
And generosity in the heart
Are two most essential friends
That make our spiritual progress
Faster than the fastest.

24138

What God finds difficult to pardon
Is not our misdeeds
But our unwillingness to confess.

24139

My Lord,
Do not allow my tiny aspiration-sun
Either to fall asleep
Or to be eclipsed!

24140

O my inspiration-mind,
O my aspiration-heart,
I want you both to work together.
Never quarrel, never!

24141

God the Justice we can never bribe —
No, not even God the Compassion.
Why?
Because He has not yet developed
That weakness.

24142

God does not like to be
On the side of the past.
He does not like to be
On the side of the future either.
He likes only to be
On the side of the present.

24143

My Lord Supreme,
Keep me awake all the time
So that I can fulfil You
Whenever Your Command comes.

24144

My Lord,
I do not want to remain
Inside any man-made pocket.
I want to be always
Inside Your Heart-Pocket.

24145

There is nothing
That is not within the reach
Of an aspiring human being.

24146

Problems there are,
But they do not have to exist
Any longer
If we make our surrender
Absolutely complete.

24147

The darkest nights of the mind
Must eventually surrender
To the illumination
Of the brightest dawns.

24148

My Lord Supreme,
I am offering my gratitude-breath
To Your Eternity's Patience-Life.

24149

Upon mankind's obedience-rock
Only
Can a peace-world ever be built.

24150

Promise
Is only a first step,
And not the goal.

24151

Today's God-realisation
Will definitely add to
Tomorrow's God-manifestation.

24152

Two inconstant realities:
My self-doubt
And my self-assertion.

24153

I am precious
In God's Eyes
Only when my heart becomes
Sleeplessly gracious.

24154

As century after century dawns,
Humanity's heart
Will eventually awaken
To Divinity's Light.

24155

The day I made
My unconditional surrender complete,
I was embraced by God
With all His Enthusiasm-Pride.

24156

We get joy
In keeping some secrets,
But God does not subscribe
To our philosophy.
He keeps no secrets
From His close associates:
Self-giving God-lovers.

24157

Alas,
I never knew how beautiful
My God-surrender-tears would be!

24158

As it requires
Full-time devotion
For mankind to please God,
Even so it requires
Full-time attention
For God to please mankind.

24159

The mind, vital and body of those
Who do not want to be
Acquainted with God's Will
Will eventually sink into the abysmal abyss.

24160

Those who have
Invaluable inner insights
Will have the power
To help transform man into God.

24161

The transformation
Of a single bad thought
Can be the manifestation
Of something divine, absolute
And supreme.

24162

Our heart's cries
Are our special messengers
From our soul to God.

24163

When I am determined
To have God-choice
As my only choice,
My goal does not remain
A far cry anymore.

24164

My Lord,
Do tell me the choice Hour
That You have selected
To liberate me
From unending bubbles of thought.

24165

My Lord,
Will we ever realise
The gravity of our spiritual disaster
When we criticise You
And do not see eye to eye with You?

24166

The choicest Blessing-Smile of God
Descended upon him
When he remained unmoved
By world-appreciation
And unperturbed by world-deprecation.

24167

No, not even hell
Can smash a gratitude-heart
That wants to please God always
In His own Way.

24168

Our aspiration-thirst
God will gladly quench
At His choice Hour.
But our ego's thirst
Definitely He will not quench
Permanently.

24169

Peace will be
By far the best promoter
Of God-manifestation on earth.

24170

Be not caught
In your self-criticism-net.
Be not caught
In your world-criticism-net.

24171

To have a good idea
In the mind,
And to perform a better action
From the heart,
Will definitely expedite
Our journey towards our goal.

24172

The best way to liberate ourselves
From the confusion-mind
Is to live inside
The cries of our heart.

24173

Every morning
I get tremendous satisfaction
When I look at the delight-rainbow-smiles
Of the dawn.

24174

We must offer
Our gratitude-heart to God
For our every progress-breath.

24175

We must transcend
Our inferiority feelings
At every moment
To be one with
The ever-transcending Reality.

24176

The ignorance-mind plays
Win-and-lose games.
The wisdom-heart plays
Only oneness-game.

24177

Right from the very beginning
Conquer your small worries and anxieties.
Otherwise, they can grow into
Very serious problems.

24178

O my Lord Supreme,
Do grant me one single boon.
Do liberate me
From my human one-track mind
That always runs towards
Ignorance-destruction.

24179

If we act from the very depths
Of our inspiration-heart
Sleeplessly,
Then God is bound to extol our virtues
To the skies.

24180

Wherever there is a oneness-heart,
God the Singer of Joy-Songs
Is available there.

24181

I thrive on my life's
Unending
God-surrender-victories.

24182

What is ignorance?
Ignorance is something
That always tempts us
To run backwards.

24183

God Himself shows our hearts
How to walk along
His Compassion-flooded Path.

24184

Wherever God goes,
He always allows
His Peace and Delight
To accompany Him.

24185

Of all the inner world's hooligans,
Disobedience
Is the worst.

24186

God has invited my soul
To give a lecture on obedience,
And He has compelled
My heart, mind, vital and body
To attend.

24187

When your life is clothed
In obedience and gratitude,
God-realisation will not remain
A far cry.

24188

Only our inner gratitude
Has the capacity
To multiply and magnify
The beauty of our aspiration-hearts.

24189

Because my heart cries
Prayerfully and soulfully,
God always has a soft spot
For my heart.

24190

My Lord Supreme
Again and again tells me
That I came into the world
Only to realise and not to socialise.

24191

My heart always comes first
In my God-expansion-life.
My soul always comes first
In my God-satisfaction-life.

24192

Because you did not
Take God unconditionally,
God cannot
Take your life seriously.

24193

Endless
Is our mind's expectation.
Beginningless and deathless
Is our heart's gratitude.

24194

We empty the mind
So that God can enter.
We fill the heart
To sing God's Glories.

24195

If you want to climb
Surrender-ladder,
You must have the courage
To change your mind.

24196

We compel God to change His Mind
Countless times.
But, poor God!
When He asks us to change our mind
Only once,
We immediately refuse.

24197

My stupid and senseless mind,
No more shall I allow you
To torture my heart-bird.

24198

Aspiration-mountain,
Realisation-mountain
And God-satisfaction-mountain
Do not have any topmost peak.

24199

Every day my soul comes
To unlock my mind-door,
But every day it sadly fails.

24200

If you want to astonish God,
Then on the strength
Of your unconditional surrender,
Open all your heart-petals
As quickly as possible.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 242, Agni Press, 1998
