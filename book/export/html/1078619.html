972 Why Do You Judge?...

WHY DO YOU JUDGE?

Why do you judge
Others' capacities by your own?
If you have more capacity
You will ridicule them
Unnecessarily.
If you have less capacity
You will fail to fathom them
Deplorably.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
