15654

At the end of your life’s journey,
If you can say to the Supreme,
“I came into the world;
I loved You and served You
  In humanity
And I surrendered
To the Inner Pilot
  In humanity,”
Then God will immediately
Embrace you and place you
  On His Throne.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 16, Agni Press, 1999
