3644 Stand Against Your Lower Self

If you do not
Stand against your lower self vehemently,
Then who can and who will?

But if you do stand against your lower self,
Then victory's crown will be all yours
In a twinkling.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 37, Agni Press, 1982
