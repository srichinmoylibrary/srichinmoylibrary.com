26. The only way to please them

Let me weep.
This is the only way
I can please Mother Earth.
Let me smile.
This is the only way
I can please Father Heaven.
Let me become.
This is the only way
I can please
Both
My Father's Head
And
My Mother's Heart.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Sound becomes, silence is, Agni Press, 1975
