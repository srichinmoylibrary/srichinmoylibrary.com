7063 My Heart Knows

My heart knows
It is not liberated.
Therefore, it does not try
To liberate my mind.
But my clever mind
Wants to liberate my restless vital,
In spite of knowing
That it is not liberated itself.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 71, Agni Press, 1983
