25. God-bird

O time-bird,
I want you
And
I need you.

O success-bird,
I need you
And
I need you.

O progress-bird,
Others may need you
But
I never need you.

O God-Bird,
Where are you?
Do you not need me?

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 3, Agni Press, 1974
