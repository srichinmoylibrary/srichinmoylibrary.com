14. Next time

God, I shall be back again.
   Next time when I come
I shall give You my all:
        My surrender.

Son, I shall be back again.
   Next time when I come
I shall give you My All:
        My Smile.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Golden Boat, part 7, Agni Press, 1974
