563 Can't You Surrender?...

CAN'T YOU SURRENDER?

O mind of man,
Do not sleep anymore.
Can't you hear God
Knocking and knocking
At your door?

O heart of man,
Can't you surrender
Your insecurity-drop
To God-Ocean
And
Become the ocean-soul,
Ocean-body
And God-satisfaction?

From: Sri Chinmoy, Europe-Blossoms, Agni Press, 1974
