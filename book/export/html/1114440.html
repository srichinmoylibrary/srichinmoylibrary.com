1040

All the cries of my heart
  Are not the same.
All the smiles of my soul
  Are not the same.
But all the surrender-songs
  Of my life
Are not only the same,
But also all-illumining
  And all-fulfilling.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 2, Agni Press, 1998
