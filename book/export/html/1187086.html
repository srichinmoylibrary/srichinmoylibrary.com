1436

Although I suffered for years
From mind-analysis,
I did nothing to cure
My self-styled mind-patient.
Now to my extreme sorrow
I am stuck with
My heart-paralysis.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, Part 15, Agni Press, 1983
