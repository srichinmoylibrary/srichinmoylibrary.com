927. Earth-bound truth and Heaven-free truth

Do you want to know
The difference
   Between
Earth-bound truth
        And
Heaven-free truth?

Earth-bound truth
Is for me today,
For you tomorrow.
   Heaven-free truth
   Is all balanced,
   Quite impartial
        And
   Always just.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Wings of Light, part 19, Agni Press, 1974
