13 October 1999 (V)

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
God does not know what to do —
Whether to cry or to laugh —
   When I say
That mine is the only way
   To realise God.
My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

13 October 1999, 7:29 a.m. Before lifting 400 lbs. with one arm sixty-seven times. (34 right, 33 left)

From: Sri Chinmoy, My Morning Soul-Body Prayers, part 10, Agni Press, 2000
