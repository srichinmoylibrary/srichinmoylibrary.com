50. Your life

Your aspiration-life grows up
But
Does not grow old.

Your desire-life grows old
But
Does not grow up.

Your God-life ascends and descends:
Ascends to please man,
Descends to please God.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
