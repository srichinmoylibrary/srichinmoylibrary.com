Caroline

Your father is he
Who sits above Space and Time.
Your father is he
Who drinks Nectar with the One.
On his vision-tree
Grows God's Rhythm and Rhyme;
In his sacrifice,
The promise of the brightest Sun.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Kennedy: the universal heart, Agni Press, 1973
