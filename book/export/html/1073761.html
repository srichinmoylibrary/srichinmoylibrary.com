Sub Rosa

(secretly)

I tell you sub rosa-
God will make you
Another God.

I have heard sub rosa
Of your present lack of faith
In your spiritual life.
I would very much love
To assist you
To regain your faith.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Come, my non-English friends! Let us together climb up the English Himalayas, Agni Press, 1988
