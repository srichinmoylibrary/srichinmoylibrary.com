276. My message

No fear!
Hence who can be
My Master, save myself?

No doubt!
Hence who can be
My Saviour, save myself?

God, I am good to You;
God, You are kind to me.
This is my message
For humanity's heart,
For Divinity's Soul.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Goal is won, Agni Press, 1974
