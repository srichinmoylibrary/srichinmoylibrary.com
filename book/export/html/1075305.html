SILENCE, POWER AND LIGHT

Silence is Bliss,
And I have it
In boundless measure.

Power is Creation,
And I have it
In measureless measure.

Light is Perfection,
And I have it
In endless measure.

From: Sri Chinmoy, The Dance of Life, part 15, Agni Press, 1973
