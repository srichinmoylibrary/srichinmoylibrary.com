1514 The Competition

His heart regularly competes
With high Heaven's beauty.
Believe it or not,
His heart always wins.
Go, you can ask God.
God always comes
To watch the competition.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 16, Agni Press, 1981
