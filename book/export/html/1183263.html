127. Are you not tired

Father, are You not tired
Of my life of never-ending weakness?
Daughter, no.
I am only tired of My never-increasing forgiveness.
Father, are You not tired
Of my earth-bound life?
Daughter, no.
I am only tired of My Heaven-free delay.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Three hundred sixty-five Father's Day prayers, Agni Press, 1974
