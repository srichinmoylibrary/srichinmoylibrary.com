217. The realisation of life's mistakes

The realisation
Of life's mistakes
Is painful
But unavoidable.
Today you realise
Your follies;
Tomorrow you will realise
That perfection is what
You wanted
And perfection is what
You undoubtedly have.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
