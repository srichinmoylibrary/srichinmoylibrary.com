105. O my empty heart

O my empty heart,
Do not cry!
God is coming.
True, He is taking time,
But I assure you
This time when He comes
Your constant love for Him
Shall make Him delighted to be back.

From: Sri Chinmoy, Transcendence-Perfection, Agni Press, 1977
