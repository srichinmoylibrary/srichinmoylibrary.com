About the Sri Chinmoy Library

In this page: Notes • Contributors • Copyright • Contact us

  • Sri Chinmoy Library is dedicated to publishing online the writings of Sri Chinmoy in electronic form and making these writings freely available.
  • At the present moment, we have published (approximately) 1,500 books out of (approximately) Sri Chinmoy's 1,600 published books.
  • We are in the process of proof-reading the published books. For the present time, if you wish to republish writings from the website, please double check with the original book.
  • Typographical errors can be reported here.

Notes about Library

Sri Chinmoy, wished his books to be available under a creative commons license (non-commercial, with attribution, non-derivative) You are welcome to republish these writings online, with an active hyperlink back to the source page.

  • For more details: see our copyright notice
  • If you wish to seek permission to use Sri Chinmoy's writings in a commerical capacity: please use our contact form

At the request of Sri Chinmoy, the author:

  • This site will publish faithful reproductions of the original first editions. In some instances, the author, later revised his works from the 1st edition; these revised editions will also be published as well as the originals.
  • The library will use British-English spelling and British-French rules on typography.
